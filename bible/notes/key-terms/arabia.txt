====== Arabia ======

Arabia is the largest peninsula in the world covering nearly 3,00,000 square kilometers. It is between the Red Sea and the Gulf of Aqaba, extending to the Persian Gulf. It consisted of Mount Sinai and modern Saudi Arabia.

===== Facts: =====

Text

  * Arabia is the region where the religion of Islam originated. It may have been the region Abraham's son Ishmael inhabited.
  * The wandering of the Israelites in the desert was in Arabia.
  * Also refers to specific people groups as in Jeremiah 25, Isaiah 21 and Ezekiel 27.
