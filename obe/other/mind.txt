====== mind ======

===== Definition: =====

The invisible, conscious thinking faculty of our brain.  The word "mind" is somtimes used for the word "brain".

  * Man's brain is the physical organ of thinking.
  * The mind of each person is the consummate total of his or her thoughts and reasoning.
  * Christians are said to have the mind of Christ because we are to think about being obediant to the Father, like the obedience of Jesus, and this is accomplished through the enabling of God's Holy Spirit.
