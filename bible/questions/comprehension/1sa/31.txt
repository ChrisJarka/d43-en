====== 1 Samuel Chapter 31 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who did the Philistines fight against and pursue?

A. They fought against Israel and pursued Saul and his sons. [31:1-2]

Q? What happened to the men of Israel, Saul, and his sons?

A. The men of Israel fled from the Philistines and fell dead on Mount Gilboa. Saul's sons were killed and Saul was overtaken by the archers and was in severe pain. [31:1-3]
