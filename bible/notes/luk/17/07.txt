===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **But which of you ** - That is, "But who among you," or "But which one of you listening."
    * **servant** - Literally, "slave."
    * **who has a servant plowing or tending sheep** - That is, "who has a servant that has a responsibility to plow your field or take care of sheep," or "who has a servant with a duty to plow your field or take care of your sheep."
    * **when he has come in from the field** - That is, "when he has entered the house after coming in from the field," orâ€‹ "when has come inside the house from the field."
    * **Come immediately and sit down to eat**- That is, "Come here at once and sit down at the table to eat."
    * **But will he not say to him**- This can also be translated as "But instead you would say to him," or "Instead, this is what you will say to him."
    * **Prepare something for me to eat** - That is, "Prepare what I would eat," or "Prepare my meal so I can eat."
    * **and put a belt around your clothes and serve me ** - That is, "dress up properly and serve me," or "dress up properly and take care of me.
    * **until I have finished eating and drinking **- That is, "until I had eaten and drank," or "until I had consumed my meal."
    * **Then afterwards you will eat and drink ** - That is, "and after these you will eat and drink," or "then after preparing my meal and serving me while I eat and drink, you can eat and drink."
