===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **borders are in the heart of the seas**  - As a port city, Tyre's borders included maritime borders far out into the sea.
  * **they have made **- "They" refers to the builders of the city.
  * **they took cedar **- "They" refers to the builders of the city.
  * **firs from Senir **  - This refers to fir trees from Mt. Senir.<
  * **mast for a ship**  - A mast, a long pole that supports the sails of a boat or ship, or a ship was not actually made; this metaphor could be translated by beginning with the phrase: "like a great ship"
