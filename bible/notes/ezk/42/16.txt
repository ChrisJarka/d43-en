===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He measured** - The word "he" refers to the man whose appearance was like brass (40:3).
  * **measuring stick**  - This is a long, thin piece of wood or metal or plastic that is used to measure something.    .
  * **the side of the sea** - This refers to the west side.
