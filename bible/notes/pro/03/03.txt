===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Do not let covenant faithfulness and trustworthiness ever leave you**  - Always be faithful and trustworthy.  (See: Litotes)
  * **tie them together about your neck, write them on the tablet of your heart**  - Both parts of verse 3 are metaphors for "keep covenant faithfulness and trustworthiness with you at all times so that you do not forget them." (See: Metaphor, Parallelism)
  * **reputation** - the common opinion that people have about someone.
  * **in the sight of God and man**  - The term "sight" is used to mean "opinion' or "judgment." (See: Metonymy)
