===== translationNotes: =====

  * **grew old and was full of days** - These two phrases mean the same thing and emphasize how long he lived. AT: "became very old" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **was full of days** - This idiom means he lived a long time. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **among the kings** - "among the tombs of the kings" or "among the graves of the kings"
  * **because he had done good in Israel, toward God, and to God's house** - The abstract noun "good" can be stated in another form. AT: "because of the good things that he had done in Judah for God and for God's temple" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
