===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Seize a pledge** - A pledge is something given to guarantee a loan. Often, a cloak was used as a pledge for a debt and would be needed for warmth.
  * **plunders spoil** - This means robbing valuables or things by force
  * **hungry** - This word means people who do not have enough food.
  * **naked** - This word means people who do not have clothes.
  * **who gives interest** - The word 'interest' refers to the money paid by a person to use borrowed money.
  * **and takes profit** - The word 'profit' in this case refers to an unfair rate of interest.
  * **practices** - This word means to live according to custom or religion.
  * **He/His** - These pronouns refer to the son who does not sin.
