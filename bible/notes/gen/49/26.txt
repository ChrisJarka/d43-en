===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The blessings of your father will be greater than the blessings of the ancient mountains**  - God will provide blessings greater than the protection of the ancient mountains
  * **will be greater than**  - "will prevail over" or "have prevailed over" (viewing the prophecy as fulfilled)
  * **the ancient mountains**  - "my parents" (different Hebrew reading)
  * **They will be on the head of Joseph**  - "They" refers to the blessings of his father.
  * **prince over his brothers **- "set apart from his brothers"
