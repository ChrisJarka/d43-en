====== chosen one ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Bible, the term "chosen one" usually refers to Jesus as the one whom God chose to be the Christ.

  * The Bible says that God chose Jesus to be the Christ before the world was created.
  * Since God chose Jesus, He (Jesus) is the only sacrifice that God accepts for the payment of mankind's sin.
  * A few times in the Bible, "chosen one" is used for people whom God appointed as special leaders over the Israelites.

(See also: Christ, Messiah)
