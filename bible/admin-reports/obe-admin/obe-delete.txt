===== OBE: Delete Term =====

~~NOCACHE~~

==== Purpose ====

To search for all the links tied to the term to be deleted. The Namespace by standard naming conventions is the tag name.

==== Pages to Delete ====

^  Pages Tagged "Delete"  |
|{{topic>en:obe?delete&nocomments}} |

==== Usage ====

  - Go to the DW (text) editor.
  - Replace "XXX" text with the OBE term URL name.
  - Hit the "Preview" Button.
  - Scroll down to find all the related links.
  - Edit the pages and delete the term links by right clicking on the links and opening the pages in a new tab or new window.
  - Hit the "Cancel" button. That will reset the edited values back to "XXX".
