===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He lifted up his eyes** - This means "he looked up." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **his mother's son, saying** - This can be translated with a new sentence: "His mother's son. Joseph said."
  * **Is this your youngest brother ... me?** - Possible meanings are 1) it is a rhetorical question; AT: "So this is your youngest brother ... me" or 2) "Joseph sought confirmation that the man was indeed Benjamin, his brother."
  * **God be gracious** - "may God be gracious"
  * **my son** - This is a friendly way one man speaks to another man of lower rank.
