~~NOCACHE~~

The script on this page searches for a string of text anywhere in the En directory. To modify the search, open in edit view and change the text between the quotation marks following the -q.  

//Note: Special characters and symbols must be preceded by a backslash "\".//

<code>
{{door43pages @:en:bible:notes -r -q="obe((:)|(\/))((other)|(kt))((:)|(\/))term" -exclude:home -simpleLineBreak -title -fontsize="90%"}}
</code>

Replace the word "term" with any OBE name.
