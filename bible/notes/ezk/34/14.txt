===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **graze** - means to eat grass peacefully
  * **I ... my** - refer to Yahweh.
  * **They** - refers to the flock/the people of Israel.
  * **I myself** - The word "myself" adds emphasis.
  * **this is the Lord Yahweh's declaration.** -  The word "declaration" emphasizes that Yahweh will certainly rescue His flock/Israel. An alternate translation is: The Lord Yahweh declares it.
  * **Annihilate**  - means to destroy or wipe out.
