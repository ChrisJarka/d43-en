====== chaff ======


===== Definition: The thin, light, worthless layer that separates from the grain and blows away in the wind during the process of winnowing. =====


    * Chaff is the husk from grain.
    * To perform the winnowing process a winnower scoops grain into a container and throws it up into the air where it can separate.
    * A special place where the process is performed is called the winnowing floor.
