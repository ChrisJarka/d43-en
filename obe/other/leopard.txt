====== leopard ======

===== Facts: =====

A leopard is a large wild cat-like animal that is brown with black spots.

  * The leopard is used figuratively to compare the suddenness of disaster because of its fierce, swift nature.
  * A leopard was used to symbolically describe one of the beasts in the visions given to Daniel.
  * A beast in the book of Revelation is also compared to a leopard.
