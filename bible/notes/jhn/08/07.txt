===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they continued**
- The word "they" refers to the scribes and the Pharisees. (See verse 3.)

  * **asking him**  - The word "him" refers to Jesus.
  * **He who is**  - "If any of you are" (without sin)
  * **among you**  - "You" is plural here. It refers to all of the crowd.
  * **let him**  -  "let that person" (Compare to UDB.)
