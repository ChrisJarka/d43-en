===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I **- The pronoun "I" is referring to Paul.
  * **delude **- This word means to lead you to a wrong place or conclusion.
  * **persuasive speech **- Speech that will convince you of something or make a concept believable.
  * **you ** <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>- The pronoun "you" is referring to the believers at Colossae.</font>
  * **your ** <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>- The pronoun "your" is referring to the believers at</font>    Colossae.
  * **good order **- This phrase refers to the how well their church is organized and how that helps them to follow Christ.
