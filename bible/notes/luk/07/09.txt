===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **not even **- The implication is that Jesus should have found this kind of faith in a Jewish person.
    * **servant**  - This literally means "slave," a person who is owned by another person.
    * **having returned to the house - **  You might also say "when they got back" or "when they returned."
