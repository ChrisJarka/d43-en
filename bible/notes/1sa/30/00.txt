====== 1 Samuel 30 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== David fought the Amalekites ==
When David and his men arrived home, they found the Amalekites had raided Zaglig and taken everything. They chased after them and caught up and destroyed them and brought back all their goods and families. Then David sent gifts from the loot to all the cities in Judah which they had travel through.
