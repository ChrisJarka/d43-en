====== Numbers Chapter 31 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Yahweh tell Moses would happen after the people of Israel had avenged the sin of the Midianites?

A. Yahweh told Moses that after the people of Israel had avenged the sin of the Midianites, then Moses would die and be gathered to his people. [31:1-2]

Q? What instructions were included in Moses' call to arms?

A. Moses told every tribe, "Arm one thousand of your men so they may go to war against MIdian to carry out Yahweh's vengence on it." [31:3-5]

Q? What was the result of Israel's battle against Midian?

A. The result of the battle was that every Midianite was killed including their kings and Balaam. [31:6-8]

Q? What did the army of Israel bring back to their camp in the plains of Moab as plunder?

A. The plunder the army of Israel brought back to their camp included the women of Midian, their children, all their cattle, flocks, and goods. [31:9-12]

Q? Why was Moses angry with the officers of the army of Israel?

A. Moses was angry with the officers because they let all the women of Midian live. [31:13-15]

Q? Why was Moses angry that the officers of the army of Israel let the wome of Midian live?

A. Moses was angry because the women of Midian caused the people of Israel, through Balaam's advice, to commit sin against Yahweh. [31:16]

Q? What command did Moses give the officers of the army of Israel?

A. Moses told the officers, "Kill every male among the little ones and every woman who has slept with a man, but take for yourselves all the young girls who have never slept with a man."[31:17-18]

Q? Which men in the army of Israel had to purify themselves?

A. All the men in the army who had killed anyone or touched a dead person must purify themselves. [31:19-20]

Q? What did Eleazar the priest tell the soldiers who had gone to war and were now outside the camp of Israel for seven days?

A. Eleazar the priest told the soldiers, "Purify yourselves, every garment and everything made of wood, put every piece of metal through the fire and wash them with water as well as your clothes, so that you will be clean and can come back into the camp." [31:19-24]

Q? Between what two groups did Moses ask Eleazar the priest to divide the plunder from the war?

A. Moses asked Eleazar the priest to divide the plunder from the war between the soldiers who went out to battle and all the rest of the community. [31:25-27]

Q? What was the tax which was to be given from the soldiers to Eleazar the priest?

A. The soldiers were to pay a tax of one out of every five hundred whether persons, cattle, donkeys, sheep, or goats to Moses who gave it to Eleazar the priest as an offering to Yahweh. [31:28-29, 32-41]

Q? What was the tax the people of Israel were to give to the Levites who take care of the tabernacle?

A. The people of Israel were to pay a tax of one out of every fifty from the persons, cattle, donkeys, sheep, and goats to Moses who gave it to the Levites who kept care of Yahweh's tabernacle. [31:30-31, 42-47]
