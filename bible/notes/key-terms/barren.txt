====== barren ======

===== Definition: =====

To be "barren" means to not be fertile or fruitful.

  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Soil or land that is barren is not able to produce any plants.</font>
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>A woman who is barren is physically unable to conceive a child.</font>
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Depending on the context, this could also be translated as, "unfruitful" or "childless" or "not fertile."</font>
