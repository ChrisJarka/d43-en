===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Is there not a woman among the daughters of your relatives, or among all our people?** - This question was asked to express his father and mother's opinions that Samson should be able to find a wife among his own people. AT: "Surely there are women among your people whom you could marry." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Are you going to take a wife from the uncircumcised Philistines?** - This question was asked to express his father and mother's shock that Samson would want to marry a Philistine and to express that they did not approve.
  * **when I look at her, she pleases me** - Samson describes the Philistine woman's beauty and how she looks by referring to his emotions when he looks at her. AT: "because she is beautiful."
  * **this matter** - Samson's request to marry the Philistine woman
