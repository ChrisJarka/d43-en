====== 2 Timothy 04 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

== "I solemnly command you" ==
Paul begins to give personal instructions to Timothy.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Crown ==
There are several different types of crowns used in Scripture as images used for different things. It appears that this crown is one achieved or rewarded at the end of a person's life.
