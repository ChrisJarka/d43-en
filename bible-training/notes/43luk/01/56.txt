===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Mary stayed with her**  - That is, "Mary stayed with Elizabeth"
    * **returned to her house**  - That is, "Mary returned to her house" "… to Mary's house."
    * **Her neighbors and her relatives**  - That is, "Elizabeth's neighbors and relatives"
    * **had magnified his mercy towards her**  - That is, "had been very merciful toward Elizabeth" or "was very kind to Elizabeth."
