====== law of Moses ======


===== Facts: =====


The Law of Moses refers to the commandments of God delivered through Moses at Mount Sinai. The law of Moses can also refer to the first five books of the Old Testament called the Pentateuch.


    * The Law of Moses was delivered originally at Mount Sinai in the desert.
    * The Law was reintroduced a second time after the original tablets were destroyed by Moses when he observed the golden calf.
    * The Law of Moses serves as a guide to reveal mankind's sinful nature.
