===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **"he commanded them not to leave Jerusalem"**  - That is, he ordered them to remain in Jerusalem.
  * **"promise of the Father"**  - This is a reference to the Holy Spirit.
  * **"baptized in water . . . baptized in the Spirit"**  - Baptism in water is a physical action of a person being immersed in water or sprinkled with water. Baptism in the Spirit is an inner or spiritual action that involves a change of heart and submitting to the Holy Spirit.
