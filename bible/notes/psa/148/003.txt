===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Praise him, sun and moon** - "I want the sun and the moon to praise God as people do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **praise him, all you shining stars** - "I want the shining stars to praise God as people do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **Praise him, highest heaven** - "I want the most far away part of the universe to praise God as people do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **above the sky** - where rain comes from
