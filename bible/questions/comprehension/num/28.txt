====== Numbers Chapter 28 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What was Moses instructed by Yahweh to tell the people?

A. Yahweh told Moses, "Command the people of Israel to offer sacrifices of food to me at the appointed times" [28:1-2]

Q? What food would be offered in the sacrifices?

A. The sacrifices would consisit of a male lamb in the morning. a male lamb in the evening and a tenth of an ephah of fine flour as a grain offering mixed with a fourth of a hin of beaten oil. [28:3-6]

Q? What offerings must be added to these regular sacrifices?

A. Drink offerings must be added to these sacrifices. [28:7-8]

Q? What must be added to the sacrifices on the Sabbath day?

A. One tenth of an ephah of fine flour and a drink offering with it must be added in addition to the regular burnt offering and drink offering. [28:9-10]

Q? What special sacrifice must be made at the beginning of each month?

A. This special sacrifice would consist of two young bulls, one ram, seven year old male lambs without blemish, fine flour as a grain offering and a drink offering with each animal as well as a male goat. [28:11-15]

Q? How is Yahweh's Passover celebrated during the first month of the year?

A. Yahweh's Passover is celebrated with a holy assembly on the first day, a feast on the second day and bread without yeast for seven days to follow. [28:16-18]

Q? What sacrifices must be made during the seven days of the Passover?

A. The people of Israel must offer the same sacrifices each day of the Passover as they did on the first day of each month. [28:19-24]

Q? What are two days when the people of Israel must hold a holy assembly to honor Yahweh, do no normal work, and make the special sacrifices to Yahweh?

A. The seventh day of the Passover and the day of the first fruits during the Festival of Weeks are two days which the people of  Israel were to carry out these commandments from Yahweh. [28:25-31]
