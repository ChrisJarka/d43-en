===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You, Son of man - All occurences of you/yourself/your refer to Ezekiel**
  * **Son of man**  - This phrase refers to Ezekiel
  * **barber's razor** - Alternate translation: "blade for cutting hair"
  * **pass the razor over your head and your beard** - This phrase refers to the action of shaving one's head and face
  * **take balances to weigh** - Alternate translation: "weigh the hair"

  * **divide your hair** - Alternate translation: "put the hair into three different piles/stacks"
  * **in the midst of the city** - Alternate translation: "in the middle of the city"

  * **I will draw out a sword after them** - Alternate translation: I, God, will attack them.
