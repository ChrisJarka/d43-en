====== Psalms Chapter 008 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Whose name is magnificent in all the earth?

A. Yahweh our Lord. [8:1]

Q? What did Yahweh create out of the mouth of babies and infants?

A. Yahweh created praise. [8:2]

Q? Why did Yahweh create praise?

A. In order to silence both the enemy and the avenger. [8:2]

Q? What did Yahweh's fingers make?

A. Yahweh fingers made heavens, the moon, and the stars. [8:3]

Q? How did Yahweh notice and payattention to the human race?

A. He made them only a little lower than the heavenly beibngs and crowned them with glory and honor. [8:5]

Q? What did Yahweh made mankind do ?

A. Yahweh made mankind to rule over the works of his hands and put all things under mankind's feet. [8:6]\\
Q? What are some things under mankind's feet?

A. All sheep and oxen, and even the animals of the field, the birds of the heavens, and the fish of the sea,

everything that passes through the currents of the seas. [8:7-8]

Q? Whose name is magnificent in all the earth?

A. Yahweh our Lord's name. [8:9]
