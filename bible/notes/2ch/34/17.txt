===== translationNotes: =====

  * **They have emptied out** - This is an idiom. AT: "They have gathered all" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **into the hand of the supervisors and to the workmen** - The supervisors and workmen are represented by their "hand" to emphasize their possession. AT: "to the supervisors and the workmen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **It came about that when** - This phrase is used here to mark an important event in the story. If your language has a way for doing this, you could consider using it here.
  * **he tore his clothes** - This is a symbolic action that indicates intense grief or sadness. AT: "he tore his clothes because he was very upset" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
