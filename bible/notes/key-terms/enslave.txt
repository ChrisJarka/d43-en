====== enslave, hold in bondage ======

===== Definition: =====

To "enslave" is to place someone under control of another. To "hold in bondage" is to keep someone under the control of another.

  * To "enslave" also means to take away a person's freedom. T<font 14px line-height: 17.8181819915772px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>o maintain their lack of freedom is to "hold in bondage."</font>
  * Another word for "bondage" is "slavery.
  * In the Bible, a person may be a slave to sin, meaning he has to sin.
  * When a person becomes a Christian, he can choose whether to commit sins or to obey God.

(See also: servant, slave, slavery other)
