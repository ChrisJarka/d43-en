====== name ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Bible, the word "name" is also used in figurative ways. These non-literal uses have many different meanings.

  * "Name" can refer to a person's reputation, as in "let us make a name for ourselves."
  * Doing something "in the name of" a person means to do it with the authority or permission of that person..
  * Speaking "in the name of God" means speaking with his power and authority, or as his representative.
  * The "name" of someone can refer to the entire person, as in "there is no other name under heaven by which we must be saved." (See: [[:en:ta:translate:metonymy|Metonymy]])
