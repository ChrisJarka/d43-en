====== behold ======


===== Definition: =====


The word "behold" means to look at something with an additional emphasis on understanding what you are seeing.


    * Jesus used this word to tell His disciples to look at and see the harvest field of mankind's souls.
    * The Apostle John used this word to describe how he and the other Apostles saw Jesus as the Lamb of God.
