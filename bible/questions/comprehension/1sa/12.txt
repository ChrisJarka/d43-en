====== 1 Samuel Chapter 12 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What was Samuel's main concern now that he was old and gray?

A. Samuel's main concern in his old age was that he might have taken something or done something to the people of Israel that needed to be restored. [12:1-3]

Q? What did the people of Israel say to relieve him of his concern?

A. The people of Israel told Samuel that as Yahweh is their witness, Samuel had not cheated or oppressed them or stolen anything from them. [12:4-5]

Q? Why did Samuel tell the people of Israel to present themselves before Yahweh?

A. Samuel told the people of Israel to present themselves so that Samuel could remind them of all the righteous deeds of Yahweh in the past.[12:6-7]

Q? What had Yahweh done for the people of Israel in the past?

A. Yahweh led the people of Israel out of Egypt and rescued them from their enemies after they acknowledged their sin before him. [12:8-11]

Q? What truth did Samuel want the people of Israel to remember?

A. Samuel reminded the people of Israel that they had wanted a king to reign over them, so now he had given them what they wanted. [12:12-13]

Q? What choice did Samuel bring before the people of Israel?

A. Samuel told the people of Israel they could obey the command of Yahweh along with their king and be followers of Yahweh or they could rebel against Yahweh's commands and experience his hand against them. [12:14-15]

Q? What challenge did Samuel lay before the people of Israel?

A. Samuel challenged the people to present themselves before Yahweh and see the great thing he would do before their eyes. [12:16]

Q? What did Samuel ask Yahweh to send so that the people of Israel would know the extent of their wickedness?

A. Samuel asked Yahweh to send thunder and rain so the people of Israel would know how wicked they had been in asking for a king. [12:17-18]
