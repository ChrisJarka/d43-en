===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues to compare Pharaoh with a monster that lives in the water.

  * **So I will spread my net over you in the assembly of many peoples** - "I will send many people to capture you out of the nations around you" (See: Metaphor).
  * **and they will lift you up in my net!** - "and they will pull you onto dry land to deliver you to me".
  * **abandon you** - "leave you stranded".
  * **cast you onto the field** - "throw you onto a field".
  * **And cause all the birds of the heavens to settle upon you; the living things of all the earth will be satisfied by you** - "and make all the birds and animals eat your flesh until their stomachs are full".
