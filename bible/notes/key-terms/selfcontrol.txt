====== self-control ======

===== Definition: =====

Self-control is the ability to control one's behavior.  The normal meaning is to control one's behavior to be good.

  * Self-control is a fruit of the Holy Spirit.
  * In the Bible, self-control refers to good behavior, that is, avoiding sinful thought, speech, and action.
