===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You counted the houses of Jerusalem, and you tore down the houses to strengthen the wall **- "The leaders considered the houses of Jerusalem and tore some down to fix the wall"
  * **But you did not consider the city's maker, who had planned it long ago**  – “But the leaders of Jerusalem did not look to Yahweh who could give them protection, and they ignored his warnings that this could happen”
