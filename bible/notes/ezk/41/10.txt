===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the priests' outer side rooms** - "the side rooms of the priests that were further out from the sanctuary"
  * **chambers** - This refers to the small rooms.
  * **cubits** - See how you translated these “long” cubits in 40:05.
  * **twenty cubits** - About eleven meters. (See: biblical distance)
  * **five cubits** - About 2.7 meters.
  * **Between the chambers it was twenty cubits wide all around the house** - Alternate translation: “This open area between the chambers was twenty cubits wide all around the temple”
  * **And the doorways of the side-chambers faced the rest of the house: one doorway for the northern way and one doorway for the southern** - Alternate translation: “And the two doorways of the side chambers faced the temple, with one facing north and one facing south”.
  * **And the width of the remaining place was five cubits all around** - Alternate translation: “The entire space between the chambers and the temple was five cubits wide, all around the temple”.
