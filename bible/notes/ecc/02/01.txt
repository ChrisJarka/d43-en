===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I said in my heart **- I decided to; I thought I would
  * **test you with happiness **- test those things which would provide pleasure or happiness
  * **just a temporary breeze **- this did not provide any answers
  * **I said about laughter, "It is crazy," and about pleasure, "What use is it?" - **  there is no lasting benefit in the failure of taking life seriously, even though laughter and pleasure are good in themselves. Being too light-harted is stupid
