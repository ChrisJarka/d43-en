===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **declaration -**  The decree of Yahweh. What He wanted Gog and man to know.
  * **brimstone - **  Burning sulphur associated with a volcano that is erupting
  * **hailstone - **  Frozen, sometimes in odd shapes, droplets of water descending on down to earth in a thunderstorm.
  * **magnify - **  Stressing the importance of Yahweh's divine power in relation to man.
  * **summon a sword against him - **  Yahweh starting an all-out war against Gog and his armies.
  * **sword against his brother - **  Gog's armies will become delirious and start attacking each other instead of the Israelis.
  * **judge with plague and fire - **  Yahweh promises to bring a number of terrible actions against Gog that will destroy his armies.
  * **magnify and sanctify myself - **  All of the above actions will show the world that he is the all-powerful God deserving of praise.
