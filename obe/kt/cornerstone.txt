====== cornerstone ======

===== Definition: =====

In the New Testament cornerstone is used metaphorically of Jesus Christ.

  * In the same way that a building's first foundation stone establishes it's position and direction, so Jesus Christ is the cornerstone of His Church.
  * Jesus is said to be the chief stone in the church, and His believers are His "little stones".
