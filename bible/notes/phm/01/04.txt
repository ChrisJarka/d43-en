===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you** - Here and in most of the letter, the word "you" refers to Philemon. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_yousingular]])
  * **that belongs to us in Christ** - The most likely meaning is "that we have because of Christ."
  * **hearts** - Here the word "hearts" refers to the courage of the believers. 
  * **brother** - Paul called Philemon "brother" because they were both believers. He was also probably emphasizing their friendship. AT: "dear brother" or "dear friend."
