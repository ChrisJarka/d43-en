===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why did David not count those twenty years old or younger?

A. He did not count them because Yahweh had promised to increase Israel like the stars of heaven. [27:23]

Q? What was Jonathan son of Uzziah was over in the fields, in the cities, and in the villages?

A. Jonathan son of Uzziah was over the storehouses in the fields, in the cities, and in the villages. [27:25]
