====== Jotham ======

===== Definition: =====

Jotham was King of Judah from 742 - 735 B.C.

  * Jotham King of Judah is listed in the geneological line of Jesus Christ.
  * Jotham is described in the Old Testament as "having done right in the sight of the Lord as his father Uzziah had done".
