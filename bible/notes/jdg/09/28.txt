===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Gaal ... Ebed** - See how you translated these names in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:06:31|9:26]].
  * **Who is Abimelech, and who is Shechem, that we should serve him?** - These rhetorical questions can be translated as statements. AT: "We people of Shechem should not allow Abimelech to rule over us, and we should not serve him." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Is he not the son of Jerub Baal? And is not Zebul his officer?** - These rhetorical questions can be translated as statements. AT: "He is just the son of Jerub Baal, and Zebul is just his officer." 
  * **Jerub Baal** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:06:31|6:32]].
  * **Zebul** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Why should we serve him?** - This rhetorical question can be translated as a statement. AT: "We should not serve him."
