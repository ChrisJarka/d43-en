===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they**  - This pronoun refers to Paul and Silas.
  * **passed through**  - "traveled through"
  * **as his custom was **  - "as his habit was" or "as his common practice was". Paul usually went to the synagogue on the Sabbath when Jews would be present.
  * **them**  - This pronoun refers to the Jews who met in the synagogue.
  * **reasoned with them **  - "explained to them" or "argued with them" or "discussed with them"
