===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **a people who are his own possession - ** See how you translated in Exodus 19:5.
  * **he will set you high above all the other nations **- AT: "Yahweh will make you more important than all the other nations."
  * **in respect to praise, to reputation, and to honor** - Possible meanings are 1) God will enable Israel to praise and honor himself (See: UDB) or 2) God will make sure that you have more praise from other people, and have a better reputation and receives more honor than the other nations do.
  * **a people that is holy to Yahweh your God** - AT: "a people that lives in a holy way, dedicated to Yahweh your God."
