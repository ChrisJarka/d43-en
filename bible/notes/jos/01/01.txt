===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **cross over** - Cross is a verb, not to be confused with the noun. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]  
    * **sole of your foot**  - This uses the sole of the foot to speak of the entire person. (See: [[en:ta:translate:synecdoche]])
    * **them, the people of Israel** - Them refers to "all these people" who are then further explained as "the people of Israel". (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_pluralpronouns]])
