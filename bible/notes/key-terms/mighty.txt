====== mighty ======

===== Definition: =====

Mighty is used to describe men who are heroic, strong, or brave.

  * The word "mighty" is sometimes used to refer to the power of God.
  * Mighty also refers to being effective and successful as in the description of Moses in Acts 7. In the OT it is often synonymous with someone who is successful in battle. The name “Almighty God” means not only powerful, but conveys the idea of God being the all-sufficient one.
