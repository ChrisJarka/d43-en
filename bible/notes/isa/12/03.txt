===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you**  - Here this refers to the people of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:formsofyou|Forms of 'You']])
  * **draw water from the wells of salvation**  - This compares salvation to a deep well that won't run dry. The people are promised an unending source of blessing. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **and call upon his name**  - This can be translated as "and praise the name of Yahweh." (See: [[:en:ta:translate:metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **proclaim that his name is exalted**  - "Tell everyone that Yahweh's name is great"
