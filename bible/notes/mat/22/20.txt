===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Jesus said to them **-"Them" refers to the Pharisees.
  * **Whose image and name are these? **- Jesus knew whose name is on the coin. (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **image**  - picture
  * **Render therefore**  - This is a command. "Because of this, give"
  * **things that are Caesar's**  -  things" refers to taxes (money)
  * **things to God**  - "give to God what he wants (tithe, honor and worship)"
  * **when they heard it**- "it" refers to the answer Jesus gave.
