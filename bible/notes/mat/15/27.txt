===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **dogs**- This is a metaphor that is continued from the last verse. (See:Metaphor)
  * **crumbs**- This is a metaphor. "a small amount of Jesus healing power"
  * **her daughter was healed**  - This is a passive phrase. "Jesus healed her daughter" (See:Passive/Active)
