===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **his appearance **- "the angel's appearance"
  * **bright as lightning**  - This is a simile describing the angel's appearance. See similes.
  * **white as snow**  - This is a simile describing the color of the angel's clothing. See similes.
  * **snow**  - white particles of frozen water that falls from the sky in cold weather when temperatures are zero degrees C or lower.
  * **like dead men**  - This is a simile describing the guards' paralysis. See similes.
