====== 1 Samuel 12 General Notes ======

=== Structure and formatting ===

Special concepts in this chapter

== Samuel's farewell address ==

Samuel asks the Israeli people what wrong he has done and they said he had not done wrong to anyone. Then he continued to warn them to obey Yahweh.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Idiom ==

When Samuel makes a speech, he uses many [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]s: "walked before you" means have been your leader, "to blind my eyes with" meaning to make me give an unfair judgement, "sold them into the hands" meaning allowed them to be conquered, "hand against you" means punish you, "all your heart" meaning completely, "empty things" meaning idols.

== Synecdoche ==

In Samuel's farewell address and the people's reply to it there are three [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]]s: "man's hand"  meaning person, "in the sight of Yahweh" meaning to Yahweh, "your servants" meaning us.
