====== Leviticus Chapter 11 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What living things did Yahweh tell Moses and Aaron that the people of Israel could eat?

A. Yahweh told Moses and Aaron to tell the people of Israel that they could eat any living thing that has a split hoof and chews cud. [11:3]

Q? What animals did Yaweh tell the people they could not eat?

A. The animals that Yahweh told the people not to eat were the camel, the rock badger, the rabbit, and the pig. [11:4-7]

Q? What animals living in the water can be eaten by the people of Israel?

A. Animals that live in the water which have fins and scales may be eaten by the people of Israel. [11:9]

Q? What kind of owls did Yahweh say could not be eaten?

A. Yahweh said that the horne owl, the screech owl, the little owl, the great owl, the white owl and the barn owl should not be eaten. [11:16,17]

Q? What kind of insects could be eaten by the people?

A. Insects that walk on legs above their feet which they use to leap on the ground may be eaten by the people. [11:21]

Q? What should the people do to four legged insects that fly?

A The people should detest any four legged insect that flies. [11:23]
