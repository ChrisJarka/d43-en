===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **will I have this pleasure**  - This means "will I experience the joy of having children." Sarah used this rhetorical question because she did not believe that she could have a child. (See: [[en:ta:figuresofspeech:rhetoricalquestion]])
  * **my lord being old also**  - This means "since my husband is also old."
  * **my lord**  - This is a title of respect that Sarah gave to her husband Abraham.
