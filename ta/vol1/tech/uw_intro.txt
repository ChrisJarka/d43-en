===== unfoldingWord Mobile App =====

The unfoldingWord mobile app is used to publish checked translations of the Bible stories. Users can also view information about each story and the checking level.  This app can be used on [[https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.unfoldingword.androidapp&hl=en|Android]] or [[https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/unfoldingword/id925570688?mt=8|iOS]] devices.
