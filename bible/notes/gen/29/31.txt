===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **was not loved**  - "was disliked" or "was hated"
    * **he caused her to become pregnant**  - "Yahweh caused her to become pregnant"
    * **to become pregnant**  - "to conceive"
    * **bore**  - gave birth to
    * **affliction**  - "not being loved", "pain"
    * **bold words**  - explanation
