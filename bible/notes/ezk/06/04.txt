===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Then your altars..their idols**  - The words "your" and "their" refer to the people of Israel who are worshiping idols at altars.
    * **and I will cast down**  - The word "I" refers to God.
    * **cast down**  - The term “cast” means the same thing as “throw.” To cast down means to throw to the ground.
