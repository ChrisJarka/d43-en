===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus is eating the Passover meal with the twelve disciples. 

*  **He gave it to them** - "Jesus gave the piece of bread to the disciples"

*  **This is my body**  - Jesus uses bread to represent his body. (See: Metaphor)

*  **This is my blood** - Jesus uses wine to represent his blood, which would be shed. (See: Metaphor)
