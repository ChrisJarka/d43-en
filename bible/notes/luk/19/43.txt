===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Jesus continues speaking outside the city of Jerusalem)
  * **the days shall come upon you**  - This could be translated as "Soon these things will happen to you."
  * **enemies**  -Or, "people who want to hurt you." This may be referring to the Roman General Titus who attacked Jerusalem in A.D. 70.
  * **barricade**  - A barricade is a wall made of earth, stones or wood.
  * **visitation**  - A visitation is when someone comes to your house, or in this case when Jesus came to the city of Jerusalem.
