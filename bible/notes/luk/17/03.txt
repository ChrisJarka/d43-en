===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **your brother**  -Or, "another Christian."
    * **rebuke him**- That is, "confront him with it."
    * **if he sins…then forgive him**- This is a conditional statement that talks about an event that will probably happen in the future.
    * **if he sins seven times…forgive him**- This is a hypothetical future situation. It may never happen, but even if it does, Jesus tells people to forgive.
    * **seven times**  - In Jewish culture, after three times, a person no longer had to forgive the person who sinned against them. See Matthew 18:21-22.
