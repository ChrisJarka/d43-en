====== bond ======

In this New Testament context "bond" means chain, fetter, or imprisonment.

===== Definition: =====

Text

  * Paul states that he has become enslaved by the cause of Christ.
  * The New Testament also speaks of the bond of peace that Christians are to keep with one another.
  * The Old Testament speaks of breaking the bonds or chains of injustice.
  * The Old Testament speaks of the individual being brought into the bond of the covenant of God.
