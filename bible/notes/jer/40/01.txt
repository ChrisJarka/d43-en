===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The word came to Jeremiah from Yahweh** - This idiom is used to announce that God gave messages to Jeremiah. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:32:01|32:1]] AT: "Yahweh gave messages to Jeremiah" or "Yahweh spoke to Jeremiah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Nebuzaradan** - This is the name of a man. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:39:08|39:9]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **who were being carried into exile to Babylon** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "whom the soldiers were about to take to Babylon as exiles" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
