====== Nehemiah ======

===== Facts: =====

Nehemiah served as the governor of Jerusalem when he returned from exile in Babylon.

  * He led the Israelites in rebuilding the walls of Jerusalem.
  * He brought new life to Jerusalem physically, socially and spiritually.
  * Nehemiah was the name of two other people in the Old Testament.
