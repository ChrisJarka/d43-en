===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is a new part of the story about Peter's imprisonment. This is background information about Herod killing James. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])

  * **Now** - This begins a new part of the story. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_newevent]])
  * **about that time** - This refers to the time when Barnabas and Saul were teaching the disciples in Antioch.
  * **laid hands on** - This means Herod had the believers arrested. AT: "sent soldiers to arrest" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **some of those from the assembly** - James and Peter specified, which implies that this refers to the leaders of the church. AT: "some of the leaders of the believers in Jerusalem" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **to mistreat them** - "to cause the believers to suffer"
  * **He killed James ... with the sword** - This was specifying the manner in which James was killed.
  * **He killed** - "Herod the king killed" or "Herod the king gave orders to kill"
