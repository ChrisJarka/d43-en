====== self-control ======

===== Definition: =====

In the New Testament self-control is a person's ability to guide their actions and reactions in conformity to the Bible's commands.

  * Self-control is a fruit of the Holy Spirit.
  * The more a person depends on God's Holy Spirit, the more self-control they will have.
