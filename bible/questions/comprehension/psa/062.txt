====== Psalms Chapter 062 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why does David wait in silence for God alone?

A. David waits in silence for God for his salvation. [62:1]

Q? What does David say God is to him?

A. David says God alone is his rock and salvation; his high tower; David will not be greatly moved. [62:2]
