===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They are expert with the sword and are skilled in warfare**  - This is repeated for emphasis and beauty in poetry. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **armed** -"equipped with a weapon" or "supplied with a weapon"
  * **terrors of the night** - This represents any danger that might come during the dark of the night.
  * **sedan chair**  - A "sedan chair" is a movable chair for one person that is closed in on all sides and  carried by two other people with the help of long poles.
