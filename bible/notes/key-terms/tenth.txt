====== tenth, tithe ======

===== Definition: =====

A tenth, or a tithe, is a 10 percent portion of one's income, whether financial or otherwise, which is presented as an offering to the Lord.

  * Tenth or tithe usually refers to giving a portion (10 percent) of one's income, produce, or livestock as an offering of thanksgiving to God, given to God's leaders, such as a priest.
  * The New Testament does not require giving a tithe to Yahweh, but instead stresses giving generously and cheerfully to support the work of Christian ministry and to help the poor.
