====== judge ======


===== Definition: =====


A judge is a person appointed to decide civil and legal matters.


    * Moses appointed men to judge matters people brought to him.
    * After the people of Israel entered the promised land, before they had kings, God appointed judges to lead in times of trouble.
    * Judges were individuals used by God to rescue the Israelites from their enemies during the period of Hebrew history prior to the anointing of the first king.
    * To judge is to make a pronouncement upon someone, either positive or negative.
