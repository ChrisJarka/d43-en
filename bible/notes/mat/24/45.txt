===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **So who is the faithful, wise servant…everything that he owns**. - Jesus is telling a parable to encourage believers to be faithful. (See: parable)
  * **So who is the faithful, wise servant, whom his master has set over his household…? **- Jesus is asking people to think about who a faithful servant is. (See: Rhetorical)
