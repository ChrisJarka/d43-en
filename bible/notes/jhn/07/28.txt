===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Jesus was crying out**
- Jesus spoke loudly for all to hear

  * **"you both know me and know where I come from"**
- statement of irony

  * **"I have not come of myself"**
- Jesus did not come by his own authority

  * **"he who sent me is true"**  - 'he' refers to God. 'is true' is a metonymy in that God is a true witness.
  * **"whom you do not know"**
- 'whom' refers to God.

  * **"I know him because I come from him and he sent me"**
- Jesus knows the Father because Jesus came from the Father, the Father sent Jesus.
