===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues speaking about the wicked people.
  * **I will choose their own blatant wrongdoing** - "I will choose their punishment"
  * **They did that which was evil in my eyes** - AT: "They did what I consider to be evil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
