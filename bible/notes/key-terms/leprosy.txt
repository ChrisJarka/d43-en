====== leprosy, Leper ======


===== Definition: =====


Leprosy is a chronic infectious disease that affects the skin and nerves and can result in paralysis and deformity.


    * Leprosy was a serious disease in Bible times because there was no cure.
    * A leper is a person with leprosy.
    * People with leprosy were isolated in areas outside of the encampment or town.
    * Leprosy is now known as Hansen's Disease and is curable.
