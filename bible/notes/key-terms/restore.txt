====== restore, restoration ======

===== Definition: =====

To restore means to bring someone or something back to its normal or healthy state.

  * When a diseased body part is "restored", this means that it has been healed.
  * The term "restoration" can refer to a payment that needs to be made, for example to pay for an animal that was accidentally killed.
  * Restoration can also mean returning to peaceful relations and unity with someone after there has been division.
  * To restore could be translated as "heal" or "turn back" or "renew" depending on the context.
  * Restoration could be translated as, <font 13.63636302948px line-height: 17.8181819915772px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>"reconciliation" or "bringing back peace and unity".</font>
