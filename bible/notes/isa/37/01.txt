===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It came about that when king Hezekiah heard their report** â€“ "When king Hezekiah heard the words of the Assyrian messengers"
  * **he tore his clothes, covered himself with sackcloth, and went into the house of Yahweh** - Hezekiah did these things to show that he was very upset by the words of the Assyiran messengers.
  * ** Eliakim, Shebna, Amoz** - These are names of men. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]
  * ** He sent ... to Isaiah** - Hezekiah sent the leaders to tell Isaiah what the Assyrian messengers said and to seek Yahweh's response.
