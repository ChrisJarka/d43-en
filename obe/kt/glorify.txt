====== glorify ======

===== Definition: =====

To glorify someone is to show how wonderful, amazing, honorable, and great he is.

  * People can verbally glorify God by telling about the wonderful things he has done. Another way to say this is to "bring God glory."
  * People can also glorify God by living in a way that shows how great and magnificent he is. This causes others to honor and glorify God, too.
  * When the Bible says that God glorifies himself, it means that he shows his amazing greatness to people, often through miracles.
  * God the Father will glorify God the Son by revealing to people the Son's perfection, splendor, and greatness, as well as other manifestations of the Son's deity.


===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * "Glorify" could also be translated as "praise" or "talk about his greatness" or "show how great he is."
