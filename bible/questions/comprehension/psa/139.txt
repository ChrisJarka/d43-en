====== Psalms Chapter 139 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What does Yahweh know, understand, and observe about David?

A. Yahweh has examined and knows David, he understands his thoughts, observes his paths, knows his ways and all his words. [139:1-4]

Q? How does David describe Yahweh's presence?

A. Yahweh's presence surrounds David, his hand is upon him and David cannot understand it. [139:5-6]

Q? Where does David say he can not excape from Yahweh's presence?

A. He says if he goes to heaven or makes his bed in sheol, Yahweh is there. If he goes to live in the uttermost parts across the sea, Yahweh's hand will lead and hold him.  [139:7-10]
