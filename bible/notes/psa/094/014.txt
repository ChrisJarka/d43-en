===== translationNotes: =====

  * **his inheritance** - "those whom he has chosen to be with him forever"
  * **justice will prevail** - The psalmist speaks of justice as if it were a soldier in a battle. AT: "justice will be stronger" or "justice win the battle"
  * **and all the upright will follow it
  * **Who will rise up to defend me against the evildoers? Who will stand up for me against the wicked?** - This can be translated as a statement. AT: "No one will defend me against evil people. No one will help me fight against wicked people." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
