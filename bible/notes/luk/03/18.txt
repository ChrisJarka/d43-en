===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **with many other exhortations he**  - That is, "with many other strong urgings he" or, "John encouraged the people many times to repent."
    * **rebuked Herod the tetrarch**  - That is, "told Herod the tetrarch that he had sinned." Herod was a tetrarch, not a king. He had only limited rule over the region of Galilee.
    * **for marrying his brother's wife**  - That is, "because Herod married his own brother's wife."
    * **he had John locked up in prison**  - That is, "he told his soldiers to put John in jail."
    * **prison**  - This is a building where criminals are kept and guarded by soldiers or police. It can also be translated as, "jail."
