===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Then your altars will become desolate and your pillars will be destroyed** - Alternate translation: "There will no longer be anyone to worship at your altars and your enemy will destroy your pillars" (See: [[en:ta:translate:active]]) The word "your" refers to the people of Israel to whom God is speaking directly.
    * **I will throw down** - The word "I" refers to God.
    * **their idols** - The word "their" refers to the people of Israel who are worshiping idols.
    * **children of Israel** - "People who lived in Israel"
