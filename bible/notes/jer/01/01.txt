[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive]]===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Hilkiah **  - a Hebrew priest at the time of King Josiah
  * **Anathoth**  - a Levitical city within the land given to the tribe of Benjamin
  * **came to him **- AT: " came to Jeremiah"
  * **thirteenth year - **"year number 13" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **his reign**  - "Josiah's reign"
  * **it also came**  - "The word of Yahweh also came"
  * **the fifth month** - "month number 5" 
  * **the eleventh year** - "year number 11"
