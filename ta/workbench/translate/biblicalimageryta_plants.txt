====== Biblical Imagery - Plants ======

===== Description =====

==== Examples from the Bible: ====

=== The idea of a BRANCH represents a person's descendant.

When this says "His branch will be cut off" it means that he will not have any descendants.

>His roots will be dried up beneath;
>above will his branch be cut off.
>His memory will perish from the earth;
>he will have no name in the street. (Job 18:17 ULB)

Isaiah wrote about one of Jesse's descendants.
>A shoot will sprout from the root of Jesse, and a branch out of his root will bear fruit.
>The Spirit of Yahweh will rest upon him, the spirit of wisdom and understanding,
(Isaiah 11:1 ULB)

Jeremiah wrote about one of David's descendants.
>See, days are coming—this is Yahweh's declaration—when I will raise up for David a righteous branch.
>He will reign as king; he will bring prosperity and carry out justice and righteousness in the land.  (Jeremiah 23:5 ULB)

=== The idea of a PLANT represents an emotion or attitude ===


  * //Sow righteousness for yourselves, and reap the fruit of covenant faithfulness//. Hos. 10:12 (ULB)
  * //Based on what I have observed, those who plow iniquity and sow trouble, reap the same//. Job 4:8 (ULB)
  * //For the people sow the wind and reap the whirlwind//. Hos. 8:7 (ULB)
  * //You have turned…the fruit of righteousness into bitterness//. Amos 6:12 (ULB)
  * //What fruit then did you have at that time of the things of which you are now ashamed?//  Rom. 6:21 (ULB)

=== The idea of a TREE represents a person ===

  * //He will be like a tree planted by the streams of water that produces its fruit in its season, whose leaves do not wither; whatever he does will prosper.//  Ps. 1:3 (ULB)
  * //I have seen the wicked and terrifying person spread out like a green tree in its native soil//. Ps. 37:35 (ULB)
  * //I am like a green olive tree in God’s house//. Ps. 52:8 (ULB)
=== A PLANT IS A PERSON ===

  * //God will likewise destroy you forever; he will…root you out of the land of the living//. Ps. 52:5 (ULB)
