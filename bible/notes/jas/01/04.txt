===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Let endurance complete its work** - Continue to stand firm under difficult circumstances. AT: "Stay strong during your troubles."
  * **that you may be** - "so that God may make your faith"
  * **that you** - The word "you" refers to the Jewish believers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **completely mature** - to be fully grown spiritually in Christ.
  * **lacking in nothing** - This can be stated in the positive. AT: "being complete." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
  * **ask for it from God, the one who gives** - "ask God for it. He is the one who gives"
  * **generously and without rebuke** - This can be stated in the positive. AT: "generously and happily" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
  * **he will give it** - "God will do it" or "God will answer your prayer"
