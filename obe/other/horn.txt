====== horn, horns ======

===== Facts: =====

Horns are permanent, hard, pointed growths on the heads of many types of cattle, sheep, and goats. The term "horn" is also used figuratively as a symbol of strength, power, authority, and royalty.

  * The horn of a ram (male sheep) was made into a musical insturment called a "rams's horn" or "shofar," which was blown for special events such as religious festivals.
  * Animal horns were also made into containers to drink from.
  * God told the Israelites to make a horn-like projection on each of the four corners of the brazen altar. Although these projections were called "horns," they were not actually animal horns.
