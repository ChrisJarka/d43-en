===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Jesus said to them**  - "them" refers to the chief priests and elders that Jesus was talking to
  * **Did you never read in the scriptures **- a rhetorical question referring to Psalm 118:22-23 (See: Rhetorical question)
  * **The stone**  - The stone that was rejected by the builders but became the most important. (See: Metaphor)
  * **This was from the Lord**  - "This" refers to the great reversal. It was God who did it.
