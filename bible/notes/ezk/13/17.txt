===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **set your face against**  - See how you translated this in 4:3.
  * **prophesy against**  - See how you translated this in 4:7.
  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh**  - See how you translated this in 5:5.
  * **sew**  - "make" or "fasten" (UDB)
  * **charms**  - objects that are believed to have magic powers, especially to prevent bad luck, but also to tell the future
  * **veils **- <font 13px line-height: 19.5px background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)/Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;;inherit;;rgb(255, 255, 255)>pieces of cloth worn by women over the head and shoulders and sometimes over the face</font>
  * **trap **- <font 13px line-height: 19.5px background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)/Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;;inherit;;rgb(255, 255, 255)>a situation in which someone is tricked into doing or saying something that they don't want to do or say; this is usually bad</font>
