====== Ruth ======

===== Facts: =====

Ruth was a Moabite woman who lived during the time of Israel's Judges. She was an early ancestor of Jesus Christ.

  * Ruth is noted for her loyalty to her mother-in-law Naomi, and Naomi's Hebrew faith.
  * Ruth was a widow who married Boaz, and became the great-grandmother of king David.
