====== cast out, throw out ======

===== Definition: =====

This refers to abandoning someone such as when Pharaoh commanded that the Hebrew babies be cast out.  It also refers to the idea of rejecting someone as in God casting someone out of His presence (Jeremiah 23:39) or to freeing someone from oppression as when Jesus cast out demons.
