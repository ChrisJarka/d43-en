====== Job Chapter 18 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Bildad answer and say to Job?

A. Bildad answered by telling Job to put an end to Job's talk and consider after they spoke. [18:1 - 2]

Q? What is Bildad asking Job?

A. Bildad is asking Job why Job is regarding them as beasts and that they are stupid. [18:3]

Q? What is Bildad telling Job?

A. Bildad is telling Job you tear at yourself in your anger, but should the earth be forsaken or the rocks be removed for you? [18:4]

Q? What is Bildad saying about the wicked person?

A. He is saying their light will be put out, the spark of his fire will not shine, the light will be dark in his tent, and his lamp will be put out. [18:5 - 6]

Q? What does Bildad continue to say about the wicked?

A. The steps of the wicked will be short, his own plans will cast him down, he will be thrown into a net by his own feet, and he will walk into a pitfall. [18:7 - 8]

Q? What will happen to the wicked man?

A. The wicked man will have a trap take him by his heel, a snare will lay hold on him, a noose is hidden for him, and a trap is in his way. [18: 9 - 10]

Q? What will terrors do to him?

A. Terrors will make him afraid and they will chase him. [18:11]

Q? How does Bildad describe the wicked man and what happens to him?

A. The wicked man will have his wealth turn into hunger, calamity will be at his side, parts of his body will be devoured and the firstborn of death will devour his parts. [18:12 - 13]

Q? What does Bildad say happens to the wicked man?

A. Bildad says the wicked man will be torn out of his tent, brought to death, and people that are not his own will live in his tent. [18:14 - 15]

Q? How is Bildad describing the wicked man?

A. Bildad describes him as roots dried up, his branch cut off, and his memory perished from the earth having no name. [18:16 - 17]
