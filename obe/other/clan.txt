====== clan ======

===== Definition: =====

Clan is another word for family or relatives. It usually refers to a group of extended family members who come from a common ancestor.

  * In the Old Testament, the Israelite tribes were counted according to their clans, or family groups.
  * Clans were normally named after their most well-known ancestor.
  * People were sometimes referred to by the name of their clan. An example of this is when Moses' father-in-law Jethro is also called by his clan name, Reuel.
  * Clan could be translated as "family group" or "extended family" or "relatives."

(See also: family, tribe other)
