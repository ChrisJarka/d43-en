===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **it is easier for a camel to go through a needle's eye**  - a camel is too large to fit through a needle's eye so he was using hyperbole to make the point that it is difficult for rich people to get into the kingdom of God. (See: Hyperbole)
  * **needle's eye **- a needle's eye is the hole in a sewing needle through which the thread is passed.
