====== Cyprus ======

===== Facts: =====

Text

  * Cyprus is an island in the Mediterranean Sea about 40 miles from the coast of the Roman province of Asia.
  * Paul and Barnabas preached together there on the first missionary journey. Barnabas and Mark visited again later.
