===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **never let me be humiliated** - AT: "do not let others humiliate me." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **rock of refuge** - AT: " like a huge rock on which I can be safe" (UDB)  AT: "like a huge rock under which I can hide" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **be my rock of refuge, a stronghold to save me** - This phrase "be my rock of refuge" is a request for protection. A second similar request is provided for emphasis. [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
