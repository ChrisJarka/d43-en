===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Day of Yahweh **- a day that is coming in prophecy of judgment and God's wrath toward sinful man
  * **the cedars of Lebanon…the oaks of Bashan** - This compares the leaders of the day trees that were greatly admired people of this time. Even they would face destruction because of human rebellion. This is a personification comparing the leaders of the day to the strong trees.
