====== Pharisee ======


===== Definition: =====


The Pharisees were a Jewish sect of religious leaders that believed in the afterlife.


    * The Pharisees were characterized by a rigid adherence to the Law of Moses, tradition, and the 600-plus laws they created.
    * In Jesus' day, the Pharisees were the primary sect teaching the Jewish Law and traditions.
    * The rule of the Pharisees was very legalistic because of the importance they placed on rituals and man-made laws and traditions.
    * The Pharisees were responsible for the rejection of Jesus and His crucifixion.
