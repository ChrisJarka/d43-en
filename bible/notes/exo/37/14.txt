===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **dishes, spoons, the bowls, and pitchers to be used to pour out the offerings- ** It is only the bowls and pitchers that are used to pour out the offerings, but all four items are made out of gold. AT: "plates and cups, and also the jars and bowls to be used for offerings. He made all of these out of pure gold", "plates and cups, along with the jars and bowls to be used for offerings, which were all made out of pure gold."
