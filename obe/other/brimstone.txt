====== sulfur ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "sulfur" is the name of a <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>yellow-colored substance called sulfur that becomes a burning liquid when it is set on fire.</font>

  * In the Bible, burning sulfur is a symbol of God's judgment on ungodly and rebellious people.
  * God rained down fire and sulfur on the evil cities of Sodom and Gomorrah.
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>In some English Bible versions, sulfur is referred to as "brimstone" which literally means "burning stone."</font>

<font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Translation Suggestions:</font>

  * Possible translations of this term could include, "yellow stone that burns" or "burning yellowish rock."
