====== psalm ======

===== Definition: =====

A psalm is words that are set to music.

  * Both Moses and David the King of Israel wrote psalms that were used by the nation of Israel in their worship of God.
  * Psalms can be used to express joy, faith, reverence as well as pain and sorrow.
  * In the New Testament Christians are instructed to sing psalms to God as a form of worship.
