====== windows of heaven ======

===== Definition: =====

The expression, "windows of heaven" is a figurative way of referring to something coming from the sky or from heaven.

  * Just as a window usually can be opened, the Bible talks about the "windows of heaven" being "opened." This can refer to:
      * rain coming from the sky.
      * God greatly blessing his people.
  * The expression "windows in heaven" is also used, with a similar meaning.
  * Many translations translate "windows" as "floodgates."
