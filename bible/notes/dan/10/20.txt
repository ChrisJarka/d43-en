===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I will tell you what is written in the book of truth** - This appears to be the answer to the question that the man asks Daniel. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_events]])
  * **what is written in the book of truth** - AT: "what the book of truth says" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **who shows himself to be strong** - This may be translated as "who proves himself to be strong" or "who remains faithful."
  * **Michael your prince** - The word "your" is the plural form, indicating that Michael is the prince of Israel. AT: "Michael, the prince of your people" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_youdual]])
