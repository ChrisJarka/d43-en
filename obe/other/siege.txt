====== siege ======

===== Definition: =====

A military engagement where an attacking army surrounds a city or village and slowly cuts off all supplies of food, water, etc.

  * When Babylon came to attack Israel, the King of Babylon used a siege tactic to weaken those inside the city.
  * During a siege attack, ramps of dirt were constructed over time that would enable the attacking army to cross over stone city walls.

(See also: other)
