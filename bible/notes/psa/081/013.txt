===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Oh, that my people would listen to me;** - "Oh, how I long for my people to listen to me;" (See: Parallelism)
  * **oh, that my people would walk in my paths.** - "Oh, that they would obey my laws." (See: Parallelism)
  * **turn my hand against** - "overcome" or "defeat" (See: Metonymy and Parallelism)
