===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **was requesting that Jesus eat with him**  - This could also be translated as ""asked Jesus to eat with him" or "invited Jesus to a meal."
    * **reclined at the table to eat**  - Or, "sat down and got comfortable for dinner." It was the custom for a relaxed meal such as this dinner for men to eat while laying down comfortably around the table.
    * **who was a sinner**  - She had a reputation for living a sinful lifestyle, perhaps as a prostitute.
    * **alabaster flask**  - This is a soft, white rock, often carved into intricate shapes and designs. It was used to hold precious things.
