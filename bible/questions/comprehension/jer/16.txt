====== Jeremiah Chapter 16 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What command does Yahweh make to Jeremiah about having a family?

A. Yahweh said to Jeremiah, "Do not take a wife for yourself, and do not have sons or daughters for yourself in this place." [16:2]

Q? What does Yahweh will say will happen to the corpses of people born in the land?

A. Yahweh says that the corpses of the people born in the land will be food for the birds of the skies and the beasts of the earth. [16:4]

Q? What strong command does Yahweh give Jeremiah about houses where there is mourning?

A. Yahweh says this: "Do not enter any house where there is mourning." [16:5]

Q? What kind of celebration does Yahweh say he is about to put to an end?

A. Yahweh says, "I am about to put an end to the sound of joy and celebration, the voices of the groom and bride." [16:9]

Q? What does Yahweh say will be the people's question when Jeremiah reports his word to them?

A. Yahweh says that the people will say to Jeremiah, ‘Why has Yahweh decreed all this great disaster against us?" [16:10]

Q? How does Yahweh compare this people's wickedness to their ancestors?

A. Yahweh says, "But you yourselves have brought about more wickedness than your ancestors." [16:12]

Q? For what does Yahweh say he will pay back double Israel's iniquity and sin?

A. Yahweh says, "I will first pay back double for their iniquity and sin for their polluting of my land with their disgusting idol figures." [16:18]

Q? What will the nations say when they go to Yahweh from the ends of the earth?

A. The nations will go to Yahweh from the ends of the earth and say, ‘Surely our ancestors inherited deceit." [16:19]

Q? What will Yahweh's people know when they know his hand and his power?

A. Yahweh says that he will cause the people to know his hand and his power, so they will know that Yahweh is his name. [16:21]
