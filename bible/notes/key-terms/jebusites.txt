====== Jebusites ======

===== Facts: =====

The Jebusites were a tribe in the land of Canaan, who were descended from a man named Jebus.

  * <font 14px line-height: 15.6800003051758px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Jebus was the leader of a city that was named after himself.</font>
  * <font 14px line-height: 15.6800003051758px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>King David conquered the city of Jebus and changed its name to Jerusalem, which it still has to this day.</font>
  * Melchizedek, the priest-king in the Old Testament who was also referred to by the author of Hebrews, was of Jebusite origin.
