====== Deuteronomy Chapter 22 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Moses tell the people of Israel to do when they would see an animal of a fellow Israelite go astray or fallen down in the road?

A. They must not bring the animal to their own house, but restore it to their fellow Israelite or help him lift it up again. [22:1-4]

Q? What behavior does Yahweh find disgusting?

A. Yahweh is disgusted when a woman wears what pertains to a man or a man puts on women's clothing.[22:5]
