====== sorrow ======

===== Definition: =====

Sorrow is the opposite of joy and is associated with pain, suffering, and trouble.

  * Although a part of human existence, sorrow is not part of God's order, but is connected to human sin.
  * For the faithful, sorrow will be removed by God in the new age.
  * As the "man of sorrows" in the gospels, Jesus is fulfilling the model put forth in Isaiah.
