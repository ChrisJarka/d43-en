====== Psalms Chapter 130 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What does the writer cry out to Yahweh?

A. The writer cries out, " Lord, hear my voice; let your ears be attentive to my pleas". [130:1-2]

Q? Why does the writer say Yahweh may be revered?

A. Yahweh may be revered because there is forgiveness with him. [130:3-4]
