====== 2 Chronicles 23 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

The end of the story of Athaliah as reigning queen and the beginning of the story of Joash as king

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===


God fulfilled his promise to David of  having a descendant of his as king. The High Priest organized a coup and made seven year old, Joash, king. Athaliah objected so the chief priest had her killed.
