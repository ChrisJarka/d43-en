====== law, principle ======

===== Definition: =====

The terms "law" and "prinicple" can refer to  a general rule or belief that guides a person's behavior. A law is also a legal rule that is typically written down and enforced by someone in authority.

??Principles are guidelines or standards for decision-making.

  * Judges apply penalties for the breaking of laws.
  * Typically most governmental organizations or cultures have a group of people enlisted, such as the police, who ensure laws are followed, and detain those who break the law to be prosecuted.
  * The laws that human governments make are usually based on some of the Ten Commandments.
  * When a general law or principle is being referred to,, "law" could be translated as "principle" or "general rule."

(See also: Ten Commandments, Law, Law of Moses, God's Law other)
