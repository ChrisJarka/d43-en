====== Zephaniah ======

===== Facts: =====

One of the twelve minor prophets of the Old Testament.

  * He lived in Jerusalem and prophesied during the reign of Josiah.
  * He chastised the people for worshipping pagan gods, especially the Babylonian mother-goddess, the Queen of Heaven.
