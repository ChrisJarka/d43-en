===== Translation Notes: =====

  *  **On that day, the root of Jesse will stand as a banner for the peoples** - This refers to the coming Messiah who will be like a flag that is  lifted high. He will draw all eyes to look to Yahweh.  See how you translated the root of Jesse in Isaiah 11:1.
  *  **his resting-place will be glorious** - This refers to Jerusalem where the worship of Yahweh will be based. (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  *  **the Lord will again extend his hand to recover the remnant of his people**- “The Lord will again reach into the nations to bring back his  people who had been taken from Judah”  (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  *  **Pathros... Elam ... Hamath **- These are names of locations where Yahweh's people had been taken.  (See: [[en:ta:translate:translatenames]])
