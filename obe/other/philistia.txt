====== Philistia ======

===== Definition: =====

Philistia is the name of a large region in the land of Canaan, located along the coast of the Mediterranean Sea.

  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The area consisted of the very fertile coastal plain reaching from Joppa in the north to Gaza in the south. It was about 64 km long and 16 km wide.</font>
  * This region was occupied by the "Philistines" who were powerful enemies of Israel.

(See also: [[Philistines]], [[Gaza]], [[Joppa]] other)
