===== Translation Notes: =====

Scripture lists the dimensions of the temple.

  * **Solomon began to build** - Solomon did not do the building himself; his servants did the work.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **480th  year** - (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaltimeyear]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **in the month Ziv, which is the second month** - This is the second month on the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last half of April and the first half of May on western calendars.  (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaltime]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **cubits**  - A cubit is 46 centimeters. (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicaldistance]])
