===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This first quotation in this section comes from one of the books Moses wrote. The second one is from the Psalms.

  * **firstborn** - Here this means Jesus. The Son of God is God's firstborn because of his importance and authority over everyone else. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **his servants** - the angels
  * **spirits...flames of fire** - Possible meanings are 1) "God has made his angels to be spirits who serve him with power like flames of fire" (UDB) or 2) God makes the wind and flames of fire his messengers.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
