====== Jeremiah Chapter 39 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who came with his army against Jerusalem and besieged it?

A. Nebuchadnezzar king of Babyton came with all his army against Jerusalem and besieged it. [39:1]

Q? What happened to the city of Jerusalem in the eleventh year of Zedekiah and on the ninth day of the month?

A. The city of Jerusalem was broken into in the eleventh year of Zedekiah and on the ninth day of the month. [39:2]

Q? What officials of the king of Babylon came and sat in the middle gate?

A. All the officials of the king of Babylon came and sat in the middle gate. [39:3]

Q? What did Zedekiah, king of Judah, and all his fighting men do when they saw the officials of the king of Babylon in the middle gate?

A. Zedekiah and all his fighting men fled at night from the city. [39:4]

Q? What did the army of Chaldeans when they overtook Zedekiah and all his fighting men do in the plains of the Jordan River valley near Jericho?

A. The army of Chaldeans pursued them and overtook Zedekiah in the plains of the Jordan River valley near Jericho[39:5]

Q? What did Nebuchadnezzar do when Zedekiah was brought to him at Riblah in the land of Hamath?

A. When Zedekiah was brought to Nebuchadnezzar, the king of Babylon, passed sentence on him. [39:5]

Q? What did the king of Babylon do to Zedekiah's sons before Zedekiah's own eyes at Riblah?

A. The king of Babylon slaughtered Zedekiah's sons before his own eyes and he also slaughtered all the noblemen of Judah. [39:6]

Q? What did the king of Babylon do to Zedekiah?

A. The king of Babylon put out Zedekiah's eyes and bound him in bronze chains in order to take him to Babylon. [39:7]
