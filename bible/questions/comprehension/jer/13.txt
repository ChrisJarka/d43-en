====== Jeremiah Chapter 13 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Where does Yahweh tell Jeremiah to wear the linen undergarment?

A. Yahweh told Jeremiah to buy a linen undergarment and put it on around his waist. [13:1]

Q? What does Yahweh tell Jeremiah to do with the undergarment when he travels to the Euphrates?

A. Yahweh tells Jeremiah to take the undergarment … travel to the Euphrates and hide it there in a rock crevice. [13:4]

Q? What was the quality of the undergarment when Jeremiah dug it out from where he had hid it?

A. The undergarment was destroyed; it was no good at all. [13:7]

Q? To what do the wicked people refuse to listen?

A. The wicked people refuse to listen to Yahweh's word. [13:10]

Q? What three things does Yahweh declare his people should bring to him?

A. This is Yahweh’s declaration, "to be my people, to bring me fame, praise, and honor." [13:11]

Q? From what does Yahweh say that he will not spare all the inhabitants of Jerusalem?

A. Yahweh says that he will not spare them from destruction. [13:14]

Q? Why does Jeremiah tell the people to not be arrogant?

A. Jeremiah tells the peole to not be arrogant, for Yahweh has spoken. [13:15]

Q? What portion of Judah will be taken captive?

A. Judah will be taken captive, all of her into exile. [13:19]

Q? Whom will God place over the people?

A. "God places over you those whom you had thought to be your friends?" [13:21]

Q? What is written to answer the question, "Why are these things happening to me?”

A. The next sentence starts, "It will be for the multitude of your iniquities." [13:22]

Q? What does Yahweh say he will do with peoples behavior on the hills and in the fields?

A. Yahweh says he will cause them to be seen, these detestable things!" [13:27]
