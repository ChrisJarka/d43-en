====== Elijah Prepared the Sacrifice [19-09] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-19-09.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

At the end of the day, **[[:en:obe:other:elijah|Elijah]]** prepared a **[[:en:obe:other:sacrifice|sacrifice]]** to **[[:en:obe:kt:god|God]]**. Then he told the people to pour twelve huge pots of water on top of the sacrifice until the meat, the wood, and even the ground around the **[[:en:obe:other:altar|altar]]** were completely wet.
