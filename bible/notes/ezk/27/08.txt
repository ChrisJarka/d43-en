===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **ones dwelling in Sidon and Arvad were your rowers ** <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>- People from these cities were strong rowers, people who move a boat by pulling and pushing oars or paddles. This may indicate that the people of Tyre are strong.</font>
  * **sages of Tyre were within you, they were your pilots **  - Sages, or the wisemen of Tyre, helped guide ships, and may refer to the strength and wisdom of those leading the city.
  * **<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The elders of Gebal and its sages were strengthening your seams</font>    **- Strengthening seams is a process to keep a boat from leaks and sinking.This may refer to the elders and wisemen were counselors to the leaders to keep the city strong.
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>** All the ships of the sea and their sailors among you were to trade your merchandise  ** - "Sailors" are men who worked on the ships. This portion may refer to the busy port and well-connected city.</font>
