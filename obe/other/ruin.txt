====== ruin ======

===== Definition: =====

To spoil, desecrate or render useless.

  * The minor Prophet Zephaniah speaks of the day of God's wrath as a day of ruin when the world will finally be judged.
  * The book of Proverbs says that ruin and destruction await those who are ungodly.
