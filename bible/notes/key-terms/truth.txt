====== truth ======

===== Definition: =====

Truth is the factual and correct quality of words or statements.

  * The opposite of a truth is a falsehood or a lie.
  * Jesus said, "I am the way, the truth, and the life." Jesus revealed God's truth in the words that He spoke.
  * Truth is all that God is and all that God says in his word, the Bible.

(See also: other)
