===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Behold, seven other cows**  - 
  * **very undesirable, and thin**  - "extremely worthless, and bony"
  * **desirable and fat**  - "healthy and salable" or "healthy looking and well-fed"
  * **Behold**  - "Listen closely"
  * **such undesirableness like them**  - "such worthlessness as they had" or "such as them with regard to undesirableness"
  * **undesirable**  - "worthless"
  * **they had eaten them up**  - "they had entered their belly"
  * **it could not be known**  - "no one could discern"
  * **they had eaten them**  - "they had entered their belly"
  * **for they were still as undesirable**  - "for their appearance was undesirable"
  * **as before**  - "just like at the beginning"
