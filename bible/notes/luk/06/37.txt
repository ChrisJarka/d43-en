===== Translation Notes: =====

    * (Jesus teaches the crowd not to judge people.)
    * **Do not judge** - "Do not judge people"
    * **and** - This could also be translated as "and as a result"
    * **you will not be judged**  - Jesus did not say who would not judge. This could be translated as "they will not judge you." If you need to make it explicit who would not judge, you could translate it as "God will not judge you" or "no one will judge you." (See: [[en:ta:vocabulary:implicitexplicit|Explicit and Implicit Information]], [[en:ta:vocabulary:active|Active or Passive]])
    * **Do not condemn** - "Do not condemn people"
    * **you will not be condemned**  - Jesus did not say who would not condemn. This could be translated as "they will not condemn you." If you need to make it explicit who would not condemn, you could translate it as "God will not condemn you" or "no one will condemn you."
    * **you will be forgiven** - Jesus did not say who would forgive. This could be translated as "they will forgive you." If you need to make it explicit who would forgive, you could translate it as "God will forgive you."
