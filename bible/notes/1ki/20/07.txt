===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **called all the elders of the land** - "commanded all the elders of the land to gather"
  * **take note and see** - These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **I have not refused him** AT: "I have agreed to his demands" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **all the people** - AT: "many people in Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
