===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **While he was going down** - "While the king's official was returning"
    * **when he began to improve** - "when he began to get better" 
    * **at the seventh hour** - This refers to the seventh hour of daylight or 1:00 PM. (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaltime]])
    * **the fever left him** - It means that after that time, the child did not have fever any more. He was cured of his fever at that moment.
