====== abomination ======

===== Definition: =====

An abomination is something that causes disgust or extreme dislike.

  * Hebrew shepherds were said to be an abomination to the Egyptians.
  * The book of Proverbs contains a list of things which are said to be an abomination to the Lord: a lying tongue, proud eyes, hands that shed innocent blood, a heart that devises evil plans, feet that are swift to shed blood, and other similar things.
