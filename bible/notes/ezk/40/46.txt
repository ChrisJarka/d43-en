===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **room facing north** - "that had its main entrance on the north side"
  * **on duty at the altar** - Possible meanings are 1) "who are in charge of the work at the altar" or 2) "who are responsible for guarding the altar."
  * **northern way**  - This refers to the north side.
  * **keeping guard** - (See: [[:en:obe:other:watch|watch]])
  * **sons of Zadok** - Zadok was a priest while David was king (2 Sam 8:17).  "Sons of" refers to his descendants.
  * **he** - This pronoun refers to the man with the measuring stick who was doing the measuring (see verse 3).
  * **cubits** - See how you translated these "long" cubits in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:05|40:05]].
  * **one hundred cubits** - About fifty-four meters. (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaldistance]])
  * **house**  - In this instance, the word "house" refers to the temple.
