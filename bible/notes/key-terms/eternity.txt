====== eternity ======

===== Definition: =====

Eternity is often thought of as unending time or a period of time without a beginning or an end. An eternity can also describe a state of being where there is no time.

  * After this present life on earth, humans will spend eternity either in heaven with God or in hell apart from God.

(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:urlterm|eternal life]])
