===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Jesus continues his parable of the wise and foolish virgins.)
  * **trimmed their lamps **- "fixed their lamps" or "adjusted their torches for burning"
  * **The foolish said to the wise**  - "The foolish virgins said to the wise virgins"
  * **our lamps are going out**  - "the fire in our lamps is dying out" or "our torches are no longer burning brightly"
  * **But the wise answered**  - "But the wise virgins answered"
  * **not be enough for us and you**  - "us" refers to the five wise virgins. "you" refers to the five foolish virgins.
  * **to those who sell**  - "to the people who sell oil"
  * **buy some - **"buy some oil"
