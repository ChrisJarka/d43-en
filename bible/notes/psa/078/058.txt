===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For they**  - The pronoun "they" here refers to the Israelites.
  * **made him angry**  - The pronouns "him" and "he" refer to God.
  * **made him angry with their pagan shrines and provoked him to jealous anger with their idols**  - These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: Doublet)
