====== word of God, word of Yahweh, word of the Lord, scripture ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "word of God" or "God's Word" refers to all that God has said to people, including spoken and written messages.

  * Other terms in the Bible that have the same meaning are, "the word of Yahweh" and "the word of the Lord."
  * The term "scriptures" refers to the written message that God told people to write. Many years after God's words were written down, people can still read these scriptures and know God's teachings. This term could be translated as "the writings" or "the written message from God."
  * It may be more natural in some languages to make this term plural and say "God's words" or "the words of Yahweh."
  * Depending on the context, other ways to translate this term could include, "the message of Yahweh" or "God's message" or "the teachings from God."
  * Often the phrase "the word of God came" or "the word of Yahweh came" is used to introduce something that God told his prophet or his people. This could be translated as, "Yahweh spoke these words" or "God spoke this message."
  * In the New Testament, Jesus is called "the Word" and "the Word of God." These titles mean that Jesus fully reveals who God is. He can do this because he is God himself.
  * In modern times, the phrase "word of God" is often used to refer to the Bible. However, the word for Bible should not be used to translate this term in a Bible translation.
