===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh - **  See how you translated this in 5:5**.**
  * **you**- This refers to God talking to Assyria.
  * **he - **  God is doing the action.
  * <font 14px line-height: 19px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>** tall in height, and he set his tree top in between the branches  ** - This is a reference to the great power of Assyria.</font>
  * **his heart was lifted up**  - "Assyria's heart was proud".
  * <font 14px line-height: 19px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>** hand of the mighty one of nations  ** - Because of Assyria's pride, God used Babylon to destroy the nation.</font>
  * **He did his works upon him**- "God did God's work upon Pharaoh".
  * **drove him away **- "cast out"
