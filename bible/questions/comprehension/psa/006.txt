====== Psalms Chapter 006 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What is David asking Yahweh to not do?

A. Do not rebuke me in your anger or discipline me in your wrath. [6:1]

Q? David is frail and his bones are shaking so what is he asking Yahweh to do?

A. Have mercy on me and heal me for my bones are shaking. [6:2]

Q? Because David's soul is troubled, what is he asking Yahweh?

A. How long will this continue? [6:3]

Q? Why should Yahweh rescue and save David?

A. Because of your covenant faithfulness! [6:]

Q? What will be remembered in death?

A. In death there is no remembrance of you. [6:5]

Q? What happens to David's bed because he is weary and groaning?

A. All night he drenches his bed with tears. [6:6]

Q? What happens to David's eyes because of his grief?

A. His eyes grow dim from grief and they grow weak because of all his adversaries. [6:7]

Q? Why does say to those who practice iniquity to get away from me?

A. For Yahweh has heard the sound of my weeping,  heard my appeal for mercy and Yahweh has accepted my prayer. [6:8-9]

Q? What happens to David's enemies?

A. All my enemies will be ashamed and greatly troubled and they will turn back and be suddenly humiliated. [6:10]
