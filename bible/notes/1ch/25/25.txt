===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This continues the list begun in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:25:09|25:9]].

  * **the eighteenth ... the twenty-first** - "the 18th ... the 19th ... the 20th ... the 21st" or "number 18 ... number 19 ... number 20 ... number 21." This shows the order in which the families were chosen. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Hanani ... Mallothi ... Eliathah ... Hothir** - Translate the names of these men as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:25:04|25:4]].
  * **twelve persons** - "12 people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
