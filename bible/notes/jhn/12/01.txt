===== Translation Notes: =====

The chief priests in Jerusalem had been looking for Jesus when he arrives in Bethany which is near Jerusalem.

  * **whom he had raised from the dead** - Jesus had raised Lazarus from the dead.
  * **Martha was serving** - It is implied that Martha was serving the dinner. (See: Implicit and Explicit information)
  * **litra** - A "litra" is a measurement--a Roman pound equal to about twelve ounces or 327.5 grams.
  * **perfume**  - The word "perfume" is a sweet smelling oil.
  * **nard** - "Nard" is a perfume made from a plant that comes from a far country. (See: How to Translate Unknowns)
  * **litra** - A "litra" is a measurement--a Roman pound equal to about twelve ounces or 327.5 grams.
  * **the house was filled with the fragrance of the perfume** - The fragrance of her perfume filled the house. (See: Active/Passive)
  * **fragrance**  - "odor" or "aroma"
