===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **ULB Genesis 35:16-20**  -
      * Benoni, Benjamin, and Jacob are male names.
      * Rachel is a female name.
      * Bethel, Ephrath, Bethlehem are place names.
      * (see: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
  * **Benoni**  - means "son of my sorrow" or "son of my grief," indicating that he was born as she was dying.
  * **Benjamin**  - means "son of he right hand" indicating his position of special favor with his father.
