===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **generations**  -The length of time between these fathers and sons varied.
  * **Mary, by whom Jesus was born **- This could be translated in the Active Voice as: "Mary, who gave birth to Jesus" (See: Active/Passive)
  * **deportation to Babylon-**  Babylonians took the Israelites to Babylon as slaves.
