===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **I see that** - "I perceive that" or "I discern that" or "I now understand that"
    * **you are a prophet** - The word "you" refers to Jesus and is singular (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
    * **you people say that** - "you people" refers to the Jews.  The "you" here is plural (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
    * **ought to worship** - or "must worship" or "should worship"
