====== 1 Chronicles Chapter 14 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did David know for sure when Hiram, king of Tyre sent messengers, cedar trees, carpenters and masons to David to build him a house?

A. When Hiram sent all these things, David knew for sure that Yahweh had established him as king over Israel. [14:1-2]
