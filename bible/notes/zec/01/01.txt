===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Turn unto me!**  - or "return to me" or "come back to me"
    * **In the eighth month of the second year of Darius' reign**  - "During the eighth month of the second year since Darius started ruling" or "On the eighth month of the second year since Darius became king"  
    * **Yahweh was exceedingly angry with your fathers!**  - or "Yahweh was very angry with your fathers"
    * **"Turn unto me!"..."and I will return to you!"**  - These are Yahweh's words to Israel. See ([[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:standard-connecting-notes:prophecy-god-as-speaker]]).
