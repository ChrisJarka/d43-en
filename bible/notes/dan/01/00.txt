====== Daniel 01 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

== Training for government jobs ==

Daniel, Shadrach, Meshach, and Abednego were chosen to be trained for service in the Babylonian Kingdom. 


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Food laws ==

The food from the king included things not allowed by Jewish dietary law. Daniel requested permission not to eat the king's food; and proved that the kings food was not necessary for good health.
