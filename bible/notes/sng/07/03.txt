===== Translation Notes: =====

Solomon continues describing his love.

  * **Your two breasts are like two fawns, twins of a gazelle** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:sng:04:04|5:5]].
  * **Your neck is like a tower of ivory** - "Your neck is tall and beautiful like the ivory tower." (Simile: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]]) 
  * **ivory** - the white tusk or tooth of an animal that is similar to bone. People use ivory to make art and to make things look beautiful. 
  * **eyes like the pools in Heshbon** - The woman's eyes have the appearance of clear, calm water. 
  * **Heshbon** - Heshbon is a place east of the Jordan River in Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **by the gate of Bath Rabbin**- "by the gate of the city of Rabbin" 
  * **nose is like the tower in Lebanon** - Towers are straight and tall, symbols of strength.
