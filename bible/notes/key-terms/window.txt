====== window, windows ======

===== Definition: =====

A window or windows are openings in a structure that allow air and sunlight to enter the structure.

  * Ancient windows in homes or commercial buildings were merely openings with wooden lattices or straw mats to regulate the opening.
  * Modern windows may have movable glass panes and screen material to keep out insects or birds.

(See also: other)
