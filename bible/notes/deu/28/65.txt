===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **there will be no rest for the bottoms of your feet** - AT: "you will have to continually wander because you have no permanent home where you can rest" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Yahweh will give you there a trembling heart, failing eyes, and a soul that mourns** - AT: "Yahweh will cause you to be afraid, hopeless, and sad" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]
  * **Your life will hang in doubt before you** - AT: "You will not know whether you will live or die"
