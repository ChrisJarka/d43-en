====== Holy One ======

===== Definition: =====

The "Holy One" is a title that refers to God.

  * <font 13.63636302948px line-height: 17.8181819915772px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>In the Old Testament, this title often occurs in the phrase "Holy One of Israel."</font>
  * <font 13.63636302948px line-height: 17.8181819915772px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The term "holy one" is also used in the Old Testament to refer to an angel.</font>
  * In the New Testament, Jesus is also referred to as the "Holy One."
