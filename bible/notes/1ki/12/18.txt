===== translationNotes: =====

  * **all Israel stoned him** - "All Israel" is hyperbole to mean the capable men of Israel who represent the rest of the people by a metonymy. AT: "all the people of Israel who were there" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **the house of David** - "House" is metonymy for David's lineage of power and prestige. AT: "the kings descended from David" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **to this day** - "Day" is metonymy for a period of time, a duration. AT: "to the time of the writer." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
