====== 2 Samuel Chapter 17 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Ahithophel advise Absalom and all the elders of Israel of how he would attack David?

A. His advice was to have a large army, carry out a surprise attack on David at night when he was weary and weak, and bring back all the people for them to rule. [17:1-4]

Q? Absalom asked Hushai for his advice about Ahithophel's plan. What was Hushai's advice?

A. He said the plan was not good. [17:5-7]

Q? What reason did Hushai say that the plan was not good?

A. He said that even the bravest soldiers would be afraid of an attack because someone would call it a slaughter of Absalom's soldiers. [17:9-10]

Q? What did Hushai advise Absalom to do?

A. He advised him to gather all of Israel together and go to battle in person so they could find David and kill him and all of his men. [17:11-12]

Q? How did Absalom and the men of Israel respond to Hushai's advice?

A. They said it was better advice than what Ahithophel had given them. [17:14]

Q? What message did Hushai want the priests to go and report to David?

A. That David should not camp at the fords of the Arabah, but should cross over so that he and his people would not be killed that night. [17:16]
