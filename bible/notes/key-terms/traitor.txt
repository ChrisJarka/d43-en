====== traitor ======


===== Definition: =====


A traitor is a person who is unfaithful or disloyal to their friends, comrades or country.


    * Judas Iscariot was a traitor because he was a disciple of Jesus, but he betrayed Jesus by leading the Roman and Jewish authorities to Him so they could kill Jesus.
