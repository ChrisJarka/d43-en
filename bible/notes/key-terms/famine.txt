====== famine ======

===== Definition: =====

Text

  * There can be a time of widespread, but general, famine as in Job 5:20.
  * There can be a specific famine caused by drought as with Joseph in Genesis 41:27.
  * A third kind of famine could be the result of a besieged city as in Samaria, 2 Kings 7:4.

(See also: other)
