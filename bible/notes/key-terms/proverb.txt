====== proverb  ======

===== Definition: =====


A proverb is a short, memorable, clever saying that expresses some wisdom or truth.


    * for example: Proverbs 8:13: "The fear of Jehovah is to hate evil."
    * or, Proverbs 26:11: "As a dog returns to its vomit, so a fool returns to his folly."
