===== Translation Notes: =====

Daniel has been praying.

  * **forsaking my sin**  - "giving up my sin"
  * **on behalf of the mountain of God's holiness**  - This refers to Jerusalem ([[en:bible:notes:dan:09:15|dan:09:16]]).
  * **at the first**  - "at the beginning"
  * **rapid** - "fast" or "quick"
  * **at the time of the evening sacrifice**  - In God's laws, the Jewish people killed an animal as a sacrifice to God each day at God's temple.
my requests before Yahweh - "my call to God"
