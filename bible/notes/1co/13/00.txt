====== 1 Corinthians 13 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

While it appears that Paul interrupts his discussion of spiritual gifts, this chapter's contents probably serve a larger function in his teaching on the use spiritual gifts.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Love ==

[[:en:obe:kt:love|Love]] is the most important characteristic of the believer. This chapter gives a full description of love and why love is more important than the gifts of the Spirit. 

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphor ==

There are many different [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|metaphors]] in this chapter. Paul uses these metaphors to instruct the Corinthians, especially on difficult topics. Spiritual discernment is often needed to understand these teachings.
