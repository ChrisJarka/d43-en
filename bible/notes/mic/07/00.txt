====== Micah 07 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This last chapter is written in a poetic form. It describes the state of the nation but tells of a better time in the future!

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

The concepts of remnant and restoration are presented in this chapter. 

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

Simile is used throughout this chapter. It is a good figure of speech to review.
