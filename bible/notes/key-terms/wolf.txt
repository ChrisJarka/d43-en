====== wolf ======

===== Definition: =====

A wolf is a fierce meat-eating animal that is similar to a wild dog. 

  * Wolves usually hunt in groups called packs.
  * Wolves are especially dangerous to sheep, who do not have any good defense against the wolves.
  * False teachers are sometimes compared to wolves because they are dangerous to believers. 


(See also: false teachers, sheep)
