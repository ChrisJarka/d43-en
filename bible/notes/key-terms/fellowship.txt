====== fellowship ======

===== Definition: =====

Fellowship is the relationship between God and those who have accepted Christ that all believers share with each other.

  * Fellowship between God and men begins when a believer professes belief in Christ and accepts His offer of salvation.
  * Church fellowship among believers begins when a believer acknowledges his or her profession of faith before a congregation and is baptized.

(See also: other)
