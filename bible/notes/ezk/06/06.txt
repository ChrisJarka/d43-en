===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **youâ€¦your** - The words "you" and "your" refer to the people of Israel.
  * **cities will be laid waste and made desolate** - Alternate translation: "Enemy armies will lay waste and make desolate cities."(See: [[:en:ta:translate:active]])
  * **the high places shall be desolate** - "no one will go to the high places any longer"
  * **your altars will be laid waste and made desolate** - Alternate translation: "enemy armies will lay waste and make desolate your altars" (See: [[:en:ta:translate:active]]) or "enemy armies will destroy your altars and no one will go there anymore" 
  * **Then they will be broken** - Alternate translation: "enemy armies will break them" The word "they" refers to the altars. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:active]])
  * **your pillars will be cut down and your works will be wiped away** - "enemy armies will cut down your pillars and wipe away your work." (See: [[:en:ta:translate:active]]) or "enemy soldiers will cut down your pillars and no one will remember what you have done."
  * **The dead will fall down in your midst, and you will know that I am Yahweh** - "You will see the enemy kill the people of Israel and realize that I am God and I have allowed this because you worshipped false gods."
