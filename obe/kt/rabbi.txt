====== Rabbi ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "rabbi" was a term of respect and honor used to address a man who was a teacher of the Jewish religion.

  * Literally, "rabbi" means "my master" or "my teacher."
  * It is also a title of honor that the Pharisees liked for people to address them with.
  * Both John the Baptist and Jesus were called "Rabbi" at times by their disciples.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * This term could be translated by "My master" or "My teacher".or "Honorable teacher."
