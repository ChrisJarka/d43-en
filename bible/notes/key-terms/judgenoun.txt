====== judge (noun) ======


===== Definition: =====


In the Bible a judge is a person appointed to preside over matters of legal decision for a nation


    * Moses appointed men to preside over and judge matters that the people brought for decision.
    * After the final resurrection the twelve Apostles of Christ will sit as Judges over the twelve tribes of Israel.
