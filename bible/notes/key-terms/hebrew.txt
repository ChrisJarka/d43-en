====== Hebrew ======

===== Facts: =====

A Hebrew is term referring to the ancestry of Abraham and his descendants.  The Hebrew people were also called Jews.

  * A Hebrew is a person who is part of the nation of Israel or of Jewish descent.
  * The Hebrew people spoke the language called Hebrew or Aramaic.
