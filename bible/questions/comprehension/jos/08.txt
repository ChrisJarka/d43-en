====== Joshua Chapter 08 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Yahweh say for Joshua to do to take the city of Ai?

A. Yahweh told Joshua to take all the people of war to Ai and set an ambush behind the city. [8:1-2]

Q? What were the people to do with the plunder from Ai that was different from Jericho?

A. Yahweh told the people they could take the plunder and cattle for themselves.

Q? When did Joshua send out the 30,000 strong and courageous men to Ai?

A. Joshua sent the 30,000 strong and courageous men out to Ai at night. [8:3]

Q? What would Joshua do with his men when they approached the city?

A. When Joshua approached the city with his men and the people of the city came out to attack, they would run away like before.  When the people of Ai were drawn away from the city, the 30,000 men behind the city will come out of hiding and capture the city.

Q? What were the men to do with the city when it was captured?

A. When the men captured the city they were to set it on fire. [8:8]
