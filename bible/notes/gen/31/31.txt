===== Important Terms: ===== 

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **ULB Genesis 31:32; Whoever has stolen your gods will not continue to live**  - "Anyone that is found with your gods shall not live."
  * **ULB Genesis 31:32; In the presence of our relatives**  - meaning: we will each have relatives present to ensure fairness
  * **ULB Genesis 31:32; whatever with me is yours and take it**  - "whatever we have in our possession that is yours, take it"
  * **ULB Genesis 31:32; Rachel had stolen them**  - "Rachel had stolen her father's gods"
