====== Jeremiah Chapter 39 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? From whom did the word come to Jeremiah?

A. The word came to Jeremiah from Yahweh. [35:1]

Q? When did this word come to Jeremiah?

A. This word came when Nebuchadnezzar, his army, all the kingdoms of the earth, the domains under his power, and all their people were waging war against Jerusalem and all of her cities. [35:1]

Q? To whom does Yahweh, God of Israel that Jeremiah should speak?

A. Yahweh, God of Israel says, that Jeremisah should go and speak to Zedekiah king of Judah. [35:2]

Q? What does Yahweh say should be said to Zedekiah?

A. Yahweh says he is about to give this city over into the hand of the king of Babylon and he will burn it. [35:2]

Q? What does Yahweh tell Jeremiah to say to Zedekiah about what will happen to Zedekiah when the king of Babylon takes the city?

A.  Yahweh tells Jeremiah to tell Zedekiah that he will certainly be seized and given into the hand of the king of Babylon. [35:]
