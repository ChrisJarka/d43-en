====== turn, turn away ======

===== Definition: =====

To turn is to physically change direction or to cause something else to change direction. There are also several figurative meanings of "turn" and "turn away."

  * To "turn away from sin" means to "stop sinning." To "turn away from idols" means to "stop worshiping idols."
  * To "turn back" means to "return" or "go back" the same way.
  * <font 14.399999618530273px line-height: 15.680000305175781px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>To "turn to" means to start doing something, such as when Israel "turned to" worshipping idols. It also implies that they turned away from (that is, "stopped") serving God.</font>
  * To turn or "turn into" means to "become" as when Moses' hand "turned white" with leprosy, or when his staff "turned into" a snake.
