====== rod ======

===== Definition: =====

A rod is a wooden shaft approximately 1m in length used as a guidance tool by shepherds.

  * Shepherds use a long staff to gently guide sheep, and can throw a rod to warn or firmly guide sheep.
  * King David spoke of God's guidance as a metaphorical rod and staff in Psalm 23.
