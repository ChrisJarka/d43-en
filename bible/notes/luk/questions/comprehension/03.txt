====== Luke Chapter 3 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why did John rebuke Herod?

A. John rebuked Herod because he had married his own brother's wife and for doing many other evil things [3:19].

Q? Who put John into prison?

A. Herod put John into prison [3:20].

Q? What happened after Jesus was baptized by John?

A. After Jesus was baptized, the heavens opened and the Holy Spirit came down like a dove [3:20-21].

Q? What did the voice from heaven say?

A. The voice from heaven said, "You are my beloved son. I am very pleased with you" [3:21].
