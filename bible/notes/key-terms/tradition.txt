====== tradition ======

===== Definition: =====

Tradition refers to customs and practices that have been kept over time and which are passed down to people in later generations.

  * Customs and practices were not required by Old Testament laws and practices, but added by religious leaders over time.
  * Today, tradition in customs and practices in the church are not mandated by scripture, but are a result of historically accepted customs and practices.
  * In the context of Galatians 1: 13-14, when Paul states he is zealous for the traditions of the fathers, he is zealous for both the customs of his generation and the Old Testament laws.
