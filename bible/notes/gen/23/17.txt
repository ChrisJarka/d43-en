===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Ephron** - See [[en:bible:notes:gen:23:10|23:10]]
  * **Machpelah...Mamre** - These are names of areas of land. (See: [[en:ta:translate:translatenames]])
  * **the field, the cave that was in it**  - "the field and the cave that was in it"
  * **sons of Heth**  - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:gen:23:03|23:3]].
  * **passed to Abraham by purchase** - "was purchased by Abraham"
  * **in the presence** - "in front" 
  * **before all those who enter the gate of the his city**  - "those gathered at the city gate." The city gate was where the leaders of the city would meet to make important decisions.
  * **his** - Here the word "his" refers to Heth.
