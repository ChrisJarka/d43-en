====== honey, honeycomb ======

===== Definition: =====

Honey is the sweet edible substance created by honeybees made from flower nectar and stored inside the beehive in a honeycomb.

  * In the Old Testament Jonathan, son of King Saul, found wild honey in the forest and ate it.
  * Israel's Judge Samson found honey inside the carcase of a dead lion and formed a riddle based upon it, "out of the eater came something to eat...".
