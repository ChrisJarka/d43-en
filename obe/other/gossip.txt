====== gossip ======

===== Definition: =====

Gossip is unproductive talking about the private and personal affairs of another person.

  * To speak negatively about someone's private life is prohibited in the New Testament.
  * Gossip is harmful to the person or persons being spoken about because the information being shared is personal and confidential.

(See also: other)
