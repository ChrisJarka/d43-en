===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **But he is unchangeable** - AT: "But no one can change him." This can also be translated as: "But God is one" or "But he alone is God."
    * **who can turn him?** - The implicit answer is: "no one." AT: "nobody can make him change his mind" or "no one can influence him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **What his soul desires** - AT: "What he wants" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
    * **there are many examples of him doing this** - AT: "he has many plans for me"
