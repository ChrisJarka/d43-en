====== crime, criminal, fugitive ======

===== Definition: =====

A crime is a sin that involves breaking a law. The term "criminal" refers to someone who has committed a crime.

  * Types of crimes include such things as killing a person or stealing someone's property.
  * A criminal is usually captured and put into prison or in some other form of captivity.
  * Often a criminal would become a fugitive and<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>have to flee to safety or wander from place to place to escape people wanting to harm him.</font>
