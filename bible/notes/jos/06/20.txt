===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the people shouted** - "the people of Israel shouted"
  * **It came about** - This phrase is used here to mark an important event in the story. If your language has a way for doing this, you could consider using it here.
  * **the edge of the sword** - Although many of the people of Israel used swords, this may refer to a violent attack or battle in general. AT: "with their sharp swords" or "in battle" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
