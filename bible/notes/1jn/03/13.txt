===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the world hates you**  - This is a metonymy which means "unbelievers hate believers." (See: Metonymy)
  * **whoever hates his brother**  - This is a metaphor which means "those who hate any of their fellow believers." (See: Metaphor)
