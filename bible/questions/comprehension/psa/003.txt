====== Psalms Chapter 003 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What have David's enemies done to him?

A. Many have turned away and attacked me. [3:1]

Q? What does David compare Yahweh to?

A. You, Yahweh, are a shield around me, my glory, and the one who lifts my head. [3:3]

Q? What does Yahweh do when David lifts his voice to him?

A. He answers from his holy hill. [3:4]

Q? Who does David not need to fear because of Yahweh's protection?

A. David will not be afraid of people who have set themselves against me. [3:6]

Q? What will Yahweh do to David's enemies?

A. He will hit all my enemies on the jaw; you will break the teeth of the wicked. [3:7]

Q? Where does salvation come from?

A. Salvation comes from Yahweh. [3:8]
