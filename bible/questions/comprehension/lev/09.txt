====== Leviticus Chapter 09 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? On what day did Moses call Aaron and his sons and the elders of Israel?

A. Moses called Aaron an his sons and the elders of Israel on the eighth day. [9:1]

Q? What five animals did Moses ask Aaron and his sons to bring to the altar?

A. Moses ask Aaron and his sons to bring a goat, calf, lamb, ox and ram. [9:2-4]
