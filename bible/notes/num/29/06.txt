===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Do this in addition** - The the special offerings in the seventh month that Moses just described are made in addition to their normal sacrifices they make at the beginning of each month and their daily sacrifices.
  * **to making the burnt offering for the first of each month, the grain offering with it** - "to making the burnt offering and the grain offering at the beginning of each month"
  * **the regular burnt offering and the grain offering with it, and their drink offerings** - "and to making the burnt offering, grain offering, and drink offerings each day"
  * **month** - See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaltimemonth]]
  * **you will obey what has been decreed** - AT: "you will obey Yahweh's decree" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **an offering made by fire to Yahweh** - AT: "an offering you burnt on the altar to Yahweh"
