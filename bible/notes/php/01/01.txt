===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Paul and Timothy **- "From Paul and Timothy." Your language may have a particular way of introducing the authors of a letter. This could be translated as "We, Paul and Timothy, wrote this letter."
  * **servants of Jesus Christ**  - The phrase “We are” is implied. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]]) You could make it clearer with a new sentence: “We are servants of Jesus Christ.”
  * **the overseers and deacons**  - "the leaders of the church"
  * **Grace to you - **The pronoun “you” refers to the believers in the Philippian Church. (See:[[:en:ta:translate:pluralpronouns]])**
