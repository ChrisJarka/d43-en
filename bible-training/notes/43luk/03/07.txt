===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **offspring of poisonous snakes**  - This is an insult similar in form and impact to a common English profane insult.
    * **to flee**  - Another possible translation could also be "to run away from".
    * **wrath that is coming**  - That is, God is going to become angry with people who disobey Him and will punish them for their disobeying Him. "Wrath" is when someone gets very angry and acts out against the person or thing which has made them angry.
