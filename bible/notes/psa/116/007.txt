===== Translation Notes: =====

The person who made this song continues to speak.
  * **Return to your rest, my soul** - The singer speaks to his own soul as though it were a different person. AT: "I can rest in peace again." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **for Yahweh** - "because Yahweh"
  * **to you** - The word "you" refers to his soul. AT: "to me."
  * **For You** - The word "You" refers to Yahweh.
  * **have freed my soul from death** - AT: "have saved me from death" or "have rescued me from death.
  * **my eyes from tears** - Tears represent crying. AT: "You have saved my eyes from tears" or "You have made it so that I do not cry." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **my feet from stumbling** - The word "stumbling" represents a disaster that destroys a person. AT: "You have saved my feet from stumbling" or "You have kept the danger from destroying me."
