===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I will say to the north…and to the south** - "I will command the rulers of the nations to the north and to the south"
  * **Hand them over…Do not hold any back** - "Allow all the people of Israel to return to their country"
  * **from afar…from the remote regions of the earth** - "from the most distant places on the earth"
  * **everyone who is called by my name** - "all those who belong to me" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **whom I have created** - "because I have caused them to become a nation"
  * **for my glory** - "in order that they would honor me"
  * **whom I have formed, yes, whom I have made** - "God has done that" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
