===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You are saying this**  - This can be translated as " this is what you have said" or "You have said."
  * **our transgressions and sins are on us **- This phrase means "Yahweh is punishing us for our transgressions and sins." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **we are decaying in them**  - "we are rotting away because of them." This phrase compares the way that their sin destroy the people to the way that flesh rots and falls of a body. Alternate translations: "we are ruined by our sins" or "we are dying in our sins." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **How can we live?** - The people ask this question to emphasize that they have no hope of living. Alternate translation: "We have no hope of living" or "We will certainly die." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **As I live** - See how you translated this phrase in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]]. 
  * **this is the Lord Yahweh's declaration** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]].
  * **delight in the death of the wicked** -this can be translated as "want the wicked to die."
  * **if the wicked repents of his way** - "the wicked person stops doing bad things"
  * **For why should you die** - Yahweh uses this question to emphasize that he does not want the people of Israel to day. Alternate translation: "Do not choose to die."
