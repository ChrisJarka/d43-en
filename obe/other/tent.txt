====== tent ======

===== Definition: =====

A tent is a portable shelter structure made of sturdy fabric that is draped over poles and attached to them to keep out rain, wind, and sun.

  * Abraham's family lived for years in large, heavy-duty tents.
  * Israel lived in tents during their wanderings through the desert of Sinai.
  * The apostle Paul's occupation was as a tent maker, which he practiced while traveling from city to city sharing the gospel.

(See also: other)
