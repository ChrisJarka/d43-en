====== 1 Peter 02 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

Some translations prefer to set apart quotations of the Old Testament. The ULB and many other English translations indent all the lines of 2:6,7,8,10, 22, which are quotations from the Old Testament.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Stones ==
Stones are used in Scripture to in different ways to describe the church. Jesus is the cornerstone. The apostles and prophets are the foundation. In this chapter, Christians are the stones used to continue to build the church.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Milk and solid food ==
This is an image used to describe maturity in Christ. It compares the immature Christian to a baby who is only able to drink milk. They are not mature enough to handle solid food, which is the more complex teachings about Jesus. Instead, they have chosen not to grown and remain like a baby who only drinks milk, the simpler and more basic teachings about Jesus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])

=== Other possible translation difficulties in this chapter ===

== "You are now the people of God" ==
Some scholars believe this indicates the church has replaced Israel in the plans of God. Other scholars believe there are are more than one "[[:en:obe:kt:peopleofgod|people of God]]."
