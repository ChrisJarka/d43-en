===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Your new moons and your appointed feasts my soul hates** - This is referring to all the special times to worship God.  It can be translated "God hates all your celebrations of the new moon and the other festivals." (See: [[en:ta:translate:synecdoche|synecdoche]])

  * **they are a burden to me; I am tired of enduring them** - This compares how God feels about the people's celebrations to  carrying a heavy object.  It can be translated as “They are a heavy load that God is tired of carrying.” (See: [[:en:ta:translate:metaphor|Metaphor]])
