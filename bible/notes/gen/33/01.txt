===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **behold** - The word “behold” here alerts us to pay attention to a surprising new part of the story.
  * **female servants** - "servant wives" or "secondary wives."
  * **four hundred men** - "400 men" (See [[:en:ta:translate:translateunknowns-numbers|Numbers - Translating Unknowns]]). 
  * **Then… in front, followed…, and followed… last of all.** - The groups are in order of status from least important to most so that the most important are also the most protected.
