===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I will go before you** - "I will lead the way"
  * **the children are young** - "the children are too young to travel fast"
  * **the flocks and herds with me have their young **- "the flocks and herds that are with me are nursing their young"
  * **overdrive them** - "drive them too hard"
  * **my lord** - This is a polite and formal way of referring to a superior. Here it refers to Esau. You can also translate this as “you” (see [[en:ta:translate:123person]]).
  * **his servant**  - This is very formal speech made toward the one who holds the power.
  * **your servant** - This is a polite and formal way of referring to the speaker. Here it refers to Jacob. You can also translate this as “me” (see [[en:ta:translate:123person]]).
  * **at the pace of the cattle** - "as fast as the cattle can go safely"
  * **cattle** - "livestock"
