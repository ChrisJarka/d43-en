===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I will make justice the measuring stick**  - I will test you people of Jerusalem to find out if you will act justly and righteously
  * **and righteousness the plumbline**  - like someone uses a plumb line to determine if a wall is straight and level (See: Metaphor)
  * **Hail will sweep away the refuge of lies**  - you will be defeated and taken away from your country because your agreement with Egypt to protect you was made by leaders lying to each other and deceiving each other
  * **floodwaters will overwhelm the hiding place**  - an army will come against you like a flood. (See: Metaphor)
