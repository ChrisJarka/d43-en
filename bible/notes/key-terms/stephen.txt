====== Stephen ======

===== Facts: =====

Stephen is remembered as the first Christian martyr.
    * Stephen was a deacon in the church at Jerusalem.
    * Stephen was accused of blasphemy by the Jews.
    * His defense of his beliefs is recorded in the book of Acts.
    * He was executed by stoning.
    * His execution was witnessed by Saul of Tarsus, later to be the apostle Paul.
