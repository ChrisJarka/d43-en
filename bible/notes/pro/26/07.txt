===== translationNotes: =====

  * **a paralytic** - a person who is unable to move or feel all or part of his body
  * **a proverb in the mouth** - A proverb in someone's mouth represents him saying the proverb. AT: "a proverb that a fool says" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Tying a stone in a sling** - In order to throw a stone very far, people would put it in a sling and swing the sling so that the stone would fly out of it very fast. The result of tying a stone in a sling can be stated clearly. "Tying a stone in a sling so that it cannot be thrown out of the sling" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **giving honor to a fool** - "honoring a fool"
