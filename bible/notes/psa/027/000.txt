====== Psalms 027 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 27 is a psalm of praise to God because God keeps him safe from all his enemies. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Because of God's help, he has nothing to fear no matter what happens.
