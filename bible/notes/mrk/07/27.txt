===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Let the children first be fed. For it is not right to take the children's bread and to throw it to the dogs.**  - Jesus uses this metaphor to tell this woman that he must tend to the needs of the Jewish people first and that gentiles are not his first concern.
  * **Yes, Lord, even the dogs under the table eat the children's crumbs**  - The woman responds with this metaphor expressing that even though she is not Jesus' highest priority, she believes that it is good and right, and costs the Jews very little for him to extend grace to her and to heal her daughter.
