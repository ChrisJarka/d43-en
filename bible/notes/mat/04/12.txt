===== Translation Notes: =====

This section describes the beginning of Jesus' ministry in Galilee.

  * **Now** - This word is used here to mark a break in the main storyline. Here Matthew starts to tell a new part of the story.
  * **John had been arrested** - AT: "the king had imprisoned John" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
