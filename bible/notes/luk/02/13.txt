===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **a large number of**  - That is, "very many members of" or, "a large group of."  
    * **the heavenly army**  - That is, "the army of heavenly beings" or, "group of angels" or, "angels from heaven." The phrase used here literally refers to an army of angels, but it could be a metaphor that refers to an organized group. (See [[:en:ta:figuresofspeech:metaphor|Metaphor]].)
    * **Glory to God in the highest**  - This could either mean "Give glory to God in the highest place" or, "Give the highest glory to God." It could also be translated as "speak of God's glory in the highest place" or, "Give God the highest praise." 
    * **may there be peace on earth among people with whom he is pleased**  - That is, "may those on earth with whom God is pleased have peace."
