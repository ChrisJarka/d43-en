===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He will be a sanctuary **  - This is a singular form of 'you' and can be translated as, "He will protect you." (See: Forms of 'you' - Singular)
  * **<font 14px line-height: 19px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>but he will be a stone that makes one trip and a rock that makes one stumble</font>    **- This is a metaphor that can be translated as " but Yahwey will cause his enemies to stumble and fall." This is also a doublet where the same thought is repeated twice for emphasis. (See: Metaphor and Doublet)
  * **to both the houses of Israel **- This is a metonym that refers to the "other people in Judah and Jerusalem."
