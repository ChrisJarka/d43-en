====== assembly ======

===== Definition: =====

An assembly is a group of people who are called together to discuss, give advice, and make decisions about both routine and important matters.

  * An assembly can be a group that is organized in an official and somewhat permanent way, or it can refer to a group of people that come together temporarily to handle a certain situation.
  * The Jewish assembly was also known as the Sanhedrin, and its members were Pharisees and Sadducees.
  * Paul was brought before a Roman assembly when he was arrested for teaching the gospel.
  * The word "assembly" can also be translated as "council".

(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:council|council]], [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:sanhedrin|sanhedrin]])
