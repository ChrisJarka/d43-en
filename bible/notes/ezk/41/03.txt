===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **cubits** - See how you translated these "long" cubits in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:05|40:05]].
  * **two cubits** - About one meter. (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaldistance]])
  * **six cubits** - About 3.2 meters.
  * **seven cubits** - About 3.8 meters.
  * **twenty cubits** - About eleven meters.
  * **Then he went inside** - The word "he" refers to the man at the gate (40:3)
  * **Then he went inside and measured the post of the doorway**  - Alternate translation: "Then he went inside and measured the post of the doorway, which was six cubits, and the doorway was six cubits in height".
  * **Then he measured its length - twenty cubits. And its width - twenty cubits to the front of the temple hall** - Alternate translation: "Then he measured its length of twenty cubits and its width of twenty cubits to the front of the temple hall".
