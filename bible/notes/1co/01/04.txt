===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I** - "I" refers to Paul, the author.
  * **always** - "very often" (UDB; See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]]).
  * **because of the grace** - Possible meanings are 1) "because of the grace" or 2) "for the grace", that is, for introduces the content of the thanksgiving.
  * **grace of God that Christ Jesus gave to you** - Alternate translation: "the grace that God gave to you who are in Christ Jesus".
  * **He has made you rich** - Possible meanings are 1) "Christ has made you rich" or 2) "God has made you rich".
  * **rich** - This refers to richness in spiritual blessings.
  * **all knowledge** - "much knowledge" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **testimony about Christ** - "the message about Christ"
  * **has been confirmed among you** - "has clearly changed your lives" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **you** - The word "you" refers to all the believers in Corinth. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_youdual]])
