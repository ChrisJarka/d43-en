====== ash, ashes ======

===== Facts: =====
Ash is the powder residue, usually black, left over after burning something. 

  * Ash is also a type of strong straight tree with light wood.
  * In the Bible ashes were often used as a symbol. 
  * People would cover themselves in ashes as a sign of grief or pain.
  * Putting ashes on your head, or someone else's head was a sign of humiliation or embarrassment.
  * When someone is striving for something vain it is said to be like "feeding on ashes."
