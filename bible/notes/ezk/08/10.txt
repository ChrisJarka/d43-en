===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I went in **- "I" is Ezekiel speaking.
  * **behold**  - The word “behold” here shows that [the person] was surprised by what he saw.
  * **There was every form of creeping thing and detestable beast**  - Ezekiel is saying there were many creeping things and detestable beasts. (See: [[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:hyperbole]])
  * **the house of Israel**  - See how you translated this in 3:1.
  * **Jaazaniah, the son of Shaphan**  - This is the name of one of the seventy elders of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
  * **They were standing in front of the images **  , ** and each man had his censer in his hand - **  "They" and "his" refers to these elders of the previous sentence.
  * **hand - **  Here "hand" is the human body's hand.
