===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **David also pronounces blessing upon the man to whom God counts righteousness without works **- This could be translated as "Similarly, David wrote about how God blesses the man whom God makes righteous without works."
  * **verses 7 and 8 - **  Verses 7 and 8 quote from the Psalms. They repeat the same concept in three different ways. This is a type of Hebrew poetry and shows that concept is important. (see: triplet)
  * **whose iniquities are forgiven, and whose sins are covered. **- This could be translated as "whose iniquities God forgives, and whose sins God covers." (see: Passive/Active)
