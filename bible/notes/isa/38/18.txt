===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For Sheol does not thank you; death does not praise you** - The words "Sheol" and "death" are used to substitute for "dead people." "Dead people cannot thank or praise Yahweh. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Those who go down into the pit do not hope in your trustworthiness** - "those who have gone to their grave no longer have hope that Yahewh will do the good that he has promised"
