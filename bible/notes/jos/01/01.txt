===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Yahweh** - This is the name of God that he revealed to his people in the Old Testament. See the note on the Important Term page concerning how to translate this. 
    * **cross over this Jordan** - "cross over" means "to go to the opposite bank of the river.' AT: "traverse from this side to the opposite side of the Jordan." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
    * **you and all this people** - "you" here refers to Joshua. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_yousingular]] and [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive]])  
    * **sole of your foot**  - Here the sole of a foot is used to refer the entire nation of Israel. AT: " Where ever your foot touches, that land will belong to Israel." (See: [[en:ta:translate:synecdoche]])
    * **I have given to you** - "you" in this phrase refers to bother Joshua and the nation of Israel. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_youdual]] and [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
