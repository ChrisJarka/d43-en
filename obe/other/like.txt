====== like, likeness ======

===== Definition: =====

To be "like" something is to have a similarity to it. The term "likeness" describes the similarities to something.

  * Man was created in God's likeness; we have similar qualities such as creativity and self-consciousness.
  * The Bible frequently uses the term "it was like", which means "it was similar to". God is described to be like a hen who gathers her chicks uner her wings; God does a similar thing when shielding His people by divinely protecting them.

(See also: image of God other)
