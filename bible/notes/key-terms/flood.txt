====== flood ======

===== Definition: =====

A flood is a great amount of water that covers over land completely so that the ground cannot be seen.

  * In Noah's time, people had become so evil that God caused a worldwide flood to come over the entire surface of the earth, even covering the mountaintops.
  * Every living person and creature on earth was killed in the flood, except for Noah and his family and all the animals they had brought with them on the ark.

(See also: Noah, ark, other)
