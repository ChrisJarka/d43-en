====== Aquila ======

===== Facts: =====

Aquila was a first century Jewish Christian who, along with his wife, Priscilla, worked with Paul in his missionary work.

  * Aquila was a native of Pontus, near the southern end of the Black Sea.
  * Both Aquila and Priscilla are mentioned several times in the New Testament, as being helpers of Paul.
  * According to church tradition, Paul named Aquila as the bishop of Asia Minor.
