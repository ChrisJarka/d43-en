===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **and has not eaten on the mountains**  - "He does not eat meat sacrificed to idols on the hilltops," (UBD) or "He does not take part in sacrifices to idols on the mountains." Shrines to idols were often on mountains or hilltops.
  * **nor lifted up his eyes to the idols**  - Another translation could be "nor look to idols for help" or "nor worshipped idols."
  * **menstruation**  - the monthly discharge of blood and tissue from a woman.
