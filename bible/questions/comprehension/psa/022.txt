====== Psalms Chapter 022 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What is David asking God?

A. David is asking God why God has abandoned him, why is is so far from saving him, and far from the words of his anguish. [22:1]

Q? What does David do in the daytime and at night?

A. David cries out in the daytime but God does not answer, and at night David is not silent.  [22:2]

Q? How does David refer to God?

A. David refers to God as Holy, sitting as king with the praises of Isreal. [22:3]
