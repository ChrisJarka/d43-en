===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues telling Isaiah what he should tell Ahaz. (See: [[en:bible:notes:isa:07:03]])
  * **Aram, Ephraim, and the son of Remaliah** - The words "Aram" and "Ephraim" refer to the kings of these lands. Also, "Ephraim" refers to the northern kingdom of Israel. Alternate translation: "Rezin the king of Aram and Pekah the son of Remaliah, king of Israel. (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  * **Remaliah** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:07:01|7:1]]
  * **have planned evil against you**  - Here "you" is singular and refers to Ahaz. (See: [[en:ta:translate:formsofyou]])
  * **and terrify her, and let us break into her**  - This refers to the people of Judah.
  * **son of Tabeel**  - It is unknown who this man is. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
