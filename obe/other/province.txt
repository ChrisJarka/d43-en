====== province, provincial ======

===== Facts: =====

An administrative term, a province is a realm or distrct usually of geographic significance over which a magistrate or governor governs.

  * In the OT provinces were the divisions of Israel under King Ahab and during the time of Esther,
  * In the NT the Roman Empire was divided into provinces among which were Macedonia, Cyprus, Asia, Syria, Egypt, and Galatia.
