===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (This passage comes from Isaiah 11:10. The root of Jesse refers to King David, since he was Jesse's physical son. But it also is referring to Jesus Christ whose ancestors were Jesse and King David.)
  * **root of Jesse** - Alternate translation: "ancestor of Jesse".
  * **and he who arises** - The pronoun "he" refers to Jesus Christ.
  * **in him will the Gentiles have confidence** - The pronoun "him" refers to Jesus Christ. Alternate translation: " in Jesus will the Gentiles have hope"
