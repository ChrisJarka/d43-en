===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Daughter of Zion...Daughter of Judah**  - (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **Covered the daughter of Zion with a dark cloud**  - The writer uses weather to illustrate the angry feelings of God and the doom of God's judgment. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **cast down the beauty of Israel**  from heaven to earth- This is a metaphor referring to Judah losing her position of influence and favor with God
  * **swallowed up**  - This is a metaphor for God's total destruction of Judah as a nation.
  * **thrown down the fortified cities **  - simile -This refers to the destruction of Israel's key cities for defense of their country.
  * **struck them down to the ground**  -This is a metaphor referring to Judah's humiliation by the Babilonians in Judah losing the war and experiencing the subsequent captivity of the royalty family.
