===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues his parable about a man paying his workers. This is continuing the story of the landowner and the workers.

  * **one of them** - "one of the workers who were hired"
  * **Did you not agree with me for one denarius** - The landowner uses a rhetorical question to reminds the worker that he had agreed with him on the wages. AT: "we already agreed on one denarius." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **a denarius** - "one day's wages" (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicalmoney]])
  * **it is my pleasure to give** - "it pleases me to give"
