====== Ruth ======

===== Facts: =====

Ruth was a Moabite woman who lived during the time of Israel's Judges. She was an ancestor of Jesus Christ.

  * Ruth is noted for her loyalty and hard-working, servant heart.
  * Ruth was a widow who became the great-grandmother of one of Israel's greatest kings, David.
