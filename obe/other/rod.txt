====== rod ======

===== Definition: =====

A rod is a wooden shaft or stick about one meter (about three feet) in length used as a tool to guide sheep by shepherds.

  * Shepherds use a long staff to gently guide sheep, and also us a rod to throw to warn or firmly guide sheep.
  * King David spoke of God's guidance as a metaphorical rod and staff in Psalm 23.

(See also: other)
