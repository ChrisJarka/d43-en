====== Dedan ======

===== Facts: =====

There were two different men named Dedan in the Bible.

  * The great-grandson of Ham was named Dedan.
  * The grandson of Abraham was named Dedan.
  * The descendants of Dedan are also referred to as the "Dedanites" or sometimes just as, <font 14px line-height: 17.8181819915772px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>"</font> Dedan.<font 14px line-height: 17.8181819915772px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>"</font>
  * Sometimes the region where the Dedanites lived is just referred to as "Dedan" in the Bible; it also says they lived in Arabia.
  * <font 14px line-height: 17.8181819915772px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Some translators may want to spell Dedan  differently in different contexts in a translation to make sure it is clear that there were two different people by this name.</font>
  * <font 14px line-height: 17.8181819915772px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>When it refers to a place this could be translated as, "the region of Dedan."</font>
