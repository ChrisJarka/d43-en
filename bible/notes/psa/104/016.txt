===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **nest**  - a place where a bird lays its eggs and takes care of its young
  * **stork**  - a large bird that has long legs and a long bill and neck
  * **heights**  - a great distance above the ground
  * **hyrax**  - a small, furry animal which lives in the rocks below Jerusalem
