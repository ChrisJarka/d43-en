===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **While he was yet speaking, another also came** - "He" refers to the messenger who spoke to Job in v.14-15.  "Another" refers to another messenger.  Alternate translation: "While one messenger was speaking to Job, another also came to him"
  * **Your sons and your daughters were eating and drinking wine in their oldest brother's house** - See how you translated this in 01:13.
  * **A great wind came from the wilderness** - This great wind could refer to a tornado or a desert sandstorm.
  * **the four corners of the house** - "the structural supports of the house"
  * **and it fell upon the young men** - Here you can begin a new sentence.  The house fell on both the men and women. Alternate translation: "The house fell on your sons and daughters" 
  * **I only have escaped to tell you** - See how you translated this in 01:15.
