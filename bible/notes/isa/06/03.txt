===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Each one called to another and said**  – “The seraphim called out to one another” or "The seraphim proclaimed to one another"
  * * **Holy, holy, holy** - Repeating the word "holy" three times indicates the completeness and extreme degree of the holiness of God. This could be translated as "Completely holy" or"The very most holy" or "Holy beyond all else" or "Extremely holy."
  * **The whole earth is full of his glory**  – This could be translated as “The glory of Yahweh fills all the earth”
