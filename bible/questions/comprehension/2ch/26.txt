====== 2 Chronicles Chapter 26 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who made Uzziah king after the death of his father Amaziah?

A. The people of Judah took Uzziah and made him king. [26:1]

Q? What did Uzziah do to the town of Eloth after he became king?

A. Uzziah rebuilt Eloth and restored it to Judah. [26:2]

Q? How did Uzziah follow the example of his father in his relationship to Yahweh?

A. Uzziah did what was right in the eyes of Yahweh and set himelf to seek God. [26:4-5]

Q? What instructions did Zechariah give to Uzziah and what happened as long as he followed those instructions?

A. Zechariah gave Uzziah instructions for obeying God and as long as he sought Yahweh, God made him prosper. [26:5-6]

Q? What was the result of God's helping Uzziah as he fought with his enemies?

A. God helped Uzziah defeat his enemies, his fame spread to other lands and he became very powerful. [26:6-8]

Q? What did Uzziah build at the gates in Jerusalem and in the wilderness?

A. Uzziah built towers at the corner gates, at the turning wall and he built watch towers in the wilderness. [26:9-10]

Q? Why did Uzziah build watch towers and why did he dige cisterns in the wilderness?

A. Uzziah did these things because he had cattle and people who farmed and he loved farming. [26:10]

Q? How is Uzziah's army of fighting men described?

A. Uzziah's army made war with mighty power to help the king against the enemy [26:11, 13]

Q? What was invented by skillful men in Jerusalem as Uzziah equipped his army?

A. In Jerusalem Uzziah built engines that were invented by skillful men. [26:14-15]

Q? How did Uzziah trespass against Yahweh, his God when he became powerful?

A. Uzziah trespassed against Yahweh when he went into the house of Yahweh to burn incense on the altar of incense. [26:16]

Q? Why did Azariah and the eighty priests say that Uzziah had trespassed when he burned incense in the sanctuary?

A. The priests told Uzziah that it is only the priests, the sons of Aaron, who are consecrated to burn incense in the sanctuary so Uzziah had trespassed. [26:17-18]

Q? What happened to Uzziah as he was holding a censer in his hand to burn incense and when he was angry with the priests?

A. While Uzziah was holding a censer and became angry with the priests, leprosy broke out on his forehead. [26:19]

Q? Why did lebrosy break out on Uzziah's forehead?

A. Lebrosy broke out on Uzziah's forehead because Yahweh had struck him. [26:20]

Q? Why did Uzziah have to live in a separate house until the day of his death?

A. Uzziah had to live in a separate house because he was a leper and he was cut off from the house of Yahweh. [26:21]

Q? Who became king after Uzziah died and was buried with his ancestors?

A. Jotham, Uzziah's son, became king in his place. [26:23]
