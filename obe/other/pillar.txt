====== pillar ======

===== Definition: =====

A large vertical stone used to hold up a roof or other part of a building.

  * A pillar is normally carved from one piece of stone.
  * When Samson was captured by Philistines in the Old Testament, he destroyed the Philistine pagan temple by pushing the supporting pillars and causing the structure to collapse.

(See also: other)
