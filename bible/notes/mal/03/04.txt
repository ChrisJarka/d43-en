===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then the offering of Judah and Jerusalem will be pleasing to Yahweh, -**  "Then once again the sacrifice of the people of Judah and Jerusalem will be acceptable to Yahweh,"
  * **as in the days of old, and as in ancient years. -**  "as it was in the past."
  * **"Then I will draw near to you for judgment. -**  " I will come close to you to judge you (or "put you on trial")."
  * **I will be a swift witness against the sorcerers, the adulterers, the false witnesses, -**  "I will be an eager witness against sorcerers, adulterers, liars,"
  * **and against those who oppress the hired worker in his wages, -**  " and against those who cheat hired workers."
  * **those who oppress the widow and the fatherless, -**  " I will speak (or 'testify', or 'witness') against those who cruelly treat widows and the fatherless,"
  * **and who turn away the foreigner from his rights, -**  "and against those who do not let foreigners that are living in their community have the same rights that the local people have,"
  * **and against those who do not honor me," says Yahweh of Hosts. -**  "because these men do not honor me," says Yahweh of Hosts."
