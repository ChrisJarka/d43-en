====== qualify, qualified ======

===== Definition: =====

To be entitled to or have credentials for a particular benefit.

  * Colossians 1 says that Jesus has made believers qualified to share in His inheritance.
  * The believer's qualification or entitlement to spiritual priveledge is not due to their own merit but solely based upon the believer's faith in Jesus' work on their behalf.

(See also: other)
