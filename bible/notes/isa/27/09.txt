===== translationNotes: =====

  * **So in this way** - Possible meanings are 1) "this" refers to Yahweh sending the people into exile as Isaiah mentioned in the previous verse or 2) "this" refers to the actions that Isaiah will mention in the next part of verse 9. 
  * **the iniquity of Jacob will be atoned for** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh will purge the sin from the Israelites" or "Yahweh will forgive the sins of the Israelites" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **iniquity of Jacob** - Here "Jacob" represents the descendants of Jacob. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **for this will be** - Here "this" refers to the actions that Isaiah will describe in the next part of verse 9.
  * **the full fruit** - This speaks of the results of an action as if it were the fruit that grows as on a tree or vine. AT: "the result" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **of turning away from his sin** - The people no longer sinning against Yahweh is spoken of as if sin were an object and the people would turn away from it. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **He will make all the altar stones as chalk and pulverized, and no Asherah poles or incense altars will remain standing** - Here "He" refers to Jacob who represents his descendants. AT: "They will completely destroy all the altars to false gods and remove all the Asherah idols" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
