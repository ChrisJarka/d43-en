====== 2 Samuel 13 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter begins the story of the troubles and killings in David's family (13-20).

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Amnon raped Absalom's sister ==
Crown prince Amnon raped Absalom's sister, who was his own half-sister. David did nothing to punish him but Absalom hated him for what he had done to his sister.
== Absalom's revenge ==
Two years later, Absalom invited all his brothers to celebrate his sheep shearing time. When Amnon was drunk, Absalom had his servants kill Amnon. Then Absalom fled to his maternal grandfather, the king of Geshur.
