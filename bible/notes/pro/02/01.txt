===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **receive**  - The word "receive" is used in the sense of "accept" or "welcome."
    * **treasure up my commandments with you**  - "learn my commandments with the same zeal that you store up physical wealth" (See: Metaphor)
    * **treasure**  - to retain carefully or keep in store, as in the mind. 
    * **incline your heart to understanding** - "earnestly want to gain understanding"
