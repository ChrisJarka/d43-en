====== Reflexive Pronoun ======

===== Description =====

text

===== Usage =====

A note is only necessary when __both the ULB and UDB are unclear about who the pronoun is referring to__.


===== Template =====

Template fields: (See: [[:en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:template|General Template]])

  - **(Required)**  List the text that includes the pronoun from the ULB in bold
  - (Optional) State a clarifying translation
  - **(Required)**  State the pronoun and identify to whom or what it refers
  - (Optional) Suggest alternate ways to translate the pronoun
  - **(Required)**  Add TA Link: (See: Pronoun Referent)
  * **(1) ULB text**  - **(3)**  State the pronoun and referent. **(5)**  (See: Pronoun Referent)

===== Bible Examples =====

Field reference numbers are included for clarity purposes only. They are omitted in the actual note.

=== Act:20:13 ===

We ourselves went ahead of Paul by ship and sailed away to Assos, where we planned to take Paul on board. 

  * **(1) We ourselves** - **(3)** The pronoun, 'ourselves', is used here to add emphasis and to separate Luke and his fellow travelling companions from Paul, who did not travel by boat. **(5)** (See: Reflexive Pronoun)
