===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I will again bring** - Here "I" refers to Yahweh.
  * **Mareshah** - You may want to make a footnote saying, "The name of this village sounds like the Hebrew word for 'conqueror.'" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Adullam** - name of a royal city in Philistia (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Shave your head and cut off your hair** - Israelites who were mourning would shave bald spots on their heads. Possible meanings are 1) "Shave larger spots than usual on your heads" or 2) "Cut off all your hair and shave your heads," stating the events in the order in which they were to occur. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **eagles** - This word can also be translated "vultures."
