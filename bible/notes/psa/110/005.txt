===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The Lord is at your right hand** - When the lord goes to battle, Yahweh stands at his right hand in order to help him. AT: "The Lord helps you in battle" or "The Lord stands at your right hand in battle." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **The Lord** - The word "Lord" refers to Yahweh.
  * **your right hand** - David is speaking to the lord, whom Yahweh has made king.
  * **He will kill** - The word "He" refers to the Yahweh who does these things through the king. So, in some sense, "He" refers to both Yahweh and the king.
  * **will kill kings** - "will cut through kings" or "will crush kings"
  * **on the day of his anger** - AT: "when shows his anger" or "on the day that he is angry." 
  * **with dead bodies** - "with corpses"
