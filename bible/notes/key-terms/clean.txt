====== clean, cleanse ======


===== Definition: =====


Clean is to be free of sin or unholiness.


    * In the Old Testament, only certain foods, animals, and hygiene practices were clean.
    * Clean relates to holiness.


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:holy|holiness]], [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:unclean|unclean]])
