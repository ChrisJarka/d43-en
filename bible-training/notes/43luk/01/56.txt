===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **returned to her house**  - That is, "Mary returned to her (Mary's) house."
    * **deliver her baby** - That is, "give birth (to her baby)" or, "bear her child."
    * **Her neighbors and her relatives**  - That is, "Elizabeth's neighbors and relatives"
    * **had been so merciful to her**  - That is, "had been very kind to Elizabeth" or, "had acted very kindly toward Elizabeth."
