===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they all seized** - This is an exaggeration to emphasize the strong feelings the people had. AT: "many people seized" or "many of them grabbed." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole|Hyperbole]])
  * **Sosthenes, the ruler of the synagogue** - "Sosthenes" was a Jewish ruler of the synagogue at Corinth. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|How to Translate Names]])
  * **beat him** - Sosthenes was physically beaten. AT: "hit him" or "punched him."
