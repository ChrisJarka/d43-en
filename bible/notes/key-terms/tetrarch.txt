====== tetrarch ======


===== Definition: =====


Tetrarch is the title of a governing official in the Roman Empire who ruled 1/4 of a province. The modern equivalent corresponds roughly to that of a lieutenant governor or perhaps the mayor of a large city.   


    * "Tetrarch" means "ruler of a fourth" indicating that the ruler did not have absolute authority.
    * "Tetrarch" can be translated as "prince."
