===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh speaking about the leaders of Jerusalem

    * **a man from them who would build up a wall** - This is used to refer to a leader putting himself between Yahweh and the people and taking responsibility to lead them back into righteousness. AT: "a man from them who would act like a wall" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
    * **stand in the breach** - This phrase means to stand in the place the wall has a gap or has been broken. A leader to defend the people of Israel like a warrior defending a city in battle. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
    * **pour out my indignation upon them!** - God describes his judgment as anger that can be poured out like water from a bowl. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
    * **I will finish them with the fire of my indignation** - This phrase means that God will punish them severely in His anger.
    * **set their way upon their own heads** - The Israelites have treated others badly in many ways. This phrase means that God will treat them as they have treated others. (See: Metaphor)
    * **declaration**  - This word means a formal announcement either verbally or in writing.
