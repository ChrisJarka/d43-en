===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Oh that people would praise Yahweh** - "Let people praise Yahweh"
  * **for his covenant faithfulness** - "because of his covenant faithfulness"
  * **for humanity** - "for all people" or "for the descendants of Adam." The phrase "the descendants of Adam" is a way of referring to "all people" because Adam was the first person and everyone is his descendant.
  * **** - Some of the people that Yahweh


  * **satisfies those **- "satisfies people"
  * **and those who are hungry he fills with good things **- "and he gives hungry people good things to eat."
