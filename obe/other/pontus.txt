====== Pontus ======

===== Facts: =====

Pontus was a Roman province during the time of the Roman Empire and the early Church. It was located along the coast of the Black Sea, in the northern part of what is now the country of Turkey.

  * People from the province of Pontus were in Jerusalem when the Holy Spirit first came to the apostles on the Day of Pentecost.
  * The Greek Christian Apollos was from Pontus.
  * Peter wrote to all the Christians who were scattered into different regions such as Pontus.
