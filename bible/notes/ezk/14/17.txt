===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **bring a sword against that land** - The word "sword" is a way to refer to an army.  One sword is used to refer to all the swords of an army. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **that land** - "the land of Israel"
  * **cut off both man and beast from it**  - This phrase refers to being killed as an act of divine judgment for sin. AT: "kill both man and beast in it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **these three men** - Noah, Daniel, and Job
