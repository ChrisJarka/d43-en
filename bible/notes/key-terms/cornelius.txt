====== Cornelius ======

===== Facts: =====

Cornelius was a non-Jewish man who was a military officer in the Roman army.

  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>He prayed regularly to God and was very generous in giving to the poor.</font>
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>When Cornelius and his family heard the apostle Peter explain the gospel, they became believers in Jesus.</font>
  * The people of Cornelius' household were the first non-Jewish people <font 14px line-height: 13.0666666030884px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>to become believers</font> .
  * Cornelius' conversion showed that Jesus had come to save non-Jewish people, too.
