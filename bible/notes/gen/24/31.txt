===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Come**  - "Come in" or "Enter"
  * **you blessed of Yahweh** - "you who Yahweh has blessed"
  * **you** - Here the word "you" refers to Abraham's servant"
  * **What are you standing outside?** - This is a rhetorical question which Laban uses to invite Abraham's servant into his house. You could translate it as "You do not need to stay outside." (See: [[en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]])
  * **unloaded**  - "took the supplies off of"
  * **to wash**  - "for washing."
