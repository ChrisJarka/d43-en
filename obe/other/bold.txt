====== bold, boldness ======

===== Definition: =====

The word "bold" describes a person who is willing to face danger with confidence.  "Boldness" is the state of being "bold."

  * In the New Testament, Christ's disciples were frequently bold in speaking and preaching about Him because the Jewish leaders rejected Christ.
  * The boldness of the Christ's early disciples speaking the good news of His redeeming death on the cross was responsible for the growth of Christianity in Israel and nearby countries.
