===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **After he was baptized **- This could be translated by saying: "After John baptized Jesus."
  * **the heavens were opened **- This could be translated as "he saw the sky opened" or "he saw the heavens opened." <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>(See: Active or Passive)</font>
  * **descending as**** a dove **- This is a simile showing how the Spirit of God gently came down upon Jesus when He was baptized. (See: Simile)
