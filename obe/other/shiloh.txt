====== Shiloh ======

===== Facts: =====

Shiloh was a walled Canaanite city which was conquered the Israelites.

  * The city of Shiloh was located west of the Jordan River and northeast of the city of Bethel.
  * During the time that Joshua was leading Israel, the city of Shiloh was a meeting place for the people of Israel.
  * At Shiloh Joshua told each of the twelve tribes of Israel which portion of the land of Canaan had been given to them.
  * Before any temple was built in Jerusalem, Shiloh was the place where the Israelites came to sacrifice to God.
  * Samuel's mother Hannah took him as a young boy to Shiloh to dedicate him to the Lord. He stayed there with the priest Eli to be trained to serve the Lord.
