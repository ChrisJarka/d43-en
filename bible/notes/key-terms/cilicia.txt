====== Cilicia ======

===== Facts: =====

Cilicia was a area in southern Asia Minor.
    * Paul was a citizen of Taursus, in Cilicia.
    * Paul spent several years in Cilicia, after his conversion.
    * Cilicia is mentioned several times in the New Testament.
    * Some of the Jews from Cilicia were among those who confronted Stephen.
