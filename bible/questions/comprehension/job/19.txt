====== Job Chapter 19 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? How does Job answer Bildad about the words he has spoken to Job?

A. Job answers Bildad asking him how long will you make me suffer and break me into pieces of wood. [19:1 - 2]

Q? How many times has Bildad reproached Job and how has he treated Job?

A. Bildad reproached Job ten times and was not ashamed that he treated Job harshly. [19:3]

Q? What does Job say to Bildad about any errors that may be true?

A. Job says the error remains his own concern. [19:4]

Q? What does Job say Bildad will do and what does he tell Bildad he should know about God?

A. Job says Bildad will vaunt himself against him and convince everyone that he is disgraced. God has caught him in God's net. [19:5 - 6]

Q? What happens when Job calls out for help?

A. Job says he is not heard, that he has not done wrong and that there is no justice. [19:7]

Q? What does Job say God has done to him?

A. He has walled up Job's way so he could not pass and Godset darkness in his path. [19:8]
