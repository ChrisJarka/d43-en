===== Translation Notes: =====

The prophet Nathan is continuing to speak to Bathsheba.

    * **Go present yourself to King David** - AT: "Go to King David"
    * **'My master the king... is Adonijah reigning?'** - Nathan is telling Bathsheba to remind King David of the promise he made to her and ask if he will keep his promise of her son Solomon being the next king.  
    * **did you not swear to your servant saying**  - AT: "You made this promise to me"
    * **Surely Solomon your son will reign after me, and he will sit on my throne**  - "Your son Solomon will be the next king, and he will sit on my throne and rule"
    * **confirm your words**  - "tell him what you are saying is true"
