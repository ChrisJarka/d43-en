====== Daniel 08 General Notes ======



=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== The vision of the ram and male goat  ==

Although specific interpretation of this vision is not given, most scholars believe Daniel saw Greece overthrowing Media-Persiabefore breaking up into four kingdoms. One of these kingdom stopped the temple worship for a while and then it was restored.
