====== chosen one ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Bible, the term "chosen one" usually refers to Jesus as the one whom God chose to be the Christ.

  * To be chosen is to be appointed for a special purpose. Before the world was created, God chose Jesus to be the Savior of his people.
  * God also uses "chosen one" to refer to certain leaders over the Israelites whom he appointed to fulfill his special purposes.
  * The people of God are also referred to as his "chosen ones."

(See also: Christ, Messiah, elect)
