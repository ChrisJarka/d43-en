====== Genesis 01:3-5 ======


===== TFT: =====

<usfm>
\v 3 God said, "I want light to shine!" And light shone.
\v 4 God was pleased with the light. Then he made the light to shine in some places, and in other places there was still darkness.
\v 5 He gave to the light the name 'day,' and he gave to the darkness the name 'night.' Then there was an evening which was followed by a morning. He called that period of time 'the first day.'

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **God saw the light **- "seeing" here means "perceiving, making a decision about, considering.
  * **it was good**
 - God judged (saw) and decided that the light was beautiful and pleasing. "Good" here isn't comparing light to anything else or making a moral judgment; it means that God was pleased with what he made.
