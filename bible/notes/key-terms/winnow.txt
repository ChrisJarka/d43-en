====== winnow, sift ======

===== Definition: =====

To winnow means to separate grain from the unwanted chaff by throwing it into the air and allowing the wind to blow the chaff away. The word "sift" is used to describe the separation of materials through shaking.

  * Winnowing forks were used to throw the grain into the air.
  * In the Bible, the image of winnowing grain is used to describe how God separates righteous people from unrighteous people.
