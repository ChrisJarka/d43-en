====== Kedar ======

===== Facts: =====
Kedar was the name of Ismael's second son. It is also the name of a city in the northern part of Arabia near the southern border of Palestine. 

  * The city of Kedar may have been named after Ishmael's son. 
  * Kedar was known for its greatness and beauty.
  * The phrase "tents of Kedar" indicates that the people of Kedar may have been nomads moving from place to place and living in tents.
  * In the Bible the phrase "the glory of Kedar" is referring to the greatness of the people.
