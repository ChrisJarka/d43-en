===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now this man bought a field with the earnings of his evil act.**  - Judas bought a plot of land with the money he received in return for betraying Jesus.
  * **Then falling head first his body burst wide open and all his bowels poured out.**  - On this plot of land, Judas fell on his head.  The fall was so severe that it caused his body to burst open and innards to fall out.
  * **It became known to all those living in Jerusalem that the field was called in their own language Akeldama, that is, The field of blood.)**  - As a result of what happened on the land, the people in Jerusalem began referring to it as "Akeldama," which means "Field of Blood."
