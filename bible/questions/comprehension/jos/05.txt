Q? What happened to the hearts of the kings of the Amorites and the Canaanites when they heard how Yahweh had dried up the waters of the Jordon until the people of Israel had crossed over?

A. When the kings of the Amorites and the Canaanites heard how Yahweh had dried up the waters of the Jordon, their hearts melted and there was no longer any spirit in them. [5:1)
