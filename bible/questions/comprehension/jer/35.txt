====== Jeremiah Chapter 35 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Yahweh ask Jeremiah to do?

A. Yahweh asked Jeremiah to go to the clan of the Rechabites, speak with them, bring them to Yahweh's house, and there give them wine to drink. [35:1-2]

Q? How did the clan of the Rechabites respond to Jeremiah's offer of wine?

A. The Rechabites would not drink any wine because their ancestor, Jonadab, commanded them to drink no wine. [35:3-6]

Q? Why did Jonadab command the Rechabites to not build any houses, sow any seeds or plant any vineyards?

A. Jonadab gave them this command because they were to live in tents all their days. [35:7]

Q? How did the Rechabites respond to Jonadab's command?

A. The Rechabites obeyed his voice by never drinking wine or building houses to live in, but they went to live in Jerusalem when king Nebuchadneszzar attacked the land. [35:8-11]

Q? How did Yahweh use the Rechabites as a positive example for the men of Judah?

A. Yahweh reminded the men of Judah that the Rechabites obeyed Jonadab's command to not drink any wine and so the men of Judah should obey the proclaimations of Yahweh in the same way. [35:12-14]

Q? What did the prophets tell the men of Judah?

A. The prophets said, "Let each person turn from his wicked way, do good deeds, no longer worship other gods, but instead come back to the land that Yahweh gave to you." [35:15]

Q? What will Yahweh do because the men of Judah have not obeyed the proclamations of Yahweh?

A. Yahweh will bring on them all the disasters he has proclaimed against them. [35:17]

Q? What promise did Yahweh give to the family of the Rechabites through Jeremiah?

A. Yahweh told them, "You have obeyed all the commands of Jonadab so there will always be someone descended from Jonadab to serve me." [35:18-19]
