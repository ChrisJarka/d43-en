===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But now** - The word "now" refers to the time since Jesus came to the earth. 
  * **without the Law a righteousness of God has been made known** - This can be translated with an active verb: "God has made known a way to be righteous that is apart from obeying the Law." (See: [[en:ta:translate:active]])
  * **witnessed by the Law and the Prophets** - The "Law" and "Prophets" refer to the parts of Scripture that Moses and the prophets wrote. Here these parts of Scripture are described as a person who speaks the truth before a judge. This can be translated with an active verb: "and what is written in the Law and the Prophets confirm this" (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]] and [[en:ta:translate:personification]])
  * **that is, the righteousness of God through faith in Jesus Christ** - This could be translated as a new sentence: "I am referring to a righteousness that God gives us when we believe in Jesus Christ."
  * **For there is no distinction** - The full meaning of this phrase can be made explicit: "For there is no difference between Jews and Gentiles." (See: [[en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]])
