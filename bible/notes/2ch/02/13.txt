===== translationNotes: =====

  * **gifted with understanding** - The abstract noun "understanding" can be stated as "understands" or "wise." AT: "who understand many things" or "who is very wise" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **Huram** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **He is skilled at work in gold ... crimson wool** - Translate many of these words as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:2ch:02:06|2:7]].
  * **fine linen** - "high quality cloth" or "the best cloth"
  * **He is the son of a woman of the daughters of Dan** - "His mother is from the tribe of Dan" or "His mother is a descendant of Dan"
