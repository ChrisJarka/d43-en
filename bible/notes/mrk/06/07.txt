===== Translation Notes: =====

  * ****  This suggests that the disciples sent by Jesus are to depend totally on God and the hospitality of his people for their sustenance.
  * **but to wear sandals, and not to wear two tunics. - ** The pairs of disciples were also not to go dressed as one prepared for a long journey.  Sandals and only one tunic indicated a person who is dressed for a short trip.
