===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **bold words**  - explanation
    * **sewed**  - "fastened" or "joined" 
    * **fig leaves**  - If people do not know what fig leaves are like, this can be translated as "large leaves from a fig tree" or simply "large leaves."
    * **and made coverings for themselves** - This can be translated as "and clothed themselves with them." They did this because the were ashamed. This implicit information can be made explicit if needed as in the UDB. (See: [[en:ta:vocabulary:implicitexplicit]])
