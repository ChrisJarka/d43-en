====== Girgashites ======

===== Facts: =====

The Girgashites were a tribe in the land of Canaan consisting of descendants of Canaan, who was son of Ham.

  * The Girgashites are mentioned only by name in the lineage of Noah and his three sons, Jephath, Shem, and Ham.
