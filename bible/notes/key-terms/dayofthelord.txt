====== day of the Lord ======

===== Description: =====

The Day of the Lord is the time when there will be a general resurrection of the dead, both of the just and unjust, and final judgment of angels and men.

  * Jesus will be the judge.
  * The Day of the Lord is also known as the Last Day.
  * The Day of the Lord cannot be predicted or known before it comes.
