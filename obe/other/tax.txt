====== tax, taxes ======

===== Definition: =====

A tax is a means of raising money to operate a local, state, or national government.  Taxes can refer to more than one tax, or it can refer to the money amount to be paid.

  * A tax is usually a scale based on value, like a percentage of the value of an item.
  * Taxes can be based on the value of land, personal property, items purchased, inheritance, or income.
  * If taxes are not paid, the government can take legal action against a person or business to get the money owed.

(See also: other)
