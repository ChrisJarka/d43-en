===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to teach about riches in the world and rewards in heaven.

  * **Jesus said to them **- Jesus is teaching the twelve disciples.
  * **in the new**** birth**  - "at renewal of all things" or "in the new age." This could refer to when our bodies are made new, and we are living in heaven with Jesus.
  * **when the Son of Man sits **- This could be translated as "when I inherit my authority and glory in heaven."
  * **you also will sit upon twelve thrones, judging **- he is promising that his disciples will also inherit authority to rule
  * **sit upon twelve thrones**  - This is a metonym for doing the work of kings. (See: Metonymy)
