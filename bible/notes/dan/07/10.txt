===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **A river of fire flowed out before him** - The quick way in which fire came from the presence of God is compared to how water flows in a river. AT: "Fire was rushing out in front of him like a stream" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **before him ... served him ... before him** All three times this refers to God, the "Ancient of Days" from [[:en:bible:notes:dan:07:09|7:9]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **millions** - This is the plural form of one million (1,000,000). AT: "a few million" or "several 1,000,000" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **one hundred million** - "100,000,000"
  * **were standing before him** - AT: "were patiently waiting for God to do something" or "were ready to obey God's command"
  * **The court was in session** - This means that there is a judge who is ready to investigate evidence and decide if a person is guilty or innocent of a crime. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the books were opened** - Those are the books that contain the evidence that will be used in court.
