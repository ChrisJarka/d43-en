====== Deuteronomy 22:13-14 ======


===== TFT: =====

<usfm>
\v 13 Suppose a man marries a young woman and has sex with her and later decides that he does not want her anymore,
\v 14 and he says false things about her, and claims that she was not a virgin when he married her.
</usfm>


===== UDB: =====

<usfm>
\v 13 Suppose a man marries a young woman and sleeps with her and later decides that he does not want her anymore,
\v 14 and suppose that he says false things about her, and claims that she was not a virgin when he married her.
</usfm>


===== ULB: =====

<usfm>
\p
\v 13 Suppose a man takes a wife, goes into her, and then hates her,
\v 14 and then accuses her of shameful things and puts a bad reputation on her, and says, "I took this woman, but  when I came near to her, I found no proof of virginity in her."
</usfm>

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **and then hates her -**“and later decides…” (UDB) Note the translation difference. Both present expression of hatred.
  * **"I found no proof of virginity in her"** - AT: "I found her not a virgin" or "I found her not a maid"
