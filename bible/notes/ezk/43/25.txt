===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You will prepare**  -The word "you" refers to the priest.
  * **he-goat**-This is a male goat.
  * **unblemished**  -This is a sacrifice that is not sick or injured.
  * **bull**- This is a male cow or ox.
  * **flock**-This is a group of animals.
  * **declaration**-This means to speak publicly about something important.
  * **this is the Lord Yahweh’s declaration**- See how you translated this in __[[https://door43.org/en/bible/notes/ezk/05/11|5:11]]__.
  * **onward it come about **- We would translate as "following days."
