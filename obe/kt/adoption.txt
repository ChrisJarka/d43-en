====== adoption ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "adoption" refers to the process of someone legally or formally becoming the child of people who are not the biological parents. It is used figuratively in the Bible to describe how God causes people to be part of his family, making them his spiritual sons and daughters.

  * Adoption into God's family occurs the moment that someone believes in Jesus Christ as his Savior.
  * As adopted children, believers are joint-heirs with Jesus Christ, having a right to all the privileges of sons and daughters of God.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * This term can be translated with a term that the target language uses to describe this special parent-child relationship.
