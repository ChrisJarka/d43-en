====== Tirzah ======

===== Facts: =====
Tirzah was an important city for the Canaanites of the Old Testament. It was one of the temporary capitals of the Northern Kingdom of Israel. Tirzah is also the woman's name.

  * It is in the Northern part of Israel but its exact location in unknown, but thought to be about 10 miles North of Shechem.
  * The Bible calls Tirzah a beautiful city.
