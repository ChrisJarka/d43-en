===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Behold**  - The word “behold” here adds emphasis to what follows. It can also be translated as “Indeed.”
  * **not upright within himself** - The word "upright" refers to something that is standing up like a person would stand.  It is used to mean something is correct.  Alternate translation: "not correct within himself" (See: [[en:ta:translate:idiom]])
  * **the righteous will live by his faith** - "the righteous will continue to live because they trust God what God has said is true"
