====== hypocrite, hypocrisy ======


===== Definition: =====


A hypocrite, as the word is used in the Bible, is a person who pretends to be more righteous than he really is. Hypocrisy is the act of being a hypocrite.


    * A hypocrite wants people to see him do good things so that they will think that he is a good person.
    * A hypocrite pretends that he does not sin.
    * A hypocrite might criticize others for doing the same sinful things that he himself does.
