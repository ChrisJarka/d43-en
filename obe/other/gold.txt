====== gold ======

===== Definition: =====

Gold is a precious metal, among the oldest known to man.

  * Gold is found in a pure state in nature.
  * At one time silver was more highly valued than gold.
  * Gold was considered a sign of wealth and prestige, adorning jewelry as well as idols.
