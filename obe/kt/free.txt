====== free, freedom, liberty ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Bible, the term "free" often means to not be a slave or to not be in bondage. <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Freedom is the state of being free; it is the opposite of being in slavery.</font>

  * The term "liberty" has the same meaning as "freedom."
  * Often the terms "free" and "freedom" are used figuratively to refer to how a believer in Jesus is no longer under the power of sin.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * The term "free" could be translated with a word or phrase that means, "not bound" or "not enslaved."
  * The term "freedom" or "liberty" could be translated with a phrase such as, "the state of being free" or "the condition of not being a slave."
