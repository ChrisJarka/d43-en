===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Egyptians will be like women** – This compares fearful, helpless women to the whole nation of Egypt who will be fearful as Yahweh attacks and punishes them. (See: [[en:ta:translate:simile]])
  * **upraised hand of Yahweh of Hosts that he raises over them** - It is as if Yahweh is raising his hand to hit someone who has done wrong. He will raise his hand in judgement to Egypt. 
  * **cause of staggering** - The fear and worry of the Egyptians will increase because of Judah
  * **anyone reminds them of her** – This can be translated "if anyone reminds the Egyptians of Judah.”
  * **because of the plan of Yahweh, that he is planning against them** – “because Yahweh is planning to come against the Egyptians” (See: [[en:ta:translate:active]])
  * **Yahweh of Hosts** - "Yahweh, leader of angel armies"
