===== translationNotes: =====

  * **against me they hope for my hurt** - "they hope that I will be hurt" or "they hope that very bad things will happen to me" (UDB)
  * **An evil disease ... holds on tightly to him** - AT: He has an evil disease that will not leave him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **An evil disease** - Possible meanings are 1) "A fatal disease" or 2) "Something evil" 
  * **has lifted up his heel against me** - "has betrayed me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
