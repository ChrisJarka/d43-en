===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **She **- This pronoun refers to Oholibah.
  * **Her **- This pronoun refers to Oholibah.
  * **Their **- This pronoun refers to the Chaldeans.
  * **Her soul was torn away from them **- An alternate translation could be, "she was angry with them" or "she was disgusted with them." (UDB).
  * **Them **- This pronoun refers to the Chaldeans.
