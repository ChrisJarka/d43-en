===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then it happened** - This phrase is used here to mark the beginning of a new message. If your language has a way for doing this, you could consider using it here.
  * **in the twelfth year, on the fifteenth day of the first month** - It was implied that Ezekiel is counting time from the day that the Babylonians brought Jehoiachin to Babylon. Alternate translation: "eleven years, one months, and fifteen days after the Babylonians took Jehoiachin to Babylon." (See: [[en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]])
  * 
  * 
  * 
  * 
  * **the word of Yahweh came to me and said** - "The Lord God said to me".
  * **cast them down**  - "throw away" or "defeat".
  * **entourage of Egypt** - "those surrounding Egypt".
  * **She and her daughters** - "Egypt and women from mighty nations". 'She' refers to Egypt.
  * **from majestic nations-to lowest lands** - "from the greatest to the least of the nations".
  * **gone down** - "went".
  * **pit** - This is the place of the dead.
