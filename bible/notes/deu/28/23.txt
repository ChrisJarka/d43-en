===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses speaks to the Israelites as if they were one man, so the words "you" and "your" here are singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **skies that are over your head will be bronze** - This metaphor compares the skies to being like bronze. AT: "skies will seem to be as hard as bronze" or "skies will no longer rain" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the earth that is under you will be iron** - This metaphor compares the earth to be hard like iron. AT: "the ground will seem to be as hard as iron" or "nothing will grow from the ground" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **make the rain of your land into powder and dust** - This refers to God sending sandstorms.
  * **until you are destroyed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "until it destroys you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
