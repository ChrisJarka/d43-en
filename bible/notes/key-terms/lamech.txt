====== Lamech ======

===== Facts: =====

Lamech is the name of two men in the book of Genesis.

  * The first Lamech mentioned is a descendant of Cain (a son of Adam) who boasted of killing two men and who married two women.
  * The second Lamech is a descendant of Seth (a son of Adam) and is the father of Noah.
