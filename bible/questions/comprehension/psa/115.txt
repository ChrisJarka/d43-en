====== Psalms Chapter 115 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? For what reason does David pray that Yahweh would bring honor to his name?

A. "To your name bring honor, for your covenant faithfulness and for your trustworthiness." [115:1]

Q? When David says, "Our God is in heaven," what does he say Yahweh does?

A. "He does whatever he pleases." [115:3]

Q? When David says the nations' idols are silver and gold, where do they come from?

A. "The nations' idols are silver and gold, the work of menâ€™s hands." [115:4]

Q? "Those idols have mouths... they have eyes..." but what do they not do?

A. "Those idols have mouths, but they do not speak; they have eyes, but they do not see." [115:5]

Q? At the end of his list, what does David say about, "Those who make them?"  

A. "Those who make them are like them, as is everyone who trusts in them." [115:8]

Q? What three groups are called upon, "To trust in Yahweh because he is your help and shield? 

A. The three groups called to trust are Israel, House of Aaron, and You who honor Yahweh. [115:9-11]

Q? When David says that Yahweh takes notice of us, what can we expect to happen?

A. "Yahweh takes notice of us and will bless us." [115:12]

Q? David says that Yahweh will bless both young and old who do what?

A. "He will bless those who honor him, both young and old." [115:13]

Q? David states that Yahweh made heaven and earth, who does he say the heavens and earth belong to? 

A. "The heavens belong to Yahweh; but the earth he has given to mankind." [115:16]

Q? What does David say about the dead or any who go down into silence? 

A. "The dead do not praise Yahweh, nor do any who go down into silence." [115:17]

Q? How long does David say that we will bless Yahweh?

A. "We will bless Yahweh now and forevermore." [115:18]
