===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **serpent and dragon **- These are the same being according 12:14.
  * **like a river**  -AT:" in large volumes" (See:Simile)
  * **dragon**  - See 12:9 where John identifies the dragon as the "devil or Satan."
  * **It opened its mouth and swallowed**** the river the dragon poured out of his mouth**. - AT:" A hole in the ground opened up and the water went down into the hole." (See:Personification)
