===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The way of the Lord is not fair​**  - An alternate translation could be "The way of the Lord is not correct."
  * **Listen, house of Israel!**  - This phrase is a command or order and deserves exclamation. An alternate translation could be "Hear, house of Israel!"
  * **Are my ways unfair?**  - The purpose of this rhetorical question is to rebuke the listeners. Another way of stating this is, "My ways are not unfair!" (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **Is it not your ways that are unfair?**  - <font 14px line-height: 17.8181819915772px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>This second rhetorical question continues the rebuke to the listeners. Another way of stating this is, "Your ways are unfair!" (See: Rhetorical Question)</font>
  * **When the righteous man turns away from his righteousness **- The phrase "turns away" has the sense of "to stop doing something."
  * **and commits iniquity**  - The word "commit" means "does" or "performs."
  * **and dies because of them **- "Them" refers to the sins or iniquity he committed. "he will die for committing those sins" (UDB)
  * **he dies in the iniquity that he has done **- This repeats the same thought expressed in "and dies because of them."  (See: Doublet)
