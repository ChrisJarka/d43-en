===== Important Terms: ===== 

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **and they untied it**  - The pronoun in 11:4 refers to "the disciples."
  * **they said to them**  - "The people said to the disciples"
  * **disciples**  - The disciples that Jesus sent.
  * **they...them...them**  - The disciples...the people...the disciples
