====== beloved ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "beloved" is an expression of affection that is used to describe someone who is loved and dear to someone else. <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>It literally means "loved (one)."</font>

  * Jesus Christ is referred to as the "Beloved" in several New Testament passages.
  * Christians are also described as "beloved" in numerous NT references.
  * In his letters, the apostle John frequently addresses his fellow believers as "beloved."

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * This term could also be translated as "loved" or "loved one" or "well-loved" or "very dear."

(See also: love other)
