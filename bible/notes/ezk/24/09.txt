===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Therefore, thus says the Lord Yahweh **- see how we translated in Ezekiel 24:6
  * **Woe to the city of blood **- see how we translated in Ezekiel 24:6
  * **I will enlarge the pile of wood **  - This means that Yahweh will make the stack or pile of wood larger.
  * **Kindle the fire **- This means to 'start the fire'
  * **let the bones be charred **  - This means that the bones will turn black from burning.
