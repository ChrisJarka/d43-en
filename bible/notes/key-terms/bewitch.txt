====== bewitch, give the evil eye to ======

===== Definition: =====

Bewitch is to cast a demonic spell on someone.  A sorcerer might case a spell, or bewitch, someone with a look of the eye, or eyes.

  * Bewitch can mean being distracted from the truth by someone.
  * bullet
