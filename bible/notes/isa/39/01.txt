===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Hezekiah rejoiced over them**  - Hezekiah welcomed the messengers with joy because of the letter and the gift the king had sent..
  * **showed them his treasury**  - he showed them everything he had of value (See: Litotes).
  * **there was nothing in his in his house or his realm that Hezekiah did not show them**
 - he showed them everything he had of value (See: Hyperbole).
