===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **curtain**- The word "curtain" refers to the curtain dividing the Holy Place from the Holy of Holies.
  * **covenant decrees**  - The words "covenant decrees" literally means ark of the testimony, and testimony refers to the Ten Commandments.
  * **continually, from morning to evening**  - These words have the same meaning used for emphasis. (See: Doublet)
  * **throughout your**  - The word "your" refers to Moses.
