====== Psalms 028 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 28 is a psalm of prayer to God that he is not be punished along with the wicked people.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

He trusted God completely and God rescued him. This is similar to psalm 7 and 17 because it is an individual lament psalm.
