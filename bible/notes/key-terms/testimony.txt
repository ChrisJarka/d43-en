====== testimony ======


===== Definition: =====


Testimony is evidence that is provided to support a fact or claim. It can be verbal or written.


    * Under an oath of truthfulness, a person gives a testimony in a court of law.
    * A testimony is given to describe how God is at work.
    * A personal Christian testimony is a public profession of one's faith.
