===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **loved** - This refers to brotherly love or love for a friend or family member. This is natural human love between friends or relatives.
  * **Could not this man, who opened the eyes of the one who was blind, have also made this man not die?** - AT: "He could heal a man who was blind, so he could also have healed this man so he would not have died" or "He did not keep this man from dying, so we know that he did not really heal the man born blind, as they say he did." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] and (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_irony]]))
  * **opened the eyes** - healed (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
