===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the descendants of the Kohathites** - The refers to men. AT: "the male descendants of the Kohathites" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **thirty years old ... fifty years old** - "30 years old ... 50 years old" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **join the company to serve in the tent of meeting** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:num:04:01|4:3]].
  * **2,750 men** - Standard practice is to write large numbers as numerals.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
