====== Jonah ======


===== Definition: =====


Jonah was a Hebrew prophet in the 8th century before Christ.


    * The Bible contains an Old Testament book of prophecy entitled Jonah, which is the history of the prophet Jonah alluding the call of God to warn the Assyrian people of Nineveh to change from their wickedness.
    * Jonah runs from God by trying to escape westward on a ship, but Jonah is thrown overboard during a storm, swallowed by a large fish, and is carried to the Assyrian shore of the Mediterranean Sea by the fish.
    * Jonah finally obeys God, and the people of Nineveh repent.


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:nineveh|Nineveh]])
