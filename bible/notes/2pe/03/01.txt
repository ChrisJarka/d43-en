===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Peter begins to talk about the last days.

  * **to arouse your sincere mind** - Peter speaks of causing his readers to think about these things as if he were waking them from sleep. AT: "to cause you to think pure thoughts" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the words said before by the holy prophets** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the words which the holy prophets said before" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the command of our Lord and Savior through your apostles** - "the command of our Lord and Savior which your apostles spoke to you"
