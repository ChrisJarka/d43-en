===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **kings that will arise from the earth** - AT: "kings who will come to power on the earth" (UDB) or "kings that will rise up from among the people of the earth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **and will take possession of** - AT: "and will become the ruler of"
  * **forever, forever and ever** - This triplet is to emphasize that this kingdom will never come to an end. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
