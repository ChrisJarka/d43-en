====== God's law ======


===== Definition: =====

God's law is all of the commands and instructions that he gave to his people Israel.

  * God gave his people many different commands and instructions when he spoke with Moses on Mount Sinai.
  * The Ten Commandments were part of God's law. They were a concise statement of some of the most important responsibilities of Israel towards God and each other. 
  * These commands deal with everyday things like worship, food, health, family relationships, and war.
  * The laws that God gave to Israel were an expression of the moral nature of God. They teach us about the holy character of God, as well as the consequences of disobeying him.
