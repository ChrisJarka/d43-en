===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **in your acting like a prostitute, lusting** - AT: "because of you have acted like a prostitute and lusted"
  * **acting like a prostitute, lusting after nations** - The reason this phrase says Oholibah acts like a prostitute with the nations is because the men she has had sex with are symbols of the wealth and power of these nations. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **became unclean with their idols** - AT: "became unclean by worshiping their idols" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **You have walked in the way of your sister** - Walking in the same path as someone else is another way to say doing the same things they do. AT: "You have done the same things as your sister" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **I will put her cup** - The word "cup" refers to Oholibah's punishment.  It represents the thing she has received. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **I will put her cup into your hand** - The word "hand" refers to Oholibah's possession. AT: "I will give Oholah's cup to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
