===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **So in this way** - Alternate translation: "By sending them away into exile"
  * **the iniquity of Jacob will be atoned for** - Here "Jacob" refers to the people of Israel. Alternate translation: "Yahweh will atone for their sins of the people of Israel" or "Yahweh will provide a payment for the sins of the people of Israel" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **for this will be the full fruit of turning away from his sin** - "A restored relationship with Yahweh will be Israel's benefit of turning away from her sins" This is the design Yahweh has in mind for the people of Israel to pay for their sins.
  * **He will make all the the altar stones as chalk and pulverized, and no Asherim or incense altars will remain standing** - "He will remove all the idols. They will be torn down and destroyed. The altars where false gods are worshiped will be destroyed"
