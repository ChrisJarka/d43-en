====== window, windows ======

===== Definition: =====

A window is an opening in a structure that allows air and sunlight to enter.  Windows is more than one window.

  * Windows in ancient homes or commercial buildings were openings with wooden lattices, straw mats, or other devices for shade or air movement control.
  * Today, windows may have movable clear or colored glass panes.
  * Modern windows can be opened to allow air flow, and a layer of screen material is used to keep out insects and birds.

(See also: other)
