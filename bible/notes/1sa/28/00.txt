====== 1 Samuel 28 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

God had stopped helping Saul and talking to dead Samuel did no good because God had left him and would not help. 

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Rhetorical question ==
Samuel use a [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] to say it was useless to ask his advice since there was no way Saul could be successful with God against him:  "What then do you ask me, since Yahweh has left you, and he has become your enemy?"
