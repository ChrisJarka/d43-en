===== Translation Notes: =====

   * **For the chief musician**  - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:psa:004:001|4:1]].
   * **on stringed instruments**  - This can also be written as a sentence: “It is to be accompanied by people playing stringed instruments” or “People should play stringed instruments with this song.”
   * **A Psalm of David**  - This can also be written as a sentence. AT: “This is a psalm of David” or “This is a psalm that David wrote”
   * **shine**  - Besides reflecting light on an object, it can indicate a response of approval or pleasant actions.
