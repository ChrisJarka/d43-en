===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **"May you be praised, Yahweh, God of Israel our ancestor, forever and ever.**  - This phrase may also be understood as, "May you be praised, Yahweh our father, God of Israel, forever and ever.
  * **and you are exalted as ruler over all** - This phrase emphasizes the preeminence of Yahweh over everything. AT: "and you are exalted as ruler over all creation." (See: Implicit/Explicit)
