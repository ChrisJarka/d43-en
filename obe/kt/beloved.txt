====== beloved ======

===== Definition: =====

An expression of affection and devotion.

  * Jesus Christ is referred to as the "Beloved" in several New Testament passages.
  * The Body of Christ, the church, is also described as "beloved" in numerous NT references.
  * The members of the church, Christians, are also referred to as "beloved" frequently in the epistles of John.

(See also: other)
