===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - In verse 19 to 42, the writer thinks about the lessons that he and his fellow citizens should learn about God and his anger and mercy. 
 
  * **wormwood and bitterness** - Wormwood is a plant that gives a liquid which is very bitter to drink. Together "Wormwood" and "bitterness" represent severe suffering. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I am bowed down within me** - The author speaks of his deep sadness and despair as if he were bowed down. AT: "I am depressed" or "I am discouraged" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **But I call this to mind** - "But I think about this" or "But I remember this"
  * **I have hope** - "I hope"
