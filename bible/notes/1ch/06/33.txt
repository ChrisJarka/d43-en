===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I Chronicles 6:33 ULB:** These were those who served with their sons.** **AT:** "**Here is a list of the musicians and their sons:"//// (UDB)
  * **Kohathites**- had the highest priestly rank, the high priests came from this family (see [[http://biblehub.com/1_chronicles/6-2.htm|1 Chronicles 6:2-15]]).
  * **going back in time **- starting from the present and going to the past
  * **Heman . . .Elkanah**  - These are the names of men. (See: How to Translate Names)
