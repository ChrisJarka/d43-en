===== translationNotes: =====

  * **It certainly flows with milk and honey** - This is an idiom that means the land is very good. It will produce many crops and provide food for all the people and animals. AT: "It certainly is a land where plenty of milk and honey flow" or "It is certainly a land that is excellent for cattle and farming" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
