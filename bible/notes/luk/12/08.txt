===== Translation Notes: =====


(Jesus continues to warn his disciples against fearing people rather than God.)
    * **Everyone who confesses me before men**  - That is, "Anyone who acknowledges before others that he is loyal to me". This could also be translated as "Whoever tells others that he is my disciple" or "If anyone confesses that he is my disciple."
    * **the Son of Man** - Jesus was referring to himself. This could also be translated as "I, the Son of Man."
    * **he that denies me before men**  - That is, "he who disowns me before people." This could also be translated as "whoever refuses to acknowledge to others that he is my disciple" or "if anyone refuses to say that he is loyal to me."
    * **will be denied**  - That is, "will be disowned." This could also be translated as "The Son of Man will deny him " or "I will deny that he is my disciple."
    * **Everyone who speaks a word against the Son of Man**  - That is, "Everyone who says something bad about the Son of Man."
    * **it will be forgiven him**  - That is, "he will be forgiven" or "God will forgive him."
    * **blasphemes against the Holy Spirit**   This could also be translated as, "Whoever speaks evil against the Holy Spirit" or "If anyone says that the Holy Spirit is evil."
