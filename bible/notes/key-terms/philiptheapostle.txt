====== Philip, the apostle ======

===== Facts: =====

Philip was one of the original twelve disciples and apostles of Jesus.

  * Philip was from the town of Bethsaida; he introduced his brother Nathaniel to Jesus.
  * Jesus challenged Philip to provide food for a crowd of over 5,000 people.
  * At the last Passover supper that Jesus ate with his disciples, he talked to them about God, his Father. Philip asked Jesus to show them the Father.
