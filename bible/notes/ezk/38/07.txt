===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Get ready!**  - "Gog" was to prepare his army, wait for others to join him, and wait for a number of years for marching orders.to attack Israel.
  * **w****atchman**  - Watchman refers to a soldier assigned as a lookout to warn against possible attack.
