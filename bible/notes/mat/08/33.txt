===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The men who had been tending the pigs**
 - "the men who were herding the pigs"
  * **and reported everything **- The men who were tending the pigs told the people in the city how Jesus commanded the demons to leave the men and go into the pigs. Then the pigs ran over the cliff into the sea and drowned.
  * **all the city **- People who lived in the city. This would not necessarily mean every sick or elderly person, for example. (See: hyperbole)
  * **region **- "the city and the surrounding area"
