===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues teaching on prayer.

  * **what things you need**  - The word "you" refers to the crowd that Jesus is talking to.
  * **sanctify your name** -This could be  translated "may everyone recognize you as holy".
  * **your name**  - The word refers to the whole person of  God. This can be translated as "yourself".  (see: synecdoche)
  * **Let your kingdom come**  -  "May you rule over everyone and everything completely"(see: UDB)
