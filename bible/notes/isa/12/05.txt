===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you inhabitants of Zion **- The form of "You" is plural and is referring to all the people living in Jerusalem. (See: Forms of 'you' - Dual/Plural)
  * **>for great in your midst ** - "the one who lives with you is mighty"
  * **Holy One of Israel**  - This refers to God and can be translated as "God of Israel"
