====== Rephan ======

===== Facts: =====

Rephan is the name of a false god of the Canaanites. There are other possible names for this god such as Chiun,

  * The Israelites worshiped Rephan and other false gods while they were wandering in the wilderness.
  * Rephan (or Chiun in the Old Testament) may refer to the star god (or Saturn).
