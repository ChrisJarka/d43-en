====== Leviticus Chapter 23 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What does Yahweh say about work and the Sabbath?

A. Yahweh says that people may work for six days, but the seventh day, the Sabbath, is to be a day of rest. [23:3]

Q? What festival is to be celebrated in the first month on the fourteenth day?

A. Haweh's Passover is to be clebrated in the first month on the fourteenth day. [23:5]

Q? What festival follows Passover on the fifteenth of the first month?

A. The Festival of Unleavened Bread follows Passover on the fifteenth of the month. [23:6]
