====== Shiloh ======

===== Facts: =====

Shiloh was a walled city in the Ephraimite hill country which was conquered by Joshua and the Israelites.

  * Before any temple was built in Jerusalem, Shiloh was the place where the Israelites would travel for the purpose of sacrificing to God.
  * In Shiloh, Samuel was dedicated to the Lord by his mother Hannah.
