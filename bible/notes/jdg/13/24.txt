===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the woman gave birth** - AT: "Manoah's wife gave birth"
  * **The child grew up** - This phrase refers to a child becoming an adult. The phrase "grew up" refers to an adult being taller than a child. AT: "became an adult" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Mahaneh Dan...Zorah and Eshtaol**  - Zorah and Eshtaol are the names of towns. Mahaneh Dan was a temporary camp as people from the tribe of Dan looked for a permanent home. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
