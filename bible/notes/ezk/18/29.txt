===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues to teach the people of Israel why his way is fair.

  * **the house of Israel** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:01|3:1]].
  * **The way of the Lord - **  This phrase refers to the actions of the Lord.
  * **How is my way not fair, house of Israel? **- This phrase is used to emphasize that the people of Israel are not in a position to question the Lord. AT: "My way is fair, people of Israel." (See [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]).
  * **How is your way not fair? - **  This question is used by Yahweh to show that he believes how the people if Israel think is not fair. AT: "Your ways are not fair" (See [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Each man among you according to his own way **- An alternate translation could be, "the actions of each man."
  * **so that they will not** -   This word "they" refers to all the transgressions of the people of Israel.
