===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues to describe Yahweh's judgment against the women of Judah. (See: [[en:ta:translate:translateunknowns]])

 
  * **ankle jewelry** - A decoration that a lady wears near her feet.
  * **head bands** - A decoration that is worn over the head and hair.
  * **crescent ornaments** - This is jewelry worn in the belief that they would protect the individual from evil.
  * **the ear pendants** - This jewelry hangs from or over the the ear.
  * **bracelets** - A decoration worn on the arm near the hands.
  * **veils** - A very thin material used to cover the head and face of a woman.
  * **headties** - A long, thin piece of cloth tied around the head or the hair.
  * **ankle chains** - A decoration worn near the feet. Often the chains hung down to make a gentle noise.
  * **sashes** - A piece of cloth that is worn around the waist or across the chest.
  * **perfume boxes** - These holders were worn around the neck to give off a pleasant smell. They were often made of alabaster stone
  * **lucky charms** - This is jewelry worn in the belief that it would bring good luck.
