===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **If Yahweh** - Verse 9 describes something that could have happened in the past but did not. (See: [[en:ta:translate:hypothetical]])
  * **Yahweh of Hosts **- "Yahweh, the Leader of the angel armies,"
  * **us...we** - Here these words refer to Isaiah and the people of Judah and Jerusalem. (See: [[en:ta:translate:inclusive]])
  * **we would have been like Sodom, we would have been like Gomorrah **- "we would have all been destroyed, like the cities of Sodom and Gomorrah were destroyed." These cities were completely destroyed by God as punishment for the evil things the people were doing.
