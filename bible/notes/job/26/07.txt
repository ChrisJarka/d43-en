===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He stretches out over the northern sky and over the empty space** - The sky and empty space are great, but God is greater.  AT: "God is greater than the northern sky and empty over which he stretches"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **He stretches out over the northern sky and over the empty space** - Some versions have "He stretches out the north over empty space" or "He stretches out the heavens over empty space."
  * **He binds up the waters in his thick clouds - The clouds are spoken of as if they were a large blanket in which God wraps the rainwater in. AT: "He wraps up the water in his thick clouds. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **but the clouds are not torn under them** - The word "them" refers to the waters. AT: "but the weight of the waters does not tear the clouds" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
