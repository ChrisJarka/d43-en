===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Peter continues quoting David.)
  * **Your holy one**  - "Your anointed or chosen one".
  * **To see decay**  - His body will not remain dead long enough to decompose. May be translated as "To experience rot".
  * **ways of life**  - "life-giving truth"
