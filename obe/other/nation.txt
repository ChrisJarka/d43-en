====== nation ======

===== Definition: =====

A nation is a group of people ruled by some form of government and often consists of a specific people group with a shared ethnicity and other common traits.

  * In the Bible, there were many nations, including the Romans, Assyrians, and Greeks.
  * The nation of Israel is a governed area of land on the east shore of the Mediterranean Sea that originally included the 12 tribes of the common ancestor Jacob, also known as Israel.

(See also: other)
