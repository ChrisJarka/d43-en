====== Psalms Chapter 112 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What promise does the writer reveal about the descendants of the man who obeys Yahweh and who greatly delights in his commandments? 

A. "His descendants will be powerful on earth; the descendants of the godly man will be blessed." [112:2]

Q? The writer says, "Light shines in the darkness" for a person who has what kind of character?

A. "Light shines in the darkness for the godly person; he is gracious, merciful, and just." [112:4]

Q? The writer says the godly person does not fear bad news, he is confident.  Why is that?

A. "He does not fear bad news; he is confident, trusting in Yahweh." [112:7]

Q? What happens to the godly person who, "generously gives to the poor;
his righteousness endures forever?" 

A. "He will be exalted with honor." [112:9]

Q? What happens to the wicked person who sees this and is angry?

A. The wicked person... "will gnash with his teeth and melt away; the desire of the wicked person will perish." [112:10]
