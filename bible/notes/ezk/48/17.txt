===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **pasture**  - A pasture is an area of land where animals eat the grass.
  * **two hundred fiftyâ€¦ **- This is a measurement in cubits.
  * **correspond**  - Here, correspond means "to match".
  * **holy contribution**  - This is the land that is reserved for the Levites.
  * **the holy contribution and its produce **- This refers to the things grown on the land reserved for the Levites.
  * **will serve as food**  - alternate translation: will feed
