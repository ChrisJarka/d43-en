====== disperse, dispersion ======

===== Definition: =====

The terms "disperse" and  "dispersion" refer spreading out in different directions.

  * In the New Testament, this term refers specifically to Christians who had to leave their homes and spread out, moving to many different locations.
  * The phrase "the dispersion" could be translated as "believers who had fled to many different places.
