===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Jesus and his disciples entered into a boat **- That is, "Jesus got into a boat with his disciples" or "Jesus and his disciples got into a boat."
    * **set sail**  - That is, "started across" or "set off."
    * **as they sailed**  - Or, "as they went."
    * **fell asleep**  - Or, "took a nap."
    * **a storm of very strong winds**  - This literally meant a "hurricane of wind" or a "cyclone of wind" or a "typhoon."
    * **their boat started filling up with water**  - explanation
