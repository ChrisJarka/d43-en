===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **A declaration about Tyre**  –“A prophecy about Tyre”
  * **Howl, you ships of Tarshish**  – “Cry out in despair you ships of Tarshish” Isaiah speaks to the ships as if they are able to hear. (see: Personification)
  * **from the land of****Kittum**  – The ships of Tarshish went out and learned of the destruction of Tarchish when they arrived in Kittum which is in the Southern part of Cyprus.
  * <font 14px line-height: 26.1333332061768px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>**  ** Be astounded, you inhabitants of the coast, you merchants of Sidon ** –</font> ** <font 14px line-height: 26.1333332061768px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>“ Be shocked you people of the coast, you sellers from Sidon”.Sidon refers to Phoenecia.</font> (see <font 14px line-height: 26.1333332061768px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>: Apostrophe) **   **</font>
  * **the grain of the Shihor region, the harvest of the Nile, was brought to Tyre; she was the marketplace of nations** <font 14px line-height: 26.1333332061768px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>- Shihor and the Nile refers to the land of Egypt.  The ships carried grain from Egypt to Tyre where all nations came to buy.</font>
