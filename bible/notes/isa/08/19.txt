===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you**  - This form of 'you' is referring to more than one person. It can be translated as "you all." (See: Forms of 'you' - Dual/Plural)
  * **Consult with**  - "Talk with"
  * **those who chirp and mutter incantations **- "those who whisper and speak in a quiet voice about what we should do in the future"
  * **But should a people not consult their God? **- This is a rhetorical question where an answer is not expected but is used to encourage the reader to think deeply. This can be translated as "But God is the one we should ask to guide us!" (See: Rhetorical Question)
