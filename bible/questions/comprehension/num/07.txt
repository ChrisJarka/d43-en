====== Numbers Chapter 07 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Moses do on the day that the tabernacle and altar were completed?

A. He anointed and sanctified them for Yahweh. [7:1]

Q? What did the leaders of Israel offer as sacrifices?

A. They brought six covered carts and twelve oxen to present in front of the tabernacle. [7:2-3]

Q? Who was Moses to give the offerings to?

A. Moses was to give the offerings to the Levites, to each one as his work needs them. [7:4-5]
