===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Epaphras, Mark, Aristarchus, Demas, Luke** - These are names of men. (See: [[en:ta:translate:translatenames]])
  * **my fellow-prisoner - **"who is in prison with me because of his faith,"
  * **greets you** - Here "you" is singular and refers to Philemon. (See: [[en:ta:translate:formsofyou-singular]])
  * **other fellow-workers - **  "the men who work with me"
  * **be with your spirit** - Here "spirit" is a synecdoche and can be translated as "be with you." (See: [[en:ta:translate:synecdoche]])  
  * **your spirit** - Here "your" is plural and refers to Philemon and all who meet in his house. (See: [[en:ta:translate:formsofyou-plural]])
