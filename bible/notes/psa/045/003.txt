===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **thigh **- The thigh is the upper part of the leg where a sword was worn by men at this time**.**
  * **In your majesty ride on triumphantly **  - AT: "You are a great king; go forward in victory"
  * **because of trustworthiness , meekness, and righteousness **- AT: "with truth, gentleness, and goodness
  * **right hand **- The words "right hand" refer to power, authority, or strength.
  * **fearful **- The word "fearful" can also refer to a deep respect.
