====== Solomon Served Other Gods [18-03] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-18-03.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

But **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:solomon|Solomon]]** loved women from other countries. He **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:disobey|disobeyed]]** **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:god|God]]** by marrying many women, almost 1,000 of them! Many of these women came from foreign countries and **brought their [[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:god-false|gods]] with them** and continued to **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:worship|worship]]** them. When Solomon was old, he also worshiped their gods.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **brought their gods with them** - They brought their idols and their methods of worshiping idols with them to Israel.
