====== Psalms 084 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 84 is a worship psalm.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Obedience ==
God blesses all who want to obey him. Praising God in his temple is better than anything else. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:bless]] and [[:en:obe:kt:temple]])
