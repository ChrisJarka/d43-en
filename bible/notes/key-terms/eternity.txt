====== eternity ======

===== Definition: =====

An eternity is a period of time without a beginning or an end. An eternity can also describe a period of timelessness.

  * In the Bible, God is said to exist "from everlasting to everlasting." The first "everlasting" means the eternal beginning and the second "everlasting" the eternal end.
  * After this present life on earth, humans will spend eternity in either heaven with God or in hell apart from God.
