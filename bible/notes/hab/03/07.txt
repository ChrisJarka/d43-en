===== Translation Notes: =====
Habakkuk continues to describe his vision of Yahweh.

    * **I saw the tents of Cushan in affliction**  - This is a cloth house the people lived in. They were used to refer to the people. Alternate translation: "I saw the people of Cushan in affliction" (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
    * **Was Yahweh angry with the rivers? Was your wrath against the rivers, or your fury against the sea**  - This question was asked to point out that Yahweh's judgement was not because of the rivers or sea, it was because of the people. "Yahweh's anger and wrath was not against the river's or the sea. (See: [[en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]])
    * **the fabric of the tents in the land of Midian trembling.**  - The tents of the people of Midian are described as "tremblimg."  The tents are used to refer to the people.  The word "trembling" is used to describe the people's fear.  They are so afraid they are trembling or shaking.  Alternate translation: "the people of Midian were very afraid"(See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
