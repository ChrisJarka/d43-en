===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Notice that I am not speaking to your children** - Here Yahweh is speaking only to those people of Israel who had known his discipline when they rebelled against him in the desert.  He is not speaking to their children who had not experienced this.
  * **his mighty hand** - Here "mighty hand" refers to Yahweh's strength. AT: "his strength" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **in the midst of Egypt** - "in Egypt"
