====== foolish ======

===== Definition: =====

<font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Foolish is the adjective form of the noun 'fool', which is an individual who often makes bad or wrong choices, and as a result is considered to be unintelligent.</font>

  * Jesus said we should not call each other 'fool'.
  * David said that fools do not believe in God, disregarding the beauty in nature.
