====== loins ======

===== Definition: =====
The part of the human body which a belt would be tied. Generally the lower part of the abdomen and the upper part of the thighs.

  * "Lions" in the Bible are often a symbol of strength and power. 
  * Sometimes the term "loins" is used when describing the bloodline of someone. 
  * The phrase "gird your loins" is referring to tucking the bottom of a shirt into your belt in order to move with ease. 

(See also: [[:En:obe:kt:power]], [[:En:obe:other:gird]])
