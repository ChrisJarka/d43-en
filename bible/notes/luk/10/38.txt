===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Now as they went on their way** - "Now as Jesus and his disciples went on their way." Since this is a new part of the story, it may be more natural in some languages to say who "they" were. You language may have a way of indicating that this is a new part of the story.
    * **who also sat at the Lord's feet**  - "who sat with the others at the Lord's feet" or "who sat at the Lord's feet as his disciples did"
