===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **From now on **- "Lastly" or "As I end this letter"
  * **no one should trouble me**  - "let no one cause me hardships" or "I give this command that no one brings me difficulties"
  * **for I carry the marks of Jesus on my body **- "I have the healed wounds in my body because of my service to Jesus" or "I still have the mark of the healed wound in my body because I belong to Jesus"
  * **May the grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with your spirit **- "I pray this that may the Lord Jesus be kindness to your spirit"
  * **brothers**  - "brothers and sisters" or "fellow members of God's family"
