====== father, fathers ======

===== Definition: =====

Text

  * In Scripture “fathers” can refer to natural fathers and spiritual fathers as where Paul said Timothy became his father through the gospel and Timothy was his son in the faith. Fathers also refers to ancestors or the entire history of the people as it is used by various generations in the OT to refer to their ancestors all the way back to Abraham.
