===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He brought me there**  - This place refers to the city mentioned in the previous verse.
  * **behold! **- Behold calls attention to something or shows wonder or surprise.  see: standard note
  * **set your heart upon all that I am revealing to you**  - This means "consider what I tell you seriously and remember it."
