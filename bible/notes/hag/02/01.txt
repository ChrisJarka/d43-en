===== translationNotes: =====

  * **In the seventh month on the twenty-first day of the month** - This is the seventh month of the Hebrew calendar. The twenty-first day is near the middle of October on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **the word of Yahweh came** - This idiom is used to introduce a special message from God. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:hag:01:01|1:1]]. AT: "Yahweh gave a message" or "Yawheh spoke this message" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **by the hand of Haggai** - Here the word "hand" refers to Haggai himself. Yahweh used Haggai as the agent to deliver his command. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:hag:01:01|1:1]]. AT: "through Haggai" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Haggai ... Zerubbabel ... Shealtiel ... Joshua ... Jehozadak** - See how you translated these men's names in [[:en:bible:notes:hag:01:01|1:1]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
