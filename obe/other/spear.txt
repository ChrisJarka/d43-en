====== spear ======

===== Definition: =====

A spear is a weapon with a long handle and sharp blade on one end that is thrown a long distance.

  * Spears were commonly used for war in biblical times. They are sometimes still used in warfare in the present day.
  * A spear was used by a Roman soldier to pierce the side of Jesus while he hung on the cross.
  * Some people in the present time still throw spears to catch fish or other prey to eat. 

(See also: other)
