===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus explains what is expected of his followers.

  * **he gave instructions**  - "he told them what to do"
  * **wherever **- "to any place"
  * **Foxes have holes, and the birds of the sky have nests**  - "no place to live" (See: Doublet)
  * **nowhere to lay his head **- "no place to sleep" (See: Idiom)
