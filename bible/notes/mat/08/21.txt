===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **permit me to go first and bury my father **- the man was saying either that he wanted to go home and take care of his father until he died or go home and take care of his father's funeral. After he had done that, he would follow Jesus.
  * **Follow me and leave the dead to bury their own dead**  - Jesus was saying the man should follow him now and leave those who are spiritually dead in their sins to take care of their dead relatives. (See: Idiom)
