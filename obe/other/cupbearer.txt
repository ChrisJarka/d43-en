====== cupbearer ======

===== Definition: =====

A trusted and faithful servant to a king or pharaoh.

  * A cupbearer would frequently have political influence with the ruler he served.
  * Nehemiah was the cupbearer for the Perisian King Artaxerxes, who allowed Nehemiah to return to Jerusalem and rebuilt the defensive wall.

(See also: other)
