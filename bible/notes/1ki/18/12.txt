===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **will carry you**  - "will send you" or "will take you"
  * **will carry you some place I do not know. Then…he cannot find you**  - "There is no guarantee you will still be here after I tell Ahab you are here."
  * **I, your servant**  - Here servant does not mean Elijah's slave. Obadiah refers to himself humbly, giving honor to Elijah.
  * **Has it not been told to you…**  - AT: "Haven't you heard" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **my master**  - Here master is a term of respect in referring to Elijah.
  * **one hundred**  - "100" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers|Translate Numbers]])
  * **by fifties**  - "in groups of 50" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers|Translate Numbers]])
