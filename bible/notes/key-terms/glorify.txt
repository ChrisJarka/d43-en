====== glorify ======


===== Definition: =====


To glorify means to show or reveal God's character by your actions or speech. The person glorifying God will attribute certain events to be the product of divine power. 


    * A person can verbally glorify God by telling about a work He has done in their life, expressing that it was God who produced the outcome of a situation.
    * In the New Testament people glorified God because of the works they say Jesus perform.
