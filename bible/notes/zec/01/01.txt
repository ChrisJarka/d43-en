===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Darius, Berechiah, **  and **Iddo.**  - See (How to Translate Names)
  * **second year of Darius' reign**  - "second year since Darius became king"
  * **exceedingly angry with your fathers!**  - or "very angry with your forefathers"
  * **"Turn unto me!"…"and I will return to you!"**  - These are Yahweh's words to Israel.
