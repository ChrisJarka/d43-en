====== light ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Bible light is metaphorically used to describe righteousness, holiness and truth. It is the opposite of sin and moral corruption.

  * Jesus said, "I am the light of the world."
  * Christians are commanded to walk in light, embracing holiness and shunning evil.
  * John stated that God is light, and there is no darkness in Him.

(See also: other)
