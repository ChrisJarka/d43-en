===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **asked him, saying**  - This can also be translated as "asked him and said" or "asked John."
    * answered and said to them - This can also be translated as "answered them, saying" or "answered them."
    * **tunic**- A tunic is a garment like a long shirt or dress that both men and women wear. Cultures that do not have tunics could translate it with the generic word "garment" or with a similar type of clothing such as "shirt."
    * **do likewise**  - That is, "do the same thing." It can also be translated here as "give food to someone who does not have any."
