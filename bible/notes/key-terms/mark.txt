====== Mark ======

===== Facts: =====

Mark was one of the men who traveled with Paul on his missionary journeys. He is most likely the author of the Gospel of Mark.

  * Mark is the same person as John Mark, who accompanied his cousin Barnabas and Paul on their first missionary journey.
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>When Peter was put in prison in Jerusalem, the Christians were praying for him at</font> John Mark's mother's house.
  * Mark was not an original apostle, but was taught by both Paul and Peter, and worked together with them in ministry.
