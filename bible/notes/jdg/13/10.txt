===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Look** - This word shows excitement or surprise. The person who says "Look" wants someone to see something, either with their eyes, or their mind.
  * **Manoah **- See translation of this man's name in [[en:bible:notes:jdg:13:01|13:2]].
  * **man**  - This word refers to the angel of the Lord in [[en:bible:notes:jdg:13:03|13:3]].
