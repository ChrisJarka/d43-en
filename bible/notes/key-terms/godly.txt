====== godly, godliness ======

===== Definition: =====

Godly is used to describe a person who reflects God's character.  Godliness is the result of godly actions.

  * Godly character in a person is seen by qualities such as love, peace, patience, kindness, self control.
  * Godliness is fruit, formed in a person submitting to God's Holy Spirit dwelling inside them.
