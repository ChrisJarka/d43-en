===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I heard**  - The prophet John continues to describe his vision.
  * **large number of**  - AT: "huge crowd of" or "very many"
  * **hallelujah**
 - This word is an exclamation (shout) of praise.  Your language may have a specific word for this.
  * **Salvation, glory, and power belong to the Lord**
 - AT: "The Lord is the source of salvation, glory and power."
  * **she herself shed **  - AT: "she wounded the servants herself"
