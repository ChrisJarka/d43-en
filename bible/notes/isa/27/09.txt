===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **So in this way, **"this way" is referring "the measure" in the previous verse.
  * **the iniquity of Jacob will be atoned for, for this will be the full fruit of turning away from his sin- **  This is the design Yahweh has in mind for the people of Israel to pay for their sins.
  * **He will make all the the altar stones as chalk and pulverized, and no Asherim or incense altars will remain standing**- "He will remove all the idols. They will be torn down and destroyed. The altars where false gods are worshipped will be destroyed."
