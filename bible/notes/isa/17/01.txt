===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **A declaration about Damascus**  - “Isaiah received this warning concerning Damascus” (See: Active/Passive)
  * **cities of Aroer**  – Aroer is another name for Moab. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
  * **the remnant of Aram**  - This refers to present day Syria. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
  * **they will be like the glory of the children of Israel**  - Isaiah says that Aram will get the same treatment as Israel. "they will be disgraced like the people of Israel " (See: [[:en:ta:translate:hyperbole|Hyperbole]])
