====== Micah Chapter 03 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who eats the flesh of Yahweh's people?

A. The leaders of Jacob eat the flesh of Yahweh's people [3:3].

__**Is this confusing if it's not literal (mentioned in book notes)?** __

Q? Why would Yahweh hide his face from the leaders of Jacob?

A. Yahweh would hide his face from the leaders of Jacob because they had done evil deeds [3:4].

Q? Why would the prophets do no divination?

A. It would be dark so that the prophets would do no divination [3:6].

Q? For what do the leaders judge?

A. The leaders judge for a bribe [3:11].
