===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Let us** - "I suggest we"
  * **visit the brothers** - Here "visit" means to "care for" or "offer to help"
  * **the brothers** - This stands for "fellow believers."
  * **the word of the Lord** - Here "word" stands for a message. AT: "the message about the Lord" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **see how they are** - "learn about the current condition of the brothers and how they are holding on to the truth given to them"
  * **to also take with them John who was called Mark** - "to take John, who was also called Mark, along with Paul and himself"
  * **Paul thought it was not good to take Mark** - The words "not good" are used to say the opposite of good. AT: "Paul thought that taking Mark would be bad" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes|Litotes]])
  * **Pamphylia** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:act:02:08|2:10]].
  * **did not go further with them in the work** - "did not continue to work with them" or "did not continue to serve with them"
