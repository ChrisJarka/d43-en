====== Reflexive Pronoun ======
Reflexive pronouns refer to someone who has already been referred to in a sentence. In English **the reflexive pronouns** are: myself, yourself, himself, herself, itself, ourselves, yourselves, and themselves. 

===== Functions of Reflexive Pronouns =====
  - To refer to a person or object that has already been mentioned in the sentence.  For example, in "I saw myself in the mirror” and ”The man bought himself a new shirt,” the words "myself" refers back to "I" and the word "himself" refers back to the "the man." 
  - To emphasize  a person or thing in the sentence, perhaps because it something that the listener might not expect.  I myself did it.  (Implied information:  It wasn’t someone else who did it.)  I gave it to John himself. (Implied: I didn’t give it to some other person)
  - To show that someone did something alone. "I did it by myself.” This can be translated as ”I did it alone” or ”Only I did it."
