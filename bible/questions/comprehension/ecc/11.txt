====== Ecclesiastes Chapter 11 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why should people share bread with seven, even eight people?

A. They should share it with seven, even eight people, for they do not know what disasters are coming on the earth. [11:2]

Q? What can people not comprehend?

A. They cannot comprehend the work of God. [11:5]

Q? For how long should people plant seed?

A. They should plant seed i n the morning until the evening. [11:6]

Q? What should people drive away from their heart?

A. They should drive anger away from their heart. [11:10]
