====== yoke ======

===== Definition: =====

To yoke or be yoked is to be joined to something or someone.

  * A physical yoke is a piece of wood that secures animals to a plow or cart for pulling.
  * A spiritual or metaphorical yoke is a heavy weight of misery such as a problem with sin or suffering.
