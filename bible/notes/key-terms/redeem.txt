====== redeem, redemption ======


===== Definition:  =====


To redeem means to purchase something with a price. In the Bible we are redeemed by a payment which God provides. Redemption is the act of redeeming.


    * Because of our sin, humans are condemned to eternal punishment separated from God. The life and death of Jesus the Christ is the complete payment (redemption) for our lives.
    * A first born animal was normally set aside for slaughter as an offering to God, but could be purchased (redeemed) by paying a set price to a Priest under the old covenant system.
    * Jesus' blood is the ONLY payment by which a human can be redeemed to God and have eternal life in His presence.
