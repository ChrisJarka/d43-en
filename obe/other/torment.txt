====== torment ======

===== Facts: =====

To torment is to cause to suffer.

  * The suffering may take the form of physical pain or agony as experienced by the worshippers of the beast in Revelation 12:10.
  * But most often, suffering may take the form of spiritual duress and affliction as experienced by Job (19:2).
  * Everlasting torment will be in the form of the lake of fire described in the book of Revelation.
