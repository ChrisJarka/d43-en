====== Holy of Holies, Most Holy Place ======

===== Definition: =====

Inside the Israelite tabernacle, and later the Jewish temple building, the "Holy of Holies" was the innermost chamber where the Ark of God was located, symbolizing God's presence.

  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Another name for this room is the "Most Holy Place."</font>
  * The high priest was the only person allowed into the Holy of Holies. He was permitted to enter it one time every year, on the day of atonement.
  * On the day of atonement, the high priest would offer incense on the altar and sprinkle with blood the cover of the ark of the covenant.
  * The Holy of Holies represents the inner access to the presence of God where Jesus' own blood is the only acceptable sacrifice for mankind's sin.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * This term could also be translated as, "Holiest Room" or "Holiest Place where God lives" or "Most Sacred Chamber Set Apart for God."
