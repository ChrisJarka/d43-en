====== sandal ======

===== Definition: =====

A sandal is a simple shoe held onto the foot with straps around the instep or ankles worn by both men and women.

  * A sandal was also used to confirm a legal transaction such as the selling of property (Ruth 4:7, Deuteronomy 27: 7-9).
  * Removing one's shoes or sandals was a sign of respect and reverence, especially in God's presence.
  * John said that he was not worthy to even untie Jesus' sandals, which would have been the task of a lowly servant or slave.

(See also: other)
