====== 1 Samuel 06 General Notes ======

=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter ends to story of the Ark among the Philistines.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

**The Philistines send the Ark back to Israel**

The leaders asked the priests and diviners what they should do with the Ark. They said to send it back with an offering of five gold rats and five gold tumors. Put them in a cart drawn by cows that have new caves and let them go wherever they want to go. If the go to Israel then they would know that God sent the sickness but if they returned to their calves then they would know the sickness just happened. The cows when straight to Israel and the Israeli were happy and sacrificed the cows to say thank you to God. Some people peeked into the Ark and God killed them. So the people sent the Ark to Kiriath Jearim.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

**Idiom**

This chaper uses three [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]]s "hand lifted off you" meaning stop punishing you, "harden your hearts" meaning become stuborn, "lifted up their eyes" meaning looked up,
