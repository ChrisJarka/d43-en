====== foolish ======

===== Definition: =====

<font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>A person who is "foolish" is senseless or unwise in his behavior and decisions. It is the adjective form of the noun 'fool', which refers to a person who often makes bad or wrong choices.</font>

  * A foolish person or fool often refers to a person who does not obey God and is contrasted to the wise person, who obeys God.
  * The Proverbs in the Old Testament give many descriptions of what a fool is like.
  * In the Psalms, David describes a fool as a person who says there is no God, who ignores all the evidence of God in his creation.
