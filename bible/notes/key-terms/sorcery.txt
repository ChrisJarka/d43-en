====== sorcery ======

===== Definition: =====

Sorcery refers to the use of magic.

  * "Sorcery" frequently means casting a magic spell on someone.
  * Biblical sorcery was seen as evil. Paul description of the sorcerer in Acts 13:10 and Galations 5:19-21. John places a sorcerer on the same level as a liar and a murderer (Rev. 9:21, 18:23, 21:8, 22:15).
