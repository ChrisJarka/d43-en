===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **These are the statutes and the decrees - **  Moses starts going into detail, in this chapter, explaining all the statutes and decrees that the people of Israel were supposed to obey.
  * **The God of your fathers - **  Moses is refering to Yahweh, the one true God, who made a covenant with Abraham, Isaac and Jacob, their ancestors.
  * **all the days that you live on the earth**  - As long as the people of Israel and their posterity lived.
  * **You will surely destroy all the places**
 - This refers to all the objects, images and built structures for idolatry worship that the other nations used.
  * **on the hight mountains, on the hills, and under every green tree** - These were the favorite places of worship that the other nations living in the land used for worship.
