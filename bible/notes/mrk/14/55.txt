===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus had just been seized in the garden by those sent by the chief priests, scribes, and elders.

  * **were seeking testimony against against Jesus** - The Jewish Council had tried to find people who would accuse Jesus of committing crimes.
  * **many brought false testimony** - People came to accuse Jesus with lies. 
  * **Their testimony did not agree** - The people's lies were exposed because they could not agree with one another.
