===== translationNotes: =====

  * **strengthened themselves** - This expression probably means that they encouraged each other (see UDB). 
  * **they formed the battle line** - This probably means that the Israelites prepared their battle lines for the next day's fighting. See UDB.
  * **they sought direction from Yahweh** - The priest cast lots according to the Law to determine God's will.
