====== stumbling block ======

===== Definition: =====

"Stumbling block" is a barrier.

  * The Greek term "skandalizo" describes a stumbling block, snare, or trap that may cause one to fall away or fail.
  * The New Testament meaning is figurative with a moral application: a wrong relationship with Christ or others can lead to spiritual disaster.
