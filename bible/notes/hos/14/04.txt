===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh is speaking.

  * **I will heal them when they turn back to me after leaving me **- Yahweh will renew his relationship with Israel when they repent and are ready to allow him to be their God. (see: Metaphor)
  * **I will love them freely **- When Israel returns to the worship of Yahweh and follows him, Yahweh will give them his love freely (as in a gift given to someone you love).
  * **I will be like the dew to Israel; he will blossom like the lily and take root like a cedar in Lebanon. **- Yahweh will provide Israel with everything it needs to grow and prosper as a nation. Israel is pictured as a young plant that will blossom and send down permanent roots in the land. (see: simile and metaphor)
  * **His branches will spread out; his beauty will be like the olive trees, and his fragrance like the cedars in Lebanon**. - Israel will grow and spread out in the land, and others will see Israel as a fruitful, blessed nation. (see: simile and metaphor)
