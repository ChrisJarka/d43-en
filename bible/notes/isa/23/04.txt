===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Be you ashamed, O Sidon** - Sidon was the founding city for Tyre, so Sidon should feel shame at the destruction of Tyre.
  * **for the sea has spoken** - The sea is speaking as a person about what has happened to Tyre - (See: [[en:ta:translate:personification]])
  * **the mighty one of the sea.** - The city of Tyre is compared to a mighty fortress where once there was great commerce and activity. It could be translated - “Tyre was once a great seaport.” (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
  * **I have not labored nor given birth, nor have I raised young men nor brought up young women** - Now that Tyre is destroyed, Sidon wants to deny that it ever gave birth to Tyre.  (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
