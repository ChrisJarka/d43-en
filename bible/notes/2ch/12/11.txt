===== translationNotes: =====

  * **It happened that** - This phrase is used here to mark an important event in the story. If your language has a way for doing this, you could consider using it here.
  * **the guards would carry them** - "the guards would carry the shields"
  * **Yahweh's anger turned away from him, so as not to destroy him completely** - Yahweh no longer being angry with Rehoboam is spoken of as if Yahweh's anger were a person who turned away from Rehoboam. AT: "Yahweh was no longer angry with him and so did not destroy him completely" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **besides** - "in addition"
