====== Ezekiel ======

===== Facts: =====

Ezekiel was a prophet of God during the exile period when the Jews were taken to Babylon.

  * Ezekiel and his wife lived in Babylon near a river, and the Jews came to him for prophetic wisdom.
  * Ezekiel prophesied about Jerusalem's destruction, restoration and eventual Millennial Temple.
