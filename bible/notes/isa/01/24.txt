===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **"Therefore"**  - "For that reason"
  * **declaration of Lord **- "the Lord declares" or "the Lord says"
  * **Mighty **- "powerful" or "strong"
  * **avenge myself against my enemies**  - “<font 14px line-height: 19px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>punish you for the evil things that you did</font>    .”
  * **you **- This is plural and refers to the people who live in Judah and Jerusalem.
  * **refine away your dross, and take away all of your impurities **- This is a metaphor that can be translated as, "<font 14px line-height: 19px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>as though you were silver and I needed to heat you very greatly to melt you and get rid of the impurities" (UDB) (See: Metaphor)</font>
