====== myrrh ======

===== Definition: =====

Myrrh is a spice that comes from the resin of a tree. 

  * Myrrh was used as a perfume and also to prepare dead bodies for burial.
  * Myrrh was one of the gifts that the visiting Magi brought when they worshipped Jesus at His birth.
  * Jesus was offered wine mixed with myrrh in order to ease the pain when he was crucified.
