====== Capernaum ======


===== Facts: =====


Capernaum was a fishing village on the North shore of the Sea of Galilee


    * The people of Capernaum did not receive Jesus' message when He preached there.
    * Capernaum was the home town of Jesus, Simon and Andrew.  After entering with his disciples, Jesus healed Simon's mother-in-law and other people with illnesses.  He also cast out demons.
