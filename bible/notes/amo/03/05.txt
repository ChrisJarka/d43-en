===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** -  God uses the questions in verses 3-6 to present examples of things that happen only if something else has happened first. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
 

  * **Can a bird fall in a trap on the ground when no bait is set for him?** - God uses this question to present an example of something that happens only if something else has happened first. AT: "A bird can fall in a trap only when bait has been set for him." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Will a trap spring up from the ground when it has not caught anything?** - God uses this question to present an example of something that happens only if something else has happened first.  AT: "A trap will spring up from the ground only when it has caught something." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Will a trap spring up from the ground** - This refers to a trap closing. When an animal steps on a trap, the trap closes and the animal cannot get out of it. AT: "Will a trap close"
  * **Will the trumpet sound in a city and the people not tremble?** - God uses this question to present an example of something that is expected to happen if something else has happened. AT: "When the trumpet sounds in the city, the people will tremble." or "When the trumpet sounds in the city, we expect that people will tremble." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Will the trumpet sound in a city** - The purpose of sounding the trumpet is to warn people that enemies are about to attack the city. AT: "Will someone blow the trumpet in the city to warn the people about an enemy attack" or "Will the warning trumpet be blown in the city" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **tremble** - The reason for trembling can be stated clearly. AT: "tremble because they are afraid" or "be afraid of the enemy and tremble" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Will disaster come on a city if Yahweh has not sent it?** - God uses this question to present an example of something that happens only if something else has happened first. AT: "A disaster will come on a city only if Yahweh has sent it." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Will disaster come on a city** - Something terrible happening to a city is spoken of as if disaster came on it. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
