===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - In verses 3:14-20, Zephaniah tells the remnant of Israel who survived the judgment that they should rejoice. 

  * **a mighty one to save you** - "he is mighty and will save you"
  * **as on the day of the appointed feasts** - This refers to the joy of the people when they attended the feasts. This can be stated explicitly. AT: "as you were joyful when you attended the appointed feasts" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **take away disaster from you** - Here to "take away" is an idiom that means to stop disaster from continuing to happen. AT: "stop bringing disaster you" or "stop punishing you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **no longer bear any shame for it** - Here shame is spoken of as if it was a heavy thing that a person had to carry. AT: "no longer be ashamed because of it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
