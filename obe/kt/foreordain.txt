====== foreordain, foreknow ======

===== Definition: =====

To foreknow is to have prior knowledge of something. T<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>o foreordain is to establish something beforehand.</font>

  * The New Testament says that Jesus Christ was established to be the Savior of the world before the world was ever created.
  * The references to foreknowldge and foreordaining in the Bible describe part of the character of God; omniscience and omnipotence.
