===== Translation Notes: =====

God continues to speak about the day of the Lord using poetry. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]], [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
    * **It will come about on that day**  - "At that time"(UDB) or "In that day".
    * **mountains will drip with sweet wine...hills will flow with milk...Judah will flow with water** - These phrases all mean the area around Jerusalem will produce plenty wine, milk and water for the people's needs. 
    * **and a fountain will come from the house of Yahweh and water the Valley of Acacia** - AT: and a fountain will come from the house of Yahweh and a fountain of water will come from the Valley of Acacia (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
    * **Egypt will become an abandoned devastation and Edom will become an abandoned wilderness** - Both of these countries are areas south of Israel where people will leave because the area has been destroyed.  This is poetic language used in war to speak about complete victory over an enemy.
    * **violence done to the people of Judah...they shed innocent blood in their land** - Both of these phrases are saying the people of Egypt and Edom killed the people of Judah in the land of Israel.
    * **shed innocent blood** - AT: "killed people who had done nothing wrong" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
