===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **it was necessary for him to** - "it was important for him to." The word "him" refers to Jesus.
    * **pass through Samaria** - Jews usually went around Samaria to avoid it.
    * **plot of ground** - "portion of land" or "piece of land"
