===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the feast**  - This use of the word "feast" refers to the Passover celebration held every year.
  * **custom of the governor **- The word "custom" here means something that a person does regularly.
  * **chosen by the crowd** -            (See: Active/Passive)
  * **notorious**  - The word "notorious" means well-known for doing something bad.
