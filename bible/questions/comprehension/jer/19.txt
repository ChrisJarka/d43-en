====== Jeremiah Chapter 19 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What three things did Yahweh tell Jeremiah to do?

A. Jeremiah go and purchase a potter's clay flask, go out to the valley of Ben Hinnom and proclaim the words Yahweh tells him. [19:1-2]

Q? What does Yahweh tell Jeremiah to tell the kings of Judah and the inhabitants of Jerusalem?

A. I Yahweh will bring disaster on this place, and make their ears tingle? [19:3]

Q? Why is God bringing this disaster?

A. Because they have abandoned God and profamed his place, and approached other gods and their ancestors, and fill the place with innocent blood. [19:4]

Q? Who did they build a shrine to?

A. They built a shirine to Baal which God did not command them to do nor would he ever consider it. [19:5]

Q? What is Yahweh's declaration?

A. He declares that Topheth will be called the Valley of Slaughter. [19:6]

Q? What does breaking of the clay flask symoblise?

A. The breaking symoblises the breaking of the people in the Tophet, so that they can not be repaired. [19:10-11]

Q? What happed on the rooftops?

A. The unclean people worshipped the stars and poured out drink offerings to other gods. [19:13]

Q? What did Jeremiah prophesy in the courtyard?

A. That God will bring this city and its towns disaster becaused they stiffened their neck and refused to listen to God's words. [19:14-15]
