===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Kidon, Uzzah** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the anger of Yahweh burned against Uzzah** - Yahweh's anger is spoken of as if it were a fire that burned the one with whom Yahweh is angry. AT: "Yahweh was very angry with Uzzah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **before God** - "in the presence of God"
  * **That place is called** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "People call that place" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Perez Uzzah** - This is the name of a place. Translator may add a footnote that says, "The name 'Perez Uzzah' means 'the punishment of Uzzah.'" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **to this day** - See how you translated this phrasein [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:04:42|4:43]]
