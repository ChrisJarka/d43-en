===== translationNotes: =====

  * **from everlasting to everlasting** - This refers to two extremes and means for all time. AT: "for all eternity" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **All the people** - This is a generalization that refers to the group of people assembled to worship Yahweh. AT: "The people" or "Everyone who was there" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
