====== Hosea 12 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter is full of metaphors and other poetic forms that communicate how Jacob their ancestor was a type for them to follow. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Jacob was a type ==

Verse 5 is building a parallel between Jacob and Israel the Northern Kingdom. In the same way that Jacob turned back to Yahweh, the Northern Kingdom should repent of their idolatry and turn back to Yahweh. 

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

Notice how there is historical reference to Jacob. Jacob was a patriarch of the people. He was the son of Isaac and the father of the twelve tribes.
