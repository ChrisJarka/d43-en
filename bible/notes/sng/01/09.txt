===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **my love** - "the one whom I love"
  * **to a mare among Pharaoh’s chariot horses** - Solomon's mare would have looked very beautiful wearing the harness of one of the chariots that Pharaoh had made for Solomon. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **Pharaoh’s chariot horses** - "Pharaoh's horses which pull chariots"
  * **ornaments** - "decorations"
  * **with silver studs** - "with spots of silver"
