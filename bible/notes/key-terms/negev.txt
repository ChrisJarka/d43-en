====== Negev ======

===== Facts: =====

Text

  * Negev - a Hebrew word that has also become an English word. It is primarily used in the English version of the Bible
  * **Negev**  - primary meaning: "a region to the south of Israel (earlier, Canaan)". It is desert.
  * **Negev**  - many times means the compass direction "south"
  * **Negev**  - sometimes just means "desert"
  * Which meaning is determined by context
