  * **After Rachel had borne Joseph, Jacob said to Laban,**  - "After Rachel gave birth to Joseph, Jacob said to Laban,"
  * **to my own home and to my country**  - "to my place, even to my land" or "to my country and to my land."
  * **and let me go**  - "that I might go."
  * **Give me my wives and my children for whom I have served you,**  - Jacob is reminding Laban of their contract.
