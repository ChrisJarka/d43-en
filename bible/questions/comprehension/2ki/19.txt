====== 2 Kings Chapter 19 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did King Hezekiah do when he heard the report?

A. When King Hezekiah heard the report, he tore his clothes, covered himself with sackcloth, and went into the house of Yahweh. [19:1]

Q? What did Hezekiah say Yahweh may do?

A. He said Yahweh may hear all the words of Rabshakeh and may rebuke the words which Yahweh heard. [19:4]

Q? What will Yahweh do to stop the king of Assyria?

A. Yahweh will put a spirit in him, and the king will hear a certain report and go back to his own land. [19:7]

Q? According to Sennacherib, in what way did God deceive Hezekiah?

A. According to Sennacherib, God deceived Hezekiah by saying Jerusalem will not be given over into the hand of the king of Assyria. [19:10]

Q? Where did Hezekiah spread the letter from the messengers ?

A. Hezekiah sprea d the letter from the messengers in the house of Yahweh. [19:14]

Q? Why should Yahweh save the people of Judah?

A. Yahweh should save the people of Judah so that all the kingdoms of the earth may know that Yahweh is God alone. [19:10]

Q? Against whom did the king of Assyria exalt his voice?

A. The king of Assyria exalted his voice a gainst the Holy One of Israel! [19:22]

Q? Who said he had dried up all the rivers of Egypt under the soles of his feet?

A. Sennacherib said he had dried up all the rivers of Egypt under the soles of his feet. [19:24]

Q? Who determined long ago that impregnable cities will be reduced into heaps of ruins?

A. Yahweh determined long ago that impregnable cities will be reduced into heaps of ruins . [19:25]

Q? What will Yahweh put in Sennacherib's nose?

A. Yahweh will put his hook in Sennacherib's nose [19:28]

Q? What will be the sign for Hezekiah?

A. The sign will be that the people of Judah will eat what grows wild, and in the second year what grows from that. [19:29]
