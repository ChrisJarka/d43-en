====== bread ======


===== Definition: =====


Bread is a staple food made from flour, milk or water and usually some leavening agent such as yeast or starter and baked.


    * Bread is usually made into a loaf shape and baked prior to eating
    * Since bread is such a staple, Jesus said, "I am the bread of life".
