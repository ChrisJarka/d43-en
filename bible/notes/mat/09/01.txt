===== Translation Notes: =====

This begins an account of Jesus healing a paralyzed man.

  * **Jesus entered a boat **- The disciples probably went with Jesus as mentioned in the UDB.
  * **came into his own city**  - "the city where he was staying". (UDB)
  * **Behold, they**  - The pronoun "they" refers to a specific group of people".
  * **their faith**  - The pronoun "they" refers to the faith of those who brought the paralyzed man to Jesus. It may include the paralyzed man himself.
  * **Son, be of good cheer **- "Young man, be encouraged" (UDB).
