====== Mesopotamia ======

===== Facts: =====

In Bible times, Mesopotamia was the name of the region between the Tigris and Euphrates Rivers, in the location of modern day Iraq.

  * means "between two rivers"
  * often thought to be the original location of Eden
  * Jews from Mesopotamia came to Jerusalem for Pentecost.
