===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **He will do this as you also help us**  - The word "He" refers to God.  The word "this" refers to rescuing Paul and Timothy from danger. The word "you" refers to the people of the church of Corinth.  The word "us" refers to Paul and Timothy. Alternate translation: "God will rescue us from danger as you, the people of the church of Corinth, pray for us.
