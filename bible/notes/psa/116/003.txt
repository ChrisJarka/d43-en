===== Translation Notes: =====

The person who made this song continues to speak.
  * **The cords of death surrounded me** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:psa:018:004|18:4]].
  * **and the snares of sheol confronted me** - The singer describes sheol as a trap that had caught him. AT: "and I almost died." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **cords of death, snare**  - (See Biblical Imagery- snare)
  * **confronted me**  - AT: "challenged me"
  * **anguish and sorrow **- AT: "suffering and grief"
  * **called on the name of Yahweh**  - (See Quick Reference, Standard Notes) AT: "prayed in the name of Yahweh".
