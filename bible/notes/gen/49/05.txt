===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **brothers**  - "like-spirited"
  * **Weapons of violence are their swords**  - "Their weapons are instruments of violence" or "Their weapons are tools of violence"
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **For pleasure **- "In their wilfulness"
  * **they hamstrung oxen**  - This is metaphoric language describing how that they killed the men of the city of Shechem, who were suffering from their recent circumcision and so could not defend themselves well (see Gen 34:25).
