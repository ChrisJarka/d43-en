===== Format =====

This module answers the question: //What is the format standard for unfoldingWord audio?//\\
==  ==

The format standards for audio are:


**Recording**

  * WAV, 44.1kHz, 16-bit, mono


**door43 upload and/or archiving**

  * WAV, 44.1kHz, 16-bit, mono
  * FLAC, 44.1kHz, 16-bit, mono   //markers?//
  * MP3, 44.1kHz, 256kbps, mono   //markers?//


**Distribution**

  * MP3, 44.1kHz, 128kbps, mono (high quality download)
  * MP3, 44.1kHz, 64kbps, mono (mid quality download)
  * MP3, 22kHz, 32kbps, mono (low quality download)
