====== mold ======

===== Definition: =====

A mold is a hollowed out piece of wood or metal used to form objects out of gold, silver, or other metals that can be poured when hot but harden when cooled. 


  * Ancient people used molds to create ceramic objects, gold and silver jewelry and cast idols.
  * To mold something means to form an object into a certain shape or likeness by using a mold or a tool tool.
  * In a figurative sense, the Bible speaks of a believer being molded into the image of Jesus Christ by the work of the indwelling Holy Spirit. [ULB reference ????]
  * This term could also be translated as, "to form" or "to shape" or "to make."
