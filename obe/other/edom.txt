====== Edom, Edomite ======

===== Facts: =====

<font 14px line-height: 1.4/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The Edomites were the descendants of Esau, who was also known as Edom.  Other names for the country of Edom were "Idumea" and "Seir."</font>

  * * <font 14px line-height: Edom was mountainous and was located to the south and east of Israel.</font>
  * The word for Edom means "red," which may refer to the fact that Esau was covered with red hair when he was born. Or it may refer to the red lentil stew that Esau traded his birthright for.
  * In the Old Testament, the country of Edom is often mentioned as an enemy of Israel.
  * God gave the Old Testament prophets many negative prophecies against Edom.
