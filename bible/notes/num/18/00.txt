====== Numbers 18 General Notes ======



=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== The pay of the Levites and Priests ==

The other tribes should bring one tenth of their income to give to the Levites. Then the Levites should give one tenth of what they received to the priests and the priests also received part of the sacrifices to eat.
