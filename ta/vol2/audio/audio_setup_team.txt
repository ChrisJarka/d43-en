===== The Team =====

This module answers the question: What do I do to setup the recording team?\\
===  ===

Before a recording project can begin, a recording team needs to be formed. There are 4 essential roles of responsibility in a recording project: the recordist, the narrator, the checker and the project coordinator. While the roles of recordist, narrator and checker are dedicated roles, tThe responsibilities of project coordinator could be handled by one of the team members, shared by team members or fulfilled by another ministry worker.   

Let's look the dedicated roles first.
