===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He took away the protection of Judah** – “Yahweh stripped away the defense of Judah”
  * **and you looked in that day to the weapons in the Palace of the Forest** - "The people looked to their storehouse of weapons to save them"
  * **You saw the breaches of the city of David, that they were many** - "The people tried to repair the breaks in the stronghold of Jerusalem"
  * **you collected the water of the lower pool** - "The leaders collected the water inside the city"
