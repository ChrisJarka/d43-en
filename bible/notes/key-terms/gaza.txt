====== Gaza ======

===== Facts: =====

In biblical times, Gaza was an important Philistine city located on the shore of the Mediterranean Sea southeast of Jerusalem. It served as the military and commercial land gate between Asia and Egypt.

  * Today, the city of Gaza is an important seaport in the Gaza Strip, which is a region of land located along the coast of the Mediterranean Sea bordered by Israel on the north and east and Egypt on the south.
