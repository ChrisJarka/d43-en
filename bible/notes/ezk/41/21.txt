===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **cubits**- (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicaldistances|Biblical distances]]).
  * **temple's appearance**  - This refers only to the outward view of this temple.
  * **Then he declared to me** - The word "he" refers to the man whose appearance was like brass (40:3).
  * **This is the table that sits before Yahweh** - This means that the table is in the front of Yahweh.
  * **hinged** - This is the part that lets the door open and close.
