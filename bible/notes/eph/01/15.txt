===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul prays for the Ephesian believers and praises God for the power believers have through Christ.

  * **Because of this** - "For this reason"
  * **your love for all those set apart for him** - "your love for all the saints in Christ" or "your love for all the believers"
  * **I have not stopped thanking God** - AT: "I continue to thank God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
