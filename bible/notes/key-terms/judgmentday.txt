====== judgment day ======


===== Definition: =====


The "judgment day" is a time when God will judge every person for what he has done in this life.


    * God will judge all people by His Son Jesus Christ.
    * The "judgment day" is an unknown time in the life of all humans in which Christ will act finally upon his just character towards that particular individual.


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:urlterm|judge]])
