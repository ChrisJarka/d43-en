====== pharisee ======


===== Definition: This term refers to a Jewish sect of religious authorities. They are only found in the New Testament and were characterized by a rigid adherence to the Mosaic laws found in the Old Testament as well as laws developed through ritual and man made traditions. =====


Text


    * bulletIn Jesus' day, the Pharisees were primarily teachers of the Jewish Law and traditions (Mark 7)
    * bullet
