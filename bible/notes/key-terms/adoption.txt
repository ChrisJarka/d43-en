====== adoption ======

===== Definition: =====

Adoption is the action by which God places us into his family, making us his sons or daughters.

  * Adoption into God's family occurs the moment that someone places their faith in Jesus Christ as their savior.
  * As adopted sons and daughters, believers are co-heirs of God with Jesus Christ
