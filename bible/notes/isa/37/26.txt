===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Have you not heard how I determined it long ago and worked it out in ancient times? Now I am bringing it to pass. You are here to reduce impregnable cities into heaps of ruins.**  – “Are you not aware that I planned long ago for you to lay waste fortified cities. Now I am accomplishing that plan.”
  * **They are plants in the field, green grass, the grass of the roof or of the field, before the east wind. –**The people in these cities and their fortifications will be like grass before an east wind which is a very hot and dry wind that withers grass – (See:Metaphor)
