===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Egypt is a man**  - This refers to the soldiers of Egypt, not the country of Egypt. "The soldiers of Egypt are men, and not Yahweh"  (See:  [[:en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:personification]])
    * **When Yahweh stretches forth his hand**  - The term “hand” is often used in reference to God's power and action.  Yahweh will punish the soldiers of Egypt whom Israel thought would be able to help them.  (See: [[:en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:metonymy]])
