\d For the Chief Musician; on stringed instruments. A Psalm, a song.
\q
\v 1 God be merciful to us, and bless us,
\q And cause his face to shine upon us; \qs [Selah\qs*
\q
\v 2 That your way may be known upon earth,
\q Your salvation among all nations.
\q
\v 3 Let the peoples praise thee, O God;
\q Let all the peoples praise thee.
\q
\v 4 Oh let the nations be glad and sing for joy;
\q For you will judge the peoples with equity,
\q And govern the nations upon earth. \qs [Selah\qs*
\q
\v 5 Let the peoples praise thee, O God;
\q Let all the peoples praise thee.
\q
\v 6 The earth has yielded its increase:
\q God, even our own God, will bless us.
\q
\v 7 God will bless us;
\q And all the ends of the earth shall fear him.
