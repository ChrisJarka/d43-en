====== avenge ======

===== Definition: =====

To avenge is to pay someone back who has caused harm to someone else. Often someone will avenge a family member or close friend who has been harmed by someone else.

  * Another expression for "avenge" is "take revenge."
  * Usually, "avenge" refers to taking action to right a wrong done to someone else while "revenge" refers to taking action against someone who has harmed ourselves.
  * God says he is the one to avenge or take revenge, not people.

(See also: other)
