===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Barak**  - The name of a man. (See: How to [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **Abinoam**  - Barak's father's name. (See: How to [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **Kedesh**  - The name of a place. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **Mount Tabor**  - The name of a mountain. (See: How to [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **ten thousand men**  - "10,000 men" (See: How to [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers|Translate Numbers]])
  * **I will draw out**  - "I will pursuade (or "convince", or "lure")
  * **Sisera**  - Translate the same as in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:04:01|Judges 4:2]].
  * **Jabin**  - Translate the same as in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:04:01|Judges 4:2]].
  * **Kishon**  - The name of a river. (See: How to [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
