===== Translation Notes: =====

    * (Zechariah continues to prophesy.)
    * **to show mercy** - That is, "to be merciful to" or "to act according to his mercy."
    * **remember** - In this context, "remember" does not mean to not forget. It means to keep a commitment or fulfill something.
    * **the hand of our enemies**  - That is, "from the control of our enemies" or "from being harmed and enslaved by our enemies." As in verse 71, "hand" refers to the harmful control or power a person has over another person.
    * **without fear** - That is, "without being afraid of our enemies."
    * **in holiness and righteousness**- That is, "in holy and righteous ways" or, "being holy and righteous" or, "while we live in a holy and righteous way."
    * **before him**- This means, "in his presence" or "according to his will."
    * **all our days** - That is, "our entire life."
