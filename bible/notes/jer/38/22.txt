===== Translation Notes: =====
Jeremiah continues speaking with King Zedekiah.
  * **All the women who are left in the house of you, the king of Judah, will be brought out to the officials of the king of Babylon** - AT: "The soldiers will bring the women who are in your palace to the officials of the king of Babylon" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **You have been deceived by your friends** - AT: "Your friends have deceived you"
  * **Your feet are now sunk into the mud** - This means you the king is in a difficult or helpless situation. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **For all of your wives and children will be brought out to the Chaldeans** - AT: "The Babylonians will take your wives and children"
  * **You will be captured by the hand of the king of Babylon, and this city will be burned** - Here "hand" refers to the king's power or his army. AT: "The king of Babylon's army will capture you, and they will burn the city" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
