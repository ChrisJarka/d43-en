===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **took them from off their hands** - The pronoun 'them' refers to the sacrifices.
    * **produced a sweet aroma**  - See how you translated v. 8: 21.
    * **offering made by fire to Yahweh** - "burnt offering to Yahweh"
    * **took the breast and raised it**  - "took the chest section of the sacrifice and raised it"
    * **ordination**  - "ceremony of becoming a priest"
