===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Paul, a prisoner** -  In this case Paul is a willing servant of Jesus Christ. Paul is compelled by His faith in the Lord Jesus Christ, to serve or preach about Christ.
  * **fellow-worker** -  "co-laborer" or "co-worker."
  * **sister** -  see brother in KT.
  * **soldier** -  "warrior"
  * **the church**-  "the group of believers that meets(UDB).
