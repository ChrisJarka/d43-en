===== translationNotes: =====

  * **May there be peace within ... may they have peace within** - These two phrases have the same meanings and are used together to strengthen each other. AT: "May the people in Jerusalem live in peace" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **peace within the walls** - Here Jerusalem is referred to by its walls. AT: "peace within Jerusalem" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **prosperity within your fortresses** - This refers to the Jerusalem and likely the surrounding areas within Jerusalem's control. At: "may there be prosperity in Jerusalem"
