====== pledge ======

===== Definition: =====

Depending on context, a pledge can be a solemn promise to fulfil a vow or an object taken as a guarantee of payment for a debt.

  * In the Old Testament the officials of Israel pledged allegiance to King David.
  * Under the Jewish law a person's cloak could be taken as a pledge to ensure repayment of a debt.

(See also: other)
