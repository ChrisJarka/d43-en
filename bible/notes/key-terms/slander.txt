====== slander, slanderer ======

===== Definition: =====

To slander means to say something negative about a person to other people.

  * Slander may be a true report or a false report, but its effect is to cause others to think negatively of the person being slandered.
  * One Hebrew word translated as slander means "a whispering" or "an evil report."
  * A person who slanders is called a "slanderer"  or  a "tale bearer" or an "informer".
  * In the New Testament, the word "slander" is used to translate a Greek word that means "to speak against someone in a way that gives them a bad name."
