====== Amos ======

===== Facts: =====

Amos was one of Israel's prophets; he lived during the same time as the prophet Isaiah.

  * Amos was originally a shepherd and fig farmer before being called as a prophet.
  * Before the time when Israel was taken captive by Babylon, Amos prophesied against Israel about their unjust treatment of people.
