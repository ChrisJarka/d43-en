====== fellowship ======

===== Definition: =====

Fellowship is the friendly relationship of a group of people who share similar interests, experiences, or views.

  * Christian fellowship is a shared relationship that believers have with one another through their relationship with Christ and the Holy Spirit. It involves showing hospitality and providing community.
  * Fellowship between God and a person is possible when one stands in a right relationship to God.

(See also: other)
