===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He makes the winds his messengers** - It is also possible to translate this "He makes his messengers like the wind" or "He makes his angels swift" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_similie]])
  * **flames of fire his servant** - It is also possible to translate this "he makes his servants to be like flames of fire"
  * **He laid the foundations of the earth** - AT: "He created the earth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]
  * **it will never be moved** - This means to no one can change the earth except God.
