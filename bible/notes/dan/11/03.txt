===== Translation Notes: =====

The angel told Daniel that 4 kings would come in Persia. King 4 would be rich and be against Greece.
 
  * **the four winds of heaven** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:dan:07:01]].
  * **it will not be given to his descendants** - AT: "his descendants will not rule his kingdom" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]
  * **his kingdom will be uprooted and given to others who are not his descendants** - AT: "others will destroy his kingdom and his descendants will not rule his kingdom" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
