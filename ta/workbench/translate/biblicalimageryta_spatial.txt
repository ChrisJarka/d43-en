=== FOUNDATION IS A FUNDAMENTAL CHARACTERISTIC OF SOMETHING ===


  * //Righteousness and justice are the foundation of your throne//. Ps. 89:14 (ULB)
  * //These are built upon the foundation of the apostles and prophets…//  Eph. 2:20 (ULB)
  * //However, the firm foundation of God stands, having this inscription, "The Lord knows those who are his," and, "Everyone who names the name of the Lord must depart from unrighteousness." //  2 Tim. 2:19 (ULB)
  * //…Not again laying a foundation of repentance from dead works and of faith in God…//  Heb. 6:1 (ULB)
=== WIDE PLACE IS SAFETY::NARROW PLACE IS DISTRESS, SUFFERING ===


  * //You have made a wide place for my feet beneath me so that my feet have not slipped.//  Ps. 18:36 (ULB)
=== HIGH POSITION IS DOMINANCE::LOW POSITION IS SUBJUGATION ===


  * //You put under me those who rise up against me.//  (Ps. 18:39 (ULB)
  * //He subjugates nations under me.//  Ps. 18:47 (ULB)
=== HIGH POSITION IS UNACCESSIBILITY (either concretely or figuratively) ===


  * //Your righteous decrees are too high for him.//  Ps. 10:5b (ULB)
=== STRAIGHT IS JUST::TWISTED IS EVIL ===


  * //You are perverse to the twisted.//  Ps. 18:26 (ULB)
  * //A person who has a crooked heart finds nothing that is good//. Prov. 17:20 (ULB)
=== DISTANCE IS UNCONCERN::NEARNESS IS CONCERN ===

  * //Why are you so far from saving me and far from the words of my anguish?//  Ps. 22:1 (ULB)
  * //Do not be far away, Yahweh.//  Ps. 22:19 (ULB)
  * //Justice is driven back, and righteousness stands far off.//  Isa. 59:14 (ULB)
  *

//As far as the east is from the west, th is is how far he has //  // removed the guilt of our sins from us. //  Psa. 103:12 (ULB)

  * //Wickedness turns away those who commit sin//. Prov. 13:6 (ULB) (i.e., turns away from the path of success)
