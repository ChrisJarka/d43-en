===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **I came to cast fire upon the earth**  - "I came to throw fire on the earth"
    * **how I wish that it was already kindled** - This is an exclamation emphasizing how much he wished this. It can be translated as "I wish very much that it was already lit."
    * **But I have a baptism to be baptized with** - Baptism is used here as a metaphor for suffering. Just as a person can be completely covered over by water, they can be covered over by suffering. This can be translated as "I will be baptized with suffering" or as a simile: "I must be immersed in suffering as a person being baptized is immersed  in water." (See: [[en:ta:figuresofspeech:metaphor]])
    * **how I am distressed until it is completed!** - This is an exclamation emphasizing how much distressed he was. It can be translated as "I am terribly distressed and will be so until this suffering is completed!"
    * **how I am distressed** - "I feel great sorrow" or "I feel burdened"
