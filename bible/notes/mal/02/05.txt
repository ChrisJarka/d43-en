===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **My covenant with him was one of life and peace.** - "The purpose of my covenant with Levi was for the priests to live in prosperity and peace." The covenant with Levi continues with those who also became priests in Levi's tribe or family group in each new generation.  
  * **and I gave these things to him as things to honor me for.** - "I gave these things to him so that he would honor me."
  * **He honored me and stood in awe of my name.** - "Levi honored me and were in awe of me." (doublet)
  * **True teaching was in his mouth** -(Idiom) See UDB.
  * **For a priest’s lips should guard knowledge** - "A priest should protect the truth and the knowledge that has been given him." 
  * **seek instruction from his mouth** "seek instruction from the priest"
  * **for he is the messenger of me, Yahweh of Hosts.** -"for he is my messenger and I am God."
