===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Solomon counted** - The readers should understand that Solomon probably commanded other people to do the actual work. AT: "Solomon had his servants count" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **They were found to be 153,600** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "There were 153,600 foreigners" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **seventy thousand ... eighty thousand** - "70,000 men ... 80,000 men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **to carry loads** - It is understood that these are loads of materials for building Yahweh's house. AT: "to carry loads of materials" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
