====== remnant======

===== Definition: =====

Remnant refers to a group of people who still live after a destructive event.

  * Isaiah referred to a group of Jews as being a remnant who survived attacks from outsiders and lived to return to the Promised Land in Canaan.
  * After the end of the Babylonian Captivity, several Old Testament prophets spoke of a remnant of Jews returning to the Promised Land as well.
  * Paul and John both refer in the New Testament to a remnant of those faithful to Jesus Christ.
