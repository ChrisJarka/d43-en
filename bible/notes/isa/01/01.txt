===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the vision of Isaiah**  - "God showed something unusual to Isaiah"
  * **Amoz **- Amoz was the father of Isaiah. Translate his name as needed into your language. (See: Translate Names)
  * **that he saw concerning****Judah and Jerusalem **- "that Isaiah saw about Judah and about Jerusalem"
  * **Judah **- The word "Judah" refers to the people of Judah.
  * **Jerusalem **- The word "Jerusalem" refers to the religious center of the Jewish people.
  * in the days of - "in the years of." (UDB) This figure of speech means when the king ruled.
  * **Uzziah, Jotham, Ahaz, and Hezekiah, kings of Judah**  - "Uzziah, Jotham, Ahaz, and Hezekiah, rulers of Judah." These are all names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
  * **Hear, O heavens, and listen, O earth **- "Listen all you angels in heaven and all you people on earth"
  * **Yahweh**  - This is the name of God that he revealed to his people in the Old Testament. See the Important Term page concerning how to translate this.
