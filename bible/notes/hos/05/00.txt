====== Hosea 05 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter continues using the poetic form exclusively. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

The author uses Ephraim and Israel interchangeably. They both refer to the North Kingdom. Judah is also mentioned and that refers to the Southern Kingdom. 

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

This chapter is full of metaphors and similes. Please review these concepts [[:en:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:vol2:translate:figs_simile]].
