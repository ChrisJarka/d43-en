====== Lebanon ======

===== Facts: =====

A beautiful mountainous region located directly north of Israel.

  * King Solomon sent laborers in shifts to Lebanon to harvest cedar trees for the construction of God's temple.
  * Lebanon is mentioned in the Song of Solomon as an area that Solomon and his bride travelled through.
