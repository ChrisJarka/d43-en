====== Psalms 073 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 73 is a wisdom psalm. It explains the problem of wicked people who seem to prosper.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Although it sometimes seems that wicked people have no problems and everything works for them. But suddenly they will be destroyed. But the righteous always have God to help them through problems.
