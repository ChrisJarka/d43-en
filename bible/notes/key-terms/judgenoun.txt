====== judge ======


===== Definition: =====


A judge is a person appointed to decide civil and legal matters.


    * Moses appointed men to judge matters that the people brought for decision.
    * After the people of Israel entered the promised land and before they had kings, God appointed judges to lead in times of trouble.
    * Individuals used as agents of national redemption during the period of Hebrew history prior to the anointing of the first king.
