===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **See**  - "Listen to this"
  * **I will lay in Zion a foundation stone**  - "Yahweh will once again establish a foundation stone for the people of Israel to build upon". This may mean that Yahweh will establish a righteous rule n Jerusalem. It may refer to Jesus who Yahweh will place as the foundation stone. (See: Metaphor)
  * **a tried stone**  - "a stone that is tested to see if it is solid"
  * **a sure foundation**  - This is a stone on which it is safe to build a house.
  * **He who believes will not be ashamed **- This is a response to the leaders scorn in verse 15.  "Anyone who believes in the foundation stone placed by Yahweh will not be sorry"
