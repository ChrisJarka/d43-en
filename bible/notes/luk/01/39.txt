===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now** – This word introduces a new part of the story. Consider how your language does this. Some translations might not use an introductory word here.
  * **in those days** – This could also be translated as, “around that time.” Or this sentence could begin with, “Soon after that” as in the UDB.
  *  **got up** – This could also be translated as, “started out” or, “got ready.”
  * **the hill country** – This could also be translated as, “the hilly area” or, “the highlands” or, “the mountainous part of Israel.” 
  * **where she went** – This could also be translated as, “and she went” or, “When she arrived,she went.”
  * **As** – That is, “When” or, “As soon as” as in the UDB.
  * **heard Mary's greeting** – That is, “heard Mary greet her.” 
  * **leaped** - That is, "moved suddenly."
  * **and Elizabeth was filled with the Holy Spirit** – In this context it could also be translated as, “immediately the Holy Spirit led Elizabeth to start praising God.”
