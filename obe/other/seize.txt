====== seize ======

===== Definition: =====

To seize is to capture a person, personal property or land by force.

  * When a village or town was taken by means of military force the soldiers and commanders would seize the goods of those being conquored.
  * When used metaphorically a person can be described as being "seized with fear". This has the same meaning as overcome with fear.

(See also: other)
