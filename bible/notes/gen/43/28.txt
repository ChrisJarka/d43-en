    * **Is this your youngest brother of whom you spoke to me?" **  - This is a question whose answer is so obvious, or the circumstances are such, that there is no answer. When spoken as a direct statement it could be translated, "So, this is your youngest brother of whom you've spoken." spoken as meeting for the first time, or as meeting for the first time in a long time. The statement is delivered in the hearing of all but directly to Benjamin. (see: [[en:ta:translate:RhetoricalQuestion]])
