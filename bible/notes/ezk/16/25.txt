===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **head of every road **- Alternate translation: "at every corner of the street" or "beginning of every street " (UDB).
  * **desecrated your beauty **- The word "desecrate" may mean to not treat or use oneself respectfully. In the UDB it is "showed people your beautiful body".
  * **you opened up your legs **- This is a phrase indicating a woman who is prepared for the sex act. In the UDB it is "offering it to have sex with every man who walked by."
  * **performed many more acts of prostitution **- The word "performed" is not a reference to an act by a performer or actor. It is a reference to a behavior. In the UDB it is "becoming a better known prostitute every day."
  * **great lustful desires **- The word "great" is not a reference to something large in size. It is a reference to the word "powerful." In the UDB it is "eager to have sex with you."
  * **so as to make me angy **- The words "so as" mean "in order to" or "will."
