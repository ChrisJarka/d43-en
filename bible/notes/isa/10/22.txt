===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **are like the sand of the seashore**- This is describing the large number of the people in Israel. It can be translated "are too many to count". (See: Simile)
  * **Destruction is decreed, as overflowing righteousness demands. For the Lord, Yahweh of Hosts, is about to carry out the destruction determined throughout the land**.-This can be translated as "The Lord, Commander of the Angel Armies is going to destroy a majority of the Israelites because it is what he said he would do."
