====== Hamor ======

===== Facts: =====

<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Hamor was a non-Jewish neighbor of Jacob.</font>

  * Jacob bought a family burial ground from Hamor's sons after Jacob reunited with his brother Esau.
  * Hamor's son Shechem raped Jacob's daughter Dinah and her brothers took revenge on Hamor and his family..
