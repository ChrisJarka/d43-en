  * **It was necessary that the scripture should be fulfille"**  - The things that have happened related to Jesus' crucifixion were necessary to fulfill the prophecies spoken of in scripture.
  * **that the Holy Spirit spoke before by the mouth of David**  - The scripture that was fulfilled was in the Book of Psalms, written by David.
