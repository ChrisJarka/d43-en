===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Two of all flesh in which was the breath of life**  - This could be translated as "two of every kind of animal that breathes." Basically it refers to animals that live on the land. 
  * **flesh** - People and animals all have flesh. Here "flesh" is used to refer to animals that live on the land. (See: [[en:ta:figuresofspeech:metonymy]])
  * **male and female of all flesh** -  This could be translated as "male and female of every kind of land animal."
  * **after them**  - This could be made more explicit: "after they entered the ark" (See: [[:en:ta:discourse:implicitexplicit]]).
