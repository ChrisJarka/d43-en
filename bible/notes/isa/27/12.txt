===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It will come about on that day that Yahweh will thresh from flowing stream of the Euphrates River, to the Brook of Egypt, and you will be gathered one by one, people of Israel**- "Yahweh will separate out his people from all over to once again be brought back to Israel"
  * **On that day a great trumpet will be blown**- (See how translated in 18:3)
  * **the perishing ones in the land of Assyria will come, and the outcasts in the land of Egypt**- "the people of Israel that were scattered will come out from where they were held in exile"
