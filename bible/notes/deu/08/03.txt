===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses continues to address all the people of Israel.

  * **He humbled you** - Yahweh made the people of Israel humble during their time in the desert.
  * **by bread only** - The bread is a symbol of food in general. AT: "Food is not the only thing people need in order to live." (See:[[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **it is by everything that proceeds out of the mouth of Yahweh**  - AT: "every word that comes from the mouth of Yahweh."
