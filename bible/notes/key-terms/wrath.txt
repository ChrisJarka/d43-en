====== wrath ======


===== Definition: =====


Wrath is a visible expression of anger. When used in the Bible, it usually refers to God's wrath.


    * God's wrath is the just penalty for those who do not repent of their sin.
    * A king or other ruler can also display wrath by punishing law breakers.
