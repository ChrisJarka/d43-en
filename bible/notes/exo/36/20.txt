===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]]

  * **vertical**  - This means going up and down rather than side to side. AT: "upright", "straight up and down"
  * **acacia wood**  - This is a tree with feathery leaves and gray thorns whose wood is hard and durable. AT: "wood from a tree called 'acacia'".
  * **cubit**  - A cubit was a little less than half a meter. (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaldistance]])
