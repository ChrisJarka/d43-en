====== Jehoiachin ======

===== Facts: =====

Jehoiachin was a king over the kingdom of Judah. He was an evil king who followed the bad example of his grandfather Manasseh and his father Jehoiakim.

  * Jehoiachin became king when he was 18 years old. He only reigned three months and after that he was captured by the Babylonian army and taken to Babylon.
