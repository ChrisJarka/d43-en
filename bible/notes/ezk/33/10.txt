  * **Thus you have said when you say**  - "You have said"
  * **our transgressions and sins are upon us **- Alternate translation: "our transgressions and sins fill our lives"
  * **we are decaying in them**  - Alternate translations: "we are ruined by our sins" or "we are dying in our sins"
  * **declaration**  - announcement
