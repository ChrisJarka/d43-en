===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Whoever steals must steal no longer, but rather, he must labor, working usefully with his hands, so that he may be able to share with the person who has need**  - "Those who have been stealing must not steal any longer. Instead, they must work hard to earn their living, so that they may have something to share with to those who are needy* (See: UDB)
  * **No corrupt speech must**  - You can translate "Let no corrupt speech" as "Let no foul language" or "Let no filthy talk".
  * **instead words**  - "instead speak words"
  * **building up others**  - "building up other believers" or "giving strength to other believers"
  * **to give grace to them who are listening**  - This can be translated as "In this way you will give grace to those who are listening". The pronoun "you" refers to all believers. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:formsofyou-plural|Forms of 'you' - Dual/Plural]])
  * **grieve the Holy Spirit of God**  - "make the Holy Spirit of God sad"
  * **by whom you have been sealed**  - This can be translated as "for he has sealed you". The pronoun "you" refers to all believers. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:formsofyou-plural|Forms of 'you' - Dual/Plural]])
