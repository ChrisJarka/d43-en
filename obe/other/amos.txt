====== Amos ======

===== Facts: =====

Amos was one of Israel's "minor prophets" and was contemporary with Isaiah.

  * Amos was originally a shepherd and fig farmer before being called as a prophet.
  * Prior to the captivity period Amos prophesied against Israel about their social injustices.
