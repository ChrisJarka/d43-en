===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **soldiers**  - That is, people serving in the military.
    * **what about us, what must we do**- That is, you told the crowd and the tax collector, what they must do, how about us, soldiers, what must we do?
    * **do not take money from anyone by force**- That is "do not take money in any situation by force."
    * **and do not accuse anyone falsely**- That is, "in the same way, do not accuse anyone falsely in any situation to get money from them."
    * **be content ****with your wages**  - That is, "be satisfied with your wages"" or "make sure to be satisfied with the pay you receive as soldiers."
