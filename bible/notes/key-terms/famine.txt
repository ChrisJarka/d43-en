====== famine ======

===== Definition: =====

A famine is a widespread disaster that involves an extreme lack of food throughout a country or region.

  * Failure of food crops can be caused by extreme weather conditions, crop disease, insects, war, or bad government.
  * The term "famine" is also used figuratively to refer to a time when God stops communicating with people as a way of punishing them, so that they no longer hear the words of the Lord.

(See also: other)
