====== shadow, shelter ======

===== <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>A dark image cast on a surface by an interposing object that intercepts and blocks the light on that surface. In reference to God it means that those in God's shadow are within the realm of his protection because they are close to him.</font>  =====

===== Definition:  =====

=====   =====

  * protection -
  * shadow as a sign of protection for Hezekiah from the Lord
