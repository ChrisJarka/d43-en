===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then he brought me to the doorway of the courtyard, and I** - Then "God" brought "Ezekiel" to the doorway of the courtyard, and "Ezekiel".
  * **He said to me** - "God" said to "Ezekiel".
  * **So I dug into the wall** - So "Ezekiel" dug into the wall.
  * **Then he said to me** - Then "God" said to "Ezekiel".
  * **abominations that they** - abominations that "Israel".
