====== Son of Man ======


===== Definition: =====


The Son of Man is a title given to Jesus.  


    * This title emphasizes that Jesus was the fulfillment of the Old Testament prophecies about the "Son of Man".
    * The title "Son of Man" also emphasizes Jesus' humanity.  While Jesus was fully man, he was also fully divine.
    * This is a title Jesus often uses for himself.
