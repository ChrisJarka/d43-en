===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **rejoice and be glad in you**  - AT: "rejoice in you and be glad in you." These 2 short phrases mean the same thing. They are used together to emphasize the idea. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **May God be praised**  - AT: "Praise God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **I am poor and needy**  - These two phrases have basically the same meaning and are used together for emphasis. AT: "I an poor and in need" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
