====== Jeremiah Chapter 49 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who should not be living in Israel's land of Gad and why?

A. Molech and his people should not be occuping Gad because the day is coming when Yahweh will again possess the land. [49:1-2]

Q? Why are the proud people in Heshbon, Ai, and Rabbah wailing,weeping, mourning and running around in confusion?

A. Because their god Milcom and his priests and officials will be taken away in exile and they were rebellious people trusting in their wealth which will not protect them. [49:3-4]

Q? Why will the people be terrified?

A. Because they will all be forced to scatter to other countries. [49:5]

Q? What will Yahweh do for the Ammon people-group in the future?

A. Yahweh will restore the fortunes of the people of Ammon. [49:6]

Q? Because there are no wise people left in Edom what does Jeremiah prophesy?

A. The people are to turn and flee and hide in deep caves because Yahweh is causing disaster to happen to all the people of Edom. [49:7-8]

Q? What is Yahweh going to do to Edom?

A. He is going to strip Edom (Esau) bare. [49:10]

Q? What does Yahweh say concerning the orphans and widows in Edom?

A. Yahweh says to leave the orphans and widows behind because he will take care of them. [49:11]

Q? What does Yahweh continue to declare about what will happen to Edom?

A. Even those who don't deserve punishment will be punished and that Bozrah (Edom) will become a horror, a disgrace, a devastation, and an object of cursing. [49:12-13]

Q? What was the message that Jeremiah heard from Yahweh?

A. That Yahweh has sent ambassadors to many nations to prepare for battle and attack Edom. [49:14]

Q? What does Yahweh say to the people of Edom?

A. He says that their nation of Edom will become unimportant and other nations will despise their country.[49:15]
