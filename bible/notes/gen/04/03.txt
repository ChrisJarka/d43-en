===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the designated time**  - "at the right time"
  * **fruit of the ground**  - "crops" or "harvest". It refers to the food that came from plants he had tended.
  * **some of the fat**  - This could be translated "some of their fat parts," referring to the fatty parts of the sheep.
  * **accepted**  - The verb "accept" in verses 4 and 5 means "look upon favorably" or "be pleased with."
  * **the fat**  - the fat from the lambs he killed
  * **was very angry**  - Some languages may have an idiom for anger such as "He burned" or "His anger burned like a fire within him."
  * **his face looked dejected**  - This can be translated as "he scowled." This means that the expression on his face showed that he was angry or jealous. Some languages have an idiom that describes what an angry person's face looks like.
