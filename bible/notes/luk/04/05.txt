===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **the devil led him up **- That is, "the devil took him up" or "the devil brought him up." This does not mean that the devil was leading the way or that Jesus was following him. Instead, it was more of an active part on the devil in putting him up there.
    * **a high place**  - That is, "a high mountain."
    * **of the world**  - The word here for "world" refers to the "inhabited world." It can metaphorically refer to the Roman Empire as the "known inhabited world."
    * **moment -**  Or, "in an instant" or "instantly."
    * **Therefore**  - That is, "since I rule over these kingdoms of the world and I can give it to whomever I desire."
    * **if you will bow down before me and worship me**  - That is, "if you, Jesus, give me honor and allegiance."
    * **all of this will be yours **- That is, "I will give all these kingdoms to you" or "all of these kingdoms I showed you will be yours to have."
