====== Elizabeth ======


===== Facts: =====


Elizabeth was the mother of John the Baptist.


    * Elizabeth was not able to bear children, but when she was too old to have children, God promised her husband that Elizabeth would bear him a son.
    * She slept with her husband, became pregnant, and gave birth to a son. They named him John.
