====== Abel ======


===== Facts: =====


Abel was the second child born to Adam and Eve.


    * Adam and Eve's firstborn son Cain murdered Abel.
    * Abel is described in the Old Testament as being a shepherd.
    * Abel pleased God with his life and offerings.
