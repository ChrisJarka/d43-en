===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Man is brought down, and the great man is humbled** - "people are humbled, and the important person is humbled." This can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh will humble people and will make the important people humble" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) 
  * **the eyes of the lofty are cast down** - "the eyes of proud people are looking down." AT: "people who were proud are now ashamed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Yahweh of hosts** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:01:09|1:9]].
  * **Yahweh of hosts is exalted in his justice** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "People will praise Yahweh of hosts because he is just" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) 
  * **the ruins of the rich people** - "the riches people's houses that have been destroyed"
  * **lambs will graze** - "lambs will eat grass"
