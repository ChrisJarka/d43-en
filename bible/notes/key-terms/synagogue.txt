====== synagogue ======


===== Definition: =====


A synagogue is a building where Jewish people assemble to pray. Since ancient times, the synagogue services have included prayer, scripture reading, and teaching about the scripture.


    * Jesus often taught in synagogues.
    * The word synagogue can also refer to the group of people meeting there.


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:pray|pray]])
