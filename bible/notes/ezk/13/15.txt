===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I**  - refers to Yahweh (13:14)
  * **annihilate**  - "destroy completely"
  * **fury**  - "violent anger" or "great anger"
  * **wall**  - a structure that separates one area from another
  * **I will annihilate in my fury the wall **- Alternate translation: "in my fury I will annihilate the wall"
  * **whitewashed**  - planned to hide a dishonest, immoral, or illegal act or situation by making it seem better than it is
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration **- See how you translated this in 5:11.
