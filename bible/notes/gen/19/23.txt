===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **reached**
 - or "entered."
  * **Yahweh rained down upon**  - "Yahweh dropped upon" or "Yahweh cast upon"
  * **sulphur**  - A yellow material that has a strong smell and burns easily.
  * **from Yahweh out of the sky**  - The phrase "from Yahweh" marks the fact that the sulphur and fire came from God's power.
  * **those cities**  - This refers primarily to Sodom and Gomorrah, but also to three other towns.
