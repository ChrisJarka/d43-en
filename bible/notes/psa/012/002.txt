===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **everyone saysâ€¦speaks**  - The word "everyone" in both phrases is used to emphasize the problem is very wide spread. (See: Hyperbole)
  * **flattering lips**  - AT: "lips that only pretend to praise"
  * **a double heart**  - AT: "a heart that is divided in opposite ideas"
  * **cut off all flattering lips**  - "bring to an end the people with flattering lips"
  * **every tongue declaring great things**  - "each person boasting more than they should" (See: Synecdoche)
  * **With our tongues will we prevail **- AT: "Speaking many words will make us victorious"
  * **When our lips speak**  - The implication is that because the word is spoken it becomes authority.
  * **who can be lord over us**  - AT: "who would bravely claim to be our ruler"
