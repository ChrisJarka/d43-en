====== frankincense ======

===== Definition: =====

Frankincense is a resin that comes from trees of the genus Boswellia. It is used for its fragrance in perfumes and incense.

  * The three wise men brought frankincense as one of their gifts to the newborn Christ.
  * Frankincense is also known as olibanum.
