====== Abiathar ======

===== Definition: =====

Abiathar was a priest for the nation of Israel during the time of King David.

  * Abiathar escaped when King Saul massacred the priests and went to David in the wilderness.
  * Abiathar also served as priest with Zadok, under the reign of King Solomon.
