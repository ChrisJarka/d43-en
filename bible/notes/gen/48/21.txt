===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **God will be with you, and will bring you back to the land of your fathers.**  - This is known as a "dying wish." But because Jacob ascribes it to God, it is also a very specific prophecy.
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **To you, as one who is above your brothers,**  - Contrast Jacob's two initial reactions to Josephs dreams in Genesis 37:10 and 37:11.
  * **To you, as one who is above your brothers, I give the mountain slope **- "I give you one portion more than your brothers"
