====== sign ======


===== Definition: =====


A sign is an event, pattern, prophecy, or figure that represents or substantiates the authenticity of meaning for the word or concept it foreshadows.


When used in the Bible, a sign is often a miraculous event which validates the authenticity of a prophet or spokesperson sent from God.


    * As a sign, God ignited a water-drenched offering that the prophet Elijah had prepared.
    * Jesus performed miraculous signs to show that he was the Messiah.
    * God validated the apostles' ministries by means of signs they performed.
    * Moses showed his God-given authority through the signs of the ten plagues of Egypt when dealing with Pharaoh to set the Israelites free.
