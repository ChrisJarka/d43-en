===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **furious** - "very angry"
    * **he will show no mercy** - The "he" is the neighbor whose wife has committed adultery with you. AT: "he will not limit the pain he will cause you" or "he will hurt you as much as he can" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_123person]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
    * **when he takes his revenge** - "in the moment of his revenge" AT: "when the moment arrives when he can take his revenge"
    * **takes his revenge** - If a person takes revenge, it is to cause hurt to the person who hurt him first.
    * **compensation** - "payment"
    * **bought off** - To buy someone off, means that by paying money, you make that person do something that they would otherwise not do. Especially if that person plans to follow the law and you pay to make them not follow the law.
    * **though** - "eventhough" AT: "despite the fact that"
    * **offer** - "give" AT: "promise"
