===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continued to speak to Abraham while Abraham dreamed.

  * **will come here**  - They would come back to the land where Abram was then living, the land Yahweh had promised to give to him.
  * **go to your fathers**  - "go to your fathers" refers to dying. In ancient Israel, people would be buried in family tombs. Each generation would be placed in the same grave site.
