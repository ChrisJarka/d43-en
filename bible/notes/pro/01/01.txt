===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **To learn wisdom and instruction**  - One purpose of the proverbs is to give wisdom and instruction to the reader.
  * **to teach words of insight** - Another purpose of the proverbs is to teach the reader so that he understands the true nature of things.
  * **right**  - "proper" or "without fault"
  * **just**  - To be "just" is to treat others in accordance with God's laws.
  * **fair**  - To be "fair" is to act in agreement with what is thought to be good or acceptable.
