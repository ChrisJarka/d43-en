===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues speaking to Abraham.

  * **obeyed my voice**  - "obeyed me"
  * **Abraham returned to his young men**  - "Abraham and his son went back to Abraham's servants." Only Abraham was named because he was the father, but his son went with him.
  * **they departed**  - "they left that place"
  * **Abraham lived at Beersheba**  - "Abraham stayed at Beersheba"
