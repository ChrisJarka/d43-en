====== exile, the Exile ======

===== Definition: =====

Exile is the forced absence of people from their home or normal habitat.

  * The Exile is a period in biblical history (598-538 BC) when many citizens of Judah and Jerusalem were forcibly taken from their land to Babylon.
  * In the NT the exile is figuratively used to descrive life on this earth as in "this world is not our home."  Hebrews 11:13
