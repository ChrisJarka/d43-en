====== citizen ======

===== Definition: =====

A citizen is someone who lives in a specific city or country. It especially refers to someone who is recognized officially as being a legal resident of that place.

  * Depending on the context, this could also be translated as "inhabitant" or "official resident."
  * They could be subjects of a certain ruler.
