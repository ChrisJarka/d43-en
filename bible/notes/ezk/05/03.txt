====== Ezekiel 05:3-4 ======

===== ULB: =====

<usfm> \v 3 But take a small number of hairs from them and tie them to your sleeves. \v 4 Then take more of the hair and throw them into the midst of the fire; and burn them in the fire; from there a fire will go out to all the house of Israel. </usfm>

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **tie them to your sleeves** - "bind them to your sleeves" or "connect these to the end of the cloth on your arms" (UDB).
  * **a fire will go out to** - Alternate translation: "a fire will move from here to" or "a fire will spread" (UDB). This is also a figure of speech telling the reader that this fire will spread.
