===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **"While he was yet speaking, another also came" -**  "another" here is referring to another messenger, or servant, in addition to the one Job is already speaking with.
  * **"A great wind came from the wilderness" **- This great wind could refer to either a tornado or to something very similar to a desert sandstorm.
  * **"struck the four corners of the house"**- The idea here is that the wind struck the structural supports of the house.
  * **"it fell upon the young men" **- The term translated here as "young men" includes women and is referring to both Job's sons and his daughters.
