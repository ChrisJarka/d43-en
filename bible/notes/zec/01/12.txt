===== translationNotes: =====

  * **to Jerusalem and to the cities of Judah** - Here the words "Jerusalem" and "cities" refer to the people who live in those cities. AT: "to the people of Jerusalem and to the people of the cities of Judah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **talked with me, with good words, words of comfort** - These phrases describe words that are both good and comforting. AT: "encouraged me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hendiadys]])
