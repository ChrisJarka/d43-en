===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Yahweh continues to challenge Job.
  * **General Information:** - Yahweh questions Job to emphasize that he causes it to rain and thunder and Job does not. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])

  * **Who has created the channels for the floods of rain, or who has made ... grass sprout up?** - This can be translated as a statement. AT: "Only I have created the channels for the floods of rain, and only I have made ... grass sprout up." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **the floods of rain** - "the torrents of rain"
  * **a path for the thunder** - "a way for the rumble of thunder to be heard." 
  * **on lands where no person exists, and on the wilderness, in which there is no one** - These two phrases have nearly the same meaning. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **where no person exists** - "where there are no people"
  * **to satisfy** - The implied information is that it is the rain that meets the needs of the land to grow grass. AT: "so that the rain can satisfy the needs of" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **devastated and desolate** - "ruined and wasted." These two words have nearly the same meaning and emphasize the ruined and empty nature of these regions. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hendiadys]])
  * **with grass** - "the new grass" or "the fresh grass." This is grass that is just starting to grow.
  * **make the ground to sprout with** - "the ground would support new grasses to grow"
