===== Organizing the List of Projects in translationStudio-Desktop v10 =====

//This answers the question:// //How can I organize my projects?//

//In order to understand this topic, it would be good to read://
  * //[[en:ta:vol2:tech:tsd_navigate]]//

Use the 3-dot menu to go to the 'Home' page with title of 'Your Translation Projects.'

=== New for version 10 ===
Sort the projects by columns and then projects by book or in alphabetical order. Click on the header of the sort options, then:

**Choose the order of the information in the columns**:
  * Project then Language
  * Language then Project
  * Progress then Project

**Then sort the Project Column by**:
  * Book Order
  * Alphabetical Order
