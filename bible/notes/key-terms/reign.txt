====== reign ======

===== Definition =====

To reign means to rule over a certain country and its people. The reign of a king is the time period during which he is ruling.

  * Reign is typically used in reference to rulers whose countries have a monarchy.
  * Jesus Christ will reign over the whole world when he returns. The Bible says that Christians will reign with Him.
