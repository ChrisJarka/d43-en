===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **That the dream was repeated to Pharaoh is because the matter has established by God, and God will soon do it.**  - Joseph means that God has spoken to Pharaoh in a doublet for a reason; the same reason that God has just revealed to Joseph, to emphasize that the message is from God, and to emphasize that it is happening now.
