===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses speaks a poetic song to the people of Israel. He continues to quote Yahweh's words. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **Were it not that I feared the provocation of the enemy** - "If it were not for their enemies' taunts" or "My concern is that their enemies would wrongly boast"
  * **judge mistakenly** - "misunderstand"
  * **Our hand is exalted** - Here "hand" represents the strength or power of a person. AT: "Our strength has triumphed" or "Our power has won" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
