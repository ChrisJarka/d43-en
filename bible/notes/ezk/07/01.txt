===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The word of Yahweh came to me and said**  - The word of Yahweh refers to God who is speaking. (See: [[:en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:personification|Personification]])
  * **thus says the Lord Yahweh to the land of Israel**  - The land of Israel is used to refer to the people of Israel. Alternate translation: "thus says the Lord Yahweh to the people of Israel"  (See: [[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:metonymy]])
