===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **brought** - See how you translated this in v. 8: 6.
    * **laid their hands on** - "placed their hands upon"
    * **horns of the altar** - See how this was translated in 4:6.
    * **poured out the blood** - "spilled the blood" or "let the blood drip down"
    * **set it apart for God** - "placed it off to the side for God's use" or "put it by itself for God's purpose"
    * **set it apart for God in order to make atonement for it** - The word 'it' these two times refers to the altar.
