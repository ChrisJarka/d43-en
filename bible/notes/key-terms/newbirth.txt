====== born again, new birth, spiritual birth ======

===== Definition: =====

Born again, new birth, spiritual birth are all phrases used to describe people returning to the sinless spiritual state of mankind before the sin of disobedience of Adam and Eve in the Garden of Eden.

  * At the fall of Adam and Eve in the Garden the spiritual life of all mankind was put to death.
  * In the Old Testament, the sacrificial system was used to bring the people of God to a sinless spiritual state, but sacrifices had to be done continually.
  * With the death and resurrection on Jesus, God made available once and for all times the final sacrifice, the way for people to have their spiritual life restored, through faith in Him.
