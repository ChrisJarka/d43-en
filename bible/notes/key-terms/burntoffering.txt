====== burnt offering, offering by fire ======

===== Definition: =====

A burnt offering or offering by fire is a type of sacrifice to God that was consumed by fire on an altar.

  * God commanded the Jewish people to offer burnt offerings twice daily.
  * A lamb was typically offered as a burnt offering, with it's skin being given to the priest.

(See also: sacrifice, altar, priest)
