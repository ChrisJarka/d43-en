====== eagle ======

===== Definition: =====

A large, powerful bird of prey which is often used as a symbol for freedom and power.

  * The Psalms state that those who trust in the Lord will soar as an eagle does. This is a metaphor used to describe how God's power affects a person.
  * The Old Testamant says that King David's companion Jonathan was as "swift as an eagle."
