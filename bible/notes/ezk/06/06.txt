===== Translation Notes: =====


    * ** cities will be laid waste** - "Enemy armies will destroy your cities"
    * **laid waste and made desolate**  - "destroyed and no one will go there anymore"
    * ** the high places shall be desolate**  - "The people will no longer go to the high places."
    * ** I am Yahweh**  - God is saying that He is God and the idols being worshiped in Israel are not.
