====== Joshua Chapter 07 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why did Yahweh's anger burn against the people of Israel?

A. Yahweh's anger burned agains the people of Israel because Achan took some things that were dedicated to destruction. [7:1]

Q? What did the spys that were sent by Joshua to Ai report back to Joshua?

A. The spys that were sent to Ai reported to Joshua that only a small army could take Ai because there were few people there. [7:3]
