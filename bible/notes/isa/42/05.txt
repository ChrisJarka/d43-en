===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **This is what God Yahweh says, he who created the heavens and stretched them forth; he who spread out the earth and gives life in it; he who gives breath to the people on it, and life to those who live on it: **  - Yahweh is identified as the One who created the heavens and the earth and the people on the earth.  AT: "Yahweh our God created the sky and spread it out.  He also created the earth and everything on it.  He gives breath to all the people on the earth and causes them to live." UDB
  * **gives breath to the people on it, and life to those live on it**  - These phrases emphasize that Yahweh gives life.   (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **I, Yahweh, have called you in righteousness and will hold your hand.**  - The words of Yahweh are recorded for the servant.  Yahweh tells the servant that he called the servant and will be with him. 
  * **I will keep you and set you as covenant for the people**  - Yahweh continues to encourage the servant and tells him that the servant will be the covenant between Yahweh and the people.
  * **as a light for the Gentiles**  - Yahweh tells the servant that the servant will be a light for all the people of the earth.
