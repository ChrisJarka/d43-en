===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **midnight** - at 12am (at night), or the time in the middle of the night between evening and dawn
    * **throughout Egypt** - Alternate Translation: "all over Egypt"
    * **firstborn** - the child in a family born first, or the oldest child in a family
    * **behind the hand mill** - means a person who "works grinding grain." "Grain" is a small, hard seed from a plant used for food (such as wheat, corn, and rice), especially to make bread. 
    * **cattle** - animals raised for food, including large and small animals
