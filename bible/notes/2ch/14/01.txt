\p \v 2 Asa did things that Yahweh his God considers to be right and good. \v 3 His workers got rid of the altars to worship foreign gods that were at the shrines. ===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Abijah slept with his ancestors- **  AT: "Abijah died" (See: Metaphor)
  * **the land was quiet ten years- **  AT: 'there was peace in the land for ten years"
  * **sacred stones pillars**- Here "sacred' is used to describe religious objects that are used for worshiping false gods, not Yahweh.
  * The UDB makes this chunk clearer.
