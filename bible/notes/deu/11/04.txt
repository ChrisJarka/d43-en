===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Neither did they did not see - ** Moses continues, as in verse 2, using a negative statement to emphasize the positive. The people of Israel he is addressing now, did see (positive), God's following actions. (See: Double Negatives)
  * **what he did**  - what Yahweh did
  * **to this place**  - The place the people of Israel are while Moses is speaking to them, before they crossed the Jordan River.
