====== 2 Kings 17 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter ends the story about Israel. Now the story continues with Judah alone.


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Israel continued to worship idols so God allowed Assyria to conquer them and take them away. Assyria brought people from other lands and settled them in what used to be Israel's territory and the new people worshipped their own Gods and Yahweh as well.
