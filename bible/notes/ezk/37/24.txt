===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **David my servant will be king**  - David had been king over Israel in the distant past. Possible translation: "Someone like David my servant will become king over them."
  * **shepherd**  - This word suggests that the king will be like a shepherd who guides his sheep. The word also recalls King David who had been a shepherd when he was young. (See: Metaphor)
  * **walk according to my judgments**  - "follow my laws"
  * **keep my statutes**  - "live according to my decrees"
  * **dwell**  - live
  * **the land that I have given to my servant, to Jacob, where your fathers abided**  - Yahweh had given the land of Israel to Abraham and his descendants, beginning with Abraham's son Isaac and Isaac's son Jacob. This was the land where the Israelites lived before they were taken to Babylon as captives. Alternate translation: "They will live in the land I gave to my servant Jacob, the land where your ancestors lived."
  * **into perpetuity**  - "forever"
  * **chief**  - "king"
