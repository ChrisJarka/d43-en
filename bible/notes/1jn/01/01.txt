===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **That which was from the beginning**  - The phrase That which was from the beginning refers to Jesus, who existed before everything was made. You could translate this as "We are writing to you about the one who existed before the creation of all things."
  * **beginning**  - "beginning of all things" or "the creation of the world"* 
  * **which we**  - Here the word "we" refers to John and those who knew Jesus when he was on this earth but does not include the people he is writing to. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:exclusive|Exclusive]])
  * **contemplated**  - "thought deeply about"
  * **hands handled**  - "we have touched with our hands"
  * **Word of life**  - This refers to Jesus, who causes people to live forever.
  * **was manifested**  - "appeared" or "became visible" or "was revealed"
  * **have seen**  - "have seen it"
  * **bear witness**  - "give our report about it"
  * **the eternal life**  - This phrase also refers to Jesus, who cause us to live forever.
