===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **mountain of Israel**  - Refers to the rebellious exiled people of Israel.
  * **they are desolate**  - Without hope.
  * **they have been given to devour**  - Given themselves over to be killed.
  * **made yourself great against me with your mouth**  -They were speaking evil against Yahweh.
  * **multiplied your words against me**  - Used all of the evil terms they knew to downplay Yahweh.
