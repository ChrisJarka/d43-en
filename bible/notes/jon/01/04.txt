===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **mighty tempest -**  "violent storm" (UDB)
  * **Soon it appeared -**  "The men thought"
  * **to be broken up -**  "to break apart"
  * **Then the sailors -**  "Then the men on the ship"
  * **his own god -**  "his own idol." The word “god” is used to refer to false gods and idols that people worship.
  * **They threw the ship's cargo -**  "The men threw the goods off the ship"
  * **to lighten it -**  "to help the ship float better"
  * **But Jonah -**  The word "but" describes the contrast between Jonah's actions and the actions of the other men.
  * **down into the innermost parts of -**  "inside"
  * **lying there fast asleep -**  "asleep." This refers to the literal meaning of sleep.
