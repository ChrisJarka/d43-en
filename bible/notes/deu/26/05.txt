===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **"My ancestor was a wandering Aramean** - This is the beginning of the statement that the Israelite male should make when bringing his basket. This statement continues until the middle of 26:10.
  * **a wandering Aramean** - This refers to Jacob who was the ancestor of all Israelites. He lived for many years in Aram-Nahairam, a region located in Syria.
  * **and stayed there** - "and lived the rest of his life there"
  * **There he became a great and prosperous nation.** This means that the descendants of Jacob/Israel became a large nation.
  * **populous nation**  - "A nation of many people."
