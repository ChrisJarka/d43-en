===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **sons be like plants who grow to full size in their youth** - This is likely a reference to their sons being healthy. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile|Simile]])
  * **daughters like carved corner pillar, shapely like those of a palace** - This is likely a reference to the beauty of their daughters.
  * **may our sheep produce thousands and ten thousands in our field**  - AT: "may our sheep have many offspring to fill our fields" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
