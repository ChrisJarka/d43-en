===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I went to the prophetess **- This is an euphemism. It<font 14px line-height: 19px;/Lato, sans-serif;;rgb(51, 51, 51) font-family: Lato, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 19px;;;inherit>is a mild or polite way of referring to something that is unpleasant, embarrassing or considered to be socially unacceptable.</font> This can be translated as "I slept with my wife, who was a prophetess" (UDB) or "I had sex with my wife, who was a prophetess"
  * **Call his name**  - "Give him the name"
  * **For before the child knows to cry**  - "because before the child can say"
  * **the riches of Damascus and the plunder of Samaria **- This can be translated as, "the valuables of Damascus and Samaria"
  * **will be carried away**  - "will be taken away"
