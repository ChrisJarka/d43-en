===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues to speak about Jerusalem as if the city were his unfaithful wife. The city represents the people who live there. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])

  * **do not walk in their ways or after their disgusting actions** - The way a person lives is spoken of as if the person were walking on a path. AT: "do not act like them or do the disgusting things that they do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
