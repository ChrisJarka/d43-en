===== translationNotes: =====

  * **twenty-five years old ... twenty-nine years** - "25 years old ... 29 years" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Abijah** - This is a woman's name. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **what was right in the eyes of Yahweh** - The word "eyes" here represents seeing, and seeing represents judgment. Yahweh saw and approved of Hezekiah's actions. See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:2ch:14:01|14:1]]. AT: "what Yahweh judged to be right" or "what Yahweh considered to be right" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
