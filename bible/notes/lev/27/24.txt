===== translationNotes: =====

  * **to the man from whom it was bought, to the land's owner** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "to its original owner from whom the man bought it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **All the valuations must be set** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The priests must determine the estimated values" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **by the weight of the sanctuary shekel** - This refers to the official value of the shekel.
  * **shekel** - See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bmoney]]
  * **Twenty gerahs** - "20 gerahs." This equals one shekel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]])
