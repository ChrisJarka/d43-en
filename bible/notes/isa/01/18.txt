===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **let us reason together** - "let us think about this together." Here the word "us" refers to God and includes the people of Judah. (See: [[en:ta:translate:inclusive]])
  * **sins are as scarlet, they will be white as snow**- God is comparing someone's sins being forgiven to something with a bright red color becoming white. The color "white" often refers to holiness or purity. The word "snow" refers "sins are like a red stain on a white cloth, they will be washed clean to be white again." (see: Metaphor)
  * **red like crimson, they shall be as wool**- (see: Doublet)
