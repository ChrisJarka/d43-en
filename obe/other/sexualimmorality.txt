====== sexual immorality ======

===== Definition: =====

Sexual immorality is the term for all sexual activity outside of the boundaries of God-ordained marriage

  * The New Testament commands Christians to turn away from sexual immorality.
  * Sexual immorality also includes homosexual practices.
