===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Why are you discouraged, my soul** - The author spoke to his inner self as if he spoke to another person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **who is the help of my face and my God**  - The author refers to the part of the body people recognize each other with to speak about himself. being. AT: "he is my help and he is my God." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
