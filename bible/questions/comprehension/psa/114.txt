====== Psalms Chapter 114 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? When Israel left Egypt, what did Judah and Israel become?

A. "Judah became his sanctuary, Israel his kingdom." [114:2]

Q? The writer says the sea looked and fled and the Jordan turned back.  What does he say the mountains and the hills do?

A. "The mountains skipped like rams, the hills skipped like lambs." [114:4]

Q? Why does the writer say the earth should tremble? 

A. "Tremble, earth, before the Lord, at the presence of the God of Jacob." [114:7]

Q? What does the writer say that Yahweh turned the rock and the hard rock into? 

A. "He turned the rock into a pool of water, the hard rock into a spring of water." [114:8]
