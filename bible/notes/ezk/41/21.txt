===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **sanctuary **- It is important to distinguish this as the very holy place in the temple; see UDB.
  * **temple's appearance**  - this refers only to the outward view of this temple.
  * **<font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>This is the table that sits before Yahweh -</font>    ** <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>this states that the table is in the presence of Yahwah. (see Personification)</font>
  * **he - ** This is the man whose appearance was like brass (40:3).
  * **hinged- ** a usually metal piece that attaches a door, gate or cover to something and allows it to open and close.
