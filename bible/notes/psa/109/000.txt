====== Psalms 109 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 109  is a psalm of vengeance against an enemy who has lied about him.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

This enemy lied about him. So he asked God to completely destroy his enemy and his enemy's family.
