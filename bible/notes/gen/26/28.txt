===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they said** - This refers to Abimelech, Ahuzzath, and Phicol. This does not mean they all spoke at the same time. It means one person spoke, but all them agreed with what was said. This could be translated as "One of them said."
  * **have clearly seen**  - "know" or "are certain"
  * **So let us make a covenant**  - "So we want to make a covenant"
  * **as we have treated you well**  - "as we have done only good to you" or "as we have done nothing but good to you."
  * **you are blessed by Yahweh**  - This can be translated with an active verb: "Yahweh has blessed you." (See: [[en:ta:translate:active]])
