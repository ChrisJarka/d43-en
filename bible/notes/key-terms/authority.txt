====== authority ======


===== Definition: =====


Authority is the power of influence and control over others. This term is commonly used in reference to governments or organizations, but it can also be used in reference to one person's power over another person.


    * Governments have power to control the behavior of their citizens by passing law.
    * Individuals often exercise "authority" through personal influence and accomplishments.
