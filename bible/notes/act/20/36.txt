===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **fell on Paul's neck**  - "began to hug him closely"
  * **kissed him **- An expression of brotherly or friendly love in the Middle East. A kiss is on the cheek.
  * **never see his face again**  - "never see Paul again on this earth." Face is used here to refer to Paul as a person. (see metonymy)
