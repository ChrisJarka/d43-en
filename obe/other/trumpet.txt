====== trumpet, horn ======

===== Definition: =====

An instrument for producing musical tones or calls to attention.

  * A trumpet was commonly made of either metal, sea shell or an animal horn.
  * Trumpets were most commonly blown to produce a signal for Israel's public assemblies, and for calls to muster for battle.
  * In Revelation, angels blow their horns to signal the beginning of the outpouring of the wrath of God on the earth.

(See also: other)
