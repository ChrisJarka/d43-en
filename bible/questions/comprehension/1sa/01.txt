====== 1 Samuel Chapter 01 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who were the two wives of Elkanah?

A. The wives of Elkanah were Hannah and Peninnah.[1:1-2]

Q? How many children did Hannah have?

A. Hannah had no children. [1:2]

Q? Who were the priests at the temple where Elkanah worshipped and sacrificed to Yahweh?

A.Eli, Hophni and Phinehas were all priests to Yahweh. [1:3]

Q? What were the portions of meat that Elkanah gave his wives?

A. To Peninnah he gave a regular portion, but to Hannah he always gave a double portion. [4-5]

Q? How did Peninnah treat Hannah?

A. Peninnah provoked Hannah severely to the point where Hannah would cry and not eat. [1:6-7]

Q? What did Hannah do because she was so deeply distress about not having children?

A. She went to the house of Yahweh and prayed and wept bitterly. [1:9-10]

Q? What does Hannah vow to Yahweh?

A. Hannah vows that if Yahweh would give her a son she would give him to Yahweh and no razor would touch his head. [1:11]

Q? Because Hannah was speaking to Yahweh from her heart what did Eli, the priest, think about her actions?

A. Eli sees Hannah's lips moving but doesn't hear her voice so he thinks she is drunk from wine. [1:12-14]

Q? What did Hannah assure Eli she was doing?

A. Hannah assured Eli that she was not drunk but was speaking to Yahweh from her heart concerning her closed womb and the provocation she was getting from Peninnah.[1:15-16]

Q? What did Eli say to Hannah that caused her to go on her way, eat and no longer feel sad?

A. Eli told her to go in peace and may the God of Israel grant her the request she was asking him. [1:17-18]

Q? When Hannah conceived and gave birth to a son what did she name him?

A. Hannah named her son Samuel because she asked for him from Yahweh. [1:20]

Q? Why did Hannah not go with her husband to the temple for the yearly sacrifice?

A. Because she was still nursing her son. [1:21-23]

Q? What did Hannah take with her along with her son to the house of Yahweh to give to Eli the priest?

A. Hannah took a sacrifice of a three year old bull, one ephah of meal, and a bottle of wine to give to the priest. [1:24-25]

Q? Hannah prayed for a child and Yahweh gave her a son so what did she give Yahweh?

A. Hannah is giving her son to Yahweh for as long as he lives. [1:26-28]
