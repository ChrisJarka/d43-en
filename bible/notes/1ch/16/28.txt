===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Ascribe to Yahweh** - "Give praise to Yahweh" or "Praise Yahweh"
  * **Ascribe to Yahweh glory and strength** - The abstract nouns "glory" and "strength" can be stated as adjectives. AT: "Praise Yahweh because he is glorious and strong." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **give to Yahweh the glory due to his name** - The abstract noun "glory" can be stated as a verb or adjective. AT: "glorify Yahweh just as his name deserves" or "proclaim that Yahweh is glorious just as his name deserves" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **due to his name** - Here "his name" refers to the person of God. AT: "due to him" or "he deserves" or "he is worthy to receive" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Bow down to Yahweh in his holy splendor** - The implied information is that the person is to wear clothes that show that he understands that Yahweh is holy. AT: "Bow down to Yahweh, and wear suitable clothes because he is holy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
