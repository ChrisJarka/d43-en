===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh: Because of what- "**The Lord Yahweh says, 'Because"
  * **of what Edom has done in executing vengeance against the house of Judah - **"Edom is guilty of taking revenge on the people of Judah,"
  * **has done **- "did." (See: Active/Passive)
  * **therefore, thus says the Lord Yahweh: **  - "Therefore, the Lord Yahweh says,"
  * **cut them off **- "seperate"
  * **I will strike my hand against Edom - **"I will punish Edom by my hand." (See:Figures of Speech)
  * **Teman to Dedan**  - Two regions at opposite ends of Edom.
  * **They will fall by the sword! - **"They will die by the sword."
