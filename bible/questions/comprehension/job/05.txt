====== Job Chapter 05 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What does Eliphaz say will happen to a foolish and silly man?

A. He says anger kills the foolish man and jealousy kills the silly one. He cursed the home of the foolish person. [5:2-3]

Q? Who is crushed at the gate because there is no one to rescue them and who will eat the harvest?

A. The children of the foolish person are crushed at the gate and the people who are hungry will eat the harvest. [5:4-5]

Q? Why don't difficulties and trouble come out of the ground?

A. He says mankind makes his own trouble. [5:6-7]

Q? Why does Eliphaz say he would turn to God?

A. He says that God does great, marvelous, and profound things. He gives rain on the earth. [5:8-10]

Q? What other reasons does Eliphaz say why he would turn to God?

A. He says God will raise to safety those that mourn, frustrates the schemes of the crafty, and traps the wise in their own craftiness. [5:11-13]

Q? From what does God save the needy and poor person?

A. He saves the poor person from the sword in their mouths so the poor person has hope, and saves the needy person from the hand of the mighty people. [5:15-16]
