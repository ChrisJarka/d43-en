====== 1 Kings Chapter 02 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did King David tell his son Solomon ?

A. David told his son to always fear and obey God. [2:3]

Q? What was Yahweh's promise to David?

A. Yahweh promised to place a son of David on Israel's throne. [2:4]

Q? What was King David's tell Solomon about Joab son of Zeruiah?

A. King David told Solomon to put him to death. [2:6]

Q? What did the sons of Barzillai do for King David?

A. The sons of Barzillai came to David when he fled from Absalom [2:7]

Q? What did Shimei do to King David?

A. Shimei violently cursed King David [2:8,]

Q? What did Kind David tell Solomon to do to Shimei?

A. David told Solomon to kill Shimei [2:9]
