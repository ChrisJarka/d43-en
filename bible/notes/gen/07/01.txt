===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Come …into the boat **- This could be translated as "Go…into the boat" or "Enter the boat."
  * **you **- The word "you" is singular. It refers to Noah (See: [[en:ta:vocabulary:formsofyou]])
  * **righteous before me** - This means righteous in God's sight.</font>
  * **in this generation**- This refers to all the people living at that time.
  * **clean animals** - These were animals that were acceptable for use in sacrifices and as food.
  * **unclean animals** - These animals were brought onto the boat only to preserve their kind. They were not considered suitable for food or for sacrifices.
  * **the face of the earth** - This means "the surface of the earth."
