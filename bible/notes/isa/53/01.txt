===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Who would have believed what we heard? **- "Only a few people will believe what we will tell them about God's servant." (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **And the arm of Yahweh, to whom was it revealed?**- "And Yahweh will reveal his power to them." (See: Synecdoche)
  * **For he grew up before Yahweh like a sapling, and like a sprout out of parched earth**  - God's servant is compared to a very young tree. AT "For his servant grew up in the sight of Yahweh, fragile like a very young tree, like a weak young plant that shoots up, a stem that is growing in dry ground." (See: Simile)
  * **he had no remarkable appearance or splendor; when we saw him, there was no beauty to attract us **- "There was nothing majestic about his appearance, no beauty that would draw our attention to him."  "we" and "us" who is this referring to?????????????
