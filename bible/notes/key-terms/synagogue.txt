====== synagogue ======


===== Definition: A building or place in a city devoted to Jewish religious gatherings; the term also refers to a group of Jewish people gathered for religious purposes. =====


A synagogue normally refers to a building where Jewish people worship.


    * The word synagogue, while used only once in the Old Testament, is used 56 times in the New Testament.
