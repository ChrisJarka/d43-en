===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **pour out my fury upon **- "Punish" or "unleash my punishment upon". (See:Metaphor)
  * **stronghold **- This was a place of great military strength.
  * **cut off the multitude of **- "separate the many people of"
  * **Sin**  - This is the name of a place in Egypt. (See: How to translate names)
  * **No**  - This is the name of a city in Egypt.
  * **grievous anguish **- "great pain" (See:doublet)
  * **broken up **- This implies that they will not be destroyed but seperated into small pieces.
  * **Memphis **- This was a powerful city in Egypt.
  * **have enemies **- Here, this implies that Memphis will lack peace because of all of her enemies.
