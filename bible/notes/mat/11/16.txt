===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues talking to the crowd. Here he starts a parable by comparing the people of that day to children sitting in the marketplace.

  * **It is like **- This phrase introduces a simile that continues through the end of verse 17. (See: Simile)
  * **marketplace **- public outdoor area, usually where food and other items are sold
  * **We played the flute for you**  - "We" refers to the children sitting in the marketplace; "you" refers to their playmates.
  * **flute **- a long, hollow musical instrument that is played by blowing air in over one end
