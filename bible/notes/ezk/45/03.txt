===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **So with this measure you will measure** - Alternate translation: "measure an area".
  * **twenty-five thousand long and ten thousand wide** - These measurements are in cubits  (See: Biblical distances).
  * **property**  - Alternate translation: "a piece of land".
