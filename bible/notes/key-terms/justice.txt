====== justice, justly ======


===== Definition: =====


Justice is the fair and reasonable treatment of all people under the laws of God and Country.


    * To act "justly" means to act in a way that is fair and reasonable as measured by law.
    * To receive justice means to be treated fairly under the law; either being protected by law or receiving due punishment for breaking the law.


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:judge|judge]])
