\v 9 Then the priest will take some from the grain offering to gratefully remember Yahweh's goodness, and he will burn it upon the altar, an offering made by fire, a sweet aroma to Yahweh.
\v 10 What is left of the grain offering will be Aaron’s and his sons’. This is something set apart for Yahweh from the offerings to Yahweh made by fire.
