===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I** – The word "I" here refers to Yahweh.
  * **remove the hedge** – A hedge is used to protect the vineyard. It can be translated as “ break down the border.”
  * **a pasture** – “a grassy place where animals are kept”
  * **I will lay it waste, and it will not be pruned nor hoed** – This can be translated as “The ground will be useless, and it will not be used to grow crops.” (See: [[en:ta:translate:active]])
