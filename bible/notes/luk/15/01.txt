===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **were coming to Jesus**  - That is, "were drawing near to Jesus," or "were gathering around near Jesus." 
    * **to listen to him **- That is, "in order to hear him teach, or "to listen to what he will say."
    * **grumble to each other**  - That is, "complain to each other," or "began to complain to each other."
    * **this man welcomes sinners**  - This can also be translated as, "this man admits sinners into his presence" or, "this man accepts sinners."
    * **even eats with them**- Eating indicates spending time with them. Jesus accepted invitations from such sinners, who were considered non religious by the Pharisees and the scribes.
