===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **and I hope to come to you soon** - "although I hope to come to you very soon"
  * **But if I delay** - "but in case I cannot go there soon" or "but if something prevents me for being there soon"
  * **the household of God...must be guided** - Paul speaks of the group of believers as a family. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **pillar and support of the truth** - Paul speaks of the believers bearing witness to the truth about Christ as they were together a a platform and pillar supporting a building. The platform is expressed as a synecdoche of its parts, the base, and the upright. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]], [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
