===== Translation Notes: =====

    * ** my word** - By using the phrase "my word," Jesus means the whole message that he has shared with the people. (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])

    * **you are ... you heard** - In vv 37-38, every use of the word "you" refers to the Jewish leaders who do not believe in Jesus. (See: [[en:ta:translate:formsofyou-plural]])
