===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Abraham complained to Abimelech concerning a well of water that Abimelech’s servants had seized from him.** - This can be translated as "Abraham told Abimelech that Abimelech's servants had seized one of his wells."
  * **seized from him** - "taken from Abraham" or "had taken control of" (UDB)
  * **I have not heard of it until today** - This can be translated as "and I did not hear about it until today" or "This is the first time I have heard about it."
