====== right hand (of God) ======

===== Definition: =====

The right hand is an idiom that refers to the place of honor next to a ruler or to a host of a banquet. It means "on his right side" or "beside him to the right".

  * The Bible describes Jesus as right now sitting at the right hand of God as he awaits the appointed time for his return.
  * When the angel came to give a message to the priest Zechariah, he stood to the right of the altar where Zechariah was burning incense.
