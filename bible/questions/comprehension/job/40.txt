====== Job Chapter 40 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Yahweh continue to speak to Job and ask him?

A. Yahweh asked Job if anyone should criticize or correct him and to let that person answer God. [40:1 - 2]

Q? How did Job answer Yahweh?

A. Job answered Yahweh by saying he was insignificant and put his hand over his mouth that he had spoken once then twice but would not proceed. [40:3 - 5]

Q? How did Yahweh answer Job?

A. Yahweh answered Job from out of a fierce storm telling him to be a man and that Job would answer the questions Yahweh asked. [40:6 - 7]

Q? What next did Yahweh ask Job?

A. Yahweh asked Job if he actually would say Yahweh was unjust and condemn Yahweh. Yahweh asked him if he had an arm like God's or had a voice like him. [40:8 - 9]

Q? What does Yahweh continue to ask Job?

A. Yahweh asks Job to cloth himself with glory, dignity,  arraying himself in honor and majesty. To scatter his anger around. Yahweh asks Job to look at those who are proud and bring them down. [40:10 - 11]
