==== Create or Login Choices ====

The welcome screen will be displayed with a request to create an user account.

**NOTE:** You may use a pseudonym instead of your real name. A pseudonym is a name that cannot be traced back to you.

==== Terms to Know ====

**Offline Account** – user has full use of the program except for uploading to Door43.

**Door43 Account** – user has full use of the program and can upload to Door43 (requires Internet connection.)


=== Login with Door43 Account  ===

  - On the opening screen, tap on the 'Login with Door43 Account' link. The login screen opens.
  - Type in your Door43 account information.
  - Tap the blue 'Continue' button.


=== Create a New Door43 Account  ===

  - On the opening screen, tap on the 'Create a Door43 Account' link to create a new Door43 user account.  The Door43 Account Creation window opens. (This requires an Internet connection.)
  - Tap on the gray 'Full Name or Pseudonym' words and type in the name, or pseudonym, of the translator into the field.
  - Tap on the 'Email Address' line to enter the email address of the translator.
  - Enter a password in both fields. Tap on the 'Show Passwords' words at the bottom of the screen to display the passwords.
  - Confirm the information.
  - Tap on the blue 'Continue' button to begin registration.


=== Local Login or Offline Account ===

  - On the opening screen, tap on the 'Create offline Account' link. The login screen opens.
  - Tap on the gray 'Full Name or Pseudonym' words and type in the name, or pseudonym, of the translator into the field.
  - Tap on the blue 'Continue' button to continue.

**Note:** Tap on the 'Create offline Account' if you do not want to create a new Door43 account at this time or are not able to connect to the Internet.

=== Note: This screen can be accessed (to change login or translator settings) by following these steps: ===

  * From the 'Your Translation Projects' screen:
    * Tap the 3-dot menu
    * Choose Log out

OR

  * Tap on the user name found in the very top dark green bar.
