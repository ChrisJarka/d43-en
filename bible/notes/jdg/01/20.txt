===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **three sons** - The leaders of the people groups are used to refer to the entire group. (See: [[en/bible/team-info/training/quick-reference/figures/synecdoche]])
    * **Anak** - Anak was a very tall man whose descendants were also very tall. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **to this day** - "until now" The day this was originally written.
