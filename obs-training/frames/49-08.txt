====== God Hates Sin [49-08] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-49-08.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

**[[:en:obe:kt:jesus|Jesus]]** also told us that **[[:en:obe:kt:god|God]]** hates **[[:en:obe:kt:sin|sin]]**. When **[[:en:obe:other:adam|Adam]]** and **[[:en:obe:other:eve|Eve]]** **[[:en:obe:kt:sin|sinned]]**, it affected all of their **[[:en:obe:other:descendant|descendants]]**. As a result, every person in the world sins and **is separated from God**. Therefore, everyone has become an enemy of God.


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **is separated from God** - This can also be translated as, “cannot live with God” or, “cannot be near God” or, “cannot have a relationship with God.”
