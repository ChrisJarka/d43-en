===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **incited the people**  - "stirred the people up to be angry"
  * **seized**  - "grabbed"
  * **council**  - This was the most important Jewish Council. It was called the Sanhedrin.
  * **fixed their eyes on him**  - "looked intently at him." This is an idiom for "staring at him." (see: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
  * **was like the face of an angel**  - This is a simile like comparison with the point of similarity likely being "shining" which is not mentioned. AT: "was shining like the face of an angel" (UDB). (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile|Simile]])
