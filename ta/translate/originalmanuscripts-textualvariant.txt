====== Textual Variant ======

A textual variant happens when a translator makes changes to the original text

===== Translation Suggestions =====

===== Examples from the Bible =====

[[en:bible:notes:act:08:36|Acts 8:37]] Some ancient authorities add, in whole or in part, v. 37. Philip said, "If you believe with all your heart, you may be baptized." The Ethiopian answered, "I believe that Jesus Christ is the Son of God."
