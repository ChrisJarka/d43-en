===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Jesus said to them **- Jesus is teaching the twelve disciples.
  * **Truly, I say to you**  - Jesus used this expression to stress the importance of what he is about to say.
  * **in the new**** birth**  - This could refer to when our bodies are made new, and we are living in heaven with Jesus.
  * **when the Son of Man sits **- could be translated "when I inherit my authority and glory in heaven."
  * **you also will sit on twelve thrones, judging**
- He is promising that his disciples will inherit authority to rule over the twelve tribes of Israel"
