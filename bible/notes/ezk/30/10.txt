===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh** - See how you translated this in 5:5.
  * **the multitudes of Egypt** - "the many people of Egypt".
  * **by the hand of Nebuchadnezzar** - Nebuchadnezzar and Babylon will be the ones who bring this punishment about.
  * **terror of nations** - "extremely ruthless soldiers".
  * **fill the land with the slain** - "there will be a great many people who are killed" (See: hyperbole).
