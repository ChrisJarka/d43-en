====== Jeremiah Chapter 09 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What does the prophet wish?

A. He wishes to weep day and night for those among the daughter of my people who have been killed. [9:1]

Q? Why does he want someone to give him a place for travelers in the wilderness to stay, where he could abandon his people?

A. He wants to leave them since all of them are adulterers, a band of traitors. [9:2]

Q? What do the sinful people say?

A. They say false things with their tongues, which are their deceitful bows, but they are not great in faithfulness on the earth. [9:3]

Q? Why should each of them guard against their neighbors and brothers?

A. They should guard against them for every brother is a deceiver and every neighbor walks in slander. [9:4]

Q? Why are they exhausted?

A. They are exhausted from committing iniquity. [9:5]

Q? What is Yahweh's declaration?

A. It is Yahweh's declaration that they live in the midst of deception; in their deceit they refuse to acknowledge him. [9:6]

Q? What does Yahweh of hosts say?

A. He says that he is about to test them, and he will examine them. [9:7]

Q? How do they plan evil for their neighbors even though they proclaim peace with their mouths?

A. With their mouths they proclaim peace with their neighbors, but with their hearts they lie in wait for them. [9:8]

Q? What questions does Yahweh declare about this?

A. Should I not punish them because of these things; should I not avenge myself on a nation that is like this? [9:9]

Q? What will the prophet sing?

A. He will sing a song of mourning and wailing for the mountains, and a funeral song will be sung for the meadows. [9:10]

Q? What will Yahweh make of Jerusalem and Judah?

A. He will turn Jerusalem into piles of ruins, a hideout for jackals, and he will make Judah's cities ruined places without inhabitants. [9:11]

Q? How does the prophet describe the land after it has perished?

A. it is destroyed like the wilderness, where there is no one to pass through it. [9:12]

Q? Why does Yahweh say this has happened?

A. It is because they have abandoned his law that he set before them, because they do not listen to his voice or walk by it. [9:13]

Q? What does Yahweh say he will do?

A. He will scatter them among the nations that they have not knownâ€“neither they nor their ancestors; he will send out a sword after them until he has completely destroyed them. [9:16]

Q? What should the women who are skilled at lamenting do?

A. Let them hurry and sing a mournful song over us, so their eyes may run with tears and their eyelids flow with water. [9:17-18]

Q? How do the wailing people in Zion feel when they are devastated?

A. They are greatly ashamed, for for they have abandoned the land since their houses were torn down. [9:19]

Q? What should the women teach their daughters?

A. They should teach their daughters a mourning song, and each neighbor woman a funeral song. [9:20]

Q? What has come and what does it do?

A. Death has come through their windows; it goes into their palaces and destroys children from outside, and young men in the city squares. [9:21]

Q? What does Yahweh tell the prophet to declare?

A. The corpses of men will fall like dung in the fields, and like grain stalks after the reapers, and there will be no one to gather them. [9:22]

Q? What does Yahweh say?

A. Do not let the wealthy man take pride in his riches, for if a man takes pride in anything, let it be in this, that he has insight and knows Yahweh. [9:23]

Q? With what does Yahweh declare he acts?

A. Yahweh acts with covenant loyalty, justice, and righteousness for it is in these that he takes pleasure. [9:24]

Q? What days are coming?

A. Days are coming when he will punish all the circumcised who are such only in their body. [9:25]

Q? Why will Yahweh punish Egypt and Judah, Edom, the people of Ammon, Moab, and all the people who cut the hair on their heads?

A. He will punish them for all these nations are uncircumcised. [9:26]

Q? Why will he punish the house of Israel?

A. All the house of Israel has an uncircumcised heart. [9:26]
