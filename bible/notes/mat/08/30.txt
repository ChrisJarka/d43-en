===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of Jesus' healing two men with demons.

  * **herd of many pigs**  - pigs were despised by the Jews because in their dietary laws pigs were on the list of unclean animals. These pigs were owned by the Gadarenes.
  * **If you cast us out**  - the "us" in this sentence is plural, exclusive.
  * **Jesus said to them **- Jesus is speaking to the demons inside the man.
  * **The demons came out and went into the pigs**  - in this case, the demons left the man and entered the animals.
  * **rushed down the steep slope**  - could be translated "ran quickly down the steep hill"
  * **perished in the water**  - "drowned"
