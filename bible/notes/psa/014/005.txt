===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They tremble with dread**- "They" refers to those who commit iniquity. 
  * **tremble with dread** - "shake with fear"
  * **righteous assembly** - "those who love God"
  * **You want to humiliate the poor person** - "You" refers to the wicked. 
  * **humiliate** - "shame"
