====== wonders ======

===== Definition: =====

Wonders are miraculous acts performed by God's power.

  * Some wonders were God's acts of redemption while others are God's acts of judgment.
  * Some of God's wonders were the physical healing of sick or disabled people.
  * They are special displays of God's power; often being used in the phrase "signs and wonders."

(See also: other)
