===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Revive me** - "Give me strength" or "Give me energy"
  * **with raisin cakes** - "by feeding me raisin cakes" or "by giving me raisin cakes"
  * **raisin cakes** - many dried grapes that people have pressed together into flat patties 
  * **refresh me with apricots** - "support me by giving me apricots" or "help my by giving me apricots
  * **** -


  * **for I am weak with love** - She is weak from making love. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **embraces** - "holds"
