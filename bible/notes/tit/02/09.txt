===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **their masters** - "their own masters"
  * **in everything**  - "in every situation" or "always"
  * **please them** - This could be translated as "make their master happy" or "satisfy their master."
  * **show good faith** - This could be translated as "be faithful to their master" or "demonstrate they are worthy of their master's trust."
  * **decorate our teaching**  - This could be translated as "demonstrate the truth we teach in a way that attracts others to our message." The word here for "decorate" means to "set in order, or arrange" or "to put in an orderly fashion."
  * **God our Savior**  - "our God who saves us"
  * **in every way** - "in everything they do."
