===== Translation Notes: =====

All of these verses give pictures of Yahweh's great power.

  * **is heard over the waters** - "is heard above the waters"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **over the waters**  - "louder than the waters"
  * **over many waters**  - "over the large bodies of water"
