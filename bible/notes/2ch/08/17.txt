===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they went to Ophir went servants of Solomon - **  they went to Ophir with servants of Solomon. (See discussion comment)
  * **talent**  - (See: [[:en:bible:notes:2ch:08:biblical-money|biblical money]])
  * **talent - **  a round disk or loaf to measure money, gold and silver.  The value changed in different places and times. In ancient Israel, a talent of gold weighed 200 pounds (91 kg) and a talent of silver weighed 100 pounds (45 kg). A talent of gold was double the weight of a talent of silver.
