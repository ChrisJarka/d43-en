===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **divide its price** - "divide the money" or "divide the money they receive"
  * **if it was known**  - "if the owner knew"
  * **a habit of goring in time past** - AT: "had gored before"
  * **pay ox for ox** - The owner of the ox that killed must give a new, live ox to the owner of the dead ox.  
  * **become his own** - "His" here refers to the owner of the ox that killed.  AT: "will belong to the owner of the ox that killed."
