====== the word of Yahweh came ... saying ======

===== Description =====

"The word of Yahweh came to … saying" is an idiom that is used to introduce a special message from God.

The notes below are templates for developing notes for specific books. For those books it would be good to write notes with cross-references to earlier verses in those books.

===== Standard Notes =====

**The word of Yahweh came to … saying** - This idiom is used to introduce a special message from God. AT: "Yahweh gave a message to …. He said" or "Yawheh spoke this message to …" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
