===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Do I greatly rejoice in the death of the wicked, . . . not in his turning away from his way so he will live?**  - The purpose of this Rhetorical Question is to emphasize a point and could alternately read "I do not greatly rejoice in the death of the wicked, but I do rejoice in his turning away from his way so he will live." (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **the Lord Yahweh’s declaration**  - Synonyms for declaration include "announcement" or "proclamation". An alternate translation could read "the Lord Yahweh strongly stated."
  * **not in his turning away from his way​**  - "Turning away from" means to stop doing something. "His way" refers to his sinful actions. An alternate translation could read, "not in his stopping his sinful actions."
  * **he/his**  - refers to "the wicked."
