    * **That the Gentiles, who were not pursuing righteousness, attained righteousness, the righteousness by faith.**  - "The Gentiles were not pursuing righteousness, but they attained righteousness by faith in Jesus Christ."
    * **But Israel, who did pursue a law of righteousness, did not arrive at it.**  - But Israel, who did pursue a law of righteousness, did not attain righteousness by keeping the law. (See: [[en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit|Implicit]])
