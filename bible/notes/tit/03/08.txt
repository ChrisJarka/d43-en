===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **speak confidently about **- This can also be translated as, "strongly affirm" or "confidently assert"
  * **set their minds on **- That is, "think with care" or "be thoughtful about" or "care for" or "give attention to"
  * **that he put before them - **  This can also be translated as, "that he offers them" or "that he wants them to be involved with."
  * **trust - ** That is, "having believed" or "believed"
