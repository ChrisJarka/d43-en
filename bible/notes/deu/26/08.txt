===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This continues what the Israelite must say when he brings his first crops to Yahweh. 

  * **Yahweh brought us** - Here "us" refers to the people of Israel that were living in Egypt. The speaker includes himself as one of the people whether he lived in Egypt or not. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
  * **with a mighty hand, with a display of his power** - The metonym "mighty hand" refers to Yahweh's power. These phrases mean basically the same thing and emphasize the greatness of Yahweh's power. AT: "with his mighty power." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **with great fearsomeness** - "with acts that terrified the people who saw them"
  * **a land that flows with milk and honey** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:deu:06:03|6:3]].
