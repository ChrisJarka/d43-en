===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Because I sinned against Yahweh, I will bear his rage**  - AT: "It's against Yahweh that I have sinned, so now I must bear my punishment. 
    * **until he pleads my cause, and executes judgment for me. He will bring me to the light,
and I will see him rescue me in his justice.**  - AT:All is not lost, for he will plead my cause and execute judgment on my behalf. I will again stand in the light,  by the justice that he will provide.
