===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the man, him **- These refer to Jesus found in verse four.
  * **crown of thorns**  - made with a plant that has many thorns along its stem
  * **purple robe**  - Typically worn by a person of royalty.
  * **he was wearing the crown of thorns and the purple garment**  - They mocked Jesus' claim to royalty.
