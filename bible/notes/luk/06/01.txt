===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **grain fields**- large sections of land where, in this case, wheat seed had been scattered in order to grow more wheat.
    * **heads of grain**- this is the topmost part of the wheat plant, which is a kind of large grass, which holds the mature grain or seeds of the plant.
    * **something that is not lawful to do on the Sabbath**  - Jesus' disciples were being accused of harvesting and threshing, work which was prohibited on the Day of Rest under Mosaic law.
