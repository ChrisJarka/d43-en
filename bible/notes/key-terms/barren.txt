====== barren ======

===== Definition: =====

To be "barren" means to not be fertile, to not be fruitful.

  * <font 14px line-height: 17.8181819915772px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Soil or land that is barren is not able to produce any plants.</font>
  * <font 14px line-height: 17.8181819915772px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>A woman who is barren is physically unable to conceive a child unless by Gods intervention.</font>
  * <font 14px line-height: 17.8181819915772px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Depending on the context, this could also be translated as, "unfruitful" or "childless" or "not fertile."</font>

(See also: other) Greek:steiros; from root ster.Eng., (Sterile) is primarily used in connection with the natural significance of not producing children, Not producing fruit.
