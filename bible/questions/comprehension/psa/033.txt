====== Psalms Chapter 033 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What does the writer say to do?

A. The writer says to rejoice in Yahweh. [33:1]

Q? What does the writer say to do?

A. The writer says to give thanks to Yahweh and sing praises to him. [33:2]

Q? What does the writer say to do?

A. The writer says to sing Yahweh a new song, play skillfully and sing with joy. [33:3]
