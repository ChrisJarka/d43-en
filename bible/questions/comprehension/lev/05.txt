====== Leviticus Chapter 05 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What are three sins that make one guilty?

A. If anyone witnesses something about which he is required to testify, or touches anything God has designated as unclean, or if anyone swears rashly with his lips, then he will be guilty. [5:1-4]

Q? What two things must the one who is guilty of a sin do?

A. The one who is guilty of a sin must confess whatever sin he has commited and bring his guilt offering to Yahweh. [5:5,6]

Q? What is the animal that should be brought to Yahweh for a guilt offering?

A. A female goat or sheep should be brought to Yahweh for a guilt offering. [5:6]

Q? If he cannot afford to buy a lamb, what can he bring to Yahweh for a sin offering?

A. If he cannot afford a lamb, he can bring to Yahweh for a sin offering two doves or two young pigeons. [5:7]

Q? If he cannot afford two doves or two young pigeons, what can he bring for his sin offering?

A. If he cannot afford two doves or two young pigeons, he can bring a tenth of an ephah of fine flour without any oil or incense on it. [5:11]

Q? What offering must be brought if anyone violates a command and sins by failing to pay anything that is required by Yahweh?

A. This offering must be a ram without blemish from the flock valued in silver shekels. [5:15]
