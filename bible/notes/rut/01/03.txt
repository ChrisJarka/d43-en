===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **she was left with her two sons**  - that is, " Naomi had only her two sons with her" (UDB).
  * **took wives**  - married
  * **of the women of Moab**  - Naomi's sons married women that were from the tribe of Moab. These were foreigners and worshipped other gods.
  * **of the one**  - of one of the women
  * **of the other**  - of the other woman
  * **ten years**  - After Elimelek died, Naomi's sons took wives, and they lived in Moab for ten years. After ten had passed, Mahalon and Chilon also died.
  * **leaving Naomi**  - Meaning, Naomi was widowed and left behind alone in a foreign land.
