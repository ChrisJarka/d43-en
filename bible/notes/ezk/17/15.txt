===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **to acquire horses and a multitude of people **- The king of Jerusalem wants to make a military alliance with the Egyptians.
  * **Shall he prosper? - ** The writer is emphasizing the point that the king will fail in his effort. Alternate phrasing could be: "He will not prosper!" (See: Rhetorical Question)
