===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Kiriath Jearim...Abinadab...Eleazar**  - explanation
  * **<del>They consecrated his son Eleazar to keep the ark of Yahweh.</del>**  -  <del>consecrate his son Eleazar - Eleazar was not of the priestly tribe but was dedicated by the people of the town to take care of the ark and ensure that no one repeated the sin of opening the cover and looking into it.</del>
  * **All the house of Israel lamented and wished to turn to Yahweh**  - "All Israel was sorry for disobeying Yahweh and wanted to do the things he asked of them". <del>The nation of Israel begins to feel genuine sorrow for their sin of idolatry and neglect of Yahweh and they now want to do the things He desires.</del>
