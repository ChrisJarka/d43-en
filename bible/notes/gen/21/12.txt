===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **"Do not be grieved because of the lad**  - "Do not be upset about the lad, Ishmael"
  * **and because of your handmaid.**  - "and about your handmaid, Hagar."
  * **Do all that Sarah says to you. Listen to her **- "Regarding all the Sarah says to you, obey her."
  * **your descendants**  - "your seed."
  * **the son of the handmaid**  - refers to Ishmael.
  * **your descendant**  - "your seed."
