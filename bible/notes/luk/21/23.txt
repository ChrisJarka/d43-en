===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **there will be great distress upon the land**  - Possible meanings are 1) the people of the land will be distressed and 2) there will be physical disasters in the land.
  * **wrath to this people**  - "there will be wrath to his people". This can be translated as "this people will experience wrath" or "this will will be punished."  Possible meanings are 1) Israel's enemies will be angry at the people of Israel and 2) God will be angry at the people Israel.
  * **they will fall by the edge of the sword**  - "they will be killed by the edge of the sword." This refers to being killed by enemy soldiers. (See: [[:en:ta:figuresofspeech:metonymy]])
  * **they will be led captive into all the nations** - This can be translated with an active clause: "Their enemies will capture them and take them to other countries." (See: [[en:ta:vocabulary:active]])
  * **Jerusalem will be trampled by the Gentiles**  - Possible meanings are 1) the Gentiles will destroy the city of Jerusalem, 2) the Gentiles will destroy the people of Jerusalem, and 3) the Gentiles will conquer Jerusalem and occupy it.
