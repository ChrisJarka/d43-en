====== The Two Disciples [42-01] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-42-01.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

On the day that **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]** was **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:raise|raised]]** from the dead, two of his **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:disciple|disciples]]** were going to a nearby town. As they walked, they talked about what had happened to Jesus. They had hoped that he was the **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:messiah|Messiah]]**, but then he was killed. Now the women said he was **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:life|alive]]** again. They did not know what to **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:believe|believe]]**.
