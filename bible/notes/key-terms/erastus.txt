====== Erastus ======

===== Facts: =====

Erastus was a first century Christian, who helped Paul in his missionary work.
    * He was some sort of public official in the city of Corinth.
    * He went on at least one missionary journey into Macedonia, with Timothy.
    * Erastus was in Corinth when Paul wrote Romans and 2 Timothy.
