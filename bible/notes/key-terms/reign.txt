====== reign ======

===== Definition =====

To reign means to rule as king over a certain country and its people. The reign of a king is the time period that he is ruling.

  * Various kings have taken turns ruling the kingdoms of this world.
  * Jesus Christ will reign over the whole world when He returns. The Bible says that Christians will reign with Him.
