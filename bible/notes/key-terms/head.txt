====== head ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Bible the word head is metaphorically used to describe an authority figure.

  * Jesus is called the head of the church; just as our own head directs our body so Jesus directs His church.
  * A husband is called the head of his wife. In a Biblically mature household the husband is given the responsibility of providing direction to his wife and family.

(See also: other)
