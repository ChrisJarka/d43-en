===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Behold**  - This can be translated as “Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you.”
  * **Behold, the rulers of Israel, each one by his own power, have come to you to pour out blood!**  - "Look, each king has come on their own to kill you".
  * **You have despised**  - The people of Jerusalem have despised.
  * **in order to pour out blood**  - for the purpose of killing.
  * **they eat upon the mountains**  - This means the slanderous men eat meat sacrificed to idols in the mountains.
  * **They perform wickedness in your midst**  - This means that they do evil things all around you.
