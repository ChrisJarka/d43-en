===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **those who are set on fire,**  - "fiery beasts" or "beasts who breathe fire"
  * **whose teeth are spears and arrows** - AT: "whose words cut like spears" (See:[[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **spears and arrows** - These two words are very similar and if only one is known in your culture it is acceptable to list just one. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * whose tongues are sharp swords.**  - AT: "who injure people by what they say" (See:[[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
