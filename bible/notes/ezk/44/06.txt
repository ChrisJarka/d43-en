===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **...profane it—my house!**- Alternate translation: to disrespect my house; here, "my sanctuary" and "my house" refer to the same thing.  The repetition shows the anger of God. (see: parallelism)
  * **violated the covenant**- This means they did not follow the rules of the covenant
