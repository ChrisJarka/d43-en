===== Translation Notes: =====

See [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames]].

  * **The sons of Dedan were Asshurim, Letushim, and Leummim** - The word "sons" here refers to descendants. The Asshurim, Letushim, and Leummim were nations.
  * **All these** - This refers to the people named in verses 2 through 4.
