===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now** - This word is used here to mark a change from the story to information about Ephron.
  * **sons of Heth** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:gen:23:03|23:3]]
  * **in the hearing**  - "in the presence" or "in front"
  * **of all those who enter the gate of his city**  - "all those who had gathered at the gate of his city." The city gate was where matters were settled and requests were heard. Those who gathered there were the leaders of the city who made the important decisions.
  * **his** - Here the word "his" refers to Ephron.
  * **I give it to you in the presence of the sons of my people**  - "with my community as a witness, I give it to you"
  * **give**  - This type of giving could have been a land grant from a greater lord to a lesser one (Abraham). Such a gift may have put Abraham in debt to Ephron.
  * **in the presence of**  - "in the sight of" or "in front of"
  * **I give it to you to bury your dead**  - "I give it to you. Bury your dead."
