====== member ======

===== Definition: =====

A New Testament concept describing the parts of the individual believer's body relating to the Body of Christ, that is, all believers as a group.

  * Jesus Christ is the Head of the Body and individual believers function as the parts of the Body as the Holy Spirit provides a gift or gifts to each believer.
  * The Head of the Body and its members, the believers, constitute the church.
