====== 1 Samuel 08 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

**Saul and Samuel**

Chapters 8-16 begins a new part of the story. The people want a king and God chooses Saul to be their king. (Chapters 8-10) Then 11-16 is about Saul's reign.  


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

**The people want a king**


When Samuel was old, he appointed his sons to be judges. But they were corrupt and took bribes. So the people came and asked Samuel to appoint a king for them. Samuel asked God and  he said to give them a king but warn them what a king will be like. So Samuel warned them that a king would oppress them and demand much from them. But they still wanted a king.


=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Idiom ==

When the people tell why they do not want Samuel's sons as judges  they use an [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]: "did not walk in his ways" meaning did not do what he would have done.
