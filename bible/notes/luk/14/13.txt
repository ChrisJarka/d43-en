===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (Jesus continues speaking to the Pharisee who had invited him to his home.)
    * **blessed**  - God will make you happy.
    * **bold words**  - explanation
    * **they cannot repay you**  - That is, "they are unable to pay you back." It could also be translated as, "they lack the money to have a similar party for you."
    * **in the resurrection of the just**  - That is, "when the righteous people are resurrected from the dead."
