====== Psalms 015 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 15 is a wisdom psalm telling what people should be like to live with God. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

This psalm tells who is acceptable to God. There are several good actions mentioned. He treats his neighbor right, not slandering, not taking a bribe or interest from him and keeps his promises to him even when it turns out to be to his hurt.
