====== 2 Chronicles Chapter 20 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? For what reason did the people of Moab Ammon and Meunites come against Jehoshaphat?

A. They cameagainst Jehoshaphat to do battle. [20:1]

Q? Where did the people of Judah come from to seek Yahweh?

A. They came to seek Yahweh from all the cities of Judah. [20:4]

Q? Where did Jehoshaphat stand in the assembly of the people?

A. He stood at the house of Yahweh, in front of the new courtyard. [20:5]

Q? What promise of Yahweh did Jehoshaphat repeat when the people cried to him in their affliction?

A. He said that when the peoplecry to Yahweh in their affliction, and he would hear them and save them. [20:9]
