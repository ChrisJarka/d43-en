===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now to him that is able to keep you from stumbling** - "Now to God who is able to keep the beloved people from stumbling" - God will help you maintain a firm understanding of and commitment to him. 
  * **and to place you before the presence of his glory** - God will allow you to enjoy and worship his glory.
  * **without blemish in exceeding joy** - without sin and in great joy.  (A reference to justification.) 
  * **through Jesus Christ our Lord** - we are able to see God because Jesus Christ has forgiven us, and the Lord sees Jesus' purity, not our sin. 
  * **be glory, majesty, dominion and power, before all time, and now, and for evermore. Amen** - God always had, has, and always will have these characteristics of glory, absolute leadership and complete control of all things.
