===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The waters closed over me, to take my life**  - The waters covered me to drown me.
  * **I went down to the bases of the mountains**
 - "I sank down to where the mountains start rising from the bottom of the sea" (UDB)
  * **The earth with its bars closed upon me for ever**
 - I thought I would be forever in a prison inside the earth.
  * **Yet you brought up my life from the pit**
 - But, you saved my life from the place of the dead.
