===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Son of man**  - Ezekiel (me)
  * **prophesying out of their own minds**  - prophesying from their own thoughts, not inspired
  * **prophets who follow their own spirit, but who have seen nothing**  - prophesying from their own thoughts, not inspired
  * **jackals**- a wild dog found in Africa and Asia
  * **like jackals in the wastelands**  - like wild animals preying on others in the desert.
