====== Tyre ======


===== Facts: =====


Tyre is an ancient Phoenician port city on the Mediterranean Sea 40 kilometers south of Sidon.


    * Tyre still exists today in the country of Lebanon.
    * At one time in Jesus' ministry, some people from Tyre came to hear Jesus speak.
