======  Matthew 17 General Notes ======

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Elijah ==
Malachi [[:en:obe:kt:prophet|prophesied]] that one day Elijah would return before the [[:en:obe:kt:christ|Messiah]] came. This great prophet of Israel never died. Jesus explains that John the Baptist fulfilled this prophecy.

== "He was transfigured" ==
The glory of God is seen as a great light in Scripture. The always produces [[:en:obe:kt:fear|fear]] in the person who sees it. During this event, called the "transfiguration," these two disciples were able to see Jesus is all of his [[:en:obe:kt:glory|glory]].
