====== head ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Bible the word head is used in a figurative way to refer to someone who has authority over others.

  * <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Jesus is called the head of the church; Just as a person's head guides and directs the members of its body what to do, so Jesus guides and directs the members of his body, the Church.</font>
  * A husband is called the head of his wife. He is given the responsibility of providing direction to his wife and family.
