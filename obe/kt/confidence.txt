====== confidence ======

===== Definition: =====

Confidence is the feeling of trust that comes from knowing that something is certain.

  * Believers have confidence in God's promises, knowing that he never lies and He always fulfills his word.
  * Confidence that is based on trusting God will remain even though life's circumstances may become extremely difficult.
