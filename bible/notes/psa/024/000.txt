====== Psalms 024 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 24 is a psalm of praise to God as the almighty God. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Only the righteous can come to worship God.

Please note the use of rhetorical questions. Each question in verse 3, 8, and 10 form a unit with a response. See [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]].
