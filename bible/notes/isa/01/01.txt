===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The vision of Isaiah...that he saw** - "This is the vision of Isaiah...that Yahweh showed him" or "This is what God showed Isaiah"
  * **Amoz** - Amoz was the father of Isaiah. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Judah and Jerusalem** - "Judah" refers to the southern kingdom of Israel. "Jerusalem" was its most important city. AT: "those living in Judah and Jerusalem" or "the people of Judah and Jerusalem." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **in the days of Uzziah, Jotham, Ahaz, and Hezekiah, kings of Judah.** - AT: "when Uzziah, Jotham, Ahaz and Hezekiah were kings of Judah."
