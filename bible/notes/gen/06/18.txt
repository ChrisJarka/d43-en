===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **with you**  - The word "you" is singular. God was making his covenant with Noah. (See: [[:en:ta:vocabulary:formsofyou]])
  * **but I will establish my covenant with you** - God commanded Noah to take his family and specific numbers of all the animals onto the boat, and God promised to protect everyone in the boat.
