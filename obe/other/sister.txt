====== sister ======

===== Definition: =====

A sister is a female person who shares at least one biological parent with another person. It is also used figuratively.

  * <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>In the New Testament, "sister" is sometimes used figuratively to refer to a woman who is a fellow believer in Jesus Christ.</font>
  * Usually the New Testament writers used the term "brothers" to refer to all believers, both men and women. When they used "sister" they were referring to a specific woman who was a fellow believer
  * .Sometimes they used the phrase, <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>"brothers and sisters" to emphasize that they were talking about all believers in Christ.</font>

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * It is best to translate this term with the literal word that is used in the target language to refer to a natural or biological sister, unless this would give wrong meaning.
  * Other ways to translate this could include, "sister in Christ" or "spiritual sister."
