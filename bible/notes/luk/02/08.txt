===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **in that same region**  - That is, "in that area" or, "near Bethlehem."
    * **keeping watch over**  - That is, "taking care of" or, "guarding in order to keep them safe."
    * **flocks of sheep**  - That is, "groups of sheep."
    * **at night**  - That is, after the sun had set and it was dark.
    * **an angel of the Lord**  - That is, "an angel from the Lord" or, "an angel who serves the Lord" or, "an angel who was sent by the Lord."
    * **glory **  - Could be translated as "prestige" or, "honor" or, "greatness."
    * **the glory of the Lord was shining all around them**  - This could also be translated as "the Lord's glory was radiating like a bright light all around them" or, "they saw the Lord's greatness as a bright light all around them." 
    * **appeared**  - That is, "came."
    * **they were very afraid**  - That is, "they became very afraid." The shepherds reacted with great fear to the sudden appearance of an angel of the Lord.
