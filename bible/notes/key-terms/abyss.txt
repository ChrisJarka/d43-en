====== abyss ======


===== Definition: =====


An abyss is a large hole or chasm without a bottom.


    * In the Bible the abyss is referred to as a place where fallen angels are punished.
    * The word "abyss" could also be translated as "bottomless pit" or "deep chasm."


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:hell|hell]], [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:hades|hades]])
