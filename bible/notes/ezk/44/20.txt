===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **shave**  - to cut the hair, wool, etc., off (someone or something) very close to the skin
  * **hair **  - a thin threadlike growth from the skin of a person or animal
  * **trim their heads**  - make the hair on their head neat by cutting it
  * **scissors**  - a tool used for cutting paper, cloth, etc., that has two blades joined together in the middle so that the sharp edges slide against each other
  * **inner court**  - (see: Temple)
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>** widow  ** -</font>    a woman whose husband has died
  * **outcast**  - someone who is not accepted by other people
  * **wives**  - several women who are married
  * **lineage**  - the people who were in someone's family in past times
  * **married**  - having a husband or wife
