====== Christ ======


===== Facts: =====


In the Bible, the word Christ means "anointed one" or "selected one." In the New Testament the term "Christ" was synonymous with the Jewish term "Messiah."


    * The Jewish scripture foretold that a man would come and be anointed as Messiah and King.
    * Jesus of Nazareth is the man whom the scripture foretold and is the Messiah or Christ.
