====== father, forefather ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "father" literally refers to a person's male parent. There are also several figurative uses of this term, which often include the meaning of being the leader or founder of a group of people.

  * The terms "father" and "forefather" are often used to refer to the ancestors of a certain person or people group. <font 14px line-height: 15.6800003051758px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>This could also be translated as,</font>    <font 14px line-height: 15.6800003051758px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>"ancestor" or "ancestral father."</font>
