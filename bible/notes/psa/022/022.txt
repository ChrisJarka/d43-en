===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I will declare your name**  - The word "name" refers to the total person of Yahweh. (See: Metonymy)
  * **in the midst of the assembly**  - AT: "while I am with the large gathering of people who worship God"
  * **stand in awe of him**  - AT: "rise up in respect for the most powerful God"
