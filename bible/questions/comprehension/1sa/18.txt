====== 1 Samuel Chapter 18 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? How strongly was the soul of Jonathan was bound to the soul of David?

A. The soul of Jonathan was bound to the soul of David, and Jonathan loved him as his own soul. [18:1]

Q? What did Jonathan take off and give to David with his armor?

A. Jonathan took off the robe that he was wearing and gave it to David with his armor. [18:4]

Q? Over whom did Saul set David?

A. Saul set him over the men of war. [18:5]

Q? What had the singers ascribed to David, which displeased Saul?

A. Thesong displeased Saul and he said that they have ascribed to David ten thousands. [18:8]

Q? What did Saul think as he threw the spear at David?

A. Saul threw the spear, for he thought that he would pin David to the wall. [18:11]

Q? Why was David propsering in all his ways?

A. David was prospering in all his ways, for Yahweh was with him. [18:14]
