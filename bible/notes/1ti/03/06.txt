===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He should not be a new convert** - "He should not be a new believer" or "He should not be someone who became a believer a short time ago" or "He must be a mature believer"
  * **and fall into condemnation as the devil** - Paul speaks of the experience of being condemned for having done wrong as if it were a hole that a person could fall into. AT: "become proud like the devil did and because of that be condemned as the devil was" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **those outside** - Paul speaks of the church as if it were a place, and of unbelievers as if they were physically outside of it. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])  
  * **fall into disgrace** - Paul speaks of disgrace as if it were a hole that a person could fall into. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **fall into...the trap of the devil** - "allow the devil to trap him." Paul speaks of temptation to sin as if it were a trap set for an animal. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
