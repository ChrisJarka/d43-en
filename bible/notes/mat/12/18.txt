===== Translation Notes: =====

This account continues to explain how the actions of Jesus fulfilled one of the prophecies of Isaiah. These words are God speaking through what Isaiah wrote.

  * **I ** - The pronoun "I" refers to God.
  * **well pleased** - "thinks well of" or "approves"
  * **him** - The pronoun "him" refers to Jesus.
  * **he ** - The pronoun "he" refers to Jesus.
  * **announce - "**show" or "tell"
