====== Psalms 043 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 43 is a psalm of deliverance. It is a plea for rescuing from his enemies.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

He should not be discouraged because he trusts in God to deliver him.
