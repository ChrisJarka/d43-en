===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For I will destroy every beast from beside the great waters;**  - "I will kill every animal that lives near the Nile River."
  * **And the foot of man will no longer stir the waters up,****neither will the hooves of beasts stir the waters! - **"No men or animals will live near the water and stir it up." (See:Metonymy)
  * **Then I will calm their waters and make their rivers run like oil. -**  "Then I will let the waters of Egypt become calm and run as smoothly as oil flows." (See Simile)
  * **<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>this is the Lord Yahweh’s declaration</font>    ** <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>-</font>    "I, the Lord God, want you to pay attention.")
