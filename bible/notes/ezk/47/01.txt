===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **waters were going out from under the threshold**  - This means that water flows out from the ground below the house.
  * **for the front of the house was eastward**  - The front of the house faces where the sun rises and the water flowed east.
  * **and the waters were going down from under the opposite side of the house towards the sea from the south side**  - Water was also coming from under the part of the house that was south and flows west toward the sea.
