====== gold ======

===== Definition: =====

Gold is a yellow-colored precious metal that is very valuable

  * Gold is a high-quality metal that is found in a pure state in nature and is long-lasting.
  * In Bible times, objects that were made out of gold include idols, jewelry, altars, parts of the ark of the covenant, and other objects used in the tabernacle or temple.
  * Sometimes when something is described as "gold" it means that it has a gold overlay, but is not solid gold. In those cases a word like "golden" or "gold-overlaid" could be used.
