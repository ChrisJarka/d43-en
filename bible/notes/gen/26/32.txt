===== Translation Notes: =====

  * ** and told him about the well that they had dug. They said, "We have found water."** - "and spoke to him because of the well that they had dug; they said, "We have found water."" 
  * **and he called the well Sheba**  - "So he called the well Sheba." Translators may also add a footnote that says “The name Sheba means 'oath.'”
