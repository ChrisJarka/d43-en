====== Jeremiah Chapter 52 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What kind of king was Zedekiah?

A. Zedekiah was an evil king just like Jehoiakim. [52:1-2]

Q? What did king Nebuchadnezzar do while he was near Jerusalem?

A. He camped opposite it and besieged the city for two years. [51:4-5]

Q? When and where were Zedekiah and his men overtaken?

A. When the famine was severe in the city and the city was broken into then the Chaldeans pursed the king and overtook him and his men in the plains of the Jordon river near Jericho. [52:6-8]

Q? What did the king of Babylon in Riblah do to Zedekiah and his sons?

A. The king slaughtered Zedekiah's sons before his eyes and then put out Zedekiah's eyes, bound him in chains and took him to Babylon where he was put in prison until he died. [52:9-11]

Q? What did Nebuzaradan, a servant of the king of Babylon, do in Jerusalem?

A. He burned the house of Yahweh, the king's palace, all the houses and important buildings plus destroyed the walls around Jerusalem. [52:12-14]

Q? What did Nebuzaradan do to the poorest of people in Jerusalem?

A. He took some of them into exile and some were left to work the vineyards and fields. [52:15-16]

Q? What became of the bronze, gold and silver in the house of Yahweh?

A. The Chaldeans took it all away. [52:17-19]

Q? What did the commander of the bodyguard (Nebuzaradan) do?

A. He took Zephaniah and many other city officials and put them in prison. [52:24-25]

Q? What did the king of Babylon at Riblah do with the prisoners that Nebuzaradan brought to him?

A. He put them to death. [52:26-27]

Q? How many Judean people were exiled?

A. There were 4,600 people exciled from Jerusalem. [52:28-30]

Q? Who released Jehoiachin king of Judah from prison?

A. Evil Merodach, king of Babylon released him. [52:31]

Q? How did Evil Merodach treat Jehoiachin when he was released from prison?

A. He spoke kindly to him and gave him a seat of honor. He removed Jehoiachin's prison clothes and had him eat at his table for the rest of his life. Plus a regular food allowance was given to him. [52:32-34]
