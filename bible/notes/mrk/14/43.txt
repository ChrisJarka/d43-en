===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **while he was still speaking **- While Jesus was still speaking, Judas arrived.
  * **a large crowd was with him **- A large crowd from the chief priests, the scribes, and the elders was with Judas.
  * **the betrayer of him had given them a sign **- Judas had given the chief priests, scribes, and elders a signal.
  * **Whomever I kiss, he is the one**  - it was customary to greet one another with a kiss, usually on both cheeks.
  * **"And he kissed him." **- "And Judas kissed Jesus."
