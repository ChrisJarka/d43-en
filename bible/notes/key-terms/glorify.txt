====== glorify ======


===== Definition: To glorify means to show or reveal God's character by your actions or speech. The person glorifying God will attribute aspects of their life to divine power and not their own. =====


    * A person can verbally glorify God by telling about a work He has done in the person's life, expressing that it was God who produced the outcome of a situation.
    * A person can glorify God by living a life that shows His power at work in them, making them like Himself.
