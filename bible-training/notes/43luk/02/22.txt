===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **purification**-readiness to re-enter every day life
    * **to present him to the Lord**  - note
    * **As it is written**  - This can also be translated as "They did this because it is written"
    * **male that opens the womb**  - This is an idiom for "first-born offspring that is a male." Although the law was about the first-born offspring of both people and domestic animals, in this context, it could also be translated as "first-born son."
    * **a pair of** - That is, "two."
