===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This concludes the list begun in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:25:09|25:9]].

  * **the twenty-second ... the twenty-fourth** - "the 22nd ... the 23rd ... the 24th." or "number 22 ... number 23 ... number 24." This shows the order in which the families were chosen. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Giddalti ... Mahazioth ... Romamti-Ezer** - Translate the names of these men the same as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:25:04|25:4]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **twelve persons** - "12 people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
