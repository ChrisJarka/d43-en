===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **posted**  - This means that guards were placed at certain areas or gates in order to keep watch over that place.
  * **gatekeepers' divisions **- These were the groups of Levites (descended from Korah and Merari) that had been seleted to serve as gatekeepers.
