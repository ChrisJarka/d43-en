====== born again, born of God ======

===== Definition: =====

"Born again" means a spiritual awakening in becoming a child of God by faith. "Born of God" refer to people who are children of God, that is, people of Christian faith.

  * The Bible, especially the first three chapters of the Book of John, speaks of two ways of being born â€“ a physical birth and a spiritual birth.
  * A spiritual birth is the second way of being born and means "born again."
  * The Book of 1 John says that those who believe in God are "born of God."
  * Being "born again" is a spiritual awakening that means being made a new creation in Christ from being spiritually dead.
