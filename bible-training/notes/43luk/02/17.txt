===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **what had been said to them** - That is, "what they had been told" or, "what the angel and told them."
  * **this child** - This could also be translated as "the baby."
  * **treasuring them in her heart** - That is, "carefully remembering them" or, "joyfully remembering them." A treasure is something that is very valuable or precious. Mary considered the things she was told about her son to be very precious. (See: [[en:ta:figuresofspeech:metaphor|Metaphor]]) 
  * **returned to** - That is, "went back to."
  * **their fields** - That is, "the grassy fields where they had been tending their sheep." This could be translated as "sheep fields."
