====== brimstone ======


===== Definition: =====


Brimstone is the biblical name for the yellow element we call sulfur.  Brimstone melts and burns at relative low temperatures, producing irritating gases.  Fire and brimstone are symbols of God's judgment on ungodly and rebellious people.
