===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **My days are swifter than a running messenger**  - This phrase describes the days as being able to run like a person. (See: Personification)
    * **running messenger** - "runner" or "running man"
    * **my days flee away; they see no good anywhere**  - See above. Also, the second part of this clause describes the days as being able to see like a person.
    * **they see no good anywhere**  - "days that have not been good" or "no good came from them"
    * **They are as fast as papyrus reed boats**  - Job compared the speed of the days passing by to the speed of fast boats. (See: Simile)
    * **They are as fast as**  - "They pass quickly by like"
