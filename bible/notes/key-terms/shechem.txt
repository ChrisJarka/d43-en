====== Shechem ======

===== Facts: =====

Text

  * Shechem is a town in Canaan where Jacob settled after being reconciled to his brother Esau (Genesis 33). He bought land from the sons of Hamor, which later became his family burial ground, where Jacob's sons buried him.
