===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You will write them**  - The text is still refering to the people of Israel writing the commandments, God's laws.
  * **on the doorposts of your house and on your city gates**
 - God's laws were to be accessible and visible throughout the community.
  * **that your days may be multiplied**  - "that your days may be increased." Long life was one of the blessings for the person who obeyed God's commandments.
  * **Yahweh swore to your fathers**  - This portion refers to the promisse God made to Abraham, to Isaac, and to Jacob.
  * **as many day as the heavens are high above the earth** - "as long as the heavens continue stretched over the earth." The meaning could be "to the end of time."
