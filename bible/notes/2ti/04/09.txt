===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Try to come to me quickly **- "Do your best to come to me as soon as possible" or "Make a way to come to me the in the shortest time possible"
  * **for - **  because
  * **He loves **- "because he (Demas) loves" or "for he loves"
  * **this present world **- if this refers to the temporary things versus the future reward, it can be translated as"the pleasures and comfort of this world." But if it refers to safety against dying being with Paul, this can be translated as "his life at this time."
  * **so has gone- **"so he (Demas) has gone"
  * **Crescens went . . and Titus went . . -**  Paul was just stating that Crescens and Titus went somewhere but not he saying that they also love this world like Demas.
