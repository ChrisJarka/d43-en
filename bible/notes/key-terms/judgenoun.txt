====== judge (noun) ======


===== Definition: =====


In the Bible a judge was a person appointed to decide legal matters for the nation of Israel.


    * Moses appointed men to judge matters that the people brought for decision.
    * After the final resurrection the twelve Apostles of Christ will sit as Judges over the twelve tribes of Israel.
