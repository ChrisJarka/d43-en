===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They were foolish in their rebellious ways** - It is implied that these are people that Yahweh has rescued. AT: "Yahweh rescued people who had become foolish because of their rebellious ways." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **in their rebellious ways** - The word "ways" refers to how a person acts. AT: "because they rebelled" or "because they refused to obey." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **they drew near to the gates of death** - "arrived at the gates of death." The gates represent the time when a person passes from life into death. AT: "they almost died." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Then they cried...their distress** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:psa:107:011|107:13]].
