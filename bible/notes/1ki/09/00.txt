====== 1 Kings 09 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

There are two parts to this chapter. 9:1–9 is a dream in which God warned Solomon that he and his descendants must not worship idols or the temple would be destroyed. Then 9:10–28 tells of Solomon's extensive building and his partnership with Hiram, King of Tyre.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

It is necessary for people to stay faithful to God and not worship idols.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphor ==
'Walk" for what you do. "if you walk before me as David your father walked"
