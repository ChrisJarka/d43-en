===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh**  - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:05:05|5:5]].
  * **Because you are more tumultuous than**  - Alternate translation: "because your sinfulness is worse than" or "because you have been more unruly than"
  * **that surround you**  - Alternate translation: "that are all around you."
  * **Behold**  - This can be translated as “Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you.”
  * **I will execute judgments**  - Alternate translation: "I will carry out judgments" or "I will perform judgments."
