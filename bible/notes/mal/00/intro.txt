====== Introduction to Malachi ======

===== Part 1: General Introduction =====

===Outline of Malachi===

  - The authority of the prophecy: The word of Yahweh (1:1)
  - The love of Yahweh for Israel, and his rejection of Esau (Edom) (1:2–5)
  - A message against the priests (1:6–2:9)
  - A message against Judah: they have broken faith, in divorce and inter-marriage (2:10–16)
  - A message about the day of judgment (2:17–3:6)
  - A message about the tithe (3:7–12)
  - A message about those who were treated shamefully by others, but who were faithful to Yahweh (3:13–18)
  - Encouragement to remember that Yahweh will punish the wicked and that he will send Elijah before the “great and terrible day of Yahweh” (4:5–6)
