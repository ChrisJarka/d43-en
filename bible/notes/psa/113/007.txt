===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He raises** - "Yahweh raises"
  * **the poor out of the dirt** - This phrase describes poor people as sitting in the dirt. This refers to their poverty or low status in life. AT: "poor people out of their poverty" or "the poor from their low status." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **from the ash heap** - Beggars sat on piles of ashes. AT: "from being beggars."
  * **so that he may seat him with princes** - When Yahweh give the poor and need person a seat with princes, he makes that person equal with the princes. AT: "in order to make them equal with princes."
  * **his people** - "the people of Yahweh"
