===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **considered**  - This could be translated as "remembered" or "thought about."
  * **all the other animals**  - This could be translated as "all the domestic animals." By combining the wild and domestic animals the text intends to include all the animals in the vessel. But the focus is on Noah.
  * **the fountains of the deep **- This refers to very deep, underground water sources.
  * **the windows of heaven**  - This is figurative language referring to the rain falling from the sky.
  * **and it stopped raining**  - this could be translated "so the rain from the sky was held back."
  * **bold words**  - explanation
