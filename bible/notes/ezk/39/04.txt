===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **fall upon the mountains-**  This says the army will be destroyed as they invade Israel.
  * **you and your- **  This refers to the ruler and his people.
  * **Birds of prey and living things of the field for food**  - Alternate translation: "As the soldiers die in batttle they will lay on the ground and be eaten by the birds and animals." Also points to the time
  * **face of the field -**  ground where the soldiers fall when the;y die in battle.
  * **I myself declare it**  - Lord Yahweh is telling.
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration **- See how you translated this in 5:11.
  * **send out fire**  - Lord Yahew is telling Gog another way he will destroy Magog. This time it will be by fire.
  * **dwelling securely on the coasts -**  No place, even people on the coastlines , will not be safe.
