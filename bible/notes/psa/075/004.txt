===== Translation Notes: =====

God continues to speak.

  * **Do not be confident of victory. Do not be so sure of victory; **  - This idea is stated twice for emphasis. (See: Doublet)
  * **do not speak with your head held so high**  - AT: "do not speak so pridefully"
  * **head head so high**  - This expression describes pride or arrogance. (See: Metonymy)
