====== perish, perishing ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "perish" literally means to die, cease to exist, be lost, or be destroyed. In the Bible, it especially has the meaning of being punished for eternity in hell.

  * People who are "perishing" are those who are destined for hell because they have refused to believe in Jesus for their salvation.
  * John 3:16 shows that "perish" means the opposite of living eternally in heaven.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * Depending on the context, other ways to translate this term could be, "die eternally" or "be punished in hell" or "be destroyed."
  * Make sure that the translation of "perish" can include living eternally in hell and does not only mean "cease to exist."
