===== translationNotes: =====
 
  * **arise against it ... live in it** - Here "it" refers to Babylon in both phrases.
  * **her land** - The word "her" refers to Babylon.
  * **make her land a desolation** - The abstract noun "desolation" can be translated as a verb. AT: "destroy her land" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **In those days and at that time** - These phrases mean the same thing and emphasize the importance of that future time. Translate this as in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:33:14|33:15]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **this is Yahweh's declaration** - Yahweh speaks of himself by name to express the certainty of what he is declaring. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:01:07|1:8]]. AT: "this is what Yahweh declares" or "this is what I, Yahweh, declare" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **seek Yahweh their God** - Seeking Yahweh their God represents either 1) asking God for help or 2) thinking about God and obeying him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **They will ask** - The word "They" refers to the people of Israel or the people of Judah who are returning from Babylon to Jerusalem.
  * **join ourselves to Yahweh** - This is figurative language used to explain a spiritual connection to Yahweh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **that will not be forgotten** - This states that the covenant will be obeyed forever. It can be stated in active form. AT: "that no one will forget" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
