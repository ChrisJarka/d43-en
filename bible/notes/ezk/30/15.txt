===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues to describe the way he will punish Egypt.
  * **For I will pour out my fury upon Sin** - "For I will act in anger against Sin" or "Because I am angry, I will do bad things to Sin" 
  * **Sin** - This was an important city in Egypt. (See: [[en:ta:translate:translatenames]])
  * **stronghold** - A stronghold is a place that is well protected from enemies by strong buildings and by fighting men. 
  * **cut off the multitude of No **- "kill the very large number of people in No"
  * **No**  - This is the name of an important city in Egypt.
  * **grievous anguish **- "great pain" (See:doublet)
  * **broken up **- This implies that they will not be destroyed but seperated into small pieces.
  * **Memphis **- This was a powerful city in Egypt.
  * **have enemies **- Here, this implies that Memphis will lack peace because of all of her enemies.
