====== woe ======


===== Definition: =====


Woe is a term that expresses great sorrow or distress


    * In Matthew 18 Jesus says that woe will come upon anyone who causes someone to sin
    * Jesus said that woe would come to the cities of Chorazin and Bethsaida because they did not repent when He preached there
