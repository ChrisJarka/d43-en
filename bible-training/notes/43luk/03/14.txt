===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **soldiers**  - That is, "people serving in the military".
    * **what about us? What must we do**  - That is, "You told the crowd and the tax collector what they must do. How about us soldiers, what must we do?" John is not included in the words "us" and "we". (See: [[en:ta:vocabulary:exclusive|Exclusive]])
    * **Do not take money from anyone by force**  - That is, "Do not force people to give you money."
    * **and do not accuse anyone falsely**  - That is, "In the same way, do not accuse anyone falsely in order to get money from them" or, "Do not lie by saying that an innocent person has done something illegal."
    * **Be content with your wages**  - That is, "Be satisfied with your pay" or, "Be satisfied with what you are paid."
