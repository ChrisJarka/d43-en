===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **And he was driving out a demon** - That is, "And Jesus was driving a demon out of a person" or "And he was making a demon leave a person."
    * **when the demon had come out** - That is "when the demon came out of the person" or "when the demon left the man."
    * **mute**  - That is, "unable to speak." The demon apparently was making the person it possessed unable to speak
    * **by Beelzebul ... he drives out demons** â€‹ - This could also be translated as "It is Beelzebul ... who enables him to drive out demons"
