====== Psalms 114 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 114 is a psalm of praise to God for his miracles in bringing the people of Israel to the Promised land.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

He praised God for opening up the Red Sea for the people to cross, for providing the earthquake that stopped the Jordan River so the people could cross and providing water from a rock for the people to drink.
