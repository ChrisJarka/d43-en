===== Translation Notes: =====

The metaphor of comparing the city of Tyre to a beautiful, well-built ship continues.

  * **they carved **- "They" refers to the builders of the city of Tyre.
  * **your rudders from the oaks of Bashan **- ""you strong." This phrase may indicate that Tyre had great power. Rudders are steering devices on a ship made from oak from Bashan. (See: Metaphor)
  * **your cabins ivory from the island of Kittim**- "you beautiful." This phrase indicates that they spared no expense in making the city beautiful.
  * **your sails were colored linen from Egypt **- "you powerful and beautiful."  This phrase indicates that the sails were oth powerful and beautiful.
  * **banner **- "flag"
