====== Finding Rebekah [06-02] ======


{{:en:obs:obs-06-02.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


After a very long journey to the land where **[[:en:obe:other:abraham|Abraham's]]** relatives lived, **[[:en:obe:kt:god|God]]** led the **[[:en:obe:other:servant|servant]]** to **[[:en:obe:other:rebekah|Rebekah]]**. She was the **granddaughter of Abraham's brother**. 


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **granddaughter of Abraham's brother** - Specifically, she was the daughter of Abraham's brother's son. Her grandfather was Abraham's brother.
