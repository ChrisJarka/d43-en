====== steward, manager ======


===== Definition: =====


===== A house manager/steward was a servant in a rich family's large house who was placed in charge of the the children, the other servants and the property. He was so powerful that his name meant "house-law." =====


A servant is a person who serves another. 


    * A servant may work in a household by cleaning, cooking, or doing other chores.
    * A servant may serve as a manager of another's household, that is, taking care of the property and directing the work of other servants.
