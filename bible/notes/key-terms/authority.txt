====== authority ======


===== Definition: =====


Authority is the power of influence and control of a person or group of people over other individuals. This term is commonly used in reference to governments or organizations, but can be used to refer to a single person's power over another person also.


    * Governments have power to control the behavior of their citizens by passing law.
    * Individuals often exercise authority over others by personal influence and accomplishments.
