====== Syria ======


===== Facts: =====


Syria was a Roman province located in the region of modern-day Lebanon and Syria, which is northeast of Israel.


    * The inhabitants of Syria are also referred to as "Aramaeans" or "Aram" and are descendants of Aram, a descendant of Shem.
    * Saul went to the city of Damascus in Syria with plans to persecute Christians there, but Jesus stopped him.
