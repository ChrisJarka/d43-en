===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Paul begins his speech to the crowd in Jerusalem. In verse 2 Paul is not speaking, rather background information is given. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])

  * **Brothers and fathers** - This was a polite way of addressing men who were Paul's age, as well as the older men in the audience.
  * **listen to my defense** - "please listen to my defense"
  * **I will now make to you** - "I will now explain to you" or "I will now present to you"
  * **the Hebrew language** - "their Hebrew language"
