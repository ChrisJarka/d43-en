====== biblical time ======

===== Definition: =====

During Bible times, people often used calendars and ways of marking time that are different from modern methods. 

**hour** - Hours were not always counted from midnight, as is common today. They were sometimes counted from sundown. For more information, see [[:en:obe:other:biblicaltimehour]].

**watch** - a period of time at night during which a watchman or guard for a city would be on duty looking out for any threat from an enemy. For more information, see [[:en:obe:other:biblicaltimewatch]].

**day** -  For more information, see [[:en:obe:other:biblicaltimeday]].

**month** -  For more information, see [[:en:obe:other:biblicaltimemonth]].

**year** -  For more information, see [[:en:obe:other:biblicaltimeyear]].


(See also: [[en:obe:kt:hour]])
