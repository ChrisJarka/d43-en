====== Psalms Chapter 149 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? How should everyone praise Yahweh?

A. Everyone should sing a new song to Yahweh and sing praises joined with other faithful ones. [149:1]

Q? Why should Israel rejoice in Yahweh?

A. Israel should rejoice in the one who made them a nation. [149:2]

Q? In whom should the people in Zion rejoice?

A. The people of Zion should rejoice in their king. [149:2]

Q? How should everyone praise Yahweh?

A. Everyone should praise Yahweh's name with dancing and sing praises to him with tambourine and harp. [149:3]

Q? Why should everyone praise Yahweh?

A. Yahweh takes pleasure in his people and glorifies the humble with salvation. [149:4]

Q? Godly people should do what?

A. Godly people should rejoice in victory and sing for joy. [149:5]

Q? The people of Zion should have what in their mouths and hand?

A. The people of Zion should have praises of God in their mouths and a sword in their hand. [149:6]

Q? What do the people of Zion do with the sword?

A. The people of Zion execute vengeance on the nations and acts of punishment on the peoples. [149:7]

Q? What will the people of Zion do to their kings and nobles?

A.  The people of Zion will bind their kings with chains and their nobles with iron shackles. [149:8]
