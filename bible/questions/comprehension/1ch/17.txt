====== 1 Chronicles Chapter 17 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did King David notice about where the ark of the covenant of Yahweh was staying even though David was settled in a house?

A. David noticed that the ark of the covenant of Yahweh was staying under a tent.  [17:1]

Q? What did Yahweh say that Nathan was to tell David about building a house in which Yahweh was to live?

A. God told Nathan to tell David that he was not to build a house in which Yahweh could live. [17:3]

Q? Why did Yahweh take David from the pasture and from following the sheep?

A. Yahweh took David from the pasture and from following the sheep so that he would be ruler over Yahweh's people Israel.  [17:7]

Q? What did Yahweh say that he would do for David's name?

A. Yahweh said that he would make David a name like the name of the great ones who are on the earth. [17:8]

Q? Who would no longer oppress the people of Israel after Yahweh gave them a place where they could live and be troubled no more?

A. Wicked people would no longer oppress Israel as they did before. [17:9]
