====== Leviticus Chapter 04 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What kind of offering does the fourth chapter of Leviticus talk about?

A. The fourth chapter of Leviticus talks about the sin offering. [4:3]

Q? How many times must the priest sprinkle blood from the sin offering before Yahweh, before the curtain of the most holy place?

A. The priest must sprinkle some blood from the sin offering seven times before Yahweh, before the curtain of the most holy place. [4:6]

Q? What parts of the bull are to be carried out to the place cleansed for Yahweh and poured on the ashes?

A. The skin of the bull and any remaining meat, with its head, legs and inner parts  and its dung are to be carried out to the ashes. [4:11]

Q? If the whole assembly of Israel sins without wanting to sin, who is to lay their hands on the sin offering?

A. The elders are to lay their hands on the sin offering if the whole assembly of Israel sins without wanting to sin. [4:15]

Q? What will happen to the assembly of Israel if they follow the directons for the sin offering?

A. If the assembly if Israel follows the directions of the sin offering, they will be forgiven. [4:20]

Q? What is the animal to be offered if a ruler sins?

A. If a ruler sins, he is to offer a male goat without blemish. [4:23]

Q? Who is to bring a female goat or lamb without blemish to be a sin offering if they sin?

A. Common persons are to bring a female goat or lamb without blemish to be a sin offering if they sin. [4:27,32]]
