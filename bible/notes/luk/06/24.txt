===== Translation Notes: =====



    * **woe to you who**  - “How sad you will be” or “What sorrow you will have” or “How terrible it will be for you” or "trouble will come to you
    * **you have already received** - Or, "you are receiving."
    * **who are full now** - "whose stomachs are full now" or "who eat much now"
