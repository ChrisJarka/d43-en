===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For thus says the Lord Yahweh: -**  "The Lord Yahweh says,"
  * **I will gather Egypt from peoples who they are scattered among, - **"I will bring the people of Egypt home from where they were scattered."
  * **and I will restore the fortunes of Egypt - **"I will return the wealth of Egypt"
  * **land of Patros, -** This is the very northern part of Egypt. (See:How to Translate Names)
  * **a lowly kingdom **- "an unimportant nation"
