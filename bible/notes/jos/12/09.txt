===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Enaim**  - A name (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Jarmuth**  - A name. Translate the same way as in Joshua 10:3, 5, 23, 15:35, 21:29 and Nehemiah 11:29.
    * **Lachish**  - An important city. Translate the same way as in Joshua 10:3, 5, 23, 31-35, 15:39, 2 Kings, 14:18, 18:14, 17, 19:8, 2 Chronicles 11:9, 25:27, 32:9, Nehemiah 11:30, Isaiah 36:2, 37:8, Jeremiah 34:7 and Micah 1:13. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])  
    * **Eglon**  - Translate the same way as in Joshua 10:3, 5, 23, 24, 36, 37, 12:12, 15:39, Judges 3:12, 14, 15, 17 and 24.
    * **Gezer**  - A Canaanite city-state in the foothills of the Judaean Mountains.  (See:[[en:obe:other:canaan]], [[en:obe:other:judea]] and [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
