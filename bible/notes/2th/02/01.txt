===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now**  - This word is used here to mark a new section of the letter.
  * **we request **- The pronoun "we" refers to Paul, Silas, and Timothy. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:exclusive|Exclusive]])
  * **request **- "urge" (UDB)
  * **you**  - “You” is plural and refers to the fellow believers in the Thessalonian church. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:formsofyou-plural|Forms of 'You' - Dual/Plural]])
  * **that you be not easily shaken **- "that you will not be fearful" or "that you will be not upset". If your language does not have passive voice, then use that you "think calmly about" (UDB).
  * **by word, or by letter ****as from us**  - "by spoken word or by written letter as coming from us"
  * **so as to believe **- "telling you"
