====== Zacchaeus ======

===== Facts: =====

Zacchaeus was a tax collector from Jericho who climbed a tree in order to see Jesus

  * Zacchaeus was completely changed when he believed in Jesus. He even gave back four times the amount of money he had cheated from people.
