===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **decay** - to be slowly rot
  * **he will see decay** - The word "see" compares the ability to see something the experience of it. The word "decay" is used to refer to death because a living things decay after they die. AT: "he will experience death" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Wise men die; the fool and the brute alike perish** - The author refers to all people by referring to those who have the most and least wisdom. AT: "All men perish" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **brute** - a violent man
