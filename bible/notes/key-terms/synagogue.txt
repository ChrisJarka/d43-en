====== synagogue ======


===== Definition: =====


A synagogue is a building where Jewish people assemble to pray. In New Testament times the services included prayer, scripture reading, and teaching about the scripture.

    * Jesus often taught in synagogues.
    * The word synagogue can also refer to the group of people meeting there.
