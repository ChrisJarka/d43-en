====== reconcile, reconciliation ======

===== Definition: =====

To reconcile is when two or more offended persons solve their differences and forgive each other.

  * A husband and wife who have difficulties in their marriage can reconcile to each other.
  * Friends who have been offended and split apart reconcile to become friends again.
  * Reconciliation is possible between nations who have been at war with each other.
