===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you willl eat them**  - "Them" refers to the list of offerings from the previous verse.
  * **before Yahweh - **"in the presence of Yahweh"
  * **in the place that Yahweh your God will choose - **  From the territory divided between the tribes of Israel, Yahweh would chose one location for worship. (that later came to be Jerusalem, inside the territory of the tribe of Judah)
  * **within your gate**  - “within your community” or “inside your city.” Cities often had walls around them to keep out enemies, and gates for people to go in and out. (See: Metonymy)
  * **everything that you put your hand to **  - "all you undertake" or "all the work of your hands." (See: Metonymy)
  * **Pay attention to yourself **  - "Be careful" or "Take care that"
  * **that you do not forsake **- (See: Litotes)
