===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now early on the first day of the week, while it was still dark, -**  "While it was still dark in the morning of the first day of the week," or "While it was still dark on Sunday morning,"
  * **Mary Magdalene came to the tomb; - **"Mary Magdalene went to the tomb;"
  * **she saw the stone rolled away from the tomb. -**  There had been a stone that covered the opening of the tomb, and she saw that the stone had been moved.
  * **So she ran and came to Simon Peter and to the other disciple whom Jesus loved, -**  Mary ran back toward where she had come from and met Simon Peter and John.
  * **and she said to them, -**  "and she told them, 'They have taken the Lord from the tomb,"'
  * **Cand we do not know where they have laid him.” -**  "and I (or "we") do not know where they have put him!'"
