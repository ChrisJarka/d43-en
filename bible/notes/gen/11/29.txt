===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **took wives**  - "married wives"
  * **Iscah** - Iscah was one of Haran's daughters.
  * **barren** - This means that she was not able to get pregnant or give birth to any children.
  * **Now** - This word in English is used to introduce some background information about Sarai. In later chapters people will see why this is important information.
