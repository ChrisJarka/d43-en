====== Introduction to Zephaniah ======

===== Part 1: General Introduction =====

===Outline of Zephaniah===

  - The reasons Yahweh will judge Judah: the Day of Yahweh (1:1-2:3)
    - Judgment on Baal worshipers (1:2-6)
    - Judgment is from Yahweh (1:7-13)
    - Judgment is coming soon (1:14-18)
    - Invitation to “seek Yahweh” and escape the Day of Yahweh (2:1-3)
  - Judgment for the nations (2:4-15)
    - Philistia (2:4-7)
    - Moab and Ammon (2:8-11)
    - Egypt (2:12)
    - Assyria (2:13-15)
  - Judgment on Jerusalem, the city of oppressors (3:1-8)
  - The promise that Yahweh will purify, restore, gather, and bless his Covenant people (3:9-20)
