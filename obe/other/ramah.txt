====== Ramah ======

===== Facts: =====

Ramah was an ancient Israelite city located about 8km north of Jerusalem. in Benjamin near Gibeon.

  * Ramah was located in the area where the descendants of Jacob's wife Rachel lived.
  * When the Israelites were taken captive to Babylon, they were first brought to Ramah before moving to Babylon.<font 14px line-height: 22.399999618530273px;/sans-serif;;rgb(37, 37, 37) font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 22.399999618530273px;;;inherit></font>
  * Ramah was the home of Samuel's mother and father.
