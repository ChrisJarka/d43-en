====== twelve tribes of Israel ======

===== Definition: =====

The twelve tribes of Israel refer to the twelve sons of Jacob <font 14.4444446563721px line-height: 21.7777786254883px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>and their descendants,</font>

  * Jacob was the grandson of Abraham, and God changed Jacob's name to Israel. Both the name "Jacob" and the name "Israel" refer to the same person.
  * When the Israelites took possession of the land of Canaan, it was divided among these twelve tribes.
  * The descendants of Levi received no assigned region so they could work as priests for the people.
  * Joseph's sons, Ephraim and Manasseh, each received tribal property because Jacob had blessed them , counting them as his own sons..
