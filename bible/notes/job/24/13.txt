===== Translation Notes: =====
Job continues speaking.

    * **rebel against the light** - AT: "hate the daylight"
    * **they know not its ways** - "they do not recognize the roads during in the light"
    * **nor do they stay in its paths** - "nor do they stay on the paths when it is day time"
    * **with the light** - "before the daylight"
    * **he is like a thief** - AT: "he steals from others" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
