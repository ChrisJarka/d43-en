====== palm ======

===== Definition: =====

A tall tree with large leafy branches protruding out at the top.

* The branches with long leaves form a fan pattern.

<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>* Palm trees usually grow in warm climates, some bearing familiar fruit like the coconut or dates.</font>

* When Jesus entered Jerusalem riding on a donkey those who were awaiting the Messiah laid palm branches on the ground in front of Him.

(See also: other)
