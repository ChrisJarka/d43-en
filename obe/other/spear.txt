====== spear ======

===== Definition: =====

A thrown weapon with a long handle and a sharp blade on one end.

  * Spears were weapons used by soldiers in biblical times.
  * A spear was used by a Roman soldier to pierce the side of Jesus while He hung on the cross to prove that Jesus was dead.
