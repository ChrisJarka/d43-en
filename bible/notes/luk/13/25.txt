===== Translation Notes: =====

    * (Jesus continues to answer the question about who will be saved.)
    * **you will start to stand outside** - Jesus was talking to a crowd. The form of "you" is plural. (See: [[en:ta:vocabulary:forms ofyou]])
    * **Get away from me**  - "Go away from me"
    * **you unrighteous workers** - "you evildoers" or "you wicked people"
