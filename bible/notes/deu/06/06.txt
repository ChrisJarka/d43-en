===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I am commanding you **- Yahweh is still addressing the people of Israel.
  * **will be in your heart**  - This metaphor means that Yahweh is commanding the people of Israel to always remember the commandments, statutes and decrees, by keeping them inside their hearts, their most inner part. (See: Metaphor)
  * **teach them**  - "teach the commandments, statutes and decrees"
