====== 2 Chronicles Chapter 03 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why did Solomon begin to build the house of Yahweh at the threshing floor of Ornan the Jebusite on Mount Moriah?

A. Solomon began to build the house of Yahweh there because that was where Yahweh had appreared to David his father. [3:1-2]

Q? What were the dimensions of the foundation which Solomon laid for the house of God?

A. The dimensions of the foundation for the house of God was sixty cubits long and twenty cubits wide, using the old style cubit. [3:3]

Q? What did Solomon use to build the interior of the house of God?

A. Solomon fashioned the ceiling and the main hall with pine, overlaid it with fine gold, and decorated the house with precious stones. [3:4-7]
