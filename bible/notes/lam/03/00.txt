====== Lamentations 03 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===


==His terrible troubles ==

Everything has gone wrong and he is miserable.

== He hopes in God ==

God is faithful and will not punish forever but will rescue those who trust him.

== His prayer of anguish ==

He prayed to God to take up their cause against Those who are destroying them.


=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Chiasm ==

The book of Lamentations is a giant chiasm which means this center part; the part expressing hope in God, is the most important part of the book.
