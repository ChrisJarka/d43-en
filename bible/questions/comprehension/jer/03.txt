====== Jeremiah Chapter 03 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? To what kind of woman does the prophet compare this land?

A. This land is that woman whom a man sends away, so she goes from him and becomes another man's wife. [3:1]

Q? What have they done because they sat waiting for their lovers, like a nomad in the wilderness?

A. They have polluted the land with their prostitution and wickedness. [3:2]

Q? What happened becasue of their wickedness?

A. The spring rains were withheld and the late rains did not come. [3:3]

Q? What are they told to do because they have proclaimed they would commit evil?

A. So continue to do it. 3:5]

Q? What did Yahweh say to himself when he saw Israel act like a promiscuous woman?

A. He said that after she does all these these, she will return to me, but she did not return. [3:7]

Q? What did God do to the apostate Israel?

A. He sent her out and gave her a decree of divorce. [3:8]

Q? What is Yahweh's declaration about Judah?

A. Then after all of this, neither did her faithless sister Judah return to me with all her heart, but with a lie! [3:10]

Q? What is Yahweh's declaration that the prophet should proclaim to the north?

A. Return, faithless Israel--this is Yahweh's declaration--"I will not always be angry with you, since I am faithful." [3:12]

Q? What must the people acknowledge?

A. They must acknowledge their iniquity, for they have transgressed against Yahweh their God. [3:13]

Q? What will Yahweh give them?

A. He will give them shepherds after his heart, and they will shepherd them with knowledge and insight. [3:15]

Q? then what will happen?

A. Then it will happen that they will increase and bear fruit in the land in those days. [3:16]

Q? What will they proclaim about Jerusalem, and what will happen there?

A. This is Yahweh's throne, and all the other nations will gather at Jerusalem in Yahweh's name. [3:17]

Q? What will Judah and Israel do together?

A. They will come together from the land of the north to the land that Yahweh gave their ancestors as an inheritance. [3:18]

Q? What does Yahweh want to do or them?

A. He wants to honor them as his son and give them a pleasant land, an inheritance more beautiful than what is in any nation. [3:19]
