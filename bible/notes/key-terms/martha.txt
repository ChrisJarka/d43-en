====== Martha ======


===== Facts: =====


Martha is a woman mentioned briefly in the New Testament who followed Jesus and supported Him from her own personal resources.


    * Martha had a younger sister named Mary who also followed Jesus.
    * Martha was distracted by meal preparation while her younger sister sat and listened to Jesus teach.
