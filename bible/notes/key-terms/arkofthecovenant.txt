====== Ark of the Covenant ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Old Testament the Ark of the Covenant was a gold overlayed wooden boxlike structure that was placed inside Israel's worship temple.

  * By God's command the Ark contained objects of remembrance that were spiritually significant for the Israelites.
  * When Israel's High Priest presented appropriate sacrifices at the Temple, God's presence would appear over the Ark.
  * As a result of Israel's disobedience and captivities the Ark was lost and eventually destroyed.
