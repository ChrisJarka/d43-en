===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **his wife said to him** - "Job's wife said to him"
  * **do you still hold fast to your integrity** - "are you still trying to be faithful to God?"
  * **Renounce God** - "reject God"
  * **You talk like a foolish women** - Job is not calling his wife a fool but that she is talking like a fool. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile|Simile]]) Alternate translation: "You talk as if you're a foolish woman"
  * **from the hand of God** - God does not have hands, but His actions are described in humans terms. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) - Alternate translation: "from God" 
  * **that we should receive good at the hand of God and not evil?** - "that we should receive only good things from God and not bad things as well?"
  * **sin with his lips** - "sin by speaking against God"
