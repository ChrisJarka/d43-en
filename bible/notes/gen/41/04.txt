===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **undesirable and thin**  - "bony and worthless" or “ugly and thin” or “nasty looking and thin”
  * **desirable and fat**  - "healthy and salable" or “healthy looking and well-fed”
  * **woke up**  - "awakened"
  * **Behold**  - "Listen closely"
  * **ears**  - "heads" (see UDB).
  * **wholesome**  - "healthy"
