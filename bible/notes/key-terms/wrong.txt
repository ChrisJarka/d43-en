====== wrong, mistreat, hurt ======

===== Definition: =====

To wrong, mistreat or hurt means to inflict physical or emotional damages upon another person or persons.

  * An individual person can wrong, mistreat or hurt another individual person.
  * A group can also inflict the same upon an individual or another group, for example when a government persecutes the Christian Church.

(See also: other)
