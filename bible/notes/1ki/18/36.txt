===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **It happened at the time** - AT: "This is what happened at the time"
    * **the prophet came near** - AT: "the prophet went to the altar" 
    * **that I am your servant, and that I have done all these things at your word** - These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
    * **I have done all these things at your word** - It is implied that Elijah is referring to both the drought and gathering all the people on Mount Carmel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
    * **Hear me ... hear me** - This is repeated twice for emphasis.
    * **turned their heart back again to yourself** - Here "heart" represents trust in God, and "turn their heart back again to yourself" means that God would cause the people to trust him again. AT: "turned their heart back to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
