===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I will not restrain my mouth **  - "I will not stop talking"
  * **the anguish of my spirit **  - meaning, In Job's spirit he does not understand why he is suffering so severely.
  * **the bitterness of my soul **  - meaning, His suffering he is becoming bitter in his soul.
  * **I will speak . . . I will complain . . . **- These two statements explain why Job will not restrain his mouth. They express the same idea in different words. (See: ([[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]]))
  * **sea monster **  - this is a Figure of Speech referring to the large creatures in the deep of the sea. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]))
  * **Am I the sea or a sea monster, that you place a guard over me? **  - This is a question that does not expect any answer but implies a "no" from the hearer. AT: I am not the sea or a sea monster, that you place a guard over me. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
