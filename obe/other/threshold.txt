====== doorpost ======

===== Definition: =====

The "threshold" and the "doorpost" are both parts of a door to a building or house. The threshold is the part on the bottom that you step over;

  * <font 14px line-height: 11.1999998092651px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The "doorpost" is a vertical beam on both sides of the door which support the top of the door frame.</font>
  * Just before God helped the Israelites escape from Egypt, he instructed them to kill a lamb and put its blood on their doorposts.
  * In the Old Testament, a slave could place his ear next to the doorpost of his master's house and his master would hammer a nail through the slave's earlobe into the doorpost, as a sign of permanent ownership.

(See also: Passover, Egypt other)
