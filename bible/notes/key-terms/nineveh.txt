====== Nineveh, Ninevite ======


===== Facts: =====


Nineveh was a principle city in Assyria.  A Ninevite is a person who lived in Nineveh.


    * God sent the prophet Jonah to warn the Ninevites to turn from their wicked ways. They did, and God did not destroy them.
    * The Assyrians later conquered the kingdom of Israel and carried the people away to Nineveh in 722 B.C.
