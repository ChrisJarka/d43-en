===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **came to**  - Or, "arrived at." The literal version is written as though the narrator is already at the tomb and sees the women arrive there. The dynamic version is written as though the narrator sees the women leave an unnamed place and go to the tomb.
  * **prepared**  - The women had taken some time after Sabbath ended at sundown, on the evening of the first day (see Gen 1:5), to procure, mix, and otherwise prepare the spices to anoint Jesus' body.
