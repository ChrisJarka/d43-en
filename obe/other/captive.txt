====== captive, captivity ======

===== Definition: =====

To  be taken and held as a political prisoner against your will.

  * The Israelites were taken into captivity by the Babylonians because of their disobedience to God.
  * This concept can apply to a person who is held captive in a jail because of a crime committed, but in the Bible the majority of references to captivity refer to Israel being punished by God.

(See also: other)
