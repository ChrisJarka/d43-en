====== Deuteronomy Chapter 23 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

Q? Who would not be allowed to enter into the assembly of Yahweh?

A. The man who is wounded in the testicles, has his penis cut off, or the illegitimate child is not allowed to enter into the assembly of Yahweh. [23:1-2]
