===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[:en:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **Song of Ascents** - see how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:120:001|120:1]]
  * **out of the depths** - AT: "from my lowest dispair" or "in my deep sadness." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **let your ears be attentive** - AT: "please listen" personfication
  * **my voice for** - AT: "my" (see: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **for pleas of mercy**  - AT: "asking for mercy" or "when I pray for mercy"
