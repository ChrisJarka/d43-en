===== Install translationKeyboard on an Android Device =====

This module answers the question: How do I install translationKeyboard on an Android device?\\
Before you start this module have you learned about: ['tK_intro']

Watch the {{:en:ta:tech:installing_translationkeyboard-android_v2.mp4|Installing translationKeyboard}}  One video or follow these directions:
**Note: ** Installing requires an Internet connection.
   - Tap the **Google Play Store**  icon on the Android device to open Google Play Store.
  - In the Play Store, click on the magnifying glass at the top right of the window.
  - Tap **Search Google Play**  and type translationKeyboard (do not use spaces).
  - Tap the magnifying glass or close the keyboard.
  - When the Play Store has found the program, tap the **tK**  icon.
  - Tap **Install**  on the next screen.
  - Tap **Accept**  to accept the default settings of the program.
  - The program will download and then install.
  - Once the program is installed, tap **Open**  or use the Android back button to close the Google Play Store. The tK icon should appear on the Android screen.
