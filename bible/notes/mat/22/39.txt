===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **commandment is like it **- "it" refers to the great and first commandment. (See: Simile)
  * **You will love your neighbor as yourself**  - This is a quote within a quote. Christ is quoting Leviticus 19:18. This could be translated as "you will care for your neighbor as you care for yourself."
  * **On these two commandments depend the whole law and the prophets**  - This could be stated as "The whole law and the prophets depend on these two commandments."
