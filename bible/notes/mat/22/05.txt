===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus continues telling a parable. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_parables]])

  * **those people** - "the guests the king invited"
  * **did not treat his invitation seriously** - "ignored his invitation"
  * **laid hands on** - This is an idiom. AT: "seized" (UDB). (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **killed those murderers** - It is implied that the king's soldiers killed the murderers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
