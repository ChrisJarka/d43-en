===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Whoever gives **- "Anyone who gives.**"**
  * **one of these unimportant ones, even a cup of cold water to drink, because he is a disciple **- This can also be translated "one of these little ones a cup of cold water because he is a disciple of mine" or "one of the least of these my disciples even a drink of cold water."
  * **truly I say to you **- Jesus is speaking to his twelve apostles.
  * **he will in no way lose his reward**  - This is a negative way of saying the positive is true. Anyone who does a small thing because he is a disciple will be rewarded. It can also be translated "that person will certainly receive his reward." (See: Litotes)
