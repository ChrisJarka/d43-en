===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **"For I, Yaweh, do not change; -**  "I am Yahweh and I do not change."
  * **therefore you, O children of Jacob, have not consumed. -**  "That is why the children of Jacob have not already been destroyed."
  * **From the days of your fathers you have turned aside from my ordinances and have not kept them. -**  "Since the days of your ancestors you have turned your back on (or 'defied') my rules (or 'laws' or 'ordinances') and have not kept them."
  * **But you say, "How will we return?" -**  (Rhetorical Question) "But you ask, "How can we return when we have never left you (or 'gone away') ?"
