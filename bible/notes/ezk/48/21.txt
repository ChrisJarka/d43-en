===== Important Terms: =====

  * Levites

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The rest**  - The land that has not yet been given away.
  * **holy contribution**  - The land for the temple.
  * **property of the city**  - The land where the city can be built.
  * **twenty five thousand…**  - These measurements are in cubits.
  * **corresponding to the portions **- The size of the land matches the size of the land given to to the 6 tribes of Judah mentioned earlier in the chapter.
  * **will be in its midst**- alternate translation: will be in the middle
