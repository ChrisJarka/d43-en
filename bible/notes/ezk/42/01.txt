===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he**  - the man whose appearance was like brass (40:3)
  * **outer court**  - outer courtyard
  * **to the northern way **- on the north side of the outer courtyard
  * **hall**  - <font 13px line-height: 19.5px background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)/Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;;inherit;;rgb(255, 255, 255)>a usually long, narrow passage inside a building with doors that lead to rooms on the sides; in this case, the passage is outside</font>

  * **inner court**  - inner courtyard
  * **pavement**  - stone path
  * **gallery**  - a raised section (of rooms, in this case)
