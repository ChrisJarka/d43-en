===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **garment**  - piece of clothing
  * **like a garment**  - The water covered the whole earth the way a robe covers a body. (See: Simile)
  * **the waters recede**  - AT: "the waters move away gradually"
  * **your thunderous voice**  - AT: "your roaring voice"
  * **they fled**  - AT: "the waters fled" (See: Ambiguity)
