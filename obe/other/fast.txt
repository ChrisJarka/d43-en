====== fast ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "fast" means to stop eating food, or certain foods and drinks, for a period of time, such as for a day or more.

  * The term "fast" can be used figuratively to refer to intentionally not doing something or to give up something in order to please God.
  * Fasting can help people to focus on God and pray without being distracted by food.
  * Jesus condemned the Jewish religious leaders for fasting for the wrong reasons. They fasted so that others would think they were righteous.
  * Sometimes people fast in order to show that they are very sad or grieved about something.
  * This can be translated as "to refrain from eating" or "to not eat."
  * The noun "fast" could be translated as "time of not eating" or "time of abstaining."

(See also: [[:en:obe:other:jewishleaders|Jewish leaders, Jewish authorities, religious leaders]])
