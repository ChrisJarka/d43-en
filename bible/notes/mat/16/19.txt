===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus concludes his reply to Peter and the disciples.

  * **"I will give to you"**  - This is Jesus speaking to Peter.
  * **keys of the kingdom of heaven **- This refers to the ability to open the way for people to become the people of God. (See: Metonymy)
