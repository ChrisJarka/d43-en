===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I thank my God always, making mention of you in my prayers** - "Every time I pray for you I thank God for you."
  * **I** - Here the word "I" refers to Paul.
  * **you** - Here the word "you" is singular and refers to Philemon. (See: [[en:ta:translate:formsofyou-singular]])
  * **hearing of your love, and of the faith which you have in the Lord Jesus and of your faithfulness toward all the saints** - "I have heard of the faith you have in the Lord Jesus and of your love for all believers."
  * **I pray that the sharing of your faith may be effectual, in the knowledge of every good thing which is in us for Christ.** - This could also be translated as
  * **us** - Here the word "us" refers to all believers. (See: [[en:ta:translate:inclusive]])
  * **the hearts of the saints have been refreshed by you, brother** - "you have encouraged the believers, dear brother in Christ"
  * **hearts** - Here the word "hearts" refers to the strength and the courage of the believers. (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
