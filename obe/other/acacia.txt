====== acacia ======

===== Definition: =====

The acacia is a common tree growing in modern-day Palestine, which was also plentiful there during ancient times.the land of Canaan and Israel. The wood from the acacia tree was very durable and was used It is the primary source of the durable wood used to construct the Ark of the Covenant.

  * The hard orange-brown wood is ideally suited for cabinetmaking.
  * The KJV uses the name Shittah tree rather than acacia.
