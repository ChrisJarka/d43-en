====== 2 Chronicles Chapter 12 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What negative action did Rehoboam take when his reign was established?

A. Rehoboam and all Israel abandoned the law of Yahweh. [12:1]

Q? Why did Shishak, king of Egypt, come up against Jerusalem?

A. Shishak came up against Jerusalem because the people had been unfaithful to Yahweh. [12:2]

Q? What was the response of Rehoboam and the leaders of Judah when Shemaiah the prophet told them that they had been given over into Shishak's hand because they had forsaken Yahweh?

A. Rehoboam and the leaders of Judah humbled themselves and proclaimed that Yahweh is righteous. [12:5-6]
