====== Psalms 044 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 44 is a deliverance psalm. It is a prayer for God's help against enemies. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:deliverer]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Enemies ==
When Israel entered the Promised Land, God fought for  them and drove out their enemies. Since their enemies have now defeated them, they are in need of God's help. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:promisedland]])
