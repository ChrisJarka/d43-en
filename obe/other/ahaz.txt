====== Ahaz ======

===== Definition: =====

Ahaz was a wicked King over Judah from 732 BC to 716 BC, about 140 years prior to the captivity of Israel and Judah by Babylonia.

  * Ahaz was 20 years of age when he started to rule over Judah, and he ruled for 16 years.
  * Ahaz was more loyal to the Assyrians and their gods than to the true God of Abraham.
  * Ahaz had an Assyrian altar built in Judah to reflect the one that the Assyrians used in worship.
