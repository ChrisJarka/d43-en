===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he came up to Jesus **- "Judas came up to Jesus"
  * **and kissed him **- "and greeted him with a kiss"
  * **laid hands on Jesus **- This is a figure of speech that means seized Jesus by force and arrested him. (See: Idiom)
