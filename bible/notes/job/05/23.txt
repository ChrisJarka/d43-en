===== Translation Notes: =====

The writer continues to use parallelism in each of these verses, conveying a single idea using two different statements to emphasize assurance regarding the natural world, the homestead, and one's descendants. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])  

  * **you will have a covenant with the stones in your field** - "your covenant is with the stones of the field" or "there will be harmony between you and the natural world"
  * **you will be at peace with the wild beasts** - "the wild animals will be at peace with you."
  * **wild beasts** - AT: "wild animals" or "untamed animals"
  * **bold words** - explanation
  * **You will know that your tent is in safety** - AT: "You will know that your tent is peaceful"
