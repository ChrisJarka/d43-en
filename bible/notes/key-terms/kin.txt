====== kin, kinsman ======

===== Definition: =====

A "kin" is a person's blood relative. The word "kinsman" refers specifically to a male relative.

  * The term "kin" can refer to a close relative such as a parent, grandparent, or sibling, or a more distant relative such as an aunt, uncle, or cousin.
  * In ancient Israel, if a man died, his brother or a nearest male relative was to marry his widow and support her.
