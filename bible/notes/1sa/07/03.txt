===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **return to Yahweh-**  AT:"start to do what Yahweh says" - If a person is disobeying Yahweh, then they have left Yahweh. When they stop disobeying Yahweh and do what he says, then they are turning around to come back to him.
  * **whole heart**  -AT: "a thorough conviction of mind and will" - In order to perform deeds consistent with that new conviction, it involves a total commitment of the person to do what is the will of Yahweh.
  * **hand of the Philistines**  - AT:"control of the Philistines" - metaphor: This phrase refers to the power and control that the Philistines had over Israel.
