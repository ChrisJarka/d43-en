===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The Word Of Yahwey - **  Israel and Judah had many competing prophets voicing many different message as to who the true God was. Here Micah makes it clear that his vision is not his own, but grounded in the depths of who Yahweh is.
  * **I am Micah from Moresheth **- This is the name of the prophet that will be bring the phrophecy to the people of Israel and Judah. (see: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **The Morashtite **- The town of Morashtite was located in the Shfela lowland region of Judah.
  * **in the days of Jotham, Ahaz, and Hezekiah, kings of Judah**- This portion gives the reader of the length of Micah's ministry roughly from the start of King Jotham (750-731 B.C.), King Ahaz (735-715 B.C.) to King Hezekiah (715-686 B.C.)
  * **the word that he saw concerning Samaria and Jerusalem **-  [[(see:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy )]]
