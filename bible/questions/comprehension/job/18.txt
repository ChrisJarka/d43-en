====== Job Chapter 18 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Bildad answer and say to Job?

A. Bildad answered by telling Job to put an end to Job's talk and consider after they spoke. [18:1 - 2]

Q? What is Bildad asking Job?

A. Bildad is asking Job why Job is regarding them as beasts and that they are stupid. [18:3]
