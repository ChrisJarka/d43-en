===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I**  - The pronoun "I" refers to Paul. (See: [[en:ta:translate:singularpronoun]])
  * **you**  - Each use of "you" or "your" in verses 3-6  refers to the believers in the church at Philippi. (See:  [[en:ta:translate:formsofyou-plural]])
  * **fellowship in the gospel**  - "help in spreading the gospel"
  * **from the first day**  - "from the day you believed in Jesus." (See: [[en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]])
  * **I am confident**  - "I am sure" 
  * **he who**  - "the God who" or "God who"
