====== house of David ======

===== Facts: =====
The "house of David" is another way of speaking about the family or descendants of King David. 

  * Another way of saying this is "the descendants of David."
  * Because Jesus was from the lineage of David he was part of the "house of David."
  * Other "house of" expressions have the same meaning.
