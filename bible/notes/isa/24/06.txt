===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **a curse devours the earth** - The curse that destroys the earth is compared to a wild animal that eats another animal. Alternate translation: "a curse will devour the earth like a wild animal" or "Yahweh will curse the earth (UDB) (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
  * **and its inhabitants are found guilty** - "and Yahweh declares that the people are guilty" (See: [[en:ta:translate:active]])
