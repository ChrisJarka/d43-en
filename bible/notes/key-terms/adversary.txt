====== adversary ======


===== Definition: =====


An adversary is a person or group who is opposed to or against someone or something.


    * An adversary can be an individual person who tries to harm you.
    * An adversary can also be a military opponent, who intends to destroy a rival nation.
    * In the Bible, the devil is referred to as an "adversary".
