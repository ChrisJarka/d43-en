====== 2 Corinthians 01 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

The first paragraph is a reflection of a common way to begin a letter in the ancient Near East. 

== Paul's Defense ==
This begins a major part of the Epistle 1-6. People have been criticizing Paul saying he is not sincere. He counters with his motives for what he is doing.   

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Rhetorical question ==

Paul uses two [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|rhetorical questions]] for the purpose of defending himself against a charge of insincerity.
