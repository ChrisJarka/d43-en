====== Psalms 089 General Notes ======


=== Kind  of psalm ===

Psalm 89 is a worship and a deliverance psalm.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

God is mighty and has promised David an eternal kingdom. But now that kingdom is being destroyed.
