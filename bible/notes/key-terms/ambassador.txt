====== ambassador ======

===== Definition: =====

An ambassador is a diplomatic official who is chosen to represent his country in dealings with a foreign nation. Ambassadors are given the authority to communicate messages on behalf of their home government.

  * As a representative of his country, an ambassador is expected to act in a way that reflects the values and viewpoints of that country.
  * The apostle Paul said that he was an ambassador for Jesus Christ.
  * In the Bible, all Christians are said to be "ambassadors" for Jesus Christ since they represent him in this world.
