===== Translation Notes: =====

Asaph is pleading for God's help.

  * **Call to mind**  - AT: "Remember"
  * **the soul of your dove**  - This refers to the people of Israel. (See: Metaphor)
  * **a wild animal**  - AT: "a savage enemy" (See: Metaphor)
