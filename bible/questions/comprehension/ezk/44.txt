====== Ezekiel Chapter 44 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? To where did the man bring Ezekiel back?

A. The man brought Ezekiel back to the outer sanctuary gate that face east [44:1].

Q? What had been done to the east gate, and why?

A. The east gate had been sealed shut, because Yahweh the God of Israel had come through it [44:1-2].

Q? About what was Ezekiel told to think regarding the house of Yahweh?

A. Ezekiel was told to think about the house of Yahweh's entrance and exits [44:5].

Q? What disgusting actions had the house of Israel done to profane Yahweh's sanctuary?

A. The house of Israel had brought foreigners with uncircumcised hearts and flesh to the sanctuary [44:7].

Q? Why did the house of Israel allow foreigners into Yahweh's sanctuary?

A. The house of Israel allowed foreigners into Yahweh's sanctuary because Israel gave the duty of caring for the sanctuary to them [44:8].

Q? What had the Levites done who wandered away from Yahweh?

A. The Levites that had wandered away from Yahweh had performed sacrifices before their idols [44:10-12].

Q? What does Yahweh declare will happen to the Levites who wandered away from him?

A. Yahweh declares that the Levites who wandered away from him will pay for their sin and will not come near to act as his priests [44:12-13].

Q? Why does Yahweh declare that the sons of Zadok will come near to him and will stand before him?

A. The sons of Zadok will come near and stand before Yahweh because they fulfilled the duties of Yahweh's sanctuary when the people of Israel wandered away [44:15].

Q? What kind of clothes must the priests wear when they come to the gates of the inner courtyard, and why?

A. The priests must wear linen clothes when they come to the gates of the inner courtyard so that they do not sweat [44:17-18].

Q? Who is the priest allowed to marry?

A. The priest is allowed to marry a virgin from the line of the house of Israel, or a widow who was married to a priest [44:22].

Q? Between what will the priests teach the people the difference?

A. The priests will teach the people the difference between the holy and the profane, the unclean from the clean [44:23].

Q? What is the priest's role in a dispute between Israelites?

A. The priest stands to judge between the people with Yahweh's decrees, and must be just [44:24].

Q? Why do the priests not receive any property or inheritance in the land of Israel?

A. The priests do not receive any property or inheritance in the land of Israel because Yahweh is their inheritance and property [44:28].

Q? How will the priests have food to eat?

A. The priests will eat the food offerings, the sin offerings, the guilt offerings, and the best of the firstfruits of all things [44:29-30].
