====== Zephaniah Chapter 03 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What are the princes of the violent city like?

A. The princes of the violent city are like roaring lions. [3:3]

Q? When will Yahweh dispence justice?

A. Yahweh will dispense justice from morning to morning. ]3:5]

Q? What did Yahweh hoped the people would do?

A. Yahweh hoped the people to fear him and accept correction. [3:7]

Q? What decision has Yahweh made about the nations?

A. Yahweh has decided to assemble the nations and pour out his anger and wrath on them. [3:8]

Q? What will Yahweh then call the people to do?

A. Yahweh will call the people to serve him standing shoulder to shoulder. [3:9]
