====== Joshua 10 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

== Conquest of the Promised Land continued (Judges 2-12) ==

Joshua defeats the five cities from the south.


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

"For Yahweh was waging war on behalf of Israel."

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Idiom ==
 
The people of Gibeon use an idiom when they called for help from Israel: "Do not withdraw your hands," meaning do not stop protecting.
