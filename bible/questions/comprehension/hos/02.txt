====== Hosea Chapter 02 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What will Yahweh do if __the prostitute __ does not put away her prostitution?

A. If __the prostitute __ does not put away her prostitution , Yahweh will strip her naked and show her nakedness as on the day that she was born. [2:2-3]

Q? Why will Yahweh not have any mercy on the prostitute's children?

A. Yahweh will not have any mercy on her children, for they were children of prostitution. [2:4]

Q? Why will the prostitute return to her first husband?

A. The prostitute will return to her first husband , for was it better for her then than it was __now__. [2:7]
