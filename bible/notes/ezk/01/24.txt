===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then I heard** – Ezekiel is still talking about the vision.
  * **their wings** - "the wings of the living creatures"
  * **Like the noise of rushing water** - The sound of the creatures' wings was loud just like the noise of rushing water is loud. Alternate translation as a new sentence: "The sound of their wings was loud like rushing water" or "The sound of their wings was very loud." (See: [[en:ta:translate:simile]]) 
  * ... Like the voice of the Almighty.. the sound of a rainstorm...like the sound of an army – Ezek
