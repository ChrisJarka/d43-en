====== Job Chapter 18 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Bildad answer and say to Job?

A. Bildad answered by telling Job to put an end to Job's talk and consider after they spoke. [18:1 - 2]

Q? What is Bildad asking Job?

A. Bildad is asking Job why Job is regarding them as beasts and that they are stupid. [18:3]

Q? What is Bildad telling Job?

A. Bildad is telling Job you tear at yourself in your anger, but should the earth be forsaken or the rocks be removed for you? [18:4]

Q? What is Bildad saying about the wicked person?

A. He is saying their light will be put out, the spark of his fire will not shine, the light will be dark in his tent, and his lamp will be put out. [18:5 - 6]

Q? What does Bildad continue to say about the wicked?

A. The steps of the wicked will be short, his own plans will cast him down, he will be thrown into a net by his own feet, and he will walk into a pitfall. [18:7 - 8]
