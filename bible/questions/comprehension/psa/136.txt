====== Psalms Chapter 136 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? To whom should thanks be given because he is good and his faithfulness endures forever?

A. Yahweh, the God of gods, the Lord of lords should receive thanks. [136:1-3]

Q? Why does his covenvant faithfulness endure forever?

A. He alone does great wonders, made the heavens, spread out the earth above the waters and made great lights; the sun to rule by day and the moon and stars to rule by night. [136:4-9]
