====== Potiphar ======

===== Facts: =====

Potiphar was an important official for the Pharaoh of Egypt d<font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>uring the time when Jacob, his wives, and his children were living in the land of Canaan. Potiphar was the captain of the guard.</font>

  * Potiphar bought Jacob's son Joseph as a slave and appointed him to be in charge of his household.
  * When Joseph was falsely accused of doing wrong, Potiphar had Joseph put in prison.
