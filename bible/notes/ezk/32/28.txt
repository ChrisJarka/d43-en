===== Translation Notes: =====

  * So you will be destroyed in the midst of the uncircumcised! - "You will be killed by the sword in the middle of other heathen and lie next to them."
  * **Edom is there with her kings and all her chieftains - **"Edom is there with her kings and imortant leaders."
  * **who gave their strength to those slain by the sword! - **"Powerful men helped those who were fighting,"
  * **They themselves will lie with the uncircumcised and with the ones going down to the pit!**
  - "but they too, were killed and will go down to the grave with the others."
