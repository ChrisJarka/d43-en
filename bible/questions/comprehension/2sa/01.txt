====== 2 Samuel Chapter 01 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did David do after the death of Saul?

A. After the death of Saul David returned from attacking the Amalakites and remained in Ziklag for two days. [1:1]

Q? Where did the man with torn clothes and dirt on his head come from?

A. The man came from Saul's camp. He escaped from the camp of Israel. [1:2-3]

Q? What did the young man say about Saul and Jonathan?

A. The young man said Saul and Johnathan his son were dead. [1:4]
