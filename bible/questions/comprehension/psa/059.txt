====== Psalms Chapter 059 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? How does David ask God to rescue him from his enemies?

A. David asks God to set him on high away from those who rise up against him. [59:1]

Q? Who gather themsleves together against David?

A. The mighty evildoers gather themselves together against David. [59:3]

Q? Who does David ask Yahweh to not be merciful to?

A. David asks, "do not be merciful to any wicked transgressors." [59:5]

Q? How does David expect God to meet him?

A. God will meet me with his covenant faithfulness. [59:10]
