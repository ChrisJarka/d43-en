====== Deuteronomy Chapter 05 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? When Moses called all Israel what did he say to them?

A. He said for them to listen to the statutes and the decrees that he would speak to them that day, so that they may learn and keep them. Yahweh did not make this covenant with their ancestors but with them. [5:1-3]

Q? Why did Moses stand between Yahweh and the people when Yahweh reveled his word to them?

A. Because they were afraid of the fire from which God was speaking. [5:4-5]

Q? Who does Yahweh say he is?

A. He says, "I am Yahweh your God, who brought you out of the land of Egypt, out of the house of slavery." [5:6]

Q? Why should the people not have any other gods before Yahweh?

A. Because Yahweh says I am Yahweh your God. [5:6-7]

Q? What does God say they should not make, bow down to, or serve?

A. They should not make carved figures or anything like that is in heaven, earth or in the water. [5:7-8]

Q? Yahweh says he is a jealous god. What will he do if the people bow down or serve anyone but him?

A. Yahweh will punish the ancestors' wickedness by bringing punishment on the children's children. [5:9]

Q? What will Yahweh do for those who love him and keep his commandments?

A. He will show covenant faithfulness to them. [5:10]

Q? Whom will Yahweh not hold guiltless?

A. Those who take his name in vain. [5:11]

Q? What does Yahweh say about the seventh day after they had worked for six days?

A. He wants the people, including their families, servants, and visiting foreigners, to observe the Sabbath day and keep it holy, by not doing any work. [5:12-14]

Q? Why does Yahweh command them to keep the Sabbath day?

A. He wants them to remember that they were a servant in the land of Egypt and that God brought them out by his mighty hand his display of power. [5:15]

Q? Why did Yahweh command the people to honor their father and mother?

A. He commanded them to honor them so that they may live a long time in the land that God gave them and so that it may go well with them. [5:16]

Q? What were they commanded not to do?

A. They should not murder, commit adultery, steal, give false witness against their neighbor, and not covet his wife, house, field, servants, animals, or anyhing that belongs to him. [5:17-21]

Q? These words were spoken out of the fire by Yahweh. What did God do then?

A. He wrote them down on two tablets of stone, gave them to Moses and did not add any more words. [5:22]
