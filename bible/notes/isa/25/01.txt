===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I will exalt you, I will praise your name** - Here "your name" refers to Yahweh. I will honor and praise you, Yahweh. (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  * **in perfect faithfulness** -  "you have done them as you said you would"
  * **you have made the city a heap** - "you have caused cities to become heaps of rubble"
  * **a fortified city** - "a city with strong walls around it"
  * **a fortress of strangers into no city** - "you have caused palaces in foreign countries to disappear; they will never be rebuilt"
  * **a city of ruthless nations will fear you** - "people in nations whose leaders show mercy to no one will revere you"
