====== forsaken ======

===== Definition: =====

Forsaken is the past tense of the word forsake, meaning "to abandon" or "to leave".

  * The Bible tells us that God will never forsake those who have believed in Jesus.
  * In the gospels the disciples had forsaken their occupations and fishing nets to follow Jesus.
  * The biblical prophets frequently describe the people of Israel as forsaken by God because they worshiped idols and not him.
