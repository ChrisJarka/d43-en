===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Place your burdens on Yahweh** - Here the author is speaking to other godly people. People's troubles are spoken of as if they were burdens that they have to carry. Trusting God to help us when we have troubles is spoken of as putting our burdens on him so that he can carry them for us. AT: "Give Yahweh your problems and he will carry them for you" or "Trust God to help you with all your troubles like someone who trusts a stronger person to carry his load " (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **he will sustain you** - Taking care of someone or helping someone when he has troubles is spoken of as supporting. AT: "he will take care of you" or "he will help you"
  * **a righteous person to totter** - A person who is about to be severely harmed by some sort of disaster is spoken of as if he is tottering or swaying and is about to fall down. AT: "a righteous person to sway and fall down" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **But you, God** - The author turns his attention to speaking to God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_apostrophe]])
  * **will bring the wicked down into the pit of destruction** - This means that God will cause them to die and go where evil people go when they die. AT: will bring the wicked down into Sheol"
  * **the wicked** - AT: "wicked people"
  * **bloodthirsty and deceitful men** - A person who is eager to kill people is spoken of as if he wanted to drink blood. AT: "people who are deceitful and want to kill others" or "deceitful murderers" 
  * 
  * **half as long as others** - AT: "half as long as other people live" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
