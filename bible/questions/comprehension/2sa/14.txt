====== 2 Samuel Chapter 14 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Joab do after he perceived that the king desired to see Absalom?

A. After Joab perceived that the king desired to see Absalom, Joab sent word to Tekoa and had a wise woman brought to him. [14:1]]

Q? Briefly what did Joab want the wise woman to do?

A. Joab wanted the wise woman to pretend to be a mourner and to go speak to the king the words Joab told her to. [14:2-3]
