===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The Word of Yahweh came to me and said** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:16|3:16]].
  * **Son of man** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:02:01|2:3]].
  * **Set your face against** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:04:01|4:3]].
  * **the mountains of Israel**  - "the mountains in the land of Israel"
  * **prophesy to them** - God tells Ezekiel to speak to the mountains as though they were human beings. The message is for the people of Israel, but is spoken to the mountains to make it more powerful. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:personification|Personification]])
  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:05:05|5:5]].
  * **Behold** - This can be translated as “Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you.”
  * **I am bringing a sword** - This means that God is going to use war to punish Israel. "I am bringing war" (See: [[:en:ta:translate:metonymy|Metonymy]])
