===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Adah and Zillah, listen to my voice; you wives of Lamech, listen to what I say** - Lamech said basically the same thing twice to show how important it was that his wives listen to him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Adah ... Zillah** - Translate these names as in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:04:18|4:19]].
  * **a man for wounding me, a young man for bruising me** - Refers to only one person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **for wounding me** - "because he wounded me" or "because he hurt me"
  * **If Cain is avenged seven times, then Lamech** - Lamech knows that God will avenge Cain seven times. AT: "God avenges Cain seven times, so Lamech" or "Since God will punish anyone who kills Cain seven times, Lamech" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **then Lamech will be avenged seventy-seven times** - Lamech speaks of himself as if he were someone else to show that he thinks of himself as very important. "then I, the great Lamech, will be avenged seventy-seven times." This can be made active. AT: "God will punish whoever kills me, the great Lamech, seventy-seven times" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
