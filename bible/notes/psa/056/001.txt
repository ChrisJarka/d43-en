===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[en:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **For the chief musician**  - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:psa:004:001|4:1]].
  * **Jonath elem rehokim **  - This may refer to a style of music. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **A Psalm of David**  - This can also be written as a sentence. AT: "This is a psalm of David" or "This is a psalm that David wrote"
  * **michtam**  - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:psa:016:001|16:1]] 
  * **when the Philistines took him in Gath** - "when the Philistines captured him in Gath"
  * **someone wishes**  - "my enemy wishes"
  * **he fights**  - The word "he" refers to "my enemy" or "David's enemy"
  * **there are many**  - "many of them" or "many of my enemies"
