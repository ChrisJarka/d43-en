===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **cry** - Alternate Translation: "wail" or "call for help" 
    * **against any of the Israelites not even a dog will bark** - an Idiom meaning "be completely safe." Alternate Translation: "The Israelites will all be completely safe". (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
    * **dog** - a 4-footed animal covered in fur which usually lived in the streets
    * **bark** - the noise that a dog makes
    * **your servants** - "your" refers to Pharaoh, the king of Egypt
    * **come down to me** - "me" refers to Moses, who is speaking
    * **I will go out** - "I" refers to Moses, who is speaking
    * **he went out from** - "he" refers to Moses
