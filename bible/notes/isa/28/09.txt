===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **To whom will he teach knowledge, and to whom will he explain the message?** - They were not expecting an answer to this question. The question was used as sarcasm to ridicule Yahweh. (See: Rhetorical Question) 
    * **To those who are weaned from milk or to those just taken from the breasts?** - This is also a Rhetorical Question intended to ridicule Yahweh. Does Yahweh think that they are little children who have recently been weaned?
