===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **in that same region**  - That is, "in that area" or, "near Bethlehem."
    * **keeping watch over**  - That is, "taking care of" or, "guarding in order to keep them safe."
    * **at night**  - That is, after the sun had set and it was dark.
    * **glory**-Could perhaps be translated as prestige or honor. Same OL word used when Satan showed Jesus the world in all its' glory.
    * **of the Lord**  - That is, "from the Lord" or, "who serves the Lord" or, "who was sent by the Lord."
    * **flocks of sheep**  - That is, "groups of sheep."
    * **appeared**  - That is, "came."
    * **they were very afraid**- That is, "they became very afraid." The shepherds reacted to the sudden appearance of an angel of the Lord with great fear.
