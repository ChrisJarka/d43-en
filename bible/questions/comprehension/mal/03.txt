====== Malachi Chapter 03 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? After Yahweh sent his messenger of the covenant who would prepare his way, what would happen?

A. The Lord, whom Israel sought, would suddenly come to his temple [3:1].

Q? When would the offering of Judah and Jerusalem be pleasing to Yahweh?

A. It would be pleasing after the purification of the sons of Levi, when they were refined like gold and silver [3:3-4].

Q? After the offerings of Judah and Jerusalem were pleasing to Yahweh through the purification of Levi's sons, would would Yahweh do?

A. He would draw near to Israel for judgment and be a swift witness against those who did not honor him [3:3-5].

Q? Even though the people of Jacob had turned aside from Yahweh's ordinance from the days of their fathers, why had they not been consumed?

A. They had not been consumed because Yahweh does not change [3:6-7].
