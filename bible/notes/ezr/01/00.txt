====== Ezra 01 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

The story of the first Jews to return from Persia to Judea (Ezra 1-2)

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

King Cyrus allowed them to return because he wanted them to rebuild the temple. Those who stayed behind gave gifts to those who were going to enable their journey and resettlement.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metonymy ==

" Cyrus' voice went out over his entire kingdom" meaning his command/law was told everywhere in his kingdom. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
