====== armor ======

===== Definition: =====

Armor is a soldiers full defensive, combat equipment. A helmet, breastplate, and coverings for legs and feet are among armor parts.

  * Armor is used figuratively by Paul in Ephesians to describe all the resources God provides those who accept his command.
  * God's armor to fight Satan include truth, righteousness, the gospel of peace, faith, salvation, and the Spirit.
  * Although it is provided by God, it must be put on by men if temptation and evil are to be overcome.
