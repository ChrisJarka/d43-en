===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **but my heart was awake** - AT: "but my mind was awake." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **undefiled** - "flawless," "perfect," or "upright"
  * **my dove**  - The word "dove" refers to a small, gentle wild bird. The one he loves isn't a real bird. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **my head is wet with dew, my hair with the night's dampness** - This is repeated for emphasis and for the beauty of the poetry used here. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **dew** - drops of water or mist that form during the cool of the night when the temperature drops
