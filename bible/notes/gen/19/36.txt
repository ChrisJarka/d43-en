===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **were pregnant by their father**  - "got pregnant from their father" or "became pregnant by their father" or "conceived children with their father"
  * **He became**  - "He is"
  * **the Moabites of today**  - "the Moab people who are now living." The word "today" refers to when the author of Genesis was living. Many years after Lot's family lived and died, the author was born and wrote this.
  * **the children of Ammon** - "the descendants of Ammon" or "the Ammon people"
