====== Twelve Tribes of Israel ======

===== Definition: =====

The twelve tribes of Israel refer to the twelve sons of Jacob <font 14.4444446563721px line-height: 21.7777786254883px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>and their descendants,</font>

  * Jacob was the grandson of Abraham, and God changed Jacob's name to Israel. Both the name "Jacob" and the name "Israel" refer to the same person.
  * When the people of Israel took possession of the land of Canaan, it was divided among these twelve sons, or tribes.
