===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you spend the night**  - "you camp in the scrub"
  * **caravans of Dedanites**  - These were the people traveling in groups from the town of Dedan in Northwest Arabia.
  * **inhabitants of the land of Tema**  - "people who live in the city of Tema in northwest Arabia"
  * **fugitives**  - "refugees"
  * **for they have fled from the sword, from the drawn sword**  - "they are fleeing in order not to be killed by their enemies' swords"
  * **from the bent bow, and from the weight of war**  - "and not to be shot in battles by arrows"
