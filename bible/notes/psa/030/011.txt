===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You have**  - The words "You have" appears twice in verse 11, both refer to Yahweh.
  * **removed my sackcloth **  - Sackcloth is clothing worn during mourning.
  * **clothed me with gladness **  - This represents how God replaces our sadness with joy.  (See: metaphor)
  * **So now my honored heart will sing praise **  - AT: "So now my glory will sing praise"
