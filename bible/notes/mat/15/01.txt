===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **violate- **"go against the Law of Moses"
  * **our ancestors-**  This is all Jewish people.
  * **they do not wash their hands- **"They" are Jesus disciples**.**
  * **Jesus said to them-**  Jesus is speaking to the Pharisees and scribes only.
  * **And you-why do you-**  You refers to Pharisees and scribes
  * **for the sake of your traditions**-to follow what your ancestors taught you..
