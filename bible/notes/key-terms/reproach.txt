====== reproach ======

===== Definition: =====

To reproach someone means to criticize or disapprove of that person's character or behavior.

  * <font 14px line-height: 1.4/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The noun "reproach" can mean criticism or disapproval.</font>
  * <font 14px line-height: 1.4/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The expression "above reproach" means that there is little or nothing to criticize about that person.</font>
  * <font 14px line-height: 1.4/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>As either a noun or verb, "reproach" can be something we receive as a result of our actions or words.</font>
  * The word "reproach" can also mean "shame" or "disgrace" and these are alternate ways it could be translated.
