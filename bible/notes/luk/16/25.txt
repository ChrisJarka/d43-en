===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (Jesus continues telling his story.)
    * **Child**  - This could also be translated as "Son." The rich man was one of Abraham's descendants.
    * **good things** - That is, "fine things" or "pleasant things."
    * **in like manner evil things** - This can also be translated as, "received evil things" or "received things that caused him to suffer."
    * **now he is comforted here** - That is, "now he is receiving comfort."
    * **agony** - That is, "acute pain."
    * **And besides all this** - That is, "regardless of this reason" or "in addition to this reason."
    * **a great chasm has been firmly placed** - This can also be translated as, "there is a great chasm between you and us."
    * **a great chasm**  - That is "a steep, deep and wide valley" or "a big separation" or "a huge ravine" (UDB).
    * **those who want to cross over** - That is, "those people who want to cross over the chasm" or "if any want to cross over."
