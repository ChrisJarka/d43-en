====== Pharisee ======


===== Definition: =====


The Pharisees were an important group of Israel's religious leaders in Jesus' time.


    * Of all the Jewish leaders, the Pharisees were the most strict in obeying the Laws of Moses and other Jewish laws and traditions.
    * The Pharisees believed in life after death; they also believed in the existence of angels and other spiritual beings.
    * They were the religious group that was the most active in opposing Jesus and the early Christians.


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:term|Jewish leaders, Law of Moses]])
