===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **rule **– This could also be translated “have authority”.
  * **your **– This pronoun refers to all the believers at Colossae.
  * **you **– This pronoun refers to all the believers at Colossae.
  * **live **– This could be translated “dwell” or “reside”.
  * **admonish **– This could be translated “gently warn” or “caution”.
  * **bold words**  - explanation**With**** all wisdom teach and admonish one another **– This could read “Teach and admonish one another with all wisdom”.
  * **him **– This pronoun refers to the Lord Jesus.
