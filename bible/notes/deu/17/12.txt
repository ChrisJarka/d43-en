===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **anyone who acts arrogantly - **  See how you translated the term in 1:43 and 18:20.
  * **you will put away the evil from Israel**  - Possible meanings of 'the evil' are 1) that scandal, bad example or 2) that evil person who ignore judicial sentence or advice.
  * **and act arrogantly no more**  - this echoes the front phrase, but in negative sense (See: Parallelism, Litotes)
