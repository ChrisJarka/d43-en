====== unleavened bread ======

===== Definition: =====

Bread that is unleavened has been baked without yeast.

  * Unleavened bread was used as ordinary food and was required for certain Jewish rites.
  * Only unleavened bread was used for Passover along with bitter herbs.
  * It symbolized the haste in which Israel departed from Egypt because they did not have time to bake the bread by    adding yeast.  
  * Baked cereal offerings made at the altar in the temple always had to be unleavened.
  * The apostle Paul used the term bread without yeast to symbolize the church being sincere and truthful.
