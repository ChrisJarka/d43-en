===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **All the land offering** - "Altogether the land offering"
  * **will measure** - "will be a square measuring"
  * **cubits** - See how you translated these "long" cubits in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:05|40:05]].
  * **twenty-five thousand cubits** - about 13.5 kilometers. (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaldistance]])
  * **In this way you will make the holy offering of land, together with the land for the city.** - "You will offer the holy offering and also the property of the city"
  * **the holy offering** - the land that the people of Israel gave to Yahweh for the Levites, the priests, and the temple
