====== Psalms Chapter 015 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? How does the person who may stay in Yahweh's tabernacle walk?

A. He walks blamelessly. [15:1-2]

Q? What does this person not do with his tongue?

A. He does not slander with his tongue. [15:3]

Q? Who does this person despise?

A. He despises the worthless person. [15:4]

Q? Who does this person honor?

A. He honors those who fear Yahweh. [15:4]

Q? When this person swears to his own disadvantage, what does he not do?

A. He does not take back his promises. [15:4]

Q? When this person lends money, what does he not do?

A. He does not charge interest. [15:5]

Q? What does this person not take bribes to do?

A. He does not take bribes to testify against the innocent. [15:5]

Q? What will never happen to the person who does these things?

A. He will never be shaken. [15:5]
