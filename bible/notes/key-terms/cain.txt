====== Cain ======

===== Facts: =====

Cain was the son of Adam and Eve and the brother of Abel. Cain killed Abel in a fit of jealousy, after God accepted Abel's sacrifice, which was pleasing to God, and God rejected Cain's sacrifice, which was not pleasing to God.
