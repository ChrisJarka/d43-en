===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **A declaration about Dumah** - "A message from Yahweh about Edom"
  * **one calls to me from Seir** - "someone from Edom has been calling me"
  * **Watchman, what is left of the night? Watchman, what is left of the night?** - This is asking how much longer until Babylon is destroyed. (See: [[en:ta:translate:doublet]])
  * **also the night** - "it will soon be night again"
  * **if you will inquire, inquire, and then come back again** - "come back and ask again"
