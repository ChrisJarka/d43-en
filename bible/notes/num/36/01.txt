===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Then the leaders of their ancestor's families in the clan founded by Gilead** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Then the leaders of the families in the clan of Gilead" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Machir** - See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:num:26:28|26:29]].
  * **You were commanded by Yahweh** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh commanded you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **to give the share of Zelophehad our brother to his daughters** - Zelophehad's daughters received the land since he died and had no sons. See 27:1-8.
  * **Zelophehad** - See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:num:26:33|26:33]].
