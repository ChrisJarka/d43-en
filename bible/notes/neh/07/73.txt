===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **So the priests, the Levites, the gatekeepers, the singers, some of the people, the temple servants, and all Israel, lived in their cities. By the seventh month the people of Israel were settled in their cities** - This is a figure of speech where two phrases or sentences say the same thing in a different way. AT: "The people of Israel were settled in their cities by the seventh month." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
