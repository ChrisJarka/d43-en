===== Translation Notes: =====

Chapters 5-7 are a single event. Jesus went up on a hill and taught his disciples.

  * **He opened his mouth**  - "Jesus began to speak"
  * **taught them**  - Jesus taught his disciples.
  * **"Blessed are the poor in spirit"**  - "The poor in spirit are blessed" or "God blesses those who are poor in spirit."
  * **"blessed are"**  - Possible meanings are 1) "God causes good things to happen to" or 2) "God is pleased with." (UDB).
  * **"the poor in spirit"**  - These are people who are humble or without selfish pride.
  * **"those who mourn"**  - These are people who are sad because of sin.
  * **"they will be comforted"**  - This could be translated with an active phrase: "God will comfort them." (See TAlink: Active)
