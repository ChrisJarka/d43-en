===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to teach his disciples. This event began in 5:1.

  * **let him**  - the pronoun refers to the one who takes away your coat.
  * **whoever **- 'Whoever' can be any person.
  * **one mile **-This <font 14px line-height: 17.8181819915772px; background-color: rgb(238, 238, 238);/Lato, sans-serif;;inherit;;rgb(51, 51, 51) font-family: Lato, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 17.8181819915772px; background-color: rgb(238, 238, 238);>This was the distance a Roman soldier cour force someone to carry something for him.</font>
  * **with him**  - this refers to the one who compels you to go.
  * **go with him two**  - This means to go twice the distance one was forced to go.
