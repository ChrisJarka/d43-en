===== translationNotes: =====

  * **all Israel was recorded in genealogies** - This can be translated in active form. The reader should understand that the people who wrote the genealogies included the names of the people who had already died. AT: "The Israelites recorded all of themselves in genealogies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **genealogies** - family records that name ancestors and descendants
  * **They were recorded in the book ... Israel** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "They wrote the names in the book ... Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the book of the kings of Israel** - This refers to a book that no longer exists.
  * **they were carried away in exile** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "The Babylonians carried them away in exile" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
