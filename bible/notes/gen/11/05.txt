===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **came down **- from his normal place in heaven. This describes God in human terms, to help us understand him.
  * **to see**  - "to observe"
  * **one peopleâ€¦ same language**  - The people were part of a single cultural group that spoke the same language.
  * **they are beginning to do this**  - alternate: "they are beginning to make this happen"
  * **soon, nothing will be impossible**  - if a double negative is uncomfortable in your language, try: "soon, anything may be possible"
  * **let us go down**  - God is speaking either to the angels or to himself as the Trinity.
  * **confuse**  - This could also be translated "mixed up" and describes how, by God's intervention, people began to speak different languages.
  * **their language**  - the language they spoke before God confused their language.
