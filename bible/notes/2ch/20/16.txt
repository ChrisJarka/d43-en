===== translationNotes: =====

  * **go down against ... Go out against** - This is an idiom that means to go and meet the enemy army for battle. AT: "march against ... March out against" or "Go to battle against ... Go to battle against" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **See, they are** - "Indeed, they are" The word "see" is used here as an idiom to draw attention to what is said next. The people cannot currently see the enemy army. AT: "They are" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **pass of Ziz** - This is a narrow valley between two mountains southeast of Jerusalem. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the rescue of Yahweh** - The word "rescue" can be expressed as a verb. AT: "Yahweh rescue you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **Judah and Jerusalem** - Here these places represent the people from them. AT: "people of Judah and Jerusalem" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
