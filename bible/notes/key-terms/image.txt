====== image, graven image ======

===== Definition: =====

Image is an actual or mental picture of somebody or something.  Graven image is a physical object that is said to be a picture of what a god looks like, such as a carving of a man or animal.

  * The creation of images in the Old Testament to represent God was forbidden.
  * Man is created in the image of God, meaning man has some of the divine characteristics, such as having a spirit that continues to live eternally after natural death.
  * Jesus is the image of the invisible God, meaning that He is the exact representation of God in human form.
