====== possession ======

===== Definition: =====

A possession is something that belongs, or is owned, by a person or persons.

  * Possession sometimes refers to permanent ownership and occupation of the land God promised to the nation of Israel.
  * Possession can refer to the influence of Satan over a person.
  * It also refers to obtaining victory or property as in “possess the gates of their enemies.”
  * God's people are also referred to as His possession, meaning they belong to Him in a covenant relationship where He is exclusively their God and they are exclusively His people.

(See also: other)
