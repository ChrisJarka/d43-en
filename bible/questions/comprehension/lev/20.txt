====== Leviticus Chapter 20 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What will happen to anyone among the people of Israel who gives their child to Molech?

A. Anyone who gives their child to Molech will be put to death. [20:2]

Q? What will Yahweh do to the man if the people do not put him to death?

A. If the people do not put him to death, Yahweh says he will cut him off from his people. [20:3]

Q? Who does Yahweh tell the people not to turn to?

A. Yahweh told the people not to talk with those who talk to the dead or the spirits. [20:6]

Q? What is the result of a man having sexual relations with another man?

A. A man who has sexual relations with another man has done something detestable and both should be put to death. [20:13]

Q? If a man or woman have sexual relations with an animal, what should be done with them?

A. The man, the woman, and the animal must all be put to death. [20:15,16]
