====== slay, slain, to slay ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "slay" is another word for "kill." Usually it means to kill a person or animal in a forceful or violent way.

  * The phrase "the slain" refers to slain people, that is, people who have been killed by force.
  * The Bible says that "Abraham took out his knife to slay his son Isaac" but the angel of the Lord stopped him.
  * The prophet Ezekiel said that God would slay those who worship idols.
