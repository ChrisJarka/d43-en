====== Numbers Chapter 33 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why were the people of Israel able to openly leave Rameses without opposition from the Egyptians?

A. The people of Israel faced no opposition from the Egyptians because the Egyptians were busy burying all their firstborn. [33:3-4]

Q? Why did the people of Israel choose to camp at Elim?

A. The people of Israel choose to camp at Elim because there were twelve springs of water and seventy palm trees at Elim. [33:9]

Q? What important resource was lacking when the people of Israel camped at Rephidim?

A. There was no water found for drinking when the people of Israel camped at Rephidim. [33:14]

Q? What significant loss happened when the people of Israel were at Mount Hor?

A. Aaron the priest went up to Mount Hor at Yahweh's command and he died there forty years after the people of Israel had come out of Egypt. [33:38-39]

Q? What clear command did Yahweh give to the people of Israel through Moses?

A. Yahweh made it clear to the people of Israel that they must drive out all of the land's inhabitants and destroy all their carved figures and shrines. [33:50-52]

Q? How did Yahweh want the people of Israel to divide up the land which they were to possess?

A. Yahweh wanted the people of Israel to divide up the land by lot giving a larger share of land to the large clans and a small share of land to the small clans according to their ancestor's tribes. [33:53-54]

Q? What would be the consequence if the people of Israel did not drive out the land's inhabitants?

A. If the people of Israel did not drive out the land's inhabitants, then the people whom they allowed to stay would become like objects in their eyes and thorns in their sides to make life difficult for them and Yahweh would do to the peple of Israel what he intended to do to the inhabitants of the land. [33:55-56]
