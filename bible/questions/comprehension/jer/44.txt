====== Jeremiah Chapter 44 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What was the word that came to Jeremiah concerning all the judeans who lived in the land of Egypt?

A. The word that came to Jeremiah from Yahweh says this: ' You yourselves have seen all the disasters that I brought on Jerusalem and all the cities of Judah. See, they are ruins today; there is no one to live in them. This is because of the wicked things they did to offend me by going to burn incense and to worship other gods. These were gods that neither they themselves, nor you, nor your ancestors knew. So I repeatedly sent all of my servants the prophets to them. I sent them to say, 'Stop doing these abominable things that I hate.' [44:2-4]

Q? What did the judeans do after Jeremiah gave this word of the Lord?

A. They did not listen; they refused to pay attention or turn from their wickedness. [44:5]

Q? What did Yahweh do when the judeans did not listen and refused to pay attention or turn from their wickedness?

A. Yahweh's fury and his wrath were poured out and kindled a fire in the cities of Judah and the streets of Jerusalem. They became ruins and devastations. [44:6]

Q? What does Yahweh ask the people?

A. Yahweh asks the people "Why are you doing great wickedness against yourselves? Why are you causing yourselves to be cut off from among Judah - men and women, children and babies? [44:7]

Q? How did they offend Yahweh?

A. They offended Yahweh with the deeds of their hands, by burning incense to other gods in the land of Egypt. [44:8]

Q? What did Yahweh say was going to happen to them in Egypt?

A. Yahweh said they are going there so that they will be destroyed and be a curse and a reproach among all the nations. [44:8]

Q? What is Yahweh asking the people about?

A. Yahweh is asking the people if they have forgotten the wickedness committed by their ancestors and by the kings of Judah and their wives. He is also asking them have you forgotten the evil committed by yourselves and your wives. [44:9]

Q? How do the people respond to Yahweh?

A. They are not humbled, they do not honor Yahweh's law or decrees and they do not walk in them. [44:10]
