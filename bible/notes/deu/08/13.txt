===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues to tell the children of Israel Yahweh's words as if the Israelites are one person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
 
  * **your herds and your flocks** - "your herds of cattle and your flocks of sheep and goats"
  * **multiply** - "increase in number" or "become numerous"
  * **all that you have multiplies** - "you have a lot more things" or "you have a lot more possessions"
  * **your heart might become lifted up** - This is an idiom. AT: "you might become proud" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **out of the house of bondage** - Here "house of bondage" refers to the time they were slaves in Egypt. AT: "out from the place where you were slaves" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
