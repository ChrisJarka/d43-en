====== royal ======

===== Definition: =====

Those things which pertain to a king, queen, prince, or princess.

  * Examples of things that are royal are luxurious clothing, food, palaces, and transportation.
  * Israel's King David lived in a royal palace that was larger and more refined than all other buildings in Jerusalem.

(See also: other)
