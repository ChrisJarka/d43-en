====== integrity ======

===== Definition: =====

Honest with strong moral principles especially when nobody else is observing.

* In the book of Genesis Joseph displayed integrity when he would not consent to immorality with Potiphar's wife.

* The book of Proverbs says that it is better to be poor and have integrity than to be rich and have corrupt values.
