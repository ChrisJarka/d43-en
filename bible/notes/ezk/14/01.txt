===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **came to me and sat before me - **"came to me and sat down in front of me" (UDB)
  * **men have taken idols into their heart - **  "men worship idols, and they are allowing idols to induce them to sin." (UDB)
  * **before their face - **  Alternate translation: "in front of them."
  * **be inquired of **  - "should I answer them" (UDB)
  * **house of Israel - ** The phrase “house of Israel” refers to the entire nation of Israel and could be translated as the “people of Israel”
