====== 1 Samuel Chapter 19 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why did Jonathan tell David to be on his guard and hide himself?

A. Saul was seeking to kill David, therefore Jonathan told him be on guard in the morning and hide himself in a secret place. [19:2]

Q? What did Jonathan tell Saul that David's deeds brought to him?

A. Jonathan told Saul that David's deeds had brought Saul good. [19:4]

Q? What did Saul swear after listening to Jonathan?

A. Saul listened to Jonathan and swore that as Yahweh lives, David will not be put to death. [19:6]

Q? What happened when David again went out and fought with the Philistines?

A. David fought with the Philistines and defeated them with a great slaughter. [19:8]

Q? Why did Saul send messagers to David's house to watch him?

A. Saul sent messengers to David’s house to watch him that he might kill him in the morning. [19:11]

Q? How did Michal help David when he fled and escaped?

A. Michal let David down through the window. He went and fled, and escaped. [19:12]

Q? What did Saul plan to do when he ordered for David to be brought to him in the bed?

A. Saul said for the messagers to bring David up to him in the bed, so that he could kill him. [19:15]

Q? What did Michal use at the head of the household idol in the bed to make it look like David?

A. The household idol was in the bed along with a pillow of goats’ hair at its head. [19:16]

Q? When David fled and escaped, who did he go to see in Ramah?

A. David fled and escaped, and went to Samuel in Ramah. [19:18]

Q? What happened to the messagers of Saul that caused them to prophesy?

A. The Spirit of God came on the messengers of Saul, and they also prophesied. [19:20]

Q? After sending messagers for a third time what did Saul do?

A. Saul sent messengers again the third time, then he also went to Ramah. [19:21-22]

Q? How long did Saul lay naked before Saul?

A. He also prophesied before Samuel and lay naked all that day and all that night. [19:24]
