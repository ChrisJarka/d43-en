====== overseer ======


===== Definition: =====

A term used in the New Testament for church leaders and also for Jesus as the Head of the church in the book of 1 Peter.

  * The word is used several times in the Old Testament to refer to someone who is in charge of getting a job done or those who held positions of authority.
