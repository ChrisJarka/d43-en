====== behold ======


===== Definition: =====


The word "behold" means to look at something, with an additional emphasis on understanding what you are seeing.


    * Although "behold" literally means "see" or "look," it is used in a special way to point to something important that will be said next or that someone should pay special attention to.
    * Often "behold" is used to announce a new event or a new part of a story such as the introduction of a new character.
    * Depending on the context, some of the ways this word might be translated would be, "look" or <font 12.6666669845581px/arial;;#000000;;#ffffff>"listen" or</font> "pay attention to this" or "take note of this."
    * Other languages might have a special word like this that functions in a similar way.
