===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **foot of the mountain** - "base of the mountain" or "bottom of the mountain"
  * **Moses took half of the blood and put it into basins; he sprinkled the other half onto the altar** - This confirms the covenant between God and the people of Israel. The blood in the basin represents the people of Israel. The blood splashed on the altar represents God.
