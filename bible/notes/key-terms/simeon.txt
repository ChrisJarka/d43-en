====== Simeon ======

===== Facts: =====

In the Old Testament Simeon was the second son of Jacob and Leah.

  * Simeon fathered the tribe or people group called by the same name.
  * The tribe of Simeon occupied a territory in the southern part of Israel's territories; in conjunction with the tribe of Judah.
