===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **The good man** - "The morally good person." The word "man" here refers to both male and female.
    * **from the good treasure of his heart** - "from among the good things in his heart." This is a metaphor. The good things that a person thinks are compared to valuable things that people store carefully in a safe place. (See: [[en:ta:figuresofspeech:metaphor|Metaphor]])
    * **brings forth that which is good** -  "brings out good things" or "produces life that is morally good, or "lives his life in a morally good way."
    * **the evil man**  -  "the morally evil person." 
    * **from the evil treasure of his heart**- That is, "out of what is morally evil in his heart," or "from the evil that person keeps inn his heart."
    * **brings forth that which is evil* -  "produces life that is morally evil," or "lives his life in a morally evil way."
    * **For from the abundance of his heart  his mouth speaks**  - "For his mouth speaks about the things that his heart is full of." or "What is in a person's heart affects what he says." the That is, "What a person keeps in his heart is what a person will talk about," or "what a person highly values in his heart will show itself in how he speaks."
