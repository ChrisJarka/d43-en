====== cast out, throw out ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "cast" means the same thing as "throw." To "cast out" or "throw out" someone or something means to force that person or thing to go away.

  * <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>In a figurative sense, "cast out" or "cast away" can mean to reject someone.</font>
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>To "cast out demons" could be translated as "force the demons to leave" or "drive the evil spirits out" or "expel the demons."</font>
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Depending on the context, other ways to translate this could be, "force out" or "send away" or "get rid of" or "drive out."</font>
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>To cast a net means to throw the net into the water.</font>
