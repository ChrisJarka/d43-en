====== Asherah ======

===== Facts: =====
Asherah was the false goddess spoken about in the Old Testament usually from the nation of Phoenicia and Assyria. 

  * Asherah was the goddess of motherhood and fertility.
  * The idol of Asherah was built out of wood. 
  * In the Bible the name Asherah is sometimes made plural to be "Asherahs" this is speaking about the small wooden idols of the goddess each family would have in their home.
