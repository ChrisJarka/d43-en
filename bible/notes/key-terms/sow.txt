====== sow, sower ======

===== Definition: =====

To sow means to put seeds in the ground in order to grow plants. A sower is a person who sows.

The method of sowing or planting seeds varies, but one way is to take handfuls of seeds and scatter them on the ground.

The word "sow" can also be translated as "plant."  And "sower" can be translated as "planter" or "farmer."
