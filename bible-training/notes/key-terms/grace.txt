====== grace, favor ======


===== Definition: =====


Grace is showing kindness to someone by doing kind things for them or giving them gifts. Grace is an attribute of God.


    * Grace can refer to God's willingness to show kindness to someone or to the actual act of showing kindness to someone.
    * God gives his grace freely. It is not something that people can earn.
    * Grace can also be translated as, kindness, favor, or good will.
