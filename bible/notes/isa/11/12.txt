===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He will set up a banner for the nations** - The banner is the symbol of power. AT: "Yahweh will set up the Messiah like a flag for the nations to see" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **and those hostile to Judah will be cut off** - In this context, this expression refers to the people of Israel, the northern kingdom, who were often enemies of the people of Judah, the southern kingdom. This phrase can be stated in active form. AT: "and he will remove those in Israel that are hostile towards those in Judah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
