===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Peter continues his speech to the believers that he began in [[:en:bible:notes:act:01:15|1:16]].
  * **General Statement:** - Based on the situation with Judas that he just recounted, Peter is recalling a passage in The Book of Psalms that he believes relates to the current situation. The quote ends at the end of this verse.

  * **For it is written in The Book of Psalms** - Peter says this phrase to let the people know that he is quoting from The Book of Psalms.
  * **The Book of Psalms** - This book is part of the Scriptures. AT: "The Book of Hymns" or "The Book of Songs." 
  * **Let his field be made desolate** - A property falling apart and overgrown suggests the owner is dead.
  * **and do not let even one person live there** - The land is unclean (taboo) or is unfit to live on.
  * **let someone else take his position of leadership** - "may he be replaced in his leadership position"
