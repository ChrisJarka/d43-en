===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he received the gold from them, fashioned it in a mold, and made it into a molded calf- ** AT: "He poured the gold into a mold and made a statue that looked like a young bull." (UDB)
  * **he received the gold from them**  - It is implied that Aaron melted the gold. AT: "They gave Aaron the gold and he melted it in a fire." (See: Implicit and Explicit Information)
