===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You**- Here the word "you" is plural and refers to the people who live in Judah. (See: You plural)
  * **Why are you still being struck?**- "There is no reason for God to continue punishing you!" (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **Why do you rebel more and more?**- "Why do you continue to sin against Yahweh?" (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **<font 14px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The whole head is sick</font>    , the whole heart faint. From the sole of the foot to the head there is no part unharmed - ** God has punished Judah again and again because of her sin. Isaiah compares Judah to a person who is ill from head to foot. (See: Personification)
