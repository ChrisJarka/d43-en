====== Arabah ======

===== Facts: =====

The term "Arabah" refers to a desert region   is the name of a large region consisting of three distinct areas: the Jordan Valley, the Dead Sea, and the territory from the Dead Sea to the Gulf of Aqabah. 

  * This barren region was traversed by the Israelites on their journey from Egypt.
  * The "Sea of the Arabah" is also called the "Salt Sea" or the "Dead Sea."
