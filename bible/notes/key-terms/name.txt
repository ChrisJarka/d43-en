====== name ======


===== Definition: =====


The word "name" is often used in the Bible to refer to more than just the simple label or title of someone of something. This non-literal use has many different meanings.


    * "Name" can refer to a person's reputation, as in "let us make a name for ourselves."
    * Doing something "in the name of" that person means to speak on their behalf, or as if they were there.
    * Speaking "in the name of God" represents speaking with his power and authority.
    * The "name" of someone can refer to the entire person, as in "there is no other name...by which we must be save."
