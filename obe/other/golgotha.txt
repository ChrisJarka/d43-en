====== Golgotha ======

===== Definition: =====

Golgotha is the place where Jesus was crucified.

  * Originally, it was an Aramaic word meaning "The Place of the Skull".
  * Golgotha was a hill outside of Jerusalem.
  * It is more commonly called "Calvary".
