===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They will besiege you in all your city gates, until your high and fortified walls come down everywhere in your land, walls in which you had trusted. **- "They will attack you city gates, until all the fortified walls in your land are knocked down, the walls you trusted to protect you."
  * **They will besiege you within all your city gates throughout all the land that Yahweh your God had given you **- This means that the enemy would attack all the towns in the land.
  * **the fruit of your own body, the flesh of your sons and of your daughters **- (See: Parallelism) AT: "your own children, the flesh of your sons and of your daughters"
  * **will put upon you **- AT: "will distress you"
