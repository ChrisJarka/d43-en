===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **by my strong hand** - This is an Idiom meaning "because I am strong." Alternate Translation: "because of my power." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
    * **my strong hand** - "my" refers to Yahweh, who is speaking
    * **will let them go** - "them" refers to the Israelites
    * **his land** - "his" refers to Pharaoh, who is speaking. Alternate Translation: "Egypt"
