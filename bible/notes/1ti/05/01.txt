===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Do not scold an older man **- "Do not rebuke an older man in a bad way"
  * **but exhort him as if he were your father **- "but appeal to him as you would speak to your father"
  * **exhort younger men as if they were your brothers**
 - "appeal to younger men just like your brothers" or "treat younger as brothers"
  * **exhort older women as mothers**
 - "treat older women as if they were your mothers" or "appeal to older women just like appealing to your mother"
  * **younger women as sisters**
 - "appeal to younger women just like appealing to your sisters" or "treat younger women like you will treat your sister"
  * **in all purity**  - "Treat younger women with purity in thought and actions" or "Treat all women with purity" or "with holiness in dealing with all of them"
