====== Sanhedrin ======

===== Definition: =====

The Sanhedrin was the highest Jewish rabbinic judicial council.

  * The Sanhedrin paid Judas Iscariot thirty pieces of silver to betray Jesus.
  * Jesus was tried by the Sanhedrin before He was sent to Pontius Pilate.
  * Paul also had charges brought against him before the Sanhedrin.

(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:council|council]], other)
