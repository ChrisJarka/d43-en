====== 2 Samuel 14 General Notes ======

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Reconciliation ==
David missed his son, Absalom, who was in exile. Joab uses this woman to reconcile David and Absalom. 

=== Important figures of speech used in this chapter ===
== Parable ==
The woman uses a type of parable to convince David that his actions were wrong. He treated a stranger worse than he treated his own son. This [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_hypo|hypothetical situation]] was intended to convict David os his [[:en:obe:kt:sin|sin]]. She uses David's own words against him.
