===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **midnight** - "the middle of the night"
  * **in front of Hebron **- "across from Hebron"
  * **posts** - supports for the city gates. These posts were probably made from tree trunks, or were large iron bars, and were anchored into the ground. The city gates that swung open were attached to these posts.
