====== Cornelius ======

===== Facts: =====

  * Conelius was a military officer in the Roman army that occupied the land of Israel.
  * He was a believer in God, who prayed and gave to the poor.
  * He and his household became Christians when the Apostle Peter spoke to them.
  * He and his household are the first non-Jewish people in the New Testament to become Christians.
  * His conversion to Christianity showed that Jesus was reaching out to non-Jewish people.
