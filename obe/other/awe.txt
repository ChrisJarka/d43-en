====== awe, awesome ======

===== Definition: =====

Awe is the sense of amazement and respect that comes from seeing something great, powerful, magnificent.

  * The visions of the glory of God seen by the Prophet Ezekiel were awesome.
  * Typical awe-induced human responses to God's presence are: prostration, covering the face, fear, trembling.
