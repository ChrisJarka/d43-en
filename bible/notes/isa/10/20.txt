===== Translation Notes: =====

  * On that day- Isaiah is talking about a day in the future that offers hope for the people of Israel.
  * **will no longer rely on him who struck them**- The word "him" here is the king of Assyria.
  * **A remnant of Jacob**- These are the Israelites that survived the destruction brought on by the Assyrians.
