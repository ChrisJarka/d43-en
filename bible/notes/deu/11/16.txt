===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Pay attention to yourselves**  - "Be careful" or "Beware"
  * **so that your heart is not deceived - ** Negative statement that can also be stated as "lest your heart be enticed." (See: Litotes)
  * **so that the anger of Yahweh is not kindled against you - ** Another negative statement that can also be stated as "then the anger of Yahweh will burn against you."
  * **so that he does not shut up the heavens**  - Another negative statement that can also be stated as "and he will shut up the heavens."
