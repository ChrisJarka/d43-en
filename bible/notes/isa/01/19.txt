===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **If you be willing and obedient**-" if you will listen to my instruction and obey those instructions"
  * **you will eat the good of the land**.- "your land will be productive and you will have much to eat" (Is 65:21-22)
  * **but if you refuse and rebel**-" but if you refuse to listen and instead disobey my instructions"
  * **the sword will devour you**-" the sword (of your enemies) will kill you and lay waste your land"
  * **for the mouth of Yahweh has spoken it**. -" because Yahweh has told us that is what He will do" (Lev 26:33)
