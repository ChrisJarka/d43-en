===== translationNotes: =====

  * **eight years old ... three months and ten days** - "8 years old ... 3 months and 10 days" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **what was evil in the sight of Yahweh** - Here "sight" here represents judgment. Yahweh saw and did not approve of Jehoiachin's actions. AT: "what Yahweh judged to be evil" or "what Yahweh considered to be evil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **brought him to Babylon** - "brought Jehoiachin to Babylon"
  * **the house of Yahweh** - Here "house" represents the temple. AT: "the temple of Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **his relative** - "Jehoiachin's relative"
