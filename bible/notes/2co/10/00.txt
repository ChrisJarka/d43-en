====== 2 Corinthians 10 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

Some translations prefer to set apart quotations of the Old Testament. The ULB and many other English translations indent the last line of 10:17, which is a quotation from the Old Testament.
  
**Return to his defense**

In Chapters 10 through 13, Paul goes back to his defense.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

**Paul’s Defense**

Paul’s enemies have said that Paul sounds strong in his letters but when he is there he is weak.  Paul says that he will use God’s power to show his enemies that he is in authority.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

**Metaphor**

In verses 10:3-6 Paul uses many metaphor [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]s from war: "destroy strongholds"  refers to difficult things to destroy , "take every thought captive into obedience to Christ" means to make every people think be what Christ wants them to think. 

**Idiom** 

Paul uses several statements using the [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] “flesh” to mean using human means. “we are living according to the flesh,” “we walk in the flesh,” "according to the flesh" He also uses “walk” to mean live, "not boast beyond limits” for not claiming credit for what someone else has done, “Look at what is clearly in front of you” meaning you should easily see this as true.
