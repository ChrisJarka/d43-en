====== Ezra 05 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

The story of the building of the temple and re-establishment of the temple worship continues in this chapter.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Rebuilding the temple ==
The prophets Haggai and Zechariah encouraged the Jews to begin again building the temple. This was very important to life in Judah.
