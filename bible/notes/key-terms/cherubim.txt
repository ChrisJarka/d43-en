====== cherubim ======

===== Definition: =====

Cherubim is plural for cherub, and is a special type of angel in Scripture. They are always described as being in close proximity to God, and play very important roles.

  * Cherubim were assigned the east side of the Garden of Eden to guard the way to the Tree of Life.
  * Two cherubim faced each other on the cover of the Ark of the Covenant, where the High Priest would sprinkle blood on the Day of Atonement.
  * Cherubim are considered second highest in the orders of angels.
  * Cherubim are depicted often as having the body of a lion or bull, with eagle wings and a human face.
