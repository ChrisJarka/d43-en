===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **When all the land of Egypt was famished, the people cried to Pharaoh for food.**  - The people had paid the market price for grain, and they had paid the high market price as grain became scarce. Now there was no grain to be had, anywhere.
  * **was famished**  - "became hungry"
  * **"Go to Joseph and do what he says."**  - "See Joseph and follow his direction."
  * **the whole land**  - "the whole earth"
  * **All the earth was coming to Egypt to buy grain from Joseph,**  - "people, clan leaders, and kings from all the earth were coming to Egypt to buy grain from Joseph," or "All the earth came to Joseph to buy grain,"
