===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Mamre**  - Amorite King/Chief
  * **the tent doorway**  - "at the opening of the tent" or "at the entrance of the tent."
  * **heat of the day**  - "hottest time of day"
  * **He looked up and, behold, he saw three men standing **  - or "He looked up and saw, and behold three men were standing."
  * **behold**  - indicates a sense of "surprise" in regard to the events that follow.
  * **across from him**  - or "near him" or "next to him."
  * **bowed low to the ground **- bowing is a symbol of respect
