===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **See, we are going up to Jerusalem**  - This is speaking about all twelve disciples and Jesus going together towards Jerusalem.
  * **The Son of Man will be delivered to the chief priests and scribes**  - This can be made active by saying "The chief priests and scribes will receive the Son of Man."
  * **They will condemn him to death**  - This is referring to the chief priests and scribes telling Jesus that he will die.  
  * **For them to mock**  - "them" here is speaking about the Gentiles. 
  * **He will be raised up**  - This can be made active by saying "Christ will rise from the dead."
