====== know, have relations with ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Bible, to know or have relations with someone is euphemistically referring to sexual intercourse.

  * Adam knew Eve in the Garden of Eden and she conceived children.
  * In the passages listed below, sinful homosexual men desired to have sexual relations with certain Biblical characters.
