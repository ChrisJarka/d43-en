====== 1 Chronicles Chapter 08 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What were the descendants of Eduh compelled to do?

A. The descendants of Ehud were compelled to move to Manahath. [8:6]

Q? What happened after Shaharaim divorced his wives Hushim and Baara?

A. Shahariam married Hodesh and became the father of her children in the land of Moab. [8:10]

Q? For what were the sons of Elpaal known?

A. The sons of Elpaal build Ono and Lod with its surrounding villages and drove out the inhabitants of Gath. [8:12-13]

Q? For what were Jeroham's sons, who lived in Jerusalem, known?

A. Jeroham's sons were clan heads and leaders. [8:26-28]

Q? Where did Mikloth and his family live?

A. Mikloth and his family lived near their relatives in Jerusalem. [8:32]

Q? Who was the father of Saul?

A. Kish was the father of Saul. [8:33]

Q? For what were the sons of Ulam, who were descendants of Benjamin, known?

A. The sons of Ulam were fighting men and archers. [8:39-40]
