===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The king had cast them ... Solomon did not weigh** - Solomon would have commanded his workers to do this work. AT: "The king had his workers cast them ... Solomon did not have them weigh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **plain of the Jordan** - "flat land near the Jordan River"
  * **Succoth ... Zarethan** - names of cities (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the weight of the bronze could not be measured** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "no one could measure the weight of the bronze" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
