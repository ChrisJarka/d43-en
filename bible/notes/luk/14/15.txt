===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **one of them who sat at the table with him**  - That is, "one of the other guests who sat with Jesus."
    * **Blessed is he**  - That is, "Blessed is the man" or, "Blessed is the person, or, "Blessings come to the person."
    * **that will eat bread** - This could also be translated as, "who will eat at the meal" or "Blessed is the man who will eat at the feast." The word "bread" is used to refer to the whole meal. (See: [[en:ta:figuresofspeech:synecdoche|Synecdoche])
    * **But he said to him**  - That is, "Jesus said to the fellow guest."
    * **he sent his servant**  - That is, "the man giving the feast sent his servant."
    * **to those who were invited**  - That is, "to those he had invited."
