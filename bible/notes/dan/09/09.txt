===== Translation Notes: =====

Daniel continues praying to the Lord.

  * **turned aside** - The words "turned aside" pictures Israel's disobedience. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **poured out**  - The words "poured out" is a picture of how God will administer His punishment on people who disobey. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
