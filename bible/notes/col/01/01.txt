===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Paul **- This book is a letter from Paul and Timothy to members of the church at Colosse. It might be helpful to insert the word "From Paul" or the phrase "This letter is from Paul" at the beginning to understand this is a letter.
  * **we always pray for you **- This hyperbola was used to indicate that Paul and Timothy prayed very often and sincerely for the believers receiving this letter. See hyperbola.
