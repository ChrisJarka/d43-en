===== translationNotes: =====

  * **People will come again** - The implied information is that Jerusalem is where the people will come. AT: "People will come again to Jerusalem" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **will go to another city** - This is a metonym for the people in the other city. AT: "will go to the people of another city" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **beg** - Here the word "beg" means to plead or ask urgently.
  * **before the face of Yahweh** - Here "face" is a metonym for Yahweh's presence. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:zec:07:01|7:2]]. AT: "in the presence of Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **seek Yahweh of hosts** - Those who want to know Yahweh and please him are spoken of as if they are literally seeking to find Yahweh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **mighty nations** - This is a metonym for the people in the mighty nations. AT: "people of mighty nations" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
