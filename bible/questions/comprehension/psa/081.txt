====== Psalms Chapter 081 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What is the writer telling everyone to do to God of our strength and to the God of Jacob?

A. He is telling them to sing aloud and shout out for joy. [81:1]

Q? What instruments is the writer telling the people to play as they sing a song?

A. The people are playing the tambourine, and the pleasant lyre with the harp. [81:2]

Q? When should the people blow the ram's horn?

A. They should blow it on the day of the new moon, the day of the full moon, when our feast day begins. [81:3]
