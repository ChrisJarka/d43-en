  * **Isaiah**  - Isaiah was a prophet of God. <font 14px line-height: 19px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Translate his name as needed into your language.   * **Amoz **- Amoz was the father of Isaiah. Translate his name as needed into your language. (See: [[https://door43.org/en/ta/translate/translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
  * **concerning - **"about"
  * **Judah and Jerusalem **- “Judah” refers to the southern kingdom of Israel. “Jerusalem” is its most important city. “Judah” and “Jerusalem” are metonyms that refer to the people who live in these areas. This can be translated as “those living in Judah and Jerusalem.” (See: [[https://door43.org/en/ta/translate/metonymy|Metonymy]])
