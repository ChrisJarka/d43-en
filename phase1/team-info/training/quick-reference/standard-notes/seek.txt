====== Seek / Seeking ======

===== Definition: =====

This phrase occurs often.

  * [[:en:phase1:team-info:training:quick-reference:standard-notes:seek#"Try to kill"]]
  * [[:en:phase1:team-info:training:quick-reference:standard-notes:seek#"Desire something"]]
  * [[:en:phase1:team-info:training:quick-reference:standard-notes:seek#"Seek Yahweh"]]


===== Standard Notes: =====

==== "Try to kill" ====

  * **seeking your life** - This phrase represents wanting or trying to kill someone. AT: "are wanting to kill you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])

<code>
  * **seeking your life** - This phrase represents wanting or trying to kill someone. AT: "are wanting to kill you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
</code>


==== "Desire something" ====

  * **you are seeking the priesthood also**  - Wanting to have the priesthood is spoken of as if they were looking for it. AT: "you want to have the priesthood too" or "you want to be priests also" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])

</code>


==== "Seek Yahweh" ====

  * **sought Yahweh** - Seeking Yahweh represents choosing to know, worship, and obey him. AT: "chose to obey Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])

<code>
  * **sought Yahweh** - Seeking Yahweh represents choosing to know, worship, and obey him. AT: "chose to obey Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
</code>


  * **seek Yahweh their God** - Seeking Yahweh their God represents either 1) asking God for help or 2) thinking about God and obeying him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
