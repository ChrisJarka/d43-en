===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **with bitterness in my spirit's rage**  - The words "bitterness" and "rage" are types of anger. Alternate translations: I was indignant and my spirit was full of anger" or "I was very indignant and angry."
  * **hand of Yahweh**  - The term "hand" is often used to refer to God's power and action.  Alternate translation: "the power of God." (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]], [[en:ta:translate:anthro]])
  * **for the hand of Yahweh was deeply impressed upon me**  - The "impressed" means to press or push in and create the form of what has been pressed. It is like pressing or pushing a hand into mud and creating the form or impression of a hand. God impressed his ideas on to Ezekiel and they became a part of him. Alternate translation: "because the desire of Yahweh had become a part of me"
  * **Tel-abib** - a town in Babylon, about 80 kilometers southeast of the main city which was also called Babylon. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames]])
  * **the Chebar canal**  - a waterway that people dug to bring water from the main river to where they lived and where their crops grew. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames]])
  * **overwhelmed in amazement**  - The word "overwhelmed" refers to having a powerful emotion that often comes by surprise and takes all of a person's focus.
