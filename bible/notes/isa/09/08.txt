===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **against Jacob**  - "against the descendants of Jacob"
  * **and it fell on**** Israel **- This refers to the punishment of Israel.
  * **<font 14px line-height: 19px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>who say in pride and with an arrogant heart</font>    **- This is saying the same thing in two ways for emphasize (See: doublet)
  * **<font 14px line-height: 19px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>but we will rebuild with chiseled stone; the sycamores have been cut down, but we will put cedars in their place</font>    **- This seems to imply pride and arrogance that the rebuilding will be with even better material.
