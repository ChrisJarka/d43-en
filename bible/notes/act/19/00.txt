====== Acts 19 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

== "So the word of the Lord spread very widely in powerful ways" ==
This is the major theme of this chapter and the book of Acts overall.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Receiving the Holy Spirit ==

Many scholars believe that this indicates the Spirit began to indwell, or live inside their heart in order to guide them. Others believe that this gave the ability to speak in tongues in addition to indwelling them.

== Baptism ==
There are several baptisms mentioned in this chapter. John the Baptist's baptist was about repentance. Christian baptism is about identifying with Christ. The baptism of the Spirit is the same as "receiving the Spirit" mentioned above.

== Temple of Diana ==
The temple of Diana was important for the people of Ephesus economically. Many people came to their city to see this amazing temple and bought statues while they were their. The people's belief in Jesus caused problem for these businesses.
