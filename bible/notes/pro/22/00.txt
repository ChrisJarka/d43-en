====== Proverbs 22 General Notes ======

=== Structure and formatting ===

Chapter 22 continues the section of the book (Chapter 10-22:16) which is attributed to Solomon and is filled mainly with individual Proverbs. 

The second half of this chapter and the first half of the next chapter are attributed to the Wise Men. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Sayings of the wise men ==

Notice in verse 17, how this phrase begins a new section of proverbs. 

== Parallelism ==

Proverbs are often written without any surrounding context and in two lines of text. Each line will have a certain relationship to the other line. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

Many of the proverbs are stated as promises or commands but they are intended to be advice.  

== Rhetorical Questions ==

With this new section of proverbs, you will notice more use of the rhetorical question. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])

== Themes ==

There are individual proverbs that run along common themes: wise vs foolish, money, lazy vs diligent, truth telling, wicked vs righteous, sluggard, pride vs humility, integrity vs crookedness etc.
