===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **your covenant with death will be dissolved,** - your agreement with Sheol will not stand**- Yahweh says that the leader's boast made in verse 15 will not be true. Death and Hell will fill the land.
  * **when the raging flood passes through**  - "when the vast army of Assyria overwhelms you like a flood, they will trample you into the ground" (See: Metaphor)
