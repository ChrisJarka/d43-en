====== 1 Samuel 25 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Revenge ==
Abigail persuaded David not to [[:en:obe:kt:avenge|avenge]] himself but to leave revenge to God. After David decided to kill all of the men in Nabal's household, Abigail convinced David not to avenge himself. He agreed. 

 

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Foreshadowing ==
It was [[:en:obe:kt:sin|sinful]] for David to marry Abigail because he was already married. It was against the [[:en:obe:kt:lawofmoses|Law of Moses]]. His sin was eventually carried on to his son Solomon. This is a type of foreshadowing, or an anticipation, of the downfall of Israel.
