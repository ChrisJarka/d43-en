===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **of his knowledge** – The literal translation of the Greek is “in the knowledge of him”, where “him” is referring to Jesus Christ.
  * **eyes of your heart may be enlightened** – This is a metaphor talking about the coming of God’s light to the inclination or desires of the human soul (mind, intellect and emotions).
  * **confidence** – See the word “hope” in Important Terms
  * **riches of the glory of his inheritance **- This is referring to the greatness or abundance of his glorious inheritance.
  * **those who are set apart for him **- The Greek word from which this phrase comes means “morally blameless” or “consecrated” or “holy ones”. This could be translated.as “his saints".
