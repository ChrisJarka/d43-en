====== first fruits ======

==== Definition: ====

The term "first fruits" refers to the first crops of fruits and vegetables that the Israelites harvested from their fields. These first fruits were considered to be of the best quality and were given to God as a sacrificial offering.

  * This term is also used figuratively in the Bible to refer to a firstborn son as being the first fruit of the family. That is, because he was the first son to be born into that family, he was the one who carried on the family name and honor.
  * Because Jesus rose from the dead, he is called the "first fruits" of all who have died but who will some day come back to life because they have put their trust in him.
  * Believers in Jesus are also called the "first fruits" of all creation, indicating the special privilege and position of those whom Jesus redeemed and called to be his people.
