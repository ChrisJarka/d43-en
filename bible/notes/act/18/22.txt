===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **landed**  - "arrived"
  * **he went up**  - that is, he travelled to the city of Jerusalem
  * **greeted the Jerusalem church**  that is, he greeted the members of the church of Jerusalem
  * **went down**  - downhill in elevation from Jerusalem
  * **departed**  - "went away" or "left"
  * **encouraged**  - "strengthened"
  * **bold words**  - explanation
