====== devour ======

===== Definition: =====

To devour is to eat up or consume in an aggressive manner.

  * Paul warned the Galations not to devour one another, meaning not to destroy one another. (Gal. 5:15)
  * The birds of the air greedily ate the seed sown on the roadside.  (Luke 8:5)
