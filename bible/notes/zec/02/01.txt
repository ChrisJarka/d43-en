===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I lifted up my eyes** - The word "eyes" represents the person who looks. AT: "I looked up" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **measuring line** - a rope with a certain length that a person would use to measure buildings or large sections of land 
  * **So he said to me** - "So the man with the measuring line said to me"
