====== Psalms Chapter 118 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What are the two reasons the writer says, "Give thanks to Yahweh?" [118:1]

A. The writer says to give thanks to Yahweh because, "He is good, for his covenant faithfulness endures forever." [118:2]

Q? What is the phrase this writer repeats for the fourth time when he says, "Let the loyal followers of Yahweh say..." [118:4]

A. He repeats, "His covenant faithfulness endures for ever." [118:4]

Q? When the writer called out to Yahweh, Yahweh answered him by doing what? 

A. "Yahweh answered me and set me free." [118:5]

Q? The writer says it is better to take shelter in Yahweh than do what?

A. "It is better to take shelter in Yahweh than to put confidence in man." [118:8]
