====== Gentile ======


===== Facts: =====


A Gentile is someone who is not a physical descendant of Abraham.


    * In Scripture, Gentiles are occasionally referred to as the "uncircumcised" because the Hebrews were commanded to circumcise their male children.
    * Now that Jesus has died for all mankind, Paul states that Jew and Gentile are the same to God: God will answer all who call on Him, both Jew and Gentile.
