====== 1 Samuel 07 General Notes ======

=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter completes the section on Samuel as the leader of Israel. (Chapters 1-7)

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

**Israel begins again to worshiping Yahweh**

Samuel tells the people to get rid of all their idols and worship Yahweh alone. So they all met a Mizpah to pray and dedicate themselves to God. When the Philistines heard they were all gathered together, they attacked but God thundered against them and the Israel army was able to defeat them. They had twenty years of peace and Samuel settled disputes between the people.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Idioms ==

Whenever Samuel speaks to a group of people, he uses many  [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]]s: "to turn to" means to worship, "with your whole heart" means completely, "rescue from the hand" means rescued from the power.
