====== call on the name of Yahweh, call on the name of the Lord ======

===== Description =====

The phrase "call on the name of Yahweh" is used in the Old Testament. The phrase "call on the name of the Lord" is used in the New Testament.

===== Standard Notes =====

  * **call on the name of Yahweh** - "pray in the name of Yahweh” or “trust in the name of Yahweh” or “worship Yahweh"
