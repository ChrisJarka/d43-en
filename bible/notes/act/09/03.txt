===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (After The high priest gave Saul the letters, he left for Damascus)
  * **As he was traveling**  - Saul left Jerusalem and is now traveling to Damascus.
  * **it happened that**  - This is an expression to mark a change in the story, that something different is about to happen.
  * **a light shone...out of heaven ** - from the sky
  * 
  * **he fell upon the ground**  -
  * 
  * **why are you persecuting me?** - The Lord was rebuking Saul in the form of a question. It can be translated "You are persecuting me!". (See: [[:en:ta:figuresofspeech:rhetoricalquestion]])
