====== imitate, imitation ======

===== Definition: =====

To imitate someone is to copy that person's behavior by acting as they act; an imitation is an object that is an exact copy of another usually with the intent to deceive.

  * Christians are taught to imitate Jesus Christ by walking in love and righteousnes as He did.
  * The Apostle Paul told the early church to follow him in the same way he follows Christ.
