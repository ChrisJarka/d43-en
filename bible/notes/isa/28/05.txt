===== translationNotes: =====

  * **In that day** - Here "day" represents a period of time in general. AT: "At that time" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Yahweh of hosts** - Translate this as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:01:09|1:9]].
  * **will become a beautiful crown and a diadem of beauty** - The people honoring Yahweh as their true king is spoken of as if he would become their beautiful crown that they wear. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **a beautiful crown and a diadem of beauty** - These mean the same thing. AT: "a beautiful crown" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **a spirit of justice for him who sits in judgment** - This can be reworded so that the abstract noun "justice" is expressed as the verb "be just." AT: "Yahweh will cause the judges to be just" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **a spirit of justice** - Here "spirit of justice" means that a person has the characteristic of justice, that is, the person is just. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **sits in judgment** - This idiom means the person has the authority to judge. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **and strength for those who turn back their enemies at their gates** - Here "to turn back" is an idiom that means to defeat someone in battle. AT: "and Yahweh will cause the soldiers to be strong so that they defeat their enemies when they attack their city" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
