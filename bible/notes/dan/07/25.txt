===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He will speak words against the Most High** - This means that the newest king will openly disagree with the Most High. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_123person]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **He will try ... into his hand** - This refers to the newest king, not the Most High.
  * **These things** - "religious laws and festivals"
  * **These things will be given into his hand for one year, two years, and half a year** - AT: "He will control these religious laws and festivals for three and a half years."
  * **but**  - "then"
  * **court** - A court is an assembly for judging people and deciding their punishment.
  * **But the court will be in session and they will take his royal power away to be consumed and destroyed at the end** - AT: "Then the most high court will meet and they will take his kingdom away, consuming and destroying him at the end."
