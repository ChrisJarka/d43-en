===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Ascribe to Yahweh, you clans of peoples, ascribe to Yahweh glory and strength** - "You clans of peoples, understand for yourselves and tell others that Yahweh is glorious and strong" See how "Acknowledge that Yahweh ... acknowledge that Yahweh has glory and power" is translated in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:029:001|29:1]].
  * **Ascribe to Yahweh the glory due to his name** - "Understand for yourselves and tell others that Yahweh's name is glorious." See how "Give to Yahweh the honor that his name deserves" is translated in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:029:001|29:2]].
  * **his name** - "him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **his courts** - the temple courtyard where the priests sacrificed animals to Yahweh
