===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses continues to address all the people of Israel.

  * **eat bread without lack - **  The word bread encompasses the entire food group.
  * **stones are made of iron, and out of whose hills you may dig copper - ** This describes the abundance of metals in the land, necessary for them to build tools, weapons and instruments for daily life.
  * **You will eat and be full**  - This means that they would have more than enough food to eat.
