===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Bilhah** - This is a the name of a woman. (See: [[en:ta:translate:translatenames]])
  * **bore Jacob a son **- "gave birth to a son for Jacob"
  * **God has listened to me**  - This can be translated as "God has judged me"
  * **heard my voice **- Here "my voice" is a synecdoche and can be translated as "heard me." (See: [[en:ta:translate:synecdoche]])
  * **Dan** - Translators may also add a footnote that says "the name Dan means 'he judged.'"
