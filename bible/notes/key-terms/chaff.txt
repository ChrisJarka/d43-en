====== chaff ======


===== Definition: =====


A thin, dry protective covering of a cereal grain seed that is indigestible to humans and is generally considered to be worthless. Therefore, the chaff must be separated from the grain seeds and are discarded.


    * A person scoops grain into a container and throws it up into the air, and the chaff is separated from the grain seeds by the wind.
