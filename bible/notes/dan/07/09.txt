===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **thrones were set in place** - AT: "someone set thrones in their place" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Ancient of Days** - This is a title for God that means he is eternal. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **took his seat** - AT: "sat down" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **His clothing was as white as snow** - These are compared to show his clothes are very white. AT: "His clothing was very white"
  * **His clothing** - This implies the God wears clothing like a person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * * **the hair of his head was like pure wool** - These two are compared to say something about what God's hair looked like. Possible meanings 1) it was as white as clean, pure wool 2) it was thick and curly (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **hair of his head** - This implies God has hair like a person.
  * **flames of fire** - This implies that God is sitting on a throne made of fire. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **its wheels** - That is the wheels of God's throne. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **burning fire** - This basically means the same as "flames of fire" in the previous line and can be translated the same if it is hard to find two different expressions in your language.
