===== translationNotes: =====

  * **your case** - "your disagreement"
  * **do not disclose another's secret** - "do not share your neighbor's secrets with other people"
  * **and an evil report about you** - The words "evil report" are a metonym for "a report that you are an evil person." AT: "and he will bring an evil report about you" or "and he will tell others that you are an evil person" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **that cannot be silenced** - These words can be translated in active form. AT: "you will not be able to stop him from bringing the evil report" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
