====== Numbers Chapter 05 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Yahweh speak to Moses concerning who, male or female, to send away from the camp?

A. He was to send away everyone with an infectious skin disease, oozing sores or everyone who touched a dead body and thus were unclean. [5:1-4]

Q? What did Yahweh speak to Moses about men and women who commit sins?

A.They must confess the sin and pay back to the one he has wronged the price of his guilt and add one fifth more. [5:5-7]

Q? Who receives the payment and what does he do with it if the wronged person has no relative to receive the payment?

A.  Yahweh receives the payment, through a priest, along with a ram. Every offering will then belong to that priest. [5:8-10]

Q? What does Yahweh tell Moses to do with a wife who sins against her husband?

A. The man should bring his wife to the priest and bring a drink offereing for her. He also needs to bring a grain offering of jealousy,as a possible indicator of sin. [5:15]

Q? What is the priest to do?

A. He must place the woman before Yahweh, and take a jar of holy water and mix dust from the floor of the tabernacle into the water. The priest then puts the grain offering of jealousy in her hands.The priest, holding the bitter water, must cause her to swear an oath that can bring down a curse on her. [5:18-19]

Q? What will the curse cause?

A. Her stomach will swell, and her thighs waste away. She will admit her guilt. [5:20-22]

Q? What must the priest do with these curses?

A. The priest must write these curses on a scroll, and then wash away the written curses in the bitter water. Then make the woman drink the bitter water. Next the priest will take a portion of the grain offering and burn it on the altar. [5:23-26]

Q? What will happen to the woman if she has committed a sin against her husband?

A. Her abdomen will swell and her thigh will waste away and the woman will be cursed among her people.[5:27-28]

Q? What will happen to the woman if she has not committed a sin against her husband?

A. She will be free and able to conceive children. [5:28]

Q? What is the law of jealousy?

A. It is a law for a woman who strays away from her husband and is defiled. It is also a law for a man with a spirit of jealousy when he is jealous of his wife.[ 5:29-30]
