===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **A psalm of David** - Possible meanings are 1) David wrote the psalm or 2) the psalm is about David or 3) the psalm is in the style of David's psalms.
  * **and its fullness** - "and everything that fills it"
  * **For he has founded it upon the seas and established it on the rivers** - AT: For he formed its foundation on the seas and built it on the deep waters"
  * **the seas ... the rivers** - These phrases are used together to refer to the huge deep ocean below the earth.
