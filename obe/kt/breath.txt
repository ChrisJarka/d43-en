====== breath ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Bible the breath of God can refer to His Holy Spirit and also to His life-giving power.

  * At creation Adam became a living soul when God breathed into him the breath of life.
  * Jesus breathed on the disciples and said "receive the Spirit".
