====== Savior ======


===== Facts: =====


A savior is a person who rescues or delivers others from danger. It can also refer to one who is a preserver, provider and sustainer.


    * "Savior" is used as a title for Jesus Christ because he provides salvation from the power and penalty of sin, for those who believe in him.
    * In the Old Testament, God is referred to as Israel's savior due to his many acts of preservation, provision and sustenance.
    * If possible, "savior" should be translated with a word that is related to the verb "save."


(See also: salvation, Jesus)
