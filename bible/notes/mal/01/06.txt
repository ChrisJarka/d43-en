===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **A son honoreth his father, and a servant his master-**  "'A son honors his father, and a servant honors his master."
  * **if then I am a father, where is mine honor ****and if I am a master, where is my fear?' ****-**  "If I am your father and I am your master, where is the honor and respect that I should receive from you?"
  * **Jehovah**-This is an old English language way to say the word "Yahweh."
  * **despise**-This means to treat someone with great hatred.
  * **despise my name**-"despise me"(See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  * **you say, 'Wherein have we despised your name?' - **"You say, 'How have we despised your name?'" This question is a rebuke.  It may be stated as: "You have despised me but you say you have not!"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **You offer polluted bread upon mine altar. -**  "You offer unacceptable bread to me on my altar."
  * **Wherein have we polluted thee?** - "How have we been unacceptable to you?'" (Rhetorical Question)
  * **The table of Jehovah is contemptible.** - "The alter of Yahweh is to be despised."
