====== 2 Chronicles 36 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This is the end of the story of Judah as an independent country. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

 God had warned the people through what the prophets had said to them, that he would punish them. But they refused to listen to the prophets or to stop their evil deeds. So God finally punished them by letting the Chaldeans conquered them. But Cyrus, the Persian allowed them to return to Israel.
