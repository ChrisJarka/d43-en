===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **vigorous** - "strong and youthful"
  * **Aven, Pi-Beseth and Tehaphnehes** - These are city names in Egypt. (See:How to translate names).
  * **the day will hold back**  - "it will be a dark day" or "it will be a sad day"
  * **break the yoke**  - "defeat the control" (See: Idioms).
  * **her**  - This refers to the city Tehaphnehes.
  * **a cloud covering her** - "she will be sad" or "she will mourn". (See: Metaphor).
  * **her daughters will walk in captivity** - This is a reference to putting a city's daughters into salavery. And in biblical old testament times it was more offensive statement to say "We will put your daughters into slavery", then to state the same their men.
  * **execute judgments** - The word execute here means to complete a plan or task as planned. It does not mean "to put to death."
