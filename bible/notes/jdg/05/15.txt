===== translationNotes: =====

  * **And my princes** - "And Deborah's leaders"
  * **Deborah** - See how you translated her name in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:04:04|4:4]].
  * **Issachar** - Here "Issachar" refers to the tribe of Issachar. AT: "the tribe of Issachar" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Barak** - See how you translated his name in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:04:06|4:6]].
  * **rushing after** - "following after" or "hurrying after"
  * **there is great searchings of heart** - Here "heart" refers to their conscience. AT: "They feel very guilty because they were not willing to join Deborah and Barak in their war." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
