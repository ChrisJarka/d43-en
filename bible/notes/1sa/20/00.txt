====== 1 Samuel 20 General Notes ======



=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Jonathan warns David ==
David tells Jonathan that Saul is still trying to kill him. Jonathan promises to find out if this is true. David misses dinner two days in a row and Saul asks about him. Jonathan said he went home for an annual sacrifice. Saul got angry at Jonathan and told him he would never be king unless they killed David and he threw his spear at Jonathan. Jonathan warned David to run away but first they both promised to always be friends.


=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphor ==
David uses a powerful [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and an oath to impress Jonathan with what danger he was in: "But as truly as Yahweh lives, and as you live, there is but a step between me and death."
