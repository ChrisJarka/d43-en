====== snare, trap ======

===== Definition: =====
A snare or trap is something that catches someone or something by surprise and keeps them from escaping. 
  * Sometimes the Bible uses "trap" or "snare" to speak about temptation. 
  * The Bible says that Satan places snares in front of people.
