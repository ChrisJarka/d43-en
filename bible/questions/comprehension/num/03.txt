====== Numbers Chapter 03 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What are the names of Aaron's sons?

A. Their names are Nadab, Abihu, Eleazar and Ithamar. [3:1-2]

Q? Which of Aaron's sons served as priests with Aaron?

A. Eleazar and Ithamar served with their father Aaron as priests. [3:3-4]

Q? Which tribe was presented by Moses to Aaron to help him serve as priests?

A. The tribe of Levi served as priests with Aaron. [3:5-6]

Q? What were the duties of the Levi tribe?

A. They must perform the duties on behalf of Aaron and the whole community, they must care for all furnishings and they must help the tribes carry out the tabernacle service. [3:7-8]

Q? Who did Yahweh set apart for himself?

A. All the firstborn in Israel both people and animals and the Levites. [3:11-13]

Q? Who did Yahweh command Moses to count in the tribe of Levi?

A.Moses was to count every Levi,male, descendant one month old and older. [3:14-16]

Q? How many sons and clans came from Levi

A. Levi had three sons and eight clans. [3:17-20]

Q? What must the clan of the Gershonites who camped on the west side of the tabernacle do?

A. They are to care for the tabernacle curtains under the outer coverings and for the entrance of the tent of meeting.

they must care for the tent, the tent covering, . They must also care for the courtyard hangings, the courtyard that surrounds the sancturay and the altar. They must care for the ropes of the tent meeting and everything in it.
