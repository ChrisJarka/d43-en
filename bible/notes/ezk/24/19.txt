===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You, I**  - are pronouns referring to Ezekiel
  * **us**  - pronoun referring to people of Israel
  * **House of Israel**  - This means the nation of Israel
  * **Behold**  - see how we translated in Ezekiel 24:15
  * **are profaning my sanctuary**  - are disrespecting God's house
  * **bold words**  - explanation
