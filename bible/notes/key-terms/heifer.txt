====== heifer ======

===== Definition: =====

A heifer is a young cow of the cattle family that has not given birth to a calf.

  * Cattle are large four-legged farm animals with short hair that can be just about any color
  * A cow is a female, a bull is a male, and a castrated bull is called a steer.
  * The word "cattle" is plural and includes cows, bulls, and steers.
  * Cattle are raised mainly for milk, meat, and hides.
