====== everlasting, eternal ======

===== Definition: =====

These three terms have very similar meanings. Something that will always exist or that lasts forever is said to be everlasting or eternal.

  * Some English versions use "eternal" to refer to God and "everlasting" to refer to concepts such as "everlasting covenant" or "everlasting possession."
  * The word "eternal" describes the existence of God outside of time, with no beginning and no end.
  * The terms "eternal life" and "everlasting life" are used in the New Testament to refer to believers living forever in heaven.
  * These terms could be translated as "that never ends" or "that has no time" or "that continues without stopping."
