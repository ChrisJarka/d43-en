===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It will not be lifted up any more among the nations - **"it will not be great among the nations."
  * **and I will diminish them so they -**  "I will weaken them"
  * **and they will no longer be an object of confidence for the house of Israel,**  - "and Israel will no longer have confidence to ask them for help." or "Israel will no longer dare to ask them for help."
  * **bringing remembrance of iniquity when their faces turn after Egypt. - **"When Israel thinks about the sin and punishment of Egypt, it will remind them of their own sin when they asked Egypt for help instead of trusting me."
  * **Then they will know that I am the Lord Yahweh!’” - **"Then they will understand very well that I am the Lord Yahweh!"
