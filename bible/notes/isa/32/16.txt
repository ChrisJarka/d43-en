===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **juistice will reside in the wilderness**  - people will act justly in those desert areas
  * **and righteousness will live in the fruitful field**  - people will act righteously in those fertile fields
  * **the work of righteousness**  - the result of acting righteously
  * **the result of righteousness, quietness and confidence forever**  - the result of righteousness is that the land will be tranquil, and people will be secure forever
  * **my people will live in a peaceful habitation**  - my people will live in their homes peacefully
  * **in secure homes **- in safe homes
  * **in quiet resting places**  - in places of calm and rest
