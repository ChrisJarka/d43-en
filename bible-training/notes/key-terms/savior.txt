====== Savior ======


===== Facts: =====


A person who rescues or delivers others from danger. It can also refer to one who is a preserver, provider and sustainer.


    * As Savior, Jesus Christ provides salvation from the power and penalty of sin, for those who believe in Him.
    * As Savior, Jesus Christ has a daily ministry to His followers to equip them for their daily lives of service to Him.
    * In the Old Testament God is referred to as Israel's Savior due to His many acts of preservation, provision and sustenance.


(See also: Salvation, Christ)
