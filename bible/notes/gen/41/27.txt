===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **thin and undesirable cows that came up**  - See how you translated a similar phrase in 41:3.
  * **seven thin ears scorched by the east wind**  - See how you translated similar phrases in 41:6.
  * **This is the thing which I spoke to Pharaoh.**  - Here the Joseph uses Pharaoh's title in speaking to him as a way of honoring him. Alternate translation: “These events will happen just as I have told you.”  
  * **revealed**  - "made known"
  * **Look**  - "Pay attention to what I am saying."
