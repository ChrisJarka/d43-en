===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **His - ** This pronoun refers to the Israelites.
  * **Serve them if you will refuse to listen to me**  - An alternate translation could be, "go ahead and serve them (idols) if will not obey me.
  * **Them**
- This pronoun refers to Israel's idols.

  * **You**  - This pronoun refers to Israel.
  * **Me**  - This pronoun refers to Yahweh.
