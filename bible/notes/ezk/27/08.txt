===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Arvad** - the name of a city on an island about 201 kilometers north of Tyre. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **rowers** - people who move the oars to move the ship
  * **ones dwelling in Sidon and Arvad were your rowers **  - People from these cities were strong rowers. Rowers are people who move a boat by pulling and pushing oars or paddles. This may indicate that the leaders (rowers) of Tyre were strong and effective.
  * **sages of Tyre were within you, they were your pilots **  - Sages, or the wisemen of Tyre, helped guide ships, and may refer to the strength and wisdom of those leading the city.
  * **The elders of Gebal and its sages were strengthening your seams **  - Strengthening seams is a process to keep a boat from leaks and sinking.This may refer to the elders and wisemen were counselors to the leaders to keep the city strong.
  * **All the ships of the sea and their sailors among you were to trade your merchandise **  - "Sailors" are men who worked on the ships. This portion may refer to the busy port and well-connected city.
