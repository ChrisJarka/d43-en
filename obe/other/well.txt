====== well ======

===== Definition: =====

A well is a hole in the ground that collects water either from rain or underground springs. M<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>ost wells are manmade.</font>

  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Wells were very important features of the arid Bible lands.</font>
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>In Bible times, a well could be a deep shaft or a shallow pit. It was usually covered with a large stone to prevent anything falling in it.</font>
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Because</font> <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>water was very important for both people and livestock, t</font> <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>he right to use a well was often a cause of strife and conflict.</font>

===== <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Translation Suggestions:</font>  =====

  * If the target language has a known term for a well, that term should be used.
  * Other ways to translate this could include something like, "deep water hole" or "deep and narrow pit full of water."
