====== heart ======


===== Definition: =====


The heart is the part of the body that pumps blood. Heart refers to a hollow muscular organ of the body that has rhythmic contractions that pumps and circulates the blood in the body.


    * In the Bible "heart" is often used figuratively to refer to the mind, thoughts, and center of emotions.
    * Some cultures use the words "stomach" or "liver" to refer to the mind, thoughts, and emotions.
    * Heart can refer to one's innermost being, referring to the moral and emotional personality of a person.
    * Heart is the inner part of a thing: as the "heart of the sea" or "heart of the matter."
    * The seat of desire, inclination, or will can be indicated by heart, as in "Pharaoh's heart hardened."
