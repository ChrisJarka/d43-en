====== mighty, might ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "mighty" describes someone or something that is strong and powerful.

  * A person who has great might is "mighty," that is, "powerful." 
  * In the Old Testament, "mighty" especially describes a man who is victorious in battle.
  * "Mighty" also can refer to being powerful in affecting other people, as when Moses is described as a man who was "mighty in word and deed" in Acts 7. This could be translated as, "Moses gave powerful teachings and did miraculous things."
  * The term "might" could also be translated as "power" or "great strength."
  * The term "Almighty" describes God as having complete power to do miracles and anything else he wants to do.

(See also: [[Almighty]], [[miracle]], [[power]] other)
