===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Moses continues to sing about how the people of other nations will feel when they see God's people.

  * **Terror and dread will fall on them** - Moses speaks of terror and dread as if they were objects that could suddenly fall on people and harm them. These no AT: "They will suddenly feel terror and dread" or "They will suddenly be terrified and fearful" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **dread** - Dread is extreme fear or anxiety about something that is going to happen or might happen.
