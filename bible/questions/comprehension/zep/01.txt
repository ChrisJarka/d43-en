====== Zephaniah Chapter 01 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did God tell Zephaniah He would do to man, the birds of the heavens and the fish of the sea?

A. God said He would destroy them [1:1-2].

Q? What did God say he would do to those that worship idols and have turned away from God?

A. He would destroy them [1:4-6].

Q? Who did God say he would punish on the day of sacrifice?

A. Those who bring violence and deceit [1:9].

Q? What will God do to the wealth of those who believe in their heart that God will not do anything?

A. He will turn their wealth into plunder [1:12-13].

Q? What day will be a day of fury, a day of anguish, and a day of darkness?

A. The day of God's judgement [1:14-15].

Q? Whose blood will be poured out like dust on the day of Yahweh?

A. Those who have sinned against God [1:17].
