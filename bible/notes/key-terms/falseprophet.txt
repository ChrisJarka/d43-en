====== false prophet ======

===== Definition: =====

A false prophet is one who does not proclaim the truth, one who falsely claims to prophesy or be divinely inspired.

  * Jesus states that one will know who is a prophet and who is not by the fruit they bear.
  * A false prophet usually produces evil works.
