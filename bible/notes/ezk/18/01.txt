===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **came to me**** again**  - "gave me another" (UBD)
  * **me**  - The word "me" refers to Ezekiel.
  * **What do you mean, you who use this proverb concerning the land of Israel and say,**** ‘Fathers eat sour grapes, and the children’s teeth made blunt’?**  - Ezekiel is using a question to scold his listeners. Also, he is using a proverb to communicate that the people of Israel think it is unfair to be punished for their parents' sins. An alternate translation for the proverb is, "The consequences of the parents' wicked deeds pass on to their children." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]). (See: [[en:ta:translate:idiom]]) (See: [[en:ta:translate:RhetoricalQuestion]]).
  * **land of Israel**  - This refers to the people of Israel. (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
