===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The people of Israel question Ezekiel and Ezekiel tells them what Yahweh has said to them.

  * **The word of Yahweh came to me** - "Yahweh spoke to me." Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:01:01|1:1]].
  * **the house of Israel** - Here the "house" represents the people of Israel. AT: "the people of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Behold** - The word "behold" here alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows.
  * **pride of your strength** - AT: "pride in the building that you have made" 
  * **the desire of your eyes** - AT: "the building that you delight to look at" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **So your sons and your daughters ... will fall by the sword** - This refers to their enemies by their swords. AT: "So your sons and daughters ... will be killed in war" or "So your enemies will kill your sons and your daughters ... with their swords" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
