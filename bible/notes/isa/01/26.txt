===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **I will restore your judges as at the first, and your counselors as at the beginning**  - God will again establish honest, wise, and just leaders for Israel.
    * **you will be called**  - Jerusalem. This concludes the idea starting in Is 1:21.
    * **city of righteousness**  -  displaying the quality of being morally good and ruling in a way that pleases God
    * **a faithful town**  - a fortress built on and for God alone
    * **after that you will be called the city of righteousness, a faithful town**  -  This will be partially true in the near future, and fully true in the heavenly Jerusalem.
