===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Caesar Augustus**  - That is, "King Augustus" or, "Emperor Augustus." Augustus was the man who was the first emperor of the Roman Empire. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]].)
    * **sent out a decree **- A decree is a must-do order or requirement that has to be followed. It was sent out for it originated from Rome, and was spread and announced to the different parts of the world under Rome.
    * **that a census be taken of all the people living in the Roman world**- A census is an official count and registration of all the people in a country for the purpose of collecting taxes. This can also be translated as, "that they register all the people living in the Roman world" or, "that they count all the people in the Roman world and write down their names."
    * **the Roman world**  - That is, "the part of the world controlled by the Roman government" or, "the countries ruled by the Roman emperor" or, "the Roman empire."
    * **Quirinius**  - Quirinius was the man who was appointed to be the governor of Syria. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
    * **his own town**  - This could also be translated as, "the town his ancestors lived in."
    * **to be registered for the census**  - This could also be translated as, "to have their name written in the register" or, "to be included in the official count."
