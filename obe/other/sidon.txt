====== Sidon ======


===== Facts: =====


Sidon was the oldest son of Canaan, who was a great-grandson of Noah. There is also a city called Sidon.
 
    * The city of Sidon is located on the coast of the Mediterranean Sea in a region that is now part of the country of Lebanon.
    * The people who lived in ancient Sidon were mostly Gentiles.
    * In the Bible, Sidon is closely associated with the city of Tyre, and both cities were known for their wealth and the immoral behavior of their people.
