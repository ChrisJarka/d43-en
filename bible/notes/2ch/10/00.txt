====== 2 Chronicles 10 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This begins the section on Rehoboam as king. ( Chronicles 10-12)

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

The people asked Solomon's son, Rehoboam to reduce the heavy taxation and forced labor that Solomon had demanded but Rehoboam refused. So the ten northern tribes broke off and made Jeroboam their king.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphor ==

The people use the [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] of a yoke to complain about the high taxes and forced labor that Solomon had demanded from the people. "Your father made our yoke difficult."
