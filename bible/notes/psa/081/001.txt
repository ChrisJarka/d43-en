===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **God our strength**  - "God who gives us strength"
  * **Shout out for joy**  - "Shout with joy"
  * **the God of Jacob**  - AT: "the God of Israel, the nation of Jacob's descendants" (See: Metonymy)
  * ** play the tambourine, the pleasant lyre with the harp ** - "play the tambourine, the lyre, and the harp"
  * **wh en our feast day begins **- AT: "and on the days when our feasts begin"
