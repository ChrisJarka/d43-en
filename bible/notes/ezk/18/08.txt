===== Translation Notes: =====
 Yahweh continues to describe the actions of the righteous man.

  * **interest**  - This word refers to the money paid by a person to use borrowed money.
  * **injustice** - the unfair treatment of all people under the laws of God and country.
  * **he turns his hand to justice** - This phrase refers to accepting profit earned from doing the things that are right.  The phrase "turns his hand" describes putting out his hand to be given something. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])  
  * **establishes truth between men- **  This means he determines what is right between men, that is, which man is telling the truth.  It could also mean that he speaks wisely if there is a dispute.
  * **who walks in my statutes and keeps my judgments to act truthfully**  - Both of these phrases are speaking of someone who obeys the laws about how to treat other people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]].
