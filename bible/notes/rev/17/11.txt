===== translationNotes: ===== 

  * **The beast...is himself also an eighth king** - The beast has ruled or will rule one as one of the seven kings, and then he will rule again as the eight king. AT: "the beast that lived before and then was not alive will be the eighth ruler" (UDB)
  * **but he is one of those seven kings and he is going to destruction** - He will be evil like the seven rulers were, but God will surely destroy him. These rulers are understood to be evil.   (See:[[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
