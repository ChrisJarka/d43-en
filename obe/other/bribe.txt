====== bribe ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "bribe" refers to anything of value, such as money or goods, that is given to someone in authority in order to influence that person to do something.

  * Usually a bribe involves trying to influence someone to lie or TO break a LAW OR regulation in order to get something done.
  * After Jesus' resurrection the soldiers who guarded His empty tomb were offered money to change their story about what happened.
  * The Bible forbids giving or taking bribes.
  * The term, "bribe" could be translated as "dishonest influence" or "payment for favors."
  * "To bribe" could be translated with a word or phrase that means, "pay to influence (someone)" or "pay to have a favor done" or "pay to have a regulation ignored."
