====== heart ======


===== Definition:  =====

In the Bible "heart" is used figuratively to refer to the mind, thoughts, and center of emotions of a person.

    * Heart can refer to a person's innermost being, referring to the moral and emotional personality of a person.
    * Some cultures use the words "stomach" or "liver" to refer to the mind, thoughts, and emotions.
    * The seat of desire, inclination, or will can be indicated by heart, as in "Pharaoh's heart hardened."
