===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It will come about on that day that Yahweh will punish the host of the high ones on high, and the kings of the earth on the earth**  - "One day, Yahweh will punish the rebellious beings in theheaven and the proud kings on the earth "  (See: [[:en:ta:translate:active|Active or Passive]])
  * **punish the host of the high ones on high**  - This is referring to rebellious angels.
  * **they will be sentenced**  - Yahweh will speak the punishment to be given to the rebellious angels and kings.
  * **Then the moon will be ashamed, and the sun disgraced**  - In the presence of Yahweh, the light of the moon and sun will seem less bright.
  * **reign on Mount Zion and in Jerusalem**  - Mount Zion was the center of worship for the people of Israel. Yahweh will be worshiped in the holy place of Mount Zion, which was the highest point in Jerusalem and was the location of the Temple. Also, Yahweh will rule the earth from Jerusalem. Sometimes Mount Zion refers to Jerusalem, both the spiritual center and political center of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit|Explicit and Implicit Information]])
