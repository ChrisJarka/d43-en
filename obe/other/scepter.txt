====== scepter ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "scepter" refers to an ornamental staff or wand held by a ruler such as a king.

  * Often scepters were carved from wood.
  * The scepter was a symbol of royalty and authority which added to the decorum of a King or Monarch.
  * In the Old Testament, God's scepter is described as a scepter of righteousness.
  * In prophecy, Christ was foretold to be a symbolic scepter that would come from Israel to rule over all nations.
