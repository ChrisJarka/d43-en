===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **And already the axe has been placed against the root of the trees **- This metaphor  means that the punishment is about to start. It can also be translated as "It is as if the axe has already been placed against the root of the trees" or "God is like a man who has placed his axe against the root of the trees." (See: [[:en:ta:figuresofspeech:metaphor|Metaphor]])
    * **So every tree that does not produce good fruit is chopped down **- This is a passive clause. It can also be translated with an active clause: "He chops down every tree that does not produce good fruit." (See: [[:en:ta:vocabulary:active|Active or Passive]])
    * **and thrown into the fire **- This can also be translated with an active clause: "and throws it into the fire."
