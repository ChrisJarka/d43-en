===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Break the arm of the wicked and evil man** - Here "arm" represent power. AT: "Destroy the power of the wicked and evil man" or "Make the wicked and evil man weak" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **wicked and evil** - These words have the same meaning. You can use one word to express both concepts. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **make him accountable for his evil deeds** - Making someone accountable for his deeds represents punishing him. AT: "punish him for the evil things he has done"
  * **the nations are driven out of his land** - This can be stated i active form. "Yahweh forces the people of other nations to leave his land" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
