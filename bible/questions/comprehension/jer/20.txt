====== Jeremiah Chapter 20 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why was Jeremiah whipped and put in stocks by Pashur?

A. Because he prophesied the words of Yahweh, [80:1-2]

Q? What will Yahweh do to the people of Judah?

A. Yahweh will give Judah into the hand of the king of Babylon. [80:4]

Q? What happens to the wealth and riches of Judah?

A. God will takes these things and give them to Babylon. [80:5]

Q? In spite of all the bad treatment from the people why does Jeremiah still proclaim Yahweh's word?

A. Because it is like a fire in Jeremiah's heart, a burning in his bones, he cannot contain Yahweh's word. [80:7-9]
