===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - In verses 3:11-13, Yahweh encourages the remnant of Israel who survive the judgment. 

  * **commit injustice** - "do unjust things"
  * **no deceitful tongue will be found in their mouth** - Here "tongue ... in their mouth" represents the things that the tongue enables the mouth to speak. The can be stated in active form. AT: "none of them will speak deceitful things" or "they will not say deceitful things" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **they will graze and lie down** - Yahweh speaks of his provision for the people of Israel as if they are a flock of sheep that grazes and rests in safety. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
