===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **So it came about**  - This is a way to introduce a new time period in the story.  Alternate translation: "And it happened"
  * **as I sat...before me...from his hips**  - The words “I” and "me" refer to Ezekiel.  The word "his" refers to the figure who looked like a man. 
  * **the hand of the Lord Yahweh again fell on me**  - The phrase "fell on me" refers to a feeling or event that suddenly happened.  Someone may say a feeling of cold fell on me.  "the power of God suddenly began to control me"
  * **figure like a man**  - This is the shape or form of a person's body. (See: [[en:ta:translate:simile]]).
  * **from his hips downward**  - The word "downward" means below his hips down to his feet.
  * **glowing metal**  - The words "glowing metal", refer to metal which is so hot it produces a yellow/orange light.
