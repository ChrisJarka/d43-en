===== Translation Notes: =====

    * (Jesus talks about the people's response to him and John the Baptist.)
    * **To what should I compare** - This is the start of a rhetorical question. Jesus used it to introduce a new topic. This whole question could also be translated as, "This is what I will compare this generation to. This is what they are like." (See: [[en:ta:figuresofspeech:rhetoricalquestion]])
    * **generation** - More literally, "family" or "race." Here he is referring to all the people belonging to the descendants of Israel.
    * **what are they like?**  - esus here begins an extended simile. 
    * **children** - This word generally defines a child in training from infancy up to about 8 years old.
    * **marketplace **- This was a large, open-air area where vendors and merchants would come to sell their goods.
    * **flute**  - This is a long, hollow musical instrument which is played by blowing air in or over one end.
