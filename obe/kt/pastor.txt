====== pastor ======

===== Definition: =====

The word "Pastor" translates from the Greek as "shepherd."

  * Can denote a sheep-herder, literally or figuratively.
  * Is used of Jesus Christ as the "good Shepherd."
  * Can also refer to leaders in the church.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * This can also be translated as "shepherd."
