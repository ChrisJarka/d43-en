====== foreigner ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "foreigner" refers to a person living in a country of which he is not a citizen or a native born person.

  * <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>A foreigner is also a person whose language and culture is different from your own.</font>
