===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **it came to pass** - This phrase is used here to mark the beginning of a new part of the story. If your language has a way for doing this, you could consider using it here.
  * **in the eleventh year** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:26:01|26:1]].
  * **in the third month** - It was understood that this was the third month of the eleventh year. Alternate translation: "in the third month of that year." 
  * **the Word of Yahweh came to me and said** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:16|3:16]].
  * **and to his entourage** - "and to the people of Egypt"
  * **In your greatness, who are you like**  - "who is great like you are." Possible meanings are 1) Yahweh asked a question which he will soon answer. 2) Yahweh used the question to scold Pharaoh. Alternate translation: "You think that there is no country whose power is as great as the power of your country." (UDB) (See: [[en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]])
