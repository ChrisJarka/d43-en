===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For the vineyard of Yahweh of Hosts is the house of Israel**  - This compares Israel to the vineyard that produces bad fruit. It can be stated as "The people of Israel are like the vineyard of Yahweh, Lord of the angel armies." (see: Metaphor)
  * **the men of Judah his pleasant planting**  - This compares the people of Judah with the strong vines that Yahweh planted and cared for. It can be stated "the people of Judah are his well cared for vines." (see: metaphor, Active)
  * **he waited for justice, but instead, there was killing**  â€‹ - "Yahweh waited for the people to live in a way that pleased him, but they did evil"
  * **for righteousness, but, instead, a cry for help**  -  This can be translated "for them to do right, but they used their power to hurt people who were weak."
