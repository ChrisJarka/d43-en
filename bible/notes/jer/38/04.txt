  * **for in this way he is weakening the hands of the fighting men**  - AT: "for in the way he talks the soldiers are discouraged" (See: Synecdoche)
  * **for this man is not working for safety for this people, but disaster**  - AT: "for he is not working to help us , but to cause harm"
