====== Jesus Healed the Sick [26-08] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-26-08.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Then **[[:en:obe:kt:jesus|Jesus]]** **went throughout the region of [[:en:obe:other:galilee|Galilee]]**, and large crowds came to him. They brought many people who were sick or handicapped, including those who could not see, walk, hear, or speak, and Jesus **[[:en:obe:other:heal|healed]]** them.


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **went throughout the region of Galilee** - That is, "all around in Galilee" or, "from place to place in Galilee."
