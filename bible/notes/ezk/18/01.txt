===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **came to me**  - "gave me another" (UBD)
  * **me**  - The word "me" refers to Ezekiel.
  * **land of Israel**  - This refers to the people of Israel.
  * **Fathers eat sour grapes**  - The fathers do wicked things.  Their ancestors had committed sins that deserve judgement.
  * **the children’s teeth made blunt**  - The people of Israel thought they were being punished for the wicked deeds of their ancestors. This could also be translated as, "The consequences of the parents' wicked deeds pass on to their children."
