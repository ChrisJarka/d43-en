===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **All the city was excited **- "they were angry." A large crowd of people was so affected by what it heard that it moved to do what it did. (See: hyperbole)
  * **the doors were immediately shut **- The Jews closed the temple gates once the crowd had taken Paul outside.
  * **the chief captain **- The military officer or leader of about 600 soldiers.
  * **all Jerusalem was in an uproar** - This is an exaggeration to stress that a large crowd was upset by this event. (See: Hyperbole)
