====== grain, barley, wheat ======

===== Definition: =====

Grain usually refers to the seed of a food plant such as wheat, barley, corn, or rice. It can also refer to the seed itself.

  * In the Bible, the main grain that is referred to is wheat.
  * The heads of grain are the part of the plant that holds the grain.
  * Note that some older Bible versions such as the King James Version use the word "corn" to refer to grain in general.

(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:wheat|wheat]])
