===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **In the eighteenth year**  - AT: "In year 18" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers|Translate Numbers]])
  * **three years**  - "3 years"
  * **all the sins that his father had committed before his time**  - It is implied that "all the sins" refers to idol worship. AT: "all the sins that his father, Rehoboam, had committed before Abijah's reign" (See:[[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit|Explicit - Implicit]])
  * **his heart was not devoted … the heart of David**  - Here "heart" represents the whole man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche|Synecdoche]])
