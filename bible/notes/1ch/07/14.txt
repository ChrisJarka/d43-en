  * **General Information:** - See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]

  * **Asriel, ... Zelophehad ... Peresh ... Sheresh ... Ulam ... Rakem** - These are all the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Makir, Gilead's father** - These are the names of men. Translate as you translated "Makir, father of Gilead" in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:02:21|2:21]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Aramean concubine bore** - "Aramean concubine gave birth to"
  * **Huppites ... Shuppites** - Translate these names as in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:07:11|7:12]].
  * **Maakah** - This is the name of a woman. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
