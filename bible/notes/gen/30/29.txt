===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You know**  - Jacob is restating what Laban just said to support what follows
  * **have fared**- "have done." Jacob is implying that Laban's cattle have done well.
  * **For you had little before I came**  - "your herds were small before I worked for you"
  * **and it has increased abundantly**  - "but now there is an abundant increase"
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **for my own household**  - "for my own family"
