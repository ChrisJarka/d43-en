====== womb ======

===== Definition: =====

The womb is where a baby grows inside its mother.This is an older term that is sometimes used as a euphemism. [See: Euphemism].

  * A more modern term for womb is "uterus."
  * Some languages use a word like "belly" to refer to a woman's womb or uterus.
  * Use a term that is well-known and acceptable in the receptor language.

(See also: other)
