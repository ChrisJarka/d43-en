===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the princes of Israel.
  * **you will take** - The word "you" is singular and refers to whoever is the prince in Israel. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_yousingular]])
  * **unblemished lamb - **  This is a healthy lamb with no physical defects.
  * **morning by morning- - **  It means to be done every morning.
  * **enduring and regular statute**  â€“ This is a regular practice that should never be stopped.
  * **enduring burnt offering - **  This is a regular offering that should never be stopped.
