====== promise, oath ======


===== Definition: =====


A promise is a pledge or vow or commitment to do or not to do something specified. When someone makes a promise, he assures the people he his speaking to that he will truly do what he has said he will do.

    * Promises are important parts of formal agreements such as covenants.
    * A promise is often accompanied with an "oath" or a confirmation that it will be done.
    * An oath is a strong promise or a strong declaration that what one says is true. When people take an oath, they show that what they have said is true by asking God to do them harm if it is not true.
