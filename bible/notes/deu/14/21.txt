===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **anything that dies of itself** - Yahweh does not allow the Israelites to eat animals that die a natural death because its blood has not been properly drained out, and Israelites are forbidden from eating blood with the meat. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the foreigner who is within your gate** - Here "gate" represents the entire city or town. AT: "the foreigner who is in your town" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
