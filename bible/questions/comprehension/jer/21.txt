====== Jeremiah Chapter 21 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Pashhur and Zephaniah ask Jeremiah?

A. They asked him to seek advice from Yahweh ,hoping Yahweh will do miracles for them. [21:1-2]

Q? What is the message that Jeremiah give Zedekiah?

A. Yahweh says he will fight against you with a raised hand and with wrath, fury and great anger. [21:3-5]

Q? How exactly does Yahweh fight against King Zedekiah?

A. They will die in a severe plague, sourvivers will be given to Nebuchadnezzar King of Babylo and he will kill them with the sword. [21:6-7]

Q? What is the way of life that Yahweh offers?

A. They are to go out of the city and surrender to the Chaldeans. [21:8-9]

Q? What is the way of death?

A. Anyone who stays in the city will die by sword, famine, or plague and the king of Babylon will burn the city. [21:8-10]

Q? What is Yahweh's instructions to the king of Judah?

A. The king is bring about justice and rescue the oppressed. [21:11-12]
