===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is a quotation from the prophet Jeremiah.
  * **the house of Israel** - The people of Israel are spoken of as if they were a house. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I will put my laws** - God's requirements are spoken of as if they were objects that could be placed somewhere (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **into their minds** - People's ability to think is spoken of if it were a place (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I will...write them on their hearts** - People's loyalties are spoken of as if they were a page that could be written on. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I will be their God** - I will be the God whom they worship.
