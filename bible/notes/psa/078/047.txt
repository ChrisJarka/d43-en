===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He destroyed**  - The pronouns "he" and "his" in these verses refer to God.
  * **their vines**  - The pronouns "their" and "them" in these verses refer to the Egyptians.
  * **sycamore **- This was a type of tree similar to a fig tree.
  * **lightning bolts**  - These were lightning flashes that made a loud sound of thunder and striking someone or something. AT: "thunderbolts" or "lightning flashes"
  * **lashed out**  - AT: "was let loose"
  * **He sent wrath, fury, and trouble like agents who bring disaster**  - Wrath, fury and trouble are described as being the agents of destruction. (See: Personification) (See: Simile)
  * **agents **- AT: "angels" or "messengers"
