===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **At that time, he will respond to the prayer of the destitute **- AT: "at that time he will respond to the prayers of the destitute"
  * **he will not reject their prayer.**  - AT: "he will not reject their praying." (See: Verbal noun) This is for a sense of ongoing asking.
  * **This will be written for future generations, and a people not yet born will praise Yahweh**  - AT: "These are written for future generations, and a people not yet born who will praise you, Yahweh, for what you have done." (See: Doublet)
