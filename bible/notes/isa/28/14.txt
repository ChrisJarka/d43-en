===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You have said, "We have made a covenant with death; ****with Sheol we have reached an agreement** - The leaders scorn Yahweh's warnings in verse 11.  The leaders are saying that they are safe from death and hell because they have made an agreement that death will not will not enter their land.
  * **for we have made a lie our refuge, and in falsehood we have hidden**  - "The leaders will trust in lies, and hide themselves in their deceit(See: Doublet)
