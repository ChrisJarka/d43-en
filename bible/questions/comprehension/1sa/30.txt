====== 1 Samuel Chapter 30 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did David and his men find when they came to Ziklag?

A. They found that the Amalekites had raided, attacked, burned it, and captured all the women and others and took them away. [30:1-2]

Q? How did David and his men react to what they found in Ziklag?

A. They raised their voices and wept until they could weep no more. [30:3-4]

Q? Why was David distressed?

A. Because his two wives were taken captive and the grieving people were talking about stoning him. [30:5-6]

Q? David turned to Yahweh for strength and prayed for direction. What did he want to know from Yahweh?

A. David wanted to know, if he pursued after the Amalekites would he overtake them. [30:8]

Q? What was Yahweh's answer to David?

A. Yahweh told him to pursue the troop, David would be successful, and he would recover everything that had been taken. [30:8]

Q? After David and his men kept pursuing the troop, who was found and what did they do for this person?

A. They found an Egyptian in a field, brought him to David, gave him food and water until he regained his strength. [30:9-12]

Q? What information did the Egyptian share with David?

A. That he was a young Egyptian, servant to an Amalekite who had left him behind, and he had been part of the troop that took the belongings from the places they had raided. [30:13-14]

Q? What was the Egyptian's requirement before he would show David where the raiding party was?

A. He told David to swear that he would not kill him or betray him to his master. [30:15]

Q? When the Egyptian showed David where the raiding party was, what were the raiders doing when David attacked them?

A. The raiders were celebrating because of all the booty they had taken from the land of the Philistines and Judah. [30:16-17]

Q? What did David recover after his attack on the raiders?

A. He recovered everything the Amalekites and raiders had taken, which included David's two wives, the people, and all the animals. [30:18-20]

Q? Why didn't the wicked men think that the recovered booty should be shared with those men that were left behind?

A. The wicked men told David that those left behind did not go and fight with them. Those men should only get their wives and children and then leave. [30:21-22]

Q? Why did David tell the wicked men not to act like this?

A. Because Yahweh had preserved them and had given them victory over the raiders that had come against them. They were to all share and share alike. [30:23-24]

Q? What did David do with the booty from Yahweh's enemies when he came back to Ziklag?

A. David shared the booty with many elders of Judah and other places that he and his men frequently went. [30:26-30]
