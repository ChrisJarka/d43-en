===== Facts: =====

Text

  * Abraham decieved Abimelech by telling him that Sarah was his sister rather than his wife for fear that the king would not give him safety.
  * Abimelech took Sarah but God appeared to him in a dream and warned him of Abraham's duplicity before any intimacy had taken place.
  * Abimelech returned Sarah to Abraham as God had commanded, presented gifts to him and gave them permission to settle anywhere they wished.
  * God blessed Abimelech by allowing his wife and maids to bear children.
  * Abimelech and Abraham entered a covenant regarding disputed ownership of wells which Abraham had dug at Beersheba, allowing the patriarch to prosper.
