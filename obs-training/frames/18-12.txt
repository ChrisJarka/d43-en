====== Israel Worshiped Idols [18-12] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-18-12.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

All of the **[[:en:obe:other:king|kings]]** and most of the people of the **[[:en:obe:other:kingdom-of-israel|kingdom of Israel]]** **[[:en:obe:kt:worship|worshiped]]** **[[:en:obe:other:idol|idols]]**. Their idol worship often included sexual immorality and sometimes even **child [[:en:obe:other:sacrifice|sacrifice]]**.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **child sacrifice** - They killed children as offerings to some of their idols.
