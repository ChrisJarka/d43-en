====== ark ======


===== Definition: =====

An ark is a rectangular box.

  * An ark can range in size from very small to very large.
  * In the Bible, the word "ark" is only used to refer to either the "ark of the covenant" or the ark that Noah built to escape the flood.
  * The word ark can be translated as "box", "chest" or "case".

(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:urlterm|ark of the covenant]])
