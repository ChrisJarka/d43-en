===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Their slain will be left unburied; the stench of their dead bodies will be everywhere **- "There will be so many dead bodies everywhere that they will rot and stink."
  * **and the mountains will be soaked with their blood **- This is emphasizing the amount of blood that will cover the mountains.
  * **All the stars of the sky will fade away and the sky will be rolled up like a scroll; and all their stars will fade away - **  The sky is being compared to a scroll that is rolled up signifying the end of existance. (See: Similie)
  * **as the leaf fades from off the vine, and as the overripe figs from the fig tree **- The people who have died are being compared to a dead leaf that falls from the tree and fruit that is old and rotten. (See: Doublet)
