====== James ======


===== Facts: =====


James is the name of four persons in Scripture. The most prominent man named James was the half-brother of Jesus who wrote the book of James and was a leader of the church in Jerusalem.


    * James, the son of Zebedee, was an apostle.
    * James, the son of Alpheus, was an apostle.
    * James was also the name of the father of an apostle named Judas (not Judas Iscariot).
    * James, Jesus' half-brother, did not believe in Jesus prior to the resurrection.
