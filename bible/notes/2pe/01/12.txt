===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Therefore**  - This is an application statement based on what the writer previously stated. (See: discourse information)
  * **I will be ready always to remind you of these things,** - Peter will not continuously remind.He uses this figure of speech to indicate importance of these things. See: ([[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **these things**  - The things mentioned in the beginning of the chapter beginning with 1:3.
