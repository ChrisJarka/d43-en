===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The city that went out with a thousand will have a hundred left** - The phrases "a thousand" and "a hundred" refer to a thousand soldiers and a hundred soldiers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **The city that went out with a thousand** - "The city that went out" represents the soldiers of that city going out. It can be stated clearly why they went out. AT: "The city out of which a thousand soldiers went out to fight" or "The city that sent  out a thousand soldiers to fight" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **will have a hundred left** - Here being "left" refers to not being killed by the enemy. AT: "will have a hundred soldiers who have not been killed" or "will have only a hundred soldiers still alive"
