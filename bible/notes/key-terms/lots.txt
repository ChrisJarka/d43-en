====== lot, lots, casting lots ======

===== Definition: =====

Casting lots is a practice of choice-making or determining an outcome, an attempt to take human bias out of a decision.

  * A straw is selected from a group of straws of unknown but different lengths, and the person who selects the shortest (or longest) straw is the winner (or loser).
  * Because of the large number of Levite priests, the process of casting lots was used to determine the priest who would perform a specific duty in the temple at a specific time.
