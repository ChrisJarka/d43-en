====== first fruit ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Old Testament, first fruits were the first born of livestock and the first fruits and vegetables harvested which were given as offerings to God. In the New Testament, first fruits refers to people who become Christians.

  * In the Old Testament, a believer offering first fruits to God was showing that God is the source of blessing and that God should be given the first and best of what the believer has.
