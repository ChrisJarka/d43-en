===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Assyrian, **- God is talking to the people of Assyria.
  * **the club of my anger**,- This personification describes the Assyrians as a "club", an instrument that God will use to punish other nations. (See: Personification)
  * **the rod by whom I wield my fury!**- This doublet repeats the personification above only describing the Assyrians as a "rod". (See: doublet)
  * **I am sending him**
 - God is referring to the people of Assyria when he says, "him".
