====== Jeremiah Chapter 30 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What words did Yahweh tell Jeremiah to write in a scroll?

A. Yahweh told Jeremiah, "For look, days are coming when I will restore the fortunes of my people, Israel and Judah, and bring them back to the land I gave their ancestors." [30:1-3]

Q? Why did Yahweh declare his words concerning israel and Judah to Jeremiah?

A. Yahweh declared his words because they had heard a trembling voice of dread and not of peace. [30:4-5]

Q? What news did Yahweh share with Jeremiah concerning the coming days?

A. Yahweh shared with Jeremiah that the days would be an anxious time for Jacob, but that Yahweh would break the yolk off their necks and shatter their chains so the foreigners would no longer enslave them. [30:6-8]

Q? How will Israel celebrate their freedom from enslavement?

A. They will worship Yahweh their God and serve David their king. [30:8-9]

Q? Why did Yahweh tell Israel not to be dismayed?

A. Yahweh told Israel not to be dismayed because he would bring them back from the land of captivity to a time of peace and bring an end to the nations which had scattered them. [30:10-11]

Q? Why will Yahweh discipline Israel?

A. Yahweh will discipline Israel as a cruel master because of their many iniquities and innumerable sins. [30:11-15]

Q? What was the good news which Yahweh gave to Jeremiah?

A. Yahweh told Jeremiah that everyone who consumed and plundered Israel would become consumed and plundered and that he would bring healing to their wounds. [30:16-17]

Q? What will Yahweh do for Israel?

A. Yahweh will have compassion on Israel so that a city will be built on the heap of ruins and a stronghold will exist again. [30:18]

Q? What will happen after the city has been rebuilt?

A. A song of praise and a sound of merriment will go out from Israel because they will be honored by Yahweh. [30:19]

Q? What will happen after Israel's assembly has been established?

A. Israel's leader will emerge from their midst when he approaches Yahweh. [30:20-21]

Q? What is Yahweh's declaration?

A. Yahweh's declaration is, "You will be my people, and I will be your God." [30:21-22]

Q? What will Jeremiah understand in the final days?

A. Jeremiah will understand that Yahweh's wrath will not return until the tempest of Yahweh has carried out and brought into being the intentions of Yahweh"s heart. [30:23-24]
