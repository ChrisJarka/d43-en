===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **land of Uz**  - This is the name of a land. (See: How to translate Names)
  * **he was blameless and upright**  - The words "blameless" and "upright" mean basically the same thing and are used together for emphasis. Alternate translantion: "He did what was right before God" (See: Doublets)
  * **one who honored God and turned from evil**  - This can be translated as a new sentence: "He honored God and did not do what was evil."
  * **seven sons and three daughters**  - "7 sons and 3 daughters" (See: How to translate numbers)
  * **He possessed seven thousand sheep**  - "He had 7000 sheep"
  * **three thousand camel****s**  - "3000 camels"
