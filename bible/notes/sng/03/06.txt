===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **What is that coming up** - "What is this group of people that is coming up"
  * **coming up from the wilderness** - The group of people is traveling from the desert to Jerusalem. Because the desert is low in the Jordan valley and Jerusalem is high in the mountains, the the group of people needs to go up in order to get to Jerusalem. 
  * **like a column of smoke** - People in the group were burning incense, and columns of smoke were going up into the air. So, the group of people looked like they were columns of smoke as they came up out  of the desert. AT: "with columns of smoke going up." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **a column of smoke** - Smoke rises from its source into the air. A column is an object that starts from the ground and gives support to another piece of a building. Smoke looks like a column as it rises. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **perfumed with myrrh and frankincense** - AT: "the sweet smelling smoke of myrrh and frankincense surround it." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **with all powders sold by merchants** - AT: "and the sweet smelling smokes of all powders that merchants sell." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **powders** - a fine dust made by crushing something solid, for example, dried flower petals or tree bark
  * **merchants** - people who buy and sell things or trade items for money or other things
  * **Look** - The word “behold” here shows that the young woman saw something interesting.
  * **it is the portable litter** - The word "portable" means that people can easily moved the litter from one place to another. 
  * **litter** - a bed with a cover that important people rode in while other people carried the bed
  * **sixty warriors surround it, sixty of Israel's soldiers** - This is repeated for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **warriors** - very mighty and brave men who fight for and defend their country, village, or leader
  * **surround** -  "are all around"
  * **Israel's soldiers** - "Israel's warriors"
