===== translationNotes: =====

  * **there is no injustice on my hands** - The hands represent what a person does. Having injustice on them represents having done something that is not just. AT: "there is no injustice in what I have done" or "I have not done anything unjust to anyone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
