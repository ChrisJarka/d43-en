===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you inhabitants of Zion** - "You" refers to all the people living in Jerusalem. (See: [[en:ta:translate:formsofyou]])
  * **for great in your midst** - "Yahweh, who lives with you, is mighty"
  * **Holy One of Israel** - This can be translated as "Yahweh of Israel, great and worthy of praise."
