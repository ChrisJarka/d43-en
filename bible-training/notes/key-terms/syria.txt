====== Syria ======


===== Facts: =====


Syria was a Roman province located in the region of modern-day Lebanon and Syria. This area is to the north and east of Israel. 


    * The inhabitants of Syria are also referred to as "Aramaeans" or "Aram" and are descendants from Aram, a descendant of Shem.
