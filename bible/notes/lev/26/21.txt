===== translationNotes: =====

  * **walk against me** - Walking represents behavior. Walking against God represents opposing him or rebelling against him. AT: "rebel against me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I will bring seven times more blows on you** - The noun blow can be expressed with the verb "hit." AT: "I will hit you seven times as much." This represents punishment. AT: "I will punish you seven times more severely" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * in proportion to your sins** - "I will punish you  you increase your sins" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]) 
  * **So your roads will become deserted** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "So no one will travel on your roads" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
