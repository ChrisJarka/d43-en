====== Numbers Chapter 28 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What was Moses instructed by Yahweh to tell the people?

A. Yahweh told Moses, "Command the people of Israel to offer sacrifices of food to me at the appointed times" [28:1-2]

Q? What food would be offered in the sacrifices?

A. The sacrifices would consisit of a male lamb in the morning. a male lamb in the evening and a a tenth of an ephah of fine flour as a grain offering mixed with a fourth of a hin of beaten oil. [28:3-6]
