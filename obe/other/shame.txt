====== shame, shameful, ashamed ======

===== Definition: =====

Shame is a sense of disgrace due to unrighteous or ungodly behaviors.

* The Prophet Isaiah said that those who make and worship idols will be put to shame.

* Shame can extend beyond the simple sense of disgrace to an act of humiliation by God if the offending person does not repent.
