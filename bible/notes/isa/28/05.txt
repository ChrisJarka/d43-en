===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh of Hosts** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:01:09|1:9]].
  * **will become a beautiful crown and a diadem of beauty** - This compares Yahweh to a beautiful crown or wreath of flowers. This emphasizes that those who remain after the exile will honor God, and God will restore their honor. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **a spirit of justice for him who sits in judgment** - This could be translated as a new sentence: "He will cause the leaders to be just and cause the soldiers of the city to be strong."
