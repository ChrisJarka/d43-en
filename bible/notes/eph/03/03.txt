===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **according to the revelation made known to me**  - "that God made known to me by revelation"
  * **the hidden truth about which I wrote a little previously**  - "the mystery that I briefly wrote to you about before" (See: [[:en:obe:other:mystery|mystery]])
  * **When you read about this**  - The pronoun "this" is referring to the hidden truths in the letter Paul is writing to the Ephesians.
  * **you will be able to understand**  - The word "understand" could be translated as "comprehend" or "realize".
  * **my insight into this hidden truth about Christ**  - The word "insight" could be translated "understanding". Also "hidden truth" is usually translated as "mystery". (See: [[:en:obe:other:mystery|mystery]])
  * **which in other generations was not made known to the human race**  - "This truth was not made known to people in the past"
  * **but now which has been revealed**  - "but now it has been shown" or “but now it has been made known”
