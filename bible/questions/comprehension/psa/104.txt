====== Psalms Chapter 104 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? The writer begins by calling upon his own soul to bless Yahweh. What does God appear to be, and how is he clothed?

A. "â€¦my God, you are very magnificent; you are clothed with splendor and majesty." [104:1]

Q? What does the writer say it is like when Yahweh spreads out the heavens?

A. "You spread out the heavens like a tent curtain." [104:2]

Q? How does the writer express the way which Yahweh laid, "â€¦the foundations of the earth?"

A. "He laid the foundations of the earth, and it will never be moved." [104:5]

Q? How does the writer say that Yahweh caused the waters to recede?

A. Yahweh's, "rebuke made the waters recede; at the sound of (his) thunderous voice they fled." [104:7]

Q? When the writer says, "You have set a boundary for themâ€¦, they will not cover the earth again," to what is he referring?

A. "You covered the earth with water like a garment; the water covered the mountains." [104:6]

Q? When the writer says, "He waters the mountainsâ€¦," what is the result of that action which Yahweh performs?

A. "The earth is filled with the fruit of his labor." [104:13]

Q? What is the purpose the writer points to when he says, "He (Yahweh) makes the grass grow for the cattle and plants for man to cultivate?"

A. The purpose he mentions is, "So that man may produce food from the earth." [104:14]

Q? When the writer says, "(Yahweh) planted the cedars of Lebanon," what does he say the birds and stork do in response?

A."There the birds make their nests. The stork makes the fir tree her home." [104:17]

Q? Yahweh appointed the moon and the sun to mark what?

A. He appointed the moon to mark the seasons;
the sun knows its time for setting. [104:19]

Q? What happens to the young lions when the sun rises?

A. When the sun rises, they retreat and sleep in their dens. [104:22]

Q? When the writer speaks to Yahweh what three things does he say about all his works? 

A. "Yahweh, how many and varied are your works! With wisdom you made them all. [104:24]

Q? In what way do all the innumerable creatures in the sea get food from Yahweh?  

A. "All these look to you to give them their food on time." [104:27]

Q? What happens to the creatures if Yahweh hides his face or takes away their breath? 

A. When you hide your face, they are troubled; if you take away their breath, they die and return to dust. [104:29]

Q? What are the two things that result when Yahweh sends out his Spirit?

A. When you send out your Spirit, they are created, and you renew the countryside. [104:30]

Q? When the writer says, "May the glory of Yahweh last forever," he adds that he hopes Yahweh will have what attitude toward his creation?

A. "...may Yahweh enjoy his creation." [104:31]
