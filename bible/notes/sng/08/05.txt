===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **​Who is this who is coming…leaning on her beloved? **- The women who are attending the bride are remembering the time when she was child. The time from childhood to adulthood seems to pass quickly. (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **I awakened you under the apricot tree.**  - The woman is saying to her lover that she remembers when they became lovers. (See: Euphemism)
  * **​there your mother conceived you  **- The woman is comparing their first lovemaking to the beginning of a new life. (See: Metaphor)
  * **there she gave birth to you, she delivered you - ** The woman is emphasizing the wonderful result of beginning a new life together. (See: Doublet)​
