====== blasphemy, blaspheme ======

===== Definition: =====

To blaspheme is to slander or insult God by saying things that are not true about him.

  * Blasphemy is speech or behavior that shows a deep disrespect for God. It involves speaking against him or lying about him.
  * It is blasphemy for a human being to claim to be God or to claim that there is a God other than the one true God. When Jesus said things that showed he was revealing himself to be God, the Jews falsely accused him of blasphemy.
  * "To blaspheme" can also be translated as, "to dishonor God" or "to speak disrespectfully about God."
