===== Translation Notes: =====



  * **Let the waters swarm with swarms of living creatures** - Other ways to translate this are "Let the waters be full of things that move together in large groups" or "Let many things live in the oceans." Some languages may have a word that covers all 
  * **birds** - This can be translated as "animals that fly." It can include birds and insects that fly.
  * **the expanse of the sky** - This can be translated as "the open space of the sky" or "the sky."
  * **God created**  - This is logically connected to the previous verse and could be translated "So God created."
  * **great sea creatures** - "large animals that live in the sea"
  * **after their kind**  - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:01:11|1:11,12]].
  * **every winged bird**  - Since all birds have wings, it may be more natural in some languages to say "every bird."
  * **God saw that it was good**  - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:01:09|1:10]].
