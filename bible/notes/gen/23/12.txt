===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **bowed himself down before** - “gave respect to.” This is not an act of worship, but one of humility that shows respect to someone in a higher position of authority.
  * **people of the land **- "people who owned the land" or "community"
  * **in the hearing**  - "in the presence" or "in front"
  * **Take it from me**  - "Take the money from me"
