===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **See** - The word “see” here adds emphasis to what follows. It can also be translated as "Listen" or "Indeed."
  * **your support and staff** - This can also be translated as “everything that you depend on”
  * **the might man, the warrior** - This is the beginning of a list of categories of people that God would take away from Jerusalem and Judah. These can also be translated with plural nouns: "the mighty men, the warriors."
  * **the captain of fifty** - "the captain who is in charge of fifty soldiers." This can also be translated as "the army officer" or "the military leader."
  * **fifty**  - "50" (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translateunknowns-numbers]])
