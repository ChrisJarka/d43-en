===== Translation Notes: =====

  *  **the house of Jacob shall possess their own possessions.**  - whatever God gave as a heritage to the children of Abraham, he will restore to them when they return from exile
  * **The house of Jacob will be a fire, and the house of Joseph a flame**  - 1) These phrases liken the houses of Jacob and his son Joseph to a fire that will destroy the Edomites. 2) The phrases describe the prophesy that God would avenge the cruelty caused by the house of Esau on the descendants of Jacob. (See: Metaphor)
  * **and the house of Esau will be stubble **- The Edomites are likened to stubble that is easilly burned and destroyed. (See: Metaphor)
  * **stubble**  - stumps of grain and other stalks left in the ground when the crop is cut. Usually dry and easilly burned.
