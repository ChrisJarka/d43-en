====== partial, partiality ======

===== Definition: =====

To be partial to or show partiality towards is to exercise a conscious choice to elevate one thing over another. (Favoritism)

  * The New Testament tells believers not to show partiality to rich people because of their status.
  * The Old Testament instructs kings and judges to now show partiality or favoritism when judging matters in court.

(See also: other)
