===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **joy**  - This is being used ironically. The only happiness that can come out of a godless life is being forgotten after you die. (See: irony).
  * **such a person **- AT: "the godless man".
  * **behavior **- AT: "actions," or "way of life".
  * **other plants will sprout up from the same soil in his place -**  The analogy of plants is continued from 8:8-10. "Other plants" refers to other godless people. AT: "When one wicked man dies, another will take his place". (See: Metaphor)
  * **sprout up**  - AT: "grow".
  * **same soil **- AT: "the rocky ground".
  * **his place**  - AT: "in the place of the godless man".
  * **neither will he take the hand of evildoers **- Here, "take the hand" is an idiom. AT: "he will not support evildoers," or "he will not stregthen evildoers". (See: Idiom).
