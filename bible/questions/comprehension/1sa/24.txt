====== 1 Samuel Chapter 24 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? When Saul returned from attacking the Philistines, what did he do?

A. He was told where David was in the wilderness, and went to find David and his men. [24:1-2]

Q? What happened while David and his men were hiding way back in a cave?

A. Saul went in the cave to relieve himself. David's men wanted David to take over his enemy, but instead, David cut off a corner of Saul's robe without being noticed. [24:3-4]

Q? Why would David not allow his men to attack Saul but let him leave the cave in safety?

A. David's heart afflicted him and he felt that Yahweh would not want him to harm his master, Yahweh's anointed. [24:5-7]

Q? When David left the cave after Saul, how did he show respect to Saul?

A. David called Saul his master the king and bowed his face to the ground. [24:8]

Q? How did David show Saul that he did not want to bring harm to him even though Saul wanted to kill him?

A. David revealed to him that he had been close enough to kill Saul in the cave, but had only cut the corner of his robe. [24:10-11]
