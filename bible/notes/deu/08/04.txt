===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses continues to address all the people of Israel.

  * **grow old on you** - "wear out and fall off of you" or "get so old that you could not wear it"
  * **swell up** - "hurt"
  * **during those forty years** - "during the forty years in the desert"
  * **You will think about in your heart** - Your heart is a symbol of your deep thoughts and understanding. AT: "You will understand" or "You will think about." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **as a man** - A man is a symbol of all parents. AT: "as fathers" or "as parents." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **corrects** - "disciplines"
  * **son** - Son is a symbol of all children. AT: "children" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **You will keep** - "Obey"
  * **you might walk in his ways** - "live like Yahweh wants you to live"
