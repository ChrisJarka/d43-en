====== Dan ======

===== Facts: =====

Dan was the son of Jacob born of Bilhah, Rachel's servant, and was one of the twelve tribes of Israel.  Dan was the name of the region settled by the tribe of Dan in the northern part of Canaan upon entry into the promised land.  The city of Dan was located west of Jerusalem, and existed in the time of Abram.

  * The city of Dan probably did not exist during the time the nation of Israel entered the promised land.
