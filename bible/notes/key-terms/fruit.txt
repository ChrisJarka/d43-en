====== fruit ======


===== Definition: When used Biblically fruit refers to the outcome of a person's spiritual habits. Fruit can also be used literally to describe what is produced by a tree. A passage's context will clearly define which reference is being used. =====


    * The Bible uses terms such as "sowing" and "reaping" to describe spiritual behavior patterns in a person's life.
    * If you "sow" to your old nature you will "reap" death. If you "sow" to the spirit you will "reap" spiritual fruit.
    * The Bible lists spiritual fruit as love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, meekness, self control.
