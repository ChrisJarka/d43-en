===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues to describe Yahweh's judgment against the women of Judah.

  * **rings** - A decoration worn around the finger.
  * **nose jewels** - A decoration worn in or through the nose.
  * **festive robes** - A long, loose garment with decorations that were worn over clothes for all to see.
  * **mantles** - A cloth worn over the shoulders on the outside of the clothes.
  * **veils** - See how you translated it in [[en:bible:notes:isa:03:18|3:19]].
  * **handbags** -  A bag to used to carry small things.
  * **hand mirrors** - A small surface, held in one's hand and used to see oneself.
  * **fine linen** - A soft and highly desired cloth worn by rich people.
  * **head pieces** - A cloth or small hat worn over the hair.
  * **wraps** - A decorative cloth wrapped around a woman to make herself beautiful.
