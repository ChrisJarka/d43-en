====== Sample Synecdoche Notes ======

Sample notes Acts:

  * **made with hands** - "made by people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Samaria** - This refers to the many people, who had become believers, throughout the district of Samaria. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **the Jews** - This refers to the leaders of the Jews. AT: "the Jewish leaders" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **a voice spoke to him** - The person speaking is not specified. The "voice" was probably God, although it could possibly have been an angel from God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **all the expectation of the people of the Jews** - Here "the people of the Jews" probably referred mainly to the Jewish leaders. AT: "all that the Jewish leaders thought would happen to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **After the reading of the law and the prophets** - The "law and the prophets" refer to parts of the Jewish scriptures which were read. AT: "After someone read from the books of the law and the writings of the prophets" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **stirred up the minds of the Gentiles** - Here "minds" refer to the people. AT: "caused the Gentiles to be hostile" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **by my mouth** - Here "mouth" refers to Peter. AT: "from me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche|Synecdoche]])
  * **the foundations of the prison** - Here "foundations" means the entire prison. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **God was doing mighty deeds by the hands of Paul** - Here "hands" stands for Paul's whole person. AT: "God was causing Paul to do miracles" or "God was doing miracles through Paul" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **He is saying that there are no gods that are made with hands** - Here "hands" refers to the whole person. AT: "He is saying that the idols that people make are not real gods" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **will see my face no more** - The word "face" here represents Paul's physical body. AT: "will not see me anymore on this earth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche|Synecdoche]])
  * **that he should not set foot in Jerusalem** - Here "foot" stands for the whole person. AT: "that he should not go to Jerusalem" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **heard a voice say to me** - Here "voice" stands for the person speaking. AT: "I heard someone say to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
