===== Translation Notes: =====


    * Jesus continues his parable
    * **and he cried out and said**  - that is, the rich man cried out by saying, or cried out to say
    * **have mercy on me **- This is a request from the rich man to Abraham, so it can be translated as, "please have pity on me," or "please have mercy on me."
    * **send Lazarus **- This is the expression of the requested mercy. This can be translated as "please send Lazarus," or "can you please send Lazarus."
    * **dip**-This is same word used for baptism.
    * **he may dip the tip of his finger**- This indicates the smallness of the amount requested. This can be translated as "he may wet the tip of his finger."explanation
    * **I am in anguish in this flame**- That is "I am in great pain in this flame," or 'I am suffering torment in this flame." or "I am in great agony in these flames."
    * **bold words**  - explanation
    * **and send Lazarus**  - This can also be translated as, "and send Lazarus to me"
    * **That he may dip**  - That is, "That Lazarus may dip"
