======  Matthew 28 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== "Make disciples" ==
These two verse ([[:en:bible:notes:mat:28:18|28:19-20) are commonly known as "The Great Commission" because they involve a very important command given to all Christians. Christians are to "make disciples" by going to people, sharing the gospel with them and training them to live as Christians. It is a summary one of a Christian major responsibilities.
