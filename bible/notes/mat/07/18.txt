===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to teach his disciples. This event began in 5:1.

  * **no good tree that produces rotten fruit****â€¦**  - this is a metaphor that is continued from the previous verses talking about good and bad prophets. (See:Metaphor)
  * **cut down and thrown into the fire**  - this refers to false prophets that will be thrown out.
  * **by their fruits you will know them**  - this is a metaphor referring to the deeds of the prophets that will show how they can be known as good or bad. (See:Metaphors)
