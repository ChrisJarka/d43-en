====== tent ======

===== Definition: =====

A tent is a temporary dwelling made of some type of fabric draped over poles.

  * Israel lived in tents during their wanderings through the desert of Sinai.
  * The Apostle Paul's occupation was a tent maker, which he praticed while writing and traveling for the gospel ministry.

(See also: other)
