===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **a woman came to him **- The pronoun "him" refers to Jesus, not Simon the leper.
  * **alabaster jar **- A container or flask made of alabaster, which is a soft stone having a creamy color.
  * **ointment **- This refers to a perfume or an oil that has a pleasing odor.
  * **for this waste**
 - "for pouring it out for nothing"
