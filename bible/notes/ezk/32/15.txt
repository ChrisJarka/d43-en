===== Translation Notes: =====

This ends Ezekiel's lament to the King of Egypt and the nation.

  * **When I make the land of Egypt–a land that was full–a wasteland and a desolation; - **"When I make Egypt, a land that was growing rich, a wasteland and desert,"
  * **When I strike all of her inhabitants, -**  "and when I hurt (or "punish") all of her people,"
  * **then they will know****that I am Yahweh!-**  "they" refers to "the nations"
  * **There will be a lament! -**  "People will grieve, mourn, and cry out!"
  * **For the daughters of the ** <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>** nations will lament her  ** - "The women of all nations will grieve (or cry) for her."</font>
  * **And concerning all her entourage,- **"and will grieve for all the people of Egypt."
  * **<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>this is the Lord Yahweh’s declaration</font>    - ** I, the Lord God make this clear (or "have spoken")."  See "decree" in Important Terms."
