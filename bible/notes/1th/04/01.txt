===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **we**  - The pronoun "we" refers to Paul, Timothy and Silvanus
  * **you**  - The pronoun "you" refers to the believers in Thessalonian church (See: [[:en:ta:translate:formsofyou]])
  * **received instructions**  -they were taught
  * **must walk**  - ought to live (Hendiadys)
  * **as you do walk **- they already lived in a way that was pleasing to God
  * **even more**  - continually discover more ways your life can honor God
  * **you know…instructions**  - remember what we taught you
