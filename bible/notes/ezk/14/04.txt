===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Therefore announce this to them**  - The word "them" refers to the "men from the elders of Israel"
  * **who takes his idols into his heart**  - "who considers his idols to be very important"
  * **the stumbling block of his iniquity before his face**  - Here the idols of Israel are called a stumbling block. Falling down is being compared to sinning against God. The words "before his face" mean they are in front of him and refer to looking at them in worship. "the idol that he has used to sin by worshiping it" (See: [[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:metaphor]])
  * **I, Yahweh, will answer him according to the number of their idols**  - Alternate translation: "I, Yahweh will tell him worshipping many idols"
  * **house of Israel**  - “people of Israel"
