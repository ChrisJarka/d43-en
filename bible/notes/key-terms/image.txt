====== image, graven image ======

===== Definition: =====

The term graven image or image in the Old Testament usually refers to a physical object that is said to be a picture of what a god looks like, such as a statue of a man or animal.  The creation of images in the Old Testament to represent God was forbidden.

Man is also created in the image of God, meaning man has some of the divine characteristics, such as having a spirit that continues to live eternally after natural death.

Jesus is the image of the invisible God, meaning He is the exact representation of God's nature in human form.
