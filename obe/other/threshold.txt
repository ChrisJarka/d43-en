====== threshold, doorpost ======

===== Definition: =====

Threshold and doorpost are both parts of a door to a building or house. The threshold is the part on the bottom that you step over; the doorpost is a vertical beam on both sides of the door which support the top of the door frame or header.

  * The Israelites were instructed to put blood on their doorposts during the passover observance, so the death angel would pass over them and their home.
  * As a covenant of permanent ownership, a slave could place his or her ear next to the doorpost of their master's house and then the master would hammer a nail through their earlobe, piercing the ear.
