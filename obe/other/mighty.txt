====== mighty, might ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "mighty" describes someone or something that is strong and powerful.

  * Men who are described as "mighty" are strong, brave, or heroic.
  * In the Old Testament, "mighty" often describes a man who is victorious in battle.
  * It also can refer to being effective and successful, as in the description of Moses being "mighty in word and deed" in Acts 7..
