===== translationNotes: =====

  * **house of Onesiphorus** - Here "house" refer to the people who live there. AT: "family of Onesiphorus" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Onesiphorus** - See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:2ti:01:15|1:16]].
  * **Erastus...Trophimus...Eubulus...Pudens, Linus, Claudia** - These are all names of men. The name "Claudia" may be a female name. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Miletus** - This is the name of a city to the south of Ephesus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Do your best to come** - "Make a way to come"
  * **before winter** - "before the cold season"
  * **also Pudens, Linus, Claudia, and all the brothers** - "Pudens, Linus, Claudia and all the brothers also greet you."
  * **all the brothers** - Here "brothers" means all believers whether male or female. AT: "all the believers here" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_gendernotations]])
  * **May the Lord be with your spirit** - "I am asking God to make your spirit strong." Here "you" is singular and refers to Timothy. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **May grace be with you** - "I am asking the Lord to show his kindness to all of you there" or "I am asking the Lord to show favor to all of you there." Here "you" is plural and refers to all the believers there with Timothy. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
