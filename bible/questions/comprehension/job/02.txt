====== Job Chapter 02 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What happened when the sons of God presented themselves before Yahweh?

A. Satan also came and presented himself. Yahweh asked him, "From where are you coming?" Satan answered, he was going to and fro on the earth, walking back and forth. [2:1-2]

Q? How does Yahweh describe Job to Satan?

A. Yahweh said he is my servant, there is no one like him on earth, a perfect and upright man, he fears God and turns from evil. He holds fast to his integrity, even though Satan persuaded God to act against Job. [2:3]

Q? What does Satan want to do to Job now so that Job will curse God, and how does Yahweh respond?

A. Satan wants to test Job physically by hurting his body to where he will curse God. Yahweh tells Satan that Job is in his hand but that he may not kill him. [2:4-6]

Q? How did Satan afflict Job's body and what did Job do?

A. Satan afflicted Job with severe boils from head to foot. Job took a piece of shard and scraped the boils as he sat in ashes. [2:7-8]

Q? What did Job's wife want him to do?

A. Job's wife questioned his reaction and wanted him to curse God and die. [2:9]

Q? How did Job respond to his wife?

A. He told her she talked like a foolish woman.  He asked if they should receive good at the hand of God and not evil.  Job did not sin in all this matter. [2:10]
