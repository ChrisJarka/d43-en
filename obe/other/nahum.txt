====== Nahum ======

===== Facts: =====

Nahum was one of the Old Testament's minor prophets.

  * Nahum prophesied about the desctruction of Nineveh due to their harsh treatment of Israel.
  * Nahum was from Elkosh, approximately 20 miles from Jerusalem.
