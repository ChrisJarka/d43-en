===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **devout** - That is "devoted to God" or "faithful to God."
    * **Comforter of Israel**  - That is, "the one who would comfort Israel." It is another name for the "Messiah" or, "Christ."
    * **the Holy Spirit was upon him**  - This could also be translated as "the Holy Spirit was with him." God was with him in a special way, and gave him wisdom and direction in his life. 
    * **it had been revealed to him by the Holy Spirit**  - That is, "The Holy Spirit had shown him" or, "The Holy Spirit had told him."
    * **he would not die before he had seen the Lord's Christ** - This could also be translated as "he would see the Lord's Christ before dying" or "he would see the Lord's Christ and die only after that."
