===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The crucible is for silver and the furnace is for gold; and a person is tested when he is praised **- As the refiner tests silver and gold with heat, so a man's character is tested when he is praised. 
  * **crucible** - a container used for heating substances to very high temperatures
  * **Though you should crush the fool with the pestle - along with the grain— yet his foolishness will not leave him **- AT: "Though you should crush the fool in a mortar among grain with the pestle." The nature of the fool is retained even after punishment. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **pestle** - a tool for grinding materials in a mortar
