===== Text Formatting =====

These are the USFM tags that are used in the Unlocked Bibles and how they look in the APP text.

\id - file identification\\
\ide - file encoding\\
\h - header text\\
\toc1 - table of contents: long text\\
\toc2 - table of contents: short text\\
\toc3 - table of contents: abbreviation\\
\mt - major title\\
\c - chapter marker\\
\v - verse marker\\
\p - paragraph marker\\
\b - blank line\\
\s - section header\\
\s1 - section header: first level\\
\sr - section reference\\
\sp - speaker\\
\s5 - chunk divider\\
\q - quotation\\
\q1 - quotation: first level indent\\
\q2 - quotation: second level indent\\
\add ... \add* - inserted text or comments not in original\\
\f + \ft ... \f* - footnote\\
