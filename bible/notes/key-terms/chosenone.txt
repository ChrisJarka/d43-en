====== chosen one ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Bible, the term "chosen one" usually refers to Jesus as the one whom God chose to be the Messiah.

  * To be chosen is to be appointed for a special purpose.
  * The people of God, individuals and certain leaders are also referred to "chosen."

(See also: Christ, Messiah, elect)
