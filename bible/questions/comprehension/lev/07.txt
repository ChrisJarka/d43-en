====== Leviticus Chapter 07 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What offering is like the guilt offering?

A. The sin offering is like the guilt offering. [7:7]

Q? What may the priest keep from anyone's burnt offering?

A. The priest may keep the hide of that offering. [7:8]

Q? Which offering belongs to the priest?

A. The grain offering belongs to the priest. [7:9,10]

Q? What must be done with the meat of the sacrifice that has not been eaten by the third day?

A. The meat of the sacrifice that has not been eaten by the third day must be burnt. [7:17]

Q? What must be done to anyone who eats fat from an animal or blood from a bird or an animal?

A. Anyone who eats fat from an animal or blood from a bird or animal must be cut off from his people. [7:25, 26]

Q? What must be given to the priest after the fat has been burned on the altar?

A. After the fat is burned on the altar the right thigh will be given to the priest. [7:32]
