===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh is speaking to the house of Israel.
  * **a new heart and a new spirit** - See how you translated "heart" and "spirit" in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:11:19|11:19]].  
  * **in your innermost parts** - "within you" or "inside of you"
  * **the heart of stone** - The phrase compares the people's hard heart with hard stone. Alternate translation: "the heart that is as hard as stone." See how you translated "hard hearts" in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:02:04|2:4]].  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **from your flesh** - "out of your body"
  * **a heart of flesh** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:11:19|11:19]].
  * **walk in my statutes** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:11:19|11:20]].
  * **keep my decrees, so you will do them** - See how you translated "will carry out my decrees and do them" in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:11:19|11:20]].
  * **will inhabit** - "will live in" or "will dwell in"
  * **your ancestors** - "your family that lived a long time before you"
  * **my people** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:11:19|11:20]].
  *  **I will be your God** - See how you translated "I will be their God" in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:11:19|11:20]].
