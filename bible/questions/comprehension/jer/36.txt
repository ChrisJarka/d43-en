====== Jeremiah Chapter 36 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why did Yahweh want Jeremiah to write his words on a scroll?

A. Yahweh wanted Jeremiah to write his words on a scroll so that the people of Judah might listen to all the disasters which Yahweh intended to bring on them and turn from their wicked path so Yahweh could forgive their sin. [36:1-3]

Q? What did Jeremiah command Baruch to do?

A. Jeremiah commanded Baruch to take the scroll upon which Baruch had written Jeremiah's dictation and read Yahweh's words from the scroll in the hearing of the people in his house and in the hearing of all of Judah. [36:4-6]

Q? What was Jeremiah's purpose for having Baruch read the scroll?

A. Jeremiah was hopeful that each person would turn from their wicked way. [36:7-8]

Q? What happened during the fast which was proclamed in honor of Yahweh?

A. Baruch read aloud Jeremiah's words in Yahweh's house in the hearing of all the people. [36:9-10]

Q? What happened after Micaiah saw all the officials in the secretary's room in the king's house?

A. Micaiah told them about all the words that he had heard Baruch read aloud in the people's hearing. [36:11-13]

Q? What did Jehudi say to Baruch?

A. Jehudi said, "Take the scroll in your hand which you were reading in the people's hearing and sit down and read it in our hearing." [36:14-15]
