====== 1 Samuel Chapter 18 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? How strongly was the soul of Jonathan bound to the soul of David?

A. The soul of Jonathan was bound to the soul of David, and Jonathan loved him as his own soul. [18:1]

Q? What did Jonathan take off and give to David with his armor?

A. Jonathan took off the robe that he was wearing and gave it to David with his armor. [18:4]

Q? Over whom did Saul set David?

A. Saul set him over the men of war. [18:5]

Q? What had the singers' song ascribed to David, which displeased Saul?

A. The song displeased Saul, that they have ascribed to David ten thousands, but they had ascribed only thousands to Saul . [18:8]

Q? What did Saul think as he threw the spear at David?

A. Saul threw the spear, for he thought that he would pin David to the wall. [18:11]

Q? Why was David propsering in all his ways?

A. David was prospering in all his ways, for Yahweh was with him. [18:14]

Q? When Saul told David to only be courageous for him and fight Yahweh’s battles, why did he think his hand would not need to be on David?

A. Saul thought that his hand needed not be on him, but let the hand of the Philistines be on him. [18:17]

Q? What did Saul think his daughter Michal would be for David if Saul gave her to him ?

A. Saul thought he would give her to David, so that she could be a trap for him. [18:20]

Q? Where did Saul command his servants to speak with David about becoming the king's son-in-law?

A. Saul commanded his servants to speak with David in private. [18:22]

Q? Why did David think he was lightly esteemed and not worthy to be the king's son-in-law?

A. He said that is was no small matter to be the king’s son-in-law, since he was a poor man, and lightly esteemed. [18:23]

Q? What was the only dowry the king desired from David?

A. The king did not desire any dowry, only one hundred foreskins of the Philistines. [18:25]

Q? What did David and his men do, exceeding the kings kings dowry request?

A. David went with his men and killed two hundred Philistines and brought their foreskins. [18:27]

Q? How great was David's success, so his name was highly regarded?

A. David succeeded more than all the servants of Saul, so that his name was highly regarded. [18:30]
