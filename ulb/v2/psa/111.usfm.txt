\v 1 Praise you Jehovah.
\q I will give thanks to Jehovah with my whole heart,
\q In the council of the upright, and in the congregation.
\q
\v 2 The works of Jehovah are great,
\q Sought out of all them that have pleasure therein.
\q
\v 3 His work is honor and majesty;
\q And his righteousness endures for ever.
\q
\v 4 He has made his wonderful works to be remembered:
\q Jehovah is gracious and merciful.
\q
\v 5 He has given food to them that fear him:
\q He will ever be mindful of his covenant.
\q
\v 6 He has showed his people the power of his works,
\q In giving them the heritage of the nations.
\q
\v 7 The works of his hands are truth and justice;
\q All his precepts are sure.
\q
\v 8 They are established for ever and ever;
\q They are done in truth and uprightness.
\q
\v 9 He has sent redemption to his people;
\q He has commanded his covenant for ever:
\q Holy and reverend is his name.
\q
\v 10 The fear of Jehovah is the beginning of wisdom;
\q A good understanding have all they that do his commandments:
\q His praise endures for ever.
