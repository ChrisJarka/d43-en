===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now when it became day** - "When morning came"
  * **no small excitement** - This is a type of emphatic expression. It can be translated as "great excitement" or "a lot of excitement". (See:  [[:en:ta:figuresofspeech:litotes]])
  * **regarding** - "concerning" or "about"
  * **searched for him and could not find him** - "searched for Peter and could not find Peter"
  * **he questioned them and ordered them to be put to death**  - "Herod questioned the guards and commanded soldiers to execute the guards"
  * **he went**  - Herod went
  * **on an appointed day**  - "On the day he had planned"
  * **The voice**  - "His is the voice" or "This is the voice"
  * **struck him**  - "afflicted Herod" or "caused Herod to become very ill"
  * **he didn't give**  - "Herod did not give" or "Herod failed to give"
  * **he was eaten by worms and died**  - "worms ate Herod and Herod died."
