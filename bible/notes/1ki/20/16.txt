  * **they**  - The Israelite army.
  * **he, him **- Refers to Behadad.
  * **thirty-two **- (See: Translate Numbers) "32"
  * **the young officers who serve the governors of the districts **- These are the leaders of Ahab's forces.
  * **scouts **- Soldiers sent to get information about the enemy's location, size, and equiptment.
