====== Psalms 075 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 76 is a wisdom psalm; teaching what will happen to evil people.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

God has warned the evil people that they will be punished. But he will strengthen the good people.
