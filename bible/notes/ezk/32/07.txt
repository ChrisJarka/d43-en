===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh is still speaking to Pharaoh. Yahweh compares the way that he will punish Pharaoh with scary things that could happen in the sky. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])

  * **Then when I put out your lamp** - "when I put out you light." Yahweh is comparing the way he will destroy Pharaoh to the way he would put out the flame of a lamp.
  * **I will cover the heavens** - Yahweh will cover the sky so that people on earth cannot see the light of the stars, sun and moon.
  * **darken its stars** - "I will make the stars in the sky dark." The stars will be dark because their light is gone.
  * **the moon will not shine its light!**  - "the moon will not reflect light!" or "The moon will not show light!" or "The moon will be dark!".
  * **this is the Lord Yahweh’s declaration** - "I, the Lord God, have said it."
