===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **This is the record of the descendants**  - This could be translated as "this is the account of" or "this is the family history of" and refers to the people and events that follow (11:27-25:11).
  * **Died in the presence of**  - this may reflect that fact that Terah out lived his son and does not necessarily convey the idea that Terah was present when his son died. It may also be translated as "he died before his father did."
  * **Terah's sons**  - The order in which Terah's three sons appear in the text don't tell us their birth order.
