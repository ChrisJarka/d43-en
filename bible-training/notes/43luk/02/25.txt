===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **comforter of Israel**  - another name for Messiah and Christ
    * **the Holy Spirit was upon him**  - God was with him in a special way, and gave wisdom and direction in his life
    * **it had been revealed to him by the Holy Spirit**  - That is, "The Holy Spirit had shown him" or, "The Holy Spirit had told him."
    * **the Lord's Christ**  - This refers back to "the comforter of Israel."
    * **text**  - note
