===== Translation Notes: =====

Continuation of the statement started in verse 5 and that will end in verse 10.

  * **treated us**  - This refers to the people of Israel that were living during that time. The individual here is speaking as part of the entire group.
  * **cried out to Yahweh - **"prayed with great feeling" or "pleaded to Yahweh"
  * **heard our voice**  - "heard us" (See: Synecdoche)
