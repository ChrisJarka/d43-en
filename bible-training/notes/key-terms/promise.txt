====== promise ======


===== Definition: =====

A promise is a vow or commitment to do something.

    * When someone promises to do something, he says that he will definitely do it.
    * When someone promises that something will happen, he says that it will definitely happen.
    * Promises are important parts of formal agreements such as covenants.
