===== translationNotes: =====

  * **with his heart set against the holy covenant** - This refers to the king's desire to oppose God's covenant in Israel. This may be stated in active form. AT: "he will be determined to oppose the holy covenant" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]) 
  * **his heart** - This refers to the king's desires. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **holy covenant** - Here "holy" describes God's covenant with Israel. It implies that the covenant should be honored and obeyed because it comes from God himself. AT: "God's covenant, which all the Israelites should obey"
  * **set against the holy covenant** - The king's desire to act against the holy covenant represents his desire to stop the Israelites from obeying that covenant. AT: "to stop the Israelites from obeying the holy covenant" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **He will act** - This implies that the king will do certain actions in Israel. AT: "He will do what he wants to in Israel" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
