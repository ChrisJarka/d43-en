===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues his teaching on the hill for his followers.

  * **Blessed are**  - These means God is pleased.
  * **the peacemakers**  - These are the people who help others to have peace with one another.
  * **sons of God**  - These are God's own children (See: Metaphor)
  * **those who are persecuted**  - (See UDB)
  * **for righteousness sake**  - This refers to the people who live their lives obeying God.
  * **theirs is the kingdom of heaven**  - This refers to those who live in the kingdom of God's rule and authority.
