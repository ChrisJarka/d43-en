===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]

See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]]

  * **onyx**  - This is a black stone that has stripes of white or brown.
  * **engravings**
 - This is a <font 14px line-height: 19.6px;/ ;;rgb(51, 51, 51) font-family: 'Noto Sans', sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 19.6px;;;inherit>design cut into a hard material like wood, stone, or metal.</font> See 38:23.
  * **as on a signet**  - The stones used in the ephod have names written on them, which is similar to a signet, a ring used in biblical times that often had a name written on it. AT: "engraved with names". (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
