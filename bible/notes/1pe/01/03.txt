\v 3 Praise God, who is the Father of our Lord Jesus Christ! It is because of his great mercy to us that he has given us new life. Because he has caused Jesus Christ to become alive again after he died, he has enabled us to live very confidently; that is, to fully expect to receive the things that God has promised to give us.
\v 4 He has enabled us to expect to receive an absolutely imperishable inheritance that has been preserved in heaven for us.
\v 5 God, by his mighty power, is guarding you as you trust in Jesus. He is keeping you safe so that he may, at the end of the time in which we now live, completely deliver you from Satan's power.
