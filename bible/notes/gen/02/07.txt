===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **formed** - "molded" or "shaped" or "made"
  * **eastward** - "in the east" 
  * **breath of life** - Whatever is given this breath from God is a different kind of living being than plants or animals, with a conscience and a spiritual connection to God.
  * **a garden** - This could have been an orchard of fruit trees, or a park with all kinds of trees.
