====== 2 Samuel 01 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

Some translations prefer to set apart quotations, songs and poems. The ULB and many other English translations indent all the lines of 1:19-27, which is a song.

This chapter continues the material from 1 Samuel. 


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

= David's funeral song ==
David praised Saul and Jonathan as great warriors and Jonathan as his dearest friend. 


=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphor ==
Saul expressed his great pain through the use of a [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]: "great suffering has taken hold of me." 

== Synecdoche ==
David used two [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche|synecdoches]] to express that Jonathan and Saul were great warriors: "the bow of Jonathan did not turn back, and the sword of Saul did not return empty."
