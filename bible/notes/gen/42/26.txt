===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **As one of them opened his sack to give his donkey feed in the lodging place, he saw his money. **- "One of them opened his sack to give some feed to his donkey at the lodging place. He saw his money."
  * **to give his donkey feed**  - "to give some feed to his donkey"
  * **in the lodging place**  - "at the lodging place"
  * **Behold**  - "Look!", "Whaa?" (in the vernacular)
  * **Look at it; it is in my sack. **- "Look in my sack!"
  * **And their hearts sank and they turned trembling to one another. They said,**  - "Their hearts sank. They trembled one to another, saying,"
  * **"What is this that God has done to us?"**  - The brothers fear God's wrath and punishment (seen in verses 21 and 22) for plotting Joseph's death and then selling him into slavery.
