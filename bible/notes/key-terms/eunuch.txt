====== eunuch ======

===== Definition: =====

A eunuch is a man who has been castrated.

  * In ancient times, these men were often a king's servants who were set as guards over the women's quarters (as in the story of Esther).
  * Some eunuchs were  important government officials, as is probable in the Acts 8 account of the Ethiopian eunuch that Phillip encountered.

(See also: other)
