====== Hamath, Lebo-Hamath ======

===== Facts: =====

Hamath was a Hittite city in northern Syria, north of the land of Canaan. The name Lebo-Hamath probably refers to the same city.

  * King David had favorable relations with the king of Hamath.
  * Hamath was said to be one of Solomon's storehouse cities where provisions were kept.
  * The land of Hamath was where King Nebuchadnezzar of Babylon had King Zedekiah killed.
  * some versions translate "Lebo-Hamath" as "entrance to Hamath" or "border of Hamath" which are also possible meanings.
