===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They will devour Israel with open mouth**  - "like a wild animal tears another animal apart and devours it." (UDB) See: Metaphor.
  * **For in his anger Yahweh will not stop**  - God is still very angry with the people
  * **his hand will still be stretched out to strike**  - God's outstretched hand will punish beyond what the people had already experienced.
