===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **nor **- "and you must not make"
  * **earth beneath- **"earth below"
  * **bow down to them or worship them**  -The word "them" refers to carved figures or falses gods.
  * **jealous- ** God wants His people to only worship Him and to be their only God.
  * **punish ancestors' wickedness by** bringing punishment on the descendants- ****God will punish children for the sin of their parents.
  * **convenant faithfulness**- God will be faithful to His convenant.  He will keep His promise.
  * **thousands of those**- "many generations"
  * **keep- **"obey"
