====== Introduction to Malachi ======

===== Part 1: General Introduction =====

===Outline of Malachi===

  - The authority of the prophecy: The word of Yahweh (1:1)
  - The love of Yahweh for Israel, and his rejection of Esau (Edom) (1:2-5)
  - Oracle against the priests (1:6-2:9)
  - Oracle against Judah: they have broken faith, in divorce and inter-marriage (2:10-16)
  - Oracle on the day of judgment (2:17-3:6)
  - Oracle on the tithe (3:7-12)
  - Oracle of those who were treated shamefully by others, but proven true by Yahweh (3:13-18)
  - Appeal to remember Yahweh will punish the wicked; and he will send Elijah before the “great and terrible day of Yahweh,” (4:5-6)
