====== ambassador ======

An ambassador is a diplomatic person who represents his or her country while living in another country.

===== Definition: =====

A representative who exemplifies the attitudes, actions, values and customs of their Nation or Country

  * In the Bible the Apostle Paul said that he was an ambassador for Jesus Christ.
  * Christians are called to be ambassadors for Jesus Christ since they represent His Heavenly Kingdom to this world.
