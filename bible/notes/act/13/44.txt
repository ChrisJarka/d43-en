===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **almost the whole city**  - The "city" represents the people in the city. AT: "almost all of the people in the city." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **almost the whole city**  - This phrase is used to show the great response to the Lord's word. AT: "a large part of the city." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole|Hyperbole]])
  * **the Jews**  - "Jews" here represents the Jewish people. AT: "the Jewish leaders." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **they were filled with jealousy**  - "the Jewish leaders were filled with jealousy" or "the Jewish leaders became very jealous"
  * **insulted him**  - "insulted Paul"
