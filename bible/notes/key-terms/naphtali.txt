====== Naphtali ======

===== Facts: =====

Naphtali was the sixth son of Jacob in the Old Testament book of Genesis.

  * Naphtali was also one of the twelve tribes of Israel.
  * It was the northernmost tribe in Israel and is mentioned in both the Old and New Testaments.
