====== Moloch, Molech ======

===== Facts: =====

Molech was a false god in the Old Testament. Other spellings are "Moloch" and "Molek."

  * People who worshiped Molech would even sacrifice their children to him.
  * Some of the Israelites also worshiped Molech instead of the one true God, Yahweh.
