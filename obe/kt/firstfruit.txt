====== first fruit ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Old Testament, first fruits were the first born of livestock and the first fruits, vegetables and grains harvested which were given as offerings to God. In the New Testament, first fruits refers to people who become Christians.

  * In the Old Testament, a believer offering first fruits to God was showing that God is the source of blessing and that God should be given the first and best of what the believer has.
  * This practice acknowledged the sovereignty of God over his creation and that the first or best of the harvest belonged to him.
  * In the New Testament the phrase usually refers to the early converts to the faith.
