====== Numbers Chapter 27 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why did Zelophehad die in the wilderness?

A. Zelophehad died in the wilderness not because he was one of those who gathered against Yahweh in the company of Korah, but he died because of his own sin. [27:1-3]

Q? What question did the daughters of Zelophehad bring before Moses?

A. The daughters of Zelophehad asked, "Why should our father's name be taken away from among our father's relatives?" [27:1,4]

Q? What new ruling did Yahweh give to Moses because of the inquiry of Zelophehad's daughters?

A. Yahweh told Moses that if a man dies without a son, then his inheritance must pass to his daughter and if there is no daughter, then to the man's brothers or to his nearest relative. [27:5-11]

Q? Why was Moses to be gathered with his people instead of going into the land which Yahweh had given them?

A. Moses was to be gathered with his people because he and Aaron rebelled against the command of Yahweh by striking the rock in anger to cause the water to flow from it. [27:12-14]

Q? What request did Moses make to Yahweh?

A. Moses requested that Yahweh appoint a man over the Israelite community who would lead them so the people would not be like sheep that have no shepherd. [27:15-17]

Q? Why did Yahweh instruct Moses to lay his hand on Joshua, son of Nun?

A. Yahweh instructed Moses because Joshua was a man in whom Yahweh's spirit lived. [2718-19]

Q? How much authority did Yahweh command Moses to give to Joshua?

A. Moses was to give Joshua the authority to seek Yahweh's will by the decisions of the Urim and command all Israel to obey him. [27:20-21]

Q? How did Moses respond to Yahweh's commsnd?

A. Moses placed Joshua before Eleazar the priest and all the community and laid his hands on him as he commanded him to lead as Yahweh had instructed him. [27:22-23]
