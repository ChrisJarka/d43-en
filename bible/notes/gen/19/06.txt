===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Lot went out the door **  - "Lot went out the door of his house" or "Lot went out unto them at the entrance."
  * **after himself**  - "behind him" or "after he went through"
  * **I beg you, my brothers**  - "I plead with you, my brothers"
  * **my brothers**  - "Brothers" is a figure of speech; here it means "my fellow citizens."
  * **do not act so wickedly**  - "do not do something so wicked" or "do not do such a wicked thing"
  * **Look**  - "Pay attention" or "look here"
  * **not had relations with any man**  - "not had sex with any man"
  * **whatever is good in your eyes**  - "whatever you desire" or "whatever you want"
  * **under the shadow of my roof**  - "under the protection of my house"
