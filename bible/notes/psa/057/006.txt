===== Translation Notes: =====

David continues to speak.

  * **they, themselves**  - "my enemies" or "David's enemies"
  * **a net for my steps;**  - "a trap for me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **my soul is bowed down** - AT: "I became troubled" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **have fallen into the middle of it.**  - AT: "fell into the pit they dug."
