====== Samson ======

===== Facts: =====

Samson was a "judge" from the tribe of Dan.

  * He was given superhuman strength through a Nazarite vow not to cut his hair.
  * Delilah betrayed him and he was captured by Philistines who gouged out his eyes.
  * His final act was to pull down the walls of the temple of Dagon, killing many Philistines.
