===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Zerubbabel ... Shealtiel ... Joshua ... Jehozadak ... Haggai** - Translate these men's names as in [[:en:bible:notes:hag:01:01|1:1]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **obeyed the voice of Yahweh ... the words of Haggai** - The voice and the words are metonyms for the persons. AT: "obeyed Yahweh and Haggai" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the people feared the face of Yahweh** - Possible meanings are 1) the face could be a synecdoche for the person. AT: "the people feared Yahweh" or 2) the face could be a metonym for the person's presence. AT: "the people were afraid to be in Yahweh's presence" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **this is Yahweh's declaration** - "this is what Yahweh has declared" or "this is what I, Yahweh, have declared"
