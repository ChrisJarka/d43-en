====== Greece, Grecian, Greek ======

===== Facts: =====

At the time of the writing of the New Testament, Greece was a Roman province; even though the language spoken by most people in the Roman Empire was Greek, not Latin.

  * Many churches were established in Greece, during the first century, among these were churches in Corinth, Thessalonica, and Phillipi.
  * The Greek empire of Alexander the Great was conquered by Rome before the time of Jesus.
  * The spread of Greek culture and language throughout the Mediterranean world impacted the writing of the New Testament and the spread of Christianity throughout the Roman Empire.
  * The New Testament texts were originally written in Greek, not Hebrew or Aramaic.
