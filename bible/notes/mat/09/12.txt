===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus is answering a question that the Pharisees asked.

  * **When Jesus heard this **- "this" refers to the question the Pharisees asked about why Jesus eats with tax collectors and sinners.
  * **People who are strong in body **- This is a metaphor that means, "people who are healthy."
  * **You should go **- the "you" refers to Pharisees.
