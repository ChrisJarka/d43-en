====== John Mark ======

===== Facts: =====

John Mark was an assistant to the Apostle Paul and Barnabus during their New Testament ministry travels.

  * John Mark deserted his mission when the conditions became difficult in the city of Pamphylia.
  * John Mark was the author of the Gospel of Mark.
