====== 1 Samuel 03 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This is the beginning of Samuel as the one who speaks for God. (Chapters 3-7)

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

**God speaks to Samuel**

God spoke at night to Samuel when he was a small boy telling him that he would punish Eli's family. As Samuel grew up God gave him messages many messages and people came from all over Israel to find out what God had to say. 


=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

**Metonymy** 

In talking to God, Samuel uses a [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] says "your servant" when talking about himself.

**Idiom**

God's prophet uses many [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]s: "ears will shake" meaning will shock everyone who hears, "carry out" meaning do,  "may God do to you, and more if" this is a formula for a curse meaning that God will punish severely if the person does what the curse says he will do, "Yahweh was with him" God helped him, "from Dan to Beersheba" meaning all of Israel.
