===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses continues addressing the people of Israel:

  * **that you will set the blessing**  - AT: "you will proclaim the blessing" or "you will pronounce the blessing"
  * **Mount Gerizim**  - Translate the same as in Deuteronomy 27:12, Judges 9:7, and Joshua 8:33. (See: Translate Names )
  * ** Mount Ebal ** - Translate the same as in Deuteronomy 27:4, 11, 13, and Joshua 8:30, 33. (See: Translate Names )
  * **Are they not beyond the Jordan**
 - on the side opposite to where the Israelites were when Moses was speaking to them.
  * **oaks of Moreh**  - "plains of Moreh." Probably the name of a city. Translate the same as in Genesis 12:6. (See: Translate Names )
