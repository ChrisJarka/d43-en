====== prostitute, harlot ======


===== Definition: =====


A prostitute or Harlot is a person who allows or performs sexual acts for pay


    * Typically a prostitute is a female, however there are also male shrine prostitutes used in idol worship.
    * A woman called Rahab is named in the Bible as being a Harlot, however she believed God's message and was redeemed.
