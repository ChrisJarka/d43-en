====== defile, defiled ======

===== Definition: =====

To defile or be defiled is to be polluted or dirty, especially in a moral sense in relation to God.

  * Ceremonially, the Jews could defile themselves before God by not properly following the prescribed religious ceremonies.
  * In the New Testament, to become defiled can refer to a Christian believer who allows the world's ways to corrupt them.
  * There is another lesser used meaning of defile meaning to defraud a virgin of her virginity outside of Biblical boundaries.
