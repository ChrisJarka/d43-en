====== Deuteronomy Chapter 02 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Where did Moses say they took their journey?

A. They took their journey into the wilderness, by way to the Red Sea, around Mount Seir as Yahweh had spoken to Moses. Then Yahweh told Moses to turn and go northward because they had gone around the mountains long enough. [2:1-3]

Q? What did Yehweh command them to do and what were they not to do?

A. They were to pass through the border of their brothers, descendants of Esau, who lived in Seir - who would be afraid of them. Yahweh told them not to fight for he would not give them any of their land; for Yahweh had given Mount Seir to Easu as a possession. [2:4-5]

Q? How were they to get food and water?

A. They were to buy food and water from the people of Seir so they could eat and drink because Yahweh had blessed them for all the work they had done. Yahweh had known of their walking through the wilderness and had been with them for forty years, and they lacked nothing. [2:6-7]

Q? Where did they pass?

A. They passed by Seir where their brothers lived and turned and went by way of the wilderness of Moab. [2:8]

Q? What did Yahweh tell them they were not to do in Moab and why?

A. They were not to trouble Moab or fight with them because Yahweh was not giving them that land. Yahweh had given Ar to the descendants of Lot. [2:9]

Q? Where else were they to go and how long did it take them?

A. Yahweh told them to go over to the brook Zered which took them 38 years to do. [2:13-14]

Q? What happened to the men fit for fighting?

A. All the men fit for fighting were gone as Yahweh had sworn to them, because Yahweh's hand was against that generation to destroy them. [2:14-16]

Q? What did Yahweh tell Moses to do after all the men were dead and why?

A. Moses was to pass over Ar, not trouble or fight with the people of Ammon.  Yahweh was not giving that land to them because he had given it to the descendants of Lot. [2:17-19]

Q? What land and king had Yahweh given to them?

A. Yahweh told them to go and pass over the valley of Arnon because he had given them the king of Heshbon and his land. They were to possess the land and fight with him, because Yahweh would put the fear of them on the peoples there causing them anguish. [2:24-25]

Q? What words of peace did Moses send to the King of Hashbon?

A. Moses sent messengers to the king requesting that he be allowed to pass through the land on the highway, not turning to the right or left. [2:16-17]
