===== Translation Notes: =====
  * This is a continuation of a parable
  * **choked them**  - This is an idiom that is saying that the plants are being killed by the weeds.  This can be translated as "killed the good plants"
  * **yielded fruit**  - This can be translated "much fruit came from these plants". 
  * **He who has ears, let him listen**- This metonymy is relating having ears and listening to gaining understanding or knowledge. (See: Metonomy.)
