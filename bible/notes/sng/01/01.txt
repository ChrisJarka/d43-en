===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The Song of Songs, which is Solomon's** - This book of the Bible is made up of poems which are the writings of Solomon.
  * **Oh, that you would kiss me** - Since the kiss is an important way to express physical love between a man and a woman, the woman asks the man to kiss her.
  * **your love is better than wine** - Love is better than wine because kisses show desire and cause rejoicing. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **anointing oils** - ​The act of putting sweet smelling oils was used to set people apart from others. It indicates wealth. 
  * **delightful fragrance** - "a sweet smell"
  * **your name is like flowing perfume, so the young women love you** - The woman realizes that her lover has a good reputation which attracts other young women to him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **let me celebrate your love; it is better than wine** - Since wine is used during special occasions, the woman is comparing the happiness of those special times to the happiness she feels in being in love with the man. 
  * **[The woman speaking to herself] The king has brought me into his rooms** - Solomon is not only her lover, he is the king. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]]) 
  * **It is natural for the other women to adore you**  - Because the woman is in love with the man, she can understand why other women may want to be with him.
  * **adore** - "admire" or "honor"
