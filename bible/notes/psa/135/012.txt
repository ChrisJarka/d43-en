===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He gave their land for an inheritance** - Possible meanings 1) God gave Israel this land so that they would pass it on as an inheritance to their descendants or 2) God gave this land to the people of Israel and they inherited it from him. 
  * **Your name** - His name represents his fame or reputation. AT: "Your fame" or "Your reputation" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
