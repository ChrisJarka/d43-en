=== Why Translate the Questions? ===

We want all translations of the Bible to communicate clearly the message that God wants them to communicate. One tool that we are providing so that the OL translators can make sure that their translations are communicating correctly is this set of translationQuestions. The OL translators will use these Questions to conduct community checks of each chapter of the Bible that they translate.
