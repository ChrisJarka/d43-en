===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Shur** - This was the name of a place south of Canaan and east of Egypt.
  * **wilderness**  - This can be translated here as "desert." The wilderness area she went to was a desert.
  * **my mistress**  - A mistress has authority over her slave. This can also be translated as "my owner." 
  * **handmaid**  - "slave-girl" or "female servant" or "female attendant"
