Q? Where does Yahweh tell Moses to send some men?

A.Yahweh told Moses to s end some men to examine the land of Canaan, which he have given to the people of Israel. [13:2]

Q? What was the rank of the men who were sent from among the people of Israel?

A. All of the men were leaders among the people of Israel. [13:3]

Q? What was the name Moses used for the son of Nun who was chosen?

A. Moses called Hoshea son of Nun by the name of Joshua. [13:16]

Q?What does Moses tell the men to observe about the cities in the land?

A.Moses commanded them to see w hat cities are there? Are they like camps, or are they fortified cities. [13:19]

A.Moses commanded them to s ee what the land is like, whether it is good for growing crops or not, and whether there are trees. [13:20]
