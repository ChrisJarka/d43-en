===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You**  - refers to the women who sew
  * **handful**  - an amount that you can hold in your hand
  * **barley**  - a kind of grain
  * **crumb**  - a very small piece of something, usually food
  * **preserve **- to keep someone safe from harm or loss
  * **heard**  - listened to (someone or something)
