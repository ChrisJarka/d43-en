====== Ahab ======

===== Facts: =====ael from 875 to 854 BC.

  * King Ahab influenced the people of the kingdom of Israel to worship false gods.
  * The prophet Elijah confronted Ahab and told him that there would be a severe drought for three and a half years as punishment for the sins that Ahab caused Israel to commit.
  * Ahab and his wife Jezebel did many other evil things, including using their power to kill innocent people.
