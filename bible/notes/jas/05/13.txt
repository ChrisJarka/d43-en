===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Is anyone among you suffering? He must pray.** - This is a rhetorical question James uses to teach his audience. This could be translated as a statement: "If anyone is suffering, he should pray." (See: [[en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]])
  * **Is anyone cheerful? Let him sing praise.** - This is a rhetorical question James uses to teach his audience. This could be translated as a statement: "If anyone is happy, he should sing songs of praise."
  * **Is anyone among you sick? Let him call** - This is a rhetorical question James uses to teach his audience. This could be translated as a statement: "If anyone is sick, he should call."
  * ** the prayer of faith will deliver the sick person, and the Lord will raise him up** - The phrase "the prayer of faith will deliver" describes the prayer as the one who will heal the sick person. It is the Lord who hears the prayer and heals the person. This could be translated as "the Lord will hear the prayer of faith and heal the sick person." (See: [[en:ta:translate:personification]])
