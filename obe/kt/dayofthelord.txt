====== day of the Lord, day of Yahweh ======

===== Description: =====

The phrase "day of the Lord" or "day of Yahweh" is a term that refers to a time of judgment for Yahweh's enemies.

  * The word "day" in this phrase probably refers to a "time" or "occasion" that is longer than 24 hours. It begins when the Lord Jesus comes back to judge sinners, and continues until his rule over all creation is permanently established.
  * This term is used in the Old Testament to refer to times when Yahweh punish his enemies or his people in his wrath.
  * The phrase "day of the Lord" in the New Testament often refers more specifically to the time when there will be a final judgment of all who have rebelled against Yahweh and rejected Christ. The term "Lord" probably refers to the "Lord Jesus" in these contexts.
  * This final time of judgment and resurrection on the day of the Lord is also known as the "last day". The resurrection of the righteous and the unrighteous will also happen at this time.
  * The Bible sometimes uses the term "day of the Lord" to refer to other times of judgment, such as when Jerusalem was attacked by Babylon and Rome.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * Depending on the context, other ways to translate "day of the Lord" could include, "time of the Lord" or "time when the Lord Jesus will return to judge sinners."
  * Depending on the context, "day of the Lord" could also be translated as "time of Yahweh" or "time when the Lord Jesus will return to judge."
