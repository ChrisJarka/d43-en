====== Deuteronomy Chapter 09 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Where are the people of Israel going and what will they do there?

A. Israel will cross over the Jordan, take over nations and cities greater than themselves, including the sons of Anak who are great and tall. [9:1-2]

Q? What will happen before Israel crosses Jordan?

A. Yahweh will go before them and will destroy the nations, cities, and the sons of Anak like a devouring fire. [9:3]
