====== Sea of Galilee ======


===== Facts: =====


The Sea of Galilee is a lake in eastern Israel and is connected to the Dead Sea by the Jordan river.


    * The towns of Capernaum and Bethsaida were located on the shores of the lake.
    * The other names for the Sea of Galilee include: the Sea of Chinnereth, the Lake of Gennesaret and Yam Kinneret.
