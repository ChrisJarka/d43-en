====== perish, perishing ======

===== Definition: =====

Perish means cease to exist, lost, or destroyed.  Perishing in the Bible frequently refers to the lost people who reject God or Jesus.

  * John 3:16 says, "For God so love the world that He gave His only begotten Son, that whoever believes in Him should not perish but have everlasting life."(NKJ)
