====== oak ======

===== Definition: =====

An Oak is a tall tree with a big trunk.

  * Oak trees are frequently referred to in the Old Testament.
  * The seed of an oak tree is called an acorn.
  * An oak tree has full green leaves that spread out widely typically in the shape of a half circle.
