===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **slaughter**  - This word means to kill in a bloody or violent manner.
  * **flash like lightning**  - "flashing in a brief, sudden or intermitten way" (See: Simile)
  * **Should we rejoice?**  - This rhetorical question is to emphasize the point that there will be no rejoicing in this horrible act. "The people of Judah will not celebrate about being pinished like this." (UDB)  (See: Rhetorical question)
  * **The rod of my son rejects every tree**  - Alternate translation: "My son rejects every rod of discipline."
  * **rod**
 - This word describes a piece of wood used for discipline.
  * **my son**  - This means God's people.
  * **So it is given to be polished to be seized in the hand!** <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>- The rod is fashioned so it can be held in the hand.</font>
