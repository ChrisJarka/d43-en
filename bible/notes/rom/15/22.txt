===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Therefore also I was hindered many times from coming to you**  - Paul says here that he was prevented many times from visiting the believers in Rome.
  * **But now, since I no longer have any place in these regions**  - Paul no longer has a reason for remaining in these regions because the good news had been already preached
  * **since I have had for many years longing to come to you **- Paul has been wanting to visit them for many years.
