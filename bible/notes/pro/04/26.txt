===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Make a level path for your feet** - AT: "Prepare what you want to do well" or "Plan ahead and avoid danger"
    * **a level path ... your ways** - The path or way refers to the things you do. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
    * **your feet ... your foot** - "yourself" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
    * **secure** - free from danger or harm; safe
    * **Do not turn aside to the right or to the left** - This implies that the road straight ahead is the only morally good way. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
    * **turn your foot away from evil** - AT: "keep away from evil" or "avoid evil"
