===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **scourged**  - hit Jesus with a whip that had pieces of metal or bone fastened to it
    * **plaited**  - twisted together (UDB)
    * **crown**  - See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_irony]]
    * **crown of thorns**  - made with a plant that has many thorns along its stem
    * **purple robe**  - Purple was a very expensive color only used by the most important people. They mocked Jesus by pretending he was a king. He really is the king, so note the irony (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_irony
]])
    * **Hail**  - greeting with raised hand normally only for Caesar
