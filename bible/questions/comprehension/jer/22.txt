====== Jeremiah Chapter 22 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Yahweh tells the King of Judah, his servants and his people to do what?

A. They are to listen to the word of Yahweh, act fairly and justly, help those who have been robbed, rescue the oppressed, to not mistreat foreigners, orphans, or widows, and not commit violence or pour out innocent blood. [22:1-3]

Q? What happens if they do not listen to these words of Yahweh's declaration?

A. The royal palace will become a ruin. [22:5]

Q? How does the palace become a wilderness?

A. Yahweh chooses destroyers to come against them and cut them off. [22:6-7]

Q? Why has Yahweh acted in this way toward the great city?

A. Because they abondoned the covenant of Yahweh and bowed down and worshipped other gods. [22:8-9]

Q? Why should the people not weep for the dead but rather weep for those who go into captivity?

A. Because the captives will never return or see the land of their birth again. [22:10]

Q? What does Yahweh declare a good king should do?

A. He should do justice even for the poor and needy, do righteousness, and know Yahweh.[22:15-16]

Q? Why won't the people lament for king Jehoiakim?

A. Because his eyes and heart focus on unjust profits, he pours out innocent blood, and oppresses and crushes others. [22:17]

Q? What kind of burial will they give the king?

A. He will be buried like a donkey, dragged away and thrown out of Jerusalem. [22:19]

Q? What was the custom of the people since their youth?

A. They did not listen to God's voice. [22:21]

Q? When they do not listen what will happen to them?

A. They will lose their shepherds, their friends will be captives, they will be ashamed and humiliated. [22:22]

Q? What does Yahweh declare will happen to Jehoichin?

A. Even though he is king of Judah, he will be given to those he fears, to the king of Babylon, and he and his mother will die away from the land where he was born. [22:24-26]

Q? What does Yahweh tell Jeremiah to write about Jehoiachin?

A. That Jehoiachin will be childless, he will not prosper, none of his descendants will succeed nor rule Judah on David's throne. [22:30]
