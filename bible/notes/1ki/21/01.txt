===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It later came about that**  - This indicates a new part of the narrative.
  * **Naboth the Jezreelite**  - This is the name of a man from Jezreel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **me, I, my **- Refers to Ahab.
  * **your, you **- Refers to Naboth.
  * **vs1 & 2 **- Word order is changed, but the meaning is the same.
