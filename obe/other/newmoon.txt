====== new moon ======

===== Definition: =====

A new moon is the first phase in the moon's Earthly orbit; having a completely dark appearance resembling an eclipse.

* In the Bible the new moon was used to mark the beginnings of certain time periods.

* The Israelites celebrated a new moon festival signified by the blowing of a ram's horn.
