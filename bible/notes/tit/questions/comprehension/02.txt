====== Titus Chapter 2 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What are some character traits that older men in the church should display?

A. Temperance, dignity, soundness in faith, in love, and in perseverance [1:2].

Q? What are some character traits that older women in the church should display?

A. Reverence, not gossiping, not drunkards, teachers of good [1:3].

Q? What should the older women teach the younger women?

A. To love their husbands and children, be sensible, pure, and good housekeepers [1:4-5].

Q? In what way does Paul say that Titus should be a model to those in the church?

A. When Titus teaches, shows purity, dignity, and healthy words that do not need to be corrected [1:7-8].

Q? What does Paul instruct slaves who are believers to do?

A. Obey their masters, not steal, and show good faith [1:9-10].

Q? When slaves behave as Paul has instructed, what effect does that have on others?

A. It makes the teaching about God our Savior attractive to others [1:10].

Q? Who can the grace of God save?

A. Everyone [1:11].

Q? What does the grace of God train us to deny?

A. Ungodliness and worldly passions [1:12].

Q? What hope do believers look to receive?

A. The appearance of the glory of our great God and Savior Jesus Christ [1:13].

Q? Why did Jesus give himself?

A. In order to pay the price to set us free from lawlessness, and to make a pure people eager to do good [1:14].
