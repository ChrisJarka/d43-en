====== Psalms Chapter 077 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Asaph do in the day of his trouble?

A. He sought the Lord, prayed all night, and reached out his hands. [77:2-3]

Q? What did Asaph recall to mind during the night?

A. He called to mind the song he sang and thought carefully to understand what had happened. [77:6]

Q? What is Asaph's sorrow?

A.His sorrow is the changing of the Almighty's right hand toward the psalmsist and his people. [77:10]
