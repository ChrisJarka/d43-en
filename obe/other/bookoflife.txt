====== book of life ======

===== Definition: =====

The book of life is a book in which God has written the names of the redeemed believers from all ages.

  * Revelation describes The Lamb of God's book of life; so called because the blood of His sacrifice qualifies those whose names are written in it.
  * The book of life was written before time began.
