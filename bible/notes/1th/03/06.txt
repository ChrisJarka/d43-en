===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **came to us** - The pronoun "us" refers to Paul and Silvanus.
  * **from you** - "from being with you" The pronoun "you" refers to the believers in the Thessalonian church.  (See: [[:en:ta:translate:formsofyou-plural]])
  * **you always have good memories** - "you frequently have good memories" (See: [[:en:ta:translate:hyperbole]])
  * **you long to see us** - "you desire to see us" (See: [[:en:ta:translate:idiom]])
