===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he also took**  - The word "he" is the eagle in Ezekiel's vision.
  * **planted it like a willow - **  Willows are planted by cutting a branch off and putting it in the ground, cut-end first.
  * **its branches turned toward him **- The branches of the vine turned towad the eagle.
  * **its roots grew under him**  - The roots of the vine grew under the Eagle.
  * **sent out shoots **- A shoot is a new growth sent out by a plant.
