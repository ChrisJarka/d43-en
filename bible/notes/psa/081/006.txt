===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **"I - ** God is speaking
  * ** removed the burden from his shoulder,- **God removed the forced labor from the people.  (See: Metaphor)
  * **his hands were freed from holding the basket**  - The people of Israel were no longer forced to do hard labor. (See: Metaphor)
  * **I answered you from a dark thundercloud- **  God's presence and glory were shown to Israel in OT times in a dark cloud.
  * **I tested you at the waters of Meribah- **  God tested the children of Israel to see if they would trust him to supply water in the desert of Meribah.
