===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Festus entered the province **- Possible meanings are 1) Festus is simply arrving in the area or 2) Festus is arriving in the area to begin his rule as translated by UDB.
  * **he went from Caesarea up to Jerusalem **- "not going north but going up to a higher elevation"
  * **brought accusations against Paul -**  This is a formal presentation of charges in court. Alternate translataion "accused Paul of violating laws"
  * **they spoke strongly to Festus **- "they were begging Festus. " or "they begged Festus. "
  * **might call him **- "might send him"
  * **they could kill him along the way**  - They were going to ambush Paul to kill him on the way.
