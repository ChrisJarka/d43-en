===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Give counsel, execute justice; ** The word counsel here means to give advice.
  * **provide some shade like night in the middle of the day**- The Moabites are asking for protection from their enemies.
  * **Let them live among you, the refugees from Moab**- Alternate translation "Allow the refugees from Moab to live with you."
