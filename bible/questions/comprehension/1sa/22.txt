====== 1 Samuel Chapter 22 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who besides David's father's house all gathered to him at the cave of Adullam?

A. Everyone who was in distress, every one who was in debt, and every one who was discontented—they all gathered to him. [22:2]

Q? How long did David's father and mother stay with the king of Moab?

A. He left them with the king of Moab and his father and mother stayed with him the whole time that David was in his stronghold. [22:4]

Q? Who had been discovered along with David?

A. David had been discovered, along with the men who were with him. [22:6]

Q? What does Saul accuse his servants of not revealing that his son was doing?

A. Saul said that none of his servants revealed to him that his son had incited his servant David against him. [22:8]

Q? What two things does Doeg the Edomite tell Saul that Ahimelech gave to David after praying to Yahweh for him?

A. He told him that Ahimelech gave him provisions and the sword of Goliath the Philistine. [22:10]

Q? What did Saul say Ahimelech did for the son of Jesse besides giving him bread and a sword?

A. Saul said that Ahimelech had given the son of Jesse bread, and a sword, and had prayed to God that he might help him. [22:13]

Q? About what does Ahimelech ask the king if that day was the first time?

A. He asked the king if that day was the first time he had prayed to God to help David. [22:15]

Q? What were the servants of King Saul not willing to put out their hand to do?

A. The servants of the king would not put out their hand to kill the priests of Yahweh. [22:17]

Q? Who did Doeg kill with the edge of the sword in Nob, the city of the priests?

A. He attacked Nob, the city of the priests, both men and women, children and infants, and oxen and donkeys and sheep. He killed them all with the edge of the sword. [22:19]

Q? Where did Abiathar go when he escaped the killing?

A. Abiathar escaped and fled after David. [22:20]

Q? For what did David tell Abiathar he was responsible ?

A. David said that he was responsible for every death in his father’s family. [22:22]
