====== Numbers Chapter 33 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why were the people of Israel able to openly leave Rameses without opposition from the Egyptians?

A. The people of Israel faced no opposition from the Egyptians because the Egyptians were busy burying all their firstborn. [33:3-4]

Q? Why did the people of Israel choose to camp at Elim?

A. The people of Israel choose to camp at Elim because there were twelve springs of water and seventy palm trees at Elim. [33:9]
