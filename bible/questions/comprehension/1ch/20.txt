====== 1 Chronicles Chapter 20 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? When David remained in Jerusalem, what did Joab do?

A. Joab led the army into battle, devastated the land of the Ammonites, and attacked and defeated Rabbah. [20:1]

Q? After taking the crown off of the king's head and placing it on his own, what did force the people to do?

A. He forced them to do hard labor. [20:2-3]
