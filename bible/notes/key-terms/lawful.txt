====== lawful ======


===== Definition: =====


Something is lawful if it is in accordance with a binding rule.


    * In the Bible, lawful indicates conformity to either the civil law, ceremonial law, or the Law of Moses.
    * "Lawful" can be translated as "permitted" or "allowed" or "legal."


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:lawofmoses|Law of Moses]])
