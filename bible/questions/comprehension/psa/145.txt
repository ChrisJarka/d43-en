====== Psalms Chapter 145 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who will David extol and bless?

A. David will extol his God and King; blessing God's name forever and ever. [145:1]

Q? How often does David bless God?

A. Every day David will bless God. [145:2]

Q? Who is great and greatly to be praised, whose greatness is unsearchable?

A. Yahweh is great and greatly to be praised, his greatness is unsearchable. [145:3]

Q? Who will praise God's deeds and proclaim his mighty actions?

A. One generation will praise God's deeds and the next will proclaim his mighty actions. [145:4]

Q? Who will meditate on the majesty of God's glory and on God's marvelous deeds?

A. David will meditate on the majesty of God's glory and on God's marvelous deeds. [145:5]

Q? Who will speak of the power of God's fearsome, mighty actions declaring his abounding goodness and sing about God's righteousness.

A. The generations will speak of the power of God's fearsome mighty actions declaring his abounding goodness and sing about God's righteousness. [145:6-7]

Q? Who is gracious and merciful, slow to anger and abounding in covenant faithfulness?

A. Yahweh is gracious and merciful, slow to anger and abounding in covenant faithfulness. [145:8]

Q? Who is good to all; whose tender mercies are over all his works?

A. Yahweh is good to all; his tender mercies are over all his works. [145:9]

Q? What will thank and bless Yahweh?

A. All Yahweh has made will give thanks to him and his faithful ones will bless him. [145:10]

Q? Who will speak of the glory of Yahweh's kingdom and tell of Yahweh's power?

A. Yahweh's faithful ones will speak of the glory of his kingdom and tell of his power. [145:11]

Q? Who will make known to mankingd God's mighty deeds and the glorious splendom of his kingdom?

A. Yahweh's faithful ones will speak of the glory of his kingdom and tell of his power. [145:11]

Q? Who will make known to mankind God's mighty deeds and the glorious splendor of his kingdom?

A. Yahweh's faithful ones will make knownto mankind God's mighty deeds and the glorious splendor of his kingdom. [145:12]

Q? Whose kingdom is an everlasting kingdom and whose dominion endures throughout all generations?

A. Yahweh's kingdom is an everlasting kingdom and his dominion endures throughout all generations. [145:13]

Q? Who supports all who are falling and raises up all those who are bent over?

A. Yahweh supports all who are falling and raises up all those who are bent over. [145:14]

Q? Whose eyes wait for Yahweh and is given food at the right time?

A. Eveyone's eyes wait for Yahweh and are given food at the right time. [145:15]

Q? Who satisfies the desires of every living thing as he opens his hand?

A. Yahweh opens his hand and satisfys the desire of every living thing. [145:16]

Q? Who is righteous in all his ways and gracious in all he does?

A. Yahweh is righteous in all his ways and gracious in all he does. [145:17]

Q? Who is near to all those who call to him and to all who call to him in trustworthiness?

A. Yahweh is near to all those who call to him and to all who call to him in trustworthiness. [145:18]

Q? Who fulfills the desire of those who honor him, hears their cry and saves them?

A. Yahweh fulfills the desire of those who honor him; he hears their cray and saves them. [145:19]

Q? Who watches over all those who love him but will destroy all the wicked?

A. Yahweh watches over all those who love him, but he willd destroy all the wicked. [145:20]

Q? Whose mouth will speak ou the praise of Yahweh and wants all mankind to bless his holy name forever and ever?

A. David's mouth will speak ou the praise of Yahweh and thinks all mankind should bless Yahweh's holy name forever and ever. [145:21]
