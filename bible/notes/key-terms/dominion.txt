====== dominion ======

===== Definition: =====

Dominion means governing or ruling with power, control, or authority.

  * Jesus Christ is said to have dominion over all the earth for ever as prophet, priest, and king.
  * Satan's dominion has been defeated by Jesus Christ's death on the Cross of Calvary forever.
  * At creation, God said that man is to have dominion over fish, birds, and all creatures on the earth.

(See also: other)
