===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Paul continues speaking)
  * **Do you believe the prophets?**  - Paul asks this in order to remind Agrippa that he did already believe in the Jewish prophets, and thus should be able to accept what Paul is saying about Jesus. (See: Rhetorical question)
  * **In a short time would you persuade me and make me a Christian?**  - Agrippa is saying there is no way Paul could make Agrippa believe in Christ by such a short speech. (See: Rhetorical question)
