===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then she said,**  - "Then Rachel said,"
  * **See** - This can be translated as “Look” (UDB) or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you.”
  * **Bilhah** - This is the name of Rachel's female servant. (See: [[en:ta:translate:translatenames]])
  * **on my behalf** - This means that Bilhah's children will belong to Rachel. It could be translated as "for me."
  * **I will have children by her** - This could be translated as “I will build my family through her.”
