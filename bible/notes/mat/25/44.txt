===== Important Terms: =====

  * truly
  * eternal punishment
  * righteous
  * eternal life

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then they will also answer **- The pronoun "they" refers to the unrighteous at the King's left hand.
  * **Truly I say to you **- This can be translated “I tell you the truth”.
