====== chosen one ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Bible, the term "chosen one" usually refers to Jesus as the one whom God chose to be the Christ.

  * The Bible says: God chose Jesus to be the Christ before the world was created.
  * Occasionally, "chosen one" is used for people whom God appointed as special leaders over the Israelites.

(See also: Christ, Messiah)
