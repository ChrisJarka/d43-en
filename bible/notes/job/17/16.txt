===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Will hope go down with me...** - AT: "Hope will not go down with me..." or "I will no longer hope when I go down..." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **gates of sheol** - People pictured sheol as a place that people entered into through a gate.
  * **when we** - The word "we" refers to Job and his hope.
  * **descend to the dust** - "go down into the dirt." People go into the ground when they die and the dirt covers them.
