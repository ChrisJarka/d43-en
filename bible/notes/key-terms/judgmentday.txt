====== Judgment Day ======


===== Definition: =====


Judgement Day is a day that God has planned when He will bring all mankind before Him to examine how they lived their lives.


    * Judgement day is known only to God.
    * The Apostle Paul preached and warned that a day was coming where God would judge all mankind by His Son Jesus Christ.
