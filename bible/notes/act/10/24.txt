===== translationNotes: =====

  * **On the following day** - This was the next day after they left Joppa. The journey to Caesarea took two days. 
  * **they came** - "Peter, the men from Joppa who accompanied Peter, and Cornelius's messengers"
  * **Cornelius was waiting for them** - Here "waiting" means he was expecting them.
  * **he had called together his relatives and his close friends** - "he" and "his" refers to Cornelius.
