===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **the crooked roads will become straight **- Crooked and straight are opposites, the first is negative and the latter is positive. Crooked can also mean winding or zigzag or simply, not straight will be made straight.
    * **rough ways will be made smooth**- Rough and smooth are opposites. It may indicate a road that is bumpy that will be flattened or made smooth without bumps anymore.
