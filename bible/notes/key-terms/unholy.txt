====== unholy, common, defiled ======

===== Definition: =====

Anything that is common or unholy is something that has not been set apart by God as having special significance to Him.

  * In the Bible, certain animals were considered unholy to God, meaning people should not eat their meat.
  * An object or person becomes defiled when it was considered by God to be holy, but it later becomes unholy or unclean.  An example of this is a person who is temporarily considered defiled due to an infectious disease and is separated from people to avoid spreading the illness.  n Old Testament Priest who is considered holy, but becomes temporarily defiled by touching something considered to be defiled, such as a dead body.
