===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **household** - all the people who live in a house together, usually a large family with servants
    * **seventy** - This number 70 is the same as 7 groups of 10. If your language has no way to express this number, you can translate it as "many" or "a large number."
    * **Joseph was in Egypt already** - Alternate Translation: "Joseph was in Egypt earlier" or "Joseph lived in Egypt before his brothers"
