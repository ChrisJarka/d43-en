===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then they said**  - "They" refers to the priests and Levites in the previous section
  * **to him**  - "him" refers to John
  * **Tell us so that we** - The pronouns "us" and "we" are both exclusive, referring to the priests and Levites who are speaking. 
  * **He said** - Alternate Translation: "John said"
  * **I am the voice of one crying in the wilderness ** - This figure of speech is a metaphor for a person who is speaking loudly in a desolate (barren) place. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Make the way of the Lord straight ** - This figure of speech is a metaphor meaning "Get ready." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
