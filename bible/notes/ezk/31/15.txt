===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:10|3:11]].
  * **On the day when he went down to Sheol** - "On the day that the cedar went down to Sheol. The phrase "went down to Sheol" means "died." Alternate translation: "On the day that the cedar died" or "On the day that the cedar was chopped down." (See: [[en:ta:translate:idiom]])
  * **I brought mourning to the earth** - This can be translated as "I caused mourning." It was as though Yahweh made the waters, Lebanon, and the wild trees all mourn for the cedar.
  * **I covered the deep waters for him** - "I covered the spring waters for the cedar." The word "covered" possibly refers to putting on cloths for mourning. Alternate translation: "it was a
  * **languished**  - This means the trees continued for a long time in an unpleasent state.
