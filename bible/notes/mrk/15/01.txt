===== Translation Notes: =====

Before this, Jesus had been arrested by the temple guards and met with the high priest.

  * **handed him over** - "delivered him" or "transferred him" (See: Idiom)
  * **You say so**  - "You present it as so"
  * **were presenting many charges**  - "were accusing Jesus of many things" or "accused Jesus of many things"
