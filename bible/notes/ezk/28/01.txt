===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **thus says**  - This is phrase means "this is what the Lord says".
  * **seat of the gods-**  "in a place to show power over other people".
  * **in the heart of the seas**  - "in a place in the center of the sea, far away from common people".
  * **though your heart is set****like the heart of God**- "you think and boast that you are like God". The word "though" introduces the idea of contrast.
  * **no secret amazes you **- "nothing surprises you".
  * **Behold**  - The word “behold” here adds emphasis to what follows. It can also be translated as “Indeed.”
