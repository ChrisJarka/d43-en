===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Your thunderous voice **- This is personification, referring to the thunder as the voice of God (See: Personification)
  * **thunderous**  - AT: "very loud"
  * **lightning lit up the world**  - The lightning seemed to light up the whole world. (See: Hyperbole)
  * **Your path went through the sea and your way through the surging waters**  - These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: Doublet)
  * **your footprints**  - This is a personification of God's presence making a path through the sea.
  * **You led your people like a flock**  - This simile compares God's people to a flock. (See: Simile)
  * **by the hand of**  - The phrase “by the hand of” here means "by the action of" or "through the action of."
