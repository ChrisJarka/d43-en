===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You hypocrites **- The word "You" refers to the Pharisees and scribes.
  * **well did Isaiah prophesy**  - Isaiah told the truth in this prophecy.
  * **he said**  - This can be translated as "when he reported what God said."
  * **but their heart is far from me**  - "but they do not honor me in their hearts" or "but they do not love me"
  * **in vain**  - Vain has the meaning of useless or without effect. It could be translated "without effect."
  * **the commands of people**  - "rules that people make up"
