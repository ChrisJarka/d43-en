===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **our captivity**  - this took place in Babylonia (as mentioned in the UDB)
  * **the city**  - this city here is Jerusalem (as mentioned in the UDB)
  * **God brought me in visions**  - Ezekiel wasn't physically transported. He was shown by God
  * **the city was struck **- Alternate translation: After the Babylonians had destroyed the city (see page: Active/passive)
  * **structure of a city**  - alternate translation: ruins
