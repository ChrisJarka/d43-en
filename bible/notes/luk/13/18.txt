===== Translation Notes: =====

    * (Jesus continues to speak to the people in the synagogue.)
    * **Then he said**  - that is, then Jesus said
    * **what will I compare it to?**  - This is a rhetorical question that introduces what Jesus wants to talk about it. It introduces a simile.
    * **It is like a mustard seed**  - This is a simile
    * **mustard seed**  - The mustard seed is a very small seed.
    * **birds of heaven**  - That is, "birds of the sky" It could also be translated as "birds that fly in the sky" or simply "birds."
    * **bold words**  - explanation
