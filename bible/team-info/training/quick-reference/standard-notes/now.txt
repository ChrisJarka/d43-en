====== Now ======

===== Description =====

The Hebrew conjunction //w// is sometimes used to introduce a change or new information. The Greek //kai// and //de// are used in similar ways. Modern languages often have similar forms.


===== Standard Notes =====

  * **Now**  - This word is used here to mark a change from the story to background information about…. (See: Background information)
  * **Now**  - This word is used here to mark a new part of the story.
  * **Now**  - This word is used here to introduce a new part of the story and also background information about…. (See: Background information)
  * **Now**  - This word is used here to introduce a new person in the story.
