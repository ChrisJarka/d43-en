====== Simon the sorcerer ======

===== Facts: =====

Simon the magician, as opposed to Simon Peter or Simon the tanner.

  * Simon was a magician in Samaria who was converted through the preaching of the evangelist Phillip (Acts 6:5).
  * He was so impressed by the healing miracles performed by the apostles that he offered to buy them. Severely admonished by Peter, Simon repented.
