====== propitiation ======

===== Definition: =====

To turn away wrath, usually by means of an offering.

  * The blood of Jesus Christ is the propitiation to God for mankind's sins.
  * God's wrath toward Christian believers is completely removed by Jesus' propitiating sacrifice.

(See also: other)
