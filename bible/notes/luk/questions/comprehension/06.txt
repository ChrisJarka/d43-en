====== Luke Chapter 6 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What were Jesus' disciples doing on the Sabbath that the Pharisees said was against the Law?

A. They were picking heads of grain, rubbing them between their hands, and eating the grain.

Q? What title did Jesus claim that gave him the authority to say what was lawful on the Sabbath?

A. Lord of the Sabbath

Q? When Jesus healed the man with the shriveled hand on the Sabbath, how did the scribes and Pharisees react?

A. The were filled with anger, and talked about what they might do to Jesus.

Q? What was the name given to the twelve men that Jesus chose?

A. The apostles.

Q? What kind of people did Jesus say were blessed?

A. The poor, hungry, weeping, and hated for the Son of Man's sake.

Q? According to Jesus, why should such people rejoice and leap for joy?

A. Because they will have a great reward in heaven.
