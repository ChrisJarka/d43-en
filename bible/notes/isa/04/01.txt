===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **On that day seven women will take hold of one man **- refers to the time when many men died in battle leaving seven unmarried women to one man
  * **On that day the branch of Yahweh will be beautiful and glorious, and the fruit of the land will be tasty and delightful for those survivors in Israel.**- this is a vision of the future when the Messiah comes and the land will be restored for the people that remain in Jerusalem
