===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Ask them** - "Ask Egypt and her army"
  * **Go down** - It is implied that they go down to Sheol. Alternate translation: "Go down to Sheol." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **They** - "Egypt and her army"
  * **will fall among** – This phrase means "will die and go into the ground with." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Egypt is given to the sword** - Alternate translation: "A sword will kill Egypt" or "An attacking army will kill Egypt." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **her enemies will seize her and her entourage** -  "enemies will drag off Egypt and her army." The word "Egypt" is used to refer to "the people of Egypt." Alternate translation: "enemies will drag off the people of Egypt and the army of Egypt." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])




  * **Who will you surpass in beauty? -**  "Who are you more beautiful than?" Surpass means 'out do', 'go beyond' 'improve on' or 'exceed.'
  * **Go down and lay with the uncircumcised! -**  "Go and be like the evil dead that have not followed Yahweh."
  * **They will fall in the midst of those slain by the sword! - **"You will die among the ungodly who were killed by the sword!"
  * **She is given to the sword; - **"Egypt uses war to fulfill it's goals;"
  * **they will seize her and her entourage! - **"however the ungodly will capture many of the people of Egypt!"
  * **The strongest of the warriors will declare to him from the midst of Sheol,-**  "Mighty warriors ( or 'leaders') that were strong but are now dead, will declare to the king from Sheol,"
  * **to his hero!-**  "and his helpers"
  * **‘They will go down! - **"They have come down to be with us in Sheol!"
  * **They will lay with the uncircumcised who were slain by the sword!**  - "They have the same punishment as the ungodly who have been killed by the sword."
