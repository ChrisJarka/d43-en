====== destroy, blot out ======

===== Definition: =====

To "blot out" or "destroy" means to completely get rid of something or someone.

  * Blot out can be used to refer to removing something completely as when God forgives sins and removes them from His memory.
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Destroy can mean to ruin, kill, or break something into pieces.</font>
