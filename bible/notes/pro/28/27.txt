===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The one** - "The person"
  * **the poor** - This refers to poor people in general. AT: "poor people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **lack nothing** - This double negative is used for emphasis. AT: "have everything they need" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **whoever closes his eyes to them will receive many curses** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the poor will give many curses to whoever closes his eyes to them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **whoever** - "anyone who"
  * **closes his eyes to them** - Closing the eyes represents not responding to the needs of the poor. AT: "ignores them" or "chooses not to help them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **when wicked people rise** - This is an idiom that means when wicked people gain power or start to rule. AT: "when wicked people rise to power" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **people hide themselves** - "people go into hiding." How people respond when wicked people rise is spoken of as if the people hide from those in power, although they would not actually be hidden. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_rpronouns]])
  * **when wicked people perish** - Here "perish" means to go away, fall from power or be destroyed.
  * **increase** - "multiply" or "rise to power"
