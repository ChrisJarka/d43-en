====== Isaiah Chapter 51 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who are the "rock" and "quarry" those who pursue righteousness and seek Yahweh, are supposed to look at?

A. The "rock" and "quarry" are Abraham, their father, and Sarah, who bore them [51:1-2].
