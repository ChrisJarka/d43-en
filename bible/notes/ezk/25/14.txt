===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **So I will set my vengeance on Edom by the hand of my people Israel** - "So I will use my people Israel to take revenge against the people of Edom"
  * **and they will do to Edom according to my anger and my fury** - "and they will carry out my anger and fury against the people of Edom"
  * **So they will know my vengeance!**- "So the people of Edom will know that I have taken revenge against them"
  * **this is the Lord's declaration** - "this is what the Lord Yahweh declares" or "this is what the Lord says will happen"
