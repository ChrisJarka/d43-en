===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **your** - Here the word "your" refers to Abraham
  * **all the nations of the earth will be blessed" - "I, the Lord, will bless all the people living everywhere" (See: [[en:ta:translate:active]])
  * **obeyed my voice**  - "obeyed me" (See: [[en:ta:translate:synecdoche]])
  * **my** - Here the word "my" refers to Yahweh.
  * **Abraham returned**  - "Abraham and his son went back" Only Abraham was named because he was the father, but his son went with him. (See: [[en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]])
  * **young men** - "servants" (UDB)
  * **they departed**  - "they left that place"
  * **Abraham lived at Beersheba**  - "Abraham stayed at Beersheba"
