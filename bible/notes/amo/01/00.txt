====== Amos 01 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This book is written in a poetic form by a farmer. So you can imagine that the text will include many references to agricultural concepts. 

You will notice that the phrase ""For three sins of Judah, even for four," is used to begin each of these oracles. This is not intended to be a literal count but is an idiom showing a large number of sins. Please review [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]].
