====== ark ======

===== Definition: =====

An ark is a rectangular wooden box varying in size, depending on its purpose.

  * In the Bible, the word "ark" is used to refer to either the "ark of the covenant" or the boat that Noah built to escape the worldwide flood.
  * Depending on the context, "ark" can be translated as "box", "chest" or "very large boat".

(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:arkofthecovenant|ark of the covenant]])
