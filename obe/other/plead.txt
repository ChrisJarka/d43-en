====== plead, pleading, petition ======

===== Facts: =====

To plead is to beg, implore, or appeal to another for a certain desired result.  When David spared King Saul's life in the cave he prayed that "The Lord therefore by judge and decide between you and me; and may he see and plead my cause, and deliver me from your hand."  1   Samuel 24:15
