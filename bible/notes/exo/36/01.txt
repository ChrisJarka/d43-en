===== Translation Notes: =====

See: Translate Names

  * **Bezalel and Oholiab**
- Two of the leaders of the craftsmen that God chose to work on the sanctuary. See how you translated these names in 31:2, 31:6, 35:30, and 35:34.

  * **following the instructions**  - This means doing something just like someone told you. AT: "make everything just as Yahweh has commanded" (UDB), "making everything exactly like Yahweh told them".
