===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **so that they will walk about like blind men **- See (Simile) "so that they will grope (or 'move') around like blind men"
  * **Their blood will be poured out like dust, and their inner parts like dung! **- See (Simile) and (Doublet) "Your blood will be poured out in the dust and your dead bodies will lie rotting on the ground!"
  * **Neither their silver nor their gold will be able to save them**  - "Your silver and gold will not help save you"
  * **day of Yahweh's fury!**  - "day of Yahweh's great anger!"
  * **The fire of Yahweh's wrath will consume the whole land,**  - "The fire of Yahweh's great anger will devour (or 'consume') the land totally (or 'completely'),"
  * **for the annihilation that he will bring against all the inhabitants of the land will be terrifying!**  - "for the destruction that he will bring against all the people of this land will cause great fear!" or "for the destruction that he allows in this land will cause great fear in all the people."
