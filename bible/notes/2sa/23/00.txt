====== 2 Samuel 23 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter has two parts: 1-7 is a psalm David composed, thanking God for his blessings on his family. 8-39 is a list of the famous people in David's army along with some of the special deeds some of them did. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

God has greatly blessed David and his family and made them a blessing to others. But wicked people are a problem to everyone.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===


== Rhetorical question ==
David expresses God's care for him in a [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]: "Does he not increase my salvation and fulfill my every desire?"
