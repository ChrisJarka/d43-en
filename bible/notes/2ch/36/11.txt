===== translationNotes: =====

  * **twenty-one years old ... eleven years** - "21 years old ... 11 years" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **what was evil in the sight of Yahweh his God** - Here "sight" represents judgment. Yahweh saw and did not approve of Zedekiah's actions. AT: "what Yahweh his God judged to be evil" or "what Yahweh his God considered to be evil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **who spoke from the mouth of Yahweh** - Here the word "mouth" represents the words that Yahweh spoke. AT: "who spoke the words that Yahweh spoke to him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
