===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the people of Jerusalem and of the land of Israel.
  * **In order to melt their heart** - This phrase means "In order to make them very afraid." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **to multiply their stumbling blocks** - "to make things very difficult for them"
  * **their gates** - The word "their" refers to the people of Jerusalem.
  * **It is made like lightning** - AT: "The sword is like lightning." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **set free to butcher** - Possible meanings are 1) "I took it out of its sheath so that I could slaughter people" or 2) "I sharpened it so that I could slaughter people."
  * **wherever your sharp edge wishes** - AT: "wherever I have assigned your sharp edge to go."
  * **I will also hit my two hands together** - Possible meanings are 1) "I will clap my hands triumphantly" or 2) "I will clap my hands to show that I am very sad about what has happened."
  * **I, Yahweh, declare it** - See how you translated "I, Yahweh have declared this" in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:05:15|5:15]].
