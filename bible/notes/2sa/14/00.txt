====== 2 Samuel 14 General Notes ======

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

David love Absalom and longed for him to return. Joab noticed David's wish to see Absalom and sent a woman with a story to let David understand this in a different way. So David told Joab to go get Absalom but did not let him come to him. After three years, Absalom sent Joab to tell the king to either execute him or pardon him. David pardoned him.
