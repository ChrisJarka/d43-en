====== 1 Chronicles 19 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This is the beginning of the story about the war against the Ammonites. (1 Chronicles 19 and the first part of 20)

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

The young king of Ammon insulted David. He realized that David was going to attack him for that, so he hired mercenaries from Aram to help him. But Israel defeated the Aramean mercenaries.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Idiom ==

"they had become a stench to David" meaning David hated them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
