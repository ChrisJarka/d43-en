===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **third day**  - This means day three.
  * **there was a wedding**  - Alternate Translation: "A wedding happened" or "A wedding occurred" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **wedding**  - In Jewish culture, a wedding feast lasted one week (seven days). If the family did not provide enough food and drink, it brought shame on the family.
  * **Jesus also was invited**  - The family of the husband (groom) was responsible for inviting guests. Alternate Translation: "The groom's family invited Jesus" or "They also invited Jesus" (UDB). (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit|Explicit and Implicit]]) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
