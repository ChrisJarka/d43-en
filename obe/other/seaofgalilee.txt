====== Sea of Galilee, Sea of Chinnereth, Sea of Gennesaret ======


===== Facts: =====

The Sea of Galilee is a lake in eastern Israel. In the Old Testament it was called the "Sea of Chinnereth."

    * The water of this lake flows south through the Jordan River down to the Salt Sea.
    * The towns of Capernaum and Bethsaida were next to the Sea of Galilee.
    * Other names for the Sea of Galilee include: the "Sea of Tiberias" and the "Lake of Gennesaret."
