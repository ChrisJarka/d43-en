===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **My heart is fixed **- "I am trusting completely in you"
  * **my honored heart**
 - "with my soul"
  * **I will wake up the dawn**
 - This is written as though the dawn is a sleeping person who must be awakened. The psalmist wishes to rise first and bring in the morning light with his music. AT: "I will wake up before the sunrise and play my music" (See: personification)
