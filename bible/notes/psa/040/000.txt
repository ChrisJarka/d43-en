====== Psalms 040 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===
Psalm 40 is a psalm of deliverance; praising God for the way God had been rescuing him.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

The author was trying to avoid sinning in what he said. Life on earth is short. He wanted God's forgiveness before he died.
