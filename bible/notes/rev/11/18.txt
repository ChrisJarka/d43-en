===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - The twenty-four elders continue praising God.


  * **were enraged** - "were extremely angry"
  * **your wrath...your servants...your name** - The word "your" in these phrases refers to God.
  * **your wrath has come** -  God being ready to show his anger is spoken of as his wrath were a thing that could come. AT: "You are ready to show your anger" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **The time has come** - The time being right to do something is spoken of as if the time to do it is something that can come. AT: "The time is right" or "Now is the time" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **unimportant** - "lowly" or "low" or "of lower status"
