====== patient, patience ======

===== Definition: =====

Patient is holding up, enduring, or continuing under difficult cirumstances.  Patience is the quality of someone to hold up, endure, or continue under difficult circumstances.

  * The Bible tells us to be patient when facing difficulties because God is with us.
  * God is patient with us through His mercy, even though we are sinners worthy of the judgement of death.
