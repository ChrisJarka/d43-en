====== clan ======

===== Definition: =====

Clan is another word for family. This can refer to immediate and extended family originating from a common ancestor.

  * In the Old Testament when the Israelite tribes were counted they were counted according to their clans or family groups.
  * In Judges it says that Gideon was of the clan of Abiezer. Many other biblical characters are referred to in this way.

(See also: other)
