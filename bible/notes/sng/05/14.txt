===== Translation Notes: =====

The young woman continues to describe her beloved.
  * **His arms are rounded gold set with jewels** - "His arms are cylindars of gold that have jewels all over them." The woman uses this image to say that his arms are beautiful and precious. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * * **his abdomen is ivory covered with sapphires** - "his belly is smooth ivory that has sapphires all over it." The woman uses this image to say that his belly is beautiful and precious. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **ivory** - the white tusk or tooth of an animal that is similar to bone. People use ivory to make art and to make things look beautiful.

  * **ivory** - "Ivory" is a hard white substance that comes from the tusk of elephants or other large animal.
