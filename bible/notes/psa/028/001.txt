===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[:en:ta:workbench:translate:poetry|Translating Poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]]

  * **I cry out**  - AT: "I call out loudly."
  * **my Rock, do not ignore me**  - The word "Rock" refers to Yahweh who is stong and solid. (See: metaphor)
  * **respond to me**  - The word "respond" can be understood as "answer me."
  * **soon be with those who go down to the grave**  - AT: "soon be with those who have already died and are in the grave."
  * **Hear the sound of my pleading**  - AT: "hear the sound of my voice"
