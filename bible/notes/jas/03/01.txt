===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **many of you** - The word "you" refers to James' audience. (See: [[en:ta:translate:formsofyou-plural]]).
  * **my brothers **- "my fellow believers"
  * **knowing that** - "because"
  * **we will receive greater judgment**  - The reason God will judge teachers more severely can be stated more explicitly: "God will judge us more severely because we have no excuses when we sin since we know and understand his word better than other people." (See: [[en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]])
  * **we** - Here "we" refers to Paul and other teachers. (See: [[en:ta:translate:exclusive]])
  * **stumble**  - This is a metaphor which compares a person's moral failures to a person falling down as he walks down a road. This could be translated as "fail" or "sin." (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
  * **does not stumble in** - This litotes can be stated more positively: "can always control." (See: [[en:ta:translate:litotes]])
  * **his words** - "the words he speaks"
  * **perfect** - "spiritually mature"
  * **his whole body** - This is a metonym that can be translated as "all of his behaviors" or "all of his actions." (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
