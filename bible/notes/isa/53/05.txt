===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But he was pierced because of our rebellious deeds, ****he was crushed because of our sins **- "He was stabbed and beaten because of our sins" (See: Doublet)
  * **the punishment for our peace was on him **- "he took the punishment so that we could be at peace in our souls"
  * **and with his wounds we are healed **- "and because he took the physical punishment for us, we are healed"
