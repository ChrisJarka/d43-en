====== Ruth ======

===== Facts: =====

Ruth was a Moabite woman who lived during the time when judges were leading Israel. She was an early ancestor of Jesus Christ.

  * Ruth is best known for her loyalty to her widowed mother-in-law Naomi, traveling with her back to Israel and helping to provide for her.
  * She also committed herself to serving Naomi's God, the one true God of Israel.
  * Ruth became the great-grandmother of King David, who is the most well-known ancestor of Jesus.
