===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

Samson tells his riddle. Since it is supposed to be hard to understand, do not translate it in a way that people will immediately know what it means.


  * **Out of the eater, something to eat** - AT: "Out of the eater came something to eat" 
  * **the eater**  - "the thing that eats"
  * **out of the strong, something sweet** - AT: "out of the strong came something that is sweet"
  * **the strong** - "the thing that is strong"
  * **his guests** - This refers to the people that were invited to his feast. "the men at at Samson's feast"
