====== fear ======

===== Definition: =====


Fear is what a person feels when he knows that he, or someone he cares about, is in danger or could be be harmed. It is an unpleasant often strong emotion caused by expectation, awareness of danger, anxious concern, dread, fright or just being scared.


    * The word "fear" can refer to extreme terror or to a respect and carefulness around the thing or person that can cause the harm.
    * "To have fear" can be translated as "to fear" or "to be afraid."
    * The “fear of God” is something that people feel about him when they know that God is perfectly holy and hates sin. This fear motivates people to obey God and to hate and shun all form of evil.
    * “Fear of God” can be translated as "respect for God" or, “reverence for God” or, “awe of God”.
    * “To fear God” can be translated as "to be afraid of God" or “to revere God” or, “to be in awe of God” or, “to respect God.”
    * Fear of God is respecting Him, obeying Him, submitting to His discipline, and worshipping Him in awe.
