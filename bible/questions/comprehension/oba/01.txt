====== Obadiah Chapter 01 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? For what purpose did Yahweh send an ambassador among the nations?

A. Yahweh sent an ambassador among the nations to cause them to rise up for battle against Edom [1:1].

Q? What was the sin of the Edomites?

A. The Edomites had pride in their heart and believed that they could not be brought down to the ground [1:3].

Q? What is another name used for the nation of Edom?

A. Esau is used as another name for the nation of Edom [1:6,8].

Q? Who will deceive and prevail against Edom?

A. The men who had an alliance of peace with Edom will deceive and betray them [1:7].

Q? Why will Edom be covered with shame and cut off forever?

A. Edom will be covered with shame and cut off forever because of the violence they did to their brother Jacob [1:10].

Q? What happened on the day that Edom stood aloof from Jacob?

A. On that day, strangers entered the gates of Jerusalem and carried away its wealth [1:11].

Q? What did Yahweh tell Edom not to do regarding Judah in the day of its distress?

A. Yahweh told Edom not to gloat, rejoice, or loot their wealth in the day of Judah's distress [1:12-13].

Q? What did Yahweh say will return on Edom's head?

A. Yahweh said that Edom's own deeds will return on its head [1:15].

Q? What will some in Mount Zion be able to do despite the distress?

A. Some in Mount Zion will be able to escape despite the distress [1:17].

Q? How many of Edom will escape the judgment of Yahweh?

A. There will be no survivors in Edom after Yahweh's judgment [1:18].

Q? Who will then possess the mount of Esau?

A. Those from the Negev will then possess the mount of Esau [1:19].

Q? From where will the mount of Esau then be judged?

A. The mount of Esau will then be judged from mount Zion [1:21].
