====== Hebrew ======

===== Facts: =====

A person who speaks Hebrew or Aramaic, or a Jew.

  * A person who is part of the nation of Israel or of Jewish descent.
  * Those who practiced the Jewish religion.
  * Jewish Christians, regardless of language or locality.
  * The term "Hebrew" and "Israelite" used interchangeably.
