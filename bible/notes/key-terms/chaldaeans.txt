====== Chaldeans, Chaldea ======

===== Facts: =====

Chaldea is a region in Southern Babylonia. Chaldeans are the people who lived in this land.

  * Chaldea later became know as Babylonia around 600 BC
  * Jerusalem was captured and the Israelites were exiled to Babylonia in 597 BC.
  * Jerusalem and the temple were destroyed by Nebuchadrezzar of Babylonia in 587 BC.
