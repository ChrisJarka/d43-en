===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **border - **  The place where two pieces of land meet.
  * **and the east side between Havran and Damascus and between Gilead and The land of Israel will be the Jordan River **- The eastern boundry will begin at Havran and go to Damascus and from there go south to the Jordan River.
  * **You will measure from the border to the eastern sea, and this is the east side**- From that point to the eastern sea will be the east border.
