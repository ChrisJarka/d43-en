====== Law of Moses, Law of the Lord, God's Law ======


===== Facts: =====


The Law of Moses is the commandments that God gave Moses for the people of Israel to follow. The phrase "Law of Moses" can also refer to the first five books of the Old Testament because Moses wrote them.


    * God gave Moses this law at Mount Sinai in the desert, and Moses gave it to the people.
    * The Law of Moses serves as a guide to reveal mankind's sinful nature.
    * Depending on the context, "The Law of Moses" can also be translated as "the law that Moses gave" or "the books that Moses wrote."
    * "God's law" can also be translated as "the law that God gave" or "the law that God commanded."
