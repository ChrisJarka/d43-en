===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Geshur ... Aram** - These are names of people groups named after ancestors. Translate "Aram" as in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:01:17|1:17]].
  * **Havvoth Jair and Kenath** - These are the names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Makir ... Gilead ... Hezron ... Caleb ... Ashhur ... Tekoa** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Ephrathah** - This is the name of a woman. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **bore him** - "gave birth to his son"
