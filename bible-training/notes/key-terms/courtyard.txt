====== courtyard ======


===== Definition: =====


A courtyard is an area that is open to the sky, but surrounded by a building or walls. It has no roof.


    * God's tabernacle was a tent set up in a courtyard. The courtyard was surrounded by walls made of heavy cloth curtains.
    * <font 13px/arial;;#000000;;#ffffff>The temple building in Jerusalem had a courtyard around it. The walls surrounding this courtyard were made of stone.</font> 
    * God's people were permitted to go into the courtyard to worship God, but only the priests were permitted to go into the tabernacle or temple.
    * <font 13px/arial;;#000000;;#ffffff>This could be translated as "the land immediately surrounding the temple" or, "the temple grounds" or, "the temple enclosure."</font>
