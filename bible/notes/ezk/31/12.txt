===== Translation Notes: =====

The parable about the cedar continues.

  * **His branches fell off onto the mountains and in the valleys, and his boughs were broken in every channel of earth**  - "Assyria was left a broken nation".
  * **all the people of the earth went down into its shadow and abandoned him**  - "the nations who came to Assyria before are not the to help her."
