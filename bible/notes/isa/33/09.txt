===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The land mourns and withers away** - Isaiah describes the land of Judah as a person who is very sad because it once was beautiful but now it is ruined.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **Lebanon is confounded and withers away** - Isaiah describes the trees of Lebanon as a person who is ashamed. They are ashamed because they were once mighty and beautiful but now they are rotting.
  * **Sharon is like a desert plain** - "The plain of Sharon where flowers and trees once grew is now dry like a desert"
  * **and Bashan and Carmel shake off their leaves** - "and the leaves have fallen off the trees of Bashan and Carmel"
