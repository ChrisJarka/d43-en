====== 2 Samuel 01 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===
This chapter is the beginning of the story of David as king which is what the whole of the book of 2 Samuel is about. It also is the beginning of the expansion of David's kingdom  (1-12) and the beginning of the story of his reign in Judah (1-4).
This chapter is divided into two parts: In verses 1-16 David learns of Saul and Jonathan's death. In verses 17-27 he sings a funeral song for them.


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== David told of Saul and Jonathan's death ==
Even after Saul's death, David respected that God had chosen Saul as king. When a person hoping for a reward claimed he had killed Saul, David had him killed.
== David's funeral song ==
David praised Saul and Jonathan as great warriors and Jonathan as his dearest friend. 


=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphor ==
Saul expressed his great pain by the [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] "great suffering has taken hold of me." 
== Idiom ==
The people expressed their sorrow in the results of the battle with two [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]s __fallen__ meaning died and __by the sword__ meaning in battle in the sentence "they had fallen by the sword." David used an idiom "Your blood is on your head" meaning your death is your own fault..
== Synecdoche ==
David used two [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]]s to express that Jonathan and Saul were great warriors: "the bow of Jonathan did not turn back, and the sword of Saul did not return empty."
