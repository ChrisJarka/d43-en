===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **me** - This refers to Isaiah.
  * **'Maher-shalal-hash-baz'** - This Hebrew phrase is translated "quickly take by force and steal everything."
  * **I will summon faithful witnesses to attest for me** - "I will ask honest men to watch me"
  * **Uriah…Zechariah…Jeberekiah** - These are names. (See: [[en:ta:translate:translatenames]])
