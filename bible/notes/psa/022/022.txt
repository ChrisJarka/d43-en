===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I will declare your name** - AT: "I will make known your name" or "I will talk about your character" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **my brothers** - AT: "my fellow Israelites" (UDB) or "my fellow worshippers of Yahweh"
  * **in the midst of the assembly** - AT: "while I am in the middle of a large gathering of people who worship God"
  * **Stand in awe of him** - AT: "Rise up and be unable to speak before him"
