====== Lamentations Chapter 05 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What is Jeremiah asking Yahweh to do?

A. Jeremiah is asking Yahweh to call to mind what has happened to them and to see their shame. [5:1]

Q? What does Jeremiah tell Yahweh has happened to them?

A. Jeremiah says that their inheritance has been turned over to strangers, their houses to foreigners, they have become orphans with no more fathers, their mothers are like widows, their water costs silver to drink, and their own wood is sold to them. [5:2 - 4]
