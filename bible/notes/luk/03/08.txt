===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **produce fruits that come from repentance**  - The idea of a plant growing fruit is being compared to doing and saying things that show a change of mind (or heart, stomach, liver, etc.) about past thoughts and actions. (See: [[:en:ta:figuresofspeech:metaphor|Metaphor]])
    * **saying within yourselves** - That is "saying to yourselves" or "saying in your heart" or "thinking"
    * **We have Abraham for our father**  - That is, "Abraham is our ancestor."
    * **raise up children of Abraham out of these stones**  - That is, "turn these rocks into descendants of Abraham."
