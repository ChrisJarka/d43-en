===== translationNotes: =====

  * **So Yahweh was angry with his people ... he despised his own people** - "So Yahweh was angry with his people and despised them" 
  * **He handed them over to the nations** - The phrase "handed them over" means that he gave them over to their enemies' control. AT: "God allowed them to be captured by the nations" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **those who hated them** - "people who hated them"
