====== tetrarch ======


===== Definition: =====


Tetrarch is the title of a governing official in the Roman Empire


    * "Tetrarch" means "ruler of a fourth" indicating that the ruler did not have absolute authority.
    * "Tetrarch" can be translated as "prince."
