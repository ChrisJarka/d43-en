====== Phoenicia ======

===== Facts: =====

Phoenicia was a coastal region along the Western edge of the fertile crescent.

  * In the New Testament era, the capital of Phoenicia was Tyre.
  * Phoenicia was known for its cedar trees, purple dye and their ability to travel by sea.
