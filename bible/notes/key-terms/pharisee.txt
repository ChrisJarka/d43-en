====== Pharisee ======


===== Definition: =====


The Pharisees were an important group of Israel's religious leaders in Jesus' day.


    * The Pharisees were the strictest observers of the Law of Moses, even endeavoring to extend that with their oral traditions.
    * The Pharisees believed in the resurrection of the dead, angels, and the spirit.


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:term|term, Law of Moses]])
