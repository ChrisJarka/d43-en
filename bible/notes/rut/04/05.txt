===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **from the hand of Naomi** -  Possible meanings are 1) "from the hand of Naomi," or 2) "from the hand of Naomi and from Ruth the Moabitess." 
  * **Ruth..., wife of the dead** - "Ruth..., the widow of Elimelech's son"
  * **raise up the name of the dead**  - "for Ruth to bear a child to Elimelech's family and land.
  * **damaging my own inheritance**  - "negatively effect my inheritance to my children" implying that he would have to give some his own wealth to the children that Ruth may bear.
  * **take my right of redemption**  - Or "I will not redeem it; redeem it yourself" or "redeem it instead of me."
