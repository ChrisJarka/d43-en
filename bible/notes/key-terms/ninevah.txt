====== Nineveh, Ninevite ======


===== Facts: =====


Nineveh was a principle city in Assyria.  A Ninevite is a person who lived in Nineveh.


    * In the prophetic book of Jonah, God instructed Jonah to warn the Ninevites to change from their wicked ways.
