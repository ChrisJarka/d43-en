===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh**  - Here the word "Yahweh" is emphatic. A possible translation may be "Yahweh himself."
  * **Yahweh visited Sarah as he had said**  - This can be translated as "Yahweh came to Sarah as he had said he would" or "Yahweh came to help Sarah has he said he would."
  * **bore a son to Abraham **  - "gave birth to Abraham's son"
  * **in his old age**  - "when Abraham was very old"
  * **at the set time of which God had spoken to him**  - "at the exact time that God had told him it would happen"
  * **Abraham named his son, the one who had been born to him, whom Sarah bore to him, Isaac.** - "Abraham named their newborn son Isaac."
  * **Abraham circumcised his son Isaac when he was eight days old**  - "When his son Isaac was eight days old, Abraham circumcised him"
  * **Eight days**  - 8 days (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translateunknowns-numbers]])
  * **had commanded him**  - "had commanded Abraham to do"
