===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **You sweep them away as with a flood and they sleep;**  - AT: "Lord, you destroy the people as with a flood and they die" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
    * **sprouts up**  - starts to grow
    * **blooms**  - begins to grow and develop
    * **evening**  - the early hours of the night
    * **withers**  - weakens or fades
