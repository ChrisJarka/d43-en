===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to teach his disciples. This event began in 5:1.

  * **built his house upon the sand**  - This means that the person did not build his house on solid rock, but rather on small loose grains (or tiny particles) of rock, which are not stable.
  * **and it fell**  - "the house broke completely apart"
  * **and great was its fall**  - This means the winds and floods destroyed this house.
