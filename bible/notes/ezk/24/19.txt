===== Translation Notes: =====
The people of Israel question Ezekiel and Ezekiel tells them what Yahweh has said to them.

  * **The Word of Yahweh came to me and said** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:16|3:16]].
  * **the house of Israel** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:01|3:1]].
  * **Behold**  - AT: “Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you”
  * **pride of your strength ** - AT: "pride from the things you have done"   
  * **the desire of your eyes** - Ezekiel refers to the things the people of Israel selfishly want by referring to the part of their body they see those things with.  AT: "your desire for things because they seem better than what you have" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **the lusts of your soul** - Ezekiel refers to the emotional desires of the people of Israel by referring to the part of them that makes them different from other people. AT: "your great desire to be better than other people"
  * **sons and daughters who you have abandoned**  - When the Babylonians took Israelites into exile, some of the Israelite children were left behind. These children will die.
