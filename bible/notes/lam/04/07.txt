===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **her leaders** - Here the word "her" refers to Jerusalem.
  *  **shine like snow...white as milk** - The writer compares the purity of the leaders to the pure white of snow and milk. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **more ruddy than coral...form like sapphire** - The writer compares the beauty of the leaders to the beauty of red coral and blue sapphire. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **darkness blackens their appearance** - This may mean either 1) that the sun has darkened the leaders' skin or 2) that the soot from the fires that burned Jerusalem has covered their faces.
  * **they are unrecognizable** - AT: "No one can recognize them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **as dry as wood** - The leaders' skin has become as dry as wood due to lack of adequate food and water.
