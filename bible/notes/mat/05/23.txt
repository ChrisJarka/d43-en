===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to teach his disciples. This event began in 5:1.

  * **you**  - Jesus was talking to a group of people about what might happen in their individual lives.
  * **offering your gift**  - "giving your gift" or "bringing your gift"
  * **and there remember**  - "and when standing at the altar it comes to your mind"
  * **your brother has anything against you**  - "another person can recall some harm or damage caused by you"
  * **be reconciled**  - "correct the wrong"
  * **first be reconciled with your brother **- could be translated as: "first make it right with your brother" (See: Active or Passive)
