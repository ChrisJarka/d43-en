===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **in the first day of the month**  - The specific month of the year referenced here is unclear.
  * **the word of Yahweh came to me**  - The word of Yahweh came to Ezekiel.
  * **<font 14px line-height: 19px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Tyre has said against Jerusalem</font>    **- The people of Tyre made a statement directed toward the people of Jerusalem.
  * **aha**  - This is a statement expressing a sudden victory.
  * **The gates of the people are broken- **  The gate protecting the city from enemies was broken and allowed soldiers to enter the city and conquer it.
  * **She has turned to me **- The world, "she", would now go to Tyre, "me", for trade.
  * **I will be filled up as she is laid waste**  - <font 14px line-height: 19px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>"I will be successful as she is destroyed."</font> As Jerusalem was destroyed, Tyre believed that it would profit from their fall. (See:Metaphor)
