====== Psalms Chapter 101 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? David begins by singing about what two qualities?

A. "I will sing of covenant faithfulness and justice." [101:1]

Q? When David says that he will walk in the way of integrity, what is the question he asks?

A. "Oh when will you come to me?" [101:2]

Q? What kind of people will David look to have sit at his side and serve him?

A. The people David will have sit at his side are the faithful of the land.  Those who walk in integrity will serve him. [101:6]
