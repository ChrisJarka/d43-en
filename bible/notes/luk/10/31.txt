===== Translation Notes: =====

    * (Jesus continues telling his story.)
    * **by chance a certain priest** - This was not something that any person had planned. But it gives the audience the hope that someone might help the hurting man.
    * **and when he saw him** - "and when the priest saw the injured man." 
    * **he passed by on the other side**  - "he walked past the man on the other side of the road"
