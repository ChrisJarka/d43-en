===== Translation Notes: =====

(These verses are a continuation of Ezekiel's lament (oracle) to the King of Egypt, meaning the Egypt nation.)

  * **<font 14px line-height: 15.6800003051758px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>For I will give your flesh to the mountains</font>    and **- This metaphor shows the extent to which Yahweh will completely destroy and people to the extent that their body parts will be scattered and divided. (See: Metaphor)
  * **fill the valleys with your corpses**  - This hyperbole shows the extent to which Yahweh will leave a reminder of his judgement on the land of the King of Egypt. (See: Hyperbole)
  * **<font 14px line-height: 15.6800003051758px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Then I will irrigate the land of your infection up to the mountains with your blood, \q And the stream beds will be filled with you</font>    **- "your bodies, bones and blood will be spread out, "irrigate" through the land and up the mountains and the "stream beds" or "the original channel where water runs" will be filled with the human parts. This personnification shows how after God's judgement, he will leave a reminder to other peoples and nations. (See: personification)
