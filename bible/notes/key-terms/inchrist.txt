====== in Christ, in Jesus, in the Lord ======

===== Definition: =====

The terms "in Christ," "in Jesus," and "in the Lord" are similar and relate to the relationship of those with saving faith in Jesus.

  * These three terms refer to a believer's relationship or fellowship with Christ, belonging to Christ, in harmony with Christ, or accountable to Christ.
  * The three terms are also synonymous with being a believer in Christ.

(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:christ|Christ]])
