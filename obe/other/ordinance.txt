====== ordinance ======

===== Definition: =====

An ordinance is a public regulation or law that commands what people in a certain society should do.

  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Usually an ordinance is a custom that has become well-established through years of practice.</font>
