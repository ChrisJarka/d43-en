====== Claudius ======

Claudius, also known as Tiberias Claudius Nero Germanicus, was Roman emperor of the Roman Empire from 41 to 54 A.D.

===== Facts: =====

  * As emperor, he invaded Britain and made it an imperial province.
  * Claudius expelled many Jews from Rome during his reign including Prisca and Aquila.
  * Aftre he was poisoned by his wife, Nero became emperor.
