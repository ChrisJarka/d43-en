===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Ah, Assyrian** - Yahweh is addressing one warrior but is actually talking to the entire the army of Assyria. (See: [[en:ta:translate:personification]])
  * **the club of my anger, the rod by whom I wield my fury** - Yahweh is referring to the army as a "club" and a "rod"; instruments that He will use to punish other nations. (See: [[en:ta:translate:personification]])  (See: [[en:ta:translate:doublet]])
  * **I am sending himâ€¦I ordered him**  The word "him" here also refers to the people of Assyria and can be translated as "I am sending them." or "I ordered them.**
