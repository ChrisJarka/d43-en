====== Judah ======


===== Facts: =====

Judah was one of Jacob's twelve sons. God had promised that some of his descendants would be the kings of Israel, and that one of them would rule forever.

    * Judah's descendants are referred to as the tribe of Judah.
    * When the kingdom of Israel split between the tribes in the north and the tribes in the south, the southern kingdom was called Judah.
    * The southern region of Palestine was called Judea. This word comes from the name of the southern kingdom, "Judah."
    * The word "Jew" comes from the name "Judah."
