  * **"In the same way the chief priests were mocking him with each other, along with the scribes"**  - "The chief priests and scribes were also mocking Jesus amongst each other"
  * **He saved others, but he cannot save himself- ** The Jewish leaders point out the irony in the fact that Jesus cannot or is not saving himself in this situation.
