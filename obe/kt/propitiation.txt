====== propitiation ======

===== Definition: =====

A propitiation is the substitute offering that satisfies God's holy anger.

  * The offering of the sacrificial blood of Jesus Christ is the propitiation to God for mankind's sins. Jesus bore our sin, and God's wrath for our sin, on the Cross.
