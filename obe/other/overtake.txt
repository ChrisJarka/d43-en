====== overtake ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "overtake" refers to gaining control of someone or something. 

  * It usually means to catch up to something after pursuing it.
  * When a military wins a battle they "overtake" the enemy. 
  * When a predator overtakes its prey, it means that it pursues and catches its prey.
  * curse overtakes someone, troops overtake
