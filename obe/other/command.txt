====== command, to command ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "command" refers to a verbal or written statement that requires someone to do something.

  * God commands us to obey His teachings. That includes commanding all people to repent and follow Jesus.
  * Sometimes a command tells people what they should do, such as "Honor your parents."
  * Other times a command tells people what they should not do, such as, "Do not steal."

(See also: other)
