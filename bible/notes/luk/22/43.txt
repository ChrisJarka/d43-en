===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Being in agony, he prayed**  - "He was in agony, and he prayed"
    * **his sweat became like great drops of blood falling down on the ground**  - It is possible that this actually happened. But it is more likely that this is a simile that compares his sweat drops to blood drops. (See: [[:en:ta:figuresofspeech:simile]])
