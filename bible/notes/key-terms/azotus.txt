====== Azotus, Ashdod ======

===== Facts: =====

Azotus is the Greek name for the Old Testament city of Ashdod. It was one of the main cities of the Philistines and was located in southern Canaan, near the coast of the Mediterranean Sea.

  * Although Ashdod was part of the land that God had given to the tribe of Judah, it still remained under Philistine control and the temple of the Philistine's false god Damon was there.
  * Ashdod is most well-known as the place where the ark of the covenant was taken after it was stolen by the Philistines.
  * In the New Testament, Ashdod is called Azotus and was one of the cities where Philip preached the gospel.
