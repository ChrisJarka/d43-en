====== Leviticus Chapter 03 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What fellowship offering may be offered from the herd?

A. A fellowship offering may be either male or female without blemish offered from the herd.  [3:1]

Q? Where is the fellowship offering to be killed?

A. The fellowship offering was to be killed at the door of the tent of meeting. [3:2]

Q? What parts of the fellowship offering must be removed and placed on the altar?

A. The fat that connects to the inner parts, the lobe of liver and the kidneys must be removed and placed on the altar.  [3: 3-4]

Q? Is there any difference in a fellowship offering that is taken from the flock instead of the herd?

A. No. [3:6-8]

Q? What different thing must be removed from the fellowship offering that comes from the flock?

A. The entire fat tail cut away close to the back bone must be removed if the fellowship offering comes from the flock.  [3:9]

Q? What does Yahweh tell the people not to eat in any place they will ever live?

A. Yahweh tells the people not to eat fat or blood any place they will ever live. [3:17]

Q? Who only does the fat belong to?

A. The fat only belongs to Yahweh. [3:16]
