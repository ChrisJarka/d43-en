====== serve ======


===== Definition: =====


To serve someone is to do something that benefits that person. Someone may serve because he is obligated to serve, or he may serve simply because he wants to.

    * "To serve God" can refer to worshiping God or to doing what God commands.
    * Though Jesus is God, he said that he came to serve and to give his life to save people.
    * "To serve someone" can also be translated as, "to minister to someone" or, "to work for someone."

(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:servant|servant]])
