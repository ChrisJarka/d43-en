===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Let us lift up our heart with our hands to God**  - People in those times raised their hands to pray; they should also sincerely seek God from their deepest beings. AT: "We should not only life our hands when we pray; we also also be sure to pray sincerely from deep within." (See: Metaphor)
    * **against you**  - "You" refers to God.
    * **You have covered yourself with anger**  - God is so angry that it is as if he has wrapped a long robe around him, which represents anger. AT: "He has clothed himself with anger." (See: Metaphor)
