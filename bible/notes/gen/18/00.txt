=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Three men ==
The three men who looked at Abraham were probably angels. This is evident from Abraham's reaction to them. Also, it appears that Abraham knew that he was speaking directly to Yahweh through these men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]).
