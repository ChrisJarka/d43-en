====== unclean ======


===== Definition: =====


A term that signifies a state of dirtiness, evilness, impurity, or moral failure. In the Old Testament the term signified a blemish that made that person or sacrifice unacceptable and impure according to the law of God.


    * An Old Testament priest who did not properly prepare himself to serve would be considered unclean.
    * Any person who does not have their sin forgiven is unclean before God.
