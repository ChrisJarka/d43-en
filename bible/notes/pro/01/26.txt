===== Translation Notes: =====

The personification of wisdom continues to speak, describing the consequences of refusing her offer of wisdom.

  * **calamity**  - an event that causes great harm and suffering
  * **terror**  - strong feeling of fear
  * **when your fearful dread comes like a storm** - The way great fear will come upon the person is compared to the way that a powerful storm strikes. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **and disaster sweeps over you like a whirlwind**  - In a similar expression to the first part of the verse, the way troubles strike are compared to the way a damaging, swirling wind sweeps over an area. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **disaster**  - a sudden event causing great damage or hardship
  * **distress**  - pain or agony
  * **anguish** - extreme suffering, grief, or pain
