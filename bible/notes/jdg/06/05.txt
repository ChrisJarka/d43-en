===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Whenever they and their livestock** - "Whenever the Midianites and their livestock"
  * **they would come as a swarm of locusts** - The Midianites are compared to a swarm of locusts because they came in with a great number of people and their livestock ate everything that grew. AT: "They would come with their animals, which would eat up all the growing crops" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **it was impossible to count** - This expresses that the number is very great. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
