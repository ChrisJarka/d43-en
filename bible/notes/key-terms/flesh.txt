====== flesh ======

===== Definition: =====

Flesh refers to the physical form of a man, woman or animal.

  * In the Bible, "flesh" can figuratively refer to a person's descendants. This is with the phrase "flesh and blood."
  * "Flesh" is also a way of referring to a person's sinful nature or Christians living in sin.
  * In an animal, the "flesh" is the meat of the animal.
  * Depending on the context, "flesh" can be translated as "body", "person", "skin", "living creatures" or "sinful nature".

(See also: other)
