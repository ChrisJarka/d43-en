===== translationNotes: =====

  * **from the leader of the synagogue** - Implied is that they came from his house, since he himself was still with Jesus. AT: "from the house of the leader of the synagogue" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Why trouble the teacher any longer?** - The people asked this question because they did not understand the power of Jesus. AT: "There is nothing the teacher can do!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
