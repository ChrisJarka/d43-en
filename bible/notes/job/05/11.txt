===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He**  - God
  * **He does this in order to set up on high those who are low**  - alt "He sets up on high those who are low"
  * **set up on high **- AT: "gives honor to", "exalts".
  * **to raise to safety those who mourn in ashes**  - alt "to raise to safety those who mourn". This is parallel to "set up on high thos who are low". See: Parallelism
  * **frustrates**  - "breaks," or "brings to nothing". God causes their plans to fail.
  * **schemes**  - "thoughts," or "plans"
  * **crafty**  - "clever", or "sensible," or in a negative sense, "dishonest," or "tricky". This word is parallel to "the wise" in verse 13.
