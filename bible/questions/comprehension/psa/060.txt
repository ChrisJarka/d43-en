====== Psalms Chapter 060 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What has God done to them, and what is being requested of God?

A. He has cast them off, broken them down, and has been angry with them; God should restore them again. [60:1]

Q? What has God done to the land, and what is being requested of him?

A. He has made the land tremble and torn it apart; God should heal its breaches. [60:1]

Q? What has God shown them, and what has he made them drink?

A. He has shown them severe things, and made them drink the wine of staggering. [60:3]
