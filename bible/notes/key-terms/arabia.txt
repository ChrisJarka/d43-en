====== Arabia ======

Arabia is the largest peninsula in the world covering nearly 3,00,000 square kilometers. It is between the Red Sea and the Gulf of Aqaba, extending to the Persian Gilf.  It consisted of Mount Sinai and modern Saudi Arabia.

===== Facts: =====
