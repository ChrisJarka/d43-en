====== church, Church ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "church" or "Church" refers to a group of people who believe in Jesus. <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>In the Bible, this term always refers to Christian believers, and never to a building.</font>

  * <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>.In English, this word is capitalized ("Church") when it refers to all believers everywhere in the whole body of Christ. But it is not capitalized ("church") when it refers to a local group of believers in one place.</font>
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>When it refers to a local church, often the location is also mentioned, as in, "the church at Ephesus."</font>
  * The original meaning of the word for "church" in the Bible is "called out (to meet together)." It refers to an "assembly" or "gathering."

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * The term "church" could be translated as a "gathering together" or "assembly" or "congregation.".
  * Make sure that the term used to translate "Church" can be used to refer to all believers.
  * It is best to translate this term with a word that does not refer to a building.
