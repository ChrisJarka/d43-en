===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **fell sick** - This does not mean that he fell down or that he injured himself in a fall. It means he became sick or ill.
    * **Please arise** - "Please get ready"
    * **saying that** - AT: "he said that" or "he predicted that"
    * **Take with you ten loaves, some cakes, and a jar of honey** - These items were to be given to Ahijah as payment for his help. These go along with the disguise because a king's wife would probably take better gifts. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
    * **some cakes** - "hard biscuits"
