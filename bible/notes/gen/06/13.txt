===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **all flesh** - This refers primarily to human beings.
  * **the earth is filled with violence through them** - "the earth is filled with violence because of them"
  *   * **make for yourself a boat**  - boat (Hebrew, "TBT") can be translated "ark" or "large box". What makes it a "boat" is that it is constructed to float and survive on the open water in heavy weather and for a significant time.
  * **cypress wood**  - The exact tree of type is unknown. You may use the name cypress or the name of a tree that is good for making a boat with.
  * **pitch**  - This is a sticky resin or oily substance that people put on a boat to stop water from getting into the boat. People make it by boiling tar that It oozes from the ground in some places.
  * **three hundred cubits** - "138 meters." You may use the Hebrew measuring units from the ULB or the metric units from the UDB or your own culture's units if you know how they compare to the metric units.  You may also write a footnote that says "Three hundred cubits is about 138 meters."
