===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Greet one another with a holy kiss**  - This was a near-eastern custom of greeting. If your culture has a different custom, you may want to substitute it.
    * **Philologus...Nereus...Olympas**  - These are the names of men. (See: [[en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
    * **Julia**  - This is the name of a woman. (See: [[en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
