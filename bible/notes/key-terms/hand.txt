====== hand, hands ======

===== Definition: =====

Refers to a person's physical hand which, when laid upon another person, can be a means through which God's power is manifested as in Acts 8.  Hand refers to an individual's power or presence being shown, as in the prayer to God "Extend your hand to heal.  It includes receiving power or control over another person as in Scripture where God says "I delivered them into your hand."
