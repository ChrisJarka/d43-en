====== Jeremiah Chapter 27 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Jeremiah gets a word from Yahweh for which kings?

A. The king of Edon, Moab, Ammon, Tyre and Sidon. [27:1-2]

Q? What is the message for the kings?

A. All their nations will serve Nebuchadnezzar, king of Babylon, his sons, and his sons' sons. [27:5-7]

Q? What will happen to the nations that do not serve Nebuchadnezzar?

A. Yahweh declares he will punish that nation with sword, famine, and plague. [27:8]

Q? If you listen to deceitful prophesy what will happen to you?

A. Yahweh will send you away from your land and you will die. [27:9â€“10]

Q? The nation that takes the yoke of the king of Babylon will receive what?

A. They will rest in their land, cultivate it and make homes. [27:11]

Q? What was the message Jeremiah gave to King Zedekiah?

A. If he wants to live he needs to serve the King of Babylon. [27:12]

Q? What will happen to Zedekiah if he listens to the lies of his prophets?

A.Yahweh will drive Zedekiah out, he will perish, both he and the prophets. [27:14-15]

Q? What does Jeremiah say to the priests and the people?

A. They are not to listen to the words of the prophets, they are to serve the king of Babylon and live. [27:16-17]

Q? What did Nebuchadnezzar take and not take from Judah and Jerusalem?

A. He took Jehoiachin and all the nobles. He did not take the objects from the temple. [27:19-20]

Q? What happened to the objects of the temple that remained?

A. They were brought to Babylon and remained there until the day Yahweh restored them. [27:21-22]
