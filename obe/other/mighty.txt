====== mighty, might ======

===== Definition =====

The terms "mighty" and "might" refer to having great strength or power.

  * The phrase,  "mighty men" often refers to men who are courageous and victorious in battle
  * God is also referred to as the "mighty one."
  * The phrase, "mighty works" usually refers to the amazing things God does, especially miracles.
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>This term is related to the term,  "a</font> <font 14px line-height: 19.6px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>lmighty" which is a common description for God, meaning that he has complete power.</font>

===== Translation Suggestions =====

  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Depending on the context, t</font> <font 14px line-height: 19.6px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>he term "mighty" could be translated as "powerful" or "amazing" or "very strong."</font>
  * In Acts 7, Moses is described as a man who was "mighty in word and deed.". This could be translated as, "Moses gave powerful teachings and did miraculous things" or "Moses spoke God's word powerfully and did many amazing things."
  * The term "might" could also be translated as "power" or "great strength."
  * Depending on the context, "mighty works" could be transalted as, "amazing things that God does" or "miracles" or "the God doing things with power."
