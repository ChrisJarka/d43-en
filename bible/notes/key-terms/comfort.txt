====== comfort, console ======

===== Definition: =====

Comfort means to encourage, refresh, relieve, soothe, or console.

Text

  * The Greek root of comfort is paraclete which describes a major role of the Holy Spirit to be a comforter.
  * In the Sermon on the Mount Jesus congratulated those who mourn because they are to be comforted.
