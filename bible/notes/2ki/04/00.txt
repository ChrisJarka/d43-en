====== 2 Kings 04 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

The story of Elisha continued with a group of stories about miracles Elisha did. (2 Kings 4-6) 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Elisha helps a widow economically and barren woman with conceiving a son, a dinner removing poison, multiplying food to feed many people.
