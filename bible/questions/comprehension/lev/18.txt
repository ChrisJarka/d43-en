====== Leviticus Chapter 18 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What two places did Yahweh tell the people they could not do like the people there?

A. Yahweh told the people they could not do like the people of Egypt or Canaan. [18:3,4]

Q? With whom did Yahweh tell the people they could not have sexual relations?

A. Yahweh told the people they could not have sexual relations with any close relative. [18:6-16]
