===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It will be that just as you were an example**  - "You are a symbol (or "example")" Other nations saw the results of God's punishing Judah and Israel.
  * **of a curse to the nations,**  - AT: "of a cursed nation,"
  * **I will save you,**  - "I will rescue you,"
  * **house of Judah and house of Israel,**  - See (Metonym) "people of Judah and Israel,"
  * **but let your hands be strong!**  - See (Idiom) "but work hard to complete the temple!"
  * **For Yahweh of Hosts says this **- "Yahweh of Hosts says,"
  * **do harm to you **- "punish you"
  * **your fathers provoked my anger**  - "your ancestors angered me" or "your ancestors made me angry,'"
  * **and did not relent,**  - "and I did not change my mind,"
  * **so also will I return and plan in these days to do good to Jerusalem and the house of Judah! **- AT: "but now I will bless Jerusalem and Judah"
  * **Do not fear! **- "Do not be afraid!"
