  * **You** - This refers to Yahweh.
  * **they rejoice before you like the joy at harvest time, as men rejoice when they divide the plunder** - This compares the joy of the people to the joy at harvest or when dividing the things captured at battle. (See: [[en:ta:translate:doublet]]) See: [[en:ta:translate:simile]])
