====== remnant ======

===== Definition: =====

Remnant refers to a group of people who survive a destructive event, such as foreign invasion, or difficult circumstances, such as those in the New Testament who remain faithful to their beliefs under persecution.

  * Isaiah referred to a group of Jews as being a remnant who survived attacks from outsiders and lived to return to the Promised Land in Canaan.
  * After the end of the Babylonian Captivity, several Old Testament prophets spoke of a remnant of Jews returning to the Promised Land as well.
  * John refers in the Book of Revelation to a remnant of those faithful to Jesus Christ.
