===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the fruit of your womb** - AT: "the baby in your womb" or "the baby you will bear" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **And why has it happened to me that the mother of my Lord should come to me?** - Elizabeth was not asking for information—rather, she was showing how surprised and happy she was that the mother of the Lord had come to her. AT: "How wonderful it is that the mother of my Lord has come to me!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **the mother of my Lord** - This refers to Mary. AT: "you, the mother of my Lord" (UDB)
  * **jumped** - "moved suddenly" or "turned forcefully"
  * **blessed is she who believed** - AT: "you who have believed are blessed" or "because you believed, you will be glad"
  * **the things that were told her from the Lord** - AT: "the message that the Lord gave to her" or "the things that the Lord told you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
