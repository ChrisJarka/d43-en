===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **house of David** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:07:01|7:2]]
  * **Is it not enough for you to test the patience of people? Must you also test the patience of my God?** - Isaiah uses a question to scold the descendants of David who were ruling Judah. Alternate translation: "You test the patience of of people. Now you even test the patience of my God!" (See: [[:en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **young woman** - This young woman is morally pure and not married.
  * **young woman will conceive** - "a young woman will become pregnant"
  * **his name Immanuel**  - Immanuel means //God with us//.
  * **curds and honey**  - This expression implies that the land has been devastated by a foreign nation and food is scarce. The curds are a milk product and honey is produced by bees. This food would still be available even though their crops are destroyed.
