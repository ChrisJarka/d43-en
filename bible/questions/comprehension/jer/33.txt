====== Jeremiah Chapter 33 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Yahweh say to Jeremiah?

A. Yahweh said, "Call to me, and I will answer you. I will demonstrate great things to you, mysteries that you do not understand." [33:1-3]

Q? What was Yahweh's prophecy concerning the houses in the city?

A. Yahweh's prophecy was that the Chaldeans are coming to fight and to fill the houses with corpses of people who will be killed. [33:4-5]

Q? What good news does Jeremiah share with the people of Judah?

A. Yahweh will pardon all the iniquities of the people and bring about healing and a cure and bring them abundance, peace, and faithfulness. [33:6-8]

Q? How will the streets of Jerusalem change?

A. Yahweh told Jeremiah that right now the streets are desolate and uninhabited, but once again will be heard the sounds of rejoicing and celebration, for the city will become an object of his joy. [33:9-11]

Q? What does Yahweh say to Jeremiah?

A. Yahweh says, "In this desolate place, there will again be in all the cities a grazing place for shepherds who lead their flocks to lie down." [33:12-13]

Q? What has Yahweh promised to do?

A. Yahweh has promised to make a righteous branch to grow for David so that he can carry out justice and righteousnes in tthe land. [33:14-16]

Q? What does Yahweh say to Jeremiah?

A. Yahweh says, "A man from David's line will never be lacking to sit on the throne of the house of Israel, nor to offer sacrifices. [33:17-18]

Q? What comparison does Yahweh use to describe the increase of David's descendants?

A. Yahweh says that David's descendants will be as numerous as the hosts of heaven and the sand of the seashores. [33:22]

Q? What have the people declared about Yahweh's punishment of Judah?

A. The people have declared that Yahweh has now rejected the two clans that he chose, so they are no longer a nation. [33:23-24]

Q? What does Yahweh say to Jeremiah?

A. Yahweh says that he will not reject the descendants of Jacob and David, but will bring back their fortunes and be compassionate toward them. [33:25-26]
