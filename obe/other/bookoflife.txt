====== Book of Life ======

===== Definition: =====

The Book of Life is a book in which God has written the names of the redeemed believers from all ages.

  * Revelation describes the book as the Lamb of God's Book of Life; so called because the blood of His sacrifice qualifies people of faith whose names are written in it.
  * The Book of Life was written before the foundation of the world.
