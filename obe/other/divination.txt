====== divination ======

===== Definition: =====

The term, "divination" refers to the practice of  communicating with dead people or spirits in order to get information about the future or other unknown things.

  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Often this practice is part of religions that don't worship God. It is sometimes called "soothsaying" or "fortune telling."</font>
  * Divination is often superstitious and includes such practices as performed by interpreting patterns in animal bones or in remnants of a drink left behind in a cup.
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>In the Bible, God rebukes those who give others a false divination, claiming to have had a vision or message from God, but they haven't.</font>
  * The Bible also forbids the practice of divination that tries to communicate with evil spirits.

(See also: other)
