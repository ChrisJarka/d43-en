====== Ephesians 06 General Notes ======

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Slavery ==
This passage does not condone slavery as an acceptable practice. The teaching on this subject would have been rather radical at that time because masters were not expected to treat their slaves in such a pleasant way. Overall, Paul's focus is on living on in a way that pleases God despite the circumstances of one's life. Remember that Paul is in prison when he writes these words.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Armor of God ==
This is an extended [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] that describes how a Christian can protect themselves from spiritual forces that attack them in their daily life.
