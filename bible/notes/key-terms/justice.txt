====== justice, justly ======


===== Definition: =====


Justice is equal treatment for all people under the law.


    * Just laws, actions, and decisions are morally right and fair.
    * To rule justly is to make decisions that are morally right and to treat people equally.


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:judge|judge]])
