\v 16 If someone sets apart for the honor of Yahweh some of the property that belongs to him and his family, its value will be determined by the quantity of seed that would be needed to sow that entire tract of land. It will be ten pieces of silver for each 220 liters of seed.
</usfm>

===== translationNotes: =====


  * **then the estimation of its value will be made in keeping with the amount of seed required to plant it** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "then he will estimate its value based on the amount of seed a person would need to plant it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **One homer of barley will be valued at** - AT: "The value of one homer of barley is"
  * **homer** - A homer is 220 liters. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bvolume]])
  * **fifty shekels** - A shekel is 11 grams. "50 shekels" or "550 grams" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bmoney]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
