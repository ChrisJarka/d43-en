===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Holy is his name!**  - The word "Holy" comes first in this sentence to show its special importance. Some languages may need to say, "His name is holy!"
    * **he who is powerful**- Another way to translate this would be, "God who is powerful." 
    * **HIs mercy is for**  - This could be translated as, "He is merciful to."
    * **generation after generation**  - This means, "every generation" or "all generations."
    * **fear him**  - The word "fear" in this context means to deeply honor, respect, obey and hold in awe.
