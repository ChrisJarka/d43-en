===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Let the children first be fed. For it is not right to take the children's bread and to throw it to the dogs.**  - Jesus uses this metaphor to tell this woman that he must tend to the needs of the Jewish people first before the gentiles, like her, who were sometimes referred to as dogs.  Alternate Translation: "I must minister to the Jews first before the gentiles."  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Yes, Lord, even the dogs under the table eat the children's crumbs**  - The woman responds with this metaphor expressing that even though she is not a Jew, she believed he could help her.  Alternate Translation: "Yes, Lord, but you can serve even a gentile such as I in this small way"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
