====== Psalms 116 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 116 is a psalm of praise for saving his life.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

The author was very sick and expected to die but God healed him. So he will pay his vow and sacrifice a thank offering.
