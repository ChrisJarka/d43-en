===== translationNotes: =====

  * **As for the importation of horses from Egypt and Kue for Solomon** - "Importation" is the act of bringing something into one country from another country. This can be stated as a verb. AT: "As for the horses Solomon imported from Egypt and Kue" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **Kue** - This is the name of a region. Some think that Kue was the same as Cilicia, in Asia Minor. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **at a price** - "at a set price" or "for money"
  * **six hundred shekels of silver ... 150 shekels** - A shekel is a unit of weight equal to about 11 grams. AT: "about 6.6 kilograms of silver ... about 1.7 kilograms" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]])
  * **six hundred** - "600" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **exported** - to send something out of one country into another
