===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **So they did that - ** The disciples had the crowd sit down in groups.
    * **and he **- This refers to Jesus.
    * **loaves**  - These are specific amounts of baked bread. You may also say, "whole breads."
    * **looking up at heaven**  - That is, "while looking" or "after looking."
    * **to set before**  - That is, "to hand out to" or "to pass out to" or "to serve to."
    * **were picked up - ** That is, "they picked up" or "the apostles picked up" or "the disciples picked up."
