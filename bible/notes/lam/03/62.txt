===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The lips ... and their accusations, come against me all the day** - The author speaks of the people accusing him all day as if their accusations were soldiers that come to attack him all day. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **The lips of those who rise up against me** - Here the lips represent what the people say. AT: "The words of those who rise up against me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **those who rise up against me** - "those who attack me" 
  * **how they sit and then rise up** - These two actions together represent everything the people do. AT: "everything they do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
