===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He should not be a new convert -**  "he should not be a new believer" or "he should not be someone who became a believer a short time ago"
  * **and fall into condemnation like the devil" **- "that will lead into condemnation like that of the devil"
  * **He must also have a good ****reputation ****with**** those outside**  - "It is necessary that those who do not believe in Jesus think well of him" or "He must also have a good reputation from those outside the church"
  * **he does not fall into disgrace**  - "he does not bring shame (or reproach) upon himself (or upon the Lord)"
  * **and (he does not fall into) the trap of the devil**  - "and he does not allow the devil to trap (or ensnare) him"
