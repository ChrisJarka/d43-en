====== Psalms 030 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 30 is a psalm of praise to God. The psalmist was almost dying but God rescued him. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

If he had died, it would not have been a help to God. But since God rescued him he can praise God.
