===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The tribes go up there, the tribes of Yahweh, as a law for Israel** - AT: "Israel went to house of Yahweh the in obedience to his command" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **leaders sit on thrones, for judgment** - AT: "Leaders are ready to judge" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **for house of David** - AT: "the people whom David rules" or "the people of Israel"
