===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **ardor of my anger - **  Yahweh is so upset to the point that He is going to take extreme action against Gog's armies.
  * **fire of my rage - **  Indicates the depth of His anger,
  * **shake before me - **  Yahweh begins to show His anger by causing an earthquake that kills a large number of Gog's troops.
  * **fish…creatures of the earth - **  All fish, animals, and insects who live on the earth will witness Yahweh's power.
  * **every man upon the face of the land -**  Human beings will also witness the power.
  * **steep places - **  Reference to ravines or canyons?
  * **walls fall - **  All of the structures built by man would collapse.
