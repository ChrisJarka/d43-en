===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They did not**  - The pronouns "they" and "them" in this verse refer to the Israelites.
  * **his power**  - The pronouns "his" and "he" in these verses refer to God.
  * **he performed his terrifying signs in Egypt and his wonders in the region of Zoan**  - These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: Doublet)
  * **region of Zoan**  - This refers to the area around the city of Zoan, which was in Egypt. AT: “field of Zoan”
