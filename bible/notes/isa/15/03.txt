===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **in the ****square ****everyone**** wails, melting in tears ** – The destruction will be so great that everyone will cry and mourn
  * **Heshbon, Elealeh, Jahaz - **  These are names of cities and towns. (See: How to translate names)
  * **armed men of Moab**  - "soldiers of Moab"
  * **their souls tremble in them**  - “they will be completely filled with fear”
