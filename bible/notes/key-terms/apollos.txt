====== Apollos ======

===== Facts: =====

Apollos was a Jew from <font 14px line-height: 13.0666666030884px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>the city of Alexandria in Greece</font>    who was well-educated in the Hebrew Scriptures and who was an eloquent speaker.

  * Apollos was instructed by two Christians in Ephesus named Aquila and Priscilla.
  * Paul emphasized how he and Apollos, and other teachers, were part of a team working together in God's harvest.
