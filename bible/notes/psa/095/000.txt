====== Psalms 095 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 95 is the first of six worship psalms (Psalms 95-100)

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

God made everything and takes good care of people. They should not be stubborn like the Israel people were in the desert under Moses.
