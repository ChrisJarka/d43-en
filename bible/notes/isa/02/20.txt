  * **people will throw away their idols of silver and of gold - "**people will get rid of all their gold and silver idols**"**
  * **that they have made for themselves to worship- "**that they made to worship**,"**
  * **they will throw them away to the moles and bats- "**they will throw them to the bats and rats**."**
  * **to go into the caves of the rocks- "**then they will crawl into caves**"**
  * **and into the clefts of the ragged rocks- "**and hide in holes in the cliffs**."**
  * **from before the terror of Yahweh- "**they will try to escape from Yahweh**"**
  * **<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>and from the glory of his majesty</font>    **- "they will fear what he will do because he is glorious and awesome,"

  * **whose life-breath is in his nostrils**  - "because they are as powerless as a person's breath" (See: metaphor)
