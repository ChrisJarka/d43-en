===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Pahath Moab**  - translate as in Ezra 2:6,10:30, Nehemiah 3:11, 7:11, 10:14
    * **Eliehoenai**  - translate as in I Chronicles 26:3
    * **Zerachiah**  - translate as Zerahiah in I Chronicles 6:6, 51,Ezra 7:4
    * **Shecaniah**  - translate as in I Chronicles 24:11, II Chronicles 31:15, Ezra 8:3
    * **Ben Jahaziel**  - translate as in I Chronicles 16:6 and II Chronicles 20:14
    * **Adin**  - translate as in II Samuel 23:8, I Chronicles 11:42, Ezra 2:15, Nehemiah 7:20, 10:16
    * **Ebed**  - translate as in Judges 9:26, 28, 30, 31, 35, Jeremiah 38:7, 8, 10, 11, 12, 39:16
    * **Jeshaiah**  - 
    * **Athaliah**  - names of people (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Two hundred, three hundred, fifty, and seventy males**  - the number of men and boys (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
