===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **it**  - The word "it" refers to the space at the doorway (41:17)
  * **decorated**- This means to make something beautiful usually by putting something on it.
  * **decorations of cherubim and palm trees** - This refers to images or carvings of cherubim and palm trees.
