====== bribe ======

===== Definition: =====

Money or goods given to someone to persuade them to lie.

* The Bible forbids giving or taking bribery to pervert truth.

* After Jesus' resurrection the soldiers who guarded His empty tomb were offered money to change their story about what happened.

(See also: other)
