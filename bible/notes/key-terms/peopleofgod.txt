====== People of God, His People, God's People ======


===== Facts: =====


God's people are the group of people who were born of the seed of Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob.


    * Jacob was given a new name, Israel, after he wrestled with the angel.
    * Jacob had 12 children; these children became the heads of the 12 Tribes of Israel.
