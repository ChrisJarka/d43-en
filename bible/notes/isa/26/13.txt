===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **but we praise your name alone** - Your name refers to the name of Yahweh and in turn Yahweh himself. This is a metonymy that is using the word "name" instead of "Yahweh." (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  * **you came in judgement** - "you punished those rulers"
