===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Be on your guard** - "Be ready for what people will do to you" (UDB)
  * **as a testimony to them** - Jesus is warning his disciples that they will have to stand before rulers and be beaten. The meaning of the word testimony can be clarified. AT: "to show them what is true" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
