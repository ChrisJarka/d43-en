====== Jeremiah Chapter 37 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? How did King Zedekiah respond to the words of Yahweh as proclaimed by Jeremiah the prophet?

A. Zedekiah did not listen to the words of Yahweh. [37:1-2]

Q? What message did KIng Zedekiah and Zephaniah the priest send to Jeremiah the prophet?

A. The message was, "Pray on our behalf to Yahweh our God." [37:3]

Q? What happened when Pharaoh's army came out from Egypt?

A. The Chaldeans who were besieging Jerusalem heard the news and left Jerusalem. [37:5]

Q? What message did Yahweh give to Jeremiah after Pharaoh's army had gone back to Egypt?

A. Yahweh's message was, "Do not deceive yourselves by saying the Chaldeans are leaving us, for they will return to fight against the city, capture and burn it." [37:6-10]

Q? Why did Jeremiah leave Jerusalem soon after the Chaldean army had left?

A. Jeremiah went out from Jerusalem to take posession of a tract of land in the land of Benjamin. [37:11-12]

Q? How was Jeriah a chief guard at the Benjamin gate mistaken about Jeremiah's intent?

A. Jeriah thought that Jeremiah was deserting to the Chaldeans. [37:13]
