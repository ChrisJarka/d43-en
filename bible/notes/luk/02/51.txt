===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **he went back home with them**  - That is, "Jesus went back home with Mary and Joseph."
    * **was obedient to them**  - That is, "obeyed them" or, "was always obeying them."
    * **treasured**  - That is, "carefully remembered" or, "joyfully thought about." A treasure is something that is very valuable or precious. Mary considered the things her son did and said to be very precious. (See: [[:en:ta:figuresofspeech:metaphor|Metaphor]])
    * **grow in wisdom and stature**  - That is, "become more wise and strong."
    * **increased in favor with God and people**  - That is, "people liked him more and more, and God blessed him more and more."
