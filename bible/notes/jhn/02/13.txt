===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **up to Jerusalem** - This means that they traveled from a lower place to a higher place. Capernaum is north of Jerusalem but is at a lower elevation. Jerusalem is built on a hill. 
    * **in the temple**  - This refers to the outer courtyard of the temple where the non-Jewish people were allowed to worship. 
    * **those that sold** - People could buy animals at the temple and sacrifice them to honor God.
    * **money changers** - These people would take money from other places and change it to money that could be paid to the temple. Jewish and non-Jewish people from around the world would travel to the temple to honor God and would have to change their money in order to buy animals for sacrifice.
