===== Translation Notes: =====

The angel continued to explain to Daniel what was going to happen.

  * **The king will do**  - This is the king of the North, last mentioned in [[en:bible:notes:dan:11:28|11:28]].
  * **He will exalt and magnify himself**  - The words "exalt" and "magnify" are praise terms used together to add emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]]).
  * **God of gods** - "the God above all false gods"
  * **until the wrath is completed** - This is either the wrath of the king of the North (who became enraged in [[en:bible:notes:dan:11:29|11:30]]) or the wrath of God.
