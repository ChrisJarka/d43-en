===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **who has required this of you, to trample my courts?**  -  This is a rhetorical question that can be stated as "no one told you to defile my courtyard while you perform all those rituals." (See:  [[en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]]) 
  * **meaningless offerings**  - "worthless gifts to God”
  * **Incense is an abomination to me**  - "incense is offensive to me"
