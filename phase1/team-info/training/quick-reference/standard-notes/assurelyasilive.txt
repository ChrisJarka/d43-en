====== As surely as I live, As surely as Yahweh lives ======

===== Description =====

This expression is used to show that something is true. It is often used in a promise.
===== Standard Notes =====

  * **as surely as I live** - God said this to show that what he was about to say was true. Alternate translation: "as true as it is that I am alive, it is true that."
