===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **we had parted** - The word "we" refers to Luke, Paul and those traveling with them. (See: [[en:ta:translate:exclusive]])
  * **we took a straight course to the city of Cos** - "we went straight to the city of Cos" or "we went directly to the city of Cos"
