====== feast ======


===== Definition: =====


A feast is an event where a group of people take part in a very large meal. Feast can also refer to official holidays usually with religious overtones.


    * The Jews had scheduled feasts as a part of their law. The feasts celebrated events in which God had done great works for them.
    * During feasts a person could bring whatever their means allowed, whether small or large portions.
    * Feasts could be for a day, or several days.
