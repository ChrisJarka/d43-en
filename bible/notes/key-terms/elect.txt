====== elect, chosen ones, Chosen One ======

===== Definition: =====

To elect means to choose something or someone. The phrase "the elect" literally means "the chosen (ones)" or "the chosen (people)."

  * The word "chosen" also means to be selected or appointed for a special task or role. It is sometimes used in the Bible to refer to certain people such as Moses and King David whom God had appointed as leaders over his people.
  * God "elected" (chose) people to love him and to bear good spiritual fruit.
  * In a few Old Testament passages, the term "elect" or "chosen one" is used to refer to the nation of Israel as God's chosen people.
  * In the New Testament, the term "the elect" or "the chosen (ones)" refers to people who have been saved by God through faith in Jesus.

"Chosen One" is a title that refers to Jesus, who is the chosen Messiah.

  * It also occurs as, "Yahweh's Chosen One" or "God's Chosen One" or "Chosen One of God".
