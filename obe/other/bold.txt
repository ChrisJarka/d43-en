====== bold, boldness ======

===== Definition: =====

The terms "bold" and "boldness" refer to having courage and confidence to speak the truth even when it is difficult or dangerous..

  * In the New Testament, Christ's disciples were frequently bold in speaking and preaching about Him in spite of the danger of being put in jail by Jewish leaders.
  * The boldness of Christ's early disciples in speaking the good news of his redeeming death on the cross was responsible for the growth of Christianity in Israel and nearby countries.
