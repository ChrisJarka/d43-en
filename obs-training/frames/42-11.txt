====== Jesus Went to Heaven [42-11] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-42-11.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

Forty days after **[[:en:obe:kt:jesus|Jesus]]** rose from the dead, he told his **[[:en:obe:kt:disciple|disciples]]**, “Stay in **[[:en:obe:other:jerusalem|Jerusalem]]** until my **[[:en:obe:kt:godthefather|Father]]** gives you **[[:en:obe:kt:power|power]]** when the **[[:en:obe:kt:holyspirit|Holy Spirit]]** **comes on you**.” Then Jesus went up to **[[:en:obe:kt:heaven|heaven]]**, and **a cloud hid him from their sight**. Jesus sat down **at the right hand of [[:en:obe:kt:god|God]]** to rule **over all things**.

//A Bible story from: Matthew 28:16-20; Mark 16:12-20; Luke 24:13-53; John 20:19-23; Acts 1:1-11//


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **comes on you** - That is, "comes down upon you" or, "comes onto you."
  * **a cloud hid him from their sight** - This could also be translated as, "he disappeared into a cloud."
  * **at the right hand of God** - This could also be translated as, "at the right side of God."
  * **over all things** - That is, "over everything."
  * //A Bible story from// - These references may be slightly different in some Bible translations.
