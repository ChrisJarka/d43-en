====== scribe ======


===== Definition: =====


A scribe in biblical narrative was a Jewish scholar or teacher who was charged with copying and preserving the books of the Old Testament.


    * Scribes also copied, preserved and interpreted the oral law.
    * At times, scribes were important government officials.
    * Important biblical scribes include Baruch and Ezra.
