====== blameless, without defect, perfect ======

===== Definition: =====

These terms are synonymous with a person who obeys God wholeheartedly, but does not imply that the person is sinless.

  * Abraham and Noah were considered blameless before God.
  * The terms “without defect” and “perfect” can also refer to ceremonial cleanness of sacrificial animals.
