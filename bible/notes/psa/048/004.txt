===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They saw it** - The word "it" refers to Jerusalem.
  * **then they were amazed** - AT: "and the city's size amazed them"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **they were dismayed** - AT: "the city's defenses dismayed them because their armies were not strong enough to attack the city" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Trembling took hold of them** - This phrase means the people were very afraid. If something holds you it controls you.  The word "trembling" refers to fear because it describes a person who shakes because they are very afraid. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
