===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **From the rising of the sun to its setting** - "From east to west." The place where the sun rises is furthest east and the place where it sets is the furthest west. AT: "In every place on the earth" or "Everywhere on the earth." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **Yahweh's name should be praised** - AT: "people should praise Yahweh's name." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Yahweh is exalted above all nations** - This idiom means AT: "Yahweh rules over all the nations" or "Yahweh is greater than all the nations."
  * **his glory reaches above the skies** - AT: "his glory is greater than the sky."
