===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues speaking to Ezekiel.
  * **When you have completed these days**  - "These days" refers to the days Ezekiel lies down on his side to illustrate the siege of the kingdom of Israel.
  * **carry**  - Translate this as you did in 4:4.
  * **set your face toward** - See how you translated this in 4:7
  * ** you will prophesy against it**  - The word "it" refers to Jerusalem.
  * ** behold** - This can be translated as “Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you.” 
  * ** I am placing bonds on you**  - The word "I" refers to God.  The word "you" refers to Jerusalem.
