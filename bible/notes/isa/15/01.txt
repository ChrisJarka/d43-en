===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **A declaration about Moab** - "A message from Yahweh about the people of Moab"
  * **Ar, Kir, Dibon, Nebo, Medeba**  - These are names of cities and towns. (See: [[en:ta:translate:translatenames]])
  * **Moab laments over Nebo and over Medeba** - "The people of Moab will weep because of what happened to Nebo and Medeba, towns in the south"
  * **All their heads are shaved bare and all their beards are cut off** - This was done to show that they are grieving.
