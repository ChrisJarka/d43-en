====== Numbers Chapter 28 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Yahweh say the sacrifices to him produced?

A. Yahweh said the sacrifices to him produced a sweet aroma for him. [28:2]

Q? What two animals, one in the morning and one in the evening, would be offered in the regular burnt offerings each day?

A. The sacrifices in the regular burnt offerings in the mornings and in the evenings would be male lambs without blemish. [28:3-4]

Q? What else did they have to offer with the regular burnt offering?

A. They had to offer a tenth of an ephah of fine flour as a grain offering mixed with a fourth of a hin of beaten oil. [28:5]

Q? What was the drink offering to Yahweh that had to be added to those regular burnt offerings?

A. One fourth of a hin of strong drink had to be added to these sacrifices and poured out in the holy place. [28:7-8]

Q? What had to be offered on the Sabbath day?

A. On the Sabbath day, two male lambs, each a year old without blemish and fine flour mixed with oil, and the drink offering had to be offered. [28:9-10]

Q? What special animal sacrifices had to be made at the beginning of each month?

A. At the beginning of the month the animals of the special sacrifices were two young bulls, one ram, and seven one year old male lambs without blemish. [28:11]

Q? How was Yahweh's Passover to be celebrated during the first month of the year?

A. Yahweh's Passover was to be celebrated with a holy assembly on the first day, a feast on the second day and bread without yeast for seven days to follow. [28:16-18]

Q? What sacrifices must be made during the seven days of the Passover?

A. The people of Israel must offer the same sacrifices each day of the Passover as they did on the first day of each month. [28:19-24]

Q? What are two days when the people of Israel must hold a holy assembly to honor Yahweh, do no normal work, and make the special sacrifices to Yahweh?

A. The seventh day of the Passover and the day of the first fruits during the Festival of Weeks are two days which the people of Israel were to carry out these commandments from Yahweh. [28:25-31]
