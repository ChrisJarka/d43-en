===== translationNotes: =====

  * **My heart is fixed, God, my heart is fixed** - Being confident is spoken of as one's heart being firm rather than shaken or easily moved. The phrase is repeated to show that he has complete confidence in God and will not change. AT: "I am completely confident in you, God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **I will sing praises** - AT: "I will sing praises to you, God"
  * **Wake up, my honored heart** - The psalmist speaks as though his own heart were a person who could wake up. Preparing to do something is spoke of as waking up. AT:  
  * **wake up, lute and harp** - The psalmist speaks as though the lute and harp were people who could wake up. AT: (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_apostrophe]])
  * **I will wake up the dawn** - The dawn is spoken of as if it were alive, and the dawn happening is spoken of as if it were waking up. Getting up before dawn happens is spoken of as causing the dawn to wake up. AT: "I will get up before dawn" or "I will get up before the sun rises" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
