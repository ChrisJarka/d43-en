===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **These are the clans**  - This refers back to all the people listed in chapter 10.
  * **according to**  - "listed by"
  * **From these were the nations spread over the earth** - "From these clans the nations divided and spread over the earth" or "These clans divided from each other and formed the nations of the earth."
  * **after the flood**  - This can be made more explicit: "after the flood destroyed the earth" (see [[:en:ta:discourse:implicitexplicit]]).
