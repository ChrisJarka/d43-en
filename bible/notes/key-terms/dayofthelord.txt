====== day of the Lord ======

===== Description: =====

The phrase "day of the Lord" refers to a time when there will be a final judgment of all who have rebelled against God. <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>This final judgment will include destruction of the earth.</font>

  * At the time of the "day of the Lord", God will be exalted and everyone will know and acknowledge that he is the Lord.
  * The phrase "on that day" also frequently precedes descriptions of what will happen at that time. The "last day" is also used to refer to this time period.
  * The resurrection of the righteous and the unrighteous will also happen at this time.
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>This final time of judgment and resurrection is also known as the "last day". Jesus promised his followers that he would raise them to life "on the last day".</font>
  * The day of the Lord cannot be predicted or known before it comes. Jesus said it would come as unexpectedly as a "thief in the night".
