===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **crush all the captives of the earth under his foot**  - This refers to how the conqueror punishes people as though he were stepping all over them. (See: Metaphor)
    * **turn aside the justice of men**  - This means to deprive men of receiving justice, as if it were bent aside. (See: Idiom)
    * **before the face of**  - This means in the presence of. (See: Idiom)
    * **suppress a person in his cause** - This means to refuse to give a person justice in a court.
    * **does not the Lord see?**  - This is a cry of frustration that the Lord does not respond to their suffering by helping them. AT: "The Lord should respond to our suffering!" (See: Rhetorical question)
