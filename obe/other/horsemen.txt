====== horsemen ======

===== Definition: =====

Horsemen were men who rode horses into battle.

  * Horses were used to pull chariots and carts. Horsemen would ride horses into battle.
  * Israel, however, did not maintain a large number of horsemen.
  * They believed that the use of horses in battle was a pagan custom that placed too much emphasis on power for victory rather than on Yahweh.
