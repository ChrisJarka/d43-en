===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **but the soles of their feet were like the hooves of a calf** - "but their feet looked like calf hooves" or "but their feet looked like the feet of calves"
  * **hooves of a calf** - the hard part of a calf's foot
  * **that shone like polished bronze** - The feet of the creatures were shiny like bronze is shine. Bronze is a bright looking metal. Alternate translation: "that were shiny like bronze when it has been polished." (See: [[en:ta:translate:simile]])
  * **on all four sides**- "on all four sides of their bodies." (See: [[en:ta:translate:ellipsis]])
  * **For all four, their faces and wings were like this** - "All four of the creatures had faces and wings"
  * **and they did not turn as they went** - "and the creatures did not turn as they moved"
