===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Go up now, look**  - AT: "Go look"
  * **His servant went up and looked**  - AT: "His servant looked"
  * **seven times**  - "7 times" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers|Translate Numbers]])
  * **At the seventh time**  - AT: "when he looked 7 times"
  * **a cloud as small as a man's hand**  - The size of the cloud is being compared to the size of a man's hand. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile|Simile]])
  * **Go up and say to Ahab…**  - AT: "Go tell Ahab to leave quickly, or the rain will stop him." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations|Speech Quotations]])
