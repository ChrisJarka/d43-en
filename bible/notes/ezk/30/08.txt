===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then they** – "Then the Egyptians"
  * **will know that I am Yahweh** – See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:06:06|6:7]].
  * **when I set fire in Egypt** – Fire refers to the destructive fires that often occurs in war. Alternate translation: "when I destroy Egypt with fire" or "when I send an attacking army to burn Egypt with fire." (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]]) When Egypt is destroyed just as Yahweh said it would be, the Egyptians will know that Yahweh is real and has power.
  * **all of her helpers are destroyed** - Alternate translation: "I destroy all of the allies of Egypt" or "I break all those who help Egypt." (See: [[en:ta:translate:active]]) 
  * **messengers will go out from before me in ships** - "I will send out people in ships to do what I want them to do" or "I will send out people in ships to give my message to Cush." Yahweh will send people to destroy Cush so that the people of Cush will understand his message that only Yahweh is God.
  * **to terrorize a secure Cush** - Alternate translation: "so that they will make Cush afraid, even though Cush thinks that it is safe from danger."
  * **Behold** - This can be translated as “Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you.”
  * **It is coming** - This word is a reference to the day of judgment that is coming.
