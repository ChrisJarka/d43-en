===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Pay attention and listen to my voice; be attentive and listen to my words** - Both clauses mean the same thing and are used together for emphasis.(See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **Does a farmer who plows all day to sow, only plow the ground? Does he continually break up and harrow the field?** - Isaiah uses a question to make them think deeply. AT: "A farmer does not plow the ground over and over. He does not keep turning over the soil." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
