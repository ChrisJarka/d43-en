====== Numbers Chapter 14 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did the whole community of Israel say to Moses and Aaron as a wish

A. The whole community said to Moses and Aaron that they wished they had died in the land of Egypt. [14:2]

Q? What did Joshua and Caleb tell the community would happen if Yahweh was pleased with them?

A.Joshua and Caleb said that i f Yahweh is pleased with them, then he would take them into this land and give it to them. [14:8]

Q? Why did Joshua and Caleb say protection would be removed from the people of the land?

A.Joshua and Caleb said that the protection of the people of the land would be removed from them, because Yahweh was with the children of Israel. [14:9]
