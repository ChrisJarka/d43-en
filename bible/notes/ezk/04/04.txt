===== Translation Notes: =====

  * God is using Ezekiel's actions to warn the Israelite people about their sin.
  * **set the sin of the house of Israel against it** - The word "it" refers to Ezekiel's carving of the siege against Jerusalem.
  * **house of Israel** - "the Israelites" (UDB) (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  * **you will carry their sin** - "you will bear their sin" 
  * **lie down against it** - The word "it" refers to Ezekiel's carving of Jerusalem
