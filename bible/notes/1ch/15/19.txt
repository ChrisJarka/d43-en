===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This continues the list of men whom the Levites appointed to sing and play music in the tabernacle. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])

  * **The musicians Heman, Asaph, and Ethan were appointed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The Levites appointed the musicians Heman, Asaph, and Ethan" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **cymbals** - two thin, round metal plates that are hit together to make a loud sound. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:13:07|13:8]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **Alamoth ... Sheminith** - The meaning of these words is not clear but may refer to a style of music. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **led the way** - "led the other musicians" or "led the processional"
