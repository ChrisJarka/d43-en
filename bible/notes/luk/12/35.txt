===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Jesus continues to teach his disciples.)
  * **Let your clothing be kept belted around your waist in readiness to serve**  - This can be translated as "Be dressed and read to serve."
  * **let your lamps be kept burning**  - "keep your lamps burning."
  * **lamps**  - These were small bowls with a wick and olive oil for fuel.
  * **be like people looking for their master**  - This is a simile. It compares the disciples being ready for Jesus to return to servants who are ready for their master to return. (See: [[:en:ta:figuresofspeech:simile|Simile]])
  * **returns from the marriage feast**  - "returns home from a marriage feast"
