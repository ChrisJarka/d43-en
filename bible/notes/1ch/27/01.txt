===== translationNotes: =====

  * **This is the list** - "These are the names"
  * **the commanders of thousands and of hundreds** - The army had commanders who lead groups of soldiers of different sizes. AT: "the commanders of groups of 1,000 soldiers and the commanders of groups of 100 soldiers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **in various ways** - "in different ways" or "in many ways"
  * **Each military division served month by month throughout the year** - This means that each division took turns serving periods of a month at a time.
  * **Each military division** - "Each group"
  * **twenty-four thousand men** - "24,000 men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **throughout the year** - "all year long"
  * **Over the division** - This is an idiom. AT: "In charge of the division" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **first month** - "1st month." This is the first month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of March and the first part April on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **Jashobeam ... Zabdiel ... Perez** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
