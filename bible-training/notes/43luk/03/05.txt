===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (The quote from Isaiah's prophecy continues.)
    * **Every valley will be filled**  -
    * **Every mountain and hill will be made level**  -
    * **the crooked roads will become straight **- That is, "the winding roads will be straightened."
    * **rough ways will be made smooth**- This can also be translated as "the bumpy roads will be smoothed out."
    * **see the salvation of God**  - This could also be translated as, "will learn about how God saves people from sin."
