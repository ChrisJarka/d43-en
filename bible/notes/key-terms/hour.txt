====== hour ======

===== Definition: =====

An hour is a unit of time used to divide up a day or night into even parts. It also has several figurative meanings.

  * <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>In the Bible, the Jewish system of hours was different from the modern-day system. The Jews counted daylight hours starting at sunrise and counted night hours starting at sunset.</font>      * The "third hour of the night" would be around 9 in the evening. Often night hours are referred to as a "watch."
      * <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>It is best to translate times as approximate, such as, "around 6 in the morning" or "about 3 in the afternoon,"</font>
      * <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Sometimes a more general expression is used, such as "early in the morning" or "late in the afternoon."</font>
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The following are some figurative uses of "hour":</font>      * <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The phrase, "in that very hour" means "at that moment" or "at that time."</font>
      * Sometimes "hour" refers to a regular, set time to do something such as, "the hour of prayer."
      * When the "hour had come" for Jesus to suffer and be put to death, it means that this was the appointed time that God had decided long ago that this would happen.
      * When used figuratively, the term "hour" can be translated as "time" or "moment" or "appointed time."

(See also: watch, watchman other)
