====== Herod the Great ======


===== Facts: =====


Herod the Great was a non-Jew who was the king of Judea at the time Jesus was born.


    * Herod was famous for the work he ordered to be done on the Jewish temple in Jerusalem.
    * He was cruel and had many people killed. When he heard that another king of the Jews had been born in Bethlehem, he had all the baby boys there killed.
    * His son and his grandson were were also named Herod and ruled as kings of Judea.
