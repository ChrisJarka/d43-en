====== Hezekiah ======

===== Definition: =====

Hezekiah was the 13th King of Judah just prior to the captivity period (715-686 B.C.)

  * Hezekiah instituted godly reforms by cleansing the temple and removing idol worship from Judah.
  * God performed a miracle for Hezekiah by having the sun go backwards on the sundial of Ahaz.
