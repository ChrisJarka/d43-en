====== Jeremiah Chapter 36 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why did Yahweh want Jeremiah to write his words on a scroll?

A. Yahweh wanted Jeremiah to write his words on a scroll so that the people of Judah might listen to all the disasters which Yahweh intended to bring on them and turn from their wicked path so Yahweh could forgive their sin. [36:1-3]

Q? What did Jeremiah command Baruch to do?

A. Jeremiah commanded Baruch to take the scroll upon which Baruch had written Jeremiah's dictation and read Yahweh's words from the scroll in the hearing of the people in his house and in the hearing of all of Judea. [36:4-6]
