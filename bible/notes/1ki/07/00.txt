====== 1 Kings 07 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter has a description of the palace Solomon built and the things used in the temple courtyard cast out of bronze and the furnishing inside the temple that were made of gold. It is the last chapter about the building of the temple.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Solomon hired a man from Tyre good at casting thing out of bronze.The bronze objects were made by hollowing out the shape of the object in a clay bank of the river and then pouring in the hot melted liquid bronze and letting it cool.He made two large pillars and a large water reservoir set on the backs of 12 bronze cows. He made all the instruments for use in the sacrifices outside the temple. He made the furnishing inside the temple out of gold.

Solomon spent 13 years building a palace for himself that was much bigger than the temple.
