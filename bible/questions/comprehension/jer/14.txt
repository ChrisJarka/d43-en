====== Jeremiah Chapter 14 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What do the servants do when after searching for water, they return unsuccessful?

A. The servants return unsuccessful; they cover their heads ashamed and dishonored. [14:3]

Q? How do the doe treat their young because there is no grass?

A. The doe leaves her young in the fields and abandons them, for there is no grass. [14:1]

Q? What does Jeremiah ask Yahweh to do though our iniquities testify against us?

A. Though our iniquities testify against us, Yahweh, act for the sake of your name. [14:7]

Q? How does Jeremiah describe the hope of Israel?

A. Hope of Israel, the one who saves Israel in the time of distress. [14:8]
