===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They said to him **- "Then they said to Abraham"
  * **"There, in the tent."** - "She is there, in the tent."
  * **He said, "I will certainly return to you..."**  - "God said, 'I will certainly return to you...'"
  * **when the season comes around**  - "when this season comes around next year, in about a year"
  * **behold** - "make note" or "pay attention." The word “behold” here alerts us pay attention to the surprising information that follows.
  * **in the tent doorway**  - or "at the opening of the tent" or "at the entrance of the tent."
  * **behind him**  - "behind Abraham"
