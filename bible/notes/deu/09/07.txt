===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Remember and do not forget**  - It would be acceptable to translate only "remember" or "do not forget," since they mean the same. (See: [[:en:ta;vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
    * **you provoked**  - In these verses, the word you refers to the entire people of Israel, and even more specifically to their ancestors. (See: [[:en:ta;vol1:translate:figs_youdual]]) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
