====== well ======

===== Definition: =====

A well is a hollow in the ground  that collects water either from rain or underground springs. Very important features of the arid Bible lands, a well could be a deep shaft or a shallow pit. The right to use a well was often a cause of strife and conflict. Water was critical for both people and livestock.
