===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **On the day** - "On some day in the future"
  * **you** - This refers to the Israelite people.
  * **and from the hard labor which you were required to perform** - This is a figure of speech and refers to the Israelites being cruelly treated as slaves. (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  * **you will sing this taunt song against the king of Babylon** - "you will make fun of the king of Babylon by singing a song like this"
