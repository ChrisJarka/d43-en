====== Pentecost, Festival of Weeks ======

===== Facts: =====

Pentecost is the Greek name for the Festival of Weeks which was a Jewish festival that took place fifty days after Passover.

  * <font 13.63636302948px line-height: 17.8181819915772px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The Day of Pentecost was the first day of the Festival of Weeks.</font>
  * <font 13.63636302948px line-height: 17.8181819915772px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The Festival of Weeks was held to celebrate the end of the grain harvest. It was also a time to remember when God first gave the Law to the Israelites through Moses.</font>
  * In the New Testament, the Day of Pentecost is especially significant because it was on this day that God poured out the Holy Spirit on Jesus' followers after Jesus had risen from the dead and gone back to heaven.
