====== chaff ======


===== Definition: =====


====      A thin, light husk that separates from grain and blows away in the wind when tossed into the air. ====


    * Chaff is the unwanted husk from grain.
    * A person scoops grain into a container and throws it up into the air where it can separate from the chaff.
