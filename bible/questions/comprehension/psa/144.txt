====== Psalms Chapter 144 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who trains David's hands for war and his fingers for battle?

A. Yahweh trains David's hands for war and his fingers for battle. [144:1]

Q? How does Yahweh help David?

A. Yahweh is David's covenatn faithfulness, his fortress, David's high tower, the one who rescues David, his shield, the one in whom he takes refuge, and the one who subdues nations under him. [144:2]
