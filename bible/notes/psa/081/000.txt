====== Psalms 081 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 81 is a wisdom psalm. It is meant to be sung at New Moon and Full Moon ceremonies.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

If Israel would obey and worship God, he would destroy their enemies.
