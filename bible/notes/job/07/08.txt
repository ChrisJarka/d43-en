===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Verse 8**  - These are separate sentences or phrases that say the same idea in a different way. It is frequently used in Hebrew poetry for emphasis, for teaching, or to communicate more precisely. (See:<font inherit/inherit;;#FF0000;;inherit>Parallelism</font> )
  * **Sheol**  - AT: "the grave"
  * **As the cloud . . . So he who . . . **- Job is describing death in terms of the vanishing clouds. As the cloud vanishes . . . so he who dies disappears. (See:<font inherit/inherit;;#FF0000;;inherit>Simile</font> )
  * **As a cloud is consumed and vanishes away **- AT: "As a cloud fades away and vanishes" d
  * **he who goes down to Sheol will come up no more **  - explanation
  * **verse 10**  - The second phrase in verse 10 says the same thing as the first phrase in different words. (See <font inherit/inherit;;#FF0000;;inherit>Doublet</font>    )(See also:<font inherit/inherit;;#FF00FF;;inherit>Parallelism</font> )
