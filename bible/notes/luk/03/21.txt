===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **while all the people **- That is, "when all the people." All the people refer to those present with John.
    * **heaven opened up **- That is, "the sky opened up." This is more than a simple clearing of clouds.
    * **dove - **  A small, gentle bird often used in the temple sacrifices and for food. It is similar to a pigeon.
    * **in bodily form like a dove **- That is, "in physical form looking like a dove."
    * **voice**** came out from heaven **- That is, "someone spoke from heaven." It may be necessary here to say, "God spoke from heaven."
    * **my beloved Son**  - That is, "my Son whom I love."
    * **very pleased**- That is, "well pleased in Christ at the time of his entry into ministry." This indicates God's approval.
