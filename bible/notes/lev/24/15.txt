===== translationNotes: =====

  * **You must explain** - Yahweh is speaking to Moses.
  * **must carry his own guilt** - The word "carry" is an idiom meaning "to be responsible for." AT: "is responsible for his guilt" or "must be punished by the people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **must surely be put to death** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The people must surely put him to death" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
