===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the book of the generations of Adam **- This could be translated as "the register of the family line of Adam." The word "generations" is a plural and indicates who and what Adam generated or gave birth to.
  * **the likeness of God **- This does not mean physical likeness, but likeness in man's role as ruler over the creation.
  * **named them Man**  - "Man" is the same word as "Adam."
