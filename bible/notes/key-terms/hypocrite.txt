====== hypocrite, hypocrisy ======


===== Definition: =====


A hypocrite is someone who gives a false appearance of their ideals or beliefs.


    * A hypocrite was the word for an actor in ancient Greece. The reason for this was they showed themselves to the world in a way that was different from reality.
    * Hypocrisy is the pretense of having high ideals or beliefs.
