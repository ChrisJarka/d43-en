====== understand, understanding ======

===== Definition: =====

To grasp the meaning of or to comprehend.

  * Understanding an idea or concept allows a person to act according to the known outcome.
  * Psalm 119 says that "the unfolding of your word gives light," meaning that it causes us to know and understand God's ways.
  * While walking on the road to Emmaus Jesus opened the disciple's minds so they could understand the scriptures pointing to Him as Messiah.

(See also: other)
