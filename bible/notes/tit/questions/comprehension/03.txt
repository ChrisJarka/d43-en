====== Titus Chapter 3 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What should the believer's attitude be toward rulers and authorities?

A. Submission and obedience, being ready for every good work [3:1].

Q? What leads astray and enslaves unbelievers?

A. Their passions and pleasures [3:3].

Q? Through what means did God save us?

A. Through the washing of new birth and renewal by the Holy Spirit [3:5].

Q? Are we saved because of good works which we have done, or because of God's mercy?

A. God's mercy [3:5]

Q? After he justifies us, what does God make us?

A. His heirs [3:7].

Q? What should believers set their minds on?

A. The good works that God puts before them to do [3:8].

Q? What should believers avoid?

A. Foolish debates [3:9].

Q? Who must be rejected after one or two warnings?

A. Anyone who is causing divisions among the believers [3:10].

Q? What must believers set before themselves in order to be fruitful?

A. Doing good works that meet urgent needs [3:14].
