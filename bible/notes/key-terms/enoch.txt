====== Enoch ======

===== Facts: =====

The great-grandfather of Noah.
    * The father of Methusaleh.
    * He had a close relationship with God.
    * When he was 365 years old, he was taken by God.
    * This is not the son of Cain.
    * Mentioned several times in the Old and New Testaments.
