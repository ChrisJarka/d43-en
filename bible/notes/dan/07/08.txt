===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **three of the first horns were wrenched out by the roots**  - AT "the little horn tore out three of the first horns" or "someone tore out three of the first horns" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **a mouth that boasted about great things** - Here “the horn” is speaking as if it were a person. AT: “mouth that is used to speak boastfully” or “the horn spoke boastfully” (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
