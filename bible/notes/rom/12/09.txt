===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Let love be without hypocrisy** - Alternate translation: "Let love be sincere" or "Let love be true." (See: [[en:ta:translate:litotes]])
  * **Concerning love of the brothers, be affectionate to one another**  - "Also, love and be devoted to your fellow believers.
  * **concerning honor, respect one another** - "and give one another honor and respect"
