===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He blessed him**  - Melchizedek blessed Abram.
  * **Blessed be Abram by God Most High, possessor of heaven and earth**  - You can make this active by translating it: "May God Most High, possessor of heaven and earth, bless Abram" (see [[:en:ta:vocabulary:active]]).
  * **Blessed be God Most High, who has delivered** - The phrase "who has delivered" tells us something more about God. You can also translate this: "May people bless God Most High __because__ he has delivered".
  * **Blessed be God Most High**  - This is a way of expressing thanks to God. You can make this active by translating it: "Bless God Most High" or possibly "Praise God Most High".
  * **into your hand**  - "into your control" or "into your power"
  * **a tenth of everything**  - "a tenth part of all of his possessions" or "a tenth part of all his wealth"
