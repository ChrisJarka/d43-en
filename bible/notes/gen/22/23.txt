===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Bethuel became the father of Rebekah**  - This can also be translated as "Later Bethel became the father of Rebekah" or "Later Rebekah was born to Bethuel."
  * **Bethuel…Nahor…Tebah, Gaham, Tahash, and Maacah**  - These are all names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames]])
  * **Rebekah…Milcah…Reumah**  - These are names of women.
  * **These were the eight children that Milcah bore to Nahor, Abraham's brother.**  - "These were Milcah and Nahor's eight children." This refers to the children that were listed in Genesis [[en:bible:notes:gen:22:20|22:21-22]].
  * **eight**  - "8" (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translateunknowns-numbers|]])
  * **His concubine**  - "Nahor's concubine"
  * **also bore**  - "also gave birth to"
