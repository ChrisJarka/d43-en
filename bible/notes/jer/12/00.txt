====== Jeremiah 12 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Jeremiah complains that evil men prosper ==

God answers that if Jeremiah is upset by the people of Anathoth, how will he face the enemies in Jerusalem.

== Other nations ==

If the other nations who have been against Israel leave their idols and worship God, God will bring them back to their lands.
