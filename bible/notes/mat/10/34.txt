===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to tell his apostles about the persecution they will endure as they work for him.

  * **Do not think that **- "Do not suppose that" or "You must not think that"
  * **I came to bring peace upon the earth **- "I came to earth to cause people to live together in peace" (UDB)
  * **a sword **- If "sword" would not be understood, then its figurative meaning of "division" or "disunity" can be used instead, as in the parallel passage of Luke 12:51. (See: Metaphor)
  * **set **- This can also be translated "turn"; literally, it means "divide" or "separate."
  * **a man against his father **- "a son against his father"
  * **A man's enemies**  - "A person's enemies" or "The enemies of a person"
  * **those of his own household **- "members of his own family"
