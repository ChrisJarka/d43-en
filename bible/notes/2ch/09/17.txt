===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the king made a great throne** - The author speaks of commanding his workers to build the throne as if he himself had built it. AT: "the king had his workers make a great throne" or "the king's workers made" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **a great throne of ivory** - "a great throne decorated with ivory"
  * **ivory** - Ivory is the hard, white substance from the tusks or teeth of large animals, such as the elephant or rhinoceros. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **two lions** - These were statues. AT: "two statues of lions" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
