====== sign ======


===== Definition: When used in the Bible, a sign is often a miraculous event which validates the authenticity of a Prophet or spokesperson sent from God. =====


Text


    * The Prophet Elijah's message was validated by a sign from God when fire from heaven consumed a water-drenched offering.
    * Jesus did miraculous signs to show that He was the Messiah.
    * The Apostle's words and ministries were verified by signs (miracles) which followed them.
