===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I stayed on the mountain**  - Moses continues describing what had happened, as he was doing until verse 5, before he added aseparate historical narrative inserted between parenthesis that started in verse 6 and ended in verse 9. This mountain is Mount Sinai.
  * **as the first time **- As the first time Moses had been on Mount Sinai, mentioned in Exodus 24:12, to receive the first two tablets of stone.
  * **they will go in and possess the land**  - This refers to the people of Israel possessing the land of Canaan.
  * **that I swore to their ancestors to give to them -** Yahweh had swore to Abraham, to Isaac and to Jacob that their descendents would possess the land.
