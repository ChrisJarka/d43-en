====== Jeremiah Chapter 19 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What three things did Yahweh tell Jeremiah to do?

A. Jahweh told Jeremiah to go and purchase a potter's clay flask,go out to the valley of Ben Hinnom and proclaim the words Yahweh tells him. [19:1-2]

Q? What does Yahweh tell Jeremiah to tell the kings of Judah and the inhabitants of Jerusalem?

A. "I Yahweh will bring disaster on this place, and make their ears tingle." [19:3]

Q? Why is God bringing this disaster?

A. Because the people have abandoned God and profaned his place, and approached other gods, and their ancestors and fill the place with innocent blood. [19:4]

Q? Who did they build a shrine to and what did God think about that?

A. They built a shirne to Baal which God did not command them to do, nor would he ever consider it. [19:5]

Q? What is Yahweh's declaration?

A. He declares that Topheth will be called the Valley of Slaughter. [19:6]

Q? What does breaking of the clay flask symoblize?

A. The breaking symoblizes the breaking of the people and this city, so that they cannot be repaired. [19:10-11]

Q? What happens on the rooftops?

A. The unclean people worship the stars and pour out drink offerings to other gods. [19:13]

Q? What and why did Jeremiah prophesy in the courtyard?

A. That God will bring this city and its towns disaster because they stiffened their neck and refused to listen to God's words. [19:14-15]
