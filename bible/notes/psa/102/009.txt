===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I eat ashes like bread **  -AT: "I eat ashes as my bread" (See: Simile)
  * **and mix my drink with tears- **  AT: "and what I drink is mixed with my tears."
  * **raging**  - strong and violent
  * **You have lifted me up to throw me down **-(See: Hyperbole) This does not mean the person was phyically lifted up or thrown down
