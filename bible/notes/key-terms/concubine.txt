====== concubine ======

A concubine is a wife of lower rank than a husband's primary wife.

===== Definition: =====

  * Concubines were typically female slaves.
  * In Scripture, the practice of taking a concubine is forbidden.
  * A concubine could be acquired by purchase, through military conquest, or for payment of a debt.
  * A king could have many concubines as a sign of power.
