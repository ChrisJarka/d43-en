===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **bowed low with his face to the ground**  - alternately "fell on his face"
  * **And said in his heart**  - alternately "and thought to himself" or "and said to himself silently"
  * **can a man who is a hundred years old father a child? **- This is a question that does not expect an answer, but acts like a statement (see [[:en:ta:figuresofspeech:rhetoricalquestion]]). You could translate it as a statement: "A man who is a hundred years old cannot father a child."
  * **Can Sarah, who is 90 years old, bear a son?**  - This also is a question that does not expect an answer, but acts like a statement. You could translate it as a statement: "Sarah is ninety years old and cannot bear a son."
