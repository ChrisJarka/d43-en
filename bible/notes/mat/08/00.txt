=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Miracles ==

Jesus' miracles in this chapter appear to emphasize his [[:en:obe:kt:authority|authority]] over things  beyond human control. They also show worship of Jesus is the proper response to a miracle.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

There are many different figures of speech in this chapter. It was also common for Jesus to use analogies and [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|metaphors]] in his teaching. Jesus' way of teaching was intended produce [[:en:obe:kt:faith|faith]] and to be applicable to many different situations.
