====== Micah 03 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter continues to be written in poetic form. Be careful to pay attention to all the figures of speech used.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

Is Micah recognizing cannabalism amongst the Israelites in verse 1-3? No, rather this is a vivid word picture to show how the leaders of the people were treating the Israelites. Please consult the UDB for clarification. 

=== Other possible translation difficulties in this chapter ===

== Michah ==

Note in verse 8, that Micah is speaking about himself being a godly prophet.
