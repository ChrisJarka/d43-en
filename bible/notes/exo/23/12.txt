===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **hired help** - "employed help"
  * **may rest and be refreshed** - AT: "may rest and refresh themselves" or "may rest and feel less tired" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **pay attention ** - "listen well"
  * **nor let their names be heard from your mouth** - AT: "and do not let their names come from your mouth" or "and do not let anyone else hear your mouth say their names" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
