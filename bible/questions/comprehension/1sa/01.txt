====== 1 Samuel Chapter 01 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who were the two wives of Elkanah?

A. The wives of Elkanah were Hannah and Peninnah.[1:1-2]

Q? How many children did Hannah have?

A. Hannah had no children. [1:2]

Q? Who were the priests at the temple where Elkanah worshipped and sacrificed to Yahweh?

A.The two sons of Eli, Hophni and Phinehas were all priests of Yahweh
