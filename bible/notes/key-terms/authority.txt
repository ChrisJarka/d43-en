====== authority ======


===== Definition: =====


Authority is the power of influence, control, or both over someone or something. This term is commonly used in reference to governments or organizations, but it can also be used in reference to one person's power over another person.


    * Governments have power to control the behavior of their citizens by creating laws and regulations.
    * Individuals often exercise "authority" through personal influence and accomplishments.
    * The word "authorities" refers to people, governments, or organization that have authority over others.
