===== translationNotes: =====

  * **These were the leaders David had** - "These were the leaders of David's warriors"
  * **who showed themselves strong with him in his kingdom ... to make him king** - This phrase means that both the leaders of David's army, and the full army, helped David to establish himself as king over Israel. AT: "who strongly supported David's kingdom ... to make him king" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Jashobeam** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **a Hachmonite** - This refers to a person from the Hachmon clan. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **on one occasion** - "in one battle"
