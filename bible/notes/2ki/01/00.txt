====== 2 Kings 01 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

2 Kings is a continuation of 1 Kings.

The story of Elijah continues


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

God alone is the one who controls the future. King Ahaziah sent messengers to ask the god the Philistines worshiped if he would recover from his fall. Elijah sent the messengers back to tell Ahaziah that, because he asked another god instead of Yahweh, he would not recover.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Rhetorical question ==
Elijah rebuked the king with a [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]: "Is it because there is no God in Israel that you are going to consult with Baal Zebub, the god of Ekron?"
