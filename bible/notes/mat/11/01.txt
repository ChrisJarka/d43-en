===== Translation Notes: =====

This begins a new section that moves from Jesus teaching His disciples to what happened next after He left that place.

  * **It came about that**  - This phrase is used to show that this is the beginning of an account. If your language has a way of showing the start of an account, you may use that.
  * **instructing**  - This word can also be translated as "teaching" or "commanding."
  * **his twelve disciples **- This refers to the twelve chosen apostles of Jesus.
  * **he sent by his disciples**  - This refers to John the Baptist sending a message to Jesus by way of John's own disciples.
  * **and said to him **- Jesus is the one being talked to here.
