====== Psalms Chapter 125 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What are those who trust in Yahweh like?

A. They are like Mount Zion, unshakable and forwever enduring. [125:1]

Q? To what does the writer compare the way that Yahweh surrounds his people?

A. The writer compare the way Yahweh surrounds his people is as the mountains surround Jerusalem, now and forever. [125:2]

Q? What must not rule in the land of the righteous?

A. The scepter of wickedness must not rule in the land of the righteous. [125:3]

Q? To whom is the writer requesting that Yahweh do good?

A. He is requesting Yahweh to do good to those who are upright in their hearts. [125:4]

Q? What will happen to those who turn aside to their crooked ways?

A. Those who turn aside to their crooked ways, Yahweh will lead them away with the evildoers. [125:5]

Q? What does the writer say about Israel

A. The writer says may peace be on Israel. [125:5]
