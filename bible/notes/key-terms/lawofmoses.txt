====== Law of Moses ======


==== (Law of Moses, Law of the Lord, God's Law) ====


===== Facts: =====


The Law of Moses refers to the commandments that God gave Moses for the people of Israel to follow. The Law of Moses can also refer to the first five books of the Old Testament called the Pentateuch.


    * God gave Moses this Law at Mount Sinai in the desert.
    * The Law of Moses serves as a guide to reveal mankind's sinful nature.


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:moses|Moses]], [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:tencommandments|Ten Commandments]])
