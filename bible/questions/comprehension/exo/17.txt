====== Exodus Chapter 17 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why did the people blame Moses for their situation?

A. There was no water for the people to drink. So the people blamed Moses for their situation [17:1-2].

Q? What was Moses afraid the people would do to him?

A. Moses was afraid because the Israelites are almost ready to stone him [17:4].

Q? How will water come out?

A. Moses will strike the rock with his staff. Water will come out of it for the people to drink [17:6].

Q? Who will stand on top of the hill?

A. Moses will stand on top of the hill [17:9].

Q?  What happened while Moses was holding his hands up? What happened when he let his hands rest?

A. While Moses was holding his hands up, Israel was winning; when he let his hands rest, Amalek would begin to win [17:11].

Q? How did Aaron and Hur help Moses hold his hands up?

A. Aaron and Hur took a stone and put it under him for him to sit on. At the same time, Aaron and Hur held his hands up, one person on one side of him, and the other person on the other side [17:12].

Q? Why should Moses write about the battle against the Amalek?

A. Moses should write about the battle in a book, because Yahweh will completely blot out the memory of Amalek from under the skies [17:14].
