===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **she was childless**  - "she was unable to conceive a baby"
  * **Rebekah his wife conceived**  - "Rebekah, his wife, became pregnant"
  * **The children struggled together within her**  - "The children struggled within her"
  * **Why is this happening to me**  - ""If it is ok, why is this happening?" or "Is something wrong with this pregnancy?"
  * **She went **- "so she went.", meaning; she set herself apart to pray, or went to an appointed place to pray.
  * **She went to ask Yahweh about this**  - "She went to seek Yahweh"
