====== Ephrathite ======

===== Facts: =====

An Ephrathite is an inhabitant of Ephrathata, which is the old name for Bethlehem in Judah.

  * In the Old Testament days of the Judges Naomi was an Ephrathite.
  * Jesse the father of David was an Ephrathite.
