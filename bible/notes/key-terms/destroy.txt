====== destroy, blot out ======

===== Definition: =====

Destroy: Causing the person or object to be killed as in the flood of Genesis 7.

Blot out:

  - Removing the memory or written record of a person (Deuteronomy 32:26, Revelation 3:5).
  - God completely forgiving a person's sins (Psalm 51).
