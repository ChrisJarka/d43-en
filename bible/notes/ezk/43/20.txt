===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then you will take **- The word "you" refers to the priest
  * **Then you will take some of its blood **- The word “its” refers to the young bull for the sin offering
  * **surrounding**  - near or around someone or something
  * **border**  - the line or part where an object begins or ends
  * **edging **- something that forms an edge or a border
  * **bull**  - a male cow or ox
  * **appointed part**  - "assigned" or "specifically chosen" part (See: [[:en:obe:kt:appoint|appoint]])
