====== nation ======

===== Definition: =====

A nation can be an area of land that is ruled by a king or other form of government.  A nation can also be a people group, like a tribe or ethnic group.

  * In the Bible, there were many nations ruled by kings, Romans, Assyrians, Greeks, and so forth.
  * The nation of Israel is both a governed area of land on the east shore of the Mediterranean Sea, but also the tribes of Abraham.

(See also: other)
