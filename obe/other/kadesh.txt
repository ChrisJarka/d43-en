====== Kadesh ======

===== Facts: =====

Kadesh or Kadesh-Barnea was the name of an important city in Israel's history, located in what is now the country of Syria.

  * The city of Kadesh was an oasis, a place where there was water and fertile soil in the middle of a desert.
  * Moses sent twleve spies into the land of Canaan from Kadesh Barnea.
  * Israel also encamped at Kadesh during the wandering in the wilderness.
  * It was at Meribah Kadesh where Moses disobeyed God and hit a rock to get water for the Israelites, instead of speaking to it as God had told him to do.
