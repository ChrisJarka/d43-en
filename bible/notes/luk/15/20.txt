===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (Jesus continues his parable.)
    * **and he left and came towards his father**  - That is, "he left that country  started going back to his father."
    * **still far away**- That is, "still at a distance."
    * **was moved with compassion**  - That is, "was filled with compassion for him"  or "had pity on him" or "loved him deeply from his heart.
    * **ran and hugged and kissed** - The word for kiss here is an affectionate kiss with strong emotion. It shows the father poured out his love for his son as he kissed him and hugged his neck.
    * **sinned against heaven** - That is "sinned against God."
    * **in your sight**- That is, in your presence.
