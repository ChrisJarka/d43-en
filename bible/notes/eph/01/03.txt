===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **May the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ be praised,**  - This could be worded in the active form. "Let us praise the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ." (See: [[:en:ta:translate:active|Active or Passive]])
  * **he who has blessed us**  - "for he has blessed us".
  * **he **  - This pronoun refers to the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ.
  * **us **  - This is an inclusive pronoun referring to Paul and all the believers at Ephesus. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:exclusive|Exclusive]])
  * **every spiritual blessing **  - "every blessing proceeding from the Spirit of God."
  * **For God chose us who believe in Christ before the creation of the world. He chose us so we could be holy and blameless in his sight, in the beloved one**  - <font 14px line-height: 19px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>"Before God created the world he loved us and chose us to be his people through Christ, who set us apart for God to live blamelessly in his sight." (</font>    UDB<font 14px line-height: 19px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>)</font>    This is a doublet that states in two ways that God is the one who chose us to be his people. This idea is consequently reinforced. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:Doublet]])
