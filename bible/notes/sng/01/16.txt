===== translationNotes: =====

  * **See** - "Listen carefully" or "What I am about to say is important" You could use a word in your language that tells the hearer to listen carefully.
  * **handsome** - Use the word in your language that describes a good-looking man.
  * **The lush plants** - plants that are green, moist, and grow abundantly
  * **serve as our bed** - "are what we lie down on to sleep"
  * **The beams of our house are cedar tree branches** - The woman describes the forest as though it were a house in which they were lying down. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **beams** - large logs that support the rafters of the house
  * **and our rafters are fir branches** - The woman describes the forest as though it were a house in which they were lying down. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **rafters** - pieces of wood that hold up the roof of a house
