===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Areopagus**- The supreme court of Athens. It may or may not have met on the hill Areopagus which overlooked Athens
  * **you bring some strange things to our ears**-Paul's teaching was new and sounded strange to the people of Athens.
  * **therefore, we want to know what these things mean**  -This could also be translated as “We wish to make a judgment on what is being claimed these things are.”
  * **strangers-**"foreigners" or "aliens" or a "new person to the Athenian community"
  * **spent their time-**"used their time" or "devoted their time" or "dedicated their time"
  * **telling or listening about something new**-"loved to discuss new philosophical ideas" or "loved to talk about what was new to them" (UDB)
