====== truth ======

===== Definition: =====

Truth is the factual and correct quality of words or statements.

  * The opposite of truth is falsehood or lie.
  * Jesus said "I am the truth" and God's word is truth.  Truth is all that God is and all that God says in his word, the Bible.
  * Particularly used in the Bible of divine truth

(See also: other)
