====== Herod Antipas ======


===== Facts: =====


Herod Antipas was the Roman ruler of Judea during the time of Jesus. He ruled one-fourth of the Roman Empire, and people sometimes called him king.


    * Herod Antipas ordered John the Baptist to be killed by beheading.
    * There are several people named "Herod" in Scripture.  "Herod" also known as "Herod the Great" was the father of Herod Antipas.
