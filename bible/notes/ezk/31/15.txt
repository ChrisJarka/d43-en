===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh **- See how you translated this in 5:5.
  * **He went down - **"Pharaoh (or Egypt) went down". God begins speaking to Pharaoh in 31:2, and God is still talking to Pharaoh/Egypt.
  * **languished**  - This means the trees <font inherit/Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;;inherit;;rgb(255, 255, 255)>continued for a long time in an unpleasant or unwanted situation.</font>
