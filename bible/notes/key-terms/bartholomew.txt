====== Bartholomew ======

===== Facts: =====

Bartholomew was one of Jesus' twelve apostles.

  * Bartholomew was introduced to Jesus by Philip.
  * Bartholomew's ministry was mostly in the east.
  * Bartholomew was a firsthand witness of Jesus' ascension to heaven.
