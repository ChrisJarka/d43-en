====== Job Chapter 35 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What does Elihu ask Job about how he compares himself to God?

A. Elihu asks Job if he thinks that he is more righteous than God. [35:1-2]

Q? What does Elihu tell Job and his friends to look at?

A. Elihu tells Job and his friends to look up at the sky and see it. [35:4-5]

Q? What does Elihu say could happen to God if Job sinned?

A. Elihu says that if Job sinned what harm would his sin have on God. [35:6]

Q? What effect could Job's wickedness or his rightousness have on another man?

A. Job's wickedness could hurt a man and his righteousness could benefit another son of man. [35:8]
