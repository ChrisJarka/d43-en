===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **by the rivers we sat down- **  The riverside is the usual and popular place to retire to for relaxation and congregating.
  * **poplars**  - This is the same wood Jacob used to make rods to influence the gender of the offspring of sheep and goats.
  * **hung our harps- **  They found no joy in using the harp for singing because of their exile, so they did not play the harps.
