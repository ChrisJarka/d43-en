====== soul ======


===== Definition: =====


The soul is the inner, invisible, and eternal part of a person. It is sometimes used interchangeably with "spirit".


    * Soul refers to the immaterial part of a person including the mind, will and emotions.
    * The soul is a person's "true self" or "inner person."
    * Only humans have souls; there are no references to animals having souls.
    * In the Bible, the word “soul” can refer to a person, his nature, or his eternal existence.
    * The “soul” makes a human being alive. When a person dies, his soul leaves his body.


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:spirit|spirit]])
