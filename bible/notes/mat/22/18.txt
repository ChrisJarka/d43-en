===== Translation Notes: =====
The Pharisees disciples and the Herodians had come to try to trap Jesus.

  * **their wickedness **  - "their evil intentions."
  * **Why are you testing me** - Jesus knows that they are trying to trick him by getting him to say something that would not be acceptable. (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **a denarius** - A Roman coin worth one day's wages. (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicalmoney]])
