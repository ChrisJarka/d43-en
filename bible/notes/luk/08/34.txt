===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **the men who were tending the pigs**  - Or, "those who were taking care of the pigs."
    * **they fled**  - That is, "they run quickly."
    * **reported it in the city and out in the countryside**  - Or, "they told the story in town and around the area out of town."
    * **was clothed**  - That is, "was wearing clothes" or "was dressed."
    * **in his right mind**  - That is, "with the sane mind" or "with normal state of mind" or "behaving normally."
    * **sitting at the feet ****of**  - That is, "beside" or "alongside." Considering the freed man's location, Jesus may have been teaching or talking with his disciples and the man about the demons' departure.
    * **they were afraid**  - That is, "they were scared" or "they were fearful." The fear was probably due to what happened to the man - that he has no more demons - or they feared Jesus because of everything that happened.
