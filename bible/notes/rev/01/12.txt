===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - John begins to explain what he saw in his vision.

  * **whose voice** - This refers to the person speaking. AT: "who" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **a golden belt** - a piece of cloth worn around the waist or across the chest. It may have had gold threads in it.
