====== born again, new birth, spiritual birth ======

===== Definition: =====

Born again, new birth, spiritual birth are all phrases used to describe people returning to the state of mankind before the fall, where we were alive both physically and spiritually.

  * At the fall of Adam and Eve in the Garden the spiritual life of all mankind was put to death.
  * With the death and resurrection on Jesus, God made available a way for mankind to have their spiritual life restored, through faith in Him.
