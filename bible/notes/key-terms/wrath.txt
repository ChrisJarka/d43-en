====== wrath ======


===== Definition: =====


Wrath is strong, furious, and sometimes long-lasting anger or rage. People may show their wrath by punishing the person they are angry with. Sometimes the word "wrath" is used to refer to the punishment itself.

    * God's wrath is the righteous penalty for those who do not repent of their sin.
