====== born again, new birth, spiritual birth ======

===== Definition: =====

Born again, new birth, spiritual birth are descriptions of the process of regeneration.  In regeneration, new life is given to a believer who was previously enslaved to their sin.

  * Regeneration occurs at the time of salvation and only occurs once.
  * Regeneration is a work of God
