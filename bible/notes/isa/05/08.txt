===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you**  - The rich people have cheated the poor people to get their houses and land. "You" refers to these rich people. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:formsofyou-plural|Forms of 'You' - Dual/Plural]])
  * **Lord of Hosts**  - Yahweh, Commander of angel armies
  * **ten acres**  - 10 units of land (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translateunknowns-numbers|Numbers - Translating Unknowns]])
  * **one bath**  - 1 amount of liquid (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translateunknowns-numbers|Numbers - Translating Unknowns]])
  * **one homer**  - 1 amount of dry substance (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translateunknowns-numbers|Numbers - Translating Unknowns]])
