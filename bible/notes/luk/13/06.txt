===== Translation Notes: =====

    * (Jesus continues speaking to the crowd.)
    * **this parable** - That is, "this story." It could also be translated as "this parable about the need for repentance."
    * **And he said to the gardener**  - that is, the landowner said to the person who worked the land, to his employee
    * **Look, for three years**- explanation: "For good three years in sequence or consecutively the vineyard owner has been purposely visiting and checking his garden for a fig tree fruit."
    * **Tried to find**- explanation: "Intentionally and diligently seeking to see or find fig fruit to no avail."
    * **Cut it down** - This could also be translated as, "Take it down" or "remove it"
    * **Why let it waste the ground **- explanation: "No good profitable reason to justify its occupancy of the space or ground"
    * **But he answered** - that is, the gardener, the hired man, answered the landowner by sayingâ€¦
    * **while I dig around it and put manure on it**  - or, "while I irrigate and fertalize it", or "while I work and feed the soil around it"
