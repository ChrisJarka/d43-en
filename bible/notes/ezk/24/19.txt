  * pride
  * lusts
  * soul
  * profaning (profane)
  * sanctuary

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **me, you, I**  - These are pronouns referring to Ezekiel.
  * **us, them**  - These pronouns refer to the people of Israel
  * **house of Israel**  - This means the nation of Israel.
  * **Behold**  - See how you translated this in Ezekiel 24:15.
  * **pride of your strength **- The Lord condemns their pride. They are proud that they are so strong (or so they think).
  * **the desire of your eyes **- The Lord condemns them because they see evil things that they want and because they covet (want) things that belong to others.
  * **the lusts of your soul **- "your sinful desires"
  * **are profaning my sanctuary**  - "are disrespecting God's house" or "disrespect God's house"
  * **sons and daughters who you have abandoned**  - When the Babylonians took Israelites into exile, some of the Israelite children were left behind. These children will die.
