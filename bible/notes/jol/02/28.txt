===== Translation Notes: =====
God continues to promise good things for the people of Israel.
  * **pour out** - pouring water (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **on all flesh** - This stands for all people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **old men will dream dreams** - These are special dreams where God shows images that represent a special message. AT: "old men will dream special dreams"
