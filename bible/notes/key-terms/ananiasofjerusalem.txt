====== Ananias, high priest ======

===== Facts: =====

Ananias of Jerusalem was the high priest during the time of Paul's last trip to Jerusalem.

  * Paul was arrested for disturbing a Jewish crowd in Jerusalem due to his preaching about his encounter with Jesus on the road to Damascus.
  * Ananias had ordered bystanders to slap Paul when the Roman centurion took him to the Jewish council the day after his arrest.
  * Ananias and the council elders later testified against Paul at a hearing with the Governor Felix.
