====== tongue, language ======

===== Definition: =====

The figurative meaning of "tongue" in the Bible means "language." It can also be used to mean "speech."

  * A "tongue" may be a language that is normally spoken by a people <font 14px line-height: 17.8181819915772px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>group</font> on earth or it may be a supernatural language given by the Holy Spirit.
