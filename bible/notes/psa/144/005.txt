===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **cause ... to sink ... come down ... touch ... make ... smoke .. send ... scatter ... shoot ... send** - These are requests, not commands.
  * **Cause the sky to sink** - Possible meanings are 1) bend the heavens like a crossbow with an arrow or 2) tear the sky open (see UDB).
