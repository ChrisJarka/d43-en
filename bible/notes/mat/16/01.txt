===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus arrived at a new place where the Pharisees and Sadducees began to test him.

  * **When it is evening - **  This is the time of day when the sun is setting.
  * **You**  - This is a plural you. "You" could be referring to the Pharisees and Sadducees or it could be referring to people in general in this part of the world.
