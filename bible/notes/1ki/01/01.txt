===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **old and advanced in years** - "very old" The two phrases are similar in meaning and are combined for emphasis. (See: Doublet.)
    * **They covered him** - "His servants covered him"
    * **clothes** - "blankets"
    * **covered him with clothes** - They put many blankets on King David to try to keep him warm.  
    * **Let us look** - "allow us to search"
    * **let her lie in his arms** - The young virgin would lie next to King David to keep him warm, not to have sexual relations.
