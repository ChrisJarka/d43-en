====== drink offering ======

===== Definition: =====

A drink offering was a sacrifice to God that involved pouring wine on the altar creating a pleasant aroma for God.

  * Paul refers to his life as being poured out like a drink offering, meaning his life was totally dedicated to serving God and also he was expecting to be martyred.
  * Jesus' death on the cross was the ultimate drink offering as he spilled His blood on the cross for our sins.
