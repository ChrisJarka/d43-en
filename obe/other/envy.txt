====== envy, covet ======

===== Definition: =====

"Envy" means wanting what someone else has.  "Covet" means to want very much what someone else has.

  * Envy is normally a negative feeling of resentment because of wanting someone's success, good fortune, or possessions.
  * Covet is a strong desire to have someone else's property, even someone's spouse.

(See also: other)
