====== unfaithful ======


===== Definition: =====


An unfaithful person is one who is negligent in their responsibilities and duties.


    * In marriage, someone who commits adultery is "unfaithful" to their spouse.
    * Jesus called his followers to be faithful in every area of their lives, from the insignificant to the substantial.
    * Israel was called "unfaithful" when they began to worship idols.
    * Being the bride of Christ, believers are considered "unfaithful" when they become disbelieving or treasonous to Christ, their bridegroom.


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:urlterm|term]], adultery)
