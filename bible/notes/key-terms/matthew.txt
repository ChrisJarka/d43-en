====== Matthew, Levi ======


===== Facts: =====


Matthew was the name given to the apostle Levi, the son of Alpheus.


    * Matthew means "gift of God."
    * Matthew wrote the gospel account that bears his name.
    * Matthew was a tax-collector from Capernaum.
