====== reproach ======

===== Definition: =====

To reproach someone means to criticize or disapprove of someone.

  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit></font> <font 14px line-height: 1.4;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The noun "reproach" can mean criticism or disapproval; the expression "above reproach" means that there is nothing to criticize about that person.</font>
  * The word "reproach" can also mean "shame" or "disgrace" and these are alternate ways it could be translated.
