====== Shiloh ======

===== Facts: =====

A walled city in the Ephraimite hill country which was taken by Joshua and the Israelites.

  * Before any temple was built in Jerusalem, Shiloh was the place where the Israelites would travel to for the purposes of sacrificing to the Lord and worshipping Him.
  * In Shiloh, Samuel was dedicated to the Lord by his mother Hannah.
