===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **as I have intended, so it will come to pass; and as I have purposed, so it will be** - It can be translated as, "the things that Yahweh has planned will surely happen, as he has said, so it will be." (See: [[en:ta:translate:doublet]])
  * **I will break the Assyrian in my land** - This is referring to the army of Assyria in Yahweh's land of Israel.
  * **Then his yoke will be lifted from off them** - This compares the yoke that holds the neck of animals in place to pull a cart to the yoke of slavery that Assyria placed on the Israelites.  It can be translated as, "My people will no longer be the slaves of the people of Assyria." (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
  * **and his burden from off their shoulder** - This compares slaves carrying heavy burdens on their shoulders to the harsh way that Assyria treated Israel.   It can be translated "Israel will be released from the  bondage and cruel treatment of the Assyrians."
