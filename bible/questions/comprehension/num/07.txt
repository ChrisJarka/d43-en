====== Numbers Chapter 07 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Moses do on the day that the tabernacle and altar were completed?

A. He anointed and sanctified them for Yahweh. [7:1]

Q? What did the leaders of Israel offer as sacrifices?

A. They brought six covered carts and twelve oxen to present in front of the tabernacle. [7:2-3]

Q? Who was Moses to give the offerings to?

A. Moses was to give the offerings to the Levites, to each one as his work needs them. [7:4-5]

Q? Why didn't the descendants of Kohath receive any of the carts and oxen given as offerings?

A. Because they carry the equipment and objects reserved for the tabernacle on their shoulders. [7:6-9]

Q? When was each leader to offer their goods for the dedication of the altar?

A. On their own day as Yahweh had said for them to do. [7:10-11]
