===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **If now I have found favor in your eyes**  - "If you have regard for me" or "Out of respect for me"
    * **wait**  - "Please consider this." "Wait" or "stay" is implied
    * **I have divined**  - "I have discovered by my own spiritual practices"
    * **for your sake**  - "because of you"
    * **Name your wages**  - This can be made more explicit: "Tell me how much I have to pay to keep you here." (See [[:en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]]).
    * **bold words**  - explanation
