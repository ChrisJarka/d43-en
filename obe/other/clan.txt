====== clan ======

===== Definition: =====

Clan is another word for family or relatives. It usually refers to extended family originating from a common ancestor.

  * In the Old Testament, the Israelite tribes were counted according to their clans, or family groups.
  * Clans were normally named after their most well-known ancestor.
  * People were sometimes referred to by the name of their clan.
  * This term could also be translated by "family group" or "extended family" or "relatives."

(See also: family, tribe other)
