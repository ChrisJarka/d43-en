===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they reasoned**  - This may also be translated as "They discussed" or "They considered their answer."
  * **from heaven**  - That is, "from God"
  * **stone us**  - That is, "kill us by throwing stones on us."  God's Law commanded that his people stone those of his people who mocked him.
  * **are persuaded**  - This may also be translated as "believe" or "have come to believe."
