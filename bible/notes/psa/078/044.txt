===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He turned**  - The pronoun "he" in these verses refers to God.
  * **their crops to the grasshopper and their labor to the locust**  - These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: Doublet)
  * **overran**  - AT: "destroyed"
  * **grasshopper**  - This is a plant-eating insect with long legs used for jumping.
  * **He gave their crops to the grasshopper and their labor to the locust**  - The implied information is that the grasshoppers and locusts destroyed their crops. (See: Explicit and Implicit Information)
