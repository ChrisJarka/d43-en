===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It is just as you learned it **- The pronoun "it" refers to the effect or results of the gospel message in both cases.
  * **Epaphras **- In this letter Paul says Epaphras is the one who preached the gospel to the people in Colossae. (See How To Translate Names)
  * **He**- This pronoun is referring to Epaphras.
  * **us **- This pronoun refers to Paul and Timothy, the two writing the letter.
