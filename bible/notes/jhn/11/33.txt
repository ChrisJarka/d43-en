===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he groaned in the spirit **- A groan is a sound made in either pain or sadness.  This phrase describes a prayer made in a sad situation when words cannot fully express what the person who is praying wants to say.
  * **Where have you laid him**  - The word "laid" refers to the tomb Lazarus has been placed in. Alternate translation: "Where is the tomb where you have placed his body?"
