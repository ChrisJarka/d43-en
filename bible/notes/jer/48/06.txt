===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Save your lives** - The word "your" refers to the people of Moab.
  * **become like a juniper bush in the wilderness** - The people fleeing from their cities into the desert is compared to a bush or shrub that grows in the desert. AT: "become like a shrub that grows in the wilderness" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **because of your trust in your practices** - The abstract noun "trust" as a verb. AT: "because you trusted" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **in your practices** - "in your own efforts" or "in your own work" 
  * **you also will be captured** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the enemy army will also capture you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Then Chemosh will go away into captivity** - "Chemosh" is the chief god of the Moabites. This means the enemy army will take the idol of Chemosh that the people made to worship. AT: "The enemy army will take your god Chemosh as a captive" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
