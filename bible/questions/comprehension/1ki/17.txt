====== 1 Kings Chapter 17 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What ominous message did Elijah give to Ahab?

A. Elijah told Ahab that as Yahweh lives, there will not be dew or rain unless I say so. [17:1]

Q? How did Yahweh provide for Elijah by the brook Cherith?

A. There was water from the brook and Yahweh commanded the ravens to feed him bread and meat. [17:2-6]
