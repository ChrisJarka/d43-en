===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **splendor of Chaldean pride** - Babylon was a wealthy and powerful city and its people were very proud of it.
    * **overthrown by God like Sodom and Gomorrah**  - "overthrown by Yahweh like Sodom and Gomorrah" (See: [[:en:ta:translate:simile]])
    * **Arab will not pitch his tent there**  - Arab refers to people who live in tents because they travel from place to place.
