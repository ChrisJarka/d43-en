====== criminal, fugitive ======

===== Definition: =====

A person who has committed a crime (a criminal) and is fleeing from law enforcement personnel.

  * In the Old Testament, if a person accidentally killed someone the killer could flee as a fugitive to one of the established cities of refuge. In this instance the fugitive was NOT a criminal and could live freely inside the city of refuge.
  * The book of Proverbs says that "a person who has murdered will be a fugitive, tormented by guilt until the day he dies."
