===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **and said, "God has given me another child**  - This is the reason that she named the child Seth. This implicit information could be made explicit: "and explained, 'God has given me another child.'"
  * **Seth**  - Translators may add a footnote that says "The name Seth probably means 'has given'."
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **to call upon**  - This is the language of worship and prayer, and could be translated "to invoke."
