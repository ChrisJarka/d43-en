===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **faced** - "across from" or "in front of".
  * **cubits** - (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicaldistances|Biblical distances]]).
  * **the separated side by the way to the west** - This could mean a separate area or yard on the west side
  * **He measured** - The word "he" refers to the man whose appearance was like bronze (40:3)
