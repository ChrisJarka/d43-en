===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **So what?**  - This is a rhetorical question. (See: [[en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]])
    * **Either way, whether in pretense or in truth**  -  Paul is saying that it doesn't matter if people's motives are good or bad. He is glad that they proclaim the gospel.
    * **Christ is proclaimed**  - "the gospel of Christ is preached"
    * **I rejoice in that,**  - The pronoun "that" refers to the preaching of the gospel. "I rejoice because people are preaching the gospel," 
    * **yes indeed,**  - This is an idiom. If you language has a way of emphasizing that you are saying "yes", use it. (See: [[en:ta:translate:idiom[[)
