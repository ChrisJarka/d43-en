===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Turns away from his righteousness **
- This phrase means to stop righteous behavior

  * **Commits **
- This word means to do or complete

  * **Similarly **
- This word means almost the same as.

  * **Will he live? **
- This is a rhetorical question. It is used to emphasize a point. He will live or he will not live. See the rhetorical question.

  * **Treason**
 - this word means to be unfaithful to God or country.
  * **He **
 - This pronoun refers to the referenced sinful man.
  * **Me - ** This pronoun refers Lord Yahweh.
  * **So he will die in the sins that he sins. **- An alternate translation would be "he will die for the sins that he committed."
