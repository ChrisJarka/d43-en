====== synagogue ======


===== Definition: =====


A synagogue is a building where Jewish people assemble to pray. Sometimes there are smaller rooms in the synagogue that are dedicated to the study of the Hebrew Scriptures (the Old Testament.)

    * Jesus often taught in synagogues.
