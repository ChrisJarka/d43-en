====== Jeremiah Chapter 01 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who was Jeremiah, the son of Hilkiah?

A. Jeremiah was a priest. [1:1]

Q? Who was king when the word of Yahweh first came to Jeremiah?

A. The word of Yahweh came to Jeremiah when Josiah was king. [1:2]

Q? When did Yahweh choose Jeremiah to be a prophet?

A. Yahweh chose Jeremiah before Jeremiah was born. [1:5]

Q? Why did Jeremiah say he was not qualified?

A. He said he was very young. [1:6]

Q? What did Yahweh command Jeremiah to say?

A. Yahweh commanded him to say whatever Yahweh commanded him to say. [1:7]

Q? Why should Jeremiah not be afraid?

A. He should not be afraid because Yahweh was going to be with him and rescue him. [1:8]

Q? What did Yahweh place in Jeremiah's mouth?

A. Yahweh placed his word in Jeremiah's mouth. [1:9]

Q? What did Jeremiah see?

A. He saw an almond branch. [1:11]

Q? What will Yahweh do with his word?

A. He will carry it out.

Q? in the second vision, when Jeremiah saw a heated pot, whose surface is churning, tipping away from the north, what did it mean?

A. Yahweh said to him that disaster will be opened up out of the north on all who live in this land. [1:14]

Q? What will all the tribes of the northern kingdom do when Yahweh calls?

A. They will come, and everyone will set up his throne at the entrance of the gates of Jerusalem. [1:15]

Q? What will Yahweh do when they come?

A. He will pronounce sentence against them for all their evil in forsaking him, in burning incense to other gods, and in worshiping what they made with their hands. [1:16]

Q? What will the kings of Judah, its officials, its priests, and the people of the land do against the prophet?

A. They will fight against the prophet, but they will not defeat him, for Yahweh will be with him to rescue him. [1:19]
