===== translationNotes: =====

  * **twenty years old ... sixteen years** - "20 years old ... 16 years" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **what was right in the eyes of Yahweh** - The eyes here represent seeing, and seeing represents judgment. Yahweh saw and did not approve of Ahaz's actions. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:2ch:14:01|14:2]]. AT: "what Yahweh judged to be right" or "what Yahweh considered to be right" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **he walked in the ways of the kings of Israel** - Walking represents behavior and actions. AT: "he acted the same way that the kings of Israel had acted" or "he did the things that the kings of Israel had done" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
