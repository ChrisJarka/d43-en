===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **while he was still speaking v16**- "he" is referring to the first messenger.
  * **another also came**  - "another" is referring to another messenger
  * **fire of God**  - see: metonymy
  * **from the heavens**- heavens is being used to refer to the sky
  * **while he was still speaking v17**
 - "he" is referring to the secont messenger.
  * **formed three groups**  - this may have been a strategic tactic that theives would have used.
