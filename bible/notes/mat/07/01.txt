===== Important Terms: =====

  * judge

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Judge not **- "do not judge." Jesus is speaking to His disciples.
  * **so that you may not**  - The "you" in these verses refers mainly to the disciples, but other people in the crowd may have been listening.
  * **For with the judgement you judge - "**If you condemn other people" (UBD)
  * **you will be judged -"**God will condemn you" (UDB)
  * **it will be measured to you -**  this can be stated in active form: "God will do the same to you."
