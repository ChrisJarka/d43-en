===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I said**  - I refers to Nehemiah
  * **to the nobles and the officials **- these are the officers referred to in verse 16.
  * **The work is great and extensive**  - AT: "The task of rebuilding this wall is a big task and will take a while to complete"
  * **You must rush to the place where you hear the trumpet sound and assemble there. **- AT: "At the place where you hear the sound of the trumpet, there you should gather together unto us."
