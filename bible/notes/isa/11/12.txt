===== Translation Notes: =====

  *  **He will set up a banner for the nations** - Yahweh will get the attention of all people. His presence will be as obvious as a banner that is raised high.
  * **will gather the outcasts of Israel and the dispersed of Judah** – “will gather the people taken from Israel and the scattered people of Judah"
  * **from the four corners of the earth** - "from all parts of the world”
  * **He will stop the envy of Ephraim, and those hostile to Judah will be cut off.  Ephraim will not envy Judah, and Judah will no longer be hostile to Ephraim** - Ephraim refers to the Northern Kingdom and Judah to the Southern Kingdom.  Messiah will bring peace to Ephraim and Judah.  He will end envy and hostility.
