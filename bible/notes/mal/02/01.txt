===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **And now,you priests, this command is for you.** -"Listen you priests, this warning is for you."
  * **"If you will not listen, and if you will not take it to heart to give glory to my name" says Yahweh of Hosts,**-"If you do not heed my warning and do not make it very important to give praise to my name," says Yahweh of Hosts"
  * **take it to heart**-This is another way of saying "make it very important." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **"then I will send a curse on you, and I will curse your blessings.**-"then I will send a curse on you, and  stop the blessings you are already receiving."
  * **Indeed, I have cursed them already, because you do not take my command to heart.**- "In fact, I have already begun to turn your blessings into curses because you do not think my command is important."
