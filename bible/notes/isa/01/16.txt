===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Wash, cleanse yourselves**  - This can be translated as: “wash your inner being and become clean from sin.” (See: [[en/ta/translate:idiom|Idiom]])
  * **my** - This refers to God.
  * **the oppressed** - "people who are treated cruelly"
