===== Translation Notes: =====

  *  **Lehi **- Translate this place the same way you translated it in  [[en:bible:notes:jdg:15:09|15:9]].
  * **Yahweh's spirit came on him in power** - The phrase "came on him" refers to help that Yahweh gave Samson. AT: "Yahweh's spirit added powerful strength to Samson"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **ropes that were on his arms became like burnt flax** - The ropes are compared to burnt flax because they were very easy for Samson to break. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
