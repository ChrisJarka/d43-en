====== justify ======


===== Definition: =====


The term Justify in the Bible is an action whereby God declares a person to be free of sin; as if they never sinned.


    * God declared Abraham to be justified when he believed what God told him concerning the birth of Isaac.
    * In the New Testament God justifies those who believe in Jesus Christ for forgiveness of sins.
    * A legal term used to declare that a penalty is no longer valid.
