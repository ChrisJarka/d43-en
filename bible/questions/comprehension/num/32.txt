===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

Q? Why did the descendants of Reuben and Gad want the land of Jazer and Gilead to be given to them as a possession?

A. The descendants of Reuben and Gad wanted the land of Jazer and Gilead to be given to them because it was  a wonderful place for livestock. [32:1-5]
