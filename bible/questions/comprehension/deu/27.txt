====== Deuteronomy Chapter 27 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Moses and the elders of Israel command the people of Israel to do?

A. Moses and the elders of Israel told the people, "Keep all the commandments that I command you today." [27:1]

Q? What must the people of Israel do after they pass over the Jordan to the land that Yahweh would give them?

A. When the people of Israel pass over the Jordan, they must set up some large stones with plaster and write on them all the words of the law. [27:2-4]

Q? What must the people of Israel build on Mount Ebal?

A. The people of Israel must build an altar of stone to Yahweh without using iron tools to offer burned offerings to him. [27:5-7]
