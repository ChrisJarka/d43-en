===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now in the assembly in Antioch ** - "At that time in the church at Antioch"
  * **foster-brother of Herod ** - Manaen was probably Herod's playmate or close friend growing up. 
  * **Set apart for me** - "Appoint to serve me" or "sanctify"
  * **assembly** - "congregation" or "group of believers"
  * **laid their hands on these men** - "laid their hands on those set apart for God's service" as a symbol of commissioning those men as representatives of the group
  * **sent them off** - "sent those men" to fulfill their commission
