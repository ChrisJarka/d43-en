===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **cistern** - a deep hole for storing water
  * **Malchijah** - See how you translated this name in [[en:bible:notes:jer:38:01|38:1]].
  * **There was no water in the dungeon** - Here the word "dungeon" is used because the cistern was like a dark underground prison.
