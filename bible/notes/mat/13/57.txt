===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of how the people of Jesus' home town rejected him when he taught in their synagogue.

  * **They were offended by him**  - This can be translated "They took offense at him." This sentence can be translated with an active verb: "They would not accept him." (See: Active or Passive)
  * **They **- The pronoun "they" refers to the people of Jesus' home town or region.
  * **A prophet is not without honor**  - "A prophet is not dishonored"
  * **his own country **- "his own region" or "his own home town"
  * **his own family**  - "his own home"
  * **And he did not do **- The pronoun "he" is referring to Jesus.
  * **many miracles there **- "There" refers to Jesus' home town or region.
