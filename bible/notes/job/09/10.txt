===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **marvelous things without number **- express the idea that God's marvelous works are unable to be counted.
  * **he passes on also, but I do not perceive him **- "God moves by but I do not realize it."
  * **If he catches a victim**  - clarify "catches a victim" with something like "if he wants to take someone away"
