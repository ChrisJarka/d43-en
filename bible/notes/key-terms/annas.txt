====== Annas ======

===== Facts: =====

Annas was high priest from approximately AD 6 to 15.

  * Removed as high priest by the Roman government.
  * Continued to be an influential leader.
  * Father-in-law to Caiaphas, the high priest during the ministry of Jesus.
  * Annas and Caiaphas are mentioned together.
