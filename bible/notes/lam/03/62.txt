===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The lips and the accusations ... come against me all the day** - The author speaks of his enemies accusing him all day as if their accusations were soldiers that come to attack him all day. AT: "My enemies speak against me and accuse me through the whole day" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **The lips ... of my enemies** - Here the lips represent what his enemies say. AT: "The words ... of my enemies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **how they sit and then rise up** - These two actions together represent everything the people do. AT: "everything they do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
