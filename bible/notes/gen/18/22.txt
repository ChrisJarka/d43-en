===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **turned from there**  - "they went out from Abraham's camp"
  * **UDB: Abraham kept standing in front of Yahweh, ULB: Abraham remained standing before Yahweh**  - "Abraham and Yahweh remained together"
  * **UDB: ****people who have done nothing wrong**  - "righteous people;" righteous people are those whose heart is committed to following the instruction of the Lord. It isn't an indication of sinlessness.
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **approached**  - "approached Yahweh" or "stepped closer to Yahweh"
  * **sweep away**  - "destroy"
