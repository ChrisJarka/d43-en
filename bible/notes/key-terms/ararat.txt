====== Ararat ======

===== Facts: =====

In the Bible, "Ararat" is known as a land, a kingdom and a mountain range.

  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The location of the "land of</font> Ararat"<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>was probably in the northeastern part of what is now the country of Turkey.</font>
  * Ararat is most well-known as the name of the mountains that Noah's ark <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>came to rest on</font>    <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>after the waters of the great flood began to recede.</font>
