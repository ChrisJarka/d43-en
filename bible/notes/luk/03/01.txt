===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **in the fifteenth year of the reign of**** Tiberius Caesar **- That is, "when Tiberius had been ruling as emperor for about fifteen years."
    * **during the high priesthood of Annas and Caiaphas**  - That is, "when Annas and Caiaphas were high priest."
    * **during the high priesthood**-It appears Annas and Caiaphas were acting jointly or together as high priests even though God had ordained that only one serve. Could be translated "were high priests."
    * **the Word of God came to John **- That is, "God spoke to John" as he had spoken to the prophets in Israel's past or, "God gave John his message."
    * **in the wilderness**  - That is, "while John was out in the desert" or, "while John was living in the desert.
