====== Jeremiah Chapter 21 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Pashhur and Zephaniah ask Jeremiah?

A. They asked him to seek advice from Yahweh ,hoping Yahweh will do miracles for them. [21:1-2]

Q? What is the message that Jeremiah give Zedekiah?

A. Yahweh says he will fight against you with a raised hand and with wrath, fury and great anger. [21:3-5]
