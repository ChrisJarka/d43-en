===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Jesus continues his parable of the talents that highlights the kingdom of heaven.)
  * **You wicked and lazy servant**  - "You are a wicked and lazy servant"
  * **wicked **- "evil"
  * **lazy **- "idle" or "don't want to do any work"
  * **I reap where I have not sowed, and harvest where I have not scattered **- “I harvest crops where another person sowed seed, and I gather grain where another person scattered seed” or “I gather garden food from gardens where someone else sowed the seed, and I collect garden food in fields where someone else scattered the seed”
  * **I reap where I have not sowed, and harvest where I have not scattered **- These are two parallel statements giving the same basic meaning in two different ways. (See: Parallelism or Doublet)
  * **scattered**  - The implied information is that seeds were scattered or sown.
  * **bankers **- These are persons or businesses who keep money for others. The bankers have use of the money while being kept by the banker, and the owner of the money will receive their money back with interest.
  * **received back my own **- "received back my own money"
  * **with interest **- "with some more money as well"
