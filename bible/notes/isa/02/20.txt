===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **to the moles and bats** - The word "moles" refers to small animals that dig and live underground. And, "bats" refer to small flying animals that hunt at night and sometimes live in caves. Alternate translation: "to the animals." (See: [[en:ta:translate:translateunknowns]])
  * **from before the terror of Yahweh and from the glory of his majesty** - See how this was translated in [[en:bible:notes:isa:02:09|2:10]].
  * **when he rises to terrify the earth** - See how this was translated in [[en:bible:notes:isa:02:17|2:19]]).
  * **whose life-breath is in his nostrils** - This refers to humans being mortal. Alternate translation: "who are mortal and will die." (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  * **for what does he amount to** - Isaiah uses a question to scold his audience for trusting in the humans. Alternate translation: "Man amounts to nothing!" (See: [[en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]])
