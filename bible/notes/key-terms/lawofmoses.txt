====== Law of Moses ======


===== Facts: =====


The Law of Moses refers to the commandments that God gave Moses at Mount Sinai. The law of Moses can also refer to the first five books of the Old Testament called the Pentateuch.


    * The Law of Moses was delivered originally at Mount Sinai in the desert.
    * The Law was given a second time after Moses destroyed the original tablets when he observed the golden calf.
    * The Law of Moses serves as a guide to reveal mankind's sinful nature.
