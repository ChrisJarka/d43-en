===== Translation Notes: =====

    * (Jesus speaks to the crowds of people.)
    * **with him, and he turned to them,**  - That is, "with Jesus, and Jesus turned to them." You could also follow this with "and said" or "and said to them."
    * **hate**  - That is, "love less" or "esteem less."
    * **yes, and**  - "even to the point of…"; in this verse, "…even to the point of dying for me…"
    * **carry his own cross**  - figerative phrase meaning "willing to die"; a disciple must be willing to die for Christ's treachings if necessary. This could also be translated, "carry the means of his own death or execution."
