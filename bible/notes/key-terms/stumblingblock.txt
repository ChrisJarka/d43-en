====== stumbling block ======

===== Definition: =====

"Stumbling block" is a barrier.

  * A stumbling block can be a snare or trap that may cause one to fall away or fail.
  * Figuratively, a "stumbling block" is an obstacle either preventing someone from having faith in Jesus or causing someone to be hindered in their spiritual growth.

(See also: other)
