===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **commit adultery**  - Yahweh is saying that he is like a husband and the Israelites are like his wife. If they worship other gods, it will be like they committed adultery. AT: "be unfaithful", "make…worship their gods" (UDB). (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **mold**  - A “mold” is a special container that is used give shape to an object. To mold something means to put it (usually as a liquid) in one of these containers, where it will cool and harden.
