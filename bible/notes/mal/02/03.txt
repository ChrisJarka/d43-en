===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **and I will spread dung on your faces**-This is another way of saying "I will most certainly treat you with disrespect" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **and you will be taken away with it.**-This is another way of saying and you will be punished.  See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **And you will know that I have sent this commandment to you, - **"Then you will know for sure that I have sent this warning to you,"
  * **that my covenant may be with Levi," -**  "so that my covenant with the Levites can continue,"
