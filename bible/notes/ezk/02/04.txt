===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **stubborn faces **- This phrase suggests the people's unwillingness to change.
  * **hard hearts**  - This phrase suggests the people's resistance to God and their unwillingness to hear or to love him.
  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh** - "This is the message God gives you.
