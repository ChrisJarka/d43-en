===== translationNotes: =====

  * **from the hand of Sennacherib ... from the hand of all others** - Here "hand" represents power or control. AT: "from the power of Sennacherib ... from the power of all others" or "from Sennacherib ... from all others" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **gave them rest on every side** - The abstract noun "rest" can be as "peacefully." AT: "caused them to live peacefully with all the people of the nations around them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **he was lifted up in the eyes of all nations** - Here "lifted up" is an idiom that means to honor. Also, "in the eyes" is a metaphor that represents judgment or evaluation. AT: "the people of all the nations honored him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
