====== right hand (of God) ======

===== Definition: =====

The right hand is an expression that refers to a person who is most trusted, or a person's place of honor next to a ruler or other important individual. It means "on his right side" or "beside him to the right".

  * The Bible describes Jesus as sitting at the right hand of God the Father as the head of the body of believers (the church) and in control as ruler of all creation.
