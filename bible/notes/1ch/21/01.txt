===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **adversary**  - This word may be translated "adversary", "The Adversary", or "Satan" (see: UDB).
    * **incited David**  - That is, he made David think that this was a good plan.
    * **from Beersheba to Dan**  - The Israelites considered these two cities their most southern and most northern cities; thus it means all the land of Israel. (See: Merism)
    * **do they not all serve my lord? Why does my lord want this? Why bring guilt on Israel?**  - Joab asks these questions to show David that his plan is unnecessary, is wrong before God, and will have bad results. (See: UDB; see: Rhetorical questions)
    * **Why does my Lord want this?**  - "This" refers to David's plan to count all the men of Israel.
