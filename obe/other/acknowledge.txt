====== acknowledge ======

===== Facts: =====

The term "acknowledge" means to give proper recognition to something or someone.

  * To acknowledge God also involves showing acceptance that what he says is true.
  * People who acknowledge God will show it by obeying God, which brings glory to his name.
  *
