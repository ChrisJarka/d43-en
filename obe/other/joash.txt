====== Joash ======

===== Facts: =====

Joash was the name of several men in the Old Testament.

  * When Gideon destroyed the altars of idols that the Israelites had been worshiping, his father Joash defended and supported him.
  * Many years later, a different man named Joash became king of Judah at the age of seven. He was the son of Ahaziah, king of Judah, who had been murdered. 
  * As a very young child, the aunt of Joash saved him from being killed by the evil Queen Athaliah, by hiding him away until he was old enough to be crowned king.
  * The prophet Elisha foretold that King Joash would defeat the Syrian King Ben-Hadad.
