====== 2 Chronicles Chapter 36 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? After Josiah died, who became king of Jerusalem?

A. The people of the land made Josiah's son, Jehoahaz, king and he reigned for three months. [36:1-2]

Q? How did Eliakim become king of Judah and Jerusalem?

A. His brother, the king of Egypt, removed Jehoahaz after three months and made Eliakim king, changing his name to Jehoiakim. [36:3-4]

Q? What kind of ruler was he in his reign of eleven years?

A. Jehoiakim was evil in the sight of Yahweh. [36:5]

Q? What did King Nebuchadnezzar, of Babylon, do to the king of Judah and Jerusalem?

A. He attacked him, bound him in chains, took him and some of the objects in the house of Yahweh to Babylon. [36:6-7]

Q? Who became king after Jehoiakim?

A. His son, Jehoiachin. [36:8]

Q? What kind of king was he?

A. In his short reign, he did evil in the sight of Yahweh. [36:9]

Q? How did King Nebuchadnezzar treat this king?

A. He brought him to Babylon along with more valuable things from the house of Yahweh. He named Zedekiah king over Judah and Jerusalem. [36:10]

Q? What did his eleven year reign look like?

A. He did evil in the sight of Yahweh. He did not humble himself before the prophet Jeremiah. [36:11-12]

Q? How did Zedekiah rebell against King Nebuchadnezzar?

A. He hardened his heart against Yahweh and turned away from King Nebuchadnezzar. [36:13]
