===== translationNotes: =====

  * **elders** - Here this means the men who lead, participating in matters of social justice and in religious matters such as maintaining the law of Moses.
  * **leader and commander** - These two words share similar meanings. They emphasize how important Jephthah became. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **When he was before Yahweh** - AT: "When he was at a special place where people worship Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
