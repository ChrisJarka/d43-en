===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **39:14a–"They"**refers the people (or leaders) of Israel.
  * **39:14b,15–"They"**  refers to "men set apart."
  * **39:15–"it"**  refers to the land.
  * **39:14 set apart men with the vocation- **  This means men designated for a special job/function.
  * **39:14,15 monument–**  marker seems the better word.
  * **39:15,16 Valley of Hamon-Gog **  –This is the name of a place. (See How to Translate Names)
  * **39: 16  Hamonah - **is the name of a city which means "hugh army"
