===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then a sword will come against Egypt **- The word "sword" refers to "war" or "an attack." Alternate translation: "Then Egypt will experience war" or "Egypt will be attack." (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  * **there will be anguish in Cush** â€“ "Cush will be very sad" or "Cush will be full of grief"
  * **when the slain fall - ** This is a reference to the time of death for those who are killed. Alternative translation: "when the dead are killed."
  * **her foundations are ruined **- This is likely a reference to the military power of Egypt being destroyed.
