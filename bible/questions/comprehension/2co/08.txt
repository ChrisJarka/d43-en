====== 2 Corinthians Chapter 08 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Paul want the Corinthian brothers and sisters to know?

A. Paul wanted them to know about the grace of God that was given to the churches of Macedonia [8:1].

Q? During a great test of affliction and even though the churches of Macedonia were extremely poor, what did they do?

A. Of their own free will they generously gave to the ministry of the saints as much as they were able and even beyond what they were able [8:2-4].
