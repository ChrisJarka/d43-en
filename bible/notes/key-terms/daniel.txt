====== Daniel ======

===== Facts: =====

Daniel was an Old Testament prophet who was taken captive into Babylon during Israel's captivities.

  * Daniel was promoted within Babylon when he correctly interpreted King Nebuchadnezzar's dream.
  * While in the Babylonian captivity Daniel was given the name Belteshazzar to reflect Babylon's gods.
  * Daniel was thrown into a lion's den because he refused to bow and worship the king of Babylon. God delivered Daniel from the lions.
