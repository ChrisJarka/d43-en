===== Translation Notes: =====


     * **you**  - English does not use formal and informal forms of “you.”   In this verse you is plural and neuter. It refers to all Christians.    (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]]) 
     * **the one who is speaking** - Refers to the sprinkled blood of Jesus that speaks in verse 24.
    * **they**  - The word “they” refers to the people of Israel. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_123person]])
    * **if they did not escape**  - "escape judgment" (See: [[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:discourse:implicit-explicit]])
    * **we**  - The word “we” refers to the person being spoken and to every follower of Jesus.  (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
    * **the one who warned them on earth**  - The warning on earth was the Law of Moses received at Mount Sinai.
    * **the one who warns from heaven**  - The author continues comparing an earthly father's discipline to the Heavenly Father's discipline.
