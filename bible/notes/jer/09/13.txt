===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking about the people of Judah.

  * **It is because** - "These things will happen because" (UDB) 
  * they have abandoned my law** - Yahweh speaks of the people not obeyed his law as if it was something that they had walked away from. AT: "they have rejected my law" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **they do not listen to my voice** - Here Yahweh's "voice" represents what he says. AT: "they do not pay attention to the things I tell them." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **or walk by it** - Here "walk" is an idiom for "live." AT: "or live the way I tell them to live" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **they have walked by their stubborn hearts** - Here the people "stubborn hearts" represent their stubborn desires and stubborn will. Also, here "walked" represented living. AT: "they have been stubborn and lived the way they want to live" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **and have followed the Baals** - "and have worshiped the Baals"
