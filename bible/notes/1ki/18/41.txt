===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **there is the sound of much rain** - Here "sound" represents Elijah's prediction that rain is coming soon. 
    * **Ahab went up to eat** - We are not told specifically where Ahab went to eat, just that he went somewhere, probably nearby, to eat.
    * **bowed himself down ... put his face between his knees** - This position suggests kneeling in humility and an attitude of prayer.
