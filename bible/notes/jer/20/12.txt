===== translationNotes: =====

  * **who see the mind and the heart** - The word "heart" is a metonym for what a person feels and desires, and the word "mind" is a metonym for what a person thinks and decides. AT: "who know a person's feelings and thoughts" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **those who are oppressed from the hand of evildoers** - Here the word "hand" represents power. This can be stated in active form. AT: "those whom evildoers oppress with their power" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
