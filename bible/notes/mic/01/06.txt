===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I will make** - Here "I" refers to Yahweh.
  * **will be crushed to pieces ... will be burned up** - "I will crush to pieces ... I will burn up" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **I will pull her building stones** - Here "her" refers to the city of Samaria.
  * **For from the gifts to her prostitution she gathered them, and as a prostitute's pay they will return** - Often worshiping idols included sleeping with a prostitute at the pagan temple. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
