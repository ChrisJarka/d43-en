====== Zechariah (OT) ======

===== Facts: =====

The name Zechariah was a very common name in the Old Testament.  Among the most significant are:

  * A prominent gatekeeper at the temple during the time of David (1 Chronicles 26:2,14).
  * A son of King Jehoshaphat who was murdered by his brother Jehoram (2 Chronicles 21:2).
  * A son of the priest Jehoiada who was stoned by the people when he called them to account for their apostasy (2 Chronicles 24:20).
  * A king of Israel, son of Jeroboam II, murdered after only six months on the throne (2 Kings 14:29).
  * One of twelve minor prophets, he prophesied about 520-518 B.C. during the reign of Persian kings Darius I.  His prophecy urged the returning exiles to rebuild the house of the Lord.  His work overlapped that of the prophet Haggai.
