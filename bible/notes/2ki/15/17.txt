===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Azariah, king of Judah** - Azariah was also known as "Uzziah."
  * **He did what was evil in the sight of Yahweh** - AT: "He did many things that Yahweh said were evil." (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]]) 
  * **by which he had caused Israel to sin** - "sins that caused the people of Israel to sin"
