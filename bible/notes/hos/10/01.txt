===== Translation Notes: =====

Hosea is speaking about Israel.

  * **Israel is a luxuriant vine that produces his fruit.**  - Israel is compared to a vine that is very fruitful - but the fruit has led him away from Yahweh (as seen in __09:10__). (see: Metaphor)
  * **As his land produced more, he improved his sacred pillars.**- //As his land produced more, he improved his sacred pillars//  can be made explicit: "With that money they made more stone columns in honor of their idols." (UDB) (see: Explicit and Implicit information)
  * **Their heart is deceitful;**  - This means that Israel is being dishonest toward Yahweh by their worship of idols. (see: Metonymy)
  * **now they must bear their guilt. **- //Now they must bear their guilt//  can be made explicit: "but the time has come that they must pay for their sins." (UDB) (see: Explicit and implicit information)
