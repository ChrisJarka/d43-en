===== Translation Notes: =====

  * God is using Ezekiel's actions to warn the Israelite people about their sin.
  * **house of Israel against it**  - The word "it" refers to Ezekiel's carving of the siege against Jerusalem.
  * **house of Israel**  - "Northern kingdom of Israel" (See Metonymy)
  * **lifted up upon it **- "shown against it"
  * **their sins will be lifted up upon it**  - "God will show their sins on it" or "God will put their sins on it" (See: Active/Passive)
  * **their sins will be lifted up upon it**  - The word "it" refers to Ezekiel's picture of Jerusalem
  * **lift up **- "show" or "present"
