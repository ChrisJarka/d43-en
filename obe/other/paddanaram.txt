====== Paddan-aram ======

===== Facts: =====

The homeland of the patriarchs, including the city of Haran, Paddan-aram was located probably in what is now southcentral Turkey.

  * The language of the population was Aramaic.
  * Those who remained after the patriarchs departed were known as Arameans.
