===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **A psalm of David** - Possible meanings are 1) David wrote the psalm or 2) the psalm is about David or 3) the psalm is in the style of David's psalms.
  * **sons of the mighty** - AT: "mighty men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **that his name deserves** - The phrase "his name" refers to Yahweh or his reputation. AT: "as he deserves" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]}
  * **that is suitable** - "that is right"
