===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **came to me - **  Alternate translation - "told me" or "gave me" (UDB).
  * **son of man - **  In the book of Ezekiel, this phrase usually refers to Ezekiel.
  * **and declare **- Alternate translation - "and tell them" or "say to them" (UDB).
  * **Your beginning and your birth took place in the land of Canaan; your father was an Amorite, and your mother was a Hittite.**  - This summarizes Israel's heritage.
