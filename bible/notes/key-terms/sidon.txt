====== Sidon ======


===== Facts: =====


Sidon was the firstborn son of Canaan and great-grandson of Noah. Sidon is also a city, named after its founder.


    * The City of Sidon was outside of Israel.
    * The City of Sidon exists today, and is located 40 kilometers south of modern-day Beirut on the Mediterranean coast.
