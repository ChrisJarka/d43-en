===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **in the midst of them**  - That is, "where they could all see him."
    * **Peace be to you** - "you" is plural.
    * **they were terrified and filled with fear** - "They were startled and afraid". 
    * **supposed that they saw a spirit** - They had yet to believe in their heart & mind that Jesus was indeed alive.
