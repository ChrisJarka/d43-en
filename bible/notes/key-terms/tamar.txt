====== Tamar ======

===== Facts: =====

Tamar was a daughter of King David and is listed in the New Testament as a human ancestor of Jesus Christ.

  * There are two other women in the Old Testament named Tamar.
  * Tamar is also a location mentioned in the book of Ezekiel.
