====== Leviticus Chapter 21 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What must happen to a priest's daughter who defiles herself by become in prostitute?

A. A priest's daughter who defiles herself by becoming a prostitute must be burned. [21:9]

Q? What must a high priest avoid even if it his father or mother?

A. A high priest must not go any where there is a dead body present even if it is his father or mother. [21:11]

Q? What kind of woman must the priest not marry?

A. The priest must not marry a widow, a divorced woman, or a woman who is a prostitute. [21:14]

Q? What man does Yahweh not want to approach him to perform the offerings?

A. Yahweh does not want any man with a bodily defect to approach him. [21:18]
