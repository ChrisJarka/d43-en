===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **what are they like?**  - Jesus here begins an extended simile.
    * **children**-This word generally defines a child in training from infancy up to about 8 years old.
    * **played a flute**  - a flute is a long, hollow musical instrument which is played by blowing air in or over one end of it.
    * **playing in the marketplace**- In their culture, marketplaces were large, open air areas where vendors and merchants would come to sell their goods.
    * **people of this generation**- More literally, "human beings of this family," or "people of this race." In context, he is referring to all the people belonging to the descendants of Israel.
