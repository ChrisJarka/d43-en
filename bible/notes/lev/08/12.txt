===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **He poured some of the anointing oil** - 'He' in this verse refers to Moses.
    * **sashes** - See how you translated v. 8: 7.
    * **waists** - See how you translated v. 8: 7.
    * **wrapped** - "put cloth around and around" (See: Verbal Forms)
    * **linen cloth**  - "clothing made of the flax plant"
