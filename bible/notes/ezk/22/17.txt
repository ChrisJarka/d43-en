===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Next**  - The word "Next" connects the previous message from Yahweh with the one that follows here.  An alternate translation could be "After that."
  * **the word of Yahweh came to me and said**  - Alternate translations: “Yahweh's message came to me” or “Yahweh gave me this message” or “Yahweh spoke this message to me.”
  * **dross**  - This word means the worthless material that comes out of a metal when it is purified.
  * **All of them are bronze and tin, and iron and lead in your midst. They will be like the dross of silver in your furnace.**  - Here, the idea that the people of Israel are like dross in a purification process is repeated for emphasis. Bronze, tin, iron, lead, and silver are all different kinds of metals. (See: Parallelism)
  * **behold!**  - The word “behold” here adds emphasis to what follows. It can also be translated as “Indeed.”
