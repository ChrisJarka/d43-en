===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **place a guard over my mouth**  - This is a request. AT: "please help me not to say thing that are wrong, evil or bad" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **guard the door of my lips** - This is a request. "pease help me not to say things when I should not" (See:[[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **their delicacies**  - This is a special, often expensive food and likely implies fellowship. AT: "special food"
