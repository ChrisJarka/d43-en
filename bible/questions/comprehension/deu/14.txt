====== Deuteronomy Chapter 14 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What does Moses instruct the people of Yahweh their God not do because they are Yahweh's chosen people for his own possession, more than al peoples that ar on the surface of the earth?

A. The people of Yahweh are to not cut themselves, nor share any part of their face for the dead. [14:1-2]

Q? What are the people of Israel allowed to eat, as they are not to eat any disgusting thing?

A. The people of Israel may eat: the ox, the sheep, and the goat, the deer, the gazelle, the roebuck, the wild goat, and ibex, and the antelope, and the mountain sheep.  [14:3-5]

Q? What type of animals are the Israelites allowed to eat?

A. Israel may eat any animal that parts the hoof, that is, that has the hoof divided in two, and that also chews the cud. [14:6]

Q? What animals must Israel not eat eventhough they chew cud or have the hoof divided in two?

A. The camel, the rabbit, and the rock badger are not to be eaten because they chew the cud but do not part the hoof, which makes them unclean for the Israelites. [14:7]
