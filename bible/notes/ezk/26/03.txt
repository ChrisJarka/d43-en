===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **thus says the Lord Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:05:05|5:5]].
  * **Behold** - This can be translated as “Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you.”
  * **I will raise up many nations against you like the sea raises its waves** - Yahweh compares the army that he will send to large waves in the sea. He is emphasizing the size and power of the army. (See: [[en:ta:translate:simile]])
  * **tower** - a tall building used to watch for enemies or to hide in
  * **I will sweep her dust away and make her like a bare cliffside**  - Alternate translation: "I will cause the army to completely destroy her and they will leave nothing there."
