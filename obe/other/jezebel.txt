====== Jezebel ======

===== Facts: =====

Jezebel was the wicked wife of King Ahab of Israel.

  * Jezebel influeced Ahab and the rest of Israel to worship false gods.
  * She also killed many of God's prophets.
  * Jezebel caused an innocent man named Naboth to be killed so that Ahab could steal his Naboth's vineyard.
  * The prophet Elijah prophesied against Jezebel and she received a violent death due to her wicked acts.
