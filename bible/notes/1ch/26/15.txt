===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Shallecheth**  - This is the same as **Shalleketh**  in the UDB. It is a spelling variation.
  * **Watches were established for each family. **- This means that
during a specific period of time, a family member or group would be responsible to watch over or guard a specific gate or area.
