====== sanctify, sanctification ======


===== Definition: =====


Sanctify, or sanctification, is to set apart.


    * In the Old Testament, days, people, and objects were set apart for service to God.
    * In the church, sanctification is the process of striving to separate oneself from sin and become more like Christ.
