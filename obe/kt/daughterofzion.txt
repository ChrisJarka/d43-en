====== Daughter of Zion======

===== Definition: =====
This is an affectionate title given to the people of Israel concerning their future. 

  * Zion is used prophetically as another name for Jerusalem. 
  * Daughter is a term of endearment or affection. 
  * In the Bible this is a metaphor for the patience and care that God has towards his people, the Isrealites. 

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * This could be translated "Daughter of Heaven."
  * It also could be translated "Loved one of Heaven."
