===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to talk to the crowds and disciples.

  * **I am lifted up**  - "men will lift me up." Jesus is referring to his death on the cross. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **will draw all people to himself**  - "will attract all people to himself"
  * **signify**  - indicate or show
  * **what manner of death he would die**  - "the way he would die"
