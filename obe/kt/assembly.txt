====== assembly ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "assembly" usually refers to a group of people who come together to discuss, give advice, and make decisions.

  * An assembly can be a group that is organized in an official and somewhat permanent way, or it can be a group of people who come together temporarily for a specific purpose or occasion.
  * In the Old Testament there was a special kind of assembly called a "sacred assembly" where the people of Israel would gather to worship before the Lord.
  * Sometimes in the Old Testament, the term "assembly" is used to refer to a large gathering of enemy forces, such as in a war. This could translated as "army."
  * <font 14px line-height: 15.6800003051758px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>In the New Testament, the Jewish judicial assembly or council was also known as the Sanhedrin</font> <font 14px line-height: 15.6800003051758px; background-color: rgb(238, 238, 238);/ ;;inherit;;rgb(51, 51, 51) font-family: 'Noto Sans', sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 15.6800003051758px; background-color: rgb(238, 238, 238);>.</font>

===== Translation Suggestions =====

  * Depending on the context, "assembly" could also be translated as "special gathering" or "congregation" or "council" or "army."
