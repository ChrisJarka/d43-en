====== banquet ======

===== Definition: =====

A large, elaborate multi-course meal.

  * The Bible records several kings who served banquet meals to entertain dignitaries and guests.
  * The New Testament states that Christ's church will sit and dine with Him at an event called the marriage banquet of the Lamb.
