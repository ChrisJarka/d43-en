====== 1 Samuel 18 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

== Saul tries to kill David == 
This is the first chapter of a subsection (18-20) and tells why Saul wanted to kill David.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Saul is jealous of David's popularity ==
Saul's son Jonathan loves David. David is successful in battling the Philistines and Saul is happy until the women give David more praise than  they give him. He fears that David will replace him as king. So he encourages David to fight the Philistines, hoping they will kill him. He says that if David kills 100 Philistines he will give him his daughter to marry. So David kills 200, and marries Saul's daughter, Michel.


=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphor ==
To express the deep love Jonathan and David had the author used the [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]: "the soul of Jonathan was bound to the soul of David."
