===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then he said to me** - "he" refers to God. "me" refers to Ezekiel.
  * **So I lifted up my eyes** - With "I" and "my," Ezekiel said he looked up with his own eyes.
  * **So he said to me** - "he" refers to God. "me" refers to Ezekiel
  * **So he said to me, "Son of man, do you see you what they are doing?** - This is a rhetorical question which is answered in the next sentence.
  * **do you see you what they** - "you" refers to Ezekiel; "they" refers to Israel.
  * **to make me go far from my own sanctuary** - to make "God" go far from "God's" own sanctuary.
