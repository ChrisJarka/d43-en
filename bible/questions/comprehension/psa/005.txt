====== Psalms Chapter 005 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who is David asking to listen to his call and think about his groanings?

A. David is asking Yahweh to listen to his call and think about his groanings. [5:1]

Q? To whom does David pray and how does he describe the one who listen to the sound of his call.?

A. David prays to Yahweh and describes him as his King and his God. [5:2]

Q? What certainly does not find God's approval and who will not be his guests?

A. God certainly does not approve of evil and evil people will not be his guests. [5:4]

Q? Who will not stand in God's presence?

A. The arrogant will not stand in God's presence. [5:5]

Q? Who does God hate?

A. God hates all who behave wickedly. [5:5]

Q? What will God do to liars?

A. God will destroy liars. [5:6]

Q? Who despises violent and deceitful men?

A. Yahweh despises violent and deceitful men. [5:6]

Q? Why can David come into Yahweh's house?

A. David can come into Yahweh's house because of Yahweh's great covenant faithfulness. [5:7

Q? How will David bow down to Yahweh's holy temple?

A. David will bow down in reverence. [5:7]

Q? Why does David ask the Lord to make a path straight before him?

A. Davis asks to be led in Yahweh's righteousness because of his enemies. [5:8]

Q? How does David describe his enemies?

A. David describes his enemies as having no truth in their mouths, their inward beings are wicked, their throats are open sepulchres and they flatter with their tongues. [5:9]

Q? What is David asking God to do his enemies because of their schemes, transgressions and rebellion against God?

A. David is asking God to declare that his enemies are guilty. [5:10]

Q? What three things does David ask God to do for those who take refuge in God?

A. He asks that those who take refuge in God may rejoice, shout for joy and that they may be joyful in God. [5:11]

Q? Why does David ask that those who take refuge in God may be joyful?

A. David asks for joy because those who take refuge in God are those who love God's name. [5:11]

Q? What will Yahweh do for the righteous?

A. Yahweh will bless the righteous and surround themn with his favor as with a shield. [5:12]
