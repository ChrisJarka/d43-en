====== cow, calf ======

===== Definition: =====

A cow is a bovine 4 legged mammal. A calf is it's offspring.

  * In the Bible cows and their calves were used for food, milk and as a trading commodity.
  * Cows were used in the Jewish sacrificial system; the most famous being the Red Heifer.
