===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Ramath Lehi** - This is the name of a place. It's name means "Jawbone Hill" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **very thirsty** - "needed water to drink"
  * **But now will I die of thirst?** - This rhetorical question can be translated as a statement. AT: "But now I will die of thirst." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **fall into the hands of those who are uncircumcised** - "fall into the hands" is an idiom. AT: "be captured by those godless Philistines" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
