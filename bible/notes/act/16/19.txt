===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **her - This r**efers to the slave girl who did fortunetelling as described in previous verses.
  * **the hope of their gain **- This is a metaphor referring to the power the girl had which brought her owners money. (See: Metaphor)
  * **their, they -**  This refers to the owners or masters of the slave girl.
  * **dragged them -** The owners of the slave girl dragged Paul and Silas.
  * **before**  - This can also be translated as "into the presence of" or "to be judged by."
  * **When they had brought them…they said**  - When the owners of the girl had brought Paul and Silas…the owners said.
  * **they are teaching**  - They refers to Paul and Silas
