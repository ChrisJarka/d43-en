===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **And have mercy on some people, who are in doubt,- **"Be kind to those who are not certain about the teachings they should believe." (UDB)
  * **and save some people, - **"Present the truth about Christ to people,"
  * **snatching them out of the fire; - **"trusting the Holy Spirit to save them from the evil one and hell."
  * **and on some have mercy with fear, -**  " Be kind to the people living in sin, being very careful to trust in God so that you will not to be tempted to join in with them."
  * **as you hate even the garment spotted by the flesh. -**  " Hate even their clothes, because they are made unclean by sin."
