====== Cornelius ======

===== Facts: =====

Cornelius was a non-Jewish man who was a military officer in the Roman army.

  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>He prayed regularly to God and was very generous in giving to the poor.</font>
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>When Cornelius and his family heard the gospel from the apostle Peter, they became Christians.</font>
  * The people of Cornelius' household were the first non-Jewish people in the New Testament to become Christians.
