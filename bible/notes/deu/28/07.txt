===== translationNotes: =====

  * **cause your enemies who rise up against you to be struck down before you** - AT: "cause you to defeat the armies that attack you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **but will flee before you seven ways** - Their enemies will be afraid and panic and run away from the Israelites. AT: "but will scatter before you in 7 directions" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **seven ways** - AT: "in many different directions"
  * **Yahweh will command the blessing to come on you in your barns** - AT: "Yahweh will bless your crops so you have plenty of grain to store in your barns" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **and in all that you put your hand to** - AT: "and in everything you do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
