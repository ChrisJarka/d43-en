===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Let the heavens be glad, and let the earth rejoice;**  - AT: "The heavens are glad,and the earth rejoices" The heavens and the earth are not human beings, but are being used here with expressions of emotions that people use.(See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **let the sea roar and that which fills it shout with joy.**  - AT: " The oceans and all the creatures in the oceans shout loudly with joy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **Let the fields be joyful . . . let all the trees in the forest shout** - AT: " The fields are joyful and all that are in them. All the trees in the forest shout" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
