====== Carmel, Mount Carmel ======

===== Facts: =====

A coastal mountain range in northern Israel, whose name literally means "God's vineyard"

  * The Prophet Elisha stayed in Carmel for a short period after Elijah was taken up to heaven.
  * The Carmel range is full of caves where Monks and the Essenes lived in austerity.
