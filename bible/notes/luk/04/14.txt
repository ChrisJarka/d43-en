===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **power of the Spirit **- This means that God was with him in a special way, enabling Jesus to do things that humans usually couldn't.
    * **news about him spread **  - This means that those who heard Jesus told other people about him, and then those other people told even more people about Jesus. It could be translated as "knowledge about him was passed on by people to other people."
    * **throughout the entire surrounding region **- This refers to the areas or places around Galilee.
    * **he taught-**  That is, "He was continually teaching" or "he was teaching from one synagogue to the next in the region of Galilee."
    * **everyone praised him **- That is, "everyone spoke great things about him" or "everyone spoke about him in a good way."
