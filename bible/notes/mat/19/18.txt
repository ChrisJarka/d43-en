===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you will not kill **- "you will not murder someone"
  * **you will not steal**  - "you will not take from someone else what does not belong to you"
  * **Honor your father and your mother **- "respect and obey your father and your mother"
  * **love your neighbor as yourself **- "care for your neighbor as you care for yourself"
