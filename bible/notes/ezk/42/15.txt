===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **completed measuring** - "finished measuring"
  * **the inner house** - "the inside of the temple"
  * **and measured it all the surrounding area there** - It is implied that it is the temple around which the man measured. AT: "and measured all around the temple." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
