===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh visited Sarah as he had said,**  - "Yahweh visited Sarah as he had said he would,"
  * **as he had promised**  - "as he had spoken"
  * **bore a son to Abraham **- "bore Abraham's son"
  * **in his old age**  - "even though Abraham was very old"
  * **set time**  - or "season"
  * **of which God had spoken to him**  - "God had said when he spoke with him"
