===== Important Terms: =====

  * son
  * blood
  * defile
  * neighbor

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **If he bears a … son**  - This means if he is the father of a son.
  * **who sheds blood**  - one "who murders people" (UDB)
  * **and does any one of these things and ****does not do any of those things **- Alternate Translation: "and the son does these evil things that his father would never do."
  * **instead eats upon the mountains **- Shrines to idols were often on mountain or hill tops.
  * **defiles**- This refers to sexual sins which make them unacceptable to God
