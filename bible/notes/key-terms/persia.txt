====== Persia ======

===== Definition: =====

Persia was an empire founded by Cyrus the Great in 550 BC, and its center was where modern-day Iran now is.

  * Under Cyrus' decree, the Jews were liberated from Babylonian Captivity, and the Temple in Jerusalem was rebuilt with funds provided by the Persian Empire.
  * Zoroastrianism was the predominant religion of the Persian Empire.
