====== Hivite ======

===== Facts: =====

Hivites are Old Testament descendents of Ham

  * Hivites lived in the land of Canaan, and were to be driven out by Joshua
  * The Hivites' land was described as one of milk and honey, meaning the land was prosperous and suitable to live in
