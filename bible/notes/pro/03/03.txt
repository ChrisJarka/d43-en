===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Do not let covenant faithfulness and trustworthiness ever leave you** - "Always be faithful and trustworthy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **write them on the tablet of your heart** - Here the heart represents a person's mind and what he sincerely believes.  The mind is spoken of as if it were a tablet upon which someone can write messages and commands. AT: "remember them with your mind so that you do not forget them. Let them be as if you had written these words on stone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **you will find favor ... in the sight of God** - "God will show you how happy he is with you"
  * **find favor** - This means that someone is approved of by someone else.
  * **you will find ... a good reputation ... in the sight of ... man** - "people ... will think that you are a good person"
