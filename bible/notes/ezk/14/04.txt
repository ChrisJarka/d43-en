===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But say this to them**  - This refers the "men from the elders of Israel
  * **Every man of the house of Israel (ULB) - ** This is "Israelite person" in UDB
  * **Before his face - ** The idiom “before his face” means “ahead of him” or “in front of him.”
  * **according to**  - explanation
  * **house of Israel**  - The phrase “house of Israel” refers to the entire nation of Israel and could be translated as the “people of Israel."
  * **Therefore declare to them ****- ** alternate term: say
  * **seize the house -** alternate term for "seize": grab or take
  * **because estranged **- alternate term for "estranged": separated (from)
