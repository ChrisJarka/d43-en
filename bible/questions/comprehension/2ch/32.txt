====== 2 Chronicles Chapter 32 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Sennacherib, king of Assyria, do after the acts of faithfulness?

A. He entered Judah and attacked the cities. [32:1]

Q? What did Hezekiah ask his leaders to help with?

A. He asked them to stop up all the springs and streams so the king would not find water. [32:2-4]

Q? What did Hezekiah do to guard against the enemies' attack?

A. He built up the walls and made large amounts of weapons. [32:5]

Q? What encouraging words did Hezekiah give to his people?

A. He told them to be strong, of good courage, and not be afraid because Yahweh who is greater than their enemy will help them fight. [32:6-8]
