===== Translation Notes: =====

This chapter follows the description of the temple.

  * **Then he brought me** - The word "he" refers to the man whose appearance is like brass, and who stood at the gate. (40:3)
  * **cubits** - See how you translated these "long" cubits in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:05|40:05]].
  * **six cubits wide on either side, the width of the tabernacle** - This could be translated as a new sentence: "And they were six cubits on both sides, which is the same width as the tabernacle."
  * **six cubits** - About 3.2 meters. (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaldistance]])
  * **ten cubits** - About 5.4 meters.
  * **five cubits** - About 2.7 meters.
  * **forty cubits** - About twenty-two meters.
  * **twenty cubits** - About eleven meters.
