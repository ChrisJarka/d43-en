====== Joel 01 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

Joel is written is poetic form and striking imagery. The ULB is written in poetic form but the UDB has been transferred to a prose form. If possible, use the poetic form to communicate the meaning of this book. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Starts very dramatically with the imagery of the locusts and the devastation they produce. There are five different kinds of locusts that appear to come and progressively destroy the vegetation including the crops, vineyards and even the trees. 

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

There are many instances of parallelism in chapter one. Please review [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]].
