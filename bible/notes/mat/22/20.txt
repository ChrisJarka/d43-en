===== Translation Notes: =====
The Pharisees disciples and the Herodians had come to try to trap Jesus.

  * **Whose image and name are these? ** - Jesus knew whose name was on the coin; he just wanted them to say. (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **image** - "picture"
  * **things that are Caesar's** - This refers to the taxes or any other responsibilities they might have to the Roman government.
  * **things that are God's** - This refers to giving God what he deserves.
  * **When they heard it **- "When they heard what Jesus said"
