===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Abimelech said** "Abimelech said to Abraham"
  * **Look** - The word “look” here and verse 16 adds emphasis to what follows.
  * **Settle wherever it pleases you**  - "Live wherever you want"
  * **a thousand**  - "1,000" (See: [[en:ta:translate:translateunknowns-numbers]])
  * **you are made completely right** - "I declare that you are completely innocent" (See: [[en:ta:translate:active]])
