===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **country is desolate**- the native land in which you live lies in ruins (is devastated)
  * The next two notes illustrate note 1 concerning how the country is devastated
      * **your cities are burned with fire**;- your cities lie in ashes
        * **your fieldsâ€“in your presence, strangers are destroying them - **  People who are not from your own country are destroying and consuming all the produce from your fields
      * **a waste and desolation, as overthrown by strangers**  - it will be completely destroyed by strangers (like Sodom and <font inherit/Calibri;;inherit;;inherit>Gomorrah)</font>
  * **The daughter of Zion - Daughter refers to the nation of Israel - also known as Zion**
  * The next three notes illustrate the precarious situation in which the nation is Israel in
      * **is left as a hut in a vineyard**- this is a metaphor - a temporary shelter used for guarding the vineyard
      * **as a shed in a garden of cucumbers**-this is a metaphor - a temporary shelter used for guarding the cucumber garden
      * **as a besieged city**- a city that is surrounded by enemies
