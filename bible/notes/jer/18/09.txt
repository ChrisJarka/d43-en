===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But if it does evil in my eyes by not listening to my voice** - Yahweh refers to the nation or kingdom as doing evil to mean the people of the nation or kingdom are doing evil.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **evil in my eyes**  - Yahweh refers to the way he understands what is evil by referring to the part of his body he sees with. AT: "things that I consider to be evil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **not listening to my voice** - Yahweh refers to his commands as his voice because he uses his voice to give commands. This phrase says the people did not listen to mean they did not obey what they had heard.
