===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Jesus continues his parable)
  * **they threw him out of the vineyard**  - That is, "The vine dressers threw the son out of the vineyard."
  * **What then will the lord of the vineyard do to them?**  - This is a rhetorical question. Jesus used this rhetorical question to get his listeners to pay attention to the end of the story. It could also be translated as "So now, listen to what the lord of the vineyard will do to them." (See: [[:en:ta:figuresofspeech:rhetoricalquestion]])
  * **destroy**-Or, "destroy beyond repair."
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **And when they heard it**- That is, "And when the people heard this parable."
  * **God forbid**  -Or, "may it never happen"
