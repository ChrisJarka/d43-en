===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Ahikam son of Shaphan, Abdon son of Micah, Shaphan ... Asaiah** - These are the names of men. (See:[[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **ask Yahweh's will for me** - the word "will" can be translated as a verb. Also, it is made clear in [[:en:bible:notes:2ch:34:22|34:22]] that the king means for the men to go to the prophetess of Yahweh to determine Yahweh's will. AT: "Ask Yahweh what he wants me to do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **because of the words** - "concerning the words"
  * **the words of this book that has been found** - Here "words" represents the laws. This can be stated in active form. AT: "the laws in this book that Hilkiah has found" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **For it is great, the anger of Yahweh that has been poured out on us. It is great** - Emotions such as anger are often spoken of in Scripture as if they were liquids. AT: "For Yahweh's anger towards us is great, like water that could totally wash us away" or "For great is Yahweh's anger with which he has punished us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **all that was written in it** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "all that is written in it" or "all that is in this book" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
