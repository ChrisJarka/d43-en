===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He **  - This pronoun refers to John the Baptist
  * **me, I**  - These pronouns refer to John the Baptist who is speaking
  * **I am not worthy even to stoop down to untie the strap of his sandals** - John is saying that he does not deserve to do the most unpleasant work of a servant. 
  * **stoop down**  - "bend down"
  * **baptize you with the Holy Spirit ** - This metaphor compares literal baptism that brings a person into contact with water to a spiritual baptism that brings them into contact with the Holy Spirit.
