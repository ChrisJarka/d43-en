====== everlasting, forever ======

===== Definition: =====

Something that will always exist is said to be everlasting, forever, or eternal.

  * "Everlasting" and "forever" are used in the Old Testament, frequently referring to God's never ceasing covenant with Abraham.
  * The word "eternal" describes the existence of God for all time with no beginning or no end.
  * The word "eternal" is used in the New Testament, frequently referring to believers living for all time in heaven.

(See also: other)
