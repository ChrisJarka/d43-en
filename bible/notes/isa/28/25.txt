===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **When he has prepared the ground** - "when the farmer has plowed the soil and turned over the ground"
  * **does he not scatter caraway seed, sow the cumin, put in the wheat in rows and the barley in the right place, and the spelt at its edges ** - "then he plants each kind of seed in the correct way"
  * **caraway, cumin, wheat, barley, spelt** - These are all names of plants. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **His God instructs him; he teaches him wisely** - "Yahweh helps the farmer know how to care for each kind of plant"
