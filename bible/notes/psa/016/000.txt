====== Psalms 016 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 16 is a praise psalm, thanking God for all his mercies. The New Testament considers this psalm to be a messianic psalm: a psalm about Christ.  

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

God has been so good to him and he will worship no other god. The New Testament understands the tenth verse to be about Christ's resurrection when it says that he is God's chosen/anointed one and God will not leave his body in the grave to decay.
