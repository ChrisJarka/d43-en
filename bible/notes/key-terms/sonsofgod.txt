====== sons of God ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Old Testament, the term, "sons of God" is usually used in reference to angelic beings.

  * As a reference to angelic beings, "sons of God" may include fallen angels, that is, evil spirits.
  * Different interIn Genesis 6, some interpret "sons of God" to refer to powerful political rulers or to the descendants of Seth.
  * Where there are several possible interpretations of a term, it is important to translate it fairly literally, if possible.
  * In the New Testament, the term "sons of God" can be translated as "children of God" since it refers to all believers in Jesus. The Bible often refers to believers as God's "sons."
