====== Psalms Chapter 002 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who are the rulers conspiring against?

A. They are conspiring againstYahweh and against his Messiah. [2:2]

Q? What do the rulers want to tear off and throw off?

A. They want to tear off the shackles Yahweh and his Messiah put on them and throw off their chains. [2:3]

Q? Who sits in the heavens and mocks the nations who rebel?

A. The Lord mocks them. [1:4]

Q? Who has the Lord annointed?

A. The Lord has annointed his king on Zion, his holy mountain. [1:6]

Q? What does Yahweh decree about the writer of the psalm?

A. Yahweh decrees that the writer is his son. [1:7]

Q? What will Yahweh give to the writer of the psalm?

A. Yahweh will give him the nations and the farthermost regions of the earth. [1:8]

Q? What will the writer of the psalm do to the nations that he is given?

A. The writer will break them with an iron scepter and smash them like a potter's jar. [1:9]
