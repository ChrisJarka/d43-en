===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is the end of John's letter to Gaius. He gives some final remarks and a closing greeting.

  * **I do not wish to write them to you with pen and ink** - John does not wish to write these other things at all. He is not saying that he would write them with something other than pen and ink.
  * **face to face** - "together" or "in person"
  * **Peace be to you** - "May God give you peace"
  * **Greet the friends by name** - "Greet each of the believers there personally for me"
  * **The friends** - "Your friends" or "The believers here"
