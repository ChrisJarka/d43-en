===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Because of what Paul has been writing to the Ephesians, he encourages them in how to live their lives as believers and again emphasizes the unity of believers.

  * **as the prisoner for the Lord** - AT: "as someone who is in prison because of his choice to serve the Lord"
  * **I urge you to walk worthily of the calling** - The word "you" refers to the Ephesian believers. Walking is a common way to express the idea of conducting one's life. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]], [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **with great humility and gentleness and patience. Accepting each other in love** - AT: "learn to be humble, gentle, patient and to accept each other in love"
  * **keep the unity of the Spirit in the bond of peace** - AT: "seek to live together in peace to maintain the unity of the Spirit"
