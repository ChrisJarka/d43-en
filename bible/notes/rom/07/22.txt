===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **inner man** - This refers to the part of the person that has been made spiritually alive by Jesus that desires to do God's will.
  * **body parts** - Here, “body parts” refers to the whole person. 
  * **warring** - Alternate translation: "fighting"
  * **new principle in my mind** - This is another way of saying "inner man."
  * **taking me captive by the principle of sin which is in my body parts** - Alternate translation: "Strongly influencing me to sin with my whole person."
