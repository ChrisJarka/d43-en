====== false witness ======

===== Definition: =====

False witness is lying or not telling the truth or whole truth.

  * The ninth commandment of the ten commandments says not to bear false witness, that is, do not lie.
  * A false witness can be a person who lies to deceive others.
  * False witnesses were set up to testify against Stephan in Acts 6.
