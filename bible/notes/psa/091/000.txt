====== Psalms 091 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 91 is a wisdom psalm. Those who depend on God are protected by him.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

No matter what dangers happen around him, he does not need to fear; because God is protecting him from all harm.
