====== free, freedom ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Bible, the term "free" often means to not be a slave or to not be in bondage.

  * <font 14px line-height: 13.0666666030884px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Freedom is the state of being free; it is the opposite of being in slavery.</font>
  * A "free woman" is the opposite of a "slave woman."
  * Often the terms "free" and "freedom" are used figuratively to refer to how a believer in Jesus is no longer under the power of sin.

(See also: enslaved, hold in bondage, servant, slave, slavery other)
