===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **gratify my desires with wine **  - not for the pleasure of drinking, but to stimulate or refresh himself
  * **I let my mind guide me with wisdom - **  this is an intellectual investigation
  * **to see if "it" **  - "it" referes to experimenting with wine as a solution to a person's problem
  * **under heaven **  - this expression can include what God has done as well as what people do
