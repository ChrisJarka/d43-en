===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to teach his disciples. This event began in 5:1.

  * **they will inherit the earth**  - "God will give them the earth." It will not belong to them but will be for them to enjoy.
  * **hunger and thirst for righteousness**  - "desire to live right as much as they desire food and drink" (See TAlink: Metaphor)
  * **they will be filled**  - This may be stated as an active phrase, "God will fill them." (See TAlink: Passive/Active)
  * **the pure in heart**  - "people whose heart is pure"
  * **they will see God**  - "they will be allowed to live with God"
