===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh is speaking.
  * **you will grow branches and bear fruit** - Yahweh is speaking to the mountains as though they were trees. Alternate translation: "trees will grow on you that will grow branches and bear fruit."
  * **Behold**  - The word “behold” here adds emphasis to what follows. Alternate translation: “Indeed.”
  * **I am for you** - "I want to do good things to you." The word "you" refers to the mountains of Israel.
  * **and I treat you with favor** - "I will pay attention to you to make sure that you are doing well"
  * **you will be plowed and sown with seed** - Alternate translation: "My people, Israel, will plow your land and sow it with seed."
