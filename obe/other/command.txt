====== command, to command ======

===== Definition: =====

A command is a verbal or written statement given by an authority figure that requires action from those under authority. To command is to make a statement requiring action.

  * God commands us to obey His word, He is the ultimate authority figure.
  * In the New Testament God commands all men to repent and follow Jesus.
