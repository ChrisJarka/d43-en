===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The man** - "The servant" (UDB)
  * **watched her** - "watched Rebekah" or "watched the young woman"
  * **to see**  - This is an idiom which you can translate as "to know" (UDB) or "to determine." (See: [[en:ta:translate:idiom]])
  * **had prospered his journey**  - "" or "fulfilled the purpose of his journey"
  * **gold nose ring**  - You could translate this as "gold ear ring."
  * **is there room in your father’s house**  - "is there a place in your father's house"
  * **for us to spend the night**  - "where we can stay for the night"
