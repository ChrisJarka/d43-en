====== Zerubbabel ======

===== Facts: =====

Zerubbabel was the name of two men in the Old Testament.


  * One of these was a descendant of Jehoiakim and Zedekiah.
  * a different Zerubbabel was the head of the tribe of Judah during one of the returns from the Babylonian captivity.
  * helped rebuild the temple/altar of God
  * Leading the people of Judah during the same time as Cyrus was the king of Persia. 
  * In the Bible Zerubbabel is usually associated with the high priest Joshua.
