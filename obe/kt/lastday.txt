====== last day, last days, latter days ======

===== Definition: =====

The "last days" refers generally to the time period leading up to the time when Jesus will return.

  * This time period will be an unknown length of time.
  * During this time there will be an increase in evil and disasters happening on earth.
  * The "last day" refers to the "day of the Lord" which is a time of the resurrection of the dead and a time of judgment.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

* "Last day" can be translated as "final days" or "end times."

(See also: [[:en:obe:kt:dayofthelord|day of the Lord]], [[:en:obe:kt:judgmentday|judgment day]] )
