====== Psalms Chapter 137 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did the writer say they thought about and where were they when they sat down and wept?

A. They were by the rivers of Babylon thinking about Zion. [137:1]

Q? Where did they hang their harps and what did the captors require them to sing?

A. Harps were hung on the trees and their captures required them to be happy and sing songs of Zion. [137:2-4]
