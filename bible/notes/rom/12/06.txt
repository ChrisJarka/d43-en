===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **We have different gifts according to the grace that was given to us** - "God has freely given each us the ability to do different things for him"
  * **If prophecy** - The phrase "we have the gift of" is left out but it is understood. Alternate translation: "If we have the gift of prophecy." (See: [[en:ta:translate:ellipsis]])
  * **let us prophecy according to the proportion of our faith** - Possible meanings are 1) "let us speak prophecies that do not go beyond the amount of faith God has given us" or 2) "let us speak prophecies that agree with the teachings of our faith" 
  * **If service** - "If we have the gift of service"
  * **or if teaching** - "or if someone has the gift of teaching"
  * **he who gives**  - The meaning can be made explicit: "he who gives money or other goods to people in need." (See: [[en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]])
  * **he who oversees, with diligence** - "he who leads, let him do it well"
  * **with cheerfulness** - "let him do it with cheer"
