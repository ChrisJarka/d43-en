===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is the end of John's letter. He does not give a closing greeting.

  * **Son of God** - This is an important title for Jesus that describes his relationship to God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **we know** - "We" refers to all believers.
  * **has given us understanding** - "has enabled us to understand the truth." (UDB)
  * **we know him who is true** - "him" refers to God the Father.
  * **we are in him** - See how this was translated in [[:en:bible:notes:1jn:02:04|2:5-6]].
  * **life** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1jn:01:01|1:1-2]].
  * **Dear children** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1jn:02:01|2:1]].
