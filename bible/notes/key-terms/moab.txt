====== Moab, Moabite, Moabitess ======

===== Facts: =====

Moab was the son of Lot's elder daughter. His descendants were called Moabites and the land they settled in also became known as "Moab."

  * The country of Moab was located on the opposite side of the Dead Sea from the region of Judah, where Naomi's family was from. It was southeast of the city of Bethlehem.
  * "Moabitess" is the term used when specifically referring to a Moabite woman.
