===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **If you return, Israel…return to me**  - Possible meanings are 1) "If you change your behavior, people of Israel…then change it to start worshiping me again" or 2) "If you will come back, people of Israel…if you will come back to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
  * **Yahweh’s declaration**  - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:01:07|1:8]].
  * **If you remove your detestable things from before me**  - This phrase refers to idols as things that deserve strong hatred. AT: "Remove your disgusting idols from my presence" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
  * **do not wander from me again**  - AT: "remain faithful to me"
  * **Plow your own ground, and do not sow among thorns**  - Yahweh tells the people to prepare their lives like a farmer prepares the ground for planting. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
