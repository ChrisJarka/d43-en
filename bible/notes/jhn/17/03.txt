===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **that they should know you- idiomatic use of the word 'know.' This is not mere intellectual knowledge but experiential knowledge as well.**
  *
  * **the work that you have given me to do - "The work" is a metonymy for Jesus' entire earthly gospel ministry.**
  *
  * **the glory that I had with you before the world was created - A statement referencing Jesus' existence with the Father before the world was created**
