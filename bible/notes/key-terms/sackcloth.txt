====== sackcloth and ashes ======

===== Definition: =====

The phrase "sackcloth and ashes" refers to a traditional expression of grief, mourning or repentance.

  * Sackcloth <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>was a dark garment made out of goat or camel hair that was worn as a sign of mourning, grief or humiliation. This cloth was very coarse and scratchy.</font>
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Wearing ashes or sitting on ashes were additional signs of deep mourning, emotional depression or an attitude of repentance.</font>
  * Ashes are the remains of objects that have been completely destroyed by fire, such as the remains of an Old Testament animal sacrifice.
