===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **in the day of trouble** - AT: "in the time of trouble" or "when I have troubles"
  * **he will shelter me**  - "he will hide me" or "he will protect me"
  * **booth**  - a temporary shelter
  * **in the cover of his tent** - The word "cover" indicates protection. This idea of protection is repeated here to emphasize it as well as for the beauty of the poetry. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **He will lift me high...
  * **my head will be lifted up above my enemies** - When a person's head is lifted here, it means that something is done so that people will honor him. People would honor him because he would defeat his enemies.  AT: I will have  over my  will live my head above my enemies". This means that he will cause me to win the fight against my enemies.  (See:  [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Then my head will be lifted up above**  -  This will happen The word "head" represents the entire person.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
