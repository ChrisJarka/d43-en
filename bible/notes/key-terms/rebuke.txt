====== rebuke ======

===== Definition: =====

To rebuke is to give someone a sharp verbal correction or reprimand.

  * The New Testament commands Christians to rebuke their fellows if they openly sin.
  * In the Old Testament Balaam was rebuked by his donkey.
  * The book of proverbs instructs parents to rebuke their children when they are disobedient.
