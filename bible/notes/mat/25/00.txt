======  Matthew 25 General Notes ======

=== Formatting in this chapter ===

This chapter continues the teaching of the previous chapter.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Wedding ==
In Israel, a bride and bridegroom were arranged to be married by there parents and there was a period of time before the wedding. After a period of time, it was a custom in Israel for a bridegroom to go to his virgin bride's house where she waited for him. The wedding ceremony would then take place and there was a great celebration as they traveled to the groom's home where there would be a great feast. This is a common image in the New Testament regarding Jesus and the church (also called the bride of Christ).
