====== Ruth ======

===== Facts: =====

Ruth was a Moabite woman who lived during the time when judges were leading Israel. She was an early ancestor of Jesus Christ.

  * Ruth married an Israelite man who had moved to Moab with his parents and brother. But her husband died.
  * When her mother-in-law Naomi returned to Israel, Ruth went with her to help her and to worship the one true God that Naomi and the other Israelites worshiped.
  * After they returned to Israel, Ruth married Boaz, a relative of her deceased husband. She gave birth to a son named Obed, who became the grandfather of King David.
