===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **a tenth of the people** - This could be translated as "one-tenth" or "one out of ten people"
  * **it will again be destroyed** - The word "it" refers to the land of Judah. Alternate translation: "armies will again destroy the land of Judah"  (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * ** as a terebinth or an oak is cut down and whose trunk remains, the holy seed is in its stump** - This compares the people of Judah to a tree stump that grows new branches or shoots. It means that even though Judah's enemies will destroy the people of Judah, Yahweh will later bring new life to his people. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile])
  * **terebinth** - a kind of oak tree
  * **trunk** - the thick main stem of a tree
  * **holy seed** - This phrase refers to the people of Judah. 
  * **stump** - the part of a tree that remains in the ground after the tree is cut down
