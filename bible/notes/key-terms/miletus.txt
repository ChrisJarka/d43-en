====== Miletus ======

===== Facts: =====

Miletus was a city in western Asia Minor, near the mouth of the Meander River.
    * Paul visited Miletus twice, during his missionary journeys.
    * On his first missionary journey, Paul sent word to Ephesus for the leaders of the Church to come and meet with him.
    * Paul left Trophimus in Miletus, to convalesce from an illness.
