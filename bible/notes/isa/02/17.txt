===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the pride of man will be brought low**
 - "He will cause people to no longer be arrogant"
  * **and the haughtiness of man will fall**  - "and he will cause them to stop being proud."
  * **Yahweh alone will be exalted on that day**  - "on that day people will praise and honor only Yahweh."
  * **The idols will completely pass away**
 - "All idols will disappear then."
  * **Men will go into the caves of the rocks**
 - "they will run to hide in caves in rock cliffs"
  * **and the holes of the ground**
 - "and in holes in the ground."
  * **from before the terror of Yahweh**
 - "because they are afraid of Yahweh."
  * **and from the splendor of his majesty**
 - "and of his glorious and awesome power."
  * **when he rises to terrify the earth**  - when Yahweh brings his judgment on the earth.
