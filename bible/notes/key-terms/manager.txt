====== manager ======


===== Definition: =====


A manager was a servant in a rich family's home who was in charge of the children, the other servants and the property.


    * These individuals were entrusted with stewarding or taking care of the owner's affairs and business dealings.
    * This term is also used in reference to the apostles and overseers in the early church in a metaphorical sense of taking care of the church until Christ returns.  


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:servant|servant]])
