====== Psalms 150 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 150 is a worship psalm: the seventh and last worship psalm in the final seven worship psalms. (Psalms 144-150)

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

People should praise Yahweh with every type of musical instrument.
