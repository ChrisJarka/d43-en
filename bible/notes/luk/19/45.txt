===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **it is written**  - This could be translated as "The scriptures say."
  * **house of prayer**  - That is, "a place where people will pray to me" or "a place set aside for people to pray in."
  * **a den of robbers**  - That is, "a place where robbers hide out." This This is a metaphor. It could also be translated with a simile as "like a den of robbers." (See: [[en:ta:figuresofspeech:metaphor|Metaphor]], [[en:ta:figuresofspeech:simile|Simile]])
