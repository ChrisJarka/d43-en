====== governor ======


===== Definition: =====


A governor is a person who is generally the highest appointed civil leader in a state, region, or territory. The governor generally answers to a higher authority in the national government.


    * Governor usually refers to a person who exercises absolute authority over an area but is still subject to a king or emperor.
    * Governor can also be translated as "ruler" or "overseer."
    * Governor is one who governs or rules.
    * Governor in biblical times was the ruler of a district under a king.
    * In New Testament times, a governor described Roman subordinates such as Pilate, Felix, and Festus.
