====== Lot ======


===== Facts: =====


In the Bible Lot is the Nephew of Abraham 


    * Lot chose to live in the land of Sodom, which was later destroyed by God. 
    * Lot was married and had two daughters. His wife was turned to a pillar of salt because she looked back while Sodom was being destroyed.
