====== Herod Antipas ======


===== Facts: =====


Herod Antipas was ruler in Galilee during the time of Jesus


    * Antipas was a nickname derived from Antipatros
    * Herod Antipas ordered John the Baptist to be killed by beheading
    * Jesus called Herod Antipas a "fox". This is a derogatory term
