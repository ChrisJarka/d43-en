====== Christ ======


===== Facts: In the Bible the word Christ means "Anointed One"or "selected one". =====


    * The Jewish scripture foretold that a man would come and be anointed as Messiah and King.
    * Jesus of Nazareth is the man whom the scripture foretold and is the Anointed Messiah or Christ.
    * The New Testament states that Jesus was selected as the Messiah before the creation of the world.
