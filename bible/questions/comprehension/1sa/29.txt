====== 1 Samuel Chapter 29 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did the Philistines, Israelites, and David and his men do?

A. The Philistines gathered together all their army at Aphek, the Israelites camped by the spring in Jezreel, and David and his men were in the rear guard with Achish. [29:1-2]

Q? How did Achish respond when the princes of the Philistines asked why the Hebrews were there?

A. Achish told them that David, the king of Israel, had been with him all these years and he had found no fault with him. [29:3]

Q? Why were the princes of the Philistines angry with Achish?

A. They feared that because he had brought David in with him, now David would become an enemy and would kill the Philistines' army. [29:4]

Q? What did Achish tell David to do because the princes of the Philistines did not want David there?

A. He told David to peacefully return to his home. [29:6-7]

Q? Why did David question Achish about him being sent away?

A. David wanted to know why he was being sent away after being found good in Achish's sight. [29:8]
