===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Satan will be released**  - AT: "God will release"
  * **four corners of the earth**  - This idiom–which is very old because it goes back to the time when people believed the world was flat– means everywhere on the Earth no matter how far away. Your language may have a special expression for this meaning. (See: Idiom.)
  * **Gog and Magog**  - These names represent far away countries. (See: How To Translate Names.)
  * **They will be as many as the sand of the sea**  - AT: "There will be as many of them as there are pieces of sand in the sea."
  * **They **- This word refers to all the people from the far away countries.
