===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you…us…our**  - These pronouns refer to to all Christians. (See: Inclusive)
  * **you are in Christ Jesus - ** "you are now joined to Christ Jesus" (UDB)
  * **Christ Jesus, who became for us the wisdom from God**  - Alternate meanings: "Christ Jesus, who has made clear to us how wise God is" (UDB; see: Metonymy)
  * **"Let the one who boasts, boast in the Lord"**  - Alternate meanings: "If a person boasts, he should boast about how great the Lord is" or "the one who praises himself should praise himself only in what the Lord has done for him." (UDB)
