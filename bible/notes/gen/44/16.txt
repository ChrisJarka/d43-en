===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **God has found out the iniquity of your servants.**  - meaning that Judah may be alluding to the fact that they sold their younger brother and lied to their father. (it has been discussed in similar circumstances by the brothers before.) Or he may be simply thinking they are receiving punishment for a generic sinfulness.
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **hand**  - "possession"
  * **that I should do so**  - "to go that far"
  * **cup**  - "drinking bowl"
