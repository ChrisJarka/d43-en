===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **his wife**  - That is, "Zechariah's wife".
    * **conceived**  - That is, "became pregnant" as in the UDB. Use an expression that is acceptable and will not embarrass people.
    * **hid herself**  - That is, "secluded herself" or, "stayed at home" or, "did not go out in public" as in the UDB.
    * **This is what the Lord has done for me** - This phrase refers to the fact that the Lord allowed her to become pregnant.
    * **watched over me**  - This is an idiom that means, "regarded me kindly" or, "had pity on me" or, "was compassionate toward me." (See: [[en:ta:figuresofspeech:idiom|Idiom]])
    * **to take away my disgrace among people**  - Another way to translate this would be, "and took away the reason people looked down on me" as in the UDB.
