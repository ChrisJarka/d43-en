===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Repay**- Here, this is another way of saying, do not take revenge against someone. (See: Metaphor)
  * **Repay no one evil for evil**  -Alternate translation: "Do not do evil things to someone because he has done evil things to you."
  * **As much as it lies with you - **  You are not responsible for what the other person does but do the best that you, personally, can do.
