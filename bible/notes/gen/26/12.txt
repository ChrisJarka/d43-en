===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **in that land** - "in Gerar"
  * **a hundredfold** - "a very large crop" (UDB)
  * **The man became rich** - "Isaac became rich" or "He became rich"
  * **and grew more and more until he became very great**  - "and he continued until he became very wealthy."
  * **Philistines envied him**  - "The Philistines became angry because of Isaac's wealth"
