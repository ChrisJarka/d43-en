===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For the palace will be forsaken**  - the king's palace will be empty
  * **the crowded city**  - the city that is now very noisy
  * **the hill and the watchtower will become caves**  - the forts and the watchtower will become empty and in ruins
  * **a joy of wild donkeys**  - wild donkeys will walk around wherever they want to be
  * **a pasture of flocks**  - flocks of sheep will eat grass
  * **until the Spirit is poured on us from on high**  - it will be like that until God pours his Spirit out on us from heaven.
  * **and the wilderness becomes a fruitful field **- when that happends, the deserts will become fertile fields
  * **and the fruitful field is considered as a forest**  - and abundant crops will grow in those fertile fields.
