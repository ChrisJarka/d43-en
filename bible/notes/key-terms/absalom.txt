===== Absalom =====


===== Facts: =====

Absalom, born in Hebron, the third son of David and Maacah, led a dramatic life and was a threat to his own father for a period.

  * Absalom's older brother, Ammon, raped their sister, Tamar. Therefore, Absolom had Ammon killed.
  * King David refused to see his son Absolom for five years after the murder of Ammon. Absolom hated David for doing this.
  * Absalom revolted against his father David and David fled from him.
  * David's army fought against Absalom and killed him. David was very grieved when this happened.
