===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **behold** - The word “behold” here alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows.
  * **went and took**  - "Abraham went over to the ram and took it."
  * **Yahweh will provide** - This can be translated as "Yahweh will give what is needed."
  * **On the mountain of Yahweh it will be provided**  - This can also be translated with an active clause: "On the mountain of Yahweh, he will provide." (See: [[en:ta:translate:active]])
