===== Translation Notes: =====

God continues to speak through Ezekiel about the terrible things that the people of Jerusalem have done.

  * **The nakedness of the father is revealed to you.**  - Men have had sexual relations with their mothers. This could also be stated, "Men have known their own mothers". (See: Euphemism)
  * **They have abused the unclean woman within you during her impurity.**  - This means the men have had sexual relations with women during their monthly menstruation.
  * **The man committing abomination with her neighbor’s wife and the man making his daughter-in-law shamefully unclean**  - This means men had sexual relations with their neighbor's wives and son's wives.
  * **the man abusing his sister, the daughter of his father, among you**  - This means men had sexual relations with their sisters and even half-sisters.
  * **in order to pour out blood**  - This phrase means "in order to cause someone to be killed".
  * **interest**  - This word means the additional money charged in order to borrow money.
  * **declaration**  - This words means a formal announcement either verbally or in writing.
