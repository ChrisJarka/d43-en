====== Psalms 099 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 99 is one of six worship psalms (Psalms 95-100)

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

God is just and fair. Moses, Aaron and Samuel prayed to him and obeyed him and he answered their prayers.
