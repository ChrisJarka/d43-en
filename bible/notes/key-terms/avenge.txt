====== avenge ======

===== Definition: Someone who takes action to pay back one who wronged a blood relative. =====

Avenge is pay back action taken on someone who wronged someone else.

  * Avenge and revenge have the same meaning, except that avenge is always a verb and revenge can be used as a verb or noun.
  * avenge also includes God's action in judgment of someone as in "it is mine to avenge, I will repay," where His people are urged not to take revenge against someone who has wronged them but to leave the matter with God.
