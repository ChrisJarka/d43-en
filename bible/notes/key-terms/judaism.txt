====== Judaism, Jewish religion ======

===== Definition: =====

Text

  * In Scripture, the terms refer to the practice of the religion of the Old Testament, as well as the customs and traditions that have been added to in the practice of the religion over time. Paul also refers to a person being Jewish who is one inwardly (Romans 2:29), not just outwardly based upon ancestry.
