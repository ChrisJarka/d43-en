====== Abner ======

===== Definition: =====

Abner was the cousin of King Saul in the Old Testament.

  * Abner was commander in chief of Saul's army, and introduced young David to Saul after David slew Goliath the giant.
  * After King Saul's death Abner set up Saul's son Ishbosheth as king in Israel, while David was accepted as king in Judah.
