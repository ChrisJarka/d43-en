===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Nevertheless for David’s sake**  - AT "In spite of this, because of Yahweh's promise to David"
  * **what was right in his eyes**  - Here "eyes" indicates that Yahweh saw and approved of David's actions. AT: "right according to Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **for all the days of his life**  - AT: "as long as David lived" or "for all of David's life" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche|Synecdoche]])
  * **all the days of Abijah's life**  - AT: "as long as Abijah lived" or "for all of Abijah's life"
