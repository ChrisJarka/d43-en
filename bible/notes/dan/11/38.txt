===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **god of fortresses** - This phrase describes a god who is famous for defending or attacking fortresses. (See: Synecdoche)
  * **acknowledge**  - In this case, the meaning of this term is to agree that a person holds a particular position of power.
  * **attack the strongest fortresses** - "defend his fortresses" (UDB) or "build strong fortresses"
  * **He will give honor to whoever acknowledges him.**  - The "He" refers to the king. AT: "The king will give honor to whoever acknowledges him." (See: First, Second, or Third Person)
  * **distribute the land for a price**  - There are two options for the meaning of this phrase. The ULB leaves both options open. UDB has "will reward them and give them land". AT: "sells the land for a price."
