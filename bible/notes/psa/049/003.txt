===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **My mouth will speak wisdom -**  The words from my mouth will help you to be wise.
  * **the meditation of my heart -**  The things that I think about and believe.
  * **I will incline my ear to a parable; **- The psalmist will bend his ear to listen and hear what God desires to be told.
  * **iniquity surrounds me at my heels? **  - "iniquity surrounds me" (See: Metonymy) (See: Personification) Iniquity refers to evil.
  * **Why should I fear the days of evil, when iniquity surrounds me at my heels? **  - AT: "I am not afraid of trouble, when iniquity surrounds me?" (See: Rhetorical Question)
