===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **while all the people **- This can also be "when all the people." All the people refer to those present with John.
    * **heaven opened up **- That is "the sky opened up." This is not just the clearing of the clouds.
    * **in bodily form like a dove **- That is, "in physical form looking like a dove." A dove is <font 13px background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);/ ;;#000000;;rgb(34, 34, 34) font-family: 'Lucida Grande', Verdana, Lucida, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13px; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);>similar to a pigeon, a small, gentle bird often used in the temple sacrifices and for food.</font> 
    * **voice**** came out from heaven **- That is "someone spoke from heaven." It may be necessary here to say "God spoke from heaven."
    * **very pleased**- Or "well pleased," indicating God's approval.
