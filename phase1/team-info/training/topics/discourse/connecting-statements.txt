====== Connecting Statements ======

  * [[:en:phase1:team-info:training:topics:discourse:home|Back to Discourse Information]]


=== Purpose ===

Connecting statements serve two purposes.
  - They link the current chunk of text the previous chunk 
  - They link the current chunk of text back to the discourse.

=== Examples ===

Matthew 5-7
  * 5:1
  * 5:5
  * 5:9
  * 5:11
  * 5:13
  * 5:15
