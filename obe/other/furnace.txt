====== furnace ======

===== Facts: =====

A furnace was a very large oven used for heating object.

  * In ancient times, most furnaces were used for heating metals,  such as gold or silver, and refining them.
  * Furnaces were also used in the making of pottery.
  * Sometimes a furnace is referred to figuratively to explain that something is very hot.
