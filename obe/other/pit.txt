====== pit ======

===== Definition: =====

A pit is a hole that has been dug in the ground for the purpose of trapping animals, disposing of refuse or as a military obstacle.

  * In the Jewish law a person was held liable for any animal or human injuries that occured as a result of an open pit on their property.
  * A pit can also be used as a temporary place to hold a prisoner or prisoners.
