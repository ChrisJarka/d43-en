====== Come Down from the Cross [40-05] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-40-05.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

The **[[:en:obe:other:jewish-leaders|Jewish leaders]]** and the other people in the crowd **[[:en:obe:other:mock|mocked]]** **[[:en:obe:kt:jesus|Jesus]]**. They said to him, “If you are the **[[:en:obe:kt:sonofgod|Son of God]]**, come down from the **[[:en:obe:kt:cross|cross]]** and **[[:en:obe:kt:save|save]]** yourself! Then we will **[[:en:obe:kt:believe|believe]]** you.”


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **believe you** – That is, “believe in you” or, “believe that you are the Messiah.”
