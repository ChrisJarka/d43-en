===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **a man will leave his father and his mother**  - This is about men in general. It does not refer to any particular man at any particular time. It can also be translated as "Men leave their father and mother"
  * **will become one flesh**  - Man and woman become one flesh by having a child.
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **they were not ashamed**  - This not mean that they should have been ashamed. It simply states that they were not.
