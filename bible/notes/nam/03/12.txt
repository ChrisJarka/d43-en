===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Your**  - This word refers to Nineveh.
  * **fortresses**  - This term, which could also be rendered as "strongholds" refers to all of Nineveh and the Assyrian empire. (See:[[:en:ta:translate:metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **All your fortresses will be like fig trees with the earliest ripe figs: if they are shaken, they fall into the mouth of the eater**  - The city of Nineveh will be easy to take like fruit from a tree. (See:[[:en:ta:translate:simile|Simile]])
  * **the people among you are like women**  - This phrase means "you are weak and cannot defend yourself." (See:[[:en:ta:translate:simile|Simile]])
  * **the gates of your land are opened wide to your enemies; fire has devoured the bars of your gates**  - All the defenses of Nineveh have failed. The gates are open and burning, the city has fallen. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:parallelism|Parallelism]])
  * **fire has devoured the bars of your gates**  - "the bars of your gates" are the large beams of wood that give the gates strength and which also do not allow it to be opened from outside the city. Burning these would make it impossible to close the city again.
