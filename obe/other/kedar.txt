====== Kedar ======

===== Facts: =====
A city or cities in in Northern Arabia near the border of Palestine. 

  * A son of Ishmael. 
  * Kedar was known for their greatness and beauty.
  * In the Bible the phrase "the glory of Kedar" is referring to the greatness of the people.
