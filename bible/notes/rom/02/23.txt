===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **do you…?**  - Paul uses a rhetorical question to scold the Jews for their hypocrisy. "It is wicked that you are proud of the Law, while at the same time you disobey it." (See: Rhetorical question)
  * **For **– “I say this because the Scriptures said to the Jews long ago”
  * **name of God**  - Paul uses this term to speak of God, not just his name. (See: Synecdoche)
  * **is dishonored among the Gentiles**  - Gentiles say bad things about the Jewish, based on the wicked deeds that they have seen Jews do.
