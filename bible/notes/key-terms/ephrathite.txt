====== Ephrathite ======

===== Facts: =====

An Ephrathite is an inhabitant of Ephrathata, an old name for the town of Bethlehem in Judah.

  * In the Old Testament, Naomi was an Ephrathite.
  * Jesse the father of David was an Ephrathite.
