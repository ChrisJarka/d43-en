====== Mark, John Mark ======

===== Facts: =====

Mark was one of the men who traveled with Paul on his missionary journeys.
    * Most likely John Mark, who accompanied Barnabas and Paul on a missionary journey.
    * There was a disagreement between Mark and Paul, which was eventually resolved.
    * Mark also served Peter in ministry, possibly in Rome.
    * Mark is most likely the author of the Gospel of Mark.
    * Mark was not an original apostle, but was taught by both Paul and Peter.
