====== Jeremiah Chapter 17 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Where is the sin of Judah engraved?

A. It is engraved on the tablet of their hearts. [17:1]

Q? Where are the people's Asherah poles located?

A. The Asherah poles are by the leafy trees on the high hills.[17:2]

Q? Where does Yahweh say that Judah will be enslaved?

A. Yahweh says that he will enslave Judah to their enemies in a land that they do not know [17:4]

Q? Who does Yahweh say is accursed?

A. Yahweh says that the person who trusts in mankind is accursed. [17:5]

Q? Why is the person who trusts in Yahweh blessed?

A. The person who trusts in Yahweh is blessed, for Yahweh is his confidence. [17:7]

Q? What words are used to say that the heart is sick?

A. The heart is more deceitful than anything else. It is sick; who can understand it?" [17:1]

Q? What will happen to those who abandon Yahweh?

A. All who abandon Yahweh will be ashamed. [17:13]

Q? From what job did Jeremiah not run away?

A. Jeremiahdid not run from being a shepherd following Yahweh. [17:16]

Q? What does Jeremiah ask Yahweh to send against his pursuers?

A. Jeremiah asks Yahweh to send the day of disaster against his pursuers. [17:18]

Q? Which gate of Jerusalem did Yahweh say for Jeremiah to stand first?

A. Yahweh told him to stand in the gate of the people where the kings of Judah enter and exit. [17:19]

Q?

A. [17:1]
