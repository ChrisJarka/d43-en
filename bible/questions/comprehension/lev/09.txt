====== Leviticus Chapter 09 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? On what day did Moses call Aaron and his sons and the elders of Israel?

A. Moses called Aaron an his sons and the elders of Israel on the eighth day. [9:1]

Q? What two animals did Moses ask Aaron and his sons to bring to offer to Yahweh?

A. Moses ask Aaron to bring a a calf from the herd and a ram without blemish. [9:2]

Q? What animals did Moses ask Aron to tell the people of Israel to bring to offer to Yahweh?

A. Moses ask Aaron to tell the people to bring a male goat, a calf, a lamb, an ox, and a ram to offer to Yahweh. [9:3,4]

Q? Why had Yahweh commanded them to do this?

A. Yahweh commanded them to do this that his glory might appear to them. [9:6]
