===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They shed innocent blood**  - Here "innocent blood" represents the people they killed. AT: "They spilled innocent blood" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **desecrating the land with blood**  - AT: "polluting the land with blood" or "making the land unclean"
  * **They were defiled by their deeds**  - AT: "The Israelites were unclean because of their sin"
  * **unfaithful in their actions**  - It is implied that this refers to their continued idol worship. AT: "unfaithful to Yahweh in their actions" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit|Explicit and Implicit]])
