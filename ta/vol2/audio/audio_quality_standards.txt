===== Standards for Audio Quality =====

This module answers the question: //What are the unfoldingWord standards for audio?//\\
===  ===

Our goal, through collaborative effort of the global Church, is to provide accurate, high-quality, audio recording of Biblical content with listening appeal and usability that will last a lifetime. Rather than being production-focused, we are striving for simplicity and excellence that reflects humble respect and service to God, fidelity to the message of His Word, and His love for all people, languages and cultures.
