====== Jesus Is the Messiah [43-09] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-43-09.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

“You **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:crucify|crucified]]** this man, **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]**. **But know for certain that** **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:god|God]]** has caused Jesus to become both **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:lord|Lord]]** and **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:messiah|Messiah]]**!”


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Peter continues preaching to the crowd.)
  * **But know for certain that** – This could also be translated as, “But you can know that it is true that” or, “But you can be sure that.”
