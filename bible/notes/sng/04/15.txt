===== Translation Notes: =====

The lover continues to praise the woman.
  * **You are a garden spring** - "You are a spring in a garden." The lover describes how wonderful by comparing her with good water. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **fresh water** - water that is good to drink
  * **streams flowing down from Lebanon** - Because Lebanon had mountains covered with trees, the streams from Lebanon would be clean and cool.
  * **** - 


  * ** Awake, north wind; come, south wind; blow** - He talks to the wind as though it were a person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]]) 
  * **blow on my garden so that its spices may give off their fragrance** - She is treating her body as though it is a garden. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **May my beloved come into his garden and eat some of its choice fruit** - She is letting Solomon know her body is his, and she is ready for him.
