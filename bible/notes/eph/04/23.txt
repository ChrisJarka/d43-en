===== translationNotes: =====

  * **you may be renewed in the spirit of your mind** - This maybe translated with an active form. AT: "God may help you grow closer to him and think in new ways" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **so now you put on the new man** - A person who believes in Christ becomes a new person who lives differently just as a person looks totally different when he puts on new clothes, having "put off" the old way of living ([[:en:bible:notes:eph:04:20|4:22]]) and "put on the new." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **in true righteousness and holiness** - "truly righteous and holy"
