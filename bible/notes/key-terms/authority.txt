====== authority ======


===== Definition: =====


Authority is the power of influence, control, or both over someone or something.


    * Governments and some organizations have authority over the persons subject to them.
    * A person can have authority over another.
    * Authority can also be translated as "power", "control" or "right".
    * The word "authorities" refers to people, governments, or organization that have authority over others. When "authorities" is used this way, it can also be translated as "leaders" or "rulers" or "powers".
    * God is the highest authority over all.


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:power|power]])
