===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I was born ... I was there** - This is wisdom speaking about herself. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **was born** - This continues to describe wisdom as a person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **established** - To establish something is to bring into being on a stable basis. AT: "created" or "made"
  * **the heavens** - "the sky"
  * **drew the horizon** - "drew a round circle"
  * **the face of the deep** - "the surface of the ocean"
  * **I was there** - "I existed"
