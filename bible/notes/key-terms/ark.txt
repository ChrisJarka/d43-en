====== ark ======

===== Definition: =====

An ark is a rectangular wooden box varying in size depending on it's purpose.

  * In the Bible, the word "ark" is used to refer to either the "Ark of the Covenant" or the Ark that Noah built to escape the flood.
  * The word ark can be translated as "box", "chest" or "case".

(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:arkofthecovenant|ark of the covenant]])
