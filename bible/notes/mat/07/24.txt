===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Therefore**  - "for that reason"
  * **built his house upon a rock**  - If your culture is not familiar with building on bedrock, you could translate it, "built the house on solid ground"
  * **rock**  - This is the very large, hard rock that is deep under the soil.
