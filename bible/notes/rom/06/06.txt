===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **our old man was crucified with him**- The phrase "old man" refers to a person before he believes in Jesus. He is spiritually dead and sin controls him. When  represents man's sinful nature that leads to spiritual death. This can be translated as " God views us sinners as having died on the cross with Christ". (UDB) (See: metaphor)
  * **declared**  - This can be translated as "announce" or "claimed".
