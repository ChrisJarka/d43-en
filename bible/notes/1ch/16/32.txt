===== translationNotes: =====

  * **that which fills it** - The implied information is that this refers to all the creatures living in the sea. AT: "the sea creatures" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Let the fields be joyful, and all that is in them** - "Let the fields and all that is in them be joyful." The author speaks as if the fields and the animals that live in them have emotions like people. AT: "Let it be as if the fields themselves and all the animals that live in them are rejoicing" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **let the trees in the forest shout for joy** - This speaks about the trees as if they were people who could shout for joy. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
