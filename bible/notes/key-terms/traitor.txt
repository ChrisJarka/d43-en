====== traitor ======


===== Definition: =====


A traitor is a person who is unfaithful or disloyal to their friends or country.


    * Judas Iscariot was a traitor because he was disloyal to Jesus, when he told the Roman and Jewish authorities Jesus' location, so they could capture and kill Jesus.
