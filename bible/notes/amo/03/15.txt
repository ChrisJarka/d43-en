===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the winter house with the summer house** - "all of the houses" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **ivory** - the teeth and horns of large animals
  * **this is Yahweh's declaration** - Yahweh speaks of himself by name to express the certainty of what he is declaring. Translate this as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:amo:02:11|2:11]]. AT: "this is what Yahweh has declared" or "this is what I, Yahweh, have declared" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
