===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **a luxurious tunic that reached to his hands and ankles**  - "a striped tunic" or "a tunic with long sleeves."
  * **tunic**  - "outer garment" or "coat"
  * **Israel**  - Jacob's new name given by God
  * **Israel loved Joseph more than all his sons**  - "Jacob loved Joseph more than all his other sons"
  * **he was the son of his old age**  - "Joseph was the son of his old age"
  * **He made him a luxurious tunic**  - "Israel made Joseph a luxurious tunic" or "Jacob made Joseph a luxurious tunic"
  * **His brothers saw that their father loved him**  - "Joseph's brothers saw that their father loved Joseph"
  * **They hated him**  - "They disliked him"
  * **cordially**  - "peacefully" or "in a kind manner."
