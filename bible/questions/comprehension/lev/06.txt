====== Leviticus Chapter 06 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What must one do if he sins against his neighbor?

A. If a person sins against his neighbor he must restore whatever he took or the lost thing that he found. [6:1-4]

Q? What must one do who lied about any matter?

A. If one lied about any matter, he must restore it in full and add one-fifth more. [6:5]

Q? What must someome bring to the priest for a guilt offering if he was guilty of the sins above?

A. If one was guilty of the sins  above, he must bring a ram without blemish from the flock. [6:6]

Q? How long must the burnt offering remain on the altar?

A. The burnt offering must remain on the altar all night until morning. [6:9]

Q? What must the priest put on in order to remove the ashes from the altar?

A. The priest must put on linen clothes and linen underclothes to take the ashes from the altar. [6:10]

Q? What must the priest do before carrying the ashes outside the camp?

A. The priest must take off his linen clothes and put on other clothes before carrying the ashes away from the camp. [6:11]

Q? What must be kept going at all times?

A. The fire on the altar must be kept going at all times. [6:12.13]

Q? What are the priests to do with the left over grain offering?

A. The priests may eat the grain offering left over without yeast. [6:16]

Q? What must the sons of Aaron do when their sons are anointed?

A. The sons of Aaron must present a grain offering of a tenth part of an epaph of fine flour, half in the morning and half in the evening. [6:20]

Q? How must this grain offering be prepared?

A. This grain offering must be made with oil in a baking pan, soaked, then baked in pieces. [6:21]

Q? Who will offer this grain offering?

A. The son of Aaron who is to become the new high priest must offer this grain offering. [6:22]

Q? According to the law of the sin offering, what must the priest do to the offering?

A. The priest must eat the sin offering. [6:26]

Q? What must be done with the clay pot in which a bloody garment was boiled?

A. The clay pot in which the bloody garment was boiled was to be broken. [6:28]

Q? What part of the sin offering must not be eaten?

A  No part of the sin offering must be eaten from which blood was brought into the tent of meeting to make atonement in the holy place. [6:30]
