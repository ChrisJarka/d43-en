===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **This will be the sign for you'—this is Yahweh’s declaration**  - "Yahweh declares, 'This will be proof'
  * **my words will certainly attack you with disaster**  - "my words will certainly come true and you will have disaster"
  * **I am about to give over Pharaoh Hophra, king of Egypt, into the hand of his enemies**- "I will allow Pharaoh Hophra …to be captured by his enemies" or "I will let the enemies of Pharaoh Hophra, king of Egypt, to conquer him."
  * **and the ones seeking his life**  - "and the others wanting to kill him" or "those who want to kill him"
  * **who sought his life**  - "who wanted to kill him"
