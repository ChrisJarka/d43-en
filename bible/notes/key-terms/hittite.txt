====== Hittite ======

===== Definition: =====

The Hittites were descendants of Ham through his son Canaan. By 1600 B.C. the Hittites occupied most of Europe and Palestine. They were often a threat to Israel both physically and spiritually.

  * Abraham negotiated with Ephron the Hittite for a piece of property so that he could bury his deceased wife, Sarah in a cave there.
  * Esau's parents were grieved when he married two Hittite women.
  * One of David's mighty men of valor was named Uriah the Hittite.
  * Some of the foreign women in Solomon's harem were Hittites. These women turned Solomon's heart away from God because of the false gods they worshiped.

(See also: Esau, Uriah, other)
