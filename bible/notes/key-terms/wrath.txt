====== wrath ======


===== Definition: =====


Wrath is a visible expression of anger. When used in the Bible it usually refers to God's wrath. 


    * God's wrath is the just penalty for those who do not repent of their sin. It can be displayed in this life in events such as Sodom and Gomorrah or eternal punishment in hell.
    * A King or other Ruler can also display wrath by punishing law breakers.
