====== unholy ======

===== Definition: =====

Literally, the term "unholy" means "not holy." It refers to something or someone that does not honor God.

  * This word is used to describe someone who dishonors God by rebelling against him.
  * Something that is unholy could be described as being common or profane.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * Ways to translate this term could include, "not belonging to God" or "not honoring to God" or "not godly."

(See also: common, profane, holy, holiness other)
