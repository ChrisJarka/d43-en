===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **mandrakes**- This plant is a short-stemmed herb associated with fertility. (See: Idiom)
  * **at the door**- This expression means close by or a part of our daily life. (See: Idiom)
  * **choice fruits, new and old**- In their married life the couple are celebrating all aspects of a loving relationship. (See: Metaphor)
