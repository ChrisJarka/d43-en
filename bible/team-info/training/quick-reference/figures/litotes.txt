====== Litotes ======


===== Description =====

Litotes is a strong statement made by negative phrases to strengthen a positive meaning. Litotes are made to say that the opposite idea is not true.

=== Luk:11:04 ===

"Forgive us our sins, for we ourselves forgive everyone who is in debt to us. Do not lead us into temptation."

  * **Do not lead us into temptation**  - **(4)** "Lead us away from temptation." **(5)** (See: [[:en:ta:translate:litotes]])

=== Gal:04:12 ===

"I beg you, brothers, become as I am, for I also have become as you are. You did me no wrong,"

  * **You did me no wrong**  - **(4)** "You treated me very well" or "You treated me as you should have." **(5)** (See: [[:en:ta:translate:litotes]])

=== Act:10:47 ===

"Can anyone keep water from these people so they should not be baptized, these people who have received the Holy Spirit as well as we?"

  * **Can anyone keep water from these people so they should not be baptized**  - **(3)** Peter is making a negative statement in the form of a Rhetorical question to emphasize that these people deserve to be baptized. **(4)** This can be translated as "Let these people be baptized."  **(5)** (See: [[:en:ta:translate:litotes]] and [[:en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]])
