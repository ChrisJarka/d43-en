===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **therefore - ** means, because of this..
  * **you shepherds - **  refers to the leaders of Israel
  * **hear the word of Jehovah **- Alternate translation, "listen to this message from Yahweh"or "listen to what I, Yahweh, say." (UDB)
  * **behold**  - (See standard Notes) Listen to what is about to be said.
  * **cease feeding the sheep - **  Alternate translation, "stopped taking care of the people of Israel".
