===== Translation Notes: =====

Job continues to speak.
  * **The righteous man will keep to his way** - "The righteous man will hold firmly to his way." The phrase "his way" refers to the actions of the righteous man. A righteous person continues to do what is right even when bad things happen to him.
  * **he who has clean hands** - The phrase "clean hands" is used to say that a person had done nothing wrong. AT: "he who does what is right" or "he who is innocent." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]) 
  * **will grow stronger and stronger** - It is implied that the man becomes even more resolved to do what is right. AT: "will continue to bravely do what is right." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **you all** - Job is speaking to his friends, Eliphaz, Bildad, and Zophar.
  * **come on now** - AT: "continue to argue." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
