===== with Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to teach his disciples. This event began in 5:1.

  * **Do not judge**  - Jesus is speaking to His disciples.
  * **and you will not be judged**  - The "you" in these verses refers mainly to the disciples, but other people in the crowd may have been listening.
  * **For with the judgment you judge**  -** "**If you condemn other people" (UBD) or "If you judge others"
  * **you will be judged **- "God will condemn you" (UDB)
  * **with the measure you use**- " if you condemn others, you will be condemned" (UDB) or "What you require of others, is the way you will be judged." (or punished)
