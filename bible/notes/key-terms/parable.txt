====== parable ======


===== Definition: A parable is a story that can be used to hide or reveal truth.  Jesus used parabolic language to reveal truth to his disciples, but he hid truth from unbelieving scribes and Pharisees.  An excellent use of parable occurs in 2 Samuel 12:1-7. =====
