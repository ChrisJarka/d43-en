===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The light of Israel**- This is Yahweh.
  * **will become a fire**- Yahweh's wrath is compared to a fire as we've seen in verse 16. This can be translated as "will become like a fire bringing destruction." (See: Simile)
  * **her Holy One a flame**- This refers to the people of Israel being compared to a flame and can be translated as "The people of Israel will be a part of this destruction with Yahweh."
  * **his thorns and briers**- The thorns and briers are the soldiers of the Assyrian army.
  * **Yahweh will consume the glory of his forest and of his fruitful land, soul and body**- This can be translated as "Yahweh will completely destroy the glorious farmlands,"
