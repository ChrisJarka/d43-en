====== Psalms Chapter 138 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? How will David give thanks to Yahweh and why?

A. He will gvie thanks with his whole heart, sing praises, and bow down, because of Yahweh's covenant faithfulness and trustworthiness. [138:1-2]

Q? What did Yahweh do for David when he called to him?

A. Yahweh answered him; encouraged and strengthened his soul. [138:3]

Q? What will the kings of the earth do?

A. They will give thanks for Yahweh's words, and sing of his deeds. [138:4-5]

Q? How does David describe Yahweh?

A. David says Yahweh is high, he cares for the lowly, knows the proud from afar off, he will preserve life in danger, he will reach out his hand again the enemies, his riight hand will save David, and he will be with David to the end.
