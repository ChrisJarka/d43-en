====== divine ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "divine" refers to anything pertaining to or related to God: Father, Son and Holy Spirit.

  * In Peter's second letter the term refers to Jesus Christ, who is God the Son.
  * Some ways this term is used include, "divine authority," "divine judgment," and "divine nature."

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * When describing something, the term "divine" could also be translated as, "from God" or "pertaining to God."
  * The verb "to divine" something is a different word. It means to try to figure out things in the spirit world. It is related to the word, "divination."

(See also: divination other)
