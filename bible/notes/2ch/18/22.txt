===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Now see** - "Indeed." The word "see" here adds emphasis to what follows. 
  * **has put a lying spirit in the mouth of these prophets of yours** - Here the word "spirit" refers to the attitudes of the prophets and the words "the mouth" represents what they will say. AT: "has caused your prophets to speak lies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
