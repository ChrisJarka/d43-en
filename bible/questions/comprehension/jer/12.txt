====== Jeremiah Chapter 12 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What is Jeremiah's complaint about the faithless people?

A. Jeremiah says that all the faithless people are successful. [12:1]

Q? Why does Jeremiah say that the plants in every field wither?

A. The plants in every field wither because of the wickedness of its inhabitants. [12:4]

Q? What family members have betrayed and loudly denounced Jeremiah?

A. Jeremiah's brothers and his fatherâ€™s family have betrayed him and have loudly denounced him. [12:6]

Q? To whom does the text say my own beloved people have been given?

A. The text says, "I have given my own beloved people into her enemies' hands." [12:7]

Q? To what have the shepherds turned the delightful portion?

A. The shepherds turned my delightful portion into a wilderness, a desolation. [12:10]

Q? Against what places have the destoyers come?

A. Destroyers have come against all the bare places in the wilderness. [12:12]

Q? Why should the workers be ashamed of their gain?

A. Workers are told to be ashamed of their gain because of Yahweh's anger. [12:13]

Q? What will happen to the house of Judah when Yahweh has compassion on them?

A. Yahweh will have compassion on the house of Judah and bring them back. [12:15]

Q? What is Yahweh's promise to those nations who learn to swear, "As Yahweh lives?"

A. If those nations learn to swear "As Yahweh lives" then they will be built up in the midst of Yahweh's people. [12:16]

Q? What will happen if those nations do not listen?

A. But if any of those nations do not listen, then Yahweh will uproot that nation. [12:17]
