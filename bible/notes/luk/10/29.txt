===== Translation Notes: =====

    * (Jesus continues telling his story.)
    * **But he, desiring to justify himself, said"** This could also be translated as "But he wanted to find a way to justify himself, so he said.
    * **desiring to justify himself**  - This could also be translated as, "wanting to save face", or, "wanting to appear wise"
    * **Answering, Jesus said**  - This could also be translated as, "In response, Jesus said", or, "To answer, Jesus said", or, "As an answer, Jesus said" or "In response, Jesus told him this story."
    * **he fell among robbers**  - That is, "robbers caught him", or, "he found himself surrounded by robbers", or "he was ambushed by robbers"
