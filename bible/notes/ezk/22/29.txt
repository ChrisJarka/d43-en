===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **people of the land** - the people of Israel.
    * **extortion** - using authority to take money from others.
    * **plundered**  - using force to steal from others.
    * **oppress the foreigner without justice**  - "hire foreigners for an unfair wage because they government doesn't protect them"
