===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **barley cakes- ** the grain barley baked as flat bread
  * **dung of human excrement- ** human feces
  * **children of Israel **- s
  * **house of Israel** - See the note there and how you translated "house of Israel" in verses 4:3 & 4.
  * **among the nations where I will banish them ** - in the place of Israel's captivity.
  * **banish ** - "cast out"
