====== Psalms Chapter 141 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What does David ask of Yahweh?

A. David asks Yahweh to come quickly and listen to him when he calls. [142:1]

Q? What does David want his prayer and lifted hands be to Yahweh?

A. David wants his prayer to be like incense before Yahweh and his lifted hands like the evening sacrifice. [142:2]
