===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **fulfilled everything he was saying**  - Or, "finished speaking."
    * **in the hearing of the people **- This could also be translated as “to the people who were listening” or “to the people” or “for the people to hear.” Jesus spoke with a voice loud enough for those surrounding Him to hear the truth.
    * **city of Capernaum **- Or, "Capernaum."
