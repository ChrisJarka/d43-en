====== 1 Samuel Chapter 06 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? For what period of time was the ark of Yahweh in the Philistine's country?

A. The ark was in the country of the Philistines for seven months. [6:1]

Q? Who did the Philistines call for to ask advice on how the ark of Yahweh should be sent back to its own country?

A. The Philistine people called for the priests and the diviners to tell them how to send the ark back to its own country. [6:2]

Q? What did the priests and diviners tell the Philistine people to send as a gift to the God of Israel?

A.  The priests and diviners told the Philistines to brin a gift of five golden tumors and five golden mice. [6:3-4]

Q? Why did the priests and diviners say God that had dealt severely with the Egyptians and Pharoah?

A. They said that God had dealt severly with the Egytians and Pharoah because they had hardened their hearts. [6:6]
