===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **let me fall into Yahweh's hands**  - David prefers the third choice that God has offered, that Yahweh would punish him directly with his hands. AT: "I choose to have Yahweh himself punish me" (See: Metonymy)
    * and he was grieved** - That is, Yahweh felt badly about the plague he had sent, killing people.
    * **Now draw back your hand.**  - God is telling the angel to stop making the people of Jerusalem sick with the plague, which punishment is symbolized by the angel's hand. (See: Metonymy)
    * **Ornan the Jebusite**  - This man is known by two spellings of his name, Ornan (ULB) and Araunah (UDB, following the spelling found in 2 Sam 24).
