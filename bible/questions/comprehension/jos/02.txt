====== Joshua Chapter 02 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Where did the two spys from Shittim sent by Joshua find lodging?

A. The two spys from Shittiim sent by Joshua found lodging at the house of a prostitute named Rahab. [2:1]

Q? What did Rahab do with the spys when the King's men came looking for them?

A. When the King' men came to Rahab looking for the spys, she had hid them. [2:4]

Q? What did Rahab say to the King's men?

A. Rahab said to the King's men that the spies had been there but had already left. [2:5]

Q? Why did Rahab say to the spys that she had hid them.

A. Rahab said to the spys that she had hid them because she believed that Yahweh had given them the land. [2:9]

Q? What were the reasons that Rahab gave the spys for believing that Yahweh would give them the land?

A. Rahab said to the spys that she believed Yahweh had given them the land because the waters of the Dead Sea had dried up allowing their escape from Egypt and their destruction of the Amorite kings. [2:10]

Q? What did Rahab ask the spys to do?

A. Rahab ask the spys to show mercy and to spare she and her family when they came to take the land. [2:12-13]

Q? What did the spys promise to Rahab?

A. The spys promised Rahab that they would be merciful and faithful to her if she did not tell of their business. [2:14]

Q? What did the spys ask Rahab to do to be safe?

A. The spys ask Rahab to put a scarlet rope in her window and gather her entire family in her house. [2:18]

Q? What did the spys report to Joshua when they returned from Rahab's city?

A. The spys told Joshua everything that had happened and that Yahweh was giving them the land. [2:23-24]
