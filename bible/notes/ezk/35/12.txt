===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **So you** - the word "you" refers to the nation of Edom.
  * **will know that I am Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:06:06|6:7]].
  * **you contempt when** - This can be translated as "all of the words full of contempt that"
  * **contempt** - scorn, treating others like they have no value
  * **They are desolate** - The word "they" refers to the mountains of Israel.
  * **They have been given for us to devour** - This phrase means "We can take control of them" or "We can take whatever we want from them." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **I heard you when you boasted against me with your mouth** - "So you boasted against me with your mouth." Alternate translation: "So you spoke as though you were more powerful than I am."
  * **I heard them** - "I heard the things you said"
