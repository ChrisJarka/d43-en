====== Jeremiah Chapter 48 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What does Yahweh say about Moab?

A. Terrible things will happen to the cities, no one will boast about Moab again, their enemies will try to cause Moab to no longer be a nation. [48:1-2]

Q? Why are there sounds of screaming, weeping and cries heard?

A. Because Moab has been destroyed. [48:3-5]
