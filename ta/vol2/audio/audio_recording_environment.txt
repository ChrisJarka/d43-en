===== The Recording Environment =====

This module answers the question: What is a recording environment?\\
==== ====

The recording environment is the physical space where are recording takes place, including all the non-spatial factors and attributes that make up the environment such as climate conditions, ambient noise, human activity that has impact, etc.

There are 2 general recording environments, studio and field, and we will identify some the characteristics of each as well as their advantages and disadvantages in this module.
