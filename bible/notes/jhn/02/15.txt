===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **whip of cords**- A whip is a device used to drive cattle that could also be used for punishment. Whatever word your language uses for a similar device will work.
  * **drove all of them out** - This means "made them leave." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
  * **dove** - This is another word for pigeon, which is a small bird.
  * **my Father's house** - This is a phrase Jesus uses to refer to the temple.
  * **marketplace** - This is a large, open-air area where people would come to sell their goods.
