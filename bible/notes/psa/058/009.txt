===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Before your pots can feel the burning heat, **- (See Idiom) AT: "Very quickly"
  * **your**  - David is speaking to God's people.
  * **vs 9, he**  - Refers to God.
  * **vs 10, he **  - Refers to God's people.
  * **wash his feet in the blood**  - (See Hyperbole) Strong word picture to show complete defeat of the wicked.
