===== Translation Notes: =====

The angel continues

  * **In the first year of Darius -** "In year 1 of the rule of Darius" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Three kings will arise in Persia, and a fourth -** "Three kings ... and a fourth" There were three kings who will ruled Persia, one after the other. Than another, a fourth came to rule.  AT: There were 3 kings, and king 4 came to rule and ..." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]]; and See: Ordinal Numbers)
  * **When he has gained power -** "When he grows in his influence over people"; note that "power" does not mean physical strength when used in this way.
