====== Job Chapter 40 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Yahweh continue to speak to Job and ask him?

A. Yahweh asked Job if anyone should criticize or correct him and to let that person answer God. [40:1 - 2]

Q? How did Job answer Yahweh?

A. Job answered Yahweh by saying he was insignificant and put his hand over his mouth that he had spoken once then twice but would not proceed further. [40:3 - 5]
