===== Translation Notes: =====
This continues the prophecy about the people of Babylon. Isaiah is speaking.
  * **The noise of a multitude in the mountains, as of many people!** - There are some words left out but they are understood. Alternate translation: "There is a sound of many people in the mountains!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **The noise of a tumult of the kingdoms like many nations gathered together!** - Alternate translation: "There is a loud sound of many nations gathering together!"
  * **Yahweh of Hosts** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:01:09|1:9]].
  * **is mustering** - "is gathering"
  * **It is Yahweh with his instruments of judgment, to destroy the whole land** - Isaiah compares the army to a weapon. This emphasizes that God is using them to do what he wants. Alternate translation: "They are like a weapon in God's hand that he will use to judge and destroy the whole land." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
