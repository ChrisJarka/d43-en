===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But he looked at them**  - That is, Jesus looked at the priests, scribes, and elders.
  * **What does this scripture mean** - This was a rhetorical question. Jesus used it to get the people to think about the meaning of the scripture he was about to quote.  (See: [[en:ta:figuresofspeech:rhetoricalquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **builders**  - That is, "The people who are putting up the structure." or "The men who are selecting stones for the walls." or "the professional construction workers."
  * **cornerstone**  - A stone in a visibly prominent and structurally important place.
