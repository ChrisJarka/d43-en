===== UDB: =====

<usfm>
\v 25 God showed that Christ was the one who would atone for our sins with the blood that flowed from his body when he died. God redeems us because of our trusting in Christ's having died for us. God wanted to show that he acts justly. He wanted to do that because, before Christ came, God did not punish everyone who sinned. So it seemed as though he was not being just. But he was overlooking their sins during that time,
\v 26 because he is patient. God arranged for Christ to die for us. By doing that, he now shows that he is just, and he shows that he is justly able to erase the record of sins for everyone who trusts in Jesus.
