===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You must take**  - The word "You" refers to Aaron, the High Priest.
  * **ephah**  - (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicalvolume|biblical volume]])
  * **loaves of bread**  - The loaves of bread are symbolic pointing to Jesus Christ, the bread of life.
