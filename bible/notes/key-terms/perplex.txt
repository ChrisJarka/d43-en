====== perplexed ======

===== Definition: =====

To be perplexed is to be unable to understand an idea or event that does not seem to make any sense.

  * Paul felt "perplexed" when he heard that the Galatians were
  * Other ways to translate "perplexed" could include "confused" or "bewildered" or "troubled".
