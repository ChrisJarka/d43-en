===== Biblical Imagery - Body Parts and Human Qualities =====


Biblical imagery makes use of concepts to represent other concepts.

== The concept of SERVANT represents ANYTHING THAT MOTIVATES US TO ACT == 
(see also Personification)

  * **No one can serve two masters, for either he will hate the one and love the other, or else he will be devoted to one and despise the other. You cannot serve God and wealth.** Matthew 6:24

  * **so also my word will be that goes from my mouth: it will not return to me uselessly, but it will accomplish that which I wish, and it will succeed in that for which I sent it.** Isaiah 55:11
  * Here God speaks of his message as a servant. The message goes out and does what he sent it to do.

== The concept of SON OF X shares THE QUALITIES OF X ==

  * **No son of wickedness will oppress him.**  Ps. 89:22b (ULB)
    * Another way of saying this: "No wicked person will oppress him"

== The concept NOSE (OR NOSTRILS) represents ANGER ==

  * **Then…the foundations of the world were exposed at your battle cry, Yahweh—at the blast of the breath of your nostrils**  . Psalms 18:15 (ULB)
    * "Then…the foundations of the world were exposed at your battle cry, Yahweh—when you showed your powerful anger"
  * **By the blast of your nostrils the waters were piled up….**  Exodus 15:8 (ULB)
    * In your great anger you made the waters pile up.
  * **Smoke went up from out of his nostrils, and blazing fire came out of his mouth…. **  2 Samuel 22:9 (ULB)
  * **…This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration: 'My fury will arise in my nostrils!'**  Ezekiel 38:18 (ULB)
== The concept of RAISED EYES represents ARROGANCE ==

  * **You bring down those with proud, uplifted eyes.**  Psalms 18:27 (ULB)
    * "You bring down those whose raised eyebrows show how proud they are"
== HAND REPRESENTS AGENCY OR POWER ==

  * **Yahweh has burst through my enemies by my hand like a bursting flood of water.**  1 Chron. 14:11 (ULB)
    * Yahweh has used me to burst through my enemies like a bursting food of water."
  * **Your hand will seize all your enemies; your right hand will seize those who hate you.** Ps. 21:8 (ULB)
    * "By your power you will seize all your enemies; By your power you will seize those who hate you."
  * **Look, Yahweh’s hand is not so short that it cannot save**. Isa. 59:1 (ULB)
== FACE REPRESENTS PRESENCE ==

  * **He does not hide his face from them.** Ps. 22:24 (ULB)
    * "He does not go away from them."
  * **Why do you hide your face and forget our affliction and our oppression?**  Ps. 44:24 (ULB)
  * **Your sins have made him hide his face from you and from hearing you.** Isa. 59:2 (ULB)
  * **Through all their suffering, he suffered too, and the angel from his presence**  (Hebrew: //face//) **saved them.**  Isa. 63:9 (ULB)
== THE BODY REPRESENTS A GROUP OF PEOPLE ==

== A NAME REPRESENTS ITS PERSON ==

  * **If we have forgotten the name of our God or spread out our hands to a strange god…**  Psalms 44:20 (ULB)
    * "If we have forgotten our God or spread out our hands to a strange god…"
  * …**May your God make the name of Solomon better than your name, and make his throne greater than your throne."** 1 Kings 1:47 (ULB)
    * May your God make the __fame__ of Solomon greater than your __fame__, and make his throne greater than your throne.…
  * **…Listen now to the prayer of your servant and to the prayer of your servants who delight to honor your name….** Nehemiah 1:11 (ULB)
  * **"See, I have sworn by my great name," says Yahweh. "My name will no longer be called upon by the mouths of any of the men of Judah in all the land of Egypt…."**  Jeremiah 44:26 (ULB)
  * ** "…You must no longer profane my holy name with your gifts and your idols."** Ezekiel 20:39 (ULB)
  * ** For I will make my great name holy, which you have profaned among the nations….** Ezekiel 36:23 (ULB)
