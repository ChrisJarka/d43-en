====== judge (noun) ======


===== Definition: =====


In the Bible a judge was a person appointed to decide civil and legal matters for the nation of Israel.


    * Moses appointed men to judge matters that the people brought for decision.
    * After the people of Israel entered the promised land and before they had kings, God appointed judges to lead in times of trouble.
