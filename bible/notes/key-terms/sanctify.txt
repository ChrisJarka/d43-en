====== sanctify ======


===== Definition: =====


Sanctify, or sanctification, is to make holy, or make make righteous by removing sin.


    * In the Old Testament, sanctification relates to the holiness of people striving to relate to God.
    * Sanctification in the church is a continuous process striving to separate oneself from sin.
