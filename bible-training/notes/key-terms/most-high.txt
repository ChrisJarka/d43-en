====== Most High ======


===== Facts: =====


The Most High is a title for God. The word "high" does not refer to physical height or distance, but to greatness or authority.

    * This can also be translated as, "the Most High One" or, "God Most High" or, "the Greatest One" or, "the Supreme One."
