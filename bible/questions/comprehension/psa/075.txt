====== Psalms Chapter 075 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What do the people give thanks for?

A. They thank God for revealing his presence and his wonderous works. [75:1]

Q? What will God do at the appointed time?

A. He will judge fairly, and make steady the earth's pillars. [75:2-3]

Q? What does God tell the arrogant and wicked?

A. To not be arrogant or confident in your victories or speak with your head held high.

Q? What does Asaph say about God?

A. God is judge and brings on down and raises up another. [75:7]

Q? How will Asaph praise the God of Jacob?

A. He will continually tell what God has done. [75:9]

Q? What does God say he will do?

A. He will cut off the horns of the wicked and raise up the horns of the righteous. [75:9-10]
