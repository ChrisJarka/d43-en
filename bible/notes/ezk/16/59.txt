===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **i will treat you-**  This means that God will make them responsible.
  * **despises an oath-**  This means the people do not think the oath is of any value.
  * **oath- **  This is an important promise to God.
  * **break-**  This is to fail to complete a promise.
