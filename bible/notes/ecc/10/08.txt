===== translationNotes: =====

  * **a snake can bite him** - This refers to a snake that was hiding inside the wall. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **cuts out stones** - This refers working in a quarry and cutting larger stones.
  * **can be hurt by them** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "those stones can hurt him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **is endangered by it** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the wood may injure him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
