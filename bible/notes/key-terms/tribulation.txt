====== tribulation ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Bible the term tribulation describes a time or times of great difficulty.

  * In the book of Acts it states that a Christian will endure times of tribulation or difficulty as they enter the kingdom of God, because we are walking in opposition to the course of this present world.
  * The Bible describes a period of time called "the great tribulation" to define a period of time before Jesus' second coming when God's wrath is poured out on the earth.
