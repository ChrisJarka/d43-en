===== Translation Notes: =====
See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]

     * **father-in-law** - the father of a man's wife, or the father of a woman's husband
     * **Kenite** - a people group who were nomads or wanderers and who had long been kind to the Israelites (1 Samuel 15:6). 
     * **Arad** - the name of a Canaanite city
     * **Zephath** - the name of the Canaanite town that was destroyed by Israel and renamed as "Hormah"
     * **Hormah** - "completely destroyed," which is the new of Zephath after the town was destroyed by Israel
