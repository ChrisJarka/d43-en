====== honor ======

===== Definition: =====

To honor someone or something is to show it respect, esteem or reverence.

  * Christians are encourged to honor others, but not to seek honor for themselves.
  * "Honor and glory" are often used together, especially in reference to Jesus.

(See also: dishonor)
