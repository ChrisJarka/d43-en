====== Pharisee ======


===== Definition: =====


The Pharisees were the premier group of Israel's religious leaders in Jesus' day, representing the common people in the Sanhedrin.


    * The Pharisees, unlike the Sadducees, believed in the resurrection of the dead and taught more restrictive laws and traditions than the Law of Moses.
    * Gamaliel is quoted in Acts as a Pharisee who was respected by all the people.
