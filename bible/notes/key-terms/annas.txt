====== Annas ======

===== Facts: =====

Annas was a Jewish high priest in the province of Judea from approximately AD 6 to 15.

  * Jesus was initially brought to Annas for questioning during His trial
  * Annas served for 10 years and was removed from his high priesthood by the Roman government, although he continued to be an influential leader among the Jews.
  * He was Father-in-law to Caiaphas, the high priest during the ministry of Jesus. Annas and Caiaphas are mentioned together.
