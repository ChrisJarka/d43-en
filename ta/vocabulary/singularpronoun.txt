====== Singular Pronouns ======


**Singular pronouns replace singular nouns, which are those that name one person, place, thing, or idea.** 


Common Singular Pronouns


    * I
    * me
    * myself
    * you*
    * yourself
    * he
    * she
    * it
    * himself
    * herself
    * itself
    * who*
    * which*
    * that*


* some pronouns do both; they take the place of both singular and plural nouns or pronouns.

Information on plural pronouns is here: **[[en:ta:vocabulary:pluralpronouns]]**
