====== teach, teaching, instruct, instruction ======

===== Definition: =====
To teach and instruct is when information is given to other people. 

  * A teacher is someone who teaches and instructs. 
  * The information given could be shown or spoken.
  * In the Bible teaching and instruction is typically a new piece of information that was unknown before.
