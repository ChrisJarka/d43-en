===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **After these things** - "After Jesus died"
  * **Joseph of Arimethea**  - This man is known from the town of his origin, Arimethea. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
  * **take away the body of Jesus** - Joseph asked Pilate for permission to take Jesus' body from the cross, to transport it for burial.
  * **his body**  - the body of Jesus
  * **Nicodemus**  - (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
  * **myrrh and aloes**  - spices and lotions people put with bodies before they put them in the grave
  * **about one hundred litras** - about 34 kilograms, or 75 pounds. A "litra" is a Roman unit of weight.
