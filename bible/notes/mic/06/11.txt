===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Should I consider a person to be innocent if he uses fraudulent scales, with a bag of deceptive weights** - Here as in the previous verse, God points out that his people are supposed to be above decietful practices, and they will not escape judgement. (see: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **The rich men are full of violence, the inhabitants have spoken lies, and their tongue in their mouth is deceitful.**  -  These three passages are alluding to the same thing, Israel has business that are hurting the their own. (see:[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
