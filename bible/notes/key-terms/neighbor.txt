====== neighbor ======

One who lives nearby; the one near or close to,

===== Definition: =====

Text

  * Old Testament usage generally conveys the idea of "brother" or fellow member of the covenant community and much of the law focused sustaining that relationship.
  * The expansion of the usage in the New Testament to all mankind is depicted in the parable of the Good Samaritan.
