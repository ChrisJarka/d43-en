===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **behold**  - The word “behold” here alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows.
    * **mourning**  - The word "mourning" refers to when people show great sadness after someone dies.    
    * **Tammuz**  - Tammuz was worshiped as the Babylonian god of food and vegetation.  In the middle of the summer when the days start to get shorter the people would mourn as if the god Tammuz had died with a six day funeral ceremony.  In the middle of the winter when the days start to get longer the people of Babylon believed Tammuz had come back to life.
    * **Turn again**  - God used this phrase to tell Ezekiel to look at something new.
