===== Translation Notes: =====
Verse 7 concludes the introduction to the Book of Proverbs. 

    * **The fear of Yahweh**  - "The reverent awe of Yahweh"
    * **despise**  - "dislike very much" or "hate" (UDB)
    * **they will be**  - The word "they" refers to the teaching of the father and mother.
    * **a graceful wreath for your head and pendants hanging from your neck**  - The wreath and pendants are decorations of honor, and so they picture the honor given to one who follows these teachings. (See: Metaphor)
