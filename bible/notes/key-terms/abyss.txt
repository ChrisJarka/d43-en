====== abyss ======


===== Definition: =====


An abyss is a large hole without a bottom.


    * In Scripture, the abyss is the current dwelling place of Satan and the dead who are apart from Christ.
    * The "abyss" is synonymous with the words "sheol" and "hades", but is distinct from the "lake of fire", "hell", and "gehenna" which refer to the permanent place of punishment.


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:hell|hell]], [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:hades|hades]])
