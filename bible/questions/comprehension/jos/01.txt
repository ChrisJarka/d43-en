====== Joshua Chapter 01 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Yahweh tell Joshua to do after the death of Moses?

A. Yahweh instructed Joshua to cross the Jordon and lead the people to the promised land.

Q? Why did Yahweh assure Joshua that no one would be able to stand against him?

A. Yahweh assured Joshua that no one would be able to stand against him because He would be with Joshua just as He had been with Moses.
