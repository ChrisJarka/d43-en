===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **say to the house of Israel**  - The phrase “house of Israel” refers to the entire nation of Israel and could be translated as the “people of Israel.”
  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh - **"This is what Yahweh the Lord says" (UDB)
  * **turn away from your idols - **"Stop worshiping your idols" (UDB)
  * **Turn back your faces **- "stop all your other detestable behavior!" (UDB)
