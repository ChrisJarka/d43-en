====== lawful ======


===== Definition: This term is an adjective that signifies adherence to the law.  It can refer to legal conformity: in position such as a "lawfully elected President";  legally permitted activities such as a "legal business"; sanctioned by the law, such as a "lawful marriage"; and finally a person who governs his life according to the dictates of the law. =====
