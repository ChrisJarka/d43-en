====== compassion, compassionate ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "compassion" refers to a feeling of concern and love for others who are having difficulties, as well as the actions that will help those in trouble. A person who is compassionate has these feelings of concern and also takes care of others.

  * feeling for others, like pity, mercy, concern, love, and kindness.  
  * The Bible says that God is compassionate, that is, full of love and mercy.
  
===== Translation Suggestions =====

  * The literal meaning of "compassion" is "bowels of mercy." This is an idiom that means "pity", "mercy" or compassion". Other languages may have their own idiom that means this.
  * Other ways of translating this term could include, "feeling pity for" or "showing mercy and kindness to."
