====== Japheth ======

===== Facts: =====

Japheth was the youngest of Noah's three sons who are mentioned in the Old Testament.

  * <font 14px line-height: 17.8181819915772px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>During the worldwide flood that covered the whole earth, Japheth and his brothers were with Noah in the ark, along with their wives.</font>
