====== Jeremiah Chapter 29 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who was Jeremiah's scrolls sent to?

A. They were sent to the remaining elders,captives, and to the preiests, prophets, and all the people exciled to Babylon. [29:1]

Q? What does Yahweh say to the captives?

A. Build houses, take wives, seek peace,and intercede with me on behalf of the city. [29:4-7]

Q? What is Yahweh's warning through Jeremiah to the captives?

A. He warns them to not listen to their dearms or to deceitfull prophets that Yahweh did not send. [29:8-9]

Q? What is Yahweh's plan for the captives after seventy years?

A. He will bring them back to this place and give them peace, a future and hope. [29:10-11]

Q? When will Yahweh be found?

A. When they call to him, pray to him, and seek him with all their heart. [29:12-14]

Q? What is Yahweh going to do to the people who stayed in the city and did not go out into captivity?

A. Yahweh will send sword, famine, and disease on them. [2916-17]

Q? What does Yahweh say he will do to Ahab and Zedekiah who prophesied falsely in his name?

A. He will hand them over to Nebuchadnezzar who will kill them before the eyes of the exciles. [29:20-21]

Q? What was the curse that was spoken by the captives of Judah in Babylon?

A. "May Yahweh make you like Zedekiah and Ahab, whom the king of Babylon roasted in fire." [29:22]

Q? Why did this curse happen?

A. Because of the shameful things Ahab and Zedekiah did in Israel. [29:23]

Q? What was in the contents of the letters that Shemaiah sent to Zephanaih and all the preists?

A. "Yahweh has made you priest instead of.....and plant gardens and eat their fruit." [29:24-28]

Q? Why will God punish Shemaiah and his decendents?

A. Because Yahweh did not send him and he led them to believe lies, and he proclaimed faithlessness against me, [29:31-32]
