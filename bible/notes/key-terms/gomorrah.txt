====== Gomorrah ======

===== Facts: =====

Gomorrah was a city located in a fertile valley somewhere to the east of the region of Mesopotamia.

  * Gomorrah<font 14px line-height: 13.0666666030884px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>is almost always</font> <font 14px line-height: 13.0666666030884px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>mentioned along with the city of Sodom; the people in these cities had a reputation for being wicked.</font>
  * There were many kings at war in the region where Sodom and Gomorrah were located.
  * When Lot's family got captured in a conflict between Sodom and other cities, Abraham and his men rescued them.
  * Not long after that, Sodom and Gomorrah were destroyed by God because of their wickedness.
