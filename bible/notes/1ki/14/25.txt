===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **fifth year**  - This relates to the time when King Rehoboam had already ruled for 4 years and was in the next year of his reign. (See: [[:en:ta:workbench:translate:ordinal|Ordinal Numbers]])
  * **came up against**  - he attacked (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
  * **He took everything away**  - He took with him, everything that seemed valuable. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole|Hyperbole]])
  * **He took**  - Here "he" represents the king of Egypt and the people with him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
