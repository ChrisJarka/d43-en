====== Ezekiel Chapter 03 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did the Spirit give to Ezekiel and what did Ezekiel do with it?

A. The Spirit gave Ezekiel a scroll and Ezekiel ate it [3:1-3].

Q? What did the Spirit tell Ezekiel to go and do?

A. The Spirit told Ezekiel to go and speak his words to the house of Israel [3:1,4].

Q? According to the Spirit, if Ezekiel was sent to a foreign people, how would they respond to Ezekiel's words?

A. If Ezekiel was sent to a foreign people, they would listen to Ezekiel's words [3:6].

Q? According to the Spirit, how will the house of Israel respond to Ezekiel's words?

A. The house of Israel will not be willing to listen to Ezekiel's words [3:7].
