===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Tyre, the giver of crowns** – “Tyre, a city of power and influence like kings.” (See: Simile)
  * **whose merchants are princes, whose traders are the honored ones of the earth?** - "The merchants are treated like kings because of their power and influence." (See: Metaphor)
  * **Yahweh of Hosts** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:01:09|1:9]].
  * **Yahweh of Hosts has planned it** - "Yahweh, Commander of the angel armies, has determined this in order to"
  * **dishonor her pride and all her glory** -"to humble her"
  * **to shame all her honored ones of the earth** – "to make weak those the world honors"
