====== Boaz ======


===== Facts: =====


Boaz was the husband of Ruth, and the great grandfather of King David, in the Old Testament and is an ancestor of Jesus Christ.


    * Boaz performed an action of "redeeming" Ruth and is a picture of the redemption performed by Jesus.
    * Boaz lived during the time when there were Judges in Israel (between the 14th and 11th century B.C.)
