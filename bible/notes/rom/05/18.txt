===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **through one trespass** - "one" refers to the one sin committed by Adam. Alternate translation: "because of Adam's sin"
  * **through one act of righteousness** - "one act" refers to the sacrifice of Jesus Christ.
  * **the justification of life** - "the gift of live by God"
  * **one man’s disobedience** - "one" here here refers to Adam
  * **the obedience of the one** - "one" here refers to Jesus
