===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the word of Yahweh came to** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:01:01|1:2]].
  * **you will say** - The word "you refers to the two men that king Zedekiah had sent to Jeremiah, Jehucal son of Shelemiah, and Zephaniah son of Maaseiah the priest.
  * **to seek advice from me** - The word "me" refers to Yahweh.
  * **See** - The word “behold” here adds emphasis to what follows. AT: “Indeed”


  * **fight against this city **- AT: "fight against the people of this city." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **what you will say **- "you" here means the messengers from Zedekiah.
