====== Psalms 083 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 83 is a deliverance psalm. It is a prayer for deliverance from many nations allied against Israel.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

God should destroy these enemy nations like he destroyed Israel's enemies during the time of the Judges.
