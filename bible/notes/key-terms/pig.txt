====== pig ======


===== Definition: =====


A pig or swine is an animal raised for meat. Their meat is referred to as pork.


    * Pigs were considered unclean cloven foot animals for Jews.
    * Pigs which are wild are called Boars. They have tusks and are considered very dangerous animals.
