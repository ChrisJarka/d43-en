===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Jesus begins here to tell his disciples how he will judge all people when he comes in his kingdom.)
  * **the Son of Man**  - Jesus was referring to Himself.
  * **sit on his glorious throne**  - "sit on the throne of his glory" or "sit on the throne of his praise"
  * **Before him will be gathered all the nations**  - "God will gather all the nations together before him." (See: Active/Passive)
  * **Before him**  - "In front of him"
  * **all the nations **- all people from every nation (See: Metonymy)
  * **as a shepherd separates the sheep from the goats**  - He distinguishes two distinct groups. (See: Simile)
  * **goats - **  Goats are medium-sized four-footed mammals that are similar to sheep, often domesticated or herded like sheep.
  * **He will place the sheep on his right hand, but the goats on his left**  - Jesus will put one group of people at his right hand but the others at his left. Being placed at the king's right hand rather than at the left hand would be the place of greater favor or honor.
  * **He will place**  - "The Son of Man will place"
