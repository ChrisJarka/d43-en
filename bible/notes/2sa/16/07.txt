===== translationNotes: =====


  * **villain** - someone who is evil, a criminal or lawbreaker 
  * **man of blood** - Here "blood" refers to all of men he was responsible for killing in battle. AT: "murderer" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Yahweh has repaid all of you** - Here the man speaks of Yahweh punishing his people for they had done as if Yahweh were paying them money that was owed to them. "Yahweh is punishing all of you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **for the blood of Saul's family** - Here "blood" refer to people who were killed from Saul's family. The king was responsible for them dying. AT: "for killing many of Saul's family" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **in whose place you have reigned** - David reigned as king over the same people whom Saul had previously reigned. AT: "in whose place you have reigned as king" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **into the hand of Absalom** - Here "hand" refers to control. AT: "into the control of Absalom" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
