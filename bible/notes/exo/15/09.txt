===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **sank like lead in the mighty waters**  - This means sank quickly in the water. (See: Similie) Lead is a heavy and soft metal, gray in color, that sinks very quickly in water.
  * **Who is like you, among the gods?**  - (See Rhetorical question).
  * **Who is like you, majestic in holiness, honored in praises, doing miracles? **- (See Rhetorical question and Doublet).
