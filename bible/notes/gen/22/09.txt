===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **When they came** - "When Abraham and Isaac came"
  * **to the place** - "to the mountain in Moriah"
  * **bound** - "tied up"
  * **upon the wood** - "on top of the wood"
  * **stretched out his hand and took up the knife** - "picked up the knife"
