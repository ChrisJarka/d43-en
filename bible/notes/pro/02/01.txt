===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - A father teaches his child using poetry. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **if you receive my words** - Here "words" represents a teaching. AT: "if you listen to what I am teaching you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **if you receive** - This begins a series of conditional statements that conclude in 2:5.
  * **treasure up my commandments with you** - Valuing what is commanded is spoken of as if the commandments were a treasure that should be stored in a safe place. AT: "consider my commands to be as valuable as a treasure" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **incline your heart to understanding** - "strongly desire to gain understanding" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
