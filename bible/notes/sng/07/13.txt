===== Translation Notes: =====

Solomon's wife continues to answer him.

  * **mandrakes give off their fragrance** - "Mandrakes" are plants with fist-sized pale orange fruit that stimulates and is sometimes associated with making  fertile. (See: Idiom)
  * **at the door** - "At the door" means "close by" or a part of daily life. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **choice fruits, new and old** - In their married life the couple are celebrating what they knew in the past and what they will know about each other in the future--all aspects of a loving relationship. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **stored up for you** - "heaped up for you" or "protected for you"
