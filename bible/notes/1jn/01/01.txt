===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **That** - The word "that" refers to the Word of Life which is Jesus. You could translate this as "We are writing to you about the Word of Life."
  * **which we have heard**  - Here the word "we" refers to the Apostles and those who followed Jesus while he was on the earth (See: [[en:ta:translate:exclusive]])
  * **which we have contemplated**  - "thought deeply about"
  * **hands handled** - "hands have touched"
  * **Word of life** - This refers to Jesus. This phrase could be moved to the beginning of the sentence.
  * **was manifested**  - "appeared" or "became visible" or "was revealed"
  * **have seen** - "have seen it"
  * **bear witness** - "give our report about it"
