===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **make known to you everything concerning me ** - "tell you everything that has been happening to me" (See: UDB)
  * **me ** - This pronoun is refers to the apostle Paul.
  * **fellow-slave ** - "fellow servant".  This is the same Greek word that is translated "fellow servant" in Colossians 1:7.
  * **I ** - This pronoun refers to the apostle Paul.
  * **him ** - This pronoun refers to "Tychicus"
  * **you ** - This pronoun refers to the believers in Colossae.
  * **us ** - This pronoun refers to Paul and those working with him including Timothy.
  * **he ** - This pronoun refers to Tychicus.
  * **Onesimus ** - Onesimus was a slave of Philemon in Colossae. He stole money from Philemon and ran away to Rome where he became a Christian through the ministry of Paul. Now Tychicus and Onesimus are the ones bringing Paul's letter to Colossae.
  * **your ** - This pronoun refers to the believers in Colossae.
  * **brother ** - Here the word "brother" is used to address Onesimus who is now a fellow Christian.
  * **They ** - This pronoun refers to Tychicus and Onesimus.
  * **here ** - This is an adverb referring to Rome where Paul was now in prison.
