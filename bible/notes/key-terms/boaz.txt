====== Boaz ======


===== Facts: =====


Boaz was the husband of Ruth in the Old Testament and is a part of the genealogical line of Jesus Christ.


    * Boaz performed an action of "redeeming" Ruth and typifies the redemption performed by Jesus.
    * Boaz lived during the time when there were Judges in Israel (between the 14th century and 11th century B.C.)
    * Boaz was also the name of one of the pillars in front of the temple
