====== Joshua Chapter 10 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why were the people of Jerusalem afraid of Joshua?

A. The people of Jerusalem were afraid of Joshua because Gibeon was a large city, larger than Ai, and all its men were mighty warriors. [10:2]

Q? What did the king of Jerusaalem ask the other kings to do?

A. The king of Jerusalem ask other kings to come up to him and help him attack Gibeon. [10:3-4]
