===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **make you a desolation** - "Total destruction of Edom by Yahweh."
  * **So will I do to thee**- "The Lord Jehovah will take everything away from Edom too."
  * **Mount Seir and all Edom-all of it! shall know I am Yahweh"**  The the people of Mount Seir and Edom will know that Yahweh is strong, in control, and is judging them for their wrongdoing."
  * **As all of the earth rejoices, I will make you a desolation** - “Everyone will be happy when I, the Lord Yahweh, will destroy Edom.”
