====== fill ======


===== Definition: =====


"Filled with the Spirit" is a  figure of speech meaning "controlled" or "empowered" by the Holy Spirit.


    * When a person is filled with the Spirit, the Holy Spirit guides the person and enables him to do what God wants.
    * "He was filled with the Spirit" can also be translated as "His heart was filled with the Holy Spirit" or, "The Holy Spirit lived in him" or, "He had the Holy Spirit" or "The Holy Spirit had him."
