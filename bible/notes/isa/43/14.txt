===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **your Redeemer**  - the one who rescues you
  * **I send to Babylon**  - I will send an army to attack Babylon
  * **and lead them all down as fugitives**  - they will force the people of the city to run away from their city
  * **turning the Babylonians' expressions of joy into songs of lamentation**  - they will wail in song rather than rejoice in song
  * **the Creator of Israel**  - I am the one who caused Israel to become a nation
  * **your king**  -the one who is really your king
