===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The man continues to praise the woman.

  * **are like a thread of scarlet** - Scarlet is a beautiful red color, and scarlet thread was very expensive. The woman's lips were red. AT: "are a deep red like scarlet thread" or "are red and very beautiful" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **scarlet** - a dark red color that is very similar to the color of blood
  * **is lovely** - "is beautiful"
  * **are like pomegranate halves** - Pomegranates are smooth, round, and rich red. The man thinks the woman's cheeks are beautiful and show that she is healthy. AT: "are red and round like two halves of a pomegranate" or "are red and full and healthy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **behind your veil** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:sng:04:01|4:1]].
