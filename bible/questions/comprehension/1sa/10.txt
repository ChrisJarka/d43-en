====== 1 Samuel Chapter 10 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why did Samuel pour a flask of oil on Saul's head and give him a kiss?

A. Samuel poured oil on Saul's head because Yahweh had anointed him to be a ruler over his inheritance. [10:1]

Q? What future event did Samuel share with Saul?

A. Samuel told Saul that he would find two men near Rachel's tomb who would tell him that the donkeys had been found, but now his father was worried about him. [10:2]

Q? What did Samuel tell Saul would happen when he would come to the oak of Tabor?

A. Samuel told Saul that three men going to God at Bethel would meet him carrying three young goats, three loaves of bread, and a skin of wine and they would give him two loaves of bread, which he should take. [10:3-4]

Q? What did Samuel tell Saul would happen when he came to the garrison of the Philistines?

A. Samuel told Saul that the spirit of Yahweh would rush upon him so that he would be changed into a different man and he would prophesy with a group of prophets from the city. [10:5-6]

Q? What instructions did Samuel give to Saul?

A. Samuel told Saul to go down to Gilgal and he would come to sacrifice burnt offerings and peace offerings, showing Saul what he must do. [10:7-8]

Q? What happened when Saul turned his back to leave Samuel?

A. When Saul turned to leave Samuel, God gave him another heart. [10:9]
