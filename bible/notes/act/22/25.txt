===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **thongs**  - these were strips of leather or animal hide
  * **"Is it lawful for you to scourge a man who is a Roman and uncondemned?"**  - Alternate translation: "It is not lawful for you to whip a man who is a Roman and uncondemned!" This is a rhetorical question used to make the centurion change his mind about whipping Paul. (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **"What are you about to do?"**  - This is a rhetorical question used to make the commander think and to order Paul not to be whipped. Alternate translation: "You should not do this!" (See: Rhetorical Question)
