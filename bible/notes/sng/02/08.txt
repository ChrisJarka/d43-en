===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * ** leaping over the mountains, jumping over the hills** - She is picturing his doing this to highlight his excitement about seeing her, but not saying he is actually doing this. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * ** My beloved is like a gazelle or a young stag ** - (See: Simile)
  * **lattice**  - wooden strips in a criss-cross pattern used as a screen
