====== Micah 04 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter continues the poetic form to communicate that God will restore Israel in the last days.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Remanant ==
Verses 1-8 are a vision of the future. God will restore Israel to its former glory. These verses will give hope to those about to be removed from their homeland. 

=== Other possible translation difficulties in this chapter ===

In the midst of the diatribe, Micah speaks word of a future hope and a remnant who will return to their homeland.
