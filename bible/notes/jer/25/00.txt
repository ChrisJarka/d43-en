====== Jeremiah 25 General Notes ======



=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Seventy years ==

Babylon will rule over Israel for seventy years; and then God will punish Babylon. 


=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Idiom ==

Jeremiah uses the idiom of drinking the cup of wine of God's punishment to say that Babylon will destroy all the surrounding nations and then be destroyed itself. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
