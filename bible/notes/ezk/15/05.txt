===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues the parable to Ezekiel.

  * **Behold! When it was whole -**  This word is emphatic and wants the reader/listener to listen carefully to what follows.
  * **when it was whole - ** In this situation, whole is a synonym for "complete."
  * **surely then**  - This is a phrase used to confirm a statement.
  * **when the fire has consumed it**  - Consumed is another word for "ate" or "devoured." It is a complete and total situation.
  * **it still won't make anything**  - Make is a verb and has synonyms such as "create".
  * **Therefore, thus says the Lord Yahweh**  - Therefore is a connecting word of the previous thoughts to the following thoughts. Or, therefore tells one what the following information is "there for".
  * **over the inhabitants of Jerusalem **- Inhabitants is another word for people who live in a city, region or country.
