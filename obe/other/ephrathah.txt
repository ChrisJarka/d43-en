====== Ephrathah ======

===== Facts: =====
Ephrathah is either another name for Bethlehem or it is the area surrounding Bethlehem. 

  * It is in the northern part of Israel. 
  * Someone from Ephrathah is called an Ephrathite.
  * Boaz was an Ephrathite. 
  * Ephrathah is also a person's name in the Bible.
