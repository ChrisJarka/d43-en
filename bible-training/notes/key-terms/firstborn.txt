====== firstborn ======


===== Definition: =====


The firstborn refers to the initial offspring, whether of man and woman or two animals.  In humans, it refers exclusively to the first male child.


    * In biblical times, the firstborn is given a place of prominence and a double-portion his family's inheritance 
    * This concept can also be used figuratively.
