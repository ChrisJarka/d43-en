===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The king will do**  - This is the king of the North, last mentioned in 11:28.
  * **He will exalt and magnify himself**  - The words "exalt" and "magnify" mean the same thing. The repetition is for extra emphasis. (See: Doublet)
  * **God of gods**  - "the most high God" or "the God above all other gods". See how you translated it in 2:47.
  * **until the wrath is completed**  - This is either the wrath of the king of the North (who got enranged in 11:30) or the wrath of God.
