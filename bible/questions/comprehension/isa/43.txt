====== Isaiah Chapter 43 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Yahweh generally say about his care and relationship with and for Jacob, Israel?

A. He told them not to be afraid for he had redeemed them. He said they were his and he would be with them through fire, rivers and waters. He said Israel was special and precious in his sight and that he loved them [42:1-5].

Q? What has Yahweh given in ransom and exchange for Israel?

A. He has given Egypt as ranson and Ethiopia and Seba in exchange for Israel [43:3].

Q? What did Yahweh say he would do for Israel's offspring?

A. Yahweh said he would bring them from the east and gather them from the west. He said he would bring his sons from afar and his daughters from the remote regions of the earth [43:5-6].
