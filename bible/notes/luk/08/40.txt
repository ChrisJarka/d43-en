===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **behold**  - Or, "look!" This is intended to draw the reader's attention to what comes next.
    * **leaders of the synagogue **- There was a group of men that governed the operation of a synagogue similar to a pastor and deacons or other leadership board. This man was one of the leaders of such a board.
    * **he fell down **- That is, "he laid face down."
    * **implored him**  - That is, "was begging him" or "was asking with persistence."
    * **she was dying **- That is, "she was about to die" or "she was near death."
    * **were pressing against him **- That is, "were pressing hard towards him" or "were very close around him."
