===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I have always been afflicted and on the verge of death **  - AT: "I have constantly felt afflicted and close to dying" (See: Hyperbole)
  * **I can do nothing.**  - (See: Hyperbole)
  * Your angry actions have passed over me, and your terrifying deeds have annihilated me. - I am being crushed by your anger and destroyed by your punishment.
