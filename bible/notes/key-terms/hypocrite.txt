====== hypocrite, hypocrisy ======


===== Definition: =====


In the Bible, a hypocrite is a person who pretends to be more righteous than he really is. Hypocrisy is the act of being a hypocrite.


    * A hypocrite wants to be seen doing good things so that people will think that he is a good person.
    * A hypocrite pretends that he does not sin.
    * A hypocrite might criticize others for doing the same sinful things that he himself does.
