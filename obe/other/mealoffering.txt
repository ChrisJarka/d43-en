====== meal offering ======

===== Definition: =====

A meal offering is a sacrifice to God in the form of grain or another type of food. It is different from a burnt sacrifice of animal flesh.

  * The Bible interchangeably refers to meal offerings, grain offerings and gift offerings.
  * Genesis makes reference to meal offerings with the stories of Cain's sacrifice, later with Jacob's offering to Esau, and finally with the offering made to Joseph as the Egyptian ruler by his estranged brothers.
  * Meal, or grain, offerings were sacrificed alongside burnt offerings according to Exodus 29:41.
