===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **he said to them**  - That is, "Jesus said to the Twelve."
    * **Take nothing **- That is, "do not take anything with you ," or "do not bring anything with you."
    * **for your journey**- That is, "for your trip," or "when you travel." This covers their trip from where they were and as they move from village to village until they come back to Jesus. They have to take nothing for their entire trip.
    * **staff-** Or. "a rod or symbol of authority."
    * **whatever house you enter into**  - or, "whatever house you enter", or, "any house you enter"
    * **stay there**- That is, "remain there," or "temporarily live in that house as guest."
    * **until you leave**  - The disciples have to stay in one house only per village or place they visit and stay there until they leave or depart from that village or place.
    * f**rom that place**** - **  that is, "from the town" or "from the area"
