====== father, fathers ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "father" literally refers to a person's male parent. There are also several figurative uses of "father" and "fathers."

  * The apostle Paul told the Corinthians that he had become their "father" because he was the one who shared the gospel about Jesus with them. He also used the term "son" for the young men he trained as Christian leaders (such as Timothy and Titus).
  * The terms "father," "fathers" and "forefathers" are often used figuratively to refer to a person's ancestors. Sometimes this is a general reference, such as Abraham.
  * father of many nations, our father Abraham, father of all who live in tents, father of all who play stringed instruments
  * .... to refer to their ancestors all the way back to Abraham.
