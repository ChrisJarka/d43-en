===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **To him you must cling** - "You must be careful to obey him"
  * **by his name will you swear** - By his name you will promise or commit to doing something. See how you translated a similar phrase in [[en:bible:notes:deu:06:13|6:13]].
  * **which your eyes have seen** - You have seen God do great and fearsome things and miracles. AT: "which you yourselves saw" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
