===== translationNotes: =====


  * **Take a garment of one who has put up security for a stranger** - The garment is taken so that the loan giver can sell it in case its owner does not pay the price owed. The garment is a deposit that will be returned after the debt is paid. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:20:15|20:16]]. AT: "Take a nice garment as security from the one who agrees to pay the loan of a stranger so that you can sell it if he does not repay you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **puts up security** - This means to agree to pay a loan and to give the seller something of value to hold on to until the money is paid. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:20:15|20:16]]. AT: "agrees to pay a debt" or "agrees to pay a loan" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **hold it in pledge** - To "hold something in pledge" means to hold on to it as a guarantee that a debt will be paid before the item is returned. AT: "hold onto his coat as a guarantee of repayment" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * ** Whoever gives his neighbor a blessing** - "If anyone gives his neighbor a blessing"
  * **that blessing will be considered to be a curse** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the neighbor will consider that blessing to be a curse" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
