===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:05:05|5:5]].
  * **Because of what Edom has done in executing vengeance against the house of Judah and committing wrongdoing, and avenged himself against them** - "Because Edom has done what is wrong and took revenge against the people of Judah"
  * **I will strike my hand against Edom** - See how you translated the similar phrase "I will strike my hand against you" in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:25:06|25:07]].
  * **cut them off from man and beast**- "I will completely destroy them"
  * **from Teman to Dedan** - Two cities at opposite ends of Edom. This mean God will destroy all of Edom. (See: [[en:ta:translate:translatenames]])
  * **They will fall by the sword** - "Their enemies will kill them with their swords"
