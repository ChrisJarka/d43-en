====== Psalms Chapter 145 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who will David extol and bless?

A. David will extol his God and King; blessing God's name forever and ever. [145:1]

Q? How often does David bless God?

A. Every day David will bless God. [145:2]

Q? Who is great and greatly to be praised, whose greatness is unsearchable?

A. Yahweh is great and greatly to be praised, his greatness is unsearchable. [145:3]

Q? Who will praise God's deeds and proclaim his mighty actions?

A. One generation will praise God's deeds and the next will proclaim his mighty actions. [145:4]

Q? Who will meditate on the majesty of God's glory and on God's marvelous deeds?

A. David will meditate on the majesty of God's glory and on God's marvelous deeds. [145:5]

Q? Who will speak of the power of God's fearsome, mighty actions declaring his abounding goodness and sing about God's righteousness.

A. The generations will speak of the power of God's fearsome mighty actions declaring his abounding goodness and sing about God's righteousness. [145:6-7]

Q? Who is gracious and merciful, slow to anger and abounding in covenant faithfulness?

A. Yahweh is gracious and merciful, slow to anger and abounding in covenant faithfulness. [145:8]
