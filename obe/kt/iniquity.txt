====== iniquity ======

===== Definition: =====

Iniquity is the human act or thought of wickedness, evil, wrongdoing, or ungodliness.

  * Sin, like iniquity, is the human act or thought of wickedness, evil, wrongdoing, or ungodliness.
  * Sin, unlike iniquity, is an act of wickedness, evil, or wrongdoing against God or another person. Confessing his sins to God in Psalm 51:4, David says, "Against You, and You only, have I sinned and done what is evil in Your sightâ€¦."
