====== anxious ======

===== Definition: =====

"Anxious," "anxiety," "anxiously" are words that convey worry, fear, or dread.

* In the Sermon on the Mount, Jesus clearly prohibits anxiety and urges followers to to be concerned only for the Kingdom cause and service to God.
