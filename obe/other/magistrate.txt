====== magistrate, magistrates ======

===== Definition: =====

A magistrate is an appointed official who acts as a judge.

  * Jesus warned that anyone who commits murder would answer to a magistrate.
  * When Moses was trying to split up two Hebrews who were fighting, one asked him "who made you a magistrate over us"?
