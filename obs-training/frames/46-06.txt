====== Saul Preached about Jesus [46-06] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-46-06.jpg?nolink&640x360}}


**Right away**, **[[:en:obe:other:paul|Saul]]** began **[[:en:obe:other:preach|preaching]]** to the **[[:en:obe:other:jew|Jews]]** in Damascus, saying, “**[[:en:obe:kt:jesus|Jesus]]** is the **[[:en:obe:kt:sonofgod|Son of God]]**!” The Jews were amazed that the man who had tried to destroy the **[[:en:obe:kt:believer|believers]]** now also believed in Jesus! Saul **reasoned with the Jews**, proving that Jesus was the **[[:en:obe:kt:christ|Messiah]]**. 


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Right away** - This can also be translated as, “Immediately” or, “Then.”
  * **reasoned with the Jews** - This could also be translated as, “gave the Jews good reasons for believing in Jesus” or, “spoke persuasively with the Jews.”
