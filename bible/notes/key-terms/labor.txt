====== labor, laborer ======


===== Definition: =====


Labor is the performance of physical work done with the human body. A laborer is a person who does physical work.


    * Labor is any task which uses energy and usually refers to hard physical work.
    * A laborer is a person who participates in any type of labor.
    * Labor can also be used in reference to the process of giving birth.
    * Another way to translate "labor" is "work" or "hard work."
