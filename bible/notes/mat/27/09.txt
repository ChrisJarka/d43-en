===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The author quotes Old Testament scripture to show that Judas' suicide was a fulfillment of prophecy.

  * **Then that which had been spoken by Jeremiah the prophet was fulfilled, saying** - "The prophet Jeremiah spoke this prophecy, and it had come true; he had said" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **people of Israel** - the Israelite religious leaders (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **directed me** - directed "Jeremiah the prophet"
