====== angry, anger ======

===== Definition: =====

Angry/anger is a human emotion of irritation, displeasure, or outrage.  When used as a divine characteristic it denotes God's wrath.

  * There are at least eight Hebrew words which describe anger.
      * When used to describe God's wrath there often are several modifiers as in "wrath and fierce anger"  Isaiah 13:9.
