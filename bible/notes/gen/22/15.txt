===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **By myself I have sworn, says Yahweh**  - "In //my name//, I have sworn, says Yahweh"
  * **you have done this thing**  - "you were willing to offer up Isaac as an offering"
  * **have not withheld **- "have not kept back."
  * **your son, your only son **- "your only son," "Isaac"
  * **greatly multiply**  - or "surely increase"
  * **possess the gate of their enemies**  - "triumph over their enemies"
  * **bold words**  - explanation
