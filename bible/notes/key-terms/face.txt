====== face ======

===== Definition: =====

In Genesis 7, God removing people from the face of the earth in the flood means that he removed people from all of the earth.  In Scripture, face can mean a person's presence as in God turning His face toward His people to bless them (Numbers 6).  If people face each other, they are looking at each other.
