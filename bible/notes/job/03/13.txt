===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For now I would have been lying down quietly**  - The "now" introduces a new topic, as Job starts describing what it would be like if he had died at birth
  * **I would have been lying down quietly** - "I should have been lying down quietly."Verses 13 and 14 describe something that Job wishes happened but did not happen. (See:Hypothetical) 
  * **lying down quietly**  - Alternate translation: "asleep, resting peacefully" (UDB)
  * **lying down** - This is a polite way of describing being no longer alive (See:Euphemism)
  * **I would have slept and been at rest**  - These two ideas mean basically the same thing and are repeated for emphasis (See:Doublet)
  * **slept...at rest**  - These are also polite ways of describing being no longer alive. See previous note.
