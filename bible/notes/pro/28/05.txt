===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Evil men** - People who do evil things
  * **do not understand justice** - The abstract noun "justice" can be expressed as an adjective. AT: "do not understand what is just" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **those who seek Yahweh** - Those who want to know Yahweh and please him are spoken of as if they are literally seeking to find Yahweh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **understand everything** - The implied information is that those who seek Yahweh understand all about justice. AT:"completely understand what is just" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **It is better for a poor person** - "It is better to be a poor person"
  * **who walks in his integrity** - Here walking refers to living. AT: "who lives in his integrity" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **walks in his integrity** - The abstract noun "integrity" can be expressed as an adverb. AT: "walks honestly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **who is crooked in his ways** - "who is not honest in what he does" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
