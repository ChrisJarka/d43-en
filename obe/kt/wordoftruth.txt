====== word of truth ======

===== Definition: =====

The "word of truth" is the true message that Jesus Christ takes away the sin of those who believe in Him.

  * Christians believe that the words of the Bible are true, so the entire Bible is sometimes referred to as the true Word of God.

(See also: other)
