====== bloodshed ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "bloodshed" refers to the death of human beings due to murder, war, or some other form of violence.

  * The term literally means "shedding of blood," which refers to<font 14px line-height: 19.6px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>when blood comes out of a person's body from an open wound.</font>

===== <font 14px line-height: 19.6px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Translation Suggestions:</font>  =====

  * The expression "innocent bloodshed"This term is used in different expressions such as: "bloodshed follows bloodshed"
