===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they will bring up a crowd against you-**  This is a way of saying that a mob of people will attack you.
  * **stone you and cut you-**  This is the crowd will do physical injury.
  * **burn your houses**-This crowd will set fire to houses.
  * **perform many acts of punishment**-This means the crowd will inflict atrocities on you.
  * **in the sight of many women-**  This part describes the humiliation of being exposed as weak before women.
  * **you will be so ashamed-**  The humiliation will be bad.
  * **put a stop to your prostitution- **  You will never worship idols again.
  * **no longer pay anyone- **  The destruction of your lives will be to big a price to pay to even consider idolatry.
  * **I will calm my fury -**  This means that God will stop the punishment.
  * **my anger will leave you-**  God will no longer be mad at you.
  * **I will be satisfied**- God no longer needs to punish.
  * **no longer be angry-**  God is pleased to love them as children again.
