===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **You crushed Rahab as one who is killed.** - "Rahab" is another word for "Egypt." AT: "You crushed proud Egypt almost to death." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **with your strong arm.** - AT: "with your great power." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
