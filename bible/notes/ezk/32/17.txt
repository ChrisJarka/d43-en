===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>the word of Yahweh came to me and said</font>    ** **<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>-</font>    **"The Lord God said to me,"
  * **Son of man****- **  A term God uses for referring to a human. See Important Terms.
  * **cast them down**  - throw away" or "defeat"
  * **She and her daughters -**  "Egypt and women from mighty nations" 'She' refers to Egypt.
  * **who have gone down to the pit! - **"who have gone down to Sheol." See Hades in Complete List of Important Words.
