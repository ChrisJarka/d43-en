===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **"Rabbi, the Jews were only now trying to stone you, and are you going back there again?"
**  - The purpose of this question is to remind Jesus of the danger of going back. Alternate translation could be "Surely uou don't want to go back there because the Jews are trying to stone you." (See: Rhetorical Question)
    * **Are there not twelve hours of light in a day?** - Jesus said this to explain there is still daylight. (See: Rhetorical Question)
    * **If someone walks in the daytime, he will not stumble**  - Jesus says this to teach them that while there is time to do the Father's work do it. (See: Idiom)
