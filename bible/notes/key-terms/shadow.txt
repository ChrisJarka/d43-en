====== shadow, shelter ======

===== Definition: =====

Shadow and shelter are used as metaphors to describe how God protects people.

  * In the Old Testament, God protected Israel by defeating their enemies.
  * When a person is in God's shadow, it means that God is above them and is thereby protecting them.
