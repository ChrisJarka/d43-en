===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **grope - **  to feel around like a blind person.
  * **noonday -**  AT: "at noon," or "in the afternoon".
  * **he saves **- The pronoun "he" refers to God.
  * **their mouths**  - The pronoun "their" refers to the wicked.
  * **from the sword in their mouths**  - This is symbolic language meaning that the wicked are exploiting the poor.
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **injustice shuts her mouth **- The speaker is giving human characteristics to the word "injustice". This could be translated as, "It is as though injustice shuts her mouth". This phrase is expresses a similar idea to "he saves the poor person from the sword in their mouths". (See: Personification, Parallelism).
