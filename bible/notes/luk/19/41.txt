===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **drew near** - That is, "approached" or "went near."
  * **city**  - The city is Jerusalem.
  * **wept over it**  - That is, "wept about the city" or "cried about its people" (UDB).
  * **If only you had known ... the things which bring you peace**  - This could also be translated "I wish so much that you knew ... the things which bring you peace" or "I am very sad because you do not know the things bring you peace." Jesus was expressing his sadness that the people of Jerusalem did not know these things.
  * **they are hidden from your eyes**  - This could be translated as "you can no longer see them" or "you are unable to know it" (UDB).
