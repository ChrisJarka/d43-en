====== Deuteronomy Chapter 28 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did the people of Israel have to do in order to be blessed by Yahweh and set above all the other nations of the earth?

A. The people of Israel needed to listen carefully to the voice of Yahweh and keep all his commandments. [28:1-6]

Q? What will Yahweh do for the people of Israel if they will obey his commandments?

A. Yahweh will cause their enemies to be struck down, establish them as a people holy to himself, make them very prosperous in the fruit of their labors, and bless all the work of their hands if they listen to his commandments. [28:7-14]

Q? What will happen to the people of Israel if they will not listen to the voice of Yahweh and keep all his commandments?

A. If the people of Israel will not listen to his voice and keep his commandments, then the people, their cities, their fields, their flocks and everything they do will be cursed because they have forsaken Yahweh. [28:15-21]

Q? How will the disobedience of the people of Israel affect their lives and their land?

A. The disobedience of the people of Israel will result in the people getting infectious diseases, the land facing drought and blistering heat, and the nation being struck down by their enemies. [28:22-26]

Q? How will Yahweh let the people of Israel know they have been disobedient?

A. Yahweh will attack the people with boils which cannot be healed, mental and physical confusion and cause them to be oppressed and robbed. [28:27-29]

Q? What disappointments will the people of Israel face as a result of their disobedience?

A. What the people of Israel have received will be taken from them and given to other peoples. [28:30-35]

Q? What does Moses prophesy about the people of Israel?

A. Moses says that Yahweh will take the people of Israel and the king who is placed over them to a nation they have not known where they will worship gods of wood and stone. [28:36-37]

Q? How will the disobedience of the people of Israel affect the crops which they plant?

A. They will plant much seed, vineyards and olive trees, but they will not enjoy the fruit of their labors because the worms and the locusts will take over. [28:38-42]

Q? How will the foreigner become more important than the people of Israel?

A. The foreigner will lend to them and become the head while the people of Israel will not be able to lend and become the tail. [28:43-44]

Q? What will happen because the people of Israel did not listen to the voice of Yahweh and keep his commandments?

A. All the curses will come on them and overtake them until they are destroyed because they did not listen to the voice of Yahweh and keep his commandments. [28:45-46]

Q? Why will the people of Israel serve the enemies of Yahweh?

A. The people of Israel will serve the enemies of Yahweh because they did not worship Yahweh with joyfulness and gladness of heart when they were in prosperity. [28:47-48]

Q? What did Moses prophesy about the people of Israel?

A. Yahweh would bring a nation from far away to beseige them within their city gates and this nation would have ferocious expressions with no respect for the aged or young as well as taking all the food from them and their cattle. [28:49-53]
