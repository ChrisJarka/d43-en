===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to respond to the Pharisees who criticized him for healing a man on the Sabbath.

  * **the man**  - the man with the dried up hand
  * **Stretch out your hand**  - “Hold out your hand” or “Extend your hand.”
  * **He **- The pronoun "he" refers to the man.
  * **it **- The pronoun "it" refers to the man's hand.
  * **restored to health **- "fully healed"or "cured completely"
  * **plotted against - "**planned to harm or hurt."
  * **seeking **- "searching" or "looking for"
  * **him**  - The pronoun "him" refers to "Jesus."
