====== majesty ======

===== Definition: =====

Majesty in the Bible refers to greatness.

  * Majesty frequently refers to the greatness of God.
  * Majesty can refer to people or things as when King Nebuchadnezzar calls the construction of the city of Babylon for the honor of his (Nebuchadnezzar's) majesty in Chapter 4 of the book of Daniel.
