====== 2 Chronicles Chapter 06 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? How had Solomon improved in the place where Yahweh would reside?

A. Yahweh had said he would live in thick darkness, but Solomon built him a lofty residence in which he would live forever. [6:1-2]

Q? Where did Yahweh finally decide to reside and who did he chose to rule over Israel?

A. Yahweh had chosen no city in which to build his house nor any man to be prince over Israel, but now he chose Jerusalem as the city and David to be over Israel. [6:4-6]
