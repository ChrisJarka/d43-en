===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then the word of Yahweh came to me and said** - The purpose of this is to emphasize that the author of this prophecy is God and not Ezekiel. Alternative translation: "The word of Yahweh came to me and He said." (See:Metonymy)
  * **set your face against the sons of Ammon and prophesy against them**. - See UDB.
  * **and prophesy against them. **- This is to speak judgment or warning.
