===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh is speaking.

  * **but it was only so they might be cut off** - The word "they" refers to the people of Israel. AT "but as a result of making idols, the people of Israel will be cut off" (UDB) or "but as I result, I will cut them off" 
  * **they might be cut off** - Possible meanings are 1) "the will perish" or 2) "they will be sent away."
  * **He has rejected your calf, Samaria** -  AT: "You people of Samaria, God has rejected your idol that you made to look like a calf."
  * **My anger burns against them** AT: "I am very angry with them"
  * **For how long will they stay impure?**  - Yahweh uses this question to express his anger about his people being impure. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **stay impure** - because of their idolatry
