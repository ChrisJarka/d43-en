===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh visited Sarah as he had said** - This means "Yahweh took care of Sarah as he had said he would"
  * **bore a son to Abraham **- "bore Abraham's son" or "gave birth to Abraham's son"
  * **in his old age**  - "when Abraham was very old"
  * **at the set time of which God had spoken to him**  - "at the exact time that God told him" (This refers back to [[:en:bible:notes:gen:18:13|Gen 18:14]].)
  * **the one who had been born to him, whom Sarah bore to him **- "his newborn son, that Sarah had borne" or "his newborn son by Sarah"
