====== hypocrite, hypocrisy ======


===== Definition: =====


A hypocrite is someone who gives a false appearance of their ideals or beliefs.


    * A hypocrite was the word for an actor in ancient Greece.
    * Hypocrisy is the pretense of having high ideals or beliefs.
