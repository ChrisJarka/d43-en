====== ruler ======

===== Definition: =====

A ruler is a general term for someone who has dominion or authority over other people, such as a leader of <font 14px line-height: 21.7777786254883px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>a country, kingdom, or religious group.</font>

  * In the Old Testament, a king was often also referred to with the term "ruler" as in the phrase, "appointed him ruler over Israel."
  * God is also referred to as the ultimate ruler, who rules over all other rulers.
  * In the New Testament, the leader of a synagogue was called a "ruler."
  * A type of government ruler in the New Testament was a "governor."
  * Depending on the context, "ruler" could be translated as "leader" or "person who has authority over."

(See also: king, governor, synagogue other)
