====== overseer ======


===== Definition: =====

A term used in the New Testament for church leaders and in the Old Testament to refer to someone who is in charge of getting a job done or for those who held positions of authority.

  * The term overseer is used to describe Jesus as the Head of the Church in the book of 1 Peter.
