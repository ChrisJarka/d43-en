====== tribe ======

===== Definition: =====

Tribes are groups of people joined together by a common language, culture, or through legal means.

  * In the Old Testament tribes were groups of families joined together by God.
  * God divided Israel into twelve tribes.
  * The twelve tribes of Israel were named after ten of the twelve sons of Jacob and two of Jacob's grandsons.

(See also: other)
