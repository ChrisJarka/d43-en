===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **he said to them**  - That is, "Jesus said to the twelve."
    * **Take nothing **- That is, "Do not take anything with you" or "Do not bring anything with you."
    * **for your journey**  - That is, "for your trip" or "when you travel." They are to take nothing for their entire trip, as they move from village to village, and until they come back to Jesus.
    * **staff **- A staff was a large stick that was a used for balance when climbing or on uneven ground. It could also be used for defense against attackers. It can also be translated as, "rod" or "walking stick."
    * **whatever house you enter into**  - That is, "any house you enter."
    * **stay there **- That is, "remain there" or "temporarily live in that house as a guest."
    * **from that place** - That is, "from that town" or "from that area."
