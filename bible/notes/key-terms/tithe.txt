====== tithe ======


===== Definition: =====


The tithe is ten percent (10%) of the harvest (or income) given to God.  Today, tithes and offerings are given to the church.


    * The tithe is first mentioned in Genesis 14:20 when Abraham (Abram) gave his tithe to the high priest and king Melchizedek.
    * The tithe is set at ten percent (10%) in Genesis 28:22.
