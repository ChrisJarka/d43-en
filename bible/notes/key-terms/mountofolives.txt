====== Mount of Olives ======

===== Definition: =====

The Mount of Olives was actually a mountain or large hill located near Jerusalem. It may have been covered with olive trees.

  * When Jesus returns He will stand on the Mount of Olives and defend Jerusalem.
  * The Bible records King David walking on the Mount of Olives.
