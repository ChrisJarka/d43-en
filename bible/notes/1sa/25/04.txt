===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **greet him in my name** - AT: "greet him on my behalf" as David's ambassador.
    * **shearing his sheep**  - AT: "shaving the wool off his sheep".   See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]
    * **Live in prosperity, Peace to you and peace to your house, and peace be to all that you have**  - "I wish that you may" is implied in all three clauses.   AT: "I wish that you may live in prosperity, that your house may experience peace and that all you have may experience peace."
