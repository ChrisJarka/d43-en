====== Numbers Chapter 29 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Yahweh say the people of Israel must do on the first day of the seventh month?

A. On the first day of the seventh month the people of Israel must have a holy assembly to honor Yahweh, do no normal work, blow the trumpets, and offer a burnt offering, grain offering and a male goat as well as the regular burnt offering, grain offering, and drink offerings. [29:1-6]
