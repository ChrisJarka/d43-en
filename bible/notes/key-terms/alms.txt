====== alms, charity ======

===== Definition:   =====

<font 13.910717964172363px line-height: 15.547272682189941px; /inherit;;inherit;;inherit>An offering given to those who are poor or who have particular needs.</font>

Text

  * Almsgiving may be in the form of physical charity, spiritual giving or hospitality.
  * Almsgiving is the exercising of righteousness, a recognition of blessedness, and brings reward for those who practice it.
  * In the Sermon of the Mount, Jesus warns about using almsgiving to show off.
