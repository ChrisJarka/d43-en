====== 2 Corinthians 07 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

**Paul finishes his defense and tells of Titus' Return**

This chapter has a very abrupt change: In 7:2-4 he finishes his defense. Then he talks about Titus' return and the comfort it brought.


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

**Final defense**

Paul has never harmed anyone in any way.

**Titus' return**

Things were going very badly in Macedonia, but then Titus returned from Corinth and cheered Paul up with the good news that the church there was completely obedient to Paul and treated Titus very well.


=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

**Idiom**

Paul uses six [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]s. "Make room for us" means accept and love us, "in our hearts" means we love you, "in the sight of God" means God being a witness to it, "set apart" means be made acceptable to God, "unclean" means not acceptable to God, "In the calling" means in the position.
.

**Metonymy**

He also uses a [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] for himself. "our bodies" meaning we, ourselves
