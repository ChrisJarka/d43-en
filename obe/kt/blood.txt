===== blood =====

===== Definition: =====

The term "blood," referring to the red liquid that flows out of a person or animal when injured, is used in some figurative ways in the Bible.

  * The phrase "flesh and blood" can refer to being human.
  * The phrase "my own flesh and blood" refers to people who are biologically related.
