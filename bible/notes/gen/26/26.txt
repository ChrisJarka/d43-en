===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **to him**  - "to Isaac"
  * **Ahuzzath...Phicol** - These are names of men. (See: [[en:ta:translate:translatenames]])
  * **and Ahuzzath**  - "with Ahuzzath"
  * **his friend**  - "Abimelech's advisor"
  * **captain of his host** - "the commander of his army" (UDB)
