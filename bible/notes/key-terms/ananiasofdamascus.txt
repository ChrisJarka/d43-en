====== Ananias of Damascus ======

===== Facts: =====

  * Ananias was a Christian to whom Jesus appeared after His resurrection.
  * Jesus told Ananias to go to the home where Saul was staying to lay hands on Saul and pray that he might receive his sight and be filled with the Holy Spirit.
  * Saul was blinded during his meeting with Jesus on the road to Damascus.  Saul later became the Apostle Paul
