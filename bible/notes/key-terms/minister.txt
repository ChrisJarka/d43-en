====== minister, ministry ======

===== Definition: =====

<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>To minister is to serve or care for others. A minister is a person who serves other people by teaching them about God and caring for their spiritual needs.</font>

  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Ministering to God in the temple included doing acts of worship, such as presenting him with offerings.</font>
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>With regard to people, ministry means either to serve them spiritually, such as by sharing the gospel with them, or to serve them in physical ways, such as caring for the sick or hungry.</font>
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Depending on the context, "to minister" can also be translated as "to serve" or "to care for".</font>
