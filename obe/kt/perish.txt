====== perish, perishing ======

===== Definition: =====

To perish is to die, cease to exist, be lost, or be destroyed. Perishing in the Bible frequently refers to the lost people who reject God or Jesus.

  * John 3:16 mentions that it is not God's will that any should perish, but that all who believe in Jesus will live eternally.
