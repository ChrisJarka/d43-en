====== wolf ======

===== Definition: =====

A wolf is a fierce meat-eating mammal that often hunts slyly in groups called packs.

  * Figuratively, wolves may be people who slyly create dissention and conflict in what is otherwise a calm and well-functioning group.
  * In Acts 20:29, Paul warned the elders from Ephesus that fierce  wolves would come and divide the church.

(See also: other)
