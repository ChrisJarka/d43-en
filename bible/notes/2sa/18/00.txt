====== 2 Samuel 18 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter tells of the defeat and death of Absalom, ending this sub-section on Absalom's rebellion (15-18). 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

David reorganized his army, sent them out to battle and told them to be gentle to Absalom. David's army defeated Absalom's army. Absalom fled on a mule but his hair caught in a tree limb and the mule ran on leaving him hanging. One of David's soldiers saw him and told Joab. Joab went and killed him. When David heard about this he mourned for his son, Absalom.
