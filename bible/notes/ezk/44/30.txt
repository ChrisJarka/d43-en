===== Translation Notes: =====

  * This is the best part of the harvest.
  * **anything from all of your contributions **  - alternate translation: anything that you give
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px; text-align: justify; text-indent: 17.5px; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);/Lato, sans-serif;;inherit;;rgb(51, 51, 51) font-family: Lato, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 19.6000003814697px; text-align: justify; text-indent: 17.5px; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);>** The priests will not eat any carcass or animal rent by a beast ** - The priests are not allowed to eat an animal that has been injured or killed by another animal.</font>
  * **carcass **  - the body of a dead person or animal
  * **animal**  - This is any living thing that is not a human or a plant.
  * **by a beast, whether bird or beast **  - "by any bird or beast"
