===== Translation Notes: =====

Verse 5 said that Noah did all that God commanded. Verses 6-16 give detail about how Noah obeyed all that God commanded.

Verses 6-10 briefly tell about the week that Noah and his family and the animals went into the ark.

  * **came upon the earth**  - This can be translated as "happened" or "came on the earth."
  * **because of the waters of the flood**  - This can be translated as "because of the flood that would come" or "to escape the flood water."
