===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I will multiply your descendants** - “I will cause your descendants to increase again and again” or “I will cause your descendants to be very many”
  * **like the stars of heaven** - This is a simile that emphasizes the number of descendants Isaac will have. It can be translated as "beyond what anyone can count." (See: [[en:ta:translate:simile]])
  * **all the nations of the earth will be blessed** - This is a passive clause. It can be translated as an active clause: "I will bless all the nations of the earth." (See: [[en:ta:translate:active]])
  * **obeyed my voice** - This is a synecdoche that can be translated as "obeyed me" (UDB) (See: [[en:ta:translate:synecdoche]])
  * **and kept my instructions, my commandments, my statutes, and my laws** - This can be translated as "and did everything I told him."
