===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the house of Israel** - See how you translated this in 3:1.
  * **false visions**  - This refers to something someone has seen as a dream in the daytime but it was not from God and was invented to deceive or give untrue information.
  * **deceitful oracles** - "dishonest" or "insincere" prophecies
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration** - See how you translated this in 5:11.
