====== harvest ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "harvest" refers to picking or gathering in ripe fruits or vegetables from the trees or plants on which they were growing in an orchard or field.

  * The word "harvest" can also be used to refer to the actual gathering in process.
  * The harvest time normally happens at the end of a growing season.
  * The word "harvest" is also used in figurative ways to refer to people coming to believe in Jesus or to describe a person's spiritual growth.
