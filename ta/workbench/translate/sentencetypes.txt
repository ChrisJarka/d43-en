==== Sentence Types ====

A **//sentence//** is a group of words that expresses a complete thought. The basic types of sentences are:
  * **Declarative** - These are statements. They are usually used to give information. '//This is a fact.//'
  * **Interrogative** - These are questions. They are usually used to ask for information. '//Do you know him?//'
  * **Exclamation** - These are usually used to express a feeling. '//Ouch, that hurt!//'
  * **Imperative **  or **Command ** - These are usually used to make something happen. '//Pick that up.//'

Each of these sentence types can be used for some of the purposes of the other types.
