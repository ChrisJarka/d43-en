===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Now** - This word is used here to mark a break in the main storyline. 
    * **Jesus said this in order to signal by what kind of death Peter would glorify God. After he had said this, he said to Peter, “Follow me.”**  -  John is adding his own insight. Whatever words you use to show that this is an important part of the story, you can use such words here.
