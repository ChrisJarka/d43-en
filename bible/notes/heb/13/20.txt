===== Translation Notes: =====

The author closes this letter with a blessing, and brief instruction and encouragement.

    * **Now may the God of peace, **  - The author starts invoking and pronouncing a blessing from God.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_apostrophe]])
    * **the great shepherd of the sheep, our Lord Jesus**  - Jesus is our Great Shepherd. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
    * **by the blood of an eternal covenant**  - AT: "with the purifying blood of Jesus' everlasting covenant"
    * **equip you...every good thing...working in us...that...is well-pleasing in his sight**  - This is the blessing the author asks God for: gifts that keep working to make us able to please God.
