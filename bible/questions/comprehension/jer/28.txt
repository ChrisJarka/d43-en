====== Jeremiah Chapter 28 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What does Hananiah say to Jeremiah in front of the priests and people in Yahweh's house?

A. He says that Yahweh says this:" I have broken the yoke…." [28:1-4]

Q? Did Jeremiah agree with Hananiah's words?

A. Yes, Jeremiah agrees with what Hananiah prophesied about bringing back the captives and objects. [28:6]

Q? How does one know if a prophet is a true prophet sent by Yahweh?

A. His words will come true. [28:9]

Q? What does Hananiah breaking the yoke off Jeremiah stand for?

A. Breaking the yoke stands for freedom from captivity from Babylon. [28:10-11]

Q? What happens after Hananiah breaks the wooden yoke off Jeremiah's neck?

A. The word of Yahweh comes to Jeremiah, Yahweh says, "You broke a yoke of wood…..I have placed a yoke of iron on the neck of all these nations to serve the king of Babylon…." [28:12-14]

Q? What is Yahweh's message through Jeremiah to Hananiah?

A. Yahweh will send him from the earth, he will die this year, because he proclaimed faithlessness toward Yahweh. [28:15-17]
