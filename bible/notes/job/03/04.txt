===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **let that day be darkness** - "Darkness" is [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] for something that does not exist. You can translate this as "let that day disappear."
    * **let not God** - "may God not"
    * **shadow of death**  - "the shadow caused by death." "Shadow" here refers to the absence or disappearance of things that have died (see [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_intro]]).
    * **their**  - "Their" refers to "darkness" and "shadow of death" (see [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]]).
    * **let a cloud live on it**  - "let a cloud cover it." This can be made more explicit: "let a cloud cover it so no one can see it."
    * **everything that makes the day black**  - This refers to things that block out the sun's light during the day.
