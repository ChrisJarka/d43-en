====== Psalms 149 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 149 is a worship psalm: the sixth worship psalm in the final seven worship psalms. (Psalms 144-150)

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

The people of Israel should praise God and conquer the other nations.
