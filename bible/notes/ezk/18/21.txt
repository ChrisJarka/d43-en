===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But if the wicked turns away from all his sins **- This means that the wicked does not want to do evil or be wicked anymore.
  * **performs justice and righteousness **- does something fair, right and morally good according to the laws of God.
  * **Transgressions**  -A transgression is a disobedient act that breaks a command, rule, or moral cod
