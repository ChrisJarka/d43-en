====== know, have relations with ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Bible, to know or have relations with someone is a euphemism that refers to sexual intercourse. (See: [[:en:ta:figuresofspeech:euphemism]])

  * Adam knew Eve and she conceived children.
  * In the book of Genesis, sinful homosexual men desired to have sexual relations with other men.

(See also: other)
