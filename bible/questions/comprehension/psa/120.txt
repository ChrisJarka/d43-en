====== Psalms Chapter 120 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Yahweh do when the writer called out in distress?

A. The writer says that Yahweh answered him. [120:1]

Q? What is the writer asking Yahweh to do?

A. The writer is asking Yahweh to rescue his soul from those who lie with and deceive with their tongues. [120:2]

Q? What is the writer asking Yahweh to do to those with a deceitful tongue?

A. The writer is asking Yahweh what he will inflict on those with a deceitful tongue. [120:3]

Q? What is the writer telling the people that Yahweh will do to them?

A. The writer is telling them that Yahweh will shoot them with sharp arrows and with arrowheads heated over the hot coals from wood of the broom tree. [120:4]
