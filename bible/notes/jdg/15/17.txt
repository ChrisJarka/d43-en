===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Ramath Lehi** - This is the name of a place. It's name means "Jawbone Hill" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **very thirsty** - AT: "had great need for water to drink"
  * **fall into the hands of those who are uncircumcised** - The idea of "fall" is to lose in a conflict. AT: "Those uncircumcised men will take revenge on me and defeat me"
