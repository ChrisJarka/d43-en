===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **eighth day**- That is, "the eighth day after his birth." This can also be translated as, "Eight days after the baby was born" or "When the baby was eight days old."
    * **his name was called Jesus**  - This can also be translated as "they named him Jesus" or "they gave him the name Jesus."
    * **which he had been called by the angel**  - This can also be translated as, "the name the angel had called him."
    * **text**  - note
