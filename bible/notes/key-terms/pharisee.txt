====== Pharisee ======


===== Definition: =====


The Pharisees were a Jewish sect of religious leaders who believed in the afterlife.


    * The Pharisees were characterized by a rigid adherence to the Law of Moses, tradition, and the more than 600 laws they created.
    * In Jesus' day, the Pharisees were the primary sect teaching the Jewish Law and traditions.
    * The rule of the Pharisees was very legalistic because of the importance they placed on rituals, man-made laws and traditions.
