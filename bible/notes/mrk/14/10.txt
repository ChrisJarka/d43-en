===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus and his disciples have been at dinner in Bethany.

  * **Judas Iscariot** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:mrk:03:17]]. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **might deliver him over to them** - "Him" refers to Jesus. Judas wants to get money for giving Jesus to the chief priests.  Alternate translation: "so that he might offer the chief priests help to arrest Jesus"
  * **heard it** - "It" refers to Judas' offer to help. 
  * **to give him money** -"to reward him with money for helping him."
  * **to deliver him to them** - "to help the chief priests seize Jesus."
