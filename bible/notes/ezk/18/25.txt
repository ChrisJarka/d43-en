===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Listen** - Yahweh says this as a command to get the people of Israel to listen to him. 
  * **the house of Israel** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:01|3:1]].
  * **Are my ways unfair?**  - The purpose of this question is to let the people of Israel know they are wrong for thinking God is unfair. AT: "My ways are not unfair." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]).
  * **Is it not your ways that are unfair?**  - This second question lets the people of Israel know that their point of view is unfair. AT: "Your ways are unfair!" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]).
  * **turns away from his righteousness** - The phrase “turns away from” means to stop doing something to do the opposite. AT: "changes from doing what's right to do what is evil. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **commits iniquity** - "does evil"
  * **and dies because of them** - "Them" refers to the iniquity he committed.
  * **dies because of them...dies in the iniquity that he has done**- These phrases repeat the thought of a man dying because of his sin to express that it was his fault and the fault of no one else. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
