====== Amos 08 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter is written in poetic form showing first the way Yahweh's people are sinning and next how Yahweh was going to respond.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

As you have seen in other places, there is a phrase used. "This is the declaration of Yahweh". This phrase is used to highlight what God is proclaiming.
