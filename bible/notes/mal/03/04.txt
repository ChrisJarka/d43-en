===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **as in the days of old, and as in ancient years** - This is saying the same thought in two different ways for emphasis.  Alternate translation "as it was in the past." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]]) 
  * **I will draw near to you for judgment** - " I will come close to you to judge you"
  * **I will be a swift witness against** - "I will quickly judge"
  * **oppress the hired worker in his wages** - "do not pay the hired worker the wages due him."
  * **those who oppress the widow and the fatherless** - "those who cruelly treat widows and the fatherless"
  * **and who turn away the foreigner from his rights** - "and who do not let foreigners that are living in their community have the same rights that the local people have"
