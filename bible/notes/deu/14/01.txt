===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Do not cut yourselves, nor shave any part of your face **- these were practices of the people groups the Israelites were to drive out of the land and not immitate.
  * **people for his own possession**  - AT: "people for his treasured possession" or "people for his precious possession"
  * **on the surface of the earth**  - This means all the people that live on the earth. AT: "on the face of the earth" or "upon the earth"
