===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Hamor spoke with them**  - The word "them" refers to Jacob and his sons. (See 34:6)
  * **is in love with**  - "longs for and wants to marry" (See: [[en:ta:translate:idiom]])
  * **give her to him as a wife**  - In some cultures, it was and is customary for parents to arrange their children's marriages. 
  * **intermarry**  - marry a member of a different racial, social religious, or trival group
