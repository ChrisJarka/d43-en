===== Important Terms: =====

**<font 20px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Translation Notes:</font>    **
  * Jesus continues his story illustrating forgiveness.
  * Refer to the UDB for pronoun clarity in verse 30.
  * **the first servant refused; instead, he went and threw him into prison**  - The author uses irony to highlight that the first servant did the opposite of what was expected of him considering the mercy that his master just extended to him.
