====== evangelism, evangelist ======

===== Definition: =====

Evangelism is the practice of sharing the good news of the Gospel of Jesus Christ.  An evangelist is one who shares the Gospel.

  * In early Christian history the task of the apostles was evangelism.
  * "Evangelist" as a church office was a much later development.
