====== labor, laborer ======


===== Definition: =====


Labor is the performance of physical work. A laborer is a person who performs actions of labor.


    * The Bible describes labor as the work which God has given mankind to perform in this life.
    * The Bible states that laborers are to be paid for the work which they perform.
