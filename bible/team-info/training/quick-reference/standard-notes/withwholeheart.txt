====== with my whole heart, with all my heart ======

===== Description =====

The phrases "with my whole heart"  and "with all my heart" means "sincerely"

===== Standard Notes =====

  * **with all [person's] heart**  - This means "sincerely." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])

  * **with [person's] whole heart** - This means "sincerely." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
