====== Psalms 058 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 58 is a deliverance psalm; asking God to deliver from the unjust rulers. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:deliverer]] and [[:en:obe:kt:unjust]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Deliverance ==
Although the leaders are very evil and unfair, God can deliver his people from their oppression. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:evil]] and [[:en:obe:kt:oppression]])
