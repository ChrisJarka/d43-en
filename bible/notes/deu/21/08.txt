===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses continues to tell the elders what they should say when they wash their hands over the heifer.

  * **Forgive**  - The elders of Israel are addressing Yahweh, the continuation of the quote started on verse 7.
  * **put guilt - ** AT:** **"charge guilt"
  * **innocent bloodshed**  - AT: "shedding of innocent blood" or "killing innocent people"
  * **forgiven them - **  It refers to the people of Israel.
  * **in the eyes of Yahweh**  - Yahweh sees and knows what people do. AT: “in Yahweh's sight” or “in Yahweh's thoughts”. (See: Metonymy )
