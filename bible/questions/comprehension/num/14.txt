====== Numbers Chapter 14 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did the whole community of Israel say to Moses and Aaron as a wish

A. They said to Moses and Aaron that they wished they had died in the land of Egypt. [14:2]

Q? What did Joshua and Caleb tell the community would happen if Yahweh was pleased with them?

A. They said that if Yahweh is pleased with them, then he would take them into this land and give it to them. [14:8]

Q? Why did Joshua and Caleb say protection would be removed from the people of the land?

A. They said that the protection of the people of the land would be removed from them, because Yahweh was with the children of Israel. [14:9]

Q? How did Yahweh offer to bless the clan of Moses after disinheriting the children of Israel?

A. Yahweh told Moses he would make his clan a nation that will be greater and mightier than the people of Israel. [14:12]

Q? What did Moses say the nations will say if Yahweh kills the people of Israel in the wilderness?

A. They will say that it was because Yahweh could not take this people into the land that he swore to give them, he killed them in the wilderness. [14:16]

Q? What did Caleb do because he had another spirit?

A. Caleb had another spirit and he followed Yahweh fully. [14:24]

Q? What age of people did Yahweh say would fall in the wilderness?

A. He said that the whole number of the people from twenty years old and upward would fall in the wilderness. [14:29]
