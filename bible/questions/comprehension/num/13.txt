====== Numbers Chapter 13 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Where does Yahweh tell Moses to send some men?

A. Yahweh told Moses to send some men to examine the land of Canaan, which he had given to the people of Israel. [13:2]

Q? What was the rank of the men who were sent from among the people of Israel?

A. All of the men were leaders from every tribe among the people of Israel. [13:2]

Q? What name did Moses use for Hoshea son of Nun who was chosen?

A. Moses called Hoshea son of Nun by the name of Joshua. [13:16]

Q? What does Moses tell the men to observe about the people who lived in the land?

A. Moses tells the men to observe the people who lived there, whether they were strong or weak, few or many. [13:18]

Q? What does Moses tell the men to notice about the cities in the land?

A. Moses told them to see if the cities were  like camps or fortified cities. [13:19]

Q? What does Moses tell the men to see about what the land was like?

A. Moses told them to see what the land is like, whether it is good for growing crops or not, and whether there are trees. [13:20]

Q? What did the men cut down in the Valley of Eschol?

A. When the men reached the Valley of Eshcol, they cut down a branch with a cluster of grapes. [13:23]

Q? How long did the men spend examining the land?

A. After forty days, the men returned from examining the land. [13:25]

Q? Who did they bring back word to besides Moses and Aaron?

A. They came back to Moses, Aaron, and all the community of the people of Israel. [13:26]

Q? What did the men report about the people with homes in the land?

A. The men reported that the people who make their homes in the land are strong. [13:28]

Q? What word did the men return about the cities in the land?

A. The men returned word that the cities were fortified and very large. [13:28]

Q? How did Caleb try to encourage the people to attack the land?

A. Caleb tried to encourage the people by saying, "Let us attack at once and take it, because we are well able to conquer it." [13:30]

Q? Why did the other men who had gone with Caleb say they were not able to attack the people?

A. The other men who had gone with Caleb said, "We are not able to attack the people because they are stronger than we are." [13:31]

Q? How do the men describe how small they felt in comparison with the giants of the land?

A. The men said that in their own sight they were like grasshoppers in comparison with the giants of the land. [13:33]
