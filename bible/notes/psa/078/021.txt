===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **his fire burned against Jacob, and his anger attacked Israel**  - These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: Doublet)
  * **did not believe in God and did not trust in his salvation**  - These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: Doublet)
  * **did not trust in his salvation**  - The word "salvation" can be translated with the verb "save": "did not trust him to save them" (See: Abstract Noun)
