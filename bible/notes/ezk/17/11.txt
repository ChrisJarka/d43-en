===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The Word of Yahweh came to me and said** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:16|3:16]].
  * **Don't you know what these things are saying?**  - This is a rebuke. He is asking, "Why don't you understand?" or "You should understand." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Behold!**  - This can be translated as “Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you.”
  * **her king...her princes** - The word "her" refers to Jerusalem.  
  * ** to him in Babylon**  - The word "him" refers to the king of Babylon.
