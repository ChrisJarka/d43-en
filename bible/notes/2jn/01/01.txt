===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The elder** - This refers to John, the apostle and elder. This can be made explicit: "I, John the elder, am writing." (See: [[en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]])
  * **to the elect lady and her children** - This compares the church that John is writing to and the believers that are part of that church to a woman and her children. This emphasizes that the believers are a spiritual family. (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
  * **whom I love in truth** - "whom I love because we all know the truth"
  * **and not I only** - This can be translated as a new sentence: "Not only do I love you"
  * **because of the truth that remains in us** - The full meaning can be made explicit: "because the truth of Jesus' message lives in us because we believe it, and it will live in us forever."
  * **in truth and love** - "because they are true and they love us." Alternate translation: "because they truly love us." (See: [[en:ta:translate:hendiadys]])
