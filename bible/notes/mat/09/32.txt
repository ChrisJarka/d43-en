===== Translation Notes: =====

The two blind men shared the news that that their eyes were opened.

  * **mute man**  - "a person who is not able to talk"
  * **The crowds were astonished**
 - "The people were full of wonder because it was a miracle"
  * **he drives out**  - The "he" refers to Jesus.
