===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **hearts through faith** - The Greek word for the preposition “through” has the idea of a channel or way, showing that Christ lives in the hearts of believers by means of God's gift of faith.
  * **that you be rooted and grounded in his love** - This is a metaphor that is explained well in the UDB version which says "that you will be like a firmly rooted tree and a building founded upon stone". (See: [[;en:ta:translate:metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **all those set apart for God** - This phrase represents all believers.
  * **surpassing greatness** - This is a hyperbole to express that the love of God is much greater than we can ever understand. (See: [[;en:ta:translate:hyperbole|Hyperbole]])
