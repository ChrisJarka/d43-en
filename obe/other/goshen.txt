====== Goshen ======

===== Definition: =====

Goshen is the name of a region of land that was located in the northern part of the country of Egypt.

    * When Joseph was a ruler in Egypt, his father and brothers and their families came to live in Goshen to escape the famine in Canaan.
    * They and their descendants lived well in Goshen for over 400 years, but then they were forced into slavery by the Egyptian pharaoh.
    * Finally God sent Moses to help the people of Israel leave the land of Goshen and escape this slavery.
