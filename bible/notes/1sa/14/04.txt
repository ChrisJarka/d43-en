===== translationNotes: =====

  * **one cliff was Bozez** - A "cliff" is a rocky height with a steep drop. The cliff was well-known and given the name "Bozez." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the other was Seneh** - This was the name of the other cliff. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Michmash** - "Michmash" was a town north of Jerusalem.
  * **Geba** - "Geba" was a town north of Jerusalem.
