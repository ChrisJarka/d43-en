===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They spoke against God**  - The pronoun "They" refers to the Israelites.
  * **waters gushed out and streams overflowed**  - These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: Doublet)
  * **gushed out**  - AT: "poured out"
  * **But can he give bread also? Will he provide meat for his people?**  - These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: Doublet)
  * **Can God lay out a table for us in the wilderness**  - See: Metaphor
