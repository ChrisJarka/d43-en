===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **I am in deep trouble** - Depth is used to measure trouble. AT: "I am in a great amount of trouble" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
    * **let me fall into Yahweh's hands...the hands of people** - The control of Yahweh and people are described by the part of the body that allows a person to hold something. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **and seventy thousand people died** - "and 70,000 people died" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
    * **changed his mind** - To change a decision is referred to as a change it the part of the body used to make a decision. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **draw back your hand**  - Yahweh tells the destroying angel to stop killing the people of Israel by describing the angel pulling back of the part of the body a person would use to attack another. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
    * **Ornan** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
