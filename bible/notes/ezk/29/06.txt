===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **inhabitants of -**  "people who live in"
  * **Because they have been a reed stalk - **  "for to Israel, you have been a weak ally" Reed stalk refers to a stiff rod that is weak and not usable. In times of trouble, Egypt would not come to the aid of Israel. (See:Metaphor)
  * **the house of Israel! -** "the people of Israel" or "the nation of Israel."
  * **When they seize you in the palm, ****you will be crushed and every shoulder divided,****- ** <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>"When Egypt reaches out a hand in friendship, Israel will be harmed"</font>    The palm is a part of the hand. (See:Metaphor)
  * **and when they leaned on you, **- "and as Israel relied on Egypt". The reference of the word "they" changes here.
  * **you shattered and made every loin stand up. - "**you did not help and greatly hurt her back."
