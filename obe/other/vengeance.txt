====== vengeance ======

===== Definition: =====

To punish or "pay back" someone who has harmed you.

* The Bible teaches us to not take personal vengeance for ourselves, but rather let God do so.

* In the Old Testament God sometimes used one nation to take vengeance on another nation.

(See also: other)
