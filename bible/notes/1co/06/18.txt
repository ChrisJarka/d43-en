===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Run away from**  - "Immediately remove yourself from" or "Get away from" (See: Metaphor)
  * **commit, commits - **  alternative: "do" or "act"
  * **"Every other sin that a person commits is outside the body"—but the sexually immoral person sins against his own body. **- In this instance, "Every other sin" is a particular action; "sins against" is "does wrong against" or "offends".
