====== Tabitha, Dorcas ======

===== Facts: =====

  * Tabitha and Dorcas are two names for the same person in Acts chapter 9.
  * Tabitha is the Aramaic way to say her name and Dorcas is the Greek way to say her name.
  * Tabitha was a Christian who was well known for always helping the poor.
  * She became ill and died but the Apostle Peter prayed that she would be raised from the dead and she was.
