===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **kneading trough **- a household vessel in which was dough before it was further leavened
  * **Blessed will you be **- the whole notion of blessedness ascribed to the agent when coming in and going out, AT: "all your daily businesses will have good success." (See: Idiom)
