===== translationNotes: =====

  * **It throws ... its hand ... It claps ... it hisses** - In these verses Job speaks of the wind as if it were a person attacking the wicked person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **It throws itself at him** - Here "throws itself at him" represents the wind blowing strong against him like an attacker. AT: "It blows strong against him like someone attacking him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]]
  * **he tries to flee out of its hand** - Here "hand" represents the power or control that the wind has over the wicked man. AT: "he tries to flee out of its control" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **It claps its hands at him in mockery** - Clapping the hands is a way of mocking someone. Here it represents the wind making loud noises. AT: "It makes loud noises like someone clapping his hands to mock him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **it hisses him out of his place** - The wind makes a noise as it blows, as if it were telling him to leave. AT: "it makes a hissing noise, causing him to leave his home" or "it makes a hissing noise, like someone  telling him to leave his home" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
