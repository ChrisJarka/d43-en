====== Jeremiah Chapter 24 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What is the vision Yahweh gave Jeremiah?

A. He showed him a basket of very good figs and a basket of very bad figs, so bad they could not be eaten. [24:1-3]

Q? What will Yahweh do for at the people he says are like good figs ?

A. Yahweh will set his eyes on them for good, restore them to their land, he will build them up, he will plant them, give them a heart to know him, and they will be his people and he will be their God. [24:4-7]

Q? What will Yahweh do to the people he says are like bad figs?

A. He will turn them into a disaster to all kingdoms, a disgrace, a subject for proverbs, taunts and curses, and he will send out sword, famine, plague against them until they are finished off. [24:8-10]
