====== commit, committed, commitment ======

===== Definition: =====
To commit to someone is to promise them. A commitment is a promise, or an oath made to someone or something which is binding. 

  * Commit is also the act of doing something such as "commit a sin," or "commit to help." 
  * When a person is loyal and faithful they are called committed.
