====== Sodom ======


===== Definition: =====


Sodom was a city situated in the southern part of the land of Canaan. Sodom and it's twin city Gomorrah were destroyed by God because of the wickedness of the people living there.


    * The area where Sodom was situated was very well watered and produced abundant crops. 
    * Abraham's nephew Lot chose the city of Sodom as a dwelling place.
