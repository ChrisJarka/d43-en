===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **report**  - Or, "word."
    * **spread**  - Or, "was continuing to pass through."
    * **crowd**  - Or, "mob." This word is plural, meaning crowds, or mobs, of people were coming.
    * **came together**  - Or, "were coming together as one."
