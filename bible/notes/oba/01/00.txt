====== Obadiah 01 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter is written in a poetic form about Edom or Esau. The nation that rose from the brother of Jacob. Because of their treachery, they will be punished by Yahweh. 

=== Other possible translation difficulties in this chapter ===

There are place names used from various neighboring countries and places inside the kingdoms of Israel and Judah.
