===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He leveled**** a path for his anger**  - The pronoun "he" in these verses refers to God.
  * **He leveled a path**  - AT: "He made a way" or "He made a path"
  * **did not spare them**  - The pronouns "them" and "their" in these verses refer to the Egyptians.
  * **did not spare them from death but gave them over to the plague**  - These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: Doublet)
  * **gave them over to the plague**  - See: Personification
  * **all the firstborn in Egypt, the firstborn of their strength in the tents of Ham**  - These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: Doublet)
