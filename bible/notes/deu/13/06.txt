===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **wife of your bosom **- AT: "wife you love" or "wife you dearly embrace." The word "bosom" implies a close relationship. (See: Metaphor)
  * **friend who is to you like your own soul - ** AT: "your closest friend" or "a friend you love as much as you love yourself" (See: Idiom)
  * **ancestors**- "fathers"
  * **from one end of the earth to the other end of the earth**  - This is an expression used to include any idol worshiped in the entire world, by any nation. AT: "throughout the world"
