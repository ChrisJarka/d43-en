===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Why are you upset and why do you weep?- ** Jesus asks this rhetorical question to imply that there is no reason to be upset or to weep.
  * **The child is not dead but sleeps**  - Jesus uses this euphemism to express his ability to wake the girl up, or bring her back to life even if she has actually died.
  * **They laughed at him**  - The group of people laughed at Jesus because they knew that the girl was dead and they did not believe that Jesus could wake her up or bring her back to life.
