===== translationNotes: =====

  * **whatever passes under the shepherd's rod** - "Passing under the rod" is an idiom for counting. AT: "when a shepherd counts them to decide which ones he will give to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **one tenth must be set apart to Yahweh** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "he must set apart one tenth to Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **then both it and that for which it is changed** - This can be made explicit. AT: "then both animals" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **It cannot be redeemed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "He cannot buy them back" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
