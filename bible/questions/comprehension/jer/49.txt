====== Jeremiah Chapter 49 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? According to the prophecy given to Jeremiah who should not be living in Israel's land of Gad and why?

A. Molech and his people should not be occuping Gad because the day is coming when Yahweh will again possess the land. [49:1-2]

Q? Why are the proud people in Heshbon, Ai, and Rabbah wailing,weeping, mourning and running around in confusion?

A. Because their god Milcom and his priests and officials will be taken away into captivity and they were rebellious people trusting in their wealth which would not protect them. [49:3-4]

Q? Why will the people be terrified?

A. Because they will all be forced by the enemy who surround them to scatter to other countries. [49:5]

Q? What will Yahweh do for the Ammon people-group in the future?

A. Yahweh will restore the fortunes of the people of Ammon. [49:6]

Q? Because there are no wise people left in Edom what does Jeremiah prophesy?

A. The people are to flee, turn back and hide in deep caves because Yahweh is causing disaster to happen to all the people of Edom. [49:7-8]

Q? What is Yahweh going to do to Edom?

A. He is going to strip Edom (Esau) bare. [49:10]

Q? What does Yahweh say concerning the orphans and widows in Edom?

A. Yahweh says to leave the orphans and widows behind because he will take care of them. [49:11]

Q? What does Yahweh continue to declare about what will happen to Edom?

A. Even those who don't deserve punishment will be punished and that Bozrah (Edom) will become a horror, a disgrace, a devastation, and an object of cursing. [49:12-13]

Q? What was the message that Jeremiah heard from Yahweh?

A. Yahweh has sent a messenger out to the nations telling them to get ready for battle and attack Edom. [49:14]

Q? What does Yahweh say to the people of Edom?

A. He says that he has made their nation of Edom small compared to other nations and they will be despise by people.[49:15]

Q? Because the Edonomites have deceived themselves and think they have safety in the cliff what will Yahweh do to them?

A. He will cause them to come crashing down. [49:16]

Q? What is the horror of Edom compared to?

A. It is compared to Sodom and Gamorrah and their neighbors where no one lives. [49:17-18]

Q? What will Yahweh do in Edom after he takes them from their land?

A. He will put someone, chosen by him, in charge of Edom. [49:19]

Q? What are the plans that Yahweh has against Edom?

A. He has plans to drag away the inhabitants of Teman and their pasturelands will be turned into ruined places. [49:20]

Q? What will it be like when Edom is destroyed?

A. The earth will shake, distressed shouts will be heard as far away as the Red Sea. Even the strongest warriors will be afraid. [49:21-22]

Q? What does Yahweh say will happen to the people of Damascus?

A. They will be ashamed, weak, turn away and flee in distress.They will be confused as to why their city has not evacuated. [49:23-25]

Q? What will happen when Yahweh lights a fire on the wall of Damascus?

A. The fire will devour the strongholds (of the KIng) and its young men will fall in its plazas and all the fighting men will perish. [49:26-27]

Q? What does Jereimah prophecy will happen to Kedar?

A. Nebuchadnezzar will attack and destroy the people. His army will take their tents, flocks, camels, tent hangings and all of their equipment.Terror will be all around. [49:28-29]

Q? Why will this be an easy attack for Nebuchadnezzar?

A. Because they have no gates, bars, and its people live by themselves in holes in the ground. [49:30-31]

Q. When Nebuchadnezzar takes their camels and property as war plunder what will happen to the people?

A. They will scatter and disaster will come on them. [49:32]

Q? When did Jeremiah get this prophecy from Yahweh?

A. When King Zedekiah ruled Judah. [49:34]

Q? When Yahweh breaks the bowman of Elam where will the people go?

A. The people will be scattered to all parts of the earth. [49:34-36]
