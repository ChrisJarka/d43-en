===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the fruit of your body, and the fruit of your ground, and the fruit of your beasts **- 'Fruit' means produce of the ground or offspring of animals. "Fruit of your body" means "fruit of your womb," children. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **the young of your flock** - "the lambs of your flock"
