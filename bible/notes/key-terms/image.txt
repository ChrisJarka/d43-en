====== image ======

===== Definition: =====

An image is something that is like something else, in appearance or in essence. <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>An expression in modern English is, "She is the image of her mother!" This means she looks a lot like her mother.</font>

  * God created human beings in his image, that is, in his likeness. This means that people have certain divine characteristics, such as emotions, personality, a mind, and a spirit that continues to live eternally after natural death.
  * The Bible says that Jesus is the "image" of the invisible God, meaning that He is the "exact representation" of God in human form.

<font 14px line-height: 21.7777786254883px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>(The term "graven image" or "carved image" refers to idols, which are objects made by people for worshipping false gods. This is a very  ** different meaning  ** of "image.")</font>
