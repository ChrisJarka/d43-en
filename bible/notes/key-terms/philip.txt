====== Philip, Philip the Evangelist ======

===== Facts: =====

One of the leaders of the early Christian church in Jerusalem.
    * Not Philip the apostle.
    * One of seven, chosen to care for the needy.
    * Philip eventually settled in Caesarea.
    * He had four daughters who prophesied.
    * He was host to Paul and his companions on their way to Jerusalem.
