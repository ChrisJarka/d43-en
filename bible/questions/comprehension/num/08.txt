====== Numbers Chapter 08 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What was Yahweh's command to Aaron about the seven lamps?

A. The lamps must give light in front of the it. [8:1-3]

Q? What did Yahweh speak to Moses concerning the people of Israel?

A. Yahweh said, "Take the Levites from among the people of Israel and purify them." [8:5-6]

Q? What was done to the Levites to purify them?

A. They were sprinkled with water of atonement, shaved their entire body, and washed their clothes. [8:7]

Q? What offerings where made by the Levites?

A. A grain offering and a sin offering. [8:8]

Q? What must Aaron do so that the Levites could serve Yahweh?

A. He must offer the Levites to Yahweh in front of the tent of meeting and the whole community of Israel and have the people lay their hands on them. [8:9-11]

Q? What are the two offerings the Levites must offer as an atonement before Aaron and his sons?

A. They must offer a sin offering and a burnt offering to atone for the Levites. [8:12-13]

Q? What tribe is to be separated, purified and offered to Yahweh so they can serve in the tent of meeting?

A. The Levit tribe among the people of Israel. [8:14-15]

Q? Since when have all the firstborn both people and animals been entirely set apart for Yahweh?

A. Since the day Yahweh took the lives of all the firstborn in the land of Egypt. [8:16-17]

Q? Who did Yahweh give to the people of Israel to atone for the people so no plague will harm them when they come near the sanctuary?

A. He took the Levites, instead of the firstborn, and gave them to Aaron and his sons to do the work in the tent of meeting. [8:18-19]

Q? Did Moses do everything that Yahweh had commanded him to do concerning all of the Levite tribe?

A. Yes, they did everything that Yahweh had commanded Moses concerning the Levites. [8:20-22]

Q? What did Yahweh say to Moses concerning the age limits of serving in the tent meeting?

A. Levites who are twenty-five years old and more must join the company to serve in the tent of meeting, but they must stop serving at the age of fifty years. They can help their brothers but not conintue to serve. [8:23-26]
