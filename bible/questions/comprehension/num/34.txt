====== Numbers Chapter 34 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why did Yahweh tell Moses to give a command to the people of Israel about the borders of the land of Canaan?

A. Yahweh gave Moses a command to the people of Israel about the borders of Canaan because this land would belong them as their possession. [34:1-2]
