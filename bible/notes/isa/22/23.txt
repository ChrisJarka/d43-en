===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I will fasten him like a nail in a secure place **– Yahweh will secure Eliakim in his position like a nail is pounded securly into the wall. (see: Simile)
  * **he will become a throne of glory to his father’s house **- "Eliakim will bring honor to his family"
  * **They will hang upon him all the glory of his father’s house, the offspring and descendants, every small vessel from the cups to all the jugs **- Eliakim will be the source of honor for his family like a nail  is used to hold household objects. (see: Simili)
