===== translationNotes: =====

  * **everything that is done** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "everything that people do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **under the sun** - This refers to things that are done on earth. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ecc:01:01|1:3]]. AT: "on the earth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **one destiny** - Here the word "destiny" refers to death. AT: "everyone dies" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **The hearts of human beings are full of evil, and madness is in their hearts** - Here the word "hearts" refers to the thoughts and emotions. AT: "Human beings are full of evil, and their thoughts are madness" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **madness** - "folly"
  * **they go to the dead** - The phrase "the dead" refers to dead people. Here dead people represent the place where people go after they die. AT: "they go to the place where dead people are" or "they die and go to the grave" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
