===== Important T**erms**  =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **"I"**  in these verses refers to Yahweh, who is the speaker.
  * **you and your-**  These words refer to Gog.
  * **Magog- **  a region to the north, also known as the Land of Gog
  * **Birds of prey and living things of the field for food**  - Alternate translation: "As the soldiers die in batttle they will lay on the ground and be eaten by the birds and animals."
  * **face of the field -**  ground where the soldiers fall when the;y die in battle.
  * **declare**  - Lord Yahew is telling
  * **declaration - **  Lord Yahew is this time saying strongly
  * **send out fire**  - Lord Yahew is telling Gog another way he will destroy Magog. This time it will be by fire.
  * **dwelling securely on the coasts -**  No place, even people on the coastlines , will not be safe.
