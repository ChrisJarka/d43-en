===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **fire**  - This refers to the locust swarm or army of Yahweh. The locust have the same effect on the land as a fire would. (See:[[:en:ta:translate:metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **I**  - This pronoun refers to Joel.
  * **our**  - This refers to the Nation of Israel.
  * **you**  - This refers to Yahweh.
