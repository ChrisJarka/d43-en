===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **with you**  - The word "you" is singular. God was making his covenant with Noah. (See: [[:en:ta:vocabulary:formsofyou|Forms of 'You']])
  * **but I will establish my covenant with you…**
 - God makes his covenant with a specific person, Noah, for the specific time it takes to accomplish his flood. The two parties have specific responsibilities.
  * God pledges to Noah that while he destroys the wickedness on the surface of the earth, he will protect all the occupants of the ark and deliver them safely.
  * God requires Noah to board specific people, to board specific numbers of pairs of all the animals that come, clean, and unclean, to bring food. Noah must keep them all alive. Finally, the time frame for this covenant is from the time God closes the door on the ark, until he opens it at the end of the event.
