====== measures of time ======

===== Definition: =====

Measures of time in the New Testament were based on watchman shifts,  called "watches".

  * Watches usually refer to the measure of time into which the 12-hour period at night were divided.
  * Jewish watches divided the 12-hour night period into four-hour shifts, while the Romans divided it into three-hour shifts.
  * Time was identified by the name of the watch shift, like the "sixth hour", or "ninth hour".
