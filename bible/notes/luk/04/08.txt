===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **But Jesus answered and said to him**  - That is "But Jesus responded to him,": or "But Jesus replied to him." 
    * **It is written **- Rather "it has been written." This indicates authority and truthfulness, that is implying, "it is written in the Scriptures" or "it is written in the authoritative Scriptures."
    * **you must worship**- that is, "you must bow down in front of." This is a command directed to the devil, you refers to him. This can be translated as "worship" or "devil, worship . .."
    * **you must serve him only**  - This is also a command directed to the devil. This can be translated as "serve him only," or "devil, serve him only." Him refers to the Lord.
    * **serve**- this word literally means "serve with sacrifices and prayers like a priest"
