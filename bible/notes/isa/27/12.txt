===== Translation Notes: =====

  * ** It will come about on that day that Yahweh will thresh from flowing stream of the Euphrates River, to the Brook of Egypt, and you will be gathered one by one, people of Israel**- "Yahweh will separate out his people from all over to again be brought back to Israel" or figurately to be brought back to himself.
  * **On that day a great trumpet will be blown**- A trumpet is an instrument used many times in scripture as a warning .....
  * **the perishing ones in the land of Assyria will come, and the outcasts in the land of Egypt**- the exiled
  * **they will worship Yahweh on the holy mountain in Jerusalem**- explanation
