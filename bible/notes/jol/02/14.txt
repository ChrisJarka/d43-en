===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he** - This refers to Yahweh.
  * **perhaps he will turn and have compassion**  - The word "turn" is used to refer to God ceasing to punish the people of Israel.   As if he turns from anger toward compassion. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:idiom]])
  * the whole verse is a rhetorical question.
