===== Introduction to the translationStudio-Android Program =====

//This answers the question:// //What is the translationStudio App?//

The translationStudio-Android is a mobile app where translators can do offline translating. It can be downloaded for Android (see https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.translationstudio.androidapp&hl=en ) devices. It contains the content that needs to be translated as well as translationHelps (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_help]]). Information can be shared directly from device to device and finished content can be uploaded to Door43 and digitally published.
