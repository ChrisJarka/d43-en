====== oath, swear ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Bible, an oath is a formal promise to do something for someone. It can also be an emphatic promise to tell the truth. The term "swear" means to say an oath. .

(ULB) free from my oath, denied again with an oath, that covenant is the oath, he made an oath to set us free,

(NIV)promised me on/by oath, released from this oath, swore an oath, took an oath, put someone under oath, pledge under oath

  * Abraham told his servant to swear an oath that he would find Isaac a wife from among Abraham's relatives.
  * Peter swore an oath to try to convince people that he did not know Jesus.
  * A modern-day use of the word "swear" means to use foul language. This is not its meaning in the Bible.
