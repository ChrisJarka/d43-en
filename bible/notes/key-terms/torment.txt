====== torment ======


===== Definition: =====


Torment is a term which describes extreme anguish or agony. Torment is usually inflicted upon a person by someone or something.


    * Persons who do not accept Jesus as Savior will experience eternal torment in Hell.
    * In the New Testament demons begged Jesus not to torment them.
