====== propitiation ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "propitiation" refers to something that is done to appease the wrath and justice of God.

  * The offering of the sacrificial blood of Jesus Christ is the propitiation to God for mankind's sins.
  * Jesus was our propitiation, or substitute. That is, he was punished for people's sins through his death on the cross.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * This term could be translated by, "substitute" or "appeasement" or "payment for sins."

(See also: atonement, sacrifice, offering other)
