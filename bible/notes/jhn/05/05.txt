====== John 05:5-6 ======


===== TFT: =====

<usfm>
\v 5 One of those who was there had been paralyzed for thirty-eight years.
\v 6 Jesus saw him lying there and found out that the man had been like that for a long time. He said to the man, "Do you want to become well?"
</usfm>


===== UDB: =====

<usfm>
\v 5 One man who could not walk had been there for thirty-eight years.
\v 6 Jesus saw him lying there and realized that he had been in this condition for a long time. He said to the man, "Do you want to become healthy and strong?"
</usfm>


===== ULB: =====

<usfm>
\v 5 A certain man was there who had been an invalid for thirty-eight years.
\v 6 When Jesus saw him lying there, and after he realized that he had been there a long time,
 he said to him, "Do you want to be well?"
</usfm>

===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **was there** - "was at Bethesda pool" (See translation note: [[en:bible:notes:jhn:05:01]])
    * **thirty-eight years** - "38 years" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
    * **"Do you want to be well?"** - Jesus knew the answer to the question before he asked. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **after he realized that he had been there** - "after Jesus realized that the man had been there"
