===== Translation Notes: =====

   * **For the chief musician**  - This can be written as a sentence: "This is for the director of music to use in worship."
  * **A Psalm of David**  - This can also be written as a sentence. AT: "This is a psalm of David" or "This is a psalm that David wrote"
   * **our praise waits**  - This phrase describes how the people are waiting silently and patiently to offer their praises to Yahweh (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
   * **vows will be carried out to you**  - "we will bring our vows to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
   * **prevail**  - to defeat or be victorious over an enemy.
   * **Iniquities prevail over us**  - David speaks about iniquity as if it were a person that is able to defeat or oppress. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification])
