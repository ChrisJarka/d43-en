===== Translation Notes: =====
God continues speaking to the people of the nations that sold the people of Israel into slavery.

    * **Look**  - Pay attention to what I'm about to say.  
    * **I will soon set them to leaving** - "I will soon make them determine to leave" or "I will soon make them want to leave"
    * **leaving from the place where you sold them**  - The people of Israel will be leaving the places they were slaves and come back to the land of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
    * **your recompense**  - "what you deserve"
    * **on your head**  - The word "head" is used to refer to the people of the nations that surround Israel. AT: "to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
    * **Sabeans** - The people of Sheba.  A culture to the south of Israel.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
