====== Jeremiah Chapter 26 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What does Yahweh tell Jeremiah to proclaim in the courtyard?

A. He must proclaim all the words that Yahweh commanded without cutting short any words. [26:1-2]

Q? Yahweh may relent concerning the disaster if the people do what?

A. The people will listen and turn from their wicked ways. [26:3]

Q? If the people do not listen to the words of Yahweh or of his prophets what will happen to them?

A. Yahweh will turn their city into a curse. [26"4-6]

Q? What happened to Jeremiah after he announced Yahweh's words?

A. The priests, prophets, and people formed a mob seized him and said, "You will certainly die!...." [26:7-9]

Q? Because Jeremiah prophesied against the city where was he taken to be judged?

A. He was taken before the officials at the gateway of the New Gate of Yahweh's house. [26:10-12]

Q? What does Jeremiah say about himself to the officials and the people?

A. He said; he is in their hands, they can do what is right in their owneyes, but if they kill him they bring innocent blood on themselves, and that Yahweh sent him to proclaim all thses words. [26:13-15]
