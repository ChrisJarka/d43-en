===== Translation Notes: =====

Solomon continues to describe his love.

  * **head on you is like Carmel** -â€‹ The woman is compared to a beautiful, noble mountain(See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **Carmel** - Carmel is (1) the name of a mountain, (2) a town in Palestine, or (3) a fruitful field.
  * **the hair on your head is dark purple** - AT: "the hair on your head is dark"
  * **the king** - "The king" refers to Solomon.
  * **held captive** - The woman's beauty is so desirable that the man becomes helpless when he looks at her. He is unable to leave her. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **by its tresses** - "by its curls" or "by its ringlets"
  * **your delights** - "your delicate and pleasant ways." The physical beauty of the woman gives the man pleasure and enjoyment.
