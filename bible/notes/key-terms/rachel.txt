====== Rachel ======

===== Facts: =====

Rachel was the wife of the Old Testament Patriarch Jacob.

  * Rachel was the mother of Joseph and Benjamin, two of the 12 tribes of Israel.
  * Rachel died after giving birth to Benjamin, and was buried by Jacob near Bethlehem.
