===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **he thought to himself saying**  - That is, "he said to himself."
    * **If Jesus were a prophet**  - That is, "If Jesus were truly a prophet." The Pharisee thinks that Jesus was not a prophet, otherwise he would have known something about the woman's character and life.
    * **she is a sinner**  - That is, "she is a sinner right now even though she has repented."
    * **Simon** - This was the name of the Pharisee who invited Jesus into his home. This was not Simon Peter.
