===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I** - Here this refers to Isaiah.
  * **Lord, how long?** - "How long will the people rebel against Yahweh?"
  * **He** - Here this refers to Yahweh.
  * **sent the people far away** - "took the people a long distance from their land"
  * **and the solitude of the land is great** - "and there are no people in the whole land of Israel"
