===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The waters you saw, where the prostitue is seated**  - This refers to the waters and prostitute in 17:1.
  * **The waters you saw**  - Here, "you" refers to the writer, John.
  * **peoples, multitudes, nations, languages**  - All of these terms have similar meanings and are listed for emphasis. AT: "all the people of the world".
