====== born again, new birth, spiritual birth ======

===== Definition: =====

Born again, new birth, spiritual birth are terms used to define of the process of becoming spiritually alive. All humans are born physically alive but spiritually dead and are given a spiritual birth when they accept Jesus Christ as their Savior.

  * Spiritual birth occurs at the time of salvation and only occurs once.
  * Spiritual birth is a work of God and is the means by which a person becomes a child of God.
  * At the moment of spiritual birth God's Holy Spirit begins a permanent residence in the believer.
