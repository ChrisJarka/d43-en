====== submit, in submission ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "submit" usually means to voluntarily place oneself under the authority of a person or government.

  * The Bible tells believers in Jesus to submit to God and other authorities in their lives.
  * The instruction to "submit to one another" has a different meaning of being humble and putting the needs of others before our own.
  * To "live in submission to" someone means to live in a way that pleases or obeys that person.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * The term "submit" could also be translated as, "put under the authority of" or "follow the leadership of."
  * "live in submission to" could be translated as, "do things that obey and honor."
