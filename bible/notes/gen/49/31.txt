===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **finished these instructions to his sons**  - "finished charging his sons"
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **he pulled his feet into the bed,**  - Jacob, sitting, now seeks to rest. The description is exactly what he did with his feet.
  * **breathed his last**  - an old, poetic way of saying that he stopped breathing
  * **and went to his people.**  - a gentle way of saying that Jacob died and went to be with his ancestors who had died already. (see: [[:en:ta:translate:euphemism|Euphemism]])
