====== 2 Chronicles 07 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

Solomon as king (2 Chronicles 1-9) continued 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

God appeared again to Solomon in a dream and said that he would accept the temple and honor it with his presence as long as Israel obeyed him. But if they worshipped other god and idols he would let their enemies destroy the temple.
