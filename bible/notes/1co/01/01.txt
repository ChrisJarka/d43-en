===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **called…by the will of God**  - "God called me because that is what he wanted to do" (See: Active/Passive)
  * **Sosthenes our brother **- This indicates that both Paul and the Corinthians know who Sosthenes was. Alternate translation: "Sosthenes the brother you and I know"
  * **may grace and peace be to you **- (see UDB)
  * **to you**  - The word "you" refers to the believers in Corinth. (See: Forms of 'You')
