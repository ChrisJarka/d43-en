====== Hilkiah ======

===== Facts: =====

Hilkiah was the name of the high priest during the reign of King Josiah.

  * When the temple was being repaired, Hilkiah the high priest found the Book of the Law that had been lost. He ordered the book to be brought to King Joshiah.
  * As a result of hearing the Book of the Law read, Josiah led the people of Judah back to worshiping Yahweh and obeying his laws.
  * Another man named Hilkiah had been a palace worker during the time of King Hezekiah.
