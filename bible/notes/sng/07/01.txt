===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **prince's daughter**  - While the woman was not born into a royal family, her beauty makes her seem to be the daughter of a prince. (See: Metaphor)
  * curves of your thighs -  Thighs are the upper part of a woman's legs.
  * are like jewels,,,craftsman -  The shape of the woman's thighs are like a beautiful precious stone which has been carved to be perfect by a skilled workman. (See: Simile)​
