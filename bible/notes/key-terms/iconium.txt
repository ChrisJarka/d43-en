====== Iconium ======

===== Facts: =====

Iconium was a city in south central Asia Minor, now the country of Turkey.

  * Iconium is modern day Konya.
  * When Paul and Barnabas were expelled by the Jews from the region of Antioch after their successful ministry to the gentiles in Antioch on the first missionary journey, and they went to Iconium.
  * There was conflict among the Jews and gentiles in Iconium over Paul's preaching, so they had to flee.
