===== Playback Monitoring =====

//This page answers the question:// //What listening equipment is recommended for the recording team?//

The following equipment is recommended for playback listening.

==== Headphones ====

  * Sony MDR-7506
  * Audio Technica ATH-MX30

==== Monitors ====

  * JBL LSR305 (quality studio monitors)
  * MegaVoice Solar Case Speaker (general recording, editing and distribution listening)
