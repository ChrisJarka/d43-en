===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you **- Refers to God.
  * **me, I , my **- David is speaking of himself.
  * **formed me in my mother's womb**  - God knew us before we were born, see Jer. 1:5
  * **know my life **- Some languages have two different words to refer to knowing facts and to knowing people. Make sure that the word that is used fits the context.
