====== endure, endurance ======

===== Definition: =====

To endure is to bear something difficult with patience. It also means to stand firm when times of testing come.

  * This term is similar in meaning to "persevere, perseverance".
  * This can be translated as "stand firm" or "keep believing" or "continue to do what God wants you to do".
