====== Herod Antipas ======


===== Facts: =====


Herod Antipas was the Roman ruler of Galilee during the time of Jesus. He was really a tetrarch, but people sometimes called him king.


    * Herod Antipas ordered John the Baptist to be killed by beheading.
    * Herod sent Jesus to Pontius Pilate, who then condemned Jesus  to die by crucifixion.
