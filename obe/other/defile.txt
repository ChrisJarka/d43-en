====== defile, be defiled ======

===== Definition: =====

The terms "defile" and "be defiled" refer to becoming polluted or dirty, especially in a moral sense in relation to God.

  * God warned the Israelites to not defile themselves by eating or touching things that he had declared as "unclean" and "not holy."
  * God gave them laws to follow that would help them avoid sexual sins that would also "defile" them, making them unacceptable to God.
  * In the New Testament, this term is used to explain that the true meaning of being defiled has more to do with the sinful thoughts and actions that come out from a person, not ceremonial uncleanness.
