====== Psalms Chapter 126 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did it seem like when Yahweh restored wellbeing to Zion?

A. It seemed like they were like those who dream. [126:1]

Q? What happened when Yahweh restored wellbeing to Zion?

A When Yahweh restored wellbeing to Zion our mouths were filled with laughter and our tongues with singing. Yahweh did great things for us. [126:2-3]

Q? What is the writer requesting of Yahweh?

A. The writer is requestiong that Yahweh restore their fortunes. [126:4]

Q? What will happen to those who sow in tears?

A. They will reap with shouts of joy. [126: 5]

Q? What will happen to he who goes out weeping and carrying seed for sowing?

A. He will return with shouts of joy , bringing his sheaaves with him. [126:6]
