===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I claim your covenant decrees as my heritage forever** - AT: "Your laws will belong to me forever"
  * **they are the joy of my heart** - AT: "they bring me delight" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **My heart is set on obeying** - AT: "I am determined to obey"
  * **forever to the very end** - AT: "to the end of the world" or "to the end of my life"
