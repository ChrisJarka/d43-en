====== cherubim ======

===== Definition: =====

Cherubim is plural for cherub, an angelic type of being with wings.

  * Cherubim were assigned the east side of the Garden of Eden to guard the way to the Tree of Life.
  * Two cherubim faced each other on the Ark of the Covenant, forming the Mercy Seat, where the High Priest would sprinkle blood on the Day of Atonement..

(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:angel|angel]], other)
