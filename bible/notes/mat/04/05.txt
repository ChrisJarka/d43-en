===== Translation Notes: =====

This is the first of three times that Jesus is tempted by Satan after spending 40 days in the desert.

  * **took him**  - "him" refers to Jesus
  * **He said to him-**  "He" refers to the devil; "him" refers to Jesus
  * **He will command**  - This is a reference to Psalm 91:11 and 12. "He" refers to God and "they" refer to God's angels.
