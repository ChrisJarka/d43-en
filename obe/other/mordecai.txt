====== Mordecai ======

===== Facts: =====

The Jewish cousin and guardian of Esther who became Queen of Persia.

  * Mordecai stopped a plot to kill the Persian king Ahasuerus for which he was later rewarded.
  * Mordecai wisely advised Esther during a time of Jewish persecution in Persia. Mordecai's allowed the Jews to be spared from extinction by the hands of evil Haman.
