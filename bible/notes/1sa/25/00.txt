====== 1 Samuel 25 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Revenge ==
When David decides to kill all of the men in Nabal's household, Abigail convinces David not to avenge himself but to leave revenge to God. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:avenge]])
