===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **beside the border of Zebulun **- alternate translation: next to Zebulun's land
  * **from the eastern to the western side: Gad, one portion**  - alternate translation: Gad receives a piece of land that lays east to west
  * **that you will let lots fall**  - alternate translation: that God will give you
  * **this is the Lord Yahweh's declaration**  - See how you translated this in 5:11.
