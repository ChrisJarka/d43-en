===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **from far away, from the ends of the earth**  - The same idea is repeated twice (See:[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]) and exaggerated -"from the ends of the earth"- for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **like an eagle flies to its victim**  - The enemy nation is compared to an eagle which swoops down to surprise and attack its prey. (See:[[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **a nation with ferocious expressions**  - a nation with fierce presence, cruel and ruthless
  * **until you are destroyed **- See how you translated the phrase in [[en:bible:notes:deu:28:20|28:20,]] [[en:bible:notes:deu:28:23|28:24]].
