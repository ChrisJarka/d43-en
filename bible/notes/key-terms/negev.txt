====== Negev ======

===== Facts: =====

The Negev is a desert region in the southern part of Israel.

  * The original word means "the South" and some English versions translate it this way. It could be that it is not the same location as the present day Negev Desert.
  * When Abraham lived in the city of Kadesh, he was in the Negev region ""the South").
  * The Jewish tribes of Judah and Simeon lived in this southern region.
  * The largest city in the Negev region is Beersheeba.
