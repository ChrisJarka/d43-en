====== rejoice ======


===== Definition: =====


<font 13px/arial;;#000000;;#ffffff>To rejoice is to be full of joy and gladness. It means to be very happy.</font> 


    * This term often refers to being very happy about the good things that God has done.
    * <font 13px/arial;;#000000;;#ffffff>It could be translated as, "to be very happy" or, "to be very glad" or, "to be full of joy."</font>
