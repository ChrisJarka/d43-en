===== translationNotes: =====

  * **assembled** - "called together"
  * **scheduled work** - Work that needs to be repeated, for example every day or every month.
  * **the commanders of thousands and of hundreds** - The army had commanders who lead groups of soldiers of different sizes. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:13:01|13|1]]. AT: "the commanders of groups of 1,000 soldiers and the commanders of groups of 100 soldiers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **the managers over** - The word "over" is used here as an idiom. AT: "the managers in charge of" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **property and possessions** - These two words share similar meanings and refer to all of the items and lands that the king owned. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
