===== translationNotes: =====

  * **of celebration** - The noun "celebration" can be expressed with the verb "celebrate." AT: "when you celebrate" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **you must blow the trumpets** - Moses does not blow the trumpets; rather the priest blow them. AT: "you must command the priests to blow the trumpets" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **at the beginnings of the months** - There are 12 months on the Hebrew calendar. The beginning phase of the moon with its sliver of light marked the beginning of each month in the lunar calendar. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]])
  * **your burnt offerings ... your fellowship offerings ... you to me** - In these phrase the words "your" and "you" are plural and refer to the people of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **over the sacrifices** - "in honor of the sacrifices" 
  * **These will act as a reminder of you to me** - The word "reminder", an abstract noun, can be expressed as a verb. The trumpets and the sacrifices remind God and the Israelites to think about their relationship and to remain faithful to each other. AT: (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
