====== crime, criminal, fugitive ======

===== Definition: =====

 A crime is a sin that involves breaking a law. The term "criminal" refers to someone who has committed a crime. A criminal who is running away is called a "fugitive."

  * Types of crimes include such things as killing a person or stealing someone's property.
  * A criminal is usually captured and put into prison or in some other form of captivity.
  * The term "fugitive" literally means "someone who is fleeing."
  * In the Old Testament, a fugitive was a person who had killed someone and who was running away to avoid being killed by angry relatives of the dead person or to avoid being captured and put in prison.
  * Sometimes a fugitive would have to wander from place to place because of his crime.
