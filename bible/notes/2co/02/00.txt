====== 2 Corinthians 02 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

Some translations prefer to set apart quotations of the Old Testament. The ULB and many other English translations indent 2:9 and the first line of 2:16, which are quotations from the Old Testament.

=== Special Concepts ===

== Wisdom ==
Wisdom is a major theme in this chapter. True [[:en:obe:kt:wise|wisdom]] comes from listening to the Holy Spirit. It is the Spirit who empowers Christians and helps them to understand God. The wise person is contrasted with the unspiritual person who listens to the [[:en:obe:kt:foolish|foolish]] wisdom of the world. 

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== "We have the mind of Christ" ==
This is an important [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs|metonymy|metonymy]]. Christians are said to be able to understand the things of Christ. It is common for the mind of head to be associated with knowledge or understanding. Therefore, the mind represents understanding.
