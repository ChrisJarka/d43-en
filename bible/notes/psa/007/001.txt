===== Translation Notes: =====



  * **A musical composition of David **  - This can also be written as a sentence. AT: “This is a musical composition of David" or “This is a song that David wrote.” (See: [[:en:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **concerning the words of Cush the Benjamite **- AT: "about what Cush the Benjamite said"
  * **rip me apart like a lion, tearing me in pieces with no one else able to bring me to safety - ** AT: "savagely kill me with no one else to save me." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
