===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But this is not what he intends, nor does he think this way** - "The king of Assyria does not understand Yahweh's plan"
  * **It is in his heart** - The word "his" refers to the king of Assyria.
  * **Calno…Carchemish…Hamath…Arpad…Samaria…Damascus** - These are all names of cities. (See: [[en:ta:translate:translatenames]])
