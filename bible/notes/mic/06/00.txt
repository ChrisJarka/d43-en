====== Micah 06 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter is written in the poetic form and presents a court case or lawsuit by God against His people. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Note in most of the verses of this chapter, God is laying charges in the lawsuit, but in verse 6 through 8, it is a righteous person. 

=== Other possible translation difficulties in this chapter ===

== Names in Lawsuit ==
In verse 4, Moses, Aaron and Miriam are mentioned as witnesses for Yahweh and against Israel. 
In verse 16, Ahab and Omri are mentioned. They were Kings of Israel who were evil and did not follow Yahweh.
