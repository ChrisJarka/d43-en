===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **In the beginning God createdâ€¦**  - This is an introductory statement. It gives a summary of what is told in the rest of the chapter. It can be translated as "This is about God creating the heavens and the earth in the beginning."
  * **In the beginning**  - before humans and creation existed; in the beginning of the world
  * **the heavens and the earth**  - These words are used together to mean "the universe."
  * **heavens** - This refers here to the sky.
  * **without form and empty**  - The world was in chaos. God had not yet put it in order or given it a purpose and a function.
  * **empty**  - There was nothing on the earth--only water.
  * **waters**  - This can be translated as "water" or "deep water" or "vast water."
