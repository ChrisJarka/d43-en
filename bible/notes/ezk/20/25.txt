===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I/myself/my**  - These pronouns refer to Yahweh
  * **them/they/their**  - These pronouns refer to “the children” which is the house (or people) of Israel
  * **Then I also gave them statutes that were not good and judgments by which they could not live.**  - The words statutes in this phrase do not refer to God's law.
  * **I made them unclean by their gifts**  - The word "gifts" refers to offerings and sacrifices given to God as a gift.
  * **when they caused every firstborn of the womb to pass through the fire**  - The sacrifice (or killing) of the firstborn is a common practice in the worship of other gods.
