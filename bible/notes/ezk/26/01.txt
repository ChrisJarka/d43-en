===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **in the eleventh year** - It is implied that this is the eleventh year after the Babylonians took king Jehoiachin to Babylon. Alternate translation: "in the eleventh year after the Babylonians took king Jehoiachin to Babylon." The people of Judah started counting their time in Babylon from the time the Babylonians took Jehoiachin to Babylon. (See: [[en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]])
  * **The Word of Yahweh came** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:01:01|1:3]].
  * **Son of man** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:02:01|2:3]].
  * **Tyre has said against Jerusalem** - This refers to the people of Tyre and the people of Jerusalem. Alternate translation: "the people of Tyre has said against the people of Jerusalem." (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  * **Aha!** - The word “Aha” is a sound people make when they are happy about something. In this case the people were happy because bad things happened to Israel and Judah. Alternate translation: "Yes!" or "Very good!"
  * **The gates of the people are broken** - "The armies have broken down the gates that protected the people inside of Jerusalem."
  * **She has turned to me** - Cities are often referred to as "she." The word "me" refers to the people of Tyre. Alternate translation: "The people of Jerusalem are now running to us"
  * **I will be filled up as she is ruined**  - "We will become rich while Jerusalem is destroyed"
