====== Deuteronomy Chapter 28 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did the people of Israel have to do in order to be blessed by Yahweh and set above all the other nations of the earth?

A. The people of Israel needed to listen carefully to the voice of Yahweh and keep all his commandments. [28:1-6]

Q? What will Yahweh do for the people of Israel if they will obey his commandments?

A. Yahweh will cause their enemies to be struck down, establish them as a people holy to himself, make them very prosperous in the fruit of their labors, and bless all the work of their hands if they listen to his commandments. [28:7-14]

Q? What will happen to the people of Israel if they will not listen to the voice of Yahweh and keep all his commandments?

A. If the people of Israel will not listen to his voice and keep his commandments, then the people, their cities, their fields, their flocks and everything they do will be cursed because they have forsaken Yahweh. [28:15-21]

Q? How will the disobedience of the people of Israel affect their lives and their land?

A. The disobedience of the people of Israel will result in the people getting infectious diseases, the land facing drought and blistering heat, and the nation being struck down by their enemies. [28:22-26]

Q? How will Yahweh let the people of Israel know they have been disobedient?

A. Yahweh will attack the people with boils which cannot be healed, mental and physical confusion and cause them to be oppressed and robbed. [28:27-29]

Q? What disappointments will the people of Israel face as a result of their disobedience?

A. What the people of Israel have received will be taken from them and given to other peoples. [28:30-35]
