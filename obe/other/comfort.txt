====== comfort ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Bible the term comfort is mainly used to describe the outpoured compassion of God upon His people.

  * The prophet Isaiah speaks of God comforting the afflicted amongst His people.
  * In the New Testament God comforts His church through His Holy Spirit during our times of affliction. Those who receive the comfort are then enabled to give the same comfort to others who are in affliction.
