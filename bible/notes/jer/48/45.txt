===== translationNotes: =====


  * **The ones who flee** - This refers to the people who were able to run away during the destruction of Moab.
  * **will stand in the shadow of Heshbon** - Here "in the shadow" represents protection or shelter. AT: "will hide in Heshbon for protection" or "will go to Heshbon for shelter" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **for fire will go out from Heshbon, flame from the middle of Sihon** - These two phrases have similar meanings and emphasize that Moab's destruction will begin and spread out from Heshbon, where King Sihon once lived. AT: because a fire will burn in Heshbon, which is the city where King Sihon lived long ago (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Heshbon** - This is the name of the royal city of the king of Moab. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:48:01|48:2]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Sihon** - This is the name of an Amorite king who once ruled over Heshbon. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the forehead of Moab and the top of the heads of the boastful people** - These phrases refer to the people of Heshbon and its prominent leaders. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **forehead** - the part of the face that is above the eyes, but below the hairline. A symbol of pride. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **top of the heads of the boastful people** - This phrase refers to the prominent people of Moab, such as the princes and leaders. Also a symbol of pride. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
