===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **in the sight of Yahweh**  - The word "sight" is used to refer to Yahweh's understanding. AT: "to Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
    * **Baals** - This is the plural of Baal, since there were many gods called by that name.
    * **broke away from...went after** - The worship of Yahweh or another god is compared to a person who follows them.  The phrase "broke away" means to turn away and not follow the leadership of Yahweh anymore. The phrase "went after" refers to when the people of Israel began to follow the leadership of another god. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
    * **bowed down to them** - Worship is referred to by an action used to worship a god. AT: "worshipped them" (See:[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **provoked Yahweh to anger** - "caused Yahweh to be angry"
    * **Ashtoreths** - This is the plural of Ashtoroth, who was worshipped as a goddess in many different forms.  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
