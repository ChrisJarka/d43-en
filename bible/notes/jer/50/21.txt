===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Arise against the land** - Yahweh is speaking to the people of Babylon. This can be made clear. AT: "You enemies of Babylon, arise against the land" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Merathaim** - This is another name for Babylon. This is a name that means "double rebellion." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Pekod** - This is another name for the Chaldeans. This is a name that means "visitation." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Put them to the sword** - This refers to having a sword come down on people to kill them. AT: "kill them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Put them to the sword** - Here the word "sword" represents war. Yahweh speaks of Babylon's enemies killing them in battle as if he were placing those people under the sword's power. AT: "Kill them in battle" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **set them apart for destruction** - This idiom means to destroy something completely. Translate this as in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:25:07|25:9]]. AT: "Destroy them completely" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **this is Yahweh's declaration** - Yahweh speaks of himself by name to express the certainty of what he is declaring. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:01:07|1:8]]. AT: "this is what Yahweh has declared" or "this is what I, Yahweh, have declared" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **The sounds of battle and enormous destruction are in the land** - These sounds represent the battle and destruction that cause them. They are spoken of in the present tense to indicate that this will certainly happen soon. AT: "Fierce battle and terrible destruction are soon going to happen in the land" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
