===== Translation Notes: =====

  * ** Do not fear, O land** - "O land" refers to the fields or the grounds (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]]).
  * **he will give you** - The word "he" refers to Yahweh.
  * **bring down showers on you** - The word "you" refers to the people of Zion.
