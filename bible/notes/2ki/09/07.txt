===== Translation Notes: =====

The young prophet that Elisha sent to Ramoth Gilead is continuing to speak to Jehu, whom he had just annointed as king over Israel.

  * **by the hand of Jezebel**  - AT: "by an order from Jezebel" (See: Metonomy)
  * **For the whole family of Ahab will perish, and I will cut off from Ahab every male child **- AT: "Every family member of Ahab's will die (or "be killed"), including every male child" (See: Euphemism)
