===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **house of Torgamah**  - This phrase refers to the "family unit of Torgamah."  They were the descendants of Noah's son Japheth.
  * **horses, stallions, mules **- These are all different types of horses.
  * **Dedan**  - These people lived along the Persian Gulf in Arabia.
  * **in your hand **- This indicates that the mechandise was "owned" or "recieved."
  * **horns, ivory and ebony **- Likely, these were goods that would have been available in Dedan.
