===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of the crucifixion of Jesus and how people were mocking him.

  * **Son of God** - This is an important title for Jesus that describes his relationship to God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **And the robbers who were crucified with him** - "And the robbers that the soldiers had crucified with Jesus" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
