===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Saul, who is also called Paul** - AT: "who called himself Paul (UDB)" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **he gazed at him** - "Saul looked intently at Elymas the magician"
  * **You son of the devil** - Paul is saying the man is acting like the Devil. AT: 1) "You child of the devil" or 2) "You are like the devil" or 3) "You act like the devil." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **you are full of all kinds of deceit and wickedness** - "you are always intent in causing others to believe what is not true using falsehood and always doing what is wrong"
  * **wickedness** - In this context it means to be lazy and not diligent in following God's law.
  * **You are an enemy of every kind of righteousness** - Paul is grouping Elymas with the devil. Just as the devil is an enemy of God and is against righteousness, so was Elymas.
  * **You will never stop twisting the straight paths of the Lord, will you?** - Paul is rebuking the magician for following the devil. AT: "You must stop saying that the truth about the Lord God is false" (UDB). (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **the straight paths of the Lord** - Paul was rebuking the magician for calling what was true about the Lord lies. AT: "the true ways of the Lord." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
