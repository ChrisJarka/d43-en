====== census ======

===== Definition: =====

Counting the number of people in a group.

  * In Exodus, a census was used to determine the number of men to pay a half shekel to maintain the temple.
  * In the book of Numbers, a census was taken when the Israelites left Egypt and again before they entered Canaan, counting the only the number of men.
  * The Romans conducted a census for tax purposes during the time before, during, and after Christ's birth.

(See also: other)
