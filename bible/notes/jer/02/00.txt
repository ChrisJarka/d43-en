====== Jeremiah 02 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

== The people have left Yahweh and will be punished (Jeremiah 2- 10) ==

The people have stubbornly followed other gods.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== The people refuse to learn ==

The people have worshipped other gods particularly the fertility gods of Baal and Ashtoreth. Even when punished they do not learn.


=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphor ==

Israel is compared to a female camel or a wild donkey for being unstable and lustful. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
