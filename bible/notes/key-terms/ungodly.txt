====== ungodly, godlessness ======

===== Definition: =====

Ungodliness or godlessness is the total absence of God's presence and God's character in a person's life.

  * God has provided examples of ungodliness: world at the time of Noah and Sodom and Gomorrah.
  * God pronounces judgment and wrath on the ungodly, which included each of us, at one time.
  * One of the results of Jesusâ€™ life, death and resurrection was justification for the ungodly who are repentant.
