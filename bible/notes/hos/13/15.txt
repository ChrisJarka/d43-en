===== Translation Notes: =====

Hosea the prophet is speaking.

  * **an east wind will come; the wind of Yahweh** - A wind from the east was very hot and destructive. Here it refers to the armies from the east the Yahweh will send to destroy the people of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Ephraim's spring will dry up, and his well will have no water** - Hosea continues to describe how God will punish the people of Israel. God will cause all of their water to dry up.
  * **his storehouse** - This refers to all of the peoples' possessions.
