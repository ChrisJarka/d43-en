====== 1 Peter 02 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

Some translations prefer to set apart quotations of the Old Testament. The ULB and many other English translations indent all the lines of 2:6,7,8,10, 22, which are quotations from the Old Testament.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Stones ==
Stones are used in Scripture to in different ways to describe the church. Jesus is the cornerstone. The apostles and prophets are the foundation. In this chapter, Christians in general are the stones used to continue to build the church.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Milk ==
Milk is a [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|metaphor]] used in Scripture, often contrasted with meat. Babies drink a mother's milk and this image is used to describe young or immature Christians.

=== Other possible translation difficulties in this chapter ===

== "You are now the people of God" ==
Some scholars believe that this indicates that the church has replaced Israel in the plans of God. Other scholars believe that are are more than one "[[:en:obe:kt:peopleofgod|people of God]]."
