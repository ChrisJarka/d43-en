===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Therefore thus…. says the Lord Yahweh**- This means,' for that reason, in this way…says the Lord Yahweh'.
  * **Woe**  - The term “woe” refers to a feeling of great sorrow or distress.
  * **the city of blood **  - Alternate Translation : It is a city full of murderers. (UDB)
  * **a cooking pot-**  a big pot to cook something in. The cooking pot represents the 'city of Jerusalem'
  * **rust**  - This is ' a red coloured substance that forms on metal or iron'. This refers to the bad  or wicked people of Jerusalem
  * **that rust will not come out of it**- Alternate Translation: that rust cannot be removed UDB). This means that there is so much of wickedness or corruption that nothing can be done but to destroy it.
  * **Take piece after piece from it **-This refers  the people that were in Jerusalem, of every class and rank, of every age and gender; suggesting that they should not be all destroyed at once, but some at one time, and some at another.
