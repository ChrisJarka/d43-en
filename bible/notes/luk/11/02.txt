===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **he said to them** - "Jesus said to his disciples"
    * **say** -  Or, "say things like this"
    * **sanctify your name**  - This can be translated as "May your name be revered as holy" or "Cause people to revere your holiness" or "Cause people to honor you because you are holy."
    * **Let your kingdom come**  - This can be translated as "Establish your kingdom" or "May all people soon be ruled by you in your kingdom" (UDB), or "Come and rule over all people."
