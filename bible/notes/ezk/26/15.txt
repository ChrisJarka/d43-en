===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Will not the islands quake with the sound of your downfall, the groan of the pierced when the grevious slaughter is in your midst?** - "Other people will fear after hearing about your fall and the sounds of the death of your people." (See: Rhetorical question).
  * **chiefs of the sea**  - "all the kings in the cities along the coast" (UDB).
  * **lay aside their robes and strip off their colorful clothing** - The rulers will remove their royal clothing in mourning.
  * **dress in fear** - The rulers will be overcome with fear.
