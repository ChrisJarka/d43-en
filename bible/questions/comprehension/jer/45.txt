====== Jeremiah Chapter 45 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Jeremiah the prophet dictate in the scroll to Baruch son of Neriah?

A. Jeremiah dictated to the scroll what Yahweh said to Baruch, "Yahweh, God of Israel, says this to you, Baruch. "You have said, 'Woe is me, for Yahweh has added agony to my pain. My groaing has wearied me; I find no rest.' [45:1]

Q? What does Jeremiah continue to say that Yahweh wants him to say to Baruch?

A. This is what continues to be said to Baruch, 'Yahweh says: See, what I built, I am now tearing down. What I planted, I am now pulling up. [45:2 - 4]

Q?What does Jeremiah say that Yahweh is going to do to humanity and to Baruch?

A. Jeremiah says that Yahweh declares a disaster is coming on all humanity and that Yahweh is giving Baruch his life as plunder everywhere he will go. [45:5]
