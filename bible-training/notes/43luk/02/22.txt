===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **the required number of days** - That is, "the number of days required" or, "the number of days that God required."
    * **for their purification** - That is, "for Joseph and Mary to become ceremonially clean" or, "for God to consider Mary and Joseph to be clean again."
    * **to present him to the Lord**  - This was a ceremony acknowledging God's claim on the first born children who were male.
    * **As it is written**  - This can also be translated as, "They did this because it is written."
    * **male that opens the womb**  - This is an idiom for "firstborn offspring that is a male." Although the law was about the firstborn offspring of both people and domestic animals, in this context, it could also be translated as "firstborn son." (See: [[:en:ta:figuresofspeech:idiom|Idiom]])
    * **a pair of**  - That is, "two."
    * **turtledoves**  - These are common birds that eat seeds and often live in open areas. They are small enough to be held in two hands and people would eat them.
    * **young pigeons**  - These are birds that eat seeds and often live in hilly or mountain areas. They are small enough to be held in two hands and people would eat them.
