===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Zoan … Memphis**  - These were cities in the northern part of Egypt. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
  * **who are the cornerstones of her tribes**  – "who" refers to the people of Zoan and Memphis. This can be translated “who are the leaders of all the Egyptians.” (See: [[:en:ta:translate:personification|Personification]])
  * **mixed a spirit of distortion into her midst**  - This compares someone who stirs together ingredients to get a certain effect with Yahweh who added confusion or silly ideas to the leaders of Zoan and Memphis who normally gave wise advice. It can be translated “gave them a spirit that distorts their judgment.” (See: [[:en:ta:translate:metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **like a drunk staggering in his vomit**  – This phrase compares the leadership of Egypt to a drunk man. It means that the leaders are going this way and that, and leading the people in disgusting ways. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:simile|Simile]])
  * **whether head or tail, palm branch or reed**  - See how you translated this in chapter 9:14.
