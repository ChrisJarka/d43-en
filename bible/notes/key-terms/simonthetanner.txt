====== Simon the tanner ======

.

===== Facts: =====

"Simon the Tanner" was a man who lived in Joppa, and hosted Peter while he ministered there. Probably a Christian, his profession was not approved by the Pharisees because the tanner had to come in contact with dead animals

  * After Peter raised a Christian woman named Tabitha, also known as Dorcus, from the dead, he stayed with "Simon the Tanner" in Joppa.
  * Peter had his dream about the sheet with many kinds of unclean animals on it while he slept on the roof of "Simon the Tanner's" house.
  * Cornelius, a Christian Roman centurion, was told by an angel in a vision to send men to the home of "Simon the Tanner" to summon Peter to come to Caesarea and preach to Cornelius' household.
