===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh**  - "God" or "Yahweh"
  * **You have shown favor to your land.**  - Here, "land" refers to the nation and people of Israel. (See: "Explicit and Implicit Information" and See: "Personification")
  * **You have restored the well-being - **  You, our God, have brought prosperity back"
  * **Jacob**  - "Israel" or "the Israeli people" (See: "Metonymy")
  * **the sin of your people**  - This refers to "wrong actions, words, or thoughts" of the people of Israel.
