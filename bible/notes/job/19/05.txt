===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Job continues to speak to his three friends.

  * **If indeed you will boast against me** - "Since indeed you act as though you are better than me"
  * **convince everyone that I am disgraced** 
  * **and has caught me in his net** - Job speaks of God as if he were a hunter that has violently caught an innocent animal in his net. AT: "and has trapped me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
