====== trumpet, horn ======

===== Definition: =====

A trumpet was a musical instrument made from metal, bone or shell. It was blown into in order to make a tone which could be in the form of music or a signal as used by Gideon (Judges 7:19) They were normally used in pairs and a mouth piece was added to facilitate the blowing,
