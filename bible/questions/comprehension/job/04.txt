====== Job Chapter 04 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? How did Job's friend Eliphaz answer him after listening to Job's questions?

A. He says, will you be grieved if we try to speak with you? Eliphaz also reminded him about how Job had instructed, strengthened, and his words have supported many. [4:1-4]

Q? What does Eliphaz say is Job's confidence and hope in time of trouble?

A. It is the fear of God that is Job's confidence and his hope is the integrity of his ways. [4:5-6]

Q? Who has perished and are consumed by the breath of God and his anger?

A. Those who plow iniquity and sow trouble will reap the same. [4:7-9]

Q? How is the lion discribed?

A. The lion has a fierce voice, teeth of young lions are broken, the old lion dies for lack of victims, and the cubs are scattered everywhere. [4:10-11]
