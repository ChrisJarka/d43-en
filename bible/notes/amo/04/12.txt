===== translationNotes: =====

  * **prepare to meet your God** - God says this to warn the people of Israel that they will die soon and that he will judge them. AT: "prepare to meet me, your God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **he who forms the mountains ... reveals his thoughts ... is his name** - Yahweh is speaking about himself. This can also be translated with the words "I" and "me." AT: "I who form the mountains ... reveal my thoughts ... is my name" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
