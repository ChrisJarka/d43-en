====== Elisha ======


===== Facts: =====


Elisha was a prophet in the 9th century B.C. during the reign of kings Ahab, Ahaziah, Jehoram, Jehu, Jehoahaz, and Jehoash.


    * Elisha was the prophet who replaced the prophet Elijah, according to God's instruction.
    * Before Elijah was taken to heaven, Elisha asked to receive a double portion of Elijah's spirit.
    * Elisha worked many miracles, including the healing of Naaman of Syria and the resurrection of the Shunammite woman's son.
