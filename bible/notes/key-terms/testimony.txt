====== testimony ======


===== Definition: =====


Testimony is the evidence provided to support a fact or claim. It can be verbal or written.


    * In a court of law, a person gives a testimony under oath.
    * A personal Christian testimony is a public profession of one's faith.
    * This is to give witness to something; to provide information about a particular event/ item/ etc.
