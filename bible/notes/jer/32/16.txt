===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Baruch son of Neriah**- Baruch is a son or descendant of Neriah. (See: biblical names)
  * **Woe**  - "Oh" or "Wow"
  * **with your raised arm**  - "extended arm" or "extended hand"
  * **You show covenant faithfulness**- "You keep your promise and show your faithful love"
  * **pour the guilt of men into the laps of their children after them**  - "cause the sin of men"
