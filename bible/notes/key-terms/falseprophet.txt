====== false prophet ======

===== Definition: =====

A false prophet is a person who makes a prophesy that does not come true or proclaims something contrary to scripture.

  * A false prophet incorrectly claims that his or her message comes from God.
  * Jesus stated that one will know who is a false prophet by the works that they do.

(See also: prophet)
