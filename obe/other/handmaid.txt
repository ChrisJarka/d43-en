====== handmaid ======

===== Definition: =====

A handmaid was a female slave commonly used in many households in Old Testament culture.

  * These young women were protected by the law and were usually well cared for.
  * When the wife was unable to bear children, the handmaid sometimes became a concubine or substitute wife.
