===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Therefore announce this to them**  - The word "them" refers to the "men from the elders of Israel"
  * **who takes his idols into his heart**  - "who considers his idols to be very important"
  * **the stumbling block of his iniquity before his face**  - Here the idols of Israel are called a stumbling block. Falling down is being compared to sinning against God. The words "before his face" mean they are in front of him and refer to looking at them in worship. "the idol that he has used to sin by worshiping it" (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
  * **then comes to a prophet** - A person goes to a prophet in order to hear what God has to say.  (See: [[en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]])
  * **the number of their idols**  - "how many idols they have"
  * **I may take back the house of Israel in their hearts**  - â€œI will make the people of Israel believe in me again"
  * **take back...driven far from me**  - God is using distance to show Israel's relationship to him.  He says the are far away in order to say they do not trust or believe in him.  He says he will take them back in order to make them believe in Him and look to Him for help.  (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
