===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh is speaking about the people of Israel.
  * **I will establish** - "I will make"
  * **a covenant of peace** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:34:25|34:25]].
  * **I will take them** - "I will give them." It was implied that Yahweh would give them the land of Israel. AT: "I will give them the land of Israel." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **multiply them** - See how you translated "multiply men" in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:36:10|36:10]].
  * **my holy place**  - "my sanctuary" or "my temple" (UDB)
  * **My tabernacle** - "the place where I live" 
  * **I will be their God, and they will be my people** - See how you translated "they will be my people, and I will be their God" in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:11:19|11:20]].
  * **will know that I am Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:06:06|6:7]].
  * **the Holy One of Israel** - "the one who sanctifies Israel." (See: [[en:obe:kt:sanctify]]).
  * **my sanctuary** - "my holy place" or "my temple"
  * **in their midst** - "among them"
