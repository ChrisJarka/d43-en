===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues to speak to Pharaoh.
  * **I will terrify the ears of many peoples** - This can be translated as "I will make the heart of many peoples afraid." (See: [[en:obe:other:heart]])
  * **in lands you do not know** - "in lands you do not know about." The way that Yahweh destroys Egypt will be so scary that even people in lands Pharaoh never knew about will hear about it.
  * **when I bring your collapse among the nations** - This can be translated as "when nations hear about the way I destroyed you."
  * **I will shock many peoples concerning you** – This can be translated as "I will make many peoples horrified because of what happened to you."
  * **their kings will shudder in horror concerning you** - This can be translated as "their kings will be so scared because of what happened to you that their hair will stand up." When people are very scared, the hair are on their body often goes straight up instead of lying flat against their skin.
  * **They will tremble constantly** – "The peoples and their kings will be afraid all the time"
  * **each man** - This can be translated as "each person will be afraid that he will die."
  * **on the day of your downfall** - "at the time when I destroy you"
