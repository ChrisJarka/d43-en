===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **to the people - **  this word "people" might refer to the "people" in 8:2
  * **I am the light of the world**  - see metaphor in 1:4
  * **he who follows me**  - "he" is a generic pronoun, meaning "everyone"
  * **walk in the darkness - **  metaphorical use of "walking in darkness", meaning "living" or "being" in evil behavior ("darkness" is a metaphor for 'evil')
  * **you bear witness about yourself; your witness is not true **- testifying about oneself was not customary in Jewish culture (Prov. 27:2). It was looked down upon. Also, by Jewish law, two witnesses were necessary for a true testimony or witness (Matt. 18:16; Deut. 19:15)
