====== Psalms 057 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 57 is a psalm of deliverance; praising God for they many times he has delivered him.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Since God is protecting him, he has nothing to fear from his enemies.
