===== Translation Notes: =====

**I will fasten him like a nail in a secure place, and he will become a throne of glory to his father’s house. 24They will hang upon him all the glory of his father’s house, the offspring and descendants, every small vessel from the cups to all the jugs –**Eliakim is like a nail from which all the jugs and glasses are hung from**. ** It can be translated -“ I will honor Eliakim so much that his family and his descendants will honored by God after him” (See: Simile)
