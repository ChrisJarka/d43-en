===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh of Hosts **- "Yahweh, the Leader of the angel armies,"
  * **we would have been as Sodom, we would have been as Gomorrah **- "we would have all been destroyed, like the cities of Sodom and Gomorrah were destroyed." These cities were completely destroyed by God as punishment for the evil things the people were doing.
