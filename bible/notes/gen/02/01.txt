===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **and so he rested **- This could be translated "and he ceased (from work)" or "and he stopped (working)." This verb is related to the noun "Sabbath."
  * **and sanctified it **- This could be translated "and made it holy" or "and set it apart."
  * **in it he rested from all his work **- This could be translated "on it he ceased from all his work."
  * **blessed the seventh day and sanctified it **- Maybe "honored the seventh day and called it his own"
