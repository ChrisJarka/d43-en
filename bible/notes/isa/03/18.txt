===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues to describe Yahweh's judgment against the women of Judah.

  * **Lord**  - This could be translated "Yahweh."
  * **ankle jewelry ** <font 14px line-height: 21.7777786254883px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>- A</font>    circular decoration that a lady wears around her ankle.
  * **head bands ** <font 14px line-height: 21.7777786254883px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>- A decoration that is worn over the head and hair.</font>
  * **crescent ornaments**  - This is jewlery worn in the belief that they would protect the individual from evil.
  * **the ear pendants ** <font 14px line-height: 21.7777786254883px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>- This jewlery hangs from the ear lobe, often through a hole in the ear lobe.</font>
  * **the bracelets ** <font 14px line-height: 21.7777786254883px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>- A decoration worn around the wrist.</font>
  * **the veils ** <font 14px line-height: 21.7777786254883px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>- A sheer material used to cover the head and face of a woman.</font>
  * **the headties ** <font 14px line-height: 21.7777786254883px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>- A long, thin piece of cloth tied around the head or the hair.</font>
