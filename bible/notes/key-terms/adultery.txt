====== adultery, adulterer ======

===== Definition: =====

Adultery is when a married person has sexual relations with someone who is not his spouse. An adulterer is a person who commits adultery.

  * God commanded the Israelites to not commit adultery.
  * The covenant of marriage is the only context in which God approves sexual relations.
  * Adultery breaks the promises that a husband and wife made to each other in their covenant of marriage.
  * Some languages may have an indirect polite way of talking about adultery, such as "sleeping with someone else's spouse."
  * This is different from fornication which involves people who are not married.

(See also: fornication, covenant other)
