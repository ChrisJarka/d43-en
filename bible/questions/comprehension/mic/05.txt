====== Micah Chapter 05 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? With what would strike they the leader of Israel?

A. They would strike the leader of Israel with a rod on the cheek [5:1].

Q? For how long would God give up the clans of Judah?

A. God would give them up, until the time when she who was in labor bore a child [5:3].

Q? Who would shepherd his flock in the strength of Yahweh?

A. The child would shepherd his flock in the strength of Yahweh [5:3-4].

Q? For what do the remnant of Jacob not wait?

A. The remnant of Jacob do not wait for man [5:7].

Q? What would happen when the Israelites' hand was lifted against their enemies?

A. When the Israelites' hand was lifted against their enemies, will destroy them [5:9].

Q? On whom would Yahweh execute vengeance?

A. Yahweh would execute vengeance on the nations that had not listened [5:15].
