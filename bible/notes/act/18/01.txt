===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **After these things**  - "After the events that took place in Athens"
  * **found **- Possible meanings are 1) "happened to find by chance" or 2) found after seeking with intent." The text is not clear which is the case.
  * **recently**  - This is probably sometime in the past year.
  * **commanded **- This word indicates a detailed instruction was given.
