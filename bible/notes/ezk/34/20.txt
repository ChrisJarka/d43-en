===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **v. 20 fat sheep and thin ones: **  This implies fat sheep are the well fed and cared for leaders of Israel the less fed general people of Israel.
  * **v. 21 scattered them outside the land**: An alternate translation is scattered them to distant lands.
  * **Behold- ** The word “behold” here alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows.
