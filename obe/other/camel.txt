====== camel ======

===== Definition: =====

A camel is a large, four legged animal with either one or two humps on its back.

  * The camel was the largest animal found in Palestine.
  * The camel was used mainly for travel and burden bearing.  Arabs also used camels for food, but not Israelites because camels were cud chewers.
  * Camels were valuable because they could move swiftly in the sand and could live without water fr several weeks at a time.
