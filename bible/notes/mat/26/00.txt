======  Matthew 26 General Notes ======

=== Structure and Formatting ===

Some translations prefer to set apart quotations of the Old Testament. The ULB and many other English translations indent the lines of verse 31, which is quotations from the Old Testament.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Sheep ==
This is a common image used in Scripture to refer to people in general.

== Passover ==
Jesus' death occurs in connection with  passover because he is the fulfillment of the passover lamb. It is his sacrificial death which allows one to avoid death.

== The eating of the body and blood ==
This symbolic action is practiced in nearly all church to this day in remembrance of Christ's sacrifice for the sins of man.

=== Other possible translation difficulties in this chapter ===

== Shame and cowardice ==
Throughout this chapter, the actions of the Jewish leaders are shameful and cowardly. The were afraid of the people they were supposed to lead. The subtleness of their shameful and cowardly actions may be difficult to see in translation.
