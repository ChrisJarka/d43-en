===== translationNotes: =====

  * **defiles his consecrated head** - 
  * **separated** - Here "separation" means "dedication." AT: "dedicated" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **his separated person** - This refers to Nazirite man, specifically his body, in regard to the vow he has taken. AT: "the Nazirite man" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **on the day of his cleansing** - The abstract noun "cleansing" can be stated as the verbal phrase. AT: "on the day when he makes himself acceptable to God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **That is when he must shave his head** - He does not shave his head on the seventh day, he shaves it on the eighth day. The full meaning of this statement can be made explicit. AT: "Then on the eight day, he must shave his head" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
