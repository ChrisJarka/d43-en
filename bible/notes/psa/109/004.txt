===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **In return for my love they slander me** - "Although I loved them, they accuse me" 
  * **I pray for them** - It is not clear what David prays. Possible meanings are 1) AT: "I pray that you, God, will do good things for them" or 2) AT: "I pray that you, God, will protect me from them." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
