===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **my lord Esau** - Jacob is using polite language and refers to his brother as "my lord."
  * **your servant Jacob** - Jacob is using polite language and refers to himself as "your servant."
  * **donkey** - an animal that is like a horse with long ears (See: [[en:ta:translate:translateunknowns]])
  * **sent to tell this** - The phrase "my messengers" is understood. Alternate translation: "sent my messengers to tell this." (See: [[en:ta:translate:ellipsis]])
  * **that I may find favor in your sight** - "that you may be pleased with me"
