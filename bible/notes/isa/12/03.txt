===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **With joy you will draw water from the wells of salvation** - The word "you" refers to the people of Israel.  This compares salvation to a deep well that won't run dry. Just as people need water to live, they need God's salvation so that they can live physically and spiritually.  Alternate translation: "You will joyfully draw water from the wells of salvation." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **on that day** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:12:01]].
  * **you will say, "give thanks to Yahweh** - See how you translated a similar phrase in [[en:bible:notes:isa:12:01]].
  * **call upon his name** - Alternate translation: "speak loudly to Yahweh and praise him" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **declare his deeds among the peoples** - Alternate translation: "tell everyone in the world what Yahweh has done"
  * **proclaim that his name is exalted** - "tell everyone that Yahweh's name is great"
