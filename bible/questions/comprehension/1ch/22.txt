====== 1 Chronicles Chapter 22 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? After David declared where the house of Yahweh was to be built, what did he order his servants to do?

A. He ordered them to find the foreigners living in the land of Israel and give them jobs as stonecutters to build God's house. [22:1-2]

Q? What preparations did David make for building the house of Yahweh before his death?

A. He wanted the house to be magnificent and famous and knew his son Solomon was young and inexperienced, so he provided large amounts of iron, bronze, and cedar. [22:3-5]

Q? Why did David command Solomon to build the house of Yahweh?

A. David told Solomon that Yahweh told him that David would not build the house because of all the blood that was shed. [22:6-8]

Q? How does Yahweh discribe Solomon?

A. He would be a peaceful ruler, he will build a house in Yahweh's name, Yahweh will be his father and will establish Solomon's kingdom over Israel forever. [22:9-10]
