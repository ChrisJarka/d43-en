===== Translation Notes: =====

See: Translating Poetry and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]]

  * **recount**  - "tell"
  * **Who can recount the mighty acts of Yahweh**  - The author is proclaiming the greatness of God, and does not expect an answer. (See: Rhetorical question)
  * **or proclaim**  - AT: "or who can proclaim" (See: Implicit/Explicit)
  * **in full**  - "completely"
  * **praiseworthy**  - worthy of praise
  * **or proclaim in full all his praiseworthy deeds?**  - The author is proclaiming the greatness of God, and does not expect an answer. (See: Rhetorical question)
