===== Translation Notes: =====

  * Those mentioned in verse 21 led the procession, playing their harps. The origin of the word Sheminith is not clear but may indicate a style of music they played.This is not mentioned in the UDB.
  * cymbals, stringed insturments, harps - These are different types of musical insturments.
