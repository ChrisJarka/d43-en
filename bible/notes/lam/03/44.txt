===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **You have covered yourself with a cloud** - Yahweh has made himself impossible to approach. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
    * **You have made us castoffs and refuse** - The words "castoffs" and "refuse" share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. AT: "You have thrown us away as if we were trash." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
    * **desolation and crushing** - These two words share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
