===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **when a wicked man turns away from his wickedness** - See how you translated this in Ezekiel 18:21.
  * **performs justice and righteousness** - See how you translated this in Ezekiel 18:21.
  * **soul** - This word means the inner, invisible, and eternal part of a person, the same thing as spirit.
  * **he/his** - is the pronoun refering to the wicked man.
  * **he has seen** - This word means that he has 'thought about'.
  * **turned away from** - "stopped committing" or "stopped doing".
  * **transgressions** - This word means 'a disobedient act that breaks a command, rule, or moral code.'
