===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The king sent a man from before him,**  - AT: "The king of Israel sent a man from his presence,"
  * **when the messenger came to Elisha, he said to the elders **- "as the messenger sent by the king came to Elisha, Elisha said to the elders"
  * **son of a murderer**  - Meaning someone who has the characteristics of a murderer. (See: son)
  * **to take away my head**  - AT: "to cut off my head"
  * **Is not the sound of his master's feet behind him? **- AT: "The king will be coming along right behind him. (See: Idiom)
  * **While he was still talking with them**  - "While Elisha was still talking with the elders"
  * **the messenger came down to him**. - The messenger arrived, and so did the king, as Elisha had said he would. (See: Explicit and Implicit)
  * t**his trouble**  - This refers to the famine in Samaria and all the suffering it caused.
  * **Why should I wait for Yahweh any longer?" **- "I will not wait any longer for help from Yahweh." AT: "Why should I continue to wait for Yahweh to help us?" (See: Rhetorical Question)
