====== silver ======

===== Definition: =====

A precious metal used to make coins, jewelry, vessels, and ornaments.

  * Judas was paid 30 pieces of silver for betraying Jesus.
  * The silver and gold vessels were taken from the Temple in Jerusalem when the Israelites were captured and exiled by the Babylonians in 586 BC.
  * For centuries silver was more valuable than gold.

(See also: other)
