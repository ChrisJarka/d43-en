===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **boastful words spoken by the horn** - This implies that "the horn" was able to speak like a human. â€‹(See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **I watched while ... to be burned up** - AT: "I watched while someone killed the animal, destroyed its body, and handed its body over, so someone else could burn it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **handed over** - When people are handed over it is usually when they have done something evil, and they are "handed over" to a person who will punish them. When a thing is handed over, it is simply given to someone else. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **their authority to rule was taken away** - AT: "someone took away their authority to rule"
  * **but their lives were prolonged for a period of time** - AT: "but they continued to live for a period of time" or "but someone made them live a little longer"
