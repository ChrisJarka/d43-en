===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **wipe away -**  This could be translated "totally destroy" or "annihilate." This verb pictures God as thoroughly cleaning the earth by wiping away the grime of mankind.
  * **the eyes of Yahweh -**  Yahweh does not have physical eyes, but he nevertheless sees. It could be translated "before God."
