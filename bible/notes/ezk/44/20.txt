===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **shave**  - This means to cut a person's hair very close to the skin.
  * **trim their heads**  - to make the hair on their head neat by cutting it
  * **scissors**  - This is a tool used for cutting that has two blades connected in the middle so that the sharp edges almost touch each other.
  * **widow** - This is a woman whose husband has died.
  * **outcast**  - This is someone who is not accepted by other people.
  * **wives**  - This refers to married women.
  * **lineage**  - The family that lived before a person but are now dead.
  * **the house of Israel**  - See how you translated this in 3:1.
  * **permitted to marry only virgins or widows of other priests**  - These men are only allowed to marry virgins or women who were married to dead priests.
