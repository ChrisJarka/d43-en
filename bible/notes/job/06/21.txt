===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **bold words**  - explanation
    * **Did I say to you, ‘Give something to me’?**  - Job used this question to emphasise that he did not ask for help from his friends. AT: "I did not ask any of you for anything" (See:Rhetorical Question)
    * **Offer me a gift from your wealth?**  - See above. AT: "I did not ask you to give me money"
    * **Save me from my adversary’s hand?**  - See above. AT: "I never asked any of you to rescue me from my enemies" (UDB)
    * **Ransom me from the hand of my oppressors?**  - See above. AT: "I did not ask you to save me from those who treated me badly"
