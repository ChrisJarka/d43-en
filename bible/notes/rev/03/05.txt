  * **The one who conquers** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:02:26|2:26]]
  * **clothed in white garments** - White clothes represent a pure life. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **wipe his name out of the book of life** - Removing a person's name from the book represents removing the person from life. (See: Metaphor)
