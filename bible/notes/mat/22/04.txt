===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * Jesus continues telling the parable of the kingdom of heaven.
  * **Tell them who are invited **- "Tell the people that I have invited" (See: Passive/Active)
  * **Tell them who are invited, 'See, I have prepared my dinner **- "Tell the people whom I have invited that I have prepared my dinner" (See: Indirect/Direct)
  * **my oxen and my fatted calves have been killed**  - This can be translated in an active voice as "The king has killed his oxen and fatted calves" or "my servants have killed my oxen and my fatted calves."
  * **all things**  - "everything needed for the dinner"
