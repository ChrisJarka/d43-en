===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I was a wall** - The wall is a metaphor for a woman with small breasts. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **my breasts are now like fortress towers** - Fortress towers are tall. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **so I am completely mature in his eyes** - The man now sees that she is a fully developed woman, and that pleases him. AT: "so now he thinks that I am ready for him to make love to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **I am in his eyes as one** - Here eyes are a metonym for judgment or value. AT: "I am in his judgment as one" or "he thinks of me as one" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **brings peace** - You may need to make explicit to whom the woman brings peace. AT: "brings him peace" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **peace** - "well-being"
