===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Eliakim ... Hilkiah**  - These are names of men. (see: How to Translate Names)
  * **I will clothe him with your tunic and put on him your sash, and I will transfer your authority into his hand **- "Yahweh will cloth Eliakim with Shebna's tunic and sash, and Yahweh will transfer Shebna's authority to Eliakim"
  * **He will be a father**  – He will care for Israel as a Father would care for his children – See: (Metaphor)
  * **I will place the key of the house of David on his shoulder; he will open, and none will shut; he will shut, and none will open** – <font 14px line-height: 26.1333332061768px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>As a key opens the door to a house, so</font> Yahweh will  give Eliakim unlimited power and authority over the house of David.  (see: Metonym)
