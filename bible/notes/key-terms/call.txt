====== call, calling ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "call" has several different meanings. It literally means to say something loudly to someone who is not close by. Sometimes it occurs as "call out."

  * Often in the Bible, "call" has a meaning of "summon" or "command to come" or "request to come."
  * In a figurative sense of this word, God calls people to come to him and be his people. This is their "calling."
  * The idiom "call on the name of the LORD" means to trust in the LORD and obey him.
  * "Call" can also mean "name" as in, "He is called John," which means, "He is named John" or "His name is John."
