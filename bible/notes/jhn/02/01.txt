===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **third day** - This means day three.
  * **there was a wedding** - "A wedding happened" or "A wedding occurred"
  * **wedding** - In Jewish culture, a wedding feast might last as long as one week. If the family did not provide enough food and drink, it brought shame on the family.
  * **Cana of Galilee**  - Cana refers to the small town which is in the larger area called Galilee.
  * **mother of Jesus** - The mother of Jesus was named Mary.
  * **Jesus also was invited** - The family of the husband (groom) was responsible for inviting guests. Alternate Translation: "The groom's family invited Jesus" or "They also invited Jesus" (UDB).
