====== Numbers Chapter 15 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What do the offerings at the feasts produce for Yahweh?

A. The offering at the feasts produce a sweet aroma for Yahweh. [15:3]

Q? What were the people to bring with their sacrifice as a drink offering?

A. The people were told to prepare wine as the drink offering with their sacrifice. [15:5]

Q? Who was told to follow these specific instructions about offerings?

A. All who were native-born Israelites must do these things in this way. [15:13]

Q? How is the law and decree to apply the foreigner staying with the people of Israel?

A. The same law and decree must apply to them and to the foreigner who is staying with them. [15:16]
