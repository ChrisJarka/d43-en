===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the land will shake and be in anguish** - The land is a metonym for the people who live on the land. Shaking and being in anguish are metonyms for being very afraid. AT: "the people who live in Babylon will shake with fear and be in anguish" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **anguish** - "much mental pain"
  * **against Babylon** - The city of Babylon is a metonym for the people of Babylon. AT: "against the people of Babylon" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **there is no inhabitant** - "no one lives"
