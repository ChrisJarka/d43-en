===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **In the first year of Darius … in the first year of his reign** - "In year one of Darius … in year one of his rule"
  * **the one who was made king** - AT: "the one who became king" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **over the realm** - "over the country" or "over the kingdom"
  * **Yahweh's word that had came to Jeremiah the prophet ** - This is the message that God gave to Jeremiah.
  * **that the number of years would be seventy **- AT: "that it would be 70 years" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
