====== //Ellipsis//  ======

===== //Examples from English//  =====
//In the following sentence: John can play the guitar, and Mary can, too//. The action that Mary does has been omitted.  The complete sentence would be: //John can play the guitar, and Mary can **play the guitar**, too.//

===== Examples from the Bible =====

Luke 21:10 - Then he said to them, "Nation will rise against nation, and kingdom against kingdom."  fully stated would be: "Nation will rise against nation, and kingdom //will rise// against kingdom."
