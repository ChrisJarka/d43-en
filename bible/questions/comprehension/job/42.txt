====== Job Chapter 42 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Job reply to Yahweh?

A. Job answered Yahweh and said that he knows Yahweh can do all things, his purposes can not be stopped, and that Job had spoken about things he did not understand or know about. [42:1 - 3]

Q? What did Yahweh say to Job?

A. Yahweh said to Job that he will ask Job about things and Job is to answer Yahweh. [42:4]

Q? What did Job say and do after hearing Yahweh?

A. Job said he now sees Yahweh and repents. [42:5 - 6]

Q? What did Yahweh say to Eliphaz after he had spoken to Job?

A. Yahweh said to Eliphaz that he and his two friends had not spoken right things about Yahweh as Job had done, so Yahweh's wrath was kindled against them.[42:7]

Q? What did Yahweh tell Eliphaz to do?

A. Yahweh told Eliphaz to take seven bulls and seven rams to offer for themselves as a burnt offering and to go to Job for him to pray for them. [42:8]

Q? What did Eliphaz, Bildad, and Zophar do?

A. They did as Yahweh had commanded them to do. [42:9]

Q? What did Yahweh do for Job?

A. Yahweh accepted Job. [42:9]
