====== divination ======

===== Definition: =====

Divination was a pagan form of communication with the higher powers.

  * Divination is often performed by interpreting patterns in animal bones or in remnants of a drink left behind in a cup.
  * Soothsayers practiced divination.
  * Divination was not condoned by Christians.
