===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He will be a sanctuary **  -  A sanctuary can be a place of worship or safety.  This can be translated as, "Yahweh will be a refuge"
  * **but he will be a stone that makes one trip and a rock that makes one stumble **  - This compares Yahweh to a stone that can be built into a sanctuary or it can cause people to fall when they walk blindly into it. Yahweh will be stumbling stone to those who have no faith.  It can be translated as " but Yahweh will be a stone that makes one who has no faith slip and a rock that makes one stumble"  (See: Metaphor and Doublet)
  * **to both the houses of Israel **- This refers to the people in the Northern kingdom and the Southern kingdom.  This can be translated  "to the people in Judah and Israel." (See: Metonymy)
  * **as a trap and as a snare **- This compares a bird caught in a trap to the faithless person who is caught in the trap of sin. (See: Metaphor,Doublet)
  * **inhabitants of Jerusalem **- "people living in Jerusalem"
  * **fall and be broken**  - "fall down and never get up again" (UDB)
