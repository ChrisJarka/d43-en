====== Jeremiah Chapter 42 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did all the army commanders and all the people say to Jeremiah the prophet?

A. They asked that their pleas come before him, that he pray for them to Yahweh, asking God to tell them the way they should go and what they should do. [42:1-3]

Q? How did Jeremiah respond to them?

A. He said, I have heard you, I will pray to God and I will tell you whatever Yahweh answers. [42:4]

Q? Then what did the people say to Jeremiah?

A. They said they want Yahweh to be a true and faithful witness against them. If they do not do everything Yahweh tells them to do, whether it is good or bad they will obey, so that it may be well with them. [42:5-6]

Q? What happened after 10 days?

A. The word of Yahweh came to Jeremiah and he told them, Yahweh said, "If you go back and live in this land, then I will build you and not tear you down; I will plant you and not pull you up, for I will turn back the disaster that I have brought on you. Do not fear the king of Babylon, whom you are fearing". [42:7-11]
