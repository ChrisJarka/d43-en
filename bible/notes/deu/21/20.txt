===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel as if they were one man, so the words "you" and "your" are singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])


  * **This son of ours** - "Our son"
  * **he will not obey our voice** - Here "voice" refers to the whole person. AT: "he will not obey us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **a glutton** - a person who eats and drinks too much
  * **a drunkard** - a person who drinks too much alcohol and gets drunk often
  * **you will remove the evil from among you** - The adjective "evil" can be translated as a noun phrase. AT: "you must remove from among the Israelites the person who does this evil thing" or "you must execute this evil person" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **All Israel** - Here the metonym "Israel" refers to the people of Israel. AT: "All the people of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **will hear of it and fear** - The full meaning of this statement can be made explicit. AT: "will hear about what happened to the son and be afraid of getting the same punishment" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
