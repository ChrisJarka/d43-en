===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh has satisfied his rage. He has poured out his burning anger**  - These two clauses say basically the same thing. (See: Parallelism)
  * **Yahweh has satisfied his rage**  - This means that he has shown his anger to the farthest extreme. AT: "Yahweh has shown the full extent of his anger."
  * **He has poured out his burning anger**  - God's anger is like a hot, burning liquid that he pours out. (See: Idiom)
  * **he has kindled a fire in Zion**  - When God shows his anger it is as if he burns down Jerusalem. (See: Metaphor)
