====== persevere, perseverance ======

===== Definition: =====

To persevere means to continue in a belief or a task even though it may take a long time or be very difficult.

  * To persevere can also mean to maintain Christian character under difficult trials or circumstances.
  * Perseverance is the character quality that enables a person to keep believing or doing what they should even when it is very difficult.
  * "Perseverance" has positive connotations, while the meaning of "stubborn" is similar but with negative connotations.
