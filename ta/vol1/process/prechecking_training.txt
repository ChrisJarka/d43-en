===== Training Before Checking Begins =====

This module answers the question: What should I know about checking before I begin?\\
=== Before Checking ===

It is recommended that you consult the [[en:ta:vol1:checking:toc_checkvol1_2|Checking Manual]] frequently as you check this content. Before you start checking, we recommend that you start working your way through the Checking Manual until you understand what is required at each level. As you work through the checking process, you will need to consult the Checking Manual frequently. 

Note: Content will not be published to the unfoldingWord website or mobile app until it has attained at least checking level 1.
