====== famine ======

===== Definition: =====

A famine is a widespread disaster that involves an extreme lack of food throughout a country or region.

  * Failure of food crops can be caused by weather conditions, crop disease, insects, war, or bad government.
  * In Amos 8:11 the term "famine" is used figuratively to refer to a time when God punished his people by no longer speaking to them. This could be translated with the word for "famine" in your language, or with a phrase such as, "extreme lack" or "severe deprivation."
