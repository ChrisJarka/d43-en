====== appoint ======

===== Definition: =====

To appoint means to choose someone to do a specific task or to fulfill a special role.

  * To be appointed also refers to being chosen to receive something, as in Acts 13:48: "All who were appointed for eternal life believed."
  * The term "appointed" can also refer to a chosen time, as in "the appointed time." (Examples are Daniel 8:19 and Matthew 26:18).
