===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Oh, here he comes** - The word “Oh” here adds emphasis to what follows. AT: “Indeed”
  * **leaping over the mountains,
jumping over the hills** - "leaping on the mountains, running quickly on the hills." The beloved runs quickly and gracefully like a gazelle, even over the rough terrain of the mountains and the hills.

  * **leaping over the mountains, jumping over the hills**  - She is showing his excitement about seeing her, but not saying he is actually doing this. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole|Hyperbole]])
  * **My beloved is like a gazelle or a young stag **  - She is not saying he is an actual gazelle or stag. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile|Simile]])
  * **lattice**  - wooden strips crossed in a pattern used to partially block light or access
