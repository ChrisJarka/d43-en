===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **for the sea has spoken ****T**he sea is speaking as a person about what has happened to Tyre - (See: Personification)
  * **the mighty one of the sea.**  The city of Tyre is compared to a mighty fortress where once there was great commerce and activity. It could be translated - “Tyre was once a great seaport” - (See: Metaphor)
  * **I have not labored nor given birth, nor have I raised young men nor brought up young women**- Giving birth to children is like initiating and birthing new commerce and trade agreements.  It could be translated - “but now it is no longer a place where trade is being conducted" See: Simile
