===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh gives Ezekiel his message to Gog.
  * **Son of man** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:02:01|2:3]].
  * **prophesy against** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:04:06|4:7]].
  * **Gog** - See how you translated this name in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:38:01|38:2]].
  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:05:05|5:5]].
  * **Behold**  - Alternate translation: “Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you.”
  * **I am against you** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:21:01|21:3]].
  * **I will knock your bow out of your left hand and make the arrows fall from your right hand.** - This phrase compares the way Gog will lose his army to a man who loses his bow and arrows in battle. AT: "I will destroy your military power." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
