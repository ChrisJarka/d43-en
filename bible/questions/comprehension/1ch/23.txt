====== 1 Chronicles Chapter 23 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What were some of the last things David did as king of Israel?

A. David made Solomon king in his place, and he had the Levites counted, that were at least thirty years old. [23:1-3]

Q? What jobs were these Levites given?

A. They were divided into groups; some were to oversee the work of Yahweh's house, some were officers and judges, some gatekeepers, and some were to praise Yahweh with instruments. [23:4-5]

Q? What were Aaron, his descendants, and Moses chosen to do permanently?

A. Aaron and his descendants were to consecrate the most holy items, offer incense to Yahweh, serve him, and give blessings in Yahweh's name forever. Moses
