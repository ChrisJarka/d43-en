===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **walk appropriately** - “live in a holy way” (See: [[en:ta:translate:Metaphor]])​
  * **as in the day** - “in a visible way” (See: [[en:ta:translate:Metaphor]])
  * **strife** - “plotting against and arguing with other people”
  * **jealousy** - "negative feelings against another person's success or advantage over others.
  * **put on the Lord Jesus Christ** - This means to wear Christ as if he were our outer clothing that people can see. (See: [[en:ta:translate:Metaphor]])​
  * **make no provision for the flesh** - “don’t allow your old evil heart any opportunity at all for doing wicked things”
  * **provision** - The act of providing or supplying something for the present or future needs of self or others.
