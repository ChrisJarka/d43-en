===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **and I saw one coming with the clouds of heaven like a son of man** - AT: "and I saw someone who looked like the Son of Man coming with the clouds of heaven" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * ** and was presented before him** - AT: "and someone presented the Son of Man before the Ancient of Days" or "and he stood before him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **Ancient of Days** - See how you translated this title in [[:en:bible:notes:dan:07:09|7:9]]
  * **Authority to rule and glory and a kingdom were given to him** - AT: "The one who looked like the Son of Man received authority to rule, and glory, and a kingdom"
  * **that will never be destroyed** - AT: "that no one will ever destroy"
