===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then behold**  - This marks the beginning of another event in the larger story. It may involve different people than the previous events. Your language may have a way of doing this.
  * **and said, "Yahweh says**  - AT: "and told him that Yahweh said"
  * **Have you seen this great army?**  AT: "Look at Benhadad's large army" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **place it into your hand**  - AT: "give you victory over the army" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **232**  - "two hundred and thirty two" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers|Translate Numbers]])
  * **seven thousand**  - "7,000"
