====== Crete, Cretan ======

===== Facts: =====

Crete is an island that is located off the southern coast of Greece. A Cretan is someone who lives on this island.

  * Paul traveled to the island of Crete during his missionary journeys.
  * In his letter to Titus, the apostle Paul mentioned that the Cretans at that time had a reputation for being liars and lazy.
