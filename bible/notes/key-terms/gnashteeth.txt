====== gnash teeth, grind teeth ======

===== Definition: =====

<font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>To gnash or grind the teeth means to clench the teeth and scrape them back and forth against each other. This often shows extreme pain or anger.</font>

  * The Bible tells us that gnashing teeth is one thing those in hell will do in their terrible distress.
