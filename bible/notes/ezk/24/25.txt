===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you**  - This is a pronoun referring to Ezekiel
  * **I**  - This is a pronoun referring to Yahweh
  * **Son of Man**  - see how we translated in Ezekiel 17:1
  * **will I not take in that day their refuges? The joy of their pride? The lusts of their eyes? And whatever their souls lift up? Their sons and daughters? **- Yahweh is rebuking Ezekiel (Rhetorical Question).
  * **Refugee**  - This means someone who has been forced to leave a country because of war.
  * **to give a report - **  This means to tell you what has happened or to tell you the news.
  * **In that day your mouth will be opened up to **- An alternate translation will be "when that happens, you will be able to speak again without constraint" (UDB).
  * **declare **  - This means 'to say in an official or public way.'
  * **be mute **- This means to be unable to speak or talk.
  * **will be a sign **  - This means a symbol or miracle, that Yahweh
