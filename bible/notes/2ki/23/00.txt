====== 2 Kings 23 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

The story of Josiah ended

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Josiah destroyed idolatry in Judah and in the deserted areas of Israel. But he fought against Egypt and was killed in battle. Then Egypt set up a government of Judah under it.
