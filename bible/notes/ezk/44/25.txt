===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the house of Israel about the descendats of Zadok who serve as priests.
  * **They will not go to a dead person so as to become unclean** - "They should not become unclean by approaching a dead person"
  * **a sister who has not slept with a man** - "a sister who does not yet have a husband"
  * **otherwise, they will become unclean** - "for the sake of these people they may become unclean"
  * **After a priest has become unclean** - "After a priest becomes clean" or "After a priests performs rituals to become acceptable to serve me again"
  * **Before the day** - "On the day" (UDB)
 

  * **But after he has been unclean** - After the priest is near the dead body of his relative 
  * **and on the day he come to the sanctuary, to the inner courtyard to minister in the sanctuary** After the time has passed, he should go to the sanctuary and minister.
  * **they will account seven days for him** - seven days will pass
