===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Who will you surpass in beauty? -**  Surpass means 'outdo', 'go beyond', 'improve on', or 'exceed'. "Who will you look better than?"
  * **Go down and lay with the uncircumcised! -**  "Go and be like those that do not follow the Lord Yahweh."
  * **C they will seize her and her entourage! - **"The ungodly will capture many of the people of Egypt!"
