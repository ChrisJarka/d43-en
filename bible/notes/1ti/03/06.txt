===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He should not be a new convert** - "He should not be a new believer" or "He must be a mature believer"
  * **become proud and fall into condemnation as the devil** - The phrase "fall into" is an idiom that means to receive or experience. AT: "become proud and have God condemn him the as he condemned the devil." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **those outside** - "those outside of the church." Here "outside" is an idiom that refers to unbelievers. AT: "those who are not Christians." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])  
  * **he does not fall into disgrace** - The phrase "fall into" is an idiom that means to receive or experience. AT: "nothing causes him shame before the unbelievers." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **and the trap of the devil** - Paul speaks about the devil causing someone to sin as if he were a hunter catching an animal in a trap. AT: "and so that devil does not cause him to sin." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
