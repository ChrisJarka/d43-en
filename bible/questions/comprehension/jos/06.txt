====== Joshua Chapter 06 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Yahweh promise to Joshua about Jericho?

A. Yahweh promised Joshua that he was giving Jericho into his hand. [6:1]

Q? How many times for the first six days were the men of Isreal to walk around the walls of Jericho?

A. The men of Israel were to walk around the wall of Jericho one time for six days. [6:3]

Q? What did Yahweh tell the men to do on the seventh day?

A. On the seventh day Yahweh told the men to walk around Jericho seven times and the priests give blasts on their trumpets. [6:5]

Q? What did Yahweh say would happen if the men of Israel an the priest did this?

A. Yahweh said the the walls areound Jericho would fall down if the men and priests did this. [6:5]

Q? What did Joshua command the people not to do until the seventh day?

A. Joshua commanded the people not to shout until the seventh day. [6:10]

Q? Did the people follow the commands Joshua had given to them?

A. Yes. The people did exactly as Joshua had said. [6:11-14]
