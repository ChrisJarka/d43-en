===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **your righteousness**  - Throughout this verse, the word your refers to the people of Israel collectively. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_youdual]]) 
    * **your heart**  - In this usage, the word heart refers to the people's choices or thoughts.
