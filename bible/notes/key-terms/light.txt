====== light ======

===== Definition: =====

Light is used in the Bible as a metaphor to describe righteousness, holiness and truth. It is the opposite of darkness, which represents sin and moral corruption.

  * Jesus said, "I am the light of the world" to express that he brings God's true message to the world.
  * Christians are commanded to "walk in light," which means they should be holy and avoid evil.
  * The Apostle John stated that God is light, and there is no darkness in him.

(See also: holiness, righteousness, truth)
