===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **After this**  - "Afterwards" or "After purchasing the field"
  * **in the cave of the field of Machpelah, which is next to Mamre**  - "the cave in the field that is in Machpelah next to Mamre"
  * **that is, Hebron**  - "which is Hebron"
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **So the field and the cave in it passed to Abraham as a property for a burial site from the sons of Heth**  - "So the field and the cave in it were sold by the sons of Heth to Abraham as a permanent possession for a burial site."
  * **sons of Heth**  - Hittites
