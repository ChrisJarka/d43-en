===== TFT: =====

===== ULB: =====

<usfm> \v 9 Now let them remove their promiscuity and the corpses of their kings from before me, and I will dwell in their midst forever! </usfm>

===== Important Terms: =====

  * promiscuity (fornication)


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **let them remove their promiscuity ... from before me** - "they must stop doing wrong in front of God"
  * **their promiscuity** - The word "promiscuity" refers to Israel's unfaithfulness to God when they worshiped false gods.
  * **the corpses of their kings** - "the bodies of their dead kings"
  * **I will dwell in their midst**  -  "I will live in the middle of them" or "I will live among them"(UDB)
