===== translationNotes: =====

  * **look** - "listen" or "pay attention"
  * **the big house will be smashed to pieces, and the little house to bits** - These two phrases share similar meanings. The contrast between "the big house" and "the little house" means that this refers to all houses. AT: "all the houses will be smashed into small pieces" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **the big house will be smashed to pieces** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the enemy will smash the big house to pieces" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **to pieces ... to bits** - You can use the same word for both of these phrases.
  * **the little house to bits** - This can be stated with the understood information included. AT: "enemies will smash the little house to bits" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
