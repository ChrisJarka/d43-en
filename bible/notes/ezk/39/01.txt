===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Gog**- Name of a king, (see How to Translate Names)
  * **Behold** - See Standard Translation Notes
  * **Rosh**- This is the name of an unknown people or place (see How to Translate Names)
  * **Meshech and Tubal**- Names of nations (see How to Translate Names)
  * **Mountains of Israel** -  a term used to mean the land both high and low, where the people of Israel are living.
  * **Strike your bow from your left hand and make arrows fall from your right hand.** - Alternate translation: "Your bow and arrows will not help you win the battle. They will be useless."
