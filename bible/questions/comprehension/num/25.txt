====== Numbers Chapter 25 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What caused Yahweh's anger to be kindled against Israel?

A. The men began to sleep with the women of Moab and the men of Israel started sacrificing to and worshipping Moabite gods. [25:1-3]

Q? What did Yahweh tell Moses to do to Israel's leaders?

A. Yahweh told Moses to execute all the Israelite leaders who have joined in worshiping Baal of Peor. [25:4-5]

Q? What happened to the Israelite who brought a Midianite woman into the camp in the sight of Moses?

A. Phineas the priest took a spear and thrust a spear into the bodies of both the man and the woman. [25:6-8]

Q? How many people died as a result of the plague that God had sent?

A. Those who died were 24,000 in number. [25:9]

Q? Why is Yahweh's rage turned away from the people of Israel?

A. Yahweh's rage is turned away because Phinehas was passionate with Yahweh's zeal. [25:10-11]

Q? Why did Yahweh make a covenant of everlasting priesthood with Phinehas?

A. Yahweh made a covenant with Phinehas because he was zealous for Yahweh and had made an atonement for the people of Israel. [25:12-13]

Q? Why did Yahweh tell Moses to treat the Midianitesas enemies and attack them?

A. The Midianites were deceitful and led the Israelites into evil. [25:16-18]
