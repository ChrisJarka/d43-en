  * **I will give thanks...reaches to the skies** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:psa:057:000|57:9-10]], but notice that "Yahweh" and "above the heavens" are different
  * **your covenant faithfulness is great above the heavens** - AT: "your covenant faithfulness has no end" or "your covenant faithfulness is the best and remains forever." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
