===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **He opened their minds** - "He enabled them to understand" (UBD)
    * **Thus it is written** - "This is what they wrote" (UDB). (See: [[:en:ta:vocabulary:active|Active or Passive]])
    * **in his name**  - in Christ's authority
    * **all the nations**  - "all the ethnic communities" or "all the people-groups"
