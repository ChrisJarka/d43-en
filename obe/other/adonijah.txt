====== Adonijah ======

===== Definition: =====

The fourth son of King David.

  * Adonijah tried to take the throne as King after Absalom and Amnon's death, however Bathsheba convinced David to have Solomon annointed as king.
  * Adonijah tried to make himself king again and was put to death by Solomon.
