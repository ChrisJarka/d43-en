====== purify ======

==== (purify, purification) ====
===== Definition: =====


To cause something to be pure and free from anything that contaminates or pollutes it. In a ceremonial sense, to purify someone or something is to make it fit for use in worship or in the home. In a spiritual sense to purify a person is to cleanse a person from their sin.


    * In the Old Testament, the blood of an animal was used for purification.
    * In the Old Testament, certain rituals were performed with water and ashes to ceremonially purify a priest for duty.
    * People who believe in Jesus are purified from their sin by Jesus' sacrifice.
