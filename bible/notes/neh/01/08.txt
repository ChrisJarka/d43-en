===== Translation Notes: =====

Nehemiah continues his prayer to God.

  * **call to mind**  - AT: "remember". (See: idiom)
  * **You said -**  This pronoun refers to God.
  * **scatter you**  - AT: "spread you out," or "disperse you," or "split you up".
  * **return -**  AT: "turn back," or "come back," or "become faithful again".
  * **the place **- AT: "the land of Israel".
  * **where I have chosen to make my name remain**  - AT: "where I have decided to make my name dwell," or, "where I make myself known," or "where I have shown the world who I am". (See: Idiom)
