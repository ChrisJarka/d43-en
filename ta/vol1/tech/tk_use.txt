===== How to Use the translationKeyboard in translationStudio =====

  * Tap the tS icon to open the program
  * the Edit window opens
  * Tap to type - the keyboard slides up.
  * Notice that the default language is written on the space bar of the keyboard.
  * Tap-slide your finger on the keyboard's space bar to choose another language.
  * Stop sliding the finger when the desired language shows.
