====== mold ======

===== Definition: =====

A mold is a hollowed out piece of wood or metal used to form objects out of gold, silver, or other metals that can be poured when hot but harden when cooled. 

 as a verb- the process of being made into a shape or likeness.

  * Ancient people used molds to create ceramic objects, gold and silver jewelry and cast idols.
  * The Bible speaks of a believer being molded into the image of Jesus Christ by the work of the indwelling Holy Spirit.

(See also: other)
