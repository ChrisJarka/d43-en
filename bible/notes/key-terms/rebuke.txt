====== rebuke ======

===== Definition: =====

To rebuke is to give someone a stern verbal correction in order to keep the person safe from making bigger mistakes.

  * The New Testament commands Christians to rebuke their fellows if they openly sin.
  * In the Old Testament Balaam was rebuked by his donkey.
  * The book of Proverbs instructs parents to rebuke their children when they are disobedient.
  * A rebuke is typically done to prevent the one who committed the wrong from involving themselves further in sin.
