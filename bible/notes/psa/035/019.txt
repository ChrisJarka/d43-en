===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **my enemies wrongfully rejoice over me** - "my wrongful enemies rejoice over me" or "my enemies, who are deceitful, rejoice over me"
    * **their wicked schemes** - "their evil plans"
    * **they do not speak peace** - AT: "their words do not promote peace" or "they do not want peace"
    * **devise deceitful words against those who are unsuspecting** - AT: "plot twisted words to trap those who have no reason to expect evil"
