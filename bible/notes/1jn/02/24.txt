===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **As for you**  - In 2:24-26 the word "you" is plural and refers to the people John is writing to.  (See: [[en:ta:translate:formsofyou-plural]])
  * **let that which you have heard from the beginning remain in you** - The phrase "remain in you" is an idiom that means a person continues to remember and believe what he has heard. This can be translated as "continue to trust what we have taught you about Jesus just as you have since you first became believers." (See: [[en:ta:translate:idiom]])
  * **If that which you heard from the beginning remains in you** - "If you continue to trust what we taught you"
  * **also remain in the Son and in the Father** - "always belong to the Son and to the Father"
  * **And this is the promise which he promised us** - "And this is what he promised us"
  * **he** - Here the word "he" is ambiguous and could refer to God the Father or Jesus.  (See: [[en:ta:translate:ambiguity]])
  * **us** - Here the word "us" refers to John and all believers. (See: [[en:ta:translate:inclusive]])
  * **lead you astray**  - "try to make you believe a lie" or "draw you away from God and His truth"
