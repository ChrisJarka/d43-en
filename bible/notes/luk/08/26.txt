===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **region of the Gerasenes**  - Gerasenes were people from the city called Gerasa.
    * **opposite Galilee**  -  "on the other side of the lack from Galilee."
    * **a certain man**  - This refers to a particular adult male.
    * **had demons**  - "he was controlled by demons"
    * **he had worn no clothes**  - "he had not worn clothes"
    * **tombs**  - These are places where people put dead bodies.  The fact that the man was living in them indicates that these were not holes dug down in the ground.
