===== Translation Notes: =====

The author continues to describe the bronze carts that Huram made.

    * **every stand** - This refers to each of the 10 stands that Huram made.  The stands were movable and had wheels, so the stands may also be called carts. See how you translated verse 27. (See UDB)
    * **four bronze wheels and axles** - "four bronze wheels and 2 axles" (UDB)
    * **axle** - a shaft, pole or spindle that holds the wheels of a vehicle
    * **its four corners** - "its" is a possessive pronoun. AT: "the four corners of each stand"
