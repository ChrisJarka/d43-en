====== clean, cleanse ======


===== Definition: =====


To be clean is to be free from dirt or stain. It is often used metaphorically to mean "to be free from sin or unholiness."


    * In ancient Israel, only certain foods, animals, and hygiene practices were considered clean for use or consumption.
    * In biblical usage, "clean" can reference moral purity, ceremonial purity or physical purity.


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:holiness|holiness]], [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:unclean|unclean]])
