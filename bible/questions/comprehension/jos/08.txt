====== Joshua Chapter 08 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Yahweh say for Joshua to do to take the city of Ai?

A. Yahweh told Joshua to take all the people of war to Ai and set an ambush behind the city. [8:1-2]

Q? What were the people to do with the plunder from Ai that was different from Jericho?

A. Yahweh told the people they could take the plunder and cattle for themselves.
