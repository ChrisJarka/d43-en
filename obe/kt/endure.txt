====== endure, endurance ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "endure" literally means to bear something difficult with patience. It also means to stand firm when times of testing come, without giving up.

  * In the Bible, "enduring to the end" refers to remaining loyal to the Lord and being committed to obeying him even if this loyalty causes a person to suffer.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * The term "endure" can be translated as "persevere" or "keep believing" or "continue to do what God wants you to do" or "stand firm."
  * Ways to translate "endurance" could include, "perseverance" or "continuing to believe" or "remaining faithful."
