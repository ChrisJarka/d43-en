===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For the mentality of the flesh is death** - Here, "flesh" is described as a person with a will of its own. Paul is referring to people who are concerned with pleasThe word "mentality" refers to the way a person thinks or the attitude of their mind. The word “flesh” refers to the sinful nature of human beings.  Alternate translation: For the result of having our mind set on the desires of the flesh is spiritual death."
  * **mentality of the Spirit** - This is the opposite of "mentality of the flesh."
