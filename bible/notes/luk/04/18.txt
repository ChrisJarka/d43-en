===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **favorable**  - this word originally meant "right-handed"; that is, in the culture of origin one accepted with the right hand and rejected with the left. Your culture and language may do something similar
    * **to the poor**  - that is, to those who are in extreme poverty, destitute, possibly homeless
    * **to set free those who are oppressed**  - that is, "to send with release those having been broken."
