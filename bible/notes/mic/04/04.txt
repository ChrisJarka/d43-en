===== Translation Notes: =====

  * ** Under his vine,and under his own fig trees. ** - This passage continues that theme from the previous versus. The purpose of war will be no more. People will no longer need to fight for food.
  * **In the name of its god **-  Notice that God here is spelled with a little “g,” Micah is making a statement of power and authority, their god goes unnamed and does not speak. While Micah’s God does speak and is named.
