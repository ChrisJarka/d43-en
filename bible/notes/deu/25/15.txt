===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel about using fair weights and measures when buying and selling.

  * **A perfect and just** - "A correct and fair"
  * **weight...measure** - See how you translated these in [[en:bible:notes:deu:25:13|25:13]].
  * **For all who do such things,  all that act unrighteously** - "because everyone who cheats people by using different sized weights and measures"
