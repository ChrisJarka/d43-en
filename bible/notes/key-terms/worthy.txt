====== worthy ======


===== Definition: =====


The term worthy describes someone or something which has been tested and found to have value.


    * In the New Testament Jesus is said to be the only one worthy to open the seals of God's book because He (Jesus) suffered as the Lamb of God.
    * In the New Testament some believers are considered worthy to suffer for the name of Jesus because they have been proven faithful in trials.
