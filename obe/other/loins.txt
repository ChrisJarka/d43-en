====== loins ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "loins" refers to the part of the body of an animal or person that is between the lower ribs and the hip bones.

  * The phrase "gird up your loins" is referring to tucking the bottom of a shirt or robe into your belt in order to move with ease. Some versions say "tuck your robe up into your belt." This idiom refers to preparing to work hard.
  * The loins are considered as a source of strength.
  * When referring to human beings, this term is often translated as "waist."
