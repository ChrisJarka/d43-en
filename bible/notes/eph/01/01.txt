===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Paul **- This book is a letter from Paul to members of the church at Ephesus. It might be helpful to insert the word “From Paul” or the phrase “This letter is from Paul” at the beginning to understand this is a letter.
  * **through the will of God **- The Greek word for “will” means actively chosen, so this could be translated “chosen by God” or “by the will of God”.
  * **set apart for God **- The Greek word from which this phrase comes means “morally blameless” or “consecrated” or “holy ones”. This word is often translated.as “saints”.
