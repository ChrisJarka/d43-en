===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Touch no unclean thing** - The law of Moses states what can make one unclean by touching it. AT: "Touch only those things that are clean." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
  * **I will be a Father to you** - AT: "I will care for you as a loving father cares for his children" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **and you will be my sons and daughters** - "and you will be my children" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
