====== serve ======


===== Definition: =====


To serve someone is to do something that benefits that person. Someone may serve because he is obligated to serve, or he may serve simply because he wants to serve.


    * "To serve someone" can also be translated as, "to minister to someone" or, "to work for someone."
    * "To serve God" can be translated as "to worship God" or "to do what God requires."
    * Though Jesus is God, he said that he came to serve and to give his life to save people.
    * "To serve tables" is "to give food to people" or "to wait on."
    * People who teach others about God are said to serve both God and the ones they are teaching.
    * To serve is to render obedience and worship to God.
    * To serve is to work through or perform a task or a term of duty or service.
    * Servants are attendants or ministers.
