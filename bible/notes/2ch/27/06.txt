===== translationNotes: =====

  * **he walked firmly before Yahweh his God** - Jotham's actions and lifestyle are spoken of as if they were a path upon which he walked. That he walked firmly means that he was devoted to living in this way. AT: "he faithfully obeyed Yahweh his God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **his ways** - Jotham's actions are spoken of as if they were a path upon which he walked. AT: "the things that he did" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **see** - "Indeed." The word "see" here adds emphasis to what follows.
  * **they are written in the book** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "someone has written them in the book" or "you can read of them in the book" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the book of the kings of Israel and Judah** - This is a book that no longer exists.
