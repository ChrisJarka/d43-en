===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Will you hide yourself, forever? **- (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **what uselessness**  - "what purpose";  "what unfruitfulness"
  * **the sons of man**  - "people and their descendents" (See: Metonymy)
  * **Who can live and not die or rescue his own soul from the power of sheol? **- AT: "Who can live forever, or rescue his own soul from death?" (See: Doublets and See: Parallelism) See also the Discussion section below.
