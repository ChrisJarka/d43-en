====== 1 Samuel 23 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Saul chasing David ==
Saul wanted to find and kill David. But David trusted in God and God kept on rescuing David. David rescued the town of Keilah from the Philistines who were stealing their harvest. So Saul went to Keilah to capture David. David found out and fled to the wilderness near Ziph. The men of Ziph told Saul they would help him catch David. David fled deeper into the wilderness but Saul was surrounding him when Saul heard that the Philistines had invaded, so he left to fight them.    


=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metonymy ==
Jonathan strengthened David's faith. Reassuring him with a [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] of hand for the whole person: "For the hand of Saul my father will not find you."
