===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **"Isaiah prophesied well about you hypocrites, he wrote,"** - ""Isaiah spoke words from God about you hypocrites.  Isaiah wrote," The Isaiah reference is from Isaiah 29:13-[[en/bible/notes/isa/29/13|29:13-14]]
  * **This people honors me with their lips, but their heart is far from me** - The word "lips" refers to what people are saying. Alternate translation: "The people of Israel honor me with what they say, but they do not honor me in their thoughts." (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  * **Empty worship they offer me** - "Their worship of me is in vain"
  * **teaching the rules of men as their doctrines** - "because they are only doing what other men have told them to do"
