===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **the one**  - This refers to the Babylonians.
    * **Woe to the one building a city with blood, and establishing a town in iniquity** - These two phrases are saying the same thing in different ways.  Alternate translation:"A warning to the Chaldeans who built their cities with what they have stolen from the people they have killed" (See: [[en:ta:translate:parallelism]])
    * **the one building a city with blood** -  "the one who builds a city by killing people." The phrase "with blood" refers to killing people. (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]]) Alternate translation: "the one who kills people and steals their gold in order to build a city." (See [[en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]])
    * **establishing a town in iniquity** - "The one who starts a town through evil behavior" The Chaldeans have built their cities using money stolen from the people they've killed. (See: [[en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]])
    * **Is it not from Yahweh of Hosts that peoples labor for fire and that the nations weary themselves for nothing** - This question is not asked for an answer but to help people understand that God causes the destruction of what people build. It is said in two diffent ways in order to make the meaning more clear.  Alternate translation: "Yahweh is the one who causes people's labor to be detroyed by fire and result in nothing. (See: [[en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]]) (See: [[en:ta:translate:parallelism]])
