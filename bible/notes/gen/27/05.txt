===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now Rebekah heard it when Isaac spoke to Esau his son.**  - "Now Rebekah had heard Isaac speaking to Esau his son."
  * **Esau went to the field**  - "So, when Esau went to the field"
  * **See here, I heard your father speak to Esau your brother. He said, ** - "listen, I heard your father speak to your brother Esau, saying"
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **Bring me game and make me delicious food**  - "Bring the game to me and prepare it so that it is tasty." 
  * **bless you in the presence of Yahweh**  - "bless you before the Lord"
  * **before my death** - "before I die."
