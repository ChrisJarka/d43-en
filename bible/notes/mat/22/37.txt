===== Translation Notes: =====
Jesus is answering the lawyer's question about the greatest commandment in the law.

  * **You will love the Lord your God with all your heart, with all your soul, and with all your mind**  - This is a quote from [[en:bible:notes:deu:06:04|Deut 6:5]] "Heart... soul... mind..." are representing the whole person. Each words represents a different aspect of the whole person, and the are all three used here for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
