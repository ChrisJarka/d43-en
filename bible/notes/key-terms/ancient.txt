====== ancient, of old ======

===== Definition: =====

The terms "ancient" and "of old" refer to people or objects that are very old.

  * In the Bible, the use of these terms refer to people or objects in early or earlier biblical times.
  * The term "Ancient of Days" is another name for God.
  * Ancient can refer to the old or senior people in a culture.

(See also: other)
