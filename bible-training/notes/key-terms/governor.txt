====== governor ======

===== Definition: =====


A governor was a person who was the leader of a region or territory but who was appointed by a higher authority.


    * "Governor" is a words used to translated many different Hebrew and Greek words.
    * "Governor" usually refers to a person who exercised absolute authority over an area but who still was subject to a king or emperor.
