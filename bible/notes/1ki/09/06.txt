  * **if you turn away **- "if you disobey me"

  * **if you go and worship other gods and bow down to them **- Commandments and stautes mean the same thing. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet|Doublet]])
  * **I have consecrated for my name **  - AT: "I have set my name apart for worship" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **I will cast it out of my sight **- AT: "I will not protect it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
