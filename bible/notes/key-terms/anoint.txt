====== anoint, anointed ======


===== Definition: =====


To anoint means to set something apart for service to God.  Often, anointing is accompanied by the rubbing or pouring of oil on a person as part of a ceremony.  This anointing dedicates them and begins their terms of service.


    * In biblical times, kings and priests were often anointed to begin their reign.
    * "Anointed" refers to someone who has been anointed. The Messiah is "the anointed one."
    * In the early church, anointing was also used as a ritual for the sick.
