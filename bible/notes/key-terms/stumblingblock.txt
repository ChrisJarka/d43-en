====== stumbling block ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "stumbling block" literally refers to a physical object that causes a person to trip over it and fall.

  * A stumbling block can be a snare or trap that may cause one to fall away or fail.
  * Figuratively, a "stumbling block" is something that prevents someone from having faith in Jesus or that causes someone to not grow spiritually.
