====== Psalms 092 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 92 is a psalm of praise for God's kindness and faithfulness.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

God has caused evil people to be destroyed and good people to be happy and useful even when they grow old.
