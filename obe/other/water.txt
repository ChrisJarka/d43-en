====== water, waters ======

===== Definition: =====

In addition to its primary meaning, "water" also often refers to a body of water, such as an ocean, sea, lake, or river.

  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The term "waters" refers to bodies of water or many sources of water. It can also be a general reference for a large quantity of water.</font>
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>To "water" livestock and other animals means to "provide water for" them. In Bible times, this involved drawing water up out of a well and pouring it into troughs for the animals to drink from.</font>
  * God is referred to as the Spring of living water to his people, the source of spiritual power. Jesus also referred to the Holy Spirit as a stream of living water (John 7:37-39).
