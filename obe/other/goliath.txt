====== Goliath ======

===== Facts: =====

Goliath was a huge Philistine soldier who was killed by David's skilled use of a sling-shot in their one-on-one battle in I Samuel Chapter 17.

  * Goliath was approximately 9 feet 9 inches or 297cm tall.
  * Goliath's armor weighed approximately 125 pounds or 56.6kg
