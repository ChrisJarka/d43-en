===== Facts: =====

<font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Arabia is the largest peninsula in the world covering nearly 3,000,000 square kilometers. It is between the Red Sea and the Gulf of</font> Aqaba<font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>, extending to the Persian Gulf. It consisted of Mount Sinai and modern Saudi Arabia</font>

  * Arabia is the region where the religion of Islam originated. It may have been the region Abraham's son Ishmael inhabited.
  * The wandering of the Israelites in the desert was in Arabia.
  * Also refers to specific people groups as in Jeremiah 25, Isaiah 21 and Ezekiel 27.
  * Paul was in Arabia
  * Midianites (Ismael's descendants) lived in Arabia
