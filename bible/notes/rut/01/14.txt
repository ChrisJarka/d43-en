===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **lifted up their voices and cried **- means that they cried out loud or wept bitterly.  (See: [[:en:ta:figuresofspeech:Idiom | Idiom]])
  * **Listen**  - "Listen" here means "please take notice."
  * **Ruth held on to her**  - That is, "clung to her". It is implied, "and refused to leave her" or "and would not leave her". (See: [[:en:ta:vocabulary:Implicit | Implicit]])
  * **your sister-in-law**  - That is, "the wife of your husband's brother" or "Orpah".
