===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the Word of Yahweh came to me and said** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:16|3:16]].
  * **Son of man** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:02:01|2:3]].
  * **Have you come to inquire of me** - Yahweh uses this question to emphasize what the elders planned to do, so that he can contrast it with his response. AT: "You say that you have come to ask if there is any message from me." (UDB) (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **As I live** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]].
  * **I will not be inquired of by you** - AT: "I will not allow you to ask for any message from me." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]].
