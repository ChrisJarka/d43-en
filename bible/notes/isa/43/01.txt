===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **what Yahweh says, he who created you, Jacob, and he who formed you, Israel**  - Yahweh, the one who established your nation , the one who caused you to become a nation, says (See: Doublet).
  * **redeemed you**  - rescued you
  * **I have called you by your name, you are mine**  - I have called you by your name, in order that you may belong to me. Now you are mine. (See: Metonymy)
