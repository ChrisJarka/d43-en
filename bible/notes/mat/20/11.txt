===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **When they received**  - This is speaking about the first workers. 
  * **Property Owner**  - This is talking about the "landowner" or the vineyard owner spoken about before. 
  * **Equal**  - This word means that two or more things, or people, are the same in some way. 
  * **You have made them equal to us**  - "Them" is referring to the workers hired at the eleventh hour, and "us" is referring to the first workers. 
  * **Borne**  - "Borne" here is not referring to a child being born, but rather it is about carrying a heavy burden.
