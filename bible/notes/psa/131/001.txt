===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[en:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **Song of Ascents** - Possible meanings are 1) "song people sang while going up to Jerusalem for a celebration" or 2) "song people sang while going up the steps into the temple" or 3) "song whose words are like steps."
  * **my heart is not proud or my eyes haughty**- "I am not proud, and I have not thought highly about myself" (UDB) (See:[[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **hopes**- AT: "expectations"
