===== Translation Notes =====

  * **when you cast lots down to the earth for an inheritance-** This is not gambling but refers to the division of the land as God describes.

  * **celebrate an offering**  - This means to perform a special ceremony for this event.
  * **a holy part of the land, twenty-five thousand sticks long and ten thousand wide **- This could be translated as a new sentence: "You will take a part of your land that measures 25,000 sticks long and 10,000 sticks wide and dedicate it to God."
  * **border **- This is the place where two pieces of land touch.
  * **the holy place **- This refers to the holy portion of land that the people will offer to Yahweh.
