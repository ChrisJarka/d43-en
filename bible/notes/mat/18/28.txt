===== Translation Notes: =====

  * Jesus continues telling his story that illustrates forgiveness.
  * **grasped**  - means to "take hold of" or "grab" (UDB adds clarity)
  * **But his fellow-servant fell down and implored him**  - Matthew highlights the similarities between the two servants to the master's response to the first servant and the first servant's response to his fellow servant. (See: Irony)
  * **implored him** - "begged him"
