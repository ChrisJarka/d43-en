===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **This is Wickedness**  - The woman in the barrel is used as a symbol to refer to the wickedness of the people of Israel. AT: "The name of this woman is Wickedness" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **I lifted my eyes**  - Zechariah writes that he has looked up by referring to the part of his body that sees.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **and wind was in their wings** - This phrase describes how the women used their wings to be carried by the wind.
  * **they had wings like a stork's wings**  - This let's us know their wings were very large.  A stork is a type of large bird that has a wingspan of 2 to 4 meters. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
