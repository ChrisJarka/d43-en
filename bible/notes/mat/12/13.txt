===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Stretch out your hand**  - “hold out your hand” or “extend your hand”
  * **He **- This refers to the man.
  * **it **- This refers to the man's hand.
  * **restored to health -**  This means to be healed fully or to be cured completely.
  * **plotted against - **  This means to have a plan to harm or hurt.
  * **seeking - **  This means to "search for" or "look for".
  * **him**  - Jesus
