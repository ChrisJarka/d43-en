====== declare, declaration ======

===== Definition: =====

To declare is to make a solemn, emphatic pronouncement verbally or in written form.

  * Many of the Bible's prophetic messages are recorded as God Himself saying "I declare" or "I emphatically say".
  * A declaration is made to call attention to the importance of what is being proclaimed.
