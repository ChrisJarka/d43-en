===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The man** - Some translations say, "Adam."
  * **called his wife's name Eve** - Adam "gave his wife the name Eve" or "named his wife Eve" (UDB).
  * **Eve** - Translators may write a footnote saying, "The name Eve sounds like the Hebrew word that means 'living.'"
  * **all living things** - This is a hyperbole for all people. AT: "all the living." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **garments of skins** - "things made out of animal skins to cover their bodies"
