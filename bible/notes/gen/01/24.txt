===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **let the earth bring forth** - animals and creatures are being diversified. These things are about to happen on the earth. See how you translated this in 1:11.
  * **cattle** - animals in general, but typically domesticated animals with four legs, as opposed to reptiles, birds, and fish. The word can refer to bovine animals such as oxen or to sheep.
  * **creeping things** - reptiles and insects.
  * **beasts of the earth**  - wild animals.
  * **It was so** - See how you translated this in 1:7.
