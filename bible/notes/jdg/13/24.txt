===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the woman** - "Manoah's wife"
  * **gave birth to a son** - This refers to childbirth. AT: "bore a son" or "had a baby boy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]) 
  * **grew up** - "became an adult" or "matured"
  * **Mahaneh Dan ... Zorah and Eshtaol** - Mahaneh Dan is the name of a temporary camp that the tribe of Dan lived in while they looked for a permanent home. Zorah and Eshtaol are the names of towns. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Yahweh's Spirit began to stir him** - Here the way Yahweh Spirit influences Samson is compared to the way a spoon stirs food in a pot. AT: "Yahweh's Spirit began to influence Samson" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
