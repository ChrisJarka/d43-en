===== translationNotes: =====

  * **her house bows down to death** - Possible meanings: 1) "those who worship in her house die young" or 2) "those who go to where she is die young" or 3) "the road to her house is the road to death." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **bows down** - Possible meanings: 1) "worships" or 2) "sinks down to"
  * **her tracks** - "her way of life" or "the paths to her house" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **those in the grave** - This refers to the spirits of dead people and is a metonym for the place of the dead. AT: " (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **go in to her** - This means to go into her house to sleep with her, as one would with a prostitute. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **they will not find the paths of life** - Possible meanings are 1) "they will not return to the land of the living" or 2) "they will never live a happy life again"
