===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **take it to heart**  -  is an idiom that means “make it very important” (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
  * **“If you will not listen, and if you will not take it to heart ... then I will send a curse on you, and I will curse your blessings** - note how the text has two conditions followed by one conclusion. The UDB has translated in a different manner.
