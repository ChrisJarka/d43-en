===== translationNotes: =====

  * **first and last** - These two opposites are given to refer to everything concerning Jehoshaphat during his reign as king. AT: "from the beginning of his reign until he died" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **behold** - "indeed." The word is used here to add emphasis to the important information that follows. 
  * **they are written in ... which is recorded in** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "they are in ... which is in" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Hanani** - Translate this man's name as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:2ch:16:07|16:7]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the history of Jehu son of Hanani** - This is a historical account that was written by Jehu. 
  * **the book of the kings of Israel** - This is a book that no longer exists. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:2ch:16:11|16:11]].
