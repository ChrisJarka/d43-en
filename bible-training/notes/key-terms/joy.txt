====== joy, joyful ======


===== Definition: =====


Joy is a feeling of deep satisfaction and delight.


    * A person can experience great joy when he is serving God and loving other people.
    * God can give people joy even when very difficult things are happening in their lives.
    * Joy is one of the Fruits of the Holy Spirit.
    * When "be joyful" describes people it can be translated as "rejoice" or "be glad," as in "I will be joyful in the God of my salvation" (Habakkuk 3:18)
    * When "joyful" describes an action or songs, it can be translated as "that shows that someone is joyful." For example, "Make a joyful shout" in Psalm 81:8 can be translated as "Make a shout that shows that you are joyful."
