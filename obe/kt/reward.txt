====== reward ======

===== Definition: =====

A reward is something that someone receives because of something he has done, either good or bad. To reward someone is to give someone something he deserves.

  * A reward can be something good or positive that a person receives because of obeying God and doing something well.
  * Sometimes a reward can refer to negative consequences that may result from bad behavior, such as in the Biblical statement "the reward of the wicked."

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * Depending on the context, the term "reward" could be translated as "payment" or "something that is deserved" or "punishment."
