====== royal ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "royal" describes persons and things which are associated with a king or queen, such as their throne, palace, clothing, and children.

  * A royal palace is the magnificent residence where a king or queen lives.
  * King David's royal palace was larger and more refined than all other buildings in Jerusalem.
  * A king wears special clothing, sometimes called "royal" robes. Often a king's robes had the color purple, which was a rare and expensive type of dye.

(See also: [[king]]. [[queen]] other)
