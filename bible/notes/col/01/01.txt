===== Translati n Notes:

=====   =====

  * **Paul **- This book is a letter from Paul and Timothy to members of the church at Colosse. It might be helpful to insert the word "From Paul" or the phrase "This letter is from Paul" at the beginning to understand this is a letter.
  * **through the will of God **- The Greek word for "will" means actively chosen, so this could be translated "chosen by God" or "by the will of God".
  * **an apostle of Christ Jesus through the will of God **- This entire phrase could be translated as "chosen by God to be an apostle of Christ Jesus".
  * **set apart for God **- <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The Greek word from which this phrase comes means “morally blameless” or "consecrated" or "holy ones". This phrase is often translated.as "saints".</font>
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>** you  ** - This pronoun refers to saints and faithful brethren mentioned above.</font>
  * **we always pray for you **- This hyperbole was used to indicate that Paul and Timothy prayed very often and sincerely for the believers receiving this letter. (See hyperbole.)
