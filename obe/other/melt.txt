====== melt ======

===== Facts: =====
Melt is to cause something to become liquid by heating it. 

  * In the Bible weapons were made by pouring melted metal into the shape of the weapon.
  * As wax drips from a candle as it is lit the wax is melting.
  * The metaphoric meaning of "melt" is to cause something to soften or disappear. 
  * The Bible sometimes says "their hearts will melt" or "they melted away."
