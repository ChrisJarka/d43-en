====== Cain ======

===== Facts: =====

Cain was the son of Adam and Eve. He committed the first murder recorded in the Bible.

  * <font 14px line-height: 17.8181819915772px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Cain killed his brother Abel in a fit of jealousy, because God had accepted Abel's sacrifice, but had not accepted Cain's sacrifice.</font>
