===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **His **- This pronoun used before "father" refers to the son's father. This pronoun used in the rest of Ezekiel 18:18 refers to the father.
  * **Extortion**- This word means to take something from someone else by using threats and/or force.
  * **Plunder **- This means robbing valuables or things by force.
  * **the midst of his people**  - This phrase means among his people.
  * **Behold **- The world "behold" here adds emphasis to what follows.
