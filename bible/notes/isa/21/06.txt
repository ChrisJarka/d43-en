===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For this is what the Lord said to me**  - then Yahweh said to me
  * **"Go, post a watchman"**  - put a watchman on the wall of Jerusalem.
  * **"he must report what he sees"**  - Tell him to shout what he sees.
  * **"when he sees a chariot, horsemen in pairs, "**  - Tell him to watch for chariots pulled by pairs of horses,
  * **"riders on donkeys, riders on camels**  - and men riding camels and donkeys, coming from Babylon.
  * **"then he must pay attention and be very alert."**  - tell the watchman to watch and listen carefully.
