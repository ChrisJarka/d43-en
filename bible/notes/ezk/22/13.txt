===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **So behold!**  - This can be translated as “Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you.”
  * **struck my hand**  - This means to "hit my hand".
  * **disperse**  - <font 14px line-height: 21.7777786254883px;/Lato, sans-serif;;rgb(51, 51, 51) font-family: Lato, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 21.7777786254883px;;;inherit>Literally this means to dash into pieces and scatter to the wind. The word “scatter” means to separate and drive off in various directions. The word disperse is very similar and the two words are often used interchangeably.</font>
  * **purge**  - This means to remove something unwanted.
  * **So you will be unclean within you**  - This means, "You will see yourself as unclean."
  * **in the eye of the nations**  - This means "what the nations will see".
