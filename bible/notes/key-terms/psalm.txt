====== psalm ======

===== Definition: =====

A psalm is a sacred song, often a poem set to music. <font 14.399999618530273px line-height: 15.680000305175781px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The Old Testament book of Psalms has a collection of these songs written by the Israelites.</font>

  * Both Moses and King David wrote psalms that were used by the nation of Israel in their worship of God.
  * Psalms can be used to express joy, faith and reverence, as well as pain and sorrow.
  * In the New Testament, Christians are instructed to sing psalms to God as a form of worship.

(See also: Moses, David, Israel)
