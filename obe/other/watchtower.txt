====== watchtower ======

===== Definition: =====

A tall structure built as a lookout position, used for various purposes.

  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Landowners built stone towers to protect their crops from being stolen.The towers often included living quarters since crops had to be guarded day and night during certain periods.</font>
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Cities built within walls would have watchtowers higher than the walls so guards called watchmen could see if any enemies were approaching.</font>

(See also: other)
