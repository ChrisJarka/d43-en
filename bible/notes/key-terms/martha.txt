====== Martha ======


===== Facts: =====


Martha was a woman mentioned briefly in the New Testament who followed Jesus and supported Him from her own personal resources.


    * Martha also had a younger sister named Mary who also followed Jesus.
    * Martha is said to have been distracted by meal preparation while her younger sister sat and listened to Jesus teach.
