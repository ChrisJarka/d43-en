===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (Jesus continues speaking)
    * **in that night **- Indicates an example that two will be in bed rather than indicating the time of the return of Jesus.
    * **there will be two men **- This can also be translated as "there will be two," or "there will be two persons."
    * **in one bed **- This can also be translated as, "sleeping in one mat," or "in one cot," or "resting in one recliner."
    * **in that night there will be two men in one bed**  - This can also be translated as "there will be two men sleeping in one mat that night" or "there will be two persons in one bed that night."
    * **the one will be taken and the other left **- This can also be translated as "one will be taken and the other will be left behind."
    * **there will be two women **- This can also be translated as "there will be two," or there will be two persons."
    * **vultures**  - Vultures are large birds that eat the flesh of dead animals that they find. You could translate this as "carrion birds" or "birds that eat dead animals" or you could use the word for a local bird that does this even if it is not exactly like a vulture.
