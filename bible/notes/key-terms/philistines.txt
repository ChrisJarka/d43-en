====== Philistines ======

A "people of the sea" who occupied the southern coast of the Aegean Sea in an area known as Philistia.

===== Facts: =====

Text

  * The Philistines were often at war with the Israelites.
  * Goliath was a warrior of the Philistine armies.
  * King David defeated the Philistines at Baal-perazim (2 Samuel 5:17-25.
