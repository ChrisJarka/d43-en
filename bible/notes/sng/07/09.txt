===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The man continues describing what he would like to do with the woman.

  * **May your palate be like the best wine** - The palate is a metonym for the lips. Wine tastes good. The man wants to kiss the woman's lips. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **flowing smoothly for my beloved** - "that flows smoothly for the one I love." The man enjoys the smooth kisses of the woman.
  * **gliding over the lips of those who sleep** - "that flows over our lips as we sleep"
