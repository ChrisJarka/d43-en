===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **hand of Yahweh**  - The term “hand” is often used in reference to God's power and action.  Alternate translation: "the power of God" (See: [[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:synecdoche]] and [[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:personification]])
  * **I fell on my face**  - This phrase is means that Ezekiel dropped to his knees and bowed his head to the ground in worship. (See: [[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:idiom]])
