===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **voice of Yahweh** - Yahweh used his voice by using his prophets' voices. See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:jer:03:23|3:25]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] or  [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
    * **Truth is destroyed** - AT: "The prophets destroyed  truth" or "The people destroyed the truth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
    * **cut off from their mouth** - AT: "they will not speak truth" or AT: "their mouths will not speak truth"
