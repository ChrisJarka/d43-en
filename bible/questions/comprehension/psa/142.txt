====== Psalms Chapter 142 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who does David cry out to and tell his troubles?

A. David crys out to Yahweh with his voice and pours out a lament to tell Yahweh his troubles. [142:1-2]

Q? Who directs David when his spirit is weak?

A. Yahweh knows his path. [142:3]

Q? What does David see when he looks to the right?

A. David sees that there is no one who cares about him, and there is no excape for him. [142:4]
