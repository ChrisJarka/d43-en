===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **immediately**  - That is, "at once" or "without waiting."
    * **glorifying God - ** Or, "praising" him.
    * **amazement took hold of everyone - ** That is, everyone who saw it was very surprised and astonished.
    * **they glorified God -**  Or, "they were glorifying God."
    * **filled with fear**  - This means they were very afraid or overwhelmed with awe at what had just happened, and were unsure what to do.
