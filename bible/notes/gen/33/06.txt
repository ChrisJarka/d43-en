===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **female servants **- "secondary wives" or "servant wives"
  * **they bowed down** - This can be made more explicit "they bowed down to show respect" (see [[:en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]]). 
  * **What do you mean by all these groups that I met?**  - "Why did you send all of those groups to meet me separately?" 
  * **To find favor in the sight of my lord**  - "So that my lord would be pleased with me"
  * **my lord**  - This is a polite way of referring to Esau, and can be translated  "you" (see [[:en:ta:translate:123person]]).
