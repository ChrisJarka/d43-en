====== pastor ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "pastor" is literally the same word as "shepherd." It is used as a title for someone who is the spiritual leader for a group of believers.

  * Can denote a sheep-herder, literally or figuratively.
  * Is used of Jesus Christ as the "good Shepherd."
  * Can also refer to leaders in the church.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * This term can also be translated as "shepherd" or "spiritual shepherd" or "spiritual leader."
