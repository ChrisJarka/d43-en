The person who made this song continues to speak.
  * **your, my, you**  - Refers to the writer of the psalm.
  * **v 8, You**  - Refers to God.
  * **v 7 **- Writer is speaking to himself, or thinking out loud.
  * **v 8 **- The writer is speaking to God.
