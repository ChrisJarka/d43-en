===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But **- The word "but" is used here to make a contrast between the lazy believers (see verse 12) versus the hard working believers.
  * **you**  - this "you" refers to believers in the Thessalonian church. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:formsofyou-plural|Forms of 'You' - Dual/Plural]])
  * **do not lose heart**  - "do not be discouraged". (See: [[:en:ta:translate:metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **take note of him**  - "publicly identify that person" (UDB).
