====== winnow, sift ======

===== Definition: =====

To winnow means to separate grain from the unwanted chaff by throwing it into the air and allowing the wind to blow the chaff away. Sometimes the word "sift" is used to mean the same thing.

  * Winnowing forks were used to throw the grain into the air.
  * In the Bible, the image of winnowing grain is used to describe how God separates righteous people from unrighteous people..It also can refer to testing a person to find out what is good or evil in them.
  * To "sift" someone is used in a similar way
  * bullet
