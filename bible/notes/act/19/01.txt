===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It came about**  - "it happened"
  * **passed through**  - "traveled through"
  * **upper country**  - This was an area of Greece which was to the north of Corinth.
  * **receive the Holy Spirit**  - Individuals experienced the presence of the Holy Spirit-God's Spirit-within themselves.
  * **receive**- This word here for "receive" means to receive with a willingness or desire to have something.
  * **we did not even hear about the Holy Spirit**  - "we had not heard that the Spirit came."
