===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh** - Here the word "Yahweh" is emphatic. A possible translation may be "Yahweh himself."
  * **visited Sarah as he had said**  - "took care of Sarah as he had said he would"
  * **bore a son to Abraham ** - "gave birth to Abraham's son"
  * **in his old age**  - "when Abraham was very old"
  * **at the set time of which God had spoken to him**  - "at the exact time that God told him" (This refers back to [[:en:bible:notes:gen:18:13|Gen 18:14]].)
  * **Abraham named his son, the one who had been born to him, whom Sarah bore to him, Isaac. **- "Abraham named their newborn son Isaac."
  * **Abraham circumcised his son Isaac when he was eight days old** - "When Isaac was eight days old, Abraham circumcised his son"
  * **Eight days** - 8 days (See: [[en:ta:translate:translateunknowns-numbers]])
  * **commanded him** - "commanded Abraham to do"
