===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **There will be a highway** - "Yahweh will make a road"
  * **as there was for Israel in their coming up from the land of Egypt** - This refers to an earlier event in the history of Israel when God made a path in the sea so the people of Israel could escape from the army of Egypt.
