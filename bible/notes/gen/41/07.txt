===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The thin ears...wholesome and full ears**  - See how you translated these phrases in 41:5-6.
  * **woke up**  - See how you translated this phrase in 41:4.
  * **it was a dream**  - "he found he had been dreaming"
  * **swallowed up**  - "ate"
  * **behold**  - The word “behold” here shows that Pharaoh was surprised by what he saw.  
  * **It came about**  - This phrase is used here to mark the beginning of a new part of the story. If your language has a way for doing this, you could consider using it here.
  * **spirit**  - "soul" or "inner being"
  * **He sent and called for all the magicians and wise men of Egypt.**  - Ancient kings and rulers used magicians and wise men as advisors.  
  * **sent and called for**  - "sent his servants to gather"
