====== Jehoshephat ======

===== Facts: =====

Jehoshephat is a name meaning "Recorder" of several Old Testament characters.

  * A priest who blew a trumpet before the ark of God was brought from the house of Obed to the city of David.
  * One of Solomon's twelve administrative officers.
  * King of Judah from 873-849 B.C. who ended warfare between Judah and Israel and instigated religious reform.
