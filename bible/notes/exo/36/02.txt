===== Translation Notes: =====

See: translate names

  * **Bezalel and Oholiab** <font 14px line-height: 19.6px;/ ;;rgb(51, 51, 51) font-family: 'Noto Sans', sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 19.6px;;;inherit>- Two of the leaders of the craftsmen that God chose to work on the sanctuary. See how you translated these names in 31:2, 31:6, 35:30, 35:34, and 36:1.</font>
  * **heart stirred within him**
 - This means that they wanted to help with the work. See how you translated this in 35:21.
  * **free-will**  - This means that they were choosing to bring offerings because they wanted to, as a free choice or free decision.
  * **free-will offerings - ** These consisted of jewelry, cloth, gems, wood and other goods. See 35:22-29.
