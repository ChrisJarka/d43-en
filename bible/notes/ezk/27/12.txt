===== Important Terms: =====

  * Tarshish
  * silver
  * souls

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Tarshish was your merchant **- The city of Tyre is buying goods from Tarshish, a far away city. This indicates Tyre's great wealth.
  * **silver, iron, tin and lead **- These were valuable goods.
  * **wares**  - "products" or "goods"
  * **Javan, Tubal, Meshech**  - These cities paid for Tyre's goods with slaves and bronze.
