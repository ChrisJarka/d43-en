====== Psalms Chapter 011 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? In what does David take refuge?

A. David takes refuge in Yahweh. [11:1]

Q? What will David say to his soul?

A. He will tell it to flee like a bird to the mountain. [11:1]

Q? How do the wicked prepare their bows?

A. They make ready their arrows to shoot in the darkness at the upright. [11:2]

Q? What is Yahweh doing in his holy temple?

A. His eyes are watching and examining the children of men. [11:4]

Q? Who does Yahweh examine?

A. He examines the righteous and the wicked [11:5]
