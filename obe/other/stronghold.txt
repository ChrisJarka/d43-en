====== stronghold ======

===== Definition: =====

A stronghold is a place that can be defended or is well protected against an attack by enemy soldiers.

  * A stronghold can be strong buildings, defensive walls, or natural barriers like rocky cliffs or high mountains.
  * Also used to refer to God as a refuge or fortress for the righteous as in Pslams 9:9.
