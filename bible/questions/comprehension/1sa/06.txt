====== 1 Samuel Chapter 06 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? For what period of time was the ark of Yahweh in the Philistine's country?

A. The ark was in the country of the Philistines for seven months. [6:1]

Q? Who did the Philistines call for to ask advice on how the ark of Yahweh should be sent back to its own country?

A. The Philistine people called for the priests and the diviners to tell them how to send the ark back to its own country. [6:2]
