====== 1 Kings Chapter 02 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why did King David give special instructions to his son Solomon.

A. David was very near death and wanted to communicate his last instructions to Solomon his son the future King. [2:1]

Q? What did King David instruct his son Solomon to do.

A. King David told Solomon that he should always fear and follow the God of Israel. [2:3]

Q? What was King David's instruction concerning Joab son of Zeruiah.

A. King David told Solomon to remember Joab's harmful actions and to put him to death. [2:6]

Q? What did King David say about the sons of Barzillai the Gileadite.

A. King David instructed Solomon to show them kindness and allow them to be his guests. [2:7]

Q? Why did King David instruct his son Solomon to kill Shimei the son of Gera.

A. Shimei was to be killed because of the wicked way in which he treated King David when David was fleeing from Saul at Mahanaim. [2:8,9]
