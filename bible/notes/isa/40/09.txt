===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **lift up your voice with strength **- "shout loudly"
  * **his strong arm **- This is referring to Yahweh's power. (See: Metonymy)
  * **his reward is with him and his recompense goes before him **- The phrase "his reward" here is referring to Yahweh's people who returning from exile and "his recompense" is Yahweh's reward of mercy and blessings that awaits his people.
