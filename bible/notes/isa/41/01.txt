===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Listen before me in silence** - "me" refers to God.
  * **you coastlands** - refers to islands or lands bordering the Mediterranean Sea and in general refers to lands that are distant from Israel.
  * **let them come near and speak let us come near together to argue a dispute.** – AT: “then let them come near to speak and reason with me.”
  * **Who stirred up this one from the east? Who calls him in good order to his service?** - AT: “Who moved and called this righteous person from the east to be at His service?”
  * **he makes them as the dust with his sword, as wind-blown stubble with his bow** - He refers to God.  AT: “He scatters and overcomes His enemies like dust or stubble with His weapons.” (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
