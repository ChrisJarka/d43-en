====== Edom ======

===== Facts: =====

Edom is a country south and east of Israel.

  * The Edomites are a people who are descendants of Esau.
  * Edom was typically an enemy of Israel.
  * Edom is frequently the subject of negative prophecy in the Old Testament.
  * The entire book of Obadiah is devoted to the future destruction of Edom.
