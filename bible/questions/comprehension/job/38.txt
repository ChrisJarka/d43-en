====== Job Chapter 38 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who is speaking to Job now out of a fierce storm?

A. Yahweh is speaking to Job out of a fierce storm. [38:1]

Q? Who brings darkness to Yahweh's plans by using words without knowledge?

A. Job brings darkness to Yahweh's plans by using words without knowledge.

Q? What does Yahweh tell Job to do when Yahweh questions him?

A. Yahweh tells Job that he must answer Yahweh's questions. [38:3]

Q? Who laid the earth's foundations and determined its dimensions?

A. God laid the earth's foundations and determined its dimensions. [38:4-5]

Q? What sang together and who shouted for joy when the God laid the cornerstone of the earth?

A. The morning stars sang together and all the sons of God shouted for joy when the cornerstone was laid. [38:6-7]

Q? To what does Yahweh compare the sea bursting out after it was shut up with doors?

A. God compares the sea bursting out of doors to coming out of the womb. [38:8]

Q? What did Yahweh put in place to mark out the boundary of the sea so that it could come only so far and no farther?

A. Yahweh put in place bars and doors to mark a boundary for the sea. [38:10-11]

Q? What does Yahweh ask Job about given orders to the morning and causing the morning dawn to know its place?

A. Yahweh asks Job if he has ever given those orders. [38:12]

Q? What stands out clearly like the folds of a piece of clothing when the earth is changed in appearance in the light of dawn?

A. All things on the earth stand out clearly like the folds of a piece of clothing in the light of dawn. [38:14]

Q? What does Yahweh ask Job to do after Yahweh questions him about what has been revealed to Job about the sea, the gates of death and the expanse of the earth?

A. Yahweh asks Job to tell him, if he knows it all. [38:16-18]

Q? What does Yahweh say to mock Job about his lack of knowledge about the place of light and darkness?

A. Yahweh says to Job in a mocking way that undoubtedly Job should know about this because of the number of Job's days is so large. [38:19-21]

Q? For what reason does Yahweh say he has kept storehouses for the snow and the hail?

A. Yahweh has kept these storehouses for times of trouble and for days of battle and war. [38:22-23]
