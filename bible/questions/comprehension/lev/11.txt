====== Leviticus Chapter 11 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What living things did Yahweh tell Moses and Aaron that the people of Israel could eat?

A. Yahweh told Moses and Aaron to tell the people of Israel that they could eat any living thing that has a split hoof and chews cud. [11:3]

Q? What animals did Yaweh tell the people they could not eat?

A. The animals that Yahweh told the people not to eat were the camel, the rock badger, the rabbit, and the pig. [11:4-7]

Q? What animals living in the water can be eaten by the people of Israel?

A. Animals that live in the water which have fins and scales may be eaten by the people of Israel. [11:9]
