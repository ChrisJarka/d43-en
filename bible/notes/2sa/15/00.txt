====== 2 Samuel 15 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter begins the sub-story of Absalom's revolt. (15-19)

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Absalom's revolt ==
For four years Absalom made friends with everyone coming to David for judgment. Then he went to Hebron, invited 200 important people as guests including David's best adviser and plotted with them to make him king. David found out and fled with those in the army loyal to him. David was willing to welcome whatever God wanted to happen. David asked his friend and adviser, Hushai, to give Absalom bad advice and to be his spy. 


=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphor ==
The author used the [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] of a thief to describe how Absalom was able to win peoples loyalty: "So Absalom stole the hearts of the men of Israel."
