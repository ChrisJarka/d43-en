===== Translation Notes: =====

The author continues the comparison between the first covenant and second covenant. 

    * **first covenant**  - Use definition of the "first covenant" from [[:en/bible/notes/heb/08/06|Hebrews 8:6-7]].
    * **regulations for worship**  - "detailed instructions or rules for worship" or "directions for worship"
    * **For in the tabernacle** - "For" in this context is used as a connecting word.  Alternative translation: "In the tabernacle"
    * **there was a room prepared, the outer room** - "there was a first room prepared"
