====== destiny ======

===== Definition: =====

An event or events that will happen to a person or people group in the future.

  * Mankind has an eternal destiny, either in Heaven with Jesus or in Hell without Him.
  * After the nations of Israel and Judah continued in disobedience to God, their destiny was to go into captivity to Babylon.

(See also: other)
