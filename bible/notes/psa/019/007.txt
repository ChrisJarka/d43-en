===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **the decrees of Yahweh are reliable** - "the rules of Yahweh are sure"
    * **to the inexperienced** - AT: "to those who have no experience"
    * **The covenant decrees of Yahweh** - "The word of Yahweh"
    * **giving insight to the eyes**  - These words are a picture of giving understanding to our minds. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
