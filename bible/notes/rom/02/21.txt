===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **do you not...do you**  - It is wicked that some of you Jewish people are doing the same wicked things you condemn other people for doing. (See: [[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:rhetorical-question|Rhetorical question]])
    * **rob temples**  - Possible meanings are: (1) "steal items from local pagan temples to sell and make a profit" or (2) "not send to the Jerusalem temple all the money that is due to God" or (3) "make fun of local gods." (See: Hyperbole)
