====== promise, oath ======


===== Definition: =====


A promise is a pledge or vow or commitment to do or not to do something specified.  it is to bring about something.


    * Promises are important parts of formal agreements such as covenants.
    * An oath is a strong promise or a strong declaration that what one says is true. When people take an oath, they show that what they have said is true by asking God to do them harm if it is not true.
    * A promise if often accompanied with an "Oath" or a confirmation hat it will be done.
