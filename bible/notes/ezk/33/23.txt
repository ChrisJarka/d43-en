===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the Word of Yahweh came to me and said** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:16|3:16]].
  * **the ones inhabiting** - "the people living in"
  * **those ruins**  - The word "ruins" whatever is left after people destroy a city. Usually "ruins" are buildings that have severe damage.
  * **are talking and say** - This can be translated as "when they talk they say."
  * broken parts of buildings remaining after destruction
