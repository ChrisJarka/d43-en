===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you will trample our iniquities under your feet. You will throw all our sins into the depths of the sea **- AT: you shall crush what has seperated us from you under your feet. Our failure shall be thrown to the sea never to be seen again.
  * **You will **- implies a future event.
  * **Give truth to Jacob and covenant faithfulness to Abraham, as you swore to our ancestors in ancient days **- AT: You shall give to us bestow to us truth and faithfulness as you did to Jacob and Abraham.
