====== Hosea 13 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter uses many poetic forms to communicate Yahweh's disgust and anger with Israel, the Northern Kingdom. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Israel and Ephraim are terms used to speak about the people of the Northern Kingdom. Samaria is the capital of the Northern Kingdom (See: 
[[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]
 
This chapter makes it clear that the Northern Kingdom is going to be decimated as a people. In essence they will no longer exist.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

Hosea uses rhetorical questions throughout this book and this chapter is no exception. Because these questions occur in poetry; they will mostly likely be rhetorical. They are not questions requesting information but rather questions indicating some emotion like anger or dissatisfaction (see: 
[[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]).
