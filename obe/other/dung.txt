====== dung ======

===== Definition: =====

Dung refers to human or animal excrement.

  * Dung was and is used in Palestine as fertilizer or dried and used for fuel.
  * As a symbol, dung was a sign of the low end a person or naton might descend.
  * The Dung Gate in the South Wall of Jerusalem was probably the gate where garbage and refuse was removed.
