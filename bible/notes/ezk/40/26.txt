===== Important Terms: =====

  * **doorposts**
  * **palm trees**
  * **courtyard**

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It had seven steps**  - "It" refers to the southern gate of the temple courtyard.
  * **its doorposts were in front of them**  - "Them" refers to the steps.
  * **way**  - alternate translation: path
  * **cubits**  - see: Biblical measures page
