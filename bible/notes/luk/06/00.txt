=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== "Eating the grain" ==
This was a culturally acceptable practice for travelers to eat small amounts of grain off of plants in field they traveled near. Farmers were required to allow this according to the [[:en:obe:kt:lawofmoses|law of Moses]]. The Pharisees considered this to be "gleaning" and forbade it because they would be [[:en:obe:kt:works|working]] on the [[:en:obe:kt:sabbath|Sabbath]].
