===== Translation Notes: =====

Christ continues to talk to the disciples and crowd after the voice comes from heaven.

  * **now is the judgment of this world**  - Christ did not come to judge the world, but his very coming condemned the world.
  * **the world**  - This refers both to the world and the people in the world. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **prince of this world**  - The phrase "prince of this world" is a title for Satan.  The word "prince" refers to his power and leadership in the world.
