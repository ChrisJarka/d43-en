====== 1 Kings 03 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter has two stories. 1-15 is the story of Solomon's dream when God asked him what he wanted and he said he needed wisdom to be a good and fair ruler. God was very pleased with his request and promised him wisdom, wealth and fame. 16-28 is the story of a court case showing Solomon's wisdom.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

There are two concepts, one from each of the sections of the chapter. God is pleased with people wanting to do their job well more than wanting to be blessed. And a mother will put the life of her child above every other concern.
