====== Psalms 118 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 118 is a psalm of war and about Christ.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

The author praised the Lord for his rescuing  him. God enabled him to overpower his enemies. The new Testament and even Christ himself saw the rejected stone made the most important as a reference to his being rejected by the Jewish leaders but honored by God, himself.
