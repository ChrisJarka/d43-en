====== 2 Kings 19 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

The story of Hezekiah continues

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

God caused the Assyrian king to fail in conquering Jerusalem because he mocked God. 

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Idiom ==

"The virgin daughter of Zion" is an [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] meaning the people of Jerusalem.  " The virgin daughter of Zion despises you and laughs you to scorn" means that the people of Jerusalem know that Assyria will fail because they have mocked God.
