===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh** - See how you translated this in 5:5.
  * **shapeless idols**  - "idols"
  * **bring an end to worthless idols** - "destroy the idols"
  * **Memphis** - This was a powerful city in Egypt.
  * **Pathros** - This is name of a city in Egypt. (See:How to translate name).
  * **Zoan** - This is the name of a city in Egypt.
  * **No** - This is the name of a city in Egypt.
