====== Psalms Chapter 010 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What does the writer feel as he approaches Yahweh?

A. The writer feels that Yahweh stands far off and hides himself. [10:1]

Q? What does the writer ask Yahweh to do to the wicked people?

A. The writer asks Yahweh to let the wicked be trapped by their own schemes.
