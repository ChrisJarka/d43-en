===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **We ourselves** - The word "ourselves" is used here to add emphasis and to separate Luke and his fellow traveling companions from Paul, who did not travel by boat. (see: [[en:ta:translate:reflexpronoun]])
  * **This is what he himself desired to do, because he planned to go by land** - "This is what Paul himself desired to do, because Paul planned to go by land." The word "himself" is used to add emphasis to "he." Paul traveled by himself.
