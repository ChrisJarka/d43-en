===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now** – This word introduces a new part of the story. Consider how your language does this. Some translations might not use an introductory word here.
  * **in those days** – This could also be translated as, “around that time.” Or this sentence could begin with, “Soon after that.”
  * **the hill country** – This could also be translated as, “the hilly area” or, “the highlands” or, “the mountainous part of Israel.” 
  * **got up** – This could also be translated as, “started out” or “got ready.”
  * **and she went** – Or, “where she went.” This also could be translated as, “When she arrived,she went.”
  * **As** – That is, “As soon as” or, “When.”
  * **heard Mary's greeting** – That is, “heard Mary greet her.” 
  * **and Elizabeth was filled with the Holy Spirit** – This could also be translated as, “then Elizabeth was empowered by the Holy Spirit” or, “immediately the Holy Spirit guided Elizabeth to start praising God.”
