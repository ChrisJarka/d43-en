====== Luke Chapter 4 ======

===== Comprehensive Questions and Answers =====


These questions will be used by translators to community checks of this chapter.


This is a draft list. Please add your suggested questions to the list, and your comments to the Discussion section.


    - **Q? Who led Jesus into the wilderness?**
      * //A. The Holy Spirit led Jesus into the wilderness [4:1].//
    - **Q? How long did Satan tempt Jesus in the wilderness?**
      * //A. Satan tempted Jesus in the wilderness for 40 days [4:2].//
    - **Q? What did the Devil challenge Jesus to do with the stones on the ground?**
      * //A. The Devil told Jesus to turn the stones into bread [4:3].//
    - **Q? What did the Devil show to Jesus from the top of a mountain?**
      * //A. The Devil showed Jesus all of the nations of the world [4:5]. //
    - **Q? What did the Devil want Jesus to do? **
      * //A. The Devil wanted him to bow down and worship him [4:7]. //
    - **Q? What did the Devil tell Jesus to do when he took him to the highest point of the temple?**
      * //A. He told Jesus to jump [4:9]. //
    - **Q? What did the Devil do after Jesus refused to jump from the temple?**
      * //A. The Devil left Jesus until another time [4:13].//
