====== 1 Samuel Chapter 21 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Of what did David say the king told him to let no one know about?

A. He said the king told him to let no one know anything about the business he was sending him, and what he had commanded him. [21:2]

Q? What did the priest answer David of the bread on hand?

A. The priest answered David and said that there was no ordinary bread on hand, but there was holy bread. [21:4]

Q? From where had the bread of the presence been removed?

A. The bread of the presence was removed from before Yahweh. [21:6]

Q? What was the work Doeg the Edomite did for Saul?

A. Doeg the Edomite was the chief of Saul's herdsmen. [21:7]

Q? Why did David say he had no weapons with him?

A. He said that he had brought no weapons with him, because the kingâ€™s business was urgent. [21:8]

Q? From whom was David fleaing when he went to Achish?

A. He fled that day from Saul and went to Achish. [21:10]

Q? What did David pretend when he changed his behavior before Achish?

A. He changed his behavior before them and pretended to be insane in their hands. [21:13]

Q? What did Achish ask his servants when he saw David was mad?

A. He asked them since you see the man is mad, why have you brought him to me? [21:14]
