====== Jeremiah Chapter 11 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? To whom did Yahweh tell Jeremiah to declare the words of this covenant?

A. Yahweh said to declare these words to each man of Judah and to the inhabitants of Jerusalem. [11:1]

Q? The people were cursed for not listening to what?

A. Cursed is anyone who does not listen to the words of this covenant. [11:3]

Q? When was this covenant given to Judah's ancestors?

A. Yahweh said that the covenant was commanded the day he brought them out from the land of Egypt. [11:4]

Q? What is the oath God wanted to confirm with Judah?

A. The oath that God would give them the land flowing with milk and honey. [11:5]

Q? Yahweh said to do what after listening to the words of this covenant?

A. Yahweh told Jeremiah to tell Judah to listen to the words of this covenant and carry them them out. [11:6]

Q? What did the people do after Yahweh brought the curses of this covenant against them?

A. Yahweh brought all the curses in this covenant against them; But the people still did not obey. [11:8]

Q? After what, or whom, did the men of Judah and Jerusalem who refused to listen to Yahweh's word walk?

A. The people of Judah and Jerusalem refused to listen to my word, and instead walked after other gods to worship them. [11:10]

Q? How will Yahweh reply to the people's calling out to him in disaster?

A. Yahweh says that they will call out to him, but he will not listen to them. [11:11]

Q? How many incense altars to Baal were made in Jerusalem?

A. The people have made altars in Jerusalem, incense altars for Baal, to equal the number of her streets. [11:13]

Q? What does Yahweh say Jeremiah is not to do for this people?

A. Yahweh told Jeremiah, "You must not pray for this people." [11:14]

Q? What has Yahweh's beloved people done that sacrifices cannot help them?

A. Yahweh says that meat of sacrifices cannot help the people because they have done evil and then been happy about it. [11:15]

Q? Why has Yahweh of hosts decreed disaster against the people of Israel and Judah?

A. Yahweh of hosts has decreed disaster against them because of the wicked acts that the house of Israel and of Judah have committed. [11:17]

Q? What part of man does Yahweh the righteous judge examine?

A. Yahweh of hosts is the righteous judge who examines the heart and the mind. [11:20]

Q? What were the people of Anathoth telling Jeremiah they would do if he kept prophesying in the name of Yahweh?

A. People of Anathoth say to Jeremiah, ''You must not prophesy in the name of Yahweh, or you will die by our hand." [11:21]
