===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **she, her, and I**  - These words refer to the people of Babylon (See: Personification)
  * **glorified herself and lived in luxury**  - thought herself better than others and expected honor and indulged in rich living.
  * **she says in her heart**  - "she thinks to herself" (See: Metonymy)
  * **I am seated like a queen**  - Here, queen refers not to the wife of a king, but to a female ruler.  "Seated" refers to being seated on her throne, where she rules.
  * **I will never see mourning**  - "I will never mourn" or "I will never be sad".
  * **in one day**  - Here, "day" does not refer to a literal 24-hour period of time, but is used to indicate a very short time.  AT: "suddenly".
  * **her plagues**  - These are the plagues God will send on her.
