===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **who deeply hide their plans** - “who cover up their real purposes”
  * **deeds are in darkness** – “acts are done in secret”
  * **Who sees us, and who knows us?** - The people believe that they are able to hide their actions from Yahweh. They question Yahweh's ability to see what they are doing. (See: [[en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]])
