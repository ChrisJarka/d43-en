===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The doors and the thresholds shook at the voices of those who were crying out **- “When the seraphim called out, their voices shook the foundations under the doorways"
  * **and the house was filled with smoke **- Alternate translation: “and smoke filled the house." (See: [[:en:ta:translate:active]])
  * **and the house **- The word "house" here is used to refer to the temple/palace of the Lord.
  * **then I said **- Isaiah is speaking.
  * * **Woe is me! For I am doomed because I am a man of unclean lips, and I live among a people of unclean lips, because my eyes have seen the King, Yahweh, Commander of the angel armies!"** - Alternate translation: "What great trouble I am in! For I have seen Yahweh, Commander of the angel armies with my eyes, even though I am always saying sinful things, and I live with a people that is always saying sinful things."
  * **a man of unclean lips**  - When Isaiah says that he is a man of unclean lips he means that he often says things that do not fit with the holiness of God. He says things that are not true, not good, or do nor represent God accurately. Alternate translation: "a man who says things that are sinful" or "a man who says things that are not right." (See: [[:en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  * **a people of unclean lips **- "a people that says things that are sinful" or "a people that says things that are not right"
  * **because my eyes have seen **- Isaiah means that he has seen Yahweh with his eyes. Alternate translation: "I have seen with my eyes" or "I have seen." (See: [[:en:ta:translate:synecdoche]])
