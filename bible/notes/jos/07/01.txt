  * **Carmi**  - Translate the same way as in Genesis 46:9, Exodus 6:14, Numbers 26:6, Joshua 7:18, 1 Chronicles 2:7, 4:1 and 5:3. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
