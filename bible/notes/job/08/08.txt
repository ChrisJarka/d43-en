===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **ages past**  - AT: "earlier generations"
  * **apply yourself to learn what those people discovered **- AT: "find out what their ancestors discovered"
  * **our days on earth are like a shadow **- This is a simile that expresses the idea that their lives on earth had not affected society in comparison to their ancestors. AT: "Our life on earth seems like a shadow". (See: Simile)
  * ** Will they not teach you and tell you ** - This question expects a "yes" answer and could be translated by a statement. AT: "You should allow them to teach you and tell you" (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * ** Will they not utter words from their hearts? ** - This question expects a "yes" answer and could be translated by a statement. AT: "You should listen to their understanding." This phrase expresses a similar idea to "will they not teach you and tell you". (See: Rhetorical Question, Parallelism)
  * **utter words**  - AT: "speak"
  * **words from their hearts**  - explanation
