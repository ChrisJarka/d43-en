===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **And being asked by the pharisees**  - That is, "And when the Pharisees asked Jesus."
    * **when the kingdom of God would come**  - This could also be translated as "When will the kingdom of God come?" (See: [[:en:ta:vocabulary:quotation|Speech Quotations]])
    * **does not come with observation**  -To watch as a guard would with a desire to control events. A person will not be able to see the kingdom with his physical eye, but will perceive it by faith.
    * **neither will they say**  - That is, "And people will not say,"
    * **within you**  - That is, "inside of you."
