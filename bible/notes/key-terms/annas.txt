====== Annas ======

===== Facts: =====

Annas was the Jewish high priest from approximately AD 6 to 15. After high priests retired, they still kept the title along with some of the responsibilities of the office.

  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Annas served for 10 years and was removed from his high priesthood by the Roman government, although he continued to be an influential leader among the Jews.</font>
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>He was father-in-law to</font> Caiaphas<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>, the high priest during the ministry of Jesus.</font> Annas<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>and</font> Caiaphas<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>are mentioned together.</font>
  * Jesus was initially brought to Annas for questioning during his trial, even though it was Caiaphas who was officially the high priest at that time.
