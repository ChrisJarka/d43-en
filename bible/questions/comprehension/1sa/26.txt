====== 1 Samuel Chapter 26 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What was Saul told that led Saul to bring three thousand men to seek David in the wilderness of Ziph?

A. Saul was told that David was hiding in the hill before the wilderness. [26:1-2]

Q? Who did David see coming after him into the wilderness?

A. David saw Saul coming after him into the wilderness. [26:3]

Q? Where did David go after seeing Saul follow him?

A. David arose and went to the place where Saul camped and saw Saul, the general of Saul's army and all the people camped around Saul, asleep. [26:5]

Q? What did Joab's brother want to do when he and David saw Saul sleeping inside the camp?

A. Joab's brother wanted to kill Saul with his spear. [26:6-7]

Q? Why did David not want Joab's brother to kill Saul?

A. David did not want Joab's brother to kill Saul because no one should extend his hand against Yahweh's annointed one and be guiltless. [26:9]

Q? How does David say Saul will die?

A. Yahweh will kill Saul, he will die on his own, or he will die in battle? [26:10]

Q? What did David ask Joab's brother to take which would show how close they were to Saul?

A. David and Joab's brother took the spear and the jar of water from near Saul's head and got away. [26:11-12]

Q? What did David do after he went over to the other side and stood on the top to the mountain far off?

A. David shouted to Saul and his camp. [26:13-14]

Q? What did David say Abner, the man who was to watch over Saul, should die?

A. David said that he should die because he did not watch after his master Saul as shown by the kings's spear and his water jug. [26:115-16]

Q? What did David ask Saul after Saul recognized David's voice?

A. David asked Saul why does my master, Saul, pursue David and what evil has David done. [26:17-18]

Q? What did David ask Saul about who stirred Saul against David?

A. David asked Saul if Yahweh has stirred Saul up against David, or if it is human beings? [26:19]

Q? What did Saul say to David?

A. Saul told David that he has sinned and will harm David no more. [26:21]

Q? Who should pay David because he was righteouse and faithful by not killing Saul eventhough he was able to get his spear.

A. Yahweh should pay each David and each man for his righteousness and his faithfulness. [26:22-23]

Q? Whose life should be valued in the eyes of Yahwh because David thought Saul's life was precious.

A. David's life should be valued in the eyes of Yahweh. [26:24]

Q? What did Saul say to David before they both went their own way?

A. Sai; said to David that he should be blessed. [26:25]
