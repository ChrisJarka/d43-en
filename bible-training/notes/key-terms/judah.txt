====== Judah ======


===== Facts: =====


Judah was the fourth of Jacob's twelve sons. God gave Judah's descendants the right to rule Israel and promised that one of them would rule the world forever.


    * Judah's descendants are referred to as the tribe of Judah.
    * When the kingdom of Israel split, the southern kingdom was called Judah.
    * The southern region of Palestine was called Judea after the location of the kingdom of Judah.
    * The word "Jew" comes from the name "Judah."
