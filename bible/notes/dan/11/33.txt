===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **understanding** - AT: "knowledge"
  * **But for days they will fall** - It is not clear who "they" refers to in 11:33 and 11:34, whether the wise or the many.
  * **refined, cleansed and made pure **- to purge in order to remove impurities, to examine something (see: parallelism).
  * **time of the end** - AT: "end of time"
