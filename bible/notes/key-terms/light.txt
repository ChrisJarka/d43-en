====== light ======

===== Definition: =====

Light is a source or presence that is absent of darkness, that provides visibility to see.

  * Jesus said, "I am the light of the world."
  * God separated light from darkness in the beginning of Creation.
  * John stated that God is light, and there is no darkness in Him.

(See also: other)
