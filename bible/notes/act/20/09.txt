===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **was picked up dead**  - This could be translated as "he was dead when they picked up his body" or "they thought he might be dead when they picked up his body."
  * **embraced him **- "Put his arms around him" (UDB).
  * **for he is alive**  - This could be translated as "for he is alive now [as the result of a miracle]" or possibly "for he is alive [and did not die as a result of his fall]".
