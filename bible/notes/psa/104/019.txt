===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the sun knows its time**  - This treats the sun as being aware, to know the time to set. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **time for setting** - This refers to the sun going down at the end of the day.
  * **You make** - AT: "Yahweh, you make"
  * **when all the beasts of the forest come out** - This is a reference to many animals who are awake at night.
