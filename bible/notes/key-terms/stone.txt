====== stone, stoned ======

===== Definition: =====

A stone is a round rock.  Stoning someone means throwing stones at him to kill him.

  * Stoning is prescribed by God in the Old Testament as a judgment to be administered by the governing authorities as a form of punishment for certain sins.
