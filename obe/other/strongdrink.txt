====== strong drink ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "strong drink" refers to an alcoholic drink.

  * Intoxicating beverages are made from either grain or fruit and have undergone a process of fermentation.  Wine. beer, ale, and cider are among alcoholic drinks..
  * Priests were forbidden to consume "Strong drink."
