====== synagogue ======


===== Definition: =====


A synagogue is a building where Jewish people assemble to pray. Sometimes there are smaller rooms in the synagogue, which are usually dedicated to the study of the Torah.


    * The word synagogue, while used only once in the Old Testament, is used 56 times in the New Testament.
