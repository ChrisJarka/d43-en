===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now ** - marks change in topic
  * **may our Lord...loved us and gave us** - "our" and "us" includes Paul's audience. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:inclusive]])
  * **Lord Jesus Christ himself** - This is an emphatic statement used to call additional attention to the Lord Jesus Christ.
  * **your** - This word is plural and refers to the believers in the Thessalonian church. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:formsofyou]])
  * ** comfort your hearts and establish them in** - "comfort you and strengthen you for"
