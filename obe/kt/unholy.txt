====== unholy ======

===== Definition: =====

Literally, the term "unholy" refers to something that has not been set apart for special service to God. It means "not holy."

  * This word is also used to describe someone who dishonors God by rebelling against him.
  * Something that is unholy might also be described as being common or profane.
  * Ways to translate this term could include, "not belonging to God" or "not honoring to God."

(See also: common, profane other)
