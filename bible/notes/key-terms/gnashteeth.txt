====== gnash teeth, grind teeth ======

Refers to the act of forcing and moving ones teeth together with such intensity that damage to the teeth may result.  Scriptural usage describes the remorse and rage of the one doing the gnashing.

===== Definition: =====

Refers to the act of pushing one's teeth together with such intensity that damage is caused to the teeth.  In Scripture, it can be an expression for extreme grief or anger.
