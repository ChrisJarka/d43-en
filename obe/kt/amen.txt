====== amen, truly ======

===== Definition: =====

Amen and truly are emphatic words used to call attention to what a person is saying.

  * Jesus used amen and truly in His preaching to emphasize His statements.
  * When used at the end of a prayer, amen means "so let it be".
  * Truly means that what a person is saying is sincere or true.
