====== 1 John 01 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This letter does not begin in the way letters in the ancient Near East traditionally began. Instead, it begins in a similar way to the Gospel of John. Both do not really have an introduction of any kind. John appears to almost begin in the middle of an idea.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Christians and sin ==
This chapter teaches that all Christians remain [[:en:obe:kt:sin|sinners]] after they come to [[:en:obe:kt:faith|faith]] in Jesus. Despite this, God still [[:en:obe:kt:forgive|forgives]] a Christian's sins. 

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphors ==
There are many [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|metaphors]] in this chapter, all of which are commonly used in Scripture. Light is always used in a positive way to describe [[:en:obe:kt:righteous|righteousness]]. Darkness is always used in a negative way to describe [[:en:obe:kt:unrighteous|unrighteousness]]. The Christian life is also described as a walk.
