===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **That will be the time**  - These statements mean that when a person grows old that person can not hear as well as one used to hear.
  * **when the doors are shut in the street** - Possible meanings include 1) older people will not hear noise that comes from the street, or 2) "street" is a metaphor for sound in general: "when the ears are not able to hear sound as well." (See: Metaphor)
  * **sound of grinding stops** - People would grind grain between millstones, two heavy stone disks. The rotating of the upper stone produced a constant rough sound.
  * **when men are startled at the voice of a bird** - Possible meanings are that 1) when men cannot sleep and awake when the birds begin to sing; 2) when men have trouble sleeping (insomnia) and awake because of the slightest noise; 3) when men are frightened by a sound, even though it is a harmless bird.
  * **and the singing of girls' voices fades away** - Possible meanings are that 1) older people will "not be able to hear well the songs that the birds sing" (see: UDB), or 2) older people will not be able to hear girls voices, even though they may speak loudly, or 3) older people will not appreciate the joy that comes from hearing young girls talk and sing.
