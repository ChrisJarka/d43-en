====== fire ======

===== Definition: =====

Fire is the physical process of high heat turning a material into another material form or forms.

  * Fire is said to destroy something by burning.
  * Burning wood by fire turns the wood into ashes, that is, carbon.
  * Fire means not only physical flames but in Scripture it is a symbol of power.
  * The final judgment of unbelievers is in the fire of hell.
