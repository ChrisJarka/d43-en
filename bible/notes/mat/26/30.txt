===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **When they **- "When Jesus and his disciples"
  * **hymn **- A hymn is a type of song; in this context, it was probably a song of praise.
  * **fall away **- "abandon me" or "run away" or "give up your faith in me"
  * **strike the shepherd **- "strike down the shepherd" or "cause men to kill the shepherd" (UDB)
  * **sheep of the flock will be scattered **- "sheep of the flock will scatter" (See: Active or Passive)
  * **raised up**  - In this context, "raised up" means being raised back to life from the dead.
