===== Translation Notes: =====
The angel continues to speak with Zechariah.

  * **goes out over the face of**- This phrase refers to the curse like a cloud that spreads to cover the face of the land.  The word "face" refers to the surface of the land like a person's face is on the surface of their head. AT: "effects" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **based on what it says on the one side**  - AT: "based on what the scroll has written on one side"
  * **what it says on the other side**  - AT: "what the scroll has written on the other side"
  * **according to their words**  - The phrase "their words" uses the words of the oath to refer to what was said in the oath. AT: "according to what they said" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **'I will send it out**  - The word "it" refers to the curse.
  * **enter into the house**  - AT: "Takes control of the house" 
  * **the house** - The house is used to refer to the men's families and everything they own. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **remain in his house and consume its timber and stones** - This means the curse will remain in control and will take every part of the house away from the thief and the one who swears a false oath. AT: "stay in control of the man's life and will slowly take everything away from him"
