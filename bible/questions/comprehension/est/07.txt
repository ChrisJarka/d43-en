====== Esther Chapter 07 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What was Esther's request?

A. She wanted her life and also her people's to be given to her [7:3]

Q? What would Esther have done if her people had only been sold into slavery?

A. If her people had only been sold into slavery, she would have kept quiet [7:4].

Q? Who sold Esther's people to be killed?

A. Haman sold Esther's people to be killed [7:4-6].

Q? What did Haman do when the king got up in a rage from the wine-drinking?

A. When the king got up in a rage from the wine-drinking, Haman stayed to beg for his life from Queen Esther [7:7].

Q? After Haman had fallen on the couch where Esther was, what did the king think Haman would do?

A. After Haman had just fallen on the couch where Esther was, the king thought he would assault the queen in the king's presence [7:8].

Q? Where did the king say to hang Haman?

A. The king said to hang Haman and Haman's house, on the gallows Haman set up for Mordecai [7:9].
