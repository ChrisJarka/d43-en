== Balaam (Numbers 22-25) concluded ==

Balaam told the young women from Moab to act friendly to the Israelite men and invite them to feasts in honor of their god, Baal. The men went to the feasts and worshipped Baal. God is angry and killed 24,000 Israelite men.
