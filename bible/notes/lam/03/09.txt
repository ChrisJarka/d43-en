===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He has blocked my path with walls of hewn stone** - God has prevented him from escaping, as would a man who blocked a road. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **hewn stone** - Stones that someone has cut into regular shapes in order to build a wall.
  * **every way I take is crooked** - Possible meanings are 1) "every road I take is impassable" or 2) "every road I take leads nowhere"
  * **He is a bear waiting to ambush me, a lion in hiding** - The writer speaks of God as a vicious animal ready to attack its prey. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **He has turned aside my paths** - Possible meanings are 1) "He has dragged me off the path" or 2) "He has caused my path to turn in the wrong direction"
