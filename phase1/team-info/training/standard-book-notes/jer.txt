====== Standard Notes for Jeremiah ======

This is a growing list of 'standard notes' that occur frequently in Jeremiah. Please use these so that there are consistent notes about the same topic throughout the book.

When a standard note includes brackets (see examples below), replace the brackets with the detail that is appropriate for the text you are working on.

==== At the beginning of the first poetic passage in every chapter where needed====

**General Information**: Jeremiah often wrote prophecy in the form of poetry. Hebrew poetry uses different kinds of parallelism. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

</code>
==== Other Notes: ==== 

**this is Yahweh's declaration** - Yahweh speaks of himself as if he were another person so that Jeremiah and the people of Judah will listen carefully to what he says. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:01:07|1:8]]. AT: "this is what Yahweh has declared" or "this is what I, Yahweh, have declared" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
