===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **inhabitants -**  "people".
  * **Because they have been a reed stalk - ** "for to Israel, you have been only a reed stalk". Reed stalk refers to a stiff rod that is weak and not usable.
  * **to the house of Israel! -**  house of Israel refers to "the people (or nation) of Israel !"
  * **When they seize you in the palm, - **  The reed stalk symbolizes the nation of Egypt. This phrase refers to when Israel needs Egyptian help. " When the people of Israel hold you in their hand,"
  * **you will be crushed and every shoulder divided, - **"she will break hurt every shoulder," She refers to the nation of israel.
  * **and when they leaned on you, - " **  "and as she leaned on you,"
  * **you shattered and made every loin stand up. - "**you gave way and hurt her back."
