====== Sea of Galilee ======


===== Facts: =====


The Sea of Galilee is a lake in eastern Israel that is connected to the Dead Sea by the Jordan River.


    * The towns of Capernaum and Bethsaida were next to the Sea of Galilee.
    * Other names for the Sea of Galilee include: the Sea of Chinnereth, the Lake of Gennesaret, and Yam Kinneret.
