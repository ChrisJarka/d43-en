===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **put on** - The Greek words convey the idea of putting on the listed character traits in much the same way as a person puts on clothing.
  * **as God’s chosen ones--holy and beloved** - “as God's chosen people who are holy and dearly loved”. (See: [[en:ta:translate:Active]])
  * **a heart of compassion, kindness, lowliness, meekness, and patience ** - "a heart that is compassionate, kind, humble, gentle and patient".
  * **heart of compassion** - literally “bowels of mercy”. This phrase could be translated as “heart of empathy” or “heart of consideration”.
  * **kindness ** - “goodness” or “gentleness”. The Greek indicates moral excellence. Could be translated as “goodness” or “gentleness”.
  * **lowliness** - “humbleness of mind” or “lowliness of mind” or “modesty”. The Greek literally means “humility of mind”. 
  * **meekness** - “gentleness”. The Greek word implies a calmness of the soul toward God rather than outward expression of feelings.
  * **patience** - The Greek conveys the idea of "fortitude" or "long-suffering" or "self-restraint".
  * **Bear with one another **– This phrase implies "to put up with or endure each another". The idea is to work together in harmony and love.
  * **complaint against ** - This phrase could be translated as “quarrel with".
  * **have love ** - This can also be translated as “put on”. See note above regarding the phrase "put on".
  * **is the bond of perfection ** - This could be translated as “binds us perfectly together” or “binds us together in perfect harmony”.
