===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **So it will be on that day –this is Yahweh's declaration–**  - "Yahweh says that on that day
  * **that a cry of distress -**  " a loud cry of distress"
  * **will come from the Fish Gate,**  - The Fish Gate is one of the gates in the wall surrounding Jerusalem that allowed people to enter and leave.
  * **wailing from the Second Quarter,**  - "loud crying from the newer part of Jerusalem," The Second Quarter was one of the newer parts of Jerusalem.
  * **and a great crashing sound from the hills.**  - "and great crashing sounds from the hills around the city."
  * **Wail, inhabitants of the Market District, **- "Cry out, inhabitants of the Market District," The Market District is the area where all buying and selling in the city took place.
  * **for all the merchants will be ruined; all those who are weighted down with silver will be cut off.**  - "for all the merchants and traders will be killed;"
