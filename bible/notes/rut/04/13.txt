===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Boaz took Ruth **  - "Boaz married Ruth" or "Boaz took Ruth as a wife"
  * **He went in to her **  - This is a polite way of saying that Boaz and Ruth consummated their marriage, they intimately knew each other as husband and wife. Many languages have acceptable ways of expressing this. (See: [[:en:ta:figuresofspeech:euphemism]])
  * **gave her conception**  - "allowed her to become pregnant"
  * **Let his name be famous**  - This refers to his reputation and character.
  * **restorer of life **  - Naomi can return her focus to God's mercy, blessing, and joy after suffering the loss of her husband and two sons.
  * **who is better to you than seven sons**  - though Naomi had two sons, none of them produced any offspring. However, Ruth bore a grandson to Naomi by Boaz.
