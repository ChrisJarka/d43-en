===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Wisdom cries aloud**  - Wisdom is personified as a woman who is shouting to the people in the city. (See: Personification)
    * **raises her voice**  - "speaks with a loud voice"
    * **head of the noisy streets**  - "place where busy streets meet"
    * **cries out**  - "shouts" or "speaks loudly"
    * **How long**  - Each of the three times "how long" appears in verse 22, it starts a rhetorical question that challenges those who lack wisdom to consider their ways.
    * **bold words**  - explanation
