====== uncircumcised, uncircumcision ======

===== Definition: =====

I

<code>
When used with reference to the male body, this refers to the physical sign of being part of God's Old Testament people, typically done on male babies eight days after birth.
  A male who converted to Judaism as at a later age in Old Testament times had to be circumcised.
  The Bible refers to people who are stubbornly disobedient to God as being uncircumcised at heart.
  The Bible refers to people who gladly obey and are fully dedicated to God as being circumcised to God or circumcised at heart.
</code>
  * When used figuratively the term uncircumcised refers to those whose hearts are hardened toward the grace of God.

(See also: other)
