====== sow, sower ======


===== Definition: =====


To sow is to spread seed by hand over soil to be cultivated. A sower is one who sows.


    * A modern term for a sower is a farmer.
    * Sowing is done by taking a handful of seed and sprinkling it as the sower walks.
