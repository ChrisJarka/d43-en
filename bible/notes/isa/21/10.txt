===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **My threshed and winnowed ones**  - This is comparing the suffering of the people of Judah to grain that was threshed and winnowed. (See: Metaphor)
  * **children of my threshing floor**  - This is referring to the people in Judah.
  * **winnowed **- This is a process of removing the chaff from the kernel of wheat. It is thrown up into the air for the wind to blow away the chaff.
