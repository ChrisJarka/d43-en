====== lute, lyre ======

===== Definition: =====

A lute and lyre are small size (1m)  stringed musucal instruments played by strumming their strings.

  * A lute is very similar to a modern day acoustic guitar, having a wooden sound box and extended neck on which the strings are stretched.
  * A lyre looks very much like a small harp, having an open frame and no wooden sound box.

(See also: other)
