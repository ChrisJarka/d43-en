===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then they cried...their distress.** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:psa:107:004|107:6]], except here "he rescued them out" should be "he brought them out."
  * **Then they** - The word "they" refers to the sailors.
  * **He calmed the storm** - "He made the storm quiet"
  * **the waves were stilled** - AT: "he made the waves still." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **he brought them** - "he guided them"
  * **their desired harbor** - When the sailors were in the danger of the storm, they wished that they were in the safety of a harbor.
