===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **the city **- Nazareth was not a big city in northern Israel, but was a small town. The word used in your language should mean something between the size of a village and a city.
    * **where he had been brought up **- That is, "where he grew up" or "where he stayed as a child until adulthood."
    * **As was his custom**  - It was the usual practice of Jesus to go to the synagogue on the Sabbath day.
    * **he entered the synagogue**  - That is, "he went inside the synagogue."
    * **It was written -**  Or, "It has been written."
    * **scroll of the prophet Isaiah -**  This refers to the book of Isaiah written on a scroll.
