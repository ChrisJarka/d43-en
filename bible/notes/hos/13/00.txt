====== Hosea 13 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter uses many poetic forms to communicate Yahweh's disgust and anger with Israel, the Northern Kingdom. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Israel and Ephraim are used to speak about the people of the Northern Kingdom. Samaria is the capital of the Northern Kingdom. 
This chapter makes it clear that the Northern Kingdom is going to be decimated as a people. In essence they will no longer exist.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

Hosea uses rhetorical questions throughout this book and this chapter is no exception. Because these questions occur in poetry is more than likely they are rhetorical. They are not questions requesting information but rather questions indicating some emotion like anger or dissatisfaction.
