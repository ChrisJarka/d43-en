===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Nadab** - "Aaron's son, in this case. This is not the same person as the 'Nadab' in First Kings."
  * **Abihu** - Abihu is Aaron's son.
  * **censer** - "a container for fire and incense which priests used"
  * **offered unapproved fire** - "offered up fire that was not according to Yahweh's instructions"
  * **devoured** - "ate" or "consumed"
  * **before Yahweh** - "unto Yahweh"
