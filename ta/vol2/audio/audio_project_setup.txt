===== Project Setup =====

This module answers the question: What is meant by project setup?\\
====  ====

Project setup refers to a wide range of planning, communication, logistics, assembly of team members, equipment and many other factors that must be accomplished before you can press the record button to start the first session. Excellence in project setup is one of the best ways to have an easier, faster, lower-cost, lower-stress recording project. Let's look at the various areas of project preparation and setup.
