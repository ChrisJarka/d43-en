===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh: Because - "**The Lord Yahweh says, 'Because…'"
  * **Edom has done in executing vengeance against the house of Judah - **"Edom is guilty of taking revenge on the people of Judah"
  * **Judah and committing wrongdoing, and avenged himself against them;**  - "Judah, and has done wrong things against them.
  * **therefore, thus says the Lord Yahweh: I will**  - Because of this, the Lord Yahweh says, "I will…"
  * **I will strike my hand against Edom - **"my hand" See "Figures of Speech - metaphor" Could be translated, "I will punish Edom"
  * **I will make them a wasteland, - **"I will cause their land to not grow any crops,"
  * **They will fall by the sword! - **"They will die by the sword."
