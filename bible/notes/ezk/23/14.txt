===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **She **- This pronoun refers to Oholibah.
  * **Increased her Prostitution **- This phrase means that Oholibah engaged in prostitution more often.
  * **Her **- This pronoun refers to Oholibah.
  * **Turban **- This word means a male head covering.
  * **Officers **- This word means important Chaldean leaders.
  * **Their **- This pronoun refers to the Chaldean leaders.
  * **Land of their birth **- This phrase refers to the place where the Chaldeans were born.
