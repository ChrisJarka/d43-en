===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **will dash you to pieces**  - This means that the enemies of Nineveh are going to destroy them so much that there will be few of them. 
    * **Man the city walls**  - This means the city should set guards on the walls for protection. 
    * **make yourselves strong, assemble your armies.**  - This means the guards and army need to be prepared for the fight.  
    * **For Yahweh is restoring the majesty of Jacob, like the majesty of Israel**  - This means that Yahweh will bring back the glory of Jacob like the glory of Israel. 
    * **and destroyed their vines**  - This means that the enemies of Nineveh destroyed all the food sources.
