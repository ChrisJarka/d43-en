====== Hittite======

===== Definition: =====

The Hittites were descendants of Ham through his son Canaan. By 1600 B.C. they occupied most of Europe and Palestine. They were often a threat to Israel both physically and spiritually.

  * Abraham negotiated with Ephron the Hittite for a piece of property containing the cave of Machpelah so that he could bury his deceased wife, Sarah.  By tradition that property is located in Hebron.
  * Esau's parents were grieved when he married two Hittite women.
  * One of David's mighty men of valor was named Uriah the Hittite.
  * Solomon's heart was turned from following God by the foreign women he kept, some of whom were Hittite.
