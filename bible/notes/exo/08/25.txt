===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **sacrifice to your God in the land**  - The "in the land" means "in this land" of Egypt which is not too far away.
    * **we make sacrifices that are an abomination** - or "we make sacrifices that are a disgrace" or "we make sacrifices that are detestable"
