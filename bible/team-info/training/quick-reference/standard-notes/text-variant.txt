The Ethiopian answered, "I believe that Jesus Christ is the Son of God." \f* \v 38 So the Ethiopian commanded the chariot to stop, and they went down into the water, both Philip and the eunuch; and Philip baptized him. </usfm>

== Translation notes: ==

* **verse 37**  - This verse was omitted because some of the older, more reliable, ancient texts did not include the verse. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:originalmanuscripts|Original Manuscripts]])
