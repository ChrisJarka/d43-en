====== reconcile, reconciliation ======

===== Definition: =====

To reconcile is the process of bringing disputing persons or groups to a friendly relationship. Reconciliation is the end of a disputing relationship and the beginning of a friendly relationship.

  * Mankind can only be reconciled to God through the death of His Son Jesus Christ.
  * A husband and wife who have difficulties in their marriage can reconcile with each other.
  * Friends who have been offended and split apart can reconcile to become friends again.
