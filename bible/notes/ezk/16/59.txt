===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I will treat you-**  This means that God will make them responsible.
  * **despises an oath-**  The sense of the is the people do not think the oath is of any value.
  * **break a covenant ****-**  This sentence means fail to complete a promise. The word "promise" may also be translated "covenant."
  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh -** See how you translated this in 5:5.
