====== Psalms 144 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 144 is the first of seven worship psalms that finish up the Book of Psalms.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Psalm 144 is a prayer for safety and prosperity for the nation.
