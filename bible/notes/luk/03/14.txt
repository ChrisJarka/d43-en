===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **soldiers**  - That is, "people serving in the army."
    * **asked Him**-Or, were asking him continually or repeatedly perhaps signifying different soldiers asked the same question.
    * **What about us? What must we do?**  - That is, "You told the crowd and the tax collector what they must do. How about us soldiers, what must we do?" John is not included in the words "us" and "we." (See: [[:en:ta:vocabulary:exclusive|Exclusive]])
    * **Do not take money from anyone by force**  - That is, "Do not force people to give you money." This can also be translated as "Take money from no one by using force." The soldiers were using force to take money from people. The command has to obeyed immediately without delay.
    * **and do not accuse anyone falsely**  - That is, "In the same way, do not accuse anyone falsely in order to get money from them" or, "Do not lie by saying that an innocent person has done something illegal." The soldiers were making false charges to get money. This command must also be obeyed immediately without delay.
    * **Be content with your wages**  - That is, "Be satisfied with your pay" or, "Be satisfied with what you are paid."
