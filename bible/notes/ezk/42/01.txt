===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Next, he sent me**  - The word "he" refers to the man whose appearance was like brass (40:3).
  * **forecourt** - This the part in front of the court.
  * **cubits** - See how you translated these "long" cubits in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:05|40:05]].
  * **one hundred cubits** - About fifty-four meters. (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaldistance]])
  * **fifty cubits** - About twenty-seven meters. 
  * **twenty cubits** - About eleven meters. 
  * **pavement** - "stony path" or the area of the courtyard where people walk.
  * **gallery** - This is were the people gather in a group. (See:  [[:en:ta:translate:translateunknowns-temple|gallery]]).
  * **three stories** - "three levels".
