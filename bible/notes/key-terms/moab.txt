====== Moab, Moabite, Moabitess ======

===== Facts: =====

Moab was the son of Lot's elder daughter. His descendants were called Moabites and the land they settled in also became known as "Moab."

  * The country of Moab was located east of Judea, on the opposite side of the Dead Sea. It was southeast of the Judean city of Bethlehem, <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>where Naomi's family was from</font> .
  * The term "Moabitess" can be translated as, "Moabite woman" or "woman from Moab."
