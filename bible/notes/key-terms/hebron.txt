====== Hebron ======

===== Facts: =====

Hebron was a city located in the high rocky hills about 20 miles south of Jerusalem.

  * The city was built around 2,000 B.C. during the time of Abram and was mentioned numerous times in the historical accounts given in the Old Testament.
  * Hebron had a very prominent role in David's career. Several of his sons, including Absalom, were born there.
  * The city was destroyed about 70 A.D. by the Romans.
