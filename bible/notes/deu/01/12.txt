===== Translation Notes: =====

  * Moses is speaking to the people of Israel.
  * **loads**  - Moses is tired of dealing with the problems of the Israelites and leading them where they are supposed to go. He is comparing this responsibility with a load that is heavy for him to carry. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]
  * **heads over you - ** leaders who have authority
  * **disputes**  - arguments, disagreements
