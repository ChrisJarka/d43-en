===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **us**– This pronoun is referring to Paul and all other believers.
  * **the hidden truth **– This phrase could translated as “mystery".
  * **the plan **– This is more correctly translated “his plan”. (See UDB)
  * ​**his **– This pronoun refers to God.
  * **he **– This pronoun also refers to God.
