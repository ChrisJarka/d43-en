===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **who carves out evil gains for his house** "who works to get dishonest profits for his family"
    * **his**  - The word "his" refers to the Babylonian/Chaldeans.
    * **set his nest on high** - This means that the person is building his house far from trouble. 
    * **safe from the hand of evil**  - The phrase "hand of evil" means to be where bad things or bad people can do harm. "safe from where bad things can happen" (See: [[en:ta:translate:personification]])
    * **devised shame** - "planned dishonor"
    * ** by cutting off many people** - Removing a person from your life is compared to cutting a branch off of a tree.  "by removing many people form your life" (See: [[en:ta:translate:idiom]]
    * **have sinned against your own soul** - Possible meanings: 1) "caused problems for yourself" or 2) hurt yourself"
    * **stones will cry out from the wall** - This means that the house built by sin will call out and tell others. (See: [[en:ta:translate:personification]]) 
    * **the rafters of timber will answer them** - This means that the walls will echo the news.(See: [[en:ta:translate:personification]])
