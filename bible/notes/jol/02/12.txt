===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **all your heart**  - This phrase means all of a person, and at the same time also represents the "heart" of the nation of Israel. (See:[[:en:ta:translate:metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **fast**  - Sometimes people fast in order to show that they are very sad or grieved about something.
  * **Tear your heart and not only your garments**  - The Israelites tore their clothes as a sign of grief. This phrase is saying to not just engage in the outward expressions of grief. (See:[[:en:ta:translate:idiom|Idiom]])
