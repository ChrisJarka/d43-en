===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Your God ... your strength** - The word "your" refers to the people David is speaking to.
  * **to us**  - The word "us" refers to the people David is speaking to and to himself. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
  * **your power** - Here the word "your" refers to God.
