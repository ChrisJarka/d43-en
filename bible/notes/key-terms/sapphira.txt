====== Sapphira and Ananias ======

===== Facts: =====

Sapphira and her husband Ananias were professing believers who lived in Jerusalem during the time of the early church.

  * Sapphira joined her husband in lying to the apostles about the amount of money they had made in profits from the sale of their property.
  * Because of this lie, God killed Sapphira and Ananias.
