====== Nehemiah Chapter 03 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who built and consecrated the Sheep Gate as far as the Tower of the Hundred and the Tower of Hananel?

A. Eliashib with his brother priests, the men of Jericho, and Zaccur did [3:1-2].

Q? Who built the Fish Gate?

A. The sons of Hassenaah built it, next to Meremoth, Meshullam, Zadok, and the Tekoites [3:3-5].

Q? Whose leaders refused to do the work ordered by their supervisors?

A. The Tekoites' leaders refused to do the work [3:5].

Q? Who repaired the Old Gate?

A. Joiada and Meshullam repaired it, next to Melatiah and Jadon [3:6-7].

Q? Who repaired Jerusalem as fas as the Broad Wall?

A. Uzziel and Hananiah repaired it, next to Rephaiah, Jedaiah, and Hattush [3:8-10].

Q? Who repaired another section along with the Tower of Ovens?

A. Malchijah son of Harim and Hasshub repaired it, next to Shallum along with his daughters [3:11-12].

Q? Who repaired the Valley Gate as far as the Dung Gate?

A. Hanun and the inhabitants of Zanoah repaired it [3:13].

Q? Who repaired the Dung Gate, and who repaired the Fountain Gate and the wall of the Pool of Siloam?

A. Malchijah son of Rechab repaired the Dung Gate, and Shallun repaired the Fountain Gate and the wall of the Pool of Siloam [3:14].

Q? Who repaired from the place across from the tombs of David to the house of the mighty men?

A. Nehemiah repaired that, and after him the Levites, Rehum, and Hashabiah [3:16-17].

Q? Whe repaired another section opposite the ascent to the armory, at the buttress?

A. The countrymen from the district of Keliah, including Bavvai, repaired it, next to Ezer [3:17-19].

Q? Who energetically repaired from the buttress to the door of the house of Elisahib the high priest?

A. Baruch energetically repaired it [3:20].
