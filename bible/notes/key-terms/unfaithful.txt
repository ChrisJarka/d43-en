====== unfaithful ======


===== Definition: =====


An unfaithful person is one who does not fulfill their vowed responsibilities and duties.


    * In marriage, someone who commits adultery is "unfaithful" to his or her spouse.
    * Israel was called "unfaithful" when the people began to worship idols.


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:urlterm|faithful, adultery]])
