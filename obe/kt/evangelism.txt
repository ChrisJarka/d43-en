====== evangelism, evangelist ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "evangelism" refers to the practice of sharing the good news about Jesus Christ. An evangelist is a person who shares this good news (gospel).

  * The literal meaning of "evangelism" is "telling the good news."
  * The literal meaning of "evangelist" is "good news teller." 
  * Jesus sent his apostles out to spread the good news about the kingdom of God.
  * All Christians are exhorted to share this good news and some of them have been given a special spiritual gift to do this. These people have the "gift of evangelism" and are called "evangelists."

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * Other ways to translate "evangelism" could inlcude, "sharing the good news (about Jesus)" or "proclamation of the good news" or "widespread preaching of the good news."
  * The term "evangelist" could be translated as, "someone who preaches the good news" or "teacher of the good news" or "good news proclaimer."

(See also: good news, gospel, spirit, spiritual, gift other)
