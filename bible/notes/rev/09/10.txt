===== translationNotes: =====

  * **They had tails** - The word "They" refers to the locusts.
  * **with stingers like scorpions** - "with stingers like scorpions' stingers" or "with stingers that could cause terrible pain as scorpions' stingers can" 
  * **scorpions** - See how you translated "scorpion" in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:09:05|9:5]].
  *  **in their tails they had power to harm people for five months** - 1) for five months they had power for five months to harm people or 2) they could sting people and the people would be in pain for five months
  * **the bottomless pit** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:09:01|9:1]].
  * **Abaddon ... Apollyon** - Both names mean "destruction." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]] and  [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_transliterate]])
  * **there are still two disasters to come** - "there are still two disasters that will happen" or "two more terrible things will happen"
