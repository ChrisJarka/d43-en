====== Deuteronomy Chapter 27 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Moses and the elders of Israel command the people of Israel to do?

A. Moses and the elders of Israel told the people, "Keep all the commandments that I command you today." [27:1]

Q? What must the people of Israel do after they pass over the Jordan to the land that Yahweh would give them?

A. When the people of Israel pass over the Jordan, they must set up some large stones with plaster and write on them all the words of the law. [27:2-4, 8]

Q? What must the people of Israel build on Mount Ebal?

A. The people of Israel must build an altar of stone to Yahweh without using iron tools to offer burned offerings to him. [27:5-7]

Q? Why was it important for the people of Israel to hear the voice of Yahweh and obey his commandments that day?

A. It was important for the people of Israel to hear and obey Yahweh's commandments because on that day they became the people of Yahweh. [27:9-10]

Q? What did Moses command the people of Israel on that same day?

A. Moses told the people, "The tribes of Simeon, Levi, Judah, Issachar, Joseph, and Benjamin must stand on Mount Gerizim to bless the people and the tribes of Reuben, Gad, Asher, Zebulun, Dan, and Naphtali must stand on Mount Ebal to pronounce curses." [27:11-13]
