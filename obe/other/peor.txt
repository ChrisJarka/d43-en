====== Peor, Mount Peor, Baal Peor ======

===== Facts: ====

Mount Peor was a mountain located northeast of the Salt Sea,l in the region of Moab, east of the Salt Sea. This was also the land where the tribe of Reuben lived.

  * The name "Beth Peor" was another name for the city of Peor.
  * "Baal Peor" was a false god of the Moabites that was worshiped at Mount Peor.
