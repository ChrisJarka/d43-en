====== letter, epistle ======

===== Definition: =====

A "letter" is a message written on parchment or paper sent to a person or group of persons.  An "epistle" is a letter of any kind, but may be thought of as more formal and written to instruct.

  * The New Testament books written by Paul, John, James, Jude, and Peter are letters, or epistles, of instruction written to Christians in various cities in Italy, Greece, and Asia Minor.
