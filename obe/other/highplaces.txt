====== high places ======

===== Definition: =====

High places were elevated mounds of earth where idol worship altars were built and used.

* Israel sinned against God by building high places and worshipping idols on them.

* When a God-fearing King took office in Israel a common practice for him was to remove the high places and abolish idol worship.
