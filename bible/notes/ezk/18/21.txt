===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But if the wicked turns away from all his sins **- This means that the wicked stops doing evil or being wicked.
  * **He/Him/His**  - This pronoun refers to ' the wicked'.
  * **performs justice and righteousness **- This phrase means doing something fair, right and morally good according to the laws of God.
