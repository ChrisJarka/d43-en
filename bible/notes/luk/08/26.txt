===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **region of the Gerasenes**  - that is, "the area around the city of the Gerasenes" or "the area belonging to the Gerasenes"
    * **opposite Galilee**  - that is, "on the other side from"
    * **stepped out**  - or, "got out"
    * **had demons**  - That is, "he was controlled by demons."
    * **he had worn no**  - That is, "he had not worn."
    * **tombs**  - That is, "place were persons are buried or memorialized."
    * **man**  - here this means "adult male."
