===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - This is the end of the part of the story that began in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:12:01|12:1]], where the writer tells of growing opposition to Jesus' ministry.

  * **him who told him** - "the person who had told Jesus that his mother and brothers were waiting to see him"
  * **Who is my mother? And who are my brothers?** - These two questions can be translated as one statement. AT: "I will tell you who are really my mother and brothers." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **whoever** - "anyone"
  * **Father** - This is an important title for God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
