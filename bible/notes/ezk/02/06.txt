===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **thou** - The Spirit addresses Ezekiel with this word meaning "you."
  * **Son of man**  - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:02:03|2:03]].
  * **you are with briers and thorns and you dwell with scorpions** - This is a metaphor describing the people of Israel who will not treat Ezekiel kindly when he tells them what God says. (See: [[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:metaphor]])
  * **briers and thorns** - Briers are bushes with sharp points coming off of the branches.  The sharp points on the branches are called thorns.
  * **scorpions** - A scorpion is a small animal with two front claws, six legs and a large tail with poisonous stinger.
  * **Do not fear their words**  - "Do not be afraid of what they say."
  * **dismayed by their faces** - "upset at the way they look at you"
