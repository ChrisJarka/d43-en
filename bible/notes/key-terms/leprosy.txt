====== leprosy, leper ======


===== Definition: =====


In the Bible, "leprosy" is a term used for many different, incurable skin diseases.  When a person had one of these skin diseases, he was considered unclean.


    * A "leper" is a person who has leprosy.
    * People with leprosy were isolated from other people in areas outside of the encampment or town.
    * Today, leprosy is a chronic, curable, infectious disease that affects the skin and nerves, and frequently causes deformity.


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:unclean|unclean]])
