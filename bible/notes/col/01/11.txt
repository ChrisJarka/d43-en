===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **We pray you**- The pronoun "we" refers to Paul and Timothy and the pronoun "you" refers to all the believers in Colossae.
  * **strengthened in every ability according ​to ****the power of his glory **- This could be translated "impowered by his glorious might".
  * **qualified us**  - The pronoun "us" refers to Paul, Timothy and all believers in Colossae.
  * **share **- This word could be translated "piece" or "part". This would mean that we will be given a portion of the inheritance of God's children.
  * **in light **- This could be translated as "in the glory of his presence".
  * **set apart for him **- The Greek word from which this phrase comes means “morally blameless” or "consecrated" or "holy ones". This word is often translated as “saints”.
  * **him **- The pronoun "him" is referring to the Father.
