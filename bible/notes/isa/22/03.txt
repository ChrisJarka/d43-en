===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **but they were caught without a bow **-"but they were caught fleeing without any means of self defense"
  * **Therefore I said, "Do not look at me, I will weep bitterly **- Isaiah was so upset to see the leaders captured that he wept bitter tears.
  * **the destruction of the daughter of my people **- Daughter was used to refer to cities and nations.
