====== bewitch, give the evil eye to ======

===== Definition: =====

Bewitch is to cast a demonic spell on someone. The idiom "give the evil eye to" may relate to someone like an evil person or a sorcerer who might cast a spell, or bewitch, someone with a look of the eye, or eyes.

  * Bewitch can mean being distracted from the truth by someone.

(See also: other)
