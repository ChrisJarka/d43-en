  * **Yahweh will make the splendor of his voice heard and show the motion of his arm in storming anger and flames of fire, with windstorm, rainstorm, and hailstones. **-Yahweh is shown as having human characteristics such as a voice an arms to express His power – “Yahweh will speak and cause a devouring fire and a terrible storm with winds and hail to show his majesty and power” -( See: Synecdoche)
