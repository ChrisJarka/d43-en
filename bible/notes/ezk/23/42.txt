===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues to refer to the cities of Jerusalem and Samaria as if they were two prostitutes. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])

  * **the sound of a crowd who were with worries was with you** - The sound of a crowd is used to refer to the crowd of people. AT: "There was a noisy crowd of careless people with you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **drunkards were brought** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "drunkards had come" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **drunkards** - This refers to people who often get drunk. However, some modern versions translate "Sabeans," that is, men from Sheba. 
  * **ornate** - beautifully decorated or carved
