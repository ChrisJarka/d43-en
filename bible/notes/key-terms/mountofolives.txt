====== Mount of Olives ======

===== Definition: =====

The Mount of Olives was a mountain or large hill located near the city of Jerusalem. It was probably named for the groves of olive trees covering it.

  * Jesus and his disciples went to the Mount of Olives on several occasions, the last one being the night before he was killed.
  * When Jesus returns, he will stand on the Mount of Olives and defend Jerusalem.
  * This can also be translated as, "Olive Tree Mountain."
