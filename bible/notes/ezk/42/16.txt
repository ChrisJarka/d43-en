===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He **- the man whose appearance was like brass (40:3)
  * **measuring stick**  - <font 13px line-height: 19.5px background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)/Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;;inherit;;rgb(255, 255, 255)>a long, thin piece of wood, metal, plastic, etc., that is used to measure something</font>
  * **around**  - this is confusing and possibly unnecessary
  * **the side of the sea **- "the west side, which was the side of the sea"
