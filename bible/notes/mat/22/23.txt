===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Teacher, Moses said, 'if a man dies,…**  - They were asking him about what Moses wrote in the scriptures. This could be stated as an indirect quote "Moses said that if a man dies…" (See: Indirect quote)
  * **his brother **- "his" refers to the dead man's brother.
