====== offer, offering ======

===== Definition: =====

As used in the Bible, an "offering" is something that is given to God as a sacrifice. The term "offer" means "to give" or "to present" something as a sacrifice.

  * There are different types of offerings or sacrifices that God required. Some of these offerings were the "burnt offering," the "sin offering," the "grain offering," and the "drink offering."
  * The names of the offerings depended on what was being offered and for what purpose.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * "To offer (a sacrifice)" could be translated as "to give" or "to present" or "to sacrifice." Other ways to translate "offering" could be "gift" or "sacred gift."
