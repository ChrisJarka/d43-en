====== Psalms Chapter 079 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What happened when the foreign nations came into Israel?

A. They defiled the temple, they turned Jerusalem into a heap of ruins, They gave the bodies of God's faithfull people to the birds and the beasts, and they shed their blood like water, and was no one left to bury them. [79:1-3]

Q? How do the neighbors treat God's people?

A. The neighbors reproach, mock, and deride them. [79:4-5]

Q? Who gets God's wrath?

A. The nations that do not know or call upon God's name will get God's wrath. [79:6]

Q? What does the psalmist want from the God of his salvation?

A. He wants God to forget the sins of his forefathers,God's merciful actions, his help,to save them and forgive their sins. [79:8-9]

Q? How does God show the greatness of his power to his servants?

A. He avenges their blood, he hears the prisoner's groans, he keeps them alive. [79:10-11]

Q? The psalmist identifies God's people as sheep who do what?

A. They give thanks forever and tell God's praises to all generations. [79:13]
