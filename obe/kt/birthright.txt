====== birthright ======

===== Definition: =====

Birthright refers to the benefits owed to a person due to their birth order. Normally this concerns the firstborn son.

  * Esau sold his birthright to his brother Jacob for a meal. Because of this Jacob inherited a blessing instead of Esau.
  * The blessing of the birthright was usually a double portion of the father's normal inheritance. A King's firstborn son would be awarded his father's throne based on birthright.
