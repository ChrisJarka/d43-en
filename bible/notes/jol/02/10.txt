===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **The earth shakes in front of them,
The heavens tremble,
The sun and the moon are darkened,
And the stars stop shining**  - There are so many locust that the earth and sky shakes, and the all the objects in the heaven cannot be seen. (See:[[en:ta:translate:hyperbole]])
    * **For the day of Yahweh is great and very terrible.
Who can survive it?**  - This emphasizes that there is no escape from the "day of Yahweh." (See:[[en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]])
