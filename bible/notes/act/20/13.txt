===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **We ourselves**  - The reflexive pronoun, 'ourselves', is used here to add emphasis and to separate Luke and his fellow travelling companions from Paul, who did not travel by boat.
    * **he himself**  - 'himself' is used to add emphasis to 'he'. Paul traveled by himself.
    * **This is what he himself desired to do, because he planned to go by land**  - "This is what he desired to do, because he himself planned to go by land."
