===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **plunder** - "Plunder" is what people and animals steal from the person who owns it. The shepherds and the wild animals have been stealing the sheep and the goats from the flock that that belonged to Yahweh.
  * **I will judge between the sheep** - See how you translated "I will be a jdge among sheep" in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:34:17|34:17]].
  * **I will raise up one shepherd over them** - "I will assign one shepherd to be in charge of my sheep and the goats"
  * **will shepherd them** - "will feed and take care of them
  * **my servant David** - Yahweh said "David" to refer to someone who would be a descendent of David. Alternate translation: "a descendent of my servant David." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[en:obe:other:descendant]])
  * **will be a shepherd over them** - "will be their shepherd"
  * **will be their God** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:11:19|11:20]].
  * **will be a chief in their midst** - "will be their ruler" (See: [[en:obe:other:ruler]])
  * **I, Yahweh, have declared this** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:05:15|5:17]].
