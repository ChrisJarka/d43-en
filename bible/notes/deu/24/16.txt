===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The parents must not be put to death for their children** - AT: "You must not execute the parents because of something bad that one of his children did" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **neither must the children be put to death for their parents** - AT: "and you must not execute the children because of something bad that their parents did" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **everyone must be put to death for his own sin** - AT: "you should only execute a person because of something bad that he did himself" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
