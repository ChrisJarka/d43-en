===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **build up a wall** - In ancient Israel, walls were a primary form of defense for a city. God is looking for someone who will defend Israel against His accusations. (See: Metaphor)
    * **stand in the breech** - This phrase means to stand in the space between two things. There is a relationship gap between God and His people. He is looking for someone to stand before Him on behalf of the people. (See: Metaphor)
    * **breech**  - This word means, the back part of a gun barrel where a bullet is loaded.
    * **pour out my indignation upon them!** - God describes the execution of His judgment in terms of anger that is poured out. It flows. It touches everything, like water that is poured out. (See: Metaphor)
    * **I will finish them with the fire of my indignation** - This phrase means that God will punish them severely in His anger.
    * **set their way upon their own heads** - The Israelites have treated others badly in many ways. This phrase means that God will treat them as they have treated others. (See: Metaphor)
    * **declaration**  - This word means a formal announcement either verbally or in writing.
