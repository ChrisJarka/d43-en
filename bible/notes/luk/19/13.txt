===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Jesus continues telling his parable.)
  * **gave them ten minas**  - This could also be translated as "gave them each one mina."
  * **ten minas**  - This could also be translated as "ten valuable coins" or "a large amount of money". A mina was worth about what someone would be paid in four months.
  * **citizen**  -Possibly referring to the Jews.
  * **delegation**  -Possibly referring to the Jewish leaders who challenged Jesus and eventually had Him arrested.
  * **profit**  -May refer to the increase to the Kingdom produced by the work of God's people.
  * **doing business**  - this may mean "selling and buying or displaying, demonstrating, conversing, and wooing people's interest to buy your article or material or commodity in a market place. However, in the gospel language, this may mean bearing witness for Christ Jesus, evangelizing or proclaiming the good news, preaching the gospel to win and nurture believers in Christ Jesus and for Christ."
