====== seek, sought ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "seek" means to look for something or someone. The past tense is "sought."

  * This term can also mean "try to" do something.
  * seek an opportunity, seek the Lord, seek justice, seek Yahweh, seek him out, seek protection
  * To "seek" or "look for" an opportunity means to "try to find a time" to do a particular thing
  * To "seek Yahweh" means to spend time and energy getting to know him and learning to obey him.

(See also: other)
