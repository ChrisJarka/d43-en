====== test ======


===== Definition: =====


A test refers to difficult circumstances that God allows in a person's life for their sanctification. The agent of testing in the Bible is usually Satan.


    * After fasting 40 days in the desert, Jesus was tested by Satan with three temptations.
    * The Pharisees and Saducees tested Jesus by asking him questions to try to trick him.
    * The Bible states that we should never test God.
