===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues the speech he began in [[:en:bible:notes:jol:02:24|2:25]], promising good things for the people of Israel.

  * **It will be that everyone** - "This is what will happen: everyone"
  * **everyone who calls on the name of Yahweh will be saved** - The name is a metonym for the person. This can be translated in active form. AT: "Yahweh will save everyone who calls on his name" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **on Mount Zion and in Jerusalem** - These refer to the same place. AT: "on Mount Zion in Jerusalem" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **among the survivors, those whom Yahweh calls** - "among the survivors there will be those whom Yahweh calls" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **survivors** - people who live through a terrible event like a war or a disaster
