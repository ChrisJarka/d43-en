====== fellowship ======

===== Definition: =====

Fellowship is a shared relationship that believers have with one another through our relationship with Christ and the Holy Spirit.

  * Fellowship between God and men begins when a believer professes belief in Christ and accepts His gift of salvation and the indwelling of the Holy Spirit.
  * Church fellowship among believers begins when a believer acknowledges his or her profession of faith before a congregation and is baptized.

(See also: other)
