===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But Saul**  - The story changes from Philip to Saul. Better translated as "Meanwhile, Saul…" (UDB).
  * **…still speaking threats even of murder against the disciples…**  - the abstract noun "murder" can be translated as, "…still speaking threats, even to murder the disciples…" (see: [[:en:ta:translate:abstract-nouns|Abstract Nouns]])
  * **asked him for letters**  - "asked the high priest for letters of support"
  * **if he found…he might bring**  - 'he' refers to Saul
  * **who belonged to the Way **- "who followed the teachings of Jesus Christ”
  * **prisoners to Jerusalem so that the Jewish leaders could judge and punish them.**  - the text, "so that the Jewish leaders could judge and punish them" (UDB) is implied information regarding Saul's intent for the letters. (see: [[:en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit|Explicit and Implicit Information]])
