===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Lift up your eyes** - This phrase commands a person to look up by referring to their eyes. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **a lead covering**  - This refers to a heavy cover. Lead is a heavy metal.
  * **a woman under it sitting in it**  - AT: "a woman sitting inside of the barrel"
