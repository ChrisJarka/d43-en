===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **six hundred shekels of gold** - A shekel is not a coin but a measure of weight of around 11 grams. 600 shekels is approximately 6.6 kilograms of gold
    * **he called on Yahweh** - David prayed to Yahweh.
    * **the angel put his sword back into its sheath.** - This means that the angel put away his sword, showing that he would do no further harm to Jerusalem.
    * **sheath**  - This is protective covering for the sharp sword.
