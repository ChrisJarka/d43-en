===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **establish my covenant with you**  - alternate translation: I promise you
  * **you will remember and be ashamed**  - God is telling them that they will remember their sins and be embarrassed.
  * **you will no longer open your mouth to speak because of your shame **- God is saying that they will not be proud about what they did anymore and they will be embarrased about what they did.
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration**  See how you translated this in 5:11.
