====== chaff ======


===== Definition: =====


A thin, light husk that separates from grain and must be discarded.


    * Once separated from the grain, the chaff can be blown away by the wind if tossed in the air.
    * A person scoops grain into a container and throws it up into the air where it can separate from the chaff.
