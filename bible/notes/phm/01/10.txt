===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **concerning - **  "about"
  * **child - **  see "Key Terms"
  * **Onesimus - **  see "How to Translate Names."
  * **whom I have begotten in my bonds -**  "who became my spiritual child in Christ while i am in jail" or "who became my spiritual child while I am in jail."
  * **who once was unprofitable -**  "He was once useless" or He was useless to you before"
  * **but now is profitable-**  "but now is useful" or "helpful"
  * **I have sent him,**** himself, back to you - **"I have sent Onesimus back to you."
  * **he who is my very heart: - **"He is very dear to me." or "I love him dearly."
  * **whom I would rather have kept with me who am in chains for the gospel. - **"I would rather keep him here with me while I am in jail chains for the sake of the gospel"
  * **that on**** your behalf -**  "that he might serve me in your place." (DLB) or "that he would help me in your place.
