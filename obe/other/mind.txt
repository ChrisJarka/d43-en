====== mind ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "mind" refers to the part of a person that thinks and makes decisions.

  * A person's brain is the physical organ of thinking.
  * The mind of each person is the total of his or her thoughts and reasoning.
  * To "have the mind of Christ" means to be thinking and acting as Jesus Christ would. It means being obedient to God the Father, obeying the teachings of Christ, being enabled to do this through the power of the Holy Spirit.
