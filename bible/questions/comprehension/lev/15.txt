====== Leviticus Chapter 15 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Yahweh say to Moses and Aaron about a man who has a flow of infected fluid?

A. Yahweh said to Moses and Aaron that any man who has a flow of infected fluid coming out of his body is unclean. [15:3]

Q? What happens to the person who touches the person who is unclean because of a flow infected fluid?

A. Anyone who touches the unclean person will be unclean himself and will need to bathe, wash his clothes and will be unclean until evening. [15:7]
