===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Why are you still being struck?**- Isaiah is asking all the people a rhetorical question. "Why is Yahweh still punishing you?"
  * **Why do you rebel more and more?**- Isaiah answers his own question with a question - "Why are you continuing to sin against Yahweh?"
  * **The whole head is sick**- "The leaders and authorities over Israel are infected with sin"
  * **the whole heart faint**- " all the people are weary"
  * **From the sole of the foot to the head there is no part unharmed**- "everyone and everything is infected with sin"
  * **only wounds, and bruises, and fresh open wounds; they have not been closed, cleansed, bandaged, nor treated with oil.**-"your sins are like open sores that have not been dealt with."
