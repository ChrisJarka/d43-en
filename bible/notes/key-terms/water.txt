====== water, waters ======

===== Definition: =====

Water refers to a body of water, such as an ocean, sea, lake, or river. Waters refers to bodies or sources of water.

  * Waters can generally refer to a group of bodies of water or multiple water sources.
  * In Scripture, God is referred to as the Spring of living water to His people, the source of spiritual power. Jesus also referred to the Holy Spirit as a stream of living water (John 7:37-39).
