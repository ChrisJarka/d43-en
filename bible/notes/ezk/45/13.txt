===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the princes of Israel.
  * **that you must present** - The word "you" possibly refers to 1) the house of Israel or 2) the princes of Israel.
  * **must present** - Possible meanings are 1) "must give" or 2) "must collect."
  * **ephah** - about 22 liters (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicalvolume]])
  * **homer** - about 220 liters (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicalvolume]])
  * **for every homer of wheat** - It is implied that this is the amount of wheat that that the people harvest. AT: "for every homer of wheat from the harvest." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **you will give** - Possible meanings are 1) "you will give" or 2) "you will take."
  * **bath** - about 22 liters (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicalvolume]])
  * **kor** -  (See: Biblical weights).
  * **One lamb from the flock, from two hundred** - This means that for every 200 sheep, one must be given as an offering and 199 may be kept.
  * **this is the Lord Yahweh’s declaration** - See how you translated this in [[:en/bible/notes/ezk/05/11|5:11]].
