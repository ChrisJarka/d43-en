====== messenger ======

===== Facts: =====

A messenger is the one who who is sent with a message.

  * An angel can convey a public or private message.
  * A COURIER is a messenger who is part of a royal messenger service.
  * A HERALD is a messenger who may issue a public decree.
  * A PROPHET is a messenger sent by God with a special message for his people.
  * The FORERUNNER of the Messiah, John the Baptist, is a messenger.
  * Apostles are also considered to be messengers of the Gospel.
