===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **And he called out**  - That is, the blind man called out.
  * **Son of David**  - Jesus was a descendant of King David. It is here used as a royal title describing the anointed king that would come to Israel.
  * **son of David**  - this is the same person as Jesus of Nazareth. However, the address "son of David" has Messianic overtones. This blind man's address is on the same level as Peter's confession in Mt. 16:16.
  * **have mercy on me**  - Or, "show me pity or compassion"
  * **And the ones - ** That is, "And the people"
  * be quiet - That is, "be silent." bold words
  * **bold words**  - explanation
