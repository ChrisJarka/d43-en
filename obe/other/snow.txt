====== snow ======

===== Facts: =====

Frozen flakes of precipitation typically falling in the high altitudes of mountains.

  * Snow falls occasionally in Jerusalem in the winter but does not normally accumulate.
  * The whiteness of snow is symbolic of purity. Scripture states that "though our sins are as scarlet they shall be as white as snow."
  * The visiions of the glorified Christ in the book of Revelation state that His hair was "while like snow."
