===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **So they sent their sister Rebekah**  - "So the family sent Rebekah"
    * **Our sister**  - Rebekah was not the sister to everyone in her family. This idiom is used to show deep feelings for Rebekah. This could be translated "Our dear Rebekah."
    * **may you be the mother of**  - The prayer is not for her to give birth to all these children. This idiom means may she have this many descendants. This could be translated as "may you, through your descendants, be the mother of." (See: [[en:ta:translate:idiom]])
    * **thousands of ten thousands** - This hyperbole could be translated as "more than you can count." ([[en:ta:translate:hyperbole]])
    * **possess the gate of those who hate them**  - "rule over their enemies"
