===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **peace be with you** - See note in [[:en:bible:notes:jhn:20:19|20:19]].
  * **they are forgiven for them** - "God will forgive them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **they are kept back** - "God will not forgive" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **As the Father has sent me, so I am sending you...he...said to them, “Receive the Holy Spirit** - God the Father sent God the Son who sends believers in the power of God the Spirit. The Spirit lives in those who have believed on God the Son. Translate the names of God's persons, "Father, Son, and Spirit," literally in your language to maintain the original names chosen by God for his persons.
