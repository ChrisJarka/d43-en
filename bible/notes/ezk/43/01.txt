===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Next he brought me**- The word "he" refers to the man with the apppearance of bronze (40:3)
  * **eastern way**- This is the "eastern side" or "east side" (UDB)
  * **behold**- The word “behold” here adds emphasis to what follows.
  * **like the sound of many waters**- "like the crashing noise made by rushing waters" (See: [[:en:ta:translate:simile|Simile]])
  * **the earth shined with his glory**-This means, "the earth was bright because of his glory"
