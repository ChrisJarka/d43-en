===== Translation Notes: =====
God continuing to speak to the guards judging the people of Israel.

  * **in the outpour of your wrath**  - "as you pour out your great anger"
  * **fell on my face**  - "bowed down on the ground"
  * **Alas**  - The word "alas" is an expression showing dismay. Find such a word that expresses dismay in your language.
