====== Jeremiah Chapter 32 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What was happening when Yahweh came to Jeremiah in the tenth year of Zedekiah's reign?

A. The army of the king of Babylon was besieging Jerusalem and Jeremiah was imprisoned at the house of the king of Judah. [32:1-2]

Q? Why did Zedekiah imprison Jeremiah?

A. Zedekiah imprisoned him because Jeremiah prophesied that Jerusalem and Zedekiah would be captured by the hand of the Chaldeans. [32:3-5]

Q? What did Yahweh ask Jeremiah to do?

A. Yahweh asked Jeremiah to buy a field from Hanamel in Anathoth for seventeen shekels of silver. [32:6-9]

Q? What did Jeremiah decide to do about purchasing the field in Anathoth?

A. Jeremiah signed and sealed the deed of purchase, weighed the silver in the scales, and gave the sealed scroll to Baruch in he presence of the witnesses. [32:10-12]

Q? What did Jeremiah tell Baruch to do with the sealed scroll?

A. Jeremiah told Baruch to take the scrolls with the receipt of the purchase and place them in a new jar so they will last for a long time. [32:13-14]

Q? What message of hope did Yahweh want to give through this purchase of land?

A. Yahweh wanted all the people to know that houses, fields, and vineyards would be purchased again in this land. [32:15]
