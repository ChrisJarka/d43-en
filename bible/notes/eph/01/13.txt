\v 13 You Ephesians also heard the true message, the good message about how God saves you. People put their seal on something to show that it belongs to them. Similarly, when you believed in Christ, God showed that you also belong to him by sending you the Holy Spirit as he promised to do.
\v 14 The Holy Spirit is like a deposit. That is, he guarantees us that we will receive all that God has promised to give us, at the time when God will give to those who belong to him everything that Christ freed us to receive. God also assures you that you belong to him, in order that you would praise him for his greatness.
