===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I refers to Isaiah.
  * **I heard the voice of the Lord say**  – Change this phrase from passive to active-“The Lord said to me” (See: Active)
  * **Whom shall I send; who will go for us**  – I refers to Yahweh. Us refers to the trinity.
  * **Listen, but do not understand; see, but not perceive**  –" The people will hear the words and see the warnings, but they will not obey Yahweh"
