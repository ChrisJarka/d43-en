====== Psalms 103 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 103 is the first of three psalms of praise to  God. (Psalm 103-105)

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

God is good to us by forgiving sin and not punishing us as much as we deserve. His loving kindness goes on from generation to generation.
