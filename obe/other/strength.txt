====== strength, strengthen ======

===== Facts: =====

The term "strength" refers to being strong physically, emotionally, or spiritually. To "strengthen" means to make someone or something stronger. These terms can also have several other meanings.

===== Translation Suggestions =====

  * In general, the term "strengthen" can be translated as "cause to be strong" or "make more powerful."
