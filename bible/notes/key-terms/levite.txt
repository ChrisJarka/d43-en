====== Levite ======


===== Definition: =====


A Levite is a person who is a member of the Israelite tribe called Levi.


    * The Levites were responsible for the temple and religious ceremony.
    * All Jewish priests were part of the Levites.
    * They were set apart and dedicated to serving God in the temple.
