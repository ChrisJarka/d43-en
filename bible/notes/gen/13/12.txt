===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **lived**  - "settled" or "stayed"
  * **the land of Canaan**  - This could also be translated as "the land of the Canaanites."
  * **he set up his tents as far away as Sodom**  - This means either "Lot set up his tents near Sodom" or "Lot would encamp near Sodom".
