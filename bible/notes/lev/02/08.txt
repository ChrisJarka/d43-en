\v 8 And you shall bring the grain offering that is made of these things to Jehovah: and it shall be presented to the priest, and he shall bring it to the altar.
\v 9 And the priest shall take up from the grain offering the memorial thereof, and shall burn it upon the altar, an offering made by fire, of a sweet savor to Jehovah.
\v 10 And that which is left of the grain offering shall be Aaron’s and his sons’: it is a thing most holy of the offerings of Jehovah made by fire.
