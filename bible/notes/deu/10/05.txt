===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I turned**  - Moses turned
  * **came down from the mountain - ** Moses came down from Mount Sinai.
  * **put the tablets in the chest**  - Moses places the two tablets written by Yahweh inside the ark. This is the same ark that many years later would be placed inside Solomon's temple.
