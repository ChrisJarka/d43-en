===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **make you a desolation** - "Total destruction of Edom by Yahweh."
  * **So will I do to thee****-**"The Lord Jehovah will take everything away from Edom too."
  * **Mt  Seir and all Edom , all of it, shall know I am Jehovah - "**  The the people of Mt Seir and Edom will know that Jehovah is strong, in control, and is judging them for their wrongdoing."

Brought from chunk 13-15:
  * **When the whole earth rejoiceth I will make you desolate** - “Everyone will be happy when I, the Lord Jehovah , will destroy Edom.”
  * **You Edom did rejoice over the inheritance of the house of Israel because it was desolate** - “Edom was happy because Israel had nothing."
  * **So will I do to thee** - “the Lord Jehovah will take everything away from Edom too.”
  * **Mt Seir and all Edom, all of it, shall know I am Jehovah** - “the people of Mt Seir and Edom will know that Jehovah is strong, in control, and is judging them for their wrongdoing.”
