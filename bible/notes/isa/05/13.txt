===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **my people**  - refers to the nation of Israel chosen by God
  * **my people have gone into captivity**
 - "my people have been taken to other countries as slaves"
  * **for lack of understanding**  - "because they have no knowledge of Yahweh"
  * **death has made its appetite greater and has opened its mouth very wide**  - This phrase describes death as having a growing appetite. A large number of Israelites were dying.  It can be translated as "death is preparing to receive the large crowd of people dying in their sins" (See: [[:en:ta:translate:personification|Personification]])
  * **their elite, the people, their leaders, and the revelers and jubilant among them, descend into Sheol.**  - This statement shows that the people of Israel are becoming more sinful. "More and more people of Israel, their leaders, those who enjoy wild parties, those who sing and shout outloud, will be thrown into Sheol."
