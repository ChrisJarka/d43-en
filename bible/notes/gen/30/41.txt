===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **so that they might conceive among the sticks**  - "to increase the chances that they would conceive among the sticks"
  * **might conceive**  - "might breed" or "might mate"
  * **among the sticks**  - "in front of the sticks"
  * **the feebler animals**  - "the weaker animals"
  * **so the feebler animals were Laban's, and the stronger were Jacob's.**  - "So the feebler offspring belonged to Laban, while the stronger offspring belonged to Jacob." You can make this even more explicit: "So the feebler offspring did not have rings or spots and so belonged to Laban, while the stronger offspring did have rings or spots and so belonged to Jacob." (See: [[:en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]]).
