===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **I**  - The pronoun "I" in verses 9-10 refers to Paul. (See:[[en:ta:translate:singularpronoun]])
    * **your, you**  -  The pronouns "your" and "you" in verses 9-11 refer to the Philippian believers. (See:[[en:ta:translate:pluralpronouns]])
    * **that your love may abound**  - "that your love may grow more and more"
    * **in knowledge and complete understanding**  - "in knowledge and that you will be able to understand  clearly"
    * **I do this**  - "I pray this way"
    * **you may be sincere and without offense in the day of Christ.**  - "you may be honest and blameless until the day Christ returns."
    * **Also**  - "I also pray"
    * **Verse 11**  - See UDB.
