===== Important Terms: =====

  * Chaff
  * **Yahweh of Hosts**  = Yahweh, leader of the angel armies
  * fire

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The horde of your invaders will be like small dust, and the multitude of the ruthless ones as chaff that passes away. -**  This phrase expresses the weakness (like small dust) and insignificance (like chaff) of the invading army in the hands of God. (See: Metaphor)
  * **You**  –“ the invaders”
