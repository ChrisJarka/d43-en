====== compassion, compassionate ======

===== Definition: =====

Compassion is an emotion of feeling for others, like pity, mercy, concern, love, and kindness.  Compassionate describes someone with these feelings.

  * The Bible says that God is compassionate, that is, full of love and mercy.
  * While God is compassionate, He says in Exodus 33:19 that, "I will have compassion on whom I will have compassion."
