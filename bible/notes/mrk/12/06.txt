===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They will respect my son** - The owners thinks the farmers will show more respect to his son than to the servants and give him some of the grapes.  
  * **the tenants** - "the grape farmers who are leasing the vineyard from the owner"  
  * **Come, let us kill him, and the inheritance will be ours** - "if we kill the owner's son, then there will be no one to inherit the property when the owner dies, so the property will be ours.
