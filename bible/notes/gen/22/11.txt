===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Here I am**  - See how you translated this is [[en:bible:notes:gen:22:01|22:1]].
  * **Do not lay your hand upon the lad, nor do anything to harm him** - Both of these phrases mean basically the same thing: Do not hurt the boy in any way." God made sure that Abraham would hear and understand him. (See: [[en:ta:figuresofspeech:doublet]])
  * **seeing that you have not withheld**  - "because I see that you have not withheld"
  * **withheld your son…from me**  - "held back your son…from me" or "refused to give your son…to me"
  * **your son, your only son**  - "your only son." See how your translated this in [[en:bible:notes:gen:22:01|22:2]].
