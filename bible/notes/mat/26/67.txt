===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then they** - Possible meanings are "Then some of the men" or "Then the soldiers."
  * **spat in his face** - This was done as an insult.
  * **Prophesy to us** - Here "prophesy to us" means "tell us by means of God's power." It does not mean "tell us what will happen in the future."
