===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **those who are untaught **- AT: "those who lack experience" or "those who are young and naïve"
  * **she says**  - The woman who is talking is the foolish woman, introduced in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:09:13|9:13]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person|First, Second or Third Person]])
  * **those who have no sense**  - AT: "do not have wisdom" or "do not have common sense"
  * **Stolen waters … and bread eaten …**  - These two phrases basically mean the same thing; both are meant to strongly encourage stealing. At the same time they are a euphemism for sexual activity outside of marriage. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism|Euphemism]])
  * **the dead**  - "dead people" or "the ghosts of dead people"
  * **the depths of sheol**  - The depths here refers to the part of sheol where the unbelievers are suffering.
