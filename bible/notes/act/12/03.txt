===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **After he saw that this pleased the Jews- "**After Herod was aware that his actions pleased the Jews"
  * **he proceeded to arrest Peter also- "**Herod then gave orders to arrest Peter also"
  * **That was**- "This happened" or "Herod did this"
  * **After arresting him, he put him in prison- **"After Herod commanded soldiers to arrest Peter, Herod commanded soldiers to put Peter in prison"
  * **four squads of soldiers **- That is, four groups of soldiers (see UDB) . Each group has four soldiers that guard Peter one group at a time, they maybe in 3 hours shifts or six hours shift, two soldeirs at his side and two soldiers by the entrance.
  * **to guard him- **"to guard Peter"
  * **he was intending to bring him to the people**  - "Herod planned to judge Peter in the presence of the people" or "Herod planned to judge Peter in the presence of the Jewish people"
