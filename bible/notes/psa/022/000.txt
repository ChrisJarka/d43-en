====== Psalms 022 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 22 is a messianic psalm. It contains references to events during the crucifixion of Christ. It is also a psalm of individual lament and praise. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Christ was despised and taunted by his enemies; but finally people all over the earth will bow down to him.

This psalm is quoted in different places in the New Testament. Verse 1a is cited in Matthew 27:46; Mark 15:34 as the words of Christ on the cross; Many of the events in the narratives of the crucifixion are mentioned in this Psalm.
