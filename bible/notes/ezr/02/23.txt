===== Translation Notes: =====

The numbers of people in groups listed  -  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]] and [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])

  * **Anathoth**  - Translate the same way as in Joshua 21:18, 1 Kings 2:26, 1 Chronicles 6:60, 7:8, Nehemiah 7:27, 10:19, 11:32, Isaiah 10:30, Jeremiah 1:1, 11:21, 23, 29:27 and 32:7-9.
  * **Azmaveth**  - Translate the same way in 2 Samuel 23:31, 1 Chronicles 8:36, 9:42, 11:33, 12:3, 27:25, Nehemiah 7:28, 12:29.
  * **Chephirah**  - Translate the same way as in Joshua 9:17 and Nehemiah 7:29.
  * **Beeroth**  - Translate the same way as in Joshua 9:17, 18:25, 2 Samuel 4:2 and Nehemiah 7:29.
  * **Geba**  - Translate the same way in Joshua 21:17, Judges 20:33, 1 Samuel 13:3, 2 Samuel 5:25, 1 Kings 15:22, 2 Kings 23:8, 1 Chronicles 6:60, 8:6, 16:6, Nehemiah 7:30, 11:31, 12:29, Isaiah 10:29 and Zechariah 14:10.
