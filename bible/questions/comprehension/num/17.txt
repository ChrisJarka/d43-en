====== Numbers Chapter 17 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Yahweh say to Moses?

A. Yahweh instructed Moses to "Speak to the people of Israel. Take twelve staffs, one from each leader of each ancestor's tribe. Write each man’s name on his staff." [17:1-2]

Q? Whose name did Yahweh instruct Moses to place on the staff of Levi?

A. Yahweh instructed Moses to write Aaron’s name on Levi’s staff. There must be one staff for each leader from each ancestors’ tribe. [17:3]

Q? What will happen to the staff of the man that Yahweh has chosen from the twelve staffs in the tent of meeting in front of the covenant decrees, where Yahweh meets with the Israelites?

A. When Moses places the staffs in the tent of meeting in front of the covenant decrees, where I meet with you, It will happen that the staff of the man whom I choose will bud. I will cause the complaints from the people of Israel to stop, which they are speaking against you." [17:4-5]

Q? When Moses talked to the people of Israel, who gave Moses their staffs?

A. When Moses spoke to the people of Israel. All the tribal leaders gave him staffs, one staff from each leader, selected from each of the ancestral tribes, twelve staffs in all. Aaron’s staff was among them. [17:6]

Q? What happened to Aaron's staff when Moses deposited the staffs before Yahweh in the tent of the covenant decrees?

A. When Moses deposited the staffs before Yahweh in the tent of the covenant decrees, Aaron’s staff, for the tribe of Levi, had budded. It grew buds and produced blossoms and ripe almonds! Moses brought out all the staffs from before Yahweh to all the people of Israel. Each man found his rod and took it. [17:7-9]

Q? What did Yahweh instruct Moses to do that he obeyed?

A. Yahweh said to Moses, "Put Aaron’s staff in front of the covenant decrees. Keep it as a sign of guilt against the people who rebelled so that you may end complaints against me, or they will die." Moses did just as Yahweh had commanded him. [17:10-11]

Q? Why did the people of Israel tell Moses that they will die?

A. The people of Israel spoke to Moses and said, "We will die here. We will all perish! Everyone who comes up, who approaches Yahweh’s tabernacle, will die. [17:12-13]
