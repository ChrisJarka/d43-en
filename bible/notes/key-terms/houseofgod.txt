====== house of God ======


===== Definition: This term describes the place that God dwells; usually the tabernacle or temple in the Bible.  In modern usage this term corresponds to a building that is utilized for religious purposes. =====


Text


    * Other modern terms that are synonymous with House of God are "House of Prayer" or "House of Worship."
