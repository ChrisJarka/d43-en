===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses continues to address all the people of Israel.

  * **a land of** - "a land with" or "a land that has"
  * **barley**  - a grain used to make soups, stews, and bread
  * **pomegranates ** - a red fruit full of seeds that people can eat or use to make juice
