====== 1 Samuel Chapter 30 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did David and his men find when they came to Ziklag?

A. They found that the Amalekites had raided, attacked, burned it, and captured all the women and others and took them away. [30:1-2]

Q? How did David and his men react to what they found in Ziklag?

A. They raised their voices and wept until they could weep no more. [30:3-4]

Q? Why was David distressed?

A. Because his two wives were taken captive and the grieving people were talking about stoning him. [30:5-6]

Q? David turned to Yahweh for strength and prayed for direction. What did he want to know from Yahweh?

A. David wanted to know, if he pursued after the Amalekites would he overtake them. [30:8]

Q? What was Yahweh's answer to David?

A. Yahweh told him to pursue the troop, David would be successful, and he would recover everything that had been taken. [30:8]

Q? After David and his men kept pursuing the troop, who was found and what did they do for this person?

A. They found an Egyptian in a field, brought him to David, gave him food and water until he regained his strength. [30:9-12]

Q? What information did the Egyptian share with David?

A. That he was a young Egyptian, servant to an Amalekite who had left him behind, and he had been part of the troop that took the belongings from the places they had raided. [30:13-14]
