===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **all the curses** - This is a reference to the curses associated with disobeying the Law of Moses. 
  * **so that they might provoke me to anger with all the deeds they have committed-**- "andthey have made me angry with what they done" (See: Difficult words or phrases; see: UDB)
  * **therefore my anger will be poured out on this place, and it will not be extinguished**  - AT: "and I will punish them greatly until my punishment is complete" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
