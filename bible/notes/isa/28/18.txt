===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues to speak to the people of Jerusalem.

  * **Your covenant with death will be dissolved, and your agreement with Sheol will not stand** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "I will end the deal you think you have with death" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **covenant with death ... agreement with Sheol** - Possible meanings are 1) the leaders of Jerusalem used magic or sorcery to try and make an agreement with the gods of the place of the dead so that these gods would protect them from dying or 2) this is a metaphor that speaks of the leaders making an agreement with the leaders of Egypt. The leaders of Jerusalem were so confident that the Egyptians would protect that it was like they made an agreement with the gods of the place of the dead. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:28:14|28:15]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **will not stand** - "will not last"
  * **When the raging flood passes through, you will be overwhelmed by it** - The enemy soldiers attacking the people of Jerusalem is compared to a flood that comes through and drowns the people. AT: "When the enemy army comes like a flood, it will destroy you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **you will be overwhelmed by it** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "it will overwhelm you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **morning by morning** - This is an idiom. AT: "everyday" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **by day and night** - Here "day and night" mean "continually all day long." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
