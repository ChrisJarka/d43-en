===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connection Statement:** - Paul continues his instructions for what Titus is to teach the Cretans.

  * **Do not waste time on** - "So do not spend time listening to." Titus is to tell the Cretans not to waste time on these things.
  * **Jewish myths** - This refers to the false teaching of the Jews.
  * **turn away from** - "cause people to stop believing in"
