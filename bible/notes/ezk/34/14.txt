===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **v.14 "graze"**means to eat grass peacefully
  * **v.14-16–"I" and "my"**refer to Yahweh**. "They"**refers to the flock/the people of Israel.
  * **v.15–**"I myself": The word "myself" adds emphasis.
  * **v.15–"this is the Lord Yahweh's declaration."**  The word "declaration" emphasizes that Yahweh will certainly rescue His flock/Israel. An alternate translation is: The Lord Yahweh declares it.
  * **v.16 **  The injustices of verses 3 and 4 are reversed. The lost, the outcast, the broken, and the sick will be cared for by Yahweh/Shepherd and the fat and strong leaders destroyed.
  * **v.16 "Annihilate"**  means to destroy or wipe out.
