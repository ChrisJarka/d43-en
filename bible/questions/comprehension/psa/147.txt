====== Psalms Chapter 147 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why should Yahweh be praised?

A. God should be praised because it is good, and pleasant, and suitable to praise God. [147:1]

Q? How does Yahweh help his followers?

A. Yahweh rebuilds jerusalem, gathers the scattered people of Israel, heals the broke hearted and binds their wounds. [147:2-3]
