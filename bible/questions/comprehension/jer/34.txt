====== Jeremiah Chapter 34 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? When did Jeremiah receive the word from Yahweh?

A. Jeremiah received Yahweh's word when Nebuchadnezzar king of Babylon and all of his army were waging war against Jerusalem and her cities. [34:1]

Q? What did Yahweh say to Jeremiah?

A. Yahweh said, "I am about to give this city over into the hand of the klng of Babylon who will burn it and seize you" [34:2-3]

Q? What did Yahweh tell Zedekiah king of Judah?

A. Yahweh told Zedekiah, "You will not die by the sword, but you will die in peace and they will burn your body." [34:4-5]

Q? What happened when King Zedekiah established an agreement with the people in Jerusalem to proclaim freedom for all the Israelite servants?

A. The people freed all of their servants, but later changed their minds and enslaved their servants once again. [34:8-11]
