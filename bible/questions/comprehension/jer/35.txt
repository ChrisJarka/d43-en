====== Jeremiah Chapter 35 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Yahweh ask Jeremiah to do?

A. Yahweh asked Jeremiah to go to the clan of the Rechabites, speak with them, bring them to Yahweh's house, and there give them wine to drink. [35:1-2]

Q? How did the clan of the Rechabites respond to Jeremiah's offer of wine?

A. The Rechabites would not drink any wine because their ancestor, Jonadab, commanded them to drink no wine. [35:3-6]

Q? Why did Jonadab command the Rechabites to not build any houses, sow any seeds or plant any vineyards?

A. Jonadab gave them this command because they were to live in tents all their days. [35:7]

Q? How did the Rechabites respond to Jonadab's command?

A. The Rechabites obeyed his voice by never drinking wine or building houses to live in, but they went to live in Jerusalen when king Nebuchadneszzar attacked the land. [35:8-11]
