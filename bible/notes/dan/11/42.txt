===== Translation Notes: =====

The angel continued to explain to Daniel what was going to happen.

  * **He will extend his power** - He is the king of the North. AT: "The king of the North will extend his power"
  * **He will have authority** - He is the king of the North. AT: "The king of the North will have authority"
  * **Libyans** - people who live in Libya, a place near Egypt and Ethiopia
