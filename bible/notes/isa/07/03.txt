===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Isaiah…Shear-Jashub…Ahaz…Rezin…Pekah…Remaliah**  - These are names of men. (See: How to Translate Names) See how you translated most of these names in 7:1
  * **Shear-Jashub**  - The name of Isaiah's son means //a remnant will return.  // His presence was a constant reminder to Isaiah of Yahweh's faithfulness.
  * **conduit**  - This refers to an "aqueduct" which was a channel that brought water into the city.
  * **him**  - Ahaz
  * **two smoldering firebrands**  - This phrase refers to the kings Rezin and Pekah. (See: Personification)
