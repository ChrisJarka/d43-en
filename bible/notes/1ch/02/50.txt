===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Hur ... Shobal ... Salma ... Hareph** - These are all the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Ephrathah** - This is the name of a woman. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **father of Kiriath Jearim ... father of Bethlehem ... father of Beth Gader** - These are all the names of towns. The towns are metonyms for the people who live in the towns. AT: "founder of Kiriath Jearim ... founder of Bethlehem ... founder of Beth Gader" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
