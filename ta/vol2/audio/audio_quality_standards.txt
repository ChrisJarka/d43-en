===== Audio Quality Standards =====

This module answers the question: //What are the unfoldingWord standards for audio quality?//

Our goal, through collaborative effort of the global Church, is to provide accurate, high-quality, audio recording of Biblical content with broad listening appeal and longevity. Rather than being production-focused, we are striving for a simple excellence that faithfully communicates the message of His Word and His love for all people, languages and cultures.
