===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **for** - Paul explains why people need to hear the gospel.
  * **the wrath of God is revealed** - Alternate translation: "God reveals his anger." (See: Active/Passive)
  * **against** - "towards"
  * **<font 14px line-height: 15.6800003051758px; text-align: justify; text-indent: 17.5px;/Lato, sans-serif;;rgb(51, 51, 51) font-family: Lato, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 15.6800003051758px; text-align: justify; text-indent: 17.5px;;;inherit>all ungodliness and unrighteousness of</font> <font 14px line-height: 15.6800003051758px; text-align: justify; text-indent: 17.5px;/Lato, sans-serif;;rgb(51, 51, 51) font-family: Lato, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 15.6800003051758px; text-align: justify; text-indent: 17.5px;;;inherit>mankind</font> **- Alternate translation: "all the ungodly and unrighteous things that people do."
  * **hold back the truth** - Alternate translation: "they suppress (or hide) the true information about God."
  * **that which is known about God is visible to them** - Alternate translation: "they can know about God because of what they can see."
  * **for** - Paul shows why these people know things about God.
  * **God enlightened them** - Alternate translation: "God showed it to them."
