===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **How the mighty have fallen, and the weapons of war perished** - These two lines share similar meanings and emphasize that Israel's warriors are dead. AT: "How the mighty men have died in battle! There is no one left to wield the weapons of war!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **the mighty** - Here this phrase refers to Jonathan. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **have fallen** - This is a polite way to say "have died in battle." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **Jonathan is killed** - AT: "Jonathan has died in battle" or "The enemy has killed Jonathan" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
