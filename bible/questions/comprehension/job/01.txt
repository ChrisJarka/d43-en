====== Job Chapter 01 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? How is Job described?

A. He lived in Uz, he obeyed God, avoided evil things, had seven sons and three daughters, owned sheep, camels, oxen, and donkeys, and had many servants. He was the richest man in all the area east of the Jordan River. [1:1-3]

Q? After Job's sons held feasts with their brothers and sisters in their houses, what would Job do?

A. Job would summon them and ask Yahweh to purify them from any sins they may have. He would kill animals and offer them as sacrifices one for each of his children. [1:4-5]

Q? What did Yahweh say to Satan when the angels and Satan came and gathered together in front of him?

A. Yahweh asked Satan where he came from. Satan responded he had been traveling the earth to see what was happening. Yahweh asked Satan if he had noticed Job and the right way that Job was living and honoring him. [1:6-8]

Q? How did Satan respond to Yahweh about Job?

A. He told Yahweh that what he said was true, that Job honors him, that Yahweh protects him, his family, and everything he owns. But if Yahweh took away everything that Job would curse him in front of everyone. [1:9-11]

Q? Why did Satan think he could attack Job?

A. Yahweh gave Satan permission to take everything from Job, but not to harm him in his own body. [1:12]

Q? What did the messenger tell Job while they were feasting at Job's oldest son's home?

A. He said that a group of men came and attacked them when they were plowing in the field.  They killed all Job's servants in the field and took away all the oxen and donkeys.  He was the only one that escaped. [1:13-15]
