===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You, Son of man**  - All occurences of you/yourself/your refer to Ezekiel
  * **Son of man**  - The phrase "Son of man" refers to Ezekiel
  * **barber's razor**  - "blade for cutting hair"
  * **pass the razor over your head and your beard**  - "Pass the razor over your head and your beard" refers to shaving one's head and face.
  * **take balances to weigh**  - "use a scale weigh"
  * **in the midst of the city**  - Alternate translation: "in the middle of the city"
  * **I will draw out a sword after them**  - Alternate translation: I, God, will attack them.
  * **take a third of the hair**  - "take one of the three different piles"
  * **I will draw out a sword**  - "I" refers to God
