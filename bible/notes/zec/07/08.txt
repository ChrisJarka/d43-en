===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The word of Yahweh came to Zechariah**  - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:zec:07:01|7:1]].
  * **Let each man do this** - The word "this" refers to how a person should judge.
  * **widow**  - a woman whose husband has died
  * **orphan**  - a child whose parents have died
  * **sojourner**  - a traveler who lives in a foreign place for a short period of time
  * **plot** - a plan with evil intent
