===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **On that day-**  "On that day" refers to the time when many men died in battle.
  * **On that day the branch of Yahweh will be beautiful and glorious,**- This refers to a time in the future, "But some day, Israel will be very beautiful and great," (UDB)
  * **and the fruit of the land will be tasty and delightful for those survivors in Israel.**- "and the people who are still in Israel will enjoy the fruit of the land." (See: Parallelism)
