====== Psalms 040 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===
Psalm 40 is a psalm of praise. Many see it as a messianic psalm, a psalm about Christ.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

God is not interested in sacrifices but wants people to obey him and fulfil his plans for them.
