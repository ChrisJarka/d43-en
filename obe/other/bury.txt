====== bury, buried ======

===== Definition: =====

To bury is to place something in the earth under the surface of the soil.

  * It is a custom in many parts of the world to bury the dead in the earth in wrappings or a box called a coffin.
  * A cemetery is a parcel of land where the dead are buried.
  * In Romans burial is a symbol of death to the sinful self.  Immersion in baptism is a symbol of that burial.
