====== 2 Samuel 10 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

The last extension of David's kingdom was thrust upon him by the foolish act of the young king of Ammon (5-11). This chapter is also the beginning of a sub-section that deals with the war between Israel and Ammon (10-13).

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Nahash, the king of Ammon, had been good to David when David was hiding from Saul. So when he died, David sent comforters to his son. But his son thought they were spies and treated them shamefully. Then realizing that he had angered David, he hired mercenaries to fight Israel. The Israeli army defeated the mercenaries so they called for more troops. Again Israel defeated them, so they made peace with David and would not help out Ammon any more. 

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphor ==
The Ammon people used a [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] comparing themselves to a bad smell: "they had become a stench to David" when the realized they had made David very angry.
