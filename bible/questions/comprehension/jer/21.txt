====== Jeremiah Chapter 21 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Pashhur and Zephaniah ask Jeremiah?

A. They asked him to seek advice from Yahweh, hoping Yahweh will do miracles for them. [21:1-2]

Q? What is the message that Jeremiah give Zedekiah?

A. Yahweh says he will fight against them with a raised hand and with wrath, fury and great anger. [21:3-5]

Q? How exactly does Yahweh fight against King Zedekiah?

A. They will die in a severe plague, the sword and the famine and survivers will be given to Nebuchadnezzar King of Babylon and he will kill them with the sword. [21:6-7]

Q? What is the way of life that Yahweh offers?

A. They are to go out of the city and surrender to the Chaldeans. [21:8-9]

Q? What is the way of death?

A. Anyone who stays in the city will die by sword, famine, or plague and the king of Babylon will burn the city. [21:8-10]

Q? What is Yahweh's instructions to the king of Judah?

A. The king is to listen to the word of Yahweh, bring about justice and rescue the oppressed. [21:11-12]

Q? Who is Yahweh against?

A. Yahweh is against the inhabitants of the valley and the people on the rock of the plain. [21:13-14]

Q? What is Yahweh's declaration because of their practices?

A. He will light a fire in the thickets, and it will consume everything around them. [21:13-14]
