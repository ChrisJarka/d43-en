====== crown ======

===== Definition: =====

A crown is a circular ornamentation worn on the head of Kings, Queens, Princes.

  * Crowns normally are made of a precious metal and embedded with precious stones to produce a visible show of wealth.
  * Jesus' crown at the culmination of His first earthly ministry was a crown of thorns, given not as honor but an infliction of pain and disgrace.

(See also: other)
