===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he also took**  - The word "he" is the eagle in Ezekiel's vision.
  * **planted it in the seed-field** - "planted the seed in a field for growing plants" or "planted the seed in an agricultural field"
  * **He set it beside a large body of water** - "the eagle planted the seed in a place where there was a lot of water"
  * **planted it like a willow - ** - Willow trees grow in places where there is a lot of water. If the eagle planted the seed like a willow, it means that he planted it in a place with a lot of water. This repeats the concept of the previous phrase.   * **its branches turned toward him **- The branches of the vine turned towad the eagle.
  * **its roots grew under him**  - The roots of the vine grew under the Eagle.
  * **sent out shoots **- A shoot is a new growth sent out by a plant.
