====== kin, kinsman ======

===== Definition: =====

Someone's "kin" is that person's relative. The word "kinsman" refers specifically to a male relative.

  * The term "kin" can refer to a close relative such as a brother or sister, or to a more distant relative such as a cousin.
  * If a man died without children, his brother or a nearest relative was to marry his widow and raise up a son to live on his land and carry on his posterity in Israel.
