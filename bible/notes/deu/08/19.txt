===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses continues to address all the people of Israel.

    * **walk after other gods**  - "follow other gods" or "try to please other gods"
    * **and serve them**  - "and serve the other gods"
    * **and worship them**  - "and worship the other gods"
    * **I testify against you**  - "I warn you" or "I tell you in front of witnesses" 
    * **this day**  - "right now"
    * **you shall surely perish**  - "all of you will die" or "all of you will be destroyed"
    * **before you**  - "in front of you" 
    * **listen to the voice**  - "obey the commandments"
