===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **scarlet wool** - "red wool"
  * **sea cow** - "a large sea animal that eats plants"
  * **onyx** - "striped gemstone" The colors of this stone vary from white and black, white and brown and red
  * **precious**  - "valuable" or "treasured"
  * **stone**  - "gem"
  * **precious stones to be set** - AT: "precious stones for someone to set." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
