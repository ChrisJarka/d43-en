====== Andrew ======

===== Facts: =====

Andrew was the brother of Simon Peter, a follower of Christ, and one of the twelve apostles.

  * Peter and Andrew were mentioned as the first disciples of Jesus.
  * Jesus called them to follow Him while they were fishing near Capernaum located at the north cost of the Sea of Galilee.
  * Peter and Andrew were disciples of John the Baptist when they met Jesus earlier at Bethabara located in the area east of Bethany and the Jordan River (John 1:28-42).
