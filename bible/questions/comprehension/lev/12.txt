====== Leviticus Chapter 12 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Yahweh say to Moses about women who gave birth to a male child?

A. Yahweh said to Moses that a woman who gave birth to a male child would be unclean for seven days and on the eighth the child will be circumsized. [12:2,3]

Q? What must a woman do following her being unclean?

A. She will go through purification for thirty-three days and not enter the tabenacle or touch anything holy during this time. [12:4]

Q? What must a woman do diferently if she gives birth to a female child?

A. If a woman gives birth to female child she must be unclean for two weeks and go through purification for sixty-six days. [12:5]

Q? What must a woman do at the end of her purification?

A. At the end of her purification a woman must bring to the priest a one year old lamb for a burnt offering and a pigeon or dove for a sin offering. [12:6]

Q? What if the woman who gave birth to a child could not afford a lamb?

A. If the woman could not afford a lamb, she must bring two young pigeons or two doves for the burnt and sin offerings. [12:8]

Q? What will happen to the woman after she does these things?

A. The woman will be clean after the priest offers the offerings for her atonement. [12:8]
