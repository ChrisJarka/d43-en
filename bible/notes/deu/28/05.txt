===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Blessed will be your basket and your kneading trough** - "You will have plenty of fruit and grain" The basket represents the fruit it carried, and the kneading trough represents the bread which was prepared in it.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **kneading trough **- bowl used to knead bread
  * **Blessed will you be when you come in, and blessed will you be when you go out.**- To "come in" and "go out" refers to all of life's activities. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
