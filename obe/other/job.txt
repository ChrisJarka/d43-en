====== Job ======

===== Facts: =====

Job was a faithful man who lived in a time after the time of the Great Flood but before the time when Abraham lived on earth..

  * The book of Job in the Bible tells about Job's suffering due to many catastrophic events in his life, yet he maintained his faith in God through it all.
