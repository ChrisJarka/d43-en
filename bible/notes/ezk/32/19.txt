===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Who will you surpass in beauty? -**  "Who are you more beautiful than?"  Surpass means 'outdo', 'go beyond' 'improve on' or 'exceed.'
  * **Go down and lay with the uncircumcised! -**  "Go and be like the evil dead that have not followed the Lord Yahweh."
  * **They will fall in the midst of those slain by the sword! - **"You will die among the ungodly who were killed by the sword!"
  * **She is given to the sword; - **"Egypt uses war to fulfill it's goals;"
  * **they will seize her and her entourage! - **"however the ungodly will capture many of the people of Egypt!"
  * **The strongest of the warriors will declare to him from the midst of Sheol,-**  "The dead warriors that were strong will declare to the king from Sheol,"
  * **to his hero!-**
  * **‘They will go down! - **"they have come down to be with us in Hades!"
  * **They will lay with the uncircumcised who were slain by the sword!**  -"They have the same punishment as the ungodly who have been killed by the sword."
