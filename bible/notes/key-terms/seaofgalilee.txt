====== Sea of Galilee ======


===== Facts: =====


The Sea of Galilee is located in the Old Testament region of north Palestine called Galilee.


    * The Jordan River runs through it.
    * The towns of Capernaum and Bethsaida were located on the shores of the lake.
    * Another name given in the Old Testament is the Sea of Chinnereth.
    * Another name given in the New Testament is the Lake of Gennesaret.
    * The modern Hebrew name is Yam Kinneret,
