===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **commandment is like it **- "it" refers to the great and first commandment.
  * **You will love your neighbor as yourself**  - This is a quote within a quote from Leviticus 19:18.
  * **You will love **- In this case, “will” is used as a form of command, not a future expression.
  * **On these two commandments depend the whole law and the prophets**  - <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>This could be stated as "The whole law and the prophets depend on these two commandments."</font>
