====== Deuteronomy Chapter 25 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? How will a judge resolve a dispute between two men in court?

A. He will acquit the righteous and condemn the wicked to be beaten in his presence with the ordered number of blows to not exceed forty blows so the wicked will not be humiliated. [25:1-3]

Q? What safeguard did Yahweh institute so that the name of a man would not perish from Israel?

A The safeguard was that if two brothers lived together and one of them died without having a son, the wife of the dead man must not be married outside the family, but the husband's brother must take her as his wife so that the firstborn she bears will succeed in the name of that man's dead brother. [25:5-6]

Q? What allowance did Yahweh give to the people of Israel if the man's brother did not wish to take his brother's wife?

A. The allowance was that the brother's wife must go to the elders at the gate and inform them that he will not perform the duty of a husband's brother whereby the elders will gain his confirmation so that the brother's wife will in the presence of the elders take off his sandal spit in his face, and tell him that this is what is done when a man does not build up his brother's house. [25:7-10]
