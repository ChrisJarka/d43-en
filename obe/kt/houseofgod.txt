====== house of God ======


===== Definition: =====


In the Bible, the phrase "house of God" refers to the place where God resides or is worshipped. 

  * "House of God" is also used for the tabernacle or the temple.
  * This phrase is also sometimes used to refer to the people of God.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * It is possible to translate "house of God" as "temple" or "church," but these a "temple" is technically where sacrifices are offered and the Bible does not use "church" to refer to a place of worship.

(See also: [[tabernacle]], [[en:obe:kt:temple]], [[peopleofgod]])
