===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **truth, every man with his neighbor.**  - "truth to every person."
  * **Judge with truth, justice, and peace in your gates.**  - "Deliver judgments in your courts that are just and true, and that lead to peace."
  * **And let no one plot evil in your heart against his neighbor,**  - "Don't plot evil (or "Don't scheme") against anyone,"
  * **nor love false oaths; **- "or love to tell lies."
  * **for all these are things that I hate! '"—this is Yahweh's declaration.**  - "Yahweh declares (or "says"), "I hate all these things."'
