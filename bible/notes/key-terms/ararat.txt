====== Ararat ======

===== Facts: =====

The "land of Ararat" was a region of land located in the northeastern part of what is now the country of Turkey. It is most well-known as the name of the mountains that Noah's ark <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>came to rest on</font> <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>after the waters of the great flood began to recede.</font>
