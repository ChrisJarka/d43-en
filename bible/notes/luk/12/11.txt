===== Translation Notes: =====

    * (Jesus continues speaking to his disciples. He assures them that God will help them, so they should not afraid of people who might bring them to trial.)
    * **when they bring you**  - This could also be translated as  "when they bring you to trial" or "when people bring you to court."
     * **authorities**  - That is, "those who have authority over people." 
    * **will teach you**  - This could also be translated as, "will tell you."
    * **in that hour**  - That is, "at that time" or "then."
