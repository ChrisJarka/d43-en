===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Concerning diligence, do not be hesitant. Concerning the spirit, be eager. Concerning the Lord, serve him.** - "Do not be lazy in your duty, but be eager to follow the Spirit and to serve the Lord"
  * **Rejoice in the confidence you have about the future** - "Be happy because your confidence is in God"
  * **Be patient in your troubles** - AT: "Be patient when difficult times come"
  * **Keep on praying** - AT: "And remember to constantly pray"
  * **Share in the needs of the believers** - This is the last item in the list that began in [[:en:bible:notes:rom:12:09|12:9]]. "Concerning the needs of the believers, share with them" or "As for" or "When fellow Christians are in trouble, help them with what they need."
  * **Find many ways to show hospitality** - "Always welcome them into your home when they need somewhere to stay"
