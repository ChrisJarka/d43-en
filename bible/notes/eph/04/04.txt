===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **one body** - All believers in the family of God as like the various members of a human body. (See: [[;en:ta:translate:metaphor|Metaphor]])
    * **one Spirit** - This is referring to the Holy Spirit.
    * **called in one** - The word "called" could be translated "specially chosen" or "appointed" as suggested in the Key Term. 
    * **in one confident expectation** - This can be translated as "in one sure hope".
    * **your calling** - This phrase could be translated “your purpose” or “God's purpose for you” or “God's special work for you” as suggested in the Key Term.
    * **one Lord, one faith, one baptism** - 
    * **one God and Father of all, who is over all and through all and in all** - The word "all" could be translated as "everything".
