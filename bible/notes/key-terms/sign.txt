====== sign ======


===== Definition: =====


A sign is an object or event or action that is meant to communicate some meaning. It may be something that people give or something that God gives. It may also be something that God commands people to do.

    * God made the rainbow as a sign of his promise that he would never destroy the earth again by water.
    * God caused some of his prophets to do miracles as a sign that they were truly sent from God. 
    * God commanded the Israelites to circumcise their sons as sign of God's covenant with them.
    * When an angel told some shepherds that the Messiah was born, he told them that the sign they would see was an baby wrapped in cloths and lying in a feeding trough.
    * Jesus performed miracles as signs that he was truly the Messiah.
    * Judas kissed Jesus as a sign to the religious leaders that Jesus was the one that they should arrest. 
    * God validated the apostles' ministries by means of signs they performed.
    * Depending on its context, "sign" could be translated as: signal, symbol, mark, evidence, proof, or gesture.
