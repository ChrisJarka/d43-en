===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **in the hearing of the sons of Heth**  - "in the presence of the sons of Heth"
  * **of all those who enter the gate of his city**  - "and all those who had gathered at the gate of his city." This phrase refers to the important people who were allowed to make decisions for the city. The city gate was also where matters were settled and requests were heard.
  * **I give it to you in**** the presence of the sons of my people**  - "with my community as a witness, I give it to you"
  * **give**  - This type of giving could have been a land grant from a greater lord to a lesser one (Abraham). Such a gift would have put Abraham into subjection under Ephron.
  * **in the presence of**  - "in the sight of."
  * **I give it to you to bury your dead**  - "I give it to you, bury your dead."
