====== sanctuary ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "sanctuary" literally refers to a place that is sacred and holy. It also can refer to a place that provides protection and safety.

  * In the Old Testament, often the term "sanctuary" is used to refer to the tabernacle or temple. It especially refers to the structure where the "Holy Place" and "Most Holy Place" were located.
  * God refers to the sanctuary as the place where he lived among his people, the Israelites.
  * He also called himself a "sanctuary" or safe place for his people.
  * In the New Testament the reference is more broadly used to include both pagan and Jewish temples.
  * The New Testament describes the believer's body as a sanctuary for the Holy Spirit.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * Depending on the context, the term "sanctuary" could be translated as "holy place" or "sacred building" or "God's holy dwelling place" or "place of protection" or "place of safety"
