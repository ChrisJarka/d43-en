====== firstborn ======


===== Definition: =====


The firstborn refers to the initial offspring, whether of man and woman or of two animals. In humans, the term refers exclusively to the first male child born to the man and woman.


    * In biblical times, the firstborn is given a place of prominence and a double portion his family's inheritance
    * This concept can also be used figuratively.
