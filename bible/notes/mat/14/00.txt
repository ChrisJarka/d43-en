====== General notes about Matthew 14 ======


**Formatting in this chapter**\\

Verses 3-12 mention events that have previously occurred. Therefore, there is a break in the chronology of the narrative. Its purpose is explanatory and the translator may choose to set this section apart from the rest of the chapter to indicate a break in the narrative. Sometimes this is done with parentheses. 

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Irony ==

Herod believe that breaking an oath was sinful, and caused him to lack integrity, so he killed John the Baptist. Murder was a much greater sin and killing an innocent man showed that he lacked integrity. This is [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_irony|irony]].
