====== works ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Bible, the term "works" refers to what someone does, including what he thinks or says.

  * God's works are all the things he does or has done, including creating the world and other miracles.
  * A human being's works can be either good or evil.
  * The Holy Spirit empowers believers to do good works, which are also called "good fruit."
  * People are not saved by their good works; they are saved through faith in Jesus. 
  * On judgment day, everyone will give an account to God for their works. Those who are not trusting in Jesus to save and empower them to do good works will be condemned.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * Other ways to translate "works" might be, "deeds" or "actions" or "things that people do."
  * Make sure that the word used to translate this term does not exclude words and thoughts.
  * When referring to God's works, other ways to translate it could be, "wonderful miracles" or "mighty acts."

(See: miracle, wonder,sign, Holy Spirit, Spirit of God, Spirit of the Lord other)
