====== Psalms 058 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 58 is a deliverance psalm; asking God to deliver from the unjust rulers.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Although the leaders are very evil and unfair, God can deliver his people from their oppression.
