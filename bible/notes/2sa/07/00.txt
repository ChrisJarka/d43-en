====== 2 Samuel 07 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This is the finishing of the small interruption about the "House of God."

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

David wanted to honor God by building him a temple. God told that he could not build it but his son would build it. And God was causing David to have a dynasty that would go on forever. 

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Euphemism ==
God tells David that the temple will be built after David's death by using a [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]] for his death: "your days are fulfilled and you lie down with your fathers"
