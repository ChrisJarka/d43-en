===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **as Paul was about to be brought **- "When the soldiers were ready to bring Paul"
  * **the fortress **- The place where soldiers live. It is a military camp or building.
  * **the captain **- "a military officer of about 600 soldiers"
  * **Egyptian -**  Shortly before Paul's visit, an unnamed man from Egypt had launched a revolt against Rome in Jerusalem. Later he escaped "into the desert", and the commander wonders if Paul might be the same man.
  * **terrorists **- "persons who kill and harm others who disagree with the terrorist"
