====== 2 Samuel 24 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

David ordered the leaders of his army to count how many men of fighting age there were. God was displeased and offered David three punishments. David chose three days of epidemic. The angel of death was ready to strike Jerusalem; and David saw him and offered a sacrifice to God on the top of the hill in Jerusalem. Then the sicknesses stopped. David decided that is where his son should build God's temple.
