====== drunk ======

===== Facts: =====

To be intoxicated by alcoholic beverage.

  * The Bible tells believers not to be drunk with alcoholic drinks, but to be controlled by God's Holy Spirit.
  * In the Bible drunkenness is considered to be unwise, and corrupts good judgement.
