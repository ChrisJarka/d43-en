===== Translation Notes: =====

    * (Jesus continues to teach the crowd about the importance of obeying him.)
    * **foundation** - "base" or "support" 
    * **without a foundation** - This could also be translated as "but he did not build a foundation first."
    * **the torrent of water** - "the fast-moving water" or "river"
    * **flowed against** - "crashed against"
    * **collapsed** - "fell down" or "fell apart"
