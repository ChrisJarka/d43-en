====== face ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Bible, "face" is used literally to refer to the front of a person's head where the eyes, nose and mouth are. It can also be used in several figurative ways.

  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The "face of the waters" or the "face of the ground" refers to the "surface" of those objects.</font>
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The phrase "your face" can be used to mean "you."</font>
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>To "face each other" means that people or things are positioned in such a way that they are looking at each other or that the front of each of them is toward the other.</font>
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Being "face to face" means that two people are seeing each other in person, at a close distance.</font>
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The idiom "before his face" means "ahead of him" or "in front of him."</font>

(See also: other)
