===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the twenty-fourth day of the ninth month** - This is the ninth month of the Hebrew calendar. The twenty-fourth day is near the middle of December on Western calendars. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:hag:02:10|2:10]].
  * **the day that the foundation of Yahweh's temple was laid** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "the day that you laid the foundation of Yahweh's temple" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Is there still seed in the storehouse?** - Yahweh is preparing the people for the promise he is about to give them. This rhetorical question can be translated as a statement. AT: "You can see that there is no seed in the storehouse." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **The vine, the fig tree, the pomegranate, and the olive tree** - "Your grape vines, fig trees, pomegranate trees, and olive trees"
  * **the pomegranate** - This is a type of sweet fruit. You may need to make explicit that the tree is being spoken of. AT: "the pomegranate tree" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
