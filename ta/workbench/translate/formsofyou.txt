====== Forms of 'You' ======

==== Singular / Dual / Plural ====

Some languages have more than one word for ‘you’ depending on how many people are being spoken to. If they are speaking to one person, they use the ‘singular’ form of ‘you’. If they are speaking to more than one person, they use the ‘plural’ form of ‘you.’ Some languages have a ‘dual’ form for speaking to two people, and some have other forms for speaking to three or four people.

===== Formal / Informal =====

Some languages have more than one form of you depending on the relationship between the speaker and the person he is talking to. The formal form of 'You' is used when speaking to someone in authority or someone that a person does not know very well. The informal form is used when speaking to a peer or someone with less authority or a family member or close friend.
