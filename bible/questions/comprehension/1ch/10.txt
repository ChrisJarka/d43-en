====== 1 Chronicles Chapter 10 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What happened to the sons of Saul on Mount Gilboa?

A. The Philistines pursued the sons of Saul and killed them. [10:1-2]

Q? How did Saul die in the battle with the Philistines?

A. Saul was wounded by the Philistine archers and then fell on his own sword when his armor bearer refused to kill him with his sword. [10:3-4]
