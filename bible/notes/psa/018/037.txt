===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I smashed them so that they were unable to rise ** - AT: "I crushed them so that they were unable to rise"
  * **put strength on me like a belt for battle** - This is a word picture for a soldier preparing for battle by putting on the belt that holds his weapons. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
