===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **we have waited for him and he will save us** - "we trusted in him and he has rescued us"
  * **we will be glad and rejoice in his salvation** - "we should rejoice because he has saved us"
  * **for on this mountain the hand of Yahweh will rest** - "Yahweh will protect and bless Jerusalem" (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  * **Moab will be trampled down in his place** - "but he will crush the people in the land of Moab"
  * **even as straw is trampled down in the dunghill** - This is comparing Moab to straw that is trampled in the manure and left to rot. (See: [[en:ta:translate:simile]])
