===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **set your face**  - "turn toward"
  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh**- "This is what God says"
  * **Behold!**  - This can be translated as “Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you.”
  * **execute**- "to do or perform"
  * **I will be sanctified in her **- "I will show my holiness"
