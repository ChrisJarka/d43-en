====== 2 Kings 03 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

Story of Elisha continued 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

God makes the difference between defeat and victory. The armies of Israel, Judah and Edom united to attack Moab from the desert for failure to pay tribute. They ran out of water and so were in desperate condition. They asked Elisha what to do and he said God would give them water and victory over Moab.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Idiom ==

They described Elisha with the [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] "who poured water on the hands of Elijah" meaning who served Elijah.


=== Other possible translation difficulties in this chapter ===

"Anger came on Israel" It is not clear who was angry or why he/they were angry.
