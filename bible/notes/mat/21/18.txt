===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **nothing on it except leaves**  - The leaves and figs came about the same time of year, so Jesus had reason to expect some fruit for his hunger. There were leaves on the tree, but no fruit.
  * **the fig tree dried up **- the fig tree withered, all the life went out of it, and it died
