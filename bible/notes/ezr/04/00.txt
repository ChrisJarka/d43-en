====== Ezra 04 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

The story of the building of the temple and re-establishment of the temple worship continued (Ezra 3-6) 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

The people of the surrounding nations offered to help to build the temple. Because of Jewish exclusiveness and ethnic pride, the Jews refused their help. So the other people became their enemies and tried to hinder the work. They persuaded the king of Persia to stop the Jews building.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Idiom ==

"weakened the hands of the workers?" meaning they discouraged the workers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
