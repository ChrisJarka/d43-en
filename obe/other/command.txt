====== command, to command ======

===== Definition: =====

A command is a verbal or written statement that is intended to require action by an authority figure. To command is to be directed to follow the commandments or directives.

  * God commands us to obey His word, He is the ultimate authority figure.
  * In the New Testament God commands all men to repent and follow Jesus.
