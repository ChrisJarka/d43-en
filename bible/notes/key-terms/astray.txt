====== astray, go astray, led astray ======

===== Definition: =====

To go astray or be led astray is to walk away from the path set by Christ.

  * The foot path is symbolic of the Christian life, and is often also referred to as a Christian's "walk".
  * David mentioned this in Psalms, when he said, "Your word is a light to my path."
