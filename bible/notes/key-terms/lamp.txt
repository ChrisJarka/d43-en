====== lamp ======

===== Definition: =====

A lamp is a vessel containing a fuel source, usually oil, that could be ignited to give out light.

Text

  * Lamps were pottery pieces made from clay.
  * The lamp was a powerful symbol in scripture: the lamp symbolizing light and life as opposed to death and darkness.
