====== Jeremiah Chapter 32 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What was happening when Yahweh came to Jeremiah in the tenth year of Zedekiah's reign?

A. The army of the king of Babylon was besieging Jerusalem and Jeremiah was imprisoned at the house of the king of Judah. [32:1-2]

Q? Why did Zedekiah imprison Jeremiah?

A. Zedekiah imprisoned him because Jeremiah prophesied that Jerusalem and Zedekiah would be captured by the hand of the Chaldeans. [32:3-5]

Q? What did Yahweh ask Jeremiah to do?

A. Yahweh asked Jeremiah to buy a field from Hanamel in Anathoth for seventeen shekels of silver. [32:6-9]

Q? What did Jeremiah decide to do about purchasing the field in Anathoth?

A. Jeremiah signed and sealed the deed of purchase, weighed the silver in the scales, and gave the sealed scroll to Baruch in he presence of the witnesses. [32:10-12]

Q? What did Jeremiah tell Baruch to do with the sealed scroll?

A. Jeremiah told Baruch to take the scrolls with the receipt of the purchase and place them in a new jar so they will last for a long time. [32:13-14]

Q? What message of hope did Yahweh want to give through this purchase of land?

A. Yahweh wanted all the people to know that houses, fields, and vineyards would be purchased again in this land. [32:15]

Q? How did Jeremiah pray to show his confidence in Yahweh?

A. Jeremiah prayed, "Yahweh, you alone have made the heavens and the earth by your great strength and with your raised arm, for nothing you say is too difficult for you to do." [32:16-17]

Q? What qualities of Yahweh does Jeremiah reveal to the people?

A. Jeremiah says that Yahweh shows covenant faithfulness, is great in wisdom, mighty in deeds and was able to bring the people of Israel out of the land of Egypt. [32:18-21]

Q? What did Yahweh do for the people of Israel?

A. Yahweh gave them possession of the land which he had sworn to give to their ancestors. [32:22-23]

Q? Why did Yahweh bring disaster upon the people of Israel?

A. Yahweh brought disaster upon them because they did not obey his voice or live in obedience to his law. [32:23]

Q? What did Yahweh ask Jeremiah to do when the city of Jerusalem was being given into the hand of the Chaldeans?

A. Yahweh told Jeremiah that he should purchase a field for himself with silver and have witnesses witness it. [32:24-28]

Q? Why did Yahweh allow the Chaldeans to fight against and set fire to the city of Jerusalem?

A. Yahweh allowed this to happen because of all the wickedness the people of Israel and Judah had done to provoke him. [32:29-32]

Q? What did the people of Israel do to provoke Yahweh?

A. The pepole of Israel turned their backs on Yahweh, placed detestable things in the house of Yahweh, and buit shrines ior Baal in order to sacrifice their sons and daughters. [32:33-35]
