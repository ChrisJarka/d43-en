====== neighbor ======

===== Definition: =====

A neighbor is a person who lives nearby.

  * Old Testament usage of the idea of "neighbor" as "brother" or fellow member of the covenant community.
  * The last six commandments focus on relationship with others, that is, one's neighbors.
  * An example of defining one's neighbor is given in the New Testament parable of the Good Samaritan.

(See also: other)
