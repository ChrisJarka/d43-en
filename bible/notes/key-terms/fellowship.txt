====== fellowship ======

===== Definition: =====

Fellowship is the friendly relationship of a group of people who share similar interests, experiences, or views.

  * Christian fellowship is a shared relationship that believers have with one another through their relationship with Christ and the Holy Spirit.
  * Fellowship between God and a person begins when that person professes trust in Christ and accepts God's gift of salvation through faith.
  *
