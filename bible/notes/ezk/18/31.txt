===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Your/You **- These pronouns refer to the "house of Israel" or the people of Israel.
  * **Cast away all of your transgressions​**  - The phrase "cast away" means to "throw off or away."
  * **and make a new heart and a new spirit for yourselves​**  - This phrase refers to an internal change that needs to take place in the inner part that thinks, feels, and decides. "and get a new way of thinking" (UDB)
  * **declaration**  - See how you translated this in v. 30.
