===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Truly, I say to you**  - Jesus used this expression to stress the importance of what he is about to say.
  * **it is easier for a camel to go through a needle's eye- **  a camel is too large to fit through a needle's eye, and it is also hard for a rich person to give everything away(See Hyperbole and Metaphor)
  * **camel - **  This is a very large animal, larger than a cow or a horse.
  * **needle's eye **- a needle's eye is the hole in a sewing needle through which the thread is passed
