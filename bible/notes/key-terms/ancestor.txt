====== ancestor ======


===== Definition: =====


An ancestor is a person who was born and lived before others in their family


    * An ancestor lived at least one generation before their descendants
    * A descendant is a person who is born after their ancestor


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:descendant|descendant]])
