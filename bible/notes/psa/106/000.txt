====== Psalms 106 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===


Psalm 106 is the fourth of five psalms of praise to  God. (Psalm 103-107)

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Israel continually sinned in the desert but God took them on to the Promised Land.
