====== Edom, Edomite ======

===== Facts: =====

<font 14px line-height: 1.4/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The Edomites were the descendants of Esau, who was also known as Edom.</font>

  * The word for Edom means "red," which may refer to the fact that Esau was covered with red hair when he was born. Or it may refer to the red lentils that Esau traded his birthright for. Interestingly, much of the soil of the land of Edom was also of a red color.
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The country or kingdom of Edom</font>    <font 14px line-height: 1.4/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>was mountainous and was located to the south and east of Israel.</font>
  * Other names for the country of Edom were "Idumea" and "Mount Seir."
  * In the Old Testament, the country of Edom is often mentioned as an enemy of Israel.
  * Old Testament prophets spoke negative prophecies against Edom;
