===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh** - See how you translated this in 5:5.
  * **They will eat their bread with trembling and drink their water with shaking** - “to shake” or “to vibrate” This is referring to being so afraid that one's body, or hands, would shake while they ate or drank. (See: doublet).
  * **cities that were inhabited will be desolate** - "active cities with residents will be destroyed" or "uninhabited" like "ghost towns"
  * **land will become a wasteland** - "land will be turned into a land with nothing" or "land we become a desert"
