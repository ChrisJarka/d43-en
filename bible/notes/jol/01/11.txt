===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you farmers… you vinedressers**  - The word "you" refers to these specific groups of people within the nation of Israel. (See:[[:en:ta:translate:formsofyou|Forms of 'You']])
  * **barley** - This is a type of grass.  The seeds of barley can be used to make bread.  It is similar to wheat but produces seeds during warmer months instead of the cooler months like wheat. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **the harvest of the fields has perished…The vines have withered…All the trees of the field have withered...joy has withered away from the descendants of mankind** - The word "wither" refers to a plant that has dried up and died.  These phrases are listing things that have died the land of Israel because of lack of water.  The joy of the children in Israel is listed as something that has dried up like all the people's sources of food. Alternate translation: "all the plants that produce food have dried up and died, the joy of the children in Israel has decreased to nothing" (See:[[:en:ta:translate:parallelism|Parallelism]])
