===== translationNotes: =====

  * **bowed his head with his face to the ground ... fell down before Yahweh** - There acts of worship that express humility and submission to God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **All Judah** - Here "Judah" refers to the people from Judah. AT: "All the people of Judah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Kohathites and Korahites** - These are the names of people groups descending from Kohath and Korah. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **with a very loud voice** - This speaks of all the people praising Yahweh as if they together had one voice. AT: "with very loud voices" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
