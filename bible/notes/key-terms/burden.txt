====== burden ======

===== Definition: =====

To say that someone has a burden means that there is a heavy responsibility or duty for that person to bear.

  * To place a burden on someone is to give him a responsibility or duty.
  * The burden can be removed from someone by taking away a responsibility or duty.

(See also: other)
