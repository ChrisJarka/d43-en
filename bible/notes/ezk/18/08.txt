===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **who - ** Ezekiel continues to describe the actions of the righteous man.
  * **interest**  - This word refers to the money paid by a person to use borrowed money.
  * **he turns his hand ****to **- "he performs" or "he attends to"
  * **establishes truth between men- **  This means he determines what is right between men, that is, which man is telling the truth.  It could also mean that he speaks wisely if there is a dispute.
  * **to act truthfully**  - This means to behave according to truth (faithful to God's law).
  * **this is the Lord's declaration - **  This is what the Lord stated clearly.
