====== The Kind of Judah Was Punished [20-08] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-20-08.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

**To [[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:punish|punish]]** the **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:king|king]]** of **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:kingdom-of-judah|Judah]]** for rebelling, **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:nebuchadnezzar|Nebuchadnezzar's]]** soldiers killed the king’s **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:son|sons]] in front of him** and then **made him blind**. After that, they took the king away to die in prison in **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:babylon|Babylon]]**.


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **To punish** - Nebuchadnezzar punished the king of Judah by telling his soldiers to do these things.
  * **in front of him** - This may be translated as, "where he could see," or "so that he could see it," or “before his eyes.” 
  * **made him blind** - This may also be translated as, "destroyed his eyes."
