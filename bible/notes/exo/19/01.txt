===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **In the third month** - This is about the middle of May on western calendars. (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaltimemonth]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **had gone out from** - AT: "had left"
  * **Rephidim** - This is an area on the edge of the wilderness of Sinai where the people of Israel had been camping. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
