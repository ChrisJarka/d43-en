====== Luke ======

===== Facts: =====

Luke was a co-worker with and helper of Paul in his missionary journeys.
    * Luke is said to have been born in Antioch.
    * It is thought that Luke was a physician by trade.
    * He helped Paul in his missionary work.
    * He wrote the Gospel of Luke and the book of Acts.
    * Luke is mentioned is several of Paul's letters.
