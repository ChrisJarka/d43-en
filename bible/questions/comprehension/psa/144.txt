====== Psalms Chapter 144 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who trains David's hands for war and his fingers for battle?

A. Yahweh trains David's hands for war and his fingers for battle. [144:1]

Q? How does Yahweh help David?

A. Yahweh is David's covenatn faithfulness, his fortress, David's high tower, the one who rescues David, his shield, the one in whom he takes refuge, and the one who subdues nations under him. [144:2]

Q? Who is David amazed that Yahweh has noticed and thinks about?

A. David is amazed that Yahweh notices man. [144:3]

Q? What things does David list about Yahweh?

A. Yahweh causes the sky to sink and come down, touches the mountains and makes tehm smoke, sends flashes of lightning and scatters David's enemies, Yahweh also shoots arrows to bring confusion to his enemies. [144:5-6]
