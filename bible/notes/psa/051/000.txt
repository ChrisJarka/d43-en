====== Psalms 051 General Notes ======


=== Kind of  psalm ===

Psalm 51 is a psalm of repentance from sin.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

He was truly sorry fo having sinned. He repented and God forgave his sins. God wants true sorrow for having sinned, not sacrifices.
