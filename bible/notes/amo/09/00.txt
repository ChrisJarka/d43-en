====== Amos 09 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

The last chapter is written in poetic form and continues to show the awesome and terrible judgment of Yahweh on his people. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Place Names ==
You will notice that this chapter refers to various places inside or outside the land of Israel. Please review [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]. 

As in other chapters, the phrase "the declaration of Yahweh" is used. This phrase is used to highlight what God is proclaiming. Please make sure that you are consistent in translating this phrase throughout the book.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

Text

=== Other possible translation difficulties in this chapter ===

As in other places in the book, there are place names used from various neighboring countries and places inside the kingdoms of Israel and Judah.
