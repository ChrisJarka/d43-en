===== translationNotes: =====

  * **stirred up against Jehoram the spirits of the Philistines and of the Arabians** - AT: "caused the Philistines and Arabians to become very angry with Judah"
  * **of the Philistines and of the Arabians who were near the Ethiopians** - The phrase "who were near the Ethiopians" refers only to the Arabians. The Philistines were located on the other side of Israel. 
  * **No son was left to him except Jehoahaz, his youngest son** - "They took away all of his sons except Jehoahaz, his youngest son" or "The only son they left to him was Jehoahaz, his youngest son"
