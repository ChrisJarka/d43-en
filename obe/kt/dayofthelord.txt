====== day of the Lord ======

===== Description: =====

The phrase "day of the Lord" refers to a time when there will be a final judgment of all who have rebelled against God.

  * The "day of the Lord" may refer to a moment in time or it may be more general and refer to a longer period of time.
  * This final judgment on the day of the Lord will include destruction of the earth. The resurrection of the righteous and the unrighteous will also happen at this time.
  * This final time of judgment and resurrection is also known as the "last day". For example, Jesus promised his followers that he would raise them to life "on the last day".

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * Depending on the context, other ways to translate "day of the Lord" could include, "time of the Lord" or "moment when the Lord Jesus returns" or "time when the Lord Jesus will return as judge."
