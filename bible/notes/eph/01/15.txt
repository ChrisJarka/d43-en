===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Because of this** - “For this reason”.​
  * **those set apart for him** - “those who are morally blameless” or “those consecrated” or “the holy ones”. 
  * **him** - This pronoun refers to the Lord Jesus. This word is often translated as “saints”.
  * **I have not stopped thanking God** - This is a hyperbole meaning "I thank God often". (See: [[en:ta:translate:hyperbole]])
  * **you** - This pronoun is inclusive. it includes all believers in the church at Ephesus. (See: [[en:ta:translate:exclusive]])
