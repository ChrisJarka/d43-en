===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I** - Here the word "I" refers to Abraham's servant.
  * **Perhaps the woman will not follow me.** - This is hypothetical situation that could possibly happen. You could translate it as "What if the woman will not come back with me?" (See: [[en:ta:translate:hypothetical]])
  * **before whom I walk** - "whom I serve"
  * **he will prosper your way**  - "he will make your journey successful"
  * **family line** - "family"
  * **But you will be free … if … relatives**  - "Then you will be free … when … relatives."
  * **But you will be free from my oath if you come to my relatives and they will not give her to you. Then you will be free from my oath.** -"But, if my relatives will not give her to you, then you are free from my oath." This is a hypothetical situation that Abraham does not think is likely.
