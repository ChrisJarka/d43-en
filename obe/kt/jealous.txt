====== jealous, jealousy ======

===== Definition: =====

Jealousy is a very strong desire to protect the purity of a relationship; as in God toward man or a man toward his wife.

  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>When used in the Bible jealousy refers to God's strong desire that His bride remains pure and unstained by sin, or that His name be kept pure amongst His people.</font>
  * Jealousy can also describe the feeling that a spouse has for their partner who is being unfaithful or perceived to being unfaithful, in the marriage relationship.
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Jealousy is not the same as envy; envy is a strong desire to possess what someone else possesses.</font>
