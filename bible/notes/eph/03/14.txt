===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For this cause** - "Because God has done all this for you" (See: [[:en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]]) This goes back to the words in Ephesians 3:1 which refers to the Jews and Gentiles becoming one body in Christ.
  * **I bow my knees to the Father** - "I bow my knees in prayer to the Father" or "I humbly pray to the Father" (See: [[:en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]])
  * **that he would grant you** - "that he would give you".
  * **who is in you** - The word "you" refers to all the believers in Ephesus. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:formsofyou]])
