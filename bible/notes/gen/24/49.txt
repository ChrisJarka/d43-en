===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now therefore** - "Now" (UDB).
  * **you** - Here the word "you" is plural and refers to Laban and Bethuel.
  * **loyalty**  - "graciousness."
  * **faithfulness **- "trustworthiness" or "truthfulness."
  * **But if not, tell me, so that I may turn to the right hand, or to the left."**  - "But if not, tell me, so that I may keep looking."
