====== redeem, redemption ======


===== Definition: =====


To redeem is to purchase something at a price. In the Bible, believers are redeemed by a payment that God provides. Redemption is the act of redeeming.


    * Because of sin, humans are condemned to eternal punishment and separated from God. The death of Jesus paid the complete payment (redemption) for sin.
    * A first born animal was normally set aside for slaughter as an offering to God, but could be purchased (redeemed) by paying a set price to a priest under the Law of Moses.
    * Jesus's blood is the only payment by which a human can be redeemed to God and have eternal life in his presence.
