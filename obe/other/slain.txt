====== slay, slain, to slay, slayed ======

===== Definition: =====

To slay is to kill by force.

  * The Bible says that "Abraham took out his knife to slay his son Isaac" but the angel of the Lord stopped him.
  * The Prophet Ezekiel said that God would slay those who worhip idols.
  * Slain are those who have been killed, slayed is past tense of slay.
