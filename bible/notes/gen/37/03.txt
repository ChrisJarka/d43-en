===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **a luxurious tunic that reached to his hands and ankles**  - "a striped tunic" or "a tunic with long sleeves."
  * **tunic**  - "outer garment."
  * **ULB Genesis 37:3 Israel**  - Jacob's new name given by God
  * **ULB Genesis 37:3 he was**  - "Joseph was"
  * **ULB Genesis 37:3 he made**  - "Israel made"
  * **ULB Genesis 37:3 his sons… his old**  - "Israel's sons… Israel's old"
  * **ULB Genesis 37:3 his hands**  - "Joseph's hands"
  * **ULB Genesis 37:4 his**  - "Joseph's"
  * **ULB Genesis 37:4 him**  - "Joseph"
