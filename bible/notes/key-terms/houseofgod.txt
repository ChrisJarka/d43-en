====== house of God ======


===== Definition: =====


The place where God dwells can be called the house of God.


    * In the Bible, the "house of God" usually refers to the tabernacle or temple.
    * The "house of God" is also used in reference to the "people of God".


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:tabernacle|tabernacle]], [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:temple|temple]])
