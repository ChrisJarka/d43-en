====== proclaim ======

===== Definition: =====

\\
To proclaim is to announce something publicly and boldly.

  * Often in the New Testament, proclaiming has to do with teaching the good news about Jesus to many people in many different cities and regions.
  * Other ways to translate "proclaim" could include "openly preach (the word of God)" or "publicly declare (the good news about Jesus)".

(See also: other)
