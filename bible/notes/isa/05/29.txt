===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Their...they** - This refers to the soldiers from the far away nation in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:05:26]]
    * **Their roaring will be like a lion, they will roar like young lions. They will roar and seize the prey** - This shows that the fierceness and cruelty of the invading force will be like the roar of lions as they seize their prey and drag it away.   (See: [[:en:ta:translate:simile]])
