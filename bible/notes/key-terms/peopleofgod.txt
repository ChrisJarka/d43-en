====== People of God, His People, God's People ======


===== Facts: =====


God's people are the group of people who were born of the seed of Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob.


    * Jacob was given a new name, Israel, in the Bible. He had 12 children; each of these children became the head of the 12 Tribes of Israel, from which all Jews have come.
