===== translationNotes: =====

  * **invitations** - These invitations were either verbal messages that the maids were supposed to recite, or they were written letters addressed to the people whom Wisdom wanted to invite.
  * **her maids** - Young women or girls, in the service of a respectable, adult woman, such as Wisdom.
  * **she calls out** - "she proclaims" or "she summons" AT: "she loudly recites her invitation"
  * **Who is naive ... the one lacking good sense** - These two phrases describe the same group of people, namely people who need more wisdom in their lives. 
  * **naive** - "inexperienced, immature"
  * **turn aside here** - "leave your path and come into my house"
