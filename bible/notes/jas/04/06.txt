===== translationNotes: =====

  * **But God gives more grace** - How this phrase relates to the previous verse can be made explicit: "But, even though our spirits may desire what we cannot have, God gives us even more grace, if we will humble ourselves." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **God resists** - "God opposes"
  * **the proud** - "proud people"
  * **the humble** - "humble people"
  * **So** - "Because of this"
  * **submit to God** - "obey God"
  * **you** - Here this pronoun is plural and refers to James' audience. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **Resist the devil** - "Oppose the devil" or "Do not do what the devil wants"
  * **he will flee** - "he will run away"
