===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **things that are held as valuable** - "things which people usually regard as valuable" (See: Active/Passive)
    * ** is low and despised ** - This means the 'rejected'.
    * **to boast**- This means 'to talk about himself or herself in a proud way.'
    * **He...him**  - This is a pronoun referring to God
