<usfm> \v 12 Then he must cut it into pieces, with its head and its fat, and the priest will lay them in order on the wood that is on the fire, which is on the altar, \v 13 but the inner parts and the legs he must wash with water. ===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **cut it into pieces**  - AT: "cut the animal for sacrifice into pieces"
  * **priest will lay them**  - The pronoun 'them' refers to the pieces of meat.
  * **made to him by fire**  - AT: "made to him in the fire"
