===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you barren woman** - Here Yahweh is comparing the people of Jerusalem to a barren woman. (See: Metaphor)
  * **you who have not given birth** - This too is a comparison of the people of Jerusalem to a woman who has never given birth to children. (See: Metaphor)
  * **you who have never been in birth labor** - Yahweh, says that they are like a childless woman who has been abandoned by her husband, but will have more children now than the married woman. (See: Metaphor)
