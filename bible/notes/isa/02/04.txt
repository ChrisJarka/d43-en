===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He will judge** - "Yahweh will judge"
  * **will render decisions** - "will solve disputes"
  * **they will hammer their swords into plowshares, and their spears into pruning-hooks** - The nations will turn weapons of war into tools for farming. A "plowshare" is a blade that digs into soil. A "pruning-hook" is a knife used for cutting off branches from a plant. Alternate translation: â€œthey will make their swords into plow blades and their spears into pruning knives."
  * **lift up sword** - Alternate translation: "fight."
