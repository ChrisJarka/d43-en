======  Matthew 24 General Notes ======


=== Structure and Formatting ===

This chapter can be set apart from other sections because Jesus begins to [[:en:obe:kt:prophet|prophesy]] about the future. A new title or heading can be helpful for the reader.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== End times ==
This chapter seeks to answer the question: how will we know when Christ returns at the end times? The teachings of this chapter will be difficult to understand without a translation of many other books like Daniel and Revelation.

== Noah ==
In the days of Noah, there was a great flood which God sent as punishment for the [[:en:obe:kt:sin|sins]] of man. There was many warnings about this coming punishment but the punishment began suddenly. This is the comparison being made by Jesus.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphors ==

There are many [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|metaphors]] in this chapter to describe the future. Some scholars understand this entire chapter to be an extended metaphor. It is best not to take this into account when translating.

=== Other possible translation difficulties in this chapter ===

== "Let" ==
This word is often used in the ULB to indicate a command or strong request. There are many different ways to form a command. Therefore the translator has some freedom in determining how to translate the concept created by the word "let."
