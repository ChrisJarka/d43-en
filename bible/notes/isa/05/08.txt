===== Translation Notes: =====

Isaiah announces God's judgment.

  * **to those who join house to house, who join field to field**  - "to those who take more and more houses, and who take more and more fields"
  * **The Lord of Hosts told me, Many houses…yield but an ephah**  - This is an indirect quote. Alternate translation: "The Lord of Hosts told me, 'Many houses…yield but an ephah.'" (See: [[:en:ta:translate:quotation|Speech Quotations]])
  * **10 acres**  - An "acre" is the amount of land that a team of two oxen could plow in one day.
  * **one bath**  - "22 liters of wine" (UDB)
  * **one homer of seed will yield but an ephah**  - An "ephah" is a basket. A homer equals ten ephahs or ten baskets.
