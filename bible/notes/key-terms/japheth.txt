====== Japheth ======

===== Facts: =====

Japheth was the youngest of Noah's three sons who are mentioned in the Old Testament.

  * Along with the rest of Noah's family, Japheth and his wife were kept alive in the great ark when the worldwide flood covered the whole earth.
