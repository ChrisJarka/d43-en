===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[:en:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **For the chief musician** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:004:001|4:1]].
  * **A psalm of David** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:003:001|3:1]].
  * **rescue me**  - This is a request. AT: "Please save me"
  * **preserve me**  - This is a request. AT: "keep me safe"
  * **Their tongues would like serpents** - "Their tongues can be very destructive" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]] and [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **vipers' poison is on their lips** - A viper is a poisonous snake capable of killing a person.  AT: "their lips have deadly poison"
  * **selah** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:psa:003:001|3:2]].
