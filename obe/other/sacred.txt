====== sacred ======

===== Definition: =====

Sacred refers to something that is elevated or revered for it's connection to God or a deity.

  * Scripture is sacred because it is God's word.
  * In the Old Testament Israel corrupted themselves by worshipping sacred objects from pagan religions.
  * Sacred objects do not necessarily have an intrinsic value of their own, but are assigned value in relation to deity.

(See also: other)
