===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **"Now will I arise," says Yahweh; "now I will be lifted up; now I will be exalted** - Yahweh is saying the same thing in several ways for emphasis. This can be translated as, "Now I will arise and show that I am very powerful." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **You conceive chaff, you give birth to stubble** - This is comparing the plans of the people of Assyria to chaff and straw that is useless and of no value. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **The peoples will be burned to lime, as thorn bushes cut down that are burned in the fire** - This is comparing the burning of people to thorn bushes that are cut down and burned in the fire. This can be translated as, "Your people will be burned until only ashes remain like thorn bushes are cut down and burned up in a fire." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
