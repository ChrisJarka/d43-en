===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh is speaking to the house of Israel.
  * **will summon the grain** - This phrase describes the grain as a person who is a servant of Yahweh. Alternate translation: "will cause there to be grain in the land of Israel." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **multiply it** - "and will cause there to be very much grain"
  * **put famine upon you** - "cause you to suffer because of a famine"
  * **put famine upon you** - "the food that grows in the countryside"
  * **you will no longer bear the shame of famine among the nations** - "the nations will no longer make you feel ashamed because you suffer from famine" 
  * **your wicked ways** - "the wicked manner in which you lived"
  * **you hate yourelves** - This can be translated as "you show loathing on your face." See how you translated "Then they will show loathing on their face" in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:06:08|6:9]].
  * **disgusting deeds** - See how you translated "disgusting actions" in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:05:09|5:9]].
