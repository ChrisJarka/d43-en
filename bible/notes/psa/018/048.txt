===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I am set free** - AT: "God has set me free" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **lifted me above** - This is a picture of the Psalm writer's position (not his physical position), a picture of God's giving him more honor than his enemies.
  * **who rose against me** - “who rebelled against me”
  * **violent men** - "cruel men" or "savage men"
