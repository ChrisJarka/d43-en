===== translationNotes: =====

  * **give true allegiance to his son** - "Show his son that you are truly loyal to him"
  * **when his anger quickly ignites** - The king's anger is spoken of as if it were a fire that could be started. AT: "when he suddenly becomes very angry." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **take shelter in him** - Asking the king for protection is spoken of as taking shelter in him. AT: "ask the king to protect them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
