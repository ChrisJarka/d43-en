===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Let me fall into the hand of Yahweh rather than into the hand of man** - Here "hand" represents power to harm or punish Israel. AT: "Let me be punished by Yahweh, rather than be punished by people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Let me fall** - The people of Israel are the ones who would die from the plague, but David is personalizing this judgment as if he himself was being killed.
  * **hand of man** - Here "man" is used in the generic sense of "people."
  * **his merciful actions are very great** - "Yahweh is very merciful"
  * **on Israel** - Here Israel is a metonym that represents the people of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **seventy thousand people died** - "70,000 people died" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **changed his mind about the harm** - Here "mind" represents his decision. AT: "decided not to destroy Jerusalem" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Enough!** - This exclamation means "You have killed enough people!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclamations]])
  * **draw back your hand** - This is an idiom that means to stop doing something. AT: "do not kill attack the people of Jerusalem" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Ornan** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
