====== arrogant ======

===== Definition: =====

Outwardly proud, lacking humility, boastful.

  * The Bible says that men should not talk arrogantly, because God weighs men's actions.
  * The Prophet Isaiah told Israel that "the arrogant would be made low". (humbled)
