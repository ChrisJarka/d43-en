====== proverb ======


===== Definition: =====


A proverb is a short statement that expresses some wisdom or truth.

    * Proverbs are powerful because they are easy to remember and repeat. 
    * Proverbs often include examples from everyday living, and can sometimes be difficult to understand if the hearer does not live in the same setting.
    * King Solomon was known for his wisdom and wrote over 1000 proverbs.
    * Jesus often used proverbs when he taught people.
