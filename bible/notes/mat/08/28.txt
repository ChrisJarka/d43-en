===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **to the other side**  - "to the other side of the Sea of Galilee"
  * **country of the Gadarenes **- The town of Gadara is about 6 miles southeast of the Sea of Galilee. The Gadarenes were not Jews.
  * **theyâ€¦were very violent, so that no traveler could pass that way**  - The demons were so dangerous that no one could go through that area.
  * **What do we have to do with you**  - Demons are saying that Jesus should leave them alone. (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **to torment us before the set time**  - The demons know that God has decided on a specific time when he will punish them and they know that Jesus is God. They are asking Jesus if he is going to punish them now instead of waiting for that time.
