====== Mary (Sister of Martha) ======


===== Facts: =====


Mary, the younger sister of Martha, is mentioned briefly in the New Testament as being attentive to Jesus' teachings.


    * Mary's older sister Martha became angry because Mary would not help Martha with meal preparation.
    * Jesus praised Mary for listening to Him and not letting acts of service diminish the hearing of His word.
