====== Jesse ======


===== Facts: =====


Jesse was the father of David


    * Jesse was from the town of Bethlehem
    * Jesse was from the tribe of Judah
    * David was the youngest of Jesse's 8 sons
    * The Bible traces Jesus's lineage through Jesse 
    * Isaiah gives a lengthy prophecy about one of Jesse's descendants
