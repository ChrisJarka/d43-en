====== clean, cleanse ======


===== Definition: =====


To be clean is to be free from dirt or stain.  It is often used metaphorically to mean "to be free from sin or unholiness."


    * In the Old Testament, only certain foods, animals, and hygiene practices were clean.
    * In biblical usage, "clean" can reference moral purity, ceremonial purity or physical purity.


(See also: [[holiness]], [[unclean]])
