===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The Word of Yahweh came to me and said**
- See how you translated this in 3:16.

  * **"Set your face toward" **- See how you translate this in 4:7.
  * **Gog **- "This is the name of a leader or "prince" "head" "chief " who lived in the land of Magog."
  * **Magog **- "Magog is an unknown area, usually thought of the north of Israel, also known as the Land of Gog."
  * **Rosh,Meshech, and Tubal**  - "These are names of historic places that were in existed in Ezekiel's day."
  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh **- See how you translated this in 5:5.
  * **Behold**  - This can be translated as “Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you.”
