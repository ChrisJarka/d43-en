====== Psalms 146 General Notes ======

=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 146 is a worship psalm: the third worship psalm in the final seven worship psalms. (Psalms 144-150)

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Trusting people to save is useless because they just die. But God blesses everyone who trusts in him.  He provides everything people need.
