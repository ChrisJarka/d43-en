===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **barley cakes- ** the grain barley baked as flat bread (See: [[en:ta:translate:translateunknowns]])
  * **bake it on human excrement- ** God is telling Ezekiel to use human solid waste as a fuel source for fire.
  * **children of Israel **- This is another name for the people of Israel.
  * **house of Israel**  - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:01|3:01]].
  * **I will banish them** - The word "banish" means to send someone away by force or because of a crime.
