===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Doeg ... herdsmen** - You may need to say clearly that Doeg saw what David did. AT: "Doeg ... herdsmen, and he saw what Ahimelech did" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Doeg** - man's name (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **detained before Yahweh** - Possible meanings are that 1) Doeg had some religious work regarding Yahweh to do there. AT: "for some work before Yahweh" or 2) Yahweh detained Doeg there. AT: "because Yahweh had detained him there" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **herdsmen** - the keepers and protectors of a herd, especially of cattle or sheep
