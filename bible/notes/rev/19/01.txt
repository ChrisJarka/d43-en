===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **After these things**  - "these things" refer to what John heard and saw in chapter 18.  AT: "After the mighty angel finished speaking".
  * **I heard**  - Here, "I" refers to the writer, John.
  * **large number of**  - AT: "huge crowd of" or "very many"
  * **hallelujah **- This word is an exclamation (shout) of praise. Your language may have a specific word for this.  AT: "Praise God" or "Let us praise God".
  * **Salvation, glory, and power belong to the Lord **- AT: "The Lord is our Savior!  He is glorious and powerful!"
  * **who corrupted the earth**  - "The earth" is used here to refer to all the people of the earth.  AT: "who corrupted all the people of the earth" (See: Metonymy)
  * **the blood of his servants**  - This is referring to the prostitute killing God's servants.  AT: "the prostitute killing his servants" (See: Idiom)
  * **she herself shed **  - This emphasizes that the prostitute was the one that killed God's servants.
