===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Can a blind person guide another blind person?**- The answer to this question is no. This can be translated as "A blind person cannot guide another blind person, can he?"
    * **they would both fall into the pit, would they not?**- The answer to this question is a yes. This can be translated as "Would they not both fall into the pit?"
    * **A disciple is not greater than his teacher**- That is, "A disciple is not more trained than his teacher, or "a disciple does not have more knowledge than his teacher."
    * **everyone when he is completely trained will be like his teacher**- That is "every disciple who has been trained well will be like his teacher" or "every student who has been taught in the best way will be like his teacher." Original language means to adjust down or fit togetherâ€‹.
