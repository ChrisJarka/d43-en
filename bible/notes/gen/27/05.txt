===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now**  - The word "now" is used here to mark a change from the story to background information about Rebekah. (See: [[en:ta:translate:writingstyles|Background Information]]).
  * **Rebekah heard it when Isaac spoke to Esau his son** - "Rebekah had heard Isaac speaking to Esau his son"
  * **See here** - The phrase "see here" adds emphasis to what follows." You can translate it as "Listen carefully."
  * **Bring me game** - "Bring game to me." The word "game" refers to an animal that someone hunts for food. This could be translated as "Come back with the animal you kill."
  * **make me delicious food**  - "prepare it so that it has a pleasing taste"
  * **bless you in the presence of Yahweh**  - "bless you before Yahweh"
  * **before my death** - "before I die"
