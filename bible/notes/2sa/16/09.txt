===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Abishai... Zeruiah**  - These are the names of men. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Why should this dead dog curse my master the king?** - This question is asked to express displeasure to David. AT: This man should not be allowed to speak to the king this way." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]) 
    * **this dead dog** - This phrase means "worthless person." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
    * **What have I to do with you, sons of Zeruiah?** - This question is asked to rebuke the sons of Zeruiah. AT: "I will have nothing to do with you, sons of Zeruiah."
    * **Who then could say to him, 'Why are you cursing the king** - This question is asked to emphasize that Yahweh may be behind the cursing of the king. AT: "Then no one could question his cursing of the king."
