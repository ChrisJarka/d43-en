====== 1 Samuel 05 General Notes ======

=== Structure and formatting ===

The story of the Ark in the Philistine cities continues

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

**The Ark in the Philistine Cities**

The Philistines took the Ark of God to Ashdod and put it in their idol temple but their idle fell down in front of it and the people got sick with bubonic plague. So they took it to Gath and the people of Gath began dying of the plague. So they took it to Ekron but the people of Ekron were afraid of it and did not want it there. So they decided to send it back to Israel.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Idiom ==

This chapter is interesting because it uses an [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]] in two slightly different forms: "hand was heavy/hard" both meaning was severely punishing.
