===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - King Solomon continues praying.

  * **is required to swear an oath** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "someone requires him to swear an oath" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **this house** - This refers to the temple.
  * **bringing what he has done upon his own head** - To bring someone's conduct on him represents punishing him for his bad conduct. AT: "punishing him as he deserves to be punished" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **on his own head** - Here the "head" refers to the whole person. AT: "on him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **give to him according to his righteousness** - "give him what he deserves because he is innocent"
