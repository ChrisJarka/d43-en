====== Deuteronomy Chapter 11 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What four things does Yahweh want his people to always keep?

A. Yahweh's people will love their God and always keep his instructions, his statutes, his decrees, and his commandments. [11:1]

Q? What did Israel's children not know or see?

A. Your children have not known or seen the punishment of Yahweh your God. [11:2]

Q? What did Yahweh use to overwhelm the army of Egypt when they pursued after Israel?

A. Yahweh made the water of the Red Sea overwhelm the army as they pursued after Israel. [11:4]

Q? What have the eyes of Israel seen?

A. The eyes of Israel have seen all the great works of Yahweh that he did. [11:7]

Q? Why should the people keep all the commandments?

A. They should keep the commandments so that they may be strong, go in and possess the land, and that their days may be prolonged. [11:8-9]

Q? How and why is the promised land different from the land of Egypt?

A. In Egypt, Israel cared for and worked the land but in the promised land, of hills and valleys, God always cares for the land. [11:10-12]

Q? What would Yahweh give them if they listen to his commandments, love and serve him with their heart and soul?

A. Yahweh will give them the former and latter rain, that they may gather their grain, new wine, and oil. He will give grass in their fields for their cattle, the people will eat and be full. [11:13-15]
