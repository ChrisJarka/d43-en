====== soul ======


===== Definition: =====


This word means the life (psyche) of the person – and includes the mind, will and emotions.


Text


    * Only humans have a soul. When the word “soul” is used in the Bible – it can mean living now in a physical body using the mind/will/emotions, or it can mean life later in a heavenly body.
    * The word “soul” can refer to a person, the nature of the person or the afterlife of a person.
    * The “soul” is the essence of being alive as human beings.


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:term|term]] spirit, body )
