===== translationNotes: =====

  * **sent a message** - This means that he sent a messenger to give a message to the king. AT: "sent someone to give a message" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **is the word that Yahweh has spoken** - "is what Yahweh has said"
  * **The virgin daughter of Zion ... shakes her head at you** - These two phrases both mean the same thing and refer to the inhabitants of Jerusalem. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **laughs you to scorn** - "laugh at you" or "make fun of you"
  * **shakes her head** - This is a gesture of scorn. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **The virgin daughter of Zion ... the daughter of Jerusalem** - Both of these phrases have the same meaning. The "daughter" of a city means the people who live in the city. See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:01:07|1:8]].  AT: "The people of Zion ... the people of Jerusalem" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Whom have you defied and insulted? And against whom have you exalted your voice and lifted up your eyes in pride? Against the Holy One of Israel.** - Yahweh uses questions to ridicule the king of Assyria. This can be written as a statement. AT: "You have rebelled against, and spoken against, and lifted yourself above Yahweh, the Holy One of Israel." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **And against whom have you exalted your voice
and lifted up your eyes in pride** - The phrase "exalted your voice" means to shout loudly.
