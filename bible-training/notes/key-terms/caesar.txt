====== Caesar ======

===== Facts: =====


Caesar was the title given to the ruler of the Roman Empire.

    * Augustus was the first Caesar. He ruling the Roman Empire when Jesus was born.
    * "Caesar" can be translated as: "King" or, "Emperor" or, "Czar."
