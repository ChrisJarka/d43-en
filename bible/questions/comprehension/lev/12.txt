====== Leviticus Chapter 12 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Yahweh say to Moses about women who gave birth to a male child?

A. Yahweh said to Moses that a woman who gave birth to a male child would be unclean for seven days and the the eighth the child will be circumsized. [12:2,3]

Q? What must a woman do following her being unclean?

A. She will go through purification for thirty-three days and not enter the tabenacle or touch anything holy during this time. [12:4]
