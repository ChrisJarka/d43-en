====== holy city ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Bible the term holy city refers to the city of Jerusalem in the Old Testament, and the New Jerusalem which descends from God out of heaven as the place where his people will live.

  * The moral, civil and ceremonial aspects of Judaism were practiced in the holy city of Jerusalem
  * The New Jerusalem is a city where God Himself will live among his people.

(See also: Jerusalem, heaven)
