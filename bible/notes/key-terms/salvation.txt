====== salvation ======

===== Definition: =====

Salvation refers to deliverance, preservation or rescuing someone from danger, difficulties, evil, or enemies. In the Bible, salvation refers to what God does when he rescues people from the punishment by forgiving them their sins.

  * When God rescues his people from their enemies, he gives them salvation from their enemies.
  * When God forgives people's sins, he gives them salvation from their sins and from the punishment they deserve.
  * Salvation in the Biblical sense speaks of the spiritual and eternal deliverance granted by God to those who accept his conditions of repentance and faith in the Lord Jesus.
  * Salvation refers to the maintenance of peace and harmony. It is deliverance or freedom from factors which constrain and confine.
