====== lay, sleep ======

===== Definition: =====

Literally, the words "lay" and "sleep" mean for someone to rest in a horizontal position.

  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Figuratively, these words refer to having sexual relations with someone.</font>
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The Old Testament uses the term "to sleep with their fathers" to describe a person's death.</font>
