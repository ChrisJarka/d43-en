===== translationNotes: =====

  * **brought on them** - Yahweh sent the army to attack Jerusalem. AT: "brought about an attack on them by" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the commanders of the army of the king of Assyria** - The commanders were accompanied by their soldiers. AT: "the commanders of the army of the king of Assyria and their soldiers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **took Manasseh in chains, bound him with fetters, and took him off to Babylon.** - Here Manasseh being taken as a prisoner is represented by him being bound by chains and fetters. Fetters were chains placed around the feet. AT: "siezed Manasseh, bound him in chains, and took him as a prisoner to Babylon." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
