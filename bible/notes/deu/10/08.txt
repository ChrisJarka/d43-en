===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **At that time - **  This is the continuation of the separate historical narrative inserted inside parenthesis that started in verse 6 and is going to end in verse 9.
  * **to bless people in his name, as today**
 - to bless the people of Israel in Yahweh's name, as the Levites were still doing at the time Moses was writing this text.
  * **no portion nor inheritance of land **- The tribe of Levi did not receive a portion of the promissed land when they arrived there.
  * **as Yahweh your God spoke to him** - "Him" refers to Levi, the father of the tribe of Levi, a figure of speech representing the entire tribe of Levi. (See: Metonymy)
