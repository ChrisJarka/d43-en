====== ransom ======

===== Definition: =====

Ransom is a sum of money or other payment that is demanded or paid for the release of a person who is held captive.

  * As a verb, "ransom" means to make a payment or to do something self-sacrificially in order to rescue someone who has been captured, enslaved or imprisoned.
  * Jesus allowed himself to be killed as a ransom to rescue or free sinful people from their enslavement to sin.
  * This term could also be translated as, "to pay to release" or "to pay a price to free" someone.

(See also: redemption)
