===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It came to pass - **  This phrase is used to mark the beginning of a new part of the story.
  * In the seventh year, on the tenth day of the fifth month â€“ This phrase refers to tenth day of the fifth month of the calendar that they used at the time and seven years since a specific point of history. (See TA Link on How to Translate Names & Biblical Times)
  * **Came to inquire **
- This phrase means that someone or a group arrived to ask for information.

  * **Sat before me **
- This phrase means to sit in front of someone, face to face.

  * **Me **  - This pronoun refers to Ezekiel.
