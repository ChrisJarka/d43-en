====== olive ======

===== Definition: =====

<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The olive is the small, oval fruit from an olive tree, which is mostly grown in the regions surrounding the Mediterranean Sea.</font>

  * Olive trees are short and are a type of large evergreen shrub with tiny white flowers. They grow best in hot weather and can survive well with little water.
  * <font 14px line-height: 21.7777786254883px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Olives are green in color, changing to black as they ripen.</font> They are used for eating and extracting oil.
  * Olive oil was used not only for cooking but also for lighting lamps and in religious ceremonies.
  * In Scripture, olive trees and branches are used figuratively to refer to people.
