====== lay, sleep ======

===== Definition: =====

Literally, the words "lay" and "sleep" mean for someone to rest in a horizontal position.

  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Figuratively, these words refer to having sexual relations with someone.</font>
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Another figurative term, "to sleep with their fathers," describes a person's death.</font>

(See also: other)
