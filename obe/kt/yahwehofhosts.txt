====== Yahweh of hosts, God of hosts ======

===== Definition: =====

\\
"Yahweh of hosts" refers to the leader of the army of angels.

  * "Yahweh" is one of the many names of God, so the term "Yahweh of hosts" is frequently stated as "God of hosts"  or "Lord of hosts."
  * Angels are normally referred to as hosts, even as an army of hosts.

(See also: other)
