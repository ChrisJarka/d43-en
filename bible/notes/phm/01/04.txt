===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I thank my God always, making mention of you in my prayers** - "When I pray for you, I always thank God (UDB).
  * **I** - Paul wrote this letter. The words "I" and "me" in this letter all refer to Paul.
  * **you** - Here and in most of the letter, the word "you" is singular and refers to Philemon. (See: [[en:ta:translate:formsofyou-singular]])
  * **hearing of your love, and of the faith which you have in the Lord Jesus and of your faithfulness toward all the saints** - "I have heard of your faith in the Lord Jesus and of your love for all believers."
  * **I pray that the sharing of your faith may be effectual, in the knowledge of every good thing which is in us for Christ.** - This could also be translated as "I pray that the knowledge of all the good things that are in us for the benefit of Christ will make your participation in the faith effective."
  * **us** - Here the word "us" refers to all believers, including Philemon. (See: [[en:ta:translate:inclusive]])
  * **the hearts of the saints have been refreshed by you** - Here the word "hearts" is a metonym and refers to the courage of the believers. Also, "have been refreshed" is a passive clause. This could be translated as an active clause: "you have encouraged the believers." (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]] and [[en:ta:translate:active]])
  * **brother** - Paul called Philemon "brother" because they were both believers." He was also probably emphasizing their friendship. It can also be translated here as "dear brother" or "dear friend."
