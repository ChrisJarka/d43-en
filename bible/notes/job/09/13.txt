===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **the helpers of Rahab**  - this is not the Rahab of Jericho but a sea creature.
  * **How much less could I answer him, could I choose words to reason with him? **- Job is using a rhetorical question and parallelism to show that he can not answer back to God. AT: "What could I say to answer or even question him?" See: Parallelism. See: Rhetorical Question.
