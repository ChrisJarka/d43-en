====== world ======

===== Definition: =====

In the most general sense, world refers to that which is not part of the heavens, including humanity and created things  on the Earth.

  * The Apostle John's use of the word "world" includes all of humanity, past, present and future. Biblical authors often focus on the earth and human activity and organization in it.
  * Jesus refers to the "world" as the place he arrived in after leaving heaven.
  * refers In Galatians 6:14, Paul is rejecting the corrupt world system with its fleshly attractions and works based religions.
  * Paul pictures the world being crucified by him and and sees himself on the cross from the world's perspective.
