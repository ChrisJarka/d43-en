===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The waters of the sea will dry up, and the river will dry up and become empty** – “The waters of the sea will become low, and the rivers will become dry beds” (See: [[en:ta:translate:doublet]]) This will be a punishment for the people of Egypt who are proud of their rivers and who became wealthy from their rivers.
  * **the rivers  will become foul** – "The rivers will be slow and still instead of flowing with fish as they had in the past"
  * **the reeds and flags will wither away** - "The plants along the river will die"
