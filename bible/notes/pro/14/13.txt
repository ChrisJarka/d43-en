===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **be in pain** - "experience pain" or "hurt"
  * **grief** - sorrow, sadness
  * **The one who** - AT: "The person who"
  * **what his ways deserve** - AT: "what he deserves, based on how he lived"
  * **what is his** - AT: "what belongs to him" or "what he has a right to" Possible meanings are: 1) the good person will get the reward he deserves 2) the good person will get what belonged to the unfaithful person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
