====== palace ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "palace" usually refers to the building or group of buildings where a king lives, along with his family members and servants.

  * The high priest also lived in a palace complex, as mentioned in the New Testament.
  * Palaces were very ornate, with beautiful architecture and furnishings.
  * The buildings and furnishings were constructed of stone or wood, then often overlaid with expensive wood, gold, or ivory.
  * Many other people also lived and worked in the palace complex, which usually included several buildings and courtyards.

(See also: other)
