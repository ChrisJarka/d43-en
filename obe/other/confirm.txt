====== confirm, confirmation ======

===== Definition: =====

To "confirm" something is to say that it is true, firm, or sure.  "Confirmation" is the true, firm, or sure establishment of something.

  * The apostle Paul confirms the good news of the of Christ in his letters in the New Testament.
  * Paul preached the confirmation of Christ's redeeming death on the cross in Israel, Asia Minor, and Rome.
  * The modern use of the word "confirmation" as a ceremony for acceptance into the church is not found in the Bible.
