====== tribulation ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Bible the term tribulation refers to a period of great difficulty.

  * The Book of Acts states that Christians will endure times of tribulation, because Christians live in opposition to the world.
  * The Bible describes a period of time called "the great tribulation" to define a period of time before Jesus' second coming when God's wrath will be poured out on the earth.
