====== children, child ======

===== Definition: =====

The terms "children" and "child" have several figurative uses.

  * Often the term "children" is used to mean "descendants," as in "children of Jacob."
  * Sometimes the phrase "children of" is used to mean "having the characteristics of" or "people who are like" something or someone. Some examples of this would be, "children of the light," "children of obedience" or "children of the devil."
  * The phrase "children of the promise" refers to "people who receive what God promised them."
  * The phrase "children of God" refers to people who belong to God through faith in Jesus.
  * Jesus sometimes called his followers children.
  * In their letters, Paul and John called believers their children.
  * Sometimes new believers or those who are not strong in their faith are referred to as "children."
