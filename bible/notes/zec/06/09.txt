===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the word of Yahweh came to me, saying,** - This idiom is used to introduce a special message from God. AT: "Yahweh gave a message to me. He said," or "Yahweh spoke this message to me:" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Heldai, Tobijah, and Jedaiah ... Jehozadak** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **this same day** - "today"
  * **take the silver and gold, make a crown** - "make a crown with the silver and gold"
