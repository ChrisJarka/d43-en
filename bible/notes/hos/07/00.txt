====== Hosea 07 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter continues using poetic forms to show Yahweh's anger about His peoples' sin. The people are not seeking Yahweh; they are seeking other gods and looking salvation from other nations like Egypt and Assyria. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Baking ==

Baking of bread is an extended metaphor in this chapter. Yahweh uses various aspects of this activity to show His displeasure with their sins. 

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphor ==

Metaphors are used frequently but their intended meaning may be obscure and so please study the Translator Notes carefully. 
You will notice that the author has used baking metaphors (for example, loaves, oven, baker, flat cakes). 

=== Other possible translation difficulties in this chapter ===

This chapter begins by using two different names for the same group of people: Israel and Ephraim. It also mentions Samaria which was the capital of the Northern Kingdom.
