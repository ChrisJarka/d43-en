====== trespass ======

===== Definition: =====

To "trespass" means to break a law or to violate the rights of another person.

  * A trespass can be a violation of moral or civil law committed against another person.
  * This term is related to the terms "sin" and "transgression."
  * All sins are trespasses against God.
