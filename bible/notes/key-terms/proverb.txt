====== proverb ======


===== Definition: =====


A proverb is a short statement that expresses some wisdom or truth.


     * One of the books of the Bible is a collection of proverbs written mostly by King Solomon, who was known for his wisdom.
     * Jesus often used proverbs when he taught people.
