===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **MENE, MENE, TEKEL, UPHARSIN** - Translate these as they are written here. They are Aramaic words that were written on the wall.
    * **you are weighed in the balances and found lacking** - AT: "King Belshazzar, God has judged you and found you unworthy to be king." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
