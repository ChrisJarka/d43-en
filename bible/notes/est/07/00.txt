====== Esther 07 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

== Haman's fall concluded (Esther 5-7) ==

At the second feast, Esther begged the king to save her life and the life of her people from Haman. The king was told that Haman planned to hang Mordecai; so he had him hung on the gallows he built for Mordecai.


=== Other possible translation difficulties in this chapter ===

== A cultural action with a meaning ==

When "the servants covered Haman's face" they were showing that he had been condemned to be executed. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
