====== shadow, shelter ======

===== Definition: =====

Shadow and shelter are metaphorical terms used to describe being protected by God Himself.

  * In the Old Testament God gave Israel shelter in Himself by defeating Israel's enemies.
  * Being in God's shadow means that God is over you and thereby protecting you.
