===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **god of fortresses**  - This phrase describes a false god, worshipped for defending or attacking fortresses.
  * **acknowledge**  - To agree that a person holds a particular position of power.
  * **attack the strongest fortresses**  - "defend his fortresses" (UDB) or "build strong fortresses"
  * **He will give honor to whoever acknowledges him.**  - The "He" refers to the king. AT: "The king will give honor to whoever acknowledges him." (See: First, Second, or Third Person)
  * **distribute the land for a price**  - Possible meanings: 1) the king will reward his followers by giving them land for free (UDB) 2) the king will sell land to his followers (perhaps land the king has stolen from his enemies).
