====== giant ======

===== Definition: =====

A giant usually refers to a person who is unusually large in height and strength.

  * Goliath, a Philistine soldier that fought David, may be called a giant because he was a very tall, large and strong man.
  * The Nephilim of Noah's day were called giants (Genesis 6).
