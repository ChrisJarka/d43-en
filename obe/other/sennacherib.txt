====== Sennacherib ======

===== Facts: =====

Sennacherib was a powerful king of Assyria who made the city of Nineveh great.

  * King Sennacherib is known for his wars against Babylon and the kingdom of Judah.
  * He attacked Jerusalem during the time of King Hezekiah and the prophet Isaiah.
  * The books of Kings and Chronicles records the events of his reign.
