====== John Chapter 20 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? When did Mary Magdalene come to the tomb?

A. She came to the tomb early on the first day of the week [20:1].

Q? What did Mary Magdalene see when she got to the tomb?

A. She saw the stone rolled away from the tomb [20:1].

Q? What did Mary Magdalene do after she saw the stone rolled away from the tomb?

A. She ran and came to Simon Peter and to the other disciple Jesus loved [20:20].

Q? What did Mary Magdalene say to the two disciples?

A. She told them, "They have taken away the Lord out of the tomb, and we do not know where they have laid him." [20:2].
