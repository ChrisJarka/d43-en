====== 2 Chronicles 26:11-13 ======

===== TFT: =====

<usfm>

===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Jeiel ... Maaseiah, the officer ... Hananiah** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the heads of ancestral houses, fighting men** - Here "heads" is a metaphor for leaders, and "ancestral houses" is a metonym for extended families or clans. Apparently the soldiers were divided into groups according to extended families or clans, and their leaders in battle were their family or clan leaders. AT: "the clan leaders who led their clans in battle" or "the clan leaders who led the fighting men"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **2,600 ... 307,500** - "two thousand six hundred ... three hundred seven thousand five hundred" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Under their hand was an army** - Here "their hand" represents their authority. AT: "Under their authority was an army" or "They commanded an army" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
