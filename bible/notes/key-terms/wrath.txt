====== wrath ======


===== Definition: =====


Wrath is strong, furious, and sometimes long-lasting anger or rage. People may show their wrath by punishing the person they are angry with. 


    * Wrath in the Bible usually refers to God's anger at those who sin against him.
    * Sometimes the word "wrath" is used to refer to the punishment that comes as a result of wrath.
    * God's wrath is the righteous penalty for those who do not repent of their sin.
