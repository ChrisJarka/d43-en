====== Berea ======

===== Facts: =====

In New Testament times, Berea was a prosperous city in southeast Macedonia, about 50 miles south of Thessalonica.

  * Paul and Silas were smuggled out of Thessalonica to avoid Jewish opposition and retreated to Berea.
  * Another spelling is Beoea.
  * Berea is the modern city of Verria.
