===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **someone who tells secrets** - Some versions read, "Someone who is angry."
  * **makes faces angry**  - AT: "makes other people angry." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **a corner of the housetop** - This refers to the edge of the house's roof. Ancient Israelites spent much time on their roofs, where it was often cooler than inside the house.
