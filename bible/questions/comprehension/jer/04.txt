====== Jeremiah Chapter 04 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? To whom should Israel return?

A. It should be to Yahweh that they return. [4:1]

Q? What will the nations do if Israel swears that Yahweh lives in truth, justice, and righteousness?

A. The nations will ask for Yahweh's blessing, and they will praise him. [3:2]

Q? What will happen if the men of Judah and inhabitants of Jerusalem are not circumcised to Yahweh nor remove the foreskins of their heart?

A. Yahweh's fury will go out like fire and burn, with no one able to quench it. [4:4]

Q? Why must the signal flag be lifted up and pointed toward Zion?

A. This must be done because Yahweh is bringing disaster from the north and a great collapse. [4:6]

Q? Who is comng out and who is setting out?

A. A lion is coming out from his thicket and someone who will destroy nations is setting out. [4:7]

Q? Why should they wrap themselves in sackcloth, lament, and wail?

A. they should do this for the force of Yahweh's anger has not turned away from them. [4:8]

Q? What will happen in that day?

A. The hearts of the king and his officials will die, the priests will be appalled, and the prophets will be horrified. [4:9]

Q? What will be said of this people and Jerusalem in that time?

A. A burning wind from the plains of the desert will make its way to the daughter of his people; it will not winnow or cleanse them;a wind far stronger than that will come at his command, and he will pass sentence against them. [4:11-12]

Q? What must Jerusalem cleanse?

A. Jerusalem must cleanse its heart from wickedness, so that it might be saved. [4:14]
