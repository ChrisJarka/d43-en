====== exhort ======

==== (exhort, exhortation) ====

===== Definition: =====


To exhort is to give a strong encouragement to do something. Usually an exhortation is to follow righteousness and avoid sin.


    * The New Testament encourages us to exhort each other to do right.
    * We are to exhort using gentle and kind words, not harshly or abruptly.
