====== Psalms Chapter 147 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why should Yahweh be praised?

A. Yahweh should be praised because it is good, pleasant and suitable. [147:1]

Q? How does Yahweh help?

A. Yahweh helps rebuild jerusalem and gathers the scattered people of Israel. [147:2]
