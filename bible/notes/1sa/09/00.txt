====== 1 Samuel 09 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

**Samuel chooses Saul to be their king**

Saul's father's donkeys strayed away and Saul went looking for them. When he could not find them he went to Samuel to ask him to ask God where they were. Samuel said the donkeys had been found and then invited Saul as the guest of honor to a feast and gave him a place to sleep the night. 


=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===


== Rhetorical questions ==

Saul uses two [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]s to convince Samuel that he is not important: "Am not I a Benjamite, from the smallest of the tribes of Israel? Is not my clan the least of all the clans of the tribe of Benjamin?"
