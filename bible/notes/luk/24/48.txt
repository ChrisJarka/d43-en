===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **You**  - "You" plural
    * **You are witnesses** - "You are to tell others that what you seemed about me is true". There are two components that Jesus was expecting of his disciples: 1) the fact that they have observed Jesus' life, death and resurrection. 2) They tell people about the truth of Jesus' life, death and resurrection.
    * **promise of my Father** - "my father promised to "
    * **clothed with power**  - "be empowered" or "receive power".
    * **from on high** - "power from above" or "power from God"
