===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **dread** - the terror that comes from the threat of an enemy.
  * **bold words** - explanation
  * **birds **- This is a general term for things that fly (See [[:en:bible:notes:gen:01:24|Gen 1:25]]).
  * **everything that creeps along the ground **- This includes all types of reptiles (See [[:en:bible:notes:gen:01:24|Gen 1:25]]).
  * **They are given into your hand**  - "They are given into your control." This can be translated with an active verb: "I put them into your control." (See: [[:en:ta:vocabulary:active|Active or Passive]])
