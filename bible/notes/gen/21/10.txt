===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **she said to Abraham**  - "Sarah said to Abraham"
  * **Drive out**  - "send away" or "get rid of" (UDB)
  * **this handmaid and her son** - This refers to Hagar and Ishmael. Sarah probably did not refer to to them by name because she was angry with them.
  * **with my son, with Isaac**  - "with my son Isaac"
  * **This thing was very grievous to Abraham**  - "Abraham was very unhappy about what Sarah said"
  * **because of his son**  - This can be translated as "because it was about his son" or "because the handmaid's son was his son." (See: [[en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]])
