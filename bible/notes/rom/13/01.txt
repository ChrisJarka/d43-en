===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Let every soul be obedient to **- "Every Christian should obey"
  * **higher authorities**  - "government officials". This is better than the UDB's "civil officials" given the double meaning of "civil".
  * **for**  - "for the following reason"
  * **<font 14px line-height: 15.6800003051758px; text-align: justify;/Lato, sans-serif;;rgb(51, 51, 51) font-family: Lato, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 15.6800003051758px; text-align: justify;;;inherit>the ones that exist</font> **- "the people who are in authority"
  * **that authority **- "that government authority"
