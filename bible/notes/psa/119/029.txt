===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Turn from me the path of deceit**  - AT:  "Turn me from following false ways" or "Turn me from following ungodly ways" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **deceit** - Possible meanings are 1) "lying" or 2) "believing lies" or "following lies."
  * **the way of faithfulness**  - AT: "to follow your truths" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
