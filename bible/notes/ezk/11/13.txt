===== Translation Notes: =====

God continues to tell the people of Israel of God's judgment through the prophet Ezekiel.

   * **And it came about** - This phrase is used here to mark an important event in the story. If your language has a way for doing this, you could consider using it here.
   * **Alas** - The word "Alas" shows regret and means "too bad" or "woe."
   * **Pelatiah, the son of Benaiah** - Leader of the people of Israel mentioned in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:11:01|11:1]].
   * **I fell on my face** - See note in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:01:27|1:28]].
