====== Psalms Chapter 034 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? How often does David praise Yahweh?

A. David praises Yahweh at all times.  [34:1]

Q? What will be the result of David's praise?

A. The result of David's praise may be that the oppressed will hear and rejoice. [34:2]

Q? What is David asking others to do with him?

A. David is asking others to praise Yahweh and lift up his name together. [34:3]

Q? When David sought Yahweh what happened?

A. Yahweh answered David and gave him victory over all his fears. [34:4]

Q? What happens to those who look to Yahweh?

A. They are radiant and their faces are not ashamed. [34:5]

Q? What did Yahweh do when he heard the oppressed man cry?

A. Yahweh saved him from all his troubles. [34:6]

Q? What does the angel of Yahweh do when he camps around those who fear him?

A. The angel of Yahweh rescues him. [34:7]

Q? What is the result of tasting and seeing that Yahweh is good?

A. The man is blessed who takes refuge in him. [34:8

Q? What is the result when Yahweh's chosen people fear him?

A. There is no lack for those who fear Yahweh. [34:9]
