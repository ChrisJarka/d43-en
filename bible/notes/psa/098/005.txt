===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **with the harp, with the harp and melodious song. **- AT: ", play the harp and sing delightful songs." (See:[[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]  and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **melodious**  - delightful or pleasing
  * **With trumpets and the sound of the horn, make**  - AT: "Some should blow trumpets and other horns, to make" (See:[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **trumpet**  - musical instrument
  * **horn**  - could be another musical instrument or the shofar/rams horn
