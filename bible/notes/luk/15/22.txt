===== Translation Notes: =====


    * Jesus continues his story
    * **â€¦ Father said**- That is, the father instructs or give instruction to his servants.
    * **Let us make merry**- That is, let us celebrate, delight ourselves, or be in festive mood
    * **Let us**  - The father includes the entire household in this celebration.
    * **best robe**  - This was a mark of the father's preference. A similar garment was given to Joseph, placing him above his twelve older brothers.
    * **ring**  - This was a sign of son-ship and of delegated authority.
    * **sandals**  - Putting on and taking off sandals in Jewish culture were used symbolically to confer property to other people. Ruth 4:7-8 is one such example. The father continues to give the youngest son unmerited blessing and favor. It is as if the Father is giving his son rights to his property, though the son has already squandered all of his inheritance.
    * **fattened calf**  - The fattened calf was usually saved for special festival days.
    * **kill**  - that is, "slaughter" or, "sacrifice".
    * **make merry**  -Or, "to celebrate"
    * **my son was dead**  - he was spiritually dead and returned to life through repentance
