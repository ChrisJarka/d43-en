===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **eighth day**  - That is, "the eighth day after his birth" or, "eight days after the baby was born" or, "when the baby was eight days old."
    * **his name was called Jesus**  - That is, "they named him Jesus" or, "they gave him the name Jesus."
    * **which he had been called by the angel**  - This could also be translated as, "the name the angel had called him" or, "That was the name he had been called by the angel."
    * **before he was conceived in the womb**  - This could also be translated as, "before he was conceived."
