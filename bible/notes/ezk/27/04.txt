===== Translation Notes: =====

Continuation of metaphor that began in part b of verse 3.

  * **borders are in the heart of the seas**  - As a port city, Tyre's borders included maritime borders far out into the sea.
  * **they have made **- "They" refers to the builders of the city.
  * **they took cedar **- "They" refers to the builders of the city.
  * **your planks with firs from Senir **  - This phrase refers to the city's solid construction and to fir trees from Mt. Senir, which is also called Hermon Mountain. (See: Metaphor)
  * **They took cedars from Lebanon to make a mast for you**  - Using cedars from Lebanon indicates that the best materials were used to construct the city. The mast referring to the city's great heighth and strength.
