===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus told a parable to show that when the kingdom of God comes, God will judge people.

  * **like a net that was cast**  - "like a net that was thrown" or "like a net that fishermen threw"
  * **drew up on the beach** - "pulled the net up onto the beach"
  * **containers** - "buckets" (UDB) or "baskets"
