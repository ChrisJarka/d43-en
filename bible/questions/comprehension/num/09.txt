====== Numbers Chapter 09 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? When are the people of Israel to observe the Passover while they are in the wilderness of Sinai?

A. They are to observe the Passover on the fourteenth day of the first month, at evening every year. [9:1-5]

Q? Why did Moses go to Yahweh with a question?

A. Because there was a man who was unclean, because of coming in contact with a dead man, and wanted to keep the Passover but was questioning Moses as to why he coundn't. [9:6-8]

Q? What was the answer to Moses' question to Yahweh?

A. Yahweh told Moses that if a man is unclean because of a dead body, or are on a long jouney, he may still keep the Passover. [9:9-10]

Q? What were some rules for keeping the Passover?

A. They must eat it with unleaven bread and bitter herbs. they must leave none of it until morning and they must not break a bone of the animal. [9:11-12]

Q? What could cause a man to be cut off from his people?

A. If any person who is clean and not on a journey fails to keep the Passover that Yahweh required. [9:13]

Q? What would be the law for the Passover for a stranger or foreigner born in the land?

A. He must keep the Passover and do all the commands and obey the laws required. [9:14]

Q? When did the people stop their moving and set up camp around the tabernacle?

A. They set up camp when the cloud covered the tabernacle and appeared like fire at night. [9:15-17]
