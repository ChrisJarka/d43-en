===== Translation Notes: =====

  * God (See 8:17) continues to talk in front of Ezekiel.
  * **instrument of destruction**  - "tool to destroy"
  * **instrument of slaughter**  - "tool used for violent death"
  * **linen**  - The word "linen" is a smooth, strong cloth made from a plant.
