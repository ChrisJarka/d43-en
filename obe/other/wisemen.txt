====== wise men ======

===== Facts: =====

Wise men or "magi" were pagan priests whe served in various cultures outside of Israel.

  * Aside from the Nativity story in Matthew, wise men are not seen in a favorable role.
  * In some Mediterranean countries wise men were seen as professional scholars.
  * Wise men were seen as being adept at magic.
  * The number of wise men in the Matthew account is derived from the number of gifts presented to the Christ child.
