====== famine ======

===== Definition: =====

This term is used to describe an extreme lack of food on a large scale.

  * Famine can be caused by the weather, crop disease, insects, or war.
  * Famine can be used figuratively to refer to a punishment from God when He stop communicating with man.
  * Scripture also speaks of a famine of hearing the Word of the LORD such as Amos 8:11.

(See also: other)
