====== Nehemiah 01 General Notes ======



=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Beginning the work ==

Nehemiah went to Jerusalem, surveyed the problem and suggested to the people that they should rebuild the wall. 

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Idiom ==

"they strengthened their hands" meaning they encouraged each other. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
