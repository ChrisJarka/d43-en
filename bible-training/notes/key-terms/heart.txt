====== heart ======


===== Definition: =====
The heart is the part of the body that pumps blood.

Text
    * Some cultures use the word heart to talk about the part of us that has emotions.
    * Other cultures use other body parts such as "stomach" or "liver." 
    * When "heart" is used to refer to the part of us that has emotions, use the word that your culture uses.
