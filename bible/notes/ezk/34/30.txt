===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then they** - Possible meanings are 1) "Then the nations" or "Then the people of the nations" or 2) "the people of Israel."
  * **that I am Yahweh their God** - This can be translated as "that I, Yahweh the God of the people of Israel, am with the people of Israel." This phrase means that Yahweh helps them. Alternate translation: "Then they will know that I, Yahweh their God, am helping them." (UDB) (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **They are my people** - this can be translated as "and that they are my people."
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]].
  * **For I will establish my flock as the flock of my pasture** - This can be translated as "You, sheep and goats, are my flock, the flock of my pasture." The words "sheep and goats" are implied. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]) 
  * **They will be my people** - This can be translated as "You are people" or "You are men."
  * **I will be your God** - This can be translated as "I am your God."
