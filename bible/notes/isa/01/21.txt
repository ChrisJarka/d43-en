===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **faithful city**  - Isaiah personifies Jerusalem. The faithful city refers to Jerusalem. There was a time that Jerusalem was faithful to Yahweh. (See: Personification)
  * **prostitute **-
  * **she**  - This pronoun refers to Jerusalem and its people.
  * **full of murderers**  - This description shows that the morals of Jerusalem had declined.
  * **Your silver has become impure**  - This is a metaphor to show how sinful the people have become. Adding something. (See: Metaphor)
  * **your wine mixed with water - **  This is a metaphor to show weak the people have become. Taking away something. (See: Metaphor)
