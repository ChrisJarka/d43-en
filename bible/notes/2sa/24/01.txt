===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the anger of Yahweh was ignited against Israel, **- "" Yahweh was outraged with Israel**,""**
  * **and he moved David against them **- ""and he provoked David to be outraged as well""
  * **count Israel and Judah**; ** count all people; the total number of men fit for battle **  - All phrases refer to the total number of men only and were included in the tribes of Israel and Judah, that were to be tallied and able to go to battle.
  * **Dan to Beersheba **- This is the author's note concerning the location of the tribes during the time of the writing.
