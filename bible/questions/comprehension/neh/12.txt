====== Nehemiah Chapter 12 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? In what book were the descendants of Levi and their leaders of families recorded?

A. They were recorded in the book of the annals up to the days of Johanan. [12:23]

Q? Why did the people seek out the Levites wherever they lived at the dedication of the wall of Jerusalem?

A. They brought them to Jerusalem to celebrate the dedication. [12:27]

Q? What did the priests and Levites do?

A. They purified themselves, and then purified the people, the gates, and the wall. [12:30]

Q? Where did the leaders of Judah go on the day of celebration?

A. They went up to the top of the wall. [12:31]

Q? Where did the two appointed large choirs go to give thanks?

A. One choir went to the right on the wall and the other went in the other direction. [12:31,38]
