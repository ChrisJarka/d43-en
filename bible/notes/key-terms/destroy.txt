====== destroy, blot out ======

===== Definition: =====

1.Causing the person or object to be killed as in the flood of Genesis 7.

2. Removing the memory or written record of a person (Deut 32:26, Rev 3:5).

3, God completely forgiving a person's sins (Psalm 51).
