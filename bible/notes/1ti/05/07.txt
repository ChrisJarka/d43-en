===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Preach these things **- "Command these things as well" or "Also, teach these things with authority"
  * **the believers**  - or "those who hear you" (UDB). The Greek has only a third-person plural ("they") verb with no overt subject.
  * **so that the believers may be beyond reproach**  - "so that no one can accuse them of bad behavior"
  * **But if someone does not provide**
 - "But if someone does not help the needs" or "But if someone does provide the neesds"
  * **for his relatives **- "for all his family members" or "for those living at his home"
  * ****for those of his own household**
 - **"for his family" or "for those family living in his home"
  * **he has denied the faith**
 - " he has denied what he has believed in" or "he has turned his back on his beliefs"
  * **is worse than an unbeliever** - "is worse than those who do not believe Jesus" or "those who do not believe in Jesus is better than him
