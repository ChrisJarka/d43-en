===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **tested**-Word can mean to test or to tempt, depending on the context. Here it is tempt.
    * **sought from him a sign from heaven **- That is, "tried to have him  do" or "demanded from him."
    * **a sign from heaven**  - That is, "perform a miracle that is from heaven."
    * **is made desolate**  - That is, it is turned into a wasteland.
