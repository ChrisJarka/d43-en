===== Translation Notes: =====

James and John had just asked Jesus if they could sit next to Jesus when he ruled in his kingdom on earth.

  * **When the other ten disciples heard about this, they began to be very angry with James and John** - The rest of the disciples were upset with James and John because they asked for a position of honor and they did not like that James and John wished to be in authority positions over the rest.
  * **those who are considered rulers** - "those who are thought to be rulers"
  * **dominate**  - to "have control of" or "have power over" 
  * **exercise**  - "make use of their"
