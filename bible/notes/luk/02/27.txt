===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **came**  - That is, "went."
    * **led by the Holy Spirit**  - That is, "directed by God."
    * **the parents**  - That is, "Jesus' parents."
    * **the custom of the law required**  -
    * **received him**  - That is, "took him."
    * **Now let your servant depart in peace**  - That is, "I am your servant. Let me depart in peace." Simeon was referring to himself.
    * **depart**  - This is a euphemism meaning "die." (See: [[:en:ta:figuresofspeech:euphemism|Euphemism]]) The Greek word used is in present tense, and implies a continual journey (toward death).
    * **according to your word**  - That is, "as you have said" or, "because you said you would."
