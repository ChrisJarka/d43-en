====== blasphemy, blaspheme ======


===== Definition: =====


To blaspheme is to slander or insult God by saying things that are not true about him.

    * Blasphemy is speech or behavior that shows a deep disrespect for God.
    * Blasphemy is much more serious that simply using the wrong words to address God. It is refusing to acknowledge his true nature.
    * It is also blasphemy to say that someone other than God is also God.
    * "To blaspheme" can also be translated as, "to dishonor" or "to speak disrespectfully."
