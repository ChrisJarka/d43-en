<font 24px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Translation Notes:</font>

  * **will inherit Judah as his rightful possession**  - "will choose Judah as his special portion".
  * **his/himself/he**  - These pronouns refer to Yahweh.
  * **rightful**  - This word means "to have a valid or just claim."
  * **holy land**  - This term refers to Israel and Judah. Judah is in the southern part of Israel.
  * **and will once again choose Jerusalem for himself. **- His temple is in Jerusalem.
  * **Be silent, all flesh, before Yahweh;**  - To "be silent" means "to be very quiet." "Everyone be still and worship Yahweh;"
  * **for he has been roused from out of the heavens!**  - "for he is paying much attention (or "becoming very active" or "springing into action") from his holy dwelling in heaven!"
  * **roused**  - This word means to be "woken up" or "stirred" or "is paying attention."
