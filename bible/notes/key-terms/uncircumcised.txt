====== uncircumcised, uncircumcision ======

The terms "uncircumcised" and "uncircumcision" refer to the state of a male who has not been physically circumcised.

  * <font 14.399999618530273px line-height: 15.680000305175781px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>In the Old Testament, circumcision was required as a physical sign of being part of God's people, Israel. It was typically done on male babies eight days after birth. A male who has not had this procedure done is uncircumcised.</font>
  * In the Bible, people who have an <font 14.399999618530273px line-height: 15.680000305175781px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>"uncircumcised heart" or who are "uncircumcised in heart"</font>    are people who are stubbornly disobedient to God and who are not his people.
