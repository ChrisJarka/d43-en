===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It will happen in that day** - This refers to a future time after everything mentioned in the previous verses are fulfilled.
  * **I will destroy your horses among you, and will demolish your chariots** - The people of Israel used horses and chariots in battle. God was angry because the people trusted in their weapons of war to protect them more than they trusted God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
