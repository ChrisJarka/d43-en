====== Shem ======

===== Facts: =====

Shem is one of the three sons of Noah who went with him into the ark during the great flood in the book of Genesis.

  * Shem is the father of the Semitic people group who eventually became the nation of Israel.
  * The Bible records the length of Shem's life to be nearly 600 years.
