===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **in the fifteenth year of the reign of**** Tiberius Caesar **- That is, "when Tiberius had been ruling as emperor for about fifteen years." 
    * **during the high priesthood of Annas and Caiaphas**  - That is, "when Annas and Caiaphas were high priest."
    * **the Word of God came to John **- That is, "God spoke to John" as he had spoken to the prophets in Israel's past. 
    * **in the wilderness**  - That is, while John was out in the desert, a place where few if any plants grow and very little rain comes, away from the towns and cities.
