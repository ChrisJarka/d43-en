====== firstborn ======


===== Definition: =====


The firstborn refers to the initial offspring, whether of people or animals. In humans, the term refers exclusively to the first male child born to the man and woman.


    * In biblical times, the firstborn son is given a place of prominence and a double the amount of his family's inheritance
    * This concept can also be used figuratively.
