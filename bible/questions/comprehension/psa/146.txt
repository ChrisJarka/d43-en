====== Psalms Chapter 146 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? How long should I praise Yahweh?

A. I will praise Yahweh as long as I live. [146:2]

Q? How long should I sing praises to God?

A. I will sing praises to God as long as I live. [146:2]

Q? Where should noone put their trust?

A. Do not put your trust in princes or in mankind, in whom there is no salvation. [146:3]

Q? What happens when a person's life's breath stops?

A. When a person's life's breath stops, he returns to the ground; on that day his plans end. [146:4]

Q? Who is blessed?

A. Blessed is he who has the God of Jacob for his help, whose hope is in Yahweh his God. [146;5]

Q? What has Yahweh made?

A. Yahweh made heaven and earth, the sea, and all that is in them. [146:6]

Q? Who observes trustworthiness forever?

A. Yahweh observes trustworthiness forever. [146:6]

Q? Who executes justice for the oppressed and gives food to the hungry?

A. Yahweh executes justice for the oppressed and gives food to the hungry. [146:7}

Q? Who frees the prisoners?

A. Yahweh frees the prisoners. [146:7]

Q? Who opens the eyes of the blind; raises up those who are bowed down; and loves the righteous people

A. Yahweh opens the eyes of the blind, raises up those who are bowed down, and loves the righteous people. [146:8]

A.
