====== harvest ======


===== Definition: =====


The word harvest is used to describe the action of picking and processing the fruits or crops which a farmer has grown.


    * Harvest normally happens at a prescribed time at the end of a growth season.
    * 'Harvest' can be used to describe the byproducts of a person's spiritual life. The Bible discusses "harvesting the fruits of righteousness."
