====== Numbers Chapter 36 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What exception had Yahweh commanded for the passing on of shares of land in Israel?

A. Yahweh had commanded that a man like Zelophahad who had only daughters could pass on his shares of land to them. [36:1-2]

Q? What difficulity would develop if Zelophahad's daughters married men from another tribe in Israel?

A. If Zelophahad's daughters married men from another tribe in Israel, then their share of land would be removed from their ancestors' share and added to the share of the tribe they joined. [36:3]

Q? What would happen when the jubilee of the people of Israel would come?

A. When the year of jubilee would come, the daughter's share would be joined to the share of their husband's tribe and taken away from their ancestors' tribe. [36:4]

Q? What command did Moses give through Yahweh to the people of Israel?

A. Moses gave the people of Israel a command that in cases like Zelophehad's daughters, they could marry whom they think best, but only within their father's tribe. [36:5-6]
