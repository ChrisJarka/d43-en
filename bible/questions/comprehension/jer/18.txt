====== Jeremiah Chapter 18 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Where does Yahweh tell Jeremiah to go to hear a word from him?

A. Yahweh told Jeremiah to go out to the potterâ€™s house, for he would have Jeremiah hear his word there. [18:2]

Q? What happened to the object of clay that the potter was molding while Jeremiah watched?

A. The object of clay that the potter was molding was ruined in his hand. [18:4]

Q? With whom does Yahweh asks if he can act like the potter?

A. Yahweh asks, "Should I not be able to act like this potter with you, house of Israel?" [18:6]

Q? What may Yahweh do to a nation which turns from evil after hearing his proclamation?

A. Yahweh says that if the nation about which he has made a proclamation turns from its evil, then he will relent from the disaster that he was planning to bring upon it. [18:8]

Q? How can Yahweh change his good plans when a nation does not listen to his voice?

A. If that nation will not listening to his voice, then Yahweh will stop the good that he had said he would do for them. [18:10]

Q? How will the men of Judah and Jerusalem act according to their own plans?

A. They will act according to their own plans; Each one of them will do what his evil, stubborn heart desires. [18:12]

Q? What does Yahweh say to ask the nations?

A. Yahweh says to ask the nations, who has ever heard of such a thing as this? [18:13]

Q? What paths have Israel left to walk lesser paths?

A. They have left the ancient paths to walk lesser paths. [18:15]

Q? What will be the body actions of everyone who passes by the land of Israel?

A. Everyone who passes by the land of Israel will shudder and shake their head. [18:16]

Q? What do the people no longer do while attacking Jeremiah with their words?

A. They plan to attack him with their words and no longer pay attention to anything he proclaims. [18:18]

Q? What action does Jeremiah ask to be remembered from his standing before Yahweh?

A. Remember how I stood before you to speak for their welfare. [18:20]

Q? What does Jeremiah request for the children of his enemies?

A. Jeremiah asks Yahweh to hand over their children to famine. [18:21]

Q? When does Jeremiah ask Yahweh to act against his enemies?

A. Jeremiah asked Yahweh to act against them in the time of his wrath. [18:23]
