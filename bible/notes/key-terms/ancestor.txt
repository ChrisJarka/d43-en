====== ancestor ======


===== Definition: =====


An ancestor is a person born more than one generation before another person in their family line.


    * "Ancestor" is mostly used to reference a relative in the distant past. 
    * A descendant is a person who is born after their ancestor.


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:descendant|descendant]])
