====== Titus Chapter 2 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What are some characteristics that older men in the church should display?

A. Temperance, dignity, soundness in faith, in love, and in perseverance [1:2].

Q? What are some characteristics that older women in the church should display?

A. Reverence, not gossiping, soberness, teaching of good [1:3].

Q? What should the older women teach the younger women?

A. To love and obey their husbands, to love their children, to be sensible, pure, and good housekeepers [1:4-5].

Q? What should Titus do to be a model to the believers?

A. Teach, show purity, dignity, and healthy words that do not need to be corrected [1:7-8].

Q? How are slaves who are believers to behave?

A. They are to obey their masters, not steal, and show good faith [1:9-10].

Q? When slaves behave as Paul has instructed, what effect will that have on others?

A. It makes the teaching about God our Savior attractive to others [1:10].

Q? Who can the grace of God save?

A. Everyone [1:11].

Q? What does the grace of God train us to deny?

A. Ungodliness and worldly passions [1:12].

Q? What hope do believers look to receive?

A. The appearance of the glory of our great God and Savior Jesus Christ [1:13].

Q? Why did Jesus give himself?

A. In order to pay the price to set us free from lawlessness, and to make a pure people eager to do good [1:14].
