===== Translation notes =====

  * **appointed feasts** - The religous calendar of Israel is starting again.
  * **in their midst** -  The leader must be in the building when the worship is taking place.
  * **go out**  - Alternate translation: "leave".
  * **for he must go out straight before him** - The people who enter the temple must leave using a different gate than the one they used when they entered.
