====== Ezra 05 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

The story of the building of the temple and re-establishment of the temple worship continued (Ezra 3-6) 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

The prophets Haggai and Zechariah encouraged the Jews to begin again building the temple. They told the governor of the providence that Cyrus had ordered them to build the temple and he asked the king if that was so.
