===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **his sons and his daughters** - The word "his" is a pronoun referring to Job. Alternate translation: "Job's sons and daughters."
  * **eating and drinking wine** - Only "drinking" is referring to the consumption of wine. "Eating" is implying some form of food.
  * **a messenger came to Job and said** - The text following this phrase is what the messenger spoke to job. He finishes his story at the end of verse 15.
  * **plowing** - Plowing is a process by which soil is prepared for seeds to be planted.
  * **Sabeans** - The Sabeans were a group of people from the land of Sheba which was located in the present day country of Yemen.
  * **attacked** - To attack is to come after someone with the intention of causing harm.
  * **they have killed the servants with the edge of the sword** - Alternate translation: "The Sabeans killed the servants with swords."
  * **I only have escaped to tell you** - "I" is refering to the messenger mentioned at the beginning of verse 14. Alternate translation: "I am the only one who has escaped, and the because of this I am the only one who is able to tell you about what happened."
