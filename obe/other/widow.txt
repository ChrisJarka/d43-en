====== widow ======

===== Definition: =====

A widow is a woman whose husband has died. The term can also be used figuratively to designate a city.

* A widow in Hebrew culture was often treated harshly and given few rights.

* Widows in the early Christian community were a special order, helping to rear children, caring for her parents, showing hospitality, and assisting the needy.

(See also: other)
