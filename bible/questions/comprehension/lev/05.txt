====== Leviticus Chapter 05 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What are three sins that make one guilty?

A. If anyone witnesses something about which he is required to tesstify, or touches anything God has designated as unclean, or if anyone swears rahly with his lips, then he will be guilty. [5:1-4]

Q? What two things must the one who is guilty of a sin do?

A. The one who is guilty of a sin must confess whatever sin he has commited and bring his guilt offering to Yahweh.  [5:5,6]
