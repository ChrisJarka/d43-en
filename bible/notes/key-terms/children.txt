====== children, child ======

===== Definition: =====

A child is defined by the Torah as someone under the age of 20 years old.

  * Children were later defined as being mature adults according to rabbinic tradition when they reached puberty, typically around the age of 12-13 years old.
  * Children in the Torah were different from adults primarily because they were not subject to taxation and serving in the military.

(See also: other)
