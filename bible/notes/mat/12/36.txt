===== Important Terms: =====

  * day of judgment
  * account
  * idle word
  * justified
  * condemned

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I - **  Jesus
  * **you -**  Pharisees
  * day of judgment - This is the day each of us will stand before God to be judged.
  * give an account -This could mean "to answer for" or "to be judged by" the words we have spoken.
  * idle words - This could mean "harmful, wasteful words" , bringing sadness to God and pushing people away from his kingdom.
  * **they - **  people
  * **by your words you will be justified **- The words we speak show if we are truly God's children. Through Christ, God justifies his children and welcomes them into heaven.
  * **by your words you will be condemned ****-****** The words we speak show if we are serving ourselves and pleasing Satan. Living for ourselves, without Christ, will lead to our condemnation on judgment day**.**
