===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you - **  This refers to the Jewish people who received the commandments from God
  * **commit adultery**  - This means to have sexual relations with someone who is not your spouse.
  * **bear false witness - **  This means to speak a lie about another person
  * **honor your father and your mother **- This means to give respect and obedience to one's parents.
  * **love your neighbor as yourself **- This means to treat your neighbor as you would want to be treated, in a giving and caring way.
