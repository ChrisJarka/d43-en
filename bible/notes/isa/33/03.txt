===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **At the loud noise** - "When you speak like thunder"
  * **when you arise** - "when you show your power." Here "you" refers to Yahweh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **the nations are scattered** - AT: "the people from the nations run away" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Your spoil is gathered as the locusts gather; as locusts leap, men leap on it** - This compares people taking the possessions of their defeated enemy to locusts stripping off all the leaves of the plants. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **Your spoil is gathered** - Here "your" is plural and refers to the people of the nations. AT: "The people of Judah gather your possessions" or "We gather your possessions" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
