===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you are like a lovely song to them **- The message which Ezekiel brings from Yahweh to the people is compared to a lovely song. (See: Simile)
  * **when it comes–Behold! It comes **- The word "it" refers to the message from Yahweh.  The word “behold” here adds emphasis to what follows. It can also be translated as “Indeed.”
