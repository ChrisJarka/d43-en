====== Arabia ======

===== Facts: =====

<font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Arabia is the largest peninsula in the world, covering nearly 3,000,000 square kilometers. It is located</font>    <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px/inherit;;inherit line-height: 1.4;;;inherit>between the Red Sea and</font>   <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px/inherit;;inherit line-height: 1.4;;;inherit>the Persian Gulf.</font>

  * According to the apostle Paul, Mt Sinai was located in Arabia.
  * Arabia is the region where the religion of Islam originated. It may have been the region Abraham's son Ishmael inhabited.
  * The wandering of the Israelites in the desert was in Arabia.
  * Also refers to specific people groups as in Jeremiah 25, Isaiah 21 and Ezekiel 27.
  * Paul was in Arabia
  * Midianites (Ismael's descendants) lived in Arabia
