====== Psalms 148 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 148 is a worship psalm: the fifth worship psalm in the final seven worship psalms. (Psalms 144-150)

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

All creation including humans should praise God for his greatness.
