===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **For the Scriptures say,**  - See: [[en:ta:translate:personification}Personification]]
    * **Greek**  - When used in this way to refer to someone who is not a Jew, other ways to translate “Greek” would be, “Gentile” or “non-Jew.”
    * **and is rich to all who call upon him**  - "and he richly blesses all who call on him"
    * **the name of the Lord**  - In this phrase, "the name" represents the entire person of the Lord Jesus Christ, not just his name. (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
