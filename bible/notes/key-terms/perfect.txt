====== perfect, complete, mature ======

===== Definition: =====

The Biblical terms perfect, complete, mature all refer to the aim of our Christian growth, ie. maturity.

  * Being perfect and mature in our Christian maturity does not imply we are sinless, but rather obedient.
  * The New Testament book of James states that trials and patience produce completeness and maturity.
  * Study of the scripture and obedience to it brings perfection by allowing God's Holy Spirit to conform us to His image.
