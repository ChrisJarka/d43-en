===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses is still speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **If someone is found killed**  - This describes something that could happen in the future. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_hypo]])
  * **If someone is found killed**  - AT: "If someone finds a dead person." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **lying in the field**  - This is a polite way to say that the person in the field is dead. AT: "dead in the field."  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **it is not known who has attacked him**  - AT: "nobody knows who attacked him."
  * **who has attacked him**  - This is a polite way to say that the man killed him. AT: "who has killed him."
  * ** measure to the cities that are around him who has been killed ** - "measure the distance from the dead body to the cities that are close by" or "measure how far it is from the dead body to the cities that are near by"
