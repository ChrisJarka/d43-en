=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Ahab had introduced the worship of Baal, the god people thought brought rain. So God decided to cause a drought. Elijah told Ahab that it would not rain or dew until he, Elijah, said so. Then he had to hide so that Ahab would not kill him for bringing the drought.
