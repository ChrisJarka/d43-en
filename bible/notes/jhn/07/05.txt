===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **My time has not yet come... your time is always ready** - This is referencing the time when Jesus' work would be completed and his identity would be fully revealed to the world. Possible translation 1:"It is not yet time for me to bring my work to an end." (UDB) or Possible translation 2: "Jesus' is not free to go to Judea and reveal himself fully yet." (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
  * **your... you** - These refer to Jesus' brothers.(See: [[en:ta:translate:formsofyou-plural]])
  * **The world cannot hate you** - This is referencing unbelievers through Jesus' brothers. (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  * **it... its** - These refer to "the world" (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
