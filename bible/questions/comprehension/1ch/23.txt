====== 1 Chronicles Chapter 23 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What were some of the last things David did as king of Israel?

A. David made Solomon king in his place, and he had the Levites that were at least thirty years old counted. [23:1-3]

Q? What jobs were these Levites given?

A. They were divided into groups of overseers of the work of Yahweh's house, officers and judges, gatekeepers, and others to praise Yahweh with instruments. [23:4-6]

Q? What were Aaron and his descendants chosen to do permanently?

A. Aaron and his descendants were to consecrate the most holy items, offer incense to Yahweh, serve him, and give blessings in Yahweh's name forever. [23:13]
