===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **the leaders of the Levites**  - the people who led
    * **who stood opposite them to give praise and to give thanks, responding section by section,**  - meaning the structure of worship. It is known as "antiphonal" or "call and response"
    * **obedience to the command of David**  - meaning, King David directed how the Levitical priestly organization was to function in worship
    * **gatekeepers standing guard at the storerooms of the gates**  - "gatekeepers who guarded the temple storerooms by the gates"
    * **and in the days of**  - meaning, fixing the date by listing those in charge at the time, "and during the leadership of"
    * **Hashabiah, Sherebiah, Jeshua, Kadmiel, David, Mattaniah, Bakbukiah, Obadiah, Meshullam, Talmon, Akkub, Joiakim, Jeshua, Jozadak, Nehemiah, Ezra**  - these are male names (see: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
