===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **A quarreling wife is like the constant dripping on a rainy day -**  contentions of a wife are like a soaking rain without cover, it is troublesome and uncomfortable. Similar meaning is observed in Proverbs 19:13. (See: Simile)
  * **Restraining the wind **- to keep the wind under control.
  * **Or trying to catch oil with your right hand**  - AT: "Or his right hand meets with oil." (See: Synecdoche)
