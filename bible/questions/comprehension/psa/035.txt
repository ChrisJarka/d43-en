====== Psalms Chapter 035 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? How does David want Yahweh to help him?

A. David wants Yahweh to work against, fight against, grab his shields, and rise up, then use his spear and battle axe to fight those against David. [35:1-3]

Q? What does David wish upon those who want to hurt David?

A. David wants them to be shamed, dishonored, turned back, confused, blown before the wind, and their way to be dark and slippery. [35:4-6]

Q? Who will chase and drive away the enemies of David?

A. The angel of Yahweh drives them away and chases them. [35:5-6]

Q? What do David's enemies do without cause?

A. They set their net and dig a pit to kill David. [35:7]

Q? How does David rejoice in his salvation?

A. David is joyful, and uses his strength to praise Yahweh for rescuing the oppressed, poor, and needy. [35:9-10]

Q? Why is David sorrowful?

A. Because unrighteous witnesses rise up to accuse him falsely, and they repay him evil for good, David is sorrowful. [35:11-12]

Q? How does David respond to the enemies' troubles?

A. David wears sackcloth, fasts for them. goes about in grief and bends down in mourning for them. [35:14]

Q? How do the enemies respond to David's troubles?

A. They rejoiced, gathered against him, tore at him, taunted him, and gnashed at him with their teeth. [35:16]

Q? When will David thank Yahweh in the assembly?

A. David wil thank Yahweh when he is recued from their destructive attacks. [35:17-18]
