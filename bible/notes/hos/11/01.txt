===== Translation Notes: =====

God is speaking of caring for Israel like a parent for a child.

  * **When Israel was a young man I loved him,**  - Yahweh is saying that he loved the nation of Israel in the early years of their history, when they worshiped him. (see: Metaphor)
  * **and I called my son out of Egypt.**  - This refers to the time in the history of Israel when they as a nation were slaves in Egypt, and Yahweh sent Moses to lead them out.
