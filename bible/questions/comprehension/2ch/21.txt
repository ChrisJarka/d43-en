===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why did Jehoshaphat give the throne to Jehoram?

A. He gave the throne to Jehoram, because he was his firstborn. [21:3]

Q? What did Jehoram do to his brothers after he was established as king?

A. When he had firmly established himself as king, he killed all his brothers with the sword. [21:4]

Q? Why did Yahweh not wish to destroy the house of David?

A. Hedid not wish to destroy the house of David, because of the covenant that he had made with David. [21:7]
