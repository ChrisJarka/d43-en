===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **what is low and despised** - This means the people whom the world rejects.  
  * **things that are regarded as nothing** - "things (or people) that are rejected"
  * **things that are held as valuable** - "things which people usually regard as valuable" (See: Active/Passive) or "things people think are worth money or worth respect"
  * **to boast** - This means "to talk about himself or herself in a proud way" or "to brag" or "to praise himself" (UDB). 
  * **He, him** - This is a pronoun referring to God.
