  * **Since** - "Because"
  * **we...our** - All occurrences of "we" and "our" refer to all believers and should be inclusive. (See: [[en:ta:translate:inclusive]])
  * **through our Lord Jesus Christ** - "because of our Lord Jesus Christ"
  * **through him** - "because of Jesus"
  * **Through him we also have our access by faith into this grace in which we stand ** - This describing a person coming into the presence of a king or ruler.
