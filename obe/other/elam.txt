====== Elam ======

===== Facts: =====

Elam was the son of Shem, who was the son of Noah.

  * Elamites were a people group who lived in the area of present day Iran, descendants of Elam.
  * In the Old Testament, Chedorlaomer was mentioned as the King of Elam.
