====== learned men, astrologers, magicians ======

In Matthew's account of the birth of Christ these men were the "wise men" who visited the Christ child bearing gifts.  They came from the East and came a long way.  They were aware of the stars and had studied the the ancient writings.  Exactly where they came from and who they were is unknown.
