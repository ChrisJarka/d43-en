===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **thrones were set in place** - AT: "someone set thrones in their place" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Ancient of Days** - This is a title for God that means he is eternal. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **He took his seat** and **His clothing** and **the hair on his head** - This implies the God has a body and can sit down, wears clothing, and has hair like a person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **took his seat** - AT: "sat down" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **His clothing was as white as snow** - These are compared to show his clothes are very white. AT: "His clothing was very white"
  * **the hair of his head was like pure wool** - These two are compared to say something about what God's hair looked like. Possible meanings 1) it was very white (UDB) 2) it was thick and curly. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **flames of fire ... burning fire** - This implies that God's throne and the wheels of his throne are made of fire. Both phrases mean basically the same thing and can be translated with the same words if you have to. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **its wheels** - That is the wheels of God's throne. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_123person]])
