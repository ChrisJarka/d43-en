===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **before**  - "in front of"
  * **Do not provoke him, for he will not pardon**  - AT: "If you provoke him, he will not pardon"
  * **provoke**  - "anger" or "upset"
  * **My name is on him**  - AT: "He is my divine messenger" or "He carries my divine authority"
  * **indeed**
 - "in fact" or "fully"
