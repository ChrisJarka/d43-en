====== command, to command ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "command" refers to a verbal or written statement that requires someone to do, or to not do, something.

  * God commands us to obey His word, He is the ultimate authority figure.
  * In the New Testament God commands all men to repent and follow Jesus.
