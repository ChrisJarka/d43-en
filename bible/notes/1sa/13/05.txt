===== Translation Notes: =====

Saul had called the soldiers together to join him at Gilgal when Jonathan had defeated the Philistine soldiers in Geba.

    * **thirty thousand chariots, six thousand men**  - 30,000 chariots, 6,000 men (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
    * **troops as numerous as the sand on the seashore** - AT: "groups of soldiers as many as there are pieces of sand by the sea"
    * **encamped at Michmash, east of Bethaven** - The word "encamped" means "made their camp"
    * **Michmash** - "Michmash" is a place meaning "hidden." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Bethaven**  - "Bethaven" means "house of pride" or "house of self-love." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
