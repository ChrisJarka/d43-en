====== James ======


===== Facts: =====


James is the name of four persons in Scripture.  The most prominent James was the half-brother of Jesus who wrote the book of James and was a leader of the church in Jerusalem.


    * One James, the son of Zebedee, was an apostle
    * Another James, Son of Alpheus, was an apostle
    * Another James was the father of Judas (not Judas Iscariot)
    * James, the half-brother, did not believe in Jesus prior to the resurrection
