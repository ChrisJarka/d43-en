====== in Christ, in Jesus, in the Lord ======

===== Definition: =====

The saving union of faith between the believer and Jesus.

  * Relationship or fellowship with Christ.
  * Synonymous with being a believer in Christ.
  * Belonging to Christ.
  * In harmony with or accountability to Christ.
  * Christ working in the life of the believer.
  * Mystical union of believers with Christ as members of his body.

(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:christ|Christ]])
