====== lawful ======


===== Definition: =====

Something is lawful if it is in accordance or agreement with a binding rule or law.

  * In the Bible, if something is said to be "lawful" it means it is permitted by the Law of Moses and other Jewish laws.
  * "Lawful" can be translated as "permitted" or "allowed" or "legal."

(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:lawofmoses|Law of Moses]])
