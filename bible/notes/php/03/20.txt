===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Verses 20 and 21**  - Paul now contrasts the heavenly-minded focus of true believers to the earthly-minded focus of the enemies of Christ he spoke of in verses 18-19.
  * **Because as for us, our commonwealth exists in heaven,**  - "But our homeland is in heaven," or "But our true home is in heaven,"
  * **from where we also wait for a savior, the Lord Jesus Christ**  - "and we are waiting for a savior, the Lord Jesus Christ, to return to earth from heaven."
  * **He will transform our lowly bodies -**  " He will change our weak, earthly bodies"
  * **into bodies formed like His glorious body**  - " into bodies made like His glorious body." This is a simile comparing our new heavenly bodies to Christ's heavenly body (See: [[en:ta:translate:simile]]))
  * **formed by His power to control all things**  - He will change our bodies with the same power He uses to control all things."
