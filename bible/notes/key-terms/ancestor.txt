====== ancestor ======


===== Definition: =====


An ancestor is a person born more than one generation before another person in their family line.

    * An ancestor is someone's grandparents, great-grandparents, great-great grandparents, etc.
    * "Ancestor" is mostly used to to refer to a relative in the distant past.
