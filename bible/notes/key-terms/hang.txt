====== hang ======

===== Definition: =====

To hang something is to suspend something, like a person, above the ground.

  * Death by hanging typically is done using a rope noose tied around a person's neck and suspended from an elevated object, like a tree limb. Judas killed himself by hanging.
  * Jesus' death was by hanging on a wooden cross, suspended by nailing his hands or wrists and his feet to cross.

(See also: other)
