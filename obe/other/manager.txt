====== manager, steward ======


===== Definition: =====

The term "manager" or "steward" refers to a servant in a rich family's home who was in charge of the property, the other servants, and even the children.

  * Managers and stewards were entrusted with taking care of the owner's affairs and business dealings.

(See also: [[servant]])
