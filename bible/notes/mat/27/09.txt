===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then that which had been spoken by Jeremiah**  - This means that Jeremiah spoke this prophecy and it had come true. Jeremiah's words on this were not recorded in our written history.
  * **people of Israel **  - referring to the Israelite religious leaders (See: Metonymy)
  * **the potter's field **  - probably a field which Jeremiah bought to bury strangers (This is not recorded in written history by Jeremiah.)
