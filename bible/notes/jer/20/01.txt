===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Pashur**  - Pashur's father was actually Melchiah and they were descendents of Immer. Pashur was not a high priest, but he was overseer, or captain, of the Temple. (See: Implicit/Explicit and Translate Names)
  * **Immer**  - Immer was not Pashur's father, but was an ancestor of Pashur's who had served as a high priest in the Temple.
  * **Pashur beat Jeremiah **- This could mean that Pashur struck Jeremiah with his own hands, or that Pashur ordered the customary thirty-nine lashes with a whip (commonly referred to as forty stripes minus one).
  * **stocks**  - Stocks were a wooden frame with holes in it for a person's feet, hands, or head that a person would be locked into as a form of punishment.
  * **Upper Gate of Benjamin **- The Upper Gate of Benjamin is the northern gate of the upper (inner) court of the Temple, and is to be distinguished from the city gate of the same name.
