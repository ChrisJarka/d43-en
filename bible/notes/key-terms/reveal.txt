====== reveal, revelation ======

===== Definition: =====

To reveal something is to cause it to be seen or known. A revelation is the act of revealing or disclosing information.

  * In the context of Galatians 1, Paul's revelation means that he received his knowledge about the person of Christ directly from Him, not being taught it by another person.
  * Jesus revealed himself, meaning Jesus appeared to him and spoke to him audibly. This is the same thing that John experienced as recorded in the book of Revelation.
  * God's revelation in general refers to God showing that he exists, causing his power to be seen in his creation of the world and everything in it.
  * to reveal his Son in me; Jesus himself revealed it to me, God revealed to me that I should go
