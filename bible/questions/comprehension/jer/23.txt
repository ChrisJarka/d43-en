====== Jeremiah Chapter 23 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? How does God describe the shepherds in Jeremiah's day?

A. The shepherds scatter the sheep, drive them away and do not care for them. [23:1-2]

Q? What does God declare he will do for his flock?

A. He will gather them, return them to a grazing place and the will be fruitful. He will raise up shepherds they need not fear. [23:3-4]

Q? What does God say about the coming future?

A. He will raise up a righteous branch who will reign as king. They will propser and he will carry out justice and Israel will be secure. [23:5-6]

Q? Rather than Yahweh bringing the people out of Egypt what will they say?

A. They will say Yahweh will bring them back from other lands so they can stay on their own soil. [23:1-8]

Q? Why is Jeremiah's heart broken?

A. Because of Yahweh and his holy words. [23:9]

Q? Do to the prophets and priests wickness what does Yahweh declare?

A. They will be pushed down and disater will come against them. [23:11-12]

Q? What are the offensiveness of the prophets?

A. They prophesied by Baal, led Yajwe's people off the right path, committed adultery and walked in the way of deceit and encouraged evildoing. [23:13-14]

Q? What does God make these prophets do?

A. He makes them eat wormwood and drink poisonous water. [23:15]

Q? Why does Yahweh say do not listen to the prophets?

A. Because they have deluded you. Their visions are from their oun minds, not from Yahweh's mouth. [23:16]

Q? How does the prophet discribe Yahweh's fury?

A. His fury is a storm, a tempest whirling around the head of the wicked. [23:19]

Q? What does Yahweh say is wrong with these prophets?

A. They just appered he did not send them. He did not proclaim to them they just prophesied. They did not go to his council meeting, so they did not cause his people to hear his word. [23:21-22]

Q? By asking questions what does Yahweh say about himself?

A. He is a God near and far, that he can see in every secret place, he is a God who fills the heavens and the earth. [23:23-24]

Q? What are the dreams of the deceitful prophets planning to do?

A. They are planning on making my people forget my name in favor of Baal's name. [23:25-27]
