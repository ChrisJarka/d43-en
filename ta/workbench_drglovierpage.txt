==== Intro to the translationStudio App ====

The translationStudio mobile app is a mobile app where translators can do offline translating. It can be downloaded for Android (see [[https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.translationstudio.androidapp&hl=en|https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.translationstudio.androidapp&hl=en]] ) devices. It contains the content that needs to be translated as well as translationHelps (see [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_help|Help with Translating]]). Information can be shared directly from device to device and finished content can be uploaded to Door43 and digitally published.
