===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **to be baptized**  - That is, "for John to baptize them."
    * **Do not collect****more money**- That is, "do not ask for more money." This has the idea of "begin not to collect more tax money and continue this practice." The tax collectors were collecting more money than they should. They should do this as proof of their repentance.
    * **than you are supposed to**- That is, "than you are required to."
