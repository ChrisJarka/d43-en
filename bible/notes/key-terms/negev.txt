====== Negev ======

===== Facts: =====

The Negev is a desert region in the southern part of Israel. 

  * The Jewish tribes of Judah and Simeon lived in the Negev. 
  * The largest city in the Negev region is Beersheeba.
  * Abraham lived in the Negev region in Kadesh.
