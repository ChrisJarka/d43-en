===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **grew more and more **- "became richer and richer" or "prospered increasingly."
  * **The man became rich, and grew more and more until he became very great.**  - "Isaac became rich, and kept growing richer until he became very powerful."
  * **became very great **- "became very rich" or "became very wealthy."
  * **large household**  - "large staff"
  * **many sheep and cattle**  - "possessions of sheep and cattle."
  * **He had many sheep and cattle, and a large household. The Philistines envied him.**  - "Becaue he had many sheep and cattle, and a large staff, the Philistines envied him."
