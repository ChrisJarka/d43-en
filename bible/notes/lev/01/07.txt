===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **feed the fire**  - keep the fire burning (See: Idioms)
  * **the priests, are to place the pieces, the head and the fat**  - 'The pieces, the head and the fat' are parts of the animal sacrifice. (See: Implicit-Explicit)
  * **its inward parts and its legs**  - 'Its' refers to the animal in the fire for sacrifice.
  * **for me…..to me**  - to Yahweh
  * **an offering made to me by fire**  - AT: "a burnt offering to Yahweh" (See: Active - Passive)
