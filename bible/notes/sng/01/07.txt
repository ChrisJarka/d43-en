===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **whom my soul loves **- The woman is describing to the man that she not only loves him with her physical, mortal body, but also with her eternal. spiritual soul.
  * **where do you feed your flock?…noontime? **- Because of her great love for the man, she wants to know where he is and what he is doing. She wants to be with him.
  * **Why should I be like someone who wanders…flocks of your companions? **- The woman asks the man to tell her where he goes and what he does in  because they are lovers, not just casual friends. (See: Rhetorical Question)
