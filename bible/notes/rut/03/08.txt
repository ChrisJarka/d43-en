===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **your female servant**  -
  * **Spread your cloak over your female servant**  -
  * **near kinsman**  - Or "redeemer" in the extended family who could marry a childless widow and have a baby with her to bear her dead husband's name.
  * **it came to pass **- Or "it happened."
  * **at midnight**  - Or "in the middle of the night."
  * **was startled **- Or "was frightened" or "trembled."
