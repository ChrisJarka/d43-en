===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **My, I, me **- David is speaking.
  * **you **- Refers to God.
  * **depths of the earth**  - See metaphor
  * **recorded in your book**  - Ex. and Rev. 17 - a book of remembrance lists our names from the foundations of the world.
