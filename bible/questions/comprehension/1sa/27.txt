====== 1 Samuel Chapter 27 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why did David think that he needed to escape into the land of the Philistines?

A. David felt that escaping to the land of the Philistines was the only way he could escape Saul. [27:1]

Q? What made Saul no longer look for David, his men and their households and David's wives.

A. Saul heard that David and those with him had fled to Gath, so he no longer looked for them. [27:2-4]

Q? What did David ask and get from Achish the Philistine?

A. David asked for a place in one of the cities in the country, if he has found favor, and Achish gave him Ziklag. [27:5-6]

Q? What did David and his men do with the men and women, animals and clothing from the raids on the nations as far as Egypt.

A. David and his men killed the people and took the animals and clothing and then returned to Achish. .[27:8-9]

Q? How would David answer Achish when he returned from a raid?

A. David said that he raided the south of Judah, south of Jerahmeelites or south of the Kenites so that Achish thought he attacked Israel. [27:10]

Q? Why did David keep no people alive and bring them to Gath?

A. David killed everyone so that they would be unable to tell what David had done. [27:11]

Q? Why did Achish think that David has made his people of Israel utterly abhor David, and David would be his servant forever?

A. Achish believed David's lies that he had raided the people of Israel which made he think the people of Israel would utterly abhor David. [27:12]
