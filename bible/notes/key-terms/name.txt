====== name ======


===== Definition: =====


The word "name" in the Bible can refer to the word that identifies a particular person or place, such Abraham (the name of particular man), or Egypt (the name of a particular country.


    * The word "name" can also refer to a person's reputation.
    * Speaking in the name of God represents his power and authority.
