====== boast, boastful ======

===== Definition: =====

To boast is to talk about one's self or one's status in a prideful and arrogant way. Often boasting is meant to make one person seem better than another.

  * The Bible also uses the word in a positive sense, as a synonym for praise as in "Let him who boasts, boast in the LORD."
