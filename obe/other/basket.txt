====== basket ======

===== Definition: =====

The term  "basket" refers to a container made of woven material.

  * In biblical times, baskets were probably woven with strong plant materials, like wood.
  * A basket could be covered with a waterproof substance so that it could float.
  * The basket that Moses' mother put him in as a baby is the same word that is used to refer to Noah's "ark."

(See also: [[:en:obe:other:ark]], )
