===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **He...you** - the word "He" refers to the enemy army coming to attack Nineveh.  The word "you" refers to Nineveh. 
    * **will dash you to pieces**  - This phrase compares the destruction of Nineveh to breaking a pot into many pieces. Alternate translation: "will destroy you" (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
    * **Man the city walls**  - This means the city should set guards on the walls for protection. 
    * **Man the city walls, guard the roads, make yourselves strong, assemble your armies.**  - All of these phrases mean Nineveh should prepare for war.  It is said many times to show how important the warning is.  Alternate translation: "prepare for war" (See: [[en:ta:translate:parallelism]])
    * **For Yahweh is restoring the majesty of Jacob, like the majesty of Israel**  - This means that Yahweh will make Jacob and Israel into great and admired nations again. 
    * **and destroyed their vines**  - Vines are parts of plants that produce grapes and are used to make wine. They are used to refer to Jacob and Israel's source of wine. (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
