===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **people**  - The word "people" refers to the Israelites.
    * **him**  - the word "him" refers to the Babylonian/Chaldeans.
    * **Woe to the one increasing what is not his! For how long will you increase your the weight of the pledges you have taken**  - "How much longer will you live well at other people's expense" (See: [[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:rhetorical-question]])
