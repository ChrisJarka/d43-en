====== kiss ======

===== Definition: =====

A kiss is an endearing action whereby one person presses their lips to another person's lips or face.

  * Some cultures kiss each other's face as a form of greeting.
  * A kiss can communicate deep love for another person, such as a spouse.
  * Some cultures kiss each other goodbye, as when Orpah kissed Naomi.
