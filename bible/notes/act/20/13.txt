===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **We ourselves went**  - "Ourselves" adds emphasis and separates Luke and his traveling companions from Paul, who did not travel by boat. (see: [[:en:ta:translate:reflexpronoun|Reflexive Pronoun]] and [[:en:ta:translate:exclusive|Exclusive]])
  * **sailed away to Assos**  - Assos is a town located directly below present day Behram, Turkey. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
  * **he himself desired **- "Paul desired" (see: [[:en:bible:notes:act:20:en:ta:translate:reflexpronoun|<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Reflexive pronoun</font>    ]] )
  * **we took him**  - (See: [[:en:ta:translate:exclusive|Exclusive]])
  * **went to Mitylene**  - (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]])
