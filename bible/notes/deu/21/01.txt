===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses is still speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **If someone is found killed**  - The author gives a hypothetical example of a killing. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_hypo]])
  * **If someone is found killed**  - AT: "If someone finds a dead person." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) 
  * **lying in the field**  - "a dead person who is lying in a field"
  * **and it is not known who has attacked him**  - AT: "and nobody knows who attacked him."
  * **who has attacked him**  - This is a polite way to say that the man killed him. AT: "who has killed him." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **and they must measure to the cities that are around him who has been killed** - "and they must measure the distance from the dead body to the cities that are close by" or "and they must measure how far it is from the dead body to the cities that are near by"
