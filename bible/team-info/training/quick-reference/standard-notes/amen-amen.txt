====== Amen, amen ======

===== Description =====

The repeated "amen" appears often in John. It is normally translated in English as "Truly, truly."


===== Standard Notes =====

  * **Truly, truly** - This is a way of marking emphasis, (“Listen! This is important!”) Translate it however you would add emphasis to an important phrase in your language. (See: Idiom)
