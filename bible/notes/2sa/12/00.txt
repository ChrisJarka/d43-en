====== 2 Samuel 12 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This is the final extension of David's kingdom (5-12), the end of the war with Ammon (10-12).

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== David's punishment ==
God sent a prophet to confront David. He said that because David had Uriah killed, there would always be killing in his family. Also his baby would die. David prayed hard that the baby be allowed to live but he died. Then Bathsheba had another son who was named Soloman.
== The capital of Ammon captured ==
The Army of Israel captured the capital of Ammon. They took much loot and forced the people to work hard for them. 


=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metonymy ==
The prophet used the [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] of __sword__ for killing and __house__ for family: "So now the sword will never leave your house."
