====== destroy, blot out ======

===== Definition: =====

To "blot out" or "destroy" means to completely get rid of something or someone.

  * Blot out means to remove something completely.
      * It can be used in a positive sense, as when God "blots out" sins by forgiving them and choosing not to remember them. One way to translate this could be, "erase."
      * It can be used in a negative sense, as when God "blots out" a people group, destroying them because of their sin.
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Destroy can mean to "ruin," "kill," or "break into pieces."</font>
