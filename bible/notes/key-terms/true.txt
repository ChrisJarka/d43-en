====== true, truth ======

===== Definition: =====

The terms "true" and "truth" are used to talk about things that are facts, things that actually happened, and statements that were actually said.

  * True things are real, genuine, actual, rightful, legitimate, or factual.
  * The truth is an understanding, belief, fact, or statement that is true.
  * Truth is all that God is and all that God says in his word, the Bible.
  * Jesus revealed God's truth in the words that he spoke.
  * The opposite of "true" is false. The opposite of "truth" is deception, or a lie.
  * The term "true" could also be translated by "real," "factual," "correct," "right," or "certain."
  * The term "truth" could also be translated by "fact," "certainty," or "principle."
