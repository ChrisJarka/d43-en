===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Truly, truly**  - This is an idiom marking emphasis. Translate it however you would add emphasis to an important phrase in your language. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
    * **born again**  - In the original language, this word can mean "born again" or "born from above" ("born spiritually"), and it is not clear which one Jesus means here. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ambiguity]])
    * **How can a man be born when he is old?**  - This is a rhetorical question which Nicodemus uses for emphasis. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **He cannot enter a second time into his mother's womb as be born, can he?**  - This is a rhetorical question which Nicodemus uses for emphasis. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **a second time** - The word "second" means times 2. Alternate Translation: "again" or "twice" 
    * **womb**  - the part of a woman's body where a baby grows. Alternate Translation: "uterus" or "belly"
