===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus went to the hill to teach his followers.

  * **opened his mouth **- This refers to Jesus beginning to speak.
  * **taught them**  - Jesus taught his followers.
  * **Blessed - **  This means that God is pleased.
  * **are the poor in spir**it - This means one who is humble or without pride.
  * **those who mourn - ** These are people who are sad because of sin.
  * **they will be comforted**  - This could be translated with an active phrase: "God will comfort them." (See: Active)
