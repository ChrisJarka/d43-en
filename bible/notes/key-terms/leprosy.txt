====== leprosy, leper ======


===== Definition: =====


In the Bible, "Leprosy" is a term used for many different, incurable skin diseases.  When stricken with one of these skin diseases, a person was considered unclean.


    * A "leper" is a person with leprosy.
    * People with leprosy were isolated in areas outside of the encampment or town.
    * Today, Leprosy is a chronic, curable, infectious disease that affects the skin and nerves, frequently resulting in paralysis and deformity.
