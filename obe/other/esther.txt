====== Esther ======

===== Facts: =====

An Israelite woman who became Queen to King Ahasuerus of Persia in 482 B.C.

  * Esther's life demonstrates God's love and protection for His people even during their exile period
  * The story of Esther illustrates God's sovreign control over the events of history, causing all things to work together for the good of those who love Him.
