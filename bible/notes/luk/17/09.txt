===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **unworthy servants**- This is, literally, "useless slaves." The word here can also be translated as "unfit, or unprofitable."
    * **he does not thank**  - Literally, "he doesn't have favor towards."
    * **Even so**  - That is, "In the same way".
    * **he does not thank... does he?**  - this is a question expecting a negative answer.
