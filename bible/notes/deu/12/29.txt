===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **cuts off the nations before you**  - "destroys the nations before you"
  * **go in to dispossess them**  - "go in to drive them out"
  * **pay attention to yourself**  - "be careful" or "beware"
  * **you are not trapped into following **- "you don't fall into the trap" or "you are not ensnared or caught-up into following" - This means worshiping other gods would lead the people of Israel to ruin like an animal caught in a hunter's trap . (See: Metaphor )
  * **into investigation their gods**  - "into inquiring about their gods"
