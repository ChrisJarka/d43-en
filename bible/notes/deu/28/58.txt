===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **glorious and fearful name **- honored and awesome (See: Doublets)
  * **Yahweh will make your plagues terrible**  - AT: "Yahweh will make your plagues extraordinary " or "Yahweh will make surpassing plagues"
  * **of long duration **- this phrase appears twice here, this longlasting effect also means certainty in moral sense.
