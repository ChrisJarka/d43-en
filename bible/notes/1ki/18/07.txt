===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Is it you, my master Elijah?** - Obadiah had already bowed down and knew it was Elijah. He is using this question to express surprise. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **Look** - This word is used to draw attention to what he has to say.
    * **Go tell your master, 'Look, Elijah is here'"** - AT: "Go tell Ahab that I am here." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations]])
