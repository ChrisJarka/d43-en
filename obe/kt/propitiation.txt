====== propitiation ======

===== Definition: =====

A propitiation is the substitute offering that satisfies God's holy anger.

  * The offering of the sacrificial blood of Jesus Christ is the propitiation to God for mankind's sins. Jesus bore the sins of believers, and God's wrath for those sins, on the cross.
