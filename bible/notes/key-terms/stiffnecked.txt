====== stiff-necked ======

===== Definition: =====

To be 'stiff-necked' is to have an attitude of continual disobedience to God and a refusal to respond to His correction.

  * The Israelites were stiff-necked in their attitude toward the Prophets who foretold the coming of the Messiah.
