== Barak and Deborah (Judges 4-5) ==

Barak fought Sisera and defeated him.


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== A woman killed Sisera ==

Barak said to Deborah, "If you go with me, I will go, but if you do not go with me, I will not go." She said, "I will certainly go with you. However, the road on which you are going will not lead to your honor, for Yahweh will make a woman defeat Sisera by her strength."
