====== Salt Sea ======

===== Facts: =====

Granular salt which is extracted from sea water.

  * Sea salt was used in ancient times as a source of preservation for food products.
  * The Hebrews used salt in their offerings to God under the Mosaic law.
  * The Mosaic law also had a "covenant of salt" where a newborn child was rubbed with salt for symbolic dedication purposes.
