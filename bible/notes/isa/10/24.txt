===== Translation Notes: =====
  * **the Assyrian** - This may refer to 1) the army of Assyria, 2) the people of Assyria, or 3) the king of Assyria.
  * **He will strike you with the rod and raise his staff against you** - Possible translations are 1) "They will attack you with rods and sticks" or 2) 
"The king will send his army to attack you with rods and clubs.
  * **as the  Egyptians did** - The full meaning can be made explicit: "as the Egyptians struck your ancestors many years ago." (See: [[en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]])
