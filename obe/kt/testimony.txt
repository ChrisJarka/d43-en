====== testimony, testify ======

===== Definition: =====

Testimony is the evidence provided to support a fact or claim. It can be verbal or written. It also refers to a statement that someone makes about something they have experienced and know to be true.

  * In the first five books of the Old Testament, the term "Testimony" is used to refer to the two tablets of the Ten Commandments
  * "false testimony" against your neighbor
  * In the remainder of the Old Testament, testimony is used to refer to God's rules, statutes, and laws.
  * In the New Testament, testimony is used of evidence which bears witness of the life and works of Christ as facts, and as a witness of that living truth in the life of Christ followers. It sometimes has a negative sense for those who do not want to accept this truth.
