===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For I rejoice in the Law of God with the inner man**  - "The new me, that has been made spiritually alive by Jesus and desires to do God's, rejoices in the Law of God".
  * **But I see a different principle in my body parts, warring against that new principle**  - This describes the conflict present between the new spiritually alive nature and the old sinful nature. See: (UDB). See: ([[:en:ta:translate:metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:translate:synecdoche]])
