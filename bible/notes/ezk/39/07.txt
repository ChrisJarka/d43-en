===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For I will make my holy name known in the midst of Israel -  **  Alternate translation: "I will cause my Israelite people to know that I am holy" or "I will enable my Israelite people to know that I am holy" (UDB).
  * **I will no longer allow my holy name to be profaned. - ** Alternate translation: "I will stop them from damaging my repupution" or "I will no longer allow people to damage my reputation." (UBD)
  * **Behold–**  Standard notes: adds emphasis to what follows.
  * **This day that I have declared is coming and it will take place–this is the Lord Yahweh's declaration. - ** Alternate translation: "I, Yahweh the Lord, declare that the day when these things will happen is coming."
