====== everlasting, forever, eternal ======

===== Definition: =====

Bascially these three terms have the same meaning. Something that will always exist is said to be everlasting, forever, or eternal.

  * Some English versions use "eternal" to refer to God and "everlasting" to refer to concepts such as "everlasting covenant" or "everlasting possession."
  * The word "eternal" or "everlasting" describes the existence of God with no beginning and no end. He exists outside of time.
  * The word "eternal" or "everlasting" is used in the New Testament to refer to believers living forever in heaven.
