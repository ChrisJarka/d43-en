===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **navel**- a small indention or raised area in the middle of a person's stomach which is formed at birth.
  * **is like a round bowl**- The woman's navel is indented and the lover thinks it has a perfect shape. (See:Simile)
  * **may it never lack mixed wine - **  Since wine mixed with water was served with meals, the lover does not want his beloved to be wihout food or drink. (See: Personification)
  * **belly is like a mound of wheat**- A handful of wheat kernels which are pale in color can be formed into a small pile. By comparing the mound of the Beloved's belly to wheat (which is a food staple), he shows his love for her. (See: Simile)
  * **encircled with lilies**- The beauty of the beloved is compared to the beauty of flowers.(See: Metaphor)
