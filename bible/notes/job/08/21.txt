===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he will yet fill your mouth with laughter**
 - This expresses the same idea as "your lips with shouting". Bildad is telling Job that God will once again make him happy. AT: "God will make you happy again". (See: Parallelism)
  * **he **- This pronoun refers to God
  * **your mouth**
 - Here, "your" refers to Job.
  * **shouting**  - This is an expression of happiness. AT: "shouts of joy."
  * **those who hate you**  - AT: "your enemies".
  * **<font 14px line-height: 19px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>clothed with shame</font>    **- AT: "It will seam like your enemies are clothed with shame". (See: Metaphore)
  * **will be no more**  - AT: "will not last," or "will come to nothing". The wicked will cease to exist.
