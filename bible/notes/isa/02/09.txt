===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - In 2:9 Isaiah was speaking to God. In 2:10-11 Isaiah was speaking to the people.

  * **The people will be bowed down, and individuals will fall down** - The phrases used together mean the same thing and are used together for emphasis. This can be stated in active form. AT: "God will make very person made to bow down and be humble" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **therefore do not accept them** - Here Isaiah is talking to Yahweh.
  * **Go into the rocky places** - Here Isaiah is again talking to the people of Judah.
  * **hide in the ground** - "hide in the holes in the ground"
  * **from the terror of Yahweh** - This can be reworded so that the abstract noun "terror" is expressed as "terrifying." AT: "to get away from Yahweh's terrifying presence" or "from Yahweh because you will be extremely afraid of him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]]) 
  * **the glory of his majesty** - "his awesome power" or "his great power" 
  * **The lofty gaze of man will be brought low, and the pride of men will be brought down** - The phrases used together mean the same thing and are used together for emphasis. This can be stated in active form. AT: "He will make every prideful person humble" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Yahweh alone will be exalted** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "people will praise only Yahweh" (See:[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **on that day** - This refers to the time when Yahweh will judge all people. AT: "on the day that Yahweh comes to judge everyone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
