===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **My days are past** - AT: "My lifetime is finished." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **my plans are over** - "my plans will never happen"
  * **even the wishes of my heart** - AT: "even the things that I wished for to myself will never happen." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]]) 
  * **These people** - Job is probably referring to his three friends, Eliphaz, Bildad, and Zophar.
