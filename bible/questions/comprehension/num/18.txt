====== Numbers Chapter 18 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? For what did Yahweh say that he held only Aaron that his sons and Aaron's ancestors responsible?

A. Yahweh told Aaron that his sons and his ancestor's clan would be responsible for all sins committed against the sanctuary. [18:1]

Q? Who did God say would be responsible for all sins committed by anyone in the priesthood? 

A. Only he and his sons would be responsible for all sins committed by anyone in the priesthood. [18:1]

Q? Which tribe would help Aaron and his sons when they served in front of the tent of the covenant decrees?

A. The tribe of Levi, Aaron's ancestor's tribe, would join and help Aaron and his sons when they served in front of the tent of the covenant decrees. [18:2]

Q? Where could the Levites not go as they served Aaron and the tent?

A.The Levites could not go near anything in the sanctuary nor  the altar. [18:3]

Q? What would happen if the Levites came near to anything in the sanctuary or altar?

A. If the Levites came near to anything in the sanctuary or altar, they and Aaron would die. [18:3] 

Q? Who could not come near Aaron?

A. A foreigner could not come near Aaron. [18:4]

Q? For what would the Levites take responsibility?

A. The Levites would join Aaron to take care of the tent of meeting and all the work connected with the tent.  [18:4]

Q? Why did Aaron and his sons have to take care of the sanctuary and the altar?

A. Aaron and his sons had to take care of the sanctuary and the altar so that Yahweh's anger did not come on the people of Israel again. [18:5]

Q? What gifts did Yahweh give to Aaron and his sons and why did he give them?

A. Yahweh chose the Levites as a gift to Aaron, given to Yahweh to do the work connected to the tent of meeting. [18:6]

Q? Who only were allowed to exercise the priesthood regarding everything connected with the altar and everything inside the curtain?

A. Only Aaron and his sons could exercise the priesthood regarding everything connected with the altar and everything inside the curtain. [18:7]

Q? What would happen to any foreigner who approached the altar of Yahweh inside the curtain?

A. Any foreigner who approached the altar of Yahweh inside the curtain mwould be put to death. [18:7]

Q? What holy offerings to Yahweh would be given to Aaron and his sons as their ongoing share?

A. The things from the most holy offerings that people of Israel gave to Yahweh that were not completely burned up would be Aaron and his sons' ongoing share. [18:8-9]

Q? What other offerings would be for him and his sons?

A. Every sacrifice that the people brought--including every grain offering, every sin offering, and every guilt offering--would be for Aaron and his sons. [18:9]

Q? How was every male Levite to eat those offerings?

A. Every male Levite was to eat those offerings as something most holy, solemnly reserved for Yahweh. [18:10]

Q? To whom did Yahweh give, as their ongoing share, the gifts set apart from all the raised offerings of the people of Israel?

A. Yahweh gave the gifts set apart from all the raised offerings of the people of Israel to Aaron's ritually clean sons and daughters as their ongoing share. [18:11]

Q? What other things was given to Aaron's family?

A. All the best of the oil, the new wine, the grain, the firstfruits that the people gave to Yahweh, and the first ripe produce of their land that the people brought to Yahweh would belong to Aaron and those that were clean in his family. [18:12-14]

Q? What must the Israelites certainly buy back from Aaron and his family?

A. The people of Israel are to buy back every firstborn son, and the firstborn male of unclean animals because all the firstborn which the people offer to Yahweh, both of man and animal, will be Aaron's family's. [18:15]

Q? At what time are the Israelites to buy back the firstborn male?

A. After becoming one month old, the people may buy the firstborn male of unclean animals back. [18:16]

Q? What animals are not to be bought back, but are reserved for Yahweh and made into a sacrifice which Aaron and his sons will eat?

A. The firstborns of a cow, a sheep, or a goat must not be bought back because they are holy, reserved for Yahweh and their meat will be Aaronâ€™s. [18:17-18]

Q? What does Yahweh say will be given to Aaron, his sons and his daughters as an ongoing share, because these offerings stand as a binding covenant forever, before Yahweh with Aaron and his descendants?

A. Yahweh gave all the presented offerings of holy things to Aaron, Aaron's sons and Aaron's daughters, as an ongoing share. They stand as a binding covenant forever, before Yahweh with Aaron and Aaron's descendants."[18:19]

Q? Who does Yahweh tell Aaron will not have an inheritance among the people of Israel?

A. Yahweh told Aaron that he and all the descendants of Levi have no inheritance in the peoples land because Yahweh gave the tithes of Israel as their inheritance in return for their service. [18:20-21]

Q? Who does Yahweh tell Aaron will die from now on if they come near Yahweh's tent of meeting?

A. Yahweh tells Aaron that from now on the people of Israel must not come near the tent of meeting, or they will be responsible for this sin and die. [18:22]
