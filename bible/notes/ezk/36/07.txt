===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:05:05|5:5]].
  * **will lift up my hand to swear** - "have lifted up my hand to swear." This phrase means "I have sworn." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **that the nations that surround you will certainly carry their own shame** - This can be translated as "that other nations will certainly mock the nations that surround you."
  * **surround you** - The word "you" refers to the mountains of Israel.
