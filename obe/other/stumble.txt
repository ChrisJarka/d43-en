====== stumble ======

===== Definition: =====

To "stumble" literally means to almost fall when walking or running. Usually it involves tripping over something.

  * Figuratively, "stumble" means to sin or to falter in one's faith.
  * The literal meaning of stumbling is that it is not a complete fall.
  * In a figurative sense, stumbling means to endanger your faith through sinning.
  * If possible it is best to literally translate this term or to use a different idiom in the language that communicates the same meaning.

(See also: [[stumblingblock|stumbling block]] other)
