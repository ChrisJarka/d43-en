====== lord, Lord, master ======

===== Definition: =====

"Lord" is a title for someone who has ownership or authority over other people.

  * In the Old Testament of many English versions, the term LORD is used to translate God's personal name "Yahweh,"
  * The word for "lord" can also be translated as "master" or "ruler."
  * God is often called "Lord" or "the Lord" because he has ultimate authority over all people and all creation.
