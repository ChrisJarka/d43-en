===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **a land of**  - The land is going to be described by its water and food abundance. AT: "a land with plenty of" or "a land abundant with."
  * **into valleys and among hills**  - The abundance of water would be present from the lowest (valleys) to the highest (hills) locations in the land.
  * **barley**  - a grain
  * **pomegranates **- a fruit
