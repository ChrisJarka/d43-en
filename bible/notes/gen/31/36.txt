===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **ULB Genesis 31:36 was angry**  - "became angry."
  * **ULB Genesis 31:36 and argued**  - "and quarreled"
  * **ULB Genesis 31:36 you have hotly pursued after me?**  - "you have been in hot pursuit of me?"
  * **ULB Genesis 31:37 For you have searched all my possessions**  - "Now that you have gone through all my possessions"
  * **ULB Genesis 31:37 What have you found of all your household goods?**  - "What have you found that belongs to you?"
  * **ULB Genesis 31:37 Set them here before our relatives**  - "Lay anything you have found in front of our relatives"
  * **ULB Genesis 31:37 they may judge between us two**  - "they may judge between the two of us"
