====== Ramah ======

===== Facts: =====

Ramah was an ancient Israelite city in Benjamin near Gibeon.

  * When Israel was taken captive to Babylon, the Jews were sent to Ramah first before moving to Babylon.
  * Ramah was the home of Samuel's mother and father.
