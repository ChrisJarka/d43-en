===== unfoldingWord Mobile App =====

The unfoldingWord mobile app is a mobile app where checked unfoldingWord translations are published and distributed. It can be used on [[https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.unfoldingword.androidapp&hl=en|Android]] or [[https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/unfoldingword/id925570688?mt=8|iOS]] devices.
