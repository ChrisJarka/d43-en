===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the account of the forced labor which King Solomon imposed** - "the account of Solomon requiring men to work"
  * **the Millo** - Possible meanings are 1) "the terrace system" or 2) "the landfill."
  * **Pharaoh king of Egypt had gone up** - The person is a metonym for the army he commands. AT: "The army of Pharaoh, king of Egypt, had gone up" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
