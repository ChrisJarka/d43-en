===== translationNotes: =====

  * **completely amazed at him** - "completely amazed." If you need to be more specific, it can stated what they were amazed by. AT: "completely amazed at what he had done" (UDB)
  * **but their minds were slow to understand** - The disciples did yet fully understand Jesus' power. AT: "for they did not understand how powerful he was" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **what the loaves meant** - The phrase "the loaves" refers to when Jesus had multiplied the loaves of bread. This can be stated more clearly. AT: "what it meant when he had multiplied the loaves of bread" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
