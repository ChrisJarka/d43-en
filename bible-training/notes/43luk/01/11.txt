===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **of the Lord ** - That is, "from the Lord" or, "who serves the Lord" or, "who was sent by the Lord."
    * **appeared to him** - That is, "suddenly came to him" or "was suddenly there with Zechariah."
    * **Your prayer has been heard **- That is, "The Lord has heard your prayer" or, "The Lord has heard your request" or, "God has heard what you asked him for." The following is implied and could be added: "and will grant it." God did not just hear what Zechariah prayed for; he was also going to do it.
    * **will bear you a son** - That is, "will bear a son for you" or, "will give birth to your son."
    * **will call his name John** - That is, “will give him the name John” or, “will name him John.”
