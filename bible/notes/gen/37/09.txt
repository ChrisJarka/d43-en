===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **dreamed another dream**  - "had another dream"
  * **His father rebuked him. He said to him**  - "Israel scolded him, saying"
  * **What is this dream that you have dreamed?** - This is a [[en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]]. Israel was scolding Joseph. If your language would use a rhetorical question for that purpose, then use it. Otherwise you can translate this: "You should not dream about such things!"
  * "Will your mother and I and your brothers actually come to bow down to the ground to you?** - This is another [[en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]] which means: "It is not right that your mother and I and your brothers should bow down to the ground before you."
  * **bow down to the ground to you?**  -  "bow down to the ground before you?"
