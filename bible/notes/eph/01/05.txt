===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **destined us in advance for adoption **  - “determined in advance to adopt us”
  * **us**  - This pronoun refers to all believers.
  * **adoption **– This word refers to being placed in God’s family as an adult son or daughter rather than just a child.
  * **<font 10.5pt line-height: 107% color: rgb(51, 51, 51);/inherit;;rgb(51, 51, 51);;inherit>through Jesus Christ</font>    ** <font 10.5pt line-height: 107% color: rgb(51, 51, 51);/inherit;;rgb(51, 51, 51);;inherit>- This has the idea of a channel or way, showing that God brought believers into his family by way of the work of Jesus Christ.</font>
  * **he **– This pronoun refers to God in both verses.
  * **his beloved one **– This refers to Jesus Christ.
