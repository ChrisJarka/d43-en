===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh** - See how you translated this in 5:5.
  * **In the day when he went down to Sheol** - "On the day that Assyria died" or "On the day that Assyria was conquered".
  * **I covered the deep for him, and withheld its river** - "I made the waters mourn for her".
  * **languished**  - This means the trees continued for a long time in an unpleasent state.
