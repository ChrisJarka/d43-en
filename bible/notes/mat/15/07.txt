===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You hypocrites**- "You" refers to the Pharisees and scribes.
  * **well did Isaiah prophesy- ** Isaiah told the truth in his prophecy.
  * **he said- **  God speaking through Isaiah**.**
  * **This people**  - The Jewish ancestors who pretended to be good, but did not honor God.
  * **in vain**-"useless," "without effect"
  * **their doctrines the commands of people**-"Their teachings are rules taught by people."
