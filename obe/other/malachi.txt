====== Malachi ======

===== Facts: =====

Malachi was one of Israel's minor prophets around 420 B.C.

  * Malachi prophesied during the period when Israel's temple was being rebuilt after returning from the Babylonian captivity.
  * Malachi was most likely a contemporary of Nehemiah and Ezra.
