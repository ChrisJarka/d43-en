===== Translation Notes: =====

  * Jesus continues telling the parable of the kingdom of heaven.
  * **Then the king said to the servants, 'Bind this man…' **- This can be written as an indirect quote "Then the king told his servants to bind this man…." (See Indirect/Direct)
  * **outer darkness **- "hell"
  * **grinding of teeth **- An idiom that represents the painful response of those that God punishes. (See: Idiom)
