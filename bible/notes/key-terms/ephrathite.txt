====== Ephrathite ======

===== Facts: =====

An Ephrathite was an inhabitant of Ephrath, which was an ancient name for the town of Bethlehem in Judah. It is also sometimes translated as "Ephraimite."

  * In the Old Testament, Naomi was an Ephrathite, as was Jesse the father of King David.
