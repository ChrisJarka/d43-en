====== tribute ======

===== Definition: =====

A tribute is a payment or gift from one authority to another for good will or protection, or it could be a toll or a tax.

  * In Bible times, traveling kings or rulers might pay a tribute to the king of the region traveled through for good will and protection.
  * When Pharisees tried to trick Jesus asking if it were lawful to pay tribute, or taxes, to Caesar, Jesus acknowledged Caesar's right to assess and collect taxes.

(See also: other)
