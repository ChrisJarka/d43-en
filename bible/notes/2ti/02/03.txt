===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Suffer hardship with me**  - Possible meanings are 1) "endure suffering as I do." (see UDB) or 2) "share in my suffering."
  * **No soldier serves â€¦ competes by the rules**  - These are the first two of three metaphors (or parables) Paul gives Timothy. (The third is in verse 6. See: [[:en:ta:figuresofspeech:metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **No soldier serves while entangled in the affairs of this life**  - "No soldier serves while involved in the everyday business of this life" or "No soldier serves while unless he is set apart for his service"
  * **entangled** - This metaphor compares someone too busy to serve God to a person caught in a trap.
  * **superior officer**  - "the one who enlisted him as a soldier"
  * **he is not crowned**  - "he does not win the prize"
  * **competes by the rules**  - "strictly obeys the rules" or "strictly competes according to the rules"
