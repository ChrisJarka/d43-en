===== translationNotes: =====

  * **to God, who does all things for me** - AT: "I will cry to God, who does all things for me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]]) 
  * **the man who** - This probably refers to people in general. AT: "people who" or "my enemies who" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_genericnoun]])
  * **God will send out to me his covenant faithfulness and his trustworthiness** - God's being faithful and trustworthy is spoken of as if he were sending out his faithfulness and trustworthiness. AT: "God will be faithful and trustworthy to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
