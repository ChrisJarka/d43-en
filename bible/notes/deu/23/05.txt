===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **would not listen - ** This means that he did not pay attention, or he did not regard. (See: Idiom)
  * **Yahweh your God turned the curse into a blessing for you **- 'turned' means 'shifted' or 'changed' here. The incident refers to Numbers 23:11.
  * **peace or prosperity **- this refers to their general welfare (See: Merism)
  * **during all your days**  - AT: "all your days forever"
