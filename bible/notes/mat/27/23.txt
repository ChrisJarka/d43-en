===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **And he said, "Why, what crime has he done?"**
- "And Pilate said, 'Why, what crime has Jesus done?'"

  * **they**  - the crowd of people demanding Jesus' death
  * **but instead a riot was starting**
- "the people started to riot" or "the people cried out exceedingly" (See: Active/Passive)

  * **he took water, washed his hands **- This was a symbolic body gesture which showed that Pilate felt that he was not responsible for this judgment. (See UDB)
  * **innocent of the blood**  - "not responsible for shedding this innocent man's blood"
