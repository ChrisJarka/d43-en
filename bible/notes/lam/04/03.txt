===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the jackals reveal the breast to nurse their cubs**  - This means that female jackals freely allow their babies to receive milk from them.
  * **jackals**  - These are small wild dogs that people thought were mean and despicable.
  * **the daughter of my people**  - This is a Semitic way to speak of the women of Jerusalem.
  * **cruel as the ostrich in the desert**  - The ostrich is a large bird that people thought abandoned their eggs after they laid them. AT: "cruel as a bird that lays eggs and then abandons her babies." (See: Simile)
