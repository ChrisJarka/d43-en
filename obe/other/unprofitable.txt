====== unprofitable ======

===== Definition: =====

To be useless or fruitless.

  * The Apostle Paul's disciple named Philemon was said to be an unprofitable servant prior to becoming a Christian.
  * In a New Testament parable Jesus called the unredeemed people of the world "unprofitable servants" because their lives do not give glory to God.

(See also: other)
