====== endure, endurance ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "endure" means to bear something difficult with patience. It also means to stand firm when times of testing come, without giving up.

  * In the Bible, "enduring to the end", as in Luke 21, or "enduring" in the face of suffering, frequently refers to the concept of remaining loyal to the Lord and committed to following him, even if this loyalty causes a person to suffer.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * The term "endure" can be translated as "persevere" or "keep believing" or "continue to do what God wants you to do" or "stand firm."
  * Ways to translate "endurance" could include, "perseverance" or "continuing to believe" or "remaining faithful."
