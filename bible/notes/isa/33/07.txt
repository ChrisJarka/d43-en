===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Look** - This is to get their attention. "See what is happening"
  * **their envoys cry in the streets; the diplomats hoping for peace weep bitterly** - "Israel's messengers have tried to make peace with Assyria.  They will cry bitterly because they did not succeed" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **Treaties are broken** - "The rulers of Assyria have broken their peace treaty with Israel"
  * **witnesses are despised** - The Assyrians hate the people who made the peace treaties.
