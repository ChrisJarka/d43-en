====== Lebanon ======

===== Facts: =====

Lebanon is a beautiful mountainous region located directly north of Israel.

  * King Solomon sent laborers in shifts to Lebanon to harvest cedar trees for the construction of God's temple.
  * Ancient Lebanon was inhabited by Phoenician people.
