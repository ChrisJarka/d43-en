===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Remember Lot's wife**  - That is, "Think about what happened to the Lot's wife," or "Keep in mind what happened to the wife of Lot."
    * **Whoever seeks**-Or, "whoever investigates for ways" or "strongly desires"
    * **Whoever seeks to gain his life will lose it**  - That is, "Whoever tries to secure his life for himself will lose it" or "if a person tries to save his life and possessions, he will lose it all."
    * **Loses**-Or, "destroys", to give one's life & desires up to destruction in favor of God's will
    * **but whoever loses his life will save it**- That is, "but if a person is willing to lose his life because of me, he will save it."
