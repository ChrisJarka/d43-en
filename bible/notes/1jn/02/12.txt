===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **my little children **- This is a metaphor referring to the "followers of Jesus." (See: Metaphor)
  * **I write to you, fathers**  - This is a metaphor referring to "more mature believers in Jesus." (See: Metaphor)
  * **I have written to you, little children**  - This is a metaphor referring to "people who are immature in their belief in Jesus." (See: Metaphor)
  * **young men, because you are strong**  - This is a metaphor which means "because you are faithful." (See: Mataphor)
  * **overcome **- This means to conquer or win a challenge.
