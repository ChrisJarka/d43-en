====== gift ======

===== Definition: =====

A gift is something that is given from one person to another person for free.

  * The gift of salvation is something God gives us in the form of Jesus, His Son, who took our punishment on the cross.
  * A common practice in biblical times that we still do today is to give money to the poor.
