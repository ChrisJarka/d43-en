====== Timothy ======

===== Facts: =====

Timothy was a young man from Lystra who accepted Christ on Paul's first missionary trip and who joined several of Paul's ministry travels.

  * His mother Eunice was a Jew and a believer in Jesus, but his father was a Greek.
  * The elders and Paul formally appointed Timothy for the ministry by placing their hands on him and praying for him.
  * Two books in the New Testament, I and II Timothy, are written by Paul providing guidance to Timothy.
