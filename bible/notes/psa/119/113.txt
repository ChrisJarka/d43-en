===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **ZAYIN:** This is the fifteenth letter of the Hebrew alphabet. In the Hebrew language, each line of verses 113-120 begins with this letter.
  * **double mind**  - This means they can not make up their mind. They are not sincere.
  * **my hiding place**  - This means God is like a place where we can hide from our enemies.(See: Metaphor)
  * **my shield**  - This means God is like a shield that protects us from our enemies. (See: Metaphor)
