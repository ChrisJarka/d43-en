===== Translation Notes: =====

Verse 29 is a summary of of this chapter.

    * **God did not forget Abraham**  - "God remembered Abraham" or "God remembered what he had promised Abraham."  This tells why God rescued Lot.
    * **out of the midst of the destruction**  - This can be translated as "away from the destruction" or "out of the area that was being destroyed" or "away from danger."
