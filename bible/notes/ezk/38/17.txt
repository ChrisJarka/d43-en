===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Are you the one I spoke of in former days**  - Lord Yahweh is speaking to Gog to remind him that His prophets have warned Israel about the coming battle.
  * **My fury will arise in my nostrils**  - This term is a metaphor to explain how in time, God himself will defend Israel. He will be a divine warrior who will completely  destroy Gog and his armies.
