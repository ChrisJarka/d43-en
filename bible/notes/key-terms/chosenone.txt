====== chosen one ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Bible, the term "chosen one" usually refers to Jesus as the one whom God appointed to be the Messiah.

  * God also refers to his people as being chosen by him. Certain leaders were also called "chosen one."The people of God, individuals and certain leaders are also referred to "chosen."
  * To be chosen is to be selected or appointed for a special purpose.
  * Sometimes English Bible versions translate the word "chosen" as "elect."

(See also: Christ, Messiah, elect)
