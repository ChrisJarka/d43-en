  * **Amram, Izhar, Hebron ... Uzziel** - Translate the names of these men the same as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:23:12|23:12]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Shebuel ... Gershom ... Eliezer ... Rehabiah** - Translate the names of these men the same as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:23:15|23:15-17]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **His relatives from the clan of Eliezer** - These were his relatives who did this work with him. AT: "His relatives who did the work with him from the clan of Eliezer" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
