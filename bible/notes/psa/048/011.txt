===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Mount Zion be glad** - Mount Zion is used to refer to the people of Jerusalem because it is closely associated with the city. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the daughters of Judah** - Smaller cities in Judah are referred to as daughters because they developed within the nation. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])  The smaller cities refer to the people who live in those cities. AT: "the people who live in the smaller cities in Judah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
