====== Exodus Chapter 17 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Why did the people blame Moses for their situation?

A. There was no water for the people to drink. So the people blamed Moses for their situation [17:1-2].

Q? What was Moses afraid the people would do to him?

A. Moses was afraid because the Israelites are almost ready to stone him [17:4].

Q? How will water come out?

A. Moses will strike the rock with his staff. Water will come out of it for the people to drink [17:6].

Q?  Who will stand on top of the hill?

A. Moses will stand on top of the hill [17:9].
