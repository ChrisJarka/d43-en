====== water, waters ======

===== Definition: =====

Water or waters refers to the bodies of liquid which God has created on the Earth to provide mankind with needed nourishment.

  * Water may refer to a standing lake or also refer to a moving body such as a river.
  * Waters can refer to groups of lakes or multiple water sources.
  * Manmade reservoirs and aqueducts may also be called water or waters.

(See also: other)
