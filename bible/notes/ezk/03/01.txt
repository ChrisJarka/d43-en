===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He said to me**  - The word "he" refers to the Spirit, who is speaking to Ezekiel.
  * **what you have found **-This phrase refers to the scroll.
  * **Eat this scroll, then go speak**  - This phrase suggests that God wants the scroll's message to become a part of Ezekiel in order to share it with the people of Israel.
  * **feed your stomach and fill your belly with this scroll**  - These are two ways For God to tells Ezekiel to eat the scroll.  (See: [[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:doublet]])
  * **It was as sweet as honey **- "It tasted sweet as honey tastes sweet."
