====== Herodias ======

===== Facts: =====

Herodias was the wife of King Herod in Judea during the time of John the Baptist.

  * Herodias was originally the wife of Herod's brother Phillip but was unlawfully married to King Herod instead.
  * John the Baptist rebuked Herod and Herodias for the unlawful marriage, for which John was beheaded.
