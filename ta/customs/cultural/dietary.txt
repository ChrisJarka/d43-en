====== Dietary Customs ======


According to OT times there were only two meals a day Morning and evening. They had midday rest and after this they a number of important activities.  During the midday rest Abraham sat in the door of his tent for siesta, Genesis 18:1, Saul went into a cave, 1 Samuel 24:3, and Ish-Bosheth stayed in his house 2 Samuel 4:5. the women had to prepare the evening meal on the fire for the men when they returned from work. The meal normally consisted of vegetables or a lentil stew, Genesis 5:29, 34; Daniel 1:12, and was dihed out with the common pot with a piece of bread. Leviticus 11 of which meat could be used and might be eaten , meat was only added to the stew during Passover and the meal would be finished with fresh or dried fruit.
