====== sword ======

===== Definition: =====

A sword is a flat-bladed weapon used to cut or stab, usually with a blade length of 60 to 91cm.

  * Both John the Baptist and the Apostle James were beheaded with a sword.
  * The Psalms say that a person's tongue can injure a person like a sword
  * The Bible is figuratively compared to a sword because of it's ability to cut open our thoughts and motives.

(See also: other)
