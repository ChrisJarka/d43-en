====== Tyre ======


===== Facts: =====


Tyre is an ancient Phoenician port city on the Mediterranean Sea 40 kilometers south of Sidon.


    * Tyre, Lebanon, exists today and is still a port city.
    * At one time in His ministry, Jesus was near Tyre and Sidon and people from these towns heard Him speak (Mark 3:8).
