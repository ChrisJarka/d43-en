====== fear ======

===== Definition: =====

Fear is what a person feels when he knows that he, or someone he cares about, is in danger or could be be harmed.

    * The word "fear" can refer to extreme terror or to a respect and carefulness around the thing or person that can cause the harm. 
    * "To have fear" can be translated as "to fear" or "to be afraid."
    * The “fear of God” is something that people feel about him when they know that God is perfectly holy and hates sin. This fear motivates people to obey God. 
    * “Fear of God” can be translated as "respect for God" or, “reverence for God” or, “awe of God”. 
    * “To fear God” can be translated as "to be afraid of God" or “to revere God” or, “to be in awe of God” or, “to respect God.”
