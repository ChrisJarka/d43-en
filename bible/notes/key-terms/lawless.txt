====== lawless, lawlessness ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "lawless" means rebelling against the law. The general state of people being lawless is known as "lawlessness."

  * A lawless person does not obey God's laws.
  * Some day there will be an attitude of total lawlessness, or rebellion, throughout the world.
  * The word "lawless" could also be translated as, "rebellious" or "disobedient" or "law-defying".
  * The word "lawlessness" could be translated as, "rebellion" or "disobedience".
