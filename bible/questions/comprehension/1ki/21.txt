====== 1 Kings Chapter 21 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Ahab the king of Samaria want from Naboth the Jezreelite?

A. Ahab, king of Samaria, wanted the vineyard for Naboth ehich was near his palace, [21:1,2]

Q? What did Ahab offer Naboth for his vineyard?

A. Ahab offered to give Naboth a better vineyard or pay him money. [21:2]

Q? Why did Naboth refuse to give his vineyard to Ahab?

A. Naboth refused to give his vineyard to Ahab because it was the inheritance of his ancestors. [21:3]

Q? What did Ahab do when Naboth refused to to give up his vineyard?

A. Ahab became resentful and angry, went to bed, and refused to eat any food. [21:4]

Q? Who told Ahab to get up from bed, eat and be happy?

A. Ahab's wife, Jezebel, told him to get out of bed, eat and be happy. [21:7]

Q? What did Jezebel do to make sure Ahab would get Naboth's vineyard?

A. Jezebel had two dishonest men falsely accuse Namoth of blasphemy against God and the king at a public fast. [22:10]

Q? What would be the penalty for Namoth's blasphemy?

A. He would be taken out and stoned to death. [22:10]
