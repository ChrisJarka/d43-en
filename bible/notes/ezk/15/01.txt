===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh is talking to Ezekiel in this section.

  * **The Word of Yahweh came to me and said** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:16|3:16]].  
  * **Son of man :** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:02:01|2:3]].
  * **vine tree : **  Alternate translation: "grape bearing tree" or "wood of a grapevine" (UDB).
  * **trees of the thicket: **  This refers to many vines, shrubs and small bushes in a group.
  * **do they take a peg from it: **  Alternate translation: "do they take parts of the vine" or "do people take pieces of the vine" (UDB).
  * **if it cast into the fire for fuel - **  "cast" is another word for throw.
  * **cast into the fire for fuel**  - "fuel" is a word for materials used to start or keep a fire burning,
  * **the fire has devoured both ends **- "devoured" means "ate."
