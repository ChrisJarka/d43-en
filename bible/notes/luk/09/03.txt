===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **he said to them**  - That is, "Jesus said to the Twelve."
    * **Take nothing **- That is, "do not take anything with you ," or "do not bring anything with you." 
    * **for your journey**- That is, "for your trip," or "when you travel." This covers their trip from where they are and as they move from village to village until they come back to Jesus.
    * **whatever house you enter into**  - or, "whatever house you enter", or, "any house you enter"
    * **stay there**- That is, "remain there," or "temporarily live in that house as guest."
    * **until you leave**  - That is, "and leave," or "depart
    * ****from that place**  - ** that is, "from the town" or "from the area"
