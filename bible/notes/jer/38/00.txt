====== Jeremiah 38 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

== Opposition to Jeremiah, concluded (Jeremiah 36-38) ==

Jeremiah's enemies put him in a cistern but a man from Kush pulled him out. 


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Surrender and live ==

If the king obeyed God and surrendered to the Babylonians, he would be allowed to live and the city would not be burned. But if he refused, he would be captured and the city burned.
