===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You have said** -  The word "You" is  refers to the nation of Edom.
  * **These two nations and these two lands will become mine** - Edom wanted to rule over the kingdom of Israel and the kingdom of Judah and wanted to own their land. (See: [[en:obe:other:kingdomofisrael]] and [[en:obe:other:kingdomofjudah]])
  * **and we** - The word "we" refers to the people of Edom.
  * **will possess** - "take control of" or "become owners of"
  * **when Yahweh was present with them** – "but Yahweh was there" or "but Yahweh was in the land of the kingdom of Israel and the kingdom of Judah." It was implied that because Yahweh was there, he did not allow the people of Edom to take control of Israel and Judah. Alternate translation: "but Yahweh was there and did not allow you to take control of them." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **as I live** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]].
  * **this is the Lord Yahweh's declaration** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]].
  * **I will do according to your anger and according to your animosity** - It the was implied that Yahweh would do these things to the nation of Edom. Alternate translation: "I will will do things to you with an anger like yours and with jealosy like yours." The nation of Edom was angry with Israel and Judah and jealous of them. 
  * **that you had in your hatred of Israel** - This can be translated as "the same kind of things that you did to the people of Israel and Judah because you hated them."
  * **I will show myself to them** - "I will be known by the people of Israel and Judah." Alternate translation: "The people of Israel and Judah will know who I am."
  * **when I judge you** - The word "you" to the nation of Edom.
