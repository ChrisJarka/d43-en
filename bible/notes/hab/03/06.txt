===== Translation Notes: =====
Habakkuk continues to describe his vision of Yahweh.

    * **measured the earth** - "judged the size of the earth"
    * **eternal mountains...everlasting hills** - The words "eternal" and "everlasting" are used to mean the mountains and hills have been there for a very long time and will continue to exist for a long time into the future.(See: [[en:ta:translate:hyperbole]])
    * **the everlasting hills bowed down**  - The refers to the hills as something that could bow down to worship  God. It is used to say they were flattened to the ground. (See: [[en:ta:translate:personification]])
