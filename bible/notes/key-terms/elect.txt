====== elect, chosen ======

===== Definition: =====

The word "elect" means "chosen" and refers to those God chose to be his people. It is sometimes translated as "God's chosen people".

  * God "chose" or "elected: people to love him and serve him and to bear spiritual fruit.
  * God's elect or God's chosen ones refers to God's people who have been saved by him and who have been made his children.
  * "The elect" is often used to refer to the Church as a whole or to the Old Testament nation of Israel.
