====== Hannah ======

===== Facts: =====

Hannah was the mother of the prophet Samuel in the Old Testament. She prayed silently in the temple that God would give her a son and promised to dedicate the son to the Lord's service. The Lord answered her prayer and she brought Samuel to the temple to learn from and serve the Priest Eli.
