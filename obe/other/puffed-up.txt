====== puffed up ======

===== Definition: =====

A term referring to an arrogant attitude or an attitude of superiority.

  * The New Testament speaks of "being puffed up in pride toward one another" as character which is the opposite of love.
  * 1 Corinthians states that having scriptural knowledge without love only puffs a person up and makes them feel superior to others.
