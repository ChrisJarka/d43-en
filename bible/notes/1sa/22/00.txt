====== 1 Samuel 22 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

There are two stories in this chapter: David gathering a group of followers and Saul having all the priests and their families killed.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== David assembles a force of unhappy people ==
David hid in the wilderness and people in trouble came to him. Soon he had a force of 500 men. The prophet told him to leave the wilderness.

== Saul and the High Priest's family ==
Doeg tells Saul that the High Priest gave David food and a sword and prayed for him. Saul ordered the Priests to come to him and then accused them of encouraging David to rebel. He told Doeg to kill the priests. Doeg killed the priests and all their families except one who escape to David.
