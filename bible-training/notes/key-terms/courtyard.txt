====== courtyard ======


===== Definition: =====


A courtyard is an area that is open to the sky, but surrounded by a building or walls. It has no roof.


    * God's tabernacle was a tent set up in a courtyard.
    * The courtyard was surrounded by walls made of heavy curtains.
    * God's people were permitted to go into the courtyard to worship God, but only the priests were permitted to go into the tabernacle.
