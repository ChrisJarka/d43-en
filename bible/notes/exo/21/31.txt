===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **gored -**  "injured with its horns"
  * **thirty shekels of silver**  - This equals 330 grams of silver. A shekel weighed eleven grams. (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicalmoney|biblical money]])
  * **the ox must be stoned**- AT: "you must kill the ox by stoning" (See: Passive.)
