===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh** - See how you translated this in 5:5.
  * **So I will make an end to the multitudes of Egypt** - Alternate translation: "I will make it so that Egypt will no longer have a lot of people."
  * **the multitudes of Egypt** - "the many people of Egypt".
  * **by the hand of Nebuchadrezzar** - Nebuchadrezzar  will be the one who brings this punishment about.
  * **He and his people with him! Terrors of nations brought** - Alternate translation: "I will bring Nebuchadrezzar and his people, whom the nations are afraid of." (See: [[en:ta:translate:active]])
  * **to destroy the land** - "so they will destroy the land." Yahweh will bring Nebuchadrezzar and his people so that they will destroy the land.
  * **fill the land with the slain** - "there will be a great many people who are killed" (See: hyperbole).
