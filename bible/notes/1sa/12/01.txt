===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I have listened to everything you said to me **- "I have done everything that you told me to do" (UDB)
  * **I have set a king over you **- "I have given a king to rule you" (UDB)  This phrase pictures a king sitting on a throne, and is referring to the entire period of time he will rule.  (See: [[: en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **the king walking before you... I have walked before you... **- These expressions means that these lives are in view for the people to actually see.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **old and gray **- "I am now old, and my hair is gray" (UDB)
