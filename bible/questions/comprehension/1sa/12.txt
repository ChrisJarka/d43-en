====== 1 Samuel Chapter 12 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What was Samuel's main concern now that he was old and gray?

A. Samuel's main concern in his old age was that he might have taken something or done something to the people of Israel that needed to be restored. [12:1-3]
