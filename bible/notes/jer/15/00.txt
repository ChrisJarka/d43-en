====== Jeremiah 15 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Do not pray for the people ==

God told Jeremiah not to pray  for the people because God was punishing them.

== Jeremiah's complaint ==

Jeremiah complained that everyone was against him. God told him that he would protect him from all his enemies.
