===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now Jesus loved Martha and her sister and Lazarus** - This is background information. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
  * **love** - This refers to the love that comes from God that is focused on the good of others, even when it does not benefit oneself. This kind of love cares for others, no matter what they do. 
  * **Martha and her sister** - Some languages will need to indicate that Mary is the younger sister of Martha. Lazarus was younger than his sisters. AT: "Martha and her younger sister, Mary, and their younger brother Lazarus." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicitinfo]])
