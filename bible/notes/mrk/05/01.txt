===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - After Jesus calms the great storm, he heals a man who has many demons but the local people in the area of the Gerasenes are not glad about his healing and they beg Jesus to leave. 

  * **the sea** - This refers to the Sea of Galilee, which is also called the "Lake of Chinnereth."
  * **Gerasenes** - This name is used to refer to the people who live in Gerasa. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **with an unclean spirit** - This is an idiom meaning that the man is "controlled" or "possessed" by the unclean spirit. AT: "controlled by an unclean spirit" or "that an unclean spirit possessed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
