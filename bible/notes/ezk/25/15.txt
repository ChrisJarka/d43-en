===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh: - **"The Lord Yahweh says,"
  * **executed vengence with distain **- "with great hatred"
  * **the****refore: thus says the Lord Yahweh**  - "Lord Yahweh says**"**
  * **Behold** <font inherit/inherit;;rgb(51, 51, 51) line-height: 1.4;;;inherit>- This can be translated as “Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you.”</font>
  * **stretch out my hand -** This indicates that Yahweh will bring punishment upon the Philistines.
  * **Cherethites -** This is a people associated with the Philistines. (See:"How to Translate Names")
  * **For I will execute a great vengeance against them with furious punishments, -**  "I will get great revenge on them and show that I am angry with them by the way I punish them." (UDB)
  * **so they will know that I am Yahweh when I set my vengeance upon them! - "**they will know that I, Yahweh the Lord, have the power to do what I say that I will do." (UDB)
