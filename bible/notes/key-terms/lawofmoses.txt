====== law of Moses ======


===== Facts: =====


The Law of Moses refers to the commands which God delivered through Moses. 


    * The Law of Moses was delivered originally at Mount Sinai in the desert.
    * The Law was reintroduced a second time after the original recipients had disobeyed and died.
    * The Law of Moses serves as a guide to reveal mankind's sinful nature.
