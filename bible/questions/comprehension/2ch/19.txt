====== 2 Chronicles Chapter 19 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What good was found in Jehoshaphat's heart?

A.  Some good was found in him and he had fixed his heart to seek God. [19:3]

Q? Where did Jehoshaphat place judges?

A. He placed judges in the land throughout all the fortified cities of Judah. [19:5]

Q? Where did Jehoshaphat say Yahweh was at when the Judges passed judgement?

A.H e told the judges that Yahweh was with them in the act of judging. [19:6]

Q? What kind of heart did Jehoshaphat say the judges in Jerusalem should have in reverence for Yahweh?

A. He instructed them that in reverence for Yahweh, judge faithfully, and with a perfect heart. [19:9]

Q? What will not come down on them if the people do not to sin against Yahweh?

A. They are told to not sin against Yahweh, so that anger does not come down on them. [19:10]

Q? With whom does Jehoshaphat pray that Yahweh abides?

A.He prayed for Yahweh to be with those who are good. [19:11]
