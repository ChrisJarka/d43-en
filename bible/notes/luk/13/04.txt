===== Translation Notes: =====

    * (Jesus continues speaking to the crowd.)
    * **Or those ** - This sentence could also start with "Or consider those" or "Think about those," or "What about those."
    * **those eighteen people in Siloam on whom a tower fell and killed them** - This could also be translated as "the eighteen people who died when the tower at Siloam fell on them."
    * **were worse offenders**  - That is, "were more sinful", or "were more evil", or "behaved worse."
    * **other men** - That is, "other people."
    * **No, I say ** - That is, "I tell you they were not", or,"Definitely not" or "Certainly not."
    * **will perish in the same way** -
