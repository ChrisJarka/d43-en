===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **A declaration** - "A message from Yahweh"
  * **about the desert by the sea** - "about a land soon to become a desert"
  * **like stormwinds from the Negev** - An enemy army will come in like a whirlwind from the south.
  * **from a terrible land** - They are from a people that is greatly feared, a people that terrify their enemies.
  * **Go up and attack,Elam; besiege, Media;** - Yahweh is commanding the enemies to destroy Babylon.
  * **I will stop all her groaning** - Yahweh will cause the groaning and suffering that Babylon caused to cease.
