===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **undesirable and thin** - See how you translated this phrase in [[en:bible:notes:gen:41:01|41:3]].
  * **desirable and fat** - See how you translated this phrase in [[en:bible:notes:gen:41:01|41:2]].
  * **Behold, seven ears** - The word “behold” here shows that Pharaoh was surprised by what he saw.          
  * **ears of grain** - The ears are parts of the corn plant on which the seeds grow.   Alternate translation: "heads of grain" (UDB)
  * **came up on one stalk** - This is the thick or tall part of a plant.  Alternate translation: "came up on one stem"
  * **wholesome and good** - This could be translated as a new sentence: "They were healthy and good."
  * **thin and scorched by the east wind** - "that were thin and burned by a hot wind that came from the east" 
  * **sprouted up** - "grew up" or "developed"
