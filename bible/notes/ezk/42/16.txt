===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He measured** - The word "he" refers to the man whose appearance was like brass (40:3).
  * **measuring stick**  - See how you translated this "stick" in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:05|40:05]].    
  * **cubits** - See how you translated these "long" cubits in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:05|40:05]].
  * **five hundred cubits** - About 270 meters. (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaldistance]])
  * **two cubits** - About one meters.
  * **the side of the sea** - This refers to the west side.
