===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the inhabitants of Teman** - "the people who live in Teman"
  * **Teman** - Translate the name of this city as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:49:07|49:7]].
  * **They will certainly be dragged away, even the smallest flock** - This continues speaking of Yahweh punishing the people of Edom as if he would come like a lion and attack the sheep. This can be stated in active form. AT: "He will drag them away, even the smallest flock" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **even the smallest flock** - Here the youngest and weakest people of Edom are spoken of as if they are the smallest flock. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Their pasturelands will be turned into ruined places** - AT: "He will turn their pasturelands into ruined places" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
