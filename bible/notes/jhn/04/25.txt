===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **(the one who is called Christ)** - These could be the words of the woman (see UDB) or of the writer (see [[:en:bible:notes:jhn:01:40|1:41]]).
    * **to us** - inclusive: "to you Jews and to us Samaritans" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive]])
