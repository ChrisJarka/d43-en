===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He said** - Alternate translation: "Jesus said". All occurrences of "he" refers to Jesus throughout these verses
  * **Let us go elsewhere** - The word "us" refers to Jesus and his disciples. "some place else" or "to other places"
  * **He went throughout all Galilee** - "He traveled all over Galilee".
