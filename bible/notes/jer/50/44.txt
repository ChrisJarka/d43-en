    * **Behold**  - The word “behold” here adds emphasis to what follows.
    * **He goes up** - The word "he" refers to the invader from the north. See 50:41
    * **I will quickly cause them to run from it** - The word "them" refers to the people of Babylon; the word "it" refers to the invading people.
