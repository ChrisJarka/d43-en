===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **vivid**  - "understandable" or "clear" or "expressive"
  * **the words of the wise are like goads** - A goad is a long stick with a point on the end, used to prod an animal to move. AT: "A wise person says things that push people in the right way." (See: Simile)
  * **Like nails driven deeply** - Such a nail provides stability. AT: "Like a man who drives nails deeply into wood in order to make a stable building." (See: Active/passive; see: Simile)
  * **by one shepherd** - Possible meanings are, 1) by one shepherd, myself, the writer, or 2) by one Shepherd, God, or 3) some other teacher, or 4) any teacher at all.
  * **shepherd** - Like a shepherd, he guides his hearers into the good path. (See: Metaphor)
