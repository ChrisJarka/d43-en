====== 1 Samuel 16 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

== 1 Samuel 16-31  Saul and David ==
 1 Samuel 16:1-13 tells about God choosing David for the next king and David receiving the Holy Spirit. 16:14-23 tells of the Holy Spirit leaving Saul and David playing the harp for him. 


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Special concepts in this chapter
== David chosen to be the next king ==
Humans judge others by what they look like but God judges people by their true inner character. So God rejected David's older brothers in favor of David, who truly loved and obeyed him.
==  Saul tormented by an evil spirit ==
God used Saul's tormenting spirit and David's soothing harp playing to get David into court to learn the way government was conducted.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

==Rhetorical Question ==
God scolds Samuel with this [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] for his unwillingness to accept God's decision: "How long will you mourn for Saul, since I have rejected him from being king over Israel?"
