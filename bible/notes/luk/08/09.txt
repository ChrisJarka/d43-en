===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **asked Him**- Or, "were asking Him"
    * **to you has been granted to understand**  - This could also be translated as, "you have been given the privilege of understanding" or "God has given you the gift of understanding" or "God has made you able to understand"
    * **the mysteries**  - or "the hidden things".
    * **the rest of the people will only be taught with parables**  - or, "everyone else will be taught with stories",
    * **seeing they will not really see**  - or "though they look, they will not see" or "though they see, they will not perceive"
    * **hearing they will not really understand**  - or "though they listen, they will not hear" or "though they hear, it will not make sense"
