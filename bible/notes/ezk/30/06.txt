===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Thus says Yahweh!** - "This is what Yahweh says!" or "Yahweh says this." This sentence introduces what the Lord would say.
  * **So the ones** – "In this way, the ones" or "In this manner, the ones"
  * **who support Egypt** - "who help Egypt"
  * **will fall** - "will die" or "come to ruin"
  * **the pride of her strength will go down** – "the strength of Egypt, which made it proud, will be gone" or "the strength of Egypt, which made it proud, will be gone"
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration** - See how you translated this in 5:11.
  * **midst**  - "center" or "middle".
