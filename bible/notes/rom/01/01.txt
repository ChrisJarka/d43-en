===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Paul**  - “From Paul.” Your language may have a particular way of introducing the author of a letter. This could be translated as “I, Paul, wrote this letter.”
  * **called to be an apostle, set apart for the gospel of God**  - This could be translated as a new sentence and with active verbs: "God called me to be an apostle and chose me to tell people about the Gospel. " (See: Active/Passive)
  * **he promised before by his prophets** - God promised his people that he would set up his kingdom. He told the prophets to write these promises in the scriptures.
  * **who was born out of the descendants of David according to the flesh**  - Here the word "flesh" refers to the physical body. When Jesus became a human he was born into the family of David. This could be translated as "who is a descendant of David according to the physical nature" or "who was born into the family of David."
