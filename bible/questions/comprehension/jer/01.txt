====== Jeremiah Chapter 01 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? When did the word of Yahweh come to Jeremiah, son of Hilkiah, who was one of the priests at Anathoth in the land of Benjamin?

A. The word of Yahweh came to Jeremiah in the days of Josiah, in the thirteenth year of his reign, and in the days of Jehoiakim son of Josiah, king of Judah, until the fifth month of the eleventh year of Zedekiah sn of Josiah, king of Judah, when the people of Jerusalem were taken away as prisoners. [1:1-3]

Q? According to the word of Yahweh, when was Jeremiah chosen?

A. Before Yahweh formed Jeremiah in the womb, Yahweh chose Jeremish; before Jeremiah came out of the womb, Yahweh sanctified him. [1:5]

Q? Why did Jeremiah protest against his selection?

A. He said he was too young. [1:6]

Q? What did Yahweh command Jeremiah to do?

A. Yahweh told him he must go everywhere Yahweh sent him and must say whatever Yahweh commands him. [1:7]

Q? What was Yahweh's declaration?

A. "Do not be afraid of them, for I am with you to rescue you." [1:8]

Q? What did Jeremiah see?

A. He saw an almond branch. [1:11]

Q? in the second vision,When Jeremiah saw a heated pot, whose surface is churning, tipping away from the north what did it mean?

A. Yahweh said to him that disaster will be opened up out of the north on all who live in this land. [1:14]

Q? What will all the tribes of the northern kingdom do when Yahweh calls?

A. They will come, and everyone will set up his throne at the entrance of the gates of Jerusalem. [1:15]

Q? What will Yahweh do when they come?

A. He will pronounce sentence against them for all their evil in forsaking him, in burning incense to other gods, and in worshiping what they made with their hands. [1:16]

Q? What will the kings of Judah, its official, its priests, and the people of the land do against the prophet?

A. They will fight against the prophet, but they will not defeat him, for Yahweh will be with him to rescue him. [1:19]
