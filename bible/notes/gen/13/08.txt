===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **strife** - "hostility" or "fighting"
  * **after all, we are family** - this means "because we are family" or "because we are kin"
  * **family**  - or "kinsmen" or "relatives."
  * **Is not the whole land before you?"** - this is a rhetorical question. It can also be translated: "The whole land is available for you" (see [[:en:ta:figuresofspeech:rhetoricalquestion]]).
  * **Go ahead and separate yourself**  - or "kindly separate yourself." This is a polite way of speaking.
  * **between you and me - **  alternately, if your language requires it: "between you and between me" (Hebrew requires that structure, English does not. Your language may also require it)
  * between you and me,** and ** between your herdsmen... - alternately: or
