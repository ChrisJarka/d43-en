====== 2 Samuel Chapter 17 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Ahithophel tell Absalom and all the elders of Israel his plan of attack would be on David?

A. His plan would be to have a large army, carry out a surprise attack on David at night when he was weary and weak, and bring back all the people for them to rule. [17:1-4]
