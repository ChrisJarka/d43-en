====== Psalms Chapter 135 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who should praise Yahweh?

A. The servants of Yahweh, those who stand in Yahweh's house, and those in the courtyards should praise Yahweh. [135:1-2]

Q? Why should they praise and sing to Yahweh?

A.They should praise Yahweh for he is good and they should sing for it is pleasant to do. [135:3]

Q? What does the writer know about Yahweh?

A. He knows that Yahweh is great, he is above all gods, and whatever Yahweh desires he does in heaven, earth and in the seas. [135:5-6]

Q? How does the writer show that God is great?

A. Yahwey brings the clouds, makes lightening bolts accompany the rain, and brings the wind out of his storehouse. [135:7]

Q? What did Yahwey do in Egypt?

A. He killed the firstborn man and animals of Egypt and he sent signs and wonders against Pharaoh and all his servants. He attacked many nations, killing many kings.[135:8-10]

Q? How did Yahweh defend his people and have compassion on his servants ?

A. He gave their land as a heritage to Israel his people. [315:12,14]

Q? What do the nations' idols look like?

A. They are made of silver and gold, have mouths, eyes, and ears but they do not speak, see, hear, nor breathe. [135:15-17]

Q? Who made the nations' idols?

A. The men who are like them and trust in them. [135:18]

Q? Who blesses Yahweh?

A. The descendants of Israel, Aaron, Levi, and those that honor Yahweh. [135:19-20]
