====== Paran ======

===== Facts: =====

Paran was an unpopulated desert east of Egypt and south of Palestine.  After Sarah ordered Abraham to throw out the slave Hagar and her son Ishmael,  they went to Paran.

  * After the exodus from Egypt, the children of Israel went through Paran.  From Kadesh-barnea, Moses sent 12 spies to search out the land and bring back a report.
