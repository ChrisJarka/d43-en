===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Woe to you, destroyer who has not been destroyed** - This is a warning to the people of Assyria.
  * **Woe to the betrayer whom they have not betrayed** - This is repeating the same thing as above. The Assyrians have destroyed and betrayed others but have not been destroyed or betrayed yet.
