===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is the end of the Gospel of John. Here the author, the Apostle John, gives a closing comment about himself and what he has written in this book. (See: [[End of Story]])

  * **the disciple** - "the disciple John"
  * **we know** - AT: "we in the church know"
  * **even the world itself could not contain the books** - Jesus did many more miracles than what John thought could be recorded in many books. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
