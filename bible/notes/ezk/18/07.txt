===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **has returned the pledge to the debtor**  - A pledge is something given to guarantee a loan. Often, a cloak was used as a plege for a debt and would be needed for warmth by evening.
  * **spoils**  - This word refers to valuables taken from others.
