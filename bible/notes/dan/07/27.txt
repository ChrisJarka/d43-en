===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **greatness of the kingdoms** - That is everything that is great or good about the kingdoms. AT: "all that is good things about the kingdoms" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **He is the end of the matter** - "That is what I saw in my vision" (UDB). Daniel says this to let the people know that he has finished talking about his vision. If you have a way of doing this in your language, you can use it.
