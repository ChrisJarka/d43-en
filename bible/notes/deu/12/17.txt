===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you may not eat within your gates **- The list of offerings that follow are not to be eaten at their homes or towns, but only eaten at the place Yahweh specified in verse 5.
  * **within your gates**  - “within your community” or “inside your city.” Cities often had walls around them to keep out enemies, and gates for people to go in and out. (See: Metonymy )
