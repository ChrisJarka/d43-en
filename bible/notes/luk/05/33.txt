===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **fast**  - This means to abstain from food for the purpose of spiritual alertness.
    * **wedding attendants**  - These are the friends of the man getting married, selected to be part of the wedding ceremony in some way.
    * **bridegroom**  - This refers to the man getting married.
