===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **me **- This pronoun refers to Yahweh.
  * **set your face **- This phrase means to turn and look in another direction.
  * **your **- This pronoun refers to Ezekiel.
  * **rant **- This word means to speak out strongly.
  * **thicket **- This word means a forest.
  * **declaration **- See how you translated these commands in 20:3.
  * **behold - **  The word “behold” here alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows.
  * **I - **  This pronoun refers to Yahweh.
  * **you - **  This pronoun refers to Israel.
  * **devour - **  This word means to quickly and completely destroy something. An alternate translation could be, "completely consume" or "burn up" (UDB).
  * **fiery flame - **  This phrase means a large and hot fire.
  * **every face - **  This phrase means each person.
