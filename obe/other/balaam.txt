====== Balaam ======

===== Facts: =====

Balaam was a pagan prophet during the time when the Israelites were at the Jordan River preparing to enter the Promised Land of Canaan.

  * Balaam was from the city of Pethor which was located in the region around the Euphrates River.
  * The Midianite king Balek was afraid of the strength and numbers of the Israelites so he hired Balaam to curse them.
  * God blocked Balaam from 
      * The donkey he was riding dodged the Angel three times, and Balaam beat the donkey after each time.
      * The donkey could see the Angel, but Balaam could not.
      * After three times being beaten, the donkey speaks to Balaam asking why he was being beaten.
      * Then Balaam could see the Angel, who told Balaam not to curse the Israelites, but to bless them.
      * When he met King Balek, Balaam did not curse the Israelites, but followed the instructions of the Lord.
