===== Translation Notes: =====


    * <font 13px/arial;;#000000;;#ffffff>Caesar Augustus</font> - That is, "King Augustus" or "Emperor Augustus." Augustus was the name of the man who was the first emperor of the Roman Empire. <font 13px/arial;;#000000;;#ffffff>(See</font> [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]].<font 13px/arial;;#000000;;#ffffff>)</font>
    * **that a census be taken of all the people living in the Roman world**- A census is an official count of all the people in a country. In this case they wrote down the names of all the people in a government record. This can also be translated as "that the names of all the people living in the Roman world be written down" or "that they write down the names of all the people living in the Roman world" or "that they count all the people living in the Roman world and write down their names.
    * **the Roman world **  - That is, "the part of the world controlled by the Roman government" or "the countries ruled by the Roman emperor" or "the Roman empire."
    * **Quirinius**  - Quirinius was the name of the man who was appointed to be the governor of Syria<font 13px/arial;;#000000;;#ffffff><font 13px/arial;;#000000;;#ffffff>
(See</font> [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames|How to Translate Names]].<font 13px/arial;;#000000;;#ffffff>)</font>
