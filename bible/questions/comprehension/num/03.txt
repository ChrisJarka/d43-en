====== Numbers Chapter 03 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Of what is this the history?

A. This is the history of the descendats of Aaron and Moses. [3:1]

Q? What are the names of Aaron's sons?

A. The names of Aaron's sons are Nadab, Abihu, Eleazar, and Ithamar. [3:2]

Q? Why did Nadab and Abihu fall dead before Yahweh?

A. Nadab and Abihu fell dead before Yahweh when they offered unacceptable fire to him. [3:4]

Q? Which of Aaron's sons served as priests with Aaron?

A. Eleazar and Ithamar served with their father Aaron as priests. [3:4]

Q? Which tribe did Yahweh say was to help Aaron serve as priest?

A. The tribe of Levi was to help Aaron. [3:5-6]

Q? What were the duties of the tribe of Levi?

A. They must perform the duties on behalf of Aaron and the whole community, care for all furnishings, and help the tribes with the tabernacle service. [3:7-8]

Q? Who did Yahweh give to help Aaron and his sons?

A. Yahweh gave the Levites to Aaron and his sons to serve the people of Israel. [2:9]  

Q? Whom did Yahweh set apart for himself?

A. Yahweh set apart for himself the Levites and all the firstborn in Israel both people and animals. [3:11-13]

Q? Who did Yahweh command Moses to count in the tribe of Levi?

A. Moses was to count every Levi, male, descendant one month old and older. [3:14-16]

Q? How many sons and clans came from Levi

A. Levi had three sons and eight clans. [3:17-20]

Q? What must the clans of the Gershonites who camped on the west side of the tabernacle do?

A.They must care for the tent, and the tent covering. They must also care for the courtyard hangings, the courtyard that surrounds the sanctuary and the altar and they must care for the ropes of the tent meeting and everything in it. [3:24-26]

Q? What must the clans from Kohath who camped on the south side of the tabernacle do?

A. They must take care of the sanctuary, and the sanctuary curtain and everything in it. [3:27-32]

Q? What must the clans from Merari who camped on the north side of the tabernacle do?

A. They must care for the boards of the tabernacle, the crossbars, posts, bases, all of the hardware and everything related to them. [3:33-37]

Q? What must Moses and Aaron and sons who are camped on the east of the tabernacle do?

A. They are responsible for the duties of the sanctuary and the duties of the people of Israel. [3:38-39]

Q? What did Moses do with the buy-back money from those who exceeded the number of the Levites replaced instead of the first born among the people of Isreal?

A. He gave the money to Aaron and his sons as Yahweh had commanded him. [3:44-51]
