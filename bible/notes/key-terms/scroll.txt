====== scroll ======


===== Definition: =====


A scroll is a piece of leather that is written on and rolled up when not being used.


    * A scroll could be sealed with a wax insignia, ensuring that its contents were free from alteration.
    * Scrolls were used for the writing and copying of documents, including Scripture.
    * Scrolls that were used for copying the Torah were made from the skin of a kosher animal.
