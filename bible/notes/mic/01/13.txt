===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Lachish**  - The name of this city means "invincible." (see: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **You, Lachish, were the beginning of sin ** - The sin that reaches Jerusalem by the end of the verse finds its start here. (see:[[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **for the daughter of Zion **- Here Micah is comparing Lachish to a motherly figure that has daught the surrounding area to sin. (see:[[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **transgressions of Israel were found in you**- As earlier in the verse God is pointing out where the problem started. A possible AT: The root of Israeal's transgressions was in you.(see:[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **Moresheth Gath** - The name of this city means "winepress owner."
  * **Achzib**  - The name of this city means "lying."
