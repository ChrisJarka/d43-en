===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - All of the names here are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])

  * **numbered 956** - "numbered nine hundred fifty-six people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **heads of fathers' houses for their fathers' houses** - "leaders of their extended families." Extended families are people related to each other who usually live in different houses, what the UDB calls "clans."
