====== Jonathan ======

===== Facts: =====

Jonathon was a common name iin the Old Testament meaning "Yahu has given."

  * Most notable was King Saul's eldest son, "swift as an eagle and strong as a lion,"  who became David's best friend and was killed by Phillistines at Mount Gilboa along with his father and brothers.
  * Other Jonathons:  A Levite house priest who ministered to the tribe of Dan (Judge 17); Son of Abiathar the priest who served David (2 Sam. 15:24); David's uncle (1 Chrn. 27:32); and numerous others.
