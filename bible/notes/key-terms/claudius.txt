====== Claudius ======

Tiberias Claudius Nero Germanicus was Roman emperor of the Roman Empire from 41 to 54 A.D.

===== Facts: =====

Text

  * As emperor he invaded Britian and made it an imperial province.
  * Claudius expelled many Jews from Rome during his reign including Prisca and Aquila. Acts 18:2
  * He was poisoned by his wife and Nero became emperor.
