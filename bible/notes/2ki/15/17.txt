===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **In the thirty-ninth year** - "In year 39"
  * **Uzziah king of Judah** - Uzziah is also known as "Azariah."
  * **He did what was evil in the sight of Yahweh** - AT: "He did many things that Yahweh said were evil" or "Yahweh saw all he did and Yahweh said that what he had done was evil" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]]) 
  * **who had caused Israel to sin.** - "the wicked sins of Jeroboam son of Nebat, were the cause of Israel sinning as he did."
