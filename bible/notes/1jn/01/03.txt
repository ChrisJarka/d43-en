===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **that which we have seen and heard we declare to you also**  - "We declare to you also what we have seen and heard"
  * **we...us...our** - These pronouns refer to John and those helping him write the letter. (See [[en:ta:translate:exclusive]])
  * **you** - The word "you" is plural and refers to John's audience. (See: [[en:ta:translate:formsofyou-plural]]
  * ** have fellowship with us. And our fellowship is with the Father**- Theword fellowship here is refering to a close friendship. These clauses could be translated "be our close friends and we are friends with God the Father"
  * ** Christ**- The word Christ is a title, not a name and it means "the chosen one". Here it is referring to God choosing Jesus to be our savior. 
  * **that your joy may be made complete** - "to make your joy complete" or "to make you completely happy" (See: [[en:ta:translate:active]])
