===== Translation Notes: =====

This psalm is a song that teaches people.\\
See: [[en:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]
  * **Blessed is the man who** - "How good it is for the person who" or "How fortunate is the person who"
  * **obeys Yahweh** - "respects Yahweh" or "reveres Yahweh" or "fears Yahweh"
  * **greatly delights** - "takes great pleasure"
  * **His descendants will be powerful on earth** - "His descendant will be a mighty warrior in his land" or "His offspring will be a great hero in his country"
  * **the godly will be blessed** - "a righteous generation will be blessed." AT: "Yahweh and other people will bless descendants who do what is right." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
