====== face ======

===== Definition: =====

As a noun, face is the front of a person's head. Face can refer to how a person's face shows emotion. Face can refer to the side or surface of an object. As a verb, face refers to position or to confront somebody or something directly.

  * A person's face has the eyes, nose, and mouth.
  * The face of an object is the surface of a side, like the face of a building.
  * An example of facing something directly would be to "face the judge in a court of law."
  * As position, when people face each other, they are looking at each other.

(See also: other)
