====== Isaiah 26 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

Some translations prefer to set apart extended quotations, prayers or songs. The ULB and many other English translations indent the lines of chapter 26, which is a song to be sung during the reign of Jesus. .

=== Other possible translation difficulties in this chapter ===

== "That day" ==
It appears that some aspects of this prophecy are still to be fulfilled in the last day before Yahweh reigns on earth in Jerusalem. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:fulfill]] and [[:en:obe:kt:lastday]])
