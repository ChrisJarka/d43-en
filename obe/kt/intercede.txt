====== intercede, intercession ======

===== Definition: =====

The terms "intercede" and "intercession" refer to making requests to someone on behalf of another person.

  * The expression "make intercession" means the same thing as "intercede."
  * God's Holy Spirit intercedes for us by praying to God on our behalf.
  * A person can intercede for another person by making a request <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>on their behalf.</font> to someone in authority.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  *

Other ways to translate "intercede" coudl include, "plead for" or "urge (someone) to do something (for someone else)."

  *

The term, "intercessions" coudl be translated as "appeals" or "requests" or "urgent prayers."

  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The phrase "make intercession for" could be translated as, "make requests for the benefit of</font> <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>" or "make an appeal on behalf of" or "ask God to help" or "appeal to God to bless" (someone).</font>
