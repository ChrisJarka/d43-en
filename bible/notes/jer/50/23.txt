===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh speaks to the people of Babylon.

  * **How the hammer of all the lands has been cut apart and destroyed** - The army of Babylon is spoken of as if it were a hammer. The destruction of this army is spoken of as if the hammer was to be cut up and destroyed. This can be stated as a simile. AT: "The mighty army of Babylon that conquered all the nations is like a hammer that will be broken into pieces and destroyed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **How Babylon has become a destroyed place among the nations** - Babylon was a nation like the other nations, but it will become a complete ruin, totally destroyed.
  * **horror** - a very strong feeling of fear, dread, and shock
  * **I have set a trap for you and you were taken** - The destruction of Babylon is spoken of as if Yahweh had caught them in a trap like an animal. AT: "I set a trap and caught you in it as a hunter catches an animal" or "You people of Babylon, I have set a trap for you"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **you were taken ... You were found and captured** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "I have captured you ... I found you and captured you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
