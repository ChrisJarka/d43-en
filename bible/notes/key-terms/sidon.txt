====== Sidon ======


===== Facts: =====


Sidon was the firstborn son of Canaan and great-grandson of Noah. Sidon is also a city, named after its founder.


    * Ancient Sidon was one of the first cities of Phoenicia.
    * The City of Sidon exists today in the country of Lebanon. It is located 40 kilometers south of Beirut on the Mediterranean coast.
