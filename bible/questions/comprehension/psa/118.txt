====== Psalms Chapter 118 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What are the two reasons the writer says, "Give thanks to Yahweh?" [118:1]

A. The writer says to give thanks to Yahweh because, "He is good, for his covenant faithfulness endures forever." [118:2]

Q? What is the phrase this writer repeats for the fourth time when he says, "Let the loyal followers of Yahweh say..." [118:4]

A. He repeats, "His covenant faithfulness endures for ever." [118:4]

Q? When the writer called out to Yahweh, Yahweh answered him by doing what? 

A. "Yahweh answered me and set me free." [118:5]

Q? The writer says it is better to take shelter in Yahweh than do what?

A. "It is better to take shelter in Yahweh than to put confidence in man." [118:8]

Q? The writer says, "All the nations surrounded me," what did he do in Yahweh's name?

A. "All the nations surrounded me; in Yahweh's name I cut them off." [118:10]

Q? When the writer says, "They attacked me to knock me down,
but Yahweh helped me," what three things does he go ahead to say that Yahweh is to him? 

A. "Yahweh is my strength and joy, and he is the one who rescues me." [118:14]

Q? What kind of shout is heard in the tents of the righteous?

A. "The joyful shout of victory is heard in the tents of the righteous." [118:15]

Q? The writer says that he will not die, but live and then do what two things?

A. "I will not die, but live and declare the deeds of Yahweh." [118:17]

Q? When the writer says to open the gates of righteousness, what does he say he will then do? 

A. "Open to me the gates of righteousness; I will enter them and I will give thanks to Yahweh." [118:19]

Q? When the writer says, "I will give thanks to you, for you answered me," who does he then say that Yahweh has become?

A. He says, "I will give thanks to you... you have become my salvation." [118:21]
