===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The declaration of the word of Yahweh to Israel by the hand of Malachi** - "Yahweh spoke these words to Israel through Malachi"
  * **by the hand of Malachi** - through the action of Malachi (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **But you say, "How have you loved us?"** - This question indicates doubt, or it questions the truth of God's words. God is using a question to rebuke the people. AT: "But you say, 'You have not loved us!'" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Was not Esau Jacob's brother?** - This may also be expressed as a statement. AT: "You know that Esau was Jacob's brother." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **declares Yahweh** - "Yahweh has solemnly said this"
  * **I have loved Jacob** - Here "loved" implies a relationship of Yahweh to Jacob by means of a covenant between them.
  * **Esau I have hated** - Here "hated" implies that there was no covenant between Yahweh and Esau.
  * **his mountains** - That is, the hill country of Edom. 
  * **and I have made his inheritance a place for the jackals of the wilderness** - The presence of wild animals such as jackals was a frequent symbol in the Old Testament of land deserted by its the people who once lived there.
  * **his inheritance** - That is, the region in which Esau's descendants, the nation of Edom, occupied.
