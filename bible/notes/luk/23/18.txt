===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they cried out all together** - "All the people in the crowd shouted"
  * **Away with this man** - "Execute this man (Jesus)"
  * **for** - "because of his involvement in" or "because of his crime of"
  * **a certain rebellion** - "trying to persuade the people of the city to rebel against the Roman government" (UDB)
