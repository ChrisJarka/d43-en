====== Sadducee ======

===== Definition: =====

Sadducees were a devout, aristocratic Jewish sect during the times of Jesus Christ

  * Sadducees maintained the Jewish Temple during Jesus' time
  * Sadducees were believed to have derived their name from the first High Priest Zadok
