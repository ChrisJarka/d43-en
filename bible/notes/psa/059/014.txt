===== translationNotes: =====


  * **let them howl like dogs** - The psalmist speaks of his enemies threatening to attack people as if they were dogs howling, growling, or barking at people. AT: "Let them threaten to attack us"
  * **wander up and down** - "wander back and forth" or "wander through the streets" 
  * **satisfied** - "content"
