===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **behold** - The word “behold” here alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows.
  * **pitcher**  - a pottery (fired clay) jar of medium size, generally with one or two handles
  * **Bethuel...Nahor** - These are names of men. (See: [[en:ta:translate:translatenames]])
  * **Milcah** - This is the name of Nahor's wife.
  * **The young woman was very beautiful and a virgin.**- "She was a very beautiful young woman."
