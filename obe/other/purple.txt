====== purple ======

===== Facts: =====

Purple was the most prized and valuable color of the Middle East.

  * It was obtained in Biblical times from secretion from a mollusk of the Mediterranean Sea and was mainly used to dye woven materials.
  * The name "Canaan" means "land of the purple."
  * The Greek term for Phoenicia means red-purple.
  * Among the Hebrews purple clothing was considered a sign of wealth, distinction and royalty.
  * Lydia is described as a dealer of purple goods in Luke 16:19.
  * Jesus is clad in a purple robe at the crucifixtion as a mockery to his claim of being King of the Jews. Mark 15:17
