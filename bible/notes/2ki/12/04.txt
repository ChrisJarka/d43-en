===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **special objects that are to be brought into the house of Yahweh** - "sacred offerings to buy things for the temple." (UDB)
  * **assessed** - To assess means to set the amount that a person is required to pay, as in a tax. AT: "required to give,"
  * **motivated by Yahweh in their hearts to give** - "inspired by Yahweh to give" or "directed by Yahweh to give" or "that they themselves decide to give" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **every one of them from the taxpayers** - AT: "every priest must take the money from the people who pay it,"
  * **maintain the temple** - "keep the temple in good condition,"
