===== Translation Notes: =====
Job continues speaking.
  * **For God has made my heart weak** - AT: "For God has made me weak with fear" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **the Almighty has terrified me** - This means the same as the first part of the sentence. They emphasize how scared Job is of God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **It is not that I have been cut off by darkness, nor does the thick darkness cover my face** - AT: "I am not afraid of the darkness that surrounds me. It is God who frightens me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
