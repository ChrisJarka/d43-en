====== Joshua 07 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Sin brought defeat ==

It was great sin to take what was to be destroyed. Because of their sin, Yahweh withheld victory from the Israelites. Sin resulted in defeat in battle. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:sin]])
