===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **like burning coals of fire or torches**  - Ezekiel says that the creatures look like burning coals because they were bright and glowing. Alternate translation: "were bright and glowing like coals burning with an intense fire or like torches." (See: [[en:ta:translate:simile]])
  * **and there were flashes of lightning** - "and the fire was very bright, and lightning came out from the fire"
  * **The living creatures came swiftly, and when they turned they look like lightning** - Ezekiel compares the creatures with lightning because they move very fast like lightning. Alternate translation: "were going forward and coming back so quickly that they looked like lightning."
