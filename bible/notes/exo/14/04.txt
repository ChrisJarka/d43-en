===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **I** - The pronoun "I" used two times in verse 4 refers to God. 
    * **harden Pharaoh's heart** - This means that God caused Pharaoh to oppose God's will more strongly. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **get honor**  - AT: "be honored"
    * **camped as they were instructed**  - **AT:** "did as God had commanded" or "obeyed God"
    * **fled**  - AT "run away"
    * **"What have we done in letting Israel go free from working for us?"**  - He did not ask this question to anyone in particular, and did not expect an answer. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
