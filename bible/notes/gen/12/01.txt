===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now Yahweh said to Abram, "Go from your country, and from your relatives, and from your father’s household**  - meaning: Leave your life, your comforts, your security…
  * **Go from your country, and from your relatives, and from your father’s household**  - "Go from your land, from your family, from your father's household",
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **I will make of you a great nation**  - "I will start a great nation through you"
  * **and make your name great**  - "and you will be remembered"
  * **whoever dishonors you I will curse **  - "I will curse whoever treats you in a shameful way" or "if anyone treats you as worthless, I will curse him"
  * **Through you all the families of the earth will be blessed** - This can be translated with an active clause: "I will bless all the families of the earth through you."
  * **Through you** - This can be translated as "Because of you" or "Because I have blessed you."
