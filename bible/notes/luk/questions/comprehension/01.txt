====== Luke Chapter 1 ======


===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who were the "eyewitnesses" that Luke mentions in v. 2?

A. The "eyewitnesses" were the ones who were with Jesus from the beginning of his ministry. They saw all the things that Jesus did [vv. 1-2].

Q? What did some of the eyewitnesses do after they saw what Jesus did?

A. They wrote down an account or story of what Jesus did [v. 2].

Q? Why did God consider Zechariah and Elizabeth to be righteous?

A. God considered them to be righteous because they obeyed his commandments [v. 6].

Q? Why did Zechariah and Elizabeth have no children?

A. They did not have children because Elizabeth was unable to bear a child. Now she and Zechariah were very old [v. 7].

Q? What work was Zechariah doing in the temple in Jerusalem?

A. Zechariah was serving as a priest [v. 8].

Q? What did Zechariah do in the temple?

A. He burned incense to God [v. 9].

Q? What did the people do while Zechariah was in the temple?

A. The people stayed outside in the courtyard and were praying [v. 10].

Q? Who appeared to Zechariah while he was in the temple?

A. An angel of the Lord appeared to Zechariah in the temple [v. 11].

Q? How did Zechariah act when he saw the angel?

A. When Zechariah saw the angel, he became very afraid [v. 12].

Q? What did the angel say to Zechariah?

A. The angel told Zechariah not to be afraid and that his wife Elizabeth would have a son. His son's name would be John [v. 13].
