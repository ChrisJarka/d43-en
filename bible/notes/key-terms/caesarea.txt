====== Caesarea ======

===== Facts: =====

[sĕsˊə rēˊə] ( Gk. Kaisareia). A city on the Palestinian coast, about 37 km. (23 mi. ) south of Mt. Carmel, and about 105 km. (65 mi. ) northwest of Jerusalem.

  * The Apostle Peter first preached to the Gentiles in Caesarea
  * Caesarea was a city that the Apostle Paul sailed from to reach Tarsus
