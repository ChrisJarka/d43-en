===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **she bore Jacob no children**  - "she had not give birth to children for Jacob"
  * **Rachel envied her sister**  - "Rachel was jealous of her sister"
  * **Jacob’s anger was burned against Rachel**  - "Jacob was very angry with Rachel"
  * **Give me children, or I will die**  - This was exaggerated language.
  * **who has kept you from having children**  - "who has withheld from you the fruit of your belly"
