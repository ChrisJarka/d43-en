===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Because of you** - Here "you" refers to the priests, prophets, and leaders of the previous verse.
  * **Zion will be plowed like a field, Jerusalem will be a heap of ruins, and the hill of the temple will be like a forested hill.** - Micah means that Jerusalem will be completely destroyed. A large city with many people living there will become like a field or forest. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **Zion will be plowed like a field** - AT: "An enemy army will plow Zion like a field" or "An enemy army will completely destroy Zion" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
