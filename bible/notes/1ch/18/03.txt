===== translationNotes: =====

  * **David then defeated Hadadezer** - Here David and Hadadezer are spoken of as if they were alone, but the reader should understand that these kings represent their armies that were with them. AT: "David and his army then defeated the army of Hadadezer" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Hadadezer** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Zobah** - This is the name of a country. 
  * **was traveling to establish his rule by the Euphrates River** - "was going to the Euphrates river to conquer people in that area"
  * **a thousand chariots** - "1,000 chariots" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **seven thousand horsemen** - These are soldiers who rode on horses. "7,000 horsemen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **twenty thousand footmen** - These are soldiers who walked. "20,000 footmen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **hamstrung** - to partially cripple a person or animal by cutting the hamstring muscle on the back of the thigh
  * **reserved** - to keep for a special use
  * **a hundred chariots** - "100 chariots" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
