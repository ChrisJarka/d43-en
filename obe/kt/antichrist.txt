====== antichrist ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "antichrist" refers to a person or teaching that is against Jesus Christ and his work.

  * The apostle John wrote that a person is an antichrist if he says that Jesus is not the Messiah or if he says that Jesus is not God who became a human. There are many antichrists in the world.
  * The Bible also teaches that there is a general spirit of antichrist in the world which opposes Jesus' work.
  * The New Testament book of Revelation teaches that a man called the "antichrist" will be revealed at the end of the great tribulation. This man will be empowered by Satan to attempt to destroy the church and Israel, but he will be defeated by Jesus.

(See also: Messiah, Christ, revelation, tribulation other)
