====== blasphemy ======


===== Definition: =====


Blasphemy is to speak disrespectfully about God.


    * Blasphemy against the Holy Spirit is the unforgivable sin.
    * Blasphemy violates the Third Commandment. It was a crime punishable by death in ancient Israel.
