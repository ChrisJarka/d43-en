===== translationNotes: =====

  * **when the power of his house increases** - The word "power" here refers to wealth or riches. Possible meanings are 1) "when he gains more wealth in his house" or 2) "when his family becomes richer."
  * **he will take nothing away** - "he will take nothing with him to the grave"
  * **his power will not descend with him** - The word "descend" refers to when a person dies. AT: "his power will not go with him when he dies" or "he will not keep his power when he dies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
