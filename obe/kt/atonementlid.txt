====== atonement lid ======

===== Definition: =====

The top, or cover for the ark of the covenant that Moses built in the wilderness.

  * On top of the atonement cover were two gold cherubim. These were a visual depiction of God's presence and power.
  * The Old Testament speaks of God's presence resting on the atonement cover when He met with Israel.
