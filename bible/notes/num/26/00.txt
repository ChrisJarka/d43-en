====== Numbers 26 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

== Counting the people ==

Moses counted the people, tribe by tribe.


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== A new generation ==

None of the adults who came out of Egypt with Moses were still alive except the two faithful spies, Joshua and Caleb  just as God had said when the people of Israel refused to enter the land before when the spies frightened them.
