===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues reminding the people of Israel about what Yahweh said.

  * **You will call to mind** - This is an idiom. AT: "You must remember" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **by a mighty hand and by a display of power** - The phrase " a mighty hand" refers to Yahweh and his power. These two phrases mean basically the same thing and emphasize the greatness of Yahweh's power. AT: "by showing his great power" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
