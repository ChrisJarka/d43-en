====== 1 Samuel Chapter 31 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who did the Philistines fight against and pursue?

A. They fought against Israel and pursued Saul and his sons. [31:1-2]

Q? What happened to the men of Israel, Saul, and his sons?

A. The men of Israel fled from the Philistines and fell dead on Mount Gilboa. Saul's sons were killed and Saul was overtaken by the archers and was in severe pain. [31:1-3]

Q? What did Saul ask his armor bearer to do because Saul was in severe pain and he was afraid his enemy would come and abuse him?

A. Saul asked him to draw his sword and kill him. [31:4]

Q? What did Saul do when his armor bearer was afraid and would not kill him?

A. Saul took his own sword and fell on it. [31:4]

Q? What did the armor bearer do after he saw that Saul was dead?

A. He fell on his sword and died. [31:5]

Q? Why did the men of Israel from the other side of the valley and beyond the Jordan abandon their cities and flea?

A. They fled because they saw that the other men of Israel had fled and that Saul and his sons were dead. [31:7]

Q? What did the Philistines do with Saul's body and armor that they found when they came to strip the dead?

A. The Philistines cut off his head and stripped off his armor. They fastened his body to the city wall of Beth Shan and his armor was put in the temple of the Ashtoreth. [31:8-10]
