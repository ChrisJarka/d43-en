===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **And it came about that as they were on the way to Jerusalem**- This can be translated as "Now while on the way Jerusalem," or "Now as he was traveling towards Jerusalem."
    * **he was traveling through the middle of Samaria and Galilee**- This can also be translated as, "he was passing between Samaria and Galilee," or "he was passing through a place between Samaria and Galilee."
    * **As he entered into a certain village **- This can also be translated as "As he was entering a certain village" or "As he was about to enter a certain village."
    * **there he was met by ten met who were lepers**  - This can also be translated as "ten men who were lepers met him," or "ten lepers met him."
    * **a certain village**- Literally, "some village."
    * **who stood far away from him **- This can also be translated as "who stood at a distance from him."
    * **they lifted up their voices, saying **- This can also be translated as "they called with a loud voice, saying," or "they spoke with a loud voice."
    * **Master **  - This refers to someone who has been put in charge over a group of people, and not someone who owns someone else. It is a different word than is usually translated as "Master" and does not have quite the same level of authority. You may want to translate it as, "Boss" or, "Foreman."
    * **leper**- This was a generic term for a skin disease that was highly contagious. According to the laws of Moses, people with this disease were forced to live outside of the city, away from all other people. If they had to pass by other people they had to shout, "Unclean, unclean".
    * **have mercy on us**- This can also be translated as "please have mercy on us," or to clarify what is the request, "please show us mercy by healing us of leprosy."
