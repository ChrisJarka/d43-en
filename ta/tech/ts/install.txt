====== Installing the translationStudio APP on an Android device ======


Watch the {{:en:ta:tech:installing_translationstudio_-_android_v2.mp4|Installing translationStudio}}  One minute 15 seconds video or follow these directions:

Note: Installing requires an Internet connection.

  * Tap on the Google Play Store Icon on the Android device. Google Play Store opens.
  * In the Play Store search for the program. Click on the magnifying glass at the top right of the window.
  * Tap on the words "Search Google Play" and type in translationStudio (do not use spaces).
  * Tap the magnifying glass or close the keyboard.
  * When the Play Store has found the program, tap on the tS icon.
  * Tap "Install" on the next screen.
  * Tap "Accept" to accept the default settings of the program.
  * The program will download and then install. Once the program is installed there will be two choices Open or Uninstall.
  * Use the Android back button to close the Google Play Store.
  * The tS icon is now on the Android screen.\\

translationStudio is now installed.
