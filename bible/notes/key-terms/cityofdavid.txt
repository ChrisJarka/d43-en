====== city of David ======


===== Facts: =====


The "city of David" is another name for Jerusalem.


    * Jerusalem is referred to as the "city of David" because he lived there while he ruled Israel.
    * The "city of David" may also refer to <font 13px/arial;;#000000;;#ffffff>Bethlehem</font> the place of David's birth.
