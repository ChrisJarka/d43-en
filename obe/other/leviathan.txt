====== Leviathan ======

===== Facts: =====

An large extinct creature mentioned in the earliest writings of the Old Testament.

  * Leviathan is described as a large creature, strong and fierce and able to make the water around him "boil".
  * Isaiah the prophet refers to Leviathan as "the gliding serpent". Some Old Testament scholars believe Leviathan to be a type of dinosaur.
  * Job writes from first-hand knowledge of Leviathan; the animal was most likely alive during his lifetime.
