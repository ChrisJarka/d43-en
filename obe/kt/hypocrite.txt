====== hypocrite, hypocrisy ======


===== Definition: =====


A hypocrite is a person who pretends to be more righteous than he really is. Hypocrisy is the act of being a hypocrite.


    * Hypocrites want to be seen doing good things so that people will think that they are good people.
    * Hypocrites often pretend that they do not sin.
    * Hypocrites might criticize others for doing the same sinful things that they themselves do.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * In english, a "hypocrite" is called "two-faced."
  * "Hypocrite" can also be translated as "fraud" or "fraud."
