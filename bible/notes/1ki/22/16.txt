===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the king said to him**  - "him" Refers to Micaiah the prophet.
  * **I require…tell me**  - Here "I" and "me" refer to King Ahab.
  * **how many times must I require**  - "I have told you many times" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **I saw all Israel**  - Here "I" refers to Micaiah, and "all Israel" refers to the armies of Ahab and Jehoshaphat.
  * **scattered…like sheep who have no shepherd**  - "They are behaving like people who have no one to lead them." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile|Simile]])
  * **These have no shepherd**  - These soldiers have no leader. It is implied that their leader was killed. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit|Explicit and Implicit]])
