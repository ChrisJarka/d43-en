===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Those who are coming after us** - "Our enemies who are chasing after us." This refers to the Babylonian army.
  * **we can find no rest** - This speaks of being able to rest as if "rest" were an object that could be found. AT: "we are unable to rest" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **We have given ourselves to ... to get enough food** - This phrase "given ourselves" is an idiom. AT: "We have made a treaty with ... so that we would have food to eat" or "We have surrendered to ... to have enough food to remain alive" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **they are no more** - This refers to them being dead. AT: "they have died" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **we bear their iniquities** - Here the punishment received is represented by the "iniquities" that it was a punishment for. AT: "we bear the punishment for their sins" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
