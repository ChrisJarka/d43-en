===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement** - King Solomon continues praying.

  * **carry out what you have promised** - This is a request. To carry out what one has said is an idiom meaning to do what one has said. AT: "please do what you have promised" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **You will not fail to have a man** - God speaking in terms of David having a man implies that the man would be a descendant of David. This can be stated positively. AT: "you will always have a descendant" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
  * **a man in my sight to sit on the throne of Israel** - The phrase "in my sight" here implies that God would choose the man and the man would want to obey God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **to sit on the throne of Israel** - The throne is a metonym for the activity of the one who sits on the throne. AT: "to rule over Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **to walk in my law, as you have walked before me** - The way a person behaves is spoken of as if that person were walking on a path. AT: "to obey my law, as you have obeyed me" or "to be faithful to my law as you have been faithful to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **that the promise you made to your servant David will come true** - "that the things that you promised your servant David will happen"
