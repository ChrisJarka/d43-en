===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **breath of life**  - This phrase could alternatively be translated "spirit of life." It describes more than just the act of breathing, it describes the aspect of life that sets living breathing creatures apart from the rest of God's creation.
  * **Two of all flesh**  - This could be translated as "Pairs of every kind of flesh" or "Pairs of every variety of flesh."
  * **after them**  - This could be translated "after him" or "behind him," that is, after Noah had entered the boat.
