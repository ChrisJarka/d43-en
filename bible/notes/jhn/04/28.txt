===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **The woman left her water pot** - "The woman left her water pot behind" or "The woman abandoned her water pot" 
    * **the people** - "the people of the town" or "the inhabitants of her town"
    * **This could not be the Christ, could it?** - This question suggests that Jesus is the Christ then asks for confirmation. Another way of putting it, "could he indeed be the Christ?" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]]) 
    * **They left the town and came to him**  - That is, "they got out of the town and went where Jesus was"
