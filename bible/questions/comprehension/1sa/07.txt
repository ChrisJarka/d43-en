====== 1 Samuel Chapter 07 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did the men of Kiriath Jearim do to Abinadab's son Eleazar so that he could keep the ark of Yahweh?

A. The men of Kiriath Jearim consecrated Abinadab's son Eleazar to keep the ark of Yahweh. [7:1]

Q? Why did all the house of Israel lament during the twenty years that the ark remained in Kiriath Jearim?

A. All the house of Israel lamented during that time and wished to turn to Yahweh. [7:2]

Q? What did the people of Israel need to remove from among them in order for Yahweh to rescue them from the hand of the Philistines?

A. The people of Israel needed to remove the foreign gods and Ashtaroth from among them and worship Yahweh only. [7:3-4]

Q? What did the people of Israel admit that they had done when they gathered together at Mitzpah and poured water out before Yahweh?

A. The people of Israel admitted that they had sinned against Yahweh. [7:5-6]

Q? What did the people of Israel hear that made them afraid and ask Samuel to call out to Yahweh for them?

A. The prople of Israel heard that the rulers of the Philistines attached Israel. [7:7-8]

Q? What did Yahweh do when Samuel took a nursing lamb, offered it as a whole burnt offering to Yahweh and cried out to Yahweh?

A. Yahweh answered Samuel when he offered the nursing lamb as a burnt offering to Yahweh. [7:9]
