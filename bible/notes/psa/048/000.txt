====== Psalms 048 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 48 is a psalm of praise for Jerusalem.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Jerusalem has mighty defenses. Foreign nations will be amazed. It is something to remember and tell your children about.
