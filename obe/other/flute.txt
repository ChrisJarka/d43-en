====== flute ======

===== Definition: =====

The flute was a musical instrument made of reeds, bones, or wood. It was also called a "pipe."

  * This instrument was commonly used by shepherds to calm their flocks of sheep or goats.
  * Flutes were some of the musical instruments that King Nebuchadnezzar commanded to be played as a signal for people to worship his idol.
  * Pipes were used to play either sad music or joyful music to praise God.
