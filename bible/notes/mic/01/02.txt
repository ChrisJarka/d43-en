===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Listen, all you peoples. ** **Listen, earth, and all that is in you **- Here starts the judgement that God is presenting to Samaria and Jerusalem. The theme of a court case will continue to come up throughout the prophecy.
  * **Let the Lord Yahweh be a witness against you, the Lord from his holy temple **-
  * **Look, Yahweh comes out of his place; he will come down and tread on the pagan shrines on the earth. The mountains will melt under him; the valleys will break apart like wax before fire, like waters that are poured down a steep place. **-  (see: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
