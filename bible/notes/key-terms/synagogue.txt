====== synagogue ======


===== Definition: =====


A synagogue is a building where Jewish people assemble to pray, worship, and learn about God's word. 


    * The word synagogue, while used only once in the Old Testament, is used 56 times in the New Testament.
    * Jesus sometimes taught in synagogues.
