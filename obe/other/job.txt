====== Job ======

===== Facts: =====

Job was a faithful man who lived in a time after the flood but before Abraham.

  * The book of Job in the Bible tells about Job's suffering due to many catastrophic events in his life, yet he is patient with God and maintains his faith.
  * At the end of the book of Job, he is blessed because he maintained his faith and trust in God.
