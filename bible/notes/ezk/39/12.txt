===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **39:12 House of Israel**  –This means the people of Israel
  * **39:12,13 them- This refers to the corpses of the people of the nations that captured Israel.**
  * **39:12 And the house of Israel will bury them in order to purify the land seven months–**  Alternate translation or phrase oder-"For seven months the people of Israel will bury the foreign corpses".
  * **39:13 day of renown-**  This means a day of memorial or fame
