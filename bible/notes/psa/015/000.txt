====== Psalms 015 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 15 is a wisdom psalm telling what people should be like to live with God. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

This psalm outlines a person who is godly and sees others from a godly perspective. There are several good actions mentioned. He treats his neighbor right, not slandering, not taking a bribe or interest from him and keeps his promises to him even when it turns out to be to his hurt. 

== 10 Moral Conditions for Worship ==

Scholars have noted that this contains a list of 10 different conditions. It might have been used to help remember what were important if you wanted participate in worship at the temple. List exists of 3 positives in verse 2, 3 negatives in verse 3, 2 positives in verse 4, followed by 2 negatives in verse 5.
