===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **In the ninth year of the reign of King Zedekiah, in the tenth month, and on the tenth day of the month** - AT: "On the tenth day of the tenth month of the ninth year of the reign of king Zedekiah, it happened that"
  * **came with all his army against Jerusalem** - AT: "came with his whole army to fight against the people of Jerusalem." or "came with his whole army to conquer Jerusalem."
  * **the people of the land** - These are the inhabitants of Jerusalem, including refugees from the surrounding villages that fled to Jerusalem when the war started.
