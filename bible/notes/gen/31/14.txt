===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **ULB Genesis 31:14; "Is there any portion or inheritance for us in our father’s house?"**  - This is a rhetorical question; the speaker states a fact as a question to involve the listner more deeply. Alternate translations could be, "Our inheritance is long since gone.", "There won't be a shred of money left for us when our father dies." (See: [[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:rhetorical-question|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **ULB Genesis 31:15; "Are we not treated by him as foreigners?"**  - This is a rhetorical question; the speaker states a fact as a question to involve the listner more deeply. Alternate translation could be, "We are no longer regarded by him as family but as foreigners." (See: [[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:rhetorical-question|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **ULB Genesis 31:15; for he has sold us** - "he has sold us out of his family for his own gain."
  * **ULB Genesis 31:15; completely devoured**  - The sisters state their emotional assessment of their father's treatment of their inheritance. Alternate translations could be, "completely used up" or "completely consumed." (See:[[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:hyperbole|Hyperbole]])
  * **ULB Genesis 31:16; God has taken away**  - "God has snatched"
  * **ULB Genesis 31:16; are now ours and our children's**  - "belongs to us and to our children."
  * **ULB Genesis 31:16; whatever God has said to you, do it **- "do all that God has told you."
