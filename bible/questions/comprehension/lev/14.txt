====== Leviticus Chapter 14 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Where must the priest examine the diseased person on the day of his cleansing?

A. The priest must examine the diseased person outside of the camp to see if the infection is healed. [14:3]

Q? What did the priest command the diseased person take to bring about his cleansing?

A. The priest commanded that the diseased person take two live clean birds, cedar wood, scarlet yarn and hyssop. [14:4]

Q? After the priest sprinkles the mixture of blood, water, cedar wood and hyssop over the diseased person seven times, what does he do with the remaining bird?

A. After the priest sprinkles the mixture over the diseased person seven times, he releases the remaining bird to fly into the fields. [14:7]

Q? What must the person being cleansed do after the priest has pronounced him clean?

A. The person being cleansed must wash his clothes, shave off all of his hair, bathe himself in water and live outside his tent for seven days. [14:8,9]
