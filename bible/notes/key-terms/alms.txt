====== alms, charity ======

===== Definition: =====

An offering given to those who are poor or who have particular needs.

  * To give "alms" may be in the form of physical charity, spiritual giving or hospitality.
  * To give "alms" is the exercising of righteousness, a recognition of blessedness, and brings reward for those who practice it.
  * In the Sermon of the Mount, Jesus warns about using almsgiving to show off.

(See also: other)
