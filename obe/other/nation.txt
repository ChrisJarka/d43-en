====== nation ======

===== Definition: =====

A nation is a group of people ruled by some form of government. It often consists of a specific people group with a shared ethnicity and other common traits.<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit></font>

  * The term "nation" often has the idea of a formal government, culture, and political boundaries.
  * In the Bible, there were many nations, including the Israelites, the Philistines, the Assyrians, the Babylonians, the Romans, and the Greeks.
  * A nation can refer to a country, but not always. It is important to check the context.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * <font 14.399999618530273px line-height: 15.680000305175781px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Depending on the context, t</font> he word "nation" could also be translated as, "people group" or "people" or "country" or "kingdom."
  * If a language has a term for "nation" that is distinct from these other terms, then that term can be used wherever it occurs in the Bible text, as long as it is natural and accurate.
