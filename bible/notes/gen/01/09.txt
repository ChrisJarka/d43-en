===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Let the waters…be gathered.**  - This can be translated with an active verb: "Let the waters…gather" or "Let the water…come together" (UDB)
  * **dry land**  - This refers to land that was not covered with water. It does not refer to land that was too dry for farming.
  * **He saw**  - This could be translated as "And he saw."
