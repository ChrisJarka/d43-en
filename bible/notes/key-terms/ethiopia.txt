====== Ethiopia ======

===== Facts: =====

Ethiopia was a country in Africa south of Egypt in biblical times. Ethiopia was located on the very southern part of the Nile River

  * Today this area is the nation of Sudan.
  * Other biblical names were Cush and Nubia.
