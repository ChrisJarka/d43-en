====== winepress ======

===== Definition: =====

A winepress is a device that is used to squeeze clusters of grapes and extract the liquid from them.

  * Winepresses were used in Israel to create wine for various occasions.
  * The term wine press is also used figuratively to describe God's wrath being poured out on the nations.
