====== Colossae ======

===== Facts: =====

Colossae was a city in New Testament times located in Asia Minor about 100 miles east of the Mediterranean Sea coast at Ephesus.

  * The book of Colossians was written by Paul to the believers at Colassae to correct heresies that arose within the church.
