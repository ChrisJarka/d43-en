====== Job Chapter 37 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What makes Elihu's heart tremble?

A. The noise of God's voice and the sound from his mouth causes Elihu's heart to tremble. [37:1-2]

Q? How is God's voice described as he does things that we cannot comprehend?

A. God's voice is described as thunder. [37:4-5]

Q? Why does God stop the hand of every man from working?

A. God stops the hand of man from working so that all people may see his deeds. [37:7]

Q? For what reason do the beasts go into hiding and stay in their dens?

A. The beasts go into hiding and stay in their dens because of the storms that come. [37:8-9]

Q? Who is it that gives ice, freezes the waters, weighs down the clouds with moisture and scatters lightning through the clouds?

A. It is God who gives ice, freezes the waters, weighs down the clouds with moisture and scatters lightning through the clouds [37:10-11]

Q? For what reasons does God give guidance to the clouds and cause them to do whatever he commands?

A. God makes all this happen sometimes for correction, sometimes for his land, and sometimes as acts of covenant faithfulness. [37:12-13]

Q? What does Elihu want Job to stop and think about?

A. Elihu wants Job to stop and think about God's marvelous deeds. [37:14]

Q? What happens when God forces his will on the clouds?

A. God makes lightning bolts flash in the clouds. [37:15]

Q? What are other things that Elihu asks Job if he understands?

A. Elihu asks Job if he understands the floating of the clouds and how his garments become hot. [37:16-17]

Q? What does Elihu ask Job about the sky?

A. Elihu asks Job if he can spread out the sky as God can. [37:18]

Q? Why does Elihu say that he and others cannot lay out their arguments before God?

A. Elihu says that they cannot lay out their arguments because of the darkness of their minds. [37:19]

Q? What is it that people cannot look at after the wind has passed and the sky is cleared of its clouds?

A. After the sky is cleared of its clouds, people cannot look at the sun when it is bright in the sky [37:21]

Q? What does Elihu say that the Almighty does not do to his people?

A. Elihu says that the Almighty does not oppress people. [37:23]

Q? To whom does Elihu say that the Almighty does not pay attention?

A. Elihu says that the Almighty does not pay attention to those who are wise in their own minds. [37:24]
