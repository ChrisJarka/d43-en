====== Leviticus 25 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Sabbath and Jubilee Years ==

Every seventh year, the land should not be planted. Every fiftieth year Israelite slaves must be freed and all land that has been bought returned to its original owners.
