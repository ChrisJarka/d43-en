====== Hannah ======

===== Facts: =====

Hannah was the mother of the prophet Samuel in the Old Testament. She was one of two wives of Elkanah.

  * <font 14px line-height: 17.8181819915772px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Hannah was not able to conceive a child, which was a great grief to her.</font>
  * <font 14px line-height: 17.8181819915772px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>At the temple, Hannah earnestly prayed for God to give her a son, promising to dedicate him to serving God.</font>
  * <font 14px line-height: 17.8181819915772px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>God granted her request and when the boy Samuel was old enough, she brought him to serve at the temple under the leadership of the priest Eli.</font>
  * God also gave Hannah other children after that.
