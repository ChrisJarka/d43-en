====== Psalms Chapter 004 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who does David expect to answer when he calls?

A. God of his righteousness will answer when David calls. [4:1]

Q? Who will have mercy on David and listen to his prayer?

A. God of his righteousness will have mercy on him and listen to his prayer. [4:1]

Q? What are the people doing to David's honor?

A. They are turning his honor into shame. [4:2]

Q? Who does Yahweh set apart?

A. He sets apart the godly for himself. [4:3]

Q? How and when do you meditate?

A. Meditate in your heart silently on your bed. [4:4]

Q? What should we sacrifce and to whom should we put our trust?

A. Offer the sacrifices of righteousness and put your trust in Yahweh. [4:5]

Q? What will many say?

A. Many will say," Who will show us anything good?" [4:6]

Q? What is David asking Yahweh to do?

A. Yahweh, lift up the light of your face on us. [4:6]

Q? What has Yahweh given David's heart?

A. Yahweh has given my heart more gladness

than others have when their grain and new wine abound. [4:7]

Q? Who makes me safe and secure when I lie down in peacful sleep?

A. You alone, Yahweh, make me safe and secure. [4:8]
