===== Translation Notes: =====

See [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames]].

  * **Asshurim, Letushim, and Leummim** - The letters "im" at the end of these words shows that they were groups of people and not the names of individual men.
  * **All these** - This refers to the people named in verses 2 through 4.
