  * **brought him before the judgment-seat ** - "took him to be judged by the governor" 
  * **contrary to the law** - The Jews were perhaps purposely making Paul's offenses against Jewish law or custom sound like crimes against Roman law. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:ambiguity|Ambiguity]])
