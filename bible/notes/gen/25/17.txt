===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **These are the years of the life of Ishmael, 137 years**  - "Ishmael lived 137 years" or "Ishmael lived one hundred and seven years" (See: [[en:ta:translate:translateunknowns-numbers]])
  * **was gathered to his people**  - "joined his family members who had already died"
  * **They lived** - "His descendants settled" (UDB)
  * **from Havilah to Shur**  - "between Havilah and Shur"
  * **as one goes toward**  - "in the direction of"
