===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **we **- Luke, the author of Acts, traveled with Paul on this journey.
  * **we learned **- "we learned from the people" or "we found out from the residents"
  * **native people **- The word here for "native people" refers to people who do not speak Greek. Some have translated "native people" as "barbarians'" but this is not correct. .Alternate translataion: "residents of the island" or use a word that does not give the idea of a kind of people that are uncivilized or violent.
  * **not just ordinary kindness**  - "a great deal of kindness" (See: Litotes)
  * **they lit a fire -**  "they put together twigs and branches and burned them"
  * **welcomed us all **- Possible meanings are 1) "welcomed all of the people from the ship" or 2) "welcomed aul and all his companions."
