====== law, principle ======

===== Definition: =====

A law is a legal rule against doing something that is typically written down and enforced by legal representatives from within a governmental organization or cultural group. Principles are guidelines or standards for decision-making.

  * Judges apply penalties for the breaking of laws.
  * Typically most governmental organizations or cultures have a group of people enlisted, such as the police, who ensure laws are followed, and detain those who break the law to be prosecuted.
  * Man's laws are usually based on some of the Ten Commandments, like do not steal or murder.
  * Principles for Christian thought and decision-making should be based on the Bible, like the Ten Commandments.

(See also: other)
