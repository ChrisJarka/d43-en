====== ark ======


===== Definition: =====


An ark is a rectangular box.


    * An ark can range in size from very small to very large.
    * In the Bible, the word "ark" is only used to refer to either the "ark of the covenant" and the ark that Noah built to escape the flood.
    * The word ark can be translated as "box", "chest" or "case".
