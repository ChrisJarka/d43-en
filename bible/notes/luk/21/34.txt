===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **so that your hearts are not troubled** - "so that you are not distracted"
  * **debauchery**  - "unrestrained self-indulgence" or "indulging too much in things that make you feel good." This can also be translated with a specific examples such as "too much feasting."
  * **the worries of life** - "worrying too much about this life" 
  * **that day** - This can be translated more specifically as "the day the Son of Man will come."
  * **that day will come on you suddenly as a trap** - "that day will come when you are not expecting it." (See: [[en:ta:figuresofspeech:simile]])
