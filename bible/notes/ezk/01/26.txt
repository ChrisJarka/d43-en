===== translationNotes: =====

  * **over their heads** - "over the heads of the living creatures"
  * **was the likeness of a throne** - Here the abstract noun "likeness" means that what Ezekiel saw looked like a throne. The word can be translated with a verbal phrase. AT: "was something that looked like a throne" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **sapphire** - a very valuable stone that is clear blue and very shiny
  * **on the likeness of the throne was** - The abstract noun "likeness" can be translated with a verbal phrase. AT: "on what looked like a throne there was" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **a likeness like the appearance of a man** - The abstract nouns "likeness" and "appearance" can be translated with verbal phrases. If you need to tell who this is, you should probably identify him as Yahweh ([[:en:bible:notes:ezk:01:01|1:3]]). AT: "something that looked similar to what appeared to be a man" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
