===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the one who sent me**  - This phrase is referring to God.
  * **I AM WHO I AM**  - Jesus is quoting God when he spoke to Moses. This is a sacred name for God well known among the Jews.
  * **Truly, truly**  - Jesus says this twice to emphasize the importance of what he is about to say.
