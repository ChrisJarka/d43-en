===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **She **- This pronoun refers to Oholah
  * **Mine **- This pronoun refers to Yahweh.
  * **Her **- This pronoun refers to Oholah
  * **Encroach **- This word means to slowly move into a piece of land that is not yours.
  * **Them **- This pronoun refers to the Assyrian men.
  * **Herself **- This pronoun refers to Oholah.
