===== Translation Notes: =====

The introductory "but" shows that a different eagle is being describe than the one before. 

  * **great eagle** - The eagle is large, powerful, and impressive.
  * **behold **- The word "behold" here alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows.
  * **The vine turned its roots towards the eagle** - "The roots of the vine grew towards the eagle"
  * **from the plant terrace** - "from the place where the vine was planted"
  * **so it would be watered** - "so the eagle would water the vine" (See: [[en:ta:translate:active]])
    * **It had been planted** - Alternate translation: "the first eagle had planted the vine."
