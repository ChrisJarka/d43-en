===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the great waters; - ** This refers to the Nile River
  * **make their waters run like oil - **  This simile compares the water now running as oil, smoothly and peacefully, without interruption, in contrast to the monster stirring up and making the water muddy as in verse 2. (See: simile)
  * **<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>this is the Lord Yahweh’s declaration</font>  ** <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>-</font>    "I, the Lord God, want you to pay attention."
