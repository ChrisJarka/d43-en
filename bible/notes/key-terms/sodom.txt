====== Sodom ======


===== Definition: =====


Sodom was a city in the southern part of Canaan. Sodom and its twin city, Gomorrah, were destroyed by God because of the wickedness of the people there.


    * The area where Sodom was situated was very well watered and produced abundant crops.
    * Abraham's nephew, Lot, chose the city of Sodom as a dwelling place.
