===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **showed covenant faithfulness to him** - This is faithfulness because of the covenant that God made with Joseph's ancestors. It can be translated as “showed faithfulness to him because of his covenant” or “showed kindness to him because of his covenant.”
  * **Whatever they did there, Joseph was in charge of it **- "Joseph was in charge of whatever they did there."
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **Whatever he did, Yahweh prospered **- "Yahweh prospered whatever he did."
  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **The prison warden did not worry about anything that was under his care, because Yahweh was with him. Whatever he did, Yahweh prospered.**  - These two sentences reinforce God's faithfulness to Joseph. An alternate translation could be: "That God was faithful to Joseph can be shown by the fact that the prison warden allowed Joseph command responsibility over the administration of his prison."
