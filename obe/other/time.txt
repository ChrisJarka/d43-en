====== time ======

===== Facts: =====

SymboThis is not just the measurement of the passing of common time measurements such as minutes, hours, days or months. lically in the Bible a "time" often refers to a specific season when certain events will transpire.

  * This is not just the measurement of the passing of common time measurements such as minutes, hours, days or months.
  * Both Daniel and Revelation speak of a "time" of great trouble that will come upon the earth.
  * In prophecy the terms "time, times and half a time" refer to a 3 and 1/2 year period during the great tribulation period at the end of this present age.
