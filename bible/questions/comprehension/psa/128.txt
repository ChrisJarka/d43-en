====== Psalms Chapter 128 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What happens to those who honor Yahweh and walk in his ways?

A. Everyone who honors Yahweh and walks in his ways is blessed. [128:1]

Q? How does the writer describe one who enjoys what his hands have provided.?

A. That person will be blessed and prosper. [128:2]

Q? What does the writer say the wife of the blessed man is like?

A. He says she is like a fruitful vine. [128:3]

Q? What does the writer say the children of the blessed man will be like?

A. He says the children will be like olive plants. [128:3]

Q? What will happen to the man who honors Yahweh?

A. The man who honors Yahweh will be blessed. [128:4]

Q? What is the writer asking Yahweh to do for the man who is blessed from Zion?

A. The writer is asking that the man blessed from Zion may see the prosperity of Jerusalem all the days of his life and that he may live to see his chilodren's children.[128:]
