====== children, child ======

===== Definition: =====

Literally, a child is defined by the Torah as someone under the age of 20 years old. Figuratively, child or children refer to the people of God the Father, or believers.

  * Jesus sometimes called his followers children.
  * In their letters, Paul and John called believers their children.
  * Children can also mean Christians that are not strong in their knowledge of Christ, or they may be new believers.

(See also: other)
