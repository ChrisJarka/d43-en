===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For he breaks me with a tempest **- "Tempest "is another word for "storm" and is used as a metaphor for troubles. AT: "He brings trouble upon me."
  * **and multiples my wounds without cause **- AT: "gives me many wounds for no reason."
  * **catch my breath**  - literally to breathe a breath. AT: "have relief from my pain." See: Doublet
  * **he fills me with bitterness **- bitterness could be clarified with "more suffering."
