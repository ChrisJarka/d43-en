===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **sought to lay hands on him**  - That is, "They looked for a way to arrest him."
  * **with his speech** - That is, "with what Jesus was saying" or "with what Jesus was teaching."
  * **deliver him up to**  - This may also be translated, "bring him to" or "hand him over to" or "put him into the custody"
  * **the rule**  - a law enforced by a governor
