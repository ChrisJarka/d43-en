====== sanhedrin ======

===== Definition: =====

A Jewish rabbinic judicial council.
    * The sanhedrin paid Judas Iscariot thirty pieces of silver to betray Jesus.
    * Jesus was tried by the sanhedrin, before being sent to Pontius Pilate.
    * Paul also had charges brought against him before the sanhedrin.
  

(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:council]], other)
