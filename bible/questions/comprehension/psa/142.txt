====== Psalms Chapter 142 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who does David cry out to and tell his troubles?

A. David crys out to Yahweh with his voice and pours out a lament to tell Yahweh his troubles. [142:1-2]

Q? Who directs David when his spirit is weak?

A. Yahweh knows his path when David's spirit is weak. [142:3]

Q? What does David see when he looks to the right?

A. David sees that there is no one who cares about him, and there is no escape for him. [142:4]

Q? Who does David say is his refuge, his portion in the land of the living.

A. David says of Yahweh that Yahweh is David's refuge and his portion in the land of the living. [142:5]

Q? What does David ask of Yahweh?

A. David asks Yahweh to listen to his call, rescue him from his persecutors, and bring his soul out of prison so that David can give thanks to Yahweh's name. [142:6-7]

Q? What will happen to David because Yahweh has been good to him?

A. The righteous will gather around David because Yahweh has been good to him. [142:7]
