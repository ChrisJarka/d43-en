====== Introduction to Proverbs ======

===== Part 1: General Introduction =====

**Outline of Proverbs**

   - Introduction and Solomon’s place (Prov. 1:1-7)
  - Collection of sayings (Prov. 1:8-9:18)
  - First collection of Solomon (Prov. 10:1-22:16)
  - First “Sayings of the Wise Men” (Prov. 22:17-23:14)
  - Other sayings (Prov. 23:15-24:22)
  - Second “Savings of the Wise Men” (Prov. 24:23-34)
  - Second collection of Solomon (Prov. 25:1-29:27)
  - The Words of Agur (Prov. 30:1-33)
  - The Words of Lemuel (Prov. 31:1-9)
  - The Virtuous Woman (Prov. 31:10-31)

**What is the Book of Proverbs?**

Most societies have their own proverbs, which are usually short sentences well-known for their meanings and understood by everyone who speaks that language.
