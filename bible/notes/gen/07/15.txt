===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **breath of life**  - This phrase could alternatively be translated "spirit of life." It describes more than just the act of breathing, it describes the aspect of life that sets living breathing creatures apart from the rest of God's creation.
  * **Two of all flesh**  - This could be translated as "two of every kind of land animal"
  * **flesh **- This refers to all creatures living on land.
  * **after them**  - This could be made more explicit: "after they entered the ark" (See: Explicit and Implicit Information).
