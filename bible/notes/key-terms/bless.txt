====== bless, blessing ======

===== Definition: =====

To "bless" someone or something is to state a desire for positive and beneficial things to happen to them. In Bible times a father would often pronounce a formal blessing on his children.

  * When people bless others, they cause beneficial things to happen to them.
  * When<font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>people 'bless" God, this means they are praising him.</font>
  * When God blesses something, he causes it to be fruitful and to have a positive effect on others.
  * This can also be translated as, "to provide abundantly for" or "to be very kind and favorable toward". A phrase like, "God has brought great blessing to" might be translated as, "God has given many good things to" or "God has provided abundantly for" or "God has caused many good things to happen to".
  * A statement like "he is blessed" could also be translated as "he will greatly benefit" or "he will experience good things" or "God will cause him to flourish". A phrase like "Blessed is the person who…" could be translated as "How good it is for the person who…"
  * When Jesus "blessed" food, this could be translated as "praised" or "thanked" God for the food.
