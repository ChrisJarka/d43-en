===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Malchus** - (See: [[en:ta:translate:translatenames]])
    * **sheath**  - the cover for a sharp knife or sword so it would not cut the owner
    * **The cup my Father has given me**  - cup here means the amount of suffering Jesus will accept. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
    * **should I not drink it?** - "I must drink it." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes
]])
