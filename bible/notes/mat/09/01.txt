===== Translation Notes: =====

The people begged Jesus to leave their territory.

  * **came into his own city**  - "the city where he was staying" (UDB)
  * **Behold, they**  - "they" refers to "some people"
  * **their faith**  - This refers to the faith of those who brought him.
  * **Son, be of good cheer **- "Young man, be encouraged" (UDB). This is not the son of Jesus.
