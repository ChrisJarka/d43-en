===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **cubits** - (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicaldistances|Biblical distances]]).
  * **hearth** - This refers to the floor around the altar.
  * **the hearth will be ... square**  - This means the hearth will have four equal sides.
  * **border** - This is the line or part where an object begins or ends.
  * **surrounding borders** - This refers to the area around the border.
  * **groove** - This is a long, narrow cut in a surface.
  * **facing east** - This means the steps face the east side.
