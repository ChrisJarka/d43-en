====== bow and arrow ======

===== Definition: =====

<font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The bow and arrow is a weapon used in battles against enemies or for killing animals for food.</font>

  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>A bow is a curved weapon made out of wood, bone, metal, or other hard material, such as a deer's antler. It is strung with a string, cord, or vine, in order to shoot arrows.</font>
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>An arrow is a thin</font>    <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>shaft with a sharp, pointed head on one end.</font>    <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>In ancient times, the arrows could be made of a variety of materials, such as</font>    <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>wood, bone, stone, or metal</font>    <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>.</font>
  * Bows and arrows were commonly used by nomadic peoples, hunters, and warriors.
  * The term "arrow" is also sometimes used in the Bible as a symbol of enemy attacks or of divine judgment.
