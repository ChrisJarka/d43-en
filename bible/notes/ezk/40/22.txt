===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Its/It **- These pronouns refer to the north gate.
  * **niches**  - These were small rooms.
  * **corresponded **- The measurements were the same as for the east side starting (verse 6). An alternate translation "measured the same." (UDB)
  * **the gate that faced the way to the east**  - Alternate translations are "the gate to the east." or "the gate on the east side" (UDB)
  * **inner**  - This term refers to the courtyard that was on the other side of the entry room. There was an inner and outer courtyard to the temple.
  * **the gate to the north and to the east, and he measured from one gate to the other gate - 100 cubits**  - An alternate translation could read "the gate to the north and to the east. He measured the distance between the gates on either side of the entry room to be 100 cubits."
  * **Cubits**  - (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicaldistances|Biblical distances]])
