===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **There was with us there a young Hebrew man, a servant of the captain of the guard.**  - Here the chief cupbearer continues to speak to Pharaoh.  Alternate translation: A young Hebrew man, a servant of the captain of the guard, was with the baker and me in jail."
  * **a young Hebrew man**  - This phrase refers to Joseph. 
  * **captain of the guard**  - See how you translated this phrase in 40:3.
  * **We told him**  - "We told him our dreams"
  * **and he interpreted for us our dreams**  - "and he explained their meaning"
  * **He interpreted for each of us according to his dream.**  - "He explained the exact meaning of each of our dreams."  (See: [[en:ta:translate:doublet]])  
  * **It came about**  - This phrase is used here to mark an important event in the story. If your language has a way for doing this, you could consider using it here.
  * **as he interpreted for us, so it happened.**  - "What he said would happen later occurred."
  * **Pharaoh restored me to my post** - Here the cupbearer uses Pharaoh's title in speaking to him as a way of honoring him.  Alternate translation: "You allowed me to return to my job."
