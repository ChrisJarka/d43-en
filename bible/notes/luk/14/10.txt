===== Important Terms: =====



===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (Jesus continues speaking to the people at the Pharisee's house.)
    * **the lowest place**  - seat or place of lesser or least honor
    * **go up higher**  - move to a seat or place of higher honor
    * **you will be honored in the presence of all**  - you will be honored by the host before the other guests
    * **exalts himself**  - to think or believe that oneself is more honorable or worthy than others
    * **humbles himself**  - to think or believe that oneself is equal to or **no ** more honorable or worthy than others
    * **bold words**  - explanation
