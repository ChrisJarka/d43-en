===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Eglaim, Beer Elim, Dimon** - These are names of cities and towns. (See: How to translate names) 
    * **For the waters of Dimon are full of blood** - "The stream near Dimon will become red from the blood of people who have been killed"
    * **but I will bring even more upon Dimon** - I refers to Yahweh. This can be translated as, "I will cause even more trouble on the people of Moab."
