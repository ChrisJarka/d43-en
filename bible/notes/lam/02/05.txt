===== translationNotes: =====

  * **swallowed up** - This speaks of the Lord completely destroying Israel as if he were animal who ate them. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:lam:02:01|2:2]]. AT: "completely destroyed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **her palaces ... her strongholds** - Israel is spoken of as if it were female. 
  * **He has increased mourning and lamentation within the daughter of Judah** - The words "mourning" and "lamentation" can be expressed as verbs. AT: "He has caused more and more people to mourn and lament" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **daughter of Judah** - This is a poetic name for Jerusalem, which is spoken of here as if it were a woman. AT: "Judah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_Personification]])
  * **He has attacked his tabernacle like a garden hut** - This speaks of the tabernacle being easily destroyed as if it were a garden hut. The Lord did not destroy it himself, rather he caused Israel's enemies to destroy it. AT: "He has caused their enemies to attack his tabernacle as easily as if it were a garden hut" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **a garden hut** - a very small building for holding farming tools or for sheltering someone who is guarding a garden
  * **He has destroyed the place of the solemn assembly** - The Lord did not destroy it himself, rather he caused Israel's enemies to destroy it. AT: "He has caused the place of the solemn assembly to be destroyed" or "He has caused their enemies to destroy the place of the solemn assembly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **caused both solemn assembly and Sabbath to be forgotten in Zion** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "caused the people in Zion to forget both solemn assembly and Sabbath" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **in the indignation of his anger** - "because he was extremely angry with them" (UDB)
