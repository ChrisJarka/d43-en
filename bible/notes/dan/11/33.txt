===== Translation Notes: =====

The angel continued to explain to Daniel what was going to happen.

  * **give understanding** - AT: "teach knowledge and wisdom" 
  * **But for days they** - It is not clear who "they" refers to in 11:33 and 11:34, whether to the wise or the many or to both the wise and the many.
  * **they will fall by the sword and by flame, they will be taken as prisoners and robbed of their possessions** - AT: "their enemies will kill them with sword and with fire, take them captive and steal their possessions" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **refined, cleansed, and made pure **- These words each have similar meanings for emphasis.
  * **that they may be refined, cleansed, and made pure** - AT: "that God may refine them, cleanse them, and make them pure" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **time of the end** - AT: "end of time"
