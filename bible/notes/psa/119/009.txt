===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Beth**  - This is the second letter of the Hebrew alphabet. In the Hebrew language, each line of verses 9-16 begins with this letter.
  * **your word**  - Here this means "the word of God".
  * **I seek you**  - Here this means "I try to serve you."
  * **stray from your commandments**  - “walk away from your commandments.” Disobeying God is spoken of as wandering away from his commandments. AT: “disobey your commandments.” (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
