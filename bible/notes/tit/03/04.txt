===== translationNotes: =====

  * **when the kindness of God our savior and his love toward mankind appeared** - "when God our savior displayed his love and kindness to people"
  * **toward mankind** - "for humans"
  * **saved us through** - "saved us by means of" or "saved us by"
  * **washing of new birth** - This is making one clean inside by one's spiritual new birth.
  * **renewal** - "being made new." The Holy Spirit made one A new person.
  * **according to his mercy** - "in the same measure as his mercy" or "because he had mercy on us"
