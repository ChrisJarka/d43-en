====== Numbers Chapter 04 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What must the descendants of Kohath do in serve in the tent of meeting?

A. They are to care for the most holy things reserved for Yahweh in the tent of meeting. [4:1-4]

Q? What must Aaron and his sons do to prepare to move?

A. They must go into the tent, take down the curtain that separated the most holy place from the holy place and cover the ark of the covenant decrees with it. They must cover the ark with sea cow skins, spread a blue cloth over the table of the bread of the presence, and insert poles to carry it. [4:5-7]

Q? What must always be on the table?

A. Bread must always be on the table of the bread of the presence. [4:7-8]

Q? How are the lampstands to be prepared for moving?

A. They must put a blue cloth cover over the lampstand along with all its accessories and then covered it with sea cow skins and put on a carrying frame. [4:9-10]

Q? How is the gold altar prepared for moving?

A. It must remove the ashes from the altar and all the equipment they use in the work of the altar and spread a purple cloth on it then cover it with a blue cloth, and a covering of sea cow skins and then insert the carrying poles. [4:11-14]

Q? Who comes to carry the sanctuary after Aaron and his sons complete covering the sanctuary and all its equipment?

A. The descendants of Kohath come to carry the sanctuary forward. [4:15]

Q? What must Eleazar son of Aaron care for?

A. He must care for the oil for the light, sweet incense, the regular grain offering, the anointing oil, the entire tabernacle and all that is it. [4:16]

Q? What does Yahweh warn Moses and Aaron about concerning the Kohathite tribal clans?

A. They are not to be removed from the Levites, they must not go into the sanctuary for they will die.
