===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **answered and said to him**  - That is, "responded to him" or "replied to him."
    * **It is written **- Rather, “it has been written.” This implies authority and truthfulness, such as “it is written in the Scriptures” or “it is written in the authoritative Scriptures.”
    * **you **- This refers to the people in the OT who received God's Law.
    * **worship**  - That is, "bow down in front of."  It literally means "to physically bow down in front of," but it symbolizes submitting to, honoring, and obeying the one to whom you bow.
    * **serve**  - This literally means "offer sacrifices and prayers" like a priest. In this context it also means "do the will of" or "obey" like a servant.
    * **him**  - This refers to the Lord.
