====== the angel of Yahweh ======

===== Definition: =====

Christian scholars believe the angel of Yahweh to be Jesus Christ, the second person of the holy trinity.

  * In the old testament "the angel of Yahweh" is synonymous with "the angel of the Lord".
  * In the old testament "the" angel of Yahweh is distinct from "an" angel of Yahweh making Him distinct from created angels.
  * The angel of Yahweh speaks as God, identifies Himself as God and assumes the responsibilities of God. It is therefore assumed that the angel of Yahweh is a person of the holy trinity.
