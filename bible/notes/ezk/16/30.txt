===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Why is your heart so weak?**  - Yahweh is asking why the desire of the people was not strong. 
    * **this is the Lord Yahweh's declaration**  - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]].
    * **deeds of a promiscuous and shameless woman?**  - The word "deeds" may also be translated "actions" or "activities." The word "promiscuous" may also be translated "a person who has many sex partners." The word "shameless" may be translated "no shame." 
    * **ritual places**  - Alternate translation: "shrines" (UDB).
    * **head of every street **  - This may be translated at "every corner of the street" or "in every city square" (UDB).
