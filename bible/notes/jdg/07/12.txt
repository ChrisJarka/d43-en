===== translationNotes: =====

  * **as thick as a cloud of locusts** - The author speaks of the army as if it was a swarm of locusts AT: "camped closely together" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **they were more in number than the grains of the sand on the seashore** - The author uses a hyperbole, an exaggeration, to emphasize that there were very many camels. AT: "There were a very great number of camels" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
