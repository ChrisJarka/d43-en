===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **good pastures** - "Good pastures" are places where there are a lot of plants that are good for sheep and goats to eat.
  * **their grazing places** - "places where they can eat". Sheep "graze" when they move around from place to place and eat plants.
  * **good places for grazing** - "places that are good for grazing"
  * **abundant pastures** - "places that has a lot plants that are very good for sheep and goats"
  * **I myself** - The word "myself" adds emphasis.
  * **will shepherd** - "will feed and take care of""
  * **will make them lie down** - "will give the sheep and the goats a place to lie down and rest"
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]].
  * **will seek** - "will look for"
  * **the lost** - The words "any sheep or goat" is left out of this phrase, but they are intended to be understood. Alternate translation: "any sheep or goat that is lost." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **restore the lost** - "and bring back any sheep or goat that had been chased out of the flock so that it can be part of the flock"
  * **will bind up the broken sheep** - See how you translated "do not bind up the ones who are broken in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:34:04|3:4]].
  * **heal the sick sheep** - "heal any sheep or goat that is sick"
  * **will annihilate** - "will kill" or "will destroy"
