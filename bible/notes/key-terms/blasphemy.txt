====== blasphemy, blaspheme ======


===== Definition: =====


To blaspheme is to slander or insult God or to show a deep lack of respect for God.


    * Blaspheme can also be translated as "dishonor" or "speak disrespectfully".
    * Blasphemy is speech or behavior that shows a deep disrespect for God.
