====== Meshech ======

===== Facts: =====

Meshech is the name of a son of Noah's son Japheth. A different person named Meshech was a grandson of Noah's son Shem.

  * There was also a region of land called Meshech, which was probably named after one of the men with this name. This could be translated as, "the region called Meshech" to distinguish it from a person by that name.
  * The region of Meshech may have been located in part of what is now the country of Turkey.
