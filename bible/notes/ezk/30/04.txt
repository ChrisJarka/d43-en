===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **come against **- "attack" or "fight"
  * **anquish **- great pain
  * **when the slain fall - ** This is a reference to the time of death for those who are killed. Alternative translation: "when the dead are killed."
  * **her foundations are ruined **- This is likely a reference to the military power of Egypt being destroyed.
