====== Artaxerxes ======

===== Facts: =====

A king of Persia about 464 to 424 BC.

  * During the captivity of the Isrealites in Persia, Artaxerxes allowed Ezra and other Jewish leaders to return to Jerusalem to teach the Isrealites the Law of the Lord.
  * After Ezra, Artaxerxes allowed Nehemiah to return to Jersulem to rebuild the city's defensive wall.
