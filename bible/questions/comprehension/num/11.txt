====== Numbers Chapter 11 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? To what words of the people was Yahweh listening?

A. The people complained about their troubles as Yahweh listened. [11:1]

Q? What did Moses do when the people called out to him?

A. The people called out to Moses, so he prayed to Yahweh. [11:2]

Q? What were the people of Israel asking for when they began to weep?

A. The people of Israel began to weep and say, "Who will give us meat to eat?" [11:4]

Q? Where would the people find Manna?

A. The people would walk through the camp ground and gather manna. [11:8]

Q? When would the manna fall on the camp?

A. When the dew fell on the camp in the night, the manna also fell. [11:9]

Q? What did Moses say to Yahweh about his treatment?

A. Moses said to Yahweh, "Why have you treated your servant so badly?" [11:11]

Q? To where does Moses ask if he should carry the people?

A. Moses said to Yahweh, "Should I carry them to the land that you swore to their ancestors to give them?" [11:12]

Q? What were the people asking for when were weeping before Moses?

A. They were weeping in front of Moses and saying, "Give us meat to eat." [11:13]

Q? Who did Yahweh tell Moses to bring to him?

A. Yahweh told Moses to bring to him seventy of Israel’s elders. [11:16]

Q? What does Yahweh say the leaders will do after he puts some of the Spirit on them?

A. Yahweh said that the leaders will bear the burden of the people with Moses. [11:17]

Q? Who will supply the meat for the people to eat?

A. Yahweh will give you meat, and you will eat it. [11:18]

Q? For how long does Yahweh say will the people eat meat?

A. Yahweh says that the people will eat meat for a whole month. [11:20]

Q? What does Yahweh tell Moses he will see about Yahweh's word?

A. Yahweh says to Moses, "Now you will see whether or not my word is true." [11:23]

Q? What did the seventy elders do when the Spirit rested on them?

A. When the Spirit rested on the seventy elders, they prophesied. [11:25]

Q? What happened to the two men listed who remained in the camp?

A. The Spirit also rested on the men who had remained in the camp. [11:26]

Q? What was Moses' wish for all Yahweh's people when Joshua was jealous for his sake?

A. Moses said to Joshua, "Are you jealous for my sake? I wish that all of Yahweh’s people were prophets and that he would put his Spirit on them all!" [11:29]

Q? How far from the camp did the quail fall?

A. The quail fell near the camp, about a day’s journey on one side and a day’s journey on the other side. [11:31]

Q? How long were the people busy gathering the quail?

A.The people were busy gathering quails all that day, all the night, and all the next day. [11:32]

Q? How did Yahweh act in anger, while the people were still chewing the meat?

A.W hile the people were chewing, Yahweh became angry at them and attacked them with a very great disease. [11:33]
