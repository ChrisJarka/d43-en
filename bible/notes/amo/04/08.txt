===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Two or three cities staggered** - Here "cities" represents the people of those cities.  AT: "The people of two or three cities staggered" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) 
  * **I afflicted you with blight and mildew** - Here "afflicted you" represents afflicting their crops. AT: "I afflicted your crops with blight and mildew" or "I destroyed your crops with blight and mildew" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **blight** - This is a disease that dries and kills plants. It is caused by the hot wind from the desert.
  * **mildew** - This is another disease that kills plants.
  * **you have not returned to me** - Returning to God represents submitting again to him. Translate this as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:amo:04:06|4:6]]. AT: "you have not submitted again to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **this is Yahweh's declaration** - Yahweh speaks of himself by name to express the certainty of what he is declaring. Translate these words as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:amo:02:11|2:11]]. AT: "this is what Yahweh has declared" or "this is what I, Yahweh, have declared" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
