===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Jesus said to him,**  - Jesus is quoting the scriptures to the lawyer. The sentences following this are a quote within a quote. (The entire quote is contained in verses 37-40.)
  * **You will love the Lord your God with all  your heart, with all your soul, and with all your mind.**  - This is a quote from Deuteronomy 6:5.
  * **heart**  - <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>(See: Metaphor)</font>    Also see the UDB.
