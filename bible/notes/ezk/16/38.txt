===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **shedding of blood** - To shed blood means to commit murder.
  * **I will bring on you the bloodshed of my anger and passion** - God's revenge will result in destruction and violence.
  * **I will give you into their hand** - Alternate translation: "I will hand you over to your enemies".
