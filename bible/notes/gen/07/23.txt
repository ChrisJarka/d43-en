===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **wiped out**  - "wiped away" or "destroyed"
  * **They were all destroyed**  - This can be translated with an active clause as "Yahweh destroyed them all" or "Yahweh wiped them all out." (See: [[en:ta:vocabulary:active]].)
  * **and those with him**  - "and the people and animals with him"
  * **were left** - "remained" or "lived" or "remained alive (UDB)
  * **overcame**  - "prevailed over" or "was in control of" or "overwhelmed"
