====== Jeremiah 31 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

== God will restore Israel and Judah, continued (Jeremiah 30 - 33) ==

The northern tribe of Israel will repent and be restored.


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== The New Covenant ==

God will make a new agreement that will bring forgiveness of sins and a desire to obey God. 


=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphor ==

Jeremiah compares God's repopulating Israel and Judah to planting a garden. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
