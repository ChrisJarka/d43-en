  * **The word of Yahweh of Hosts came to me and said** - Yahweh of hosts said to me

  * **City of Truth ... the Mountain of Yahweh of Hosts** - (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the holy mountain** - the mountain that belongs to Yahweh
