  * **what looked like glowing metal with the appearance of fire**  (See Simile)
  * **him **-This refers to the man sitting on the throne.
  * **rainbow **- band of color in the sky
  * **When I saw it, I fell upon my face **- The word "it" refers to the fire and brightness in 1:27.
