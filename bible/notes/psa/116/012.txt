===== Translation Notes: =====

The person who made this song continues to speak.
  * **How can I repay...** - The singer uses this question to emphasize that repay Yahweh for the kind things that he has done for him. AT: "I cannot repay..." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **I will raise the cup of salvation** - AT: "I will offer a drink offering to Yahweh in order to thank him for saving me." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **call on the name of Yahweh**  - "will pray in the name of Yahweh” or “will trust in the name of Yahweh” or “will worship Yahweh." See how you translated "called on the name of Yahweh" in [[en:bible:notes:psa:116:003|116:4]].
  * **in the presence** - "in front of" or "before"


  * **his kindnesses to me **  - AT: " the gentle good deeds he does for me"
  * ** cup of salvation **- AT: "a drink offering"
  * **call on the name of Yahweh**  - (see Standard Notes)
