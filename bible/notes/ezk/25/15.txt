===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh: - **"The Lord Yahweh says,"
  * **"Because of what the Philistines have done through vengeance—etc. - "**Because of what the Philistines have done through vengeance, with malice in their soul, trying to destroy Israel in everlasting enmity"
  * Disdain
  * **therefore: thus says the Lord Yahweh:- **  Lord Yahweh says**:-**
  * **Behold! I will stretch out my hand against the Philistines, -**  "Behold! (Look! or pay attention. See Quick Reference Guide.) I will use my power against the Philistia people." "I" refers to the Lord Yahweh.
  * **I will cut off the Cherethites and destroy the remnant of the sea coast! - **  "I will destroy the Kereth people-group and all those who live along the coast of the Mediterranean Sea."
  * **Cherethites -**  "Kereth people-group" (See "How to Translate Names")
  * **For I will execute a great vengeance against them with furious punishments, -**  "I will get great revenge on them and show that I am angry with them by the way I punish them." (UDB)
  * **I will execute a great vengeance against them with furious punishments, **  When I get revenge on them with great punishments,
  * **so they will know that I am Yahweh when I set my vengeance upon them!’” - "**they will know that I, Yahweh the Lord, have the power to do what I say that I will do." (udb)
