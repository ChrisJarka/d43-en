====== Numbers Chapter 21 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did the Canaanite king of Arad do when he heard that Israel was traveling by the road to Atharim?

A.The king of Arad fought against Israel and took some of them captive. [21:1]

Q? What did Yahweh do in response to Israel's vow, "If you give us victory over these people, then we will completely destroy their cities."?

A. Yahweh listened to Israel and gave them victory over the Canaanites. [21:2-3]

Q? What was the place called where the Israelites destroyed the Canaanites and their cities?

A. The place was called Hormah. [21:3]

Q? What happened to the people's hearts as they traveled from Mount Hor by the road to the Red Sea to go around the land of Edom?

A. The people's hearts became very discouraged. [21:4]

Q? What was the complaint of the people against God and Moses?

A. The people complained against God and Moses and asked why they had been brought out of Egypt to die in the wilderness and they said they hated the miserable food. [21:5]

Q? What happened when Yahweh sent poisonous snakes among the people?

A. The snakes bit the people and many people died. [21:6]

Q? What did the people ask Moses to do after they confessed that they had sinned and had spoken against Yahweh and Moses?

A. The people asked Moses to pray to Yahweh to take the snakes away from them. [21:7]

Q? What did Yahweh tell Moses to do after Moses prayed for the people?

A. Yahweh told Moses to make a snake and attach it to a pole. [21:8]

Q? What happened if a person looked at the bronze snake after he was bitten by a snake?

A The person who looked at the bronze snake after he was bitten survived. [21:9]

Q? What are the places where the people of Israel traveled and camped?

A. They traveled on and camped at Oboth and Lye Abarim in the wilderness that faces Moab toward the east. Then they traveld on and camped in the Valley of Zered and on to Arnon River. [21:10-13]

Q? Where was the well from which the people were given water?

A. The people were given water when they traveled to Beer. [21:16]

Q? What was the theme of the song the Israelites sang?

A. The theme of the song was about the well that the leaders of the people dug to give them water. [21:17-18]

Q? To what places did the Israelites travel after leaving Mattanah?

A. The Israelites traveled to Nahaliel, Bamoth and to a valley in the land of Moab. [21:19-20]

Q? To whom did the Israelites send messengers?

A. The Israelites sent messengers to Sihon king of the Amorites. [21:21]

Q? What was the Israelite's request to Sihon?

A. The Israelites requested to pass through the land of the Amorites. [21:21-22]

Q? How did the king respond to the request of the Israelites?

A. The king refused to allow Israel to pass through his border and attacked Israel in the wilderness. [21:22]

Q? What was Israel's response to the attack from Sihon king of the Amorites?

A. The Israelites attacked the army of Sihon and took their land and cities from the Arnon to the Jabbok rivers. [21:24-26]

Q? How was the battle between Sihon king of the Amorites and the former king of Moab remembered?

A. The battle was rememberd in a poem by those who speak in proverbs. [21:26-30]

Q? What happened after Moses sent men to look at Jazer?

A. The Israelites took its villages and drove out the Amorites who were there. [21:32]

Q? What did Og do when Israel turned and went up by the road of Bashan?

A. Og and all his army went out to fight the Israelites at Edrei. [21:33]

Q? What did Yahweh say to Moses about Og and his army?

A. Yahweh told Moses not to fear Og and to do to him as the Israelites did to Sihon king of the Amorites. [21:34]

Q? How did Moses and the people of Israel respond to Yahweh's instructions?

A. Moses and the people killed Og, his sons, his army and his people and took over his land. [21:35]
