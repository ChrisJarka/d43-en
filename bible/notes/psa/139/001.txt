===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **A psalm of David  **- This is a song that David wrote.
  * ** you**
 - Refers to God.
  * **examined **- To look closely and carfully to learn more about it.
  * ** me **- This is David talking.
  * **know when I sit down and when I get up**  - "know everything about me"
  * **understand my thoughts from far away**  - God has complete knowledge about us; we cannot hide from God's knowing about us.("from far away")
