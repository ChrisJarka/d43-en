====== Numbers Chapter 30 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What command did Yahweh give to the people of Israel through Moses?

A. Yahweh commanded, "When anyone makes a vow to me, or swears an oath to bind himself with a promise, he must not break his word." [30:1-2]

Q? What will happen if a young woman who lives in her father's house makes a vow to Yahweh and her father hears her vow but says nothing to reverse her?

A. If a young woman makes a vow to Yahweh without her father reversing it, then all her vows will remain in force. [30:3-4]

Q? What will happen if a young woman who lives in her father's house makes a vow to Yahweh and her father stops her on the day that he hears of it?

A. If a young woman makes a vow to Yahweh and her father stops her on the day he hears of it, then none of the vows or promises by which she has bound herself will remain in force. [30:5]

Q? What will happen if a young woman who is married makes her vows or speaks rashly with her lips and her husband hears about it and does nothing to stop her?

A. If a young woman who is married makes her vows to Yaheweh without her husband stopping her, then her vows will remain in force. [30:6-7]

Q? What will happen if the young woman's husband stops her on the day that he hears about her vows?

A. If the young woman's husband stops her on the day that he hears about her vows, then he cancels the vow that she has rashly made with her lips. [30:8]

Q? What will happen if a widow or a divorced woman makes a vow to Yahweh?

A. The widow or divorced woman makes a vow, then everything by which she has bound herself will remain in force against her. [30:9]

Q? What will happen if a woman in her husband's family makes a vow that binds her to deny herself something and her husband hears of it and says nothing to her?

A. If a women in her husband's family makes such a vow and her husband says nothing to her, then all her vows must remain in force because her husband confirmed them by saying nothing. [30:10-11,13-14]
