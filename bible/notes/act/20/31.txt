===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Paul continues to speak.)
  * **be on guard **- This has the idea of being awake and being alert so can be translated as "watch out" as in UDB or "stay alert" or "continue to be alert."
  * **Remember **- Not meaning they have forgotten but in "continue to remember" or negatively, "do not forget."
  * **be on guard. Remember **- These can be 1) two commands as in ULB, or 2) "be on guard as you remember" or 3) "be on guard by remembering"
  * **for three years I did not stop instructing**  - He did not teach them continuously for three years, but over the space of three years.
  * **I did not stop instructing**
 - Can also be 1) "I did stop warning" or 2) "I did not stop encouraging with corrections"
