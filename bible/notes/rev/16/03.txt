===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **second angel**  - "angel number two"
  * **the sea**  - This refers to all bodies of salt water in the world, not just one particular sea (See: Synecdoche)
  * **it became like the blood of a dead person**  - "It" refers to the water of the sea.
  * **like the blood of a dead person - **  the water became in every way like the blood of a dead person, including the red color, the stinky smell, and the thick consistency.
