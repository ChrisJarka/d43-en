====== Bashan ======

===== Facts: =====

An Old Testament reference to a country situated just east of the Sea of Galilee; and is modern day Syria.

  * Genesis 14 records that a war amongst several kings took place at Bashan.
  * During Israel's exodus travels, they fought and took Bashan. Later King Solomon obtained supplies from that region.
