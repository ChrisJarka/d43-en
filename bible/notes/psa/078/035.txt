===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They would call**  - The pronouns "they" and "their" in these verses refer to the Israelites.
  * **that God was their rock and that the Most High God was their rescuer**  - These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: Doublet)
  * **God was their rock**  - The implied information is that God would keep them safe. See: Metaphor
  * **flatter him with their mouth and lie to him with their words**  - These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: Doublet)
  * **they would flatter him with their mouth**  - AT: "they would try to fool God with their words"
  * **their hearts were not firmly fixed on him, and they were not faithful to his covenant**  - These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: Doublet)
  * **their hearts**  - See: Synecdoche
