====== Jebusites ======

===== Facts: =====

The Jebusites were a tribe in the land of Canaan, who were descended from a man named Jebus.

  * <font 14px line-height: 15.6800003051758px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Jebus conquered the ancient city of Jerusalem and named it after himself, so it was called "Jebus" for awhile. Later the name was changed back again.</font>
  * Melchizedek, the priest-king in the Old Testament who was also referred to by the author of Hebrews, was of Jebusite origin.
