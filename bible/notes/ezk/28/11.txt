===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Further the word of Yahweh came**  - "Again the word of Yahweh came"
  * **the word of Yahweh came to me** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:03:16|3:16]].
  * **lift up a lamentation**  - sing a funeral song or a song of sorrow or mourning.
  * **thus says the Lord Yahweh**  - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:05:05|5:5]].
  * **keeper of perfection, full of wisdom and perfect in beauty -**  This is someone who has all authority and power. great wisdom, and complete beauty.
  * ** carnelian, chrysolite, and onyx! Topaz, malachite, and jasper! Sapphire, emerald, and beryl!  ** - These are all precious stones of different color.
  * **workmanship**  - skill in making things
  * **tamborine** - This is a hand held percussion musical instrument.
  * **pipes- **  musical instrument played by blowing
  * **creation **- "birth"
