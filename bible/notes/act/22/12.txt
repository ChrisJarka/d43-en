===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Ananias** - See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:act:05:01|5:1]].
  * **devout man according to the law** - Ananias was very serious about following God's law.
  * **well spoken of by all the Jews who lived there** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the Jews who lived there spoke well of him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Brother Saul** - Here "Brother" is a polite way to address someone. AT: "My friend Saul" (UDB)
  * **receive your sight** - The abstract noun "sight" can be translated with the verb "see." AT: "see again" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **In that very hour** - This was a customary way of saying something happened immediately. AT: "At that instant" or "Instantly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
