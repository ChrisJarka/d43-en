===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **My Father...has given them to me...no one is able to snatch them out of the Father’s hand** - God the Father and God the Son with their own power protect those who believe. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **the Father's hand** - The word "hand" is used to refer to God's possession or his control and protection. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **I and the Father are one** - Jesus, God the Son, and God the Father are one. They are persons of the one and only, true God (along with the Spirit). They are one in purpose, power, authority, compassion, and holiness. They are God. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
