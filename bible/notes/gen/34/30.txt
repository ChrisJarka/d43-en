===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **brought trouble on me** - "caused great difficulties for me"
  * **to make me stink** - "by making me repulsive"
  * **I am few in number.**  - "The nation of Israel is small."
  * **gather themselves against me**  - form an army
  * **Should Shechem have dealt with our sister as with a prostitute?**  - "Should Shechem have treated our sister as a prostitute?" (See: en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion)
