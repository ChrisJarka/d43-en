===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **went there**  - "went to Caesarea". This was where Paul was.
  * **After five days **  - Five days after Paul was taken to Caesarea.
  * **an orator - This refers to someone who speaks in court for such knows the Roman laws. . Alternate translation "a lawyer"' or "an attorney" or one who speaks to accuse"**
  * **When Paul stood before the governor - "When Paul was in the presence of the governor who was the judge in the court"**
  * **began to accuse him - "began to speak against him" or "began to bring violations of Roman law against him"**
  * **bold words**  - explanation
