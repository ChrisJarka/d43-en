===== Door43 (English) =====

Welcome to Door43, an openly-collaborative platform for creating, translating, and distributing open-licensed discipleship resources in any language.

==== Resources ====

These are the resources currently available for translation and use:

  * **[[:en:obs|Open Bible Stories]]** – A visual mini-Bible in any language, in text, audio, and video formats.​
  * [[:en:obs:notes|translationNotes]] – Translation and exegetical notes for Open Bible Stories.
  * [[:en:lexicon|Open Bible Lexicon]] – A Biblical lexicon, intended for integration with Open Bible Stories and Bible translations.
  * [[:en:library|Open Christian Library]] – A growing catalog of open-licensed Biblical resources.
  * [[:en:ta|translationAcademy]] – A modular, media-based, self-paced training course for translators and translation facilitators.
