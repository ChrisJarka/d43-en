====== John 21 General Notes ======

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphors ==
Jesus uses the metaphors "feed my lambs," "tend my sheep" and "feed my sheep" in this chapter as acts that a shepherd would complete. Peter would now be a shepherd of the people of God.
