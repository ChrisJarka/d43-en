===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **These men**  - "Jacob, his sons, and the people of Israel"
  * **trade in it**  - "buy, sell, and exchange goods with us"
  * **for** - "because"
  * **really**  - Shechem uses this word to add emphasis to his statement.  Alternate translation: "indeed" or "certainly"
  * **the land is large enough for them**  - Biblical tribes needed sufficient land and water for their families, livestock, and crops. Alternate translation: "There is plenty of land for them to live here." (UDB)
  * **take their daughters...give them our daughters**  - See how you translated similar phrases in 34:9.
