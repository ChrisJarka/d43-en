===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Thus says Yahweh!** - "This is what Yahweh says!" or "Yahweh says this." This sentence introduces what the Lord would say.
  * **So the ones** – "In this way, the peoples" or "In this manner, the nations"
  * **who support Egypt** - "who help Egypt"
  * **will fall** - "will die" or "come to ruin"
  * **the pride of her strength will go down** – "the strength of Egypt, which made it proud, will be gone" or "the strength that made Egypt proud will come to ruin"
  * **From the tower of Seveneh** – Ezekiel refers to towns on the borders of Egypt in order to say that the attacking army will destroy all of Egypt. Alternate translation "In all of Egypt" or "From the northern border of Egypt to the southern border of Egypt"
  * **they will fall by the sword** – Alternate translation:"the attacking army will kill people in Egypt"
  * **So they will be desolate in the midst of the desolated lands** - "So Egypt will be ruined and will be surrounded by countries that are ruined" or "so Egypt will be deserted and will be surrounded lands that are deserted"
  * **and its city will be in the midst of wasted cities** – "and the cities of Egypt will have ruined cities all around them" or "all around the cities of Egypt there will be other cities that enemies have destroyed"
  * **in the midst of**  - "among" or "surrounded by"
