====== drink offering ======

===== Definition: =====

A drink offering was an Old Testament sacrifice to God that involved pouring wine on the alter to create a pleasant aroma for God to enjoy.

  * The Apostle Paul refers to his life as being poured out like a drink offering, meaning his life was totally dedicated to serving God and he was expecting to be martyred.
