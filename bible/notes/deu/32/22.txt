===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **a fire is kindled by my anger**  - Moses is comparing the anger of Yahweh to the start of a fire. "Kindled"is a metaphor for the beginning of Yahweh's anger , like a fire is kindled . AT: "For a fire breaks out in my anger" (See: Metaphor)
  * **Burns to the lowest Sheol**  - AT: "burns down to the realm of the dead below" This metaphor compares Yahweh's anger with a fire that does not stop burning, it burns down to the lowest depths.
