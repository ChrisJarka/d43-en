===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Lehi** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:15:09|15:9]].
  * **Yahweh's Spirit came on him with power** - The phrase "came on him" refers to help that Yahweh gave Samson. This is an idiom. AT: "Yahweh's spirit made Samson very strong" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **ropes on his arms became like burnt flax** - The author speaks of the ropes  as if they were burnt flax. Burnt flax is very weak. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **flax** - fibers from the flax plant used for making threads and cloth
