===== Using the Graphic Editor =====

The graphic editor allows the writer to see how the final text will look while they are writing.

Watch the 3 minute [[http://youtu.be/5Wt5qJbMCPc|video ]]on how to use the Graphic Editor.

To open the editor right click on the pencil icon to the right of the window. The editor window opens.

Type in the open area of the edit window. The toolbar at the top works like a standard word processor.
