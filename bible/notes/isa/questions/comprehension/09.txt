====== Isaiah Chapter 09 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What will happen to her who was in anguish?

A. Her gloom will be dispelled [9:1].

Q? What lands did God humiliate in earlier times?

A. God humiliated the land of Zebulun and the land of Naphtali [9:1].

Q? What will God do to Zebulun and Naphtali in the later time?

A. He will make them glorious [9:1].

Q? On who has the light shone?

A. The light has shone on those who have lived in the land of the shadow of death [9:2].

Q? Why has the joy of the nation increased?

A. The nation's joy has increased because God has shattered the yoke of the nation's burden, the beam across the nation's shoulder and the rod of the nation's oppressor [9:3-4].

Q? What is the name of the one who will bear the rule on his shoulder?

A. His name will be called Wonderful  Cousellor, Mighty God, Everlasting Father and Prince of Peace [9:6].
