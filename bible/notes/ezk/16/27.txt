===== Translation Notes: =====

 Yahweh continues to speak about Jerusalem and the people of Israel as his unfaithful wife.

    * **I will strike you**  - Yahweh is saying He will punish the people. 
    * **I will hand your life over to your enemies**  - Yahweh is saying He will give the lives of the people to their enemies.
    * **the daughters of the Philistines**  - Yahweh will hand them over to the weakest ones of their enemies. 
    * **in the land of the merchants of Chaldea**  - The land of the Chaldeans is also where Babylon was located. Many of the Babylonians were merchants.
