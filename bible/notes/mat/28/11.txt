===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the women**  - This refers to Mary Magdalene and the other Mary.
  * **they gave**  - "the priests and elders gave"
  * **told them**  - "told the soldiers"
  * **say to others**  - "tell other people"
  * **his body**  - "Jesus' body"
  * **we were sleeping**  - the soldiers were sleeping
