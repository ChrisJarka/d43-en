====== dung ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "dung" refers to human or animal excrement.

  * Animal dung  is used in ancient adn modern times as fertilizer for enriching the soil. It is also dried and used for fuel.
  * Dung is used figuratively to refer to sinking to a very low moral condition.
  * The "Dung Gate" in the South Wall of Jerusalem was probably the gate where garbage and trash were taken out of the city.
