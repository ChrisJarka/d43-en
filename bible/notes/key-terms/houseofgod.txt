====== house of God ======


===== Definition: =====


In the Bible, the phrase "house of God" refers to the tabernacle or temple. \\
This phrase is also used to refer to the people of God.


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:tabernacle|tabernacle]], [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:temple|temple]], [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:peopleofgod|people of God]])
