===== translationNotes: =====

  * **in all this** - "when I punish you like this" or "when I discipline you like this"
  * **you still do not accept my teaching** - Accepting his teaching represents responding rightly to it. In this case his teaching was discipline, and responding rightly to it is choosing to obey him. AT: "you still do not accept my discipline" or "you still do not obey me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **walk against me** - Walking represents behavior. Walking against someone represents opposing him or fighting against him. AT: "oppose me" or "fight against me" or (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I will also walk against you** - Walking represents behavior. Walking against someone represents opposing him or fighting against him. AT: "I will also oppose you" or "I will also fight against you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I myself will hit you with even seven times more blows** - Blows represent disasters that God would send as punishment. AT: I myself will cause seven times as many disasters to come against you" or "I will punish you seven times more severely" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **for your sins** - The noun "sins" can be expressed with the verb "sin." AT: "because you continue to sin against me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
