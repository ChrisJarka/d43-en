====== adversary ======


===== Definition: =====


An adversary is a person or group who is opposed to someone or something.


    * An adversary can be an person who tries to oppose harm you.
    * An adversary can be a military opponent of a nation.
    * In the Bible, the devil is referred to as an "adversary" and an "enemy".
    * Adversary may be translated as "opponent" or "enemy", but it suggests a stronger form of opposition.
