====== wise men ======

===== Facts: =====

Wise men were astrologers or soothsayers who served in various cultures outside of Israel.

  * Wise men were called to interpret King Nebuchadnezzar's dream in the book of Daniel but were unable to.
  * In some Mediterranean countries wise men were seen as professional scholars.
  * Wise men were users of magic arts, most likely empowered by spiritual darkness.
