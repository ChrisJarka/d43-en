====== Laban ======

===== Facts: =====

In the Old Testament Laban was the Uncle of Jacob.

  * Jacob lived with Laban, and took his daughter Rachel as his wife.
  * Laban tricked Jacob and made him marry his oldest daughter Leah first, before giving Rachel to Jacob as wife
