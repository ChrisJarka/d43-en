===== Translation Notes: =====

Ezekiel is describing a message given to him by Yahweh.
  * **the Word of Yahweh came to me and said** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:16|3:16]].
  * **Son of man** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:02:01|2:3]].
  * **prophesy and say** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:21:08]]. 
  * **thus says the Lord Yahweh**  - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:05:05|5:5]].
  * **Howl**  - "Wail" or "Cry out with grief." This command is to the Egyptians and the nations that were their allies.
  * **Woe is this day** – "This day is very frightening" or "Very bad things will happen today"
  * **The day is near** - It was implied that "the day" was the time when the people would wail because Yahweh was punishing them. The word "day" refers to the day when the people will wail. Alternate translation: " "This day will come soon" or "Soon, it will be the day"
  * **The day is near for Yahweh** – It is implied that on that day, Yahweh will do the things that are about to be described. Alternate translation: "It will soon be the day when Yahweh will do what he has decided to do." (See: [[en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]])
  * **a day of clouds**  - This phrase compares the scary and destructive day to a stormy day with clouds that is scary and destructive. Alternate translation: "a day that is scary and destructive." (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]]).
  * **a time for nations**  - "a time when Yahweh will punish the nations"
