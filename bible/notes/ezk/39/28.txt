===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they** - refers to the people of Israel
  * **nations** - the people that live in large areas of land that are controlled by their own governments
  * **gathered** - brought (things or people) together into a group
  * **land** - a country or nation
  * **no longer** - generally refers to time: no more time allowed
  * **leave them there** - allow them to stay where they are
  * **hide my face** - to avoid (someone), similar to 'turn my back on' (see: Synecdoche)
  * **pour out my Spirit** - to freely express the Spirit: to talk freely about the Spirit
  * **the house of Israel** - See how you translated this in 3:1
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh’s declaration** - See how you translated this in 5:11.
