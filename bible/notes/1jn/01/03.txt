===== translationNotes: =====

  * **That which we have seen and heard we declare also to you** - "We declare to you also what we have seen and heard"
  * **have fellowship with us, and our fellowship is with the Father** - "be our close friends, and we are friends with God the Father"
  * **our fellowship** - It is not clear if John is including or excluding his readers. You may translate this either way.
  * **Father...Son** - These are important titles that describe the relationship between God and Jesus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **so our joy may be complete** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "to make our joy complete" or "to make ourselves completely happy." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
