====== scribe, expert in the Jewish law ======


===== Definition: =====


A scribe in biblical narrative was a Jewish scholar who was responsible for copying and preserving the books of the Old Testament.


    * Scribes also copied the written law as well as preserving and interpreting the oral law.
    * At times, scribes were important government officials.
    * Important biblical scribes include Baruch and Ezra.
