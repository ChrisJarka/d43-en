====== Philip, Philip the Apostle ======

===== Facts: =====

Philip was one of the original twelve disciples of Jesus.
    * Philip was from Bethsaida.
    * Philip introduced Nathaniel to Jesus.
    * Jesus questioned Philip about how to feed the 5,000.
    * At the last supper, Philip requested that Jesus show them the father.
