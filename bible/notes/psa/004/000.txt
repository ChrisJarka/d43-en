====== Psalms 004 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 4 is a psalm of deliverance. It is an evening song, intended to be sung in the temple accompanied by musical instruments during evening worship. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

God is protecting all who truly trust in him for protection.
