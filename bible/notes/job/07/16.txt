===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **loathe**  - "despise"
  * **to always be alive**  - "to live forever"
  * **my days are useless ** - "my days are pointless", "my days are of no value", Job is contrasting his days now of extreme suffering. to his former days.
  * **that you should pay attention to him ** - "that you should magnify him"
  * **that you should set your mind on him ** - "that you should direct your attention to him"
  * **that you should observe him ** - "that you should carefully inspect him"
  * **verses 17-18**  - each of these verses contains a figure of speech where two different phrases illustrate the same idea (see: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_doublet]]). If many doublets occur together, they can be considered a form of poetry that clarifies meaning, or teaches (see: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]).
