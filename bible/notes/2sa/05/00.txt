====== 2 Samuel 05 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This is the beginning of the story of David's expanding kingdom. (5-11) 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== David made king of all Israel ==
The elders of all the tribes came to Hebron to make David their king. 
== David conquers Jerusalem ==
David brought the combined army to Jerusalem and conquered it by going in through the water shaft. He enlarged the city and strengthened the defenses, and made it his capital.  King Hiram of Tyre sent David cedar lumber and workmen to build David a palace.
== David and the Philistines ==
David always consulted God before fighting a battle. The Philistines invaded Israel trying to capture David. David asked God if he should attack the Philistines, and God said yes; so he defeated them. They invaded again and God told him to attack them from the rear and he defeated them again.  


=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===
== Simile ==
David expressed God's help against the Philistines with the [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]]: "Yahweh has burst through my enemies before me like a bursting flood of water."
