====== endure, endurance ======

===== Definition: =====

To endure is to bear something difficult with patience. It also means to stand firm when times of testing come without giving up.

  * This term is similar in meaning to "persevere, perseverance".
  * Depending on the context, other ways to translate this can be, "stand firm" or "keep believing" or "continue to do what God wants you to do".
  * "Enduring" to the end as in Luke 21 or "enduring" in the face of suffering in the Bible frequently refers to the concept of remaining loyal to the Lord and committed to following him, even if this loyalty causes a person to suffer.
