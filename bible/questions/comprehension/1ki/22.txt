====== 1 Kings Chapter 22 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? How long was there no war between Aram and Israel?

A. For three years there had been no war between Aram and Israel. [22:1]

Q? Who is the king of Judah?

A. The king of Judah is Jehoshaphat. [22:2]

Q? What did the King of Israel ask Jehoshaphat to do with him?

A. The King of Israel ask Jehoshaphat to go to Ramoth gilead and make war on the king of Aram. [22:4]
