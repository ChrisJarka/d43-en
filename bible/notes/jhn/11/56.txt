===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They were looking for Jesus,** - The word "they" refers to
  * **Now the chief priests** - This is background information that explains why the Jewish worshipers were wondering if Jesus would come to the festival or not. If your language has a way to mark background information, use it here. (See: background information)
  * **What do you think? **- The speaker here was wondering if Jesus would be foolish enough to appear at the festival because there was the danger of being arrested  He asked others around them for their opinion.
  * **That he will not come to the festival?"** - The speaker is voicing what the subject of the first question is, and wondering what Jesus' decision will be about attending the festival. It implies that the speaker's opinion is that Jesus will not attend.
