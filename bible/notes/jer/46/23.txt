===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They**  - This refers to the invading army of the nation of Babylon.
  * **this is Yahweh's declaration**  - AT: "This is what Yahwah says,"
  * **it is very dense - ** The refers to the very large number and closeness of cities in Egypt**.**
  * **locusts**  - This is a type of insect that travels in very large groups and can cause great destruction by eating crops.
  * **daughter... will be made ashamed** - Jeremiah is saying that the people of Egypt will be violated in every way. That the women and girls will be violated by the soldiers of Babylon. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **he hand of people from the north**  - Here this phrase means, "the power of the nation of Babylon." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
