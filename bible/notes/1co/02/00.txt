====== 1 Corinthians 02 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

Some translations prefer to set apart quotations of the Old Testament. The ULB and many other English translations indent 2:9 and the first line of 2:16, which are quotations from the Old Testament.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Paul continues the discussion of why he did not come and commends them for disciplining the man who had sinned but told them it was time to forgive and comfort the man now.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

Metaphor [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]: A door was opened to me meaning I had a good opportunity.
Spreads the sweet aroma – spread of a smell. the spread of the Gospel is compared to the spread of a good smell
Simile [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]]: like so many people who sell the word of God for profit. Paul is contrasting himself to salesmen.
Idiom [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]: in the sight of God means  conscious of being in God’s presence.
Litotes [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]]: not ignorant


=== Other possible translation difficulties in this chapter ===

Paul uses the phrase “in the presence of Christ: It means that Christ is a witness to Paul’s forgiveness.
Aroma of death to death – To the person who refuses to believe the Gospel brings eternal death.
Aroma of life to life – To the people who believe the Gospel it brings eternal life and more life.
