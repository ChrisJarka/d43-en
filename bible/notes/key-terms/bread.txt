====== bread ======


===== Definition: =====


Bread is a basic food in all cultures made from flour, milk or water, and usually some leavening agent, such as yeast or starter, and then baked.


    * Bread is usually made into a loaf shape and baked.
    * Jesus said, "I am the bread of life" in a spiritual sense to indicate that He provides our spiritual needs.
