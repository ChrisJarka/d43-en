===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to Tyre.
  * **Aram was a dealer in your many products** - "Aram was one of your merchant because you had very many goods"
  * **emerald** - a green stone that is very valuable
  * **purple** - "purple cloth" or "purple yarn"
  * **colored cloth** - "cloth with many different colors"
  * **pearls** - beautiful pieces of hard what material that come from creatures in the sea
  * **ruby** - a red stone that is very valuable
  * **Minnith** -the name of a town east of the Jordan River (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **millet** - a small type of grain that people used to make bread
  * **balsam** - a sweet smelling gum or resin that comes from a tree
  * **your enormous wealth** - "your very large amount of wealth"
  * **Helbon** - the name of a place about 5.6 kilometers north of Damascus
  * **Zahar** - the name of a place
