===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Diblaim **- Father of Gomer. No other information is known about him.
  * **Valley of Jezreel **- Area around Jezreel.   This is where Jehu killed various members of King Ahab's family, including Ahab's wife Jezebel.  See II Kings 8 - 10.
