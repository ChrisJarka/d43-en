===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Epaphras** -  Epaphras was the person who preached the gospel to the people in Colossae. (See [[en:bible:notes:col:01:07|1:7]])
  * **one of you​​ ** - "from your city" or "your fellow townsman" (See UDB)
  * **strives for you in prayer​ ** - "prays earnestly for you" (See UDB)
  * **fully assured​ ** - "confident" or "perfect"
  * **I bear witness of him, that he works hard for you​ ** - "I can say that he has worked very hard for you" (See UDB)
  * **Laodicea ** - This was a city very close to Colossae where there was also a church.
  * **Hierapolis ** - This was another city close to Colossae.
  * **Demas ** - This is another man who worked with Paul.
  * **salute ** - "greet"
