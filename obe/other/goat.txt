====== goat ======

===== Definition: =====

A goat is a four-legged mammal related to sheep, domesticated thousands of years before Israel.

  * Palestinian goats were commonly black in color and the principal source of milk in Israel.
  * Goat skins were used for tent coverings and wine bottles and the flesh was consumed as meat.
  * The size of a goat herd was an indication of the owner's status.
  * Goats were important animals of sacrifice, especially at Passover.
