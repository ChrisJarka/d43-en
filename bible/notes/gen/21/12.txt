===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **"Do not be grieved because of the lad**  - "Do not be upset about the young man, Ishmael"
  * **and because of your handmaid**  - "and about your servant woman, Hagar."
  * **Do all that Sarah says to you. Listen to her **- "Do everything that Sarah says to you about Hagar and Ishmael" (See: [[en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]])
  * **it is through Isaac that your descendants will be named** - "it is Isaac and his children who will be your heirs"
  * **the son of the handmaid**  - "Ishmael"
