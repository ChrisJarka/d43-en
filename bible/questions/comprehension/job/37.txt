====== Job Chapter 37 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What makes Elihu's heart tremble?

A. The noise of God's voice and the sound from his mouth causes Elihu's heart to tremble. [37:1-2]

Q? To what is God's voice compared as he does things that we cannot comprehend?

A. God's voice is compared to thunder. [37:4-5]

Q? Why does God stop the hand of every man from working?

A. God stops the hand of man from working so that all people may see his deeds. [37:7]

Q? For what reason do the beasts go into hiding and stay in their dens?

A. The beasts go into  hiding and stay in their dens because of the storms that come. [37:8-9]

Q? Who is it that gives ice, freezes the waters, weighs down the clouds with moisture and scatters lightning through the clouds?

A. It is God who gives ice, freezes the waters, weighs down the clouds with moisture and scatters lightning through the clouds  [37:10-11]

Q? For what reasons does God give guidance to the clouds and cause them to do whatever he commands?

A. God makes all this happen sometimes for correction, sometimes for his land, and sometimes as acts of covenant faithfulness. [37:12-13]

Q? What does Elihu want Job to stop and think about?

A. Elihu wants Job to stop and think about God's marvelous deeds. [37:14]
