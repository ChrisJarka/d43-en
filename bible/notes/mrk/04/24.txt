===== translationWords: =====
===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He said to them** - "Jesus said to the crowd"
  * **Pay attention to what you hear** - "Pay attention to what you hear me say"
  * **you will be measured, and it will be added to you.** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God will measure, and he will add it to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **to him will be given more...from him will be taken away even what he has** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "to him God will give more...God will take away from him even what he has" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
