===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **These **- This pronoun refers to the actions done to Oholibah in 23:24-29
  * **You **- This pronoun refers to Oholibah.
  * **Their **- This pronoun refers to the Chaldeans, Babylonians and Assyrians.
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px; text-align: justify; text-indent: 17.5px; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);/Lato, sans-serif;;inherit;;rgb(51, 51, 51) font-family: Lato, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 19.6000003814697px; text-align: justify; text-indent: 17.5px; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255);>** You have walked in the way of your sister ** - You have behaved like the non-Jewish people.</font>
  * **I will put her cup into your hand **- This phrase is a metaphor where Yahweh says he will put Oholah's cup (which represents her punishment) into Oholibah's hand (which means that Oholibah will receive the same punishment as her older sister). (See Metaphor).
