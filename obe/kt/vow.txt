====== vow ======

===== Definition: =====

A vow is a promise that a person makes to God for protection, provision or presence.

  * A vow is voluntary, but once made it becomes legally binding on the person who made it.
  * Failure to keep a vow makes the person who made the vow subject to God's judgment.

(See also: other)
