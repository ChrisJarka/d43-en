===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **whole world lies** - "World" is a way in which some biblical writers refer to the people living in the world who are in rebellion against God, and the world system that is affected in every way by the corrupting power of sin (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the evil one** - "the evil one" is a way of referring to Satan. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
