===== translationNotes: =====

  * **led them out** - "led the apostles out of the jail"
  * **in the temple...into the temple** - "in the temple courtyard...into the temple courtyard. They did not go into the temple building where only the priests were allowed.
  * **all the words of this Life** - This is a reference to the gospel message that the apostles had already been proclaiming. Possible meanings are 1) "all this message of eternal life" or 2) "the whole message of this new way of living."
  * **about daybreak** - Although the angel led them out of the jail during the night, the sun was rising by the time the apostles reached the temple courtyard.
  * **sent to the jail to have the apostles brought** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "sent someone to the jail to have them bring the apostles." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
