====== harvest ======


===== Definition: =====


The word harvest is used to describe the action of picking and processing the fruits or crops that a farmer has grown.


    * The harvest normally happens at the end of a growth season of a crop.
    * The word "harvest" can be used to describe the byproducts of a person's spiritual life. The Bible speaks of "harvesting the fruits of righteousness."
    * "Harvest" can refer to people coming to faith in God by spreading the good news of Jesus Christ.
