====== declaration of Yahweh, declaration of the Lord ======

===== Description =====

This phrase occurs often in prophesy, and needs to be distinguished from "the word of Yahweh." The phrase appears in several forms including:

  * declaration of Yahweh
  * declaration of Yahweh of Hosts
  * declaration of the Lord
  * declaration of the Lord, Yahweh of Hosts
  * 

===== Standard Notes =====

The first occurrence in a book should include the translation alternates:

  * **declaration of Yahweh** - ”what Yahweh has declared” or ”what Yahweh has solemnly said”

Following occurrences in the same book can refer back to the original explanation after the pattern below:

  * **declaration of Yahweh of Hosts** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:xx:xx|x:x]].

  * **declaration of Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:xx:xx|x:x]].
