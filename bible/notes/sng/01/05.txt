===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I am dark but lovely. **- The woman is comparing the color of her skin to the color of the light-skinned women who were helping her prepare for the wedding.
  * **dark like the tents of Kedar **- The nomdic tribes in Kedar used black goat hair to build their homes. The woman is comparing her skin to these fine, beautiful tents. (See: Simile)
  * **lovely like the curtains of Solomon. **- Since Solomon was a king, he would have beautiful cloth decorations in his palace. (See: Simile)
  * **Do not stare at me because I am dark…scorched me. **- The woman is explaining to the women of the palace that her skin is dark because she has been outside where the sun could tan her skin.
  * **My mother's sons were angry with me; they made me keeper of the vineyards. **- The woman continues to explain that the sun made her dark because her selfish brothers forced her to work outside in the sun.
  * **but my own vineyard I have not kept. **- The woman is comparing her outward appearance to a vineyard. Because she was made to work outdoors, she has lost her light skin: a sign of beauty. (See: Metaphor)
