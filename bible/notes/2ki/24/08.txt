===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Nehushta ... Elnathan** - Nehushta is the name of a woman. Elnathan is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **what was evil in the sight of Yahweh** - The sight of Yahweh represents Yahweh's judgment or evaluation. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:2ki:03:01|3:2]]. AT: "what was evil in Yahweh's judgment" or "what Yahweh considers to be evil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **he did all that his father had done** - Here "all" is a generalization. It means he did the same sinful things as his father. AT: "he committed the same kinds of sins his father had committed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
