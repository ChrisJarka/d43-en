===== translationNotes: =====

  * **his good deeds done** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the good deeds that he did" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **what is written** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the words" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) 
  * **his deeds ... are written** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "men have written all of his deeds, from beginning to end" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) 
  * **all his deeds, from beginning to end** - This refers to all of the significant things that he did from the beginning of his reign as king to the end of his life. AT: "all of his deeds" or "everything he did from the beginning of his reign to when he died" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **The Book of the Kings of Judah and Israel** - This is a book that no longer exists.
