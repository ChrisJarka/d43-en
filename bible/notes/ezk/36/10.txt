===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh is speaking to the mountains of Israel
    * **I will multiply men from all the house of Israel on you mountains** - "I will make is so there are many people on you, the whole entire house of Israel"
    * **Then the cities will be inhabited and the desolations rebuilt** - Alternate translation: "Then the people of the house of Israel will live in the cities and will rebuild the ruins." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) 
    * **I will multiply man and beast** - "I will make it so that there are very many people and animals"
    * **they will multiply and be fruitful** - "they will become very many people and have very many children"
    * **you to be inhabited as you previously were** - "people to live in you as they did before"
    * **and I will bring your prosperity as in your beginning** - "and I will make everything even better for you than they were before"
    * **will know that I am Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:06:06|6:7]].
    * **will possess you** - The word "you" is singular and refers to "the land of Israel." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_yousingular]])
    * **bereave them of their children** - "cause them to have no children." The land cause the people to have no children because of famine and wild animals. (See: [[en:bible:notes:ezk:05:15|5:17]])
