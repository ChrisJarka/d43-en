====== Zephaniah ======

===== Facts: =====

Zephaniah was one of the twelve minor prophets of the Old Testament. He lived in Jerusalem and prophesied during the reign of Josiah. He chastised the people for worshipping pagan gods, especially the Babylonian mother-goddess, the Queen of Heaven.  He also warned the people of Judah about the dangers of magical practices and that foreign powers may be used by Yahweh to discipline them.
