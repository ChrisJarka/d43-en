===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The gold has become completely tarnished; the purest gold has changed.**  The people of Jerusalem should be precious and beautiful, like gold; because of their fall, it is as if they have lost their glow. (See: Metaphor) (See: UDB)
  * **tarnished **- to become dull, rusted, no longer shiny,
  * **holy stones**  - The sacred stones that made up the temple before the invader destroyed it.
  * **poured out**  - AT: " scattered in the streets" (See: UDB) (See: Personification)
  * **at the head of every street**  - This refers to intersections of streets, or at the corner of the streets.
  * **sons of Zion**  - This is a Semitic way of speaking of the inhabitants of Zion, that is, Jerusalem. (See: Idiom) (See: UDB - "the young men of Jerusalem")
  * **they are regarded as nothing but clay jars made by potter's hands**  - Jars and other vessels made of clay and ceramic were the cheapest and most disposable vessels. AT: "The inhabitants of Jerusalem are now almost worthless." (See: Metaphor)
