===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yahweh**  - This is the name of God that he revealed to his people in the Old Testament.
  * **The declaration of the word of Yahweh to Israel by the hand of Malachi.-**  " Yahweh spoke to Israel through Malachi." "By the hand of" is a figure of speech called Metonymy.  The phrase “by the hand of” someone means “by” or “through” the action of that person. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:metonymy|Metonymy]]).
  * **But you say, "How have you loved us?" -**  This question indicates doubt, or questions the truth of God's words. God is using a question to rebuke the people.  (See: [[:en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion|RhetoricalQuestion]])
  * **declares Yahweh. - **"says Yahweh."
  * **Yet I have loved Jacob, -** Yahweh chose Jacob to have a relationship with Jacob as his God.   
  * **but Esau I have hated. - **  Yahweh rejected Esau.
  * **I have made his mountains an abandoned devastation, -**  "I have made his high lands unfit to live in,"
  * **and I have made his inheritance a place for the jackals of the wilderness." - **  "and I have made his inherited lands a desert where only jackals (or 'desert animals' or 'wild dogs') can live."
