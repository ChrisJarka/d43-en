===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **See** - "Listen to this"
    * **I will lay in Zion a foundation stone** - This is comparing the person that Yahweh will place in Jerusalem to a foundation stone. (See: Metaphor)
    * **tried stone** - "tested to see if it is solid"
    * **a sure foundation** - This is a stone on which it is safe to build a house.
