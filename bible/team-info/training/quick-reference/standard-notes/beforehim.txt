====== before him ======

===== Description =====

The phrase "before him" has several different uses. 
 
//The outcry against this place is so great before Yahweh...// (Genesis 19:13) \\ 
  * **before Yahweh** - in Yahweh's awareness \\ 

//So the men whom Moses had sent to look at the land all died by the plague before Yahweh// (Numbers 14:36) \\ 
  * **before Yahweh** - by Yahweh's judgment and power \\ 

//My land is before you. Live wherever you like in it.// (Gen. 20:15) \\  
  * **before you** - accessible to you
===== Standard Notes =====


  * **before him**  - AT: "in his awareness" or "and he saw/heard it" or "and he knew it" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **before him**  - AT: "in his awareness" or "and he was listening" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **before his eyes** -  AT: "in his awareness" or "and he saw/heard it" or "and he knew it" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **before him** - AT: "accessible to him" or "and he could take it" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])


When writing the note, replace "him" and "he" or "his" with the phrase that fits the context. \\ 
If the standard note has "saw/heard," choose the one that fits the context. \\ 
Make sure that the tense fits the context.
