====== Jesus Appeared to the Women [41-08] ======

{{:en:obs:obs-41-08.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

As the women were on their way to tell the **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:disciple|disciples]]** the good news, **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]** appeared to them, and they **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:worship|worshiped]]** him. Jesus said, “Do not be afraid. Go and tell my disciples to go to **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:galilee|Galilee]]**. They **will see me** there.”

//A Bible story from: Matthew 27:62-28:15; Mark 16:1-11; Luke 24:1-12; John 20:1-18//


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **will see me** - That is, "will meet me" or, "can meet with me."
  * //A Bible story from// - These references may be slightly different in some Bible translations.
