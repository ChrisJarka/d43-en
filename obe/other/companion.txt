====== companion ======

===== Facts: =====

The term "companion" refers to a person who goes with someone else or who is associated with someone else, such as in a friendship or marriage.

  * A companion stands along side of someone as they go through life, supporting and encouraging that person through both good and bad times.
  * Depending on the context, this term could also be translated as, "friend" or "fellow traveler" or "supporting person who goes with."
