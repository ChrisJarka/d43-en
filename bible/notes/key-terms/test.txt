====== test ======


===== Definition: =====


A test refers to difficult circumstances God allows in a person's life. To "put something to a test" can also be used to mean "to challenge something or someone for proof."


    * God allows "tests" for many reasons.
    * The agent of testing in the Bible is usually Satan.
    * After fasting 40 days in the desert, Jesus was tested by Satan with three temptations.
    * The Pharisees and Saducees put Jesus to a test by asking him questions to try to trick him.
    * The Bible states that man should never "put God to a test."
