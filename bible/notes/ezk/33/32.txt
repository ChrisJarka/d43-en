===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh is speaking to Ezekiel
  * **a lovely song** - Possible meanings are 1) "a love song" or "a song about love" or 2) "a pleasing song" or "a beautiful song."
  * **to them** - "to the people of Israel that come to listen to you"
  * **a beautiful sound** - It implied that this describes the song. Possible meanings are 1) "a song that sounds beautiful" or 2) "a song that someone sings with a beautiful voice." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **that is well-played on a stringed instrument** - Alternate translation: "and someone plays very well on a stringed instrument." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **stringed instrument** - A "stringed instrument" is something that has strings and is used to make music.
  * **will obey them** - "will obey your words." This can be translated as "will do what you tell them to do."
  * **when all of this happens** - The word "this" refers to all of the bad things that Ezekiel had warned would happen to the people of Israel because they did not obey Yahweh." (See: [[en:bible:notes:ezk:07:05|7:5]] and [[en:bible:notes:ezk:33:01|33:3]])
  * **behold! it will happen** - The word “behold” here adds emphasis to what follows. It can also be translated as “Indeed.” Alternate translation: "indeed it will happen."
  * **that a prophet has been among them** – This can be translated as "that I really sent you as a prophet."
