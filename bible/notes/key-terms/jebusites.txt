====== Jebusites ======

===== Facts: =====

The Jebusites were a tribe in the land of Canaan, who descended from Jebus, the leader of the city named after himself. Jebus was conquered by King David, who gave the city of Jebus the name it carries to this day, Jerusalem.

  * Melchizedek, the priest-king referred to by the author of Hebrews, was of Jebusite origin.
