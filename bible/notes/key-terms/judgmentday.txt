====== judgment day ======


===== Definition: =====


The "judgment day" refers to a future time when God will judge every person for what he has done in this life.


    * God will judge all people by his Son, Jesus Christ.
    * On judgment day, Christ will judge people on the basis of his just character.
    * This can also be translated as "judgment time" or "time of judgment."


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:urlterm|judge]])
