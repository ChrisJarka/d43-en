====== Psalms Chapter 016 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What does David want God to do?

A. David wants God to protect him because he takes refuge in God. [16:1]

Q?

A.

Q? Who are the saints who are on the earth?

A. They are the noble people In whom David delights.[16:3]

Q? What will happen to those who seek out other gods?

A. Trouble will be increased to those who seek out other gods. [16:4]

Q? Will David worship other gods?

A. No, he will not pour out drink offerings of blood to their gods or lift up their names with his lip[s. [16:4]

Q? What does David recognize Yahweh does for him?

A. Yahweh holds David"s destiny, gives him pleasant places, and a pleasing inheritance. [16:6]

Q? Why does David bless Yahweh?

A. David blesses Yahweh because Yahweh counsels him all the time. [16:7-8]

Q? Why is David"s heart glad and exaulting Yahweh?

A. David will live in security, knows that Yahweh will not abandon his soul, and that David will not see the pit because of Yahweh's covenant faithfulness. [16:10]

Q? What benefits does David get from Yahweh?

A. Yahweh teaches David the path of life, gives him joy, and delights that comes from abiding in  Yahweh's right hand forever.
