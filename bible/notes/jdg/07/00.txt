====== Judges 07 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

== Gideon continued (Judges 6-8) ==

God tested Gideon two times and then caused the Midianites to run away.


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== God gets all the credit ==

God said, "There are too many soldiers for me to give you victory over the Midianites. Make sure that Israel will not boast against me, saying, 'Our own power has saved us.'"

== The dream interpreted ==

The other man said, "This is nothing other than the sword of Gideon (the son of Joash), the Israelite. God has given him victory over Midian and all their army."
