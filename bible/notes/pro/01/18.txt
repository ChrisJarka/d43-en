===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **these men**  - "These men" refers to the sinners who are the subject of the previous verses.
    * **lie in wait**  - Translate as you did in verse 11. 
    * **they set a trap for themselves**  - "they make plans to ruin the innocent, but they end up being ruined themselves"
    * **injustice**  - "unfair treatment"
