===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I** - Yahweh
  * **his** - refers to the Egyptian ruler's
  * **destroy his counsel** – “confuse his plans”  
  * **I will give the Egyptians into the hand of a harsh master and a strong king will rule over them** - “I will give the Egyptians over to a harsh and cruel ruler”
  * **Yahweh of Hosts** - ”Yahweh, leader of angel armies”
