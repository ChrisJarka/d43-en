====== Psalms 051 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 51 is a psalm of repentance from sin. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:repent]] and [[:en:obe:kt:sin]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Repentance ==
The psalmist was truly sorry for having sinned. He repented and God forgave his sins. God wants true sorrow for having sinned, not sacrifices. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:true]] and [[:en:obe:kt:forgive]])
