===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Jesus was crying out** - Jesus spoke loudly for all to hear.
  * **you both know me and know where I come from** - By using the word "you," Jesus is speaking to all of the people who were around him in the Temple. (See: [[en:ta:translate:formsofyou-plural]])
  * **I have not come of myself** - Jesus uses the phrase "not ... of myself" to show that his authority came only from God. (See: [[en:ta:translate:reflexpronoun]])
  * **he who sent me is true** - The word "he" refers to God. Alternate translation: "He who sent me is a true witness."  (See: [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  * **whom you do not know** - The word "whom" refers to God.
  * **I know him because I come from him and he sent me** - Jesus knows the Father because Jesus came from the Father. The Father sent Jesus.
