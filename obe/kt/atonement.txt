====== atonement ======

===== Definition: =====

Atonement deals with removing sin in order to have a right relation with God.

  * In the Old Testament, atonement for the sins of the Israelites was accomplished by a blood sacrifice, that is, by taking the life of an animal.
  * In the New Testament, Christ's death on the cross is an atonement for sin, that is, it removes the sins of the people of God to create a right relation with God.
  * Christ's death was the last blood sacrifice needed.
