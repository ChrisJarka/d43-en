====== overseer ======

===== Definition: =====

An overseer is a leader who exercises responsibility in the church.

  * In the Old Testament, an overseer held a position of responsibility or who was in charge of getting a job done.
  * The term overseer is used to describe Jesus as the Head of the Church.
  * Many believe an overseer means the same as the words "elder" or "bishop."
