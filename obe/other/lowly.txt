====== lowly, lowliness ======

===== Definition: =====

Humble, meek, without pride.

  * Jesus is the ultimate example of lowliness by taking on the form of a servant and being born in an animal's bed.
  * God opposes the proud and haughty, but gives His grace to the lowly.
