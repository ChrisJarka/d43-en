===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Bring the full tithe into the storehouse, -**  "Bring all the tithes into the storehouse,"
  * **so that there may be food in my house. -**  "so that there will be enough food in my temple."
  * **And test me now in this," says Yahweh of Hosts, -**  "If you test me,"
  * **"if I will not open to you the windows of heaven and pour out a blessing on you,-**  "see if I do not open up the windows of heaven"
  * **so that there will not be enough room to receive it. - **"and pour out blessings so great that you will not have enough room to store it."
  * **"I will rebuke the devourer for you, - **"I will control insects and desease"
  * **so that it will not destroy the harvest of your land; - **"so you will have a great harvest in your land."
  * **your vines in the fields will not drop their before the right time," says Yahweh of Hosts. -**  "Your vines will not drop their grapes before they are ripe."
  * **for you will be a land of delight," says Yahweh of Hosts. - **"for your land will be such a delight." or "for your crops will be so abundant."
