====== false prophet ======

===== Definition: =====

A false prophet is a person who makes a prophecy that does not come true or who teaches something that contradicts what the Bible teaches.

  * A false prophet wrongly claims that his message comes from God.
  * This term could be translated as, "person who falsely claims to be God's spokesman."

(See also: prophet)
