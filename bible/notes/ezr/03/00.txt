====== Ezra 03 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

Beginning the story of the building of the temple and re-establishment of the temple worship (Ezra 3-6) 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

They immediately began the temple worship even though the temple had not yet been built because they feared the people of the surrounding nations. Then they laid the foundation of the temple.
