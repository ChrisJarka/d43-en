===== Translation Notes: =====

Abraham's servant continues speaking to Rebekah's family.

  * **Now therefore** - "Now" (UDB).
  * ** if you are prepared to treat my master with loyalty and faithfulness, tell me.** - How they could show their loyalty and faithfulness can be stated explicitly. Alternate translation: "tell me if you will be kind and faithful to my master by giving Rebekah as a wife to his son, Isaac." (See: [[en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]])
  * **you** - The word "you" refers to Laban and Bethuel. (See: [[en:ta:translate:formsofyou-plural]]
  * **so that I may turn to the right hand, or to the left**  - Possible meanings are 1) "so that I will know what do" or 2) "so that I may continue on my journey." (See: [[en:ta:translate:idiom]])
