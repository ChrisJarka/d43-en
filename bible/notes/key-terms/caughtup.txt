====== caught up, taken, snatch ======

===== Definition: =====

God transporting a person from one location to another in a powerful, supernatural manner such as when:

  * Phillip was transported supernatural away from the Ethiopian (Acts 8);
  * the apostle Paul was transported alive into heaven (2 Corinthians 12),
  * Elijah was taken up by an angelic chariot alive into heaven (2 Kings 2).

(See also: other)
