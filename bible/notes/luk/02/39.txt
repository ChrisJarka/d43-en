===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **they**  - That is, "Joseph and Mary."
    * **that they were required to do according to the law of the Lord**  - That is, "that the law of the Lord required them to do." or "that the law which the Lord commanded required them to do."
    * **increasing in wisdom**  - That is, "and became more wise" or, "and learned what was wise."
    * **the grace of God was upon him**  - God was with him in some special way.
