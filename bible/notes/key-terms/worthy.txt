====== worthy ======


===== Definition: =====


Worthy describes someone or something that deserves respect or honor.


    * Being worthy is related to being valuable or having importance
    * A person may feel worthy in comparison to someone else who is more important.
    * "Worthy" could be translated as "deserving" or "important".
