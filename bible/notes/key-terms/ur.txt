====== Ur ======

===== Facts: =====

Ur was a city in ancient Chaldea, an area in modern day Iraq.

  * Ur was the city where Abraham was born, and from where God called him to leave.
  * Lot's father Haran died in Ur, which most likely influenced Lot to leave with Abraham when he left Ur.
