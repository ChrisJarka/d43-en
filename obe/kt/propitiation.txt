====== propitiation ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "propitiation" refers to something that is done to appease the wrath or justice of God.is the substitute offering that satisfies God's holy anger.

  * The offering of the sacrificial blood of Jesus Christ is the propitiation to God for mankind's sins.
  * Jesus was our propitiation, or substitute, and was punished by our sins through his death on the cross.
