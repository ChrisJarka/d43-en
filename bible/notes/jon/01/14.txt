===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Therefore**  - This can also be translated as "Because the sea became more violent."
  * **they cried out**  - "the men working on the ship prayed"
  * **do not let us perish on account of this man’s life,**  - "Please do not kill us because we cause this man to die." or "We are going to cause this man to die. But please do not kill us."
