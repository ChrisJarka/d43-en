===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **A voice says, "Cry." Another answers, "What should I cry?" **- "One person says, "Shout". Another person answers, "What should I shout?'"
  * **All flesh is grass -**  The word "flesh" is referring to all people. They are being compared to the grass. (See: Metaphor)
  * **and all their covenant faithfulness is like the flower of the field **- Here the people's faith is being compared to flowers in a field.
