===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the first month of the year** - The Hebrew calendar has 12 months. The new year marks when Yahweh rescued his people from the Egyptians. The first month of the Hebrew calendar is during last half of March and the first half of April on western calendars. (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaltimemonth]] and [[en:obe:other:biblicaltimeyear]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
