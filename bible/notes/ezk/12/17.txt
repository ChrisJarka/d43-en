===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The word of Yahweh came to me and said** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:16|3:16]].
  * ** Son of man** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:02:01|2:3]].
  * **with trembling**  - "to shake" or "to vibrate". This is referring to being so afraid that one's body, or hands, would shake while they ate.
  * **with shaking and worry** - "tremble" or "vibrate" Same as above but also be "preoccupied" or in fear of something. (See: [[en:ta:translate:doublet]])
