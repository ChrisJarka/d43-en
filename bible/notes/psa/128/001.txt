===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Song of Ascents**- See how you translated this in [[https://door43.org/en/bible/notes/psa/120/001|120:1]]
  * **who honors Yahweh, who walks in his ways**  - These two expressions are used together for emphasis. (See: Doublet)
  * **hands**  - Hands represents the whole person. (See: Synecdoche)
  * **What your hands provide**  - Whatever a person does to provide for himself.
  * **blessed and prosper**  - These two words show God's favor. (See: Doublet)
  * **Verse 1 & verse 2 are parallel**  - (See: Parallelism)
