===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues to answer Habakkuk and to speak of the Chaldeans as if they were one man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])

  * **the one making his neighbor drink ... you make them drunk** - The way that the Chaldeans cruelly treated other nations is spoken of as if they were a man who forces his neighbors to become drunk so that he can humiliate them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the one making his neighbor drink** - It is implied that he makes his neighbor drink wine. AT: "the one making his neighbor drink wine" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **you who add your poison** - Possible meanings are 1) the word "poison" is a metonym for the wine that he pours into the cup and forces people to drink AT: "you who pour your wine into a cup as if it were poison and force people to drink it" or 2) the person mixes poison with the wine that he pours into the cup and forces people to drink. AT: "you who add your poison to the wine that you force people to drink" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **so you can look on their nakedness** - "so you can look at them when they are naked." This refers to the practice of publicly humiliating a person by stripping them naked in front of others. AT: "so that you can humiliate them publicly by stripping them naked" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **You will be gorged with shame rather than glory** - The Chaldeans passionately pursuing their own glory is spoken of as if they were eating or drinking it greedily and excessively. Instead of attaining glory, they will only find shame. AT: "You will bring shame upon yourself instead of the glory that you seek" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **gorged** - to eat or drink so much that one cannot eat or drink more
  * **Drink of it** - Others treating the Chaldeans the way that the Chaldeans had treated others is spoken of as if the Chaldeans were to drink the wine that they had forced others to drink. AT: "Drink from the poisoned cup" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **The cup of Yahweh's right hand will come in its turn to you** - "As Yahweh has punished other nations with all his might, so he will punish you with all his might" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **The cup** - "The poisoned wine" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **right hand** - the stronger hand
  * **will come in its turn to you** - "will come to you as it did to others"
  * **disgrace will cover your honor** - "everyone will see your disgrace and no one will see your honor" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
