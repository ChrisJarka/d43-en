====== Ashdod ======

===== Facts: =====

Ashdod was an important Philistine city when the Israelites entered the land of Canaan. It was located near the Mediterranean Sea, halfway between the cities of Gaza and Joppa.

  * One time the Philistines captured the ark of the covenant and took it to the city of Ashdod.
  * In New Testament times, the city was called Azotus.
  * The present-day name of the city is Ashdod.
