====== Iconium ======

===== Facts: =====

Text

  * Iconium, now known as Konya, was a city in South central Asia Minor (now Turkey) visited by Paul and Barnabas after their successful ministry in Antioch on the first missionary journey.
  * They fled from this city after Gentiles and Jews threatened to stone them.
