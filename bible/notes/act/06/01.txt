===== Translation Notes: =====

**Grecian Jews**: These were Jews who had lived most of their lives somewhere in the Roman Empire outside of Israel, and had grown up speaking Greek. Their language and culture were somewhat different from those who had grown up in Israel.

**the Hebrews**: These were Jews who had grown up in Israel speaking Aramaic.

The church consisted of only Jews and proselytes so far.
