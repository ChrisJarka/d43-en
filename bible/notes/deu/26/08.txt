===== Translation Notes: =====

Continuation of the statement started in verse 5 and that will end in verse 10.

  * **Yahweh brought us** - This refers to the people of Israel that were living during that time. The individual here is speaking as part of the entire group.
  * **mighty hand ** - “hand” refers to Yahweh's power. (See: Synecdoche). AT: "with his strong hand." See how you translated this term in Exodus 32:11.
  * **with great fearsomeness**  - "by doing things which terrified the people who saw them."
  * **a land that flows with milk and honey** - “a land where plenty of milk and honey are available.” This means that the land is naturally productive, so there will be enough food for everyone, especially wild honey and dairy products. (See: Synecdoche)
