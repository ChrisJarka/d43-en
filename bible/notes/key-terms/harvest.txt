====== harvest ======


===== Definition: =====


"To harvest" is the action of picking and processing the fruits or crops a farmer has grown. A "harvest" is the product of "harvesting".


    * The harvest normally happens at the end of a growing season.
    * The words "harvest" can be used to describe the results of a person's spiritual life. The Bible speaks of a "harvest of your righteousness."
    * "Harvest" can refer to people coming to faith in God by spreading the good news of Jesus Christ.
