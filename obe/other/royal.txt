====== royal ======

===== Definition: =====

Those things which pertain to a King, Queen, Prince, Princess.

  * Examples of things that are royal are: luxurious clothing, food, palaces, transportation.
  * Israel's King David lived in a royal palace that was larger and more refined than all other residents of Jerusalem.

(See also: other)
