===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **These are the days of the years of Abraham’s life which he lived, one hundred seventy-five years**  - "Abraham lived 175 years" (See: [[en:ta:translate:translateunknowns-numbers]])
  * **Abraham breathed his last and died at a good old age, an old man with a full life**  - "Abraham lived a long life and died when he was very old"
  * **he was gathered to his people**  - This phrase means that after Abraham died, his soul went to the same place as his relatives who died before him. This could be translated as "he joined his family members who had already died." (See: [[en:ta:translate:idiom]])
