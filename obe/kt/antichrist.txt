====== antichrist ======

===== Definition: =====

The term antichrist means to be in opposition to Jesus Christ and His work.

  * The Bible speaks of a generalized spirit of antichrist that is at work in the world which attempts to thwart Jesus' work.
  * In the Book of Revelation there is a man called the antichrist, who will be revealed at the end of the great tribulation. This man will be empowered by Satan to attempt to destroy the church and Israel but he will be defeated by Jesus.
