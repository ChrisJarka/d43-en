===== Translation Notes: =====

In these verses Daniel continues talking to the king.
 
 * **large statue** - "large idol"

The following phrases are all in the passive voice. An active voice translation is provided. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]):

  * **head of the statue was made of fine gold** - "head of the statue was fine gold"
  * **arms were of silver** - "arms were silver"
  * **thighs were made of bronze** - "thighs were bronze"
  * **legs were made of iron** - "legs were iron"
  * **feet were made partly of iron and partly of clay**- "feet were partly iron and partly clay"
