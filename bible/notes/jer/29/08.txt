===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking to the captive Israelites.

  * **you yourselves are having** - Another possible meaning is "you are encouraging the prophets to have for you."
  * **they are prophesying deceitfully ... I did not send them out** - Possible meanings are 1) these words refer to the prophets and diviners, not to the dreams or 2) "they are prophesying deceitfully" refers to the dreams and "I did not send them out" refers to the prophets and diviners.
  * **this is Yahweh's declaration** - Yahweh speaks of himself by name to express the certainty of what he is declaring. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:01:07|1:8]]. AT: "this is what Yahweh has declared" or "this is what I, Yahweh, have declared" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
