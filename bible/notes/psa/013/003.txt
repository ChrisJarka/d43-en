===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Look at me and answer me **- AT: "Give attention and listen to me"
  * **enlighten me **- AT: "Teach me understanding"
  * **or I will sleep in death **- This means to die. (See: Euphamism)
  * **I have prevailed over my adversary **  - AT: "I have prevailed against David, my adversary"
  * **when I am brought down **- AT: "when I fall", "when I lose".
  * **me, my, I, him**
 - David is speaking to God.
