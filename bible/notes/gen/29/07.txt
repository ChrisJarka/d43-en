===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **We cannot water them until all the flocks are gathered together. They will then roll the stone**  - "We cannot water them until all the flocks are gathered and the shepherds roll the stone"
  * **from the well’s mouth**  - "from the well" or "from the opening of the well"
  * **and we will water the sheep**  - "then we will water the sheep"
