===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Send rams to the ruler of the land** - Isaiah is encouraging the people of Moab to send rams, as a gift, to the king of Judah in exchange for their protection.
  * **the daughter of Zion** - Here "daughter of Zion" is Jerusalem. (See: [[en:ta:translate:personification]])
  * **As wandering birds, as a scattered nest, so the women of Moab are at the fords of the Arnon River** - Here the 'women of Moab' can literally mean the women of the city or figuratively as the people of the city. This can be translated as "The people of Moab have been driven from their homes and are alone at the fords of the Arnon River." (See: [[en:ta:translate:simile]])
