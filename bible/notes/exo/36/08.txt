===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:other:names]]

  * **craftsmen**- This means people who are skilled at making objects by hand. AT: "artisans", "skilled workers"
  * **twenty-eight cubits, four cubits**  - "12.8 meters", "1.8 meters". <font 14px line-height: 19.6px;/ ;;rgb(51, 51, 51) font-family: 'Noto Sans', sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 19.6px;;;inherit>A cubit was a little less than half a meter.</font> (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicaldistance|biblical distance]])
  * **with the designs**  - This means that designs were sewn onto the curtains using the blue, purple, and red wool.
