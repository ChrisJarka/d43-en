===== Translation Notes: =====

* **have not been consumed** - "have not been rejected by me"

* **From the days of your fathers you have turned aside from my ordinances and have not kept them** -   "Since the days of your ancestors you have disobeyed my commands."

* **"How will we return?"** - "We have never gone away from you, so how can we return to you?" (UDB)
