===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I am just a flower of in a plain **  - AT: ** " **  I am like a common field flower from the plains of Sharon" (See: Metaphor)
  * **Plains of Sharon **- The Sharon plain is the northern half of the coastal plain of Israel.
  * **just a lily in a valley **  (See: Metaphor)
  * **As a lily is among thorns **- AT: "Like a lily growing in the midst of thorns" (See: Metaphor)
  * **thorns **- a hard, sharp point on a stem or branch of a plant that can be hurtful and cause pain.
