  * **rescue you from his hand** - Yahweh will rescue you from the power or control of the king of Babylon. See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]
  * **to save you and rescue you**  - The terms save and rescue mean the same thing to bring emphasis. AT: " to surely save you" See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]]
