====== Mary Magdalene ======


===== Facts: =====


Mary Magdalene began to follow Jesus after he removed seven demons from her. She followed Jesus for the rest of her life, and became an influential leader in the church.


    * Mary Magdalene was the first person to see Jesus after His resurrection.
    * Mary Magdalene has two other names:  the Magdalene and Mary of Magdala.
