====== cut to the heart ======

===== Definition: =====

<font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>To be "cut to the heart" is to have a strong sense of awareness of your own sin and guilt in a certain situation.</font>

  * This idiom also implies a desire to repent.
  * Other languages may have a different idiom that has this meaning.
  * Ways to translate this could include, "deeply grieved (about their sin)" or "feeling sorrow (because of their sin)."

(See also: other)
