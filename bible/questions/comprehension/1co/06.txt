====== 1 Corinthians Chapter 06 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who are the saints going to judge?

A. The saints will judge the world and the angels [6:2-3].

Q? What does Paul say the saints at Corinth should be able to judge?

A. Paul says they should be able to judge disputes between saints pertaining to the matters of this life [6:1-3].

Q? How are the Corinthian Christians handling their disputes with one another according to Paul?

A. Paul says one believer goes to court against another believer, and that case is placed before a judge who is an unbeliever [6:6].

Q? What does the fact that there are disputes at all among the Corinthian Christians indicate?

A. It indicates that this is a defeat for them [6:7].

Q? Who will not inherit the kingdom of God?

A. The unrighteous; the sexually immoral, idolaters, adulterers, male prostitutes, those who practice homosexuality, theives, the greedy, drunkards, slanderers, and swindlers will not inherit the kingdom of God [6:9-10].

Q? What did God do with the Corinthian believer who previously practiced unrighteousness?

A. They were cleansed and made holy; made right with God in the name of the Lord Jesus Christ and by the Spirit of our God [6:11].

Q? What are a couple of things Paul says he will not allow to master him?

A. Paul says he will not be mastered by food or sex [6:12-13].

Q? What are the bodies of believers members of?

A. Our bodies are members of Christ [6:15].

Q? Should we join ourselves to a prostitute?

A. No. May it never be [6:15].

Q? What happens when you join yourself to a prostitute?

A. You become one flesh with her [6:16].

Q? What happens when you join yourself to the Lord?

A. You become one spirit with him [6:17].

Q? Who do you sin against when you are sexually immoral?

A. You sin against your own body when you are sexually immoral [6:18].

Q? Why should you glorify God with your body?

A. You should glorify God with your body because your body is a temple of the Holy Spirit and because you were bought with a price [6:19-20].
