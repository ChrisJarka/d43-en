===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **When he thunders, there is the roar of waters in the heavens** - These phrases compare the voice of Yahweh to the loud sound made by thunder and rain. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **storehouse** - This is a place where things are kept for future use.
