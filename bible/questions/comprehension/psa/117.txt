====== Psalms Chapter 117 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who does this writer call upon to praise Yahweh?

A. He calls upon, "All you nations," and "All you peoples." [117:1]

Q? How does the writer expand his thought that Yahweh's, "Covenant faithfulness is great?"  

A. The writer goes on to say, "The trustworthiness of Yahweh endures forever." [117:2]
