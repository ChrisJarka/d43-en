====== profit, profitable ======

===== Definition: =====

Profit is gaining in addition to what one already owns. If something is profitable, it is expected to add wealth and abundance to the person who does it.

  * 2 Timothy 3:16 says that all Scripture is profitable.
  * I Corinthians 10:23 says that all things for Christians are lawful, but not all things are profitable for us to engage in as believers.

(See also: other)
