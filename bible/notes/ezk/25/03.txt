===== Translation Notes: =====

  * '**hear the word of the Lord Yahweh, thus says the Lord Yahweh: - **  Could read "Hear what the Lord Yahweh says:"
  * **Because you have said, "Aha! against my sanctuary when it was profaned -**  Aha! is a verbal expression of vengeful joy. Could read "You shouted with joy when my sanctuary in Jerusalem was destroyed"
  * **and against the land of Israel when it was desolate,- **'desolate' could be substituted with "ruined".
  * and against the house of Judah when they went into exile.

  * **therefore behold - **"be aware"
  * **I am giving you to the sons of the east as a property - **"'I' refers to God."
  * **I am giving you to the sons of the east as a property **- Could read, " you will be conquered (or taken prisoners) by people who come from the east."
  * **they will set up encampments against you and make their tents in you.**  - "they will set up tents and live in your country."
  * **soms of Ammon**  - see "How to Interpret Names."
