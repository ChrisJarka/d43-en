====== elect, chosen ======

===== Definition: =====

The word "elect" means "chosen." It is sometimes translated as "God's chosen people".

  * God "chose" or "elected" people to love him and serve him and to bear spiritual fruit.
  * God's elect or God's chosen ones refers to God's people who have been saved by him and who have been made his children.
  * "The elect" is also used to refer to the Church as a whole. or to the Old Testament nation of Israel.
  * In a few Old Testament passages, some English versions use the term "elect" to refer to the nation of Israel as God's chosen people; other versions have "chosen one" instead in those verses.
  * These versions also use the phrase "my elect" in one Old Testament verse where God is talking about his Messiah; other versions say "my chosen one" instead (Isaiah 42:1).
