===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **within any of your gates **  - AT: “within any of your towns.” Cities often had walls around them to keep out enemies, and gates for people to go in and out. (See: Metonymy)
  * **in your land**  - (See: List of Important Terms - Promised Land)
  * **must not harden your heart **- The term “heart” is often used figuratively to refer to a person's thoughts, emotions, desires, or will. Here the meaning of a hardened heart is to suppress natural compassion and sympathy. AT: "must not be insensitive". (See: Metonymy)
  * **nor shut your hand **- AT: “nor refuse to lend” (See: Synecdoche)
