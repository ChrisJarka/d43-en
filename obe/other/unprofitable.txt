====== unprofitable ======

===== Definition: =====

To be useless or fruitless.

  * The Apostle Paul's disciple named Philemon was said to be an unprofitable servant prior to becoming a Christian.
  * Jesus called the unredeemed people of the world 'unprofitable servants', because their lives do not give glory to God.

(See also: other)
