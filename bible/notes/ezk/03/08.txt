===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Behold**  -The word "behold" here adds emphasis to what follows. It can also be translated as "indeed"
  * **your face**  - This phrase  can be used to mean "you" I have made your face as stubborn as their faces and your brow as hard hardened as they.as their brows. In this phrase "you" and "your" refers to Ezekiel, "their" frefers to the people of Israel. Alternate translations.  I will make you as unyeilding and
  * **brow ** This refers to the forehead.
  * **rebellious**  - This means to refuse allegiance to someone (here speaks of God).
  * **A diamond, harder than Flint**  - I have made your brow like your forehead  the hardest stone harder than flint.
  * **Do not fear them... or their faces**- "Their" and "their faces" refer to the people of Israel.
