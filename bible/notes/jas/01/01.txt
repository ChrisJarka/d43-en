===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **of the Dispersion **- "living in many different places"
  * **greetings **- This means that James is happy to write to them.
  * **my**** brothers **- This does not mean biological brothers. "other Christian believers"
  * **various troubles **- "different kinds of trials"
