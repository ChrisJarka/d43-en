====== brother ======


===== Definition: =====


A brother is a male who shares at least one biological parent with another person.


    * Jewish men considered themselves to be brothers because they were descendants of Abraham.
    * Men who believe in Jesus are brothers because they have become children of God the Father.


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:godthefather|God the Father]])
