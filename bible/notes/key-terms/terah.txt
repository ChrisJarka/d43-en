===== Facts: =====

Terah was a descendent of Shem, one of Noah's three sons, and the father of Abram, Nahor and Haran.

  * Terah left his home in Ur with Abram, Lot (Terah's grandson), and Sarai (Abram's wife), and went to the land of Canaan.
  * He settled at Haran in Canaan and died at age of 205.
