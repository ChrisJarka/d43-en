====== the twelve, the eleven ======

===== Definition: =====

The Twelve refers to Jesus' Apostles that He chose during His ministry time, while the eleven refers to the same group after they had lost Judas Iscariot when he committed suicide.

  * In Matthew chapter 10 the names of the twelve Apostles are listed.
  * Mark 16 records that after Jesus had risen from the dead He appeared to the eleven during a meal.
