===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **virgin daughter of Babylon** - The "daughter" of a city means the people of the city. Alternate translations:  "the people of Babylon.” or "the people who live in Babylon."
  * **sit on the ground without a throne** - Here "throne" is used in a short form and refers to "their rule of other countries". AT: "your time to rule other countries has ended." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **daughter of the Chaldeans** - The "daughter" of a city means the people of the city. Alternate translations:  "the people of Chaldean.” or "the people who live in Babylon."
  * **Take the millstone and grind flour** - They will now be slaves and do the tasks that slaves do such as bring flour with heaving stones.
