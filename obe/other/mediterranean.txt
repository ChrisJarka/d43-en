====== Mediterranean, Mediterranean Sea, the Great Sea ======

===== Facts: =====

The Hebrew people, who were not a seafaring people, referred to the Mediterranean Sea as the Great Sea.

  * The Great Sea includes the Aegean, Adriatic, Ionian and the Levant seas.
  * To the Hebrews the Great Sea lay behind them when they faced East, so it was sometimes referred to as the Western Sea.
