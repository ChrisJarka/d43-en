===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Solomon finished the house of Yahweh and his own house** - The author speaks as if Solomon himself built the house of Yahweh and his own  house, because he had commanded people to build them and he directed their dedication. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Everything that came into Solomon's heart to make** - The heart is spoken of as if it were a container, and desires are spoken of as if they come into the heart. AT: "Everything that Solomon desired to make" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **he successfully carried out** - This is an idiom. AT: "he successfully did" or "he caused to be done successfully (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **by night** - "at night" or "one night"
  * **a house of sacrifice** - "the house where people will offer sacrifices to me"
