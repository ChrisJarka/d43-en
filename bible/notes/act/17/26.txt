===== Translation Notes: =====

Paul continues to speak.

  * **one man** - "one" This man is Adam, the father or first created of the human race. It could also refers to Adam and Eve, the first couple created.
  * **their, they** - The pronoun "their" and "they" refer to every nation of people living on the surface of the earth.
  * ** seek God** - "Search for God" 
  * **reach out to him ** - "to see a need for him" (See: [[en:ta:translate:Metaphor]])
  * **us ** - The pronoun "us" refers to every nation of people living on the surface of the earth plus Paul, who includes himself. (See: [[en:ta:translate:inclusive]])
