====== God living in Jerusalem ======

===== Description =====
Sometimes the Bible refers to God living in Jerusalem or to Jerusalem being his home. This is metonymy for God's "meeting" his people there, or to God's temple being there. Here are some notes to give an idea of how to write an explanation.

===== Standard Notes =====

  * **he who lives in Jerusalem** - God is spoken of as if he lives in Jerusalem because the temple where the Israelites worshiped him was there. AT: "he whose temple is in Jerusalem"(See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])

</code>

  * **his dwelling place is in Zion** - Mount Zion in Jerusalem is spoken of as if it is the place where God lives because his temple was there. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
