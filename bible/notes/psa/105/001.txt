===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **call on his name**  - Here "name" represents Yahweh. AT: "seek him and depend on him" or "trust him and obey him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **boast in his holy name**  - "honor his holy name" or "glorify his holy name"
    * **let the heart of those who seek Yahweh rejoice**  - Here "heart" represents the person that seeks Yahweh. AT: "the people who seek Yahweh will be glad" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
