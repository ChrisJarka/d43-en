====== wrong, mistreat, hurt ======

===== Definition: =====

To wrong, mistreat or hurt means to inflict physical, emotional, or legal damages upon another person or persons.

  * To wrong a person is to treat him or her unjustly according to God's standards in the Bible.
  * To mistreat means to treat another badly or roughly according to God's standards in the Bible.
  * Mistreatment includes not being aware of someone's suffering and refusing to show compassion on them as in those who refused to help the wounded Samaritan (Luke 10).
  * To hurt a person is to cause physical or mental pain.
  * A group or individual can wrong, mistreat, or hurt another group or individual.
