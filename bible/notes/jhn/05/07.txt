===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Sir, I do not have** - Here the word "sir" is a polite form of address. AT: "Sir, there is no one" 
  * **when the water is stirred up** - This can be translated in an active form. AT: "when the angel moves the water" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]).
  * **into the pool** - This was a hole in the ground that people filled with water. Sometimes they lined the pools with tiles or other stonework.  
  * **another steps down before me** - People went down several steps to get into the water in the pool. AT: "someone else always enters the water before me." 
  * **Get up** - "Stand up!"
  * **take up your mat, and walk** - "Pick up your sleeping mat, and walk!"
