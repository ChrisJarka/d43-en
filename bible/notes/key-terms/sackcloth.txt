====== sackcloth and ashes ======

===== Definition: =====

Sackcloth was a dark garment of goat or camel hair that was worn as a sign of mourning, grief or humiliation. Wearing ashes or sitting upon ashes were additional signs of deep mourning.

  * These rituals were not from the law and their origin is unknown.
