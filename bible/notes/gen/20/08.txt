===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He told all these things to them**  - "He told them everything that God had told him"
  * **What have you done to us?**  - "You have done something bad to us!" or "Look what you have done to us!" This is a scolding rhetorical question to Abraham. (See: [[en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]])
  * **to us** - The word "us" here is exclusive and does not include Abraham and Sarah. If your language uses an exclusive form of "we" or "us," use it here (See: [[en:ta:translate:exclusive]])
  * **How have I sinned against you, that you have brought on me and on my kingdom a great sin?**  - "I have not done anything bad to you that you should bring such a great punishment on me and my kingdom!"  (See: [[:en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]])
  * **You have done to me that which ought not to be done.**  - "You should not have done this to me."
