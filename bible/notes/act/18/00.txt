====== Acts 18 General Notes ======

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== "(Apollos) knew only the baptism of John" ==
Evan after the death of Jesus, the remained some Jews who followed the teachings of John the Baptist but had not yet heard about Jesus. Apollos belongs to this group who was faithful to God in following John the Baptist, but who awaiting the good news that the [[:en:obe:kt:christ|messiah]] had come. The baptism to which this passage refers is John's baptism of repentance. It is distinct from Christian baptism.
