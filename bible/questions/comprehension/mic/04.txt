====== Micah Chapter 04 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? When would the mountain of Yahweh’s house be established over the other mountains?

A. In the last days the mountain of Yahweh’s house would be established over the other mountains [4:1].

Q? Why would many nations want to go to the mountain of Yahweh?

A. Many nations would want to go to the mountain of Yahweh, so he would teach them his ways, and they would walk in his paths [4:2].

Q? In what do all the peoples walk?

A. All the peoples walk in the name of their god [4:5].

Q? Whom would Yahweh assemble?

A. Yahweh would assemble the lame and gather the outcast, those whom he had afflicted [4:6].

Q? Where would the daughter of Zion be rescued?

A. The daughter of Zion would be rescued in Babylon [4:10].

Q? According to the prophet, who does not know Yahweh's thoughts?

A. The nations do not know Yahweh's thoughts [4:12]

Q? To whom would Yahweh devote many peoples' unjust wealth?

A. Yahweh would devote their unjust wealth to himself [4:13].
