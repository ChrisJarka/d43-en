===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **a guard**  - A guard is a group of Roman soldiers that had as few as 4 and as many as 16 members.
  * **sealing the stone**  - This means placing the stone in a position so that it cannot be moved.
  * **placing the guard**  - This means soldiers stood in front of the stone.
