===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[:en:ta:workbench:translate:poetry|Translating Poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]]

  * **on stringed instruments**  - AT: “It is to be accompanied by people playing stringed instruments”
  * **A psalm of Asaph - **  AT: “This is a psalm of Asaph." or “This is a psalm that Asaph wrote.”
  * **His lair is in Salem, and his den is in Zion.**  - A "lair" or "den" is a wild animal's hidden shelter. These two statements emphasize that God's presence dwells with Israel. (See: Doublet)
