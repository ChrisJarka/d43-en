===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **battering ram**  - This is a piece of machinery that is used to hit or lift something else.
  * **walls**  - This refers to the walls surrounding the city to protect it.
  * **towers**- This refers to the towers surrounding the city to protect it.
  * **quake**  - "shake"
  * **hooves **- The feet of hourse.
  * **strong stone pillars **- "monuments"
