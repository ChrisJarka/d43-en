====== Psalms Chapter 136 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? To whom should thanks be given because he is good and his faithfulness endures forever?

A. Yahweh, the God of gods, the Lord of lords should receive thanks. [136:1-3]

Q? Why does his covenvant faithfulness endure forever?

A. He alone does great wonders, made the heavens, spread out the earth above the waters and made great lights; the sun to rule by day and the moon and stars to rule by night. [136:4-9]

Q? Why does the writer say Yahweh's covenant faithfulness endures forever?

A. His covenant faithfulness endures forever because with a stong hand and raised arm he killed the firstborn of Egypt, brought Israel out from among them and divided the Red Sea. [136:10-13]

Q? What did Yahweh do in the Red Sea?

A. In the Red Sea, he made the Isrealites to pass through the middle, and overthrew Pharaoh and his army. [136:14-15]

Q? How was Yahwey faithful to his people?

A. He led his people through the wilderness and he killed great and famous kings, of the Amorites and of Bashan. [136:16-18]

Q? What else did Yahweh do to give his people victory over their enemies?

A. He gave them land as an inheritance to Israel his servant, victory over their enemies, and food to all living beings. [136:19-15]

Q? Why does the writer say to give thanks to the God of heaven?

A. They were to give thanks because of God's faithfulness. [136:26]
