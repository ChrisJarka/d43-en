====== Psalms Chapter 144 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who trains David's hands for war and his fingers for battle?

A. Yahweh trains David's hands for war and his fingers for battle. [144:1]

Q? How does Yahweh help David?

A. Yahweh is David's covenatn faithfulness, his fortress, David's high tower, the one who rescues David, his shield, the one in whom he takes refuge, and the one who subdues nations under him. [144:2]

Q? Who is David amazed that Yahweh has noticed and thinks about?

A. David is amazed that Yahweh notices man. [144:3]

Q? What things does David list about Yahweh?

A. Yahweh causes the sky to sink and come down, touches the mountains and makes tehm smoke, sends flashes of lightning and scatters David's enemies, Yahweh also shoots arrows to bring confusion to his enemies. [144:5-6]

Q? How does Yahweh rescue David from the hand of foreigners who speak lies and their right hand is falsehood?

A. Yahweh reaches out his hand from above and rescues David out of the waters from the hands of foreigners. [144:7-8]

Q? To whom does David sing a new song and play a lute of ten strings?

A. David will sing a new song to God and play the lute as he sings praises to God. [144:9]

Q? Who has given salvation to kings and rescued David his servant from an evil sword?

A. God has given salvation to kings and rescued David his servant from an evil sword. [144:10]

Q? What does David say about foreigners?

A. David needs God's help to rescue and free him from their hands and their mouths speak les and their right hand is falsehood. [144:11]

Q? Who is David referring about when he talks of our sons be like plants who grow to full size in their youth, our daughters like carved corner pillars, shapely like those of a palace, may our storehouses be full with every produce and may our sheep produce thousands and ten thousands in our fields?

A. David is referring to the people of Israel. [144:12-13]
