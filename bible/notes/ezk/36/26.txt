===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh is speaking to the house of Israel.
  * **a new heart and a new spirit** - See how you translated "heart" and "spirit" in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:11:19|11:19]].  
  * **in your innermost parts** - "within you" or "inside of you"
  * **the heart of stone** - The phrase compares the people's hard heart with hard stone. Alternate translation: "the heart that is as hard as stone." See how you translated "hard hearts" in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:02:04|02:04]].  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])

  * **a heart of flesh **- "a tender, responsive heart"
  * **walk in my statutes**  - "obey my laws"
  * **statutes**  - written laws that are created by a government
  * **keep my judgments**  - "obey my rules"
