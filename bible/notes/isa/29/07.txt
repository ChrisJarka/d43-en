===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It will be like a dream, a vision of the night** - This phrase likens the destruction of the enemy will be like a bad dream. (See: [[en:ta:translate:simile]])
  * **will fight against Ariel** - "will fight against the City of David" See how you translated this in 23:5.
  * **to press upon her** - “to cause her great distress”
  * **It will be like when a hungry man dreams he is eating...It will be like when a thirsty man dreams that he is drinks, but when he awakes, he is fainting, with his thirst unquenched** – The enemies anticipate winning the battle against Israel like a hungry or thirsty man dreams of eating or drinking.  Both will end up without getting what they want. (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
  * **Yes, so will be the horde of nations that fights against Mount Zion** - “Yes, this will be the fate of the many nations that fight against Jerusalem”
