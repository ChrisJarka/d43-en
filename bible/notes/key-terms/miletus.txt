====== Miletus ======

===== Facts: =====

Miletus was a city in western Asia Minor near the mouth of the Meander River.

  * On his third missionary journey going back to Jerusalem, Paul sent word to Ephesus for the leaders of the Church to come and meet with him in Miletus.
  * Paul left Trophimus in Miletus to convalesce from an illness.
