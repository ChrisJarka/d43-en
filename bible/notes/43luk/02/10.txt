===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **do not be afraid**  - That is, "stop being very fearful" or, "stop being very afraid because of my sudden appearance."
    * **because I am about to announce to you good news**  - That is, "for I bring good news to you."
    * **great joy**  - That is, "much joy" or, "a great feeling of inner happiness."
    * **to all the people**  - This could refer to their fellow Jews only or to all nations.
    * **the city of David**  - That is, "Bethlehem" or, "in Bethlehem, the city of David."
    * **the sign **  - That is, "important sign" or, "special sign" or, "important way of finding him." The use of the word "'sign" indicates an event with an important meaning. "Sign" also refers to how they can find him.
    * **wrapped in strips of cloth**  - That is, "who was wrapped comfortably in a blanket."
