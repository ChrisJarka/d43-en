===== Important Terms: =====
 

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I will draw**  - "I will draw water" or "I will get water"
  * **camels** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:gen:24:10|24:10]].
  * **So she hurried and emptied her pitcher**  - "So she quickly emptied her pitcher"
  * **trough**  - a long open container for holding water for animals to drink.
  * **the trough** - Since the trough has not been mentioned yet, it can be translated as "into the water trough that was there for the animals' water."
  * **and drew water for all his camels**  - "until all his camels had enough to drink"
