===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[:en:ta:workbench:translate:poetry|Translating Poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]]

  * **For the chief musician. A psalm of David. **  - AT: “This is a psalm of that David wrote for the music leader"
  * **How long, Yahweh will v1…v2 enemy be exalted over me? **  - This is a series of questions and David does not expect answers. (See: Rhetorical questions)
  * **you, your **- Refers to Yahweh.
  * **me, I, me **- David is speaking.
