===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For you were slaughtered** - AT: "For they slaughtered you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **slaughtered** - If your language has a word for killing an animal for a sacrifice, consider using it here.
  * **you purchased persons for God** - "you paid the price so that people could belong to God"
  * **purchased** - This should not say that some other person received the price. AT: "redeemed" (UDB).
  * **persons for God from every tribe, language, people, and nation** - AT: "all kinds of people" or "people from everywhere" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
