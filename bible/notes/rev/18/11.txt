===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **merchants**  - AT: "people who sell items"
  * **her merchandise**  - the items which are for sale to the people of the Babylon.
  * **precious stone**  - "valuable gems" or "treasured gems"
  * **pearls**  - A “pearl” is a smooth, hard, shiny, white or light-colored bead formed inside mollusks in the sea and highly prized as a gem or to make valuable jewelry  (See: Translate unknowns)
  * **scarlet**  - red
  * **scented wood**  - wood that has a good smell
  * **vessel of ivory **- This is a vessel made from the tusks or horns of an animal such as an elephant. It is slightly white in color.
  * **cinnamon**  - a sweet spice (See: Translate unknowns)
