===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then** - This word clarifies the order of events in the story.  (See: [[en:ta:translate:eventorder]])  
  * **chief cupbearer**   - See how you translated this in 40:1.
  * **Today**  - This word clarifies the order of events in the story.  (See: [[en:ta:translate:eventorder]])  
  * **put me in custody in the house of the captain of the guard, the chief baker and me**  - "put the chief baker and me in jail in the house of the captain of the guard" (See: [[en:ta:translate:wordorder]])
  * **custody**  - See how you translated this in 40:3.
  * **captain of the guard**  - See how you translated this in 40:3.
  * **chief baker**  - See how you translated this in 40:2.
  * **We dreamed a dream the same night, he and I**  - The phrase "the same night" clarifies the order of entes in the story.  Alternate translation: "One night we both had dreams" (See: [[en:ta:translate:eventorder]] and [[en:ta:translate:wordorder]])
  * **We dreamed each man according to the interpretation of his dream**  - "Our dreams had different meanings."
