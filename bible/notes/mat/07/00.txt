======  Matthew 7 General Notes ======


=== Formatting in this chapter ===

Since this chapter shifts quickly from one topic to another, it is possible to use a line of space between topic to distinguish between the more easily.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Sermon on the Mount ==

Matthew chapters 5-7 together form one sermon or teaching by Jesus. The chapter divisions cause confusion here.

== Fruit ==

Fruit is a common image in the New Testament. It is used to describe the positive results of something, often good [[:en:obe:kt:works|works]]. In this chapter, good fruit is the result of living as God's desires one to live.
