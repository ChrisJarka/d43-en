====== thresh, threshing floor ======

===== Definition: =====

To thresh is to separate wheat grain from the rest of the wheat plant.

  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Threshing is accomplished by beating the plant against a floor called the threshing floor, an area used solely for the final process of harvesting grain.</font>
