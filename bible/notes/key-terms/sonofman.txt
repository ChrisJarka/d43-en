====== Son of Man ======


===== Definition: =====


The Son of Man is a title used in the Old Testament to refer to the Messiah. In the New Testament, it specifically refers to Jesus Christ.


    * This title emphasizes that Jesus was the fulfillment of the Old Testament prophecies about the "Son of Man".
    * The title "Son of Man" emphasizes Jesus' humanity. While Jesus was fully God, he was also fully human.
    * This is a title Jesus often uses for Himself.
