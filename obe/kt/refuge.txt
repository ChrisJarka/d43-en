====== refuge ======

===== Definition: =====

A refuge is a place of safety, shelter and protection.

  * Old Testament references describe God as the "refuge" of men.
  * A "city of refuge" describes six cities mentoned in Leviticus that could give asylum or safety to those who caused the accidental death of someone.
