===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues to tell Isaiah what he should tell Ahaz. (See: [[en:bible:notes:isa:07:03]])
  * **head of Aram is Damascus** - This means Damascus is the capital and most important city in Aram.
  * **and the head of Damascus is Rezin** - This means Rezin is king over Damascus and all of Aram. God is emphasizing that a weak king like Rezin is no threat to Judah.
  * **Remaliah** - See how you translated many of these in [[en:bible:notes:isa:07:01|7:1]]
  * **Ephraim will be shattered and will no longer be a people** - Here "Ephraim" refers to all of the northern kingdom of Israell. This can be translated with an active verb: "an army will destroy Ephraim, and there will no longer be a people of Israel"
  * **head of Damascus**  - This refers to the king of Syria, Rezin.
  * **sixty-five years**  - This is an amount of time – 65 years. (See: [[en:ta:translate:translateunknowns-numbers]])
  * **head of Ephraim**  - This refers to Samaria, the capital city of the northern kingdom of Israel.
  * **head of Samaria**  - This refers to the king of Samaria, Pekah.
  * **If you do not remain firm in faith, surely you will not remain secure**  - "Ahaz, if you do not remain firm in faith, surely you will not remain secure"
