====== Titus ======

===== Facts: =====

Titus was an early church leader who was trained by the apostle Paul and traveled with him on missionary journeys.

  * Titus was given the job of appointing church leaders on the island of Crete.
  * The New Testament book entitled "Titus" is a letter Paul wrote to Titus to encourage him and teach him.
