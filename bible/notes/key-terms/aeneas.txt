====== Aeneas ======

===== Facts: =====

Aeneas was a man in the book of Acts that was paralyzed and bedridden for eight years and lived in Lydda.

  * Peter called upon Jesus to heal Aeneas, and he was healed.
  * Aeneas was called "a certain man," indicating that he was probably a unbeliever.
