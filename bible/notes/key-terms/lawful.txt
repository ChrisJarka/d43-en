====== lawful ======


===== Definition: =====


Something is lawful if it is in accordance with a binding rule.


    * In the Bible, "Lawful" indicates legal conformity to either the civil law or the Law of Moses.
    * "Lawful" can be translated as "permitted" or "allowed".
