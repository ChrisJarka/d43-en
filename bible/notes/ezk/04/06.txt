===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues speaking to Ezekiel.
  * **When you have completed these days**  - "These days" refers to the days Ezekiel lies down on his side to illustrate the siege of the kingdom of Israel.
  * **carry**  - Translate this as you did in 4:4.
  * **the house of Judah **- "the southern kingdom." At this time in history, the kingdom of Israel was in two kingdoms.
  * **set your face** - Translate this as you did in 4:3) 
  * ** siege of Jerusalem **- "attack on Jerusalem"
  * ** behold** - pay attention to this 
  * ** placing bonds on you** - "handcuffing you"
