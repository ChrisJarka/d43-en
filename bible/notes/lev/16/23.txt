===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **and take off the linen garments **- these were special garments just worn for part of the Day of Atonement.day while he made atonement for himself, his family, the most holy place, the tent of meeting and the altar.
  * **he must leave those garments there  **- there was an area in the holy place for garments.
  * **He must bathe his body in water in a holy place**  - there was a place set aside for ceremonial washing, water came from the laver.
  * **put on his normal garments**  - his priestly robes to allow him to perform his role as high priest, instead of one needing atonement
  * **"then he must go out and offer his burnt offering and the burnt offering for the people, and in this way make atonement for himself and for the people."**Aaron, the high priest must offer burnt offerings twice a day, everyday.
