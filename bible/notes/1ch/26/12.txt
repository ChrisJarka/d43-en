===== translationNotes: =====

  * **divisions** - "groups"
  * **threw lots** - "cast lots"   
  * **both young and old** - This refers to all of the men by describing the extremes. See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:25:06|25:8]]. AT: "all of them, including the young men and the old men" or "men of all ages" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **When the lot was thrown** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "When they threw the lot" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Zechariah** - Translate this man's name as in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:26:01|26:2]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **a prudent advisor** - This is a person who shows good judgment in making decisions.
