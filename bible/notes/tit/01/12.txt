===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **one of them **- That is, "one of the Cretans," or "someone from the people of Crete."
  * **a prophet of theirs **- That is, "one who was considered as their prophet," or "one they thought as their prophet."
  * **Cretans are unceasing liars -**  That is, "Cretans are consistently lying," or "Cretans are lying all the time."
  * **bad and dangerous animals **- That is, "like harmful and dangerous animals," or "like wild beasts."
  * **They are lazy and always eat too much food **- That is, if taken literally, "They are lazy gluttons," or "They are people who want to do nothing but eat too much food." If taken figuratively, "They only act to satisfy their greediness," or "They are out to satisfy their greed for money."
  * **This account is true**  - Or, "What he said is true." That is, "These words spoken by that man (the prophet) are true"
  * **So sharply correct them**  - That is, "Therefore tell them strongly that they are wrong." That is, "With strong words tell the Cretans they are wrong."
  * **that they may be sound in the faith**  - That is, "so the Cretans' faith will be solid." This can also be translated as "so the Cretans' faith will be well-grounded" or "so the Cretans will believe the truth."
