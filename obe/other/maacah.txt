====== Maacah ======

===== Facts: =====

Maacah was one of the sons of Nahor and his concubine Reumah. Several other people in the Old Testament also had this name.

  * The name is sometimes spelled Maakah.
  * Maacah is also the name of a region.
  * The mother of Absalom was also named Maacah.
