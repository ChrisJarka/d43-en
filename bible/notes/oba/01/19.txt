===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - In verses 16-21, Yahweh speaks through Obadiah to tell the people of Judah that they will possess the land of Edom.

  * **Those from the Negev will possess the mount of Esau** - "The Israelites in the Negev will take the land of the Edomites" 
  * **those of the Shepelah** - This refers to Israelites in the western foothills of Israel who will capture the land of the Philistines in battle.
  * **They will possess** - Here "They" appears to refer to the people of Israel in general.
  * **Benjamin** - "the tribe of Benjamin" or "the descendants of Benjamin"
