===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he drove out the spirits with a word**  - Jesus spoke one word and the demons (evil spirits) had to leave the man because Jesus has authority over the demons.
  * **In this way was fulfilled - ** <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>when he healed people and cast out demons,</font> Jesus fulfilled the prophecy that God told the prophet Isaiah to speak to Israel . The prophecy said  "He himself took our sickness and bore our diseases."​
  * **He himself**  - Jesus
  * **took our sickness and bore our diseases - **  "He freed people from being sick and he made them well."
