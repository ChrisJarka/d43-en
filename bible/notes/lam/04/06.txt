===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The punishment of the daughter of my people is greater than that of Sodom** - The abstract noun "punishment" can be expressed with the verb "punish." AT: "The daughter of my people has been punished more severely that Sodom was punished" 
  * **the daughter of my people** - This is a poetic name for Jerusalem, which is spoken of here as if it were a woman. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:lam:02:11|2:11]]. AT: "my people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **it was overthrown in a moment** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God destroyed Sodom in a moment" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **no one lifted a hand to help her** - Here "lifted a hand" represents making an effort. AT: "no one made an effort to help her" or "no one helped her" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
