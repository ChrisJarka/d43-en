===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **He has given help**  - That is, "The Lord has helped." Translators may add a footnote saying "The angel had told Mary that she would bear a son who would reign over Israel forever.This was how God helped Israel."
    * **Israel, his servant**  - "Israel" here refers to the nation, or people, of Israel. In order for readers not to confuse this with the the man named Israel, it may help to translate it as "Israel, his servants".
    * **as he spoke to our fathers**  - That is, "as he promised our ancestors that he would be merciful" or "because he promised our ancestors he would be merciful".
    * **to Abraham and his offspring**  - That is, "to Abraham and Abraham's descendants."
