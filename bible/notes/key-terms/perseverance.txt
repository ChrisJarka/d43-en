====== persevere, perseverance ======

===== Definition: =====

To persevere means to continue doing a task even though it may take a long time or be very difficult.

  * To persevere can also mean to maintain Christian character under difficult trials or circumstances.
  * Perseverance is the character quality that enables a person to keep doing what they should do even when it is very difficult.
