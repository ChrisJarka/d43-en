===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **May the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ be praised, he who has blessed us **– This could be more simply worded as “Praise the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ! He has blessed us”. (See UDB)
  * **he **– This pronoun is referring to the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ.
  * **us **– This is an inclusive pronoun referring to Paul and all the believers at Ephesus.
  * **every spiritual blessing **– The are blessings proceeding from the Spirit of God.
  * **For God chose us who believe in Christ before the creation of the world. He chose us so we could be holy and blameless in his sight, in the beloved one. **- This verse can be more simply translated as “For God chose us in Christ before the creation of the world, that we should be holy and without blame before him in love.
