===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **joyful and gladness of heart **- good temper of a servant (See: Doublets)
  * **you will serve them in hunger, in thirst, in nakedness**  - it means a living condition without common necessities (See: Parallelism)
  * **​A yoke of iron **  ​- one can neither bear it well nor break it. See how you translated the term in Jeremiah 28:13 (See: Metaphor)
