===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They**  - "They" refers to God's terrifying acts of anger, as seen in verses 15-16.
  * **They surround me like water all the day long; they have all encircled me.**  - AT: "They surround me all day long." (See: Doublet)
  * **like water**  - (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **"every friend and acquaintance"**  - AT: "every person I love, or even know slightly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **"My only acquaintance is the darkness."**  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]]) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
