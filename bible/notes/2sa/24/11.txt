===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the word of Yahweh came to ... saying** - This idiom is used to introduce a special message from God. See how you translated this in . AT: "Yahweh gave a message to ... He said" or "Yahweh spoke this message to ..." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the word of Yahweh came to the prophet Gad, David's seer, saying,** - The idiom "the word of Yahweh came to" is used to introduce a special message from God. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:2sa:07:03|7:4]]. AT: "Yahweh gave a message to the prophet Gad, David's seer. He said," or "Yahweh spoke this message to the prophet Gad, David's seer:" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **David's seer** - This means Gad was the official prophet in the royal palace.
