====== Psalms 105 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===


Psalm 105 is the third of five psalms of praise to  God. (Psalm 103-107)

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

God protected Abraham and Jacob. He has worked all through Israel's history to bring Israel into the Promised Land.
