====== unlawful ======

===== Definition: =====

When used in relation to the Mosiac law, unlawful meant any action that was against the Jewish Mosaic law.

  * Unlawful actions could be related to the moral, civil or ceremonial aspects of the Jewish law.
  * In the book of Acts, Peter stated that eating "unclean" foods or associating with gentiles was unlawful for him .
