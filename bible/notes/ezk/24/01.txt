===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh speaks to Ezekiel.
  * **the word of Yahweh came to me** - AT: "Yahweh spoke to me"
  * **in the ninth year** - This is the ninth year since the Babylonians took the Israelites as captives. (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaltimeyear]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **the tenth month, on the tenth day of the month** - "the tenth day of the tenth month." This is the tenth month of the Hebrew calendar. The tenth day is near the beginning of January on western calendars. (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaltime]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **the king of Babylon has besieged Jerusalem** - The army of Babylon is referred to by its leader. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
