====== Jeremiah Chapter 09 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What does the prophet wish?

A. He wishes to weep day and night for those among the daughter of my people who have been killed. [9:1]

Q? Why does he want someone to give him a place for travelers in the wilderness to stay, where he could abandon his people?

A. He wants to leave them since all of them are adulterers, a band of traitors. [9:2]

Q? What do the sinful people say?

A. They say false things with their tongues, which are their deceitful bows, but they are not great in faithfulness on the earth. [9:3]

Q? Why should each of them guard against their neighbors and brothers?

A. For every brother is a deceiver and every neighbor walks in slander. [9:4]

Q? Why are they exhausted?

A. They are exhausted from committing iniquity. [9:5]

Q? What is Yahweh's declaration?

A. It is Yahweh's declaration that they live in the midst of deception; in their deceit they refuse to acknowledge him. [9:6]

Q? What does Yahweh of hosts say?

A. He says that he is about to test them and he will examine them. [9:7]
