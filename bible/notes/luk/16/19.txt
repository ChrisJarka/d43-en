===== Translation Notes: =====


    * (Jesus continues to speak.)
    * **rich man**  - That is, "wealthy man," or "well to do man."
    * **was clothed in purple and fine linen**  - purple dye was very expensive, as was finely spun and woven linen. "Was clothed" indicates that he wears them regularly or that he owned many of them not just a few. This can be translated as, "regularly wore purple cloth and fine linen, which were expensive clothes."
    * **was living in splendor everyday**  - That is, "was celebrating in an expensive way everyday." This may refer to "was celebrating with good and expensive food everyday" or "was enjoying good and expensive food everyday." This man was very rich.
    * **a certain beggar **- That is, "there was a certain poor man," or "there was a very poor man." Poor or beggar indicates someone who was dependent on others for his needs.
    * **was laid**-Or' "had been laid"
    * **at his gate**  - That is the gate of the rich man. Gate is the entrance to the house or the property. This can be translated as "at the gate of entrance to the house of the rich man."
    * **covered with sores**  - That is, "with sores all over his body." A sore is wound or lesion on the skin.
    * **yearned**  - strongly desired
    * **yearned to be fed **- That is "strongly desiring to be satisfied," or "strongly desiring to eat."
    * **what fell from the rich man's table**- This could refer to the leftovers that were thrown away or this could refer to food that fell down during the meal. At such,this can be translated as "by the leftovers that were thrown from the rich man's table," or " food that fell from the rich man's table as he was eating."
    * **dogs**  - dogs were an unclean animal, and for one to lick at a persons wounds indicated how sick and weak this man was, to not be able to fend off such an offensive thing.
