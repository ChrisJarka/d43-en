===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **daughter of Tarshish -**  Tyre
  * **Plow your land, as one plows the Nile, O daughter of Tarshish. There is no longer a marketplace in Tyre **–plowing land is likened to producing crops - "You Tyre are no longer a center of commerce, you will have to raise your own produce instead of depending on Egypt" - See: Metaphor
  * **has stretched out his hand ****over the sea **  – "has shown his power over the people of the earth"
  * **concerning Phoenicia**  – "concerning Canaan"
  * **oppressed virgin -**" violated virgin"
