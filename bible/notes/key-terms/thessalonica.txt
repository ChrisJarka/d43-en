====== Thessalonica, Thessalonians ======

===== Facts: =====

Thessalonica was the capital city of Macedonia, named for Alexander's half-sister.
    * Paul, along with Silas and Timothy, visited Thessalonica, on his second missionary journey.
    * A thriving church was established in Thessalonica.
    * Paul wrote two letters to the Christians in Thessalonica.
    * Thessalonica was an important city in the Roman empire, situated on a major Roman highway to the East.
