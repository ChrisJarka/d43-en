===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **place a guard (noun)...guard (verb) the door of my lips**  - earnest plea to be held back from speaking wickedly
  * **Do not let my heart desire...or participate**  - appeal to be kept from sinning
  * **May I not eat any of their delicacies**  - Breaking bread with anyone connotes fellowship; David chooses not to even contemplate sharing a meal with any wicked person.
