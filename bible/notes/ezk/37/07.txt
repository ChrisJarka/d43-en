===== Important Terms: =====

  * commanded

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I ** refers to Ezekiel.
  * **The hand of Yahweh came upon me and sent me out … - **  An alternate translation is "I prophesied the bones as Yahweh instructed me to do" or "I spoke to the bones what Yahweh commanded me to speak." (UDB)
  * **Behold! **  (See Standard Notes) - "The person was surprised by what he saw.
  * **earthquake: **  the earth vibrates and cracks and makes a noise like thunder.
  * **sinews**  or **tendons**  - a nonelastic cord inside the body that connects muscle to bone.
