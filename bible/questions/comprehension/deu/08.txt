====== Deuteronomy Chapter 08 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What should the people of Israel do and call to mind?

A. They should keep all the commandments, go in and the possess the land that Yahweh swore to their fathers, and call to mind how God humbled and tested them for forty years in the wilderness. [8:1-2]

Q? Why did Yahweh humble, make hungry, and feed them with manna, that was not known to their ancestors?

A.He did these things to make the people know that they could not live by bread alone but rather by everything that proceeds out of the mouth of Yahweh. [8:3]

Q? How did they survive the forty years?

A. Their clothing did not grow old on them and their feet did not swell. {8:4]

Q? How will the people walk in God's ways and honor him?

A. They will keep his commandments and think about how God corrects them as they correct their children. [8:5-6]

Q? What is in the good land into which God is bringing them?

A. The land will be filled with brooks of water, fountains and springs, wheat and barley, vines, fig trees, pomegrantes, olive trees, and honey. [8:7-8]

Q? Why will the people bless Yahweh for the good land that he has given them?

A. They will bless him because they will not lack bread or anything; the stones are made of iron and the hills will have copper for them to dig. [8:9-10]

Q? What were the people reminded not to do?

A. They were reminded not to forget Yahweh and neglect his commandments. [8:11]

Q? Why might the people forget Yahweh who brought them out of Egypt?

A. They might forget because they will eat and be full, they will build houses, their herds and flocks will multiply,  and their silver and gold will increase. [8:12-14]
