===== translationNotes: =====


  * **General Information:** - This psalm is a prayer for help. See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]].

  * **seek refuge in you** - Asking Yahweh for protection is spoken of as seeking refuge in him. AT: "ask you to protect me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **make me safe in your righteousness** - Possible meanings are 1) "make me safe because you always do what is right" or 2) "keep me safe as I do what you want me to do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **turn your ear to me** - "listen to me" or "do what I request you to do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Be to me a rock for refuge where I may always go** - The psalmist requests that Yahweh make him as safe as he would be if he were hiding in a large rock or cliff where his enemies could not find him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **rock ... rock** - These are hills or mountains, not rocks one can hold in one's hand.
  * **you are my rock and my fortress** - The psalmist believes that Yahweh will protect him and make him as safe as if he were hiding in a large mountain or a man-made fortress. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
