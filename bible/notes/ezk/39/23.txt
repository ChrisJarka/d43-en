===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **they ,their, and them - ** refer to the Israelites (people of Israel).
  * **I,me, my - ** refer to Yahweh.
  * **I hid my face from them. Alternate translation:**  "Yehweh ignored the people of Israel" or "I turned away from them." (UDB)
