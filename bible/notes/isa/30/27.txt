===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Look, the name of Yahweh comes from a distant place, burning with his anger and in a heavy burden. His lips are full of fury, and his tongue is like a devouring fire.** - Yahweh is depicted as a raging fire coming from the distance (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **His breath is like an overflowing torrent that reaches up to the middle of the neck** - Yahweh’s breathe is depicted as a raging flood that is about to drown men. (See Is 8:8), (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **to sift the nations with the sieve of destruction** – AT: “Yahweh will sift the nations so that nothing is left” (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **and a bridle in the jaws the peoples to send them astray** – “Yahweh will be like a bridle on an animal to lead them in a path which will cause them to fail” (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
