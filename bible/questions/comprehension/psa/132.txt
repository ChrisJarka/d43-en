====== Psalms Chapter 132 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did the writer ask Yahweh to remember?

A. He asked Yahweh to call to mind David's afflictions, how he swore to Yahweh, and how he vowed to the mighty one of Jacob. [132:1-2]

Q? What did David swear and vow?

A. He said, "I will not enter my house or get into my bed, I will not give sleep to my eyes or rest to my eyelids until I find a place for Yahweh, a tabernacle for the Mighty One of Jacob." [132:3-5]
