===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the children of your people**  - "your people" or "your nation's people"
  * **a sword upon the land**  - "destruction upon the land" (See: Synecdoche)
  * **watchman**  - someone who watches out for the safety of others
  * **establish him as a watchman**  - "appoint him their watchman"
  * **the sword as it comes upon the land **- See how you translated this in 33:1. (See: Synecdoche)
  * **horn **- a pointed musical instrument also used to signal danger or warning
  * **do not take warning **- "ignore the warning"
  * **the sword comes and takes them **- "the sword takes their life" (See: Metonomy and Personification)
  * **each one's blood will be on his own head **- Alternate translation: "it is their own fault if they die" (See: Metaphor and Synecdoche)
