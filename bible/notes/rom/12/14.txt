===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Rejoice with those who rejoice **- "Spend time with happy people and be happy along with them"
  * **weep with those who weep **- "spend time with sad people and be sad along with them"
  * ** Be of the same mind toward one another ** - "Live in unity with all other Christians."
