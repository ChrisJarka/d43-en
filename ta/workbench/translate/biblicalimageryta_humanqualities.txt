== THE NOSE (OR NOSTRILS) REPRESENTS ANGER ==

  * //Then…the foundations of the world were exposed at your battle cry, Yahweh—////at the blast of the breath of your nostrils//  . Ps. 18:15 (ULB)
  * //By the blast of your nostrils the waters were piled up….//  Exod. 15:8 (ULB)
  * //Smoke went up from out of his nostrils, and blazing fire came out of his mouth…. //  2 Sam. 22:9 (ULB)
  * //…This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration: 'My fury will arise in my nostrils!'//  Ezek. 38:18 (ULB)
== RAISED EYES REPRESENT ARROGANCE ==

  * //You bring down those with proud, uplifted eyes.//  Ps. 18:27 (ULB)
== HAND REPRESENTS AGENCY AND POWER ==

  * //Yahweh has burst through my enemies by my hand like a bursting flood of water.//  1 Chron. 14:11 (ULB)
  * //Your hand will seize all your enemies; your right hand will seize those who hate you.//  Ps. 21:8 (ULB)
  * //Look, Yahweh’s hand is not so short that it cannot save//. Isa. 59:1 (ULB)
== FACE REPRESENTS PRESENCE ==

  * //Through all their suffering, he suffered too, and the angel from his presence //  (Hebrew: //face//) //saved them.//  Isa. 63:9 (ULB)
