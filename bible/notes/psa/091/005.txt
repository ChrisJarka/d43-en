===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **afraid of terror in the night**  - AT:"afraid of things that bring great fear in the night," (See: Personification)
  * **flies**  - moving through the air
  * **day, or of the plague that roam around in the darkest,**  - AT: day. "You will not be afraid of the events caused by demons that roam around at night," (See: Personification)
  * **roam **- moving around without a plan for your movements
  * **or of the diseases that come at noontime**  - AT:"or of the evil forces that attack in the middle of the day." (See: Personification)
  * **A thousand may fall at your side and ten thousand at your right hand **-AT: "A thousand people may fall at your side or ten thousand people may fall at your right hand," (See: Implicit/Explicit)
  * **thousand**  - 1000 (See: number translation)
  * **ten thousand **- 10,000 (See: number translation)
