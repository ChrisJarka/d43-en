===== Bible Examples =====

=== Act:15:19 ===

"Therefore, my opinion is, that we should not trouble those of the Gentiles who turn to God; "

  * **we should not trouble those of the Gentiles**  - "we should not require the gentiles to become circumcised and obey the laws of Moses" (See: [[:en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit|Explicit and Implicit Information]])

=== Gen:16:15 ===

"Hagar gave birth to Abram's son, and Abram named his son, whom Hagar bore, Ishmael."

  * **Hagar gave birth**  - Hagar's return to Sarai and Abram is implict. You can make this more explicit: "So Hagar went back and gave birth." (See: [[:en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit|Explicit and Implicit Information]]).

=== Luk:23:17 ===

"Now Pilate was obligated to release to the Jews one prisoner at the feast. "

  * **he was obligated to release to them one prisoner**  - Pilate was required to do this by political custom. This implied information can be stated explicitly: "he was obligated by custom to release to them one prisoner." (See: [[:en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit|Explicit and Implicit Information]])
