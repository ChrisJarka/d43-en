====== Kadesh ======

===== Facts: =====

Kadesh or Kadesh-Barnea was a significant region in Israel's history located at the southern end of Israel's territories.

  * Kadesh was an oasis in the midst of the wilderness of Zin.
  * Abraham traveled through Kadesh on his journeys.
  * Israel encamped at Kadesh during the wandering in the wilderness.
  * It was at Kadesh that Moses was denied entrance to the promised land because of disobedience to God's instruction regarding bringing water from a rock.  (Numbers 20)
