===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **came**  - Some languages may prefer "went."
    * **led by the Holy Spirit**  - that is, directed by God
    * **the parents**  - That is, "Jesus' parents."
    * **received him**  - That is "took him."
    * **Now let your servant depart in peace**  - Simeon was referring to himself. This could also be translated as "I am your servant. Let me depart in peace."
    * **depart**  - This is a euphemism meaning "die." (See [[:en:ta:figuresofspeech:euphemism|Euphemism]].)
    * **according to your word**  - That is, "as you have said" or, "because you said you would."
