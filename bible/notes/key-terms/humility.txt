====== humility ======

===== Definition: =====

\\
Humility is the quality of being modest, humble and unpretentious.

  * Humility is serving with a modest attitude and without pretentious behavior when showing one's gifts and abilities.
  * Humility is thinking of the needs of others before our own needs.

(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:humble|humble]], other)
