====== Jeremiah 29 General Notes ======



=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Two letters ==

Jeremiah sent a letter to Babylon telling the exiles to prepare for a long stay. A false prophet in Babylon wrote back to the chief priest telling him he should punish Jeremiah for sending his letter.
