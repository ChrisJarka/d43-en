====== 1 Samuel 01 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===


This Chapter introduces Samuel. It is the beginning of the section 1 Samuel 1-7 telling about Samuel as religious leader of Israel.


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Samuel's  Birth and Dedication ==


A man named Elkanah had two wives. He loved Hannah best and favored her but she had no children. The other wife, Peninnnah had lots of children. They were jealous of each other. Every year the whole family went to the tent church to sacrifice to God. Hannah was upset because Peninnah teased her for not having children. So after dinner she went to the tent church to beg God for a son. She promised to dedicate the son to God for his whole life. God gave her a son she named Samuel and when he was weaned she dedicated him to God to live in the tent church. 

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Idiom ==

When Hannah wanted to assure Eli she was telling the truth she use an  [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] "as you live" which is type of oath formula meaning I promise you I am telling the truth.
