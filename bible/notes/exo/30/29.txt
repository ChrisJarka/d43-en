===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues speaking to Moses.
  * **consecrate them** - This refers to the items listed in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:30:26|30:26-28]].
  * **will also be reserved for me** - "will also be holy" or "will also be set aside for me"
  * **throughout your people's generations** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:12:12|12:14]].
