====== Ararat ======

===== Facts: =====

In the Bible, "Ararat" is the name given to a land, a kingdom and mountains.

  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The "land of</font>    Ararat" <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>was probably located in what is now the northeastern part of the country of Turkey.</font>
  * Ararat is most well-known as the name of the mountains that Noah's ark <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>came to rest on</font>    <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>after the waters of the great flood began to recede.</font>
  * <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>In modern times, a mountain called "Mount Ararat" is often thought to be the location of the "mountains of Ararat" in the Bible.</font>
