====== unfaithful ======


===== Definition: =====


An unfaithful person is one who is __negligent__ in their responsibilities and duties.


    * In marriage, someone who commits adultery is "unfaithful" to their spouse.
    * Jesus called his followers to be faithful in every area of their lives, from the __insignificant__  to the __substantial__.
    * Israel was called "unfaithful" when __they__  began to worship idols.
    * Being the bride of Christ, believers are considered "unfaithful" when they __become disbelieving or treasonous__  to Christ, their bridegroom.
