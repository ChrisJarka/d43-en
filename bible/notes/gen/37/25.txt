===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They lifted up their eyes and looked, and behold, a caravan** - "They looked up and they saw that a caravan"
  * **Gilead**  - This is a land to the northeast, east of the Jordan river, where Abrahams' son by Hagar, Ishmael, settled.
  * **camels bearing spices** - "camels loaded with spices" or "camels carrying spices"
  * **spices and balm and myrrh**  - "seasonings, medicines, and resins"
  * **traveling to carry them down to Egypt.**  - "bringing them down to Egypt." This can be made more explicit: "bringing them down to Egypt to sell them" ([[en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]]).
  * **What profit is it if we kill our brother and cover up his blood?**  - Judah uses this question to say that killing Joseph was not a good thing to do. Alternate translation: "We should not kill our brother and cover up his murder" or "Instead of killing our brother and covering up his murder, I have a better idea" (see [[en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]]).
