===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Therefore - ** So that your heart is not deceived as mentioned in verse 16, Yahweh is going to give them following instructions on how to keep their hearts focused on the real God.
  * **lay up these words**  - "impress these words" or "imprint these words." A objective visual action is used to explain the subjective spiritual action happening inside the heart and soul. (See: Metaphor)
  * **when you sit in your house, when you walk on the road, when you lie down, and when you get up **  - God's commandments were to be discussed and taught to the children anytime, anywhere.
