====== Psalms 005 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

 Psalm 5 is a psalm of deliverance. It was intended to be sung accompanied by flutes. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

God does not help people who do evil but he blesses those who are good and trust in him.
