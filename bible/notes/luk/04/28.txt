===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **filled with rage**  - That is, they were very angry.
    * **these things**  - That is, the things which Jesus said and the things they understood him to be implying.
    * **forced him out of the city**  - This could also be translated as "forced him to leave the city."
    * **edge of the hill**  - You could also say, "brow of the cliff."
    * **through the midst**  - This could be translated as "through the middle of" or "between" the people trying to kill him.
    * **went on his way**  - Jesus went where he had planned to go instead of where the people were trying to force him to go.
