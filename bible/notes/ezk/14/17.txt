===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **bring a sword against that land - **  Alternate translations** "**bring another nation to attack" or "bring enemy soldiers to kill the people and animals" (UDB).
  * **cut off**  - Alternate translation: "separate from" or "to kill" (UDB).
  * **and beast**  - Alternate translation: "farm animal" or "animal" (UDB).
  * **in the midst of the land**  - "in the middle of that country" or "in that country" (UDB)
  * **as I live - ** The word "I" refers to Yahweh.
  * **these three**  - This phrase refers to the men mentioned in Ezekiel 14:14 (i.e., Noah, Daniel, and Job).
  * **as I live - **  This phrase may mean "I promise".
  * **they/their own**  - The words "they" and "their" refer to the people of Israel.
  * **they would not be able to deliver even their own sons or daughters - **  Alternate translation**: **"they could not save their own children" or "they would not be able to save eve their own sons or daughters from being killed" (UDB).
  * **only their own lives would be delivered - **  Alternate translation: "they would be the only ones who lived" or "They would save only themselves" (UDB).
