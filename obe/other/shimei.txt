====== Shimei ======

===== Definition: =====

Several men in the Old Testament are named Shimei.

  * Shimei son of Gera, a Benjamite persecuted King David as he fled from Saul.
  * Several of the men named Shimei in the Old Testament served as Levites.
  * Each of the men sharing this name must be placed within their own appropriate context since they lived at different time periods in Israel's history.
