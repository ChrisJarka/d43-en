====== 1 Kings 07 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

The completion of the description of the building of the temple

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

This chapter has a description of the palace Solomon built and the things used in the temple courtyard cast out of bronze and the furnishing inside the temple that were made of gold. The bronze objects were made by hollowing out the shape of the object in a clay bank of the river and then pouring in the hot melted liquid bronze and letting it cool.
