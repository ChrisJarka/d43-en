====== Damascus ======

===== Facts: =====

The capital of the country Syria.

  * Damascus was the destination of Paul when he was converted.
  * It is this important commercial city and often plays a significant role in biblical history.
  * Damascus is the oldest, continuously inhabited city in the world.
  * Another important scripture reference regarding Damascus is Isaiah 7.
