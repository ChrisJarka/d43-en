====== Bethel ======

===== Facts: =====

Bethel was a city in Abram's time located north of Jerusalem in the land of Canaan.

  * Abram camped east of Bethel and built an altar to God after receiving God's promises.
  * When fleeing from his brother Esau, Bethel was the location where Jacob rested and dreamt of a ladder with angels going up to and down from heaven.
  * Many significant biblical events occur in Bethel.
  * An earlier name for Bethel is Luz. Bethel is mentioned more in the Old Testament than any other city except Jerusalem.
