===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Then Jesus **- That is, after his baptism in Luke 3:21-22. This can be translated as "After Jesus was baptized."
    * **being full of the Holy Spirit **- That is, "he was full of the Holy Spirit" or "he was being fully guided by the Holy Spirit."
    * **returned from the Jordan River**  - That is, "he departed from the Jordan River" or "he left the Jordan River."
    * **was led by the Spirit**- That is, "was continuously guided by the Spirit." This guidance was for the entire duration of his stay in the wilderness.
    * **in the wilderness**  - That is, "into the desert or wasteland."
    * **for forty days**- That is, "for a period of forty days" or "for a length of forty days."
    * **and was tempted there by the devil **- Jesus endured temptation for 40 days. This can be translated as "was continuously tempted by the devil to disobey God" or "was continuously being directed by the devil to disobey God."
    * **he did not eat**- "He" refers to Jesus.
    * **at the end of that time**- That is, "at the end of those forty days" or "after those forty days."
    * **he was hungry**- That is, "he became hungry" or "he felt the hunger and wanted to eat."
