====== Joshua Chapter 01 ======

What three things did Yahweh ask Joshua to be?

Yahweh ask Joshua to be strong, corageous and to obey the law.  [1:7]

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Yahweh tell Joshua to do after the death of Moses?

A. Yahweh instructed Joshua to cross the Jordon and lead the people of Israel into the land that Yahweh would give them. [1:1-3]

Q? Why did Yahweh tell Joshua that no one would be able to stand against him?

A. Yahweh told Joshua that no one would be able to stand against him because he would be with Joshua just as he had been with Moses. [1:5]
