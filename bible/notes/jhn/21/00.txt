====== John 21 General Notes ======

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphors ==
Jesus uses many shepherding [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|metaphors]]. For example, "feed my lambs," "tend my sheep" and "feed my sheep." Peter would now be a shepherd of the people of God.
