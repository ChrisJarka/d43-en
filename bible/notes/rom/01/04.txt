===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He was declared**  - The word "he" refers to Jesus Christ. The phrase "was declared" could be restated with an active verb: "God declared him." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **spirit of holiness**  - This refers to the Holy Spirit.
  * **by the resurrection from the dead**  - "by bringing him back to life after he died"
  * **we have received grace and apostleship**  - "God has graciously given me a gift. He has appointed me to be an apostle." 
  *  **we** - This refers to Paul and to the 12 apostles that followed Jesus but excludes the believers in the church in Rome. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive|Exclusive]],
  * **grace and apostleship**- AT: "gracious gift of apostleship." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hendiadys|Hendiadys]])
  * **for obedience of faith among all the nations, for the sake of his name**  - "Name" refers to Jesus. AT: "in order to teach all nations to obey God because of their faith in God." 
  * **name** - "Name" refers to the person of God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
