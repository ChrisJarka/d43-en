====== head ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Bible, the word "head" is used with several figurative meanings.

  * Often this term is used to refer to being in authority over a person or group of people, as in, "you have made me the head over nations." this could be translated as, "You have made me the ruler" or "You have given me authority over" many nations.
  * Sometimes Jesus is called the head of the church; Just as a person's head guides and directs the members of its body what to do, so Jesus guides and directs the members of his body, the Church.
  * A husband is called the head of his wife. He is given the responsibility of providing direction to his wife and family.
  * The expression, "no razor will ever touch his head" means " he will never cut or shave his hair."
