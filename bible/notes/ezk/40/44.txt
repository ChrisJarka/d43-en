===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **chambers** - A chamber is a room.
  * **singers'**  - The priests (Levites) were in charge of worship in the temple.
  * **he** - This pronoun refers to the man with the measuring stick who was doing the measuring.
  * **keeping guard** - (See: [[:en:obe:other:watch|watch]])
