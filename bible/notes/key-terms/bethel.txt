====== Bethel ======

===== Facts: =====

Bethel was a city in Abram's time located north of Jerusalem in the land of Canaan.

  * Abram camped east of Bethel and built an altar to God after receiving God's promises of land, a nation, a great name, and blessings upon his family of followers.
  * When fleeing from his brother Esau, Bethel is the location where Jacob rested and dreamt of a ladder with angels going up to and down from heaven.
  * Jacob returned to Bethel after fleeing from his father-in-law Laban and before his meeting with his brother Esau.
  * An earlier name for Bethel is Luz.  Bethel is mentioned more in the Old Testament than any other city except Jerusalem.
