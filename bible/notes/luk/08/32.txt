    * **the demons implored him **- That is, "the demons passionately asked him" or "the demons begged him."
    * **to permit them to enter into the pigs **- That is, "to allow them to enter into the pigs" or "to allow them to go inside the pigs."
    * **he permitted them **- That is, "he allowed them."
