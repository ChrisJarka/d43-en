===== translationNotes: =====

  * **So the peaceful pastures will be devastated** - Here "pastures" stands for the entire nation. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **will be devastated** - This can be put into active form. AT: "Because God is angry, he will devastate the entire nation." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Like a young lion, he has left his den, for** - Unlike other references where God is viewed as a protective lion toward Israel, here, God will punish Israel. AT: "Yahweh is coming like a young lion to punish the people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
