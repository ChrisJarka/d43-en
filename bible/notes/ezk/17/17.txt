===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **war assembly**  - "gathering of soliders and weapons in preparation for battle"
  * **heap up seige-mounts and build seige-walls**  - These are defensive preparations for war.
  * **heap**  - To put in a pile.
  * **to cut off many lives**  - "to block the advance of the army"
  * **for he**  - This refers to the King of Jerusalem.
  * **behold**  - The word “behold” here adds emphasis to what follows. It can also be translated as “Indeed.”
  * **"He gave his hand but did all these things."**<font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>- Alternate translation: "The king of Jerusalem agreed to the covenant, but broke it."</font>
