====== Psalms Chapter 132 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did the writer ask Yahweh to remember?

A. He asked Yahweh to call to mind David's afflictions, how he swore to Yahweh, and how he vowed to the mighty one of Jacob. [132:1-2]

Q? What did David swear and vow?

A. He said, "I will not enter my house or get into my bed, I will not give sleep to my eyes or rest to my eyelids until I find a place for Yahweh, a tabernacle for the Mighty One of Jacob." [132:3-5]

Q? Where will they go and what will they do?

A. They will go into God's tabernacle and will worship at his footstool. [132:7]

Q? How may the priests be clothed and who may shout for joy?

A. The priests may be clothed with integrity and God's faithful ones may shout for joy. [132:9]

Q? From whom should they not turn?

A. They should not turn away from their annointed king. [132:10]

Q? What is the oath Yahweh swore to David?

A. Yahweh swore to him to be trustworthy. If David's sons kept Yahweh's covenant and the laws, he would place one of David's descendants on David's throne. [132:11-12]

Q? Where does the writer desire to rest and live forever?

A. He desires to rest and live in Yahwey's chosen Zion. [132:13-14]

Q? What will the writer do for Zion?

A. He will abundantly bless her, satisfy her poor with bread, and clothe her priests with salvation. [132:15-16]

Q? What will the writer make, place, and who will he clothe with shame?

A. He will make the horn of David to grow, place a lamp for his anointed one, and clothe his enemies with shame. [132:17-18]
