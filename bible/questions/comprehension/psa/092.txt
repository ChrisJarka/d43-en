====== Psalms Chapter 092 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What does the writer consider to be a good thing?

A. It is a good thing to give thanks and sing praises to the name of Yahweh. [92:1]

Q? When is a good time to give thanks and sing praises to Yahweh?

A. It is a good thing to proclaim his covenant faithfulness in the morning and his truthfulness every night. [92:2]
