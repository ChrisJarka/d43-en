===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **your navel was not cut - **  Alternate translation: "your birthcord was not cut" or "your umbilical cord was not cut." (UDB)
  * **weren't swaddled with - **  "Weren't" is used for "were not." This phrase is "wrapped in strips of cloth" (UDB).
  * **You were cast out into the open field**  - "cast" means "thrown."
