===== translationNotes: =====


  * **If there is found among you** - This may be stated in active form. AT: "If you find someone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **within any of your city gates** - Here "gates" means cities or towns. AT: "living in one of your cities" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **what is evil in the sight of Yahweh your God** - Here, "in the sight of Yahweh" means what Yahweh considers or thinks about something. AT: "something that Yahweh your God thinks is evil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **transgresses his covenant** - "disobeys his covenant"
  * **anyone who has gone** - "if you find anyone who has gone"
  * **any of the host of heaven** - "any of the stars"
  * **nothing that I have commanded** - "which I have not commanded"
  * **if you are told about this** - "if someone tells you about this disobedient act"
  * **make a careful investigation** - "check carefully to find out if it is true"
  * **such a detestable thing has been done in Israel** - "someone has done such a horrible thing in Israel"
