===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **behold - **  The word “behold” here shows that [the person] was surprised by what he saw. (Standard Notes)
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>** I fell upon my face  ** - "I bowed down"</font>
  * **set your heart - **  Prepare your mind (See Metonymy)
  * **look with your eyes**  - "with your eyes" emphasizes the command to look.
  * **listen with your ears**  -"with your ears" emphasizes the command to hear.
  * **And set your heart to enter the house with all the exits of the sanctuary ****
 - ** <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>In this phrase, Yahweh asks you (Ezekiel) to prepare your mind to enter the house, and to note the exits also.</font>
  * **statutes ****
 - **"regulations"
