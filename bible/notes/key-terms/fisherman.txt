====== fisherman, fishers ======

===== Definition: =====

Fishermen or fishers are those who catch fish from the water as an occupation.

  * Peter and other apostles were fishers before being called by Jesus.
  * Since the territories of Israel were near water, much of the Bible's references are to fish, fishers and fishermen.

(See also: other)
