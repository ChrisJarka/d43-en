===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Let all bitterness and rage and anger, and shouting and insults be taken away from you, along with all evil**  - "Let God take away all feelings of being bitter, furious and angry along with shouting and insults and all acts of evil towards other believers"
  * **Let all bitterness**  - "Let all fellings of hostility" or "Let all fellings of hatred"
  * **and rage**  - "and fury"
  * **and insults**  - "and verbal abuse"
  * **and be kind to each other, tenderhearted**  - "Instead you must be kind to each other, gentle" or "Instead you must be kind to each other, full of compassion"
