====== Zerubbabel ======

===== Facts: =====
The head of the tribe of Judah during one of the returns from Babylonian captivity. 

  * Leading the people of Judah during the same time as Cyrus was the king of Persia. 
  * In the Bible Zerubbabel is usually associated with the high priest Joshua.
