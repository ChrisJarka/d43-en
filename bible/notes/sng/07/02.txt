===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **navel ****is like a round bowl** - The woman's navel is indented and is perfectly shaped. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **may it never lack mixed wine** - Possibly the lover is wishing that the woman will always remain beautiful. 
  * **belly is like a mound of wheat…lilies** -​ The woman's body is compared to grain and flowers, things of beauty in nature.
