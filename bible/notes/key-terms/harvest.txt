====== harvest ======


===== Definition: =====


The word harvest is used to describe the action of picking and processing the fruits or crops which a farmer has grown


    * Harvest normally happens at a prescribed time at the end of a growth season.
    * Harvest can be used metaphorically to describe the byproducts of a person's spiritual life. The bible used the term "harvesting the fruits of righteousness".


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:urlterm|term]])
