===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **in dazzling apparel**  - "wearing bright, shining clothes." (UDB)
  * **Why seek you**  - "Why do you seek". "You" here is plural, referring to the women who came.
  * **filled with fear**  - "became afraid"
  * **Why seek you the living among the dead?**  - This is a rhetorical question. See UDB.
