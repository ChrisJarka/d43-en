====== in his sight/eyes ======

===== Description =====

The phrase "call on the name of Yahweh" is used in the Old Testament. The phrase "call on the name of the Lord" is used in the New Testament.

===== Standard Notes =====


  * **in his sight** - AT: "where he could see" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **in his sight** - AT: "and he saw it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **in his sight**  - AT  "in his judgment" or "which he considers to be ..."  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **in the sight of all nations** - AT: “so that all the surrounding people-groups knew”  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
