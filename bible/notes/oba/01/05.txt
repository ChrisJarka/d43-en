===== Translation Notes: =====

Obadiah's has just announced to Edom that they were proud but Yaweh would bring them down.

  * **robbers** - The word "robbers" refers to those who steal.
  * **how are you cut off!** - This is a statement of shock over how serious the disaster will be that Edom will experience.
  * **would they only steal enough for themselves?...would they not leave the gleanings?**  - A double contrast is used to show the worsening conditions of Edom. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **gleanings**  - Here the word "gleanings" refers to grapes left behind after the grape pickers have finished picking.
  * **How Esau has been ransacked, and his hidden treasures sought out!**  - "See how others have ransacked Esau and sought out his hidden treasures!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]).
  * **Esau** - The people of Edom are descendants of Esau.
  * **ransack**  - The word "ransack" means to search for valuables and cause chaos and damage.
  * **sought out** - "hunted" or "looked for"
