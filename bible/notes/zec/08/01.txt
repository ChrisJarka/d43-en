===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The word of Yahweh of hosts came to me, saying,** - This idiom is used to introduce a special message from God. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:zec:07:04|7:4]]. AT: "Yahweh of hosts gave me a message. He said," or "Yahweh of hosts spoke this message to me:" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **passionate** - "jealous." Here the word "passionate" refers to Yahweh's strong desire to protect his people.
  * **for Zion** - Here "Zion" represents the people of Zion. AT: "for the people of Zion" or "for the people of Jerusalem" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **with great anger** - The implied information is that this anger is against the enemies of Zion. AT: "with great anger against her enemies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the mountain of Yahweh of hosts** - The implied information is that this refers to Mount Zion, the location upon which the city of Jerusalem was built. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
