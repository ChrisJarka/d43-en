====== Isaiah 05 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

Some translations prefer to set apart extended quotations, prayers or songs. The ULB and many other English translations indent the lines this chapter, which is a song.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Woe ==
This chapter presents a series of woes, or judgments against those who are spoken against. Most of these involve the lack of justice in Judah. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:woe]] and [[:en:obe:kt:justice]])

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Allegory ==
This chapter begins as an allegory. This is a story with a symbolic spiritual meaning. It is meant to convict the Jews that they sinned against Yahweh and there was nothing more he could have done for them. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:spirit]] and [[:en:obe:kt:sin]])
