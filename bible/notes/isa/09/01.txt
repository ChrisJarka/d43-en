===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The gloom will be dispelled from her who is in anguish** -  Alternate translation: "The Lord will release the kingdom of Judah from suffering and pain." (See: [[en:ta:translate:active]])
  * **The gloom will be dispelled** - The previous chapter described the pain and suffering which the kingdom of Judah would experience. Because the people of Judah had turned away from God, they are compared to people living in the dark.  The word "gloom" means "partial or total darkness."    See how you translated "gloom" in [[en:bible:notes:isa:08:21|08:22]].  (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
  * **will be dispelled** - "will be removed" or "will be taken away" (See: [[en:ta:translate:active]]
  * **from her who is in anguish** - The word "her" refers to the kingdom of Judah.  
  * **anguish** - "intense pain"
  * **the way to the sea, beyond the Jordan, Galilee of the nations**  - This is the area between the Jordan and the Mediterranean Sea.
  * **The people who walked in darkness have seen a great light**  - This can be translated as "Some day in the future, it will be as though the people who walked in darkness have seen a bright light." (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:translate:metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **the land of the shadow of death**  - "the land of great troubles"
