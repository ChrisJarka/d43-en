===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Mountain of Seir - **  The area occupied by the Edomites.
  * **son of man - **  Refers to Ezekiel.
  * **strike you with my hand **- Yahweh will throughly punish Gog and his followers and destroy their land.
  * **horror -  ** Filled with dread and terror.
