===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **an official of Pharaoh who was captain of the guard and an Egyptian **- "an official of Pharaoh, the captain of the guard, an Egyptian"
  * **from of the ****Ishmaelites**** who owned him **- "from the hand of the Ishmaelites" or "from the Ishmaelites."
  * **who owned him and brought him down **- "who brought him down"
  * **who owned him**  - "who possessed", "who controlled him"
  * **He was a prosperous man**  - " He became a prosperous man"
  * **He was in the house of his Egyptian master**  - "He lived in his Egyptian master's house"
