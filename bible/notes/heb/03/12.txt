===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **evil heart of unbelief**  - An evil heart is a lack of trust or belief in God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the living God**  - the only true God
  * **so long as it is called today**  - while there is still opportunity
  * **hardened by the deceitfulness of sin** - Sin deceives because it gives a false view of things rather than the clear view of truth. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
