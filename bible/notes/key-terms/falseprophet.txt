====== false prophet ======

===== Definition: =====

A false prophet is a person who makes a prophesy that does not come true or proclaims something contrary to Scripture.

  * A false prophet incorrectly claims to be divinely inspired.
  * Jesus stated that one will know who a false prophet by the fruit he or she bears.
