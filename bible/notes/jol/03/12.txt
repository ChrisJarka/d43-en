===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Put in the sickle, for the harvest is ripe**  - The means that is time for the wicked people to be cut down just like ripe grains are cut down at harvest time.
    * **Come, crush the grapes, for the winepress is full, the vats overflow**  - The wicked will be crushed (put under great pressure), just as grapes are crushed to make wine.  The pressure will be applied until the wine containers are full.
    * **their wickedness is enormous**  - "they are very wicked" or "their wickedness is great".
