===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **I will give you two thousand horses** - Rabshakeh says that the king of Assyria will give Hezekiah 2,000 horses for Hezekiah's army to ride.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
    * **if you are able to find riders for them.** - Rabshakeh continues to ridicule Hezekiah and his army, implying that he did not have many soldiers.
