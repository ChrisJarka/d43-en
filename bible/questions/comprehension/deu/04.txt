====== Deuteronomy Chapter 04 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What must the people of Israel do so that they may live, and go in and possess the land that Yahweh is giiving them?

A. They must listen and obey the laws that Moses is going to teach them so that they may possess the land. [4:1]

Q? What must the people not do?

A. They must not add to nor diminish the words that Moses commanded them so they may keep the commandments of Yahweh. [4:2]

Q? What men did Yahweh destroy and what men are alive today and why?

A. Yahweh destroyed the men that followed Baal Peor and those that clung to Yahweh are alive today. [4:3-4]

Q? Why should they keep the laws that Moses commanded them?

A. They should keep and do them so that they will have wisdom and understanding in the sight of the peoples. [4:6]

Q? What questions did Moses ask his people?

A. What other nation has a god near to them as Yahweh is when we call on him and what other great nation has laws so righteous as this law I am setting before you today? [4:7-8]

Q? Why should the people pay attention and guard their soul?

A. So that they do not forget what they have seen, but keep those things in their hearts and make them known to their children and their children's children. [4:9-10]

Q? Where was the fire and what did Moses hear?

A. The fire was on the mountain and Moses heard Yahweh's voice out of the middle of the fire, but saw no form. [4:11-12]

Q? What was the covenant that Yahweh commanded them to perform and what was it written on?

A. Yahweh commanded Moses to teach them the Ten Commandments that were written on two tablets of stone, so that they would do them. {4:13-14]

Q? What were the people told not to do?

A. They were told to be careful and not corrupt themselves by making carved figures of any form, including men, women, birds, things that creep, and fish. [4:15-18]

Q? Of what did Moses tell the people to be careful?

A. He told them that they should be careful to not be drawn away to worship the heavens, sun, moon, or stars. [4:19]

Q? Why did Yahweh bring the people out of the iron furnace, Egypt?

A. He brought them out of Egypt to be a people of his own inheritance. [4:20]

Q? Why was Yahweh angry at Moses and what were the results?

A. He was angry with Moses because of the people and would not let him go into the good land over Jordan but Moses must die there and the people will possess the good land. [4:21-22]

Q? Why is Yahweh a jealous God?

A. God has forbidden them to make any carved image because of the covenant he made with them. [4:23-24]

Q? What does Moses say he will do if the people corrupt themselves and do evil in Yahweh's sight?

A. When they do evil in the sight of Yahweh their God and provoke him to anger, Moses will call heaven and earth against them. They will perish from the land over the Jordan, and be completely destroyed. [4:25-26]

Q? What does Moses say will happen to the people?

A. Yahweh will scatter the people and they will be few in number among the nations where they will serve gods made of wood and stone. [4:27-28]

Q? When will the people find Yahweh?

A. They will find him when they search after him with all their heart and all their soul. [4:29]
