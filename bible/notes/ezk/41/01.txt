===== Translation Notes: =====

This chapter follows the description of the temple.

  * **Then he brought me - **  The word "he" refers refers to the man whose appearance is like brass, and who stood at the gate. (40:3)
  * **temple hall**  - "the hall of the temple"
  * V1: rephrase as "… doorposts. And they are six cubits wide on either side."
