===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **that which had been spoken through the prophet **- This means what a prophet had written.
  * **daughter of Zion **- refers to the Jews of Jerusalem and the land, or nation of Jews. (See: Synecdoche)
  * **Your King is coming to you, humble and riding on a donkey **- This refers to the prediction that one would come, not as king on a horse, a strong and powerful animal, but on a lowly animal
  * **colt**  - This is a young donkey.
