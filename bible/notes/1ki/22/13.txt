===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **with one mouth**  - AT: "they all agree and say the same thing." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
  * **him, me, I, he**  - These pronouns refer to Micaiah.
  * **your word**  - "what you will say." This relates to what Micaiah will say.
  * **one of them**  - "them" refers to the other prophets and excludes Micaiah. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_pluralpronouns|Pronouns - Plural]])
  * **should we go **- "we" refers to the armies of Ahab and Jehoshaphat. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_pluralpronouns|Pronouns - Plural]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive|Exclusive]])
  * **it ****is what Yahweh says**- "it" refers to the result of the battle.
  * **Yahweh will place it into the hand of the king**  - "Yahweh will give you victory," or "you will be victorious." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
