====== Leviticus Chapter 19 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What two things did Yahweh tell the people they must do?

A. Yahweh told the people that thy must respect their father and mother and keep his Sabbaths. [19:3,4]

Q? Why did Yahweh tell the people to leave the corners of their fields unharvested and to leave some of the grapes on the ground or that fell on the ground?

A. The unharvested grain and grapes must be left for the poor and the foreigner. [19:10]

Q? To whom should the people not show favoritism?

A. The people should not show favortism to someone because he is poor or rich, instead judge your neighbor righteously. [19:15]

Q? How are the people to take vengeance or hold a grudge?

A. The people are to take vengeance or hold a grude by loving your neighbor as yourself. [19:18]

Q? What must the people do when planting seeds in the field?

A. When planting the field the people must not plant two kinds of seed in the same field. [19:19]

Q? How long must a planter of a fruit tree wait before he can eat the fruit himself?

A. A planter of a fruit tree must wait until the fifth year before he can eat the fruit of the tree himself. [19:25]

Q? What pagan habits are the people told not to follow?

A.The pagan habits the people are told not to follow are shaving the sides of the head and cutting off the edges of the beard. [19:27]

Q? Who does Yahweh tell the people to arise before and honor?

A. Yahweh tells the people to rise before the gray-headed person and honor the old man. [19:32]

Q? Why does Yahweh say that the people of Israel should love the foreigner as they love themsleves?

A. Yahweh tells the people to love the foreigner because the people of Israel were once foreigners in the land of Egypt. [19:34]
