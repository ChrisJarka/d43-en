===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **sacrifice the Passover **- “offer the Passover sacrifice” (See: Synecdoche)
  * **within any of your city gates**  - AT: "within any of your towns" Cities often had walls around them to keep out enemies, and gates for people to go in and out. (See: Metonymy )
  * **at the place that Yahweh your God will choose as his sanctuary** - From the territory divided between the tribes of Israel, Yahweh would chose one location for worship. (that later came to be Jerusalem, inside the territory of the tribe of Judah)
  * **Yahweh your God will choose as his sanctuary **- AT: “Yahweh your God will choose as a habitation for his name” or “Yahweh your God will choose as a dwelling for his name.”
  * **at the going down of the sun**  - AT: "at sunset"
