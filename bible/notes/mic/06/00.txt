== Names in Lawsuit ==
In verse 4, Moses Aaron and Miriam are mentioned as witnesses for Yahweh and against Israel. 
In verse 16, Ahab and Omri are mentioned. They were Kings of Israel who were evil and did not follow Yahweh.
