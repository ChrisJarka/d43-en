====== Deuteronomy Chapter 03 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What happened on the way to Bashan and what did Yahweh say to Moses?

A. On the way to Bashan, the king of Bashan and all his people came and attacked them. Yahweh said, "Do not fear him; for I have given you victory over him and have put all his people and his land under your control. You will do to him as you did to Sihon, king of the Morites." [3:1-2]

Q? How did God give them victory and how were the people put under their control?

A. They struck the king of Bashan dead until not one of his people remained. They took all sixty of his cities, all the region of Argob, the kingdom of Og in Bashan. [3:3-4]

Q? What did the cities look like and what happened?

A. These fortified cities had high walls, gates, and bars and some cities were unwalled. Every inhabited city was destroyed as was the Sihon king of Heshbon, with women and children. All the cattle and the spoil of the cities they took as spoils for themselves. [3:5-7]

Q? What did Moses say they took out of the hands of the kings of the Amorites?

A. They took the land out of the hand of the two kings of the Amorites that were beyond the Jordan and all the cities of the plain, all Gilead, all Bashan, and all the way to the cities of the kingdom of Og in Bashan. [3:8-10]

Q? To whom did Moses give the land that they had taken possession?

A. He gave the valley of Arnon, half the hill country of Gilead, and its cities to Ruebenites and the Gadites. The rest of Gilead and all Bashan, the kingdom of Og, he gave to the half tribe of Manasseh. He gave Gilead to Machir, and gave territory from Gilead to the valley of Arnon to the Ruebenites and the Gadites. [3:12-16]

Q? What did Moses command the men of war to do?

A. He said that Yahweh had given them the land to possess and that they should pass over armed before the people of Israel. He said the wives, children and the cattle should stay in their cities that Yahweh had given them. [3:18-19]

Q? When did Yahweh say they could return to their own property?

A. Yahweh said every one of them would return after he had given them the land to possess beyond the Jordan. [3:20]

Q? What did Moses say to Joshua?

A. He told Joshua his eyes had seen all that Yahweh had done to the two kings and that Yahweh would do the same to all the kingdoms wherever Joshua would go. He also told him not to fear them because Yahweh would be the one who would fight fo him. [3:21-22]

Q? What did Moses say to Yahweh about his greatness?

A. He told Yahweh that he had begun to show him his greatness and his strong hand. He asked what god is there in heaven and earth that can do the same works and mighty acts as Yahweh? [3:23-24]

Q? Where did Moses beg to go and see?

A. He begged Yahweh to let him go and see the good land beyond Jordan and also Lebanon. [3:25]

Q? How did Yahweh answer him?

A. He was angry with him because of the people and and did not listen. Yahweh told him he should speak no more about this matter but should go up to the top of Pisgah and lift his eyes all around him for he would not go over the Jordan. [3:26-27]

Q? Who should be instructed and encouraged?

A. Moses was to instruct Joshua, encourage and strengthen him, bacause he would go over before the people and will cause them to inherit the land that you see. They stayed in the valley opposite Beth Peor. [3:28-29]
