**world** - In John, the word "world" is used at least seven different ways; rarely does it mean every man, woman and child on the face of the earth. One must always rely upon the context of the surrounding passage.
1) of those who believe in Christ and are eternally saved (Jn 3:16-17), cp Jesus' prayer in Jn 17.
2) non-believers (Jn 9:39, 14:17)
3) Mixed (Jn 12:19)
4) Physical earth (Jn 13:1)
5) All of creation (Jn 17:24, cp 1:10)
6) those who use the world (material goods - 1 Cor 7:31-34)
7) Physical appearance (1 Peter 3:3 - "world/cosmos" is translated "adornment", NKJV)
8) World system - Jn 12:31
9) Jewish religious system - (Jn 7:4)
