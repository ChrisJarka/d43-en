===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They**  - Levitical priests, the sons of Zadok (44:15)
  * **teach my people between the holy and the profane - ** This means to teach the people what is right and what is wrong.
  * **they will stand to judge with my judgment ****- **  the Levitical priests, the sons of Zadok will judge the people, using God's judgement.
  * **statutes**  - These are written rules or laws.
