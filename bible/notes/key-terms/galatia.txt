====== Galatia ======

===== Facts: =====

Galatia was a region of Asia Minor.
    * Paul addressed the letter of Galatians to churches in Galatia.
    * Where these churches were located, north or south Asia Minor, and when the letter was written is open to some debate.
    * The churches at Galatia were gentile Christian churches, which were being influenced Jewish Christians, trying to persuade them to keep aspects of the Jewish law; hence, the occasion for Paul's letter.
