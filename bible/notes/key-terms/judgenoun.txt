====== judge, judgment ======

===== Definition: =====

A judge is a person who decides what is right or wrong in civil, legal or spiritual matters. A judgment is a careful decision that is made about a matter.

  * Moses appointed men to judge disputes and other matters that the people brought to him.
  * After the people of Israel entered the promised land, before they had kings, God appointed judges to lead in times of trouble. Often, the judges rescued the Israelites from their enemies.
  * To judge is to decide whether something is good or bad, right or wrong. It can also mean to criticize or condemn.
  * The word "judgment" often refers to punishment for wrong behavior.
