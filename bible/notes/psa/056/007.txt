===== Translation Notes: =====

David continues to speak

  * **Do not let them escape**  - "Do not let my enemies escape" 
  * **your, you **- The words "your" and "you" refer to God.
  * **put my tears into your bottle**- AT: "Demonstrate that you care when I cry." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **they**  - The word "they" refers to "my tears"
