====== Jewish Law ======


===== Definition: =====


(as apposed to the Law of Moses. The laws of tradition that the Jews implemented beyond the law of moses)


Jewish law in the Old Testament included ceremonial law and civil law.


    * The law in the Old Testament consisted of the three elements of the Law of Moses, ceremonial law, and civil law.
    * The Law of Moses is the moral law defined by the Ten Commandments.
