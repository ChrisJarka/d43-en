====== myrrh ======

===== Definition: =====

Myrrh was a spice used as a perfume or to prepare a corpse for burial.

  * Myrrh was one of the gifts that the visiting Magi brought when they worshipped Jesus at His birth.
  * Jesus was offered wine and myrrh at His crucifixion.
