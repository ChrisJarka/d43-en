===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **But when your herds and your flocks multiply**  - Yahweh is still describing a situation possible of happening in the future, after they had settled in the good land.
  * **your heart might become lifted up**
 - "you might become proud"
  * **you might forget Yahweh your God**  - There was the possibility in the future of them forgetting who gave them all the prosperity they would have reached.
  * **who brought you out of the land of Egypt**  - Yahweh is reminding the people of Israel their past so that they would't became proud in themselves of their prosperity.
  * **house of bondage** - "house of slavery"
