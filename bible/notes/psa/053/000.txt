====== Psalms 053 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm is 53 is a wisdom psalm: warning evil doers that God will judge them.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

God looks down from heaven and sees that all humans are evil and corrupt.
