===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Yahweh continues his message of judgment on the people of Ammon.

  * **it will devour the palaces** - Here Yahweh's judgment is spoken of as if it were a fire that was consuming the palaces. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **with a tempest in the day of the whirlwind** - The fighting against the people of Ammon is spoken of as if it were a violent storm. AT: "and the fighting will be like a great storm" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **tempest ... whirlwind** - two kinds of violent storms
  * **whirlwind** - a strong wind that spins around and around
  * **Their king will go into captivity** - The abstract noun "captivity" can be expressed as the verb "capture." This can be expressed in active form. AT: "Their enemies will capture their king and take him away as a prisoner" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
