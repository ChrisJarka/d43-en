===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **His master saw that that Yahweh was with him and that Yahweh prospered everything that he did.**  - This doublet states a similar thought in two ways: Joseph had Yahweh's presence; Joseph enjoyed Yahweh's prosperity. An alternate translation could be: "His master saw that God was with Joseph because he prospered Joseph in everything." (See: [[en:ta:translate:doublet|Doublet]])
