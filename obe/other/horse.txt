====== horse ======

===== Definition: =====

A large four-legged animal used for transportation, recreation, and work.

  * Horses were considered a valuable possession in the Bible, mainly because of their use in war; King Solomon had thousands of them.
  * Some horses were used to pull carts or chariots, while others were used to carry individual riders.

(See also: other)
