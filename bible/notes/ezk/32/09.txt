===== Translation Notes: =====

These verses are a continuation of Ezekiel's lament to the King of Egypt, concerning the Egypt nation.

  * **I** - God is speaking.
  * **vex** - "worry" or "disturb" or "irritate" or "annoy".
  * **the hearing of many peoples** - "many peoples who hear about".
  * **when I bring your collapse among the nations** - "when I bring news of your fall among the nations". 'Your' refers to Egypt.
  * **I will appall many peoples concerning you** - "I will shock (or "amaze" or "horrify") many people who hear about you".
  * **in lands that you do not know!** - "and are living in far away lands".
  * **and their kings will shudder in horror concerning you** - "shake with fear about your punishment".
  * **swing my sword before them** - "enact judgment in front of all these people".
  * **and they will tremble constantly, each man with his wife** - "every man and wife will continually shake with fear".
  * **in the day of your downfall** - "in the day of your destruction".
