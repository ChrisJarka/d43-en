===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **blessed are your eyes **- Jesus is speaking to the disciples.
  * **blessed are your eyes, for they see; and your ears, for they hear**  - This is referring to the whole person using only the eyes and ears. (See Synecdoche.)
  * **did not see them**  - "Them" is referring to the "things."
