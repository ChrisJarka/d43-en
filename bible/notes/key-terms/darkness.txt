====== darkness ======

===== Definition: =====

In metaphorical uses in the Bible the word darkness refers to moral impurity and evil.

  * Satan's dominion is called the kingdom of darkness.
  * Anything having to do with sin is darkness.
  * The Bible states that man's unredeemed heart is filled with darkness, meaning moral corruption.
