===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues his teaching on the hill to his followers.

  * **Blessed are - **  This means that God is pleased.
  * **the meek**  - This refers to one with a gentle spirit.
  * **they will inherit the earth**  - The followers will receive all that God has planned for them.
  * **hunger and thirst for righteousness **  - This could be translated as "for people to please and obey God." (See: Metaphor) (See:UDB)
  * **they will be filled - **  This may be stated as an active phrase, "God will fill them". (See: Passive/Active)
  * **the pure in heart **- Pure in heart means any who obey God, and resist sin.
  * **they will see God **- This refers to one day that they will be where God is. (See UDB).
