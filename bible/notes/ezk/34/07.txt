===== Translation Notes: =====

Ezekiel is speaking for Yahweh.
  * **therefore** - This means "because of what I just said".
  * **the declaration of Yahweh** - See how you translated "This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration" in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]].
  * **As I live** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]].
  * **this is the Lord Yahweh's declaration** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]].
  * **plunder** - "Plunder" is what is taken by force or robbed. Alternate translation: "easy to steal" or "easy to kill."
  * **food for all the living beasts in the fields** – See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:34:04|34:5]].
  * **because there was no shepherd** - See how you translated "without a shepherd" in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:34:04|34:5]].
  * **sought my flock** - "tried to find my flock"
  * **guarded themselves** - "shepherded themselves" or "fed and cared for themselves"
  * **shepherd my flock** - "feed and care for my flock"
