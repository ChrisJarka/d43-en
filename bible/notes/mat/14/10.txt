===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of how Herod killed John the Baptist.

  * **He sent and beheaded**  - "He" refers to Herod.
  * **platter**  - This is a large plate.
  * **took up the corpse**  - "took John's dead body"
  * **they went and told Jesus **- "John's disciples left and told Jesus"
