===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **What shall I do**  - the manager asks this of himself, as a means of reviewing his options.
    * **master**  - that is the wealthy man or "lord of the house." In this case "employer" might be an acceptable translation, as the word also means "owner," but the man was not a slave.
    * **have strength to dig**  - or, "I am not able to dig".
