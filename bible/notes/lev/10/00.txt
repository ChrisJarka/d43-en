====== Leviticus 10 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

== Aaron and sons become priests (Leviticus 8-10) continued ==

God killed two of Aaron's sons for using unholy fire in their temple service.


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Mourning ==

Aaron was told that he and his remaining sons should not mourn Aaron's sons who were killed; because, as priests, they must put priest work first before their personal problems.
