===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Peter continues speaking to the believers.)
  * **It is necessary, therefore...**  - "Because of the scripture just quoted, we need a replacement Apostle."
  * **It is necessary, therefore, that the men who accompanied us all the time that the Lord Jesus went in and out among us **- "Since Judas must be replaced in the group of the twelve apostles, as we consider what men might fill that position, they must be men who were with us throughout Jesus' ministry."
  * **beginning from the baptism of John to the day that he was taken up from us, one of these must be a witness with us of his resurrection**  - Peter is elaborating on the period of time that these candidates should have been with the disciples, which is from the beginning of Jesus' ministry when he was baptized by John the Baptist to seeing Jesus ascend into Heaven. He must have also seen Jesus alive after His crucifixion.
  * **They put forward two men**  - As they looked to replace Judas, they found two men who fit the qualifications.
