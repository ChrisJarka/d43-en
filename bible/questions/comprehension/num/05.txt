====== Numbers Chapter 05 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Yahweh speak to Moses concerning who, male or female, to send away from the camp?

A. He was to send away everyone with an infectious skin disease, oozing sores or everyone who touched a dead body and thus were unclean. [5:1-4]

Q? What did Yahweh speak to Moses about men and women who commit sins?

A.They must confess the sin and pay back to the one he has wronged the price of his guilt and add one fifth more. [5:5-7]

Q? Who receives the payment and what does he do with it if the wronged person has no relative to receive the payment?

A. He must pay Yahweh, through a priest, along with a ram to atone for himself. It will then belong to that priest. [5:8-10]

Q? What does Yahweh tell Moses to do with a wife who sins against her husband?

A. The man should bring his wife to the priest and bring a drink offereing for her. He also needs to bring a grain offering of jealousy,as a possible indicator of sin. [5:15]

Q? What is the priest to do?

A. He must place the woman before Yahweh, and take a jar of holy water and mix dust from the floor of the tabernacle into the water. The priest then puts the grain offering of jealousy in her hands.The priest must cause her to swear an oath.
