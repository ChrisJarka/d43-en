===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **brothers **- referring to believers in Christ
  * **we**  - Paul, Timothy and Silas
  * **you**  - the believers in Thessalonica
  * **received instructions**  -they were taught
  * **must walk**  - ought to live (Hendiadys)
  * **as you do walk **- they already lived in a way that was pleasing to God
  * **even more**  - discover even more ways your life can honor God
