===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **tongue of fire**  - This expression can be translated "flames of fire" (see: Simile)
  * **their root…blossom will blow away**  - The sinful people will be destroyed quickly as fire easily burns dry grass. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **their…they**  - These words refer to the sinful people in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:05:18|Isaiah 05:18-19]]
  * **because they have rejected**  - Yahweh gives the reason for the sinful people's destruction.
  * **the word of the Holy One of Israel**  - This term refers to everything that Yahweh has communicated to people. This includes spoken and written messages.
