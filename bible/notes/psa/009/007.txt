===== translationNotes: =====


  * **Yahweh remains forever** - "Remain" probably represents sitting on the throne as king. AT: "Yahweh sits on his throne forever" or "Yahweh rules forever" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **he establishes his throne for justice** - The phrase "his throne" represents God's rule. Possible meanings are 1) "He rules in order to judge" or "He rules justly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **He judges the world fairly ... He makes just decisions for the nations** - These two sentences express the same meaning. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **He judges the world fairly** - Here "the world" refers to all the people in the world. AT: "He judges all the people of the world fairly." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
