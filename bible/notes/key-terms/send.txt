====== send, send out ======

===== Definition: =====

To "send" is to cause someone or something to go somewhere. To "send out" someone is to tell that person to go on an errand or a mission.

  * <font 14px line-height: 21.7777786254883px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>There is usually a specific purpose for sending or sending someone out. Often a person who is "sent out" has been appointed to do a specific task.</font>
  * Phrases like "send rain" or "send disaster" mean to cause those things to come.
