===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He disguised himself** - Josiah disguised himself so that the other armies would not recognize him. 
  * **fight with him** - The word "him" refers to Necho who represents himself accompanied by his army. AT: "fight with the army of Egypt" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **that had come from the mouth of God** - Here God is represented by his "mouth" to emphasize his speech. AT: "that had come from God" or "that God had said to him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **so he went** - The word "he" refers to Josiah who represents himself accompanied by his army. AT: "so he and his army went" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **the Valley of Megiddo** - This is the name of a place. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
