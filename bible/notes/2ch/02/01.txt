===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Now** - This marks a new part of the story.
  * **commanded the building of a house for Yahweh's name** - The word "name" is a metonym for the person. AT: "commanded his people to build a house where Yahweh may live" or "commanded his people to build a house where they could worship Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **of a palace for his kingdom** - "of a royal palace for his kingdom" or "of a palace for himself"
  * **seventy thousand men ... eighty thousand men** - "70,000 men ... 80,000 men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **to carry loads** - It is understood that these are loads of materials for building Yahweh's house. AT: "to carry loads of materials" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **3,600** - "three thousand six hundred" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Hiram** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
