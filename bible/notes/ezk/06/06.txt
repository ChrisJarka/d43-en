===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **you...your**  - The words "you" and "your" refer to the people of Israel.
    * **cities will be laid waste and made desolate and the high places shall be desolate**  - "Enemy armies will destroy your cities and no one will go there anymore and no one will go to the high places any longer."(See: [[en:ta:translate:active]])
    * **your altars will be laid waste and made desolate**  - "enemy armies will destroy your altars and no one will go there anymore" (See: [[en:ta:translate:active]])
    * ** I am Yahweh**  - God is saying that He is God and the idols being worshiped in Israel are not.
