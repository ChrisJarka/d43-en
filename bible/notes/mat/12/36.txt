===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus is continuing to speak to the people

  * **you **- Pharisees
  * **day of judgmen****t**  - This is the day each of us will stand before God to be judged.
  * **give an accoun****t **- This could mean "to answer for" or "to be judged by."
  * **idle word**  - This could mean "harmful, wasteful words."
  * **they**  - "people"
  * **by your words you will be justified**  - This means God honors you for speaking truth.
  * **by your words you will be condemned **- This means God is against you for speaking falsely.
