====== Psalms Chapter 123 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? To whom does the writer lift up his eyes?

A. The writer says that he lifts up his eye to the one who is enthroned in the heavens. [123:1]

Q? How long do the eyes of the people look to Yahweh their God?

A. They look to Yahweh until he has mercy on them. [123:2]

Q? Why are the people asking Yahweh to have mercy on them?

A. The people say they are filled with humiliation, they are more than full of the scoffing of the  insolent and with the contempt of the proud.  [123:3-4]
