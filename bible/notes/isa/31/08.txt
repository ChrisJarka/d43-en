===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Assyria will fall by the sword** - The soldiers of Assyria will be destroyed by the sword.  (See:  [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **a sword not wielded by man will consume him** - This says that Yahweh will destroy the Assyrian army.
  * **he...his...They...his princes** - These pronouns refer to the Assyrian soldiers and officers.
  * **this is the declaration of Yahweh** - Yahweh began speaking to the people in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:31:04|31:4]].
  * **whose fire is in Zion** - Fire means not only physical flames but in Scripture it is a symbol of power and judgment.
