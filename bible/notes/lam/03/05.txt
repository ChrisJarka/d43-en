===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He built up siege works against me** - The man speaks of God causing bad things to happen to him as if he were a city and God was an enemy that built up siege works against him. AT: "He attacked me like an enemy army that sets up siege works around a city" or "He surrounded me as if I were a city under attack" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **He built up siege works against me** - Siege works are ramps that an army builds around a city to enable them to climb over the city walls and invade the city.
  * **He made me live in dark places, like those who died long ago** - The man compares the darkness he experiences to the darkness of either the grave or the place where the spirits of dead people go. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **and surrounded me with bitterness and hardship** - This represents God causing him to experience much bitterness and hardship. AT: "and caused me to experience much bitterness and hardship" or "and caused me to suffer and have many problems" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **bitterness** - Here "bitterness" represents suffering. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **he shut out my prayer** - The man speaks about God refusing to listen to his prayers as though God was shutting his ears so that the man's prayers could not go into them. AT: "he refuses to hear my prayer" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
