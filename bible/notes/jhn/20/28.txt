===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you have believed** - Thomas believed that Jesus had risen from the dead. Alternate translation: "you have believed that I am alive" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **who have not seen** - Jesus is speaking about people who have not seen him alive after he had died. Alternate translation: "who have not seen me alive" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
