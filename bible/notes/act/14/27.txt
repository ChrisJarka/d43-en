===== translationNotes: =====

  * **When they arrived** - "When Paul and Barnabas arrived"
  * **God had done with them** - "God had done through Paul and Barnabas"
  * **he had opened a door of faith for the Gentiles** - The ability for the Gentiles to believe is compared to a door that God opened. AT: "God had made it possible for the Gentiles to believe." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **how he had** - "How God had"
  * **They stayed** - "Paul and Barnabas stayed"
