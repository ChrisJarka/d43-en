====== cast out, throw out ======

===== Definition: =====

To "cast out" or "throw out" someone or something means to force that person or thing to leave or cause it to be destroyed. or to freeing someone from oppression as when Jesus cast out demons.

  * <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>It can also refer to abandoning someone, such as when Pharaoh commanded the Hebrew people to cast out their newborn baby boys.</font>
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>In a figurative sense, it can mean to reject someone as in God casting someone out of His presence (Jeremiah 23:39)</font>
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>To "cast out demons" could be translated as "force the demons to leave" or "drive the evil spirits out" or to "expel the demons."</font>
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Depending on the context, other ways to translate this would be "force (you) out" or "send (her) away" or "get rid of."</font>
