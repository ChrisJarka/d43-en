====== Ezra 09 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

Ezra continues religious reforms by demanding that men with heathen wives must divorce them. (Ezra 9-10)

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

When Ezra found out that many Jews had married heathen wives, he prayed to God saying that God had been so good to let these few people return from captivity and now they have sinned by marrying heathen wives, like they had done before and like what God had punished them for. 

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Idiom ==
 
"All those who trembled at the words of the God of Israel" meaning all who were afraid of the punishment God would punish them with for disobeying him again. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
