====== Jeremiah 11 General Notes ======

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== The covenant ==

The people have disobeyed the covenant; therefore God will not help them.

== The men of Anathoth have plotted to kill Jeremiah.  ==

God will destroy them for plotting to kill Jeremiah.
