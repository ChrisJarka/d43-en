====== Psalms Chapter 140 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What are some reasons that David wants resued from the wicked?

A. David wants rescued from the wicked to preserve him from violent men.   [140:1]                                                                                        [

Q? What do the wicked do?

A. The wicked plan evil in their hearts, stir up conflict every day, their tongues wound like serpents and vipers' poison is on their lips. [140:2-3]

Q? How does David want Yahweh to protect him?

A. David wants Yahweh to keep the hands of the wicked away from David, preserve him from violent men who plan to knock him over, and protct him from the trap, net and snare that the proud have set against David. [140:4-5]

Q? What does David say to Yahweh?

A. David said, "You are my God; listen to my cries for mercy." [140:6]

Q? What does Yahweh do for David?

A. Yaweh is strong to save; Yahweh shields David's head on the day of battle, and Yahweh does not let the wickeds desires or plots succeed. [140:7-8]

Q? What does David want to happen to those who surround him?

A. David wants those who surround him to raise their heads; let the mischief of theri own lips overwhelm them, let burning coals fall on them; throw them into the fire, into bottowmless pits, never more to rise. [140:9-10]

Q? What does David want to happen to those who say evil things about others?

A. David wants anyone who says evil things about others to not be secure on the earth and to be hunted down by evil the violent man to overthrow him. [140:11]
