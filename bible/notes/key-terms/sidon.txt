====== Sidon ======


===== Facts: =====


The firstborn son of Canaan and great-grandson of Noah.  Sidon is also a city, named after its founder. 


    * The city of Sidon was outside of Israel.
    * The city of Sidon was located 40 kilometers south of modern-day Beirut on the Mediterranian coast.
