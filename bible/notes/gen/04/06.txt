===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Why are you angry and why is your face ... **  - God used these rhetorical questions to tell Cain that he was wrong to be angry and to look dejected. (See: [[en:ta:figuresofspeech:rhetoricalquestion]])
  * **will you not be accepted**  - God used this rhetorical question to remind Cain of something Cain should have already known.
  * **sin crouches at the door**  - Here sin is pictured as an animal that is ready to attack Cain. (See: [[en:ta:figuresofspeech:personification]])
