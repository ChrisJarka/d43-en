====== unclean ======


===== Definition: =====


Unclean means being sinful or unholy.


    * An Old Testament priest who did not properly prepare himself to serve would be considered unclean.
    * In the Old Testament, God told the Israelites that certain animals were clean or unclean for eating.
    * Unclean is the opposite of being clean.
