===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **ignorant and lacked insight** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing and emphasize the greatness of his ignorance. AT: "very ignorant" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **lacked insight** - AT: "understood nothing"
  * **you** - This "you" refers to God.
