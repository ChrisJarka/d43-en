===== translationNotes: =====

  * **by obedience to the truth** - You can translate this using a verbal phrase. AT: "by obeying the truth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **brotherly love** - This is natural human love between friends or relatives.
  * **love one another earnestly from the heart** - "love one another deeply and sincerely"
  * **not from perishable seed...the living and remaining word of God** -  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **imperishable** - "not perishable" or "permanent"
  * **through the living...word of God** - The living word of God refers to God's word ability to change peoples lives throughout time as if it were a real person preaching and teaching people about God.
