<font 20px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>** Translation Notes:  **</font>

  * **overshadowed them **- "them" refers to Jesus, Moses, Elijah, and the disciples Peter, James and John.
  * **there was a voice out of the cloud**
- They heard God speaking about Jesus from inside the cloud. (UDB) - implied information

  * **they fell on their face**  - "they" refers to Peter, James, and John.
  * **and were very afraid**  - These adult disciples were not afraid of clouds. This phrase indicates that some kind of unusual fear came over them with the cloud. It could be translated as “were terrified.”
