===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **that I am Yahweh their God** - AT: "that I, Yahweh their God am with them." This means that Yahweh helps them. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **They are my people** - AT: "and that they are my people"
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]].
  * **For I will establish my flock as the flock of my pasture** - AT: "You are my flock, the flock of my pasture." 
  * **They will be my people** - AT: "You are my people" or "You are people"
  * **I will be your God** - AT: "I am your God"
