====== darkness ======

===== Definition: =====

Literally, darkness means that there is no light, like at night.  Figuratively, darkness relates to wickedness.

  * Satan's dominion is darkness.
  * Anything having to do with sin is darkness.
  * Jesus said that unbelievers will be cast into the outer darkness where there will be weeping and gnashing of teeth, meaning hell.

(See also: other)
