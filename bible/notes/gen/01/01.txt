===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **In the beginning God created…**  - This is an introductory statement about the beginning of the world. It gives a summary of the rest of the chapter. It can be translated as "This is about God creating the heavens and the earth in the beginning." Some languages translate it as "A very long time ago God created…." Make sure to translate it in a way that people would understand that this is true and not just a folk story.
  * **In the beginning**  - This refers to the beginning of the world. Some languages may say "Long, long ago." Make sure 
  * **the heavens and the earth**  - These words are used together to mean "the universe." This can also be translated as "the sky, the ground, and everything in them."
  * **heavens** - This refers here to the sky.
  * **without form and empty**  - The world was in chaos. God had not yet put it in order or given it a purpose and a function.
  * **empty**  - There was nothing on the earth--only water.
  * **waters**  - This can be translated as "water" or "deep water" or "vast water."
