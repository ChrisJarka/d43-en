====== 2 Corinthians 13 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

In 13:1-10 he finishes up his defense and in 13:11-13 he has the usual closer with greetings to all and a blessing. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

When Paul comes, he will use God’s power to punish those who are sinning and not repenting. They should check themselves to be sure they are approved by God. He is praying that they will do right. He is not concerned whether they think he is right or wrong. He is writing these warnings so that he will not have to discipline them when he arrives.

Finally he sends them his greetings and closes by blessing them. 

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

**Double negative**
Paul uses a [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]] “not unapproved” meaning approved.
