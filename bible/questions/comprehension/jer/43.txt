====== Jeremiah Chapter 43 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What did Jeremiah finish proclaiming to all the people.

A. Jeremiah finished proclaiming to all the people all the words of Yahweh their God that Yahweh their God had told him to say. [43:1]

Q? Of what did Azariah, Johana and all the arrogant men accuse Jeremiah?

A. They accused Jeremiah of telling them lies. [43:2]

Q? What what did Azariah, Johana and all the arrogant men say about what Jeremiah told them that Yahweh had said?

A. They said that Yahweh did not send Jeremiah to tell them not to go to Egypt and live there. [43:2]

Q? Why did Azariah, Johana and all the arrogant men say that Baruch was inciting Jeremiah against them?

A. They said that Baruch was inciting Jeremiah against them so that Jeremiah could give them over into the hand of the Chaldeans to cause their death and make them captives in Babylon. [43:3]

Q? What did Johanan, all the princes of the army and all the people do when Jeremiah told them that Yahweh had said not to go to Egypt?

A. Johanan, all the princes of the army and all the people refused to listen to Yahweh's voice to live in the land of Judah. [43:4]

Q? Who did Johanan, all the princes of the army and all the people take away when they went to the land of Egypt?

A. They took away all the remnant of Judah who Nebuzaradan had let remain with Gedaliah and they also took Jeremiah the prophet and Baruch the son of Neriah and they went to the land of Egypt. [43:5-7]

Q? Why did Johanan, all the princes of the army and all the people go to Egypt, to Tahpanhes?

A. They went to Egypt, to Tahpanhes because they did not listen to Yahweh's voice. [43:7]

Q? What did Yahweh tell Jeremiah to do with the stones in Jeremiah's hand?

A. Yahweh told Jeremiah to take some large stones in his hands and in the sight of the people of Judah, to hide the stones in the cement pavement at the entrance to Pharaoh's house in Tahpanhes. [43:8-9]

Q? What did Yahweh say that Jeremiah should say to the people?

A. Yahweh told Jeremiah to tell the people that Yahweh was going to send messengers to take Nebuchadnezzar as his servant. Yahweh would place Nebuchadnezzar's throne over the stones that Jeremiah had buried and Nebuchadnezzar would place his pavilion over the stones. [43:10]

Q? What did Yahweh say that Nebuchadnezzar would do?

A. Yahweh said that Nebuchadnezzar would come and attack the land of Egypt. [43:11]

Q? What does Yahweh say will hapen to the people when Nebuchadnezzar comes and attacks the land of Egypt?

A. Yahweh said that when Nebuchadnezzar comes and attacks the land of Egypt anyone assigned to death will be given to death, anyone who is assigned to captivity will be taken captive and anyone who is assigned to the sword will be given to the sword. [43:11]

Q? What does Yahweh say he will do to the temples of Egypt's gods?

A. Yahweh says he will light a fire in the temples of Egypt's gods and burn them or capture them. [43:12]

Q? What does Yahweh say will happen to the land of Egypt?

A. Yahweh said he would clean out the land of Egypt just as shepherds clean vermin off their clothes. [43:12]

Q? How does Yahweh say he will go out from that place?

A. Yahweh says that he will go out from that place in victory. [43:12]

Q? What does Yahweh say he will do to the stone pillars at Heliopolis and what will he to to the temples of Egypt's gods?

A. Yahway says that he will break the stone pillars at Heliopolis and burn the temples of Egypt's gods. [43:13]
