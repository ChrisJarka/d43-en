===== Introduction =====

This module answers the question: //What is this handbook for?//\\
====  ====

This unfoldingWord Audio Handbook is yours. Our hope is for it to be a helpful companion in your audio projects, training and equipping you in the basic skills and wisdom you need to carry a project from start to finish.

We have divided the information into sections focused on various aspects of audio work. In addition, there are handy reference materials included at the back of the handbook.

This handbook is a work in progress, so if you recognize the need we are trying to meet and have any helpful tips please let us know. Occasionally this handbook will be updated and identified with version numbers. You can always find the latest version at https://unfoldingword.org.
