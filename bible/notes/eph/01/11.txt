===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **we ** - This pronoun refers to Paul and all other believers.​
  * **we were chosen** - This phrase can be translated in two ways: 1) “we have obtained an inheritance”, 2) “we were made a heritage”.
  * **the plan of the one ** - "The plan of God".
  * **his ** - This pronoun refers to God.
  * **we who were the first ** - The pronoun “we” is exclusive. It refers to Jewish believers, including Paul, but excludes believers who are Gentile (non-Jewish). (See: [[en:ta:translate:exclusive]])
