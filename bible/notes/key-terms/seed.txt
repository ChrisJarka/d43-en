====== seed ======

===== Definition: =====

A seed is the part of a plant that gets planted in the ground to reproduce more of the same kind of plant.

  * <font 14px line-height: 19.6000003814697px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>"Seed" is often used figuratively to refer to a person's offspring or descendants.</font>
  * In English the word "seed" can be plural, referring to more than one descendant.

(See also: descendant)
