===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Iâ€¦me**  - Here this refers to Isaiah
  * **I went to the prophetess **- This is an euphemism. This can be translated as "I slept with my wife, who was a prophetess" (UDB) or "I had sex with my wife, who was a prophetess" (See: Euphemism)
  * **Call his name**  - "Give him the name"
  * **'Maher-shalal-hash-baz' **  - This Hebrew name  has a meaning for this prophecy translate as in verse 1.
  * **For before the child knows to cry**  - "because before the child can say"
  * **the riches of Damascus and the plunder of Samaria **- This is expressing the same idea in two different ways. This can be translated as, "the valuables of Damascus and Samaria." (See: Parallelism)
  * **will be carried away**  - "will be taken away"
