===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It is just as you learned it from Epaphras** - "It is just as Epaphras taught you" or "You have thoroughly understood what Epaphras taught you"
  * **It is just** - "It" refers to the effect or results of the gospel message in the Colossian believers' lives.
  * **you learned** - the Colossian believers learned
  * **Epaphras** - Epaphras was the man who preached the gospel to the people in Colossae. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Epaphras, our beloved fellow servant, who is a faithful servant of Christ for us** - This means that Epaphras was doing work for Christ that Paul himself would have done if he were not in prison.
  * **He has revealed to us** - "Epaphras has made known to us"
  * **your love in the Spirit** - "The Holy Spirit has enabled you to love believers"
