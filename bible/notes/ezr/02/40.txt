===== Translation Notes: =====

The numbers of people in groups listed  - (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]] and 
See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])

    * **Kadmiel**  - Translate the same way as in Ezra 3:9, Nehemiah 7:43, 9:4, 5, 10:9, 12:8 and 24.
    * **Hodaviah**  - Translate the same way as in 1 Chronicles 3:24, 5:24 and 9:7.
    * **gatekeepers**  - Those in charge of who enters or exits the gates of the city.
    * **Shallum**  - Translate the same way as in 2 Kings 15:10, 2 Kings 15:13-15, 22:14, 1 Chronicles 2:40-41, 3:15, 4:25, 6:12, 13, 7:13, 9:17, 19, 31, 28:12, 34:22, Ezra 2:42, 7:2, 10:24, 42, Nehemiah 3:12, 7:45, Jeremiah 22:11 and 32:7.
    * **Ater**  - Translate the same way in Ezra 2:16, Nehemiah 7:21, 45, and 10:17.
    * **Talmon**  - Translate the same way in 1 Chronicles 9:17, Ezra 2:42, 7:45, 11:25 and 12:25.
    * **Akkub**  - Translate the same way as in 1 Chronicles 3:24, 9:17, Ezra 2:42, 45, Nehemiah 7:45, 8:7, 11:19, 12:25.
    * **Haitita, Shobai**  - Translate the same way in Nehemiah 7:45.
