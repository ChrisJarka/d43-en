====== fear, afraid ======

===== Definition: =====

Fear is what a person feels when he knows that he, or someone he cares about, is in danger or could be harmed.

  * The word "fear" can refer to extreme terror or to a respect and carefulness around the thing or person that can cause the harm.
  * The “fear of God” is something that people feel when they know that God is perfectly holy and hates sin. This fear motivates people to obey God and to hate all forms of evil.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * "To fear" can be translated as "to be afraid," "to deeply respect," "to revere" or "to be in awe of."
  * "Afraid" can be translated as "terrified", "scared" or "fearful."
