====== Psalms Chapter 009 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? How will David give thanks to Yahway and of what will he tell?

A. He will give thanks to Yahweh with his whole heart and tell about all his marveloous deeds. [9:1]

Q? What will David do?

A. He will be glad and rejoice in Yahweh, and sing praise to his name, Most High. [9:2]

Q? When David's enemies turn back what happens to them?

A. The enemies stumble and perish before Yahweh. [9:3]

Q? What has the rightous judge done for David?

A. The rightous judge sits on his throne and defends David's just cause. [9:4]

Q? What did the rightous judge do to the nations and the wicked?

A. He terrified the nations with his battle cry, destroyed the wicked, and blotted out their memory forever. [9:5]

Q? What happened to the enemy when the righteous judge overthrew their cities?

A. The enemy crumbled like ruins and all the remembrance of them perished. [9:6]

Q? Who remains forever and establishes his throne for justice?

A. Yahweh remains forever and establishes his throne for justice. [9:7]

Q? How does Yahweh judge the world and what does he do for the nations?

A. Yahweh judges the world fairly and makes decisions for the nations. [9:8]

Q? What will Yahweh be for the oppressed and when?

A. Yahweh will be a stronghold for the oppressed in times of trouble. [9:9]

Q? Who will know your name, Yahweh and not be abandoned by you?

A. Those who trust in Yahweh and seek him. [9:10]

Q? What does David say the people are to do to Yahweh, who rules in Zion?

A. They are to sing praises to Yahweh and tell the nations what Yahweh has done. [9:11]

Q? What does the God who avenges bloodshed not forget?

A. He does not forget the cry of the oppressed. [9:12]

Q? For what is David asking Yahweh?

A. He is asking Yahweh to have mercy on him.  [9:13]

Q? Who is David oppressed by and who can snatch him from the gates of death?

A. David is oppressed by those who hate him and Yahweh can snatch him from the gates of death.  [9:13]

Q? What does David want to proclaim and where will he rejoice in salvation?

A. David will proclaim all Yahweh's praise and will rejoice in salvation, in the gates of the daughter of Zion.  [9:14]
