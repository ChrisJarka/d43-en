===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Therefore** - "So"
  * **thus says the Lord Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:10|3:11]].
  * **Because it was so tall** - "because the cedar was so tall"
  * **he raised the top of his tree above the branches** - alternate translation: "the top of the cedar tree was in the clouds"
  * **and his heart was lifted** - This phrase means that the cedar tree became very proud or arrogant. Alternate translation: "and the cedar became very proud." (See: [[en:ta:translate:idiom]])
  * **to that height** - "because the it was very tall" or "because it was so tall" (UDB)
  * **you**  - This refers to God talking to Assyria.
  * **he**  - God is doing the action.
  * **tall in height, and he set his tree top in between the branches**  - This is a reference to the great power of Assyria.
  * **his heart was lifted up**  - "Assyria's heart was proud".
  * **hand of the mighty one of nations**  - Because of Assyria's pride, God used Babylon to destroy the nations.
  * **He did his works upon him**  - "God did God's work upon Pharaoh".
  * **drove him away**  - "cast out".
