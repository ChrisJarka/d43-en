====== Phoenicia ======

===== Facts: =====

Phoenicia was a coastal region along the Western edge of the fertile crescent.

  * In the New Testament era the capital of Phoenicia was Tyre.
  * A woman from Phoenicia asked Jesus to heal her demon-possessed daughter, to which He replied "it is not right to take the children's food and feed it to the dogs;" but He healed her daughter as a result of her retort which demonstrated great faith.. (Matthew 15:21-28)
