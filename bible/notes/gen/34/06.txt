===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Hamor...went out to**  - "Hamor...went to meet"
  * **when they heard it**  - "when they heard what had happened"
  * **The men were offended**  - "The men were outraged"
  * **he had disgraced Israel**  - "he had committed an outrageous act in Israel" The author wants us to understand that "Israel" is more than just another name for Jacob. In the same sentence, the author identifies Jacob as the father of the girl and "Israel" as every member of his family. The entire clan, the entire people-group, is disgraced. Israel as a people is disgraced.
  * **by forcing himself on** - "by having sexual relations with" (See: [[en:bible:team-info:training:topics:figures:euphemism]]
  * **should not have been done **- "should not be done"
