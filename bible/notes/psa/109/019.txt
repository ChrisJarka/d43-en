===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **May his curses...cover himself** - "May his curses be like cloths that he wraps around himself." People wore their clothes everyday. AT: "Let his curses be close to him every day like the garment he wears." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **like the belt he always wears** - AT: "and let his curses always be on him like the belt he always wears." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **May this be the reward of my accusers from Yahweh** - "This is the reward that Yahweh gives to people that accuse me."
  * **of those who speak evil things about me** - "and for people who say bad things about me"
