====== Ashdod ======

===== Facts: =====

Ashdod was a chief Philistine city when the Israelites entered the land of Canaan. It was located near the Mediterranean Sea, halfway between Gaza and Joppa.

  * When captured by the Philistines, the Ark of the Covenant was taken to Ashdod.
  * In New Testament times, the city was called Azotus.
  * The current name of the city is still Ashdod.
