====== patriarchs ======

===== Definition: =====

<font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The term "patriarchs" in the OT refers to the founding fathers of the Jewish people, particularly Abraham, Isaac and Jacob.</font>

  * It also can refer to the twelve sons of Jacob who became the 12 patriarchs of the 12 tribes of Israel.
  * <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>The term "patriarch" has a similar meaning to "forefather" but more specifically refers to the most well-known ancestral leaders of a people group.</font>

(See also: father, forefather other)
