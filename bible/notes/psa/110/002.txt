===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues to speak to the lord.
  * **Hold out the scepter of your strength from Zion** - A scepter represents the strength and power of a king. AT: "Rule as king with strength from Zion." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **will follow you of their own free will** - "will follow you because they choose to"
  * **on the holy mountains**  - This refers to the mountains of Zion.
  * **in the womb of the dawn**  - AT: "just before dawn." (See: Metaphor)
  * **go out like the dew of your youth **- AT: "go out fresh and eager." (See: Simile)
