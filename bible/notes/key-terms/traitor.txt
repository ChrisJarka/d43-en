====== traitor ======


===== Definition: =====


A traitor is a person who is unfaithful or disloyal to their friends or country.


    * Judas Iscariot was a traitor because he was disloyal to Jesus. He told the Roman and Jewish authorities where Jesus' was, so they could capture and kill Jesus.
