====== Job Chapter 05 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What does Eliphaz say will happen to a foolish and silly man?

A. He says anger kills the foolish man, his hojme will be cursed, and jealousy kills the silly one. [5:2-3]

Q? Who is crushed at the gate because there is no one to rescue them and who will eat the harvest?

A. The children of the foolish person are crushed at the gate and the people who are hungry will eat the harvest. [5:4-5]

Q? Why don't difficulties and trouble come out of the ground?

A. He says mankind makes his own trouble. [5:6-7]

Q? Why does Eliphaz say he would turn to God?

A. He says that God does great, marvelous, and profound things; God gives rain on the earth. [5:8-10]

Q? What other reasons does Eliphaz say why he would turn to God?

A. He says God will raise to safety those that mourn, frustrates the schemes of the crafty, and traps the wise in their own craftiness. [5:11-13]

Q? From what does God save the needy and poor person?

A. He saves the poor person from the sword in their mouths so the poor person has hope, and saves the needy person from the hand of the mighty people. [5:15-16]

Q? Why is the man happy whom God corrects and chastens?

A. Because God binds up wounds and his hands heal, he rescues out of troubles. [5:17-19]

Q? How will God protect Job from death, scourge of the tongue, destruction, famine, and wild beasts?

A. He will rescue him from death and the sword, he will be hidden, and he will take away his fears. [5:20-22]

Q? What will Job experience if he trusts God?

A. He will experience peace with the wild animals, know safety in his tent, no sheep will be missing, his posterity will be great, and his offspring will be plentiful. [5:23-25]

Q? What does Eliphaz tell Job to do to live a long life?

A. He tells Job to examine what he had been telling him, listen to it, and know it for yourself. [5:26-27]
