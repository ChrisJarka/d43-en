===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **then he must kill the bull** - The pronoun 'he' here refers to the one who brings the offering for sin.
    * **bull** - The bull is the animal sacrifice from among the herds.
    * **before Yahweh** - Before Yahweh means in front of the tent where sacrifices are being made.
    * **sprinkle** - This term means "to scatter" or "to let drops fall"
    * **then he must skin the burnt offering** - The pronoun 'he' here refers to the one who brings the offering for sin.
    * **cut it to pieces** - AT: "cut it into pieces"
