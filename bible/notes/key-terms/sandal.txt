====== sandal ======

===== Definition: =====

A sandal is a simple shoe held onto the foot with straps around the instep or ankles worn by both men and women.

  * A sandal was also used to to confirm a legal transaction such as the selling of property, (see Ruth 4:7, Deuteronomy 27: 7-9).
