===== Translation Notes: =====

God continues to talk in front of Ezekiel.

  * **he cried in my hearing**  - The phrase "in my hearing" means that he was close enough to be heard.
  * **Let the guards come up to the city**  - The word "guards" refer to a person who protects something.  God is calling spirit guards to defend his temple in Jerusalem.
  * **each with his weapon of destruction...each with his weapon of slaughter**  - This means the guards had different weapons they would use to kill.
  * **Behold**  - The word "behold" here adds emphasis to what follows. It can also be translated as "Indeed."
  * **linen**  - The word "linen" refers to a strong, smooth cloth made from a plant.  It is worn by many people in places that are hot. (See: [[en:ta:translate:translateunknowns]])
  * **scribe's equipment**  - This refers to tools used by a scribe.  These would include a pen with ink to use for writing.
  * **bronze**  - A dark gold colored metal.  It is made from mostly copper with tin added for strength. (See: [[en:ta:translate:translateunknowns]])
