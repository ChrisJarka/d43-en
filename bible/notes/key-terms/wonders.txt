====== wonders ======

===== Definition: =====

Wonders are miraculous acts performed by God's power.

  * Some wonders were God's acts of redemption as seen in the parting of the Red Sea, the parting of the Jordan, and the deliverance of Daniel in the fiery furnace and in the lions den.
  * Other wonders were God's acts of judgment as seen in the plagues that came upon Pharaoh and Egypt
  * Some of God's wonders were the physical healing of sick or disabled people
  * They are special displays of God's power

(See also: other)
