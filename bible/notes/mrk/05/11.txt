===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They implored him, saying, "Send us into the pigs; let us enter into them."**  - The evil spirits did not want to be cast from the region into nothingness but instead wished to have a specific place within the pigs.
  * **He permitted them to do that**  - The evil spirits were subject to Jesus' authority and they could do nothing without his permission.
