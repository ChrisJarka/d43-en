====== Joshua 02 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

== Conquest of the Promised Land (Judges 2-12) ==

Spies sent to Jericho


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Rahab's faith ==

Rahab expressed her faith in Yahweh and so was saved when she said, "for Yahweh your God, he is God in heaven above and on the earth below."


=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphor ==

That the enemies of Israel would not remain alive, they are compared to melting snow: "melt away before you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
