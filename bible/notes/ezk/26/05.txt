===== Important Terms: =====

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **a place for nets to dry out in the midst of the sea**  - Nets were typically dried in an empty place. This indicates that Tyre will be a barren place.
  * **I have declared it**  - When God says something will happen, it is not unusual for the Bible to speak of it as already completed.
  * **she will become plunder for the nations **- The nations will rob Tyre.
