====== consecrate ======

===== Definition: =====

To consecrate means to deliberately or formally dedicate something or someone to serve God.

  * The person or object that is consecrated is considered holy and set apart for God.
  * The meaning of this term is similar to "sanctify" but with the added meaning of formally setting apart someone for service to God.

(See also: holy, holiness, sanctify, sanctification)
