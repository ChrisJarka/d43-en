====== flood ======

===== Definition: =====

A flood is a great amount of water that covers over land completely so that the ground cannot be seen.

  * In Noah's time, people had become so evil that God caused a worldwide flood to come over the entire surface of the earth, even covering the mountaintops, in order to destroy all humans except Noah's family and the animals on the ark.

(See also: Noah, ark, other)
