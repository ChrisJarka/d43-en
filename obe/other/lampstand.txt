====== lampstand ======

===== Definition: =====

A lampstand is something on which to place a lamp in an elevated position to provide a broad area of light.

  * A lamp in biblical times was a bowl of olive oil or fat with a spout and wick that burned with an open flame to provide light.
  * A lampstand could be constructed of wood or iron, but golden lampstands are mentioned in the book of Revelation in John's vision while he was in heaven.

(See also: other)
