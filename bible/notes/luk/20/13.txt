===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Jesus continues his parable)
  * **the lord of the vineyard**  - That is, "the owner of the vineyard."
  * **will respect him**  - That is, "will acknowledge the authority he has over them."
  * **beloved son**  - explanation
  * **the vine dressers**  - That is, "the people who took care of grape vines" or "the grape farmers."
  * **saw him**  - That is, "saw the son" or "saw his son."
