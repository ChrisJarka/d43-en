====== Cyrene ======

===== Facts: =====

Cyrene was a Greek city on the north coast of Libya, the region also known as Cyrenaica, on the Mediterranean Sea south of the island of Crete.

  * Cyrene had communities of both Jews and Christians in New Testament times.
