====== Job Chapter 38 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who is speaking to Job now out of a fierce storm?

A. Yahweh is speaking to Job out of a fierce storm. [38:1]

Q? Who brings darkness to Yahweh's plans by using words without knowledge?

A. Job brings darkness to Yahweh's plans by using words without knowledge.

Q? What does Yahweh tell Job he must do when Yahweh questions him?

A. Yahweh tells Job that he must answer Yahweh's questions. [38:3]

Q? Who laid the earth's foundations and determined its dimensions?

A. God laid the earth's foundations and determined its dimensions. [38:4-5]

Q? What sang together and who shouted for joy when Yahweh laid the cornerstone of the earth?

A. The morning stars sang together and all the sons of God shouted for joy when the cornerstone was laid. [38:6-7]

Q? To what does Yahweh compare the sea bursting out after it was shut up with doors?

A. God compares the sea bursting out of doors to coming out of the womb. [38:8]

Q? What did Yahweh put in place to mark out the boundary of the sea so that it could come only so far and no farther?

A. Yahweh put in place bars and doors to mark a boundary for the sea. [38:10-11]

Q? What does Yahweh ask Job about giving orders to the morning and causing the morning dawn to know its place?

A. Yahweh asks Job if he has ever given those orders. [38:12]

Q? What stands out clearly like the folds of a piece of clothing when the earth is changed in appearance in the light of dawn?

A. In the light of dawn all things on the earth stand out clearly like the folds of a piece of clothing . [38:14]

Q? What does Yahweh ask Job to do after Yahweh questions him about what has been revealed to Job about the sea, the gates of death and the expanse of the earth?

A. Yahweh asks Job to tell him, if he knows it all. [38:16-18]

Q? What does Yahweh say to mock Job about his lack of knowledge about the way to the resting place of light and darkness?

A. Yahweh says to Job in a mocking way that undoubtedly Job should know about this because the number of Job's days is so large. [38:19-21]

Q? For what reason does Yahweh say he has kept storehouses for the snow and the hail?

A. Yahweh has kept these storehouses for times of trouble and for days of battle and war. [38:22-23]

Q? Why does Yahweh say that he has created channels for the floods and why has he caused it to rain on the wilderness in which there is no person?

A. Yahweh says that he causes it to rain on the wilderness in order to meet the needs of the barren and lonely regions and to make the tender grass sprout up. [38:25-27]

Q? Who is it that fathers the rain and dewdrops, births the ice and frost and causes the deep to freeze like stone?

A. It is Yahweh who makes the rain, the dewdrops, the ice, the frost and the frozen deep. [38:28-30]

Q? What is the question that Yahweh asks Job about the constellations and the sky?

A. Yahweh asks Job if he can do these things. [38:31-33]

Q? What are the things that Yahweh is asking Job if he can control?

A. Yahweh is asking Job if he can control the clouds, the rainwater or the lightning. [38:34-35]

Q? What happens to the dust and clods of earth when Yahweh pours out water on them?

A. When Yahweh pours out water on the dust it runs into a hard mass and the clods of earth clump tightly together.[38:37-38]

Q? What does Yahweh ask Job about the lioness and her cubs when they are crouching in their dens?

A. Yahweh asks Job if he can hunt down a victim for the lioness or satisfy the appetite of her cubs when they are crouching in their dens. [38:39-40]

Q? Why do the young ones of the ravens cry out to God?

A. They cry out to God when they stagger about for lack of food. [38:41]
