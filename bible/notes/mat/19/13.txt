===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **lay his hands on them and pray - **  This action was used in the Jewish culture to receive a special gift from God when a godly man touched them and prayed for them.
  * **for to such as these -**  This means to those who are like little children who have open and believing hearts.
  * **rebuked them**  - "scolded the people who were bringing the children to Jesus"'
  * **Welcome the little children**  - "Let the little children come to me"(UDB)
  * **do not forbid them**  - This could be translated “do not hinder them” or “do not prevent the little children from coming to me”.
  * **S****uch as these belongs**  - This could also be translated “belongs to people who are like these little children.”
