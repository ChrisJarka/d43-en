===== Translation Notes: =====

  * Jacob refers to Israel
  * **the glory of Jacob will become thin and the fatness of his flesh will become lean –**Israel is likened to a person who was healthy and fat who become sickly and lean **–“**Israel  will be brought low and she will become destitute”  (See:Metaphor**)**
  * **It will be as when a harvester gathers the standing grain, and his arm reaps the heads of grain. It will be as when one gleans heads of grain in the valley of Rephaim**  - Israel will be like a harvest field where the grain will taken away so Israel will be taken away to a foreign land (See:Simile)**)**
