====== uncircumcised, uncircumcision ======

===== Definition: =====

In the New Testament the terms uncircumcised or uncircumcism refer to those Jews who rejected Christ and held on to the Jewish Law.

  * The Jewish men who were of the uncircumcism were hostile to the Apostles and to the Gospel.
  * When used figuratively the term uncircumcised refers to those whose hearts are hardened toward the grace of God.
