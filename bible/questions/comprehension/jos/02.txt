====== Joshua Chapter 02 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Where did the two spys from Shittim sent by Joshua find lodging?

A. The two spys from Shittiim sent by Joshua found lodging at the house ofa prostitute named Rahab. [2:1]

Q? What did Rahab do with the spys when the King's men came looking for them?

A. When the King' men came to Rahab looking for the spys, she had hid them. [2:4]

Q? What did Rahab say to the King's men?

A. Rahab said to the King's men that the spies had been there but had already left. [2:5]

Q? Why did Rahab say to the spys that she had hid them.

A. Rahab said to the spys that she had hid them because she believed that Yahweh had given them the land. [2:9]
