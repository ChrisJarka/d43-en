===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Repay no one evil for evil** - Here the word "repay" means to seek revenge. Alternate translation: "Do not do evil things to someone because he has done evil things to you."
  * **Do good things in the sight of all people** - The phrase "in the sight of" does not mean do good things where so that everyone sees you. It refers to the way people think about things. Alternate translation: "Do things that everyone considers to be good."
  * **As much as it lies with you, be at peace with all people** - This phrase "as much as it lies with you" refers to what a person can control or what he is responsible for. Alternate translation: "do whatever you can to live in peace with everyone."
