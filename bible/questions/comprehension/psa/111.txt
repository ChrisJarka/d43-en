====== Psalms Chapter 111 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? When the writer says he will give thanks to Yahweh with his whole heart, where does he say he will do that?

A. "I will give thanks... in the assembly of the upright, in their gathering." [111:1]

Q? When the writer says, "He does wonderful things that will be remembered," what does he say Yahweh is like? 

A. "Yahweh is gracious and merciful." [111:4]

Q? What will Yahweh always call to mind?

A. "He will always call to mind his covenant." [111:5]

Q? All Yahweh's instructions are reliable and established forever.  How are they to be observed? 

A. All his instructions are... "to be observed faithfully and properly." [111:8]

Q? After the writer says that Yahweh gave victory to his people and ordained his covenant forever, what does he say about Yahweh's name?

A. "Holy and awesome is his name." [111:9]

Q? What is the beginning of wisdom?

A. "To honor Yahweh is the beginning of wisdom." [111:10]

Q? What do those who carry out his instructions have?

A. "Those who carry out his instructions have good understanding." [111:10]
