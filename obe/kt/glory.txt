====== glory, glorious ======

===== Definition: =====

In general, the term "glory" means honor, praise and splendor; it also can refer to extreme greatness. Anything that has glory is said to be "glorious."

  * In the Old Testament, glory has the idea of something being weighty, unchanging, and valuable.
  * In the New Testament, glory communicates splendor, brightness, and judgment.
  * Glory is especially used to describe God who is more glorious than anyone or anything in the universe. Everything in his character reveals his glory and his splendor.

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * "Glory" can be translated as "honor," "praise," or "worship."
