===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Bow down to Yahweh** - The implied information is that the people were to bow down in worship. AT: "Bow down to worship Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]]) 
  * **in the splendor of holiness** - The abstract nouns "splendor" and "holiness" can be translated as adjectives. AT: "because he is gloriously beautiful and holy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **tremble** - to shake because of fear
  * **all the earth** - Here "earth" represents the people. AT: "all the people of the earth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **The world also is established** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "He also established the world" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **it cannot be shaken** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "nothing can shake it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
