====== Nahor ======

===== Facts: =====

Nahor was the name of two men in Genesis.  The first Nahor was the grandfather of Abraham.  The second Nahor was the brother of Abraham.

  * Isaac's wife Rebekah was the daughter of Nahor.
  * Rebekah was from a region close to the city of Nahor, which was probably named after her great grandfather Nahor.
