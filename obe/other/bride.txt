====== bride ======

===== Definition: =====

A woman who is being married and is presented to her husband, the groom.

  * The Bible records several instances of marriage where brides are presented to their bridegrooms.
  * The New Testament metaphorically calls Christ's church "His bride" or "the bride of Christ" because we will be joined to Him for eternity.
