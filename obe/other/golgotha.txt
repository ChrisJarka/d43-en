====== Golgotha ======

===== Facts: =====

The term "Golgotha" is the name of the place where Jesus was crucified. <font inherit/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Originally, it was an Aramaic word meaning "Skull" or "Place of the Skull."</font>

  * Golgotha was located outside the city of Jerusalem, perhaps on a part of the Mount of Olives facing the city.
  * In some older English versions of the Bible, Golgotha is translated as "Calvary," which comes from the word for skull in the ancient language of Latin.
