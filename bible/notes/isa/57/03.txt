===== translationNotes: =====

  * **But come here** - God is calling all of the unfaithful people to come before him so he can judge them.
  * **sons of the sorceress** - "people who practice sorcery" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **children of the adulterer and the woman who has prostituted herself** - "people who commit adultery and who act like prostitutes." These words are metaphors for people who worship have stopped being faithful to God and are now worshiping idols. AT: "people who are unfaithful to Yahweh and who worship other gods." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Whom are you merrily mocking? Against whom are you opening ...?** - Yahweh uses questions to scold the people who are mocking him through false worship. AT: "I am the one you are mocking. I am the one you are making fun of by opening ...!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Are you not children of rebellion, children of deceit?** - Yahweh uses another question to scold the people. AT: "You are children of rebellion, children of deceit!" or "You are rebellious, deceitful children!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
