===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he** - This pronoun refers to the man with the measuring stick doing the measuring.
  * **inner court ** - The temple had an outer and inner courtyard.
  * **its** - This pronoun refers to the gate to the inner courtyard on the east side.
  * **niches** - A niche is a recess in a wall. 
  * **porches** - A porch is a covered approach in this case to the inner courtyard.
  * **cubits** - See how you translated these "long" cubits in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:05|40:05]].
  * **fifty cubits** - About one meters. (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaldistance]])
  * **twenty-five cubits** - About 31.5 meters.
