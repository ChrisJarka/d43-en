====== Twelve Tribes of Israel ======

===== Definition: =====

The Twelve Tribes of Israel refers to the twelve sons, and their descendants, of Jacob.

  * Jacob was the grandson of Abraham, and God changed his name to Israel. The names are synonymous.
  * When the people of Israel took possession of the land of Israel, it was divided between these twelve sons, or tribes.
