====== Deuteronomy Chapter 26 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What must the people of Israel do when they possess the land which Yahweh gives them?

A. The people of Israel must take some of the first of all the harvest of the land in a basket and go to the place Yahweh has chosen as his sanctuary. [26:1-2]

Q? What must the people of Israel say to the priest when they give him the basket?

A. The people of Israel must say to him, "I acknowledge today to Yahweh that I have come to the land that he swore to give to our ancestors." [26:3]

Q? What must the people of Israel say when the priest sets their basket down before the altar of Yahweh?

A. The people of Israel must say to Yahweh, "The Egyptians treated us as slaves after we became a great nation, but you saw our affliction and brought us out of Egypt to this land so now we have brought the first of the harvest to you." [26:4-10]
