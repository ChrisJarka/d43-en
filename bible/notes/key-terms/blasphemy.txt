====== blasphemy, blaspheme ======


===== Definition: =====


Blaspheme is to speak disrespectfully, dishonorably, slanderously, or degradingly. Blasphemy is a direct attack upon God.


    * In English, "blasphemy" is used exclusively to mean an attack on God by speaking disrespectfully to God or about God.
