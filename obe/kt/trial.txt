====== trial ======

===== Definition: =====

A judicial hearing where evidence is presented before a judge or jury, and an individual is found, based on the evidence, to be innocent or guilty. Trials can also be situations that prove to be difficult to endure, in which an individual is either found to be worthy or unworthy based on how they respond in the situation.

* Job was tried by Satan and found to be a just man.

* Shadrach, Meshach, and Abednego were tried in the furnace, and were not harmed by the flames.
