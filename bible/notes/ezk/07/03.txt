===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you...your**  - The words "you" and "your" refer to the people of Israel
  * **I...my**  - The words "I" and "my" refer to God
  * **I will bring all your abominations upon you**  - "I will make you experience the effects of your disgusting behavior"
  * **For my eyes will not look compassionately at you**  - God is using his eyes to represent himself. "For I will not look compassionately at you" (See: [[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:synecdoche]])
  * ** I will bring your ways upon you, and your abominations will be in your midst**  - I will make you experience the effects of your ways, while you are doing your wicked actions."
