===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **proclaimed the gospel** - The word "proclaimed" could be translated as "announced" or "declared". 
    * **the gospel and his peace** - "the gospel of peace"
    * **those who were far away** - This refers to the Gentiles or non-Jews. (UDB)
    * **those who were near** - This refers to the Jews. (UDB)
    * **For through Jesus we both have access in one Spirit to the Father** - The word "both" is referring to the Jews and the non-Jews. The word "access" indicates a person is given the right to enter into the presence of and speak to an important person, in this case God the Father. (UDB)
