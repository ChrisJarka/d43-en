===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus concludes his response to the Pharisees and scribes.
  * **General Information:** - In verses 8 and 9, Jesus quotes the prophet Isaiah to rebuke the Pharisees and scribes.

  * **well did Isaiah prophesy** - "Isaiah told the truth in this prophecy"
  * **when he said** - "when he told what God said"
  * **This people honors me with their lips** - "Lips" stand for speaking. "These people say all the right things" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **but their heart is far from me** - AT: "but they do not really love me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **They worship me in vain** - "Their worship has no effect on me" or "They only pretend to worship me"
  * **the commands of people** - "rules that people make up"
