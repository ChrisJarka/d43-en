====== alms, charity ======

===== Definition: =====

"Alms" are an offering that is given to those who are poor or to those who have particular needs.

  * Alms may be given in the form of physical charity, spiritual giving or hospitality.
  * To give "alms" is the exercising of righteousness, a recognition of blessedness, and brings reward for those who practice it.
  * Jesus warned about using giving alms to show off.

(See also: other)
