====== mighty ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "mighty" describes something or someone who is powerful, strong, or great in size or influence.

  * Men who are mighty are strong, brave or heroic.
  * In the Old Testament, "mighty" often describes someone who is successful in battle.
  * It also can refer to being effective and successful, as in the description of Moses being "mighty in word and deed" in Acts 7.
  * The name “Almighty God” not only means that God is powerful, but also conveys the idea that he is the all-sufficient one.
