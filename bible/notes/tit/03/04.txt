===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Paul reminds Titus to teach humbly because we also did not deserve mercy.)
  * **when the kindness of God our Savior and his love towards mankind appeared**  - This could be translated as "when God our Savior displayed his love and kindness towards mankind."
  * **kindness**  - "affection"
  * **toward mankind**- "for humans"
  * **saved us through** - "saved us by means of" or "saved us by"
  * **washing of new birth**  - This could be translated as "cleansing us inwardly by our spiritual new birth."
  * **renewal**  - "being made new." This could be translated as "the Holy Spirit made us new" or "The Holy Spirit made us new people" or "The Holy Spirit caused us to be born again spiritually and made us new people."
  * **according to**  - "because of "
