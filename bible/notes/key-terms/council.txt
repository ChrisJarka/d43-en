====== council ======

===== Definition: =====

A council is a group of people who are called together to discuss and give advice regarding a specific issue and to make decisions about important matters.

  * The Jewish Council was also known as the Sanhedrin and its members were Pharisees and Sadducees.
  * Paul was brought before a Roman Council
