====== Song of Solomon Chapter 06 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who is speaking to the young woman?

A. The women of Jerusalem are speaking to the young woman. [6:1]

Q? What questions do the young women ask of the young woman. 

A. Where has your beloved gone and in what direction has your beloved gone? [6:1]

Q? What do the young women want to do?

A. The young women want to seek for the young woman's beloved. [6:1]
