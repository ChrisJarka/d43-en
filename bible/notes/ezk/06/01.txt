===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **set your face**  - explanation
  * **Thus says the Lord**  - This is a common way for a prophet to introduce what the Lord has said.
  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh to the mountains**  - The prophet is speaking to the places in Israel as if they could hear.  The message is meant to be understood by the people of Israel but is spoken to the mountains to make the message more powerful. (See: [[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:personification|Personification]])
  * **Behold**  - The word “behold” here alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows.
  * ** I am bringing a sword**  - This means that God is going to use war to punish Israel. (See: [[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:metonymy|Metonymy]])
