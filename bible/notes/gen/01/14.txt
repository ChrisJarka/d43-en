===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **lights in the sky**  - This refers to the sun, moon, and stars. It can be translated as "things that shine in the sky" or "things that give light in the sky."
  * **in the sky**  - "in the expanse of the sky" or "in the large space of the sky"
  * **let them be as signs** - Let them serve as signs" or "let them show"
  * **seasons** - "Seasons" refers to appointed times for festivals and other things that people do.
  *  **for seasons, for days and years**  - Possible meanings are 1) The days and years are those of the appointed times or 2) This refers to days and years in general. The first meaning can be translated as "the days and years of the appointed times."
  * **to give light upon the earth**  - "to shine light on the earth" or "to brighten the earth"
  * **It was so**  - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:01:06|1:7]].
