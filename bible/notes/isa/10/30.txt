===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **daughter of Gallim**- The singular word "daughter" is referring to all the people.
  * **Gallim...Laishah...Anathoth...Madmenah...Gebim...Nob**- These are names of cities. (See: How to Translate Names)
  * **he will halt..his fist**- Here "he" refers to the soldiers of Assyria.
  * **the mount of the daughter of Zion**- This phrase means the people on the mount of Zion.
