====== anoint, anointed ======


===== Definition: =====


Rub or pour oil on a person as part of a ceremony to honor them or dedicate the commencement of their terms of service.


    * In the OT, kings and priests were anointed at the beginning of their term of service.
    * Ointment was also used to anoint someone.
