===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You have said** -  The word "You" is  refers to the nation of Edom.
  * **These two nations and these two lands will become mine** - Edom wanted to rule over the kingdom of Israel and the kingdom of Judah. Edom also wanted to own their land. (See: [[en:obe:other:kingdomofisrael]] and [[en:obe:other:kingdomofjudah]])


  * **As I live** - This is a idiomatic statement declaring the self sufficiency of Yahweh.
  * **this is the Lord Yahweh’s declaration**  - See how you translated this in 5:11.
  * **I will make it known among them**  - "Edom will be destroyed and know that God has judged them"
