====== chosen one ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Bible, the term "chosen one" usually refers to Jesus as the one whom God appointed to be the Messiah.

  * God also refers to his people as being chosen by him. Certain Israelite kings and other leaders were called God's "chosen one."
  * To be chosen is to be selected or appointed for a special purpose.

(See also: Christ, Messiah, elect)
