====== Checking Notes and Links ======

Created especially for those checking the formatting of or proofreading Translation Notes, this page gives examples of how various notes can and/or should be written, including some standard explanations for alternate translations.

You can search the page for a topic using (Ctrl+F) for a PC or (Cmd+F) on a Mac.


==== Links ====

We suggest that as you check the notes, you also open the following helpful pages in new tabs in your browser:

  * [[:en:phase1:team-info:training:reviewers:talinks]] - contains links to TA pages in code blocks that can be safely pasted into Door43.
  * [[:en:phase1:team-info:training:quick-reference:quick-reference-notes]] - contains brief definitions of figures of speech, and links to standard notes pages.
