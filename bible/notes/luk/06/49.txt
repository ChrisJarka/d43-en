===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus finishes teaching the crowd. This is the end of this part of the story.

  * **a man who built a house** - This metaphor compares a man who builds a house without a foundation to a person who does not obey the teachings of Jesus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **without a foundation** - AT: "but he did not dig down and build first a foundation" 
  * **foundation** - "base" or "firm support" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **torrent of water** - "fast-moving water" or "river" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **flowed against** - "crashed against" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **collapsed** - "fell down" or "came apart" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
