===== Translation Notes: =====
Nahum continues to describe the power of Yahweh over the whole world

    * **Bashan languishes, and Carmel also; the flowers of Lebanon languish**  - The word "languishes" means "weakens" or "fades."
    * **Bashan** - This is the name of an area to the east of the Sea of Galilee in Israel.  It's name means fruitful because of it's forests and farms. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames]])
    * **Carmel** - A city on Mount Carmel, west of the Sea of Galillee, near the coast of Israel.  It's name means "full of corn."(See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames]])
    * **Lebanon** - This is the region around Mount Lebanon.  It is known for it's forests, stone, flowers and wine.  It's name means "white one" and is probably a reference to the year round snow on top of Mount Lebanon. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translatenames]])
    * **The mountains shake; the hills melt; the earth collapses**  - The term "shake" refers to someone shaking in fear.  The term "melt" refer to someone's courage melting away like snow on a hot day.  The term "collapses" refers to someone who falls to the ground in fear.  These phrases are used to show how powerful God is.  Even the mountains and hills are afraid of his power. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:personification]]) (See: [[:en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
