====== 2 Corinthians 04 General Notes ======

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

**Paul spreads the Gospel**

Paul has the job of telling people about Christ. He does not use trickery to get them to believe. If they do not understand it is because Satan is blocking their understanding because God is giving people understanding. The Paul and his companions are in constant danger and trouble but they continue to spread the gospel. They are not discouraged because they are expecting a great reward from Christ.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

**Idiom**

“in the sight of God” is an [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] that means God is our witness, “the god of this world” means Satan, “the image of God” means the same as God.

**Metaphor**

There are many [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]s in this chapter:  “Light” means understanding, “Veiled’ means the meaning is hidden. “treasure in pots of clay” means the Gospel in a weak and fragile human being.

**Hyperbole**

“handed over to death” is a [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]] meaning in danger of being killed.

**Irony**
 
Paul is using [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_irony]] to shame the pride of the Corinthians.  The Corinthian believers are reigning but the apostles are suffering.
