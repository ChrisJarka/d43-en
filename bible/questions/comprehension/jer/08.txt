====== Jeremiah Chapter 08 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What does Yahweh say will happen at that time?

A. They will bring out from the grave the bones of the kings of Judah and its officials, the bones of the priests and the prophets, and the bones of the inhabitants of Jerusalem. [1:1]

Q? What will the bones be like when they are spread out in the light of the sun and the moon?

A. They will be like dung on the surface of the earth. [1:2]

Q? What will they do in every remaining place where Yahweh has driven them?

A. They will choose death instead of life for all those who are still left over from this evil nation. [1:3]

Q? To what do they hold on?

A. They hold on to treachery and refuse to repent. [1:4]

Q? How did the people feel about their wickedness?

A. No one was sorry for his wickedness. [1:6]

Q? In contrast to the animals who go on thier migrations at the right times, what do Yahweh's people not know?

A. His people do not know Yahweh's decress. [1:7]

Q? What has the deceitful pen of the scribes done?

A. It has created deceit. [1:8]

Q? What will Yahweh do with their wives and their fields?

A. He will give their wives to others and their fields to those who will possess them. [1:10]

Q? Were they ashamed when they practiced abominations?

A. They were not ashamed; they had no humility. [1:12]

Q? According to Yahweh's declaration, what will happen when Yahweh removes them completely?

A. There will be no grapes on the vine, nor will there be figs on the fig tree, for the leaf will wither, and what he has given to them will pass away. [1:13]

Q? How will Yahweh silence them? He will make them drink poison, since they have sinned against him. [1:14]

A. What will there be even though they are hoping for a time of healing?

Q? There will be terror. [1:15]

A. What will Yahweh's strong horses do?

Q? They will come and consume the land and its wealth, the city and the ones living in it. [1:16]

A. What is Yahweh's declaration about snakes?

Q? He is sending out snakes among them, vipers that they cannot charm, who will bite them. [1:17]

A. How does he describe his sorrow?

Q? His sorrow has no end, and his heart is sick. [1:18]

A. What has not happened even though the harvest is passed and the summer is over?

Q? They have not been saved. [1:20]

A. Why is Yahweh hurt?

Q? He is hurt because of the hurt of the daughter of his people; he mourns at the horrible things that have happened to her. [1:21]
