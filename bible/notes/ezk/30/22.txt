===== Translation Notes:- explanation =====

  * **Therefore** - "So" or "Because of this"
  * **thus says the Lord Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:03:10|3:11]].
  * **For I will break his arms** - "I will break both of his arms." The arms represent the power of Pharaoh. Alternate translation: "I will make Pharaoh lose all of his power." (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
  * **make the sword fall from his hand **- "make him lose power". (See:Metaphor)
  * **Then I will scatter Egypt among the nations and disperse them among the lands** - This says the same thing in two different ways to emphasize that Yahweh will punish the Egyptians very badly. Alternate translation: "Then I will certainly make the Egyptians leave Egypt and go to other countries. (See: [[en:ta:translate:doublet]]) 
  * **lands** - "countries"
  * **groan** - This is a noise that is made when one is in pain or dying.
