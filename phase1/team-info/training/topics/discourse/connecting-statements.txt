====== Connecting Statement & General Statement ======

  * [[:en:phase1:team-info:training:topics:discourse:home|Back to Discourse Information]]


=== Purpose ===

The Connecting Statement & General Statement are to address discourse level text issues. These include:
  * Participant references (pronoun identification)
  * Highlighting background or implied information. Including information that was moved to the background of the text.
  * Logical arguments and conclusions
  * Episode Identification (start, end, and continuation across chapters) 

  * **Connecting Statement:** - This is a note that relates the current chunk to the current discourse. When present, it must be the first note and separated by a blank line from the rest of the notes unless there is also a general statement. There is only one connecting statement used per chunk.
  * **General Statement:** - This is a note that addresses other discourse topics not mentioned in the connecting statement. When present, it must must immediately follow the connecting statement and be separated by a blank line from the rest of the notes. There can be one or more general statements in a chunk.

=== Format ===

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Explanation
  * **General Statement:** - Explanation

  * Follow Standard Punctuation. Phrases do not use periods. Sentences do.
  * Follow Standard formatting for ta-topic links. Ex. (See: Metaphor_link)
  * Where reasonable express connecting sentence in present tense.
