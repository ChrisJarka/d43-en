===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Let us -**  "us" refers to all true believers.
  * **as many as are mature -**  "as many as are strong in their faith"
  * **think like that - **"agree with me" Paul desires that his fellow believers would also strive to know Christ, to become more like Christ and to be willing to suffer and die for Christ.
  * **you -**  refers to those who think differently or disagree with Paul
  * **we must walk in an orderly fashion according to it -**  "we must obey whatever truth God has given to us"
