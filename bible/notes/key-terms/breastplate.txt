====== breastplate, breastpiece ======

===== Definition: =====

A breastplate is a piece of armor that a soldier wears across his chest to protect his heart during battle.

  * Breastplates could be made of wood, metal, or leather. They were intended to stop arrows, spears, or swords from piercing the chest of the wearer.
  * In the Old Testament, a breastplate was an article of clothing worn by the high priest or head priest, when he was performing his duties of service to God and the people.

(See also: priest)
