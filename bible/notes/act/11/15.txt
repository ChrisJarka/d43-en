===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Peter continues to speak.)
  * **the Holy Spirit came on them **- The Holy Spirit came to them and controlled them
  * **you shall be baptized **- God will baptize you (see active or passive)
  * **On us in the beginning - **"us" refer to the Jewish believers.  "In the beginning" refers to the Holy Spirit coming at Pentecost.  The believers who went with Peter to see Cornelius were Jewish, and the believers who are questioning Peter in this chapter are Jewish also.
