====== Inheritance Customs ======


In Jewish history, lands passed from fathers to sons and the eldest son receiving twice as much as each of his brothers.  Except in Joseph's case , Jacob left a double portion to Joseph which was given to his sons Ephraim and Manasseh.  Genesis 48 and 49.
