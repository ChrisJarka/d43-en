====== 1 Samuel 28 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Talking to the dead ==

Talking to the dead was against the [[:en:obe:kt:lawofmoses|Law of Moses]]. God or the Holy Spirit came to leave Saul because of his [[:en:obe:kt:sin|sin]] and would not help him. 

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Rhetorical question ==
Samuel use a [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|rhetorical question]] to say it was useless to ask his advice since there was no way Saul could be successful with God against him:  "What then do you ask me, since Yahweh has left you, and he has become your enemy?"
