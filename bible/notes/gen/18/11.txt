===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **bold words**  - explanation
  * **will I have this pleasure**  - The means "experiencing the joy [of having children]."
  * **my lord being old also**  - This means "since my husband is also old."
  * **my lord**  - This is a title of respect that Sarah gives to her husband Abraham.
