===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he cried in my hearing**  - The phrase "in my hearing" means that he was close enough to be heard.
  * **each with his weapon of destruction**  - This means the guards had different weapons.
  * **Behold**  - The word "behold" here adds emphasis to what follows. It can also be translated as "Indeed."
  * **weapon of slaughter**  - "tool used for violent death"
  * **a man in their midst**  - a man who was in the middle of the group
  * **linen**  - The word "linen" is a strong, smooth cloth made from a plant.
  * **scribe's equipment**  - pen and paper to use for writing
  * **bronze**  - brown metal.
