====== sanctify, sanctification ======


===== Definition: =====


To sanctify is to set apart or to make holy. Sanctification is the process of being set apart.


    * In the Old Testament certain days, people, and objects were set apart for service to God.
    * In the church, God sanctifies believers to Himself. Believers are also told to sanctify themselves to God.


(See also: [[:en:bible:notes:key-terms:holy|holy]])
