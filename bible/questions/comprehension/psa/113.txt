====== Psalms Chapter 113 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? The writer begins by calling people to praise and worship of Yahweh.  When does he say this is to be done?

A. "Blessed be the name of Yahweh, both now and forevermore." [113:2]

Q? The writer says, "From the rising of the sun to its setting, Yahweh's name should be praised." Where does he say that Yahweh is exalted and his glory reaches?

A. "Yahweh is exalted above all nations, and his glory reaches above the skies." [113:4]

Q? When the writer says that Yahweh our God has his seat on high, what does he say Yahweh looks down at?

A. Yahweh, "...looks down at the sky and at the earth." [113:6]
