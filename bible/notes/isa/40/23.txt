===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He reduces rulers to nothing and makes the rulers of the earth insignificant **- "He" here is God.  "God reduces the rulers to nothing, making them worth nothing."
  * **they are barely planted...they are barely sown...they have barely taken root in the earth **- The rulers are being compared to a seed or seedling that is not placed firmly in the ground.
