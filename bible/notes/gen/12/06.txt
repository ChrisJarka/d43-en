===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **Abraham passed through the land** - Abraham took his family with him. This can also be translated as "So Abraham and his family went through the land." Only Abraham's name is mentioned because he was the head of the family, and God had given him the command to take his family a and go.  
  * * **the land** - This can be translated as "the land of Canaan."
  * **Shechem** - This can be translated as "the town of Shechem."
  * **the oak of Moreh**  - This could also be translated as "the teacher's oak tree."
  * **Yahweh, who had appeared to him**  - This can be translated as "Yahweh, because he had appeared to him."
