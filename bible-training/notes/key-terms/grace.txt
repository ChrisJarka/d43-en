====== grace, favor ======


===== Definition: =====


Grace is unearned favor. Favor is to hold someone in high regard.  While these two English words are distinct, they are translated from one Greek word.


    * Grace is shown through acts of kindness without expectation of being paid back.
    * Grace can refer to God's willingness to show kindness to someone or to the actual act of showing kindness to someone.
    * God gives his grace freely. It is not something that people can earn.
    * Grace can also be translated as: kindness, favor, benevolence or good will.
    * It is also an attribute of God.
