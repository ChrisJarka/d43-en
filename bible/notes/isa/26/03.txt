===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the mind that is stayed on you** - "the man whose thoughts are fixed on Yahweh by faith"
  * **for in Yah, Yahweh, is an everlasting rock** - Yah is another name for Yahweh.  This can be translated "for in Yah, Yahweh, is the rock of ages." Just like a huge rock, Yahweh will keep his people safe. (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
