===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They went out** - AT: "The Israelite army left Samaria"
  * **drinking himself drunk** - (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_rpronouns]])
  * **thirty-two **- "32" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **went forward first** - AT: "left Samaria first"
  * ** Benhadad was informed by scouts...** - Scouts informed Benhadad that men were coming out of Samaria. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations]])
  * **scouts **- soldiers sent to get information about the enemy's location, size, and equipment
