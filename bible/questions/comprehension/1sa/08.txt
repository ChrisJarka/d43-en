====== 1 Samuel Chapter 08 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? In what ways did the two sons of Samuel, who were judges, not walk in the ways of their father?

A. The two sons of Samuel chased after dishonest gain, taking bribes and perverting justice. [8:1-3]

Q? What was the request which the elders of Israel made of Samuel?

A. The elders of Israel told Samuel that since you are old and your sons do not walk in your ways, now appoint for us a king to judge us like all the nations. [8:4-5]

Q? What did Samuel do when he was displeased by the request from the elders of Israel to give them a king?

A. Samuel was displeased by the request so he prayed to Yahweh. [8:6]

Q? Why did Yahweh tell Samuel to obey the voice of the people in everything they would say to him?

A. Yahweh told Samuel to obey the voice of the people because they were not rejecting him, but they were rejected Yahweh from being king over them. [8:7]
