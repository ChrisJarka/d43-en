===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to tell his apostles about the persecution they will endure as they work for him.

  * **When they deliver you up ** - "When those people deliver you up"
  * **deliver you up ** - "betray you" or "have you arrested"
  * **you** - The pronoun "you" throughout this passage refers to the twelve apostles.
  * **do not be anxious ** - "do not worry"
  * **how or what you will speak ** - This means "how you are to speak or what you are to say." (See: [[en:ta:translate:Hendiadys]])
  * **for what to say will be given to you** - This could be written as "for God will give you what to say" or "for the Holy Spirit will give you what to say." (See: [[en:ta:translate:Active]])
  * **in that hour** - "at that time"
  * **the Spirit of your Father** - If necessary, this can be translated "the Spirit of God your heavenly Father" or a footnote added to the text to make clear that this refers to God the Holy Spirit and not to the spirit of an earthly father.
  * **in you ** - "through you"
