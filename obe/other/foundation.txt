====== foundation ======

===== Definition: =====

The foundation is the base upon which something is built; a sturdy and lasting support for what is being constructed.

  * Foundation can refer to the beginning as in Matthew 13:35.
  * Christ's church is said to be built upon the foundation of the Apostles and Prophets, with Christ Himself being the cornerstone.
