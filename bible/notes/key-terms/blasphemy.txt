====== blasphemy, blaspheme ======


===== Definition: =====


Blaspheme is to speak disrespectfully, dishonor, slander or degrade. Blasphemy is the result of the action to blaspheme.


    * In English, "blasphemy" is used exclusively to mean "to speak disrespectfully to God."
