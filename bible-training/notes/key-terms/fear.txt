====== fear ======


===== Definition: =====


When used in a Biblical context, the word fear refers to a proper respect for God; similar to the concept of awe


    * In Exodus 20 Moses told the people to fear God so that they would not sin
    * In Proverbs 9 we are told that wisdom starts with the fear (or respect) of God
