===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **fire of Yahweh fell** - It is implied that the fire came from above. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
    * **licked up** - The fire is compared to a thirsty person or animal lapping or drinking water (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
    * **consumed the burnt offering, as well as the wood, the stones, and the dust** - It is implied that everything that the fire consumed was wet. (See [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
    * **Yahweh, he is God! Yahweh, he is God!** - This is repeated for emphasis and shows the people were convinced that Yahweh was God.
