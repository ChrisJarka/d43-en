====== Habakkuk ======

===== Facts: =====

Habakkuk is one of the twelve "minor prophets" of the Old Testament.

  * Habakkuk prophesies mostly about the Chaldeans (Babylonians) and is an early contemporary of Jeremiah.
  * One of Habakkuk's most influential statements is "the just shall live by his faith".
