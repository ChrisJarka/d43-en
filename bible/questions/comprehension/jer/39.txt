====== Jeremiah Chapter 39 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who came with his army against Jerusalem and besieged it?

A. Nebuchadnezzar king of Babyton came with all his army against Jerusalem and besieged it. [39:1]

Q? What happened to the city of Jerusalem in the eleventh year of Zedekiah and on the ninth day of the month?

A. The city of Jerusalem was broken into in the eleventh year of Zedekiah and on the ninth day of the month. [39:2]

Q? What officials of the king of Babylon came and sat in the middle gate?

A. All the officials of the king of Babylon came and sat in the middle gate. [39:3]
