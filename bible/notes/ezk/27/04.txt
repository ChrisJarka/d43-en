===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **borders are in the heart of the seas**  - Tyre's city borders as a port in Lebanon also included maritime borders far out into the sea.
  * **builders have perfected your beauty **- The city of Tyre had a natural beauty that was made even more beautiful by the builders with building materials from Senir and Lebanon, known for their high quality.
