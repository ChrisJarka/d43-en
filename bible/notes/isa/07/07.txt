===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **head of Syria** - This refers to the capital city, Damascus.
  * **Syria..Damascus…Rezin…Ephraim..Samaria…Remaliah** - These are names. (See: [[en:ta:translate:translatenames]]) See how you translated many of these in 7:1
  * **head of Damascus** - This refers to the king of Syria, Rezin.
  * **sixty-five years** - This is an amount of time – 65 years. (See: How to Translate Numbers)
  * **head of Ephraim** - This refers to Samaria, the capital city of the northern kingdom of Israel.
  * **head of Samaria** - This refers to the king of Samaria, Pekah.
  * **you ** - This refers to Ahaz, king of Judah.
