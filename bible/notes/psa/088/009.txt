===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **My eyes grow weary from trouble;**  - "My eyes grow tired from trouble;"
  * **I spread out my hands to you.**  - This is an action that shows total dependence. "I lift up my hands to you." 
  * **Will you do wonders for the dead?**  - "Will you do surprising things for the dead?" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Will you do wonders for the dead? Will those who have died rise and praise you? **- (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
