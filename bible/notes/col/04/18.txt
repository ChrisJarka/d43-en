===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **This greeting is with my own hand** - Paul wanted the Colossian believers to know that this letter was from him. "Greeting" here stands for Paul's words of greetings to the believers. AT: "I myself have written this phrase of greeting" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]
  * **Remember my chains** - Paul speaks of chains when he means his imprisonment. AT: "Remember me and pray for me while I am in prison." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **May grace be with you** - "Grace" here stands for God, who shows grace or acts kindly to believers. AT: "I pray that our Lord Jesus Christ would continue to act graciously toward you all" (UDB)  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
