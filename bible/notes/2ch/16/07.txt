===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Hanani** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **out of your hand** - Here the king's "hand" refers to his control. This means that his army was unable to conquer the king of Aram's army. AT: "from you" or "from your control" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Were not the Ethiopians and the Libyans a huge army, with very many chariots and horsemen?** - Hanani uses this rhetorical question to remind Asa of a previous victory. This can be written as a statement. AT: "The Ethiopians and the Libyans were a huge army, with very many chariots and horsemen." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Libyans** - These are people from Libya. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **he gave you victory** - This means that king Asa's army was able to defeat the opposing army. AT: "he gave your army victory over them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
