===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **bore for me - **  Alternate translation: "gave birth to" or "committed to me" (UDB). The "me" in this phrase is Yahweh.
  * **Is your whoredoms a small matter? - **  This question is found in the UDB phrase: "as though they were less important than your becoming a prostitute."
  * **and delivered them up to make them pass through the fire to the images**  - This phrase may be found in the following UDB phrase: "offered them to be sacrifices to these false gods!"
