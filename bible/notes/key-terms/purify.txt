====== purify ======


===== Definition: =====


To cause a substance or an item to be pure; free from adulteration, contaminates, impurities or pollutants. In a spiritual sense to purify a person is to cleanse a person from their sin.


In the Bible, believers are purified from sin by Jesus's sacrifice.


    * In the Old Testament, the blood of an animal was used for purification.
    * In the Old Testament, certain rituals were performed with water and ashes to ceremonially purify a priest for duty.
    * Precious metals can be purified with high temperatures.
