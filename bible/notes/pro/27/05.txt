===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Better is an open rebuke** - "It is better to be openly rebuked"
  * **than hidden love** - "than love that is not openly shown." The word "love" can be translated as a verbal phrase. AT: "than to be loved secretly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **Faithful are the wounds caused by a friend** - AT: 
  * **Faithful are the wounds caused by a friend** - "The wounds from a friend are trustworthy." The word "wounds" refers to the pain caused by rebuke or correction. This can be stated in active form. AT: "A friend wounds caused by a friend's rebuke" or "the wounds of a friend's correction" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **profusely** - "abundantly" or "too much"
