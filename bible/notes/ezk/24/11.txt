===== Important Terms: =====

  * bronze
  * uncleanliness (unclean)
  * fire

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then set it on its coals empty **- Alternate Translation ;Then set the empty pot on the coals" (UDB)
  * **bronze**- a hard piece of metal that is a yellowish brown colour.Alternate Translation : copper (UDB).
  * **its**  - This refers to the pot.
  * **its uncleanliness **  - This means 'unclean' or 'unfit'.
  * **She- **  is a pronoun referring to Jerusalem.
  * **become weary because of toil **- This means become tired because hard work.
