====== 2 Chronicles Chapter 18 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? How did Jehoshaphat ally himself with Ahab?

A. He allied himself with Ahab by having one of his family marry his daughter. [18:1]

Q? What did Jehoshaphat tell the king of Israel to do to find his answer?

A. He said to the king of Israel, "Please first seek the word of Yahweh for your answer." [18:4]

Q? Why did the King of Israel hate Micaiah the son of Imlah?

A. He hated him because he never prophesied anything good about the king, but only hardships. [18:7]

Q? Who did the prophets say had given Ramoth Gilead into the hand of the king?

A. They all prophesied saying that Yahweh had given it into the hand of the king. [18:11]

Q? What did the messenger tell Micaiah that the words of the prophets declared to the king?

A. He told him that the words of the prophets declare good things to the king with one mouth. [18:12]

Q? To what did Micaiah compare Israel when he saw them scattered on the mountains?

A. He said that he saw all Israel scattered on the mountains, like sheep who have no shepherd. [18:16]

Q? When Micaiah saw Yahweh on his throne where were the hosts of heaven?

A. He saw him sitting on his throne, and all the host of heaven were standing on his right hand and on his left. [18:18]

Q? What was Yahweh asking to happen when Ahab went up to Ramoth Gilead?

A. He was asking for one to entice Ahab, king of Israel, so that he may go up and fall at Ramoth Gilead. [18:19]

Q? To whom was the spirit going to become a lying spirit?

A. The spirit said that he would go out and become a lying spirit in the mouth of all Ahab's prophets. [18:21]

Q? What did Micaiah say Yahweh had decreed for the king of Israel?

A. He said that Yahweh had decreed disaster for him. [18:22]

Q? Where was Micaiah to be until the king of Israel returned safely?

A. The king said to put him in prison until he return safely. [18:26]

Q? If the king returned safely, what did that mean about Micaiah's words?

A. Micaiah said that if the king returned safely, then Yahweh has not spoken by him. [18:27]

Q? Who had the king of Aram told the captains to attack?

A. He told the captains of his chariots to not attack the unimportant or the important soldiers. Instead, attack only the king of Israel. [18:30]

Q? What did the commanders do when they realized Jehoshaphat was not the king of Israel?

A. When the commanders saw that he was not the king of Israel, they turned back from pursuing him. [18:32]

Q? What time of day was it when Ahab died?

A. About the time that the sun was going down, he died. [18:34]
