====== Clean / Unclean ======

===== Description =====

The words "clean" and "unclean" are metaphors that show up many times in the Bible. Here are suggested ways to write the notes. You may have to modify them for different contexts.

===== Standard Notes =====


  * **it is clean** - Something that Yahweh has stated is fit to touch or eat is spoken of as if it were physically clean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
