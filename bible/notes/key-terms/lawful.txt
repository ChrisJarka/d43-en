====== lawful ======


===== Definition: This term is an adjective that signifies adherence to the law. It refers to legal conformity: in position such as a "lawfully elected President"; legally permitted activities such as a "legal right to assemble for worship"; sanctioned by the law, such as a "lawful marriage"; and referring to a person who governs his life according to the dictates of the law. =====
