===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **The word of Yahweh came to Jehu** - AT: "Yahweh gave a message to Jehu" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])   
    * **against Baasha** - "to oppose" or "to confront Baasha" AT: "for Baasha" 
    * **saying** - "Yahweh said"
    * **Although I exalted you** - This phrase suggests that even though God did good things for Baasha, Baasha didn't respond appropriately.
    * **I exalted you...and made you leader** - (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
    * **out of the dust** - from a lowly and insignificant position
    * **walked in the way of Jeroboam** - Baasha did bad things like King Jeroboam had done. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
    * **made my people Israel to sin...to provoke me to anger** - (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
    * **bold words**  - explanation
