====== propitiation ======

===== Definition: =====

To turn away wrath, usually by means of an offering.

  * The offering of the sacrificial blood of Jesus Christ is the propitiation to God for mankind's sins.  That is, God's wrath due to the sin of Christian believers is completely removed by Jesus' death on the cross.

(See also: other)
