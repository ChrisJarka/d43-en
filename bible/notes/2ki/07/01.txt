===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **measure**  - (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicalvolume|biblical volume]])
  * **shekel**  - (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicalmoney|biblical money]], [[:en:obe:other:biblicalweight|biblical weight]])
  * **the captain on whose hand the king leaned**  - AT: "The king's assistant" or "An officer who was nearby helping the king"
  * **windows in heaven**  - AT: "openings in the sky for grain to fall through" (See: Explicit and Implicit)
  * **can this thing happen?**  - AT: "I do not believe it could happen!" (See: Rhetorical Question)
  * **you will watch it happen with your own eyes, but you will not eat any of it **- Because the officer did not believe Yawheh could provide the grain, he would be an eye-witness to that happening but would not eat any of it. (See: Synecdoche)
