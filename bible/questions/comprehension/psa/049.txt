====== Psalms Chapter 049 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who does the psalmist tell to hear and give ear?

A. He speaks to all you peoples, all you inhabitants of the world, both low and high, rich and poor together. [49:1-2]

Q? What will his mouth speak?

A. His mouth will speak wisdom. [49:3]

Q? What will be the meditation of his heart?

A. The meditation of his heart will be of understanding. [49:3]

Q? To what will he incline his ear and how will he begin it?

A. He will incline his ear to a parable which he will begin with the harp. [49:4]

Q? What can those who trust in their wealth not do?

A. None of them can by any means redeem his brother nor give God a ransom for him. [49:6-7]

Q? How does the psalmist describe the redemption of one's life?

A. The redemption of one's life is costly and always costs too much. [49:8]

Q? Who can live forever?

A. No one can live forever so that his body should not decay. [49:9]

Q? What happens to wise men, the fool, and the brute alike?

A. Wise men die; the fool and the brute alike perish and leave their wealth to others. [49:10]

Q? What happens to man?

A.  Man, having wealth, does not remain alive. [49:12]

Q? Who will be their shepherd?

A. Death will be their shepherd. [49:14]

Q? What will happen to them in the morning?

A. The upright will have power over them in the morning. [49:14]

Q? What will God do?

A. God will redeem my soul from the power of sheol; he will receive me. [49:15]

Q? When does the psalmist say not to be afraid?

A. Do not be afraid when one becomes rich, when the power of his house increases. [49:16]

Q? Why does the psalmist say not to be afraid?

A. He says not to be afraid because when the rich one dies, he will take nothing away, and his power will not descend with him. [49:16-17]

Q? When do men praise you?

A. Men praise you when you live for yourself. [49:18]

Q? To whom will he go?

A. He will go to the generation of his fathers. [49:19]

Q? What will he and his fathers not see?

A. They will never see the light again. [49:19]

Q? Like what is one who has wealth but no understanding?

A. One who has wealth but no understanding is like the beasts, which perish. [49:20]
