===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **soul from the sword**  - The word "sword" is used here as a picture of both the weapon as well as the enemy using it.
  * **paws of the dogs...lion's mouth...horns of the wild ox** -  These are not referring to actual dogs, lions, or oxen but they are pictures of enemies who want to kill him.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **the lion's mouth** - A lion is a fierce, dangerous cat-like animal.
