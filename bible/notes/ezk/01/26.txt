===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the firmament** – "the dome"
  * **over their heads** – "over the heads of the living creatures"
  * **was the appearance of a throne** - "was something that looked like a throne"
  * **that looked like sapphire** - "and looked like it was made of sapphire
  * **sapphire**  - a very valuable stone that is a clear blue and is very shiny
  * **and on the throne** - It is implied that the person is sitting on the throne. Alternate translation: "and sitting on the thing that looked like a throne." (See: [[en:ta:translate:implicitexplicit]])
  * **was the appearance of one who looked like a man** – "was someone that looked like a man" or "wss someone that looked like person"
