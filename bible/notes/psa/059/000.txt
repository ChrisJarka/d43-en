====== Psalms 059 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 59 is a deliverance psalm. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:deliverer]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Protection ==
The author prayed that God will defeat his enemies. God is his protector. Therefore, he will praise God.
