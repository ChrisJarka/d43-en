====== Psalms Chapter 110 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Where does Yahweh ask David's lord to sit, and for how long?

A. "Sit at my right hand until I make your enemies your footstool." [110:1]

Q? When Yahweh says, "Your people will follow you in sacred garments," what is their attitude of mind when they do this? 

A. "Your people will follow you... of their own free will on the day of your power." [110:3]

Q? What has Yahweh sworn and will not change?

A. "You are a priest forever." [110:4]

Q? When David says that, "The Lord is at your right hand," what will Yahweh do to kings on the day of his anger?

A. "He will kill kings on the day of his anger... he will kill the leaders in many countries." [107:6]

Q? David records that this lord will drink of the brook along the road. Then what will he do? 

A. "Then he will lift his head up high after victory." [110:7]
