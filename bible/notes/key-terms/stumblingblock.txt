====== stumbling block ======

The Greek term "skandalizo" describes a snare or trap that may cause one to fall away or fail.

===== Definition: =====

Text

  * The New Testament meaning is figurative with a moral application:  wrong relationship with Christ or others can lead to spiritual disaster.
  * bullet
