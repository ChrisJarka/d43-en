==== How to Get Answers ====

There are several resources available for finding answers to questions: 

  * **[[en:ta|translationAcademy]]** - This training manual has much information including:
    * Introduction - introduces the unfoldingWord project (see [[en:ta:vol1:intro:toc_intro]])
    * Process Manual - answer the question "what next?" (see [[:en:ta:vol1:process:toc_processvol1_2]]) 
    * Translation Manual - explains the basics of translation theory and practical translation helps (see [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:toc_transvol1_2]])
    * Checking Manual - explains the basics of checking theory and best practices (see [[:en:ta:vol1:checking:toc_checkvol1_2]])
    * Technology Manual - explains how to use the different technology tools (see [[:en:ta:vol1:tech:toc_techvol1_2]])
    * Audio Manual - How to do high quality audio recordings (see [[:en:ta:vol2:audio:toc_audiovol2]])
    * Gateway Languages Manual - explains issues specific to the Gateway Langauges (see [[:en:ta:vol2:gateway:toc_gatewayvol2]])
  * **Slack Chatroom** - Join the unfoldingWord community, post your questions to the "helpdesk" channel, and get real-time answers to your questions (see door43.org/en/slack )
  * **Helpdesk** - email help@door43.org with your questions
