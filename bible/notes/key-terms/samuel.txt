====== Samuel ======

===== Facts: =====

Samuel was a prophet and the last judge of Israel; he anointed both Saul and David as kings over Israel.

  * Samuel was born to Elkanah and Hannah in the town of Ramah.
  * He was conceived as an answer to Hannah's urgent prayer that God would give her a child.
  * She promised to dedicate Samuel to the Lord and sent him as a young boy to help Eli the priest in the temple.
