===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **The time is coming; the day has drawn near**  - Both "The time" and "the day" refer to when God will punish the people of Israel. Alternative translation: "Israel's punishment will happen soon" (See: [[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:doublet]])
    * **the vision is against the entire multitude**  - "the future punishment God has shown us will happen will happen to everyone"
    * **as long as they live**  - The word "they" refers to the people of Israel who sell things.
    * **they will not return**  -  God is saying that the people who are taken from Israel will never return. Alternate translation: "The sellers will never come back to the land of Israel" (See: [[en:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:discourse:implicit-explicit]])
    * **no man living in his sin will be strengthened**  - and The word "strengthened" refers to God helping a person to keep going when things are hard through hope, encouragement and physical energy. "no one who keeps disobeying God will be helped by God."
