====== restore, restoration ======

===== Definition: =====

To restore means to bring someone or something back to a former state of health, as with an individual's body, or harmony, as between people who were divided.

  * The term "restoration" can also refer to a payment that needs to be made, for example to pay for an animal that was accidentally killed.
