====== minister ======


===== Definition: =====

Text

  * To minister is to serve. With regard to God, it includes acts of worship such as ministering before the LORD with offerings (Exodus 28:43). With regard to people, ministry is either to serve them spiritually, such as by sharing the gospel with them, or to serve them in physical ways, such as caring for the sick or hungry (Matthew 25).
  * (huperetes - as in Lk 1:2)
