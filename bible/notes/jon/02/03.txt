===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You threw **- The word "You" refers to Yahweh.
  * **into the heart of the seas **- "to the bottom of the sea"
  * **And the currents surrounded me **- "And the waters closed in around me"
  * **Passed over me**  - "came over me" or "crashed above me" (UDB)
  * **I am cast out from before your eyes**  - "You sent me away from your presence" (See: synechdoche)
