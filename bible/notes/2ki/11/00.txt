====== 2 Kings 11 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter is the  beginning of the story of the revival in Judah under Joash.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

The Queen Mother of Judah, Athaliah killed all the royal children so that she could rule. Except she did not kill the baby Joash because his aunt hid him in the temple; When Joash was seven years old, the chief priest conspired with the army and made Joash king. They killed the queen mother and destroyed the temple of Baal.
