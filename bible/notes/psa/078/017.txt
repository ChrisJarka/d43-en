===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Yet they continued to sin against him**  - In these verses, the pronoun "they" refers to the people of Israel.
  * **They challenged God**  - AT: "They tested God"
  * **in their hearts**  - See: Synecdoche
  * **appetites**  - AT: "desires"
