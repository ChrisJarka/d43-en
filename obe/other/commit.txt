====== commit, committed, commitment ======

===== Definition: =====
The terms "commit" and "commitment" refer to making a promise to do something and then keeping that promise.

  * Being "committed" means being devoted or faithful in carrying out what has been decided.
  * Another meaning of "commit" means to do a certain wrong action such as "commit a sin" or "commit adultery" or "commit murder."
