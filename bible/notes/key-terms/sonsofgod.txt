====== sons of God ======

===== Definition: =====

The title, "sons of God" is usually used in reference to angelic beings, although the reference in Genesis 6 might refer to the descendents of Seth or other powerful people. 

Possible meanings:
  * Angelic beings.
  * Fallen Angels.
  * Powerful political rulers.
  * Descendents of Seth.
