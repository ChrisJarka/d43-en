===== translationKeyboard =====

The translationKeyboard is a mobile app where users can create custom keyboards for languages not supported by their mobile device. You may download translationKeyboard for [[https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.distantshoresmedia.translationkeyboard&hl=en|Android devices]].
