===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Therefore I will make the heavens tremble, and the earth will be shaken out of its place, by the fury of Yahweh, the Commander of the angel armies, and on the day of his fierce anger.**  - "Yahweh, the Commander of the angel armies, will make the heavens tremble, and the earth shake our of its place, by his wrath on the day of his fierce anger."
    * **Like a hunted gazelle**  - Gazelles are thin, graceful antelopes that live in Africa and Asia. When running, gazelles use a bounding leap which involves stiffly springing into the air with all four feet. "All the foreigners in Babylon will run around like deer that are being hunted" (UDB)(See: [[:en:ta:translate:simile]])
    * **or like a sheep with no shepherd** - Sheep are not able to take care of themselves and without a shepherd are very easy prey. After Yahweh destroys Babylon, the people will have no leader. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:simile]])
