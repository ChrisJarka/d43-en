====== Psalms Chapter 131 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? How does David describe himself?

A. His heart is not proud nor his eyes haughty, he does not have great hopes for himself. [131:1]

Q? How does David describe his soul?

A. His soul is stilled and quieted like a weaned child. [131:2]

Q? In whom and for how long does David tell Israel to hope?

A. He tells them to hope in Yahweh now and forever. [131:3]
