====== How to Get Started ======

Now that you're ready to translate the Bible, it's time to open Door 43. This application is easy to use, but if you have questions you'll be able to learn from the Door43 user community.

===== Get connected to the Door43 community! =====

The Door43 community uses Hall to communicate. When you join this community you can learn about the benefits and challenges of this application and share your ideas with the developers as well as other translators. Hall works best with Chrome, Safari, FireFox, or IE 11 (or higher).

  * To join the Door43 user community in Hall, click [[https://hall.com/invites/Hl5sUFX5flazj1u7H3aCFg|here]].
  * To join the Translators Group in Hall, click [[https://hall.com/invites/4-0kfZ-9_0pi3FtB-dt7jA|here]].
