====== Judgment Day ======


===== Definition: =====


Judgment Day is a day that God has planned when He will bring all mankind before Him to examine how they lived their lives.


    * Judgment Day is known only to God.
    * The Apostle Paul preached and warned that a day was coming when God would judge all men by His Son Jesus Christ.
