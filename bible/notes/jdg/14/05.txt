===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Timnah**  - See how this was translated in [[en:bible:notes:jdg:14:01|14:1]].
  * **roaring** - the loud, deep cry of an angry lion
  * **came upon him** - This phrase refers to Yahweh's Spirit coming to help Samson by giving him strength. AT: "strengthened him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **he tore apart the lion as easily as he would have torn apart a small goat** - The lion is compared to a weak animal to express that it was easy for Samson to tear it apart. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **tore apart** - to tear into pieces
  * **had nothing in his hand**  - AT: "did not use a weapon"
