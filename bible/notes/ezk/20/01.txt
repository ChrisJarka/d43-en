===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It came to pass** - This phrase is used to mark the beginning of a new part of the story.
  * **In the seventh year, on the tenth day of the fifth month**  â€“ This phrase refers to the tenth day of the fifth month of the calendar that they used at the time and seven years since a specific point of history. (See: How to Translate Names & Biblical Times).
  * **inquire** - This word means "to ask." In the Bible, it means to go to a prophet and ask what God says about something.
  * **before me** - This word indicates "in front of someone," that is, face to face.
