===== translationNotes: =====

  * **risen up over his father's kingdom** - To "rise up over" something is an idiom that means to control it. AT: "taken complete control over his father's kingdom" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **he killed all his brothers with the sword** - Jehoram probably did not kill them personally, but had other people do it for him. AT: "he caused all of his younger brothers to be killed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
