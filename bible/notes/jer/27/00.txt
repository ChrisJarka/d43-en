====== Jeremiah 27 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== The nations should serve Babylon ==

Jeremiah told the king of Israel and all the surrounding nations to serve the Babylonians and live in the land. If they refused Babylon would take them as captives to another land. 


=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphor ==

Jeremiah compared serving Babylon with an ox wearing a yoke so as to pull something. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
