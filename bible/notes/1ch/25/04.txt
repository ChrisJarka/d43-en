===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This continues the list of leaders of the tabernacle work which was started in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:25:01|25:2]].

  * **Bukkiah ... Mahazioth** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Heman** - See how you translated this man's name in [[:en:bible:notes:1ch:16:40|16:41]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **fourteen sons and three daughters** - "14 sons and 3 daughters" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **to honor him** - The word "him" refers to Heman.
