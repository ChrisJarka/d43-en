====== church ======

===== Definition: =====

In the Bible, the term "church" refers to a local group of believers in Jesus who regularly meet together to pray and hear God's word preached. It does not refer to a building.
 
  * Sometimes this term is used to refer to all believers everywhere in the whole body of Christ.
  * The original meaning of the word for "church" in the Bible is "called out (to meet together)." It refers to an "assembly" or "gathering."

===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * The term "church" could be translated as a "gathering together" or "assembly" or "congregation" or "ones who meet together."
  * Make sure that the term used to translate "church" can also be used to refer to all believers, not just one small group.
  * Make sure that the word used to translate this term does not just refer to a building.
  * Consider how this term is translated in a local or national Bible translation. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:translateunknowns|How to Translate Unknowns]])
