====== Hebrews 09 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter discusses how Jesus is superior to the Temple and its religious system of regulations. This chapter will be difficult to understand if the first five books of the Old Testament have not yet been translated.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Will ==
A will is a legal document that declares what will happen to a person's property after they die.

== Blood ==
Blood played an important role in the Mosaic and New Covenants. In the Mosaic Covenant, it was the blood of the sacrifices which was significant. In the New Covenant, it is the blood of Jesus that is much more important by comparison.

== Return of Christ ==
The purpose of the return of Christ is to ultimately [[:en:obe:kt:redeem|redeem]] the world.

=== Other possible translation difficulties in this chapter ===

== First covenant ==
This si a reference to the covenant made with Moses. Although it is not the "first" covenant, it is the earlier covenant being compared to the New Covenant in this chapter.
