====== Psalms 144 General Notes ======


=== Kind of psalm ===

Psalm 144 is a worship psalm: the first worship psalm in the final seven worship psalms. (Psalms 144-150)

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

Psalm 144 is a prayer for safety and prosperity for the nation.
