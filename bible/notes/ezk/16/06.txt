===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues to speak about Jerusalem as an unwanted baby.
  * **But I passed by you** - Yahweh is walking near the newborn baby.
  * **writhing in your blood** - "struggling in your own blood"
  * **I made you grow like a plant in a field** - The growth of Jerusalem is compared to the growth of a plant. AT: "I made you grow quickly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **you became a jeweled ornament** - This phrase describes Jerusalem as beautiful like a jeweled ornament is beautiful.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **your breasts became firm and your hair grew thick** - Both of these phrases are used to describe a young adult woman. 
  * **naked and bare** - Both of these words refer to someone who isn't wearing clothing. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
