===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Who has planned this against Tyre** - Isaiah uses a question to mock Tyre. Also, "Tyre" refers to the people who live in Tyre. Alternate translation: "Someone has planned this against the people of Trye." (See: [[en:ta:translate:rhetoricalquestion]] and [[en:ta:translate:metonymy]])
  * **the giver of crowns** – Here "crown" refers to the power a person has as a ruler over people. Alternate translation: “who gives people power to rule over others"
  * **whose merchants are princes, whose traders are the honored ones of the earth** - "The merchants are treated like kings because of their power and influence." (See: [[en:ta:translate:metaphor]])
  * **Yahweh of Hosts has planned it** - Yahweh, Commander of the angel armies, is greater than Tyre.
  * **dishonor her pride and all her glory** - "to humble her"
  * **to shame all her honored ones of the earth**  – "to make weak those the world honors"
