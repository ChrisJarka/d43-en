====== Mesopotamia ======

===== Facts: =====

Mesopotamia is the area between the Tigris and Euphrates Rivers in the location of the modern day Iraq.

  * In New Testament times, the area was called Mesopotamia.
  * In the Old Testament, it was a region where Abram lived before he moved to Haran and is referred to as Ur.
