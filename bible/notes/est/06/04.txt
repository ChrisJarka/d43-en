===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Haman** - Translate this man's name as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:est:03:01|3:1]]
  * **the outer courtyard** - "the first courtyard from the outside"
  * **hanging Mordecai** - It can be made clear what the purpose of hanging him was. AT: "killing Mordecai by hanging him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]
  * **the gallows he has set up for him** - The gallows was a structure on which people were killed by hanging them. AT: "the structure he had set up for hanging him. 
  * **set up** - "built"
  * **said in his heart** - AT: "thought to himself" or "said to himself" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Whom would the king take pleasure in honoring more than me?** - "Surely there is no one whom the king would take greater pleasure in honoring more than me!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
