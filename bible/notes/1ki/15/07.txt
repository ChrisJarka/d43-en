===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The Book of the Events of the Kings of Judah?**  - See 14:29 (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **Abijah slept with his ancestors**  - AT: "Abijah died" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism|Euphemism]])
  * **they buried him**  - "They" refers to people in charge of burying him.
  * **became king in his place**  - AT: "became king after him"
