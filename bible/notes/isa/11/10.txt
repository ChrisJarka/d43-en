===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **On that day** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:02:09|2:11]].
  * **the root of Jesse** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:11:01|11:1]].
  * **will stand as a banner for the peoples** - This compares the Messiah to a flag. People would put flags up to send messages to people who were far away. This emphasizes the Messiah is sending a message for everyone to come home. Alternate translation: "will stand like a flag high up on a hill and he will call all the peoples to him." (See: [[en:ta:translate:simile]])
  *  **his resting-place** - "his dwelling place" or "where he lives" (UDB). This refers to Jerusalem.
  *  **the Lord will again extend his hand to recover the remnant of his people** - “The Lord will again use his power to bring back the remnant of his people”
  *  **Pathros... Elam ... Hamath** - These are names of places. (See: [[en:ta:translate:translatenames]])
