====== slaughter ======

===== Definition: =====

To slaughter is to mercilessly attack and kill.

  * In the book of the Prophet Ezekiel God sent His angel to slaughter all those who would not follow His word.
  * 1 Samuel records a great slaughter where 30,000 Israelites were killed by their enemies because of disobedience to God.
