===== Update Languages in translationStudio-Desktop =====

//This answers the question:// //How can I add new target languages?//

//In order to understand this topic, it would be good to read://
  * //[[en:ta:vol2:tech:tsd_navigate]]//


=== From the Home window: ===

  * Click on the 3-dot menu icon.
  * Click on to choose the words "Update Languages" found at the top of the menu. 
    * The Update window opens and the process begins.
  * Click on 'CLOSE' when the process ends.

=== From the Translation Project window: ===

You cannot update the languages while working on a project. You must return to the "Your Translation Projects" screen.

  * Click on the 3-dot menu icon.
  * Click on the 'Home' menu item.
