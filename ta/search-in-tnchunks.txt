===== How to do a Search in Notes Pages =====

One way of finding examples for translationAcademy pages is to search the notes for chunks that have the topic in named in the notes. Here is the code for it.

{door43pages @:en:bible:notes -r -q="TEXT" -exclude:home -simpleLineBreak -title -fontsize="90%"}


=== How to use this ===
Click the edit button. \\
Change the word TEXT to the word or phrase you want to find. \\
Add additional curly brackets at beginning and end.\\ 
Click the preview button to see the links. \\
If you want, copy the links to the discussion section of the page you're working on. \\
Click the Cancel button on this page, so someone else can use it.
