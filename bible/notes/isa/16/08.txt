===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Heshbon…Sibmah…Jazer**- These are names of cities. (See: How to Translate Names)
  * **The rulers of the nations have trampled the choice vines**- The "choice vines" here are used to describe the people Moab. (See: Personification)
  * **that reached to Jazer and spread into the desert. Its shoots spread abroad; they went over to the sea**- "The people of Moab have scattered to the desert and the far west"
