===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **every skilled man**- AT: every man with a skill
  * **covering -**:something to hide or protect an object
  * **clasp **- something to join parts of objects together
  * **post - **  a straight support placed in the ground
  * **base -**  an object on which a post rests
  * **lid **- a cover for a container
  * **conceal **- to hide
