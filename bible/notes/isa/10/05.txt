===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Ah, Assyrian**  - Yahweh is addressing one warrior but is actually talking to the entire the army of Assyria. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:personification|Personification]])
  * **the club of my anger, the rod by whom I wield my fury**  - Yahweh is referring to the army as a "club" and a "rod"; instruments that He will use to punish other nations. (See: [[:en:ta:translate:personification|Personification]]) (See: [[:en:ta:translate:doublet|Doublet]])
