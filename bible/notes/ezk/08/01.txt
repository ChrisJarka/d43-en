===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **elders**  - These were the old and experienced people in Israel.
  * **figure like a man**  - This is the shape or form of a person's body. (See: Simile).
  * **downwards**  - This tells direction–"down."
  * The word “I” refers to Ezekiel. “Me” refers to Ezekiel. “I looked” refers to Ezekiel.
  * **glowing**  - In the case of "glowing metal", the reference is to metal which is so hot it glows white.
